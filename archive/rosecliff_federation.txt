The geography of Rosecliff is marked with calm coasts hedged by towering cliffs, tall spruces, and sprawling fields and fjords; clean air and environmental policies maintain Rosecliff, and the many nations work together to produce policies that work in the interest of preserving for future generations. 

Rosecliff Federation, a serene, peaceful region, is known internationally as a refuge for those wishing to rule nations in a sanctuary of democracy and justice. Founded originally by the sovereign nation of Maemont, the states present within the Rosecliff Federation are dedicated to making the region a more diverse and welcoming community. There is always room for nations dedicated to producing a more beautiful and tolerant world.
