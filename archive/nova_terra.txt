[b]|[u]Welcome to Nova Terra[/u]|[/b]



The lands of Nova Terra are renowned for their vast mountain ranges, sprawling forests, and hilly landscape. The region is made up of 4 great landmasses, surrounded by numerous smaller islands. A large portion of the North-West is currently occupied by [nation]Saint Riviera[/nation], which keeps a watchful eye over the surrounding lands. The South-East is occupied by the [nation]The United Districts of Rania[/nation] and its surrounding satellite states. Nova Terra is open to a wide spectrum of political orientations, and does not itself represent any one philosophy.
