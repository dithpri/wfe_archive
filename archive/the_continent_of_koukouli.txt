This is an isolated, enclosed and exclusive region.

A continent of unexplored territories, diverse peoples and rich history, Koukouli is home to a few nations struggling to survive and flourish. Its inhabitants have little to do with the outside world, and maintain little contact with nations outside of the continent. As conflicting interests and differing ideologies meet, the story of Koukouli is written.
