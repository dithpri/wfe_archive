the [b]Walfried Union[/b] (shortened form of [b]The Peaceful Democratic Union of the Holy Walfried Lateranius[/], also [b]P.D.U. Holy Walfried L.[/b]) is the only Walfridian Union. It is governed by the [i]Walfried Union Government[/i], which was founded by the [url=http://www.nationstates.net/the_land_of_the_holy_walfried_lateranius]the Land of the Holy Walfried Lateranius[/url].
