This region is the overseer of operations under [nation]New Laikland[/nation]. Einherjar will sometimes participate in raids against leftist regions, securing them to prevent them from falling, but is not directly involved in the right political movement. 


The Einherjer are the guardians of Valhalla. They are the fallen warriors who were chosen by Odin himself to be taken there, and prepare day by day for the events of Ragnarok.
