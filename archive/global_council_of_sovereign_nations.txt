Global Council of Sovereign Nations

Region Overview
The Global Council of Sovereign Nations is a post-World War roleplay region focused on realistic geopolitical storytelling, diplomacy, reconstruction, military strategy, economics, and international tension in a fractured modern world. Nations rise from the ashes of global conflict to rebuild alliances, project power, and shape a new international order.
