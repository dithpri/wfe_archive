The SMCP is a partnership of townships with mutual socioeconomic goals and progressive like minded values. The coastal geographic region is made up of nine townships that inhabit the southern tip of the state of ME. Referred to as boroughs the nine township&#39;s encompassing the SMCP share resources and community practices. The SMCP has a beneficial economy focusing on tourism and fishing industries. Leaders plan tax usage around educational and environmental investments and ensure that public infrastructure and crime prevention are top priority. Civil rights are sacred however tax rates remain high because of programs for public transport, social services, animal rights, and historical preservation.
