The Nations of the New Right is a region built upon principles of the New Right in Mattantia, its dominions in the Republican Commonwealth, and other surrounding regions, with ideologies across the right-wing authoritarian spectrum.

The NNR adheres to a new form of political conservatism, with an economically libertarian, socially moderate to authoritarian, and societally reactionary outlook on politics.

Political positions: Centre-right to far-right

Ideologies:
Conservatism
Fascism
Libertarianism
