The [i]Iberian Peninsula[/i], also known as [i]Iberia[/i], is located in the southwest corner of Europe. The peninsula is principally divided between Portugal and Spain, comprising most of their territory. It also includes Andorra, and a small part of France along the peninsula&#39;s northeastern edge, as well as Gibraltar on its south coast. With an area of approximately 582,000 km2 (225,000 sq mi), it is the second largest European peninsula, after the Scandinavian.
