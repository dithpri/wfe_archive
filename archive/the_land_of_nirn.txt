It is the Very Beginning of the Fifth Era, Aldmeri Dominion, Cyrodiil Empire, Ebonheart Pact, and Daggerfall Covenant have all been re-established.  It has been years since a Dragonborn has been seen, and Hermaeus Mora has now teamed up with Molag Bal to control the Cyrodill empire and try to unite Tamriel under their rule.
