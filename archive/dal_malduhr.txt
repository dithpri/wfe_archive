The region of Dal Malduhr was founded after an attempt at launching a space-based defense platform went horribly awry. The resulting explosion leveled the entire region, and the survivors founded a new civilization. The surrounding nations saw the explosion as an opportunity to grab more land, and as a result the survivors have been in a state of almost constant war. The surrounding nations refuse to acknowledge the existence of Dal Malduhr, and the survivors are holding out the best they can
