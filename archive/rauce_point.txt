Rauce Point (pronounced ROSS), formally the Military Region of Rauce Point, is a dependency of the nation of [nation]Aurimel[/nation]. Due to its strategic value, the area is under the military administration of the Armed Forces of Aurimel. The military administration is headed by the Commander in Chief of Aurimelian Forces in Rauce Point, who reports directly to the Minister of Defense of Aurimel. 

Within Rauce Point are 10 military bases numbered from one to ten. Each base and its immediate surrounding lands are constituted as Military Governates under the administration of Military Governors appointed and subordinate to the Commander in Chief of Aurimelian Forces in Rauce Point.

(Founded on February 25, 2024)
