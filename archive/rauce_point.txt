Rauce Point (pronounced &quot;ROH-ss&quot;), is a military outpost of the nation of [nation=noflag]Aurimel[/nation]. The region is divided into 10 Military Governorates with military governors appointed by the Aurimelian government. Each military governor is the Commander-in-Chief of all forces and the supreme civil and military authority within their respective governorate, all the while subordinate and answerable directly to the Commander-in-Chief of the Aurimelian Armed Forces.
