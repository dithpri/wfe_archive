Welcome to the United Alliance, a nation roleplaying region. Carve out an empire for yourself with cunning diplomacy, intrigue, and military prowess in our 1920s theme.

After the [url=https://www.nationstates.net/page=dispatch/id=1419990][b]Great War[/b][/url], the world entered a period of instability and hardship as the defeated nations worked to rebuild, while the victorious Alliance continued to prosper.  The Treaty of San Cleare promised the world a lasting peace, but as time goes on it will become clear that the world is poised to descend into chaos once again.
