[b][color=red]Welcome[/color][color=gold] to [/color]Angola![/b]

The [b]Republic of Angola[/b] is a country in [region]Southern Africa[/region] bordered by [region]Namibia[/region] on the south, the [region]Democratic Republic of the Congo[/region] on the north, and [region]Zambia[/region] on the east; its west coast is on the [region]Atlantic Ocean[/region] and [region]Luanda[/region] is its capital city. It is the largest &amp;amp; wealthiest of the Portuguese-speaking African states, and Portuguese influences have been felt for some 500 years. An anticolonial struggle that began in 1961 finally led to independence in 1975.

Feel free to frolic in the Benguela Current or meditate on top of Mount Moco.
