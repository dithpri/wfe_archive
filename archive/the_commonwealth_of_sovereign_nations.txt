Founded as a means of bringing about true world peace and unity, the Commonwealth of Sovereign Nations (C.S.N.) is a multinational peacekeeping organization; unifying nations under a single banner and government.

[b]The Organization of the Commonwealth[/b]
The Commonwealth of Sovereign Nations works based on a democratic system in which every member state has an equal vote and say in the running of the Commonwealth. To that end, any member state may bring forward a proposition to the Secretary-General of the Commonwealth to be voted on.
[b]In all votes, the side with a 75% majority of the votes shall win the vote.[/b]
