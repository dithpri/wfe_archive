A triple star system in the constellation Cetus consisting of a K-type main sequence star and two smaller red dwarfs. Home system of the sentient tabulons, lordions, and mortei, who refer to the system as Ingffhaestia; Gliese 105 and its three nations form the centerpieces of the &quot;Legend of Urlum the Evil&quot; sci-fi universe. 

All nations here are future tech, managed by the same person, and do not reflect IRL beliefs.
