[b]Introduction[/b]

The Authoritarian Coalition of Countries focuses on benefiting the lives and conditions of numerous people living under a fair and reasonable authoritarian rule. The coalition also seeks to improve the economy and military of its members.

[b]What are the requirements for joining?[/b]
[list=1]
[*] Your government must be be an authoritarian or a dictatorship.
[*] It is recommended to join the World Assembly, but this is completely optional.
[*] Endorse of the nations in this region to help us choose a WA Delegate.
[/list]
