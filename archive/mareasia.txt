Mareasia is a region that is west of [region]Equestria[/region], and borders [region]The Changling Kingdom[/region]. It is a region created by a brony, for bronies.

Mareasia was founded by [nation]Amareica[/nation].

Mareasia will welcome anyone who wish to move their nation here. Mareasia is basically a brony region, though it will also accept any non-bronies, just no haters. Just click the move nation name to Mareasia button to move your nation here. Nation name is the name of your nation if you didn&amp;#39;t know. So if your nation is called Equestria, it will say move Equestria to Mareasia.
