Whalers and explorers of the South Atlantic, unite!

Founded in 1811 by Jonathan Lambert and subsequently populated by maritime pilgrims and colonists, the Isles are a land of open seas; rocky, windswept crags; and sparse population, however, the states dotting its myriad of small atolls and islands have their own unique identities, many as former British Crown colonies liberated through democracy, and many as self-proclaimed kingdoms and empires by their founders.
