[b]Mumbai[/b], also known as Bombay (the official name until 1995), is the capital city of the Indian state of Maharashtra. It is the most populous city in India and the ninth most populous agglomeration in the world, with an estimated city population of 18.4 million. 

Along with the neighbouring regions of the Mumbai Metropolitan Region, Mumbai is one of the most populous urban regions in the world and the se&amp;#1089;ond most populous metropolitan area in India, with a population of 20.7 million as of 2011. &#151;Wikipedia[hr]
