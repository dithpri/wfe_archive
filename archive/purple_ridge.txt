Purple Ridge is a few mountains in the greater Blue Ridge chain that emit a distinct purple glow at sunrise and sunset. Here, the noble Militiamen of Blue Ridge conducted their anti-fascist and artistic military endeavors before the construction of Fort Joaquin was completed in the valley below. Now the region rests as a peaceful reminder of the Militia&#39;s past, ready to be used again should duty call.
