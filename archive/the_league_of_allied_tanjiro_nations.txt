On this date of March 25, 2022, we three the Republic of Purpleskye, the Federation of Tyrannospotens, and the Commonwealth of Blunight formally establish The League of Allied Tanjiro Nations. 

All nations of the said region are, and shall henceforth be considered as, allied in causes, major foreign relations, environmental regulations, and foremost founding beliefs. 

By signing below, all nations agree to the following of the above core principles and the subsequent rulings passed by a majority vote. We, the nations below, agree to the formation of a more perfect union and the bettering of our nations, region, and beyond this, the world. 

Commonwealth of Blunight      Republic of Purpleskye      Federation of Tyrannospotens
