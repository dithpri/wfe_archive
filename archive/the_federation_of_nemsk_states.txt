Founded by the proud [nation]Rodina Naskaya[/nation] as a way of consolidating the power of her many smaller members, the Federation of Nemsk States is a political entity made up of nations that share the common language of Nemsk. Nations that are part of this federation are required to use Nemsk as their primary language, but there are no restrictions against multiple national languages. The Federation of Nemsk States operates in the best interests of her constituents and decisions are made democratically by her delegates.
