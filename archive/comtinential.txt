Comtinential is a region located in the North-Western Hemisphere. It is comprised of several subcontinents and were originally used as colony sites for other nations. Over time it has developed into several nations that change their alliance statuses regularly. It has the presence of an alien race in the region, causing tension. The Commons Group is the most powerful, Columbata is the most active in attempting to increase its power, and Alphnorn attempts to remain neutral.
