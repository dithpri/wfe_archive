An untouched piece of paradise discovered by Blueberry Island citizens. It was said to be inhabited by an unknown tribe thousands of years ago, but due to a volcanic activity, the tribe was forced to evacuate the area. Now, the 3 volcanoes that is located in this region had been carefully examined and experts say that 2 of these volcanoes are dormant and one is extinct. 

This region is truly a paradise of its own from countless white sand beaches to volcanoes, you can never get tired of exploring this beauty.
