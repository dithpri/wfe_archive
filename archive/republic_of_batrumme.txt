The word &quot;Batrumme&quot; is derived from the ancient tribal word Ba&#39;th&#39;room - which translates literally to &quot;Place where there is water.&quot;

Batrumme is a sovereign republican federation of the states of Batrumme, The Tri-Cities, Synck, Shaur-Malmo, Mercii, Ajaxgigantes, San Enten &amp; Monokoni - founded in the Constitution of the Republic of Batrumme.
