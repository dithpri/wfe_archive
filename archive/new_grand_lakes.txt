New Grand Lakes is a region split into four separate nations. These nations include Emera which has the largest population and the most regional influence, Demottrea which has the largest and most powerful military and economy in the region, Vatisen which is the conservative religious center of the region and Hobbitshen which is a small community of like-minded, quiet and peaceful people.
