Peniche is a seaside region. Peniche is sought for aquatic sports. Peniche, with its typical harbour, white windmills, chapels and large sand beaches. Since ancient times Peniche has been an important fishing harbour. Besides fisheries, the economy of Peniche municipality relies on agriculture, services and tourism.
