After being forced into a political corner by President Nannita Bacco of the United Federation of Planets during the Borg invasion of the alpha and beta quadrants the Tholian Assembly made a Pact with other rivals of the Khitomer Alliance: The Romulan Star Empire, The Breen Confederacy, The Tzenkethi Coalition, The Gorn Hegemony and The Holy Order of the Kinshaya in which no Government would less valued than the other, all technology and resources would be shared as well as one goal:  To see the Federation and her allies destroyed.
