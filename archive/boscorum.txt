Boscorum means &quot;of the woods&quot;, it is located in a heavily forested area of the world. Due to these geographical reasons, as well as a protection of their natural environment, the country of Filictum has banned all cars. The Empire of Aldburg has finished its complete expansion of the northern portion of the region. Neighboring countries live in constant fear of an invasion. [nation]Aldburgh[/nation] is also a nuclear power.
