This is a region for anyone to join just looking for a fun game and to gain some political power.

Here in [region]The One and Only[/region], politics are extremely critical. With 4 political parties, a Senate, and a Regional Council, there is something for every new politician. Everything comes at a price however. Senators can be relived of their duties for improper conduct by a public court case.

The One and Only Senate will consist of 5 seats, one of which will be the Head of the Senate.

We welcome ALL nations, there is a place for everyone within [region]The One and Only[/region]!
