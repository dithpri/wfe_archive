The One and Only is a democratic region within the world of NationStates . Once founded by the nation Grand Unionata long ago in the year 2017, the original region was a bastion of democracy during a troubled time in NS history. Eventually reaching up to 30 nations at its peak, the growing region began a steady decline once its founder ceased to exist, and eventually the region itself ceased to exist.

This region is a spiritual continuation of that, seeking to provide a beacon of hope in the darkness that other nations can rally to.
