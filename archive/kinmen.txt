The islands of Kinmen &amp;#37329;&amp;#38272; serve as the last bastion of Republic of China (ROC) rule outside of Taiwan, and the closest ROC-held territory to mainland China.

Despite being only 10km from the mainland, its people have persevered and survived the onslaught of communist attacks for the past 70 years.
The islands are a testament to how the desire for liberty will always triumph over the brutality of communism; no matter the odds, freedom will flourish.
