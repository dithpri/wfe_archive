Chiron is a solar system located 13.8 Light Years  away from the Sol System containing the planet Earth.

There are a total of 9 planets, with a asteroid belt between Chiron Delta and Chiron Echo. And past Chiron Hotel, is an another belt of icy rocks, called the Yankee belt, and the final system of objects surrounding Chiron is a massive cloud of uncollapsed stardust, called the Zulu Cloud.

Chiron Charlie, and Delta have intelligent life already found on there. A race of minotaur like creatures, called Chirs, and the race of the Chons, a Centaur like creatures. Both have space flight capabilities, and often have wars about which is the best race.
