We the members of this union, grateful to Almighty God, solemnly proclaim our attachment to the Rights of Man and the principles of national Sovereignty, in order to form a more perfect society, establish justice, domestic fortification, and autonomy. 
By the morality of the said proposition and that of the self-determined people, this Sovereignty offers to the territories of member nations which have expressed the will to adhere to them new establishments found on the Delegate ideal of liberty, equality, and freedom and conceived for the purpose of their democratic development.
