The [b]Kingdom of Khotan[/b] is a Buddhist Saka state located on the southern Silk Road. Founded around 300 BCE by settlers with legendary ties to India&#146;s Mauryan Empire, Khotan served as a vital cultural bridge between India, China, and Persia. Renowned for its nephrite jade and early silk production, Khotan is a major center for Mahayana Buddhism and Sanskrit learning for over a millennium.
