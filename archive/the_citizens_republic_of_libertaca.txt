Founded in the year 2017, The Citizens Republic of Libertaca is a bi-carmel constitutional republic.&nbsp;

It has both a bi-carmel legislature&nbsp;and executive branch, a major improvement for better adaptation to the digital world.&nbsp;

Freedom is held paramount in Libertaca, as well as citizen responsibility in response to those freedoms. It is a new region, open to newcomers and veterans alike. Libertaca is aiming to be a land of freedom and prosperity, a beacon of hope in the world.&nbsp;
