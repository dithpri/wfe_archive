The western part of the Continent of Moon Lake. But being in it does not mean peace or protection. Rather than an alliance or faction, Amynsok is simply an area, so wars may be held between the nations in here. In general, Amynsok is an area located in the Continent of Moon Lake. Moon Lake is a large lake surrounded by a ring-esc continent, with a center island inside of the lake. The island has sets of island going outwards in all north soud east and west directions, but not at any other angles. This ring and island make up the Continent of Moon Lake, of which Amynsok controls the majorly western part.
