Presidential Republic led by President Tirga Mor with Robot Utopia as Vice President and New Borland representing us in the WA. We are a region of differing ideas and thought, allowing any type of government, species or form of existence. 

The Badlands of the North prides itself on diversity and democracy, allowing the leadership of the region to be chosen by the people, not just the WA delegate but also the Senators and Vice President. 

We are an interactive region, having our own Olympics, Football, and American Football tournaments as well as frequent dispatch updates regarding our members
