This the Region of Kbobbles was founded to be an alliance formed out of choice and not necessity. While affirming every nations right to liberty and sovereignty while striving for the utmost in political alliances. All Nations within this Region have elected to join and were in no way coerced and or forced to by threat of force. The Region of The Kbobbles takes pride in its diverse nature, and accepts any form of government from Monarchy to Anarchy.
