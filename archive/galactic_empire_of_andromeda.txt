The Galactic Empire Is a galaxy far, far away that consists of many newly colonized planets. Each nation that you see is a planet that has colonized and has become independent. In the newly explored and colonized galaxy of Andromeda, There is a main group of planets that rule most territory. There is also a small republic of planets that are opposed to the Empire, and want to be separate from them. This group currently remains unnamed, as they are currently unorganized.
