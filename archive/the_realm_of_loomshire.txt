[b]The Realm of Loomshire is a kingdom consisting of all territories located in the Loomshire region, which is ruled by the patriarchy Huis van Ahlbrecht.[/b]

&#149; [b]Established September 24th, 2024 AD,[/b] the Huis van Ahlbrecht would gain control of the Loomshirean Realm after the assassination of chancellor M&uuml;ller.

&#149; [b]The realm is currently in the middle of a coup[/b], which is being lead by the Loomshirite Liberation Front in an attempt to bring down the current ruling party.
