[b][color=red]Marxism is a socio-economic philosophical theory that was defined to not only analyse the world, but to change it.[/color]

Marxism is based on a historical understanding of materialist dialectics, understood via the means of the class struggle between the bourgeoisie and the proletariat. It was a theory developed by the great German philosophers Karl Marx and Friedrich Engels, but has been developed considerably by many thinkers internationally.
