NATO, OTAN, &amp;#1581;&amp;#1604;&amp;#1601; &amp;#1575;&amp;#1604;&amp;#1606;&amp;#1575;&amp;#1578;&amp;#1608;, &amp;#21271;&amp;#32422;, &amp;#12490;&amp;#12488;&amp;#12540;, &amp;#2344;&amp;#2366;&amp;#2335;&amp;#2379;, also called the North Atlantic Alliance, is an intergovernmental military alliance based on the North Atlantic Treaty which was signed on 4 April 1949. The organization constitutes a system of collective defence whereby its member states agree to mutual defence in response to an attack by any external party.  NATO&#39;s headquarters are located in Washington, D.C, United States of Federal Americas, where the Supreme Allied Commander also resides. USFA is one of the members of nations across the world, the newest of which, South Africa, joined in April 2014.
