The Midwestern United States, also referred to as the [b]Midwest[/b], is one of four geographic regions defined by the United States Census Bureau. It occupies the northern central part of the United States of America and is located between the Northeastern U.S. and the Western U.S., with Canada to its north and the Southern U.S. to its south.

The Census Bureau&#39;s definition consists of 12 states in the north central United States: Illinois, Indiana, Iowa, Kansas, Michigan, Minnesota, Missouri, Nebraska, North Dakota, Ohio, South Dakota, and Wisconsin. &#151;Wikipedia[hr]
