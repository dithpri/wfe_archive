An alternative universe which contains the Nationstates NPC nations and the Sacrochan Continent. More information is listed in Factbooks. The population of each nation is 18.5% of its NS statistic. The GDP of each nation is 9.5% of its NS statistic. Before April 25, 2019, one real day is equal to 65.9638554 NS days. After April 25, 2019, one real day is equal to 25 NS days.
