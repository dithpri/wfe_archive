An alternative universe which contains the Nationstates NPC nations and the Sacrochan Continent. More information is listed in Factbooks. The formula to find the population of each nation is (current population statistic x 0.05)+275,500,000. The formula for the GDP of each nation is (current GDP statistic x .089)1.01. Before April 25, 2019, one real day is equal to 65.9638554 NS days. After April 25, 2019, one real day is equal to 25 NS days.
