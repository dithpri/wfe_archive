[b][region]The Berghof[/region] was a historic house in the Obersalzberg of the Bavarian Alps near Berchtesgaden, Bavaria. From the elevation of 921 m the view is astounding. It is here that Adolf maintained a regular life away from the theatrics of Berlin.
