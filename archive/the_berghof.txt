[b]The Berghof is the mountain home of [nation]Der Fuhrer und Reichskanzler[/nation] in the Obersalzberg of the Bavarian Alps near Berchtesgaden, Bavaria. From the elevation of 921 m the view is astounding. It is here that the Fuhrer maintains a regular life away from the theatrics of Berlin.

[nation]Johann Flussbruecke[/nation] is the spokesman for [region]The Berghof[/region][/b]
