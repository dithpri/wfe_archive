Welcome to the Buccaneers&#39; Seas!

A roleplay focused region set in the 18th century with some fantasy elements (krakens, curses, sirens, etc.) in a fictional Earth-like world. Focusing on naval action between Empires, pirates and new independent states. Everyone is welcome here and everyone is important and will go down in history in this region, no matter if a grand admiral of an Empire or a free pirate captain of a Brig.
