A world of nations, cities and empires who seek to prove themselves amongst the rest. By peace, or by war. 

The year is 1960 and technology is becoming huge!

This is a Roleplay Centered region that seeks to add both character interaction, nation roleplay and even outside games like Arma 3, HOI4 and more. All to improve the experience of those here. Nations are encouraged to write factbooks and flesh out there nation so that better interaction can happen. 

If you wish to have your nations army created in Arma 3 please let me know! Most nations should follow a &quot;Theme&quot; of a current IRL nation so that it is easier to find items and equipment for you nation.
