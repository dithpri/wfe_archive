Zygorge is a truly free region. All nations are free to do as they please, although not all regional members have to this region is mostly based around roleplaying as the leader of your nation in the region of Zygorge. You can form alliances, wage wars or just go off and do your own thing. You can use either the regional chat or discord located at https://discord.gg/JpqnEh

When/if you choose to enter the discord, please identify which nation you are so that the region can be mapped out efficiently without any problems.
