The Northern Defense Coalition (NDC), founded on April 7, 2025, in response to growing international threats, has its headquarters at Federation Defense House in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. Currently, there are no members, but the NDC aims to expand. Its military branches include the NDC Navy, NDC CyberDefense Division, and NDC Special Operations. The NDC operates on principles of mutual defense, regional stability, and collective sovereignty, focused on protecting northern territories and resources.
