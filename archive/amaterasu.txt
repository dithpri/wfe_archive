The region of Amaterasu is named after the the most important deity of the Shinto religion, the sun goddess Amaterasu Omikami (&#145;the great divinity illuminating heaven&#146;). She is the ruler of [i]Takama no Hara[/i] (the High Celestial Plain), the domain of the kami (spirits).
