The Union of Centralist Powers was founded The Federal Republic of Wysterya. The original members where The Federal Republic of Pria Chia, The Empire of Thomasear and The Colony of Noxterrya. They were later join by The Principality of Cabrera Cruz and The United Socialist States of Cyberse. These are the officers of the U.C.P and the permanent members states. They can only be removed by an unanimous vote. The U.C.P is a democracy unless in times of emergency where a unanimous vote by the permanent members gives the founder or however is deemed fit dictatorial powers. Permanent members also have veto powers, but if their veto gets veto by another permanent member then the veto is void. Any member can become an officer.
