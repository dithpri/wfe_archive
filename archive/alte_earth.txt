It is 2184, over 100 years have passed since the cataclysmic war of 2080 ended in the total collapse of the old world. over the course of a century long dark age many nations of the world have emerged as the &quot;re-developed world&quot; such as New Seoul, The Unified states of Mexica, Nu York and The Empire of New Germany. Life in the Re-Developed regions is highly advanced and all the modern conveniences of cybernetics, robot servitors, mega malls and net cafe&#39;s abound.
