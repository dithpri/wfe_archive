Welcome to The Sovereign Frontier.

A new world is built here.

The Sovereign Frontier is a community of independent nations united by ambition, loyalty, and the belief that no nation should be forgotten. We welcome nations of all ideologies, from peaceful democracies to ruthless empires, so long as they are willing to contribute to the strength and identity of the region.

We are not merely another region.

We are a frontier.

New nations are encouraged to participate, build alliances, join regional discussions, and shape the future of our world. Active nations will find opportunity, influence, and a place among the founding powers of a new age.
