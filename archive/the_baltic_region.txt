The Baltic Sea is an arm of the Atlantic Ocean that is enclosed by Denmark, Estonia, Finland, Breschiria (Fictional) Germany, Latvia, Lithuania, Belarus, Poland, Russia, Sweden, and the North and Central European Plain.

As the name suggests, this region is a pretty realistic one in premise (with a touch of whimsy), and the ones present are usually here for the sake of lore, but feel free to insert your nation wherever you feel comfortable!
