After a large uprising took place across the majority of the United States, the country fell under a Temporary Military Confederacy. While the majority of the leading body was in agreement over the fact that a greater focus should be on the preservation of morality and the human species, they were split on the approach as to how, and the US now finds itself fractured internally, all nation-states technically under the rule of the Free Eastern States, but in reality free in all but name and defense.
