The Isles of MUN are composed of a vast series of island. These islands are home to numerous city-states as well as a few empires.  The nations in this region oppose the World Assembly and have create their own council known as The United Islands to deal with any issues that they may face. The islands have a plentiful supply of water an the best from of military might is a navy.  The most common issues of the region are scarcity of precious resources, internal conflict, foreign war with other regions, pollution (mostly from Wachovian), and rapidly growing populations.
