After earth was left polluted and war-torn, Earth sent out ships to spread humanity to the stars. Solitarius Orbem, the Lone World, was colonized in one such attempt. After soaring through space for over 200 years, many colony ships reached their destination and started the difficult process of colonizing their new world. As technology and political structures developed, all that came from earth was an eerie static. 114 years after the first planet-fall, the greatest of these nations have come together to form a coalition.
