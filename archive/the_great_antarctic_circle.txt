The Region known as The Great Antarctic Circle was started by the Penguins that founded Skinland that also wanted to hold on to their motherland. The Great Circle is home to many penguins, and is willing to take on humans that they deem acceptable, meaning, that the humans not be destructive to the penguins and their resources,are willing to negotiate with The Penguins, and respect the Penguins ideals of Equality and Unity.
