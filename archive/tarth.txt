[b]Tarth[/b] is an island in the narrow sea off the eastern coast of Westeros. It is separated from the continent by the Straits of Tarth, and is situated northeast of Shipbreaker Bay. Tarth is considered a part of the stormlands and is ruled from Evenfall Hall by House Tarth. It is sworn to Storm&#39;s End.

The island is said to be very beautiful, &quot;with its mountains and waterfalls, its high meadows and shadowed vales.&quot; It is called the Sapphire Isle for the striking blue seas in which it sits. &#151;[url=http://awoiaf.westeros.org/index.php/Tarth][color=003366][b]Tarth[/url][/color][/b][hr]
