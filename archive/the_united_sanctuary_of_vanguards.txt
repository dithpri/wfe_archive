A small nation that follows a monarchical system of government. United Sanctuary is stated to be a holy nation, which combines old traditions with scientific technology.

United Sanctuary and the Dragon Empire used to be one Nation known as the Grand Sanctuary.  However, after a civil war, the country split into those two Nations.
