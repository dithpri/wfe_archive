Deus Keyes is a long stretch of very large Islands, the largest of which is the Island and Nationstate of Galliont, which is 2.5 million square miles. The Keyes span such a large distance that the northern islands, which include Galliont, experience moderate-arctic temperatures. Southern Islands experience Tropical and warm temperatures.

Every Nationstate recognized by the Governor must officially declare that they will follow the teachings of the Church when performing their duties.
