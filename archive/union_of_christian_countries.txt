     The Union of Christian Countries exists to provide spiritual support, pastoral counseling, as well as edification for its members through the daily exposition of the Word of God.
     As it is a typically Christian Region, its members or countries must profess the Protestant Christian faith, being relevant to the Congregational, Baptist, Presbyterian, Lutheran, Methodist, Assembly or Anglican segments.
     As a political spectrum, we are right-wing, and we reject any left-wing ideologies, such as: Decriminalization of drugs, Same-sex marriage, legalization of abortion and similar things.
