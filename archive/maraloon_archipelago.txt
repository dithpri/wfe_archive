Geography: Seven main islands and 231 others, including Isla Solara (solar observatories and rainforests), Isla Viento (windsurfing and kiteboarding), and Isla Marina (marine biodiversity).

History and Culture: Discovered by Polynesian navigators, later visited by European explorers in the 1700s. Known for legends and rich cultural heritage.

Economy: Tourism, organic farming, and sustainable fishing are major industries.

Points of Interest: Solar observatories, wellness retreats, stargazing locations, eco-villages, marine sanctuaries, and ancient ruins.
