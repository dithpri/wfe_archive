New Terra is an Earth-like World in the Terra Mater system, located approximately 69.73 Lightyears from Sol.

Terra Mater houses a G-class star which is remarkably similar to Sol, having the same mass and almost the same radius, although the solar system is considerably younger, at only 888 Million years old.
New Terra is slightly less massive than Earth, coming in at 0.972 Earth Masses, and slightly smaller, with a radius of 6,082km.

It does, however, have slightly higher Gravity than Earth, with surface Gravity being approximately 1.07G.
Its atmosphere is unusually thick, at exactly 7 times the atmospheric pressure of Earth.
The planet is also notably warmer than Earth, with the average temperature up 31c.
