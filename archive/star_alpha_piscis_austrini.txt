Fomalhaut, also designated as &amp;#945; Piscis Austrini (alpha Piscis Austrini), is a variable main-sequence star of spectral class A3 in the constellation of Piscis Austrinus. Fomalhaut visual magnitude is 1.16, making it the 17th brightest star in the sky. Because of its brightness, Fomalhaut is clearly visible to the naked eye when observed from locations with dark skies, and should be also quite easily visible from light polluted areas.
