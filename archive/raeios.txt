Welcome to Raeios, a continent located to the far south of Westeros. A land abundant in the same bloodshed that covers Westeros; a blood shed for a seat upon the Glass Throne. Kings have set their sights upon the Glass Throne of Raeios in hopes of one day ruling the continent.

Pledge allegiance to a ruling house or set out to make your house known. Help control cities or control cities of your own. Make your way to the Glass Throne and rule Raeios!
