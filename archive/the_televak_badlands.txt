What was once a region torn by the revolution over a tyrannical king. What was once a wasteland where the few survived to build their own nations. What was once a destined barren world. Now is... The Televak Badlands.

History of the Region: The Free States coalition and Neogregoria were once the oppressed people under a horrible leader. After years of tension and back alley gorilla fights, A revolution was inevitable. Mass explosions, bio-weaponry, and assassinations would turn the once long unending fields of urban cityscapes into a wasteland. After years of rebuilding and planting, nations of people formed under their own flags have split the region. They are different, but they are united...
