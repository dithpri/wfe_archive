Gujarat is a western coastal region, bordered by Pakistan to the northwest and India to the east, north and south. The Arabian Sea lies to the west and southwest. Its capital is Gandhinagar, while Ahmedabad is the largest city and economic hub.

Known as the &quot;Land of the Lions and Legends,&quot; Gujarat has a rich cultural heritage, from the ancient city of Dwarka to the Harappan sites of Lothal and Dholavira. It is an industrial powerhouse, famous for textiles, petrochemicals, and diamond cutting, while also being home to Gir National Park, the last refuge of the Asiatic lion.
