Gujarat is a state on the western coast of India &#150; and a population of 60.4 million. Its capital city is Gandhinagar, while its largest city is Ahmedabad.

The Gujarati people of India are indigenous to the state and their language, Gujarati, is the state&#39;s official language.

The state traditionally has low unemployment and is widely considered one of the most industrially developed states of India and a manufacturing hub.
