[b]The single party, the secret police, the public displays of Ceasarism, even the presence of a Leader are not necessarily attributes of Fascism. The famous fascist methods are constantly revised and will continue to be revised. More important than the mechanism is the idea which Fascism has created for itself of man and freedom. With another name, another face, and nothing which betrays the projection from the past, in the form of a child we do not recognize the head of a young Medusa, [color=#0000FF]The Order of Sparta[/color] will be reborn: and paradoxically it will, without a doubt, be the last bastion of Freedom and the sweetness of living.[/b]
