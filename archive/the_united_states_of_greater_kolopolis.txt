     The United States of Greater Kolopolis is a region located in both the Gulf of Mexico and the Atlantic Ocean. The previously unexplored territory, (due to its&amp;#39; location in the bermuda triangle) was found to be a tropical paradise capable of supporting a few nations. Resources are abundant and whatever the Region does not have, they embark on dangerous seafaring trade through the triangle to conduct trade with others in the area. As such, states belonging to the Greater Kolopolis region often supports and encourages all forms of capitalism.
