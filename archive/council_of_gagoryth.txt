The Council of Gagoryth represents a small coalition of nations, all of which own land on or near the Isle of Gagoryth. The purpose of the Counci is to provide an open area of discussion, creating a level playing field for all of the members to discuss regional affairs. The Council of Gagoryth has never and will never make any executive decisions or commands, any decision made by the Council are merely a recommended course of action. 

The Council of Gagoryth is represented by [nation]Zinthos[/nation], the founder of the Council, to non-council members. [nation]Zinthos[/nation] is also responsible for insuring the safety and integrity of all Council members.
