The [region]West Cape Empire[/region] is an elite superpower which is composed of many nations. The golden land of Aureumterra and its leader, Dragonquack, is the Emperor of this land. Although there is an emporer, other nations can join political parties and debate. You can take part in polls, join the Emperor&#146;s Consiliario, which is the parliament. All interregional affairs will be handled by the Emperor, the WA delegate, and high ranking members of the Consiliario. In order to move up ranks, you must be elected, and will be promoted in a grand ceremony by the emperor himself!
