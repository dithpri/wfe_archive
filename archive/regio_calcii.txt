The supercontinent of Calcis is caught up in a fifty year civil war between the Muritian Empire of the western continent of Zatyeon and the eastern reformed continental state of Calcia which rebelled against the Zatyrian family. Meanwhile, the Calcian Federation, Mildroid, and Isla de Marcano have all taken the opportunity to declare their independence in the chaos and the Leemurites of the free empire of Calcia&amp;#39;s northeastern region have begun to solidify their own and isolate themselves further.
