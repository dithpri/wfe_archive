A region built on mutual respect.  If you help us out we will help you out, if you attack us you will be destroyed.  We believe in everyone earning their own way in life and not living off of the government.  The government is here to help you out, but not support you and your family.  Military service of 3 years is mandatory for all citizens.  After three year you will be released from service if you choose to leave.  If you want to stay you will be rewarded with a tax free bonus.
