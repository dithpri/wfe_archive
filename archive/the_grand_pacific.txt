Welcome to the Grand Pacific Region.

A place for all island nations to flourish. It is a vast, unexplored area of the Pacific Ocean that is home to a variety of strange and exotic creatures, as well as mysterious civilizations and lost cultures. 

Jules Verne wrote of expeditions to The Grand Pacific led by Captain Nemo aboard the Nautilus, and wrote of the Mysterious Island that is located in its uncharted waters. Sir Arthur Conan Doyle was notably part of a group of explorers who discovered a hidden plateau in The Grand Pacific inhabited by prehistoric creatures, namely King Kong and Godzilla.
