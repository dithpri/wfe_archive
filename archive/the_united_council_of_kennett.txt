The United Council of Kennett is a relatively small region that resides over a vast landscape of varying nations.  Despite the incredibly unique cultures found throughout the many people who reside in the region, they are joined together in their belief of equality.  Each and every individual in the United Council of Kennett are equal to everyone else across race, sexuality, gender, religion, beliefs, and anything else of the like.  The region is willing to open their borders to nations that share in their mindset, and look forward to all interactions between the United Council of Kennett and other regions.
