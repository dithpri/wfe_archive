The Iberian Peninsula /a&amp;#618;&amp;#712;b&amp;#618;&amp;#601;ri&amp;#601;n p&amp;#601;&amp;#712;n&amp;#616;nsj&amp;#649;l&amp;#601;/,[a] also known as Iberia /a&amp;#618;&amp;#712;b&amp;#618;&amp;#601;ri&amp;#601;/,[b] is located in the southwest corner of Europe and is divided among three states: Spain, Portugal, and Andorra; as well as Gibraltar, an overseas territory of the United Kingdom. With an area of approximately 582,000 km2 (225,000 sq mi), it is the third largest European peninsula, after the Scandinavian and Balkan peninsulas.
