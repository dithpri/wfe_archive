[b]Natiocracy[/b] is a system of rule by the nation within its own state, exercised through the authority of all socially useful strata, united in professional and class-based organisations. 

This political doctrine was developed by Colonel Mykola Stsiborskyy of the Ukrainian People&#146;s Republic (UNR) army in the 1930s. It became the official ideology of the Organisation of Ukrainian Nationalists (OUN-M) following the Second Grand Assembly in 1939.
