[b]Region of Reunited Muslim States[/b], also known as [b]RORMS[/b], is a historical region that was active in early 2012 and had a peak population of 120 nations.

RORMS was a target for liberation in [url=https://www.nationstates.net/page=WA_past_resolution/id=88/council=2]SC Resolution #88[/url] and was repealed in [url=https://www.nationstates.net/page=WA_past_resolution/id=117/council=2]SC Resolution #117[/url].
