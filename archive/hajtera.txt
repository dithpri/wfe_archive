Hajtera is an FT planet with a population of 2.2 billion people. It is the fourth closest planet to its sun, and one of two planets in its solar system to hold life, the other being the less advanced planet of Kurenaga. There is close communication between the two planets, with several nations of Hajtera having maintained Kurenagan colonies in the past. The largest nation is Tajtek, whose native language, Tajtekaar, is a global lingua franca.
