Hajtera is a large planet in the Kasajtam System, a solar system of 13 planets. Two planets are capable of sustaining life - Hajtera and Asan - though only Hajtera has developed intelligent life. Hajtera has a population of 2.2 billion people residing in 19 countries, the largest of which is Tajtek, with nearly a billion people. All nations are members of the International Cooperation Organisation, an organisation dedicated to peace and coexistence on Hajtera. The official languages of the ICO are Tajtekaal, Sanaz and Yindai.
