The Sovereign Empire Of Altol is a region controlled solely by The nation Of Altol, (Hence the name). Altol created this region to protect and give a home to its allied nations so they may thrive and prosper, free from outside distractions. 

---

The year is 2150, a century ago India and Pakistan annihilated each other because of a nuclear war, most of the world survived that first war. A Cold War followed between Russia and the United States, in the year 2100 a second war broke out where most of world died, some communities of people survived and started rebuilding nations, join us in this post apocalyptic world in an adventure to build your nation and establish your presence in the world.
