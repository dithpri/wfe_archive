After a group of students were empowered by their history teacher, the benevolent Mr. Benke, they began to recognize government rights and wrongs. After 80 minutes of training each day, they returned to their homelands full of knowledge which they realized was power. They each came to the conclusion that they had no choice but to start their own nations putting their newly found skills to work.

Mr. Benke is the leader of CEA South; however it was founded by Mr. Savage.  Mr. Savage was inspired from the extreme efficiency he learned from Mr. Benke through observation and experience. The Regional Animal is the Bear.
