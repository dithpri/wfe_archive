The Federation of Nation and Freedom was created by the Treaty of Friendship and Cooperation between the Republic of Yaroslavl and the Republic of Nation and Freedom in 2022. 

The parties affirmed their commitment to uphold the values of &quot;Nation&quot; and &quot;Freedom&quot; in their respective domains to the extent possible and to create an area of co-prosperity.

The combined population of the federation at the time of the founding is 628 million, 616 million being the citizens of the Republic of Yaroslavl and 12 million of the Republic of Nation and Freedom.
