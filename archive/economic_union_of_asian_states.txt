Hello and welcome to the Economic Union of Asian States.

The Economic Union of Asian States (EUAS) is a political and economic union of states located primarily in Asia. The primary objective of the EUAS is to accelerate economic growth and through that social progress and cultural development. It was made on April 11th 2022.
