The Guelphian Peninsula is one of forests, mountains and rivers with a variety of wildlife. It experiences the four seasons in all its glory. It is also known for its unpredictable climate, going from temperatures in the negatives to positives in a matter of days. It also has many islands. 

Guelphia, which first started as separate tribes, clans and kingdoms have managed to push it&#39;s way into a supreme developed and, in general, a civil region. 

The peninsula is split between numerous nations. Many political factions exists as well as religious factions, including secular nations. Recently, a series of great political uprisings and tensions have been happening between nations. Could this be the beginning of something great, or the end?
