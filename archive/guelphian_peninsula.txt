The Guelphian Peninsula is one of forests, mountains and rivers with a variety of wildlife. Many nations and political factions exist within the region and the region council attempts to have all voices heard as well as pass legislation. 

[b]Unrecognized Claims[/b]:
The follow territories are not recognised by the council and thus will not be able to vote:
   - The Republic of Cataloniana
   - The Empire of Sidae Joseon
   - The Republic of Mucho Sad
*However, if legislation is passed to recognise these claims, they will gain all the rights as an independent nation.*
