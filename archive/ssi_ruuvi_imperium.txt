The Ssi-ruuvi Imperium was the territorial holdings of the Ssi-ruuvi who reigned over a large swath of the Unknown Regions known as the Ssi-ruuk Star Cluster. The Shreeftut, elective monarch of the Ssi-ruuvi Imperium, ruled from the capital of Lwhekk and was governed by the Conclave and the Elders&#39; Council.
