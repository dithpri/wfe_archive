[b]Talath Ros[/b] is a issue-player roleplay region set on planet [b]Daira[/b]. 

[i]A penal colony established by the [/i][b]Kingdom of Yozahan[/b][i] has sent waves of convicts and war-captives to this long-forgotten &amp; recently rediscovered land. Although banished here to wither away on this cruel steppe that yields few flora and fewer fauna, the prisoners have persevered and migrated deeper into the continent to establish their own kingdoms and states. The land is unforgiving, but so too are the native [b][/i]Hilith[i][/b], descendants of a great empire of antiquity that once called this land home. The two nations are irreconcilable. This is the story of the struggle for [/i][b]Talath Ros[/b].
