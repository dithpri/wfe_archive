The [b][region]The Free United Peoples of Kyrzman[/region][/b] (sometimes referred to as [b]FUPoK[/b] for short) is a region consisting of nations on the Island of Charleston who have left the Maschint Alliance as a retaliation against the war between [nation]Greater Charles[/nation] and [nation]Great Charleston[/nation].
[hr]
[color=lavendar][u][b]The Charlesian War[/b][/u][/color]

On 4th August, 2024, 1.3 million Charlesian troops crossed the border to eastern Great Charleston, the beginning of the Charlesian war. After around 3 months, the war has came to and standstill and neither side wanted to send more troops. The war still technically goes on today, although no troops between the sides have crossed the border since early 2025.
