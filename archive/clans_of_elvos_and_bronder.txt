The states and clans in Southern Serssicx in federation against the oppression of the Eftyll. This federation is not limited to Elvos and Br&oslash;nder. All states and clans in South Serssicx are eligible to join. We are governed by the Delegate whom appoints Consuls of the following orders; War (Border Control), Finance (Embassies), Religion (Communications) and General Affairs (Polls).
