Betelgeuse is a red supergiant star located in the constellation Orion, approximately 640 light-years from Earth. It is one of the brightest stars in the night sky, with a striking red hue, and is classified as a spectral type M1-2 Ia. Betelgeuse has a massive size, about 1,000 times the diameter of the Sun, and it is nearing the end of its life.
