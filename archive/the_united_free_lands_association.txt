The United Free Lands Association (UFLA) is an association of countries bound by a atmosphere of free trade and superb civil freedoms. This is a union for the encouragement of trade and economic development and as such participants in this region are obliged to agree to strict economic and political standards. Members of the UFLA are expected to remove tariffs and other taxation on exports, subsidize transportation of goods and resources, and to provide for the development and maintenance of trade routes. Also, as to keep high ethical standards, members are expected to have a high level of civil liberty, government transparency, media freedom and a fair and swift judicial system.
