[b]Dubreach[/b], also known as [b]Dubstepreach[/b], is an independent colony of [region]Candy Land[/region]/[region]Whitereach[/region]. Dubreach is intended for Candy Land/Whitereach citizens that like dubstep and all of its subgenres. Drum and bass, house, techno, and glitch fans are welcome as well.

Our homeland is [region]Candy Land[/region]/[region]Whitereach[/region] and our puppet storage region is [region]Blackreach[/region]. We are also allies with [region]RussiaReach[/region], [region]Ponyreach[/region], [region]Bogglereach[/region], [region]DMXreach[/region], [region]HaloReach[/region], and [region]Asianreach[/region], which are also independent colonies of Candy Land/Whitereach.
