[b]This is a work in progress.[/b]

Olioris is a modern tech, Earth-based roleplay region. Everything is still being set up, however if you have any interest in helping out feel free to join!

We strive to provide an environment where individuals can contribute to an interesting counterfactual historical timeline. In practice, the history of Olioris follows a course of events overall very similar to Earth, however the developments of modern history can stray quite far from the real world.
