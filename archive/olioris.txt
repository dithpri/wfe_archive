[b]Welcome to Olioris![/b]

Olioris is a modern tech, Earth-based roleplay region. We strive to provide an environment where individuals can contribute to an interesting counterfactual historical timeline. In practice, the history of Olioris follows a course of events overall very similar to Earth, however the developments of modern history can stray quite far from the real world. If you want to participate, be sure to check out our [url=https://www.nationstates.net/page=dispatch/id=1546709]Starting Guide[/url]!

I highly suggest joining the Discord server for easier interaction and discussion!
