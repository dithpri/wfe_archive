Turba is the Latin Term for Scuffle. This Region is for those who want freedom in some ways my region will allow that. We have high fur, gold, Wine, and Oil trade. Our lands our fertile and good for farming, wildlife is plentiful with a few Animals on the Endangered list, though there are farms used to raise animals for the purpose of their meat, and are taken care of in a very human way.
The region of Turba has frost, mountains, and plains. We also have strong Iron in our soil and good mining. Turba is a good region for any country to come to.
