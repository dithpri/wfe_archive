United Republics, in long form United Republics of Imperial Nations is a sovereign and independent region of Western Europe. United Republics&#39; political capital is the city of Lurenberg (also spelled Luremberg), located in the Flu&szlig;erde region. Its economic capital is Veroja, located in the Adri&agrave;s region. United Republics is a unitary constitutional republic in the form of a semi-presidential regime.
