The UGD is founded with the best tacticians, best military equipment and military vehicles. The UGD do not just supply member nations with advanced weaponry and tactics but also with assistance in battles, conflicts or just reinforcing nations. We supply new member nations with ideas for tactics and new ideas for weaponry. In some cases when new member nations are incredibly weak and vulnerable we can supply some military equipment and the Allied Nations of Hypsinar will personally protect it until a time where that nation could grow more powerful.
