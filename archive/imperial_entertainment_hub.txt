[b]The Imperial Entertainment Hub is a sub-entity of the [region]Fifth Empire[/region] housing the [i][url=https://www.nationstates.net/page=dispatch/id=1432006] State Commission for Popular Culture[/i][/b][/url]. Various forms of public mass-entertainment are centralized and organized here such as roleplay (RP).

The Commission and the Entertainment Hub is lead and overseen by the [i]President of the Commission[/i] (to be appointed) whose immediate superior is [b]Minister[/b] [nation]Brazika[/nation][b] ; the leader of the Ministry under which the Commission exists in the [region]Fifth Empire[/region][/b].  [i]Commissional Managers[/i] are responsible for the day-to-day activities and control of the different forms and games of mass-entertainment offered and act as the first level of authority for partaking citizens; solving disputes and investigating/settling rule violations etc.
