The Delian Union (DU) is a regional union consisting of seven countries in [region]Nadeyatsya[/region]. The DU was established on 26 May 2001 in Urbs Petrae and launched on 9 July 2002 in [nation=shortnoflag]Foederatio Hesperiae[/nation]. The most important decisions of the DU are made by the Assembly of the Delian Union, a semi-annual meeting of the heads of state and government of its member states. The DU&#39;s secretariat, the Delian Union Commission, is based in Urbs Petrae, Hesperia.
