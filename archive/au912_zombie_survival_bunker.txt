[b]--- REGIONAL EMERGENCY ZOMBIE PROTOCOLS ---[/b]
1) The Regional Objective to finish with as few zombies and dead as possible.  All nations are requested to attempt to develop a cure.
2) Any nation embracing the zombies for more than 3 minutes or attacking another nation with zombie hordes will be ejected and banned.  The 3 minutes are so that if you hit the wrong button accidently you can change it.
3) Any nation attacking another nation with military forces when not requested will be ejected and banned.  &quot;Cure missiles&quot; are fine.
4) Any nation which has not begun researching a cure or military solution within 4 hours of the start of infestation will be ejected and banned.
