The Shire - Barley is used for brewing beer in the Northfarthing and the warm climate of the southern area is conducive to growing pipe-weed. Much of the rest of the land is devoted to the the cultivation of mushrooms, which, curiously enough, is a Hobbit delicacy. 

The Shire is a peaceful place! To keep the Shire safe from invaders, all new nations should join the World Assembly and endorse [nation]Beorn the Shapeshifter[/nation] and [nation]Fool of a Took[/nation].
