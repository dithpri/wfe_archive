The Imperial Houses of the Landsraad, known colloquially as the Landsraad, is the semi-democratic ruling body composed of royal houses. Said houses are composed of nobles, their families, and house servants. A royal house is founded when a noble is awarded a fief by the Emperor, head of the Landsraad.

Requirements for nations seeking recognition as a Imperial House:

1. Currency must be Imperial Mark.
2. Prefix must be &quot;Fiefdom&quot;.
3. Flag must have black background and other graphics must be colored gold. (hex:#FDBA20)
4. Demonym must be &quot;subject&quot;.
5. Must contact the Imperial House. (House Fezan)
