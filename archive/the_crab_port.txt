[b]A British port nestled on an island in the Caribbean. A frequented crossing point for merchant ships sailing to Europe and the United States. 

The port has suffered multiple attacks during its existence. Luckily, all of them have been pushed back and there are many skeletons lying on top of the gibbets or in the cold jails of the fort. 

[color=red] Aggressive pirates and other raiders are prohibited by British crown decrees[/color][/b]
