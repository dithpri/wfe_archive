A British port nestled in an archipelago in the Caribbean. It is an important point of passage between the Americas and Europe. Many rare commodities and gold circulate on galleons and ships of all kinds. 

[color=purple][b]This port has repeatedly been raided by vicious legions, but it remains an impenetrable and irreducible stronghold in the face of evil. [/b][/color]

[color=red][b][i]The jails and numerous gibbets remind raiders that aggressive piracy is prohibited by crown decree. [/i] [/b][/color]
