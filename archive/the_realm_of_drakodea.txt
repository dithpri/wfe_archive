A religious-based region where its nations - who are peaceful to those within the region but may not be much so to those outside it - all worship Dracodea, a dragon goddess whom Drakodeans believe mothered the humans who first lived in the region. The High Priestess of Ryutaki, the central nation&#39;s leader, is highly adored and who is a descendant of Drakodea herself. In fact, the High Priestess position itself is bloodline-based.
