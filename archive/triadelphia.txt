A region for nations that began at Triadelphia, Park, or anywhere in the Northern Panhandle, really.

Our region is still in its infancy, but growing rapidly.  As we grow, member nation-states must work together to develop a charter or constitution.  Suggestions are welcome.  For example charters of international organizations, see: [url=http://www.un.org/en/documents/charter/]United Nations[/url] &amp;amp; [url=http://www.nato.int/cps/en/natolive/official_texts_17120.htm]NATO[/url].
