[b]Kyushu[/b] is the third largest island of Japan and most southwesterly of its four main islands. 

As of 2006, Kyushu has a population of 13,231,995 and covers 13,760 square miles.

The island is mountainous, and Japan&#39;s most active volcano, Mt Aso (5,220 ft), is on Kyushu. &#151;Wikipedia[hr]
