Fleeing the nuclear devastation of the old USA after World War 3, the FFA established the first civilization in one of the old world hotspots, a place right between the old superpowers of the USA and the Soviet union, the bering sea region.

While the FFA relies almost entirely on nuclear power, the FFA is attempting to capitalize on the abundance of oil in the region to kickstart its economy. While the region has a very powerful conventional military, the secrets of nuclear weapons have been rediscovered and the FFA is now able to deter any future invasions
