Seated between the two former world Superpowers, the United States and Soviet Union, The Bering Sea region has only recently been inhabited; due to the cold and quite icy climate and the tensions between the superpowers.

Despite this, however, large deposits of Fossil Fuels remain under the land and the waves, which some less developed regions without the luxuries of Nuclear Fusion would love to get their hands on.

The region was first inhabited by the Federal Republic of Federal Free America, a group of people looking for a remote area in which to found their new nation free from the oppression of other nations is more inhabited regions.
