[b]Patagonia[/b] is a country at the southern end of South America. The region comprises the southern section of the Andes Mountains and the deserts, pampas, and grasslands to the east, and Valdivian temperate and Magellanic subpolar forests and deep fjords to the west. Patagonia is one of the few regions with coasts on three oceans, with the Pacific Ocean to the west, the Atlantic Ocean to the east, and the Southern Ocean to the south. 

The Colorado and Barrancas Rivers, which run from the Andes to the Atlantic, are the northern limit of Patagonia and form its border with Argentina and its border with Chile is at the Huincul Fault in the Araucan&iacute;a Region.
