[b]Patagonia[/b] is a country in South America. Its six provinces cover approximately 1.1 million km&sup2; and extend from the Atlantic Ocean to the Pacific Ocean and southward into the Southern Ocean. It borders Chile and Argentina to the north. Geographically diverse, it is a sparsely inhabited country of 5.7 million people, the vast majority residing in urban areas.

Patagonia is a parliamentary democracy and a Commonwealth realm. The current head of state is [b]King Charles III[/b] represented by [b]Governor-General Sir Antonio L&oacute;pez Garc&iacute;a[/b] and the head of government is [b]Prime Minister Andrew Shaw[/b].
