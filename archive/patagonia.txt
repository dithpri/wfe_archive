[b]Patagonia[/b] is a country in the southern half of South America, encompassing the bulk of the Southern Cone. It borders Chile and Argentina to the north. Patagonia has diverse landscapes ranging from the southern section of the Andes with lakes, fjords, temperate rainforests, and glaciers in the west to deserts, tablelands and steppes in the east. It is bound by the Pacific Ocean to the west, the Atlantic Ocean to the east, and the Southern Ocean to the south.

Patagonia is a parliamentary democracy and a constitutional monarchy in the Westminster tradition. King Charles III is head of state, represented by his Governor-General, Stanley Abercrombie, and Prime Minister Andrew Shaw is head of government.
