[b][region]Kerberos Collective[/region][/b] is harsh and unforgiving. Kerberos Collective is inspired by the world of the Kerberos Saga and Jin-Roh: The Wolf Brigade. This region serves as a battleground for those who seek to impose their will through strength, discipline, and unwavering authority. Nations that thrive here are those that are expected to embrace authoritarianism and militarism, using fear and power as the ultimate tools for governance
