A huge empire of nine African sheikdoms.

This large empire is bordered on three sides by forest and on one sides by desert. Zenegali relations with their neighbors to the northeast are strong, while relations with neighbors to the southeast are unstable. The Zenegali people follow various religions and respects their political leaders. The nine is one major city of Cairo, Algiers, Timbuktu, Tunis, Yaounde, Benghazi and Tripoli located in the north of the region. They are currently troubled by rampant disease.
