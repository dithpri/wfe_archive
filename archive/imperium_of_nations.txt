[b]Imperium of Nations[/b]

Welcome to the Imperium, a region that encompasses nations great and small of many different creeds and cultures. Member nations are encouraged to release their imaginations and establish bright and wonderful new RPs in the name of the region. If you are interested in becoming a member state please contact either the founder or WA delegate for further details. Join this ever expanding and active region if you are in search of debate, exitement, good RPs, and allies in this harsh world of Nation States.
