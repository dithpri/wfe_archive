The Petty Kingdom of Valeria, commonly known as Valeria, is a self-proclaimed micronation founded on 18 October 2022 as the Imperial Principality of Varya. Following internal restructuring, Varya was dissolved on 23 January 2023 and succeeded by the Petty Kingdom of Valeria under the rule of King Tye I and the Royal House St. Dennis.
Valeria operates as an absolute monarchy, with the monarch serving as both head of state and head of government. The Kingdom maintains claimed associations with Saskatchewan, Canada, the Scottish Highlands, and additional international properties.
