[color=lightgrey][b]The Antarctic[/b][/color][color=lightblue] is the polar region around the Earth&#39;s South Pole. The Antarctic comprises the continent of Antarctica and it&#39;s ice shelves, waters, and island territories in the Southern Ocean situated south of the Antarctic Convergence. The area covers some 20% of the Southern Hemisphere. [/color] 

[color=grey]The region had no indigenous inhabitants. A few thousand transient scientific and other personnel work in several dozen research stations. However, the region is visited by more than 40,000 tourists annually, Antarctic Peninsula area (especially the South Shetland Islands) and South Georgia Island.[/color]

[color=lightblue]The Antarctic [/color][color=lightgrey]was originally founded prior to NS record keeping. The [b]Benevolent[/b] became the seventh founder of the region on December 17, 2015.[/color][hr]
