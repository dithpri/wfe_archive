[b][color=purple][i]Eirene[/i][/color][/b], one of the Horae, was the personification of peace, and was depicted in art as a beautiful young woman carrying a cornucopia, sceptre and a torch or rhyton. She is said sometimes to be the daughter of [b][color=gold][i]Zeus[/i][/color][/b] and [b][color=gray][i]Themis[/i][/color].[/b]
