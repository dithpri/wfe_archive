The fourth idol group in the Love Live franchise that officially started at the end of 2020. Initially consisting of five members, four more members were added in April 2022. Two more members were introduced near the end of April 2023, one of which was a former rival to Liella, Wien Margarete. The other is Natsumi&#39;s sister, Tomari.

Liella members: Kanon Shibuya, Keke Tang, Chisato Arashi, Sumire Heanna, Ren Hazuki, Kinako Sakurakoji, Mei Yoneme, Shiki Wakana, Natsumi Onitsuka, Tomari Onitsuka, Wien Margarete.
