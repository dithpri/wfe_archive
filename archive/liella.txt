The fourth idol group in the Love Live franchise that officially started at the end of 2020. Initially consisting of five members, four more members were added in April 2022, with newer members to be revealed later sometime this year.

Liella members: Kanon Shibuya, Keke Tang, Chisato Arashi, Sumire Heanna, Ren Hazuki, Kinako Sakurakoji, Mei Yoneme, Shiki Wakana, and Natsumi Onitsuka.
