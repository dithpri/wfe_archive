[i]The Kerguelen Islands are a group of islands in the southern Indian Ocean and a  District of the French Southern and Antarctic Lands. They are among the most isolated places on Earth.
The main island, Grande Terre, is surrounded by a further 300 smaller islands and islets, forming an archipelago of 7,215 km2. The climate is raw and chilly with frequent high winds throughout the year. The surrounding seas are generally rough, but ice-free. There are no indigenous inhabitants.[/i]
