The Assimilated Powers of Wealth is a region of which focuses on a high economy above all things. Those within the Region are to have an economy above 70 at all times, lest they be warned of an impending dismissal of service. Those of whom have an exceptionally strong economy will be exalted.

The Assimilated Powers of Wealth is a region of which also focuses on military prowess, those nations of which are lovers of supposed peace are not allowed entry, however, those of which who frequently espouse war shalt be exalted.

 Those of whom are to be exalted will be given such accolades in endorsements.
