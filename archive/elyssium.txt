Elyssium is a medium-sized continent located in the central Pacific Ocean. The northern regions of Elyssium are mountainous, while moving south the terrain descends through hilly uplands into broad plains. Elyssium lies in a temperate climate zone and is known for the luxuriant vegetation.

The culture of Elyssium can be described as a mixture of cultures which has led to a cultural renaissance in the region. There are cultural innovations and movements that sometimes are at odds with one another. All countries in the region share certain characteristics in common such as a strong economic focus, order and constant progress, monarchical or hierarchical political systems, firm ideologies and devotion for science and arts.
