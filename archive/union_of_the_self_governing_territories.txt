We will always grant all the civil and equal rights to minorities, especially to those who have been discriminated and prosecuted all these years past. 

All the Nations and Countries constituting this region have the strong compromise to approve all the proposals that will empower equality for all the citizens and inhabitants of our countries, regardless of race, sexual orientation, age, gender, nationality, ethnic origin or any circumstance of their lives.

Any constituting Country or Nation that does not fulfil the requirements and/or the law of the region (internal international law and Nation States international law approved by the World Assembly, regardless they are members or not) will automatically be ejected from this region.
