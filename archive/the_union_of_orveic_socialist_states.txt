The UOSS, or Union Orveic Socialist States, is a Commonwealth of the over 20 tribes of Orveic peoples that make up the a union of multiple subnational Orveic republics known as counties, its government and economy are highly centralized. Although internationally recognized as a single nation, the Commonwealth is a coalition of several partially independent Orveic republics, who upon the Treaty on the Creation of the UOSS in 1937, banded under one flag.
