
[i][b]Norlandic Federation (NF) [/b][/i]

The Norlandic Federation is a supranational organization of sovereign states established to mitigate regional instability following large-scale population displacements. The bloc functions as a centralized framework for economic integration, legal standardization, and collective security.

Economic Policy
The Federation operates as a Unified Trade Zone. Member states are bound by a harmonized regulatory environment designed to facilitate capital flow and corporate efficiency. Primary objectives include the protection of industrial assets and the maintenance of a stable regional market to attract foreign investment.
