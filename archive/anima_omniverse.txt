Anima Omniverse is a collection of universe, multiverse and metaverse related to &quot;Anima&quot;. The word itself is derived from Latin word meaning soul, spirit, and breath. It is the source of all existing energy that forms the omniverse. 

This omniverse tells the age of multiversal conflicts between beings who possess anima, each with their own purposes.
