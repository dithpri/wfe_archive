Rasalhague, also designated as &amp;#945; Ophiuchi (alpha Ophiuchi), is a variable giant star of spectral class A5 in the constellation of Ophiuchus. Rasalhague visual magnitude is 2.08, making it the 58th brightest star in the sky. Because of its brightness, Rasalhague is clearly visible to the naked eye when observed from locations with dark skies, and should be also quite easily visible from light polluted areas.
