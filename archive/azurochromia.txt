The world of Earth, long home to the human race, eventually fell into increasing destruction.  Resources became scarce, global cooling devastated food supplies, and technology could no longer compensate for the scarcity.  Thus began a nuclear war, and Earth was left a ruined, desolate world with no future.  Only the technology and faith of the victorious gave them hope - a hope realized in a new world to call home, Azurochromia.
