The Holy Roman Empire of The Palatinate is the collection of Kingdoms, Lands and Empires under the autocratic rule of The Holy Roman Emperor of The Palatinate (Palatine Emperor in Short). The Holy Roman Emperor is the Highest Authority, and holds each constituent state, formerly in personal union, but now as a centralised federal state.

The reigning Holy Roman Emperor is currently His Royal and Imperial Majesty, Charles XIV, by the Grace of God and the Will of the People, Holy Roman Emperor of the Palatinate.
