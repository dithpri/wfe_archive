The Mandalore sector, also referred to as Mandalorian Space,is a sector located in the galaxy&#39;s Outer Rim Territories that contain nearly a thousand worlds that are neutral and not part of the Galactic Republic. Several star systems including the Concord Dawn, Mandalore and Phindar systems are located in the Mandalore sector.

The sector lay in a key strategic position between Coruscant and the Outer Rim Territories, via the Hydian Way.
