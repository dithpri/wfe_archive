[b]Rules:[/b]
- Nations must be democratic. No dictatorships or similar.
- Nations must join the World Assembly within 48 hours of joining the region.
- Founder holds permanent power over all domains, but does not use this power untill a crisis arises.
- Power is utilized by elected Officers.
- A vote to replace an officer can be called at any time.
- An Officer can only hold one mandate. Cannot be elected if a mandate is held. (WA delegate excluded).
- Founder can temporarily assign an Officer when a new office is created or when an old Officer quits voluntarily.
