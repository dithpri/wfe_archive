[b][color=#1fa19e]WELCOME TO THE COUNCIL OF AFRETARYN[/color][/b]

Officially the &#39;Council of Afretaryn, Tairea and Halcyon&#39; this organisation manages diplomacy between the nations on the continents of Afretaryn, Tairea and Halcyon.

Afretaryn, the largest continent, is home to the most influential nations of the three regions.

[i]This region contains nations formerly within the Southern Equatorial Council before losing control of that NS region[/i]
