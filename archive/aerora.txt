This region is a dictatorship. 
1. Any player can propose a rule at any time, provided a rule is not in the process of being voted on, and have it be voted on. Majority rule. 
2. The only member that can vote on proposals is the founder of Aerora.
3. The founder of this region must be referred to as King.
4. All members of Aerora must have an official title on the WFE.
