The region of the Third Reich comprises of Nationalsozialistische Germany and all its territories, Under a strict rule by the grand Fuhrer, all captured territories are put under extreme watch and go under a Germanization process. All captured regions are merged within the Reich to expand its size and all nations within the captured regions are considered part of the Reich. The region also compromises of some allies though it is mainly all the territories Germany has absorbed put under one region under the rule of the iron fisted Fuhrer.
