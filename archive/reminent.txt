[i]The year is 2300.

For 21 years since the One Year War of 2279, Earth has prospered under the leadership of John Bowen. 

The planet Cybertron, under the governance of Rodimus Prime and the Autobots, continues to recover from the deep scars left by the Great War.

On a remote planet, the forces of Neo Zeon have allied with Decepticon remnants led by Deathsaurus, and together they plot their revenge against their respective enemies.

Meanwhile, throughout all of this, the planet Paxia Yuss, also inhabited by humans, has developed and grown to become a major player in galactic affairs.[/i]
