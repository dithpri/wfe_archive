The second resurgence of Faeryland under this name, her Imperial Majesties Titania and Maeve have once again reached a minor accord to bring more nations into Their Benevolent Guard.
Owing to its bizarre nature as &quot;Faerie Maleficarium&quot;, as declared by the late and unlamented Lady Protectress Mattea, the geospatial nature of the region is difficult to determine.
All member-nations swear allegiance to one of the three Powers of the Nevernever: the Summer, the Winter, or the Wyld.
