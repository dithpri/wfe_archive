[b]Valencia[/b] is the capital of the autonomous community of Valencia and the third largest city in Spain after Madrid and Barcelona. It is Spain&#39;s third largest metropolitan area, with a population ranging from 1.7 to 2.5 million. The Port of Valencia is the 5th busiest container port in Europe and the busiest container port on the Mediterranean Sea. &#151;Wikipedia[hr]
