The Federated States of Toylin is a federated planetary government composed of the democratic regions of the United Socialist States of Gotemme and Klozhet, the United States of Dolliwud, the Confederacy of Forbitten, the Republic of Batrumme, the Commonwealth of Hull wai, and the United Kingdom of Adultii.

Forged in the aftermath of the Toylinian Revolution - the Federation&#39;s purpose is to restore law and order across the planet, and to ensure peace through the nations of Toylin.
