Maymarxa is a legion of nations who following the ideology of Maymarxism. 

General principle of Maymarxism includes the establishment of a powerful economy and an unrestricted autocrat, leading to intense political, social and economical reform, leading to the gradual creation and empowerment of civil liberty. The doctrine of Maymarxism ends with political freedom being granted to the masses. This will only occur after all aspects of life (social and economical) have been idealized so that the seizure of power by the masses will be successful in creating a Utopian society. 

Radical socialism, welfare, foreign aid and the tyranny of minorities are frowned upon.
