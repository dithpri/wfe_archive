Segregated North America is a separate but equal region, founded on the idea that prejudice is a natural instinct to preserve the divisions of mankind that nature evolved.
[hr]Segregated North America is intended to be a collection of states that enforce the separation of races and ethnic groups. To achieve this, World Assembly membership is prohibited due to [url=https://www.nationstates.net/page=WA_past_resolution/id=35/council=1]GA Resolution #35[/url] and [url=https://www.nationstates.net/page=WA_past_resolution/id=38/council=1]GA Resolution #38[/url]
