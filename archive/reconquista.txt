[b]The [region]Reconquista[/region] was the gradual reconquest of Islamic Iberia by the Christian kingdoms of Castile, Aragon, Le&oacute;n, Galicia, Navarre, and Portugal. from 718 to 1492, following the Umayyad Islamic conquest of Hispania. Ferdinand II of Aragon and Isabella I of Castile were instrumental in driving the Islamic invaders from Western Europe, and funding the first trip of Columbus to the Americas. 

The D&iacute;a de la Toma de Granada is a civic and religious festival held each year in Granada on the anniversary of Granada conquest, January 2 1492.

The last Islamic state in Iberia and all of Western Europe was expunged with the triumph of Christianity. 

Centuries later the process begins anew this time under Nationalism[/b]
