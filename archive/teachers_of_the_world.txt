Teachers of the World is a region founded by a high school history teacher in San Francisco, California. The region welcomes any teacher, whether they use NationStates in their classrooms, or just play for fun. It is a collaborative place to share ideas on how to incorporate NationStates and resources like it to enhance student learning. Discuss national politics, role play, share lessons, answer tough questions that challenge educators everywhere: however you want to connect and share with your fellow teachers to continue improving your classes and giving your students amazing learning experiences. 

Welcome, teachers of the world!
