[b]&amp;#127760; [color=#FFBF00]&amp;#8025;&amp;#940;&amp;#948;&amp;#949;&amp;#962; - Hyades[/color] [i]is the nearest open star cluster located about 153 light-years away from the Sun, it consists of a roughly spherical group of hundreds of stars sharing the same age, place of origin, chemical characteristics, and motion through space. An astonishing place of beauty hovering forever in space.[/i][/b]

Hyades welcomes all original species within its stars as well as other inhabitants of the Milky Way. We are a roleplaying region, aiming for creativity and deep storry-telling as well as uniqueness and world-building, provided by a stable government and fair rules.
