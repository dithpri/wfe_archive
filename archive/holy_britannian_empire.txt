The Holy Britannian Empire is a supranational union ruled by Emperor Lelouch Vi Britannia from the Imperial Capital of Pendragon.
The Empire is currently composed of ten provinces and ten colony regions:
Aravea: The political and cultural heart of the Empire.
Osiena: The military heart of the Empire.
Waldreich: The economic heart of the Empire and is also home of the Imperial Treasury and Mint
Kosovio: The most populous of the four provinces, Kosovio is known as the breadbasket due to its fertile fields 
Member of:
Commonwealth of Sovereign States(Observer)
Allies:
Grand Britannia, Neo-Assyrian Empire, Oaledonia, Batallia
