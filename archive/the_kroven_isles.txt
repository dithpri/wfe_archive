The Kroven Isles were ruled by The Republic of Kroven until a civil war broke out. The country split in 2, South Kroven now rules the 7 southern isles and is lead by the dictator Esikiel Zakari, the military general who started the Kroven civil war. The Republic of North Kroven is lead by president Jekobi Leqob, the original president of The Reublic of Kroven.
