Delcona Grand Dukedom is located near Ireland. The Father of this region is General Duke Sumit XI. He is powerful god of his military service.

Oath of Allegiance in Delcona Defense Services(as an officer or enlist soldiers) and Civil Service also Citizens:

I(Full name of person) swear by Almighty God that I will be faithful and bear true allegiance to His Majesty The First Duke of Delcona Supreme Marshall Sumit Barua, His Heirs and Successors, and that I will, as in duty bound, honestly and faithfully defend His Majesty, His Heirs and Successors, in Person, Crown and Dignity against all enemies, and will observe and obey all orders of His Majesty, His Heirs and Successors, and of the generals and officers set over me. So help me God.
