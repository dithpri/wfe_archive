A Land of high diplomacy and order. Divided between many Nations, however they will fall loyal either to  the Paixese or the Ardeuren people. Paix-Ardeur to this day remains split between the two groups and have fought many civil wars for power. The Paixese are lead by a small Nation known as Menentimus and the Ardeurens are lead by the colossus nation of Wardentimus. New nations are know to commonly form and join the Government. All are welcome in Paix-Ardeur whether they call themselves Paixese or Ardeuren.
