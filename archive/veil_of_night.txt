In the shadowy realm of &#39;Veil of Night&#39; descends gracefully, enveloping the land in mystery and tranquility. Named after Nyx, the primordial goddess of the night, this region is a haven for those who find solace in the stars and the moonlit sky. The nights here are never completely dark, as the heavens are alight with constellations telling ancient tales. Ethereal landscapes glow under the celestial light, creating an atmosphere of serene beauty and contemplation. The people of Veil of Night are nocturnal by nature, celebrating the mysteries of the night through art, astronomy, and nighttime festivals.
