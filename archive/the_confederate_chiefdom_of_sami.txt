Sami is a Terran country bordering Ursus and Kazimierz which is located in the furthest north of the continent near the Infy Icefield. Being the homeland of Elafians and other rare races, it is a primitive, loosely-banded chiefdom thriving in the frigid tundra that retains its ancient ways of life in the age of modernization. [nation]The Samifjod[/nation] live in tribes ruled by a kinship of tribal chiefs, notably the Snowpriests, who serve as both shamans and elders for their tribesmen.
