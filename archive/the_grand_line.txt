[i]The Grand Line is the ocean current that is surrounded by the Calm Belts and follows an imaginary line that runs from north-west to south-east across the middle of the world and perpendicular to the Red Line. This stretch of ocean is said to be the most dangerous place in the world, and is commonly referred to as the Pirates&amp;#39; Graveyard by people from the four Blues because of this reputation.[/i]
