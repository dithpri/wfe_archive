Blutheim is a land of striking contrasts, where towering, mist-covered mountains give way to rolling emerald valleys and deep, ancient forests. Vast rivers carve through the landscape, feeding into pristine lakes that shimmer under golden sunsets. The coastline, rugged and dramatic, is lined with towering cliffs and hidden coves. Rich in natural beauty, the nation takes great pride in its well-preserved wilderness, offering breathtaking scenery at every turn.
