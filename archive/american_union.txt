The American Union (AU) is an economic and political union of member states that are located primarily in North and South America.

The AU&amp;#39;s [i]de facto[/i] capital is in Milwaukee, located within [nation]Tessen[/nation].

The AU maintains a single market through a standardized system of laws that apply in all member states. AU policies aim to ensure the free movement of people, goods, services, and capital, enact legislation in interstate justice and security, and maintain common policies on trade, agriculture, and regional development.

Permanent diplomatic missions are being established around the world.

The national borders of each member state are listed in their national Factbooks. (See [nation]Tessen[/nation] as an example)
