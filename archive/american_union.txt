The [b]American Union[/b] (AU) is an economic and political union of member states that are located in North and South America.

The AU&amp;#39;s capital is located in the city of [b]Milwaukee[/b], within [nation]Tessen[/nation].

All AU member nations agree to support free and open trade, pursue mutual defense, and promote regional growth and development.

All member states are encouraged to list their national borders within their Factbook. (See [url=http://www.nationstates.net/nation=tessen/detail=factbook/id=main]Republic of Tessen Factbook[/url] as an example)
