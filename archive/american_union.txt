The [b]American Union[/b] (AU) is an economic and political union of democratic member states that are located in North and South America.

All AU member nations agree to support free and open trade, pursue mutual defense, and promote regional growth and development.

The AU&amp;#39;s capital is located in the city of [b]Milwaukee[/b], within [nation]Tessen[/nation]. (See [url=http://www.nationstates.net/nation=tessen/detail=factbook/id=main]Tessen Factbook[/url])
