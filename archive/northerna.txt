Northerna is a region situated in the northern hemisphere, bordered by the icy Arctic Ocean to the north and flanked by the temperate regions of Elaria to the south. 

The people of Northerna are known for their resilience and adaptability to harsh climates. They include several distinct ethnic groups, each with its own culture and traditions. The northern tribes are often described as fierce and independent, while those in the southern regions are known for their agricultural skills and trade. 

There are 2 nations in the region, &#39;Thessa Territora&#39; and &#39;Thessa Rive&#39; with the smaller one (Thessa Rive) being a revolutionary breakaway state of the other (Thessa Territora).
