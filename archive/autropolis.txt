Welcome to [color=magenta]Autropolis[/color]!

Autropolis is a supercluster orbiting a massive black hole. Life began in it on March 11th, 2003. Autropolis contains a plethora of unexplored galaxies and habitable planets. Its eight founding nations are still active today. Between 2007 and 2010, Autropolis peaked as a prominent region in NationStates.
