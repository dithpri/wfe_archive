Welcome to [color=magenta]Autropolis[/color]!

Autropolis is a star cluster orbiting a massive black hole. Life began in it on April 29&amp;#7511;&amp;#688;, 2005. There are a plethora of unexplored systems and habitable planets. Its eight founding nations are still active today. Between 2007 and 2010, this region peaked as a prominent region in NationStates.
