Founded on April April 29&amp;#7511;&amp;#688;, 2005 and peaking between 2007 and 2009, Autropolis remains an active region with all eight founding nations remaining today. The region aims to provide a home among the stars; itself a star cluster orbiting a massive black hole. A plethora of unexplored systems and habitable planets await future settlement.[hr]
