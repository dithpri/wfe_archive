Asmil is a small but troubled region. It is spilt between North and South who have been at war on and off for centuries. In an attempt to end the bloodshed the South as opened up the region to the world. 

North Asmil is a brutal dictatorship which suffers economically. Its citizens worship its leader as God by law.

South Asmil is a Liberal democracy that is flourishing due to its trade and interactions with the outside world.
