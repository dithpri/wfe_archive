[color=666600][i]The [b]Congo River[/b] is a river in Africa and the world&amp;#39;s deepest river with measured depths in excess of 720 ft. It is the second largest river in the world by discharge (after the Amazon).

The Democratic Republic of the Congo and the Republic of the Congo are named after it, as was their predecessor state, the Republic of the Congo (L&eacute;opoldville), which had gained independence in 1960 from the Belgian Congo.[/color][/i] [color=666666]&#151;Wikipedia[/color][hr]
