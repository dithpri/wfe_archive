The United Eastern American Federation, formed after the Second American Civil War, is the unity of two largest factions in Divided America. America separated into 4 main factions between February and September of 2017. Environment: The Eastern Seaboard has been completely leveled, and the land is mainly inhospitable. The Mid-east of America is slightly less so.
Government: The governments of the regions are mainly fascist and right-wing, although political freedoms and civil rights vary nation-to-nation. 
Location: the Southernmost state is Florida/Texas and the northernmost state is Pennsylvania, the westernmost state is the west tip of Texas.
