Article One: The  National Party, which is the vanguard for the socialist revolution in and the whole of NationStates, acts as the governmental expression of the working class. It is composed of the most dedicated and advanced fighters who place the interest and well-being of the people above themselves. The program of the party defines the content of the whole social and state life, as it lays a leading role in all aspects ofsociety. It is able to play this role because it has always maintained a correct revolutionary line, and it enjoys the trust, respect, and support of the people.
