                                               Beacon of Peace constitution 

We the nations of this land promise to make a caring and giving community. We promise to treat people with kindness and respect. We hope to make many great friendships. The people here have the rights of life, liberty, and the pursuit of happiness. You will receive a top class education and amazing heathcare. You may also have responsibilities such as being kind and not bullying. Nations in this region will not be judged by class or ethnicity. But by the kindness in their hearts. All men are created equal no matter the race.
