Srivijaya is an important centre for the expansion of Buddhism from the 7th to 21th century AD. Srivijaya was attacked by Siam, Chola Kingdom and Majapahit in the 11th century. They were able to defeat and annex these three nations with the help of Ancient China. Srivijaya was attacked by the Dutch in the 19th century. Srivijaya signed an alliance with Empire of Britain. This alliance gave India to Britain, allowed Britain to set up a trading port in Singapura. In return, Britain helped to annex Dutch, which had control of East Indonesia. Dutch was defeated overall. Srivijaya defeated Japan in WW2 and gave a majority of Japan and Korea to China in an alliance, in return Srivijaya got more defence.
