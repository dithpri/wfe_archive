Founded in October 9th, 2018. 

Trinion was established by three Nations: HFL, an Empire; Octoberia, a Liberal Commonwealth; and Peak States, a Conservative Confederation. Together they defend, protect and manage the Region. 

Trinion is a vast continent ruled by the Empire of HFL; which allows smaller Nations to sub-administer themselves. 

On October 10th, 2018, the Colony of Xilem was established to be the center of Treasury for the Region.
