The immense forested land of Sambar, home to the richest hunting reserves in the world. With forests spanning hundreds of miles, the thriving timber industry compliments the thriving hunting industry. Home to deep lakes, thousands of rivers and the largest known population of animals on the planet, the Sambarian landmass is a must see destination for any trophy hunter. 

Sambar Rusa is the largest country on the continent, sitting in the Southeast of the landmass. Taking up roughly 53% of Sambaria, it is a formidable powerhouse state. 

Chital is a smaller nation, formally the hostile state of Brasilia, since pacified and crushed by Sambar Rusa, with power given back to the native Chitalas. The nation takes up 28% of Sambaria.
