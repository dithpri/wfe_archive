The United Republic Socialist States of Illryicum was founded upon generations of Slavic peoples living in oppression, overcoming class warfare by their self proclaimed superiors and the resulting ideological enlightenment of this cycle. Referendums pass and lives are still lost in the name of overthrowing the imperialist power. But the time of revolution has come.
The revised vision of Tito in a modernized socioeconomic development program has become the pinnacle of the URSSI. Meritocratic accomplishment, national and individual identity and equity make the core URSSI virtues.
