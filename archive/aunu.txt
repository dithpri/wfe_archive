[b]Aunu[/b] is a region characterized by dusty red crystal deserts, fertile valleys, and expansive inland river deltas. Nations here convene as the [b]Aunuan Nomarchy[/b], a confederated mixed monarchy guided by the [b]Archnomarch[/b] of the House of Apsos. The regional legislative, the [b]Nomarchial Diet[/b], is a venue for nations to coordinate, negotiate, and come to decisions on affairs of both regional and interregional importance.
