The Organization of American States, for short, OAS, is an international organization based in Brasilia, South American Federativity, which is the only nation on the American continent.

Any nation to emerge on the continent can freely join.
The institution for life - or until there is a resignation - administered by the South American Federativity is free from any form of oppression or dictatorship.  Future associate members will be able to run for diplomatic and international image positions.
