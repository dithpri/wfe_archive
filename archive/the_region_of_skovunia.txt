The Region of Skovunia contains the members of the real-life Micronation of Skovunia. Each nation within this region represents a citizen or resident of Skovunia. All members of the region are bound to the ordinances of Skovunian Parliament. However, the freedom of speech and voting is guaranteed to the citizens. Each citizen is free to vote in the World Assembly as they choose, whether it is in line with the policies of the Nation of Skovunia, or not.
