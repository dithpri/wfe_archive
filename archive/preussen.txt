[region]Preussen[/region] or Prussia, is an integral part of Germany.

K&ouml;nigsberg was the Capital of Prussia from 1525-1701 when it was moved to Berlin. In 1871, German states united in creating the German Empire under Prussian leadership. 
In November 1918, The Kingdom of Prussia was abolished during the socialist German Revolution of 1918&#150;19. 
Free State of Prussia, existed as a state of Germany within the new Weimar Republic from 1918 until 1933 until incorporation into the Reich, and lasted until 1947.
