[hr][color=#FF0000][b]What is the Drossel Treaty ?[/b][/color]
[hr]
After the fall of the Drossel Empire, the four largest former kingdoms ([nation]Saemendre[/nation], [nation]Drossellys[/nation], [nation]Endilya[/nation] and [nation]Gryvnah[/nation]) decided to unite despite their differences, to ensure the continuity of peace in the former territories of the Empire.
[i]Cohabitation is sometimes difficult, but the pact holds firm.
[/i]
[u]New memberships are possible, with the agreement of the four sovereign members.[/u]
