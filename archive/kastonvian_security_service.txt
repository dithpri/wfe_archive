A quick reaction force operated by the Kastonvian Security Service to assist with region-wide threats to combat catastrophic world ending scenarios.

The [b]Cerberus Detachment[/b] is specialized for providing quick time physical security and containment of unique situations that require well equipped and trained onsite security forces.

The [b]Chimera Detachment[/b] is a CBRN force that is trained to deploy in chemical, biological, radiological and nuclear environments to suppress any threats present.

The [b]Cyclops Detachment[/b] is a heavily armed detachment of the KSS that is able to eradicate and put down any threats that are present in its area of operations with overwhelming firepower.
