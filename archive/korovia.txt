[color=orange][b]Welcome to Korovia![/b][/color]

Korovia is a modern tech roleplaying region dedicated to creating realistic geopolitical situations, empathizing detail and quality as opposed to quantity. 

To apply for citizenship, [url=http://www.nskorovia.com/forums/ucp.php?mode=register][B]register on the offsite forums[/B][/url] and post in the [url=http://www.nskorovia.com/forums/viewtopic.php?f=2&amp;t=11][B]application thread[/B][/url]. Citizenship is required to participate in regional roleplaying, voting, and government.
