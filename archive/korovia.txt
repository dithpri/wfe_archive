[color=darkblue]Welcome to the region of Korovia![/color]

Korovia is a Modern Tech (MT) role-play region in which various countries come together to participate in a diverse community.[hr]
To become a citizen of Korovia please sign-up on our offsite forum http://korovia.freeforums.net/ here and check out our regional http://iiwiki.com/wiki/Korovia IIwiki . It is also required that any nation that is currently a member of the World Assembly endorse the current WA Delegate and President of the region, Ohino. Please contact our Secretary of State, Noldorin Oron, for external affairs.
