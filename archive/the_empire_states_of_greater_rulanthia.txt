[b]The Empire States of Greater Rulanthia[/b]
Where YOU are what makes The Rulanthian Empire GREAT!

On the evening of the 11th of December 2015, the first of the nations that would become The Greater Rulanthian Empire came together. Some joined willingly, some were annexed from the lands of neighboring nations and some were brought kicking and screaming into the alliance by leaders who saw in it great power for themselves. But even as the Empire continues to grow there is dissent amongst its member nations, between those who stand with the Empire and those who would see it brought down. And all the while the ruler of the Rulanthian Empire watches his pawns infighting and smiles.
