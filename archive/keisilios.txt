The region of nations all share the same roots of the once powerful, ancient Empire of Keisili. The nations share a common language of Modern Keisili with a few varying dialects. The regional motto, &quot;Tuseiki Kurop Fareis Tusou Libounil,&quot; translates directly to, &quot;Our Strength Makes Us Free.&quot;

Keisiki Virosili Ke apeton os natonos everein reguo ke everos rakupou os ke an kuropei, ankeisi Empisilos ke Keisili. Ke natonos reguo an commei langet ke Moderoseis Keisili til an sparek varion anolt. Ke apetoneis sarot, &quot;Tuseiki Kurop Fareis Tusou Libounil,&quot; Tranlangei direktor kot, &quot;Our Strength Makes Us Free,&quot; reis Engeilosi.
