Argenti, the region that rebelled against Aurelia.

Argenti is the Silver lands. The golden lands no more!

Laws of the region-

No one is to leave the region, unless you get permission from the president.
No one is allowed to use the RMB, unless there is an alert or message from the regional leaders.
WA delegates are illegal.
Foreign products are illegal and anyone that holds these items will be arrested.
Guns and other weapons will not be allowed to be used by civilians.
Protests are illegal. 
Contact with the outside world is highly prohibited.
All flags are to be red, or have something red on the flag.
