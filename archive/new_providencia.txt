After their entire region was wiped out years ago by the massive armies of McBryarstan, the survivors of the raped land looked for a new home. They became a nomadic people for many months, drifting from one place to another, always on the run from McBryarstan&#39;s secret police, who&#39;s goal was to exterminate the entire Providencian race. Finally, the refugees happened upon a new land, untarnished by modern man. This region was dubbed New Providencia, and great celebration was had. However, the brand new citizens knew that they must keep their lands a secret. Word could never get out to McBryarstan that they had a new home, otherwise they would be immediately erradicated. As a new day dawns, there is cautious optimism among the Providencians...
