This is a region where all Nations Anarchic to Tyrannical are welcome. It is primarily for nations founded by students of Hochschule Rhein Waal, but all other nations are welcome to.

There is no requirements for entry and feel free to invite others to join.

To be elected for WA delegate you have to be WA member and get endorsed by other nations in the region. The Nation with the most endorsments will get to be WA delegate.
