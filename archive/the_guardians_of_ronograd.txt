We are the [b]Guardians of Ronograd.[/b]
Ronograd is a &quot;Fictional&quot; Island Located Near Russia, However, not under the control of Russia, The Island is Captive by the PoD (Patriots of Democracy), an Insurgency force whose goal is to rid us of the island. 

The goal of the Guardians is to take back the island and protect it, and Ultimately Spread to protect isolated locations that are being Held Captive by Insurgency Forces.

(Plot of &quot;Ronograd&quot; Derived from the Roblox Game &quot;Blackhawk Rescue Mission 5&quot;)
