In 1465 This land was settled by English speaking colonists who lived as a loose  federation of states however in 1629 A.D. the states of the land united together to defend their land from invading forces made up of a coalition of former colonies of another empire. After 10 years of war and the final battle in Lawithon&#39;s capital, the city of Anoraxi in 1639 A.D. the Federation of Ialleoxar defeated the coalition. This marked the true beginning of the Federation of Ialleoxar with their formal creation being in 1640 A.D. after the signing of their Constitution.
