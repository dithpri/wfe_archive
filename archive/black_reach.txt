All countries located in Black Reach MUST convert to the Imperial Credit for trade purposes and economic unity exceptions may be accepted with economically strong nations with prior trade agreements. Any Nation wishing for an exemption must apply to the region WA delegate and Region founder.

All countries are required to participate in the Unified Task Force. A military coalition intended to suppress out land raiders on the unsettled northeastern fringes of Black Reach and general defense of the Region.

Induction into the galactic Fleet will be offered to any nation with a population of 500 million or greater. This decision should not be taken lightly.
