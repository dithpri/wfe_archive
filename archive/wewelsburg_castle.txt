[color=663300][b]Wewelsburg Castle[/b] was built between 1603 to 1609 in the style of the Weser-Renaissance; it served as a second residence for the Prince Bishops of Paderborn. This triangular castle rises high above the valley of the River Alme and sits majestically on a mountain ridge in the village of Wewelsburg, a suburb of the town of B&uuml;ren in the County of Paderborn.[/color]
