In 2024, the Principality of Lopandu almost forcefully entered the Alliance. In 2026 The United Communist Republics of Bojikami were persuaded to join, it was a success. The Alliance&amp;#39;s territories are located mainly in the Pacific and Indian oceans, with Bojikami in Asia and Vsenyslavia in Europe.
