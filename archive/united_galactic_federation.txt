Welcome to The United Galactic Federation,

The United Galactic Federation is a Inter-planetary organization, that aims to promote Inter-planetary cooperation and achieve economic, political, security, military, educational and sociocultural integration among the members of the Federation.

It also aims to maintain Inter-planetary peace and security, development of friendly nations among nations, and being the center of harmonizing the actions of nations.

The Great Nova Empire once rule these lands of Galactixa, Empress Astra Lexva of the Nova Empire wanted peace and unity among all nations. She believes as long as we&#39;re united, WE ARE UNSTOPPABLE. We hope of achieving what Empress Astra dreamed of.
