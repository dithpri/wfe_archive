[b]The Union of Federative Soviet Republics (UFSR), is a socialist union in Eurasia that exists from it&#39;s foundation on the 22 of December, 1922 to the present. Nominally, the Union of multiple national Soviet republics, its government and economy are highly centralized. The country is a one-party state, governed by the Communist Party with Moscow as its capital in its largest republic, the Russian Social Federative Soviet Republic. Russians dominate the USSR, extending across the entirety of Northern Asia and much of Eastern Europe.[/b]
