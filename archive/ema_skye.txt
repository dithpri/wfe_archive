[b]Ema Skye[/b] is a forensics scientist in the Los Angeles Criminal Affairs Department who was formerly the detective in charge of Apollo Justice&#39;s first few cases. As a teenager, nine years before she became a detective, she was Phoenix Wright&#39;s investigative partner and co-counsel in the murder of Bruce Goodman and also aided Miles Edgeworth in some of his cases.

Ema Skye is responsible for introducing forensic investigation techniques to Wright, Edgeworth, Justice and Athena Cykes.

Ema Skye is also a great waifu, in fact, the greatest one there is.
