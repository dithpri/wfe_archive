
Twenty years had passed since the demonic war., due to rising tensions and the need of consolidating resource ever more so increasing World War 3 would start as a Chinese megacorporation would blitz through the entire world, but not until the Federation was formed. The people who are currently in charge of the Federation have built up influence over the past twenty years, gaining more control over the nations of Earth slowly but surely. After WW3 had ended, a new age of prosperity had begun as the Federation would start rebuilding out of the ashes of old.
