In 3274 the high andarian command decided to create a great mechanical fortress that was finished in 3278 and become the office of the Imperial Andarian Governor for this galaxy. Andromeda is divided between four main clusters that by default receive their orders from here. Later the mechanical fortress was named AAG (Andarian Andromeda Galaxy) and served as a hub of power of[region]Andarios[/region] in the Andromeda Galaxy.
