In an expedition sent by King Igor I of Beatlestania, the sailors found a little isolated archipelago of islands, who were inhabited by the people of Scottish Methland. The sailors then reported to King Igor that the land they found could be a good ally. King Igor then went to the archipelago himself, as he met the islands&#39; tyrant, Johnson. The meeting went well, as both the King and the Tyrant quickly became friends, and then made an alliance, which they called The Great Scottish Sea
