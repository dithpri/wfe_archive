[b]Welcome to the Federation of Free Nations (FFN)[/b]

The Federation of Free Nations is a region that believes in the august virtues of the justice, liberty and fraternity. Let no nation be turned away from the benevolent arms of equality and peace, and may the Federation reign supreme through the ages to come. All nations are equal here, no matter your government, no matter your religion, no matter the divisions that distinguish the nations of this region, physical or otherwise.
