Matriarcal tribes posess strong elements of 1800s Japanese Geisha Societies. Domestic Arts centeral to economic and cultural identity.  Many tribal rites are sexual in nature.  Tribes and Governments have strong democratic traditions.  Tribes not Civil governments run education, health care, and most social services.  Kubbutz like tribal areas intermingled with Laizze Faire non-tribal areas creates a unique economic mosaic.
