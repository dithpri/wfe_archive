Geshism is a transreligion spiritual movement premised on a seculare state upholding the rule of law, national defence, and property rights, with tribal government providing for social justice and education. Teen girls take the Geishan Vow, dedicating themselves as teachers or nurses.  Geishas typically have greater licence to break sexual taboo than other young women.

Things typical of Geishan Nations are: Religious freedom, Religious organiations having a tribal structure.  Elements of both Madisonian and Athenian Democracy. Languages incorperate numerous english cognates, and may be written in Hebrew script. Tribal Estates, rather than corperations, are the main vehicle for economic activity.
