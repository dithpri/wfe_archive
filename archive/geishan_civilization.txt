Geisha Empress represented by a Governess in national governments.  Courts and Ministers appointed by a popularly elected Legislature. Laws governing elections and duties of office holders can only be changed by referendum. Citizens have rights of sufferage (16yo), free expression, due process, and personal property.

Uzism is the largest religion, but there are geisha structured sects of Islam, Christianity, Taoism, Hindu, Judaism, etc.  Matriarcal tribal structured with Domestic Arts centeral to economic and cultural identity.  Many tribal rites have sexual undertones, a few being overtly sexual in nature.
