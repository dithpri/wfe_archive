Socialists of the World

Constitution of the Union of Socialists

-All nations in the Union of Socialists have the option to join World Assembly.

-All World Assembly members can vote for WA Delegate  .

-When the Union of Socialists grows to 10 or more nations dedicated to democratic socialism, access to the Union of Socialists Regional Control will be granted to the delegate representing this region.

-When the Union of Socialists has 9 or less nations, access to the Union of Socialists Regional Control will only be available to the founder of this region.
