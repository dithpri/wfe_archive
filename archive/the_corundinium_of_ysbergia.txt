The Corundinium of Ysbergia is a vast continent known for its rich, diverse landscapes and complex cultural tapestry. Situated in a colder climate zone, Ysbergia features towering snow-capped mountain ranges, dense evergreen forests, expansive tundras, and numerous river systems that nourish the land. The continent is divided into several regions, each with its own unique geography and environment.

Ysbergia is home to a variety of natural resources, including the precious corundium crystals found in its mountains, which are highly valued for their rarity and utility. The fertile river valleys and coastal areas support hardy crops, while the dense forests provide timber and other forest products.
