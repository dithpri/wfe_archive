A region of mountainous ranges, beautiful arid planes, a large plentiful coast and a remarkable polar north . Altiplano is always in constant war, as nations fight over the precious resources the land has to offer.
The most prominent nation is The Armed Republic of New Dova. New Dova founded the &quot;Altiplano Coalition States&quot; to maintain what little peace there is.
New Dova also happens to have the largest swathe of land in Altiplano, thus giving them the largest claim.
