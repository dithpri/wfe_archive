

The Imperial Legion Of Edochris is a role-play region, known for strong militaries. We are not corrupt, although some would call us that. We guard each other&#146;s backs, and we fight and die together.

 The Imperial Legion Of Edochris has several different states. However, we are not one country. We are however, a joined body of powers. The Legion acts as one body, and several of the more powerful nation&#146;s leaders meet to discuss different topics.
