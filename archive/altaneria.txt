Altaneria is a Planet with many Nations on it and very different cultures. 
The three main cultures are the Sols, who pray to the God of the Sun. The Floristins, who are one with nature itself. Then there are the Xals, who live after the agenda &quot;Honor can only be achieved by a successfull fight.&quot;
All Nations on the Planet are Members of the Altanerian Senat, whos task is to keep peace among the different cultures and Nations. The last years were very successfull with no war braking out between any nations.
