Callipolis is a new modern-tech, plausible roleplaying and worldbuilding community. A bizarre and creative world with religions, ideologies and cultures.  It is experimental, player orientated and for veterans and novices alike. We have a system of shared NPCs, a focus shared common histories and interesting and unique concepts.
