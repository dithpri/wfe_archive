-GEOGRAPHY
Western Macedonia has a mountainous nature, as most of the surface of the region is mountainous and home to many lakes and rivers. The region is also home to thick forests and great bio diversity. 

-ECONOMY 
The region of Western Macedonia has a stable, prosperous, high-tech and industrial economy.
