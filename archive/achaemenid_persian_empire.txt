&#149; [b]Greetings and welcome to the Achaemenid Persian Empire.[/b]

&#149; The [b]Achaemenid Empire[/b], or in Old Persian: [i]Parsa[/i], was an empire based in Western Asia in Iran, founded in the 6th century BC by Kurus the Great. At the height of its power, the empire ruled over 44% of the world&amp;#39;s population, the highest such figure for any empire in history. Also, its extend spanned into three continents: Asia, Europe and Africa.
