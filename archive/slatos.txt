[color=76157D][/b][b]Welcome to Slatos![/b][/color]
We are an open-minded democratic region featuring a constitutional monarchy. Citizens may vote in parliamentary elections or seek Government positions.
To become a citizen, endorse King [nation=noflag]Arkoudan[/nation], through which you delcare your loyalty both to him and to the Constitution of the region.[hr][color=gold][b]From the King[/b][/color]
The new endorsement cap is 8 for ministers, 5 for parliament members and 1 for other citizens.
