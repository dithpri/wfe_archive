The largest of the continents located on the planet of Karzivan, Sumygaia makes up around eighty-seven percent of the landmass found on the planet. In truth it is actually three smaller continents, but of such proximity and similarity that they have been classified as one.

Nations in Sumygaia: The Militant Trade Federation of Orestavia, The Empire of The Iron City of Abaddon, The Theocratic Principality of Klynom, The Holy Empire of Satan Lovers, The Dictatorship of Canadamk2 (Currently Under Invasion by Orestavia), and The Monarchic State Of Finth (Currently At War With Orestavia, Klynom, and Abaddon)
