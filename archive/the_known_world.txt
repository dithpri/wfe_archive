[b][region]The Known World[/region][/b] is a RP region set in a more modern version of Game of Thrones world. The world has gone through an industrial revolution and Westeros has been united under the House of Lannister after the Great War against the Starks and Targaryens in 310 AC. The Ice King and his army of the dead have been defeated and winter has come and gone.
