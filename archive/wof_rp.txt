This is an RP server for the world of Fallout. The land we use is the U.S.A. and Canada. The only land talked about in the Fallout Bible. An RP land entails you...

1. Make a nation specifically for an RP
2. Your &quot;nation&quot; is the faction in-game
3. All actions you do is said in chat
4. As an RP nation, you have to keep in mind that your issue choices also have to make sense in the Fallout world
