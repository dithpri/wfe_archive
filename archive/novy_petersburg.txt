Novy Petersburg is a mostly temperate region though it has an extreme polar environment to the north, which is a popular home to labor camps. The region has superb access to the Rossi Sea, and nations in the region usually have at least two port cities.
Socialism is sanctioned by the [nation]Chirpovskiy Soyuz[/nation], which uses its powers to enforce a worker&#39;s paradise in this region.
