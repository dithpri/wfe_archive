[b]Hizor[/b]: (Shivering Isles II)
Hizor was founded after the collapse of the region known as The Shivering Isles. 
Saving technology from the old region allowed use to continue work on progressing humanity beyond it&#39;s limits, here we all work peacefully towards scientific advancement and saving the flame from what was once the Shivering Isles.
