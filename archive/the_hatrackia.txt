The Hatrackia was formed as an alliance of related nations that congregated to protect against the foreign threats, such as &quot;rational thought&quot; or &quot;common sense&quot;. The Hatrackia is lead by the Hatrack, a nation led by the council of discord that votes for the nation&#39;s policy with eternal wisdom. 

The goal of the region is to separate from the world of rationality and promote the ideas of the partaking nations.
