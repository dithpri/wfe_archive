The **Atlantic Commonwealth Market (ACM)** is an international free trade organization dedicated to promoting economic cooperation, open markets, and sustainable growth among its member states. By reducing trade barriers, encouraging investment, and harmonizing commercial standards, the ACM strengthens economic ties across the Atlantic while supporting prosperity, innovation, and the free movement of goods and services.
The **Atlantic Commonwealth Market (ACM)** was founded in **1998** by the **New-English Commonwealth** to strengthen economic cooperation and promote free trade among Atlantic nations.
