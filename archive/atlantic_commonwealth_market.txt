The Atlantic Commonwealth Market (ACM) is an international free trade organization that promotes economic cooperation, free trade, and sustainable growth among its member states by reducing trade barriers, encouraging investment, and supporting the free movement of goods and services.

Founded in 1998 by the New-English Commonwealth, the ACM was established to strengthen economic ties between Atlantic nations.
