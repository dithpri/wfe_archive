The Respectable Nations is a Sporadically Growing region, on 4/10/24, the RN founded a war room and conducted its first special military operation against an opposing violent separatist region.



Founded on 3/20/24 

The Respectable Nations, commonly known as the RN, is a allegiance between multiple nations that function as a single country, conflicts in between the region are regulated and land disputes are resolved in council meetings, there is a President though there is no democratic election once a President is elected, but Vice Presidents are elected via poll elections and terms last 3 days.
