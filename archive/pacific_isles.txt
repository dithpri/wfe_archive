[B][region]Pacific Isles[/region][/b] is a confederation of independent regions in [b]Pacific Ocean[/b] founded on November 7th, 2016 to promote inter-regional co-operation due to the inability of [url=https://www.nationstates.net/page=un]World Assembly[/url] to address Pacific regions affairs.

[Color=blue]The mission of the [region]Pacific Isles[/region] is to maintain peace and security, promoting human rights, fostering social and economic development, protecting the environment, and providing humanitarian aid in cases of famine, natural disaster, and armed conflict in [b]Pacific Ocean[/b].[/color]
