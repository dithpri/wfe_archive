The Eastern Bugiblock is a region known for its astonishing nature and its wide coastlines. Political every nation is sovereign and a variety of political ideologies are used by its members. Geographical Arenbor is by far the biggest nation, going down from the north through the whole west coast, boardering all other countries. Uniting nearly every climate zone in a stunning nature and landscape. The northeasternmost nation is the cold Folkhemmetstat with its polar lights and fir forests. All the way in the south, there is the sunny mediterranean Hepta Helios. In the Middle East there is the barren and inhospitable Adoda Hittih. Western of the Arenborian coast, lays the rocky Frigate Islands.
