In a bright sparkle-laden coast, beyond the warm pink-hued beach, you encounter a new world. A postmodern soft-cyberpunk fantasy world with magic. A place of wonder and great alchemy watched over by a divine order, but one that is also gradually subdued by a manmade force that seeks to reap dominion everywhere, despite lessons of a destructive past.
