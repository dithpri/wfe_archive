The Regional Cooperation Association (RCA) is a regional intergovernmental political and military association consisting of nations in the geographical vicinity of the Empire of Confederated Realms with the proclaimed objective of &quot;Ensuring stability, security and favourable trade for the region.&quot;

The Empire of Confederated Realms is the permanent and eternal executive authority of the RCA. Its Head of State is the Chairman of the RCA.

[B]All member states must not:[/b]

Communicate with the World Assembly or any of its subsidiary organisations without case-by-case permission from the Imperial House of Shaw&#39;s delegate to the WA.

Refuse any and all requests for access to their nation by the Confederated Armed Forces of the Empire of Confederated Realms.
