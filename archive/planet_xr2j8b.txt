After a mass evacuation from an isolated island that was plagued by a virus, the original people found a planet in a nearby solar system. The ten-million original people split off to form, Our Home Planet &amp;amp; My Home Planet, some were lost for years and became The Nomadic People Of Some Forrest, and finally; One Angry Farmer took to the Borderlands in the Southern Hemisphere where generation after generation of inbred hillbillies became a functioning country.
