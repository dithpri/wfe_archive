Caelora is a sovereign region founded under the authority of [nation=long]Omis[/nation], though it stands as an entity unto itself &#151; home to a diverse collection of nations, each governed according to its own laws, values, and traditions. Caelora does not impose a unified ideology upon its member nations, instead serving as a shared space for cooperation, coexistence, and the occasional heated debate.

The region takes its name from the old Omisian word for &quot;open sky&quot; &#151; a reflection of its founding principle that many different kinds of nations can thrive under the same roof, so long as they respect the integrity of the region itself.

Caelora is administered by its founder and welcomes nations of all stripes.
