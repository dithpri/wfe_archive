On 30 August 1924, the Reichsbank began issuing the Republican paper currency; Reichsmark. The change from hard currency to paper money allowed the printing of money which through hyperinflation became useless.

1937 Reich law re-established the Reich Government&#39;s control of the Reichsbank
1939 Reichsbank renamed [region]Deutsche Reichsbank[/region] with Walther Funk as president 

The Reichsmark Paper currency, based on intangible values is replaced by the German Goldmark; on a gold standard with 2790 Mark equal to 1 kilogram of pure gold (1 Mark = 358 mg).
