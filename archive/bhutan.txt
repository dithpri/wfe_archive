[i]Bhutan is a landlocked country in South Asia, located in Eastern Himalayas. Bhutan is notable for pioneering the concept of [b][url=http://www.gnhcentrebhutan.org/what-is-gnh/]gross national happiness[/url][/b][/i]

[hr]

The region of Bhutan follows a similar goal of becoming the happiest (cheeriest) region in Nationstates.  All nations are welcome in Bhutan, as long as there is an intention to improve the [b][url=https://www.nationstates.net/page=list_nations/mode=g/region=bhutan/censusid=40]cheerfulness[/url][/b] of its residents.
