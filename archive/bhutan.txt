[i][b]Bhutan [/b] is a landlocked country in South Asia, located in Eastern Himalayas. 

The independence of Bhutan has endured for centuries and it has never been colonised in its history. Bhutan is notable for pioneering the concept of [b]gross national happiness[/b][/i]

[hr]

The Nationstates region of Bhutan follows a similar principle with the goal of becoming the happiest (Cheerfullest) region in the game. All nations are welcome to Bhutan, as long as there is an intention to improve the [b][url=https://www.nationstates.net/page=list_nations/mode=g/region=bhutan/censusid=40]cheerfulness[/url][/b] of its citizens.
