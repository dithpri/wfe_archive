[i]Bhutan is a landlocked country in South Asia, located in Eastern Himalayas. Bhutan is notable for pioneering the concept of [b][url=http://ophi.org.uk/policy/national-policy/gross-national-happiness-index/]gross national happiness[/url][/b][/i]

[hr]

The region of Bhutan follows a similar goal of becoming the happiest region in the game. All nations are welcome, as long as there is an intention to improve the happiness of its citizens. [b][url=https://www.nationstates.net/page=list_nations/mode=g/region=bhutan/censusid=40]Cheerfulness[/url][/b] is one philosophy, however leaders are encouraged to share their own paths to happiness.
