Omicron Persei 8 is a planet situated about 1000 lightyears from Earth. The rulers of the planet are Lrrr and his wife Ndnd. Its surface seems to consist mainly of deserts.

In 3017, Mike Hoochie travelled to Omicron Persei 8 to retrieve an artifact that would help clear the universe of hypnowaves. Lrrr, upset because he was using the artifact to steal cable channels from Earth, invaded New New York, and wouldn&#39;t leave until President Nixon turned Mike Hoochie over to him. The Omicronians were surprised that Mike Hoochie put up such a fight, and decided to call off their attack.
