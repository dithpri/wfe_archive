The TGE is Tyrnah&#39;s foray into establishing goodwill with other Nation States. We are excited to welcome all who focus on the development and richness of their own nations first and then seek, at a very close second, to share this growth and success with those around them that all who take part in the TGE might grow in solidarity and prosperity. We will never conscript your forces for the purposes of conquering; however, we may in times of peril request reinforcements for the purposes of defense.
