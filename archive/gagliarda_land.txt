Afterward, because of the rising tide of foreign interference in the region and the plight of other little nations, the members of the League -named &quot;Gagliardi&quot;- decided to admitt other little states with market economy -or even dictatorships- in order to oppose a united front against foreign imperialistic intrusions.

The main tasks of the League of [region]Gagliarda Land[/region] is to ensure total economic and military cooperation between the Gagliardi with little or no intrusion into its members&#39; domestic policy affairs.
