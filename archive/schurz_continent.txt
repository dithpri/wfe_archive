The continent of Schurz is one of incredible diversity. A large landmass located in the middle of the Pacific Ocean, Schurz has mountains, deserts, rainforests, and grasslands. Though all peoples living on the Schurz continent can trace their roots to a Proto-Indo-Schurzian culture that existed thousands of years ago, the people living on the continent are a diverse group of inhabitants with a wide variety of languages, beliefs, traditions, and history.
