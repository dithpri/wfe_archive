The IAO is a military and economic alliance spearheaded by the Star Republic of Osir, and open to any ally state of the Republic. to become a member, a nation must commit 10% or higher of their expenditure to both their military budget, and their industry budget each. Alternatively to spending 10% or higher on military, a nation may commit 20% or higher to their law enforcement budget or higher.

All members nations must be active at least three days a week. Should a nation not meet the three day requirement they will be kicked until they can prove they are committed to active service.
