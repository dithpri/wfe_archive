Stretching from Mother Earth and Luna, beyond Mars, and the belts of the outer planets, to the ring gates, the mighty Medina station and the 1,300 ring gates to the Laconian Empire.

Find your place, are you a belter, fighting for freedom, or maybe just sling shot racing. 

Do you dream of a Mars with free air?

Are you shrewd enough to master Earths political challenges, and still have enough power to overcome the problems of climate change and over population?

Whatever it may be there is a place for you within the Expanse
