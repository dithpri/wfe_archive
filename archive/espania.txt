Spain. Was one of the largest empires in history, it was, in conjunction with the Portuguese, the first to usher the European Age of Discovery and achieve a global scale, controlling vast portions of the Americas, the archipelago of Philippines, various islands in the Pacific and territories in Western Europe and Africa. It was one of the world&#39;s most powerful empires of the Early modern period, becoming known as &quot;the empire on which the sun never sets&quot;, and reached its maximum extent in the 18th century.
