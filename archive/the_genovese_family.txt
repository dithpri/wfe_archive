Established in March 2023, The Genovese Family is a new and influential alliance that values loyalty, strength, and honor above all else. Comprised of a tight-knit group of brothers who share common goals and interests, The Genovese Family is known for its unwavering commitment to achieving its objectives and ensuring the safety and prosperity of its members.
