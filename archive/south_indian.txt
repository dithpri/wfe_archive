Welcome to South India! Nestled in the southernmost part of the Indian subcontinent, South India is a vibrant region steeped in history, culture, and natural beauty. Bordered by the Arabian Sea to the west and the Bay of Bengal to the east, South India is known for its diverse landscapes ranging from the Western Ghats mountain ranges to the lush green forests, serene backwaters, and sun-kissed beaches. The region&#39;s rich cultural heritage is reflected in its art, architecture, and the diverse languages and dialects spoken by its people.
