A region of card collectors who are looking to dethrone the current card champions. 

POLICIES
1. If another member of this region is bidding on a card, you should not outbid them unless:
- Someone else bids more than they have 
- They telegram you otherwise
- They are inactive for 5 minutes
2. Try to monopolize cards with few copies within this region
3. Feel free to establish trade agreements with fellow alliance members
4. You must engage in trading cards. If you have a deck value less than 100 and have not made any recent trades, you will be kicked from the region
5. Do not send any threatening telegrams to members of the region.

*Anyone who violates a trade agreement will be banned from the region
