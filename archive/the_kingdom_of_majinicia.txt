The Kingdom of Majinicia is a magical place that has a Royal family that consists of the King, his wife, and three children. The Kingdom has 6 Lords, and 3 Governors. 2 lords and 1 Governor for each province in the Kingdom. The government is a constitutional  monarchy, unitary parliamentary system, with the King as the head of state. The Kingdom is a progressive nation with Gay rights and Discrimination completely illegal (you can get charged for acts of discrimination and hate crimes towards races, ages, or sexuality) 
The Kingdom is a beautiful place that has many possibilities to grow and build a life.
