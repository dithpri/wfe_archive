A continent encompasses a large region of its globe. Ruul has a desert to the north and a plains to the south. The desert encroaches on to said plains and attempts at preventing the spread is underway. The continent is poor of natural resources, with metals being sparse and agriculture stunted due to the lack of nutrient rich soil and water available. despite the continent&#39;s tough environment, its people persist. While tyranny continues to plague the continent, the present generations are laying the foundation for a prospering society for the future to thrive in.
