The Papacy of Geneva is a region for all nations with a Thrinian Catholic population in communion with Geneva. The region is lead by the Papal City although the pope has no direct involvement with the daily administration of the region. The Thrinian Catholic Church is one of the most inclusive and open christian institutions in NS allowing for great religious diversity throughout its jurisdictions.
