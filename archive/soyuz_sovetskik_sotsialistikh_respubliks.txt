The Soviet Union, officially the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics, abbreviated as the USSR (Russian: SSSR), is a socialist state in Eurasia that exists from 1922 to forever. Nominally a union of multiple national Soviet republics, still its government and economy are highly centralized. The country is an infinite party state, governed by the Communist Party, with Moscow as its capital in its largest republic, the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic. Other major urban centres are Leningrad,Kiev,Minsk,Tashkent and Novosibirsk. It is a founding permanent member of the United Nations Security Council and the Warsaw Pact.
