El Mar Caribe is home to La Republica Federal de las Antillas Mayores, as well as the Republic of the Bahamas, the Republic of Jamaica, the East Caribbean Confederation, the Republic Trinidad and Tobago, the Republic of Guyana, The Republic of Suriname, The Republic of Guiana, Venezuela, Brazil, Colombia, Panama, Honduras, Nicaragua, Costa Rica, and the People&amp;#39;s Republic of Belize. These sixteen states form the Caribbean Union, a strong multinational entity that rivals the EU in economic and military power. It serves as a strong buffer against U.S. American hegemony as well as Chinese, Russian, and European intrusion. The region is fiercely proud of its mixed African, European, and Indigenous heritage.
