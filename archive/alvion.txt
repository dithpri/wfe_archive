[b]T[/b]he bells ring in Tyria, Valastiria, signalling the initiation of a fresh hunt by self-proclaimed Queen Lady Eleonora. According to scribes, this is the highest amount of prey in this hunt since it marks Lady Eleonora&#39;s second year in power and 15 years since the pact of Linz, which saw Valastiria become a major name in Alvion&#39;s history.

Lady Eleonora has invited all foreign heads of state, nobles, merchants, and hunters to attend the great hunt festivities in Valastiria. A few lucky, and interested, individuals will be able to participate in the hunt.
