[b]What if the Axis Powers won World War 2?[/b]
The Neo Roman Empire, also known as the [b]New Roman Empire[/b] and sometimes known as the [b]Greater Italian Empire[/b] is a state dominating the Mediterranean Seas, as well as North, Middle and some of East Africa. In this world, Italy was actually prepared for World War 2 and managed to grab what it needed to rebuild its glory days. Italy turned into an economic powerhouse following the Berlin Treaties, and established many modern versions of ancient Roman ways.
