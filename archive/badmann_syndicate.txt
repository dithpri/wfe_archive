After the ruins of the London riots 2011; a new hope had arisen, the working people of London had lost their political apathy and a new system rose out of the dying ashes of the old. Number 10 downing street had been destroyed by a acetone peroxide bomb during the night, severely injuring most members. With all these factors into play the power vacuum usually taken up by a warlord lusting for power, was instead replaced by a form market anarchism. with some notes of collectivism, regular individuals could enjoy trading their goods without coercion.
