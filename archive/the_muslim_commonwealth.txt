[b]&laquo; [color=green]The Muslim Commonwealth[/color] &raquo;[/b]
~~ [i][color=green]Territory of [region]The Dominion[/region][/color][/i] ~~

[color=indigo]The Muslim Commonwealth is a region for Muslim and non-Muslim nations alike to engage in trade, military maneuvers and cultural exchanges. TMC seeks to be an exemplary example of a Muslim Region and prides itself on its commitment to Islamic unity and the rule of law. [/color]

[i]On July 30, 2014, the region of The Muslim Commonwealth was annexed by the imperial forces of [region]The Dominion[/region].[/i]
