The International military organization with allies in several regions, working to provide aid in wars. MiliGlobAllie stands for Military Global Alliance. 
MiliGlobAllie is an organization with Assosciated Nations Register, where nations may register to have help in wars. If a nation registered is attacked, they will be gauranteed the military support of MiliGlobAllie nations, and additional [url=https://www.nationstates.net/nation=cypriot_military_order_of_malta/detail=factbook/id=824433]Registered Nations[/url] if needed.
