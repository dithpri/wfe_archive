What is the Chavolence Coalition?

This is a region where we get to role-play and adapt to any circumstance from which member nations present. This region was created in order to be a safe sanctuary for the more crazy nations, the ones that likely got booted from their regions for being to creative in their role-play.

What this Coalition offers you:

Freedom to role-play to your hearts content, no rules regarding how you wish to run your nation. Do you like sci-fi, and wish to have colonies in space? You can do that. Do you fancy yourself a massive empire, stretching millions of miles? You can do that. You can do whatever you want to do, without fear of retaliation:
