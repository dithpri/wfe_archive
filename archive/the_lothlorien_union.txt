Founded in 1630 as the union of former Rhunich colonies. The Lothlorien Union (LU) is a group of 25 sovereign states which belong to 5 bands. Each band (5 states) is an autonomous Republic within the union having their own stock markets, languages, cultural tendencies and militias. Together they have one unified currency called the Unified Mint as well as a unified military called the Unified Militia. Although they are not allowed to fight amongst one another. More to come...
