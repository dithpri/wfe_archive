Manchukuo, officially the&nbsp;State of Manchuria&nbsp;prior to 1934 and the&nbsp;Empire of Manchuria&nbsp;after 1934, was a&nbsp;puppet state&nbsp;of the&nbsp;Empire of Japan&nbsp;in&nbsp;Northeast China&nbsp;and&nbsp;Inner Mongolia&nbsp;from 1932 until 1945. It was founded as a&nbsp;republic&nbsp;in 1932 after the&nbsp;Japanese invasion of Manchuria, and in 1934 it became a&nbsp;constitutional monarchy. Under the&nbsp;de facto&nbsp;control of Japan, it had limited&nbsp;international recognition.
