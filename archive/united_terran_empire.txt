With democracy failing again and again in the long run, many nations have chosen to institute a more permanent form of government. Thus allowing truly progressive societies to form where action is taken instead of being deliberated on indefinitely, only for policies to change a few short years later.

The United Terran Empire was formed with this in mind. We play a truly forward thinking society with plenty of diversity. Democracy is not frowned upon and can even be helpful in some cases, but it is a one minded government that will truly last and not risk fracturing the populace.
