One Region, indivisible, with liberty and justice for all!

Come join and live in peace.

- Come wanting to enjoy democracy and protecting others if we are called.
-  All major issues will be determined by vote in the region!
- All sides of the issues will be discussed to gain the best resolve for the region.
- The more variety of opinions, the better!
- Dissenting opinion will strongly be protected!

A region of the people, by the people, for the people!

Welcome!
