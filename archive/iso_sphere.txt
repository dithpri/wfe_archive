The [b]ISO Sphere[/b] is conjunction point of several otherwise isolated Multiverses. [nation=short+noflag]-Sakkara[/nation] discovered the [b]ISO Sphere[/b]. As other Civilizations of different Multiverses explored the [b]ISO Sphere[/b] they came into contact with [b]Sakkara[/b] they established diplomatic and trade contacts and eventually formed the [b]ISO Alliance[/b] - a free Alliance of Civilizations.

The [b]ISO Alliance[/b] is holding some low profile contacts with the [region]SICON[/region] Alliance as well as with the [region]Underverse[/region].
