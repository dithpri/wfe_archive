The Isles of Finland trace over the rivers, lakes and oceans in and near Finland. The Isles declare themselves a peace of neutrality in a deadly place, where two powers fought for years. The Isles prefer peace and strive for civil rights and Economic growth. They consist of 234 Islands that are all different nation, with different kings. They all bow down to the Prime King, though, Tom IV, or the Republic Of Neutral Isles.
