\\\[b]THE CONSTITUTION OF THE NEVIL REGION[/b]

The Nevil Region was founded in 2021 by the Kingdom of Nevilandia.

The Region was founded in order to create friendships that bridge political gaps.

The Nevil Region has five values that serve as the central tenets to our existence.

1. [i]Democracy is a human right.[/i] (optional)
2. [i]All people are people.[/i]
3. [i]It is okay to laugh at birds sometimes.[/i]
4. [i]All nations are welcome to join.[/i]
5. [i]Barbecue Sauce is a valid condiment.[/i]

Rules-
1. We encourage fun and foolishness, but if you are elected to a council role please take it seriously.
2. Elections are held when a councilors spot is challenged.
