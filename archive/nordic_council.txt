The [region]Nordic Council[/region] is a prosperous and affluent council of Northern European nations, currently headquartered in Reykjav&iacute;k ([nation]Norway and Iceland[/nation]). The Council welcomes all nations to our corner of Northern Europe and prays that we will be able to work together for the betterment of the World. 

The Council and its members are active voting nations of the World General Assembly and the Security Council.
