In the heart of central Africa, the frontier spirit engendered a hardy breed. Hard work and a generous land greatly contributed to the prosperity of all the subcontinent.

[color=#0080FF][i][b]Rhodes and his empire were on the mind of early pioneers who headed off in search of adventure and a better life.
