Aellurum is currently ruled by the Scristolian Empire, an authoritarian regime controlled by a monarch. Aellurum is a naturally beautiful land except in Scristol and other areas where industry is prevalent. Aellurum is a member of the World Assembly. Aellurum was solidified on the Eighth of September 2016 by the Scristolian Ministry of Foreign Affairs.
