The technical Western Kandarin lies to the east of the strange lands of Tirannwn, sitting on the border of the Arandar Pass, and in the western section of the kingdom of Kandarin.

This area was home to years of bloodshed between the many types of people who inhabit or have inhabited this place, including the nature-loving people of the west to the conquistador-like misled religious fanatics of the east and the smaller-in-stature isolationist people who used to inhabit this region but were vanquished.

The most notable event to occur in this forsaken area is with the new rising of the theocratic empire of West Zamorakia, a cultist gathering to worship the God of Chaos himself, Zamorak.
