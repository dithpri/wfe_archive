The [b][colour=354457]World Treaty Organization[/colour][/b] (WTO) is an international alliance between democratic nations created by the [url=https://www.nationstates.net/page=dispatch/id=830787][colour=354457][b]World Treaty[/b][/colour][/url]. Our motto is [i]&quot;Per Unitatem, Pax&quot;[/i], latin for &quot;Through Unity, Peace&quot;.

Members of the WTO form the [url=https://forum.nationstates.net/viewtopic.php?f=4&amp;t=413346][b][colour=354457]World Council[/colour][/b][/url] (WC), headed by General Secretary Alexander Waite of [nation=short]Unitum Provincias[/nation]. The [colour=354457][b]World Peacekeeping Forces[/b][/colour] (WPF), headed by Supreme Commander Alexander Waite of [nation=short]Unitum Provincias[/nation], is the sole and official military of the nations of the WTO.
