The [b][colour=354457]World Treaty Organization[/colour][/b] (WTO) is an international alliance between democratic nations based on the [url=https://www.nationstates.net/page=dispatch/id=830787][colour=354457][b]World Treaty[/b][/colour][/url].

Members of the WTO form the [url=https://forum.nationstates.net/viewtopic.php?f=4&amp;t=413346][b][colour=354457]World Council[/colour][/b][/url], headed by General Secretary Alexander Waite of [nation=short]Unitum Provincias[/nation]. They also form a collective defense alliance called the [colour=354457][b]World Command Operation[/b][/colour] (WCO), headed by Supreme Commander Richard Knolls of [nation=short]Unitum Provincias[/nation].

Wish to join the WTO? Contact [url=https://www.nationstates.net/nation=unitum_provincias][b][colour=354457]General Secretary Waite[/colour][/b][/url] with your intent to ratify the Treaty.
