**The Imperium of Old Liverpool**

From the storm-swept shores of the Mersey rises a region forged in ambition, unity, and imperial will. The Imperium of Old Liverpool is a gathering place for sovereign nations that value strength, order, and the enduring legacy of empire.

Founded under the banner of the Empire of Old Liverpool, the region stands as a symbol of imperial resurgence in a chaotic world. Here, nations unite not under weakness or indecision, but under a shared belief that stability, discipline, and ambition shape the destiny of civilizations.

The Imperium welcomes nations willing to stand beside their allies, defend their sovereignty, and contribute to the growth and influence of the region.
