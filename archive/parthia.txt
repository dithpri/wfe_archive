Home of the [i]Parthians[/i], of North-Eastern [region]Iran[/region].

For the most part, society was rural, and dominated by large landholders with large numbers of serfs and other indentured labor at their disposal. Communities with free peasants also existed. Little is known of the economy, but agriculture must have played the most important role in it. Significant trade first occurs with the establishment of the Silk road.
