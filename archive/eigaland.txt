Eigaland is an up-and-coming collective region of autonomous states. Eigaland was founded by Uphiminn. Eigaland is the Uphiminnian way of saying Land-of-power, or more accurately Land-of-power-to-do/act. 

The name is representative of the ideology of the region. In Eigaland, nations join their power to make a change in the world.

Eigaland is vast region, ranging from beautiful landscapes to unnerving terrain. The Commonwealth of Uphiminn founded this new discovered region and has the intention of making it powerful by adding hand picked states and countries to join the glorious region. There are often small quarrels between the state leaders but in every case, friendship prevails making this cohesive region a force to be reckoned with.
