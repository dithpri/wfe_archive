Careful not to smudge the fresh anti-graffiti paint on the walls of their refuge, sanitarium inhabitants once again celebrate [color=red][b]Lydia Cornwall Day[/b][/color] in honour of the renowned Ambassador whose insight and gentle persistence eased the birth of many a Resolution. Her stellar career was a tribute to the wise social policies of her home, [color=blue][b]Telidia[/b][/color], the nation which originally created this haven of peace and chuckling reminiscence.
