Castles are built and destroyed, kings and queens rule with an iron fist only to be overthrown by even the lowest of peasants. War, famine, pestilence, and death have been at the forefront of the crippled Haltrix Empire. You may have a place here, but will it ever last longer than a fleeting dream? That is only for God to decide, or whatever deity or lack there of you gave your soul too. 

Hear the heart of the dragon cry, it beats slowly for the dying empire, kill it or sustain it, that is your choice and you must be aware of the consequences of thy actions
