[list]The [b]Antarctic Alliance (AA)[/b] is an intergovernmental organization comprised of sovereign states with territory in the Antarctic Circle. Its main purpose is to manage the sustainable development and shared resources of the Antarctic continent. The AA was created to address growing geopolitical and environmental threats after the continent was legally divided among its member nations. Operating on a principle of cooperative governance, the organization is a model of peaceful international collaboration, with a council that meets annually to create and enact policies for resource management, environmental protection, scientific research, and security.[/list]
