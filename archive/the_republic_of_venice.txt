[b][color=blue]Synopsis[/color][/b]
The Republic of [u][nation]Venice[/nation][/u] was a state originating from the city of Venice in Northeastern Italy. It existed for over a millennium, from the late 7th century until 1797. It was formally known as the Most Serene Republic of [u][nation]Venice[/nation][/u] and is often referred to as La Serenissima, in reference to its title as one of the &amp;quot;Most Serene Republics&amp;quot;. The Republic&amp;#39;s modern reputation is widely based on its preference for economic supremacy over military might, despite its long history of war and conquest.
