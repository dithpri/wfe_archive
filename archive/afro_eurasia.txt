Afro Eurasia is founded by The Federation of United Malayan Republic.

The Region of Afro Eurasia is governed by the [b]Afro Eurasian Regional Council[/b], headed by the President who will be elected during the [b]Afro Eurasia Council Congress (AECC)[/b] every 4 years.

The current Presidency is held by [nation]United Malayan Republic[/nation], and will finish its term in January 2020.
