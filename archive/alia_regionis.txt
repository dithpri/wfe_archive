&amp;quot;Alia Regionis&amp;quot;, &amp;quot;Other Region&amp;quot; is a region like no other, is designed to be home to nations like no other.
It is a political union which tries to obtain a political unity, but through reserving diversities between member nations. Nevertheless, although the national system of governments is not mandatory, by nature, this union shows nature of an empire with a constitution: Democratically elected WA Delegate and symbolic monarch.
Other titles to be created on demand.
