Indonesia, officially the [b]Republic of Indonesia[/b], is a country in Southeast Asia. Situated between the Indian and Pacific oceans, it is the world&#39;s largest island country, with more than thirteen thousand islands. It has an estimated population of over 258 million people and is the world&#39;s fourth most populous country as well as the most populous Muslim majority country. The world&#39;s most populous island of Java contains more than half of the country&#39;s population. &#151;Wikipedia[hr]
