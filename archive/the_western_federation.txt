Founded by Archyand in 2034, The Western Federation held Europe in a powerful iron grip. It was a life of prosperity until Tsandia split off from Archyand, engaging in a civil war in 2042. There was an estimated 460 million casualties for Archyand and an estimated 380 million casualties for Tsandia, December 28th 2051 marked the end of the war as Tsandia was annexed back into Archyand. However the scar of the war is still evident as Archyand tried to stay on it&#146;s feet.
