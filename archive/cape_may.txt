[b]Cape May[/b] is a city at the southern tip of Cape May Peninsula in Cape May County, New Jersey, where the Delaware Bay meets the Atlantic Ocean. Cape May is one of America&#39;s oldest vacation resort destinations and was recognized by the Travel Channel as having one of the top 10 beaches in the country. &#151;Wikipedia[hr]
