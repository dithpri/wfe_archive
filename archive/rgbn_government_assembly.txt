[b]Procedure for ratifying decisions[/b]: A decision shall be drawn up and published as a poll. All members must take part before the poll is declared finished. A 3/4 majority must be achieved in order for a decision to be ratified and established as RGBN law.

If anyone expresses disagreement to the decision in question, that same person should explain the reason for disagreeing. The reason shall then be considered, and any necessary changes incorporated.
