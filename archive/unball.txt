The UNball, or the United Nationsball, is an intergovernmental organization created after Naziball and Japanese-Empire-icon Japanball&#39;s mental breakdown. He was born as the League of Nationsball, tasked with maintaining world peace. But WW2 happened, so he was reformed into the UNball, and his task now is to prevent WW3 from happening and nukes from firing, but we can all see he isn&#39;t doing a good job.
