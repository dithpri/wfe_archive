[color=blue][i][b]History:[/b][/i][/color]

In the year 69&#146;, the two grand nations agreed to a treaty that led to foundation of The Nouveu Coryan Corporation. The purpose of this treaty was to strengthen the economic and political relationships between nations. The Corporation is a state-owned company manged by the state of Nouveu Iberia and Coryana. They do everything from selling diary products to space programs to secret operations in the unknown.
