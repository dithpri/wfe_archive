[b][i]Imperium In Imperio[/i]

The Grand Principality of Niso is a proud and loyal dominion within the The Land of Kings and Emperors (LKE) Empire.[/b] 

Our home is a glorious city-state married to the Sea. Our ports welcome great ships carrying passengers and goods from different countries across the NationStates World. Outside our walls, adventure beckons. Niso is a friendly community, and like its beautiful harbors, it serves as a safe refuge for anyone willing and brave enough to drop anchor.
