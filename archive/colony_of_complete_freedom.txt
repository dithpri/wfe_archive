Constitution of the Colony of Complete Freedom:
 All nations shalt be able to join the Colony of Complete Freedom, and no attempt to prevent a nation from joining this Colony shall be permitted. All nations within the Colony may manage both their internal and external affairs however they wish. The Regional Message Board may be posted on by any nation that is either:
a) A member nation of the Colony of Complete Freedom, or
b) A member nation of a region holding embassies with the Colony of Complete Freedom

 No posts on the Regional Message Board may be deleted, and no nation in the Colony of Complete Freedom may be ejected or banned.
