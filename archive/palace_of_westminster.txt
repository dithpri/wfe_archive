The [b]Palace of Westminster[/b] is the meeting place of the House of Commons and the House of Lords, the two houses of the Parliament of the [region]United Kingdom[/region]. Commonly known as the Houses of Parliament after its occupants, the Palace lies on the Middlesex bank of the River Thames in the City of Westminster, in central London.
