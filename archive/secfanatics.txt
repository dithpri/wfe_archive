[region]SECFanatics[/region] started building embassies in other regions so the residents of those regions could participate in SEC sports discussions without needing to relocate.

They ended up getting into an embassy competition with The Embassy, and almost broke the world record for most embassies.

[region]SECFanatics[/region] was later raided and destroyed by [region]The Black Hawks[/region] after their founder, [nation]Rbbf[/nation], ceased to exist.
