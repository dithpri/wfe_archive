Nations rise and fall with each passing of a season here in Despotania. Tinpot dictatorships are established and then overthrown with predictable regularity. Democracies decay and collapse at an accelerated rate.

But a few nations have so far persisted past their predicted lifespan. Is this a sign of change for an unstable region, or a fluke to be corrected by the end of the year? Tune in next week to find out.
