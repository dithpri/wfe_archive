[b]A land rife with turmoil and bloodshed.[/b] For innumerable years, the land of Despotania was untouched by the presence of large, legitimate governments. Feuding warlords ruled over small swaths of land. It was not until recently that real civilization has risen in Despotania, starting in the West during the 19th Century.
