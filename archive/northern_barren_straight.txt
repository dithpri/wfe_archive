To the far north, there lies a straight, the Barren straight. To the south side of the straight, there lies Sombay. To the north, Kiyak. And in between Muir Island. These nations powerful as they are, and as different as they are decided to join an alliance to benefit their own common interests and strengthen their governments. 

Muir Island, as well as Sombay are WA delegates. While Kiyak is more soveriegn. The three countries control this region with total control and decide what goes on within it entirely.
