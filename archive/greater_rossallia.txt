Comprising of the states and territories under the hegemony of the Rossallian Empire. After falling under the influence of Falastur in the 18th century the Rossallians bargained for (limited) military technologies at the price of an amaranthine pact to raise numerous regiments to serve as the rank and file of the Falasturian infantry - a role they continue in proudly to this day, and which has provided them with the resources to greatly increase their own expansionist campaigns.
