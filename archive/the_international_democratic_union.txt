The International Democratic Union (IDU) is a region for anyone who wants their nation to flourish. IDU Nations help eachother domestically and internationally, as well as taking part in international politics. 

The current open positions for the IDU cabinet are:
World Assembly Delegate
Vice-Delegate for World Assembly
Minister for the Homeland
Minister for Security
Minister for Communications
Minister for Diplomacy
Minister for Research

New Nations are looked after and everyone is invited to run for a position.
