Founded after the colony ship &quot;Phoenix&quot; Crashed on the surface of the planet of 3A-1003. The survivors named the planet, &quot;Genesis&quot;, in hope to start humanity anew. After the collapse of the Neo-Babylonian Empire, different nations and cultures formed around the world, losing touch with one another. 2000 years have passed and humanity has reached the atomic age. However, the majority of the world is run by Social democrats who bathe in the wicked nature of mankind. Thus, Lutwig was founded to be the bastion of light and freedom for all mankind.
