[b]Welcome to The Farm![/b]

Here we work hard, but the harvest is worth it!

The Farm is a region that is devoted primarily agricultural processes with the primary objective of producing food and other crops; we are the basic facility in food production. The name is used for specialised units such as arable farms, vegetable farms, fruit farms, dairy, pig and poultry farms, and land used for the production of natural fibres, biofuel and other commodities. We include ranches, feedlots, orchards, plantations and estates, smallholdings and hobby farms, and includes the farmhouse and agricultural buildings as well as the land.
