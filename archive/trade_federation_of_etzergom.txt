The trade federation of Eztergom includes all the nations that are part of the Eztergom government and are ruled by Etzergom but still govern themselves in local affairs. The trade federation seeks to unite other nations under one flag, one government, and one economy. Our supreme Chancellor is Kai Lohden Dumosch and our Senate consist of the Following people, Head of Senate and secretary of economy Quincy Bash, Secretary of infrastructure Ari Knutson, Secretary of Agriculture Ethan Blair and Secretary of Industry Myself Kai Lohden Dumosch. Each Senate member holds 3 delegates except for the Head of the senate witch controls 6 delegates (Quincy Bash)
