[region]Burlandia[/region] is an ever-growing archipelago of surfaced toxic sludge off the coast of Nha Trang in Vietnam&amp;#39;s south. The Estaban clan, renowned Nha Trang pig farmers, were the original settlers of Burlandia, led by the family&amp;#39;s patriach Janus, who established [nation]Thirty Seven[/nation], which was eventually divided after a bloody civil war. Meanwhile, both new and established nations have found homes in Burlandia. Generally, relations between the diverse nations and peoples is good, and while the region is decidedly isolationist after years of oppression by the outside world, opposing both the World Assembly and Security Council, Burlandia member states are not disallowed from participating in the affairs of either.
