The Confederation of Revolutionary States is a grouping of collectives, soviets, people&#39;s republics, and other forms of worker&#39;s states. The collective points include:

Rights of Self-Determination
Abolishment of Class Society
Abolishment of Private Property
The Dictatorship of the Proletariat

The founding executive Authority, Turtle Island Collectives, will give up rule of their nation should a majority vote rule them to have violated the trust of the Confederation of Revolutionary States.
