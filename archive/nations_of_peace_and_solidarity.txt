Comprising of the Federal Republic of Hedroland and its regional allies, the nations of this alliance share a commitment to environmentalism, social progressivism, and a high standard of living, bolstered by extensive investment in public education, healthcare, and infrastructure. Discrimination is not tolerated. The nations of the alliance share common commitments to LGBT, women&#39;s, and minority rights. 

Although Hedroland holds executive authority, it acts only with the confidence of all nations in the region, which have delegate power in the Regional Assembly.
