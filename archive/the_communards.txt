[color=scarlet][b]The Communards[/b] were members and supporters of the short-lived 1871 Paris Commune. These men and women marching under a red banner would forestall the proto-communist movement in Europe and influence the works of Karl Marx.

This region is dedicated to the central tenets of Classical Marxism, Socialism, and Communism. [i]Workers of the world, unite! You have nothing to lose but your chains![/i][/color]
