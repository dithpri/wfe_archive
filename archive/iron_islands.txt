The Iron Islands is one of the constituent regions of the [region]Seven Kingdoms[/region]. Until the War of Conquest it was ruled by the Kings of the Iron Islands and then the Kings of the Isles and the Rivers.

The Iron Islands are home to a fierce seafaring people who call themselves the ironborn. While some say the archipelago is named after the abundant iron ore on the islands, the ironmen claim it is instead named after their own unyielding nature. -- [url=http://awoiaf.westeros.org/index.php/Iron_Islands][color=003366]Iron Islands[/color][/url][hr]
