[i]Auriga is a constellation in the northern celestial hemisphere. It is one of the 88 modern constellations; it was among the 48 constellations listed by the 2nd-century astronomer Ptolemy. Its name is Latin for &#39;(the) charioteer&#39;, associating it with various mythological beings, including Erichthonius and Myrtilus.[/i][b]Founded by the andarian starfleet on: 04/05/2025.[/b]
