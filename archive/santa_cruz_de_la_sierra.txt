[b]Santa Cruz de la Sierra[/b] is the largest city in Bolivia and the capital of the Santa Cruz department. Situated on the Pirai River in the eastern Tropical Lowlands of Bolivia, the city of Santa Cruz and its metropolitan area are home to over 70% of the population of the department. It is one of the fastest-growing cities in the world. The city was first founded in 1561 by Spanish explorer &Ntilde;uflo de Chavez. &#151;Wikipedia[hr]
