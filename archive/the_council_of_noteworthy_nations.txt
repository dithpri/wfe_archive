Welcome to the CONN!

The Council of Noteworthy Nations was formed after some of the main contributors to the United Nations of Tamriel split away from the group over political differences. Now they have congregated here!

This region boasts a strong Regional Founder and encourages nations to run for office, propose resolutions, or interact in the regional government in any way they desire. We have a long way to go to developing our regional identity.
