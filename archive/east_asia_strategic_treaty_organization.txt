The [region]East Asia Strategic Treaty Organization[/region], also called [b]EAST[/b], or [b]EASTO[/b], is an intergovernmental military alliance between 9 Asian countries, including the member states of [nation]Privali States[/nation], Japan, and South Korea, as well as disputed territory in the Republic of China. The organization implements the Strategic Treaty that was signed in the District of Rosales which states that any member of the organization is entitled to a military alliance which acts in the case of defense. EASTO is also responsible for the use of humane efforts around east Asia in response to natural disasters.
