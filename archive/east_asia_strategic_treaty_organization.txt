The [url=https://www.nationstates.net/nation=privali_states/detail=factbook/id=1913993]East Asia Strategic Treaty Organization[/url] ([color=yellow]EAST[/color], [color=yellow]EASTO[/color]), also called the [color=yellow]East Asian Alliance[/color], is an intergovernmental military alliance between four member states - two of which are [nation]Privali States[/nation]: the Philippines and Vietnam, and the other being South Korea and Japan. The organization was [url=]established in 2013[/url] shortly after the ratification of the Privali constitution, and implements a collective security system wherein its independent member states agree to defend each other in the case of [b]third party assault[/b].
