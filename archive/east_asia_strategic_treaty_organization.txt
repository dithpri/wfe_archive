[b]The East Asia Strategic Treaty Organization[/b] (EAST Pact, or EASTO) is a military pact founded by the Privali Confederacy, primarily the Philippines. The treaty supports a military alliance in times of conflict, and is not economically binding. It is ultimately headed by members of the East Asian Council - a collection of Permanent Representatives tasked with being the principal decision-making body.
