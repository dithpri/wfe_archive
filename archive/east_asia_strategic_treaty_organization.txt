The [region]East Asia Strategic Treaty Organization[/region] (EAST, EASTO), also called the East Asian Alliance, is an intergovernmental military alliance between four member states - two of which are [url=]Privali[/url] states: the Philippines and Vietnam, and the other being South Korea and Japan. The organization was [url=]established in 2013[/url] shortly after the ratification of the Privali constitution, and implements a collective security system wherein its independent member states agree to defend each other in the case of third party assault.
