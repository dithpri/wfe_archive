Welcome to The Eyeland of Terror!

The Eyeland of Terror is a geographical island in the middle of the ocean in which a small group of nations have declared themselves as independent entities on. Each of these nations have formed alliances with all others further strengthening the bond of not only themselves, but of the entire island. Long live the Eyeland!
