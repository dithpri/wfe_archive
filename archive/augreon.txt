[B]Welcome to Augreon[/B] - The Medieval Roleplay Region!

Augreon is a roleplay region based around the technology and principles of the 12-1400&#39;s, within the height of the medieval era, with mild fantasy elements. Based around the lands sharing the same names, the world is split between many different Human, Dwarven, Elven and Halfling nations each expanding their influence and growing their empires.

If you would like to join, please read our rules and the dispatches pinned to the world fact-book entry. You can then create your nation on the map below, fill out your application and send it to the regional admin!
