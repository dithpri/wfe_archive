The Kuomintang is a major political party in the Republic of China [i](Taiwan)[/i]. It was the sole ruling party of the country from 1928 to 1949 in Mainland China until its relocation to Taiwan, and in Taiwan it ruled under martial law until 1987. It is still active to this day.
