Shaanxi (/&amp;#643;&amp;#593;&amp;#720;n&amp;#712;&amp;#643;i&amp;#720;/;[5] simplified Chinese: &amp;#38485;&amp;#35199;; traditional Chinese: &amp;#38493;&amp;#35199;, Mandarin pronunciation: [&amp;#642;a&amp;#768;n.&amp;#597;i&amp;#769;] (listen); alternately Shensi) is a landlocked province of the People&#39;s Republic of China. Officially part of Northwest China, it borders the province-level divisions of Shanxi (NE, E), Henan (E), Hubei (SE), Chongqing (S), Sichuan (SW), Gansu (W), Ningxia (NW) and Inner Mongolia (N).
