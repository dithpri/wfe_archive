These islands are called The Huunbuul islands or in Huunbuul: Chanshingog. They are located east of Japan and has very beautiful scenery. The capital city of all the islands is Yuun-Yuun. People of these islands speak the Huunbuul/Chanshingogbul dialect. This dialect is written with the Korean &amp;#39;Hangul&amp;#39; script and has many loan words from Japanese. These islands have a very close relationship with Korea and Japan and has a strong economy. Please join!
