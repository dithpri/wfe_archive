On January 4, 2021, the 117th Congress of the United States proposed [url=https://www.congress.gov/bill/117th-congress/house-bill/51]H.R.51[/url], which, if passed, would allow for statehood for Washington, D.C., the capital of the United States. H.R.51 proposes that Washington, D.C. would be admitted as a state known as Washington, Douglass Commonwealth.
