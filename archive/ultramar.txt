Ultamar is a collection of empires who believe in a reverence to their respected emperors. The Confederation of Ultamar is well known for its well disciplined military might and power, which is used to defend its people from foreign invasion and attack from within. Should you wish to join this growing alliance or become allies, do not hesitate to ask. While the people of Ultamar are very xenophobic, we still wish to make friends and grow our empire.
