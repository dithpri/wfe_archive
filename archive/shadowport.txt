Shadowport grew from Karzstov&#146;s former military base, which became an autonomous hub for mercenaries, smugglers, and pirates under Karzstov&#146;s covert control. Casino-states like Primm Valley and Sands Hotel thrive alongside anarchies like Terribles and Resortsworld. Authoritarian regimes such as Queed Net Jam hold isolated power, while groups like Iron Sun Clans experiment with libertarian rule. The Dockside Directorate enforces Karzstov&#146;s influence in this volatile region of competing factions.
