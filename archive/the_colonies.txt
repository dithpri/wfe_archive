Founded 11/6/2020, The Colonies serves as a tool for Roleplay for &quot;The Colonies,&quot; a roleplay server based in Minecraft.

The Colonies is an Alternate Universe in which Earth&#39;s history is different. It takes place in 2020, where World Peace doesn&#39;t exist. In the looming threat of an alien invasion, the nations of the world band together to defeat a common enemy. Join us in our conquest against Isavora, the alien menace threatening us all.
