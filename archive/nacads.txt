Welcome nations and regions around the world, I present to you NACADS Headquarters where meetings will take place on the construction of the World and Regional Space Station, on construction progress, discussions on enhancements and ideas for the Space Station.  

Complaints at all levels will be discussed here, if found guilty, the nation or region will be out of the deal, if they are cleared, the reporting nation or region will have to explain how they came to the decision to denounce and will have to pay compensation to the denounced nation.

If you want to participate, read the agreement and agree that you will respect more than anything the articles cited in the text.
