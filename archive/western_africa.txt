Western Africa is the westernmost subregion of Africa. Western Africa has been defined as including nineteen countries: Benin, Burkina Faso, Gambia, Ghana, Guinea, Guinea-Bissau, Ivory Coast, Kotocoli, Liberia, Mali, Mauritania, Niger, Nigeria, Senegal, Sierra Leone, Togo, and the island nations of Cape Verde, Saint Elmo, and S&atilde;o Tom&eacute; and Pr&iacute;ncipe.
