Western Africa is the westernmost subregion of Africa. Western Africa has been defined as including 18 countries: Benin, Burkina Faso, the island nation of Cape Verde, Gambia, Ghana, Guinea, Guinea-Bissau, Ivory Coast, Kotocoli, Liberia, Mali, Mauritania, Niger, Nigeria, Senegal, Sierra Leone, S&atilde;o Tom&eacute; and Pr&iacute;ncipe, and Togo.
