Everitia (pronounced evrESHEa) is a region currently with only one nation, The Republic of Klasnia. This region is a large island region, but has some larger land masses. There is plenty room for new nations to join and new nations wanting to join (acoxpv@gmail.com) will have to be reviewed by the Everitia Regional Council.

[color=blue][u]Application Guidelines[/color][/u]
- Preferably a republic, liberal
- Political Freedoms above 65/100
- Civil Rights above 65/100
- Economy above 50/100
- Spending in Religion/Spirituality below 15%
- Old Age deaths at least 40%, Murder deaths below 20%
