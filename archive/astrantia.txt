Astrantia &#151; named after a flower that symbolizes ultimate protection and strength &#151; is a region founded to promote peace, compassion, integrity, and tenacity through its progressive policies and like-minded nations. With strong socialist and leftist roots, Astrantia seeks to create a unified array of nations that contribute to the harmony and well-being of the society of NationStates.
