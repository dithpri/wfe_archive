A region of global power, Gotham exerts a significant impact upon commerce, finance, media, art, fashion, research, technology, education, and entertainment. The home of the World Assembly, Gotham is an important center for international diplomacy and has been described as the cultural capital of the world.

Guarded by the Batman, Gotham has been cracking down on crime, while promoting social progress and civil rights. Gotham has a relatively liberal government, and is a proponent of regional equality. 

All member states of Gotham have equal rights, and governments by the people, for the people, and of the people.
