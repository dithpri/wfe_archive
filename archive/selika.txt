Selika is a conglomeration of states and nationalities with a wide variety of political opinions

[b]The Electoral System of Selika[/b]

1) Each leader, regardless of their number of nations, can only vote once.

2) The nation with the most votes becomes the WA Delegate for 1 (one) month and, after the voting period, everyone must endorse the winner.

3) All Nations must vote.

4) In the event of a tie, leading candidates votes will be anulled and, if there is still a tie, the nation with the best economy becomes the WA Delegate.

5) Voting will occur on the last day of every month and lasts for 24 hours.
