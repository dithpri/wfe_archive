Atop of the world sit nations which endure some of the harshest conditions known to humanity. 

Never ending darkness, persistent light, blazing [i]Aurora borealis[/i], wind swept ice sheets, huddled creatures and delicate flora on stone as old as the Earth itself, all feature in this land of extremes - and extreme beauty.
