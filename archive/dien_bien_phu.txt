Dien Bien Phu is a city in northwestern Vietnam. It is the capital of Dien Bien Province. The city is best known for the events which occurred there during the First Indochina War, the Battle of Dien Bien Phu

The important Battle of [region]Dien Bien Phu[/region] was fought between the Viet Minh, and the French Union. The siege of the French garrison lasted fifty-seven days, from March 13 to May 7 1954.

This was the end of French imperialism in [region]Indochina[/region]
