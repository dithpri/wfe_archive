Emporian Is a continent in the Northern Seas, a large land mass is filled with forests and lush wildlife. It usually receives winter from December to March and Spring and Summer from April to September. Founded by pioneers seeking indpendence. It was here that they were colonies to the Monarchy of Gildebrand but due to the lack of representation and unfair taxes and laws. The declared war against monarchy for independence thus causing the revolution of freedom. This war lasted for 7 years until the battle of Broods Mountain where the monarchy had  suffered a crippling defeat after losing much territory. After a formal surrender, the colonists then formed the United Provinces of Sarascen ensuring that all men have a voice and representation.
