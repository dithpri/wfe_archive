Wikaphy is an antiquated belief system revolving around dualism. Their main teachings revolve around inner peace and helping one another, and these teachings are often passed on through moments of quiet meditation and attempting to reach enlightenment. Wikaphy teachings come from spiritual leaders who are seen as mentors. They often express their beliefs through candles and clothing.
Being an antiquated belief system, Wikaphy has a rich history of traditions and customs, as well as stories from a wide range of backgrounds. Those part of this belief see Wikaphy as a way to help get rid of stress and evil.
