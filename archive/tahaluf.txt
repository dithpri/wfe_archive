Founded to bring together the countries nearby, Tahaluf sits largely within a Temperate region, with the climate to the North being Cold. The continent lies over a tectonic boundary; the Northern edge of the land features a moderately sized mountain range and some prominent canyons and valleys. Tahaluf has a particularly large dormant volcano, and powerful earthquakes are very rare and only happen every few centuries. The winds in this region are usually gentle, with large storms and extreme weather being very rare.
