[b]Welcome to [region]The General Secretariat[/region][/b] 
We are a region dedicated to the preservation of peace, positive diplomatic relations between regions and states, and the upholding of moral values.

[hr]

Officers of [region]The General Secretariat[/region] include the Secretary-General, Deputy Secretary-General, W.A. Delegate, Foreign Secretary, Home Secretary, and the Undersecretary of Moderation. There are additionally five elected Senators. These ten citizens make up the Senate, and vote on legislation. In the event of a tie, the Secretary-General will provide the tie breaking vote.
