[color=blue]In ancient Greek Polis literally means city. Ancient Greek poleis, or citystates, differed greatly, and had a rich and varied history.

The region of Polis is modelled on Athenian governance. Every citizen of Polis has a part in its running, and in that sense we are one of the freest regions in NationStates.[/color]
