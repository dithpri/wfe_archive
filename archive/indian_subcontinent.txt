A region created to focus on Indian Subcontinent but is now open to rest of the world too. The region is ruled by a liberal Democracy. Everyone is welcomed except dictators.

[b]Regional Policies-
[b]Democracy-[/b]
Democratic elections are mandated.
[b]Sultanate-[/b]
The government is ruled by a sultan and his officers (which are elected)
[b]Capitalism-[/b]
Private industry is permitted in a market based economy. 
[b]Regional climate treaty-[/b]
The regional government has signed and ratified an international climate treaty.
