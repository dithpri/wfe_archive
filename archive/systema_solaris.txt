Decades ago, at the peak of the Resource Wars, the clock finally reached midnight, and mankind was nearly wiped out. Due to their late involvement and relatively neutrality in the war, as well as their well-developed bomb shelter infrastructure, Japan and Korea were the majority of the survivors. United with survivors from around the world by a prodigal child, a brand-new global, autocratic, communist nation has been founded.

The world may have nearly been ended, but now mankind has the potential to craft a utopia.
