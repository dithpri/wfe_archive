[b]WELCOME[/b]
[b][u]The Confederacy of Kingdoms[/u][/b]

Resolved; 

I. 
That the Style of this Confederation shall be &quot;The Confederacy of Kingdoms&quot;. 

II. 
Each Nation retains its sovereignty, freedom, and independence, and every power, jurisdiction, and right of self government. 

III.
The Nations who join the Confederacy of Kingdoms hereby severally enter into a firm league of friendship with each other, for their common defense, the security of their liberties, and their mutual and general welfare, binding themselves to assist each other, against all force offered to, or attacks made upon them, or any of them, on account of religion, sovereignty, trade, or any other pretense whatever.
