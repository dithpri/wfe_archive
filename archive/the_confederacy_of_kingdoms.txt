[b][u]Welcome to the Confederacy of Kingdoms[/b][/u]

All Nations are welcome to join!

[b]Resolved[/b]
Each Nation retains its sovereignty, freedom, and independence, and every power, jurisdiction, and right, which is not by this Alliance expressly delegated to the Confederacy of Kingdoms.

The said Nations hereby severally enter into a firm league of friendship with each other, for their common defense, the security of their liberties, and their mutual and general welfare, binding themselves to assist each other, against all force offered to, or attacks made upon them, or any of them, on account of religion, sovereignty, trade, or any other pretense whatever.
