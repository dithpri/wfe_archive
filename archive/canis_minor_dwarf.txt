Founded as a mechanical fortress by the government of The Silent States between 3110 to 3120 to protect their capital, Isaakos, in the outter edge of the Canis Minor Dwarf and near a starway. It was conquered by [region]Andarios[/region] in 3264 and reconverted between 3266 to 3269 into a mechanical fortress better known as CMD and now serving as the main hub of power for the Imperial Galactic Republic in this region.
