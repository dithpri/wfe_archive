Part of the [region]Imperial Galactic Republic of Andarios[/region] better known as Province V! A mechanical fortress constructed in 3120 by forces of this galaxy and placed in the orbit of Isaakos. Between 3266 and 3269, the andarians, reconverted the space station in the orbit of Isaakos and made it in its operative base, denominating it by Canis Minor Dwarf (CMD). From this place the power of the Imperial Andarian Army and Navy spread across this galaxy!
