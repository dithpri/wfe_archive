Founded by disgruntled isolationists, Terradem was intended to be a region not tainted by the troubles of the Old World regions, namely those of little freedom and a highly authoritarian structure. The difficulty in finding Terradem poses a task too great for many potential settlers, allowing the citizens of Terradem to live in relative peace and freedom whilst enjoying the many wonders the area has to offer.


The office of WA Delegate shall be rotated every 7 days.

Rotation shall be in the order arrival. This will be made so by withdrawing endorsements until only 1 nation left has the maximum number of votes to become the next Delegate. A potential nation must be honest.

No nation shall hold office for less than 7 days.
