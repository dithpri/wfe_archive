[b]The Byzantyne Empire is composed by the Imperial Capital, [nation]costantinopoli bizantina[/nation]  and by its Provinces: &quot;Ellade e Dacia&quot;, &quot;Siria e Giudea&quot;, &quot;Asia Minore e Ponto&quot; and &quot;Egitto&quot;.
In the Empire there are also [nation]esarcato di ravenna[/nation] and [nation]esarcato di cartagine[/nation], with more autonomy because of their particular situation. [hr]
[nation]Esarcato di Ravenna[/nation] was formed by the Byzantine territories in northern Italy. 
[nation]Esarcato di Cartagine[/nation], instead, was formed by the Byzantine territories in northern Africa and Southern Spain.
