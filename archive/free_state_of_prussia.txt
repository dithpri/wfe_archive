[b][region]Free State of Prussia[/region] was the major state of the German Reich during the time of the Weimar Republic, comprising 62.5% of its territory and population. From 1933, the [i]Free State[/i] was stripped of representative democracy and ruled exclusively under the leadership of the Party and in 1935, the Free State&#39;s territory was dismantled into regional Gaue, and incorporated directly into [region]The Germanic Reich[/region].[/b]
