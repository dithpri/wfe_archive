Greater Aenea is a region very geographically similar to that of Western Europe and the Mediterranean. Its history of war has given Greater Aenea a reputation of ruthlessness and aggressiveness. While this is true for some countries in the region, others are more peaceful and democratic. Greater Aenea is known for having a great cultural history, presented in the region&#39;s art and literature.
