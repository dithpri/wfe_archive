Located outside the walls of Shalefields, high in the mountains, a ring of tall oaks encircles this flat plain. A cobblestone path leads to it from the city and can be just as harsh as the battles for which the Wolfwood is used. 

Once every couple years, every fief wanting to participate sends a Champion to fight for them in a battle royal to the death. The winner receives fame across the Kingdom of Albion and is Knighted by the King. 

That time has come again. Who will win? Who will die? Let&amp;#39;s find out.
