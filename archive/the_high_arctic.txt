Svalbard is an archipelago between mainland Norway and the North Pole also known as the High Arctic. One of the world&#146;s northernmost inhabited areas, it&#39;s known for its rugged, remote terrain of glaciers and frozen tundra sheltering polar bears, Svalbard reindeer and Arctic foxes. The Northern Lights are visible during winter, and summer brings the &#147;midnight sun&#148;&#151;sunlight 24 hours a day.
