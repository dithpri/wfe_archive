The International Kyomarch Council was founded by [nation]Kyodaar[/nation], and is so named as official council meetings are held in a hall at the summit of Mount Navigator in the mountain range of the Kyomarches on the Kyodaari mainland. The intentions of this council are to create a union in which the welfare, freedoms and rights of the people are placed first and foremost.
