The United Provinces of Reichstad is a melting-pot of various nations and cultures. This goes with caution however, as the amount of different cultures leads to different ideologies and conflicting views on various situations. Life is not peaceful, nor is it an awful place. It is one of the largest regions mankind has seen,making the Mongol Empire seem the same size as Madagascar. With the large region and many countries, war is a possibility. Traveler, visit the region with caution, for if war breaks out, the region will be annihilated.
