Founded in the year 1218 B.C., The Southern Confederation is an independent, decentralized confederacy of nations which each have their own independence and autonomy. Ahead of its time by thousands of years, The Southern Confederacy has been a hallmark of free trade, democracy, self-defense, and personal decision based on the principle that each people should be able to decide what is right and wrong for themselves, without the interference of a centralized force to mandate specifics for each independent nation.
