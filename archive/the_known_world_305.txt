After Daenerys of House Targaryen forced the surrender of Kings Landing and secured the Kingdom of Westeros under Targaryen rule once more trouble would begin to strike. A great deal of unrest would lay in the north with Lady Sansa Stark being plotted against by various houses to overthrow her from rule. On top of that a new threat brews from the far reaches of Essos, one that may end the Kingdom as we know it.
