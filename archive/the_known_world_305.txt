After Daenerys of House Targaryen forced the surrender of Kings Landing and secured the Kingdom of Westeros under Targaryen rule once more trouble would begin to strike. A great deal of unrest would lay in the north with Lady Sansa Stark  plotting against Daenerys Targaryen in order to achieve northern independence once and for all. With various treachery in court the entire stake of Westeros is at risk once more just years after being brought at peace. Will you contribute to the downfall or will you fight in the Queens name.
