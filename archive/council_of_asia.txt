[u]The Council of Asia[/u]

  The Council of Asia is an intergovernmental organization that was founded to promote unity between Asian nations. The Council of Asia is also responsible for representing the interests of its members on the global stage.

  The Council consists of two main organs. The Parliamentary Assembly, where all member states are represented on equal footing and the Secretariat which performs the day-to-day work at the Council&#39;s Headquarters in Taihoku, the capital of the Republic of Nationalist Yinmen.
