Welcome to Avathar!

Disaster has befallen our continent. A plague of undetermined origin swept through its nations and decimated almost all of them. Early analysis suggested the disease was artificially created by an unknown party. Only a handful of nations survived. Phyrexian scientists have determined that the disease has been completely exterminated. This leaves room for more nations to settle in Avathar...
