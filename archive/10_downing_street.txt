[b]10 Downing Street[/b], colloquially known in the [region]United Kingdom[/region] as &amp;quot;[b]Number 10[/b]&amp;quot;, is the headquarters of the British Government and the official residence and office of the First Lord of the Treasury, an office now invariably held by the Prime Minister of the [region]United Kingdom[/region].

Adjacent to St. James&amp;#39;s Park, Number 10 is near to Buckingham Palace, the official London residence of the British monarch, and the Palace of Westminster, the meeting place of both houses of parliament.
