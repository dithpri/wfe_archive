[B]Preamble:[/B]
The UPS upholds the four precedents of Human Rights, Human Gain, Equal Liberty, and International Cooperation.
[B]The Government:[/B]
The government of the UPS shall be composed of all member nations (each receiving one vote in every election and poll). The government of the UPS shall also include four consuls; elected by the member states. The consuls should be as follows: Consul of Defense, Consul of Domestic Policy, Consul of Diplomacy, and Consul of Trade. **NO POSITIONS ARE HELD YET**
