Memecenterius is a large, mostly unexplored world.
It has recently seen alot of conflict, as Legions from [nation]The Doom Legions[/nation] clashed with colonists from [nation]Ducklandian Empire[/nation].
Currently, the strongest nations are [nation]Damnation Prime[/nation], [nation]Dravenia[/nation], [nation]Fascist Ducklandian Wing[/nation], [nation]FelZan[/nation] and [nation]The Epic Nation Of Awesomeness[/nation], who are currently living with a fragile peace.
Technology in this world is quite advanced and some leaders wish to improve so that they can colonize other worlds for their nation.
This world also has a strong, magical presence and some believe that it is through magic that the worlds&amp;#39; destiny should be shaped.
