Founding principles of the UTF:
- All members have the right to freedom of expressions
- All members have the right to leave and secceed from the Union if it is not to their standards
- All members have the right to change the region via democratic means
- All members are equal in dignity and rights
- The rights of all members are inalienable, they persist through the most tumultuous of crises

Nations in the UTF have every right to change and demand change of the UTF governemnt. The government of the UTF is a servant to the people, as such, the people henceforth have every right to change it

May liberty prevail through the Union of the Free!
