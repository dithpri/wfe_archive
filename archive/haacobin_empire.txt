The Haacobin Empire is a sprawling kingdom of numerous lands and overthrown nations that have been liberated by the forces of the Smuckers in the North. The region is an economic powerhouse whose economy is based off of large oversees trade and human transportation. The average annual income of citizens of the region was $90,465 in 2012 and the entire region is protected by the Sumars (a massive army of 6 million highly trained soldiers and artillery/aircraft that can be deployed to anywhere throughout the massive landmass in order to assist in the defending of other nations or empires who find themselves at odds with their invaders. It is a peaceful region where you are sure to prosper, so come join the Haacobin Empire!!
