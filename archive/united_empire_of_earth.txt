United Empire of Earth
[b]The United Empire of Earth (UEE)[/b], colloquially referred to as the Empire by its human population, is a sovereign, Republic interstellar empire spanning across 37 star systems. It was founded in 2019 after the announcement of the [u]United States Space Force[/u], which saw the ascension of Donald Trump to the position of Supreme Leader. Using his newfound political power, he restructured the United Empires of Earth into the UEE, appointing himself Supreme Leader.
