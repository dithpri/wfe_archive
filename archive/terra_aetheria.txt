[list]Terra Aetheris a massive environmentally stunning and diverse region with sprawling oceans and plain swept lands filled with lust rain forests, towering mountains and grasslands fit for cattle raising and romantic rustic picture taking. Currently set in the year 2090 the world has changed, but still resembles much of yester years. [/list]
