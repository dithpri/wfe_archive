[color=navyblue]Greater Standington[/color] is a multi-party region in which nations take on roles as politicians. What this means is that this region is a country, and you are it&#39;s politicians. We take part in RP, and the aim is to hold elections and develop the political parties to a point where they become personalised to their leaders. We are also a multi-nation region in which up to 7 countries play [b]their[/b] nations in relation to the country of [color=navyblue]Greater Standington[/color]. Their aim is to increase relations, create wars, alliances betrayals, and everything good with each other and [color=navyblue]Greater Standington[/color]. But the main aim is just to have fun and RP however you like, so enjoy!
