[color=green][b]Greetings, and welcome, to the region of [region]Astural[/region]![/b][/color]

[colour=navy][b]Astural[/b] is a closed world, modern tech roleplaying region, set on the planet of the same name, with familiar and unique cultures alike thriving together. We pursue realistic nations and roleplay that have quality and effort invested into them. We are a friendly, open region, and all are welcome to join! The region is a work in progress, so feel free to give suggestions and help in the region&#39;s recruitment!
Please note that recruitment and advertisement for other regions is strictly forbidden.[/colour]
