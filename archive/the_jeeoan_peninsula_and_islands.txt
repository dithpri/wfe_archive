The mainland is occupied by Jeeo. It was said to once belong to the Japanese empire, however it was officially divided into smaller, regional governments. After further devolution and eventual Japanese withdrawal, the autonomous governments began to fight with each other. Jeeo, aided heavily by the USSR, gloriously won over the other governments and became the ruling power over the unified peninsula. There are a few small islands of to the west which are uninhabited. It is said that the Jeeoan government is raising funds to colonise the small islands. Other nations are also trying to attain them, only friends of Jeeo will succeed.
