Welcome to the Reborn Sith Empire. After the Great Sith Civil War a Sith fleet, comprised of 10 capital ships,  under the command of Darth Volus found the Gaea system and colonized the planet of Gaea. After years on infighting our glorious Emperor Vader united us and formed the Empire of Sith Ari and the Reborn Sith Empire. Join us on our conquest of becoming the strongest Empire in the galaxy.
