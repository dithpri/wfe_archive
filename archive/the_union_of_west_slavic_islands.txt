The Union Of West Slavic Island is based near the west coasts of Europe and south west coasts of Africa. We represent the Union of Slavic nations which were found on 4 islands of St. Mary, St. Stephan, Peaches and Glory. We provide military safety and economical aid to our members. We are a strong developing Union, with future ambitions to have our vote in the world arena. UWSI will be a good place for every nation that demand protection and any type of help.
