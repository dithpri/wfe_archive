Founded by technologically advanced Ferrea and supported by its agrarian rural Socialist satellite Helifeld, the Federation exists in opposition to all Liberal, Capitalist, and Conservative Monarchist states &#150; undefeated and united in a military and economical alliance against the whole world. The Federation was later joined by the totalitarian Republic of United Soviets of Eurasia, the northern country of Atlantic Hyperborea practicing a form of Feudal Socialism, the tropical and socially advanced Southern Sea Realm, the mountainous Free Land of Sylvan Vale, the interstellar Confederacy of Solaris Aetea, the Fascist Dominion of Mare Magnum, as well as the Republic of Northwestern American Union.
