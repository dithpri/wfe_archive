Founded by technologically advanced Fascist Ferrea and supported by its agrarian rural Socialist satellite Helifeld, the Federation exists in opposition to all Liberal Capitalist and Conservative Monarchist states &#150; undefeated and united in a military and economical alliance against the whole world. The Federation was later joined by the totalitarian Republic of United Soviets of Eurasia, the Northern country of Atlantic Hyperborea practicing a form of Feudal Socialism, the tropical and socially advanced Southern Sea Realm,  the mountainous Free Land of Sylvan Vale, as well as the space Confederacy of Solaris Aetea.
