This World has a few small continents on it, but is a lovely world filled with reptilian creatures, strange plants, and several animals specially evolved to kill humans. The majority of the nation is undeveloped, but will be, if more settlers come in. the Southern Region, Nochan, is home to the less developed portion of the world, and has lush forests filled with Dragons, Panthers, and Raptors. The Northern Region, Cartref, Has a More Developed area, often at war, but has lush forests home to trickster animals like Troodon, Utahraptor, and Wolves. The Western Region is mostly unestablished, but will be as time goes on, as with the Eastern Region
