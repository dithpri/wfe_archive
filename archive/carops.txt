The Caropsyne Pontanore is the shared existence of all Caropsyne people around the world. The Pontanore is a nation and major power comprising of territories on the continents of Thearea and Polythia, with various island holdings. A social democracy, the Pontanore is divided into 24 states, including Euvodora and Phosmara, and 1 associated state: Halod. As vast as the land, the ethnic diversity of the nation is striking from Caropsynes and Lindites to Hercenas and Carthens. The Pontanore is led by Premier Ilmer Yutrias who is determined to strengthen the Pontanore&#39;s influence and power in the world while maintaining the cherished social democratic system the union has always been.
