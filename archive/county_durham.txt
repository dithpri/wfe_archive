[region]County Durham[/region] is a historic county in the northeast of England within the [region]Empire of Britain[/region]. Known for its scenic landscapes, including the Durham Dales and parts of the North Pennines, the county features the iconic Durham Cathedral and Castle, as well as a rich industrial heritage rooted in coal mining and railways.
