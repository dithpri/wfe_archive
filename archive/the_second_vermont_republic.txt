[B][color=green]The Second Vermont Republic is a secessionist group within the U.S. state of Vermont which seeks to restore the formerly independent status of the Vermont Republic.[/color] 
[hr][color=navy]It is a nonviolent citizens&#39; network and think tank opposed to the tyranny of Corporate America and the U.S. government, and committed to the peaceful return of Vermont to its status as an independent republic and more broadly the dissolution of the Union.[/color][/B]
