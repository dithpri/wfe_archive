[b][i][color=#006400]Ollivanders[/b][/i][/color] wand shop is located in Diagon Alley, London, England. Owned by the Ollivander family (widely acknowledged to be the best wandmakers in Great Britain), the shop is narrow and shabby, with peeling gold letters over the door that read: Ollivanders: Makers of Fine Wands since 382 B.C.
