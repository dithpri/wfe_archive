This continent sits largely within a Temperate region, with the climate to the North being Cold.
The East side of continent lies on a tectonic boundary; the Eastern edge of the land forms a mountainous ridge.
Eozoth has a handful of dormant volcanoes, and earthquakes are small but frequent.
The winds in this region are often strong, with large storms being very common.
The seas to the West and South of Eozoth are difficult to traverse, while the seas to the East and North are calm.
Eozoth is home to a modest variety of creatures and plants, most of those are found only in this region.
