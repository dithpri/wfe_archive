The Reichstag is a historic legislative government building on Platz der Republik in Berlin that is the seat of the German Bundestag. It is also the meeting place of the Federal Convention, which elects the President of Germany. It was the site of the famous Reichstag Fire in 1933, which was a false flag attack by the Nazi party to help gain power.
