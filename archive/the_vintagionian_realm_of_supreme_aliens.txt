The Realm of Vintagion is a enormous empire of loyal vassals and independent members all loyal to Vintagion. Vintagion became a military power relatively quickly and began expanding at a astonishing rate. Never being defeated in a war, Vintagion crushed and incorporated millions of civilizations and planets, after drastically defeating the most power civilization in exsistance, the Kaltoninians, Vintagion assimilated them into the alliance on the exchange of lifetime servitude to Vintagion.
