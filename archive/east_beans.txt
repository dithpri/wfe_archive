East Beans is a sister continent to first-world West Beans. It contains the nations of Almerigo, Sparanada, Hicko, and the unofficial micronation Farmer John&#146;s Cabbage Patch. It also contains contested territories along its westernmost border.
East Beans&#146; main industries are manufacturing, mining and agriculture. Due to a series of historical conflicts with West Beans, its nations suffer from widespread poverty and high crime rates, as well a greatly increased rate of bone marrow cancer.
