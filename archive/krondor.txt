Welcome to Krondor
This is a tiny(for now) region with a medieval setting.
Everyone who is interested in some medieval RP and life in such a region is welcome!

[b]Quick Overview[/b]
Since it is a roleplay region, Krondor is a big realm. And all nations are dominions in that realm. Something like the HRE. There is no ruler known to us so all nations govern themselves in peace...
