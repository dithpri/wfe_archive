[b]Overview:[/b]
The Phoenix Project is a collection of nations from diverse ideologies and backgrounds. Currently led by the Grand Commonwealth of [nation]Pan-Hispaniola[/nation], the Phoenix Project&#39;s intention is to promote cooperation and the development of new nations who are fascist, communist, democratic, etc. The Phoenix Project does not discriminate based on past or present actions of member nations, unless the action negatively affects another member nation.

[b]Elections:[/b]
The Phoenix Project holds elections for the position of Chief Commissioner paired with Assistant Comissioner, General Manager paired with Operations Manager every two weeks. (Next election: December 30-31)
