The various Principalities that fill Middle Asia. Many of them are too small to be of political significance on the world stage. Of the various princedoms, only two stand out Bhatang which sets itself as a idealistic utopia while Bhatung, a sister state that split off on its own accord.

Bhatung maintains a policy of non-commitment and isolationist approach within Middle Asia. Bhatang meanwhile continues to grow and presents itself as a modern socialist paradise, dedicated to the welfare of its people and its continued dominance among the various principalities.
