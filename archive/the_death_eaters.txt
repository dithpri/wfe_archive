[b]The Death Eaters[/b] was an ill-advised attempt by [b][nation]Cormac Stark[/nation][/b] to found a professional raiding organization. Its lone invasion of [b][region]Crusaders of Justice[/region][/b] occurred on 27 August 2012 and made quite a buzz.

[i]The Death Eaters have been permanently disbanded.[/i]
