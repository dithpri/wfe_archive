The Armafratis Union, founded on May 22nd, 2014, is an economic, trade, and intelligence alliance between the nations of Patrasia, New Yuklaya, and Liberserism. It was first founded by Patrasian President Jacob Franklin after the First GAU War, and its purpose was to bring the three countries closer together as allies. The AU has proven to be an economic force to be reckoned with, and has remained strong over the past few years.
