The [b]Kommunistische totalistische Internationale[/b] (Communist Totalist International) or [b]Kommutati[/b], is a communist international organization, founded in [b]1958 [/b]in [b]Fililug[/b], in the [b]Soviet Socialist Republic of Andia Devene[/b], dedicated to the establishment of a communist, collectivist, and strong world government.
