Deneb Algedi, also designated as &amp;#948; Capricorni (delta Capricorni), is a variable and multiple star of spectral class A in the constellation of Capricornus. Deneb Algedi visual magnitude is 2.87. Because of its brightness, Deneb Algedi is clearly visible to the naked eye when observed from locations with dark skies, and should be also quite easily visible from light polluted areas.
