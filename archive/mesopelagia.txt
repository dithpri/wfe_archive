Mesopelagia, the Land Between The Seas, is a vast continent that stretches from Arctic to Antarctic, separating the eastern and western seas from each other, and has historically proved an appealing target for colonization from the many nations of the Old World.

Mesopelagia is an old land with many beings that would purport to be gods, but whom the continent&#39;s indigenous peoples know not to venerate. In recent centuries, however, the influx of colonists from other lands has provided these beings with the worship they have long been denied.
