The Union of Eurasian Socialist Republics, otherwise known as the UESR, is a Union of Socialist States in the region of Eurasia. Comprising of the Russian Eurasian Socialist Republic (RESR) and the Eastern European Socialist Republic (EESR). The Region was founded on March 16, 2019. The current members of it are:
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
-Russian Eurasian Socialist Republic (Founder)
-Eastern European Socialist Republic (Co-Founder)
-Yugoslav Eurasian Socialist Republic
Our process is one of Federative Democracy and Democratic Centralism, as outlined in the ideology of Marxism-Leninism. Everything is voted on by the members of the Union.
