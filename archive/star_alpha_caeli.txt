&amp;#945; Caeli is a variable and double main-sequence star of spectral class F2 in the constellation of Caelum. &amp;#945; Caeli visual magnitude is 4.45. Because of its moderate brightness, &amp;#945; Caeli is easily visible to the naked eye from locations with dark skies, while it might be barely visible, or not visible at all, from light-polluted skies.
