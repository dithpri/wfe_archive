Tiamat&#39;s Crater is a the site of the Sovereign Nation-State of Terra Nova Supreme. A large crater formed millions of years ago by an asteroid impact has become the site of Terra Nova Supreme&#39;s Capital City Rapture. With a mountain range on the west border of the crater and an ocean on the right Terra Nova Supreme&#39;s nearly 4 billion citizens enjoy a varied landscape with Rapture&#39;s urban skyscrapers reaching heights rivaling the mountain peaks visible in the distance.  This region is founded on the ideals of Order, Freedom and Trade, nations that wish to join the region should look forward to the continued embodiment of those ideals.
