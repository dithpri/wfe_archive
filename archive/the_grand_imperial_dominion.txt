Imperia was a large empire that spanned an entire continent. 
It lasted for 1489 years as a major world superpower.
Clavicus was the founder of the Grand Imperial Dominion, consisted of three partitioned states, Neo-Imperia, The Imperium State, and The Imperian Isles, once Imperia collapsed.
Nations that are allied with the three nations are also members.
