The Croxian Union of Soviet Federative Socialist Republics is a supranational union of states which once formed the Croxian Empire, and is now under the leadership of the Communist Party under President Karolus Valorum.

The Capital of the Union is: Palatine City
The Currency of the Union is: The Lire
The Religion of the Union is: State Atheism
The Animal of the Union is: The Eagle
The Flag of the Union is: The Croxian Crimson Banner
The Motto of the Union is: Workers of the World, Unite!
The Population of the Union is: 22,017,000,000

Defcon Level: Level 5 (Peacetime readiness)
