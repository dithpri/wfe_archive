The Alliance is a minor yet powerful realm of countries, the major state is the Sanguinean Empire, which also is the major contributor of defensive elements.

Located in the far east of the second northern hemisphere, this region is a hard-working industrial realm, and is one of the most successfull places of automobile manufacturing.

The Eastern Alliance is known to be quite agressive, missile silos spot the fields of the countries like warts on a frog. If a war should break out, the Eastern Alliance will most surely be one of the factors that started it.
