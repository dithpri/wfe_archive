[b][color=steelblue]Federation of South Arabia[/color][/b]

[color=green]Was formed by the merger of the British colony of Aden with the Federation of the Emirates of the South, a British protectorate. The Federation of the Emirates of the South was formed by the union of six emirates, sultanates, and sheikhdoms; by the end of 1960 there were 10 members. In Jan., 1963, the Aden colony joined the federation, which was then renamed the Federation of South Arabia. A team was sent to the 1966 Commonwealth Games in Kingston, Jamaica.[/color]
