A Certified Copy of The Charter of the Greater Mercaterian League (Treaty of Lagobel) can be accessed [url=http://www.nationstates.net/page=dispatch/id=297087]here.[/url] Signed and Ratified by the initial eight nations (Manchovia, Voltrovia, the Far Iplutian Provinces, Golachia, Mayerton, Melicizen Thurbistan and Tovratis) in 1954, all Greater Mercaterian Nations have now ratified this charter and belong to the League. The Primary Author was Manchovia, while Voltrovia provided critical input and guidance.
