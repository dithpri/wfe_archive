[b]The Commonwealth of Sakuraterra[/b] is an interplanetary socioeconomic alliance of states. It primarily facilitates free trade and open border flow between its members, but it also utilizes a unique supernational currency, [b]the San[/b], and hosts a variety of other optional functions, such as humanitarian aid, intelligence exchange, military coordination, and research cooperation. Although member states are encouraged to participate in all of these functions, only free trade and open borders are a requirement. Its lingua franca is Simplified Japanese.
