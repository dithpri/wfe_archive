The [color=red]Red Seven Sovereignty[/color] was founded, centuries ago, by seven ruthless warlords, masters of warfare and government. They called themselves the Red Seven. They operated their order in secrecy, creating a league of assassins and warmongers bent on world domination.

After centuries of shadowy machinations, the secret Red Seven Sovereignty was discovered by the World Assembly. With the original Red Seven long dead, and their empire of secret warlords and assassins crumbling, the Red Seven Sovereignty has been forced to become a functioning member of world society.

But old habits die hard, and the ruthless past of the Red Seven Sovereignty is boiling beneath the surface...
