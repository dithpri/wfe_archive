[u]The Region of Dagon[/u] welcomes [b]H. P. Lovecraft[/b] enthusiasts and scholars!

[u]Dagon[/u] is a role-play region founded in honor of [b]Howard Phillips Lovecraft[/b], 1890-1937, inspired by the Cthulhu Mythos and Lovecraftian works of weird fiction such as [i]The Shadow over Innsmouth[/i] and [i]Dagon[/i].

Civility is appreciated and academic study is well regarded in [u]Dagon[/u]. Nazis and invaders are [color=red]not[/color] welcome. [u]Dagon&#146;s[/u] nation-states may be villainous exclusively for role-playing intents and purposes.
