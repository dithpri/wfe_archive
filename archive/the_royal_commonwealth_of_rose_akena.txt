The Royal Commonwealth of Rose Akena is a syndicate of colonies, dependencies, and dominions/protectorates that are united under the Rose Empire. All nations that are present in this organisation have sworn loyalty to the Imperial Crown. From its humble beginnings on The Isle of The Rose on the planet of Acirilica, the Rose Empire became the largest and most benevolent Empire in The Galaxy of Allucia(The Milky Way).
