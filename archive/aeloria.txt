Aeloria is a densely narrative region founded by a single tribal civilization that ascended to a highly developed society after discovering a crash-landed plane and seized working technology from it. Isolated on a small initial island, Aeloria expanded outward when a submerged continental mass rose nearby, linking with the larger landmass and initiating extensive cultural and technological exchange. The people of Aeloria are described as tanned to light-skinned with facial features reminiscent of both African American and Native American populations. They speak English and a range of other languages learned through contact with neighboring regions and critical early exposure to the recovering technology.
