Tinaiaya is a world of magic and high fantasy. It is a world where magic is commonplace and where wizards and sorcerers are able to bend the laws of the universe to their will. The people of Tinaiaya are kind, generous, and fair, though they also have a strong sense of justice and honor. The people of Tinaiaya value knowledge and culture, and try to keep abreast of the latest advances in magic and technology.
