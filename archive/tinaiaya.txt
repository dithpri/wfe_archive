Tinaiaya is a world of magic and science, where science and magic are two different things. Science is considered something logical, something that is to be used, and technology is the key to the advancement of society. Magic isn&#39;t something that can be taught to a normal person, and is considered something that has to be inherited. Tinaiaya has 5 major States, ruled by the Congress Of Tinaiaya. The Congress consists of representatives of the major Nations of Tinaiaya and the wizard tower as an observer states.
