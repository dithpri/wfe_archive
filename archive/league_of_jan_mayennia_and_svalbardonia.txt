This region is the result of years of diplomatic actions between the Two great nations of the Democratic Republic of Jan Mayennia and the Democratic Republic of Svalbardonia. This coalition aims to create a Political, Economic, Military and Diplomatic union between the two nations that conform it.

Being this Coalition exclusive to the nations of Jan Mayennia and Svalbardonia, no other nations will be allowed in. Jan Mayennia and Svalbardonia are both democratic, modern, and civilized nations, and we pose no threat to any other region, or nation.
