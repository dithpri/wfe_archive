The [b][region]Multiversal Community[/region][/b] is a intergovernmental organisation of four polities in Earth (Sol 3), Dunya (Teegarden c) and the observable Multiverse. The Community is a middle power with democratic, humanist, pacifist and liberal leanings. Its leader is King Leonidas and its seat is in the Free Land of the [nation=noflag]Central Indies[/nation].
