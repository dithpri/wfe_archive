The Union of Nova Universo, parent organisation of the Multiversal Community, in compliance with the Council Order 105, is hereby dissolved. Its agencies are demitted. Its functions are patriated to its former members. Ongoing dealings with the Union are forwarded to competent organisations. The Order 105 dissolves the Union for reorganisation and reformation. This involves auditing and restructuring of its functions and agencies.
-
The Old Community Demissionary Commission confirms the revocations of its external national and organisational missions. The New Union Accession Commission confirms the applications of its external national and organisational missions.
