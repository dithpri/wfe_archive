The Enclave is a political conglomerate under the power of [nation]Allied Sovereignty[/nation]. The Conglomerate was founded The Dominion of Allied Sovereignty. Since then other nations have sprouted from new political factions, or made alliances to join the Conglomerate. 

The Conglomerate welcomes all members with open arms. As the sovereignty of each nation is valued high amongst officials of [nation]Allied Sovereignty[/nation] each member is free to govern themselves how they see fit.
