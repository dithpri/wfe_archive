Northern Ireland is the smallest Devolved Nation in the United Kingdom.
You can make a nation and play as a person.

History:
Northern Ireland was officially formed in 1922.
It has been split between Irish Republicans who wish for Northern Ireland to join the Republic of Ireland and Ulster Unionists who want Northern Ireland to remain in the UK.
In 1969, the &#147;Troubles&#148; began, a conflict between Unionists and Republicans lasting almost 30 years. Due to this, politics has been very controversial between different parties.
