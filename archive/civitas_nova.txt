 Aurelia is an authoritarian socialist state led by a single dictator. It features a centrally planned, state-owned economy with all major industries under government control. Despite the tight grip on power, Aurelia upholds strong civil rights and political freedoms, including free speech and assembly. The region is strongly pro-Israeli, maintaining close diplomatic and trade relations with Israel. It also provides comprehensive universal healthcare and education while regulating public discourse to align with state objectives. The defense force focuses on internal security and regime stability, and the government is committed to environmental sustainability through green energy and conservation initiatives.
