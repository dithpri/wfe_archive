Welcome to the TransGlobal Nations (TGN)

The TGN was formed on Sunday 03rd February 2019, with a vision for Global co-operation and alliance.

We endeavour to promote global interoperability on Economy, Trade, Defence, Environment, Human Rights, Welfare, and World Affairs through Membership of The TGN.

Members will have influence on ALL decisions, debates and legislation within the TransGlobal Nations, as well as the right to veto.
