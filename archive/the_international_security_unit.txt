The Oath-

I do declare to follow the rules, and to respect these terms with my life.

1. I swear to not betray any nation within the region, they are allies.

2. If facing trial, I will take my punishment and follow it.

3. I shalt not bribe or blackmail nations, for that brakes the trust.

4. I shalt respect the nations within the ISU

I hath sworn upon the oath, now these rules I do follow. 

Respiciunt Popularis Regiminis
