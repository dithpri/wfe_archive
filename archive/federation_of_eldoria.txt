The [b]Federation of Eldoria[/b] is a federal democratic republic composed of fifteen sovereign nation-states, five sovereign territories, and a Capital District. Known for its geographic and cultural diversity, Eldoria stretches from tropical islands and bustling coastal cities to rugged mountains, dense forests, fertile plains, and northern tundra. Its economy is driven by finance, manufacturing, agriculture, maritime trade, tourism, energy, and research. Though each nation-state maintains its own distinct identity and traditions, all are united by a shared commitment to democracy, liberty, and cooperative federalism.
