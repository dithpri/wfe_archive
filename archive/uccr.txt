The Union of Capitalist Conservative Republics- U.C.C.R are regional and allied nations devoted to the free market. Each nation is considered independent and have their own constitution but must be in accord with the constitution of the U.C.C.R. If a nation would like to join they will have to send the leader of the nation Texkentuck a message and after evaluation and agreeing to the accord and principles of the U.C.C.R a nation will be allowed to stay within the organization. Nations do not need to belong to the same region. Also a nation is allowed to join the U.C.C.R and is allowed to be a WA delegate but if the nation votes out of accord the nation will not be allowed to stay in the U.C.C.R.
