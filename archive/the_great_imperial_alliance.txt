History
The Great Imperial Alliance was made to protect the mortals from Diablo and to aid in Holy Liberation of all creation. Known shortly as the Alliance, the Alliance has many regions and areas, new ones being added when new Empires join. Due to the military role the Alliance has a system of military agreements and systems making it a feared Imperial Cohort.
