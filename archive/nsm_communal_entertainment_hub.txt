[b][color=red]The NSM Communal Entertainment Hub[/color][/b] constitutes the official arena for the execution of various games and events offered by the [url=https://www.nationstates.net/page=dispatch/id=1714425][b]Popular Culture Commission [PCC][/url][/b] of the Internal Affairs Department. Hunger Games-simulations (HG), Roleplay (RP), as well as WARZONE-tournaments etc. are organized and carried out in the [region]NSM Communal Entertainment Hub[/region] under the moderation and oversight of the [i]Commissionary Managers[/i] of each PCC Division. 

All games are played for fun and friends. Awards / prizes given are non-monetary in accordance with the clause on commercialism of NationStates rules and regulations.
