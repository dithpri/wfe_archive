Welcome to the Levant, a small but diverse region along the eastern end of the Mediterranean Sea and home to a fascinating array of cultures. The history of the Levant goes back thousands of years, with the rise and fall of numerous empires and cultures, but its heart was always Jerusalem, the eternal capital of Israel, the Jewish homeland, which has been rebuilt after almost 2,000 years of diaspora.
