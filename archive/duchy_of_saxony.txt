[b]Brief Description[/b]:
Saxony is a Confederation of States that allow individual freedom of action at the same time with the benefits of collective security. We allow nations to be admitted with a wide range of government verities. The Duchy is also used as a support network to help developing members become industrialized and for members who are experiencing an economic trouble we are here to support them in their time of need.
