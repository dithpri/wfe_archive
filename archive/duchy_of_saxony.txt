[b]Brief Description[/b]:
Saxony is a Federation that is in a personal union with the [region]Germanic Confederation[/region] under the Treaty of Dresden. All nations are welcome to establish diplomatic relations with the Saxony provided that they are not communist nor fascist. The Duchy of Saxony allows all member states to partake in the government affairs. To see more information about the Duchy of Saxony we advise you to take a look at the fact-book entries bellow.
