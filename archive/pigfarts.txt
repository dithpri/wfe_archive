Pigfarts Intergalactic School of Witchcraft and Wizardry is a school for talented young wizards and witches.
The headmaster is the famed Rumbleroar, the talking lion. Classes are taught by a variety of professors including Severus Snake and Professor McGonnagill.
Enroll today!
Disclaimer: You must have a rocketship to travel to Mars, the home planet of Pigfarts, and a spacesuit must be worn at all times or you will most likely die.
