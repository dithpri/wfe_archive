By the end of the Sorrowful war, the free cities was but a far cry of what it was, a lone Greater Kazamino which stood by againsts the wraiths held but a flicker of the glory of what was, even under the light of the Great Goddess. However with new people coming in standing together, settling in on the rubble of what was, a new age will be ushered, light will be brought forth, fights will be called, pizzas will be delivered

The Free Cities shall stand, and none may brought it down again
