The Great Azure Expanse is a vast oceanic region defined by deep blue seas, scattered islands, and long maritime traditions. It serves as a vital crossroads for trade, culture, and diplomacy, linking diverse nations through shared waters and history.

The region is known for its strong seafaring identity, reliance on marine resources, and strategic importance in international shipping routes. Despite differences among its nations, cooperation on trade, navigation, and environmental stewardship remains a defining feature of the Expanse.

Often described as both beautiful and unpredictable, the Great Azure Expanse symbolizes opportunity, resilience, and the enduring bond between people and the sea.
