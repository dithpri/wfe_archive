[b]Paris[/b] is the capital and the most populous city of France. It has an area of 41 square miles and a population in 2013 of 2,229,621 within the administrative limits. The agglomeration has grown well beyond the city&#39;s administrative limits. The Paris unit&eacute; urbaine is a measure of continuous urban area for statistical purposes, including both the commune and its suburbs, and has a population of 10,601,122 (Jan. 2013 census) which makes it the largest in the European Union.

Paris was founded in the 3rd century BC by a Celtic people called the Parisii, who gave the city its name. By the 12th century, it was the largest city in the western world.[hr]
