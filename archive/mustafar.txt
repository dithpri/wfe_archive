[color=blue]Mustafar was a scorching lava world located in the Outer Rim Territories, and had an array of valuable minerals which were mined by the Techno Union, and was the headquarters of the infamous Black Sun during the Clone Wars. This planet also served as the location for Darth Vader&#39;s Castle.[/color]
