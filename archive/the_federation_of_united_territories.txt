The [b]Federation of United Territories[/b], also known as [b]FUT[/b] is an alliance between several nations founded by [nation]The Palmetto[/nation]. It was founded in 1939 to allow them to unite against foreign threats. It would also act as a trade alliance while promoting nationalism and preventing globalism from taking a foothold in the Americas. Most of the nations are situated in the Americas, however, some are also islands who have allied with them. They have a military agreement that a declaration of war on one them is a declaration of war against all of them, and they typically fight wars together to increase their odds of winning. Imperialism is extremely common, especially with its founding nations.
