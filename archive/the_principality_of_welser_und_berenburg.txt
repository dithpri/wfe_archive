This is the realm of the Welser-Berenbergs,
A noble family of great wealth that rules the Principality of Welser-Berenburg, the family is descended from two brothers, the family&#39;s considerable power has gained them titles such as Prince, lord, marquise... etc, the family traditionally either serves in the armed forces or the temples of Vedulism, but some live as successful bankers and businessmen, and as a result a banking family branch has been created Fugger-Welser-Berenberg which not only works in the Kaiserreich but also in other regions that the kaiserreich has ties with.
