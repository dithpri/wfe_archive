MTVB region started by MarioDudeLand June 23rd, 2014.

This is only for people from a ROBLOX subforum called Movies/TV/Books, but we will form embassies with other groups and embassy members are allowed to post on our board.

Basically this is an alternate history role-play with a sliding timescale, as new things happen I will go back and add things in where they should go.

The concept of this is, a lot of really progressive nations are all in this one region starting in 1900, and it basically tracks these places that sort of look at the old times from a modern perspective, because the majority of the stuff on the history is based on actual things that the nations are doing individually.
