Kohen is a relatively large region known for its population of Pok&eacute;mon, a type of organism unlike any other in the world. Pok&eacute;mon are known for possession almost-magical abilities that allow them to combat each other with very potent powers, and are commonly trained by humans to unleash their untapped power.

The climate of Kohen is temperate, with warm summers (high of 80&deg;F) and cool winters (low of 40&deg;F). The region is largely comprised of forested areas, although plains, deserts, and mountains are common.
