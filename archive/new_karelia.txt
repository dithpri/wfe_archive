[b]New Karelia[/b] (Karelian &amp; Finnish: [i]Uusi Karjala[/i]; Russian: [i]&amp;#1053;&amp;#1086;&amp;#1074;&amp;#1099;&amp;#1081; &amp;#1050;&amp;#1072;&amp;#1088;&amp;#1077;&amp;#1083;&amp;#1080;&amp;#1103;[/i]; Swedish: [i]Ny Karelen[/i]), is a region consisting of the area around the Russo-Finland border. The region is home to various ethnic minority groups, such as the Zinns, a subgroup of Russians living in the region, and the Dzakowans, a left-wing sect of Armenian Christians.
