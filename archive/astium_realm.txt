[region]Astium Realm[/region] is located in the center on the planet of [b]Austium[/b] in the Delta Valorous system. The realm is set in a modern day, non-fantasy environment, with [u]light[/u] sci-if elements. Geographically it has a mixture of islands, large continents, and various nations settled within it; a large number of NPC nations exist within Austium.

The realm is currently undergoing a modern era [i]&quot;Cold War&quot;[/i] between various political and economic ideologies.
