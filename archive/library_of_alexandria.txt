The [b]Great Library of Alexandria[/b] in Alexandria, Egypt, was one of the largest and most significant libraries of the ancient world. The library was part of a larger research institution called the [i]Mouseion[/i], which was dedicated to the Muses, the nine goddesses of the arts.

Sci-Hub is a shadow library website founded in Kazakhstan by [b]Alexandra Elbakyan[/b], in response to the high cost of research papers behind paywalls. It provides free access to millions of research papers, regardless of copyright, by bypassing publishers&#39; paywalls in various ways.
