The Ethiopian Empire, also formerly known by the exonym Abyssinia, or just simply known as Ethiopia, was a monarchy that spanned a geographical area in the current states of Ethiopia and Eritrea. It was conquered by the Italians in 1935 in the lead up to WWII, and was released from Italian control formally known as Ethiopia following the defeat of the Axis.[/color]
