Sentosa is a sunny island in [region]Singapore[/region], home to exciting events, themed attractions, award-winning spas, lush rainforests, golden sandy beaches and resort-style hotels.
Sentosa is located off [region]Singapore[/region]&#146;s southern coast, connected to the city by road, cable car, pedestrian boardwalk, and monorail. At the Sentosa Station, Tiger Sky Tower offers panoramic views that can stretch as far as Indonesia. On the south coast, Palawan Beach is lined with food stalls and bars, and has a suspension bridge to a small offshore island. Palm-lined, crescent-shaped Tanjong Beach is more tranquil.

[region]Singapore[/region] is one of the world&#39;s greatest cities, and Sentosa Island resort is one of the city&#39;s star attractions.
