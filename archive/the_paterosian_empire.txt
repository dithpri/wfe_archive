[b]The Second Paterosian Empire[/b] or [b]the Paterosian Empire[/b], simply [b]Paterosia[/b], is an imperial federation of countries under one, imperial crown. This Imperial Crown however shares executive or ceremonial authority with other devolved crowns of its assimilated states but, this does not apply with free, democratic or dictatorial imperial states or cities.

Countries are welcome to join this imperial federation, only to swear allegiance to the Imperial Crown and its monarch: [i]Her Imperial Majesty Empress Awaia.[/i]
