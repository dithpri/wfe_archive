    [url=http://ru.studio256.wikia.com/wiki/&amp;#1050;&amp;#1060;&amp;#1057;&amp;#1056;]Windcape Federation[/url] is a confederation. It is ruled by the Soviet of federation, which consist of delegates from all the subjects. Every subject of Windcape Federation must be democratic country with the socialist economy (does not apply to [nation]Worbia[/nation]). Subjects of WF have a general army, general science and an education programme also they have open borders between eachother.

 Purposes of the Windcape Federation:
- [b]Liquidation of [url=http://ru.studio256.wikia.com/wiki/&amp;#1057;&amp;#1080;&amp;#1075;&amp;#1080;&amp;#1079;&amp;#1084;&amp;#1091;&amp;#1085;&amp;#1076;_&amp;#1044;&amp;#1088;&amp;#1086;&amp;#1079;&amp;#1076;&amp;#1086;&amp;#1074;]Zigmund[/url][/b]
- Fighting against injustice, egoism and exploitation of government, bourgeoisie or religion.
- Aspiration for scientific progress, moral and intelligence evolution of humanity.
- Defending environment and rare species.
- Protecting independence of Federation subjects.
- Making space program.
- Building communism.
