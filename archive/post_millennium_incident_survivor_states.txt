After Y2K proved to destroy the planet through the failure of dozens of electronic devices, on January 1 2000, hundreds of nuclear arms were detonated, planes and satellites fell from the sky, and cities across the planet were without water, heating and electricity. The following destruction resulted in deaths of upwards to billions of people. This region was founded by the Askunessippi Federation in what was once Southwestern Ontario in former Canada, in hopes to find other nations set up after the world ended in 2000.
