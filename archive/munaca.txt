The region of Munaca is a vast, lush region, filled with many tropical jungles and exotic geographic sites. Munaca is home to the single nation of Sagerism, led by the UMN (United Munacan Nations). Munaca is inhabited by many types of wildlife and is home to 98% people of Sagan race. The region has a rich history and is very culturally advanced. Munaca is a safe region with advanced technology and a healthy population.
