The Union of Independent Socialist Republics

The UISR is ruled by the Central Committe of the UISR. Most decisions ar made by unanimous vote, although some matters may be decided by simple majority. The committe consists of five members, three of which are elected.

Contact the Foreign Secretary [nation]Neuhannia[/nation] to request admission to the UISR. Admissions are granted by simple majority in the Central Committee
