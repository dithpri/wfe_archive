&quot;The backing of justice is armed force.&quot; - Gen. Yan Xishan

Yan Xishan Thought: After having witnessed the shortcomings of other attempted ideologies and belief systems, Yan created his own unique ideology encompassing as he stated; &quot;militarism, nationalism, anarchism, democracy, capitalism, communism, individualism, imperialism, universalism, paternalism and utopianism.&quot; Yan Xishan Thought focuses on modernization, social and economic reform, promotion of good morals, expansion of public education, strong military force and a fair justice system.
