Artifactia is a massive networks of cities dug deep in the caves underneath Europe. Deeper than what is possible to dig for those residing above, Artifactia is shielded by the very Earth and the defenses they have built to ensure it remains hidden from above. Sprawling cities and man-made country exists far and wide in these caves, the entire network stretching out deep under Europe. 

All those who wish to enter can only do so at the bequest of the proper authorities but it is highly unlikely that anyone will learn of this Magical Kingdom.
