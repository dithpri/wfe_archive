Maxia Gondouria is a war-torn region filled with nations competing for power. Arthorium is the head of the Maxian Gondourian Union. The only nation not in the MGU is the Empire of Mahamid which believes the Infidels are bad and they will change their ways. It has been attacked many times but they still stand tall and strong.

[!] Alliances [!]

MGU: Arthorium, Tunia Uninbellia, Engladore, Far The Couthslated, Gnogbatia, Benfleet and North Timons.
