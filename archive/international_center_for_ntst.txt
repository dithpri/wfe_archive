The vision behind the substance and essence of our camaraderie has an universal character, it speaks for the millions tired and disillusioned of the repeated shortcomings and failures of our contemporary political/ideological system, it calls for a unique and original alternative, responding to the need and desire for shared enterprise, for mutual harmony and true progress. We have not taken it upon ourselves to subscribe to its overarching values and principles without the preparedness for its advancement for the benefit of the whole race of man, to be followed in our lives and interactions, in all aspects and domains of our life.
