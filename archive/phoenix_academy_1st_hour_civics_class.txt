A newly developing nation of plucky pioneers who have been banished from their home nations due to not fitting in. This is where they seek their own home to establish their own distinct classroom community, where they have alternative opportunities to learn how to create a civic nation that works for them. 

(This is a first hour class from Phoenix Academy, began in September of 2022, under the presidential leadership of Mr. Daylon Thomas. The purpose of this region is for his students to explore types of government and learn about civic issues in real time in a fun and controlled way.)
