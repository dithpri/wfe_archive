[b]Continent[/b]
Continental Heladia is a large and arid landmass that houses the 2 claimant states to what was formerly the empire of Heladia. The lands have a history of being regal and rich with culture, housing societies that, until recently, were focused on the arts and the luxuries of the lands rather than the more militant and industrial stance they have taken now. 

[b]Ethnicities[/b]
Mountain Heladites are an ethnic sub-branch of the Eastern Heladite branch. They are known to have copper-colored skin, freckles, round eyes and Mediterranean-like features such as Roman noses. Commonly, they have thin eyebrows The most common eye color is brown, while the hair colors are split 50/50 between brown and black.
