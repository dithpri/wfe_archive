The American Wasteland, has seen nuclear annihilation twice, first in the great war and now in the Second US civil war. The 14 Commonwealths of America struggle to rebuild there power only starting to take back the East coast. As Operation Enduring Democracy continues to devastate the Carribean the US is in a state of destruction, the west coast is occupied by Mecha Bavaria, the post-war counterpart of Germany. As the Oligarchs struggle to find the way back to glory the population only see there nation on hard times, awaiting her return to power. Whilst  America remains in a state of lawlessness terror and murder. But the truest question is, Who will win the war for America, the Government, or UNoC, and what part will Mecha Bavaria play?
