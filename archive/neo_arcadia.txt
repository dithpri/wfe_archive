Neo-Arcadia is a group of NationStates players engaging in a game of our own making using other websites alongside NS. The players are old friends from a certain group, who played NS together long ago. Anyone that is interested in looking at our Ruleset may do so [url=https://docs.google.com/document/d/1YWWj7Da_SoY_D7kzQL9VxdIIYw4zrzAf4LAirQvHP-8/edit#]here[/url].

It is still being set up currently, gameplay tests are being conducted, and this is just a placeholder.
Not currently accepting players, but maybe in the future.
