[b][region]The Western Archipelagos[/region][/b]
A small group of islands a couple thousand kilometers southwest of North America.

[b]Philosophy[/b]
Territories in the Western Archipelagos are dedicated to the preservation of freedom. These territories hold that all forms of human association should be voluntary and that coercive measures taken upon by the state to control interactions between humans is inherently against freedom.
