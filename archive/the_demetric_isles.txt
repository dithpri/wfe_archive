A massive isle in the Dragonic Sea where the people of Demetra reside. The Demteric Isles are split into 5 pieces, the mainland,  Drachen Island, Sonnenlicht Island, Halbmond Archipelago, and the towering Elias Alps, named after the 1st president of Demetra, Leistung Elias. The Dragonic Sea is the 4th middle-west sea in the War Seas, 7 different seas (The Bronze Sea, The Nimbus Sea, The Shining Sea, The Dragonic Sea, The Revoluting Sea, The Divine Sea, and The Dark Sea) grouped together due to their notoriety for war.
