Hobbiton is a settlement located within the area of Hobbiton-Bywater in the Shire. One of the oldest settlements of the Shire, Hobbiton is known throughout all farthings. So is its renowned tavern, the Ivy Bush, and landmarks such as the Grange and Sandyman&amp;#39;s Mill. Other than that Hobbiton does not afford many facilities but depicting the most hobbity scenery.

The village is located almost at the centre of the Shire, just south of the Great East Road, at the crossroad of &amp;quot;the Bywater Road&amp;quot; and the road from Tookland which then crosses Bywater Bridge and runs on past the Hill to Overhill and beyond. -- Lotro-Wiki.com
