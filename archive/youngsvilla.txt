[b][color=red]Youngsvilla[/b][/color] is a relatively rural area, with a strong sense of community and culture.  With a homogeneous and vibrant population, [b][color=red]Youngsvilla[/b][/color] is eagerly anticipating more nations to apply for membership.

Members of [b][color=red]Youngsvilla[/b][/color] are expected to maintain respectful decorum, but can conduct themselves freely otherwise.

Members are expected to:

1. Submit at least one substantive entry in their factbook every day.  Substantive means roughly one paragraph.
2. Deal with their daily issues that arise, not just ignore them.
3. Challenge at least one other nation per day to compare statistics randomly.  You may choose any nation you wish.
4. Apply for and join the World Assembly.
