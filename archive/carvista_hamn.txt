[color=lightblue][b]Carvista Hamn[/b][/color] is a fairly sized Archipelago located in the [region]Philippine Sea[/region], just west of [region]Guam[/region]. Most people refer to the region as [color=lightblue][b]Carvista[/b][/color]. The main Island, Alfas, was settled by Swedish Colonists during the 1500s. The colony gained its independence through peaceful means in 1643 and named itself Mylar. There are a total of 6 smaller islands with 3 of them claimed by Jiyole.
