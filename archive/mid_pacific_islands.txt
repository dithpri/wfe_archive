The Mid-Pacific Islands Region is made up of several island nations that inhabit the mid-Pacific Ocean area, and some of their allies.  Only nations within this area is allowed membership into the region.  Full membership rules and governing committees will be announced in the near future.

Regional Governor: Dean Curtiss (Tilson Valley)
Regional Vice-Governor: Jacci Park (Pacifica Island)
Regional Secretary: Annette Burney (Gammon Island)
Regional Treasurer: Joan Kolony (Veazie)
Regional Security Chief: Gil Card (Lovell Island)

Regional Offices were initially proposed for Cape Cormier, Pacifica Island, however, it was decided to build them in Tilson City, Tilson Valley, to be more centrally located within the region.
