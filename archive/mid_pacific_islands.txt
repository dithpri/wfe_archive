The Mid-Pacific Islands Region is made up of several island nations that inhabit the mid-Pacific Ocean area, and some of their allies.  Only nations within this area is allowed membership into the region.  Full membership rules and governing committees will be announced in the near future.

Regional Governor: King Java I (Pacifica Island)
Regional Vice-Governor: Patrick Kitel (Tilson Valley)
Regional Secretary: Annette Burney (Gamman Island)
Regional Treasurer: Kathy Rhodes (Lynnovia)
Regional Security Chief: Gil Card (Lovell Island)

Regional Offices are in Cape Cormier, Pacifica Island, though it has been suggested that offices be moved to Tilson City to make them more centrally located in the region.
