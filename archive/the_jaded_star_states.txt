To qualify as a Jaded Star State, a State must adopt the flag of the Jaded Star.

All Jaded Star States that are members of the World Assembly must endorse Otemigoland for WA Delegate.

To become an economic ally of the Jaded Star States, a State must adopt the Jaded Star as its currency.

The Regional Password can be shared by the Regional Security Chancellor (Awaken Hollow) only. All applications for Statehood must be forwarded to Awaken Hollow.
