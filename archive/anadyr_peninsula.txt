The Anadyr Peninsula is a remote, cold, arctic peninsula where the People&#39;s Republic of Andikov resides. The winter climate tends to be harsh and cold, with temperatures reaching -35 C (-31 F), with mild summers lasting about two months. The main part of the region is consists of the Anadyr Peninsula, with the Gulf of Anadyr to the north, an Arctic Tundra to the west, the southwest has fertile plains where the majority of Andikov&#39; s agricultural exports come from. the northwest is home to the west Siberian moutain range which is rich in Uranium, Iron and Coal. The region has a lot of sea, agricultural, and uranium exports. Andikov borders The Kingdom of Duldar, which is to the southwest of the Anadyr Peninsula.
