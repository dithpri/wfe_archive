The [u][b]Allied Nations Block[/b][/u] aims towards the best of quality in a region with an active RP.Unlike other regions,we have simply only rules that make sure that the RP remains realistic and on point,thus making the experience far richer and enjoyable for all. [b]The region RP starts from the year 1800 with the major nations of France,UK,USA,Italy,Germany,Japan and Russia as its main starting players.[/b] The Allied Nations Block is working towards creating a simple but still effective region where simple rules and excellent RP can exist together in Harmony without the fuss of huge factbooks of rules,guides and such but instead experience through RP and time with other nations of the region.
