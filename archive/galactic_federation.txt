The Galactic Federation (GF), or Intergalactic Federation (IGF) is a diplomatic and political interplanetary organization with the intended purpose of maintaining galaxy-wide peace and security, developing friendly relations among planetary civilisations, achieving intergalactic cooperation, and serving as a center for coordinating the actions of member worlds. It is widely recognised as the galaxy&#39;s largest interplanetary organisation. The IGF is headquartered within the Galactic Hub Satellite, with a grounded headquarters on New Taurica.
