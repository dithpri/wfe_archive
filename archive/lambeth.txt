[region]Lambeth[/region] is a district in central London, within the [region]Empire of Britain[/region]. Located on the south bank of the River Thames, Lambeth is known for its historic landmarks, including the iconic Lambeth Palace, cultural venues, and vibrant riverside communities. The area serves as a mix of residential, commercial, and governmental functions.
