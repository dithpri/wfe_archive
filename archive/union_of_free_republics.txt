Union Of Free Republics Constitution:
1. Opinions are permitted, be civilized.
2. Issues outside of the region are not up for discussion, save they impact the region directly.
3. A two thirds vote is required when speaking of invasion. Gintzania and Voittomaa may override this.
4. All suggestions and complaints are welcome, be civilized.
5. All nations must contribute to the region, or risk expulsion. 
6. All nations will agree to military alliance with other regional members.
