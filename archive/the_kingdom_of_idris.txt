[b]Overview[/b]
The Kingdom of Idris has known many names and gone through many revolutions. Situated in the Region of Idris, the surviving Nations within the Kingdom continue to stand united against all adversities and stand for equality, peace and freedom for all.

[b]The Motto of The Kingdom of Idris[/b]
