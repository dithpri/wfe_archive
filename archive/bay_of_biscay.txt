The [b]Bay of Biscay[/b] is a gulf of the northeast Atlantic Ocean located south of the [region]Celtic Sea[/region]. It lies along the western coast of France from [region]Brest[/region] south to the Spanish border, and the northern coast of Spain west to Cape Ortegal.

The average depth is 5,722 feet and the greatest depth is 15,535 feet. &#151;Wikipedia[hr]
