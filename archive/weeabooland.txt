Weeabooland was founded on Friday, August 25, 2017. The region is mostly inactive now, but is being preserved in memory of the times had. Due to several raids, much of our old descriptions have been lost, and officers removed from office and reappointed at later dates. To prevent further raids, a password has been set. As time goes on, the descriptions may be rebuilt and we may return as a functioning region. Until then, we hope everyone the best.
