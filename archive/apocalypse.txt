The region is a post-apocalyptic wasteland set after a deadly disease wiped out most of its population. Unlike many ruined worlds, the landscape remains largely untouched&#151;forests, mountains, rivers, and cities still stand much as they once did. With the disease having run its course, the region has become an unexplored land filled with abandoned settlements, overgrown roads, and the remnants of a forgotten civilization. Nature has begun reclaiming what people left behind, leaving the region both beautiful and dangerous for anyone who dares to explore it.
