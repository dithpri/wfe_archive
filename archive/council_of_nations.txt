The Council of Nations, established circa early 2015, is a means of keeping peace and stability to the region of Kaqunland (KQ). It is made up of the states residing in the continent of Kaqunland and is the legal representative of the Union of the Republics of Kaqunland (i.e the Union of the Republics of Pingala, Askaka, Lingpapa and Singpapa) but shall not represent fully the Congress of the Union of the Republics of Kaqunland for now.
