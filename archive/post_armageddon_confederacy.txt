After the devastating Armageddon event wiped out 90% of Humanity, the remaining people came together in different nation states. New Macross was established on the site of the first and largest settlement after the event and its capital still stands there today, with the remains of the SDF-1 still in the  center of the city. PAC is a confederation of the new nations  of Earth, free from the threat of terrorism, of crime, and invasion by foreign states. Together we shall stand where the nations of old failed.
