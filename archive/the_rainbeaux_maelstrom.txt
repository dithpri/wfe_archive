The Rainbeaux Maelstrom is a broad but isolated stretch of  island-nations cut off from the rest of the world by massive oceanic storms and tidal anomalies. Though often diverse in methodologies to the point of dissonance, the factions within the Maelstrom are surprisingly non-hostile in interaction, seemingly all valuing an officiated sense of order and alliance and, more importantly, an avoidance of needless conflict.
