To preserve the existence of monarchies this region exists to strengthen their position in the world by international cooperation. Depending on contributions by member nations to the welfare of the region a &quot;College of Electors&quot; is built up out of  notable nations with ranks ranging from knight-elector to king-elector. The Noble Atlantic States holds the title of &quot;Emperor of the Union&quot;.
