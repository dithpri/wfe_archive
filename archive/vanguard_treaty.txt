The [b][url=http://www.nationstates.net/region=ussd][color=red]Vanguard Treaty[/color][/url] [/b] is a [b]mutual cooperation alliance of neutral leftist and/or anti-fascist regions (in terms of regional government)[/b] where said regions [b]help and support each other[/b], while they don&#39;t meddle with other&#39;s affairs. The Treaty works under the [b]&quot;multiple regions, one community&quot;[/b] platform, in which all regions are expected to [b]cooperate with each other[/b] in terms of culture, government and military, defining the [b][url=http://www.nationstates.net/region=ussd][color=red]Vanguard Treaty[/color][/url] [/b] as a coalition of regions that shall remain united.
