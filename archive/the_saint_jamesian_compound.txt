[nation]The Saint James Islands[/nation] is a small, diverse and ecologically stunning nation located in the [region]Democratic Socialist Assembly[/region].

Formerly a set of loosely associated British colonies, the Commonwealth was formed as a defence pact and political union. Tourism is a major industry within the Saint James Islands, with people from all over the world flocking to the Saint James Islands for its world famous beaches, stunning natural forests, colourful culture and beautiful women.

This region serves as a resting point for diplomatic corps and a depository of Saint Jamesian history.
