As one of the best kept secrets in modern times, the Hollow Earth has been covered up by the world governments. The Hollow Earth is currently home to a number of antediluvian civilizations that were granted access prior to the global flood that started the Kali Yuga. Only a few individuals are known to have been allowed entrance in the past century. Admiral Richard E. Byrd recorded his journey through the mystical land in detail and evidence suggests the Ahnenerbe traveled to it by way of a hidden south pole entrance.[hr][hr]
