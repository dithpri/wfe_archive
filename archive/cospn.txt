The Compact Organization of Sovereign Predum Nations was established on June 12, 1990 through the final signing of the COSPN Treaty by Repulaean President Marcus Reeves. COSPN is the united alliance between the four sovereign nations of the continent of Predum. Those Nations are:

The Constitutional Monarchy of Repulaea
The Federal Republic of Euneia
The Federation of Silvion
The Republic of Serany

Its headquarters is located in its founding city, Ancot, which is also the Repulaean capital.
