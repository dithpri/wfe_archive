Dragons exist. So do elves, demons, gryphons, and all the other things reserved for dreamers and the overly religious.

From the inhospitable crags of Bonecrack, to the caverns of Velbayse, to the savannahs of Gyos Prime, Yozora boasts a range of habitat that is suitable for all types of people, flora, and fauna. Their governments and beliefs are just as diverse.

Fifgarinn is the crown jewel of the named realms, and it is a melting pot where humans, dragons, and even the more warlike races come. Port cities riddle its shores. The continent has a delegate from each of the lands, though ultimately, it is whoever is most powerful at the moment that gets the main say. Magnon, the wraith of Gyos, holds most sway for now.
