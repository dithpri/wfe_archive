Dragons exist. So do elves, angels, demons, gryphons, and all the other things reserved for dreamers, the insane, and the overly religious.

Fifgarinn is the crown jewel of the named realms, and it is a melting pot where humans, elves, dragons, and even the more warlike races come. Port cities riddle its shores. The continent has a delegate from each of the other lands, though ultimately, it is whoever is most powerful at the moment that gets the main say. Currently, Magnon of Gyos and King Akcove of Tilarokk continue to gain power.
