Location: Based in the European continent and to the North. 
Climate: Moderated by prevailing southwest winds over the North Atlantic Current; more than one-half of the days are overcast.
Terrain: Mostly rugged hills and low mountains; level to rolling plains in east and southeast
Natural resources: coal, petroleum, natural gas, iron ore, lead, zinc, gold, tin, limestone, salt, clay, chalk, gypsum, potash, silica sand, slate, arable land.
Natural Hazards: winter windstorms; floods
Nationality: Anglos, Englos, Nordic
Religions: Atheist, religions are not recognised by the states, public worship is illegal.
