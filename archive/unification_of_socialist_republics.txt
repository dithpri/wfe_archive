The Unification of Socialist Republics was founded in the year 2007 by the Neo Soviet Republic as a way of Unifying other Socialist nations under one banner. This region has three branches of government: The Legislative branch, The Executive branch and The Judicial branch. The branches are governed by the Executive Committee which in turn is headed by the Premier. (Founder) Each Senior committee member heads a certain branch of the government with the regular committee members heading a certain department or agency within the region. A Committee Chairman/Chairwoman (WA Delegate) can be elected which heads the committee and reports to the premier.
