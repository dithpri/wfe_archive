The Unification of Socialist Republics is a region created as a way of Unifying other Socialist nations under one banner. This region has three branches of government: The Legislative branch, The Executive branch and The Judicial branch. Each branch is headed by a Branch Executive. Each branch executive is a member of the ruling council known as &quot;The Executive Committee&quot; the WA Delegate is also a member of the committee. The WA Delegate heads the Committee and is known as the &quot;Prime Minister&quot; Elections are held every 2 months.
Fascist Nations are banned.
Capitalist Nations can seek relations but are not allowed to join.
Democracy is welcome, as long as it is a Socialist Democracy
