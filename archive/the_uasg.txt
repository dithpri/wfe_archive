The Union of Authoritarian States and Governments:
A Region meant for Authoritarian States and Governments with impressive military power willing to frequently join in battles occurring globally in NS on a day-to-day basis. United under the Standard Credit, the U.A.S.G. currency, the U.A.S.G. is meant to be a safe haven for nations lead by warriors of honor. Harassment is grounds for expulsion as is offending another nation purposely to start a fight without just cause. The cause will be judged upon by the Prime Minister until the Regional Court is set up and running.
