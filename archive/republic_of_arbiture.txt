[color=red]A Brief History[/color]

The world ascended into chaos in 2011 with the fall of the USA and war followed. Through 2021 to 2064, nuclear warfare weapons killed the population and provoked extreme climate change. A group of survivors emerged from their shelters and divided up to land. This group of survivors became the rules of the world.

Set in 2094, nations of the republic are trying to balance the explanation of Arbiture and defending threats of the terrorist group, [nation=short+noflag]Infestus[/nation], and [region]The True US Republic[/region].
