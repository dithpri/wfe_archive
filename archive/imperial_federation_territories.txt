[b]The Imperial Federation &amp; Commonwealth[/b]

A group of Independent Member states of the Commonwealth and overseas territories of the [nation]The Galactic Triumvirate[/nation], forming an International Federation.

An intergovernmental body that contains three core branches, the Commission, the Council and the Defence Committee. Representatives of nations meet here to discuss issues of common Imperial Policy.

Contact through [nation]The Commission Secretariat[/nation].
