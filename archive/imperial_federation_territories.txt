[b]The Imperial Federation &amp; Commonwealth[/b]

A group of Independent Member states of the Commonwealth and overseas regions of the [nation]The Galactic Triumvirate[/nation], forming an International Federation.

This intergovernmental body contains three core branches, the Commission, the Council and the Defence Committee. Representatives of nations and overseas regions meet here to discuss issues of common Imperial Policy and collective action on an international scale.


Contact through [nation]The Commission Secretariat[/nation].
