This is a server that engages in alternative, semi realistic nation role play. Here you can create your own variant of a nation within the world. But overall you can role play as a variety of things such as a nation, a international corporation, or cause mayhem as a domestic or international terrorist grouping, or take on the role of being a colonial government operating under another nation. The choice is yours. Will you help being about change and a more peaceful world? Or will will you rule with an iron fist and make those who cross your path bend to your will?

The current Date is: 2000. Every 3 days is a year. each day is 4 months.

The current theme is: to be decided
