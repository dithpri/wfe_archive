Mundomeio is a tropical region that has been home to some recent instability due to revolutions, communist guerrillas, and deranged mad scientists and their attempts at cloning dinosaurs.  To the west is the Ceullada Ocean and to the east is the Xaridad Ocean. Currently, there is a state of cold war between the two largest nations in the region, Gran Tierna &amp;amp; Oikotopia, and their political allies.
