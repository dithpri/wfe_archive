[b]The Confederation of Roleplaying Nations (CORN) has the goal to keep alive small regions through the establishment of diplomatic relations with similar entities and the creation of a roleplay game. This region is the core of all the Confederate Regions.
The choice of the creation of a confederation is because of the biggest possibilities of autonomy that it leaves to each member.
It is settled in modern times.

Confederate Regions are [region]Albanian union[/region], [region]Antarctic Territories[/region], [region]Etats Unis d Armorique[/region], [region]Free state of Wisconsin[/region], [region]New Agora[/region], [region]Paradisio[/region], [region]Republic of Italy[/region], [region]Yerfilag[/region], [region]The federal republic of Latvia[/region], [region]The Hellenic empire[/region]
