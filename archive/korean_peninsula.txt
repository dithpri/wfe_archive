The [b]Korean Peninsula[/b] is a peninsula in East Asia. It extends southwards for about 680 miles from continental Asia into the Pacific Ocean and is surrounded by the Sea of Japan (East Sea) to the east, and the Yellow Sea (West Sea) to the west, the Korea Strait connecting the first two bodies of water. &#151;Wikipedia[hr]

Korean Peninsula was originally founded May, 2003.
