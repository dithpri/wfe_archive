The [b]GDN[/b] (Global Defense Initiative) is a free Alliance of Civilizations of several otherwise isolated parallel Universes (Multiverses). Originally founded by [nation=short+noflag]Kommagene[/Nation] and [Nation=short+noflag]-Sakkara[/nation] more and more Civilizations of different Multiverses came into contact with the [b]GDN[/b] and established diplomatic and trade contacts and eventually became members of the [b]GDN[/b] Alliance .

The [b]GDN Alliance[/b] is holding some low profile contacts with the [region]SICON[/region] Alliance.
