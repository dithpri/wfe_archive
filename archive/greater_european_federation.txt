[b][color=navy]Welcome to the Greater European Federation[/color][/b]
[hr][color=navy]The Greater European Federation is a European ethnocultural and ethnonational federation of gentile nations that protect rule of law and responsible society within the framework of either a Republic or Constitutional Monarchy. [/color]

The GEF was founded to promote national and regional stability, cooperation, and security between each signatory member and bring them together in the pursuit of shared values.
