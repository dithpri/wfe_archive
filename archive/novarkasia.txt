Novarkasia, officially the Federated Socialist Continent of Novarkasia (Novarkasiya Ob&#146;yedinyonnaya Sotsialisticheskaya Kontinent), is a socialist continent in the Eastern Hemisphere, led by its capital nation, The United Socialist States of Rovskaya, and united under the Novarkasian Socialist Accord (NSA). Renowned for its commitment to equality, environmental stewardship, and progressive governance, Novarkasia features diverse landscapes, renewable energy-driven economies, and a rich cultural heritage. On the global stage, it champions peace, sustainability, and social justice while opposing capitalist expansion.
