With humble beginnings, the region of the Burning World Borderlands has seen enough strife, the nations of old leaving and allowing for new roots to grow, namely the Empire of Maianna, from the ashes of an old republic, and the new Kingdom of Fossifol. With technology and culture resembling that of the late 1940s and early 1950s, and a geography/climate resembling that of the Pacific Northwest, this will make for a surely interesting part of the world.
