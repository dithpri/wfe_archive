An alliance of fictional nations set in a world where Europe is 2x Larger due to both having a larger landmass and multiple new large Britain-like Islands in the Atlantic.
It is mostly Norse Pagan and heavily Third Positionist. The Alliance is similar to the European Union and NATO mixed together. The leader of it is The Empire of Everika, which is the Alliances only superpower. Its other major member is Doofstonia.

(For Roleplay: All nations must use the Rik as their currency. 5 Riks to every USD. Divide your population by 5)
