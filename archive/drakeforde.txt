Drakeforde was founded with one clear, unifying vision: to win NationStates by developing an indomitable political influence over all regions and nations.

Drakeforde is a region for those who take the game seriously and seek to come out on top through political (and, when necessary, military) activities. If you want to play NationStates without the distractions of role-playing, forming off-site relationships, and other such, Drakeforde is for you!

One day, Drakeforde will be a global force to be reckoned with!
