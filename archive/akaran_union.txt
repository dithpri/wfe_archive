Located somewhere within the Atlantic Ocean, the Akara Isles region is home to several medium-sized islands, currently administrated by [nation]Eblorya[/nation]. As a diplomatic effort to encourage peace and to increase its influence, Eblorya established the Akaran Union, promising to cede uninhabited land to any nation interested in joining.

The Akaran Union is mostly democratic, with a moderately strong emphasis on political freedom. Its main export is beef, contributed from the vast agricultural lands spanning the lands of Eblorya. The region is known for its lush forests and beautiful coastlines, in turn leading to a booming tourism industry. It graciously accepts any diplomatic relations, even those of opposing ideologies.
