[b]The Further[/b]
[color=#1B1B1B]The Further is a place for all people to come.
This is an OOC, anarchic region, based on the movie [b]INSIDIOUS[/b]. 
This is a friendly, eerie region for all of the lost and damned souls to resign.
The Further is a dark dimension that exists parallel to our world. It is inhabited by the tortured souls of the dead. While it is not Hell, it is hinted that it may lead there, as most of the souls that inhabit it appear malevolent and the Demon seems to rule it.
