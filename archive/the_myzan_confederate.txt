Among the world, the Myzan Confederation is a group of nations brought beneath the Selucan banner to work together and against hostile invaders. Named after the Myzan wolf of Seluca, the confederate is expected to provide aid and trade with other members and act in a pack to ensure their survival against other regions both old and new. Join us, no matter your species or beliefs, so long as you understand that we need only the brave and the willing in our effort to make a better world.
