The Universe, filled with millions of galaxies, and Empires! Kings, Emperors, Lords, all fighting over their territory in space, fighting for their flags, for their beliefs, for their ideas. But under one flag, a flag that symbolizes the unity of all Empires in the Galaxy. A symbol of Peace, Order, and Equality. However, not all Empires believe in this. 

There are multiple branches you may choose to join as listed below.

The EXPLORER Branch, mainly searchers that are scattered across the universe looking for new species.

The MILITARY Branch, main fighting force for the Federation, they conquer planets and systems in deep space. 

The COMMANDING Branch, makes the executive decisions and leads the Federation.
