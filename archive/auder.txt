[color=darkgreen][b]Welcome to Auder, the land of Kings and Gods[/b][/color]

Auder is a serious roleplaying region for NationStates that is set on the fictional continent of Auder, a medieval fantasy world where gods are real, magic is extremely rare, and the kingdoms of man fight among themselves. Auder is a roleplaying region situated in Past Tech, specifically in a universe that borrows aspects of fantasy and medieval settings.
