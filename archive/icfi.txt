[b][url=http://www.wsws.org/en/special/icfi.html]ICFI[/url] - International Committee of the [color=red]Fourth International[/color], the international political organization founded by Leon Trotsky in 1938 to wage the independent proletarian struggle against the Stalinist bureaucracy in the USSR and bourgeois governments in the capitalist countries.

The ICFI is the organisation that defended the founding principles of the Fourth International in a factional struggle with the Pabloites in 1953 (International Secretariat of the Fourth International) a group of revisionists who sought to liquidate the Fourth International and move into the corrupt Social Democrat and Stalinist Parties on the bankrupt theory of [i]entrism sui generis[/i].[/b]
