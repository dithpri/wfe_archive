Bolivia, officially known as the [b]Plurinational State of Bolivia[/b], is a landlocked country located in central South America. It is bordered by Brazil to the north and east, Paraguay and Argentina to the south, Chile to the southwest, and Peru to the west.

Capital: Sucre (constitutional)
Largest city: Santa Cruz de la Sierra
Government: Unitary presidential constitutional republic
President: Evo Morales &#151;Wikipedia[hr]
