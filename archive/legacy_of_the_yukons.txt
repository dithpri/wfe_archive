The Yukon Empire once dominated this region, but the empire that the Yukons built did not last. A cataclysmic event known as the Fall shattered the Yukon Empire and inaugurated an age known as the False Tribulation. When the dust finally settled a new cast of players found themselves at center stage.

This region is a Past Tech roleplaying region inspired by the novels [url=https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fitzpatrick%27s_War][i]Fitzpatrick&#39;s War[/i][/url], [url=https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Julian_Comstock:_A_Story_of_22nd-Century_America][i]Julian Comstock: A Story of 22nd-Century America[/i][/url], and [url=https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Peshawar_Lancers][i]The Peshawar Lancers[/i][/url].
