An assortment of firms and governments who set out upon a new land, with the intention of growing and prospering without restraint. Sovereignty and industry are the values that bind the region together here.

Charter:
1. Each nation in the Confederacy shall respect her CAP peers; in turn, her sovereignty shall be recognized and protected.
2. Firms have sovereignties just like governments; no company shall die at the hand of the Confederacy.
3. The CAP has a Trade And Intelligence Network, the CAPTAIN, which shall work to benefit the members of the CAP. CAPTAIN nations will give other CAPTAIN nations the best offers in terms of trade.

We are open to any nation wishing to rule of their own accord. Sovereignty and Confederacy, now and forever!
