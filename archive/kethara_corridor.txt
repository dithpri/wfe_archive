The [region]Kethara Corridor[/region] is a strategic tri&amp;#8209;border region where the forested [nation]The Iron Mandate Stratocracy[/nation], the industrial [nation]The Iron Compact of Kasei[/nation], and the militia&amp;#8209;ruled [nation]Ferrum March Militia Union[/nation] meet along the [b]River Kethar[/b]. [b]Fort Kethar[/b] acts as the central crossing and trade gate between western timber towns, eastern industrial cities, and southern mining settlements.

The [b]Verdant Reach[/b] defines the Stratocracy&#146;s wooded highlands, the [b]Kasani Belt[/b] anchors Kasei&#146;s manufacturing core, and the [b]Ferrum Basin[/b] supports the Militia Union&#146;s extraction economy.

The fractured terrain of the [b]Shattered Pass[/b] forms the natural divide and primary flashpoint between the three powers.
