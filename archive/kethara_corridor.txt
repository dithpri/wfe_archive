[region]Kethara Corridor[/region]  is where the Compact was forged and everyone here knows it. Dravec sits at the bottom of the Iron Spine gorge like a city that was cut from the rock on purpose &#151; cold, functional, and completely unbothered by your opinion of it. The mines have been running since before the Charter. Every rifle in the Compact started as ore pulled out of this ground.

This is not a region you visit to unwind. The garrison is Class I, the population is built different, and the infrastructure exists to move soldiers and iron, not tourists. Allied states come here to be reminded what the Compact is actually made of &#151; and they leave quieter than they arrived.

The Corridor doesn&#39;t perform strength. It just has it.
