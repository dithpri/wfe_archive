The [region]Kethara Corridor[/region] is a divided region split between the forested [nation]The Iron Mandate Stratocracy[/nation] in the west and the urbanized [nation]The Iron Compact of Kasei[/nation] in the east. The two powers meet at the [b]River Kethar[/b], with [b]Fort Kethar[/b] serving as the only fortified crossing.

The west is dominated by dense woodland, rising into the Verdant Reach, where towns like Woodhaven and [b]Greenhold[/b] sit among high ridges and misted valleys. The east forms a continuous industrial megaregion, anchored by [b]Kasei Prime[/b], [b]Pyraxia[/b], and [b]Mezatran[/b], culminating in the [b]Kasani Metropolis[/b].

To the south, the [b]Shattered Pass[/b] marks the Corridor&#146;s fractured geological boundary &#151; a natural barrier and historical battleground.
