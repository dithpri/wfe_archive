The [b]Perscenian Armed Forces[/b] are the military forces of the Commonwealth of Perscenia. Divided into four branches&#151;the Army, the Navy, the Air Force, and the Marine Corps, the Perscenian Armed Forces operate under the Ministry of Defence, with its main building in London. The monarch serves as the Head of the Armed Forces, while the Prime Minister makes key decisions on the use of the armed forces. It is also referred to as [b]His Majesty&#39;s Armed Forces[/b] or the [b]Imperisian Armed Forces.[/b] ([url=https://www.nationstates.net/nation=perscenia/detail=factbook/id=2761887][i][u][b]Full article[/i][/u][/b][/url]...)
