[b][color=black]Welcome to [/color][color=red]The Glorious Confederation of Nations,[/color] where all ideologies, no matter how dangerous or awesome, will be treated equally.[/b]

[color=royalblue]We are an open and friendly region with our nations based in The Clerias Continent, where trade, politics, discourse and war takes place.
The Glorious Community is a local organisation aimed at bringing peace to every nation. Every nation, as based on our beliefs, have the right to impose any kind of governance to their people. Sanctions will only be necessary when a majority is in favor of it. Our roleplays take place in present day for maximum realism.[/color]
