Far in the North of Cavendar lies a cold wasteland. However, this land is inhabited by people of various nations, most notably and dominant in the region is the Empire of Isvataan. The Empire founded this region within Cavendar as a indication of their influence and control of the other nations nearby. Still, the Empire of Isvataan is an ally to their southern neighbors and will fight to the end to ensure the region and the empire survives.
