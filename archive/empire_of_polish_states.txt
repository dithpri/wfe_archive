The Cesarz of the Empire of Polish States (Emperor) leads this commonwealth of nations which consists of the Croatians, Serbs, Austro-Hungarians, Romanians, and of course the Bulgarians.

The other states have monarchs who rule under the authority of the Polish Emperor.

This is an empire that stretches from the Baltic Sea bordering with Finland to the western borders of Germany, the Ural mountains to the East and the borders of Greece and Turkey in the south.

 The peoples not listed above were directly absorbed by one of the member nations. They are treated fairly. In some cases.
