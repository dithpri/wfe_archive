[b]DUNR - Daveist Union of Nations &amp; Republics[/b]
[i]A subsidary of the World Daveist Organisation.[/i]

A permanent participatory union of nations in governance, military and economy, sharing the common religious and ethic identity of Daveism.

The [nation]new dahrhein[/nation] is the semi-permanent resident nation (by definition also, Exec. Authority).
The resident nation is elected by a 5 yearly democratic referendum which requires 80% vote by the general membership.

As a union which serves and focuses on domestic and regional interests rather than global issues, the union does not send a delegate to the World Assembly.
