Wellingtonia is a not like other regions, which act as cooperatives, factions or alliances. This region is one of internal turmoil. Diplomacy, geopolitics and sometimes warfare ravage the lands. Wellingtonia has a map which is manually updated after major events like battles or agreements, and all members are active both on Nationstates and discord.

If you join and show participation you WILL be added to the map.
