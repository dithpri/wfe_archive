The region of Reganos is a place torn apart by centuries of conflict. Small nations that have long been at war with one another have been brought to heel by the mighty nation of Kul Tiras. But, with Kul Tiras planning on leaving Reganos to pursue a life among the stars, it appears as if the world may once again plummet into an era of war as each nation attempts to fill the power vacuum left by the previous, most potent military power in the land.
