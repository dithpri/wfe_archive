The Grand Lodge of Hiram Abiff, named for the first Master Mason, was created as a home for fellow travelers and those interested in the craft of making good men better.  A place for those who seek knowledge in the arts and sciences and who thirst to share the light.

In keeping with the grand traditions of Freemasonry, recruitment is forbidden. 2B1ASK1

Founded by Hans Selstur, Great Architect of the Union of Selstadia.
