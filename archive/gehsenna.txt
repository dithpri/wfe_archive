[b]G E H S E N N A[/b]
[sub][i]Gehsenna, &#39;their heaven&#39;[/i] &#151; A star system in the Wodu Saka Galaxy and home to the Jenhan planetary system and homeworld, situated between the Huruwuk Nebula and the Resanih Star System, the latter being home to the Human planetary system and homeworld. Gehsenna contains 310 stars, 28 wormholes, and 5 sapient species, and is dominated by the Realm of Jenha and mostly populated by Jenhans.[/sub]
