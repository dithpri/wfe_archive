[b]G E H S E N N A[/b]
[sub]&#151; Second-smallest continent on Ekosia, home to the Union of Gehsenni Nations and 41 sovereign states. Bordered by the Wollon and Baric Oceans, the Middle Sea, and the Padnarian Mountains, the lattermost considered as its boundary with Tyongsen, the largest continent on Ekosia. One-tenth of the world&#39;s population at 500 million. One-sixth of the global economy. One-third forested.[/sub]
