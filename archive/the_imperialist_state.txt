[b][i]Information:[/i][/b]

The Imperialist State is a fictional nation that was formed after World War One uniting the countries of Italy, Austria, Hungary, North Africa, and Spain all into one Empire. 

[b][i]Era:[/i][/b]

We are set in the aftermath of World War One towards the beginning of World War Two
