Eltanin, also designated as &amp;#947; Draconis (gamma Draconis), is a multiple giant star of spectral class K5 in the constellation of Draco. Eltanin visual magnitude is 2.23, making it the 66th brightest star in the sky. Because of its brightness, Eltanin is clearly visible to the naked eye when observed from locations with dark skies, and should be also quite easily visible from light polluted areas.
