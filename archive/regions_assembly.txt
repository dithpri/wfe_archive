[i]Welcome Regions, to the Assembly of all sovereign Regions! We have the World Assembly which is been overburden in History. The Regions Assembly shall be a center-focused Interregional Organisation which shall coordinate and harmonize the Regions interregional dealings and diplomacy. The goals and mission of this organisation is to bring some order in the anarchy which exist in the World Assembly. Regions are the center-actor of diplomacy and recognising each other in these diplomatic interactions shall lead to a stable and organised World Order.
