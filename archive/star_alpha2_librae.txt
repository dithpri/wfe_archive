Zubenelgenubi, also designated as &amp;#945;2 Librae (alpha2 Librae), is a variable and multiple sub-giant star of spectral class A3 in the constellation of Libra. Zubenelgenubi visual magnitude is 2.75. Because of its brightness, Zubenelgenubi is clearly visible to the naked eye when observed from locations with dark skies, and should be also quite easily visible from light polluted areas.
