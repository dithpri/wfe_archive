The Commission is the governing body of the American Mafia, formed in 1931. The Commission replaced the &quot;Boss of all Bosses&quot; title with a ruling committee consisting of the New York Five Families bosses and the bosses of the Chicago Outfit and the Buffalo crime family. - Wikipedia
