Along the sparse northern arm of the Sethian Galaxy, the Forgotten Rift, an ancient sector of long-settled planets, sits waiting.  Waiting for something to contact them.

Ages gone, the Sybese Oasis launched a probe out toward the center of the massive galaxy.  Ever since, the sector has gradually decomposed as the resources have run out, providing a bare subsistence for the planets.

Barbarism, sickness, and want rule this region.  Still, they wait...............
