In an age of formation, the land now known as The Many Lands emerged as a diverse and untamed expanse, featuring lush forests, fertile valleys, harsh deserts, and towering mountains. Transformed by The Great Awakening, it revealed hidden treasures and unexplored regions, attracting ambitious leaders seeking to shape their own destinies. This realm offers endless possibilities for creating empires, alliances, and power structures, allowing for a dynamic and evolving landscape.
