The name &quot;Resonara&quot; is derived from the word &quot;resonance,&quot; which refers to the prolongation of sound or the reinforcement of sound waves. This region was influenced by the actions of the celestial Gods Bingus and Floppa eons ago, the name suggests a lingering and pervasive effect. The resonance metaphorically implies that the actions of these Gods continue to have a lasting impact on the planet, even in their absence.
