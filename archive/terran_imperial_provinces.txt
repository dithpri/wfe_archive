The Holy Terran Empire([region]Terran Imperial Provinces[/region]) is a large empire that is composed of provinces that have their own laws and rules with all of them having the same currency called the Terran Asi.
All provinces are required to change their currency units to Terran Asi within 3 days or face ejection from the Holy Terran Empire([region]Terran Imperial Provinces[/region]).
The following Regions have been conquered and refounded by the Holy Terran Empire([region]Terran Imperial Provinces[/region]): [region]Newtranlita[/region],[region]Frozentala[/region],and [region]Jupiter Legion[/region].
