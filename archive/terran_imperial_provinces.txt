The [color=red]Holy Terran Empire[/color]([region]Terran Imperial Provinces[/region]) is a large empire that is composed of provinces that have their own laws and rules with all of them having the same currency called the [color=red]Terran Asi[/color].
All provinces are required to change their currency units to [color=red]Terran Asi[/color] within 3 days or face ejection from the [color=red]Holy Terran Empire[/color]([region]Terran Imperial Provinces[/region]).
The following Regions have been conquered and refounded by the [color=red]Holy Terran Empire[/color]([region]Terran Imperial Provinces[/region]): [region]Newtranlita[/region],[region]Frozentala[/region],[region]Jupiter Legion[/region],[region]Juntra Meta[/region],[region]Bloc Dur Nations Unise[/region],and [region]The Grand Union Of Democratic States[/region].
