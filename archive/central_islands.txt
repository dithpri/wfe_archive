The Central Islands are a strategically vital archipelago located at the crossroads of major transoceanic trade routes. Known for their lush biodiversity, advanced civic infrastructure, and high standard of living, the islands are a hub for progressive governance and technological innovation. This region is composed of multiple semi-autonomous island nations, with The United Vanguard Union serving as its political and economic heart. Embracing liberal republican values, the Union champions participatory democracy, civil liberties, and environmental sustainability. From green energy megaprojects to digital democracies, the Central Islands stand as a model for peaceful cooperation and bold progress in a multipolar world.
