Welcome to the Region of Schakastan.

Schakastan was founded in AD 2012 by the fallen nation of Messianaria, and is now under the control of [nation]The Black Dominion[/nation]. All nations within Schakastan are required to have appropriate names and flags and must abide by the laws and decrees of the WA delegate.
