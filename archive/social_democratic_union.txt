The Social Democratic Union is an alliance of states that cherish the values of the Left-Wing movement thus espousing Socialism. Member nations strive to uphold and protect the rights of workers, create a social welfare state, strive to promote public education and create a harmonious socialist state in conjunction with Democratic principles.
