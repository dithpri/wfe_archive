Aquamarrinia is a fledgling, genial nation, notable for its irreverence towards religion. The hard-working, democratic population of 5 million Aquamarrinians have some civil rights, but not too many, enjoy the freedom to spend their money however they like, to a point, and take part in free and open elections, although not too often.

The relatively small government prioritizes Welfare, with Education, Defense, and Law &amp; Order also on the agenda, while Spirituality isn&#39;t funded at all. The average income tax rate is 14.6%.
