The Interstellar-Planetary Authority, or ISPA is a region ostensibly ruled by a delegation of representatives from the member planets, but actually controlled by the mandates, a highly technologically advanced group of authoritarian, genocidal, oppressive overlords. Planets on the edges can often get away with things, but outright rebellion will result in being crushed. Most planets that haven&#39;t been completely crushed have thriving black markets. 

Most technology is a combination of typical sci-fi tech and a kind of magic called &quot;warding.&quot;
