The Office of National Relations adheres to the principles of regional peace, tranquility, sovereignty and security. We at the Office of National Relations will help countries in need. We will assist in terms of military assets, financial assistance, humanitarian assistance and others according to the needs of the applying country. However, all aid will be withdrawn and blacklisted if the requesting country uses the aid provided for the following purposes:

-Invasion 
-War
-Murder
-Improper coup
-All actions that cause harm or danger to the security of the country or territory or innocent citizens
