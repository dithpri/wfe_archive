A region that, as a newbie, I created specifically for people who love the concepts of Republicanism and Democracy, and wish to be with likeminded people who support the same. As such, this region in inherently anti-authoritarian, with both Communism and Fascism looked upon with absolute scorn. However, so long as your people are free, you will not be punished for your ideas or speech, as the freedom to speak your mind is a fundamental right that must never be infringed, no matter how awful the ideas.
