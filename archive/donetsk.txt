[b]Donetsk[/b] is an industrial city in Ukraine on the Kalmius River. The population was estimated at 929,063&amp;#8201;(2016 est.) in the city, and over 2,000,000 in the metropolitan area (2011). According to the 2001 Ukrainian Census, Donetsk was the fifth-largest city in Ukraine. Since April 2014, the city has been controlled by pro-Russian separatists from the self-proclaimed Donetsk People&#39;s Republic.
