The continent of Mythadiea is a mystical, unexplored land. Barely touched by humans, the native races have had a hard time adjusting. With racial skirmishes and land wars aplenty, history has been far from peaceful.
Will humans make peace with the elves? And what of the reclusive dwarves? Only time will tell as the human foothold flourishes and mankind learns more about this mysterious land.
