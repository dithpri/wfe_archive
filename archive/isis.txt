The Islamic State of Iraq and Syria (ISIS) is a radical Sunni jihadist group that seeks to establish a global caliphate under a strict Sharia law.
The group persists as a decentralised global network and is internationally designated as a terrorist group due to its history of mass casualties, human rights abuses, and genocidal campaigns against religious minorities.
