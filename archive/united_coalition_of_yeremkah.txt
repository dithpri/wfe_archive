The United Coalition of Yeremkah was founded in Year 1 YD (Yeremkah Dating), in the year 2014 CE/AD. Its aims are cultural exchange, a pursuit of common brotherhood and the principles of freedom, compassion and justice for all peoples. It is a coalition of nations that meets in council once a month for mutual discussion of the most beneficial possible futures. Nations are free to have their own legislation, and the Coalition is primarily a guiding hand.
