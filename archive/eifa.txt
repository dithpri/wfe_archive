[b]EIFA - Eastern Integration for Foreign Affairs[/b]

The EIFA is a region of geographically close nations, which decided to join their forces and build a strong communicative system.

Our cardinal points shall be to avoid war, and to be able to compete with further away nations, which started to threaten us because of the advancement of the means of transports.
However incompatible our ideologies may seem to be, our nations managed to put our differences aside to build a strong coalition.

The founding countries are Antaca, Hasponie, and Lybe.
