[region]Kazimierz[/region], formally known as the United Knightly Territory of Kazimierz or the [nation]Kazimierz Alliance[/nation] for short, is a Terran country bordering [nation]Terran Columbia[/nation], [nation]Leithanian Empire[/nation], [nation]The Samifjod[/nation], [nation]Ursus Empire[/nation], and [nation]Terran Victoria[/nation] that occupies much of the northern grasslands.
It is a confederation of feudal chivalric orders with the Grand Knight serving as the head of state. The majority of Kazimierzians are of the Kuranta race, which is reflected on its national emblem resembling a knight piece.
