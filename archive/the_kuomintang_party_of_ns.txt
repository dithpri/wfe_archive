[color=Blue][b][i]The Kuomintang, officially the Kuomintang of China, sometimes romanized as Guomindang (GMD) via the Pinyin transcription system, and translated as the Chinese Nationalist Party, was one of the dominant parties of the early Republic of China, from 1912 onwards, and remains one of the main political parties in modern Taiwan. Its guiding ideology is the Three Principles of the People, advocated by Sun Yat-sen.  It is currently the ruling party in Taiwan, and holds most seats in the Legislative Yuan.  Current president Ma Ying-jeou, elected in 2008 and re-elected in 2012, is the seventh KMT member to hold the office of the presidency.[/i][/b][/color]
