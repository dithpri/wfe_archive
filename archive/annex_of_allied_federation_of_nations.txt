The Annex of the Allied Federation of Nations is a client region of the [url=region=allied_federation_of_nations]Allied Federation of Nations[/url], with its primary purpose being a place for AFN members to store their puppets.

The Annex of the Allied Federation of Nations is a member of the [url=region=the_nsidc_headquarters]NSIDC[/url].
