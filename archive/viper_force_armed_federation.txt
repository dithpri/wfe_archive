[region]Viper Force Armed Federation[/region] Political &amp; Millitary command protocol.
Viper Force CC (aka Central Command,) is the section of Viper Force focused on industrial and economic growth. Central Command has actual civilian population. Central Command is based around keeping Force X, Y, And Z, all as one unit.

The command protocol for Force Z is land, while X is focused on air, while Y is focused on the sea.
