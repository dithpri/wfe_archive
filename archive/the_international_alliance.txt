[b][color=red]THE INTERNATIONAL ALLIANCE[/color][/b]

November 2015 - May 2016

At the end of my journey I can see through time, my eyes blinded with tears. I see the star of our hopes, the emperor, who once brought peace and prosperity to this house, who built a powerful empire and united nations, from all walks of life. I see an epoch of overall happiness and now that all lies in ruin.
The old gods had arisen, brothers picked up arms against brothers, mankind clung to each others throats, ruled by a state of anarchy and mother nature took her revenge by showering plague and famine upon us. Evil is walking in this world, leaving nothing but dead cities and buried settlements behind, the land is lifeless and barren. A once proud region, faded into obscurity...
