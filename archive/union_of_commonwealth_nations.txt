[b][color=green]Union of Commonwealth Nations[/color][/b]

On September 17, 2013, the Union of Commonwealth Nations was liberated by the combined forces of Belgieria and Tinshein from the hands of a small raiding region known as [b][region]The Ring of Fire[/region][/b]. After achieving independence, it became a part of the Trade Federation until the region ceased to exist in the spring of 2014.

Refounded by the [region]Universal Trade Federation[/region].
