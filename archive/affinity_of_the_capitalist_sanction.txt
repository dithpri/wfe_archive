The Affinity of the Capitalist Sanction is a region dedicated to the rise of the free market and capitalism. It also supports liberty and small government. The Capitalist Sanction region is an extremely large island chain.

Regional features:
1. Harbitros Vote Override
 -Allows the Confederacy of Harbitros to override the votes of a bill to either pass or veto it
 -Needs four nations to agree with override before it takes motion
2. Trade Security
 -Allows nations to freely trade soldiers with one another
 -Allows nations to freely trade military utilities and machinery
 -Allows nations to buy/sell uranium and other radioactive minerals with one another
