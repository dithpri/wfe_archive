[b]Kirghizia[/b], modernly known as [b]Kyrgyzstan[/b], is a sovereign state in Central Asia. Kirghizia is a landlocked country with mountainous terrain. It is bordered by Kazakhstan to the north, Uzbekistan to the west and southwest, Tajikistan to the southwest and China to the east.
