This is the headquarters and main shipyard of the [b]Imperial Japanese Navy[/b] (&amp;#22823;&amp;#26085;&amp;#26412;&amp;#24093;&amp;#22269;&amp;#28023;&amp;#36557;). Authorised personnel only.
