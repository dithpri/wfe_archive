The Claddarian Domain is a region of the world dominated by the nation of Doronax. The Claddarian Domain was originally founded with the intention of establishing an isolated region of happy, prosperous nations, nations with priorities centered on the happiness of citizens, the education of the masses, and the mutual support and protection between them and and the other nations of the Domain. The Claddarian Domain is open to all nations with the welfare of their people at heart.
