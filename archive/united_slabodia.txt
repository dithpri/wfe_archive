Formed from the union of the remnants of Greater Slabodia after the Great Collapse with the islands of Lesser Slabodia, Slabodia rises from the ashes united!

United Slabodia is a region of nations bound by a common reverence for the life and teachings of the great prophet Dyllon Slaboda.
