[region]The Madawaskan Coalition[/region] stands firmly on the belief that everybody has a right to be represented, and everybody has a right to lead a life that fulfills them. We believe criminals should be rehabilitated, and everybody should love each other. Our Coalition currency is the [b]qotak[/b] (&#39;kot&amp;#592;k), based on the Peskotomuhkati-Wolastoqey word for dollar, which is [b]&#39;qotakisu[/b] (&#39;ko&amp;#601;t&amp;#592;&#39;giz&amp;#629;).
