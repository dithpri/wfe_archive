[region]Hoggynesian Union[/region] (HU) is a political and economic union. After the dissolution of the [region]Hoggynesian Peninsula[/region] And the [region]North Hoggynesian Peninsula[/region] , The HU united both regions and developed a single internal market through a single system of laws that applies to all Member States, in which members have agreed to act as one. The HU policy aims to ensure the free movement of people, goods, services and capital in the internal market, to enact laws in the field of justice and home affairs and to maintain a common policy on trade, agriculture, fisheries and regional development. Passport controls were abolished for travel within the Hoggynesian Free Movement Area.
