[region]Hoggynesian Union[/region] (HU) | Hoggynesische Unie | Union hoggyn&eacute;sienne | Hoggynesische Union is a federation of Hoggynesian States. Established in 2017 by [nation]socialist_republic_hoggynesia[/nation] to unite all Hoggynesian States in a single union. wich includes : Free movement of people, goods. and a single postal service : Hoggynesian Union Post (HPU) and HogRail. a combination of the rail services in the HSR, NHDPR, HFSI, SHFSR. (Official Languages of the Union are: Dutch, French, German)
