The Region of Dark Arts, Sinistry and Evil Goings On is a land full of necromancers, evil sorcerers, witches, villains and all creatures dangerous and dark. Founded by the Land Of Necromancy, Necromajica, and filled to the brim with ghastly creatures and heartless villains, The Region Of Dark Arts, Sinistry and Evil Goings On (DASEGO) is a both mystifying and terrifying place to be. Woe unto those who do not belong...
