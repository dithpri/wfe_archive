The Confederation of Democratic States is a political, economical and military confederation of states with the noble goal of promoting and developing: democracy, personal freedom, personal safety, economic stability and peace. We as a confederation will try to be as neutral as possible in any conflict.

States part of the Confederation of Democratic States have to be in compliance with the Articles of Confederation, the Constitution of Confederation, and all the laws of the Confederation.
The Articles of Confederation, the Constitution of Confederation and all confederate laws are published [url=http://www.nationstates.net/nation=balzaria/detail=factbook]here[/url].

The Capital State is [nation]Balzaria[/nation] and the Governing State currently is [nation]Tysoania[/nation].
