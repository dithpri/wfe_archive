[hr][list][color=green][b]Union of South Africa[/b][/list][hr][list]History:
Predecessor to the present-day Republic of South Africa.
Came into being 1910 May 31 with unification of Cape Colony, Natal Colony, the Transvaal and the Orange River Colony.
It included territories that were formerly part of South African Republic and the Orange Free State.
Became a Republic 1961 May 31 ([region=Republic of South Africa])
