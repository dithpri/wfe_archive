 A friendly, Near-Future RP region I created, telegram me for password/invite

   It all began two centuries ago with the dreaded &quot;War of Progress&quot;, all that is left of this dreaded war is a barren rock that was once named Earth. As oil reserves began to dwindle, tensions between nations rose drastically and thus a new space initiative began in hopes of colonizing another planet. Tensions eventually reached their climax and war was declared, the space vessels desperately fleeing the coming conflict. After many years they finally arrived upon Theta Solaria, and thus a new flag was created for a new age of peace and prosperity. Whether this peace will last has yet to be seen however...
