The region of Jatian was founded by the rulers of Seawisp and Britaly in 500B.C. It is one of the worlds oldest regions and has grown both technologically as well as environmentally, however, all this has come at a great cost with many wars and disputes. One of the greatest and well-known wars was the War for Cybrisk. In 2001 the leading party of Britaly declared war on seawisp due to a massive oil deposit that was discovered on the border, Seawisp wished to share the oil as a 50/50 split but due to the greed of the people of Britaly, a war broke out leading to Seawisps victory... So will you join this region and forge your own legend.
