The Official Warden World Summer Side RP, set in the world of Kaiserreich, a world in which the Central Powers won the First World War and socialist revolutions took over Britain and France. Now, in 1936, the stage is set for another global conflict between the Entente, the Internationale, and Mitteleuropa.
