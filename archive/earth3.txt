Third Earth or more commonly known as Earth3, is an alternate earth full of empires. The Empires of this world are ones that never fully fell, or alternate empires. Anattolia=Ottoman Empire, Souther States=Confederate States of America, Holy Kingdom of Prussia=Prussia(stayed independent), Tiwanakuu=Incan Empire, Uxmaal=Mayan Empire, Southern East Asia=Sukhothai Kingdom, Austro-Italia=Austro-Hungaria Empire, Serbia-Bulgaria, Nazi Occupied Scandinavia, Crimean Khanatte, Coresica(never fell to France), Okinawaa(stayed independent from mainland japan, eventually conquering it), Maghrreb(Alternate Morocco).
