The Aramythian Expansive Territory region is a region dominated by Aramythia and its colonies. The region&amp;#39;s borders change ever so often. However, Aramythian Expansive Territory is very isolated, and its only influence is itself and the nations on the mainland, as Aramythia is connected to these countries on a large peninsula, blocked off by a wide mountain range.

Superpower: Aramythia
Colonies: Aramythia City, North New Aramythia, Equatorial Aramythia, Aramythian Island Chain
Ally: LePrance, Sorakamonarorock (not a typo)
