
Landmass Information:
The Imperial Region Of Krazania is a small, advanced region renowned for its surplus of resources such as oil and metal. The Region&#39;s main land type is flatland (64%) mixed in with forests (33%) and marshland (3%). The large amount of flatland allows for easy construction of cities and towns, along with little to no problems with road construction. Only one small mountain range exist in the region, it being known as the Apollo Mountain Range.
