A world where the Umayyads won the 718 Siege of Constantinople. As a result, the caliphates dominate the Mediterranean and Islam expands to Italy, Anatolia, and the Balkans. The Pope flees to France and then England, and the Holy Roman Empire stands firm and united under a single dynasty. Without the Byzantine Empire, Russia converts to Catholicism and joins France and the Holy Roman Empire in crusades across Southern Europe. Encountering eastern goods in the Islamic world, the West begins to lust after silks and spices and tries to find a new trade route to India and the Far East; discovering the Americas in the 1200s.
