Rabotnichesko delo&nbsp;(Bulgarian:&nbsp;&amp;#1056;&amp;#1072;&amp;#1073;&amp;#1086;&amp;#1090;&amp;#1085;&amp;#1080;&amp;#1095;&amp;#1077;&amp;#1089;&amp;#1082;&amp;#1086; &amp;#1076;&amp;#1077;&amp;#1083;&amp;#1086;, &quot;Worker&#39;s Deed&quot;) was a&nbsp;Bulgarian&nbsp;daily newspaper that was the media organ of the&nbsp;Bulgarian Communist Party&#39;s Central Committee&nbsp;and was one of the&nbsp;People&#39;s Republic of Bulgaria&#39;s highest-circulation newspapers. The newspaper was established in 1927 and was issued from&nbsp;Sofia.&nbsp;The first newspaper was released on 5 March 1927. The paper was renamed to&nbsp;Duma&nbsp;(&quot;Word&quot;) in 1990.&nbsp;Duma, despite some financial troubles, is still issued today.
