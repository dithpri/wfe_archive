Titan is the seat of power for the Triumvirate of Yut, a loose confederation of Nations based in the Saturnian System and working for the common good.  The terraformed Titan is covered in shallow oceans, and greenery adapted for its thin atmosphere.  Breathing is possible on the surface after many years of atmosphere development.  Nations wishing to apply to the TofY should read the proceedures here: http://www.nationstates.net/forum/viewtopic.php?t=59327&amp;highlight=application
