CISA, also called as Colonial Independence Supporters Association is a political advocacy region focused on promoting decolonization, sovereignty, and cultural preservation. We unite nations and individuals who believe in self-determination and the right to independence for all peoples under colonial rule. 

[b][i]Why Join CISA?[/i][/b]
- Support Independence: Advocate for the liberation of colonized territories.
- Active Diplomacy: Engage in debates, resolutions, and international policy-making.
- Cultural Advocacy: Help preserve cultures historically suppressed by colonialism.
- Global Community: Connect with like-minded activists and nations.
- Be Part of Change: Use CISA&#146;s platform to make an impact on global decolonization.
