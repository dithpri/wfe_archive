[b]The Backstory Thus Far[/b]

There was once an ancient race who had taken the vastness of space and were pushing the limits and were at the height of their power none could stand against them for those that did fell to their weapons. However as all things do the ancient ones fell. From their ashes, the enslaved races rose to prominence in the Shashamri Galaxy. Will they stand the test of time or fall into obscurity?
