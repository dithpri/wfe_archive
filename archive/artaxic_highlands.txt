This small region, which lies beyond a vast frozen tundra and a dense pine forest, is home to the hardworking Artaxians who have retreated into the highlands over 800 years ago.

Their enterprising nature made them a strong industrial force during the last century and they are now emerging as a modern nation, in which power is valued above freedom.

Surely these strong people will rise up to the challenges ahead of them while staying true to their customs and beliefs. Who knows someday we will all be speaking Artaxian and saluting the Artaxan flag...
