A continent discovered to the west of the islands of Exodoria. Here is a land under the protection of his Serene Majesty, Reichskaiser Gunter Christoph Stolzing. Here burgeoning and strong nations alike can share their wisdom and extend their influence--when they swear fealty to the Reichskaiser and the Commonwealth of the Reichsward. All within maintain their autonomy, but are called to defend their Reich and their home as the Reichskaiser would expect of them.
