The North Western Coalition (NWC) is an organization to protect and oversee certain nations and serve as 
a way for small developing nations to unite to organize and become stronger.

[b]Current Chair:[/b] Polar Iceberg of [nation]Pengueen[/nation]
The chair is an elected office to oversee the coalition itself in both military and domestic issues, the election is held on occasions where an at least 25% of member nations feel dissatisfied. The officers are selected by the chair.

[b]Senate[/b]
The Senate is unique to the NWC. Members can telgram proposals to the Chair, and the Chair will approve or veto, and if no veto, the proposal goes to a poll posted and written (sometimes edited) by the speaker of the Senate.
