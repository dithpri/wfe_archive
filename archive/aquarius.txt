Lush tropical rainforests cover much of the region of Aquarius.
Golden beaches dotted by grand hotels make it a desired tourist destination.  Each hotel has over 1,000 feet of beach frontage and every room is required to have a scenic view.
A diverse workforce make it easy for new economic development in the manufacturing and agricultural sectors.

The region of Aquarius is under the control of the all mighty Thurb.
All Hail Thurb.
