Araliya is new MT RPing region created for RPing in realistic way. We are seeking for good players who are interested in joining us, to RP in smaller yet better with quality.

Araliya  is a continent inhabited by different nations, with different ideas about governance and different cultures. Here blood lusting dictatorships border peaceful democracies, monarchies border republics. In other words, everybody is welcome! 

Please note that ALL nations in Araliya have to set an realistic RPing population. Military realism is also appreciated thing.
