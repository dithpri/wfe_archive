Alpdevsca is a region containing the continents of Marossan, Valcania, Rabarrieh, Chalcran and Ughan as well as many islands in the Poseid&oacute;nic Ocean, including the Cranaeian Isles. 


This is primarily a region for those interested in modern tech, relatively realistic type role-plays, both diplomatic and character based. The region also uses past tech for roleplays set in time before the current date, like the 1800s for example.
