[B][Color=brown][Region]The Royal House of Waldora [/region] is a constitutional monarchy. This means that the King is formally the head of state but that his duties are mainly representative and ceremonial. The legislative and executive powers lie with the country&#146;s elected bodies. As head of state, it is the King&#39;s task to unify, represent and encourage the people.[/color][/b]

In all these roles The Sovereign is supported by members of their immediate family.
