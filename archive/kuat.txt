Kuat was a vital shipbuilding and industrial planet located in the Kuat system of the galaxy&#39;s Core Worlds region and homeworld of the Kuati people. Known for the prestigious Kuat Drive Yards corporation and its large shipyards, the planet notably produced warships for both the Galactic Republic and Galactic Empire.
