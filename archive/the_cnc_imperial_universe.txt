Alternate C&amp;amp;C Universe where the Brotherhood of Nod defeated the Global Defense Initiative and forced them to flee Earth and colonize a distant star system. Kane has finally ascended and proclaimed Neil the First Emperor of Nod and Humanity.

The Brotherhood is now called the Technocratic Empire of Nod and a new Era has begun for Mankind, both factions have started colonizing and exploring new planets. In the outer edge of the Galaxy, the Galactic Defense Initiative is preparing to take revenge and bring back Democracy to Earth.

The Galaxy is divided in 5 Major Alliances : The Technocratic Alliance, the Freedom Alliance, the Utopian Union, the Shaktur Axis and the Karatur Unity Pact.
