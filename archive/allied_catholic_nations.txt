A region for Catholic nations who resign international foreign policy to be ultimately under Papal verdict. And as such, in joining the region, are agreeing to a general pact of non-aggression, and mutual cooperation between other member Catholic nations.
If a nation would like to join the region of Allied Catholic Nations, just send a membership application to Weitenland, and we will gladly give you the password to join after a quick review to ensure the requesting nation shows signs of being Catholic.
