[list]The [b]Global Federation of Nations (GFN)[/b] is an intergovernmental organization uniting member states worldwide to advance international cooperation, peace, and sustainable development. Operating on principles of sovereignty and mutual respect, the GFN provides a crucial forum for diplomatic dialogue and collective action on global challenges, including peace and security, economic stability, human rights, environmental protection, humanitarian aid, and the strengthening of international law. Through its collaborative efforts, the GFN strives to build a more peaceful, prosperous, and just world for all.[/list]
