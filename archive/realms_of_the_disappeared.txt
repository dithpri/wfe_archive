Some nations care nothing for their citizens and allow them to think, speak, write, and sometimes even [b][I]ACT[/I][/b] according to their own petty, misguided desires. More enlightened lands, such as our glorious Founder Nation of Mercanta, realize that it is the first duty of every State to protect its people from themselves. Ironically, the more we love and protect our people, the more of them disappear for unknown reasons. If your lovingly regimented citizens are disappearing despite your efforts to guide them towards correct thinking and behavior, then join us in our quest to find the answer to this great mystery.
