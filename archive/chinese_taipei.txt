[hr]Chinese Taipei is the designated name for Taiwan. International organizations commonly employ the term &quot;Chinese Taipei&quot; upon the insistence of [b][region]the Peoples Republic of China[/region][/b] (PRC), commonly known as China, as it refuses to recognize the status of the &quot;Republic of China&quot;.
