Chinese Taipei is the designated name used by the Republic of China (Taiwan), to participate in some international organizations. International organizations commonly employ the term &amp;quot;Chinese Taipei&amp;quot; upon the insistence of the  People&amp;#39;s Republic of China (PRC), commonly known as China, as it refuses to recognize the status of the Republic of China . The Republic of China has always been a sovereign state legally representing Taiwan and the territory of China upon its establishment in 1911. Herein explained, the Republic of China as such would like to establish mutual diplomatic with her international community based on respect, dignity, and democracy.
