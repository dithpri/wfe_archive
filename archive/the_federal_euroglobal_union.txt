The union is a collective of island and coastal and continental states located in the greater Europe region, stretching from the Atlantic to the pacific. all member states have equal power and control with a federal system similar to the USA and Australia, but with echoes of the rule of the British empire. the union was founded at the end of the 3rd world war in September 2034 in an effort to unify like mined nations in a EU like superstate. by the summer of 2036 the union had 25 members and it was still growing.
