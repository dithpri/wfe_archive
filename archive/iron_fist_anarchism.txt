Iron Fist Anarchism is a region ruled by the Federation of Gamcom, known for also being an anarchist-communism region, Iron Fist Anarchism is ruled with an iron fist of Gamcom with some strict rules, but citizens also have the freedom to do whatever they please unless it breaks the laws, This region also has an absence of drug laws, Iron Fist Anarchism is also home to the Dictatorship of Strict Government and Empire of Enkyo, with a population of 72 million

Iron Fist Anarchism is still a small but highly populated region, looking to get new colonies to join it
