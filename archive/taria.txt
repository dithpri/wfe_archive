In another world, another time, there was a planet called Taria. This world had many forms of life, including the Humans, the strongest species on the planet, having created nations and united under many causes. The current year is 1732, many years past the death of what some see as their messiah, Jesso Primus. This world has a lot of people, many with a lot of different views, all following different nations. So go on, see how you will effect this world.
