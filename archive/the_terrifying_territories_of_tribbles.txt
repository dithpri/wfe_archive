Driven from their home world by the terrible Klingon wars, a few tribble survivors would up here in these fertile lands.   A few turned to a dozen, a dozen to an army, an army into a sea of fur.   Now these lands, while barren of conventional wildlife have become fit for new civilizations to spring forth, trading on fur pelts and abundant meat.   

For the nations that seek to thrive here, every day is a battle to maintain the environment against the always spreading cooing cur.
