Geography: Tactillia is a vast archipelago located in the southern hemisphere of the planet. It is surrounded by the Tactillian Sea, which is known for its treacherous currents and unpredictable weather patterns. The isles range in size from small, uninhabited atolls to large, densely populated islands with bustling cities.
Culture: Tactillia is a melting pot of cultures, with influences from Abernic, Heaven Tactical, and various other countries throughout the world. The people of Tactillia are known for their friendly and hospitable nature, and they take great pride in their unique cultural heritage.
