[b]Chihuahua[/b], with an area of 95,543 square miles, is the largest of Mexico&#39;s 32 states. It is slightly larger than the United Kingdom and is nicknamed [i]El Estado Grande[/i] (&quot;The Great State&quot; or &quot;The Big State&quot;). Chihuahua&#39;s capital city is Chihuahua City.

Chihuahua is located in Northwestern Mexico and is bordered by the states of Sonora to the west, Sinaloa to the southwest, Durango to the south, and Coahuila to the east. --Wikipedia [hr]
