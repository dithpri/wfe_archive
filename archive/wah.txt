[hr][b]What is Humanism?[/b]

[i]The WAH embraces human reason, ethics, social justice, and philosophical naturalism while specifically rejecting religious dogma, supernaturalism, pseudoscience, and superstition as the bases of morality and decision making.[/i]

[hr][b]Mission Statement[/b]

[i]To affect change in the World Assembly through unification of Nationstates desiring a better world for their children and themselves with ethics and morality for their own sake free from religious dogma and superstition.[/i]
