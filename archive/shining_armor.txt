Shining Armor is a male unicorn pony, Twilight Velvet and Night Light&#39;s son, Twilight Sparkle&#39;s older brother, Princess Cadance&#39;s husband, and Flurry Heart&#39;s father. He is the former captain of the Canterlot Royal Guard, serving under Princess Celestia and Princess Luna, and bears the title of prince as a result of his marriage to Cadance. He first appears in the two-part season two finale alongside Cadance. In the season three premiere, he and Cadance are appointed as the rulers of the Crystal Empire.
