Trillions of years ago, on Planet Flea, the tribe of the fleas had a raging war with Planet Alien. Exactly 9023 days after war was declared, 3 fleas were shot by a radioactive ray that was aimed to kill them. Instead of doing that, it actually expanded the flea&#39;s size by 1000x, turning into them into the Three Giant Fleas. Exactly 6666 days later, an asteroid known as the Massive Bunda Rock collided into Planet Flea. It created a crater 666 km wide, and in the center, a Divine Bunda Temple was summoned. Inside the Temple rested the Divine Bunda, and as the fleas were the first to discover the Bunda, they were blessed with Bundaism and were teleported to the Divine Bundaland, a land blessed with the power of Bundaism.
