The Eastern Threltic Region is the far-eastern side of the entire Threltic Region.  In the west there lie many more governments and countries.  The east has been blocked off since the Uslovian Revolution and is now home to many socialist states, all under Uslovian rule.  The Eastern Threltic Region is mainly mountinous in the north, bordering ice-lands.  The south is mainly fields and farmlands.  The middle area of the region is a dense area of forests, teaming with wildlife and small hills and lakes.
