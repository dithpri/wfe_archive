A land of rolling hills, sheltered coves, and dense beautiful forests, inland plains, and tall mountains protecting valleys teeming with life. Many species live here including deer, birds, and plant life. There are many beautiful islands offshore that protect bays and coves. These islands too are teeming with life. Off the south shore is a colorful coral reef where many species of fish live and in the north in late summer and early fall you can see the salmon run. There is a large inland lake where many species of freshwater fish live. In the north there is also an extensive boreal forest where famed birds such as the snowy owl, boreal owl, and multiple eagles live.
