Isla Nublar is the fictional island in the book and film version of Jurassic Park. Its name is intended to mean &amp;quot;Cloud Island&amp;quot; in Spanish. In the story, it is said to be 120 miles off the coast of Costa Rica, and the island measures 8 miles long and 3 miles wide at the widest point.
