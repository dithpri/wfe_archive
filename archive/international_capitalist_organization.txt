  Amerisika is an thriving Nation in the West Pacific. But there are some important events that you need to know...
Independence:
It started 1724 when rampages lead to an revolt against the Garisian Monarchy. Garisia does not exist now since it&#39;s dissolution in 1879. In 1758, the revolution ended with rebel victory. Garisia granted the rebels independence. An constitution was signed along with an peace treaty.

Neutrality (1759-1912):
After the revolt, the rebels needed to establish an strong superpower on their continent. This took an long time until 1805 when the country was strong enough. The neutrality lasted until 1912 when the APS Demo was sunk by an Germiahan ship.
