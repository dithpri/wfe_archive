Dundaschewan sat on a candy-wrapper couch, but the Jack of Spades was missing. He fell into the rose-hole of the Nile while fleeing the means of notorious brothers. Meanwhile, gay Mexicans played with the wounded kitten while the old wino looked for a better lighthouse; the roulette machine had suddenly found conscience and decided it was red. I import fog by cargo plane and the dog-eared piano is drunk with sanity and laughing. Beethovon sautees termite mounds under the dirt canopy of blues, but salvation is cheaper than hope in the winter harvest. Carry me along, Lord, carry me along, carry me along that old wailing trail.
