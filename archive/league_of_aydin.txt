Hail friends!

This RP Community is centered around a world named Paivo (a local word for Earth). The technological-era is akin to ours and the idea of nation-states and ideologies are beginning to emerge after a Cold War between Nationalist forces of Sal&#39;Lucan and Imperial forces of Brethalia. Though Brethalia might have outlived its opponent, the world is beginning to change. A new international organisation of non-aligned nations known as the League of Aydin emerges as Brethalia&#39;s millennium-old Empire begins to wane...
