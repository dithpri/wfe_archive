The Republic of Jafferian Legions is a peaceful and prosperous nation known for its highly intelligent, kind, and hardworking people. The citizens take great pride in their strong sense of community, respect, and faith, which guide their daily lives and inspire acts of generosity and cooperation. Education is highly valued, and schools and centres of learning can be found throughout the country, helping the population develop advanced knowledge in science, engineering, and the arts.
