Frenighenian Commonwealth is an unofficial name for the whole Empire of Frenighen and it&#39;s three colonies - Roubenia, Tirmidystan and Andoleen.

The name has its origins in the liberal policy of the Emperor Frederick II, who has been calling his realms a &quot;Commonwealth&quot; of all nations that living in it. In practice the &quot;Commonwealth&quot; is a single state - an absolute monarchy, in which all the political power is held by frenighenian Emperor.
