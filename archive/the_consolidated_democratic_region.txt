The Consolidated Democratic Region (full name: The Consolidated Democratic Region for the People of Brila Stelo and Incorporated Nations) is a region full of democracy and debate. It is a region for trade and a region for the sharing of ideas and intelligence. Join us if you want a region for your nation to be safe and for the citizens of your nation to enjoy their life. Follow the motto: Libereco, Unueco kaj Libereco de la Popolo - Liberty, Unity and Freedom of the People
