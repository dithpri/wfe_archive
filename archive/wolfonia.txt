Wolfonia, Created by [nation]Saperetia[/nation], is region standing for equality and freedom. The goal of this region is to be as diverse as possible. It wants to create as many embassies as possible. It currently contains 23 countries.

Embassy recuests are welcomed and encouraged!

It was once invaded, but the invasion failed, due to the invaders not having experience or numbers, and a security force coming in.
