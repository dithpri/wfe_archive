  The UFNS is a small conglomeration of minor nation states, dedicated to a common purpose, to maintain and enforce world peace. The UFNS is run by a council of nations who advise and execute cooperative decisions involved with internal and external affairs, led by the Administrative nation and the World Assembly Delegate as the direct adviser to the leading nation.  
  The elected World Assembly Delegate holds the power to veto any motion put forth. The World Assembly Delegate also reserves the power to administrate any external matters.
