Behold, the mighty region of [b][i][region]Excalibur[/region][/i][/b]! These ancient halls and venerable woods,  these mighty walls and fortified cities, these great forges and bustling taverns; Excalibur is the everlasting abode of [nation]Cobalt Chloride[/nation], and is a region for the storage of his little-used puppets.

Named for the sword of Arthurian legend, Excalibur is one of the many puppet storage regions associated with [region]Historia Novorum[/region].
