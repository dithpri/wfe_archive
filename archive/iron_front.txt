The Iron Front was a paramilitary organization formed in the Weimar Republic. It was associated with the Social Democratic Party and was created in order to combat totalitarianism. Its primary opponents were the German National People&#146;s Party (monarchists), the Nazi Party (self-explanatory), and the Communist Party (state capitalists).

It fought for liberal democracy, democratic socialism, and the trade unions. Its legacy continues today in the form of anti-fascist movements worldwide, and it remains a symbol of the global workers&#146; fight against totalitarianism in any shape or form.
