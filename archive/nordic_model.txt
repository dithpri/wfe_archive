[b]What is a Nordic model?[/b]
A Nordic Model (or Scandinavian Democracy) is a unique governmental system used in Nordic Countries. The Nordic Model system has both free-market and welfare together. Isn&#39;t it cool? We try to strive for this compromise between socialism and capitalism.
