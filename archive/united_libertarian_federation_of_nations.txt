A little bit about this region:

The (commonly referred to as the &#39;U.L.F.N&#39; or &#39;The Federation&#39;) U.L.F.N is a beautiful place for all nations that believe in freedom and peace. The Federation, which was founded by the Earth Capitol, is a technologically advanced, rich and Communist-&#39;ish&#39; region with a unique and efficient system. Food, housing and luxuries are free. Due to the kind-hearts spread across the nations, they grow food, build houses and luxuries at their own will, despite the fact that technological resources are capable of doing it themselves.
