[b]Overview[/b]  
The Catholic Alliance is a coalition of nations united by their Roman Catholic faith. Its primary goal is to support new regions, provide guidance, and promote socially conservative values across member states. The Alliance emphasizes charity, cultural preservation, and cooperation while respecting the autonomy of its members.


[b]Mission[/b]  
[list]  
[*]Aid new nations and help integrate them into the Alliance  
[*]Promote socially conservative values  
[*]Encourage cooperation and cultural preservation  
[/list]
