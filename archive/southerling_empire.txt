The Southerling Empire, a world power made of 4 united oblasts with differring peoples such as the Sevili&#39;s, Hrverman&#39;s, Otvlaches, Dwarves, Orcs, and Goblins has finally come to its demise

The break down of the Southerling Empire begins after Emperor Joseph Bozh dies at age 102 causing authority to collapse and the beginning of the civil war. Each oblast and peoples now vies for power at any cost now with past bad blood surfacing as a justifiable reason to slaughter each other. This begins the Southerling Civil War
