Gaacae, the Goddess of Arrogance and Madness, is extremely beautiful, with ashen skin, waist length, black hair, and eyes the color of deep pits. She is very tall and very muscular, and is usually laughing. She usually wears worn, ragged clothing dyed green-brown.
