The Riverlands are the Fiefs and Lordships over which House Tully of Riverrun have dominion as Lords Paramount of the Riverlands and Wardens of the Trident. The Capital of the Riverlands is the Castle of Riverrun, located where the Tumblestone River meets the Red Fork.

Head of State: The Lord of Riverrun
Head of Government: The Lord of Riverrun
Commander-In-Chief: The Lord of Riverrun
Supreme Judge: The Lord of Riverrun
Supreme Legislator: The Lord of Riverrun
