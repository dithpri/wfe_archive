The United Kingdoms of Polynesia consists a confederation of Pacific, Polynesian, and other nations. It is a constitutional monarchist region that elects a World Assembly Delegate. The region wants to establish friendly relations with other regions by opening embassies. It was constructed to help nations out and to become totally neutral from international belligerent conflicts. We are a democratic region and everyone has the right to vote and the right to join the region. If you are a republic or other government type no worries! We give aloha to everyone!
