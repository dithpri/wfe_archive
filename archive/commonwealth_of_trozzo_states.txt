Commonwealth of Trozzo States (CTS) is officially founded in February 4th, 2021 (Trozzo National Time).

CTS will replace the current Trozzo Commonwealth Alliance as the Trozzo intended to bring stability, authority, and systematic hierarchy.

Trozzo appoints New Trozzo as the head of CTS executive while Trozzo itselfs will be remain as Executive Observer and CTS Supreme Leader.

Trozzo is not intend to leave The South Pacific to bring diplomatic cooperation between The South Pacific and Commonwealth of Trozzo States (CTS).
