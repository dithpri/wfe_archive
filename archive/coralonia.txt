Coralonia is an island located 32.5 degrees North, 52.4 degrees west, placing it East of Bermuda. It&#146;s name originates from the strong presence of coral reefs on the island&#146;s shorelines. It has a total land area of 43,210 square miles, or 111,914 square kilometers. Though located almost in the middle of the Atlantic, it&#146;s considered to be part of North America due to historical and cultural ties. Coralonia comprises of two countries, East Coralonia (aka The Socialist Republic of Coralonia) &amp; West Coralonia (The Republic of Coralonia). Coralonia is known for its stunning environment, pristine, pink sand beaches, its colorful coral reefs, and its peculiar climate.
