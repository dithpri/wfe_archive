[color=4488BB][b]New Lacia[/b][/color][color=4488BB] is a region founded for the purpose of connecting many regions and being a safehaven for culture, roleplay, and offsite activities.

New Lacia belongs to every citizen of the region, regardless if they are resident, or if they are an officer or not. Lacian citizens can engage in government by petitioning the Censor or Prime Censor for change, voting on legislation, and endorsing other nations.[/color]
