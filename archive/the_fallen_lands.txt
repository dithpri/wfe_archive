These are the rules for any nation-state that chooses to join.

1) All states must pay an equal share for the upkeep and protection of the trade routes within The Fallen Lands

2) Any state that fails to pay their full share will lose protection if what is owed is not paid at the next collection.

3) Any state may seize resources and territory of states who have failed to pay or have rejected protection twice in a row.

4) Any state that attacks another state, that has paid for protection, will be subject to retaliation.

5) States may pay the protection fee of other states that fail to pay.

6) States who have their protection fee paid by another state is subject to their government and their rules.
