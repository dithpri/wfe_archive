The Confederacy of Soverign States of America
The American Collective
The Dominion of the Desseret
The English Dominion
The Great Lakes Alliance
The Empire of Sybiria
The Allied States of Pacifica
The Tribal Nations of America
The Dominon of New Spain
The Coalition of Canadian Provinces

Ruler: Emperor Mitt Romney

Legislative System: 100 member Emperial Senate (1 chamber, Emperor Romney presides)

Judicial System: Emperial Tribunal headed by Emperor Romney, Emperial Army General Rand Paul and Emperial WarFleet Admiral Paul Ryan. Emperor Romney Presides.
