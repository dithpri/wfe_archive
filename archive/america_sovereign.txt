The American Collective
The Dominion of the Desseret
The English Dominion
The Great Lakes Alliance
The Empire of Sybiria
The Allied States of Pacifica
The Tribal Nations of America
The Dominon of New Spain
The Coalition of Canadian Provinces

Ruler: Emperor Mitt Romney

Legislative System: 100 member Imperial Senate (1 chamber, Emperor Romney presides)

Judicial System: Imperial Tribunal headed by Emperor Romney, Imperial Army General Rand Paul and Imperial WarFleet Admiral Paul Ryan. Emperor Romney Presides.
