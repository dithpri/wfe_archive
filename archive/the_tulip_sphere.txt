The Sphere of Sovereignty and Non-Alignment (SoSN-A) and also informally called and commonly referred to as The Tulip Sphere (TS) -- is a regional intergovernmental organization which primarily focuses on co-operation in the fields of international geopolitics, economics and mutually beneficial international security and national defense interests on a bilateral level between signatory and member countries. Founded in the City of Tyirkinaan, (Tyirkinaan W.S.S.) Vorounovia; on the ninth of September, 2022 -- by 12 signatories including the W.S.S.V. -- its core ideology being that of multipolarism with [an emphasis of/on] anti-fascism. Indifferent towards religion/irreligion, state/market capitalism and socialism/communism.
