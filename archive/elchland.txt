A continent located in the North Atlantic that was first settled by various groups in the late 18th Century. Now those groups have evolved into modern nations each with their own histories and identities, but there still remains much undiscovered adventure within!

[B]Elchland is composed of two landmasses:[/B]
Greater Elchland: a massive island consisting of a giant inland sea, and various geographies ranging from scorching deserts, frozen tundras, swampy plains, warm beaches, and dense forests
Lesser Elchland (somtetimes called [i]Shoalsland[/i]): located immediately south of Greater Elchland and also the first part of the continent to be intensively settled

Approximately 30% of Elchland is still unclaimed wilderness.
