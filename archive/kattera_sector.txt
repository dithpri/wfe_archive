Out of the ashes of the Illuyanki Invasion, from the still warm cinders of the fallen Astanite Empire, the stars are reborn anew. For centuries the unshackled stars of Kattera have tended to the wounds left by their fallen oppressors and alien traumas, yet with a growing cry and high spirits, the many return to the void&#151;catalyzed by their dreams, their ambitions, and their fears.
