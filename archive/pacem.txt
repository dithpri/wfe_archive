Welcome to Pacem!

Pacem is a recently discovered region. Although in the future it plans to expand its influence.

There will be elections for positions on a regular basis. Any nation may run for any position an unlimited &nbsp;number of times.&nbsp;

Pacem is a region founded upon the principle of keeping the peace in the region and abroad. That is why the region was named Pacem, the Latin word for peace.&nbsp;
