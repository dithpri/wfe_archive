The Northern Pacific Regal Reich Alliance (NPRRA) is an alliance developed by Reich des Fuchses&#39;s leader, Colophon de Von Volporez, to institute peaceful barter and trade among all empires/reiches willing to join the NPRRA. Any and all fighting between allies of the NPRRA within the borders of NPRRA territory will dealt with properly by the mobile task forces of the Reich des Fuchsian military.
