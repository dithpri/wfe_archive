[b]Some Base Lore:[/b]

Welcome to the swashbuckling world of Girls und Pirates!!
The planet is war ridden, diseased, people dying everywhere. Amongst these wars and famines, many groups of Pirates have arisen, one of those being us!

We are Girls und Pirates! A merry band of swashbuckling pirates, we raid settlements, and occasionally act as mercenaries for militaries and organisations.
Girls und Pirates is a loosely formed, and completely anarchic group of cutthroat pirates, We have no rules! And no restrictions!!

We will gladly accept most who join, and do not judge most backgrounds, so whether you&#146;re left wing or right wing, we will gladly get drunk together.
