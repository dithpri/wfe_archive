The Regional Union of Castocium is a political and economic union encompassing most of the Vesperian Continent and surrounding islands. The Union was established on the 7th of July 12017 by the signing of the Treaty of Epsitus, which ended the June War between the two former regions of The Solacris Union and The Centralian Empire. Castocium covers 6,437,518 square kilometers and has has an estimated population of 6,086,999,000. The Union is made up of 3 Member States which cover the majority of the continent.
