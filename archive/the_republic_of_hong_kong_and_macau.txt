The Colonies in the Pearl River Delta have suffered many mishaps over the years, with the British and Portuguese Empire colonizing many areas, and the Peoples Republic of China gaining control of Macau and Hong Kong. in 2046, a year before the termination of the 50 years of autonomy and non-cencorship, the Peoples of Hong Kong and Macau had decided to form a provisional government to prevent that from happening. Though China threatened to invade, the United Nations had guaranteed their safety and was allowed into the General Assembly.
