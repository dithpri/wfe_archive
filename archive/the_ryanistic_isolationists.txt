 This region will show you what real freedom really is. Extremely limited government just enough to keep some semblance of order. A utopia where the great cannot be constrained by the weak. The artist fears no censor. The scientist is not constrained by morals. The free market reigns over all business. Competition is promoted and those who fall to the bottom have no safety net, no welfare to save them. This is a region where you will rise and fall by your own merits.
