[color=blue]Pripyat[/color] is an abandoned city in northern Ukraine, near the border with Belarus. Named for the nearby Pripyat River, [color=blue]Pripyat[/color] was founded on 4 February 1970, the ninth nuclear city in the Soviet Union, for the Chernobyl Nuclear Power Plant. It was officially proclaimed a city in 1979, and had grown to a population of 49,360 before being evacuated a few days after the 26 April 1986 Chernobyl disaster.
