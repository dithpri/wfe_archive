Urbs Sacra, meaning the Sacred city, truly began following the removal of the monarch Romulus, as a small tribe we began working our way to glory, until we had nearly been demolished by the barbarian hoards who ransacked our tribe for many months. After coming to a deal with the Barbarian hordes they let us be, It was this time that we began our true accension to Auctoritas, and formed our legions, and took control of an empire.
