[b]Facts about Upstate New York[/b]

[b][color=blue]-Home to Niagara Falls, the Adirondacks, the Alleghenys, and the Catskills. 
-Share a border with the canadian provinces of Quebec and Ontario
-Borders the states of New Jersey, Vermont, Pennsylvania, Massachusetts, and Connecticut   
-Largest City is Buffalo
-Major Waterways include: Lake Ontario, Lake Erie, Erie Canal, Lake Champlain, Niagara River, and St. Lawrence River
-Famous people include: Tom Cruise, James Patterson, and Humphery Bogart[/color][/b]
