The Glorious Empire: A Kingdom-by-Kingdom Breakdown
Welcome, esteemed reader, to the official (and entirely unbiased, we assure you) factbook entry for the magnificent, the sprawling, the occasionally-tripping-over-its-own-feet entity known simply as The Empire! While the Emperor sits on the Big Chair (and occasionally gets it stuck to his robes), the true flavour of the realm comes from its diverse and utterly bonkers constituent kingdoms.

Prepare yourself for a journey through lands where logic is a suggestion, common sense is treason, and the primary export is usually confusion.
