The third idol group in the Love Live franchise. Also known as Nijigaku, they were initially created for the mobile game Love Live! School Idol Festival All Stars, though would go on to have an anime of their own. Unlike the other groups, all the idols work independently. Yu Takasaki was introduced to fill the role of MC for the anime.

Manager: Yu Takasaki
Members: Shizuku Osaka, Kanata Konoe, Emma Verde, Ayumu Uehara, Kasumi Nakasu, Karin Asaka, Ai Miyashita, Setsuna Yuki, Rina Tennoji, Shioriko Mifune, Mia Taylor, Lanzhu Zhong
