The Continent of Dommum, meaning home, holds the Darkwater region. The region has a diverse history of Monarchal and Democratic uprisings and records of such, and Memian and Jogreen are the last true Monarchies, beside Sytherias&#39; headache of a government.
Jogreen has the second largest navy, and Rowinzilla has the second largest army behind Sytherias&#39;. Markica has had three uprisings, and now they are communist and bracing for yet another uprising, while the newcomer Memian is growing in power.
