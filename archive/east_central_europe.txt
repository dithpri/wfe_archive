[b]Welcome to East-Central Europe[/b]

East-Central Europe is the region between German, West Slavic and Hungarian speaking Europe and the Eastern Slavic lands of Russia, Belarus and Ukraine. Those lands are described as situated &quot;between two&quot;: &quot;between two worlds, between two stages, between two futures&quot;. In the geopolitical sense, East-Central Europe can be considered alongside Western and Eastern Europe, as one of the &quot;Three Europes&quot;.
