The [b]Kriegsmarine[/b] was the navy of Nazi Germany from 1935 to 1945. It superseded the Kaiserliche Marine (German Imperial Navy) of World War I and the inter-war Reichsmarine. The Kriegsmarine was one of three official branches &#151; along with the Wehrmacht Heer and the Luftwaffe &#151; of the Wehrmacht, the armed forces of Nazi Germany. -- Wikipedia[hr]
