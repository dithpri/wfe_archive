[b]&amp;#10016; Source of the Danube and Neckar rivers, [region]The Black Forest[/region] is a 6,009 square km area of thick woodlands, hills, lakes, traditional farmhouses and half-timbered villages. It is bounded by the Rhine valley to the west and south. Its highest peak is the Feldberg with an elevation of 1,493m followed by Herzogenhorn at 1,415m and the Belchen 1,414m.
