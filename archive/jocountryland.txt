Brought forth by Jocountry, this Empire holds several independent states, each with a different purpose. Jocountry is the authoritative nation, Alcleonthra is the industrial state. Arwain is one of the religious states. Daarev works with commerce. Angel of Your Death is a semi-penal colony, and Phuchistan deals with scholars and philosophers, while Fasimani houses much of the military, Kateville supports our region with energy plants
