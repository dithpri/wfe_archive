Escalade Island is an island located 100 miles off the coast of Washington state.
It is a continent of itself, which includes the countries of Escatopeia, Pageland, Ganoveia, Malibuia, Hon, and Brucer.

Facts:
-Area: 112,730 sq. mi.
-Discovered: Jan. 19th, 1780

Bordered By: Pacific Ocean, Washington, Oregon, British Columbia
