The Isles von der Deutsch is a social, economic, and military pact in order to ensure safety for its inhabitants. This region tolerates all ideologies that inhabit the region, some countries may allow controversial ideologies, some countries may not, but may not commit mass genocides of them. We provide safe havens for all countries in need. we protect together, but are under no pact to attack together.
