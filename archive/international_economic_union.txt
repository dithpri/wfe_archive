The [b]International Economic Union (IEU)[/b] is a global organization committed to fostering economic cooperation, stability, and growth among its member states. It serves as a crucial platform for dialogue, policy coordination, and the implementation of initiatives that address global economic challenges, promoting free and fair trade, harmonizing economic policies, and facilitating the flow of capital and labor across borders. Governed by a charter emphasizing transparency and inclusivity, the IEU unites nations from all stages of economic development in pursuit of a more interconnected, resilient, and prosperous global economy.
