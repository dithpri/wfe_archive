[b]Description:[/b]
We are a small group of nations that form a trade bloc. We consist of a common market and customs union. By joining the IEU will gain the benefits of having common policies on product regulation, freedom of movement of goods, services, capital and labour between the nations of the IEU and have a a common external trade policy.

We hope you enjoy your stay here.
