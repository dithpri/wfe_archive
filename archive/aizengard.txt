The region of [b][color=blue]Aizengard[/color][/b] has disappeared and reappeared over countless cycles due to the influences of [b][color=purple]Sousuke Aizen[/color][/b], [b][color=brown]Sauron[/color][/b], [b][color=goldenrod]Saruman[/color][/b], and [b][color=teal]Marcelino Ezmorales[/color][/b] over [b][nation]Aqua Regia[/nation][/b] while functioning at [b][i]ludicrous speed[/i][/b], causing the nation to go into plaid. This region ceases to exist whenever [b][color=teal]Aqua Regia[/color][/b] goes into plaid.

[b][color=blue]Aizengard[/color][/b] is strictly reserved for nations recognized by [b][color=teal]Aqua Regia[/color][/b] as one of the [b][color=red]La Solidaridad[/color][/b], an intergovernmental military alliance based on the [b][color=brown]Treaty of Ratatouille-Hidango[/color][/b], which promotes international justice and regional unity. While the region&amp;#39;s nations are not officially part of the [b][color=purple]World Assembly[/color][/b], it endorses most WA resolutions considered &amp;quot;fair&amp;quot;.
