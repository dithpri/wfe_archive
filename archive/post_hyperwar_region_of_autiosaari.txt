Firing of the Giza Mass Autism Array marked the end of the hyperwar. It was a last ditch effort by the emperor of Hwan Empire, Kim Il-Sung I Hwan to save his lands from the finnic hordes running rampant after the collapse of his military at the hands of finnic-proto-horse archers lead by Khan Jante and the Hwan civil war. These actions would eventually lead to the collapse of both Hwan Empire and The Proto-Finnic Holy Roman Khaganate.

With the Lemurians gone, the disputed ancestral lands of Turan in turmoil and the biggest most advanced civilizations of our time gone, a new power is rising.
