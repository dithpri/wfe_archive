Vinix (IPA: [viniks]), officially the Republic of The Archipelago of Vinix or the Vinician Republic is a country of southwestern Catholic, comprising the Island of Vinix, the Island of Vinicula and the outlying Vinician Islets in the Gulf of Vinix of the Catholic Ocean.

President Dr. Winston has signed the Catholic Charter in May 2007.
