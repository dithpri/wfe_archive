[color=blue]The Socialist Federal Republic of Yugoslavia (SFR Yugoslavia or SFRY) is a Yugoslav state that existed from its foundation in the aftermath of World War II and is a socialist state and a federation made up of six socialist republics: Slovenia, Croatia, Bosnia and Herzegovina, Serbia, Montenegro and Macedonia. In addition, this includes two autonomous provinces within Serbia: Kosovo and Vojvodina.[/color]
