After a long and global war against one another the nations of the Laurentian Coast, Lineistan and Mastonia decided to stop the fighting and unite in a single empire which would help spread democracy and liberty across their region. 
After a brief insurrection from the Mastonians and Lines, the Empire was once again united.  All parties agreed for a fair and balanced world.

80 years later, the Mastonian regime lost grip on its powerand the nation plunged into a civil war.
