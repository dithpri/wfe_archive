After a long and global war against one another the nations of the Laurentian Coast, Lineistan and Mastonia decided to stop the fighting and unite in a single empire which would help spread democracy and liberty across their region. 
Two of the original three, the Lines and Mastonians split from the Empire in pursuit of their own region. The August Rebellion began and involved all nations of both regions, from North America to Europe the war was a brief yet brutal conflict that ended in a stalemate and a peace agreement. The Empire agreed to less government intervention and the rebels agreed to rejoin the Empire.
