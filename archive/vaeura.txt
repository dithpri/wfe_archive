Vaeura is a low-fantasy region interested in casual and optionally adversarial roleplay. Alliances, trade, negotiations, and even warfare are on the table. We have one non-human nation in [nation]Chatelleux[/nation] 

Nations in the region are not held to any automatic obligations to alliance other than to protect the region from external aggression, nor are they required to adhere to singular code of governance. Nations and societies from across the political spectrum may find their home here, if they act in good faith, even if not necessarily in peace.
