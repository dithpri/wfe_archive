The [i]Uniwar Microlocation[/i] is a tiny space-time &#39;anomaly&#39; located deep inside of subspace. Thus the [i]Uniwar Microlocation[/i] is extremely inaccessible - even for advanced space-faring civilizations.

Inside of this isolated subspace singularity three different [b]human cultures[/b] evolved.

&#149; [b][nation]The Khraleans[/nation][/b] - using solely bio-organic technology. 

&#149; [b][nation]The Titan Empire[/nation][/b] - heavily relying on artificial intelligences and other autonomous technology.

&#149; [b][nation]The Sapiens Empire[/nation][/b] - uses genetically engineered organisms combined with cybernetic technology.
