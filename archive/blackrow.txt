
 
Officially known as the Allied Confederate Nations of Blackrow, we formed primarily to self-serve our independent interests and for a shared coalition of defense against hostile powers. And to prevent further hostilities from arising within the group. 

Rankings:

- Founder/WA Delegate

- Confederate Senior Executive Member

- Confederate Junior Executive Member

- Confederate Internal Relations Executive

NOTE: Confederate Executives of various offices such as economics, aviation, civil rights, and agriculture are equal in rank and holdings. 

The region of Blackrow is named after the capital of Galicia Nordland, where the nations gathered to discuss and eventually form the Allied Confederacy of Blackrow.
