Welcome to Ember!

Ember is a region thriving with life and pride! Travel through sunny valleys and frosty peaks and find multiple nations with welcoming open arms! Ember is a peaceful Region but has went through a rough past until its name change from the Anastasian Empire. The leading Nation is the Kingdom Of Gladiston. And all of ember is ruled by one leader. P.S. This leader is uncertain at the moment as we are currently going through elections.
