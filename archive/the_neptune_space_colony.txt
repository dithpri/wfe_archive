Part of the [region]Imperial Galactic Republic of Andarios[/region]! This designation refers to the 14 moons that were settled around the planet in the Solar System. The moons were: Naiad, Thalassa, Despina, Galatea, Larissa, Neptune XIV, Proteus, Triton, Nereid, Halimede, Sao, Laomedeia, Psamathe and Neso!
