The numerous tribes of Hyborian Peninsula were previously made up of nothing but nomadic pig herders. At one point, alliances between tribes became federations, and these federations are the nations we know today. The concept of capitalism has never been very clear to all but the most intelligent of the population who choose to ignore it. Despite the complete lack of economic stability on a global scale, those within the region consider themselves to be quite cultured and can co-exist with one another on a regional level.
