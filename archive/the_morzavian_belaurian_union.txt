The [b]Morzavian&#150;Belaurian Union (MBU)[/b] is a cooperative regional bloc uniting the sovereign nations of [nation]Morzavia[/nation] and [nation]Belauria[/nation]. 

Founded under the Treaty of Belgradin&#150;Morzagrad, the Union promotes economic integration, political coordination, and cultural exchange while preserving national independence. Informally known as Belmor or Morbelia, the region embodies a shared commitment to endurance, solidarity, and stability.
