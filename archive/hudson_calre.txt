[b]Back Ground[/b]
In 1854 explorers founded fertile land North West of the Hudson Bay. This grew the attention of the Dutch, and Germans which started colony&#146;s on the region. When first settling, the colonist had many troubles with the natives of the regions. The natives were the Yutz tribes, known for there brutality. After years of fighting, the colonists won and took most of the land. After the collapse of the German, and Dutch nation, the colonists received independence. These nations now make of the Hudson Calre region.
