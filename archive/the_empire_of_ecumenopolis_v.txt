Welcome to the The Empire of Ecumenopolis-V. Any state is free to join our Empire provided they agree to be bound by common policy concerning:

a) Foreign Relations
b) Defence of the Realm 
c) Telecommunications 
d) Inter-State/Kingdom/Entity trade and commerce


Further, they will be required to pledge allegiance to the King-Emperor as their Supreme Head of State but nations may continue with their existing constitutional arrangements concerning their Heads of State so long as they pledge allegiance to the King Emperor as their supreme head.
