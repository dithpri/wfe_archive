An area in which the normal laws of reality do not apply, and remnants of seemingly extraterrestrial activity lie undisturbed amongst its ruins. The Zone contains a place called the &quot;Room&quot;, said to grant the wishes of anyone who steps inside. The area containing the Zone is shrouded in secrecy, sealed off by the government and ominous hazards exist within it.

The Z O N E is a living system of traps. No one can take the same route twice, and only a Stalkerr can guide you.
