[b][color=orange]Oceania[/color][/b] is the fifth continent of the world. It contains several Pacific islands and it is subdivided in 4 regions, Australia, Melanesia, Micronesia and Polynesia. The term &amp;quot;Oc&eacute;anie&amp;quot; was coined circa 1812 by Danish-French geographer and journalist Malthe Conrad Bruun. Among its explorers stands out the British captain James Cook. [b][color=chartreuse]Oceania[/color][/b] is also the name of the &amp;quot;1984&amp;quot; nation ruled by the INGSOC.
