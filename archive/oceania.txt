[b][color=#00CCFF]Oceania[/color][/b] is the fifth continent of the world, explored by the British captain James Cook and the French admiral Louis-Antoine de Bougainville. It comprises Australia, Melanesia, Micronesia and Polynesia.

[b][color=#FF0000]Oceania[/color][/b] is also the name of a &quot;1984&quot; nation described by the British novelist George Orwell.
