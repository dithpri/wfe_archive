[b][color=#FF7F00]Oceania[/color][/b] is the fifth continent of the world, explored by the British captain James Cook and the French admiral Louis-Antoine de Bougainville. It includes Australia, Melanesia, Micronesia and Polynesia. [b][color=#FF0000]Oceania[/color][/b] is also the name of a &quot;1984&quot; nation described by George Orwell.
