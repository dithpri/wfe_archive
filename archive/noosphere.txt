The Noosphere is located nearby a far distant Star System. The Noosphere is known for it&#39;s unnatural physical environment, which led to its reclassification as Special Hyperspace Zone. The Noosphere contains various habitable Worlds and a huge amount of smaller but inhabitable Planetoids and Asteroids alike. Most notably is the Noospherian Ringworld which has been build several Eons ago by a long forgotten Race. 

Current Year: 1305 NSC
