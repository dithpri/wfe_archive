The Noosphere is located nearby a remote Star System. Known for it&#39;s unusual physical environment, it had been classified as a Special Hyperspace Zone. The Sphere contains various habitable Worlds and a huge amount of smaller but inhabitable Planetoids and Asteroids alike. Most notably is the Noospherian Ringworld which has been build several Eons ago by a long forgotten Race.
