[hr][b]Overview[/b]
[region]The State of Israel[/region] is a region closely modeled after the [url=https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/Israel]State of Israel[/url]. It&#39;s purpose is to simulate the modern nation of Israel and provide for the learning and discussion of the country. It was formed by [nation=short+noflag]New-Jerusalem[/nation]. [region]The State of Israel[/region] promotes peace through strength, personal, economic, and religious freedom, and democratic, progress-oriented values. All players are welcome to join and participate in the learning, discussion, and democracy that it offers. Further rules and information are outlined in the Constitution.
