In the Beautiful Russia of the Future, we strive for maximum freedoms, equality, low crime (including corruption) and a strong economy. 
In general, the Governor is ready to put up with authoritarianism (not radical) or with countries where &quot;democracy is being played&quot; if the population of these countries has high incomes and not too low a level of income equality. The governor tries not to interfere in the affairs of the region .Extreme dictators, without positive changes in the economy, human rights or the economy, can be expelled by voting
