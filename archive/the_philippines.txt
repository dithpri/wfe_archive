[color=black][b]Welcome to the Philippines

The Philippines[/b], officially known as the Republic of the Philippines, is a sovereign state in Southeast Asia in the western Pacific Ocean. To its north across the Luzon Strait lies [region]Taiwan[/region]. West across the South China Sea sits Vietnam. The Sulu Sea to the southwest lies between the country and the island of Borneo, and to the south the Celebes Sea separates it from other islands of Indonesia. It is bounded on the east by the Philippine Sea.[/color][hr][b][color=darkblue]Protected by [region]Republica Federativa do Brasil[/region][/color][hr][color=bronze]Elections are open to delegate![/color][/b]
