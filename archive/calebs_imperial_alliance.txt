The Calebs Haven Organization broke with its  mother country in 2011 and were recognized as the new nation of Calebs Haven  following the Treaty of Washington &amp; Miriam in 2010. Other nations were added to Calebs Haven  as the nation expanded across the continents of South American and Asia, and parts of  the Middle East in its war campaign  efforts to fight and eliminate world terrorism, rogue nations, criminal cartels, and government corruption. A number of other nations  were added to Calebs Haven through business and political agreements.The Calebs Imperial Alliance (C.I.A.) was formed after the campaign.
