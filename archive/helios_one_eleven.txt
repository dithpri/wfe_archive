After the Tahitu War, Helios is divided into 5 nations across the continent. The Brotherhood Of Steel Commonwealth, the WA delegate, and economic powerhouse. The other major nation is the enforced,stable, Empire of Lukendorf, which is the military powerhouse and also a major economic producer.

   Following the Tahitu War, three more nations allied themselves within Helios One Eleven.  A Holy Empire from the Afterlife, based on Heaven and Hell. Also joining was  Roycefus, a newly formed armed republic, and The Dominion Free Fobidown Communism, formed within Helios. 

The Region, as a whole, is allowing of joining nations, but is wary of another possible takeover.
