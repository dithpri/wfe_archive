The Bardolocin Union is a small currently two-state union that promises the Bardolocin Free Trade Area, the Bardolocin Parliament, the Bardolocin Court of Law, and the chance for even small and new nations to take leadership positions. Currently vacant positions include:
-Chief Executive Officer (non-vacant, but applications open)
-World Assemble Delegate (vacant)
-Minister of Border Control (vacant)
-Minister for Communications (vancant)
-Minister for Foreign Affairs (vacant)
-Minister for Internal Affairs (vacant)
-Minister for Exterior Image (vacant)
