[color=#1F0882][b]Welcome to the NationStates Vexillological Society (NSVS)![/b][/color]
[hr][B]Dedicated to the study and teaching of flags and good flag design. Looking for flag makers and flag lovers interested in helping to aggregate knowledge and teach others the art of flag making.[/B][hr]
Note: The NSVS is [u]NOT[/u] about elitism. We don&#39;t want to mock or flame, nor act as an exclusive club of strict rules. Provide kind and constructive criticism, there are often exceptions to the guidelines of flag design, so please refrain from needless discontent.
