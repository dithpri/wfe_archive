The Christian Evangelistic Society exists to tell people about the good news of Jesus Christ and their need for him. We are open to all Christians who would like to help fufill the great commission in Matthew 25:18-20. Also we want to be good servants to Jesus Christ (our master). Read the words of Jesus Christ in Matthew 25:14-30.  There is no password for this region but please fill out and telegram me, Western Virginia, the application sheet found here upon joining:

1. Do you believe the entire Bible to be the Word of God?
2. What must someone do to be saved?
3. Do you know Jesus as your PERSONAL SAVIOR?
4. Are you living for Jesus Christ today( doing what God is telling you to do)?
