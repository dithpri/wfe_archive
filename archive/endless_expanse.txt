Huge tracts of land, free-flowing blue water, a wealth of natural resources and sprawling forest canopies. Trade is encouraged between all nations, military and defense interests are purely to combat the supernatural or extraterrestrial, and there is a huge regional disregard for religion.

Impeccable cultural assimilation has led to the discovery of a variety of hybrid foods. Resultantly, the region is rich in a wide variety of ingredients which are carefully isolated in underwater UV-permeated capsules and harvested mechanically to prevent ecological disruption.
