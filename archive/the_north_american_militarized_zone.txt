We are small, but we are strong.  We like to be prepared for invasion, but stay peaceful as long as possible.  We are the North American Militarized Zone.  We were founded after the great biological war of 2084, in the bunkers the leaders of the four new nations of North America formed an alliance to ensure that a inhumane war like that would ever happen again.  In the year 3010 the four leaders emerged from their own bunker in a new era of prosperity for the continent.  In the year 3015 the alliance opened up membership for non-North American Countries and have sucsessfully recruted five new nations, but for peace to be kept more world nations must join......
