[color=#BA0C2F][b]Bergen[/b] is a coastal city in western Norway, known as the &quot;Gateway to the Fjords.&quot; It is surrounded by mountains and sea, giving it a scenic landscape and a rainy oceanic climate. Once an important Hanseatic trading port, [b]Bergen[/b] still preserves its historic wooden wharf area, [b]Bryggen[/b].[/color]
