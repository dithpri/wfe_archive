In a space ripped between time and space, forced to relive great wars again, and again, a group emerged, capable not of stopping the circle of destruction and reversal, but controlling it. Capable of reversing time to where they saw fit, the group established a fair regime to keep the populace calm, ready to banish anyone who used the unstable nature of this space to commit heinous acts. Since, they have seeked to expand their influence. Their existence based upon eternal war, allowed for them to adapt and overcome.

Now they have come to NationStates. Beware.
