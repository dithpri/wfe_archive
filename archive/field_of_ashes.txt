A thousand years ago, a mighty empire stood in this region. But time, fortune and nature were not kind to it, and it fell into ruin.

Over time, the great nation became ruins, ruins became rubble, rubble became dust - until the ground was desolate and bare.

Recently though, a great fire spread over the land. No-one knows for sure whence it came, but the flames burned for a full week, coating the earth in ash.

We took this as a sign that it was time to start again.
