Timeline: 
1622: Discovered by Russian explorer Admiral Yuri Kalakarov.
1745: English Settlers construct fort and small town.
1914-18: Small naval based constructed for Commonwealth shipping.
1940-44: Japanese Occupation.
1944-45: Self-Governed.
1946: British formally allow independence.
