[u]We are the Free Peoples League.[/u]

Founded by the First Council of the People&#39;s Republic of Johiki, the Free Peoples League was established after diplomatic relations amongst other unions were strained due to excessive control exerted by the rulers. In foundation, the Free Peoples League was designed from the beginning to fully respect the autonomy of all nations, yet of which still require protection from one another to combat the growing threat of oppressive states that encroach upon their territories.
