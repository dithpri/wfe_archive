The Union of Central European Monarchies (UCEM) is an intergovernmental organisation established on 24th November 2009 to unite the Monarchs their countries economically and politically after many years of wars. At its founding, the UCEM had 3 member states; there are now 6. The UCEM Headquarters resides in Vienna, with further main offices in Nice, Waldshut and Sosnowiec. The organisation is financed by assessed and voluntary contributions from its member states.
