The Allied Territories of Sonderia came to be when a many newly developing nations of diverse origins met through strange circumstances. The leaders of the first 6 Nations met together, and in realization of common goals and the positive impacts of unity, they decided to forge a Region. The unique Nations of Noreat, Embersmoke, Yurito, Ferenria, PureMarblealia, and Utozzion offer many important exports and bring differing ideas to create a powerful new society.
