The Union of the panda, lead by the wise leader Garchomp, and his comrade Pluto, was created following the conquest of the Valley of Peace, by the hand of Kai. After watching his master, Po, the dragon warrior, be slain, the great dragon Garchomp swore revenge, and united with all the pandanians of China to take back the land. Uniting with the new dragon warrior, Pluto, Garchomp led a bloody civil war against the evil followers of Kai.
