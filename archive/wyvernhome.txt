Wyvernhome has an ancient and bloody history, beginning in the ancient days of the Wyveria all the way up to present day. Technologically, some have said they are somewhat lacking in certain respects (particularly the production and development, though not innovation), but most of the nations in Wyvernhome have a decent understanding of technology, with certain ones having things like computers. The nations in Wyvernhome are primarily liberally or socially minded, and the predominant economy is socialist.
