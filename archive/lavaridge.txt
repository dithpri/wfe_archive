[i]&quot;An Excellent Place for Relaxing!&quot;[/i]

Lavaridge is a tiny island off the coast of Japan, straddling the Pacific Ring of Fire. Mount Chimney, towering high over the island provides wealth from its ash. Like a blanket, volcanic ash lightly covers the islands north. 

At Mount Chimney&#39;s southern foot, popular amongst the elderly sits Lavaridge Town. Founded near the islands famous hot springs, they are a popular tourist destination. According to legend, the hot springs calm tension, relieve muscle ache, solve romantic problems, and can cure any ailment.
