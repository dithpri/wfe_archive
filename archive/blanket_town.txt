We, the Blankonians, hereby declare this region to be soft ground!
No roof shall be made without the required two chairs and the large blanket from the basement!
No door shall be barred without the proper structural pillow support!
No person shall stand upright, and must either crawl or kneel-walk!
No food shall be kept from one another, especially large chip bags of the salty flavor!
No book shall be closed without the consent of the majority!
No animal shall be permitted, save the most excellent and most esteemed frog, Frogdrick the fourth!
