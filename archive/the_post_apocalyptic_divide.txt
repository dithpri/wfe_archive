After the Z-Day apocalypse of October 2013, a handful of nations, sponsored by the Empire of Turkmenia, gather together to rebuild and prepare for the next impending threat, which is currently unknown. Every nation is free to rule as they see please, and outside nations are always welcome. However, the Triumvirate stand together as reminder that peace will reign, and trouble-makers will be dealt with swiftly and harshly.

The Triumvirate:

The Territories of Turkmenia
The Free States of Phurlli
The Sovereign States of Shelly
