On the dawn of a new day, 7 nations meet to Discuss a treaty.
The allied states of the Nuclear Republic of Archnobyl, The Borderlands of Fallen Republic, The Empire of ECM, The Dictatorship of Boxxy City, Land of the Kittens, Papua Old-Guinea and the united states of Justice.
The Great nations came to an agreement to form a pact and live in a peaceful region together with the equally share land. The economies complement each other with the big boom in Archnobyl and the high orientation of capitalism in the Empire of ECM.
The region stays away from the world assembly and the security council as the region hosts its own council at the region.
