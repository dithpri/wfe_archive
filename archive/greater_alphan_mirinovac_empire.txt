The [b]Greater Alphan-Mirinovac Empire[/b], often referred to as the [b]Alphan-Mirinovac Empire[/b], and often mocked as the [b]Alphan Empire[/b] is a de jure constitutional monarchy and a union between [nation]Greater Alpha[/nation] and [nation]Mirinovac[/nation] ruled by a single monarch who was both Emperor of Alpha and King of Mirinovac.

After the [nation]Greater Alpha[/nation] conquest of [nation]Mirinovac[/nation], to appease the international community, an union was proposed which allow [nation]Mirinovac[/nation] an equal status with [nation]Greater Alpha[/nation]. 

However, while it is de jure a dual monarchy, it is de facto an [nation]Greater Alpha[/nation]n dominated Empire led by 
[b]The Eternal Emperor of Alpha[/b].
