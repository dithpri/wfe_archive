[b]Xerlona,[/b] a vast  and stunning region, home to large and small nations.

Geographically the region has many environments and landscapes; Ranging from a snow desert wasteland, tall mountain rages, to the great rolling plains. 

Xerlona has a council that has 2 members from each nation as ambassadors. Which meet every few days to discuss issues to international trade. A regional currency has been brought up many times, but economically powerful nations reject this idea.

A war is going on in the region, between Zelon and the member states of Xerlona. Zelon is occupying and enforcing in some former states.
