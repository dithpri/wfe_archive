[b]What is the Treaty of Hope? [/b]

[b][color=rainbow]The Central Mission: The maintenance of international peace and security. By working to prevent conflict; helping parties in conflict make peace; peacekeeping; and creating the conditions to allow peace to hold and flourish. These activities often overlap and should reinforce one another, to be effective. [/color][/b]
