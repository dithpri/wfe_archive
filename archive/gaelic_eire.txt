Failte Gasur / Cailin
Welcome Boys and Girls that have created new nations on nationstates.

and way of life. You are free to join the region and leave at your own leisure. 
If you would like to become an officer in the region, please send a telegraph to
The Grand Irish Union for approval. We hope you will join our Celtic Region of 
GAELIC EIRE
