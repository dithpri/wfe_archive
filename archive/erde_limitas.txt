In an unprecedented event... the foundations of the multiverse were shattered, a collision between two ancient multiverses DR and RTHS... led to the complete extinction of RTHS with fragments becoming something new... while DR was irreparably devastated. The cosmic Impact merged fragments of these worlds into a new reality a singular and inseparable plane, where pieces of both existences survived intertwined, at first glance Erde Limitas appears to be a familiar world... its nations, cultures and geographies mimic what once existed in the original multiverses, but a deeper analysis reveals fissures... discreet anomalies, echoes of vanished eras and phenomena that escaped the known natural laws....
