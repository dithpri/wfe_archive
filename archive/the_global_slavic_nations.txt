[colour=purple][i]The Global Slavic Nations is an intergovernmental, globalist organization that was founded in 2016 by The Global United Nations. Our goal is to bring the Slavic cultures together to create a better, peaceful world. We do this by improving social issues, and reducing global conflicts.[/i][/colour]
