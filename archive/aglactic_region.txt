The Aglactic Region, forged by the four founding nations of Aglactica, Mainlandia, Opimer and Cranil, is a governing body forged in the ideals of ideological pluralism to forge a world of understanding and tolerance. 

The members of the Aglactic region seek to push this belief so that Nationstates can be a place of earnest political debate but also a place for all to benefit from shared interest and connection that transcends political disagreement. 

We do say this with understanding that some disagreements cannot be bridged, some ideas of ours will be rejected, but the promotion of human cooperation is central to our survival. The Aglactic region shall never run away from that guiding principle.
