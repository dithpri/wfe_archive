The continent of Tyrant is situated at an almost isolated place surrounded by an extremely vast deep ocean. The climate on the mainland ranges from the polar frost to the tropical heat, although most of the surface belongs to the mild or subtropical belt. 

The native inhabitants ancestors are believed to have come to the area in the prehistorical ages lead by an obscure being often referred to as Tyrant. The current long-term TCIC supreme representative is commonly believed to be the actual genuine Tyrant still alive using a mythical advanced technology. Propaganda is probably being extremely successful in this case.
