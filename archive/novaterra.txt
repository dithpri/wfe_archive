Nova Terra is &amp;quot;New Earth&amp;quot; in Latin. Earth is plunged into a nuclear winter. A great space station is built to house over 100,000 survivors. A hundred years after the nuclear winter ends Humans return to Earth, and start to rebuild. The population returns to 6.5 billion, vegitation and wildlife is restored, and governments arise from the ashes of there predecesors. The current year is 213, and countries begin to rebuild more nuclear weapons. Welcome to Nova Terra.[/color]
