The Hanseatic League is formed to protect economic, military, welfare and social interests. It is based on democracy rather then socialism or anarchy. This league is formed to protect not only the rights of the members of the league but of the world. This league will also make peace with other regions and protect there interests. It will create embassies with peaceful regions to pledge loyalty to them and to further have good diplomatic relations. A Hanseatic World Trade Center would be opened to promote economic interest and Hanseatic League is a free-trade nation. It will also protect interests of health, educational and information technology.
