[b][region]Inkopolis[/region][/b] is a major city in the region of Inkadia and the main setting of the Splatoon series. It is the primary home of the Inkling race. It can be accessed through a manhole located in the basement of Peach&#39;s Castle. In addition to Meggy, it is also Axol&#39;s hometown.
