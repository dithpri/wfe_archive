Crystal Court is IDS Center&#146;s award-winning 23,000 square foot urban park.

In 2021, Crystal Court underwent a $5 million renovation featuring collaborative seating areas with modular furniture, enhanced lighting, and a reflection pool amongst a towering glade of ficus trees.

Crystal Court welcomes nearly 50,000 visitors per day and access to an array of unique dining, shopping, service and hospitality options. Throughout the year, Crystal Court hosts non-profit and civic events and has been home to celebrations from the Super Bowl, Hollywood movie sets, and sports teams including the Minnesota Twins and the Timberwolves
