The region is unexplored and the only known power is the Federation of Warban. Warban is the dominating power of the region and new nations are allowed to join when they would like. The disputes over land can be solved diplomatically or with military force. The Federation of Warban does not get involved in other nations political or militaristic problems unless directly threated or an act of aggression is made towards The Federation. The Federation will also provide protection to young and developing nations that want to become part of the Federation of Warban.
