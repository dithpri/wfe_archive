Umbara is a planet from the Star Wars universe. It is an extremely dark and lightless planet, home to a variety of creatures which have adapted to life in the shadows, including the Umbarans. This planet was aligned with the Confederacy of Independent Systems during the Clone Wars, and was the site of a fierce battle between the Galactic Republic and the CIS.[/color]
