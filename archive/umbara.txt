Umbara is a planet from the Star Wars universe. Umbara is an extremely dark and light-less planet. This planet is home to a variety of creatures which have adapted to life in the shadows, including the [color=blue]Umbarans[/color], which have advanced technology. This planet was aligned with the Confederacy of Independent Systems, and was the site of a fierce battle between the Galactic Republic and CIS during the Galactic Civil War (The Clone Wars).
