The [region]Margraviate of Safov[/region] is a Region committed to human rights and justice on an international scale. It was founded by residents of the village of Safov who endured the horrors of World War II and vowed never to let war, fascism, and evil overtake the world again. The Margraviate opposes all Regions and Nations that threaten human rights or international peace, and is prepared to take action against them, as well as defend others from their aggression.
