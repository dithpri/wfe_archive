The [b]R&iacute;o de la Plata[/b], also called the River Plate or La Plata River, is the estuary formed by the confluence of the Uruguay River and the Paran&aacute; River at Punta Gorda. It empties into the Atlantic Ocean and forms a funnel-shaped indentation on the southeastern coastline of South America. The River Plate is the widest river in the world, with a maximum width of 220 kilometres (140 mi).
