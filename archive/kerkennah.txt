Kerkennah Islands are a group of islands lying off the east coast of Tunisia in the Gulf of Gabes, at 34&deg;42&amp;#39;N 11&deg;11&amp;#39;E. The Islands are low-lying being no more than 13 metres above sea level. The main islands are Chergui and Gharbi. The archipelago has an area of 160 square kilometres and 14,400 people.
