Avalon is a region defined by its unwavering commitment to democracy, freedom, and collective well-being. Its founding empire, Cirosia, leads by example, with a stable political landscape and strong emphasis on human rights. Governance in Avalon is transparent and participatory, ensuring that its citizens are active participants in decision-making processes.

Avalon includes Cirosia, Outer Ciros, Rizland, Rizlish Disappointment, Por Maslarcs, Hypaoic, Nivaran, Syndaran, Felinara, Townsend, Anthusia, Neo Unian, G-Fuel Sympathizers, Hunopoc.
