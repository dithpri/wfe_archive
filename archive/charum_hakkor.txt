The [b]Unified Human Empire of Charum Hakkor[/b] is a massive ancient civilization located inside an artificially created extradimensional subspace inversion bubble physically isolated from the universe. Thus [b]Charum Hakkor[/b] is one of the most isolated and secured civilizations existing.

[b]Charum Hakkor[/b] is holding some limited contacts with the [region]SICON[/region] Alliance and the [region]Therian IsoSphere[/region].
