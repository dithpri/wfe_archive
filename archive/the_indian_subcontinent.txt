The Indian Subcontinent (also known as South Asia) is situated on the Indian Plate, projecting southwards into the Indian Ocean from the Himalayas. Geopolitically, it includes the countries of [nation]Gateica[/nation], [nation]The Deccan Confederation[/nation], West Hindustan, [nation]Purvistan[/nation], Nepal, Bhutan, The Indus Republic, Bengal, [nation]Orissie Francaise[/nation], The Kingdom of [nation]Cashmira[/nation] and the North Eastern Commonwealth. The [nation]Indian International Congress[/nation] (IIC) was established in 1971 and is the primary international agency of the region.
