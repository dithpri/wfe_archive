ATC is a region that promotes cooperation and development among member countries in order to maintain peace and security in the region. The region is comprised of several countries that have come together to form a strong alliance, building links and working together to promote freedom and peace through military strength and defensive capabilities. ATC also facilitates joint military exercises and training programs to strengthen the collective defense capabilities of member countries. Through commitment to working together, ATC aims to maintain peace and promote freedom and democracy in the region, while deterring any potential threats or aggression. - Written by New Pan Am
