[hr]A tropical archipelago consisting of six main islands and thousands of smaller, minor islands. 

The six main islands are Serokuo (Hosuni), Temagoa, Suondeki, Tamikuo, Samogoa, and Eshigoa.

The regional language is Hodoran.
