[i]Bienvenue au le Commonwealth des R&eacute;gions Sayvilliennes[/i][/color][hr][hr][color=#F9CB13]The [b][region]Commonwealth of Sayvillian Regions[/region] (CSR)[/b] is a union between the [b][region]Kingdom of Sayville[/region] (KOS)[/b] and the [b][region]Kingdom of West Sayville[/region] (KWS)[/b], created to foster the development of a shared identity between the two regional communities. The CSR is headed by two symbolic [b]Co-Heads of the Commonwealth[/b] (physically represented in this region by their respective ambassadors), positions held by the two reigning monarchs of the KOS and KOWS, and act as the public figureheads of the organization.
