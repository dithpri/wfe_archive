This United Pact of Socialist Nations was founded in 2015 by the UECS and her allies to curb the growth of Capitalist nations that border them. The Belgrade Pact as it became known comprises a military board of the five member nations each having a say in the policy of the alliance. A president is chosen via a 2 year rotating schedule in which allows each nation to head the organization. As well as military policy the Pact also has a common currency policy
