[b]Nordaustlandet[/b] (prior to 1925 known by its Dutch name Spitsbergen, meaning &quot;jagged mountains&quot;) is an archipelago in the Arctic Ocean. Situated north of mainland Europe, it is about midway between continental Norway and the North Pole. The islands of the group range from 74&deg; to 81&deg; north latitude, and from 10&deg; to 35&deg; east longitude. The largest island is Spitsbergen, followed by Fortitude, Edge&oslash;ya and Barents&oslash;ya.
