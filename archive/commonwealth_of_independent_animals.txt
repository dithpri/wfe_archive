Commonwealth of Independent Animals is an international organisation dedicated to helping and cooperating with other animals on different continents that were previously under the control of other states or in the zoo. Commonwealth of Independent Animals helps other animals, whether they are predators, herbivores, fish or birds, the organisation has a large council that takes place in Africa and Europe, as well as in Asia and Antarctica, a small part in North America, in northern part, in South America the majority vote for animal support.
