The name stands for the Great British United Kingdom of Rathlin, Neagh, Ulster, Scotland, Skye and MacNeacailsburn, the Rathlese Dominions and the British Vassal States. It consists of three Realms which are, in order of importance, the United Kingdom (of Rathlin, Neagh, Ulster, Skye and MacNeacailsburn, and Scotland), the Rathlese Dominions, and the British Vassal States
