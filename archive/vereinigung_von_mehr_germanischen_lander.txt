The Vereinigung von mehr Germanischen Lander (Union of greater Germanic lands) is a pan-Germanic region focused around uniting Germanic nations that follow the traditional Kaiser-led system of the German Empire.

We do not accept stupidly named/flagged nations for example &#39;&#39;The Empire of Goku&#39;&#39; or &#39;&#39;The Free State of Animu Luverz&#39;&#39;.
