[color=654321][b]Cayman Islands[/b]
A British overseas territory located in the western Caribbean Sea.
Comprising the islands of Grand Cayman, Cayman Brac, and Little Cayman.
It is a global offshore financial services centre and one of the leading tourist scuba diving destinations in the world.

[b]Status:[/b] Split from Jamaica in 1962, when it became Independent from the UK in August 6, 1962.
