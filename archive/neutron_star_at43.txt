The [b][i]Neutron Star AT43[/b][/i] is a standard [i]neutron star[/i] with about 2 solar masses and a size of about 20km. It is located at the fringe of the galactic halo (milky way galaxy).

The [b][i]Neutron Star AT43[/b][/i] is home of an extremely advanced,  quantum-sized [i]nanobot-civilization[/i] - the [b][i][nation=long]United Nanobots[/nation][/b][/i].
