The Sea of Solitude is a large inland sea surrounded by steppes and mountains. It is salt water but it has no outlet to the world&#39;s major oceans. In ancient times, many inhabitants of the lands along the borders of the Sea believed it to be an ocean given its size and its saltiness. It can often be a quiet, lonely place. Hence the name.

This is a roleplaying region open to whomever is willing to go with the theme. It is just a geographical region. It does not and will not have a regional government except whatever might be provided for in treaties created in roleplay. It is not a closed world. It is simply one region in the wider world and it interacts with all the world. The delegate does not have power so as to avoid the the raiding game.
