The [b]Subspace Alliance Network[/b] (short: Subspace Alliance) is a free Alliance of Civilizations of otherwise isolated parallel Universes (Multiverses). The members of the Alliance Network are among the most remote and isolated civilizations existing.

The [b]Subspace Alliance[/b] is holding some very limited low profile contacts with the &#39;[region]SICON[/region] Alliance&#39;.
