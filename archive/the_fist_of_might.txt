Region open to all ruling types with many officer posts for shared power. Regional colours are red and blue. There are 2 councils in the region, the B2 consisting of the Founder and WA delegate, and the Tribunal which is made up of 3 Tribunal Officers. The B2 is permanent but the Tribunal is elected and has the power to pass or veto laws passed by the B2. Please endorse The Founder and The WA Delegate.
