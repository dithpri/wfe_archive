[b][color=#1e2a63]ELLOREA[/color][/b], a region blanketed with fields of [b]endless verdure[/b] cushioned between the warm oceans of Etreu and Triec, is a diverse, [b]stunning, and heavenly region[/b]. Housing both democratic utopias and tyrannical dystopias, Ellorea remains a home for many [b]diverse political systems[/b] bound together as a [b]community[/b] of geographically linked nations and an intertwining political community.
