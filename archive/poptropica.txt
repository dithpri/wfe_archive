Poptropica is an online role-playing game, developed in 2007 by Pearson Education&#39;s Family Education Network, and targeted towards children aged 6 to 15. [i]Poptropica[/i] was primarily the creation of Jeff Kinney, the author of the [i]Diary of a Wimpy Kid[/i] series. As of 2015, he remains at the company as Creative Director.
