           The District Region is a one that has a firm belief in protection of its people.  The advancement of its interior technology and methods of business are of the most concern.  The District will continue to grow with increasing firmness but fairness to all who shall enter into it.  
           What was once a great empire is now a small but determined region intending to rebuild upon itself and continue to advance and grow.  The nations inside of this great region will all have their own share of responsibilities.  The teamwork way of life is strongly encouraged.
