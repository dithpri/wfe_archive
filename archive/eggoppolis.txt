Egg Incorporated&#39;s attempt to return to monkey through the Monke portal failed, but from it, Eggoppolis was found. The portal opened into a curious world with a multitude of Civilisation similar to that of Earth. It is currently unknown if the dimension and all its people, resources, and civilization were suddenly willed into existence by the Monke portal, or if this simply opened the gateway to a lost dimension. What is known is the vast, bountiful region of Eggoppolis is surely to become a contender on the world stage in years to come.
