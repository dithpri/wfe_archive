If you are looking for a brand new region upon which to make your mark then the CIU is the region for you. This region is for those who want an alliance of nations that can rise up and challenge the other regions. This region proposes titles and honors and is for those who take this seriously, however we accept all nations as long as they do not cause trouble. We are an active region with a functioning government and a constitution. We do not, however support any human rights and all dictatorships are encouraged to join.
