Alternate history of [region]the Confederacy of Free Lands[/region].

In the early 1900s, instead of supporting dictator Padraig O&#39;Hara, the Eireannian Peoples threw off the chains of Capitalism and joined their neighbours to the south as a communist state. The fall of Greater Eireann first sparked massive riots against the Gandhistani governments which then spread to the rest of the Union of Religious Nations. Faced with this, these states, led by Greater Gandhistan, must attempt to counter this new and radical threat to their established structures.

The year is currently 1922 A.D.
