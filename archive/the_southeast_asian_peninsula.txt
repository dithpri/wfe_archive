The Peninsula of Asia is Dominated by five powers, The Great and Wise Terrence, Usthar, Bloodbather, North K0rea, and Eur-Asia. There are many smaller countries in the Southeast Asian Peninsula, however they do not hold the power the main states hold. The Southeast Asian Peninsula is united under one religion, except for North K0rea who has banned religion and is at constant war with the other four superpowers.
