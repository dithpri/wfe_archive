[region]The Assembly of Apex Legion States[/region] (AALS) is an assembly formed to group up legions with similar beliefs of governing the people. The Assembly would be formed to help legions have a major voice in other Assemblies and have socialist/conservative governing patterns. Together this assembly of the top legion states and empires would join together to conquer and have the highest power possible over the people. 

Wants to :
Increase Defense / military
Have a strong voice in international governments
conquer and rule as many governments as possible
Applicable to change with the vote
