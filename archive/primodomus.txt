One of the oldest regions has recently just come out of a period of strife and anarchy caused by the collapse of the Sectatores losing control of her colonies and falling into anarchy. Pieces of this old empire now pick up the pieces and try to carve out a place for themselves while new contenders arrive and found new nations of their own out of the carcass. Only time will tell if these nations can peacefully coexist, or if peace will need to be established through force.
