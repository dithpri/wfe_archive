[b]Small Description[/b]
the United Regions of Casispiera (another name for the region), is an isolated region that intentionally cut itself from the outside world, it is also an intergovernmental organization, as they want order and peace to be still in place.

[b]Important Note[/b]
No embassies are to be expected to be built here, and any intruders will be intercepted, detained, or strike down by the Casispieran International Coast Guards.
