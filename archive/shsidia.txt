The lovely Shsidia rests between the lesser regions of Nanzhanic and Gaojia. The entire region is marked by its beautiful environment, grass, and koi. Of particular interest is the presence numbers ranging 60-100. Scientists have determined that numbers closer to 100 can be achieved by supplication to those with white board markers and through diligent hunching over sheets of paper. Although an array of peoples and nations exist within the region it still exists under one system of excellence.
