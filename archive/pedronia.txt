The Pedronian Region is comprised of a small island chain in the middle of the Atlantic Ocean. The largest of the islands, [nation=long]Alma Ruma[/nation], resides in the northernmost section of the region. 

In Pedronia, we abide by a certain set of rules and laws as stated in the Pedronian Accords, passed by the Parliament of Alma Ruma. The Pedronian Accords can be found under Legislation on the [nation=long]Alma Ruma[/nation] page.
