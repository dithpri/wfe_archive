[i]Canis Major: is a constellation in the southern celestial hemisphere. In the second century, it was included in Ptolemy&#39;s 48 constellations, and is counted among the 88 modern constellations. Its name is Latin for &quot;greater dog&quot; in contrast to Canis Minor, the &quot;lesser dog&quot;; both figures are commonly represented as following the constellation of Orion the hunter through the sky.[/i][b]Founded by the andarian starfleet on: 04/05/2025.[/b]
