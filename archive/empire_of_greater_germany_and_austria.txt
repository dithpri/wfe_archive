The [color=red]Empire of Greater Germany and Austria[/color] formed when the German Empire and Austria-Hungary united to form a powerful state, after a victory in World War One. Each ethnicity has an autonomous ethnic zone, with autonomy at 65%. The nation is allied to the Constitutional Monarchy of Switzerland, FNTP (France) and the Kalmar Union.
