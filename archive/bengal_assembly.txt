[color=green]Bengal ;Bengali: &amp;#2476;&amp;#2494;&amp;#2434;&amp;#2482;&amp;#2494;/&amp;#2476;&amp;#2457;&amp;#2509;&amp;#2455;, romanized: B&amp;#257;ngl&amp;#257;/B&ocirc;ng&ocirc;, pronounced [&amp;#712;b&amp;#596;&amp;#331;go]) is a geopolitical, cultural and historical region in South Asia, specifically in the eastern part of the Indian subcontinent at the apex of the Bay of Bengal, predominantly covering present-day Bangladesh and the Indian state of West Bengal. Geographically, it is made up by the Ganges-Brahmaputra delta system, the largest river delta in the world, along with mountains in its north bordering the Himalayan states of Nepal and Bhutan, and east bordering Myanmar.
