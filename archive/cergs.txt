The humble nation of Cergs contains an interesting mix of ideologies of seemingly opposing moralities which nevertheless managed to somehow co-operate for long enough to form a union of sorts, with a technocratically-inclined leader desperately grasping at straws in the process of attempting to balance the stresses of international co-operation and co-ordination, dealing with the policies the World Assembly throws out, and trying to de-escalate tensions between Cergs&#39; constituent nations.
