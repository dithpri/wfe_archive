A loose collection of nations, none of which are based in the South Atlantic or even allied, but use their power to protect themselves from larger regional alliances.

Vorunda, a small and inoffensive state, founded the alliance and still unofficially leads it. However, NationState LTD is the face of the alliance globally and in the World Assembly, controlled by the other nations as Shareholders. Officially, the fellow nations of the Alliance boycott the World Assembly, but still exert some influence through the delegate.
