Region type: Political and economic union of Arab Gulf states
Abbreviation: GCC
Headquarters: Al Maraziq City, Emirate of Al Maraziq

The Gulf Cooperation Council (GCC) is a regional alliance dedicated to fostering unity, stability, and shared prosperity among the nations of the Arabian Gulf.
Founded and led by the Emirate of Al Maraziq, the Council serves as a platform for cooperation in political, economic, cultural, and defense affairs. The GCC promotes collective progress through modernization and partnership, encouraging all Gulf nations with shared values and aspirations to join its vision of regional integration.
