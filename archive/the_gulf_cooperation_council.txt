The [colour=green]Gulf Cooperation Council (GCC)[/colour] is a regional alliance dedicated to fostering unity, stability, and shared prosperity among the nations of the Arabian Gulf.

[hr]Founded and led by [nation=long]al maraziq[/nation], the Council serves as a platform for cooperation in political, economic, cultural, and defense affairs. The GCC promotes collective progress through modernization and partnership.
