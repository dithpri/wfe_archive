The World of [region]Auraxis[/region] is the planet where the massive war between the [b]Terran Republic[/b], the [b]New Conglomerate[/b], and the Vanu Sovereignty take place. It consists of the four continents, [b]Indar[/b], a dry evaporated ocean, [b]Esamir[/b], a frozen flatland, [b]Amerish[/b], a lush mountainous area, and [b]Hossin[/b], a thick overgrown swampish continent.
