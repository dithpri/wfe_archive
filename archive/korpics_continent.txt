The Democratic Republic of KORPICS LAND is a fledgling, socially progressive nation, notable for its irreverence towards religion. The hard-working population of 5 million KORPICS LANDians have some civil rights, but not too many, enjoy the freedom to spend their money however they like, to a point, and take part in free and open elections, although not too often.

The relatively small government juggles the competing demands of Welfare, Defense, and Law &amp; Order. The average income tax rate is 15.3%.
