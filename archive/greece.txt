Greece, known since ancient times as Hellas, is a country in southeastern Europe.  Located on the southern tip of the Balkan peninsula, it is situated at the strategic crossroads of Europe, Asia, and Africa.  It is the birthplace of democracy, Western philosophy, the Olympic Games, Western literature, historiography, political science, major scientific and mathematical principles, and Western drama, including both tragedy and comedy.  The cultural and technological achievements of Greece have greatly influenced the shaping of the world.[/b]
