[b]Greece, known since ancient times as Hellas, is a country in southeastern Europe.  Located at the strategic crossroads of Europe, Asia, and Africa, it is situated on the southern tip of the Balkan peninsula.

Greece is considered to be the cradle of all Western civilization. As such, Greece is the birthplace of democracy, Western philosophy, the Olympic Games, Western literature, historiography, political science, major scientific and mathematical principles, and Western drama, including both tragedy and comedy.  The cultural and technological achievements of Greece have greatly influenced the shaping of the world.[/b]
