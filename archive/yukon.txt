[color=green][b]Yukon[/b], also known as The Yukon Territory, is one of Canada&#39;s three territories, in the country&#39;s far northwest, is named after the Yukon River, which means &quot;great river&quot;.
The Klondike Gold Rush (Aug 1896) was the seminal event in Yukon&#39;s history, resulting in the separation from Northwest Territories to better control the situation.
