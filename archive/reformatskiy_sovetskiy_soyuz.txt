After the downfall of the Soviet Union in [b]1991[/b], The Russian Federation was formed. Soon, after Vladimir Putin lost to Alexei Navalny in [b]2018[/b], corruption started running deep in the roots of the glorious country. After 5 years of corruption and riots in the streets, a group of ultra-nationalists decided it was time to strike. Over the years, the Bolsheviks gained enough funds to attack. The people, fueled with rage at the collapsing government also thought it was time for a change. Rising up with the Soviet-Nationalists, the people and the Bolsheviks stormed the Kremlin, and assassinated the president, Alexei Navalny. This formed the [nation]Reformed Soviets[/nation].
