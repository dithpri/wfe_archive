[b]Wolfram &amp; Hart [/b]is a powerful international and interdimensional law firm. As of 2023, the firm had branches in every major city in the world, as well as satellite offices in many areas of the world where they helped spread oppression and evil. On September 13, 2023, the firm was reopened under new management.

In addition to the many legal functions the firm performs, Wolfram &amp; Hart also maintains a Special Projects Division, Real Estate, Entertainment, Practical Science, Research and Intelligence, and Interment Acquisitions.
