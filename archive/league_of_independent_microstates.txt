[b][color=#2c5aa0]LEAGUE OF INDEPENDENT MICROSTATES[/color][/b]

The [b]League of Independent Microstates[/b] is an inter-governmental organization that lets smaller nations have their voices heard on the world stage. Membership in the League is available to all nations with an area of less than 5000 square km and a population of less than 1 million, regardless of geographic location or government type. Official working languages are English, French, Italian, and Spanish.

The current Secretary-General is [b]H.R.H. Princess V&eacute;ronique of S&eacute;bourg[/b].
