[color=gold]United Coalition Of Pacific Nations[/color][hr]

The United Coalition Of Pacific Nations is an intergovernmental organization within the [region]The North Pacific[/region]. The purposes for which the UCPN was created; is to create international peace throughout the region, and also to maintain international security between the respective nations of the region. It is also created to cater for humanitarian problems, promote and improve the respect for human rights, to solve economic situations and generally to develop friendly relations among nations.[hr]
