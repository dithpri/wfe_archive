After years of searching, we have discovered that this region does not exist. For reasons that should be obvious, the general population of its constituent nations is not aware of this fact.
The most widely accepted theory is that the Toaster of Rassilon (our overlord and master) grew jealous of its more well known associates the Rod of Rassilon, the Sash of Rassilon, and the Great Key of Rassilon. When the Polka Dot Pyjamas of Rassilon were mentioned it gave up and decided to stop existing.
