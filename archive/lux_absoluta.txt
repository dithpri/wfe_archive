Lux Absoluta
The Radiant Dominion

Lux Absoluta is a prosperous region famed for its sunlit cities, golden spires, and flourishing culture. Governed by King Lance of San Venganza, it serves as a hub of trade, art, and innovation. Known as a symbol of power and enlightenment, Lux Absoluta combines wealth, beauty, and vigilance, making it a beacon of influence in the region.
