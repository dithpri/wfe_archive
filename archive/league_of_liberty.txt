The League of Liberty, founded on June 18, 2019 by the President of Libertiria, Eleftherio Machitis, is a region in support of ideologies of liberty, freedom, and the spread of such ideas. Machitis&#39; vision is of a world where all people in all countries are free to express themselves religiously, personally, and without any involvement from government. The League despises taxation, tyranny, censorship, and corruption.

The League will always accept new members who share its beliefs.
