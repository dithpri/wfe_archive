[color=blue]Pok&eacute;mon, also known as Pocket Monsters in Japan, is a media franchise managed by The Pok&eacute;mon Company, a Japanese consortium between Nintendo, Game Freak, and Creatures.

The franchise was created by Satoshi Tajiri in 1995, and is centered on fictional creatures called &quot;Pok&eacute;mon&quot;, which humans, known as Pok&eacute;mon Trainers, catch and train to battle each other for sport.

- Second best-selling video game franchise.
- World&#39;s top-selling toy brand.
- Top-selling trading card game.
- Anime television series that has become the most successful video game adaptation with over 20 seasons and 1,000 episodes.
- Anime film series, a live-action film, books, manga comics, music, and merchandise.[/color]
