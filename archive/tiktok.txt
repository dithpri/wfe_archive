[i][b]TikTok[/b], known in China as Douyin (Chinese: [b]&amp;#25238;&amp;#38899;[/b]; pinyin: D&amp;#466;uy&amp;#299;n), is a video-sharing social networking service owned by Chinese company [b]ByteDance[/b]. The social media platform is used to make a variety of short-form videos, from genres like dance, comedy, and education, that have a duration from three seconds to one minute (three minutes for some users). [b]TikTok[/b] is an international version of [b]Douyin,[/b] which was originally released in the Chinese market in [b]September 2016[/b].
