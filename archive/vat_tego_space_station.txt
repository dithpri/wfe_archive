Vat Tego is a large Space Station above the planet [Entry Removed] that is primarily used for trade. All manner of Spacers, Mercenaries, Bounty Hunters, Smugglers, Corporations, and Traders pass through on their trips throughout the galaxy. Primarily whoever has the most credits controls the station, but officially there is no ruler of Vat Tego. Crime is reletively low due to the presence of gun-toting outlaws and guns-for-hire who are always looking for a fight and are willing to put down would-be criminals for a small fee. The station is open to all space-faring nations but Vat Tego is always prepared for invasion...
