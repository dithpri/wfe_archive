We, the United Nations of Alfheim, do hereby attest that we hold these tenents holy: That every citizen may speak or practice their beliefs as they wish, so long as such practice does not hurt or damage another citizen.
That each citizen has the right to expression of their authentic self, and has the right to understand that said expression is allowable.
That the rights of the people are more holy than the rights of the state. 
And that All Citizens Are, At Heart, Elves.
