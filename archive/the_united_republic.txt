The United Republic is a group of nations with the same goverment, but each also has a governor and council. It works similary to the United Kingdom. These nation are the Republic of Spengland and South Toiwan, the Principality of the United Islands of Homodi, the Protectorate of Spenglish East Oman, the Collective Islands of New Japan, the Federation of the Islands of the Azores and the Administrative Region of West Brest
