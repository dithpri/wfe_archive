The International Union of Socialist Youth, founded in 1907, is the biggest political youth organization in the world representing about 140 member organizations from more than 100 countries. IUSY is the Youth of the Socialist International. It brings together the socialist, social democratic and labour political youth organizations from around the world in order to fight for freedom and human rights, social justice and democracy, peaceful solutions to political problems, and ending all forms of discrimination. IUSY works by developing and strengthening its member organizations in different countries, supporting new organizations, publishing bulletins, and organizing international youth gatherings each year.
