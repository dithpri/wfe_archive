[region]Newingtonia[/region] was founded on the 3rd of May 2018.

It is a colony of the [region]Dynasty of Yikestopia[/region].  However, it still largely retains its unique Newingtonian cultural heritage, and citizens look up to the Wyvern and Southern Cross as a symbol of pride and hope.

The Newingtonia Regional Government is the governing body of the region.  It is led by a Governor, George Edmundson, who also holds the title of Governor of the Protectorate of Edmundian Newingtonia.
