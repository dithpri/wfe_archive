The Digital Confederation was founded by First Citizen Bill Casey of the Republic of Brecago on January 9, 2010. He sought to join the nations of his friends, and those they deemed cool, in an association. Its purpose is to facilitate comparison of one another&amp;#39;s results on World Assembly reports and to secure a common World Assembly delegation. All members retain absolute sovereignty over their domestic affairs, no matter how ridiculous their policies.

To prevent invasion, the regional password will change frequently.
