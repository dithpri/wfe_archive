[region]Villach[/region] is the seventh-largest city in [region]Oesterreich[/region] (Austria) and the second-largest in the federal state of Carinthia.
It is located on the Drava (Drau) River near the confluence with the Gail tributary. Situated at the western rim of the [region]Klagenfurt am Worthersee[/region] basin, the municipal area stretches from the slopes of the Gailtal Alps (Mt. Dobratsch) down to Lake Ossiach in the northeast.[/b]
