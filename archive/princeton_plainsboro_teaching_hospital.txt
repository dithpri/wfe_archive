Princeton-Plainsboro Teaching Hospital (PPTH) is a nationally recognized, research-driven academic medical center located in Princeton, New Jersey. Known for its pioneering work in diagnostic medicine, PPTH is affiliated with Princeton University and serves as a hub for cutting-edge medical research, clinical innovation, and physician training.

The hospital is consistently ranked among the top teaching hospitals in the northeastern United States, both for patient care outcomes and research contributions. It combines a rigorous academic environment with advanced clinical care, offering a full spectrum of medical services from emergency trauma to complex multi-system diseases.
