The International Third Position Alliance is a transnational coalition unified by the principles of the Third Position, an ideology standing apart from both capitalism and communism. The Alliance advocates for a society grounded in traditionalism, nationalism, and authoritarian governance, promoting a world order based on organic unity, cultural sovereignty, and a rejection of materialism. Comprising movements, organizations, and entities from various nations, the Alliance operates on a shared commitment to maintaining the identity and self-determination of each member group within a framework of ideological brotherhood.
