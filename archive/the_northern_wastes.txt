A land of varied climates, it stretches from arid deserts to frozen arctic tundras. The territory was always considered to be uninhabitable, not just because of its harsh landscape, but also because of the high amounts of radiation in certain areas caused by weapons testing. However, brave colonists, fleeing refugees and violent segregationists managed to carve out an existence in this hostile land. Those exiles and outcasts went on to found the nations that now reside in The Northen Wastes.
