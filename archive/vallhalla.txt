The glorious region of Vallhalla was founded March 3rd, 2016. This large land mass, founded by the Ordesean Empire and Tannenbergia, is a prospering and forever expanding region. The Ordesean Empire, located in the middle of the region, is the tourist destination. Tannenbergia, to the West of Ordesia, is the major economic contributor for the region. On the eastern side, The Armed Republic of The Petruccionian Republic is the major military power of the region and along with Tannenbergia, protect the region from invaders.
