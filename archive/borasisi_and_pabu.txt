66652 Borasisi, or as a binary (66652) Borasisi-Pabu, is a binary classical Kuiper belt object. It was discovered in September 1999 by Chad Trujillo, Jane X. Luu and David C. Jewitt[5] and identified as a binary in 2003 by K. Noll and colleagues using the Hubble Space Telescope. Belonging to [region]Planet Andarios[/region]!
