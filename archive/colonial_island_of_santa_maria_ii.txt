Colonial Island of Santa Maria II

- Taken on July 26, 1918
- Political Regime: Parliamentary (capital)
- Local Regime: Temporary General Government elected locally
- Capital: Imperial Duchy of Campinass (parliament)
- Local Capital: Verdun

- Belonging to the Crown of S&atilde;o Paulo and Ducados, this island has conditions of residence and the semi-autonomous government, with residents guaranteeing various manifestations and provisional government for an indeterminate time.
