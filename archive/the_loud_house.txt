The Loud House is an American animated television series and media franchise created by Chris Savino, and produced by Nickelodeon Animation Studio for Nickelodeon. It is the parent show of The Casagrandes. The series is inspired by Savino&#39;s own &quot;chaotic life growing up in a huge household,&quot; and follows a boy named Lincoln, who lives at home with his ten sisters, Lori, Leni, Luna, Luan, Lynn, Lucy, Lana, Lola, Lisa, and Lily.
