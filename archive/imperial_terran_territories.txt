This region is home to all states who recognize [nation]Glorious Terran Empire[/nation] as the sovereign and legitimate rulers of Terra and guardians of all humanity.

The terms of accepting are simple, submit to Imperial laws above all else, acknowledge the Imperial decree should a member state fight another member state, never attack the sovereign allies of the Empire, and submit 10% annual budget to the Empire as tribute.

To join, contact the Terran Magistrates and publicly swear fealty to the Empire.

In exchange for loyalty, member states have the assurance of security and safety from outside threats, free trade and migration to and from all member states, and most importantly of all, representation in the Imperial Senate.
