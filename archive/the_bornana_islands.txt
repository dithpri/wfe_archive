We can rhyme orange with banana, bornana.
The archipelago of Bornana is a collection of around seven islands occupied by three different nations, these islands are located almost right outside the western coasts of the Scandinavian region. 
The union of the USS, military area of the NSM and the free island of Bornana proper, these are the three nations that occupy and reside on these islands.
After finally coming together in 1924 to sign an agreement to provide each other with mutual respect and agreeing to maintain trade, open borders and peace, The treaty of Barange was signed.
