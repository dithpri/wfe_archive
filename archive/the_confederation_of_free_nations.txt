The Confederation of Free Nations (or [b]CFN[/b]) is a group of free countries that value strong civil and political freedoms. Whether or not you&#39;re a capitalist, socialist, or communist, you&#39;re welcome here as long as your citizens are treated well. No tyrannical, authoritarian, or absolute monarchal states are allowed to join the [b]CFN[/b], as they violate the 3 values of the [b]CFN[/b]:

[color=gold]Freedom[/color].
[color=darkblue]Liberty[/color].
[color=darkred]Justice[/color].

[i]Note: You do NOT have to be a democracy to join.[/i]
