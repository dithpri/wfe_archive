[b][u]Who are we?[/u][/b]
Here you can find a group different minded individuals that share a common interest in politics, and debate. We focus on discussing various topics of all sorts in a respectable manner with open minds.
[b][u]World Assembly Voting[/u][/b]
To the nations in our region that are members of the World Assembly, it will help you to know that all delegate votes made on behalf of Arraragis Harem will be purely democratic. It is in our best interest that the majority of the region is represented fairly.
[b][u]Debates[/u][/b]
The main reason why this region even exists is to provide an area for actual debate on various topics. Some debates may be over recent real world problems that interest us. If you ever feel impelled to discuss something you should go there.
