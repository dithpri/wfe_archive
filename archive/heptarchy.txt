The [url=http://www.nationstates.net/nation=seaxe/detail=factbook/id=76277]Heptarchy[/url] is an economic and political union of 7 member states which are located primarily in Albion. The Heptarchy operates through a system of supranational independent institutions and intergovernmental negotiated decisions by the member states. Important institutions of the Heptarchy include the Heptarchy Commission, the Heptarchy Council, the Heptarchy Central Bank and the Heptarchy Parliament.

The [url=http://www.nationstates.net/nation=seaxe/detail=factbook/id=76277]Heptarchy[/url] has developed a single market, a monetary union (Pound Zone) and is represented at the World Assembly.

[b]Dual-Presidency of the Heptarchy[/b]: Pres. Herman Van Rompuy (Heptarchy Council) AND Pres. Jos&eacute; Manuel Barroso (Heptarchy Commission)
