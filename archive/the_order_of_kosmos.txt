The Order of Kosmos is a secretive cult organization which operates throughout Greece in the 21st century. The Cult worships a deity called Kosmos and we plot to control the Hellenic world from behind the scenes for our own interests. To that end, our members infiltrated every sector of Greek society, even forcing the Pythia to be our puppet. We were directly responsible for catalyzing the Peloponnesian War, which we hope to prolong as much as possible by manipulating both sides of the conflict.
