Meropis is land situated beyond the world-ocean (Oceanus). Its inhabitants, the M&eacute;ropes, are twice as tall as other human beings and live twice as long.

Meropis is a parody of Plato&amp;#39;s Atlantis, and is mentioned in the work &amp;quot;Philippica&amp;quot;  by the ancient writer Theopompus of Chios.
