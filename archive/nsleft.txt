[b]NS[color=red]Left[/color][/b] is an alliance uniting left-wing regions for purposes of building inter-regional solidarity amongst comrades, and general advancement of the left-wing cause within NationStates. To this end, it features common forums for inter-regional communication, an organizational newspaper, internal workgroups dedicated to writing World Assembly resolutions, as well as national issues, and active participation in military gameplay.
