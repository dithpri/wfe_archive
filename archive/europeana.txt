The Conferedation of Europeana is consisted of the fledgling, pleasant nations (Inoffensive Centrist Democracies), notable for their burgeoning population. The hard-working population of 25 million Europeanans have some civil rights, but not too many, enjoy the freedom to spend their money however they like, to a point, and take part in free and open elections, although not too often. 

The relatively small (or medium-sized in case of Visegrad and Southern Europeana) governments of each nation juggles the competing demands of Defense, Healthcare, and Welfare.

Crime is moderate, with the police force struggling against a lack of funding and a high mortality rate.
