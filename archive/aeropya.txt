The Confederation of Aeropya was created at the beginning of the XIXe century following the Seventh Coalition War against the Pact Of Steel. By the Treaty of Shinza, the loose alliance became a confederation. Its first members were [nation]Heterotopya[/nation], [nation]Nanban-Ha[/nation] and [nation]Chorussia[/nation] soon join by [nation]Englaria[/nation], [nation]Carandia[/nation], [nation]Elefstani[/nation] and later [nation]Kalkaryia[/nation] and [nation]Achmenia[/nation].
