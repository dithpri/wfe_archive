After the decline and subsequent collapse of Kingsman Union, in part due to uncooperative governments and growing tensions between fellow Union members, there was a period of isolation or semi-isolation for these nations. However, after the return of stable relations between The Esteemed Fatherland Brand-Land and The Kingdom of Hungarian-people, two former superior Kingsman Union members, a new Union was established in order to preserve dominance over their nations and respective regions. 

This was the Order of the Four-Arrows, who mission statement was assert dominance; economically, culturally, scientifically and ethnically.
