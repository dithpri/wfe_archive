The United Democratic Union of Albathlan Republics (UDUAR), recognized officially as The Special Autonomous Administrative Divisions of Albathla (SAADA) by the World Assembly and other entities, is a transcontinental de jure sovereign political entity. With 4 major &quot;Special Autonomous Divisions&quot; and millions of scattered territorial enclaves and exclaves, the majority being tiny &quot;semi-autonomous estates&quot; owned by the upper-class families of the Imperial Knights.
