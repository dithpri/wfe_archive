The Crusiable Colosseum is located in the eastern North Atlantic Ocean just off the coast of Nazare&#39; Portugal.  It is built upon a giant artificial foundation created by the Calebs Haven Corps in 1995. The Colosseum is host to many athletic events such as the Olympics, Iron Man Competitions, extreme sports, but most of the sports center around martial arts and mixed martial arts. 

The Colosseum is the site for which champions desire to reach in order to test their medal against the Crusiable.  Even The Seven Capital Cities of Heaven meet at The Crusiable Colosseum  to compete for the title of The Iron Fist.
