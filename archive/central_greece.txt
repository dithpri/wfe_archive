[b]Welcome, excellent nations of the World! This represents the principle [u]geographic[/u] region of Greece, with the prefectures of Acarnania, Aetolia, Attica, Boeotia, Euboea, Evrytania, Phocis, and Phthiotis.

The national ambition of the Hellenic people is to unite in one central locality. The dream is to create a free and prosperous realm for Hellenism.

Greeks of all places are invited to live in our pleasant community. Our highly developed industries are among the best in tourism, merchant shipping, and agriculture. We also accept other nationalities friendly to worthy Greek causes.[/b]
