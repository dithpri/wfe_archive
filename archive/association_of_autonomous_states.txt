The [colour=royalblue][b]Association of Autonomous States (AAS)[/b][/colour] is a sub-region of the [region]Association of Liberal Democratic States[/region] that is home to the disputed territories/autonomous units within member states of the ALDS, as stipulated in the [b][i]Independent Paper on the Decolonisation and Independence of Non-Self Governing Territories of the ALDS[/i][/b]. We Declare our perpetual neutrality and will accept most embassy requests, as long as they adhere to our own democratic and egalitarian values.

[b]NOTE:[/b] Region currently under reconstruction.
