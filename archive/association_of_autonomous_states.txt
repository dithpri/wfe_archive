The [b]Association of Autonomous States (AAS)[/b] is a separate entity, independent from the [colour=royalblue][b]Association of Liberal Democratic States (ALDS)[/b][/colour] of which it derived from, that is home to the disputed territories within member states of the ALDS, as stipulated in the [b][i]Independent Paper on the Decolonisation and Independence of Non-Self Governing Territories of the ALDS[/i][/b]. We Declare our perpetual neutrality and will accept most embassy requests, as long as they adhere to our own democratic and egalitarian values.
