From small nations to sprawling civilizations touting splendid acropolises, The Cosmic Order is centered around one, sole objective: conquering new frontiers and creating diverse spheres of influence. The Cosmic Order&#39;s final aim is to bring the entirety of neighboring regions under our flag, exercise great power in the World Assembly, and set our collective gaze towards the stars. Each nation is run independently, without coercion from allied forces. 

Will you coalesce to bring our Great Dream to fruition? Will you join to bring to your nation great prosperity and global influence? The Cosmic Order is your home.
