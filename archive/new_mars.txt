New Mars is a quiet, peaceful backwater where one can still hear birds singing and the wind sighing in the trees.  It is inhabited by Nations that seem on the whole to be content with the peaceful life and see no reason to go rushing headlong into the 21st Century ... or the 18th, for that matter.  

Artists&amp;#39; renditions invariably involve a hay wain or windmill, and in one notable case, both.
