NewYork&#150;Presbyterian Hospital is a university hospital in New York City affiliated with two Ivy League medical schools: Columbia University&amp;#39;s College of Physicians and Surgeons and Cornell University&amp;#39;s Weill Medical College. It is composed of two distinct medical centers, Columbia University Medical Center and Weill Cornell Medical Center. A major international and regional referral center, the hospital is considered among the best in the world and is currently ranked 7th in the nation by U.S. News and World Report.
