Xanestria is a parallel dimension in which the inhabitants living on it are multiculturally integrated albeit sharing similar problems of a post-modern society. Scientific communities, magical academies, religious tolerance of most faiths, the rise of AI, and the majority of species on the planet coexist in harmony thanks to many radical reforms. Set in the 2030s, only a handful of developing countries has fully embraced the future whilst the rest still struggle amid an increasingly warming world.

Travel to and out of this region is [color=red]mostly restricted unless absolutely necessary to debate[/color], we are now ready to tackle Z-Day by having a poll as of now and will expire when the event begins. Our borders are kept open as a challenge but guarded.
