The lands of Scumberia are a patches motley of different enviroments, from the lush and mountainous northern jungles to the temperate eastern bamboo forest, and the harsh, and rocky southern salt flats.  Full of natural resources, the most famous of which being the supplies of hemp growing unchecked in the wilderness, the heavily uncharted and wild lands teeming with dangerous fauna.

Claimed by the nation of Scumistan in order to fulfill a divine message, the large island is divinely said to become home to a large network of alliances and nations, all of which are to be ready to receive the holy word of the Axe.
