[b]Welcome to the United Left[/b][hr] 

Founded in 2026, The United Left is a socially conservative, economically centre-left political region committed to national unity, strong families, and community cohesion. It combines support for workers, public services, and strategic economic intervention with a belief in traditional values, personal responsibility, patriotism, and law and order. Rejecting both free-market extremism and progressive social radicalism, The United Left advocates a balanced approach that protects national identity, strengthens communities, and delivers economic security and opportunity for all citizens.
