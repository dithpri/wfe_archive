Section I- Admission

Nations WA and non-WA may join the region,and in most cases both WA and Non-WA members have a right to vote:

1- All nations may vote for bills being voted by regional poll.
2-However only WA nations may have the right to run for WA Delegate and vote for WA Delegate unlike Non-WA nations and WA nations are recommended to endorse the WA Delegate.
3-Every New Nation which has been on more than a week has to apply to the Minister of Citizenship for a Citizenship
4-Only if a nation has citizenship it will be able to have a spot on map, vote in polls and run for official spots.
