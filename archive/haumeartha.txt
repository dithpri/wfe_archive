A New Birth

The year is 3517ce, The fallout and from World War IV (~2500ce) has cleared, and the polar caps have reformed exposed land long lost. Humanity and the technology of the ancients has been able to survived in small pockets throughout the world, although petroleum products are practically non-existent. The people who survived and their descendants are hardy, resourceful, and have come to value peace and care of their environment. They also may be slightly physic, who are in tune with a higher collective conscious. These small pockets of humanity have come together to form new nations, and governments.
