Rigel, also designated as &amp;#946; Orionis (beta Orionis), is a variable and multiple supergiant star of spectral class B8 in the constellation of Orion. Rigel visual magnitude is 0.12, making it the 7th brightest star in the sky. Because of its brightness, Rigel is clearly visible to the naked eye when observed from locations with dark skies, and should be also quite easily visible from light polluted areas.
