The Federation of [nation]Wussenberg[/nation] is an advanced and prosperous island nation known for its unity, wisdom, and harmony. As an archipelago, Wussenberg is made up of three major islands (Holton, Bremen, and Falken) and five minor islands also known as Kleininseln, each contributing to the nation&#39;s rich history and vibrant culture. The capital city, Holtenburg, is located on the island of Holton, which serves as the political and cultural heart of the nation.
