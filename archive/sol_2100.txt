It is Eighty Years into the future. In the black void of space mankind reaches out its hand to establish dominion over the solar system. For those not forging a new life on extraterrestrial colonies, life on Earth is either a realm of endless delights provided by the machines or a grim existence amidst collapsing ecosystems. Only time will tell if these are the first flattering steps into the Golden Age of Man or the passing bell of the species.
