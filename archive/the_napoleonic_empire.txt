[color=333333][i]The First French Empire, commonly known as the French Empire, the Napoleonic Empire or simply as The Empire, covers the period of the domination of France and of much of continental Europe by Napoleon I of France. Constitutionally, it refers to the period of 1804 to 1814, from the Consulate to the restoration of the Bourbon monarchy in the history of the French state, with a coda in the Hundred Days of 1815. -- [/i]bonjourlafrance.com[/color][hr]

[b][color=990000]All nations entering The Napoleonic Empire must adopt a period French Republic flag or an appropriate Napoleonic themed flag.[/color][/b]
