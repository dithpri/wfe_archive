In the 51st century, in the far odd flings of space, there are the rimworlds. Far the from the cushy rich people of the Glitterworlds, they are lawless, mean, unforgiving lands where only the strongest, wisest, and smartest survive and thrive. Beset by angry tribals, ancient mechanoids, ruthless raiders, and fellow colonists, upstarts have their work more than cut out for them.
