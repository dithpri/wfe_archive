[color=163264][b]Solomon Islands[/b] is a nation in Melanesia, east of Papua New Guinea, consisting of nearly one thousand islands.
Together they cover a land mass of 28,400 square kilometres (10,965 sq mi).
The capital is [region=Honiara], located on the island of Guadalcanal.[/color]
