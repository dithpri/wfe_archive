Ieshus is a grand alliance set in Europe. Only those deemed worth are allowed to be in the alliance.

Made by the two founding nations, Aukeland and Sydmark, the alliance grew. With more nations getting added, influence and trade increased.
Each country helps eachother out. With the three biggest nations; Aukeland, Sydmark and Remenents Of The Enclave overseeing it all.

The other smaller nations vote, help and show compassion to their other allies.
