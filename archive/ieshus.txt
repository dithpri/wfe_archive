Ieshus is a Region set in Europe. It consists of Aukeland and Sydmark.

Aukeland:
A huge, rich nation with loads of freedoms, a small military and 3+ Billion people. It is the economic state of the region.

Sydmark:
A small upcoming nation. It has a low population of ~5 Million people. The landmass is huge in comparison giving it room to grow. The nation is socialist and uses their military. It is the militarist and protectorate state of the region.
