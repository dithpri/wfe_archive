[b]THIS IS DINGO ISLAND[/b]

The Lord of Dingo Island is a Vassal of the King of Taco Island and is the ruler of Dingo Island.
The current Lord of Dingo Island is Lord [nation=short]Louisistan[/nation] of Dingo Island, Master of Laws. Therefore, Dingo Island Castle is currently also the office of the Taco Island Master of Laws.
