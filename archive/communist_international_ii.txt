The Communist International (Comintern), also known as the Third International, was an international organization founded in 1919 that advocated world communism, headed by the Soviet Union.The Comintern resolved at its Second Congress to &quot;struggle by all available means, including armed force, for the overthrow of the international bourgeoisie and the creation of an international Soviet republic as a transition stage to the complete abolition of the state&quot;.The Comintern was preceded by the 1916 dissolution of the Second International.
