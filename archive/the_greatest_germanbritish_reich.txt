Welcome to the Reich. We are German-British. 

We, The People, hold these truths to be self-evident, that all men are equal.

1ST ARTICLE
The people have the rights to Religion, Speech, and Action. No part of this Article may be violated by any member of government or Parliament, otherwise resulting in impeachment.

2ND ARTICLE
If anyone in the country is accused of any action(any felonies) that violates any laws, they have the right to a fair and speedy trial, and an impartial jury.

3RD ARTICLE (Attached to 2nd Article)
Anyone has the right to an Attorney in any Court-Federal or State regardless- and if the Defendant cannot afford one, he/she will acquire one for free.

4TH ARTICLE 
All slavery is illegal.
