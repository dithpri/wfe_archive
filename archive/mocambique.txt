[b]~&laquo; [color=seagreen]Mocambique[/color] &raquo;~[/b]
[color=firebrick][i]Territory of The Dominion[/i][/color]

Mocambique, the Portuguese name for [i]Mozambique[/i], is a country located in Southeast Africa. The region was previously occupied by several groups in history, from fascists to nationalists. On August 30, 2014, the region of Mocambique became an imperial territory of [b][region]The Dominion[/region][/b].
