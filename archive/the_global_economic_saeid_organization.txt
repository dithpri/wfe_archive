[b]Better Economic Growth Region&#148;[/b]
[color=red]The purpose of this Organization is to engage relevant parties to cooperate and contribute to the development, dissemination and implementation of economic innovations in economic theory and practice.
The Organizations goal is to transcend current national and international economic policy and practice and foster Colabrative thinking aimed at serving the needs of a 21st century globalised world.[/color]

[color=blue]Supported by leading thinkers from around the world, the intention of the Organization is to inspire and motivate worldwide changes to the current mechanisms of trade and finance, including capacity exchanges, new market models and Making trade and finance more accessible and improving the global economy.[/color]
