[b][u]The Frankfurt Constitution of 1849[/u] designated all German military forces as the &quot;German Wehrmacht&quot;. The Deutsche Wehrmacht fought with honor and valor along with other nations during two world wars, until they were formally disbanded by the [url=https://2001-2009.state.gov/r/pa/ho/time/cwr/107189.htm]Allied Occupation Forces[/url] on August 20, 1946.
