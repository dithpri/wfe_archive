Founded in 1882, Tottenham Hotspur is one of England&#146;s most iconic football clubs, renowned for its attacking football and passionate fanbase. Spurs became the first club in the 20th century to win the league and FA Cup double in 1961, marking the beginning of their legacy as a powerhouse in English football. Over the years, they have claimed two league titles, eight FA Cups, four League Cups, and three European trophies. Tottenham is all about ambition, flair, and the pursuit of glory.
