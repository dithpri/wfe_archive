The Alliance Of European Communes is simply an Alliance of independent or semi-independent communes that are located in Europe.

&amp;#128182; Money- You may use any currency you want but are heavily encouraged to use the Euro. (You can use both the euro and your own currency if wanted)

&amp;#128483; Language- You may have any national language you want.

&amp;#9962;&amp;#65039;&amp;#128332;&amp;#128333; Religion- You may have any national religion you want but are encouraged to use Eco-Christianity. (Read more about it in [nation]Of The United Communes[/nation] factbook)

&amp;#127482;&amp;#127480;&amp;#127468;&amp;#127463;&amp;#127472;&amp;#127477; Government Type- You may use any type of government you want.
