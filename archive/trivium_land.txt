What is Trivium?
Trivium is an American metalcore/melodic heavy metal band from Orlando, Florida. The band comprises vocalist and guitarist Matt Heafy, guitarist Corey Beaulieu, bassist Paolo Gregoletto, and drummer Alex Bent. Formed in 1999, the band has released 10 albums and done multiple other works.
