Conservative Chicago is a right-wing conservative region version of WATCH_DOGS&#39; rendition of Chicago on NationStates.

Chicago is the largest city in Illinois and the third largest in the United States. It is the main setting of WATCH_DOGS, and is home to the Central Operating System (CTOS) which began citywide use in 2011. It is the county seat of Cook County, Illinois, and is located on the shores of Lake Michigan in the northeastern part of the state.
