Overview
The Confederation of the Quad (CQ) is a distinguished regional coalition that emerged from the vision of four pioneering teenagers. This alliance, rooted in the northern hemisphere, has evolved into a formidable force for economic collaboration and regional security.

Historical Background
Four score and seven years ago, four teenagers constructed an alliance that would stand the test of time and conflict itself. Their visionary efforts established the foundation for what would become the Confederation of the Quad, a testament to unity and resilience.
