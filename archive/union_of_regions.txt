
[b]What is the Union of Regions?[/b]| [color=#585858]The Union of Regions is an interregional organisation founded in May 2022 as a defender of liberty and basic freedoms. The Union promotes cooperation, shared values and establishes connections between like-minded regions. The Union will defend basic freedoms and will ensure that regions are able to co-ordinate responses to raids and other forms of regional aggression. 


[b]How do I join and what happens next?[/b][/color] | [color=#585858]To join simply email [nation]The Regional Alliance[/nation] notifying your intent to join. Next, you will have to set up either a puppet nation or ask one nation to become your ambassador. They will be given the password to come here. [/color]
