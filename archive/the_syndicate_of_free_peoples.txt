The Syndicate of Free Peoples is an organization of sovereign, independent syndicates organized around the central principles of mutuality and common aid. Working together to expand the freedoms of all people, both within and without our region, is of the utmost importance to the Syndicate. All members are given equal, shared power to vote on issues and deliberate with one another to ensure that all conclusions are carefully thought through and backed by the full weight of the Syndicate&#39;s members.
