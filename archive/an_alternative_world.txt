[b]An Alternative World[/b] is a serious, real time, real scale, modern day world based upon alternative history. All nations in this region have their own alternative history, but these histories do not challenge published histories of the other nations. All political aspects within this region are conducted as if this was the real world and you, genuine heads of state. If you wish to join the region contact [nation]Y Cymraeg Rhydd[/nation], where if your history does not contradict with the established history of current member states, you will be welcomed to this region. We accept any nation, regardless of size or political ideology.
