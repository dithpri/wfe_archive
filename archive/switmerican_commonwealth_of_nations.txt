The [b][region]Switmerican Commonwealth of Nations[/region][/b], normally known as the Commonwealth, is a political association of 3 member states, all in free association with the Federal Republic of Switmerica, itself a member state. The Commonwealth dates back to the first half of 2018 when it was originally created through the [url=https://www.nationstates.net/page=dispatch/id=1133696][b]Bronlinton Declaration[/b][/url] at the 2018 Commonwealth Conference, and established its member states as &quot;being in free association&quot;.
