The Switmerican Commonwealth of Nations, commonly referred to as the Commonwealth, is a political association comprising three member states, all of which are in free association with the Federal Republic of Switmerica. The Commonwealth promotes a close and cooperative relationship among its member states, based on shared values, historical ties, and mutual respect. The Commonwealth serves as a platform for member states to engage in diplomatic and multilateral efforts, both regionally and internationally. Through their free association with the Federal Republic of Switmerica and their shared commitments, member states strive to promote peace, prosperity, and progress for the benefit of their citizens.
