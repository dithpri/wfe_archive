The [b][u]Switmerican Commonwealth of Nations[/u][/b], colloquially known as the Commonwealth, is a political association of member states which are in free association with the [b]Federal Republic of [nation]Switmerica[/nation][/b] established by the Bronlinton Declaration.

Member states have certain legal obligations to one another. They agree to confer upon citizens of member states any similar right or privilege that a citizen might possess in their country of origin, grant precedence to citizens in matters of customs, immigration, and enquiries, as well as provide consular assistance for each other in all other foreign states.
