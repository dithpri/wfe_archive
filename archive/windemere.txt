Windemere is a modern-tech roleplaying region with a focus on realism, quality writing, and diplomacy. Windemere is the ideal region for an active player seeking an engrossing and realistic roleplaying environment. Windemere is currently under construction, so all early members will have a distinct role in shaping the history and the future of the region. 

We are currently recruiting, so if you are a dedicated, skilled RPers please apply!
