Ibisstan is a region in where are main purpose is to worship the Ibis, the best bird on the planet by far. Each member is required to set their Nationial Animal to a species of Ibis, and set their religion to Ibisism, once unlocked. That is really about it, it is recomended you edit your flag to include either a image of an Ibis and the Ibisstan symbol but it is not required by any means.
