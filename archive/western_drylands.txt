[b]Western Drylands[/b] is a fertile landmass, despite the name, green mountains and rainfall at least twice a month. A lake connected to the ocean by river to the north side of the region, by the name of manduna lake, provides freshwater to all who surround it. each hill is like a painted picture, perfectly rounded and greened by the trees. Mount. Aries Casts shade over nearby villages and nations standing at 24,235 ft above sea level, making it the tallest mountain in the region.
