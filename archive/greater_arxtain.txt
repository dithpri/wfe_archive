[color=blue][b]Greater Arxtain[/b][/color] [b]is a nation off unknown waters that has been disconnected from the rest of the planet for several thousands of years. It is divided into five kingdoms which are sub-divided into twenty-four provinces which are further divided into one-hundred and four districts.[/b]
[hr][hr][b]Each Kingdom has its own distinct government, history, and cultural identity.[/b][hr]
