The region of Kalhu has a rich history and a wide variety of cultures that have risen and fallen in power over the centuries. Currently the Empire of Una Rega and its primary corporate ally, New Andon, are the most influential nations in the region. Una Rega is a militant empire that works off a system of protectorates, providing security to any nation that swears loyalty to it. New Andon is one of the most technologically advanced, economic superpowers on the planet. The standard currency across most of Kalhu are credits.
