Region: Moderna
Ruling Country: Saint Genisis

Economy

Moderna thrives on advanced industries&#151;biotech, renewable energy, and aerospace. Trade is tightly managed by the state, and all progress is viewed as a divine blessing rather than personal fortune.

Defense

The Moderna Vanguard guards Moderna with faith and technology. Troops wield energy-based weapons, and sky fleets patrol the upper atmosphere under the doctrine of &#147;Divine Deterrence.&#148;

Culture

The people celebrate Moderna Day, marking the founding of Moderna after the Unification Wars. The national symbol is the Silver Torch, representing enlightenment and divine order.
