We are Forbitten. With the statesmen of the Six Tribes - we plant a Tree of Peace.

The tribes of the Confederacy of Forbitten (Foresstia, Benfeeka, Greiz, Gremyo, Minero &amp; Chreschtduerf shall meet in its capital Foresstia City.

All the affairs of the League shall be transacted at this place before you, Forbitten and your cousin statesmen, by the statesmen of the League of Six Tribes.
