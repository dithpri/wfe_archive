[b]The Remiferian Armed Forces[/b] are the armed branches of the Principality of Remiferia, tasked with protecting its citizens and interests around the world. Though fairly small, as the nation usually likes to build relations with nations instead, they are an effective and capable fighting force, on par with regular modern-day armies. Together with various nations around the world, they fight to ensure the safety of their citizens, and bring peace to conflict regions, whether through united attacks with others or humanitarian aid.
