Welcome to the continents of North Echeverra and South Echeverra and their associated islands. The Echeverras stretch from within the Arctic Circle to the 38th parallel south. As such, Echeverra is one of the most biologically and geologically diverse regions on the planet.

[url=http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Spanish_language]Vasco[/url] and English-speaking democracies prosper in North Echeverra. Theocratic republics and dictatorships reign over most of South Echeverra, where [url=http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lusophone]Luso[/url] is the dominant language. These are general rules, of course, and there are exceptions.
