Isolated from the rest of the world, the Elkhaynian Commonwealth stands as a monument to human progress. Considering economic prowess to be laughably childish, Elkhanians instead work together toward a future where all may succeed. Elkhanians, to a person, value equality and acceptance. The Elkhaynian Commonwealth pursues sustainability first and foremost, in an effort to ensure humanity&#39;s continued happiness and progress for generations to come.
