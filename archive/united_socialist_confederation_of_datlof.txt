The [color=red]United Socialist Confederation of Datlof[/color], otherwise known as the [color=blue]Datlovian Galactic Republic[/color], is a series of states/planets joined together under the Galactic Senate, to create a larger, more powerful nation, and to bring order to the universe. 

[hr]

The national currencies of the [color=blue]Datlovian Galactic Republic[/color] are [b]Caps[/b] and [b]Datlovian Credits[/b].
