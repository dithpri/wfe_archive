[b][u]Welcome to the Ilumian League[/u][/b]
The Ilumian League is an alliance between the nations of Earth and the galaxy-wide Empire of Ilumia, for the benefit of all. The governing body of the Ilumian League is the Grand Council (GC), which is spearheaded by Empress Erana of Ilumia, Empress Caroline of Great Britain and Tsarina Natalia of Russia, and their respective representatives.
