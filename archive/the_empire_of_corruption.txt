The Empire of Corruption is a large region in the Fallout world that is in the Afro-Eurasia area. most of the area is Terraformmed to more earth-like. The Empire of Corruption&#146;s main goal is to gain power, and spread the Main Religion of the region Zithorism. We achieve this by using methods of corruption like: Enslavement, Sabotage, Piracy, Bribery, Replacement of important figures, Etc. We are also famous for our round the world Black Markets.
