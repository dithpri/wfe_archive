The Empire of Corruption is a large region in the Fallout world that is protected by a large continuous dome-like building in the Afro-Eurasia area. The area in the dome is Terraformed and all Toxic elements have been removed.  The Empire of Corruption&#146;s main goal is to gain power, and spread the Main Religion of the region Zithorism. We achieve this by using methods of corruption like: Enslavement, Sabotage, Piracy, Bribery, Replacement of important figures, Etc. We are also famous for our round the world Black Markets.


For the history of this Region look in the fact book of Sparta-X under history of The Empire of Corruption.
