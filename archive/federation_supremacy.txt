Federation Supremacy is a multi-governmental corporate conglomerate block operating out of the Xyru Base on the planetoid XC-148k4.

XC-148k4 is the largest of the planetoids in the Surya Belt, located in the outer regions of the Tellurian System.

Federation Supremacy, while primarily based in the Tellurian System, has reach extending throughout the Myrmidion Quadrant, and, via a nearby wormhole, has access to the trading ports of Ragutis IV
