The Isles of Glorious Britannia or &quot;The Isles&quot; as it is more commonly known amongst its citizenry, is a collection of 13-48 mainland islands, and several hundred smaller islands that dot the outside of the area.

It is primarily known for its de-facto overlord, Great Britannia. However, several other smaller principalities, duchies, protectorates, and fiefdom states exist within.
