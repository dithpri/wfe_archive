For those who seek power, glory, and success, welcome to Dalrawduel! 

This medieval-styled world of magic and myth will aid any nation on its path to greatness. No matter who you are, you will be welcomed warmly into a life of medieval warfare, enchantments and exploration - given that you are of decent character. No foul-mouthed oppressor is allowed entry into this alternate world. Yet should you refrain from unwholesome speech and acts, then you will undoubtedly feel at home among the open lands of our world. Here, you will find everything from elves and dwarves to great stone castles to ancient customs of men. 

For all who seek a great quest among their collection of NationStates experiences, Dalrawduel is the home for you!
