Yasser League (Yasserian Language: Jarmea Li Yasser) , formally the League of Yasser States, is a regional organization of Yasserian states in and around Yasser Archipegio in Indian Ocean. It was formed in Cabir Island on 12 April 2018 with three members: the United States of United States of Yasser, People&#39;s Republic of Yasserryan, Kingdom of Yasser
