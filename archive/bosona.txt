[b]Bosona[/b] is a large continent within the northern hemisphere of the World, and the theatre of conflicts old and new, scientific development, and geographical wonder.

Formed by the collision of two landmasses, the tri-ranges of the [b]Frolder[/b], [b]Dragon[/b] and [b]Hense[/b] define the region and provide immense wind conditions. The north is comprised of tundra and frozen desert, the south a tropical ecosystem, and the middle region fertile valleys and plains.

[b]The Bosonan National Assembly[/b] is the major organization comprising many of the nations within Bosona and acts as a diplomatic, technological, economic and trade forum for the nations to freely interact within. They gather at the Bosonan Assembly Headquarters in K&auml;hnenburg, Vharnes.
