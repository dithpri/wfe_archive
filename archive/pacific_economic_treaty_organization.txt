The Pacific Economic Treaty Organization (PETO) is an intergovernmental organization that promotes economic cooperation, trade and investment among its member states. It was founded on July 12, 2008 with the signing of the Pacific Economic Treaty (PET) in Gabane.


Principles- The Pacific Economic Treaty Organization is built on the principles of economic cooperation, free trade, sustainable development, and regional integration. It seeks to promote economic growth and prosperity in its member states through regional integration, investment, and trade.
