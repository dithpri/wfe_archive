[b][region]Lycans[/region] are members of the [region]Lycanis[/region] race and Royal House, whether originally from the ancient Royal bloodlines of [region]Hyperborea[/region], or from the newer branches established throughout the western world. All [region]Lycans[/region] are born with the ability to shift between human and [region]Lycan[/region] forms while retaining our enhanced abilities and intellect, and are hardly seen without their companion [region]Wolves[/region].

The [region]Lycanis[/region] race and companions have established a border-less society in [region]Lycan Imperium[/region] under the guidance of the eldest of the ancient Royal House, [nation]The Lycan Emperor[/nation][/b]
