The region of Arganorh, also known as the Twin Continents, is home to centuries of war, religion, language, and culture. The region is governed under the Arganorhan Union, which was set up under the Treaty of Calais after Queen Gabrielle&#39;s war.

We are a closed region at the moment and will possibly be accepting applications for citizenship at a later date.
