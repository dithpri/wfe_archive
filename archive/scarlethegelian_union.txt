[B]The SHU is an Empire of nations, headed by the 13. It contains most world powers and is ruled with an iron fist. The name comes from the founder familiy of the 13 out of the ravaged &quot;USA&quot;, and the current ruling power of the Commonwealth of the Shivering Isles. The Union has certain standards that have to be met to join.[/B]
[color][u]These include:
A Technology rating of at least 9
A high Education standard of at least 8
A military standard/rating of 10
A healthcare rating of 5
A production rating of 7
In order to join, you must know one of the members personally. 
Random ally requests will not be accepted without further negotiation. 
All types of government are accepted.
