The Kingdom of Romania (Romanian: Regatul Rom&acirc;niei) was a constitutional monarchy which existed between 13 March 1881 and 30 December 1947, specified by the first three Constitutions of Romania (1866, 1923, 1938). The Kingdom of Romania began with the reign of King Carol I of Romania who gained Romanian&amp;#39;s independence in the Romanian War of Independence, and ended with the abdication of King Michael I of Romania in 30 December 1947.
