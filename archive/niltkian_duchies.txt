A region for the successor nations of a quickly collapsing empire. These collections of Ex-duchies are floundering in the new political stage of the world and each of them holds different views and ambitions for the region. From democratic nationalists who want to see all the peoples of the islands and mainland freed with their own nation, Elective Dukedoms who&#39;s counts only wish to retain their noble rights and Independence, to the old dynasty and new ones, seeking to claim the throne and reunite the squabbling duchies to be one under a divine mandate.
