Ever since the European Union fell, Europe has been struggling with economical crisis and armed conflicts.
In 2031 the newly established Scandinavian Union(Formerly Sweden, Norway, Denmark, Finland, Estonia, Latvia, Lithuania and northern Poland and Germany) formed the soon to be &quot;Alliance of Former Europe&quot;, also known as the AFE, to bring a new golden age to the region that was once seen during the European Union.


This is purely a diplomatic RP region. No raiding or similar behavior is sought after.
