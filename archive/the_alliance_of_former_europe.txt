Ever since the European Union fell after the migrant crisis, followed by the great economic collapse, Europe has been in disarray, and only a few nations remained intact. These nations, years after the crisis ended, decided to reform the European union, but this time under a new name and system. That is what we today call the Alliance of Former Europe. 


This is purely a diplomatic RP region. No raiding or similar behavior is sought after.
