The Greater Galy Peninsula is a Region near the North Pacific. It boasts a varied and interesting history. The founding Country is Galyimpia, this region was made on the 25th of November 2018. The GGP is mainly democratic, however several countries do have lesser civil rights than others. The economy is diverse and extremely successful. The founding nation of Galyimpia believes that maintaining peace should always be any nations top most interest, however the region does contain 2 nations that hold the ability to use WMDs.
