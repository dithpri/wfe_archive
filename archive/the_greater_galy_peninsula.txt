The Greater Galy Peninsula is a Region near the North Pacific. It boasts a varied and interesting history. The founding Country is Galyimpia, this region was made on the 25th of November 2018. The first several countries to join will be granted many privileges, however the founding region of Galyimpia will always hold a permanent executive authority. A constitution will be established after at least 10 countries join the Greater Galy Peninsula region (GGP).
