Union of Sovereign Republics (USR)

Motto: &quot;Many Republics, One Future&quot;

The Union of Sovereign Republics (USR) is a federation of self-governing republics united by a common commitment to prosperity, security, and cooperation. While each republic maintains authority over its local affairs, the Union provides a strong central government responsible for defense, foreign relations, and economic coordination.

Founded on the principles of sovereignty, unity, and mutual respect, the USR seeks to ensure stability and progress for all its member republics while preserving their unique cultures and identities. The Union stands as a symbol of strength through cooperation and shared purpose.
