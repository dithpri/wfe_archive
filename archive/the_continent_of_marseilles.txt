Marseilles is a stunningly beautiful continent, known for its rich seas and beautiful waterfalls. Her rivers stretch for miles, sparkling in the sunlight this continent is renowned for.

Marseilles is also an extremely resource-rich continent. The Black Forest of Marseilles is legendary for being used to create the First Arcadian Fleet. The continent also has vast deposits of oil and iron, as well as more exotic metals like Iridium and naturally occuring Arkadium. 

Marseilles today is divided between the three powers of Arcadia-Scorpia, Arcadia-Remutordius and Arcadia-Saggitara.
