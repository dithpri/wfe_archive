The Alliance of Demarchial Nations is composed of nations who follow the political ideology of a &#39;demarchy&#39; as defined as &quot;Government by decision-makers who have been randomly selected by sortition (lot) from a pool of eligible citizens.&quot; This can be a full &#39;demarchy&#39; or a &#39;demarchial republic&#39; in which only national politics is randomly chosen from eligible citizens while local politics remain freely elected.
