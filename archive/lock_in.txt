To &quot;lock in&quot; means to enter a state of intense, uninterrupted focus on a goal, often removing all distractions to maximize productivity or performance. It is popular slang for getting serious about tasks, such as studying, working, or gaming. Synonyms include focusing, committing, zoning in, buckling down, or finalizing.
