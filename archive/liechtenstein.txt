[color=navy][b]Liechtenstein[/b]

Liechtenstein is a landlocked alpine microstate in Western Europe, bordered by Switzerland to the west and by Austria to the east.[/color]

[color=800000]FACTS:
 - Capital City: Vaduz
 - 160.0 sq km (Fourth-smallest state in Europe, after Vatican, Monaco and San Marino)
 Independence as principality 
- Treaty of Pressburg 1806 
- Independence from the German Confederation[/color]
