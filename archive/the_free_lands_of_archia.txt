The World of Archia is a planet of very loosely joined nations. The only governing rules are as follows
1) No nation is forced to do something at the will of another nation
2) War is Highly frowned upon between the nations of Achia
3) Defense of your own borders and physical safety of your own citizens are the only reasons for war
4) Must be Tolerant of other nations beliefs.
5) You cannot send propaganda into another nation without that nations consent
6) If you are being intolerant, or attacking another nations beliefs without them directly sending an invading force into your land
7) non-peaceful nagotiations and take-overs will result in a permanent Ban from Archia
