Eastern Alquan or EA for short. Is a group of countries independent from each other. We are united by a region and a common need of freedom. If needed or wanted countries can work together in a respectful way. Random occasions there may be a meeting to determine whether or not countries within this region need assistance.  
More info.
Eastern Alquan is a highly modernized region and strives for improving technology.
EA accepts many religions as long as they do not offend the majority of other countries in this region.
