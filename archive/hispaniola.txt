[b]Hispaniola[/b] is the second largest island in the Caribbean after Cuba, the tenth-most-populous island in the world, and the most populous in the Americas.

It is the site of the first European colonies founded by Christopher Columbus on his voyages in 1492 and 1493.[hr]
