Trunasia is a vast continent on the edge of future Earths oceans. It has 3 major super volcanoes, their names Veluncha the smallest measuring at 2 miles wide in diameter, Tarsees the middle sized measuring at 4.5 miles in diameter and Revaldam the higgest measuring at 7 miles in diameter. The volcanoes blow randomly and at an increased rate in the past 10,000 years.

Trunasia is full of mountains and the natives have became adapt to the enviornment within the land borders and build cities within many vast caves and caverns throughout the region. Trunasia&#39;s area land mass is 15,576 miles wide and 22,578 miles in length. Is has many lush forests and even a few great lakes within the region.
