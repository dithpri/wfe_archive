[b]Why we exist[/b]

The region [b]School of Athens [/b]was created as a response to the need for a more reflective approach to NationStates.
We are not interested in aggressive competition or uncritical roleplay. Instead, we focus on discussion, thoughtful decision-making, and building nations based on individual principles.
This region is intended for players who see NationStates as a space for thinking and experimenting with ideas, rather than just a game of mechanics.
