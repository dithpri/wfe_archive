A righteous coalition of South Pacific states founded with intent to pursue progress in all spheres, disciplines and ways of life.

Rights inherent to all new applicants:

1. The right to hold public office within the Region.
2. The free exercise of communication and the drafting of public polls.
3. The right to petition the addition or removal of another NationState by general consensus (poll).
4. The right to submit or revoke an endorsement of any other NationState.

Any action, law, policy or undertaking otherwise not explicitly forbidden by Regional Decree is permitted. 

Glory to POOP.
