Until recently, the Allusian Empire maintained total control of its territory. When the last Emperor Maximillian VII died without an hier, the Empire effectively died. An ineffective regency council came to power and due to this incompetence the Empire began to disintegrate into the member states of this region. With the establishment of New Allusia, the collapse of the Old Empire is for all intensive purposes complete. Now it is time for the seven nations of the Old Empire to deal with the consequences of the collapse.
