Gliese 667 Cc (also known as GJ 667Cc or HR 6426Cc) is a nearby extrasolar planet, or exoplanet, just 22.7 light years away from Earth. It orbits around a star Gliese 667 C that is itself a member of the Gliese 667 triple star system, in the constellation of Scorpius. Gliese 667Cc has a semi-major axis of only 0.1251 astronomical units making its year only 28.155 days long.
