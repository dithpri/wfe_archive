Welcome to the I.R. The International Republic is just as it sounds. Any nation can join us, and we follow the Republic ideology. Briefly explained, a Republic is a government run by a President figure and a senate. The senate is the voting body that will vote on all of the decisions in this region. Once you join our region you will enjoy having your voice being heard, political freedom to speak out, and a wonderful community! Every new nation that joins us is automatically a member of the senate. Check out our Discord Server for more information! https://discord.gg/x5VgNyN2
