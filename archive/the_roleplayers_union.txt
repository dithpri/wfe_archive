Welcome to [b]The Roleplayers Union[/b] (RPU), a multiplatformed roleplaying community with a rich history spanning over a decade. The RPU has years of roleplaying experience, based off trial and error, forging a stronger community.

Our membership weighs in at approximately 100 members. Our platforms include, but are not limited to Bytro Labs. We pride ourselves in the policy of [i]&quot;Quality Over Quantity&quot;[/i], striving to build a community founded on respect.
