The Golden Age of Man is a private roleplay community focused on worldbuilding, creativity and alternate history. 

Founded in 2022, our region spans across decades of roleplay with players creating the history of the world collaboratively through roleplay. Our regional history is the result of player-driven activity with each decision in each era influencing the next. 

Membership to the region restricted and is achievable only by invite from an existing member. If you would like to register an interest to join, please fill out an application here.
