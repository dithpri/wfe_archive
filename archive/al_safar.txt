&quot;Al Safar&quot; is a treaty organization created to offer protection for oppressed nations
[b]Goals of Al Safar[/b]
[list]
[*]Protect members
[*]Promote cooperation 
[*]Protect national sovereignty
[/list]
Joining is subject to party members, and membership may be revoked at anytime.
Participants are subject to the rules listed by [url=https://www.nationstates.net/page=dispatch/id=1943409]The Charter of Al Safar[/url]
