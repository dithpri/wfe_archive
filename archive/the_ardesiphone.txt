[b]Welcome to the Ardesiphone[/b]
[i]Dorisele a de Ardesiphone[/i]

[b]The Ardesiphone or the International Organization of Ardesiephone, is an international organization representing countries and regions where Ardesian is a lingua franca or customary language, or where a significant proportion of the population are Ardesian speakers.[/b]

[b]Members of the Ardesiphone[/b]
- Congo, Democratic Republic of Congo, Ardennes, Luxembourg, Rwanda, Burundi, Ghana, Togo, Morrocco, Guatemala and Suriname.

[b]Leaders of the Ardesiephone[/b]
- [i]Founder[/i]: Jan Matthysen of Ardennes.
- [i]Secretary-General[/i]: Vacant.
- [i]Minister of Internal Affairs[/i]: Vacant.
- [i]Minister of International Relations[/i]: Vacant.
