Insulis Intellectus (Islands of Intellect) is a region consisting of Pristine Tropical islands. 

It is renowned for its advanced scientific research and emphasis on knowledge and education. Religion is not well regarded throughout the region although individual nations are fully autonomous and all of its citizens are allowed to believe in whatever they want.

The Region prides itself on its tolerance of others and tries to remain peaceful as often as possible. However it does maintain a powerful military comprising of a world class Navy and Air Force used for mostly defensive purposes.
