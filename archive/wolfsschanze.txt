[i]Wolfsschanze[/i] was the codename of one of the Fuhrer headquarters in Europe and stood near Ketrzyn, in northeast Poland.

Adolf Hitler&amp;#39;s first visit to [i]Wolfsschanze[/i] was in the night of 23 June 1941 and would spend more than 800 days of his live in this complex. He departed for the last time on 20 November 1944 and would never return again.
