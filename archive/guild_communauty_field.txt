A Card Farm run by members of the The North Pacific Card Guild for member of the The North Pacific Card Guild.

Members of the North Pacific Card Guild can request nearly any cards these farmers hold according to the wish of their owner. 

Rules 
People can normally 2 cards per week
with premium request, people can request 10 extra cards per week
People cannot request  more then 2 inflated cards (10+ mv for UC and C), (25+ MV for R, UR and e)
People can request any non-legendary cards, and even legendary cards, depending on the will of the puppet&#39;s owners with card sharks points (500 ponts for a legendary card) 
First to request, first served, except Card Shark requests, a Cards Shark Request have priority over a normal request
