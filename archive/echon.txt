[b][i]What if History repeated itself on an alien world?[/i][/b]

[i]Echon[/i] is a roleplay region placed on an fictional planet, Echon, where convergent cultural and technological evolution have lead an intelligent species, the Kalon, to a history that closely mirrors earths. 
Players play as fictional nations on the planet, in a situation similar to the early age of discovery.

[b]Endorse [nation]Whoever You Want[/nation][/b].
The WA delegate is elected via who has the most votes, so endorse whoever you trust and believe is the best for the job.
