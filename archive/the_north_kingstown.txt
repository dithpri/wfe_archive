[b]Welcome to The North Kingstown! This region is for all NKHS NationStates players!

[/b]The North Kingstown is a relatively young region that is home to eleven nations. These nations are all unique and special in their own ways, however, some are more extreme than others.

If you are a part of NKHS and have a nation you would like to be a part of [region]The North Kingstown[/region], it is recommended that you only enter in one of your nations.

If you would like to have a custom flag made for your nation, please send a telegram to [nation]Astanga-Xlasho[/nation], and they will make a custom flag for you (based on what you describe) as soon as possible.
