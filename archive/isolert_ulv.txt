There are some reports of a new branch of rabies that is spreading like wildfire through Western Konechia and beyond, causing people to become hyper-aggressive. Early signs of this outbreak have begun to appear in the poorer regions of Isolert Ulv, and [i]The Pact[/i] is attempting to set up quarantine, however, this is made increasingly difficult by the WA, who are attempting to spread discontent and anger towards [i]The Pact[/i] and causing the nations to reject foreign aid.
