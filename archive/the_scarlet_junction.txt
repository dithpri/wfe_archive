The Scarlet Junction is an interdimensional rift created by the collision of the Arcturan Vortex and the J6 Warp Conduit. Officially designated a &quot;Class A Spacetime Anomaly,&quot; the Junction is an ever-shifting realm of untold wonder and peril. Great and ancient civilizations have suddenly been brought into contact, each with their own interests to advance. Join us as we tell this story.
