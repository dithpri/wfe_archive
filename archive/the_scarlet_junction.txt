[b]Welcome to the Scarlet Junction![/b]

The Scarlet Junction is an interdimensional rift created by the collision of the Arcturan Vortex and the J6 Warp Conduit. Officially designated a [i]Class A Spacetime Anomaly[/i], the Junction is an ever-shifting realm of untold wonder and peril. Great and ancient civilizations have suddenly been brought together, each with their own interests to advance. [b]Join us as we tell this story.[/b]

We accept all embassy requests, as this merely means diplomatic communication has been established between our regions. If your region would like to enter into a formal [b][url=page=dispatch/id=591573]Treaty of Interregional Friendship &amp; Cooperation[/url][/b], please contact us.
