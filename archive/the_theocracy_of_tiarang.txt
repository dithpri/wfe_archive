Head of State (de jure): Jesus Christ (Eternal King) 
Head of Government: Beng Davei (Prime Minister) 
State Philosophy: Christian Socialism 
Government Structure: One Party State (Christian Revolutionary Party), Socialist/Theocracy 
Language: Tiarangese 
Denonym: Tirangese 
State Religion: Christian 
Currency: Tiarang Gold Piece 
Capital City: Tiarang City 
National Animal: Sheep 
Armed Forces: Christian Defence Forces (CDF), comprised of the army, the Christian Revolutionary Guards (CRG), the navy, called the Christian Oceanic Guards (COG) and the air force, known as the Christian Airspace Guards (CAG).
