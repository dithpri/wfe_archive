[b][i]Scoria[/i][/b] is a region of countries in a simulation game penned NationStates&#153;, and these nations reside on the planet Scoria of Imperius (The Known Galaxy). It is an invitation-only, roleplaying region set in the near future. Scoria is home to twenty-two original nations.

[i]Scoria[/i] was founded in 2012 by a small group of friends, and over time, has evolved into much more than an idea. Branching through NationSates&#153; for four years now, [i]Scoria[/i] is home to [i]many[/i] experienced nations, and some new. It is a friendly place to be, and is welcome to those who are well-known by the founders, and to people who will treat it with respect.
