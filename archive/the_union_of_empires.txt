The Union Of Empires is the name given to a collection of allied nations belonging to a large land mass covered in lush green landscapes surrounded by a large sea. The land is made up of two main factions, the communist faction, a powerful a group of countries that come together under their communist governments and The Union of Empires, a band of small countries who have banded together temporally to stand against their communist enemies. The flag of the union is made up of the flags of the five orginal founders.....

And so is the Union of Empire, strong and powerful yet always under threat.....
