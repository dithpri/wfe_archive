Wolf 359 is a red dwarf star located in the constellation Leo. At a distance of approximately 7.9 light years from Earth, it has an apparent magnitude of 13.54 and can only be seen with a large telescope. Wolf 359 is one of the nearest stars to the Sun; only the Alpha Centauri system (including Proxima Centauri), Barnard&#39;s Star, and the brown dwarfs Luhman 16 (WISE 1049-5319) and WISE 0855&amp;#8722;0714 are known to be closer.
