In the year 2034 due to a insufficient healthcare system the many nations of Western Africa faced a plague of infectious diseases and civil wars. After the nations of Western Africa were severely weakened due to over population millions of Europeans, Asians and North Americans joined the surviving natives of West AfricaAfter 30 years these countries have become the world leaders in diversity, technological advancements, education and economic and social freedoms. Once again the revolutionary group of nations came together at the first Western African Union meeting located in Manovitz City and decided to invest heavily in the transformation of the Sahara desert into hospitable land.
