Welcome to the Olivic Lands, the lands that once were epart of the Olivic Empire and now are a group of nations that, since the fall of the Empire against the minority of the Gagnans, have been claiming the suzerainity of the city of Vicus.

A great mixture of cultures, political ideologies and races are placed here, from the Empire of Gagnania to the Confederation of the Olivic Commune.

This region was founded the Seventh of December of 2014.

Embassies and alliances must be claimed by telegrams to the heads of the region.
