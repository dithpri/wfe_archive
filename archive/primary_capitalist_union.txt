Primary Capitalist Union (PCU) - World Factbook Entry

Introduction:
The Primary Capitalist Union (PCU) is an authoritarian region in NationStates, enforcing capitalist economic principles under centralized control. Trade is tightly regulated, and alliances serve the state&#39;s interests. Cultural uniformity is maintained, dissent suppressed, and governance ruled by an iron-fisted regime. The PCU prioritizes economic dominance over environmental concerns, wielding absolute power over its territories.
