[color=blue]THE WORLD of SIMPLICITY[/color] is a Region where Nations Roleplay as Governers of the World. Members of this region is encouraged to roleplay as a patriotic politician and defend the interest of your own people. Nations can initiate war, engage alliances and participate in the handling of this region. 

[B]WHAT YOU CAN DO[/B] :
- Claim A Spot in the World Map
- Make Alliances
- Policy Making
- EXCO of this Region through voting
