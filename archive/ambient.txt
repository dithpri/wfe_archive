Ambient music is a genre of music that emphasizes tone and atmosphere over traditional musical structure or rhythm. Often &quot;peaceful&quot; sounding and lacking composition, beat, and/or structured melody,[5] ambient music uses textural layers of sound that can reward both passive and active listening,[6] and encourage a sense of calm or contemplation.[7][8] The genre evokes an &quot;atmospheric&quot;, &quot;visual&quot;,[9] or &quot;unobtrusive&quot; quality.[10] Nature soundscapes may be included, and some works use sustained or repeated notes, as in drone music. Bearing elements associated with new-age music, instruments such as the piano, strings and flute may be emulated through a synthesizer.[11][12]
