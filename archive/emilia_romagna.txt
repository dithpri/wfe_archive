Emilia Romagna is an Region of Northeast Italy.[hr]

Emilia-Romagna is known as a cultural and tourist centre, containing Romanesque and Renaissance cities, being a centre for food and automobile production and having popular coastal resorts such as Cervia, Cesenatico, Rimini and Riccione.[hr]
