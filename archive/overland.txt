Following the collapse of the Union of Overlanders, many residents of the region evacuated to safer havens. However, a few states remained behind, aligned under the banner of [nation]The Black Dragon Empire[/nation]. Now left behind to rebuild, Overland now sits open for new powers to settle and build a home. If they can survive their neighbors, new settlers will flourish in this still bountiful land.

This is a RP region. The tech levels are primarily MT/PMT(Today + 0-15 years) and FanT(Sci-fi stuff).
