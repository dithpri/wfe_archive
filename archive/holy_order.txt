Christ Centered Holy Order of Nations.
&quot;One Order Under God.
One Nation Under Order.&quot;

The Trinitarian Tenants:
Father - The Holy Order
Son - The Nations
Holy Spirit - The Citizens

The Ten Country Commandments:
1. You shall have no other Orders before me.
2. You shall make no Orders.
3. You shall not take the name of your Order in vain.
4. Keep every day Holy.
5. Honor your Order and Nation.
6. Citizens shall not murder.
7. Citizens shall not commit adultery.
8. Citizens shall not steal.
9. Citizens shall not bear false witness.
10. Citizens shall not covet.
