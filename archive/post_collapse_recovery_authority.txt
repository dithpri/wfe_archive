The Post-Collapse Recovery Authority is a post-apocalypse roleplaying region. You can play as a formal country or even as a non-state entity.

[b][u]Lore[/u][/b]
In 2008 Earth was gripped by a devastating plague which decimated entire nations and left communities to fend for themselves. However, despite this tragedy many new nations have been formed across the planet. Some of these countries hold onto old world ideologies such as Democracy and Communism, but their have also been development of new ideologies. As well as nations that had once been insignificant, have become world powers. Many of these nations came together and formed an international body to mandate their grievances, this body being the Recovery Authority
