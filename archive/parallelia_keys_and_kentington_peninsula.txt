A collection of temperate islands dot the sea near the Kentington Peninsula&#39;s harsh but fair mainland, where the primitive but exceptionally bureaucratic nation of Chookalookastanislav reigns as the sole inhabitant of the region.

At least, the only inhabitant that Chookalookastanislav officially recognizes as a sovereign state, which is hardly surprising, considering that virtually no sovereign state is officially recognized by Chookalookastanislav, only dismissed as nothing more than elaborate hoaxes and illusions conjured by dragons and witches.
