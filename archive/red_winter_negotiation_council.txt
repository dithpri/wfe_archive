As the Red Winter War ends in a bloodbath, the belligerent bled all their manpower and strength into a deadly undertanking. Peace is established, at last, between the former superpowers but it is still weak and frail. In order to maintain unity through the world, the Red Witer Negociation Council (RWNC) is formed. Within the Council, the main powers have permanent seats and minor powers are eligible up to 3 seats each per year. Diplomacy is on its way.
