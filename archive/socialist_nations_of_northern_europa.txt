The Socialist Nations of Northern Europa, full designation The United Coalition of Communist and Socialist Nations of the Territories Comprising the Whole of the Northern Portion of the Continent Europa, is a region of socialist dictatorship nation states bound together in a union. A single ruler in each nation rules over the country, and frequently communicates, shares pertinent information, and combines military forces with the rulers of the other nations in SNNE. The union was founded by the ruler of Karlarxistan.
