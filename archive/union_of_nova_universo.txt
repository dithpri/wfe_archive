The [b][region]Union of Nova Universo[/region][/b] is a supranational politico-economic union of ten polities in Earth (Sol 3), Heimat (Gliese 273b) and the observable Multiverse. The Union is a middle power with democratic, humanist, pacifist and socialist leanings. Its leader is Queen Merita and its seat is at the Free City of [nation=noflag]Nova Universo[/nation].
