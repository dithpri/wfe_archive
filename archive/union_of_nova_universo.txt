The [b][region]Union of Nova Universo[/region][/b] is a supranational politico-economic union of ten polities in [b]Earth (Sol 3)[/b], [b]Heimat (Gliese 273b)[/b] and the [b]observable Multiverse[/b]. The Union is a middle power with democratic, pacifist, environmentalist, progressive and communitarian leanings. Its seat is at the [b][url=nationstates.net/nation=nova_universo]Free City of Nova Universo[/url][/b].

The [b]Union of Nova Universo[/b] pursues an independent multilateral foreign affairs policy. It strives for a peaceful world by co&ouml;perating with nations and regions. It has a neutral defence policy focussed on deterring internal threats and mediating external conflicts.
