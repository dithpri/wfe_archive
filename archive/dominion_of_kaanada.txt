Canada is a country in North America. Its ten provinces and three territories extend from the Atlantic Ocean to the Pacific Ocean and northward into the Arctic Ocean, making it the world&#39;s second-largest country by total area, with the world&#39;s longest coastline. Its border with the United States is the world&#39;s longest international land border. The country is characterized by a wide range of both meteorologic and geological regions. It is sparsely inhabited, with the vast majority residing south of the 55th parallel in urban areas. Canada&#39;s capital is Ottawa and its three largest metropolitan areas are Toronto, Montreal, and Vancouver.
