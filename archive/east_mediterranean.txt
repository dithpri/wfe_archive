                                                   ******** Brief History******** 
After the Great Reconquest of Anatolia and the Great Hellenic Civil War, an alliance was made out of necessity between the Greek factions who dominate the East of the Mediterranean. The Kingdom of [nation]Dorilea[/nation] the massive military powerhouse which dominates most of Anatolia and most of Greece proper [b]and[/b] the  The Most Serene Republic of [nation]Constantinoupolis[/nation], a small faction of vastly rich and influential City-States with Constantinople as their capital. 
  The [region]East Mediterranean[/region] is the bastion of Hellenic, Armenian and any Orthodox affiliated civilizations. They seek to unite with and protect any other Greek and eastern European states/factions.
