Novo Nachalo is a dedicated NationStates roleplaying region that takes place in the near future.
After an event known only as the Greatest War, the political, economic, and social systems of the Old Earth collapsed and disease, death, and anarchy spread throughout the world in a new Dark Age. After many years, new civilizations are forming in this New Beginning for the world.
