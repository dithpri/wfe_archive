The year is 2280, and the Vault Dwellers of the past have finally emerged from their shelters. New nations have formed as the world is being restored to normal. Nations in this region rise and fall, as Society continues shaping itself and emerge as a regional superpower. The World Assembly is now in place of the former United Nations, and nations emerge and join together to create the new United States, with the Enclave as it&#39;s guide to a new future.
