The system of Creataris was discovered when a survey vessel launched by the Republic of Creataris discovered a binary star system with 5 habitable Planets which are: Creataris Prime, Creataris II, Creataris III, Creataris IV and Creataris V. Nations wishing to live in the Creataris system must be willing to stand up for the those who are being persecuted and even if they are classed as dictatorships they must also grant &amp;quot;privileges&amp;quot; (rights) to their citizens.
