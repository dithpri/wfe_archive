[hr][i]The Great Mosque of Samarra was built during the Caliphate of al-Mutawakkil. It is the largest mosque in the World. Built entirely of brick within a wall flanked with towers, it has a 52 meter high minaret with a spiral ramp that recalls the ziggurats of Mesopotamia.[/i]
