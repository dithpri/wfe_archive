Founded under the direct initiative of the Bundesrepublik Altenland, the region operates under a strict doctrine: Einheit durch Auswahl, Unity through Selection. Entry is not granted through application, nor chance, but solely through personal vouching by the sitting Governor. Every nation within is known, vetted, and deemed compatible with the region&#146;s ideological and structural framework.

Outsiders speculate about its nature, some call it an elite alliance, others a closed-order bloc, and a few whisper of something far more calculated.
