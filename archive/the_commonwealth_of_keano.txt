The Commonwealth of Keano is an intergovernmental organisation of member states that were mostly territories of the former Keano Empire. Member states appoint a secretary general currently former Keano Prime Minister Alan Johnson. The head of the Commonwelth is the Monarch of the United Kingdom of keno currently Queen Victoria. Member states choose to have the Monarch as head of state of there nation and wether to share the currency of the Keano Pound. All member states currently do.
