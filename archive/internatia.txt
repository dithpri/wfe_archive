Internatia is one of the world&#39;s continents. Comprising the two big islands, Avantia and Cordvern, including adjacent islands (Elysion, Algasoria, Dornata, Stelettio, Lucasina, Saari, Moniquina, Symphony Isles and many smaller).

Internatia is bordered by the Aeturnum Ocean to the north, the Granda Ocean to the west, the Southern Ocean to the south and east.
