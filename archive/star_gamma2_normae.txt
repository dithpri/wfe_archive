&amp;#947;2 Normae is a double giant star of spectral class G8 in the constellation of Norma. &amp;#947;2 Normae visual magnitude is 4.02. Because of its moderate brightness, &amp;#947;2 Normae is easily visible to the naked eye from locations with dark skies, while it might be barely visible, or not visible at all, from light-polluted skies.
