[b]Meritocracy[/b] (merit, from Latin mere&amp;#333;, and -cracy, from Ancient Greek &amp;#954;&amp;#961;&amp;#940;&amp;#964;&amp;#959;&amp;#962; kratos &#39;strength, power&#39;) is the notion of a political system in which economic goods or political power are vested in individual people based on ability and talent, rather than wealth or social class.
