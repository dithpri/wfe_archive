The Region of [b][region]Urana Firma[/region][/b] is an RP community group with a National SciFi setting.

The Region is Currently on its 2nd long running RP titled &quot;Rain&quot; or Regional Assembly of Intergalactic Nations.

The setting is at the Galactic scale with many system Spanning Nations and Empires, as well as Trade Conglomerates and Mercenary groups.

The RP is loosely structured with a preferred touch stone of real world science. [i][Even if just theoretical and a little hand wavy][/i]
