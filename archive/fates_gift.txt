Fate&#39;s Gift is an Earth-like World in the Idyll system, in an unknown location in the Galaxy.

Idyll houses a K-class star, which, is smaller and cooler than Sol, but also older, at 9 Billion years.
Fate&#39;s Gift is an unusually ideal world, and seems to have been terraformed. It has the same mass as Earth, and is only slightly smaller.

It has slightly higher Gravity than Earth, with surface Gravity being approximately 1.08G.
Its atmosphere is nearly identical to Earth&#39;s, at 1.17 times the Atmospheric Pressure of Earth.
The planet also has identical average temperature ranges to Earth, although with less variation.
