[b]Portsmouth[/b] is located mainly on Portsea Island in the [region]English Channel[/region], 70 miles south-west of London and 19 miles south-east of Southampton. It is the United Kingdom&#39;s only island city.

A naval dockyard was established in 1496 and greatly expanded after 1698. Covering more than 300 acres, the dockyard is the city&#39;s main source of employment. &#151;Wikipedia[hr]
