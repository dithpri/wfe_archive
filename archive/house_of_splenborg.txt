&#149; Casa de Splenborg[hr]The [b]Princely House of Splenborg[/b], founded 31st of August, 706.M184/184707 CE, is a prominent Gothic princely house descended from one of the numerous cadet branch of the Imperial Dynasty through the Archducal House of Drachenfels-Weisseheldenburg-Karnstein, Wilhelmine Major Line. It was named after [url=https://knowyourmeme.com/memes/spengbab]Spengbab[/url], a meme of grotesquely deformed fanart of [url=https://m.imdb.com/title/tt0206512/]Spongebob Squarepants[/url] that became a frequent character in [url=https://youtube.fandom.com/wiki/YouTube_Poop]YouTube Poops[/url] and other Internet parody.
