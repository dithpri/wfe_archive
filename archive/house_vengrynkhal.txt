[color=#010000][b]House Vengrynkhal exists to sponsor acts of chaotic evil across NationStates, of any kind.

Those with darkness for their goals will find resources, advice and willing co-plotters.
Those with darkness in their hearts will find inspiration, opportunity and comrades.
The assistance of House Vengrynkhal to both those with dark goals and dark hearts is invaluable and unique. No similar institution exists in NationStates.

To become a Patron of House Vengrynkhal, register on the [url=http://s15.zetaboards.com/Vengrynkhal/index/]forums[/url] and proceed to the area that makes sense to you.

Those who provide loyal and long term service to the House will find power, glory and influence. Those who betray it will see their dreams turn to ashes.[/b][/color]
