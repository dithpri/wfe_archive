PENCON 4      NAP - Medium 


The new Northeastern Alliance is a Alliance based on protection of freedom and equality in domestic soil and foreign soil.



There is a Pencon System of defense levels based on the current situation. This method is derived from old Pengueen.

5 - Peace with no Tension

4 - Tension at the NNWA

3 - Medium Scale Conventional War

2 - All Out Conventional War

1 - Nuclear War


A new diplomatic tension level system has been developed. It is called NAP

None - Nothing going on

Low - Threat of Attack; unlikely

Medium - Troops on border, will make early defensive plans

High - Immediate Threat

Maximum - War
