The Confederation Of Independent Nations are three powerful nations that desire their own homeland and region free of political bureaucracy of the giant regions now ruling NationStates. Our region called The Confederation Of Independent Nations (CIN) comprises the following nations: The first is our founder The Nordic Nationalist Dominion of Vlestimore, the second nation to arrive was The Dark Shadow Kingdom of United Carthage, and the third and final nation to arrive was The Islamic Free State of South Peshawar.
