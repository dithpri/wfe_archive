The Confederation Of Independent Nations are five powerful nations that desire their own homeland and region free of political bureaucracy of the giant regions now ruling NationStates. Our region is officially called The Confederation Of Independent Nations (COIN) and it comprises the following nations: The first is our founder The Nordic Nationalist Dominion of Vlestimore, the second nation to arrive was The Dark Shadow Kingdom of United Carthage, the third nation to arrive was The Islamic Free State of South Peshawar, the fourth nation to arrive was The Soviet Socialist Republic of United Soviet Panama, the latest nation to join our humble confederation is The United States of Marelda.
