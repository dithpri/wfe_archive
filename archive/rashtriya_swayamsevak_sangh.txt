About RSS :
[color=orange]The Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh (abbr. RSS; R&amp;#257;&amp;#7779;&amp;#7789;r&amp;#299;ya Svaya&amp;#7747;sevak Sa&amp;#7749;gh, Hindi pronunciation: [ra&amp;#720;&amp;#642;&amp;#712;&amp;#648;ri&amp;#720;j(&amp;#601;) sw&amp;#601;j&amp;#601;m&amp;#712;se&amp;#720;&amp;#651;&amp;#601;k s&amp;#601;&amp;#331;&amp;#609;&amp;#689;], lit.&amp;#8201;&#39;National Volunteer Organisation&#39;) is an Indian far-right Hindu and Indian nationalist volunteer organisation.It is the progenitor and leader of a large body of organisations called the Sangh Parivar (Hindi for &quot;Sangh family&quot;), which has developed a presence in all facets of Indian society and includes the Bharatiya Janata Party, the ruling political party under the Prime Minister Narendra Modi. The present Sarsanghchalak of the RSS is Mohan Bhagwat.[/color][/b]
