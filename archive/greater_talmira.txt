Greater Talmira is governed by the kingdom of Talmira. It has 4 constituent parts which act as colonies. The 1st parts is the kingdom of Talmira which acts as the governing part. Grater Talmira is conformed by:

1. The kingdom of Talmira (mainland)
2. The colony of the Carribean Talmira
3. The colony of the Makemo Islands 
4. The dominion of the Talmiran Antartica
5. The grand duchy of the Jan Minke Islands
