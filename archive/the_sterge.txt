All nations are welcome in [b]The Sterge[/b] and we encourage you to join in our wide ranging discussions on various hot button topics regarding the governing of [b]The Sterge[/b]

Founded by a mixture of far right and far left leaning nations [b]The Sterge[/b] manages to somehow find the perfect mix and create a blissful harmony for all who wish to move to our lush green pastures, flowing rivers and plentiful harvests.

One thing we can agree on is the redundancy of the WA, nations in [b]The Sterge[/b] will not be dictated to by other nations and will not be told how to act and how to treat the citizens of [b]The Sterge[/b]
