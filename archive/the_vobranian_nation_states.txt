[b]Welcome to the Vobranian Nation States[/b]
[hr]

The Vobranian Nation States is a minarchistic region which is dedicated to [url=https://www.nationstates.net/nation=vobrana]Vobrana[/url] and her allies and puppet-states. The region is defined by decentralized regional power and localized leadership. State power and election triumphing over that of centralized power and financial statism. The region is also a password protected region which heralds only the closest to [url=https://www.nationstates.net/nation=vobrana]Vobrana[/url] and her allies and will be sectioned off from outsiders and those unknown or against [url=https://www.nationstates.net/nation=vobrana]Vobrana[/url] and her allies.
