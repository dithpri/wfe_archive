The Americas
From the frozen north to the southernmost reaches of the continent, from the Atlantic coastlines to the Pacific horizons  The Americas stand as a region defined by resilience, ambition, and diversity.

This region brings together the nations of the New World, where histories intertwine, cultures flourish, and destinies collide. Whether your nation is a rising democracy, a powerful empire, a mercantile republic, or a cultural beacon, there is a place for you here. The Americas are more than just a region on the map  they are a stage for legacy. The future of the Western Hemisphere is waiting to be written.
