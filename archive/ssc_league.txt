Having stood for Talent, Virtue, and Ardor for the past three years, the SSC League has taken a great leap from a small trade league embroiled in degenerate animes into one of the most prestigious regions in the feline history. The SSC League is the largest exporter of ES. The Symbols of the SSC League are the Yellow Flower and Goldfish. 

Our official motto is &quot;scimus quod sensum&quot;.
