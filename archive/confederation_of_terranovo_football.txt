The Confederation of Terranovo Football is a sub-confederation of the DSAFF. Founded in the year 2000 the federation comprises of 16 member nations. COTEFU organizes tournaments club and national teams and qualifying paths for major DSAFF tournaments. The nations not COTEFU itself are DSAFF members

Tournaments:
- COTEFU Diamonds League
- COTEFU Crystal League
- COTEFU Frost Cup Qualifying 
- COTEFU Comrades Cup Qualifying 
- DSAFF Olimpia Games
- DSAFF Blossom League

ONLY COTEFU/DSAFF MEMBERS MAY JOIN THE REGION
