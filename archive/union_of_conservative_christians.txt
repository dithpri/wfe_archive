The Union of Conservative Christian Nations (UCCN) is an organization of nations founded on March 30, 2015 by the Federal Republic of Stellonia. The UCCN is devoted to spreading conservative and Judeo-Christian ideals around the world.
The UCCN is opposed to abortion in all or most cases. It also opposes homosexuality, same-sex marriage, and the teaching of evolution in public schools. The UCCN also supports a free-market economy and the promotion of democracy across the globe.
