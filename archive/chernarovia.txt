Chernarovia is a region located in eastern Europe, between the Russian, Polish and Ukrainian borders. It is the home of the P.R. of Chernarovia, a socialist and authoritarian state, and the E.R. of Greater Animalia in the south. The headquarters of Chernarovia&amp;#39;s communist party can be found in Cherny Orel, the capital city of the P.R. of Chernarovia.

The three main pillars of the region policy are: socialism, industrialism and ecologic management.

The diplomatic department of the Regional Government is always open to any proposal or inquiry.
