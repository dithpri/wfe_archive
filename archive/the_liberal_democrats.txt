The Liberal Democrats are a political party in the [Region]Union of Allied States[/Region].

The Lib Dems are a democratic party that exists to build and safeguard a fair, free and open society, in which we seek to balance the fundamental values of liberty, equality and community, and in which no one shall be enslaved by poverty, ignorance or conformity. We believe that the role of the region is to enable all nations to attain these ideals, to contribute fully to the regional community and to take part in the decisions which affect them.

We identify primarily as Social Liberals and Social Democrats, though we welcome other ideologies.
