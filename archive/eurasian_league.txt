Eurasia is a combined continental landmass of Europe and Asia. Eurasia covers around 55,000,000 square kilometres (21,000,000 sq mi), or around 36.2% of the Earth&#39;s total land area. The landmass contains around 5.0 billion people, equating to approximately 70% of the human population.

Eurasian League welcomes any and all who wish to join in a union of equality and freedom geared towards creating a more sustainable future with the main purpose of a cooperation in political, economic, environmental, humanitarian, cultural and other fields among its members.
