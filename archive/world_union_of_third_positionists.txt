Welcome to the [b]World Union of Third Positionists[/b], a group founded 7/6/2018 to unite all those outside of the traditional Left-Right spectrum.

[list]
[*]The [b]WUTP[/b] accepts all who fall within the label of &quot;Third Position&quot;. This includes, but is not limited to, Strasserists, National Bolsheviks, Jucheists, Ba&#39;athists, Ethnic socialists (African, Arab, etc.), and National Anarchists.

[*]The [b]WUTP[/b] staunchly rejects [color=gold]capitalism[/color], [color=red]Marxism[/color], [color=orange]globalism[/color], [color=brown]imperialism[/color], and all of their byproducts.

[*]The [b]WUTP[/b] affirms and supports the right of all peoples to an ethnic homeland, and therefore supports all national liberation movements worldwide.
