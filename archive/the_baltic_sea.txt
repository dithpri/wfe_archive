[i]The Baltic Sea is a marginal sea of the Atlantic Ocean. The sea stretches from 53&deg;N to 66&deg;N latitude and from 10&deg;E to 30&deg;E longitude. A mediterranean sea of the Atlantic, with limited water exchange between the two bodies, the Baltic Sea drains through the Danish islands into the Kattegat by way of the straits of &Oslash;resund, the Great Belt, and the Little Belt.[/i]
