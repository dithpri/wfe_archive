[i][url=https://cdn.discordapp.com/attachments/482353677848412162/487855518707351552/unknown.png]The Baltic Sea[/url] is a marginal sea of the Atlantic Ocean. The sea stretches from 53&deg;N to 66&deg;N latitude and from 10&deg;E to 30&deg;E longitude. A mediterranean sea of the Atlantic, with limited water exchange between the two bodies, the Baltic Sea drains through the Danish islands into the Kattegat by way of the straits of &Oslash;resund, the Great Belt, and the Little Belt.

The Baltic sea holds the title of the largest inland brackish sea, and is also one of the largest inland seas generally in the [url=https://cdn.discordapp.com/attachments/482353677848412162/487878789246877697/unknown.png]world at large[/url].[/i]
