Ascaria is a large continental body upon which many diverse lands are located. The Discovery of Ascaria was made by the people of Centrosia in the year 2016. The Continent was largely adopted by the people of Centrosia who had colonized it and became the ruling nation of the land.

The Ordered Governments of Ascaria was the result of this discovery and led to already existing nations falling under Centrosian rule of the region. The Ordered Governments are largely run strictly behind closed doors and the nations residing in Ascaria are highly isolationist.
