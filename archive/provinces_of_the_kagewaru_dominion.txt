Provinces of the Kagewaru Dominion is a region where every new member automatically becomes a province of the Dominion. Provinces participate in community life; promotions are awarded for their activity and loyalty&#151;City, Special City, District, Prefecture, and other high ranks. The Dominion is governed by a Monarch, a code of honor is in place, and promotion is accompanied by public ceremonies. The rules are simple: respecting the code and actively participating in regional life is the path to honor and power within the Dominion.
