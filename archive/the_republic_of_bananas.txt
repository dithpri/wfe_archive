The Republic of Bananas isn&#39;t actually a republic at all, but a far-off coastal island that has been left untouched by the human race for centuries. It was discovered by the Turthatornian people in 1300 AD, and was rediscovered in the 21st century by other nations.

The natives have recently accepted to share their land with the Chinese Sharktoothian folk, and both are willing to invite small colonies when need be.
