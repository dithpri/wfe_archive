Argentine Antarctica (Spanish: Ant&aacute;rtida Argentina or Sector Ant&aacute;rtico Argentino) is a sector of Antarctica claimed by Argentina as part of its national territory consisting of the Antarctic Peninsula and a triangular section extending to the South Pole, delimited by the 25&deg; West and 74&deg; West meridians and the 60&deg; South parallel. This region overlaps the British and Chilean claims in Antarctica. The Antarctic Treaty Secretariat being based in Buenos Aires. &#151;Wikipedia
