The World of Tomorrow is full of the challenges and rewards of the near future. Currently, this region is preparing to undergo the next great revolution in human history: the Robotic Revolution. Nations will be responsible for preparing society for this massive transition. 

The World of Tomorrow is a peaceful region, with military action taken only when absolutely merited. The World of Tomorrow hopes to become a reasoned, pragmatic voice in the World Assembly, ultimately helping guide the world towards order and prosperity for all.
