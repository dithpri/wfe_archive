  A truly democratic League of Nations. After a meticulous examination performed by the Founder (The Federation of European Social Republics) or its Delegate (as long as there is one), any applicant State may join the League, simply signing a Declaration of Mutual Independence and Respect stating that the Nation agrees to mutually guarantee the independence of all members and respect their political opinions.
  Of course, Applicants must also agree with having a form of government that does not exploits its population and also guarantees them fundamental rights as liberty of speech and non-arbitrary imprisonment. Applicants don&amp;#39;t have to guarantee specific capitalist rights, like private property or freedom of political party association.
