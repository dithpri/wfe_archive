[i]Monoceros (&amp;#924;&amp;#959;&amp;#957;&amp;#972;&amp;#954;&amp;#949;&amp;#961;&amp;#969;&amp;#962;, &quot;unicorn&quot;): is a faint constellation on the celestial equator. Its definition is attributed to the 17th-century cartographer Petrus Plancius. It is bordered by Orion to the west, Gemini to the north, Canis Major to the south, and Hydra to the east. Other bordering constellations include Canis Minor, Lepus, and Puppis.[/i][b]Founded by the andarian starfleet on: 04/05/2025.[/b]
