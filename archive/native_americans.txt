Upon venturing into the new world, Spanish and British colonists succumbed to malaria, chlamydia cholera and starvation. The only colony to stand was the Dutch colony of New Amsterdam, which traded and mixed with the Algonquian peoples as a means of survival. With access to European technology the various peoples of North Eastern America expanded across the continent, eventually spreading this technology to the Mississippian, Uto-Aztecan, Maya, Salish and Andean people. It is 2013 and the continents are dominated by a varied an burgeoning Amerindian population.
