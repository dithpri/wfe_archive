Welcome to Crovathan, a land full of diverse people, environments, and history. To the north, Wolfsnow dominates the land with cold climates and caves. To the east Pheioix, a nation recovering from the most recent war. Anf territory controlled by a alien race know as Crods. To the south is Ekoridge, a Nation just as powerful as Wolfsnow, capitalizing on democracy and culture. Skworpia, a territory controlled by a intergalactic empire with many hidden motives. To the West is Wolf Initiative. A once powerful nation that has returned from the stars.
