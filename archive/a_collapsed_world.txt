In 2057, much of human civilization collapsed with the appearance of a new disease which came into existence almost overnight. The only way that many thousands of people managed to survive was to retract to where civilization had never touched. By 2059, it was once more safe to return to where civilization had once existed. To rebuild civilization in hopes of rebuilding some semblance of civilization. This is the story of those people.
