An Empire of Individual nations, the leader of each nation within the Empire is a Senator with equal say at all Senate meetings. Senators propose and debate all matters of Law for the Empire and decide what to bring to the Emperors attention. the Consul is the head of the Senate and is the representative to the Emperor&#39;s Council who represent the Emperor. The Emperor is the supreme leader of the Empire and has final say on ALL matters.

The Empire is represented in the World Assembly by a Delegate, Delegates are chosen by the Senate but can be chosen directly by the Emperor.
