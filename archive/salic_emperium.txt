The Salic Emperium is an Empire of Individual nations. The leader of each nation is a Senator within the Emperium. Senators propose and debate all matters of Law for the Empire and decide what to bring to the Emperors attention during sessions. The Consul Pro Tempore is the head of the Senate and is the representative to the Empyrean Council who in turn represent the Emperor. The Empyrean Rex is the supreme leader of the Empire and has final say on ALL matters.

The Empire is represented in the World Assembly by a Delegate, Delegates are chosen by the Senate.
