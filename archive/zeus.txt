[color=navy][b]Zeus[/b][/color]

[color=mediumblue]Zeus, aka Dias, Jupiter. The child of Cronus and Rhea, married Herea and fathered Ares, Hebe and Hephaestus. He is also said to have fathered Athena, Apollo, Artemis, Hermes, Persephone, Dionysus, Perseus, Heracles, Helen of Troy, Minos and the Muses.[/color]

[color=midnightblue]He is respected as an Allfather, the chief of the gods, even gods that were not his children called him father. He is equated with many other gods of thunder, as well as the eagle.[/color]
