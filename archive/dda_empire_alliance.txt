A meteor strike has destroyed the world as we know it.

The year is 2022. DDA Empire Alliance is a frozen place ruled by fascists. Once glorious, Golden Gate Bridge is now frozen.

Adorable painter, DCI Harriet Thornhill is humanity&#39;s only hope. Harriet finds the courage to start a secret revolutionary organization called The Nazis.

The fight is jeopardised when Harriet is tricked by stupid navigator, DI Clarke Thunder, and injures her big toe.

Armed with Passion Power Of Spoon and Junta Military, The Nazis try their best to save mankind, but can they defeat malicious fascists and restore Golden Gate Bridge to its former glory?
