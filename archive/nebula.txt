A region beyond stars! 

Floating in the endless expanse of the cosmos, Nebula is a celestial region where intergalactic civilizations converge. A vast, ever-shifting domain of luminous gas clouds, asteroid belts, and distant star systems, it is home to advanced empires, nomadic fleets, and enigmatic cosmic entities.

The region serves as a hub of diplomacy, trade, and conflict, with factions vying for influence over its countless worlds. From hyper-advanced AI-led societies to organic species harnessing the power of black holes, Nebula is a place of wonder and peril.

Among the stars, we rise.
