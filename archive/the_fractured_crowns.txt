[color=lime][b]Welcome to The Fractured Crowns![/b][/color]

The Fractured Crowns is a Medieval Kingdom [b]strictly[/b] run on the [b]Feudal System[/b], with every nation within the region automatically being part of the hierarchy. 
At the top is the [color=blueviolet][b]King[/b][/color], of course, and many layers below, structured as the following: 

[b][color=blueviolet]King[/color]
[color=gold]Duke[/color]
[color=royalblue]Earl[/color]
[color=red]Viscount[/color]
[color=lime]Baron[/color]
[color=brown]Lord[/color][/b]

Of course there are those dirty [b]Peasants[/b] and [b]Serfs[/b] at the bottom, but those are the populations of your nation account. 
Every [b]player[/b] within the region is part of the Nobility, just varying tiers of it. 

More more in-depth info in the dispatches below. 

So if you like Medieval times, and want to be part of a functional Kingdom, [color=lime]The Fractured Crowns[/color] is the place for you!
