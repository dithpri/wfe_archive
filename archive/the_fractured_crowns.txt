Welcome to The Fractured Crowns! 

This region is a Medieval society strictly run on the Feudal System (or Feudalism). 
Each nation within the region is considered to be a &quot;Fief&quot;, a subdivision of land legally under the administration of the Fief above  (A big fief can have 3 fiefs within it, and each of those can have 3, etc). 

The earlier you join, the more fiefs/nations you will rule later on. 
New players are put under the rule of an existing fief, so the earlier you join, the more players that join later on will be put under your rule. 
Don&#39;t be too worried even if you&#39;re at the bottom of the system however, you are not a peasant, you are still a lord, and still rule over the population of your nation&#39;s account. 

Have a Noble Stay!
