A Steampunk/Dieselpunk/Interwar Tech World with fantasy race and creatures such as Elves, Trolls, Cat people, etc.

The climate is largely freezing cold in the north, temperate in central and warmer in the south.

The dominant cultures are Celtic from Wyrnsturm Empire and German from Redosia Federation.

The human race begins to colonize and dominate the region at 100 B.C though that doesn&amp;#39;t mean non-human races aren&amp;#39;t able to fight back.
