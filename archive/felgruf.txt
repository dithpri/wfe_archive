Felgruf is a large continent with various fantasy races and creatures such as Elves, Trolls, Cat people, etc.

The climate is largely freezing cold in the north, temperate in central and warmer in the south.

The human race begins to colonize and dominate some part of the region at 100 B.C.

The tech-level in Felgruf are limited to Magic, Steampunk, Dieselpunk, Interwar and Lovecraftian.
