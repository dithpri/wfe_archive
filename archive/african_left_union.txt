This is the African Left Union.
We invite everyone who shares with us leftist values such as Liberty, Equality and Fraternity to join us.
We also especially welcome movements such as Anarchism, Libertarianism, Communism, Liberalism and others.
Unity in diversity!

Kani waa Ururka Bidixda Afrika.
Waxaan ku martiqaadeynaa qof kasta oo nala wadaaga qiyamka bidix sida Xoriyadda, Sinnaanta iyo Walaalnimada inuu nagu soo biiro.
Waxaan sidoo kale si gaar ah u soo dhaweyneynaa dhaqdhaqaaqyada sida Anarchism, Libertarianism, Communism, Liberalism iyo kuwa kale.
Midnimo kala duwanaanshuhu!
