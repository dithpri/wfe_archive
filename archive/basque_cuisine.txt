Euskal Herriko sukaldaritza - Basque cuisine

Basque cuisine refers to the cuisine of the Basque Country and includes, among others, meats and fish grilled over hot coals, marmitako and lamb stews, cod, Tolosa bean dishes, paprikas from Lekeitio, pintxos (Basque tapas), Idiazabal sheep&#39;s cheese, txakoli (sparkling white wine), and Basque cider.
