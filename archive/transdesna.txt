The region of Transdesna is located within the former Sumy Oblast of Ukraine.

It is ruled by a National [nation]Directory[/nation] composed of 14 ministers acting as executives, with &amp;#1030;&amp;#1085;&amp;#1076;&amp;#1080;&amp;#1095;&amp;#1082;&amp;#1072; &amp;#1050;&amp;#1086;&amp;#1090;&amp;#1083;&amp;#1077;&amp;#1090;&amp;#1110;/Indychka Kotleti as the de facto head.
Transdesna is a one-party state, however, there are a number of unofficial factions within the party. The current Directory consists of 10 members of the Main Wing of the Communist Party, 1 progressive, and 3 members of the Central Club.
