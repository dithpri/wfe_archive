DGS is a region that stands stolidly against the advances of socialism and corporate regulation. DGS members hail from all ends of the political spectrum - big government or small - with the sole condition that socialism is to be opposed at every turn, and the virtues of capitalism held as sacrosanct.
If you are a new player of NationStates looking for a region safe from the omnipresent curse of leftism and socialism, DGS is the region for you!
