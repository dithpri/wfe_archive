New found archipelago with a larger landmass on it&#39;s center. Natives are no more advanced than steam age, but more advanced and greedy corporate states started colonizing it. The Theocracy of Mori Liv, in the most beautiful island, tries to act as a mediator of peace and politics, while the peasants of Tank Kingdom, seeing the imminent invasion of the colonists, took down the government to form an advanced, more socialist and military matriarchy to protect the people from the imminent poverty and abuses of those advanced nations.
