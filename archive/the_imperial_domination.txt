The galaxy was long ago conquered by the forces of the sith and shall remain forever under their guiding hand and jackboot. Soldiers march under darkened skies, given their colors by smoke, ash, and the shadows of warships. Quadrillions serve the great Emperor Vitiate under the Grand red, black, and white banner of the [nation]Sith Aeternum[/nation]. Slavery is common, death, even more so, the only meaning in life under the Empire is service, in every meaning of the word, to the Sith, the Government, and the Emperor.
