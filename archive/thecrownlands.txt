Overview:

The Crownlands are the central and most prestigious region of the Empire of Andsmark. Home to the capital city Ands, the Crownlands represent the beating heart of Andsmark&#39;s culture, diplomacy, and governance. Rich in history and natural beauty, the region is known for its serene landscapes, fortified cities, and the enduring wisdom of its people.

Culture and Values:

Deeply rooted in principles of diplomacy, unity, and respect, the Crownlands are guided by a philosophy of peace through strength. While Andsmark is not a nation that seeks war, its people are proud and prepared. The region emphasizes education, innovation, and neutrality, offering asylum and negotiation before aggression.
