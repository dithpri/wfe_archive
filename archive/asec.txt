[b]ASEC: Alliance for Security and Economic Cooperation[/b]

Welcome to ASEC &#151; a multilateral alliance of sovereign states united by common goals of economic integration, collective security, and political coordination.

Founded in response to a shifting global order, ASEC fosters cooperation through mutual defense pacts, trade liberalization, and regional development initiatives. Member nations retain full sovereignty while benefiting from shared infrastructure, diplomatic collaboration, and intergovernmental institutions.
