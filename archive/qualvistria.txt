Qualvistria is the new name for the former Fifth Qualvistan Republic.

Qualvistria is a regional grouping of nations historically associated with [nation]Qualvista[/nation]. Membership is invite only. It is governed democratically by the Qualvistrian Senate, with senators elected by each country for 5 year terms. The current leader of the senate is senior representative from Mageen, Senator Sylvania Sinatra.

In the absence of a WA delegate, [nation]Qualvista[/nation] is considered de-facto delegate, being the only regional WA member.
