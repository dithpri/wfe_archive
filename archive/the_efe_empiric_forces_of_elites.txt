The Empiric Forces of Elites (EFE) welcomes empires of all sizes and only those that meet its requirements
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
EFE Requirements: Nations must pay 5% of their GDP on Defese towards them and the EFE. 1 of 3 times, a nation must declare war on the warring opposition of their ally. A nation cannot declare war on members of the EFE. If done, they will be kicked out of the EFE. These rules if broken mean expulsion from EFE.
