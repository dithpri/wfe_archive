Found in 19 September 2018, U.P.H.C. this region commits to educate its member countries. The system in U.P.H.C. is called &quot;World Class Achievers and Learners&quot;, facilitated with multiple world-class facilities to provide nations the chance to practice what they have learned realistically.

Vision:
True Knowledge, Freedom of Faith, Unity of Member Countries.
Mission:
Leading economic growth, political freedom, and civil rights to its full potential, Preparing the nations as a lifelong learner.
