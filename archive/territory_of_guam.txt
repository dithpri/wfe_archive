Guam, formally the [b]Territory of Guam[/b] is an unincorporated and organized territory of the United States. Located in the northwestern Pacific Ocean, Guam is one of five American territories with an established civilian government. The capital city is Hag&aring;t&ntilde;a, and the most populous city is Dededo. In 2015, 161,785 people reside on Guam. Guamanians are American citizens by birth. Guam has an area of 210 sq mi and a density of 770/sq mi. It is the largest and southernmost of the Mariana Islands, and the largest island in Micronesia.[hr]
