Neo-Gaia has become a place where people can set up their own personal political project and unite with people who shared the same beliefs. 
There is a basic constitution set up by the original colonist which all future arrivals have to abide. Some of the more important rules include: no interpersonal and interregional violence, no intervention into other regions&#39; affairs, people are free to arrive and leave, no permanent degradation of nature and preservation of live.
