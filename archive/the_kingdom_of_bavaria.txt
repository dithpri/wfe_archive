[b][color=dodgerblue]The Kingdom of Bavaria[/color][/b]

A German state that existed from 1806 to 1918, the Bavarian Elector Maximilian IV Joseph of the House of Wittelsbach became the first King of Bavaria in 1806 as Maximilian I Joseph. As a state within the German Empire, the kingdom was second in size only to the Kingdom of Prussia.[hr]
