Aristokuvaa a Region that prides itself on a strong, firm government that emphasises safety and common sense over individual rights.

The Capital Nation is Aristokuvaa.

Benevolent Dictatorships are the preferred method as government, as the majority of people are too stupid.
Member nations, however, are encouraged but not required to adhere to this rule.

All cults, including mainstream religion, are banned. Member nations are required to follow this rule.

Aristokuvaa is a Benevolent Dictatorship, as such, the WA Delegate should be the Founder. Violation of this rule is punishable by permanent exile. Endorsement caps are to be in effect as soon as reasonably possible.
