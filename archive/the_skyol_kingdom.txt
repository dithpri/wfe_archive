In the northern mountains all across europe and Asia, new nations arise. People that were previously unheard of have risen from the unknown. These new nations have formed an alliance, The Skyol Kingdom. Though countries and regions seperate these formidable nations, they are claiming the lands around their mountainous homes. These people were barbarians, fighters, warriors, these are the descendants of the first proud warriors and they have come to take their ancestral lands back, with new weapons.
