The [b][colour=gold]Alliance of Liberal Democratic States[/colour][/b] is an intergovernmental organisation dedicated to the development and promotion of democracy and the rule of law within our member states. We also support globalisation and greater economic cooperation within the region. 

We welcome all developing nations that are transitioning or have recently transitioned into a democracy.

Our regional apparatus includes (subject to change):
[b].[/b] ALDS Regional Secretariat (Executive)
[b].[/b] ALDS Council (Quasi-Legislature: Representatives of Member Governments)
[b].[/b] ALDS Court of Human Rights and Natural Justice (Judiciary)
[b].[/b] ALDS Economic And Trade Affairs Council
[b].[/b] ALDS Centre for Cross-Cultural Dialogue and Cooperation
