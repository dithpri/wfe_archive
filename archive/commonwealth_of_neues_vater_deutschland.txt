This region encompasses all lands not geographically within the borders of the Empire of Neues Vater Deutschland. While these lands, territories, and colonies are not under direct geographical rule of His Imperial Majesty, they are nevertheless to be considered direct subjects to His rule. All inhabitants are bound by taxation, military service, and obedience to all laws and regulations passed within the Empire. All governing bodies and inhabitants of these lands owe ultimate allegiance, fidelity, and absolute submission to the person of the Emperor.
