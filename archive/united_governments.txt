Economy: The United Governments trade as much as possible with each other to keep peace, enrich culture, and benefit economically.
Military: Each nation in the United Governments has its own standing military (and when available, Colonial Defense Force). But when war rages between another region or two nations violate the [b]Treaty of Security[/b], the United Governments Defense Force (UGDF) is enacted.
[b]Treaty of Security[/b]: All nations within the United Governments, no matter their political views or religion, will work together to better the region and defend it when necessary.
