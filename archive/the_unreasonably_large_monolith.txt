For years NationStates has been plagued by the raider menace. Region after region has fallen at their hands. Hoping to commemorate the lost regions, A Large Monolith was commissioned to remember the fallen regions. However as time has passed and the number of dead regions has risen, the Monolith has grown unreasonably tall. So tall that those seeking shelter from the raiders have made their homes on the monolith itself, hoping to escape the chaos below.
Thus is the origin of, The Unreasonably Large Monolith!
