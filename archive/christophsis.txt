Cristophsis was an outer rim world in the Star Wars universe which saw heavy fighting during The Clone Wars. The crystalline planet was the site of a major battle between the CIS and the Galactic Republic, in which the Republic managed to win after using a stealth ship to break the CIS blockade over the planet.[/color][hr]
