After the great wars, factions of humans split up to wait out the fallout from the most destructive world nuclear exchange. After thousands of year of harsh fallout the lands are habitable though full of dangerous mutated creatures and radiation zones. One group of people that have come to be known as the Brotherhood of the Steel has emerged from their bunkers procuring old world tech so that they can advance their own cause.
