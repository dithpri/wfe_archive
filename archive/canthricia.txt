Canthricia is a free republic region in which all joining nations may retain their former government. The only requirements for joining are to remain loyal to Canthricia and to obey Tenebaruusian laws.
Any country can join Canthricia.
Canthricia currently has constructed embassies with the USSSO Empire and has proposed for construction of embassies with the North American Federation.
Our nations: Tenebaruus, Poophead Land, Fnafland, Trayaurusland, and the USS of America.
