Canthricia is a free republic region in which all joining nations may retain their former government. The only requirements for joining are to remain loyal to Canthricia and to obey Tenebaruusian laws.
Any country can join Canthricia.
Canthricia currently has constructed embassies with the USSSO Empire, the Confederation of Free Republics and the Central Powers and has proposed for construction of embassies with the North American Federation.
In a few weeks, the Great Canthrician Map will be constructed. All countries, publish factbooks containing a map!
Our nations: Tenebaruus, Poophead Land, Fnafland, Trayaurusland, the USS of America, Opiuchia, and New Akan.
