The one and only region in the world that follows [b]Distributism[/b], an economic and political philosophy called the third way between Capitalism and Socialism; our goal then is &amp;#39;moralistic capitalism&amp;#39; or &amp;quot;Economics as if Families Mattered&amp;quot; where, as opposed to capitalism, as many people have property as possible in a way that allows them to subsist.  

The champion of the cause was G.K. Chesterton who responded to a call in the Catholic Church by Pope Leo XIII outlined in RERUM NOVARUM.
