The NAR, Anarchic Republic, or Native Union, is a community founded by Supreme Leader:Noah S Mullins, mainly focused around Weapon Development, and Schools. This United Country allows free jobs, and Welfare. The democracy is commonly used in 16/20 states of the NAR. The main economic status is: 
-Communism
-Anarchy
-Socialism
As an Anarchic Republic, it does save some pretty beutiful landscapes like the Wanted River, the Fried Land Mountains, and the First Wind Islands, the country is located in Saudi Arabia.
