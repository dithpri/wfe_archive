After the Roman Empire was reformed, its control quickly spread across the Northern Hemisphere. It established itself as a world superpower declaring itself a greater power than the World Assembly itself. However these claims appear to be patriotic propaganda rather than the true beliefs of its Government. Any country in the region of The Empire of Nova Roma et agros captos automatically becomes a sub-state but retains partial freedom.
With the exception of Akavar,  The nation, Nova Roma et agros captos and region  are one and the same.

For now nations can apply for an Officer post and if sensible, will probably receive that post.
