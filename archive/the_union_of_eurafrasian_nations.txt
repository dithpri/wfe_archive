[b][u][region]The Union of Eurafrasian Nations[/region][/u][/b]

This is the region of Eurafrasia, also known as Afro-Eurasia. We welcome all nations located in Europe, Africa, and Asia. All we ask is that you have fun.

[u][b]Regional Government:[/u][/b]

[B]Regional Governor:[/b]
The Regional Governor has executive authority and the ability to make any change to the region at any time. They can also veto regional legislation.

[b]The High Council:[/b]
The High Council consists of four nations who make regional legislation. The Head of The High Council oversees meetings and can send regional legislation to the governor for further review. The other three council nations each represent one of the Eurafrasian sub-regions of Europe, Africa, and Asia.
