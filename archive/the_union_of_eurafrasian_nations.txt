[b][u][region]The Union of Eurafrasian Nations[/region][/u][/b]

This is the region of Eurafrasia, also known as Afro-Eurasia. We welcome all nations located in Europe, Africa, and Asia, wether it be a real nation or an alternative history nation, all are welcome. You can hang out and talk about whatever you&#39;d like, just have fun.

[u][b]Regional Government:[/u][/b]

[B]Regional Governor:[/b]
The Regional Governor has executive authority and the ability to make any change to the region at any time.

[b]The High Council:[/b]
The High Council consists of four nations who make decisions for the region. The Head of The High Council oversees meetings and is also the Regional Governor. The other three council nations each represent one of the Eurafrasian sub-regions of Europe, Africa, and Asia.
