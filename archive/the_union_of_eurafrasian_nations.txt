[b][u][region]The Union of Eurafrasian Nations[/region][/u][/b]

This is the region of Eurafrasia, also known as Afro-Eurasia. We welcome all nations located in Europe, Africa, and Asia. All we ask is that you have fun.

[u][b]Regional Government:[/u][/b]

[b]The High Council:[/b]
The High Council consists of three nations who make regional legislation. The Head of The High Council oversees meetings and has total authority over The Union of Eurafrasian Nations. The three council nations each represent one of the Eurafrasian sub-regions of Europe, Africa, and Asia.
