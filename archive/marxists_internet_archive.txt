[b]The [color=red]Marxists Internet Archive[/color] ([url=http://www.marxists.org/]MIA[/url]) is an all-volunteer, non-profit public library, started more than 20 years ago in 1990. 

In 2007, MIA has 62 active volunteers from 33 different countries. MIA contains the writings of 592 authors representing a complete spectrum of political, philosophical, and scientific thought, generally spanning the past 200 years. MIA contains these writings in 45 different languages, comprising a total size of over 53,000 documents and 29 GB of data, all created through the work of volunteers around the world.[/b]
