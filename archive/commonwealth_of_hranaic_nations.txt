The Commonwealth of Hranaic Nations was formed in 1903 following the Ramenian Civil War. The civil war resulted in the dissolution of Old Ramenia. From the ashes of Old Ramenia rose the great nations of New Ramenia, Tofundiana, and the Republic of Soyenistan. The number of Hranaic nations continues to grow.
The political landscape of the region ranges from Socialist States to Democratic Republics. Even Capitalist nations can be fruitful under the guidance of the Commonwealth
