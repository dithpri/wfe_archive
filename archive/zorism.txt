Zorism is the belief of equivalent exchange.In order to gain something one must sacrifice something of equal value.A human being is divide into four parts:
1)The body which is the physical part of the human that is bound by time,in other words it is a temporary shell(vessel).
2)The soul is the power house of the body,it is the consciousness of a human being that control s thoughts and emotions.The soul has an infinite value which can not be recreated.
3)The spirit is the bonds and chains that hold the body and soul together they are the life of a human being.
4)The shadow form is the negative of the soul which seeks to control all parts of the human.The shadow form represents the bad in the person.
