The United Democratic Confederation (UDC) strives to accomplish 3 main things:

1. Achieve global unity of democratic nations
2. Solve conflicts between nations using diplomatic solutions
3. Build a supporting region that allows young nations to grow and prosper while under the protection of powerful, developed, experienced regions.

Elections for positions take place one every two months. Positions are:

Regional Leader: Querot
Lead Regional Adviser: (open)
World Assembly Delegate: (Querot and open for election)
Security adviser: (open)
Legislative head: (open)
Diplomat (reaches out to other nations): (open)
