The First Empire of Gingerio was created after the founder, High King Nick I of Gingerio, left the current regions of the mordern world due to feelings that they did not promote shared wealth, greater independence and the stiving for higher greatness. Which, of course, The First Empire of GIngerio does. All nations in the empire are not pressured into doing things they do not want too, and are left to do as they please to a certian limit. The empire urges that all efforts must be made to keep the native landscape relitavly unchanged, but mining is allowed
