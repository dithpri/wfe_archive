[nation]Bazita[/nation] is the [url=https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/World_government]world government[/url] of the [url=https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Super-Earth]super-Earth[/url] Edeno. The nation derives its name from the [url=https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Esperanto]Esperanto[/url] adjective [i]bazita[/i], meaning &quot;based&quot;. It is ruled by a [url=https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Secularism]secular[/url] [url=https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Federalism]federal[/url] [url=https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/World_government]semi-direct democracy[/url] that oversees an [url=https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Eco-social_market_economy]eco-social market economy[/url]. Bazitans are guided by a meticulously engineered [url=https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Choice_architecture]choice architecture[/url] designed to maximize their quality of life with regard for others.

Nations are welcome and all embassy requests will be accepted. Bazita itself however takes place in its own separate universe. The inclusion of other nations in this universe is not possible, but communications are still permitted. Residents are encouraged to vote the founder as the regional delegate for the World Assembly, although this is not required.
