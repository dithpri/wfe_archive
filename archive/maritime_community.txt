The [b]Maritime Community (MARICOM)[/b] is a politico-economic union and international intergovernmental cooperation between its 5 members from multiple regions. Their primary objectives is to promote economic integration and cooperation among its members, ensuring economic equality amongst its members and coordinated foreign policy.
