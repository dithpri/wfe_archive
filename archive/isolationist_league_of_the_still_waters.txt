 The Isolationist League of the Still Waters is a quiet refuge for sovereign nations that value independence, neutrality, and restraint. Founded by states weary of endless ideological conflict, performative diplomacy, and constant activity requirements, the League exists primarily to preserve the right of nations to govern themselves in peace.

Roleplay and diplomacy are welcome but entirely voluntary. There are no mandatory Discord servers, activity quotas, or expectations of constant engagement. Silence is respected here.

Whether industrial republic, mountain kingdom, island state, or distant frontier, all nations share one principle:
In the Still Waters, a nation may finally be left alone.
