[color=663300][b]Marrakesh[/b]is a major city in the northwest African nation of Morocco, and is the capital of the mid-southwestern region of Marrakesh-Tensift-El Haouz. Located to the north of the foothills of the snow-capped Atlas Mountain.

Marrakesh is possibly the most important of Morocco&amp;#39;s four former imperial cities (cities that were built by Moroccan Berber empires)[/color]. [color=66666]&#151;Wikipedia[/color][hr]
