This region is for democratic left wing nations. Anarchists, Socialists, Greens, Democratic Communists, Left wing and civil libertarians, environmentalists, human rights activists, peace activists, natural health activists, and animal rights activists are all welcome. Capitalist nations are allowed but frowned upon. All dictatorial, totalitarian, racist, and imperialist nations are banned. Nazis, Zionists, White Supremacists, Fascists, Imperialists or anyone supporting any form of discrimination will be immediately deported and permanently banned from the region.
