[b]Bali Hai[/b], also known as the Makana Mountain, is a part of a National Tropical Botanical Garden at the beginning of the Na Pali Coast on the north shore of Kauai. The Hawaiian name for this pyramid-shaped mountain means &quot;gift from heaven&quot; in the Hawaiian language.
