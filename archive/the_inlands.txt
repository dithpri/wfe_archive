This is [b]Region B[/b], the opposing region of [b][region]The Outskirts[/region][/b] (Region A) where member nations fight in [b]Regional War[/b] to compete for a score. [spoiler=How to Move]To move, join a region created by [nation]Factoristan[/nation] called [region]The Ferry[/region]. You can then type the password into [region]The Outskirts[/region] and it will let you in.[/spoiler] Move between the two regions as you please, but not during Regional War.
