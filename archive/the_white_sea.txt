The [b]White Sea[/b] is a southern inlet of the Barents Sea located on the northwest coast of Russia. It is surrounded by Karelia to the west, the Kola Peninsula to the north, and the Kanin Peninsula to the northeast. The whole of the White Sea is under Russian sovereignty and considered to be part of the internal waters of Russia.

The White Sea is one of four seas named in English (and in other languages such as Russian) after common colour terms &#151; the others being the Black Sea, the Red Sea, and the Yellow Sea. &#151;Wikipedia
