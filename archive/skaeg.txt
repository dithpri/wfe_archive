Recently, due to reasons not yet discernible by man, a section of Balder&amp;#39;s coastline broke off from the main continent, moving off to the south and finally settling some 120000 km away. Mostly intact on this new island is the country of Poro, now free to found their own, new region. Calling their new region Skaeg, they expected to rule this largely deserted island by themselves, but Skaeg was not done morphing. Over the next few months, large sections of previously underwater land resurfaced from the depths, as Skaeg continued to shift and rumble. Most of this new territory was quickly snapped up by Poro, but news of this new land has spread, and settlers have found themselves potentially new places to settle...
