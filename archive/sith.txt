[color=red][b]The Sith, were an ancient order of Force-wielders devoted to the dark side of the Force. Emerging as a divergent faction of the Jedi Order, the Sith became mortal enemies of the Jedi and fought against them in numerous wars. Driven by emotions such as hate and greed, the Sith were deceptive and ultimately obsessed with amassing power no matter the cost.[/b][/color]
