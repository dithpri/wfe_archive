Gaelic geopolitical region based on economic liberalism, free market and individual rights. Capitalist, liberal-conservative, liberal-progressive and, above all, democratic nations are admitted.
The Gaelic Union has its own supra-state representative chamber, a regional parliament constituted on 04/29/2020.
The GU Parliament contains 250 seats and it&#39;s divided as follows:
Liberal-Progressive Alliance - 76 seats (Purple)
Gaelic Conservative Party - 60 seats (Blue)
Social Democratic Union - 53 seats (Red)
Green Alternative - 51 seats (Green)
Nation &amp; Democracy - 6 seats (Black)
Communist League - 4 seats (Red)
