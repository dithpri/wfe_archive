This region is an artifact for making information on the progress of the Rocain project more accessible to interested parties. Nothing interesting ever actually happens here except for the occasional update of Dispatches.

After a long hiatus, the Dispatches made available by this region have been brought up to date. I tried to keep the nation overviews and ranking information current on a monthly basis. But first there was the April disk crash, and then...

[b]Note:[/b] The Dispatches are now up to date as of November 2024. However, due to real-world political events, the Rocain project is on temporary, perhaps permanent, hold. Further updates may not be forthcoming.

For basic information on the Rocain project, see the region [region]Rocain Labs[/region].
