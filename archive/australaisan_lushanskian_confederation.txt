Australaisa Lushansk , officially the Australaisan Lushanskian Confederation, was a loose confederation formed  in the late 20th century between the Eastern European country of Lushansk and fellow Commonwealth Member the UAC . The confederation was founded on 1 February 1962 following an agreement between the two countries signed on 12 December 1961. It was intended to promote cooperation between the two countries, but was dissolved by Lushansk on 30 September 2029
