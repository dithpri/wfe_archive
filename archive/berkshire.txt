[region]Berkshire[/region] is a historic county in the southeast of England, within the [region]Empire of Britain[/region]. Known as the &quot;Royal County&quot; due to the presence of Windsor Castle, Berkshire features a mix of historic towns, rolling countryside, and key economic centers. The county is a hub for both tourism and modern business.
