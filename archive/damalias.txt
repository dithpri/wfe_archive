[region]Damalias[/region] is the name given to a group of islands located in the south of the world, between Africa and South America. [nation]Fahrgrave[/nation], among the better known nations of Damalias, is the elected World Assembly Delegate, whereas [nation]The High Kingdom of Arkasia[/nation] holds the title of founder. Several regional officers have been appointed to help our delegate and founder in managing Damalias.
