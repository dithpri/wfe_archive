[u]Voting Procedure/Rules[/u]:
  -There will be a three day long primary where we will narrow the Race down to the top two earning candidates
  -After this takes place we will proceed into the actual voting*
  -The winner of the second round of voting is the winner of the Delegatcy 
  -Candidates will not be permitted to vote for themselves
  -All voting will take place in poll form and endorsements will be awarded to the winner
  -A Delegates term will last 1 month
  -Voting will take place on the second Thursday of each month
