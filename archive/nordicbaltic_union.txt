Welcome to the Nordic-Baltic Union, a fictional union of the Nordics and the Baltic nations.

The union contains 6 EU members (Finland, Estonia, Sweden, Denmark, Latvia and Lithuania), 6 NATO members(Estonia, Denmark, Latvia, Norway and Lithuania) and 4 eurozone members(all the Baltic states + Finland). All members are members of the Schengen area and everyone is allowed to move, live or work freely within the union.

The union aims to bring co-operation between the Nordic states and the Baltic states.
