Kunming (/k&amp;#650;n&amp;#712;m&amp;#618;&amp;#331;/;[3] Chinese: &amp;#26118;&amp;#26126;; pinyin: K&amp;#363;nm&iacute;ng), also known as Yunnan-Fu, is the capital and largest city of Yunnan province, China.[4] It is the political, economic, communications and cultural centre of the province as well as the seat of the provincial government.
