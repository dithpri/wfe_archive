The Union of Democratic Socialist Republics is confederacy of sovereign states that share the same views of Democratic Socialism. They work under the leadership of the Democratic Republic of Karoussa. All nations who are defined as democratic socialists or liberal democratic socialists, or working actively to achieve this, are welcome in the Union.
