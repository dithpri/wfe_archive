[b]The Vasilic Sea[/b]

The Vasilic Sea is a sea filled with island nations that are organized in a confederation, the [i]Confederation of the Vasilic Sea[/i].
[hr]

[b]The Council of the Vasilic Sea[/b]
It is the administrative body of the Confederation, it is comprised of :
General Councillor
Deputy Councillor
World Assembly Delegate
Ambassador of the Vasilic Sea
President of the Vasilic Commission

[b]The Vasilic Commission[/b]
It is the legislative body of the Confederation, it is comprised of :
President of the Vasilic Commission
One representative by member state.
