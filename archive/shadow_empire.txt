Veiled in eternal twilight, this is a realm shrouded in enigma and dark power. Its eerie landscapes guard secrets untold. The elusive shadow assassins are silent and mysterious, their eyes reflecting the abyss.

Ruled by the Aryan Kings, whispers of immortality and sorcery permeate the shadows. Rituals under twin moons summon ancient spirits, their meanings lost to outsiders.

The Nightfall Phalanx strikes unseen, with tactics cloaked in mystery, bringing swift and inexplicable victories.
