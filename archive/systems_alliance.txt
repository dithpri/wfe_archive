The Systems Alliance is the multinational representative body of Earth and all human colonies in Citadel space. Backed by Earth&amp;#39;s most powerful nations, the Alliance has become humanity&amp;#39;s military, exploratory, and economic spearhead.
It is headed by Prime Minister Augustus Hahne and the Alliance Parliament.  Notable corporations supported by the Alliance:Rosenkov Materials/Elkoss Combine/Sirta Foundation/Aldrin Labs/Hahne-Kedar Corporation.
The Alliance Parliament is located on Titan Station, Saturn. The capital is located in Adelphia, USNA. The UN Security Council serves as the notable military leader of the Alliance.
