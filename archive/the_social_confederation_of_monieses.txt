[b]THE PREAMBLE OF THE CONSTITUTION OF THE SOCIAL CONFEDERATION OF MONIESES[/b]	
     In times of disparity, it becomes necessary for the peoples of a nation to revolt against the tyrannical power. In the case of Moniesians, there is an overwhelming need for justice among the common people. It is the responsibility of this union to be established, promoting the basic rights of the people, the infrastructure for which the Confederation depends on, and the general welfare of the citizens of The Social Confederation of Monieses.
