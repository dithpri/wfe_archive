[b]THE CONSTITUTION OF THE SOCIAL CONFEDERATION OF MONIESES[/b]

I. There shall be a council made up of thirteen members to decide the laws of the republic. These council members shall have the power to create a police force and a military in order to enforce said laws. Amendment of the constitution is allowed so long as the council has decided on an amendment with two thirds of the council members in agreement. A national election held every 4 years will decide who is a member of the council. Anyone who is a citizen of the republic, is not a felon, and is 30 years of age of older may run for councilship. Term length is 4 years and any one council member may only be in the council for 4 terms.
