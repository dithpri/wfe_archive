In this world of Technology and Magic, the fires of industry feed the flames of war, and its nations are protected not only by its mighty militaries, but by the power and integrity of its Knight Orders, the individuals who, by the use of Mana, can channel their arcane energies in explosions of terrible destruction. Also the multitude of species of this world garantees that difering cultures and points of view makes the flames of conflict burn even higher.
