The North American Defense Union was formed after the Ethipioaian War. The Hoosier Alliance and the Former Alpine Nations signed the Hoosier/Alpine Defense Treaty to protect their national interests. Later, it was decided to open the alliance to other nations and it was renamed. 

This is a region made up of RPers. If you wish to join contact The Hoosier Alliance or The Former Alpine Nations.
