The history of Geography Land begins with its separation from a larger landmass. Through the process of continental drift, tectonic plates gradually shifted and caused the separation of the continent from others.  Notably, a society of exceptionally intelligent dogs, and a society of average intelligence cats, leading to the development of indigenous cultures and societies, but also a countless wars and conflict between the dog and cat societies, as well as a notable dog-based Civil War which led to the fall of the Labradorian Republic, and the rise of the Cashtrevian Empire. Among the cats, the most notable society to survive into the modern era is the Kingdom of Tabbyland.
