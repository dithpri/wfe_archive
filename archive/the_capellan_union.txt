Starting off in around the year 2154, by ancient Tolrainian mercenaries when they found a planet perfect for human life. After about 100 or so terran standard years new colonists moved in and started some of the earliest cities, though the people of Capella would soon find out not all who wonder the stars are friendly. around the year 2306 the first Capellan War started when several nobles marched on the capitol wanting to rule the Capellan people.
