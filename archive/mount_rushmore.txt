The southeastern face of [b]Mount Rushmore[/b] in South Dakota&#39;s [region]Black Hills[/region] National Forest is the site of four gigantic carved sculptures depicting the faces of U.S. Presidents George Washington, Thomas Jefferson, Abraham Lincoln and Theodore Roosevelt. Led by the sculptor Gutzon Borglum, work on the project began in 1927 and was finally completed in 1941. 

Known as the &quot;Shrine of Democracy,&quot; Mount Rushmore welcomes upwards of 2 million visitors every year, and is one of America&#39;s most popular tourist attractions. &#151;Wikipedia[hr]
