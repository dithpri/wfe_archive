The Expiation of Anarchy consists of many nations, two of which: The Armed Democratic Confederacy of Retlokia, the host for the Altman Resistance, and the Empire of Neocretatia, the host for the Neoteric Empire, these two sides have been at war for 152 years, previously there was the Boreal War, and the infamous Rapturic War, which ravaged many continents leaving scars of nuclear disaster, the current year is 2056.

Nations/States can choose their faction in the message board.
