COMMANDMENTS:
I. You will have no other God but Him.
II. You will not make Idols.
III. You will not say His Name worthlessly. 
IV. Remember Sunday. 
V. Honor your Father and your Mother. 
VI. You will not kill. 
VII. You will not do cheat.
VIII. You will not steal. 
IX. You will not lie. 
X. You will not lust.
