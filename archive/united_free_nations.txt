Tiny Alliance for free nations. Founded in 2020. 
[b]Flag[/b]
The flag is known as the &quot;Flaggy Flag&quot; and has the flag of the founding nation, The Democratic States of Quruqlik, in the upper left corner. In the upper right corner is A big star representing the founding nation and below it are stars representing its other members. Every time a new member joins the region the flag is updated.
