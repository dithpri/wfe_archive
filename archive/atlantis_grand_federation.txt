Atlantis Grand Federation is an archipelago of volcanic origin, quite ancient and exhibiting a great ethnic diversity. 

Atlantis Grand Federation functions as a federation of confederacies, sharing the same flag, constitution, currency and a great devotion to Oceans.

Atlantean Antigravitational Aero-orbital crafts, the Haunebus, are famous around the world.

Fucused on mystical and scientific research as well as keeping the ancient spiritualities as cultural heritage.
