The Rift Alliance is a Republic sub-faction of powerful worlds united by their shared protests at Republic &quot;incompetence&quot; and their threat to secede. Losing any one of these planets would be a catastrophe for the Republic; losing all of them would cause the Republic&#39;s collapse.
