[b][u]Jurisdiction of the Court[/u][/b]
-The Court shall have jurisdiction over all extra-regional affairs including but not limited to disputes and agreements between regional governments, nations and polities in different regions, inter-regional organisations, alliances and treaties.
-The Court&#39;s jurisdiction shall extend to any judicial matter delegated to it by any Region or organisation.
