[color=green]The Democratic Federation of Northern Syria (DFNS), commonly known as Rojava, is a [i]de facto[/i] autonomous region in northern Syria. It consists of three self-governing regions: Afrin Region, Jazira Region, and Euphrates Region. The region gained its de facto autonomy in 2012 as part of the ongoing Rojava conflict and the wider Syrian Civil War.[/color]


This region serves as puppet storage for [nation]Yaaz[/nation][/b]
