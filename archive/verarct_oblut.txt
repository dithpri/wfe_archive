The continent of [region]Verarct Oblut[/region] is a large landmass located far from any other surrounding continents, It is surrounded by three extremely volatile oceans that prevent outer-continental travel and in extreme cases inter-continental travel.
The continents nation are no strangers to war but for the time being they are all at relative peace, however the aftermath of the Great Verarctien war that split the continents political allegiances and has left a bitter atmosphere among the nations.
