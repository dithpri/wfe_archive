[b]Global Security Alliance or GSA, [color=blue]is an intergovernmental organization responsible for maintaining international peace and security of the region.[/color][/b]

It was originally created by [nation]Greater Alpha[/nation] in response to the increasing tension between them and the [nation]United States of Atlactican[/nation] caused by a rogue Atlactican Foreign Intelligence Agency director [i][Classified][/i] unsuccessful attempt to assassinate the Imperial family.

After the incident is resolved and the [nation]united states of atlactican[/nation] joined the GSA, the purpose is changed to [color=blue][b]maintain peace between the Alliance and other organizations as well as to maintain internal security of the nations in the GSA.[/b][/color]
