The continent of Dolphonia has been refounded since the Great Disaster of 2016. History books have been left puzzled as there has been no recollection of a disaster /ever/ occurring, but rather a continent-wide subconscious decision to &quot;Fall asleep and never wake back up&quot;. After the countless remains of those who remained countless were disposed of, the continent of Dolphonia has been restored to its previous, not-entirely-habitable state.
