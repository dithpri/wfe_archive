The continent of Dolphonia is a large continent notorious for their different species of dolphins. The continent is the shape of a crescent, with several islands to the south of the continent. They have thick forests and a vast amount of resources. Dolphonia is rich in nature and culture. 

Current Dolphonian Union Rulings:
1) Dolphins are protected by international law. The Nations of Dolphonia are expected to uphold this law through their border patrol.
2) After news of a recent potential outbreak, companies are given incentives to research a cure for the new disease.
