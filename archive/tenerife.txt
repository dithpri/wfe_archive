[b]Tenerife[/b] is the largest and most populated island of the seven Canary Islands. It is also the most populated island of Spain, with a land area of 785 square miles and 898,680 inhabitants, 43 percent of the total population of the Canary Islands. Tenerife is the largest and most populous island of [region]Macaronesia[/region].[hr]
