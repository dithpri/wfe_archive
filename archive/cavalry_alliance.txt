[b]The Cavalry Alliance[/b]

The Cavalry Alliance also known as the Cav is an economic and military alliance formed by the Republic Sylvanesti, The FRAUT, and the Federal Republic of Hoffsburg.  The region was founded in 2015 due to inactivity from the former alliance leader.  It currently has a massive territory of over 1,000,000,000 km2 which has been growing exponentially in the end of 2015.  Most of the territory is heavily urbanized and industrialized in all of the member nations and two thirds of the population live in urban areas.
