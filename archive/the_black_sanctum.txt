The [b]Black Sanctum[/b] is home to many wonders, some of which are not favorable for strangers. Come and stay awhile and you might be able to get a taste of this majestic fantasy land full of wonders good and evil, big and small. We are home to many a creature and are always looking for more! Our island is mainly deep thick forest, which is almost unusable because of the diverse population of creatures. Explorers always welcome!
