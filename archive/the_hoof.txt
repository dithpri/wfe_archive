The Hoof is a region with the theme of Fallout Equestria, a fandom within a fandom that was created by Kkat&amp;#39;s Fallout Equestria. Nations in this region must go by that theme. Creators of the nations have to Fallout Equestrians.

The Hoof is region of nations with the one intention of rebuilding. Two hundred years ago a war ended the way of life for the two opponents. Life persisted however, and managed to climb up and carve out territories. The Hoof is colorful, nations with many different ideas and views, and most of all, The Hoof is growing.
