This is a region of many lush forests as well as plains and grasslands.  The flora and fauna are extremely diverse.  The nation of Gleivia reside in the plains regions while the tribal forest people of the Occaneechii reside in the forests.  Building their huts in the clearings between trees.  The Barren regions toward the south of Elsweyr are home to the rather unstable nation of Mckeezyeezy.  Frequent patrols are made by Occaneechii warriors along the border of their lush forest and the barren waste land, deterring or capturing vagrants from Mckeezyeezy  Regular trade is made between the nations of this region and the region continues to prosper.
