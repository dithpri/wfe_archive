-Geography consists of primarily cloud forests and is mainly mountainous
-Maximum temperature of Barux is 25&deg;C and temperatures may drop to -35&deg;C
-Animals that are commonly found in Barux includes the Baruxian Starling, Yaks and Lesser-Baruxian Alphine Goat while the rarest animals include the Lesser-Cloud Lynx, an alphine relative of the more commonly-found Samoatan Lynx and the Berian parasitic wasp, one of the rarest insects on the planet due to the harsh alphine climate that dominates Barux.
