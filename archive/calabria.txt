Calabria ([i]Cal&agrave;bbria[/i] in Calabrian, [i]Calavr&iacute;a[/i] in Calabrian Greek, [i]&amp;#922;&amp;#945;&amp;#955;&amp;#945;&amp;#946;&amp;#961;&amp;#943;&amp;#945;[/i] in Greek, [i]Kalavr&igrave;[/i] in Arb&euml;resh), known in antiquity as [i]Bruttium[/i] and formerly as [i]Italia[/i], it is a region in Southern Italy and forms the traditionally conceptualized &quot;toe&quot; of the Italian Peninsula which resembles a boot.
