The Emerald Highlands is a mountainous region located in the northeastern part of Novapura. The region is named after the lush green forests that cover the steep slopes of the mountains, giving it a scenic beauty that is unparalleled in the country.

The Emerald Highlands is home to a diverse range of flora and fauna, including many rare and endangered species. The forests are teeming with life, and visitors can expect to see a variety of animals such as quokkas, koalas, wallabies, and many species of birds.

The region is also known for its rich mineral resources, including copper, gold, and silver. The mining industry plays a significant role in the economy of the region, providing employment opportunities to many of the local people.
