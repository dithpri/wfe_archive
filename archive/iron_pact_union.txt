[b]The Iron Pact Union (IPU)[/b] is a militarized sovereign state dedicated to the protection, preservation, and advancement of the German people and their homeland. Founded in response to historical conflicts and modern geopolitical instability, IPU operates under a strict nationalist doctrine, emphasizing self-sufficiency, military strength, and cultural unity. With a centralized government led by a Grand Marshal, IPU enforces a disciplined society where military service is mandatory, and civil life is heavily structured around national defense. The economy thrives on a combination of state-controlled industries and militarized production, ensuring strategic independence in times of crisis.
