Located in the western hemisphere, and near the coastlines, the Western Forests are home to quiet nations that keep to themselves. 
They value peace, democracy, political and civil freedoms, and negotiation and trade. LGBT rights are a MUST for all nations. 

Warring is not outright forbidden, except within the region. The States have an excellent relationship with one another and are happy to avoid the day to day rabble and excitement of most places. Inactivity will not get someone removed unless it has been so long that the state has faded to ancient history.
