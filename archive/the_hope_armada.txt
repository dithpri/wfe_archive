A port for the fleet that truly saved the day in [region]Capitol Alliance[/region], protecting the region from the [i]zambbie threat[/i] and researching the cure that would save tens of billions of lives.

Now, the entire fleet, composing of roughly 6,000 ships from seven different, mostly derelict nations, the Hope Armada remains docked until the day that the zombie threat rises again, when it will swoop in to do what it can. Or until somebody needs a vast quantity of aging puppets for some odd reason.
