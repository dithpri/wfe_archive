The Wildlife Conservation Society (WCS) is a global 501 non-governmental organization headquartered at the Bronx Zoo in New York City, that states its mission as saving &quot;wildlife and wild places across the globe&quot;. Founded in 1895 as the New York Zoological Society (NYZS), WCS manages four New York City wildlife parks in addition to the Bronx Zoo: the Central Park Zoo, New York Aquarium, Prospect Park Zoo and Queens Zoo.
