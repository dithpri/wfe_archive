[b]The [color=red]Est&aacute;dio da Luz[/color], officially named [color=red]Est&aacute;dio do Sport Lisboa e Benfica[/color], is a multi-purpose stadium located in Lisbon, Portugal. It is commonly translated to English as &quot;Stadium of Light&quot;. The stadium is used mostly for football matches, hosting the home matches of Portuguese club [color=red]S.L. Benfica[/color], who own it. It is sometimes referred to as [i]A Catedral (The Cathedral)[/i] or [i]O Inferno da Luz[/i].

It was opened on 25 October 2003 and was built with a maximum capacity of [u]65,647[/u] (64,642 in Primeira Liga matches).[/b]
