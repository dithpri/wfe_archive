Pinkies United of New Earth (PUNE) is the premier peacekeeping and humanitarian organization of New Earth. The organization is democratic where all proposals are voted upon by all member nations. Proposals that have been passed by member nations are effective on all member nations, and all nations are required to follow them. It was founded in 2012 by the Grand Duchy of Canade.
