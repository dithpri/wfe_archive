PRO DEI GLORIA, PRO MARI&AElig; HONORVM, PRO ECCLESI&AElig; BONVM
The Lord is the wellspring of ALL power, including that to govern. It is not, in any manner, derived from the consent of the governed, as contemporary popular myth would have you believe.
Let the rule of Christ the King be accomplished through these kings, and may their conduct ever be befitting the subjects of our Lord, the King of kings.
