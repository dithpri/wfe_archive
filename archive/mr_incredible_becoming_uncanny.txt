The Mr. Incredible Becoming Uncanny meme was one of the most popular memes of early 2022. The first video of this meme was made by Musicos Cinicos on 26 September 2021. It featured three new phases which would later become phases 5, 4, and 6. Then, another YouTuber named MadameMomazos replaced the audio of phases 4-6 and created phases 2 and 7. Then, yet another youtuber named Tus Memes Semanales created phases 8-10. He also created phases 11-14. After phases 15-18 were created by Elkunderr, the meme became viral.

The meme has been used for many purposes, including disturbing facts and stories. A lot of people liked the idea of this meme, with some of them making extended phases and remakes of the original meme.
