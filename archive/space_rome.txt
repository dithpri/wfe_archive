In 2100 humanity was at the brink of extinction, all throughout the solar system countless settlements vanished. On Earth, scientists and governments decided to attempt to preserve humanity by catapulting them into space away from the dangers of the solar system. Many cities were chosen but barely any were to become as influential as Rome in 2150.
