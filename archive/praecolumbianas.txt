Praecolumbianas is a past-tech, alternate history based, RP region focused on pre-Columbian civilizations starting in the year 1444. Although its recommended that your nation&#39;s name is at least semi-historical/alternate, it is not enforced nor required. The pinned dispatch; Praecolumbianas Hub contains links to all regional dispatches and FAQs to better serve the player.
