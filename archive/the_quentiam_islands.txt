Located in the Pacific Ocean near the equator, this string of Islands was settled by Latin soliders in 200 B.C, however it was not discovered until 2017. The islands are large and has a high biodiversity due to the jungles covering the islands. In 784 A.D however, a terrible civil war broke out between the many states, and the goverment was rebuilt as a confederacy of the 12 Island States, with Quentiam Vallis being the most powerful.
