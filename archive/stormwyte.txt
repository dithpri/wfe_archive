[i]Stormwyte[/i] is a Role-playing region designed for nations with Nordic, Germanic, English, or Fantasy backgrounds. We are mainly a Fantasy Technology region, but we also invite those who role-play at the Past- and Modern-technology levels to join us.

[b]Thank you for considering Stormwyte Region. We hope that you will join us in our search for prosperity.[/b]
