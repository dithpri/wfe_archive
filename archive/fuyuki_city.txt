Fuyuki City is the location of the Heaven&#39;s Feel, a ritual established by the three Founding Families, Einzbern, Tohsaka, and Matou to reach Akasha. After the Fifth Holy Grail War, nations became interested in understanding and acquiring the Holy Grail, and so Fuyuki City became a region bustling with nations all vying for the power of the grail. Will you join the battle? Develop your magecraft and fight!
