The Union of Island Nations (UIN) is a union for all island nations that wish to keep peace in the southwest Pacific.

&#151;&#151;&#151;&#151;&#151;&#151;&#151;

The UIN as this document will be referring to the union from now on, is a place with the aim of bringing peace and tranquility for nations that are progressive with civil rights and personal freedoms. Any potentially offensive messages or content may have your nation kicked out of the UIN. This includes any mentions of nudity, sexual content, racism, homophobia, transphobia and cultural appropriation.
