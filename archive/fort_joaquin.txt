Fort Joaquin is an antiquity fortress located in a Valley of Purple Ridge, one of the few mountains in the greater Blue Ridge chain that emit a distinct purple glow at sunrise and sunset. Here, the noble Militiamen of Blue Ridge conducted their operations until the construction of Fort Mule. Now the fort rests as a stalwart reminder of the Militia&#39;s past, ready to be used again should it be required.
