[b]Welcome to BMC![/b]

BMC, also known as Brazilians Mapping Chat, is the [url=https://discord.gg/K6Yy94cGwQ]official discord server[/url] of [url=https://www.youtube.com/&amp;#64;brazilianmapping56]Brazilian Mapping[/url].

Owned by Brazilian Mapping, the server was created on Sunday, February 4th 2018 at 2:20:23 PM GMT, and has been going on ever since. It currently has 974 members (correct as of 01/05/2024), making it among the largest and longest lasting mapping servers to exist.
