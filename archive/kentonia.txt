A land of tin-pot dictatorships and struggling democracies awaits! Kentonia, led by a group of librarians turned world leaders, has become a major source of world news. See who the regional strongmen (and a few strongwomen) interact as their republics and empires battle for influence. Environmentally stunning and a complex web of diverse ecosystems, some might say that this is heaven...or hell if you are not in a position of power.
