[b][color=7B4581]Province of Morrowind[/color][/b]
[i]Morrowind, previously named Resdayn, is the Dunmer Province located in the northeast corner of Tamriel. It is dominated by the large island of Vvardenfell and its centerpiece, the Red Mountain, but also includes territory on the continental mainland. The Sea of Ghosts lies north of Morrowind.[/i]

[b][color=red]Morrowind is an Imperial Province of[/color] [region]The Septim Empire[/region][/b]
