Welcome to Adaria! Adaria is a star system that was located approximately 150 LY from the stay system &amp;quot;Sol&amp;quot; during 2155. The system contains 2 gas giants and 3 terrestrial planets. 

&#149; Liymaya, a desolate planet in an elliptical orbit around the star. It has attracted interest due to the potential for rare minerals on its surface.
&#149; Adaria, the planet that the system was named after. It is inhabited and has a generally circular orbit well within the habitable zone of the star. Map: http://i.imgur.com/Hu3vskM.png
&#149; Tassaca, a planet almost 3 times larger than Adaria. Like Adaria, Tassaca has a circular orbit and harbours life.
