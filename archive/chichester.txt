[b][u]Chichester[/u][/b]
Chichester is a small, casual, and democratic-leaning region open to all nations. It was founded by the Empire of Seion Fall, which is currently the only executive nation.

[b]October 25th, 2018[/b]
On behalf of the nations of Chichester, The Empire of Seion Fall has denied The Dominion of Trainmaster&#39;s request to construct an embassy between Chichester and Train Station after an investigation into the region revealed an alarming trend of disregarding civil and political freedoms.
