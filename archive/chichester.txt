[b]In the midst of the global pandemic of 2020, Chichester was invaded by international advertisers seeking to use the region for their own gain, with no regard for the region&#39;s existing states.[/b] Upon the return of the glorious and everlasting Empire of Seion Fall on August 2nd, all traces of this nefarious plot to force an unwanted message upon unwilling nation-states were erased. Administrative privileges have been stripped from the treacherous Republic of Guy 85. However, the Republic will be allowed to stay for a period of probation, during which the Empire will determine if further disciplinary action is necessary. Formation with the Plum Island region, presumed to be involved, has been aborted.
