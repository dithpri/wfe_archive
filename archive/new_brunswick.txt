[color=502510][b]New Brunswick[/b]

New Brunswick is one of Canada&#39;s three Maritime provinces, and the only constitutionally bilingual province (French and English) in the country. The name comes from the archaic English name of Braunschweig, a city in northern Germany. The province today is strongly influenced by the Irvings and the McCains families.
Capital: Fredericton
Population: 749,168
Area: 72,908 km&sup2;
Confederation: July 1, 1867 (Canada)
