[list]New Brunswick is one of Canada&amp;#39;s three Maritime provinces, and the only constitutionally bilingual province (French and English) in the country. The name comes from the archaic English name of Braunschweig, a city in northern Germany. The province today is strongly influenced by the Irvings and the McCains families.
