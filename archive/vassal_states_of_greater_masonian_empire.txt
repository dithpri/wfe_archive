The vassal states of the Greater Masonian Empire are semi-autonomous regions that have been allowed self oversight due to their distance from Imperial Masonia. They&#39;re still expected to follow the Empire&#39;s tenets of maintaining loyalty of the citizens the Emperor and ensuring the well-being of a state&#39;s citizens.

The Empire values strict laws that eliminate crime and criminals, as well as strong social welfare to ensure all citizens are of equal standing before the Emperor. Promoting logical, rational thought among the populace, and stamping out irrational and unproven beliefs.
