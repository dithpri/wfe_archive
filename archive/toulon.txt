[b]Toulon[/b] is a city in southern France and a large military harbour on the Mediterranean coast, with a major French naval base. 

During the Napoleonic Wars, from 1803 until 1805, a British fleet led by Admiral Horatio Nelson blockaded Toulon. &#151;Wikipedia [hr]
