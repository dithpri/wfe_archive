The U.R.G. was established after the great Battle of Rome in 3203 A.D. when Rome had fallen to the Gauls . 
The U.R.G. hosts a multitude of different cultures and religious groups. A few hundred years later, a civil war occurred but was quickly put down by Ragnar the Younger. The U.R.G. happily and currently experiences peace while going on the seasonal raids.
