[hr]A major force in Greek history, and a dominant city in Central Greece.It was a major rival of Athens, and sided with the Persians during the invasion under Xerxes. Theban forces ended the power of Sparta at the battle of Leuctra under the command of Epaminondas. The Sacred Band of Thebes famously fell at the battle of Chaeronea against Philip of Macedon.
