[i]Territory of The Dominion[/i]

Thebes on the River Nile is about 450 miles south of Cairo, capital of ancient Egypt for many centuries. A city of great archaeological importance containing Karnak, Luxor and the Valley of the Kings. It is currently an imperial territory of [b][region]The Dominion[/region][/b].
