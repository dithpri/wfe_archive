When the Principality of Sinclaria and the Free Land of Muh Six Quintilion  became disgruntled with the regional government of Sub-Slavia, the states seceded to form their own (((Confederal Republic))). While the region is open to all, it tends to look negatively upon regimes who hold communal, authoritarian, or other ideologies at odds with individualism and economic liberty.

The regions motto is &quot;Ne eius super anguis&quot;
The regions religion is One, Holy, Catholic and Apostolic Church
