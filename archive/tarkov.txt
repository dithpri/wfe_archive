Tarkov is a fictional city in the Russian Northwest, a municipal entity within the Norvinsk region, and one of the two principal cities of the Norvinsk Special Economic Zone. It is where the events of [b]Escape From Tarkov[/b] [i](the best extraction FPS without doubt)[/i] takes place. Not to be confused with the real world city of Kharkov.[/color]
