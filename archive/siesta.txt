[region]Siesta[/region], formally known as the [nation]Siestan Commonwealth[/nation], is a Terran country on a peninsula in the Sea of Clariside bordering [nation]Sargon Empire[/nation], [nation]Terran Victoria[/nation], and [nation]Terran Iberia[/nation].

It is an independent city-state republic with a city council that is under the nominal jurisdiction of Columbia as its commonwealth. Siesta is also well-known for the scenery of its tropical beaches and its proxy to a volcano that holds a vast reserve of obsidian deposits.
