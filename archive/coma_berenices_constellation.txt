[b]Founded by the andarian starfleet on: 04/05/2025![/b][i]Coma Berenices: is an ancient asterism in the northern sky, which has been defined as one of the 88 modern constellations. It is in the direction of the fourth galactic quadrant, between Leo and Bo&ouml;tes, and it is visible in both hemispheres. Its name means &quot;Berenice&#39;s Hair&quot; in Latin and refers to Queen Berenice II of Egypt, who sacrificed her long hair as a votive offering. It was introduced to Western astronomy during the third century BC by Conon of Samos and was further corroborated as a constellation by Gerardus Mercator and Tycho Brahe. It is the only modern constellation named after a historic person.[/i]
