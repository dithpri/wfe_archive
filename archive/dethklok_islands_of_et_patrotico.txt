Hello and welcome to the Dethklok islands of Et Patrotico!
This region is run by Governor Maximus Regina.  
Years ago Et Patrotico tried to take over Dethklok 1000, but they lost and became a colony of Atlanicore.  A treaty was made and Et Patrotico makes its own laws. Et Patrotico had 15 islands, some uninhibited.  So Govenor Maximus gave permission to Dethklok 1000 nations to colonize the islands. This region is the colony and territory of Dethklok 1000.
