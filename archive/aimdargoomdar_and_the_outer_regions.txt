The region [region]Aimdar Goomdar and the Outer Regions[/region] is a cold, inhospitable, and dangerous region. With wildly varying climate, regular severe weather, and sandstorms every day, this semi-desert region has an extreme environment. Formerly the ancient, rotten and musky remains of a former humanoid civilization, the area has been established as a region conceding with the creation of [nation]Aimdar-Goomdar[/nation]. This region currently supports all governments of all types and is free to join.
