Kamchatka Oblasts is a Russian Oblast located on the very east of Russia near japan and the kuril islands. The point of this is to unite Russian oblasts into one region but any other fictional or non fictional country can join as well. This area is also a very volcanic area having 300+ volcanos and 30 active ones near the ring of fire aka a volcanic region encircling the whole pacific region. We currently have 6 members all Russian oblasts in different parts of russia spanning from europe to the Ural Mountains and the members are Kaliningradd Oblast, Yakuutia Oblast, Arkhanngelsk Oblast, Petropavlovsk-Kamchatka Oblast, Krasnoyarskk Oblast and the fictional oblast aka Suca Lucrus Oblast.
