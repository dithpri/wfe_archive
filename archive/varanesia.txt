Varanesia is a small region of islands located in the Karorean Ocean. Well known for its eclectic cultures and irregular customs. Historically overlooked by other larger powers the region now boasts a population of over 2 billion inhabitants. The largest nation in the region being Karori, an economic &quot;Powerhouse&quot;, with 1.8 billion inhabitants. The nation is located on two main islands; Marsdenne Island (the largest and home to the capital), and Makara Island. The region&#39;s official languages are Vara spoken by an overwhelming majority in all nations of Varanesia, and Makarnia which is spoken only by a few living in the eastern regions on Makara Island.
