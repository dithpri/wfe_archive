The Region of Eyereal
Eyereal is a peaceful and highly developed autonomous region known for its dedication to knowledge, observation, and unity. Its citizens believe that understanding the world through careful study and cooperation leads to prosperity. Modern cities, research centers, universities, and observatories are common throughout the region, making Eyereal a center of science and innovation.
The region&#39;s geography ranges from crystal-blue coastlines and fertile valleys to snowy mountains and dense forests. Every major city features an Eye Monument, reminding citizens of their shared values of awareness, wisdom, and justice.
The government encourages education, environmental protection, and technological progress.
