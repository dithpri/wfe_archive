Turoonia Is back in charge!

Turoonia and new Turoonia are not connected, however the monarch of Nolaneia also presides over New Turoonia. Additionally New Turoonia is not a part of Turoonia in any way nor is it a part of Turoonia that broke away in any of the Turoonian Civil Wars. [b] 


No non-native Piranhas or Panthers are allowed in Turoolai nor are Joetsakeean Pounds or Bloodcoin accepted as payment. Those caught harboring any of these animals, smuggling them, or using aforementioned currencies as payment within the borders of Turoolai will be punished, not always via banning.[/b]
