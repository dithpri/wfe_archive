This is a region dedicated to Krytocratic states.

Krytocracy is defined as a government ruled by judges and the judiciary.
This can be democratic or otherwise, though nations which value justice and freedom are encouraged most.

The founding nation, Azagol, is a democratic Krytocracy, in which the head of government and head of state (Vatarzalagul) is the head of the Judiciary (The Azatarazon). Judges (Vatarazir) oversee cases (Taram) to determine law (Talash), being bound by a constitution (the Riuzdazon Azmaran) and by precedent (Talzhamir), as well as the input of the people, who can, through the legislative branch (the Loriab) veto (Yzhil) the Judges.
