The Veranian Union State is a small group of Nations willing to work with one another to achieve prosperity for themselves and their fellow members. 
Created by the Federovans, Verania is a capitalistic democratic Region, with its main purposes being for free trade, industrialization, and Imperialism. 

With recent attacks in their history and a successful defense against The Black Hawks, Verania is working towards a strong defense against other imperialistic Regions.  
With Verania being Imperialistic itself, defending regions or sieging them is [i]completely up to the situation[/i], as [b]Verania serves it&#39;s self-interest first[/b].
