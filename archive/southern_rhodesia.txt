[color=green][b]Southern Rhodesia[/b][/color]
[hr]
[color=green]A landlocked southern African Republic within the British Commonwealth of Nations. Bordered by Zambia, Mozambique, Botswana, and South Africa.

Capital: Salisbury
Area: 150,872 mi&sup2;
Population: 14.15 million
Independence: From The United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland via unilateral declaration on November 11th, 1965. 

President: Clifford Dupont
Prime Minister: Ian Smith[/color]
