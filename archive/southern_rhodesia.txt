Founded in 1890 by British settlers from the Cape who came as members of the pioneer column in search of mineral wealth and virgin land, in October of 1923, a nationwide referendum indicated a preference for responsible self government within the empire. During WW2 the colony contributed up to 26,121 of which 916 fell for King and Country, after the war came an economic boom and federation - a development which was eclipsed by UDI, Sanctions, and Civil War.
