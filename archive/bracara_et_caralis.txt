Composed of two major subcontinents and an array of islands lying in the sea between them.  Bracara, the larger of the two main landmasses, is known for its temperate coastlines, tall mountains, and wide stretching plains.  Caralis&amp;#39;s shoreline is rocky and dotted with large sheltered bays, while forests and prairies dominate the interior. The many islands of the region vary greatly in terms of size, shape, and climate.

Nations found on Bracara:
--The Holy Empire of Saint Emygdius; United Kingdon of Stoilu; Tsardom of Tsarsivi; Technocracy of Corios; Empire of Vaelstromm

Nations found on Caralis:
--Armed Republic of Galder; Constitutional Queendom of Perusio; Free Land of Saicosov; Most Serene Republic of Nessunaria; Confederacy of Iarra
