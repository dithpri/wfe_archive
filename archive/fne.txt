The FNE (Free Nations Empire) is a clandestine coalition of nations.  Applicants to the FNE must be invited by the approval of the government.  Countries seeking applicant status must exhibit the following;

1) Stability
2) Strong Economy
3) Rational Leaders
4) Population less than most populous member (currently 250 million)

All government types are accepted.  No information pertaining to FNE law may be displayed elsewhere.  The FNE reserves the right to revoke access at any time in violation of FNE law.

No more information will be disclosed at this time.
