TNAW is a fallout inspired role play region that takes place in a alternate timeline where Japan gained enough of a arsenal to bomb all the major cities in North America, setting it into a deadly nuclear holocaust. Nearly 80 years later, in 2020, North America has devolved into a series of tribes, salvager packs, and people of authority attempting to bring back what was lost.
