[color=#0000FF]Tijah is a two-island continent located in the Pacific region. Inhabitated by tribesmen, and colonists, this continent had amazing resources, and was colonised by the United States in 1900. However, the tribesmen were able to repel the US troops, and claimed soverigenty of the islands. The League of Nations recognized the islands as a soveriegn state in 1905, and the United States were forced to cease operations on the islands.
