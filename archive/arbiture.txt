Arbiture is a joining of nations after &quot;The Great Disaster&quot;, an event in which all nations were destroyed due to a various factors including war, famine, climate change, and civil uprising. After the destruction of the nations new coalitions and unions were formed from the remaining ashes of the world superpowers. Having seen the horrors of modern warfare, these new nations joined together in a pact to resolve all disputes through diplomacy. But it is not long after that a new group rises to challenge Arbiture&#39;s authority and war will once again consume the globe.
