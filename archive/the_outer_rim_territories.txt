The Outer Rim Territories is a part of the Star Wars  galaxy, The Outer Rim is a sparsely populated region of the galaxy located outside the Mid Rim before Wild Space and the Unknown Regions.

This area of space is the biggest region in the entire galaxy, it is home to a diverse range of worlds including (some notable planets to mention) Tatooine; Ryloth; Naboo; Kamino; Mustafar; Polis Masa &amp; Florrum. 

These territories are currently under the tough, ruthless and demanding control of the Governor of The Outer Rim: Grand Moff Wilhuff Tarkin, of The Oppressed Peoples of The Outer Rim Territories..
