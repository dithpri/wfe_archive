The nation-states of [b]Northwest Asia[/b] have, as of February 14th, 2017 chartered a political-economic international block in an effort to raise the region&#39;s interregional stature. The Northwest Asian international block revolves around two intertwined institutions, the Council on External Affairs (CoIA), and the Council on External Affairs (CoEA).

This nation block is best suited for those with an interest in writing/debating policy, model government, and light worldbuilding.
