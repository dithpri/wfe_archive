[b][color=fire]The British Commonwealth[/color][/b]

The Commonwealth is an association of 53 independent states consulting and co-operating in the common interests of their peoples and in the promotion of international understanding and world peace. The Commonwealth&#39;s 2 billion citizens, about 30% of the world&#39;s population, are drawn from the broadest range of faiths, races, cultures and traditions.
