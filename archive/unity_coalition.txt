Unity Coalition was once an idea by new and idealistic nations to create a region of political unity, but it quickly failed due to isolation of the coalition from others and lack of attention to it. It seemed as though it was dead, but the former UC military commander, [nation]The Republic of Brenland States[/nation], came back and turned it into a colony for [region]The Region Of Allied Nations[/region]. It is to be controlled by its former founder The Federal Republic of [nation]Neo-Trinity[/nation].
