After decades of calamity caused by the collapse of the overstretched German Empire, a series of nations have stood strong through it all. With nations ranging from city states to new empires, these are the States of the Former German Empire.

We span from the heart of the heart of the Fatherland to the edges of the Mediterranean, to the rugged coasts of Scandinavia, to the Cliffs of Dover and the fringes of Minsk. Come join us, you are welcome here.
