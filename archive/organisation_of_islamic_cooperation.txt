The [b][region]Organisation of Islamic Cooperation[/region][/b] (OIC) is a supranational alliance of Muslim-majority states committed to advancing collective security, economic development, and political coordination across the Islamic world. With sweeping authority, the [b]General Secretary[/b] acts as the supreme executive. The [b]Chief Wazir of Regional Command[/b] oversees the internal workings of the organisation and is the successor to the General Secretary.
