The Evan Militia is a group of Evan Empire funded supporters helping to promote the Evan Empire and take down aggressor nations. In order to accomplish this, the Evan Militia advertises the Evan Empire through diplomatic means and also conducts missions to help remove the nations that oppose the Evan Empire and its allies.

The Evan Militia is supported by the Evan Empire as funds for defense purposes as well as nations that contribute to the Militia through the National Coalition of Evan Militias (NatCoEM). The NatCoEM is the international arm of the sub department of the Evan Militia hosted and managed by the supreme general of the Evan Militia.
