The region of Nigrum Montem is home to five nations, The Queendom of Tadjilala, The Queendom of Dextorian Republic, The Queendom of Maislandia, The Holy Empire of Slawoskistoria and the great Imperium of Arulean. Embassies are held in The Nations of Ridgeview and The Splendid Commonwealths of Liberty. The regional anthem is &quot;Hello&quot; by Lionel Richie, it is heard in every corner of every nation within the borders of Nigrum Montem. The flag of Nigrum Montem is red, with the coat of arms in the middle, and golden borders.
