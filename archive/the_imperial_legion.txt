[color=red][b]The Imperial Legion[/b][/color] was founded on January 10th, 2011, as the brainchild of feeder tyrant [b][nation]Durkadurkiranistan[/nation][/b]. During its cosmically brief year of activity, it reached a height of over 400 nations, forged bonds with the raider community through the Imperial Attack Force (IAF), played host to many talented roleplayers who often found themselves at odds with one another, and ultimately served as a springboard into Durk&#39;s next project: [b][region]Exshaw[/region][/b].

The region now rests under the care of one of its scions, [b][nation]Septalon[/nation][/b] a.k.a. [b][nation]Reventus Koth[/nation][/b].[hr]
