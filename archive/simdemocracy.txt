SimDemocracy is an internet community based on the principles of democratic control by the users of SimDemocracy of the laws, moderation, control and leadership of SimDemocracy.

Founded during 2019 SimDemocracy has proved that an internet community can be democratically organised by its own users.

This Region is a newly founded territory of SimDemocracy, primarily for the members of SimDemocracy however those who are not currently a part of SimDemocracy may also join this Region. 

We encourage all those who join this Region to also join our subreddit (r/simdemocracy) and our discord: https://discord.gg/GqDVaxSXJU

The details as to how this Region is governed are established by the laws of SimDemocracy.
