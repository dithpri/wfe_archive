Welcome to the [b]Lake Country[/b], an isolated area of the planet Naboo located approximately two hours outside Theed. A region of great natural beauty, it is located in a valley dotted with numerous lakes and surrounded by mountains. Wide meadows of grass and wildflowers sit among the waterfalls and lakes, and much of the planet&#39;s native shaak population make their homes here. [nation]Varykino[/nation], private estate of Senator Padm&eacute; Amidala and other members of the Naberrie family, is situated on a small island in the Lake Country.
