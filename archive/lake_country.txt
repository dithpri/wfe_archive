The [b]Lake Country[/b] is an extremely isolated area of the planet Naboo. A region of great natural beauty, it is located in a valley dotted with numerous lakes, and is surrounded by mountains. A large proportion of the Country is covered in bucolic meadows where herds of gentle shaaks grazed openly. [nation]Varykino[/nation], the private estate of the Naberrie family, is situated on a small island in the Lake Country.
