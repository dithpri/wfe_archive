The [region]Alliance for Peace and Freedom[/region] is a far-right European political group that maintains contacts with conservative circles in Russia and support the policies of Vladimir Putin, hence the overtures sent to Russian regions by [region]The European Federation[/region] to bring European-Russian members into the fold.

[region]Alliance for Peace and Freedom[/region] believes in [i]&quot;A Europe based on ancient concepts and deep roots in history&quot;[/i]

[u]Ideology[/u] 	

[color=red]Ultranationalism[/color]: extreme devotion to one&#39;s own nation
[color=red]Third Position[/color]: opposition to both communism and capitalism
[color=red]Anti-Zionism[/color]: opposition to a Jewish-majority state in Palestine
[color=red]Euroscepticism[/color]: integration weakens the Nation State
[color=red]Anti-globalism[/color]: opposed to communism, capitalism and free trade.
[/b]
