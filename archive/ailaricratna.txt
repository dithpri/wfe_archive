the purposes of this place
1. a haven for nations wrongfully exiled by invasion of any type.
2. a home region for any nation that wishes. 
3. a place for any exiled nations to discuss taking steps to regain control of their invaded region.
4. welcomes any nation attempting to liberate invaded peaceful regions/liberation regions, and welcomes any nation attempting to invade aggressive invasive regions.

unwelcome nations.
1.nations that took part in invading any region not known for invading peaceful regions.
2. nations that violate the laws of nationstates
3. recruiters.
