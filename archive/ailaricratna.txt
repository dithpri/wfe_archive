In the near future, the expansion of the universe caused a dramatic shift in the laws of the universe, just as it did during the first moments of existence. this shift caused a radical new force to appear, humanity called it the force of magic. everyone had some small control over the force, using it for small tricks, no one really having mastery over their small skills. over time, large &quot;discharges&quot; of the force built up by the uncontrolled subconscious use by humanity began damaging the earth, reshaping continents and altering the animals and people, the discharges became worse and worse, and after a century, the world looked nothing like it had before, the continents had been replaced by one.
