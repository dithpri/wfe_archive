The Tyrrhenian Confederation is a free and democratic regional alliance of states.

The official lenguages of the Confederation are: 
-Ligurian
-Corsican
-Sardinian
-Emilian
-Italian
-Neapolitan
-Sicilian 

The Confederation has the following institutions: 
-The [b]Confederate Executive Council[/b], composed of four [b]Secretaries[/b] and an [b]Executive[/b] [b]Secretary[/b] who acts as president. --The [b]Confederate Assembly[/b], composed of deputies elected on a proportional basis in each member state.
