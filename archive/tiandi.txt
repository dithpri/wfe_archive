[b][color=#40BFFF]Tiandi[/color][/b] is the union of [color=#40BFFF]Heaven[/color] and [color=#FFBF00]Earth[/color], and all which they encompass. 

A heavenly, celestial world located in a far-off section of the NS universe, Tiandi is home to a myriad of cultures, religions, and political systems. It is a region dedicated to good roleplay and worldbuilding. All claims are made on the offsite forum.[hr]
