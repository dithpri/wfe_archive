[b]Zheleznogorsk[/b] is a closed city in Krasnoyarsk Krai, Russia, with a developed nuclear industry.

It was established under the Soviet Union as Krasnoyarsk-26 in 1950 for the production of weapons-grade plutonium and contributed greatly to Soviet nuclear programs and its nuclear arsenals.

The flag of this closed city - &quot;bear splitting an atom&quot;, is one of the most well-known and iconic regional flags of the Soviet Union/Russia.
