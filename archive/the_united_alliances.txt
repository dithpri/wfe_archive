The United Alliances

The United Alliances are the subset of Nations within the interplanetary government formed by Exactra&#146;s Government in the hopes of the star systems colonization and habitation. The capital system of The United Alliances is  TRAPPIST-1. Alliances may introduce their own policies and laws as long as they will protect member states in the case of an attack and respect the basic values of Exactrain.

We are the hope of humanity and science. Our constitution is to be followed to ensure this is maintained.

To ensure the alliances maintains power of the universal stage, please endorse our delegate to the HA (Humankind Assembly)
