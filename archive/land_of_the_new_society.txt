[b]Land of the New Society[/b]
The Land of the New Society is a Asian PT region, the RP starts at 1980. NATO, the USSR  and the USA will also be present in this RP. One major key note is that the South Koreans win the Korean War and the Kims escape into China by crossing the Tumen River, the South Vietnamese win the Vietnam War, the Khmer Rouge never takes power and the Pathet Lao are not able to win the Laotian Civil War.

Current Year: 1980
Current Month: January (footnote, two days is equal to one month)
