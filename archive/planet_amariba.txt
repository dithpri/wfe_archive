[color=gold]Planet Amariba is a habitable Earth-Planet found in the 36 Ophiuchi Trinary System, also known as the [region]Limba System[/region]. The planet was discovered by Brazillian Explorer Starships and was named after the ruined ancient Brazillian City.[/color]

[color=green]*Classic member of the [region]Human Systems Federation[/region].[/color]
