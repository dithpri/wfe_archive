The Alliance Graeco houses Hellenic/Greek nations that desire a say in legislation, and unification for a greater cause.

After a bloody two century war in the Balkans, the nations have finally agreed to have peace. They have formed an alliance to prevent further aggression, but will it last? This is all decided by the players, and this group is made to be a unique roleplay experience.

Sicca Regius is the current Emperor of the Alliance, but this may change with further developments.
