The Union of Trans-Steppia is a large region of various nations of and surrounding the Great Steppe Lands with the Eastern European regions to the North West, Arabic States to the South, Central Asian States to the South East and the Tibetan Buddhist and Mountainous States to the far East, Tundra to the North and Nomadic Territories to the Centre of the Steppe. Founded initially by the great powers of Aravanka of the Eastern European Region and Tsongyerpa of the Tibetan Buddhist region in order to establish trade between the two great nations, founding a joint capital of Lhatsho-Ikraina in order to administrate this new region.
