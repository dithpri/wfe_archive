[B][color=orange]Cali is a city in western Colombia and the capital of the Valle del Cauca Department. With a population of 2.5 million, Cali is the third largest city in the country, after Bogot&aacute; and Medell&iacute;n. It has one of the fastest growing economies and infrastructure in the country because of its geographical location. The city was founded on 25 July 1536 by the Spanish conquistador Sebasti&aacute;n de Belalc&aacute;zar.[/Color][/B]
