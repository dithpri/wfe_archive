&quot;Democratic Unity is an alliance of individuals, organizations, and nations who have come together to promote and protect democracy. Our alliance is based on the fundamental principles of democracy, such as the rule of the people, equality before the law, human rights and freedom of expression. Our unity lies in our dedicated commitment to democratic values and the protection of democracy as a key pillar of our society.

We believe that democracy strengthens our communities and enables citizens to actively participate in the decision-making process. Democratic Unity works to support and strengthen democratic institutions, develop civil society, and promote a transparent and participatory political environment.
