The long expanses of Red Desert conceal the cities and territories of thriving nations and empires with harsh weathers, little natural resources, and deadly indigenous creatures. Top researchers advise against traversing the sands of the Red Desert on foot due to the cause of death of at least 98.7% of corpses retrieved from the sands being failure of living; however, more tests are required to confirm these findings.
