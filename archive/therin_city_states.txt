A region for the characters of Scott Lynch&#39;s &quot;Gentelman Bastard Sequence&quot; series of novels. These face numerous picaresque tribulations in Therin City-States.

The cities of Camorr and Tal Verrar are located on the continent&#39;s shores. Up North, Karthain, the sister city of Lashain, both of which sit on The Amathel (Lake of Jewels) can be found at the head of the River Cavendria.
