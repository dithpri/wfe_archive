The [b][region]Union of Nova Multiverso[/region][/b] is a supranational politico-economic union of four polities in Earth (Sol 3), Dunya (Teegarden c) and the observable Multiverse. The Union is a middle power with democratic, pacifist and socialist leanings. Its seat is at the [b][url=https://www.nationstates.net/nation=central_indies]Kingdom of the Central Indies[/url][/b].

The [b]Union of Nova Multiverso[/b] pursues an independent multilateral foreign affairs policy. It strives for a peaceful world by co&ouml;perating with nations and regions. It has a neutral defence policy focussed on deterring internal threats and mediating external conflicts.
