The [b][region]Union of Nova Multiverso[/region][/b] is a supranational politico-economic union of five polities in Earth (Sol 3), Heimat (Tau Ceti b) and the observable Multiverse. The Union is a middle power with democratic, pacifist, environmentalist, progressive and communitarian leanings. Its seat is at the [b][nation=noflag]Nova Multiverso Headquarters[/nation][/b].

The Union of Nova Multiverso pursues an independent multilateral foreign affairs policy. It strives for a peaceful world by co&ouml;perating with nations and regions. It has a neutral defence policy focussed on deterring internal threats and mediating external conflicts.
