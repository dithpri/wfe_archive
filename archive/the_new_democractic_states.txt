The New Democratic States (NDS), previously known as The Nove Zone, is an area where many neonations are formed, all of them with a respect for human rights, with The Peoples&#39; Democratic Republic of Nova-land (PDRNL) being the trend-setter. They are known for the New Democratic Union (NDU), an authority over the nations of NDS, owned by PDRNL, which meets in Bell City, Nova-Land.
