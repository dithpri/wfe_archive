[region]Greenwich[/region] is a historic district of London, the capital of the [region]Empire of Britain[/region], located in southeastern London along the south bank of the River Thames. It is internationally renowned for its maritime heritage and for being the location of the Prime Meridian, from which Greenwich Mean Time (GMT) is derived.

Sister city: [region]Canary Wharf[/region]
