[b]Greenwich[/b] is an area of London, England, located 5.5 miles east-southeast of Charing Cross. Greenwich is notable for its maritime history and for giving its name to the Greenwich Meridian (0&deg; longitude) and Greenwich Mean Time. &#151;Wikipedia[hr]
