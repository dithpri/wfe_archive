Archipelagos rife with war, these lands belong to the once thought gone power of Bovvalia. Islands lay bare to either waste, or the grey industrialization of an undying hunger for expansion. These territories surround the mouth of a volcano, lain dormant for many-a year; for the more zealous of the many species occupying the fortress-nation, it&#39;s merely the mouth of God.
