Alioth, also designated as &amp;#949; Ursae Majoris (epsilon Ursae Majoris), is a variable hypergiant star of spectral class A0 in the constellation of Ursa Major. Alioth visual magnitude is 1.77, making it the 31th brightest star in the sky. Because of its brightness, Alioth is clearly visible to the naked eye when observed from locations with dark skies, and should be also quite easily visible from light polluted areas.
