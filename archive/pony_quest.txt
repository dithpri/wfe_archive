Join Rarity and her friends as they embark on an epic adventure to save the 5 regions of Equestria from Princess Twilight who has now gone corrupt from power and has locked Princess Celestia in the dungeon.

Bosses:
Princess Luna controls Orkshire
Queen Chrysalis controls Coldvale
Discord controls Death Valley
Sunset Shimmer controls Scaryville
Twilight controls Equestria

Over 200 hours of repetitive yet addicting gameplay
