{[color=blue]Current Year: 1E 21[/color]}

Founded in the year 21 of the First Era, Prima Terra is a mostly unexplored region. It was first established as a region by King Nairos of Praesulia, who set out as the first to explore the region.

Currently, King Nairos and the Praesulians claim a loose authority over the region and its borders.
