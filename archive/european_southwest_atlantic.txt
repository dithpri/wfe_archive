European Southwest Atlantic is a region where is located the Principality of Porto Henrique and its insular domains. 
The area extends from the Iberian Peninsula (where is situated the continental territory of Porto Henrique) to west, to the middle of the Atlantic Ocean (Porto Azores) and to the southeast, near the African coast (Porto Madeira).
The Dominions of Porto Azores and Porto Madeira belong to the Principality of Porto Henrique.
