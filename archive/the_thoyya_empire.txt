The Thoyya Empire is the left over government from the fall of the Icarus Empire, The founding nation of the Thoyya Empire is The Sovereign Atlas States, There for it believes that all nations under it&#39;s name shall rule itself accordingly, unless they wish to declare war.
If war is declared against another in the Thoyya Empire then It will be a declaration of war against the entire Empire.
