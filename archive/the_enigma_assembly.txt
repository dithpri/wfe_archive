Not much is known about this collective group of nations, being highly secretive and engaging each other only in the shadows, never publicly acknowledging their alliance. The only known meeting between these nations occurred 3 years ago and was discovered by Pulitzer-winning investigative journalist, Emilio Estefan, who wound up dead in a ditch in the capital of Isfahan 2 days later.

The cabal have been compared to many other clandestine organizations such as the Bilderberg Group and the Bohemian Grove.
