In 1990 Arstotzka won the civil war of Arstotzkan land and became United. Today the nation is called United Arstotzka. Under difficult circumstances it was decided to create an assembly of several nations whom are willing to enter the pact of the United Arstotzkan Region. In 1995 this came operative and the assembly is hereby growing. The border of Arstotzka is currently open. Glory to Arstotzka.
