An archipelago of islands on the Kenian Sea with one major island.

Isolated from most known regions.

Fully controlled by the Thiongo Emirate and its true homeland.

Rich in resources such as oil, iron ore, silicon, silver, gold and many other naturally occuring metal ores.

The region, due to its vast size of islands has various climate zones such as desert regions in the midlands of the main island and cool climate conducive for farming at the foot of the Krohan Mountain Ranges.

Tropical weather in most islands in addition to the rich flora and fauna makes this region a desirable tourist destination.
