Asiamerica is a largeish continent, located in the Atlacific Ocean. Many countries call this landmass their home, including Stanistan to the Southeast, Crovos to the North, and Yurep in the West. The different countries all have vastly different Cultures, ranging from Murica&#39;s fast food and gunshops, to Crovos, which can only be described as a competent North Korea.
