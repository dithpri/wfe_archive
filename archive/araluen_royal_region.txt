Araluen Royal Region, AKA; ARR. Was appointed its own region as crown princess Madelyn became queen. The Region together maintains a small army for the use of its nations of 320 soilders, 180 cavelry men, and a force of 200 archers. ARR also has a Ranger Corps maintained by the 13 orignal Rangers. With many more in training. The Ranger Corps helps maintain order for King Crafters. Most of this Region is based of stuff from the Ranger&#146;s Apprentice series by John Flanagan.
