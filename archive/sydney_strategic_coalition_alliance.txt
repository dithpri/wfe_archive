The Sydney Strategic Coalition Alliance is formed in Sydney, Aventhia. Where coalition of governments and states join as one for a benefit of economic, military and scientific progress and development. 

The headquarters of the SSCA is located at Sydney. With the Military Headquarters being located at Berlin, Belka.
