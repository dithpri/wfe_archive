A region consisting of highly varied governments under names which reference Aztec/Mayan mythology. The nations and their policies are as follows:

[nation]Ilhuicatl-Teteocan[/nation] - An (eventually) totally controlled dictatorship with high levels of spirituality; essentially a theocracy. They only thing more notorious than their compulsory, highly religious military service is the strong stance against heretics.

[nation]Ilhuicatl-Teoiztac[/nation] - A flawlessly run nation which provides some of the highest economic freedoms in general. A pit full of hedonism and indulgence, which is being sought to be quickly ended. 

[nation]Ilhuicatl-Xoxoauhco[/nation] - A republic; actually functions.
