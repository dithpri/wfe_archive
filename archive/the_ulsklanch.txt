The Ulsklanch is a a region that is a conglomerate of nations who have bound themselves to a law which is upheld by the promise of free trade, military aid, diplomatic aid, economic aid, unbiased judicial review, free speech and press, freedom to vote, and freedom to run the country as pleased so long as it does not interfere negatively with neighbouring nations within the region. The Ulsklanch is a region which is protective of one another and resolves intraregional conflict by diplomatic means first. Respect and unity is expected of nations in this sovereign region.
