The land is predominantly covered in volcanically-active land, with taiga to the South-West, dusty desert to the East, and frozen fertile grassland to the South-East. The weather is usually cold with frequent snow. Snowy days outnumber sunny ones.

Verns is a well cultured region. There is a higher concentration in the arts of literature, the visual arts, the graphic arts, the plastic arts, the decorative arts, the performing arts, music, and architecture. Vernisians display their wealth via their clothing and academic training reveals their intelligence.
