Hello and welcome to The Unity Pact (TUP)! The Unity Pact (TUP) is a defence and economic alliance between nations of The South Pacific. The alliance is composed of strong friends and allies that work together on defence and economic-related issues and ideas. TUP has their own military force called The Unity Pact Armed Forces (TUPFA) which is comprised of an army, navy and air force. All nations part of TUP have a multilateral free-trade agreement between member states called the The Unity Pact Trade Agreement (TUPTA). Any nation that attacks a TUP member will be attacked by TUP. TUP will seek to support peace, free-trade, environmental protection, unity, and strong international relations in the TSP.
