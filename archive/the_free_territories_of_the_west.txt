The Free Territories of the West is a region of many nations, with varying ideologies. Major events that have occured in the region are the Deser civil war, The Eastern Deser war of aggression, and the independence of Austedeser. Many nations in the region embrace civil liberties, while others trust the government to make the right decisions for its people. On either side, the region seems to stay in a balanced peace between the ideologies. However, trouble seems to be brewing in the East...
