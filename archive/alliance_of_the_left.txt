The Alliance of the Left is a region dedicated to and composed of nations attempting to build societies free of exploitation of man by man: societies which adhere to the high ideals of Socialism and other leftist ideologies that advocate freedom and prosperity for the People. This region is for left-leaning, free, and democratic nations, which may or may not have governments.

Despite this, the Alliance of the Left is also dedicated to pursuing peaceful international policies, including healthy relations with those capitalist countries that are free and democratic.
