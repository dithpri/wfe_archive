Centered around its ancient capital in the Sea of Serenity, the [color=pink][b]Kingdom of the Silver Millennium[/b][/color] and its lunar inhabitants were tasked with the mission of guiding and protecting the peoples of Earth during their evolutionary process. With its elegant, classical architecture and state-of-the-art technology, the Silver Millennium served as a beacon of peace and prosperity in the Solar System and was ruled over by the benevolent Queen Serenity.
