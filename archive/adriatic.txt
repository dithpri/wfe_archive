Adriatic Region 

The Adriatic Sea is a body of water separating from the Serbian Soviet Union Mainland peninsula spreading into the Major, Minor and Micro Islands regions of the Adriatic Sea. The Northern Part of the Adriatic Region is colder ranging from -80 Degrese Celsius in Winter to 34 Degrese Celsius in Summer. The Southern part of the Adriatic Region is hotter and has a complete different climate than the Northern Adriatic Region ranging from 5 Degrese Celsius in Winter to 53 Degrese in Summer.
