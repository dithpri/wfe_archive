Henri-Philippe [b]Petain[/b] (1856-1951) was a World War I French general who was later imprisoned for treason. 

Petain collaborated with the Nazi regime and adopted repressive measures against Jews. Tried for his actions at the end of World War II, Petain was sentenced to death before his sentence was commuted to life imprisonment.
