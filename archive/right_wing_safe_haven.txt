Introduction:
This region maintains the belief that any nation towards the right end of the political spectrum should be granted amnesty and protection from the prosecution of other nations and regions. Henceforth, any nations with radical policies shall be treated with fortitude in respect. 

The only case of which a nation may not belong here is if they broadcast messages of hate directly, attempting to press their beliefs on other nations of any region.

Some of the radical ideologies accepted: Fascism, Monarchism, Right-Wing Dictatorships, anarchies, etc.
