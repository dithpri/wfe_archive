The Ideologically Different Institutions of Opposite Thoughts and Societies (I.D.I.O.T.S. for short) Is a region open to and thriving in, differences of its habitant nations. It firmly believes that strength can be found not in similarities but in differences. In pursuing this single common ideology, it hopes to be at the forefront of understanding and appreciation in the world. 

The only things to which I.D.I.O.T.S. is opposed are:

1. The rejection of Ideologies by which other nations govern themselves

2. A refusal to live by the common Ideology and prime doctrine of I.D.I.O.T.S.

I.D.I.O.T.S will support member states in their endeavors and goals. It will also provide economic backing.
