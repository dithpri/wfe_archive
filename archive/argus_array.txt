Welcome to The Argus Array

The Argus Array is one of the most advanced deep-space observation stations in the galaxy, positioned on the fringes of Federation space. Equipped with a network of powerful subspace telescopes and sensors, it is capable of detecting distant celestial phenomena and anomalies from light-years away.
