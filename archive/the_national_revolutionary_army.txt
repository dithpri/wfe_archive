The National Revolutionary Army (NRA; &amp;#22283;&amp;#27665;&amp;#38761;&amp;#21629;&amp;#36557;), sometimes shortened to Revolutionary Army (&amp;#38761;&amp;#21629;&amp;#36557;) before 1928, and as National Army (&amp;#22283;&amp;#36557;) after 1928, was the military arm of the Kuomintang (KMT, or the Chinese Nationalist Party) from 1925 until 1947 in the Republic of China. It also became the regular army of the ROC during the KMT&#39;s period of party rule beginning in 1928. It was renamed the Republic of China Armed Forces after the 1947 Constitution, which instituted civilian control of the military.
