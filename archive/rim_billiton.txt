[region]Rim Billiton[/region], formally known as Rim Mining Industry Association (RMIA) or [nation]Rim Billiton Association[/nation] for short, is a Terran country located at the southeasternmost corner of the continent close to [nation]Terran Kazdel[/nation] and [nation]Yan[/nation].
Homeland of the predominant Cautus populace, it is a corporatocracy ruled by an alliance of family communities, a unique political system in Terra that is absent of a central government, in which these grassroot communities set up their self-governing mines and guilds to form a confederation.
