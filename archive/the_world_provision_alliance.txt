[region]The World Provision Alliance[/region]
----------------------------------

The World Provision Alliance is an alliance that was proposed by the Nation of [nation]Mercil[/nation], seeing the ongoing conflicts the world has to offer Mercil decided to found the WPA, World Provision Alliance, an alliance that would vow to help the world be a less conflicting place. Whether this is by supplying materials to countries that need it or giving military aid to stop a war, or even to help with climate change. The WPA has overall wanted less conflict and a safer earth but will not hesitate to use lethal force if necessary.
