hume, pron. (1) Indefinite personal and relative pronoun, presupposing no referent. Useful esp. in writing solipsistic treatises, sc. &quot;to hume it may concern.&quot; v. (2) To commit to the flames, bury, or otherwise destroy a philosophical position, as in &quot;That theory was humed in the 1920s.&quot; Hence, exhume, v. to revive a position generally believed to humed.
