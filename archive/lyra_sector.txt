The Lyra Sector is a politically structured and economically integrated region of interstellar space anchored around the historic Lyra star grouping. Known for its calm governance architecture and high scientific output, the sector is considered one of the most stable and technologically advanced regions in its quadrant.

Rather than a loose frontier, Lyra is characterized by mapped transit corridors, regulated hyperlane traffic, and long-standing interplanetary accords.
