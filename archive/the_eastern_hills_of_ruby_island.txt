This hilly region is located to the east of the great mountain range &#39;The Withered Peaks&#39; which runs from north to south on the western part of &#39;The Ruby Island&#39;.

HISTORY ::
This island, covering an area of 4.578 million sq. km, was discovered by several tribes of people evading ice age, approximately 12,500 years ago. Since then, this island has been home to a civilization of these people.
In revolution of 1946, all small states merged to form &#39;The Republic of Hbkay&#39;.
