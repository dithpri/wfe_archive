[b]Tetris[/b] is a tile-matching puzzle video game originally designed and programmed by Soviet Russian software engineer Alexey Pajitnov. The first playable version was completed on June 6, 1984, while he was working for the Dorodnitsyn Computing Centre of the Academy of Science of the Soviet Union in Moscow.

More than 170 millions of Tetris copies have been sold, making it the second best selling game of all times.
