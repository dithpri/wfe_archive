Political Alliance for Freedom,
More than a regular union; an alliance. A group restrict to the nations who share the same values, in search for freedom and meritocracy through principles.
Created in 1932/33 in order to go against Joef Wilken, president and dictator of Workeland since 1928 until his impeachment.
After the impeachment, the group remained existing like &amp;quot;Political Alliance for Freedom&amp;quot;, now in order to ensure the freedom of Workelands and allied countries to the PAF.
