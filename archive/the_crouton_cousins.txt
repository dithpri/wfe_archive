We are a small, independent, peaceful region. Anyone is welcome. The Crouton Cousins were founded by [nation]Davissia[/nation] in February, 2015. Many of us were once proud, founding members of The Feather Friends. The Great Death of Summer 2014 left the Feather Friends decimated. Now, we seek the former glory of The Feather Friends and more as we recover and seek an independent existence in NationStates.
