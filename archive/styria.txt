[b]Styria[/b] is a state or Bundesland, located in the southeast of Austria. In area it is the second largest of the nine Austrian federated states, covering 6,332 square miles. It borders Slovenia and the Austrian states of [region]Upper Austria[/region], [region]Lower Austria[/region], [region]Salzburg[/region], Burgenland, and [region]Carinthia[/region].
