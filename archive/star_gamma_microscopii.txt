&amp;#947; Microscopii is a double giant star of spectral class G6 in the constellation of Microscopium. &amp;#947; Microscopii visual magnitude is 4.67. Because of its moderate brightness, &amp;#947; Microscopii is easily visible to the naked eye from locations with dark skies, while it might be barely visible, or not visible at all, from light-polluted skies.
