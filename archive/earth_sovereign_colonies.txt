[U][B][COLOR=DeepSkyBlue]Geography of Earth Sovereign Colonies[/COLOR][/B][/U]
The Sovereign Colonies of Earth (also dubbed Earth Sovereign Colonies) were founded in 1950 as a collection of various nations post World War Two. Feeling strength in numbers, these nations signed an independence treaty to protect and collaborate with eachother.

The United Socialist States of Chassau is the founder, and leading nation in the efforts going on in the ESC.
