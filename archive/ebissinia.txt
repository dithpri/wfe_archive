Ebissinia is an extremely large earth-like planet mostly covered in water. The land is largely dominated by large and widespread tribes of nomadic people. Each with their own personal culture and beliefs. Some of the tribes believe only in strength and conquering their brother tribes is the only way to prosper. Others believe in a more peaceful way of life. They enjoy the life of simple merchants, traveling the continent interacting with  the other tribes. Eventually 2 tribes became more civilized and adopted the ways of science and more complex forms of government. Beginning the Escovan-Anbian partnership.
