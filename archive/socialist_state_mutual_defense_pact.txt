The [color=red][b]SSMDP[/b][/color] is an international coalition of socialist/Communist countries created with the purpose of providing stability, security, and peace of mind to the governments and citizens of all member nations. At the core of the organization is the mutual defense pact sworn to by all participating parties; this guarantees that if one member of the [color=red][b]SSMDP[/b][/color] is attacked by any outside entity, all other member nations are duly obligated to employ any neccesary military or diplomatic force to defend and aid the targeted nation. We support Marxism, Leninism, Marxism-Leninism, Stalinism, Maoism, Trotskyism, Titoism, Castroism, among other schools of thought.
