The [color=blue][b]Socialist State Mutual Defense Pact[/b][/color] is an international coalition consisting of socialist and Communist countries which was created with the goal of providing stability, security, and peace of mind to the governments and citizens of all member nations. At the core of the organization is the pact sworn to by all participating parties; this guarantees that if one member of the [color=green][b]SSMDP[/b][/color] comes under attack by any outside entity, all other member nations are duly obligated to employ any necessary military and/or diplomatic forces at their disposal to defend said nation.

The SSMDP was founded by the Empire of Kuross Tarky.
