The Federal Republic of Success depends upon hardworking citizens and strong leaders to allow for a strong economy, happy people, and as much freedom for the people as possible while still retaining an excellent economy so that the people stay wealthy.

Laws of the Federal Republic of Success:
- You must be economically self-sufficient
- You cannot prevent or delay innovation
- No person, group, organization, clan, posse, government, etc. shall assist a non law abiding citizen
- Failure financially, intellectually, socially, or otherwise is grounds for deportation
