[b]Info:[/b]
Haugalandet is a region created mainly for Norwegians
from Haugalandet, Nord Rogaland, but as long
as you understand Norwegian you may apply to join
this region.

[b]History:[/b]
Haugalandet was founded by Arne of Vikjaa,
Jonas den allmektige of Nedstrand and 
Vikingen Heimdal of Jofurrhofn on the 12. September 2014
