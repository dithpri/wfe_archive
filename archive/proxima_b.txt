Proxima B was the first interstellar colony of earth, and when split into numerous nation states it has become a paradigm of peace and prosperity through the use of carefully applied fascism. only the true race, Kekistani, are permitted to walk the halls of Proxima B, as it is the planet gifted to them by PEPE. the current population of 5 million is due to the shortfalls of interstellar colonization, and more nations are needed to fully colonize this corner of space.
