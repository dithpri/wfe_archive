[color=black]22 March 1939, Lithuania voluntarily transferred [region]Memelland[/region] to Germany to prevent war. Lithuania had annexed the region in January 1923 and had held the East Prussian lands until given the ultimatum to withdraw. The [region]German Army[/region] marched in to secure the borders and liberate the German populace.[/color][/b]
