A region known for its widespread evergreen forests, mountain ranges, snowcapped extinct volcanoes, and its stunning coastline. It has a series of governments adhering to democratic socialism. Cascadia has a robust welfare state providing universal healthcare, free university education, and childcare. It has the largest ferry system and most extensive high speed rail systems in the world. The region is strictly anti capitalist and encourages the development of democratic socialist, liberal democratic socialist, and liberal utopian governments.
