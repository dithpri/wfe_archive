A caliphate is an Islamic state led by a supreme religious as well as political leader known as a caliph. The caliphate represents the political unity of the entire community of Muslim faithful (the ummah) ruled by a single caliph. The head of state, the Caliph, and other officials are representatives of the people and of Islam and must govern according to constitutional and religious law (Sharia).

The caliph, should be elected by Shura &#150; elected by Muslims or their representatives.
