A region of Islamic Unity and Faith. The Caliphate following the guidance of Prophethood remaining with the Community of Believers for as long as Allah wills it to remain.

The Imams agree that the Caliphate is an obligation, and that Muslims must appoint a leader who would implement the injunctions of the religion, and give the oppressed justice against the oppressors. It is forbidden for Muslims to have two leaders in the world whether in agreement or discord.
