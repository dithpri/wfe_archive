The Islamic form of government representing the political unity and leadership of the Muslim world. The authority of the Caliph as head of state comes from the fact that he is the successor to the prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him).

Our region advocates international Muslim friendship and unification. The delegate is not a Caliph, but calls on all the regions of the [i]Ummah[/i] to unite behind a worthy candidate, who should be elected by official representatives of the Islamic community in accordance with Sharia law.

[b]The Caliphate[/b] is the only form of governance that has full approval in traditional Islamic theology. It is the core political concept of Sunni Islam by the consensus of the Muslim majority.
