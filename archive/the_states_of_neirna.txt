The Region of Neirna allows everyone to have a voice in this world via. the council of the countries. 
The Region of Neirna was founded on December 30th, 2023.
The council of Countries are made up of 3 main parts.
 1- The Highest is called The Vulrian Council overseeing the creation all laws and international matters
 2- The Middle house is called The Lumoi Council overseeing the internal matters and the approval of laws
 3- The Lower House is called The Yuna&euml; Council overseeing the Criminal matters 

Original Laws
a- All Countries Shall Adhere to These Laws
b- All Criminal matters shall be judge by a person in the Yuna&euml; council.

The Capital of This Place is in Helminiak, Unalet
