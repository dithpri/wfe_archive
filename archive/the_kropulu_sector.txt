On the far reaches of the known galaxy, the last of humanity was attempting to find new life in the outer reaches of what we knew.  They found it.  Named the Koprulu Sector by what was left of the astronomy teams after crash landing, humanity had created a new home amongst the hundreds of new, life-sustaining worlds they had found in this little patch of nowhere.  Now,  centuries later, it has become a heavily populated area with hundreds of new sub-factions of humanity now vying for control in an endless quest for power.
