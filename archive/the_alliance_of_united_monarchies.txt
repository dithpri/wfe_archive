The Alliance of United Monarchies is a region for absolute monarchies and constitutional monarchies only. Their are two political parties in the AUM. 1) the Absolute Monarchist Party (AMP), and 2) the Constitutionalist Monarchist Party (CMP). The AMP believe that the regions king should have complete power and appoint nations into power, while having complete control over the regions security. However the CMP believe nations should be elected and have significant decision making, while the regional king has say only on matters of regional security in extreme emergencies.
