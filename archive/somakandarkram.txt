The island region of Somakandarkram is located far from the mainland of the world effectively isolating this region from most others.

Due to high concentrations of certain types of kelp and sea life, the seas and ocean around this region has a red hue to it. At times certain times of the year the waters even seem to be of a dark purple color.

The large and small land masses that make up regions islands come in many varieties, from vast stretches of blue colored grassy plains to deserts of white sand. Also the region is home to the famed spotted brown mountains called &amp;quot;The Draids&amp;quot; which was discovered by the popular explorer Hu Flung Poo.
