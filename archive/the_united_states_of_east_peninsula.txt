Legislation of The United States of East Peninsula (USEP):
Short version:

An attack on one nation within our region will be seen as an attack on all nations within the region.

Each nation shall treat every other nation with mutual respect.

There shall not be any regional arguments, only regional debates.

Each nation is able to experience fair trade deals with all other nations within the region.

Every nation within the region is free to have their own national laws.
