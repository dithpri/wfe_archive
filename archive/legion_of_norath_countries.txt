the Legion of Norath Countries is a Region that quickly popped up in the open areas of the Atlantic Ocean. the Region lies completely within the Northern Hemisphere. The Region mainly consists of one small continent (Norath) which has 3 major countries, Contego Sector, Unigodia and Piyr. 

All around the small island continent are hundreds of unclaimed islands some of which contain larger areas that can hold multiple countries. 

The Contego Sector is a Federal Republic lead by a single Supreme Representative. the Region prides itself on allowing all member nations to exists in solitary and develop how they want.
