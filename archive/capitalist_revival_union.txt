This region is a region of normally Constitutional Monarchies that push to revive capitalism and spread it across the world. Led by the founder Fie Lan and the Cabinet nations in this region enjoy many benefits of working as a union. They enjoy semi-open boarders between member states as well as with some &quot;associated nations&quot; namely the Grand Duchy of Chus Kruthe. The nations also engage in tariff free trade between each other, a shared currency regulated by the CRU Currency Organization or &quot;CRUCO&quot; and receive investments in both their infrastructure as well as private businesses from the CRU Stabilization and Investment Authority or &quot;SIA&quot; which also works with CRUCO to deal with economic difficulties in regions. 
Current Currency: The Baht
