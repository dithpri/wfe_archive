Magna Terra is a fictional medieval land characterized by lush forests, rolling hills, and imposing castles. It operates under a feudal system with nobles ruling over peasants. Political power is marked by alliances and conflicts among noble houses, while knights uphold a chivalric code of honor and bravery. The Church holds significant influence, and the economy thrives on agriculture, trade, and craftsmanship. Culturally rich, Magna Terra features storytelling, music, and festivals that celebrate its history and traditions.
