The [b]Federation[/b] of [b]United Allied Nations[/b] and [b]States[/b] or [b]FUANS[/b] is the supranational union of former sovereign states, which now share the same Executive, Legislative and Judicial Branches of State, share the same Head of State, as well as the same Diplomatic Service and Armed Forces, along with all other aspects of a sovereign state. In practice, the federation is itself a sovereign state, with it now being politically, legally and economically impossible for any member state to leave the federation.

The current President of the Federation is Charles Jones, who has served as president since December 13th 2010.
