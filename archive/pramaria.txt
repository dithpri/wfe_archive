Welcome to Pramaria, a fantasy/sci-fi planet far but not too close to Earth.

This region has roleplaying intent. You will be able to roleplay events, news, wars, trade, diplomacy, and truly run your very own nation.

You will be able to make your own lore, technology, cultures, or bring your very own. Once you apply, you can have your own claim on the map.
