[color=navy]WELCOME TO FIJI
An island nation in the South Pacific Ocean east of Vanuatu, west of Tonga and south of Tuvalu.
The country occupies an archipelago of about 322 islands, of which 106 are permanently inhabited, and 522 islets.
Viti Levu and Vanua Levu, account for 87% of the population.
The name Fiji is the old Tongan word for the islands, which in turn is derived from the Fijian name Viti.[/color]
