The region of Eastern Australia is defined as all parts of Australia that are not [region]Western Australia[/region].

Provisionally, this covers four states:
- [region]Victoria[/region]
- [region]New South Wales[/region]
- [region]Queensland[/region]
- [region]Tasmania[/region]
and two territories:
- [region]Northern Territory[/region]
- [region]Australian Capital Territory[/region]

Each state and territory is controlled by its own founder.
