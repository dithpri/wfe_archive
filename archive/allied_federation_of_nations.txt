The Allied Federation of Nations is an intergovernmental treaty and collective defense organization. The AFN exists to encourage dialogue and cooperation on issues such as collective security, human rights, and trade. 

The [region]Annex of Allied Federation of Nations[/region] is a client region of this alliance. Please join our regional party, [url=https://www.nationstates.net/page=dispatch/id=839518]The United Federation[/url].
