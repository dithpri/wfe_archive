The United Nations of [region]Ciralano[/region] (UNC) is an intergovernmental organization to promote international co-operation. The organization was established on 11 October 2016 in order to prevent conflict. At its founding, the UNC had 4 member states. The headquarters of the United Nations of [region]Ciralano[/region] is in Pangolin City, [nation]Sawdros[/nation], and experiences extraterritoriality. Further main offices are situated in Celesse, The Grand Palace, and Yelenad. The organization is financed by assessed and voluntary contributions from its member states.
