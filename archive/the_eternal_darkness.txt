After the sudden destruction of the chronosphere by powers unknown, nothing remains, save for the darkness of the void... 

Time has ceased to exist along with all other dimensions. There is no future, past or present. All of knowledge, every soul, an entire universe forever lost, without a trace. There exists no texts, no ruins, no graves, derelicts or distant echoes of radio waves for one to investigate... 

No one will ever know of what was or what could have been...
