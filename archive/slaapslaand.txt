Welcome! This is the home region of the Slaps.  All nations in this region have some line of descent back to the great civilization of Slaps.  The Slap civilization lasted a millenia, starting with its holy founding by King AJ the First.  The civilization thrived and lived peacefully until the Great Slick Wars.  Started by the great Warlord Bretrick, they tore the nation apart, scattering and dividing her peoples.

Slaapslaand still exists today, comprised of people striving to regain even a vestige of the former glory of the Slap.
