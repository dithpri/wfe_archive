A dark part of the multiverse sea, a once violent and dangerous part of the world, now abandoned after years. Only shadows remain here, for now...

The Molelands were once a large region full of activity but due to an unknown event of apocalyptic significance, all the nations were wiped out. Now nations return here, for land, fortune or pilgrimage, or maybe something else all together. We will not ask if you do not wish to speak on your reasons.
