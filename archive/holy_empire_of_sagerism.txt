As if it seemed like yesterday, a new ruler has been appointed. [b]Hinata Hyuga[/b] is in control of the Nation. Quintanilla has been thrown out of the palace for self-declaring herself without any authority. There has been reports that the Sager Clan has been raising an army to take back the nation. Meanwhile, the south side of Sagerism has rebelled. Provinces: Guan Hai, Resue, and Harlod Yint are the most of the territory the rebels have conquered. Empress Hinata Hyuga is having to deal with these problems while cutting military funds for the new space exploration funds. Also, reports say that Lord Hinata Hyuga is actually has Sager Clan blood in her. She denies all claims made.
