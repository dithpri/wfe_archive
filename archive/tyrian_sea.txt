[spoiler=OOC Information]This region is intended for RP with a tech level of more or less modern-day. It is set on &quot;NS Earth,&quot; where real-life nations are also understood to exist at a distance from us. Embassy requests are generally denied, with the exception of other MT RP regions (so as to allow participation).[/spoiler]

The Tyrian sea is a large body of water surrounded by continental land and filled with archipelagos. It is rich in gold and poor in weapons. The natural reaction to that brought it to where it is today.
