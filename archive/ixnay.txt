Ixnay is a region that has been around almost as long as nationstates.net! Its nations are new and old, and either type is welcome! We seek to foster a lively role-playing community where all nations are heard, so telegram our WA Delegate if you wish to join us! Make sure you recommend the region to any friends and allies - new nations are always welcomed! Make sure also to endorse and support your regional delegate, and to vote in regional polls!

[color=maroon]One nation in Ixnay per human player please! Ixnay is a strict [b]&amp;quot;No Puppet Zone!&amp;quot;[/b] [/color] Send your puppets to [region]Oriental Constanta[/region]!
