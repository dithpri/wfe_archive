[b]Minorca[/b] is one of the [region]Balearic Islands[/region] located in the [region]Mediterranean Sea[/region] belonging to Spain. It takes its name from being smaller than the nearby island of Majorca.

Mahon, conventionally known as Port Mahon in English, is a municipality and the capital of Minorca, located in the eastern part of the autonomous community of the Balearic Islands, Spain. Mahon has the second deepest natural harbour in the world: 5 km long and up to 900 metres wide. The water is deep but it remains mostly clear due to it being slightly enclosed. &#151;Wikipedia [hr]
