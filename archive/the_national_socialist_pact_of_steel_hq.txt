[b]Overview[/b] 
This region is the main base of operations for the National-Socialist Pact of Steel - an alliance with the sole purpose of keeping [url=http://aryanism.net/]Aryanist political and social movements[/url] prevalent and influential on NationStates. 
The Pact is comprised of National-Socialist, Fascist, and other like-minded regions, as well as those sympathetic to our cause in general.

[i]All member regions must have a diplomatic officer here to represent them.[/i]
