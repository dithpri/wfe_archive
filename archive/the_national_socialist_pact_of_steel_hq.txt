[b][color=red]Willkommen Kameraden to the National-Socialist Pact of Steel HQ.[/color][/b]

[b]Overview[/b] 
This region is the main base of operations for the National-Socialist Pact of Steel - an alliance with the sole purpose of keeping right- and far-right-wing policies prevalent and influential on NationStates. 
The Pact is comprised of National-Socialist, Fascist, and Conservative regions, as well as those sympathetic to the right in general.

[i]All member regions must have a diplomatic officer here to represent them.[/i]
