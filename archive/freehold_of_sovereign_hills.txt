 The Freehold of Sovereign Hills is a bastion of freedom, independence, and unshakable personal rights. Founded on the ideals of liberty and free expression, the Freehold is a region where the government exists only to protect the rights of its citizens, ensuring that personal freedoms are never compromised. With a minimal government structure, the region empowers its people to live as they see fit, fostering an environment where individuals are free to challenge authority, speak openly, and pursue their dreams without restriction.
