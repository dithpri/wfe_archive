~ [i]Established since 2005[/i] ~

[b]Motto:[/b] [i][color=darkfire][b]Terra Transmarina Britannica[/b][/color][/i]

The British Overseas Territory is a diverse region of new nations that serves as a center of commerce and unity, dedicating to the protection of its members and the perservation of Equal Justice Under Law. BOT is a constitutional monarchy devoted to expanding to every corner of the globe.
