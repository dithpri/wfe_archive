The United Kingdom of Nerdland (UKON) was originally created on the 19th of February, 2017 when  the 4 nations of Idiotnation, Popcornville, Anathoria and Spackartia: all devastated by war with each other, decided to form together to create a single country. Soon after, on the 21st of March, 2017, the Republic of Breadstick Nation joined as well.
