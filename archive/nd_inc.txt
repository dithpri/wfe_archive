New Domica Inc. is a corporate military sponsored by several nations throughout the world. These sponsorships are kept secret, however. 

ND Inc. possesses a powerful naval force comprised of large fleets as well as strong special forces. Ships are equipped with railguns and the like. 

ND Inc. possesses a large stockpile of long-range missiles and a variety of projectiles and bombs. 

Infantry is equipped with ballistic plasma guns, pistols equipped with silencers and dense projectiles, and other high caliber weaponry.

Aircraft &amp; vehicles are equipped with durable armor and research is being done into antimatter and more.

Individual goods may be purchased via our catalog and supplemental military services may be purchased via consultation.
