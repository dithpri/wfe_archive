Eastland? I thought you said [b]Weastland![/b]
----------------------------------------------------------------------------

20 Nov 17: Official flag of Weastland, the Pink Star, Hoisted for the First Time
20 Nov 17: Board Post Suppression Banned
23 Nov 17: Blanet Barth joined Weastland
27 Nov 17: A democratic poll was held for the last time, as it was decided by the majority to nullify all polls henceforth.
04 Dec 17: Due to civil unrest, the government of Bicken Hill can no longer be viewed as legitimate and has had all diplomatic ties severed with the Official Weastland Council.
