Neon Genesis Evangelion (&amp;#26032;&amp;#19990;&amp;#32000;&amp;#12456;&amp;#12532;&amp;#12449;&amp;#12531;&amp;#12466;&amp;#12522;&amp;#12458;&amp;#12531;), colloquially [b]Evangelion[/b], is a Japanese media franchise created by Hideaki Anno, originally owned by Gainax and currently owned by Khara. Most of the franchise features an apocalyptic mecha-action story that revolves around the paramilitary organization NERV and its efforts to fight hostile beings called Angels, using giant humanoids called Evangelions (or EVAs for short) piloted by select teenagers.
