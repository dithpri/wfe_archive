The Anarchist State of Alaska is a state administrated by anarchy and has territories which are called regions, each region has the freedom to conserve its language and culture, the military of the regions are administrated by the mainland (A.S.A)

We accept any kind of immigrants. Most citizens (including immigrants) has to go through some light military training, which can last a maximum of 2 months, with the exception of disabled people.

Our main capital is Anchorage, which is a highly advanced futuristic-like metropolis.

Our main allies are: United States, Russia, Japan and Palestine.

Its common to see soldiers and armored vehicles in our territories, our military is one of our most important things.
