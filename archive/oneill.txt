A collection of states petitioned to join the O&#146;Neill Confederacy(OC). The OC opted to allow and join the confederation. These states alternatively opted to join together sharing common ideals and goals. Now known as the Commonwealth of O&#146;Neill  States, or simply the Commonwealth. They have formed a government based on the OC with elected governors to rule over each state and a President at the head of them all. The eastern borders are often disputed with the Contested Zones in the North Central Earthen Continent.
