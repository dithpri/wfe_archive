Palestine A.K.A The Sultanate of Palestine is a Constitutional Monarchy in the middle east north of the Kingdom of Judah and border Judah. After Judah gain independence from Egypt in the south, Muhammad a Arab lead a revolt against Egypt in the north with all the help of Arabs they manage to kick out Egypt from the north and Muhammad made himself Sultan and establish a Sultanate on August 16 2014. The Sultan is the head of state and The Prime Minister is the head of government. The Sultanate is a Arab State and Islam is State religion. Sultan Muhammad and The Sultanate of Palestine number 1 enemy is King Eren of The Kingdom of Judah.
