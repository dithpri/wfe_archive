[region]Kero Kero Bonito[/region] (KKB) are a British indie band from London, England. The band consists of vocalist Sarah Midori Perry and producers Gus Lobban &amp; Jamie Bulled. &nbsp; The band&#39;s earlier work was influenced by J-pop such as Kyary Pamyu Pamyu, as well as dancehall, and video game music, however, their work following the band&#39;s 2018 EP [nation]TOTEP[/nation] was influenced by indie rock bands such as Mount Eerie. Perry, who is half-Japanese, sings and raps in both Japanese and English.
