[b][color=#0000FF]The Meridianese Defense Force[/b][/color]

The Meridianese Defense Force, or the MDF, is the military force of the grand democratic region of [region]Meridian[/region]. The MDF is currently practically empty, and will only be filled and utilized if Meridian needs it. If you&#39;d like to be involved with the MDF, or if you&#39;d like to join a democratic, well-run region, check out [region]Meridian[/region].
