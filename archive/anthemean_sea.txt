The several large islands of the Anthemean Sea surround it&#39;s largest, Anthemea Island, where Supreme Governor Anthemius and his people reside. Anthemea is rich with crops such as rice, cherries, and cassava, but attempts to heavily limit meat consumption, and the massive kudu and lyrebird populations are mostly used for milk and eggs. A stereotype of Anthemeans are their skill with the bardiche.
