[color=#660066]QJ-890 has seen glorious days along with the darkest of times in the universe; billions of years ago, white-blue Population III stars made up nearly all matter in QJ-890 and gave this particular galaxy its blessed reserves of iron, uranium, gold, and other dense metals alongside lighter elements which included carbon and oxygen. For a time, sapient species flourished in yellow-white dwarf systems and constructed cosmopolitical entities that sailed with the cyclical ascent and decline of many stellar systems; time took its course, and so the organizations of yore fell into mythology as newer interstellar alliance chains replaced their deceased brethren.[/color]
