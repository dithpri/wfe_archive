[b]Lega Cattolica Unita[/b] ([i]United Catholic League[/i]) is a community of Catholics loyal to the teachings of the Roman Catholic Church. We hold traditional Catholic beliefs, including in the Trinity and the Immaculate Conception. With the Holy Spirit at our side, we are devoted to the Catholic faith. Additionally, we do not discriminate against political ideology (with the exception of communists and socialists). Supporters of Democracy, Monarchy, or Fascism are welcome.
