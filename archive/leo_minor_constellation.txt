[b]Founded by the andarian starfleet on: 04/05/2025![/b][i]Leo Minor: is a small and faint constellation in the northern celestial hemisphere. Its name is Latin for &quot;the smaller lion&quot;, in contrast to Leo, the larger lion. It lies between the larger and more recognizable Ursa Major to the north and Leo to the south. Leo Minor was not regarded as a separate constellation by classical astronomers; it was designated by Johannes Hevelius in 1687.[/i]
