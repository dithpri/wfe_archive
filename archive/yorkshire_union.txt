Being one of the worlds most diverse Regions, with Far Right Dictatorships all the way to Far Left Marxist inhabitants, one would be let off for expecting trouble. However, the nations of the new and small Yorkshire Union seem to get on in harmony. With very little in the way of trouble, crime rates low and an almost unearthly peace between opposite political viewpoints, it is hard to not love the place. Extremely attractive scenery is hard to miss in any of its nations, and a true bond resides between all of its members; a nationalistic pride that is almost contagious.
