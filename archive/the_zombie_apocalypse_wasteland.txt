[b][u]Overview:[/u][/b]
Some time ago, a flesh-eating zombie outbreak decimated the previous nations that were here, leaving no trace.  A handful of nations have since started calling the region home.

[i]Founding Nations:[/i] Rochlad (Founder), Liche, Marach, Valstock

[b][u]Politics[/u][/b]
The Wasteland recognizes that each nation has a right to govern their nation as they see fit.  Flesh-eating zombies are a danger in the wastelands between the nations, although some nations have accepted the rights of the undead to &amp;quot;live&amp;quot; as they please, while others are militant anti-zombie types that slaughter the undead on sight.
