The Nexus is a virtual reality inside a quantum supercomputer orbiting Pluto, powered indefinitely by a cold fusion reactor.

The Somnesowl Empire originally created it, but the inhabitants of the Nexus have also broken up into other countries.  To prevent a war within the Nexus, the Nexus Alliance was created.

The Nexus Alliance states that nations within the Nexus may not have war with each other.  Despite this, many countries are provoked by the Immortal named [url=http://www.nationstates.net/nation=somnesowl/detail=factbook/id=199645#apoch][b]Apoch[/b][/url].
