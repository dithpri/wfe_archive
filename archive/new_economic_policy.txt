[b]New Economic Policy (NEP)[/b] was an economic policy adopted by the Soviet Union which replaced its previous economic policy - the War Communism policy.

It allowed small scale of private ownership of means of production and a free market. It resulted in a huge boom of Soviet Union&#39;s economy.

NEP inspired economic policies which China, Vietnam and Laos are using.
