Convened by the International Liaison Directorate as a standing space for coordination among the world&#39;s socialist, communist, and left-aligned nations.

The name recalls a 20th-century East German committee that channeled support to international liberation movements. Solidaritatskomitee exists in that same spirit: not a single doctrine handed down from a center, but a shared table for nations who&#39;ve each arrived at socialism by their own road.

Member nations retain full independence. Solidaritatskomitee coordinates, on WA strategy, on regional defense, on culture and worldbuilding, it does not command.

Currently in its founding phase.
