[b]The United Straight and Normal Nations (USNN)[/b] is a global intergovernmental organization established by the signing of the Charter of Straight Nations on 6 October 2025 with the articulated mission of maintaining international peace and security, to develop friendly relations among states with straight and normal people, to promote international cooperation, and to serve as a centre for harmonizing the actions of states in achieving those goals.
