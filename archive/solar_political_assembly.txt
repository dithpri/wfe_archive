Solar Political Assembly is where the all nations can lobby for changes within the regions, once these changes have been debated they will be put to the vote and then if the majority agree then the issue will be debated by SASC and voted on.  

Things that can be discussed here are the following:- 

Region Issues
Regional Laws and Rules
Role Play issues and laws
