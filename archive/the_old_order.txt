This is a past to present Roleplaying area, of which you are allowed to freely role-play how you want. The start era is 1982, and all of civilisation was lost due to a mass explosion of chemical weapons within ww1 and the very early 1920&#39;s. But, not is all lost. For the past years, the civilisations scattered around the world have got back to near normal ways, with 1960&#39;s tech. The rage of war is nearby, as countries are being established and minimal war can not be kept. This could create another destruction of the earth, and with the alien species coming near and near, it is only a matter of Time before a global invasion is complete.
