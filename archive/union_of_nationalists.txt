Welcome to the [b]Union of Nationalists[/b] ([b]UoN[/b]), an international Union to achieve the National ideal.

[b]Our three principles[/b]:
[color=bronze]&amp;#10026;[/color] We acknowledge the crucial importance of the Nation, and accept the State as a tireless pursuer of its interests.

[color=bronze]&amp;#10026;[/color] We accept a wide range of nationalist ideologues; from democrats to oligarchs to autocrats.
