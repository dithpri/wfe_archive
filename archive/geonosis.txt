[b][color=red]Geonosis, was the desert home planet of the Geonosians. It was the Confederacy of Independent Systems&#39; first capital and hosted its major battle droid foundries. It was the site of the Battle of Geonosis, the opening conflict of the Clone Wars, as well as the subsequent invasion by the Galactic Republic. Following the cessation of the Clone Wars, the Death Star would be constructed above the planet and placed under the command of Grand Moff Tarkin.[/color][/b]
