The constitution of the United Socialist Republic of Black Mesa 

Section 112-A of the constitution: 

All members of the Republic must follow the orders of the founding nation

The founding nation shall decide whether a nation shall become part of the Republic

The Republic will not accept any nations with malicious intent towards the Republic

The founding nation has the right to exile members of the Republic at any given time

In any war scenario, the Republic is expected to assist any member of the Republic, regardless of circumstance

The WA delegate will not possess as much power as the founding nation

These are the basic rules and will most likely be updated soon. If you have any questions, telegram me or the Orion Star. Have fun!
