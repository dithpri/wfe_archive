The Confederacy of the Allied French States is a confederacy formed to protect the union of the smaller French states, since the French Empire&amp;#39;s breakup in the 1800s. We lend military aid and financial aid to each other, and each country in the confederacy gets one ambassador to vote in elections and other votes. The Confederacy is an invite only region, and it is password protected.
