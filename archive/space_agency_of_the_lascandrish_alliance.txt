The space agency of the Lascandrish alliance is an agency created by the nations of the Lascandrish alliance that have started a space program. The leading power in this field is the Republic of Lascandra. The other nations of the Lascandrish alliance developed their own program by shared informations from Lascandra and financial aid from the ministry of research and development of the Lascandrish alliance. The goal of this agency is to make the Lascandrish alliance a strong power in this field and give new opportunities to scientists and students and boost the scientific advancement in the region.
