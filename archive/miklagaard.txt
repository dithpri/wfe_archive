The region of Miklagaard is lesser known outside of myth and legend, but the truth is often stranger than fiction. Military privateers in their south seas have become famous as pirates, with a bit more of a Robin Hood perspective than would be expected. In recent years since the invention of the steam engine, a competing faction of pirates have taken to the sky - often tongue-in-cheek referred to as Sky-Pirates. But in truth, the flying machines were simply the beginning of the real Steampunk revolution.
