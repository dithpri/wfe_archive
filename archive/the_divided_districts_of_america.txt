The Divided Districts of America is a rather large region of America that have sprung into Martial-Law and the area surrounding these said Districts have been bombed and are plagued by lawless murderers and pure hell.
Therefore meaning, these Districts are rapidly protected by the remains of the USAF, USMC, USN, USNG, and the remains of the Local Law Enforcement (Which have been militarized with Riot Gear and Heavy Ballistic Vests, along with Military standard carbines.)

Within these fair Districts, we intend on keeping Law &amp; Order and stopping mayhem in its tracks.
