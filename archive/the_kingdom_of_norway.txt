[color=red][b]~ The Kingdom of Norway ~[/b][/color]
[i]Norway is a country in the north of Europe. It is the western part of the Scandinavian peninsula. The mainland of Norway is surrounded by a coast on the west side and borders Russia, Finland, and Sweden to the east. The coast touches the Oslofjord, Skagerrak, and the North Sea. The Kingdom of Norway has been independent since 1905. The national day is May 17, which celebrates Norway&#39;s constitution of 1814. The parliament is called Stortinget and its members are elected by the people every 4 years. About 5 million people live in Norway. The capital is the city of Oslo.[/i]
