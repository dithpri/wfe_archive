We are a region that has been created to include all nations that would be be considered to be &quot;centrist&quot;. Whether you are on the center-left, center-right, or in the dead center, you are welcome here. We have presidential elections monthly, and the president is able to appoint a vice-president, secretary of state, secretary of homeland security, and secretary of the interior. The authority of each position will be detailed below:

President: Appearance, Border Control, Communications, Embassies, and Polls
Vice President: Appearance, Border Control, Communications, Embassies, and Polls
Secretary of State: Embassies
Secretary of Homeland Security: Border Control
Secretary of the Interior: Communications and Polls.
