The United Southeastern Organization or USO is a unique economic and political union between twelve Southeastern countries. Our goals are to foster economic cooperation: the idea being that countries that trade with one another become economically interdependent and so more likely to avoid conflict. The United Southeastern Organization has its own legislature, the Council of the USO.
