The Core Worlds, also known as the Corusaunt Core, the Inner Core, or simply the Core, are the area of the galaxy that bordered the Deep Core and contained some of the most wealthy, powerful, and prestigious planets in the known universe.

Alderaan, Brentaal IV, Earth, Chandrila, Corellia, Corulag, Corusaunt, Duro, Eufornis Major, Ganthel, Hosnian Prime,Alpha Centari I, Kuat, Plexis, and Tinnel IV were all Core Worlds.
