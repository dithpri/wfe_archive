The Core Worlds, also known as the Corusaunt Core, the Inner Core, or simply the Core, was the area of the galaxy that bordered the Deep Core and contained some of the wealthiest and most prestigious planets.

Alderaan, Brentaal IV, Chandrila, Corellia, Corulag, Corusaunt, Duro, Eufornis Major, Ganthel, Hosnian Prime, Kuat, Plexis, and Tinnel IV were all Core Worlds.
