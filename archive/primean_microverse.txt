The [b][i]Primean Microverse[/b][/i] is an extremely inaccessible, extra-dimensional sanctuary, located deep inside the subspace. It is completely isolated from the universe.

The [b][i]Primean Microverse[/b][/i] is the territory of [b][i][nation=short+noflag]The Primean Federation[/nation][/i][/b]. The [i]Federation[/i] artificially created the [i]Microverse[/i] thousands of years ago.
