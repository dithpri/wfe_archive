For the past four centuries, Empires have risen, fallen, and persevered. From the Old Illustrious Kingdoms and Empires of Europe to the Young Upstart ones in The New World, to the Exotic Barbarism of Darkest Africa and The Orient. The Great Powers of the world have adhered to an unspoken code of conduct between one another, keeping the peace. Now in the year of our Lord 2000 A.D. The world has enjoyed a long period of relative tranquillity.
