[b][u]Welcome to the Arab League![/u]

The Arab League is a large  congregation of Arab nations, serving as a superstate to coordinate the affairs of its member countries, promote democracy, equality and foster international cooperation. The Arab League seeks to bring together both Arab and non-Arab nations, so that they may govern themselves under a strong and united federal government.

All nations are required to endorse the WA Delegate [nation=short]Anarchestan[/nation] in order to strengthen the region and promote stability.[/b]
