Established on age-old principles, the Federation of Unified Republics was created after Winston&#39;s Rebellion and the collapse of the Party in late 1984. Following bloody revolution, FoUR gave up its Pacific territories and focused on main-land Europe. Today it enforces traditional society, that for over 20 years was brutally oppressed.

Republican Principles:
If you seek to apply for a position or propose legislation, say so in chat and the Legislative branches (Assembly and Senate) will create a poll. The Executive powers (Founder and Consul) will have final say in all legislative decisions.
