[color=goldenrod]Ideology:[/color]
The collapse of the Roman Empire lead to a power struggle across the region, out of which, in an alternate Universe, the Federation of Unified Republics formed, and FoUR continues on the rule and order the Roman Empire struggled to create.

[color=crimson]Republican Principles:[/color]
If you seek to apply for a position or propose legislation, say so in chat and the [color=turquoise]Legislative branches (Assembly and Senate) [/color]will create a poll. The [color=indigo]Emperor)[/color]will have final say in all legislative decisions. The [color=indigo] Emperor[/color] can also initiate legislative polls.
