[region]Dundee[/region] is a coastal city in eastern Scotland, part of the [region]Empire of Britain[/region]. Located on the north bank of the Firth of Tay, Dundee is known for its rich industrial heritage, cultural institutions, and growing technology and biomedical sectors. The city blends historic architecture with modern developments along its waterfront.
