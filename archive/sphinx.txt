[b][color=#DAA520]Sphinx[/b]

[i]&quot;Land of the Pharaohs, where the Nile breathes life into golden sands.&quot;[/i]

[b]Overview:[/b]
Sphinx stretches along the sacred Nile, a realm of pyramids, sphinxes, and ancient temples. Its people honor the gods through timeless rituals and grand festivals, guided by the divine Pharaoh.

[b]Culture:[/b] Hieroglyphs, sun worship, monumental architecture.

[b]Military:[/b] Sacred guards and desert scouts protect the land and its treasures.

[b]National Symbol:[/b] The Sphinx, watching over the eternal sands.[/color]
