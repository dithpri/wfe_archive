[b]F&oacute;dlan[/b]: A continent divided between four powers, an aging empire in the south, a troubled kingdom in the north, a confederation of squabbling nobles in the east, and a powerful church influencing the affairs of all three. For now, these powers coexist in peace, despite internal issues...

This region is themed after the 2019 RPG Fire Emblem: Three Houses and 2022 ARPG Fire Emblem Warriors: Three Hopes.
Embassy requests from fellow Fire Emblem/Nintendo themed regions will be accepted.
