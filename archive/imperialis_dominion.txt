Within its boundaries, diverse landscapes and cultures converge, yet all pledge unwavering loyalty to the Emershian crown. This dominion is more than just land; it is a testament to Emershia&#39;s imperial supremacy, a region where the empire&#39;s influence and authority remain unchallenged and absolute.
