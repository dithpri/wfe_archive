**New Fangoria**

**Background:**
New Fangoria, a South Pacific region of diverse islands and coastal areas, is emerging as a vital economic and cultural center.

**Economy:**
Key sectors include agriculture (tropical fruits and spices), fisheries, and growing hospitality and eco-tourism industries.

**Politics:**
Comprised of semi-autonomous territories and independent nations, New Fangoria promotes regional cooperation for economic and cultural development.

**Culture:**
Celebrates a blend of indigenous traditions and modern influences through vibrant arts, music, and festivals.
