This archipelago, which lies deep within the northern latitudes of the mighty Trinity Sea, consists of two independent nations: Mendanau and Bircheshire Isle.

Bound together since the Age of Antiquity, the Moonlit Archipelago has been a model of continued prosperity since those early days. The two nations are now thought to be some of the most advanced societies in the known world.
