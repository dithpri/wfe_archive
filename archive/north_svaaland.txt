A collective group of independent nations joined together in order to provide mutual defense against any enemies, domestic or foreign. North Svaaland is currently composed of seven nations, with the prevalent leaders being the Kingdom of Narcopholei, led by King Mainard, and the Northumbrian Empire, spearheaded by Emperor John III. The World Assembly Delegate is the Imperial Foreign Affairs Consul Antony Paladius, a senior minister in the Northumbrian government.
