The [nation]Hongshan Academy of Sciences[/nation] (HAS), formally known as the Affiliated Hongshan Academy of Sciences of [region]Tianshi Bureau of Great Yan[/region], is a faction on Talos-II. Established in the aftermath of the First Aggeloi War by a team of Tianshis originally dispatched from [region]Yan[/region], Hongshan has evolved into a vast research institution specializing in agriculture and cutting-edge scientific research.
