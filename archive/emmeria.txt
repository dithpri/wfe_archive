[color=blue]Emmeria[/color], officially the [b]Republic of Emmeria[/b], is the largest country on the continent of Anea and a leading military superpower in Strangereal. It shares borders with Estovakia to the east and Nordennavic to the northwest. The Fuscum Sea separates Emmeria from Yuktobania to the south.
