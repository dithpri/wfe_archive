[b][region]Azawad[/region][/b] is an unrecognised state that was unilaterally declared after conflict in 2012, comprising the Malian regions of Timbuktu, Kidal, Gao, as well as a part of Mopti region, all of which are internationally recognised to be part of the Republic of Mali. The territory claimed by Azawad borders Mali to the southwest, Burkina Faso to the south, Mauritania to the west and northwest, Algeria to the north, and Niger to the east and southeast. It straddles a portion of the Sahara and the Sahelian zone. Gao is its capital and largest city.
