the CPCSN (central people&#39;s coalition of socialist nations) is a regional coalition of countries trying to create a place were freedom, equity, and equality are the birthright of all people. this coalition will practically be a place of free trade, open borders, and will aid humanitarian efforts both within the nations of the coalition and abroad. prerequisites for joining will be as follows:
your nation must be a democracy; your nation must at least have moderate civil rights and political freedom; your nation must be at least a 50.0 on the Marx-Engels Emancipation Scale; your nation cannot be authoritarian, a dictatorship, consumerist, or a corporate state.
