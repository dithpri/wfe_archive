Allpa is a watery planet with many islands. The greatest influence on the planet is the Commonwealth of ChinchWayuq. The name of the planet is Wayuqi (a similar language to Quechua) for &quot;land&quot;. The Ayucu name for the planet is Sudusz, which translates to &quot;soil&quot;. Allpa is a planet with diverse wildlife, especially in the planet&#39;s expansive oceans.
