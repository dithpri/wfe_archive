The Global Technocratic Union is in the sub-Technate of earth, and is the leader of the sol Technate. All countries on earth are in The Global Technocratic Union. 
The Global Technocratic Union is friendly with all nations within the sol Technate, though it is most closely allied with the Global Martian Technocratic Union. Other sub-technates include Venus, Mercury, the asteroid belt, Pluto, the kuiper belt, and the Oort Cloud. Experimental floating cities exist on the gas planets, although they are not considered official nations.
