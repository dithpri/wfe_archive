Tokyo is the capital of Japan and the political, economic and cultural center of the country. It is located in the southern part of the Kanto region, bordering Chiba Prefecture to the east, Yamanashi Prefecture to the west, Kanagawa Prefecture to the south, and Saitama Prefecture to the north. Important facilities such as the Imperial Palace, the National Diet Building, and the Tokyo Stock Exchange are concentrated in the city center. The administrative district also includes islands such as the Izu Islands and the Ogasawara Islands, and is the southernmost and easternmost point of Japan.
