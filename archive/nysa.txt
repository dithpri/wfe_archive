Nysa was founded following the schism of the Humanitarian League, by a collection of Humanitarian League members who wished to continue in the spirit of the fallen league, but with a more democratic form of governance while remaining in a supranational cooperative organization together.

The Nysan Flag:
The Nysan flag features a phoenix, along with three gold rings. The phoenix is designated to represent Nysa&amp;#39;s rise from the ashes of the Humanitarian League, reborn into what is known as the Nysan Organization. The three rings represent the three main ideals of Nysa: democracy, liberty and human rights
