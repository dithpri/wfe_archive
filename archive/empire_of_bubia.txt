The phrase evil empire was applied to the Empire of Bubia notably by [nation]Southern Ageena[/nation], who took an aggressive, hard-line stance that favored matching and exceeding the Empire&amp;#39;s strategic and global military capabilities, in calling for a rollback strategy that would, in his words, write the final pages of the history of the Bubian Empire. The characterisation demeaned the Bubian Empire and angered Emperor Bubia; it represented the rhetorical side of the escalation of the Ageena-Bubian war.
