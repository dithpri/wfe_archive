The year is 2050. Welcome to the land formerly known as Allegheny County, PA. After years of plague, war, and famine, the county has become a barren wasteland, and as factions fight for control of the land, several leaders come together to discuss, fight, and work towards a future: whether that future is good or bad is still up in the radioactive air.
