[b]What is the WFS?[/b]
The World Freedom Syndicate is an organization dedicated to the preservation and creation of world peace and security. Founded in the November of 2022 by the Southern American Federation, it exists to protect those who cannot protect themselves in our world. Any nation capable of protecting others is applicable to join us and our expanding influence. 

[b]Our Mission:[/b]
The motto of the WFS is &#147;For the New World&#148;. It is dedicated first and foremost for the protection and progression of our member states. It&#146;s second goal is protecting those who cannot protect themselves. If countries or peoples needs protecting, then that&#146;s where we come in. Democracy, peace, cooperation, and progression are our ideals.
