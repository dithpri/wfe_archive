[b]221B Baker Street[/b] is the London address of the fictional detective Sherlock Holmes, created by author Sir Arthur Conan Doyle. Baker Street in the late 19th century was a high-class residential district, and Holmes&#39; apartment would probably have been part of a Georgian terrace. &#151;Wikipedia[hr]
