Nintika Region is a Region formed by Gila spreading out through out the three region of Hilka Continent. With different culture the three region separated forming the Nintika, Vikiho, and Dajuka Region. In 17 B.C. the three region formed a union called the &quot;Hilka Union&quot; being allied til 1276 A.D. when corruption, war, and weaponry drove Vikiho and Dajuka Region to extinction. Leaving Nintika as the surviving region.
