Training of the prospective leaders of the provincial governments of the new Marnagarthean Earth continues here after [region]Marnagarthean Hegemony[/region] is done with them. Each government has assumed the most suitable basic government type, but more work is required in areas like education and foreign policy to adapt to local conditions. When this advanced training is completed, the new human governments will be ready for integration into the Marnagarthean Commonwealth.
