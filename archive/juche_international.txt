Juche (Korean: &amp;#51452;&amp;#52404;, lit. &#39;subject&#39;; Korean pronunciation: [t&amp;#597;ut&amp;#597;&amp;#688;e]), usually left untranslated,[1] or translated as &quot;self-reliance&quot;, is the official state ideology of North Korea, described by the government as Kim Il-sung&#39;s &quot;original, brilliant and revolutionary contribution to national and international thought&quot;.[2] It says that &quot;man is the master of his destiny&quot;,[3] that the North Korean masses are to act as the &quot;masters of the revolution and construction&quot;, and that by becoming self-reliant and strong can a nation achieve true socialism.[3
