[color=black][b]Hesse is one of the 16 [i]Bundesl&auml;nder[/i] of Germany, located in the centre of the country. It was previously a part of the Holy Roman Empire, becoming an Electorate in 1803, just before the Empire&amp;#39;s dissolution in 1806.[/color]

[color=red]Some of the various smaller states making up Hesse were annexed by Prussia in 1866, while the rest joined the German Empire in 1871. After 1918, Hesse became a republic within Germany.[/color]

[color=orange]After WWII, Hesse was re-established in its current form in 1946. Its capital is now Wiesbaden, and its largest city is Frankfurt Am Main. It is the &amp;quot;greenest&amp;quot; [i]Bundesland[/i] in Germany, with 42% of its area covered in forest.[/color][hr][color=black][region]The Union of German Nations[/region] welcomes you to Hesse![/color][/b]
