The Rebel States of Heleth is a region  steeped in the ancient history of Nationstates. The vast plateau of Heleth was home to many societies famed for their ruthless military caste; after years of turmoil on the plains, the tribes decided to unify under the banners of Coressimo and Mikesan who instituted great reforms all across the plain, and founded the infamous Royal Helethian Army (RHA), a name which once struck terror in to the hearts of their neighbours. 

Though once home to ruthless warriors and raider royalty, Heleth now stands as a memorial to faded glories; ruins now stand in the place of great palaces and fortresses. Though one may never tell when the hereditary war thirst of the Helethians will take them once more...
