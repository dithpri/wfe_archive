[b]The Pantheon[/b] is an inter-governmental organization that aims to enhance cooperation between independent countries. It was founded on November 3, 2013 as The Pantheon of Free States. Its aims include accelerating economic growth, social progress, cultural development among its members, protection of regional peace and stability, and opportunities for member countries to discuss differences peacefully.
