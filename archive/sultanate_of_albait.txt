The Sultanate of Albait is the only sultanate in the world and was formed by the exiled nephew of the first king of Saudi Arabia. The arab nation is 15,000 square miles big which is half of the size of the UAE. The sultanate is known for being the most free arab. The sultanate consists of 3 Albaiti Provinces, Al-Duamen, Al-Shahriyah and Al-Beit. The capital city is Al-Bait but the government operates in the city of Shah An-Muhommet in the province of Al-Shariyah.
