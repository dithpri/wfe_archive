The [b]Ctan Underspace[/b] is a remote, isolated region - located deep inside the subspace. It is a cluster of different subspace sanctuaries physically isolated from the universe - each with its own isolated space-time-reality. 

The [b]Ctan Underspace[/b] is controled by the advanced hypercivilization of [b][nation=short+noflag]the Ctan Species[/Nation][/b] and protected by the extremely massive [i]Ctan Defense Forces[/i].
