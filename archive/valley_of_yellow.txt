Cape Yellow has a friendly landscape and environment, with relaxing hot springs and red-leafed trees as two of the many environmental treasures. All the nations of Cape Yellow are kind towards foreigners, and anyone who would like to join the region may. Political freedom is very important in Cape Yellow, as well as integrity and cheerfulness. If who would like to join the region of Cape Yellow, please think about whether you could help this region become a better place. Thank you for considering to join Cape Yellow.
