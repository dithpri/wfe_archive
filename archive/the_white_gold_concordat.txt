The White Gold Concordat is a Supranational government. Meaning that each Nation can manage itself by its own government, but by agreeing to the Concordat, they agree to answer to the Regional Government. The Regional Government is not in place to control each and every Nation under its rule, but to Bring together each nation to complete a common goal and protect the people of that region in the event of tyranny within a member nation.
