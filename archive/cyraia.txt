Cyraia is a fantasial and interesting region with a highly tropical climate, large sea and its own strong mountain range. It is home to many bizarre species and mythological creatures, where magic and science-fiction meet and where fighter pilots fight dragon-riding warriors. In a world with such diversity and magical chaos, Cyraia stands tall as a Region of new and developing power and strength after the collapse of the Region&amp;#39;s nations led to a series of new nations starting to develop.
