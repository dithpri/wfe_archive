Yokohama (Japanese: &amp;#27178;&amp;#27996;, pronounced [jokohama] &amp;#9432;) is the second-largest city in Japan by population[1] and by area, and the country&#39;s most populous municipality.[a] It is the capital and most populous city in Kanagawa Prefecture, with a population of 3.7 million in 2023. It lies on Tokyo Bay, south of Tokyo, in the Kant&amp;#333; region of the main island of Honshu. Yokohama is also the major economic, cultural, and commercial hub of the Greater Tokyo Area along the Keihin Industrial Zone.
