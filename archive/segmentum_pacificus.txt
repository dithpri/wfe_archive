A small region in the far north of the Pacific, home to a few dozen archipelago that experience freezing climates.

The region has experienced recent turmoil as the Novus Ordo Imperialis has taken control of the capital of unified states in this region, this has caused the collapse of order in the region.

War is rampant in the region as factions fight for control with the dominant faction being the Novus Ordo Imperialis.

The future looks bleak for this location, if new leaders do not take the opportunity to forge nations of their own this area, then the new Empire my take full control.
