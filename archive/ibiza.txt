[b]Ibiza[/b] is a Spanish island in the [region]Mediterranean Sea[/region] off the eastern coast of the Iberian Peninsula. It is 93 miles from the city of [region]Valencia[/region]. It is the third largest of the [region]Balearic Islands[/region] in area, but the second-largest by population.
