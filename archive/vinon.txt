What was once a blank blurb on the collective maps of explorers everywhere is now being colonised in the name of human progress. Some say the recent surge of immigrants is diluting what little chance the newborn society has at a firm regional identity, while a vocal opposition insists that the building of a culture which glorifies diversity with harmonious cooperation is not only feasible, but essential to maintaining the idyllic lifestyle which the people insofar enjoy. In any case, the future growth and flourish of the land lies yet undetermined in the adolescent hands of all who might take up the cause, and who might resolve themselves to make a new life in the virgin opportunities of [b]Vinon[/b].
