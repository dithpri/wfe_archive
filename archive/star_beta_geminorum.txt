Pollux, also designated as &amp;#946; Geminorum (beta Geminorum), is a variable and multiple hypergiant star of spectral class K0 in the constellation of Gemini. Pollux visual magnitude is 1.14, making it the 16th brightest star in the sky. Because of its brightness, Pollux is clearly visible to the naked eye when observed from locations with dark skies, and should be also quite easily visible from light polluted areas.
