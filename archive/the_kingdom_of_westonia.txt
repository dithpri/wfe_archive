The Kingdom of Westonia, with its capital located in the bustling city of Naragan, serves as the monarchical body for several small colonies and protectorates. With a deeply rooted history of tradition converged with social liberalism, Westonia welcomes any and all nations to join in the fellowship of monarchical tradition.

Head of the Commonwealth: HRH. Henry Louis Windsor III, King of Westonia and his other royal colonies, Head of the Commonwealth, and Supreme Governor of the Church of Westonia
Secretary General: Lord William Farel Canarven, Secretary General of the Kingdom of Westonia Commonwealth Secretariat
Prime Minister: Cornelius Humberton IV, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Westonia Commonwealth Ministry
