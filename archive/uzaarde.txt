&amp;quot;[b][color=green]uzaarde[/color][/b]&amp;quot; means &amp;quot;[i]the source[/i]&amp;quot; in an ancient language from this region.

The region encompasses three continents and several island groups. An isthmus connects the continents of Meiorca and Menarca. The third continent remains unexplored and is assumed to be uninhabited.

Nations of Uzaarde generally embrace diversity and democratic or publicly supported government.
