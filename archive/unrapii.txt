Welcome to the world of Unrapii! Unrapii is a region created for the purpose of documenting and archiving happenings and events in the fantasy world of Unrapii. Nations representing organizations/factions in Unrapii will be created and will join the Unrapii region. Each nation&#39;s own factbooks will represent their events, and global or regional events will be placed in the Unrapii region&#39;s factbooks.
