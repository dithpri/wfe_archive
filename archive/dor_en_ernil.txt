Dor-en-Ernil comprises all the land between the rivers Gilrain and Ringl&oacute;-Morthond including the hilly penninsula called Belfalas. To the west lies Anfalas, Lebennin to the east. The Realm of Dor-en-Ernil is an autonomous province in the heart of southern Gondor. Two of the most important ports lay within its borders and the capitol, Dol Amroth is neighbored to the famous elven city of Edhellond. The name &amp;quot;Dor-en-Ernil&amp;quot; is sindarin for &amp;quot;Land of the Prince&amp;quot;. The Principality has long prized its autonomy from the central realm.
