Tok-Toks Islands are the liliputian and well hidden islands of the isolationist Tok-toks birds nation who set their home there next to the Black Parrots islands and the Bulbul islands.
They enjoy freedom peace and serenity in this eden looking toxic waste free paradise.
There are coral reefs with numerous species of fishes and erm [i]almost[/i] no active volcanoes...But ecofriendly tourists are welcome !
