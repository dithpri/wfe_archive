The [b]Greater Alphan Empire[/b] or the &quot;[b]GAE[/b]&quot;, commonly referred to as &quot;[b]the New Empire[/b]&quot; or &quot;[b]the New Imperium[/b]&quot; is the galactic polity dominated by [b][nation=long]Greater Alpha[/nation][/b] united in the name of the [b]Emperor of Mankind[/b]. 

Formed after the [b]Second Great Crusade[/b], the [b]Peace of Terra[/b] resulted in [b]OSN[/b] and [b]CSR[/b] factions absorbed into the Empire, while also preserving their sovereignty and alliances. The leading members of both factions are represented in the [b]Imperial Council[/b] representing the interest of the nations of each faction in the Empire.
