The [b]Greater Alphan Empire[/b] or the &quot;[b]GAE[/b]&quot;, commonly referred to as the &quot;[b]Alphan Empire[/b]&quot; or the &quot;[b]New Imperium[/b]&quot; is the galactic conglomerate of nations dominated by [b][nation=long]Greater Alpha[/nation][/b] and united in the name of the [b]Emperor of Mankind[/b]. 

Formed after the [b]Third Galactic War[/b], the [b]Peace of Terra[/b] resulted in the [b]Coalition of Free Nations (CFN)[/b], the [b]Aturan League[/b], and the [b]Vytergrad Security Pact (VSP)[/b] factions joining the Empire, while also preserving their sovereignty and alliances. The leading members of these factions are represented in the [b]Imperial Council[/b] representing the interest of the nations of each faction in the Empire.
