[b]Provisional founding treaty statement:[/b]
The Euranian Union is an intergovernmental treaty and collective defence organisation, whose members are subject states of the [region]The Second European Empire[/region]. The Euranian Union exists to encourage dialogue and cooperation among the constituent states of the Euranian continent, on issues such as collective security, human rights, and trade. [nation]Morrainea[/nation] currently serves as Secretary General. 

[b]Union with the SEE[/b]
The Euranian Union is a constituent region and dependency of the [region]The Second European Empire[/region]. Our Minister to the SEE is currently [nation]Felksland[/nation]. For issues which do not concern the Empire, [nation]Gotesbourg[/nation] is the responsible Commissioner for External Affairs.
