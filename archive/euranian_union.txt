[b]Provisional founding treaty statement:[/b]
The Euranian Union is an intergovernmental treaty and collective defence organisation founded on the principles of the Treaty of K&uuml;lnsburg. The Euranian Union exists to encourage dialogue and cooperation among the constituent states of the Euranian continent, on issues such as collective security, human rights, and trade. The business of the Euranian Union will be conducted principally on the basis of multi-lateral talks, but also by way of the Euranian Council, which consists of the Heads of State of member-states, and the General Assembly, whose members are specially appointed diplomatic staffs and the responsible ministers of the Union&#39;s respective governments.
