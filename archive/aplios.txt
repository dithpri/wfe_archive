Aplios is a WWI Era Tech Fantasy region. This meaning that Nations within this region can have fantasy races, and magic. Imagine Dwarves fighting in the trenches with Drow. Elves on blimps throwing bolts of lightning at Orcs as they fly around attacking in propeller fighting planes! Gnomes riding on steam powered trains firing machine guns at Trolls as they try to raid the steam powered locomotive! This and much more awaits in Aplios!
