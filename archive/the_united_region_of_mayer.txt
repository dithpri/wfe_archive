[i][color=Red]Regional Summary[/color][/i]

The United Region of Mayer is a region that is heavily based on the United Kingdom and European politics. Forming political parties, having public discourse and debates, and even scheduling regional state visits are just some of the many things in store for the new region. Countries can appoint representatives for the region to vote on regional legislation and for the leader of the region. Come and join the region today!
