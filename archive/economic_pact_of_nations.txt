 [b]Welcome to the Economic Pact of Nations![/b]

The Economic Pact of Nations or the EPON is an Alliance created by rich and proud Nations to improve the economic strength of the World and to Stabilize it. Each nation wishing to be a Class (S) EPON member must have at least &pound;1,000 Billion.

You can communicate freely with all members or join our discord.

Class (EPON) is reserved for Founder
Class (WAEPON) is for WA delegate
Class W is obtained by being in WA
Class EX is for Nations that cease to exist
