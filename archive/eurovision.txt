[b]The Eurovision Song Contest, often known simply as Eurovision or by its initialism ESC, is an international song competition organised annually by the European Broadcasting Union.

The Junior Eurovision Song Contest (often shortened to JESC or Junior Eurovision) is an international song competition which has been organised by the European Broadcasting Union (EBU) annually since 2003.

This region is dedicated to featuring daily Eurovision and Junior Eurovision entries.
