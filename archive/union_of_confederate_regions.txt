The Union of Confederate Regions is a confederacy created by the merger between Aerope, Regime of Ascendancy, Beastkin Imperial Conglomerate of Langor and AUSHER, The Third party and The British Republic . One representative from each region is given access to the Union Council.

Citizens of all regions are equal, as are member regions of the Union. WA Membership is highly encouraged for all members (exluding the Union Council).
