Hello and welcome to [color=#BF40BF]Universum Aethereum[/color]. We are a wholly dedicated and tightly knit community of writers and role-players. Here we are all dedicated to the continued evolution and expansion of our carefully crafted universe and the stories we built in it. 

Our RP universe is a Science Fiction setting where humanity&#39;s golden age came to an abrupt end. It burrows elements from several well known sci-fi media and combines these elements with other sci-fi tropes and runs them through a system of rules and balances to carefully allow creative freedom while tying the origins of these elements into the canon. 

We encourage you to join the [url=https://discord.gg/JuryGhAtr8]Discord[/url] for general Inquiries and better communication.
