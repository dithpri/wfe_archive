The Croxian Union of Soviet Socialist Republics (Croxian Union or CUSSR) is a highly centralised supranational union of One-Party Social Democratic Republics, in which the Communist Party of the Croxian Union (CPCU) hold a constitutionally enshrined monopoly of power. Other political parties exist, indeed, there is a mainstream Liberal Party and a mainstream Conservative Party, both of which are legal, and both of which form the official opposition to the governing Communist Party.
