The Confederation of Nations (CN) is an intergovernmental organization aiming to maintain international peace and security, develop friendly relations among nations, achieve international cooperation, and be a centre for harmonizing the actions of nations.[2] It is the world&#39;s largest and most familiar international organization.[3] The CN is headquartered on international territory in New York City and has other main offices in Geneva, Nairobi, Vienna, and The Hague.

Like the UN and the Council of Nations in Bok  , It was established after World War II with the aim of preventing future wars, succeeding the ineffective League of Nations.
