The [b]Sea of Na&igrave;beth region[/b] is an intergovernmental organization supporting the nations around the Sea of Na&igrave;beth, a large inland sea. The organization&#39;s founding members are [nation]Shevei[/nation], [nation]Mortson[/nation], and [nation]Sevirbadhu[/nation], the latter being the long-time cultural and diplomatic centre of the region. The lands around the Sea of Na&igrave;beth are mostly mountainous in the north and northeast, and fertile plains fading to desert on the southern coast. The Sea of Na&igrave;beth organization (SoNo) is the region&#39;s centralized point for communication and diplomacy. Its headquarters are in the Sevirbadhi capital of Kuto and is lead by a rotating chair, currently occupied by Shevei.
