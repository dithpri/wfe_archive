Somewhere upon the frigid waves of the ocean, avoided by the forceful currents themselves, exists an eldritch expanse of our Earth, forever shrowded beneath clouds blacker than the deepest cavern. What lies within this realm of eternal night is beyond all knowledge, so only tales as old as mankind itself can provide some consolation as one stares into an eerie abyss in which no light has ever shone.
Oddly enough, the majority of the said tales speak of strange and incomprehensible cities and realms, creatures and people which install terror in one&#39;s heart at mere contemplation of them.
