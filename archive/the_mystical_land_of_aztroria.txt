Welcome, to the Mystical land of Aztroria!!

We are a growing community focused on developing our own world and meeting new people.

Aztroria is a land of our own creation and is sectored into different realms and ruled in various and extremely different ways by the 7 Royal Houses, Kvell (Pride), Cupidity (Greed), Ardour (Lust), Invidiare (Envy), Gormandize (Gluttony), Dies Irae (Wrath) &amp; Torpid (Sloth), whom are based off of the Seven Deadly Sins. 

We are a Fun, laid back region where you can enjoy yourself and relax, maybe enjoy and partake in some RP, and just generally just have fun!
