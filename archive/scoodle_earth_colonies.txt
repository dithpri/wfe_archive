During the reign of Seldo the Third, many groups of Scoodles fled from his tyranny and headed for planet Earth, arriving from the depths of space. (During this time, Seldo the Third had taken control of the InterGalactic Union, so it became necessary to take refuge on an obscure planet, such as Earth, which is located outside of the Union.) On Earth, the Scoodles found a place not entirely unlike their previous home, and so decided to settle down and begin a new chapter in Scoodle history. Though the different nations in the Scoodle Earth Colonies may not share the same beliefs or values, they do hold two things in common: [color=teal]the remembrance of their home planet, Scoodle Star, and the hope in the future of Scoodlekind.[/color]
