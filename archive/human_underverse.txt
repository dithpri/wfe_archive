The [b]Human Underverse[/b] is as a tiny isolated universe located deep inside of subspace. It is extremely inaccessible from real-space and was therefor colonized by humanity to have an eternal sanctuary - a sanctuary where the human species can prosper and live a harmonic life in peace, hedonism and security for all future.

Reflecting the importance of the [i]Underverse[/i] as a new, eternal and secure homestead for the human race the name of the state officially became 
[b][nation=long]the Underverse[/nation][/b].
