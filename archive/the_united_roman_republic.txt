The city of San Marino and the Western Roman Empire in Illyria ruled by emperor Julius Nepos united under a new Roman Republic. Nepos would serve as Consul and an elected official from San Marino would serve as the other consul. A senate made up of half Sanmarinese and half Illyrian would also govern the new Republic.
