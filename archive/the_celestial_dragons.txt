The Celestial Dragons, an elitist and aristocratic group, hold a position of unrivaled power within the World stage. Descendants of noble houses that supported and joined King Sno the Light, in his journey of guiding unfortunate souls during a time of war and destruction, to a land that today would be known as, the Kingdom of Filopi.

Those who join gain the status of a &quot;Celestial Dragon&quot; and is invited to live in the palaces of Monsetrouvette, the Kingdom&#39;s capital, but only to those in power.
