[color=004D33][b]Western Sahara[/b]
officially [region=Sahrawi Arab Democratic Republic]

Territory of northwestern Africa
bordered by [region=Morocco], [region=Algeria] and [region=Mauritania], with the [region=Atlantic Ocean] to the west.
One of the most sparsely populated territories in the world, mainly consisting of desert flatlands.

[b]Capital:[/b] [region=Laayoune] (claimed), [region=Tifariti] (defacto)
Population of the Free Zone is estimated between 30 and 40 thousand inhabitants.
Population of the Moroccan occupied zone is estimated at 500,000 west of the Wall.
The defacto capital was moved from [region=Bir Lehlou] in 2008 to [region=Tifariti].
