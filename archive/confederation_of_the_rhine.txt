The Confederation of the Rhine is a league of sovereign Duchies and Kingdoms united under the [nation]Supreme French Empire[/nation]. Formed to secure mutual defence and economic strength, it bound its member states through shared military command, coordinated trade policy, and a Federal Council of Governors. Though each realm retained internal authority, foreign policy and war powers rested with the confederation&#146;s central leadership. Its rise reshaped Central Europe and marked the transition from fragmented principalities to a structured imperial bloc.
