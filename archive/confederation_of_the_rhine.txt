The Confederation of the Rhine is a league of sovereign Duchies, Kingdoms and Empires united under the [nation]Supreme French Empire[/nation]. Formed to secure mutual defence and economic strength, it bound its member states through shared military command, coordinated trade policy, and a Federal Council of Governors. Though each realm retained internal authority, foreign policy and war powers rested with the Confederation&#146;s central leadership. In the past, membership of this Confederation was limited to German States. Now, due to political and global changes, membership has expanded across Europe and beyond.
