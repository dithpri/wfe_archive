Welcome one and all to Treachery, a game of deception and detective work for residents of Europe.  Players divided into faithful and traitors. Here&#146;s how it works:

How to play:

Player count: Minimum 10, maximum 20.

Traitor count: 3-4 traitors.

Game ends: when 4 or less players remain. If just one traitor remains they take it all.

1 challenge, 1 banishment and 1 murder each night (unless otherwise stated) murders take place over telegram.

Each challenge will take place around 6:00pm GMT.
