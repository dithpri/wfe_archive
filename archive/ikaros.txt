[hr][b]IKAROS[/b] is a social roleplaying region based on an Earth in 2175, long after climate change has flooded and desertified much of the world&#39;s land. Building on the ruins of the Old World, states must navigate a new geopolitical landscape marred by a fragile ecosphere, land scarcity, and the ever-present specter of their history.
