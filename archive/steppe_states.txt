This region is set in a fantasy world where a great horde that dominated the steppe of the equator has fallen. It is up to you to reclaim glory and rise from the ashes of the fallen centerum horde! The north is defined by vast plant life but with fearsome predators and wild deases lurking in the trees. The south is split between a vast plain and a tundra formed by the kalin meteor crash and vast deforestation, greatly expanding the already existing polar permafrost. The equator features a large steppe called tue vanari and a smaller steppe called the drazi (named after the national currency of the centerum horde). The steppe became the home of humans and has rather unique and valuable metals
