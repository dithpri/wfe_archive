This is a friendly, open, roleplaying region. (still working on map &amp; other region features)

The Nations of Asioul are a collective of sovereign nations, colonies and territories on the planet Asioul. 
Originally discovered by Reavas III and further colonized by Reavas IV, this planet is a collection of lush, forested continents. Several lakes, rivers, valleys and mountains cover the landscape. 

Currently, only 2 nations inhabit the planet. Reavas IV and The Advocates.
