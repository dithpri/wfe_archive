The Ionian Archipelago is in the Ionian sea, between Calabria and Peloponnese. It consists of the islands of D&#39;Fenidche, Halivaara, Spiyeronje, Kurmuka, Taeki, Pikujoe and many other small islands and islets. D&#39;Fenidche contains the prefectures of Astareo, Huryunko, Ertre, Keksojeo and Tyhyth. Halivaara is its own prefecture and Kurmuka, Taeki, Pikujoe, Raswe and Kojewako make up the prefecture of Kurmuka.
