The [b]Ionian Archipelago[/b] is in the Ionian sea, between Calabria and Peloponnese. It consists of the islands of [b]D&#39;Fenidche[/b], Halivaara, Spiyeronje, Kurmuka, Taeki, Pikujoe, Raswe, Kojewako and many other small islands and islets. [b]D&#39;Fenidche[/b] contains the prefectures of [b]Astareo[/b], [b]Huryunko[/b], [b]Ertre[/b], [b]Keksojeo[/b] and [b]Tyhyth[/b]. [b]Halivaara[/b] is its own prefecture and Kurmuka, Taeki, Pikujoe, Raswe and Kojewako make up the prefecture of [b]Kurmuka[/b].
