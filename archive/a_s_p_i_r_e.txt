[url=https://www.nationstates.net/nation=wheath/detail=factbook/id=main#history]The Astral Socio-Political International Research Endeavor[/url] was composed of Phaethontid nations of Primus Mundi that orbited Helios that defeated the [url=https://www.nationstates.net/nation=kosmopolity]Kosmopolitan Republic[/url]. The flag representing A.S.P.I.R.E. was jokingly referred to as &#39;the Escalator.&#39;
