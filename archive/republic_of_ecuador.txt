Ecuador, officially the [b]Republic of Ecuador[/b], is a representative democratic republic in South America, bordered by Colombia on the north, Peru on the east and south, and by the Pacific Ocean to the west. It and Chile are the only two countries in South America that do not have a border with Brazil. Ecuador also includes the Gal&aacute;pagos Islands in the Pacific, about 620 miles west of the mainland.[hr]

Capital: Quito
Largest city: Guayaquil
Government: Unitary presidential constitutional republic
President: Rafael Correa[hr]
