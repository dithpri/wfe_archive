[b][region]Devonport[/region] is a harbourside suburb of Auckland, in the [region]The Dominion of New Zealand[/region] . It is located on the North Shore, at the southern end of a peninsula that runs southeast from near Lake Pupuke in Takapuna, forming the northern side of the Waitemata Harbour. The suburb hosts the Devonport Naval Base of the [region]Royal New Zealand Navy[/region], the main facility for the country&amp;#39;s naval vessels, but is best known for its harbourside dining and drinking establishments and its heritage charm.
