Mebi oso na hit choda op nodataim. 
Osir gonplei nou ste odon nowe 
Kom folau, soso na gyon op
sin yu in ona moun sad
nowe ste daun 
gouthru klir hashta yu soujon,kom taim osa fail kom daun gon graun-de 
oso seingeda bilaik moubeda fous oso gada in 
oso laik won kru en oso throu daun gon choda 
ai swega klin na set raun kom yu feva
