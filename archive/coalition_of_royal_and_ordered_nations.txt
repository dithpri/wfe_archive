The [b]Coalition of Royal and Ordered Nations[/b] or also known as [b]C. O. R. O. N. A. S.[/b] is an intergovernmental organization and economic alliance of monarchist states led and founded by the [u]Kingdom of Merinaya[/u], seeking to preserve traditional royal governance and regal stability.

[u][b] The [region]Coalition of Royal and Ordered Nations[/region] does not accept nor endorse fascism, communism or any extremist ideology[/b][/u]
