The Serene Union of Nation-States with Ultra-Civilized Societies (SUNSCIVSOC) is a region-based supranational organization composed of twelve (12) member-states. The purpose of the organization is to, by the will of the Governor-State, improve the security and welfare of all nation-states belonging to the region.

The SUNSCIVSOC&#39;s founder and incumbent governor is the Serene Empire of Venitalica.
