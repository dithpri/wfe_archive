Kish was begotten by [nation=short]Nimrud[/nation], which boasts of the world&#146;s first potentate and whose hero was a mighty hunter in the face of the Lord. The principal cities of this kingdom were Babylon, Uruk, and Akkad, all of them in the land of Sumer.

Whichever dynasty controlled the city was recognized as [i]Shar Kishshati[/i], and was considered preeminent in Mesopotamia. By the time of Sargon, the title became to mean &#147;lord of the universe.&#148;
