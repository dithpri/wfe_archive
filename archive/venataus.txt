The Kodelan - Wolfstonian - Yukosian War Report

18.04.2015 - Kodela declares war on Yukos
20.04.2015 - Wolfstonia declares war on Kodela
21.04.2015 - Wolfstonia stations troops on Yukos-Kodela border along side Yukos Peace Keepers. Kodela prepares to attack Yukos.
22.04.2015 - Kodelan forces cross the river Yendis and begin attack on Yukosian border. Kodelan troops met with strong resistance at border and fortify their positions.
