Founded in 1632 by Angel Ernando Santana as a safe haven for those who were struck by wars, mainly funded by Unio Sirke Tare. In past years it has grown helping people all around the world with its many agencies, such as the USTH (Unio Sirke Tare Hospital), which helps people around the world. Or the USTFSS (Unio Sirke Tare Food and Shelter Supply), helps those around the world who are homeless.
