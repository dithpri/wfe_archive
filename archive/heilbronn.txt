[region]Heilbronn[/region] is the sixth-largest city in the state of [region]Baden Wurttemberg[/region], Germany. 

The northern city on the Neckar is a former Imperial Free City and is the seat of Heilbronn County. Heilbronn is also the economic centre of the Heilbronn-Franken region that includes most of north-east [region]Baden Wurttemberg[/region].

Heilbronn is known for its wine industry and is nicknamed K&auml;thchenstadt, after [url=https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Heinrich_von_Kleist]Heinrich von Kleist[/url]&amp;#39;s Das K&auml;thchen von Heilbronn.
