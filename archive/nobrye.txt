[b]THE REGION OF NOBRYE[/b]

Long ago, the island region of Nobrye was inhabited by a once powerful empire, the Empire of Nobryenia. Empires around the world had become advanced enough to inflict devastating damage amongst others using their arsenal of energy weaponry, leading to a complete collapse of society on a worldwide scale. With that, came the unmonitored use of said weapons. Billions came under fire as the world engulfed in chaos. 

Only a couple million were left, it was their responsibility to rebuild what their predecessors had done, but societal structure and history vanished long ago. This is where the story begins
