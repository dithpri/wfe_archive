The [region]Destiny Union[/region] [color=red](DU)[/color] consists of all nations located in Earth, Venus, Mars, the Moon, the Reef and Saturn.

[hr]

The STORY of this region is based off the science fiction game Destiny. After the Golden Age was attacked by the Darkness. Nations come together with others and fight back for their universe.

[hr]

The ROLEPLAY of this region is part of the full Destiny Union universe.
