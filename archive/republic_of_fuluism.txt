The large government juggles the competing demands of Defense, Spirituality, and Law &amp; Order. The average income tax rate is 54.7%, and even higher for the wealthy.

The fair Fuluish economy, worth 172 billion Pounds a year, is driven entirely by a combination of government and state-owned industry, with private enterprise illegal. The industrial sector is led by the Uranium Mining industry, with major contributions from Arms Manufacturing, Woodchip Exports, and Information Technology. Average income is 34,556 Pounds, and distributed extremely evenly, with little difference between the richest and poorest citizens.
