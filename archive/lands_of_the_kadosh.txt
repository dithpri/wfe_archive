The word Kadosh has ancient origins, and signifies this Region is considered sacred, and thus intended to denote the elevated character of the citizens and the sublimity of the truths and which distinguish it and its possessors from the other regions. It is said by Ancients, a person bore a token called a Kadosh, to apprise others that the bearer of this mark was a master of himself and possessed the privilege of &amp;quot;traveling in foreign lands, to earn wages, to better support himself and family, and contribute to the relief of the poor and penniless as he see fit&amp;quot; and to signify he has the capabilities and willingness to defend himself and others.
