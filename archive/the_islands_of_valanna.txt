General Information:

-The Islands of Valanna are a cluster of small islands and atolls tightly packed together. With most islands connected by bridges, the Islands stay in touch easily.
-The Islands of Valanna showcase beautiful beaches, ancient Native Valannan (Bababoan) ruins, and incredible architecture. Due to it&#39;s past being a victim of imperialism, incredibly preserved 1st world Georgian and Victorian architecture is found almost everywhere.
