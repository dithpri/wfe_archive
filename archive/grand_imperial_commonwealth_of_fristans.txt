Official Name: The Grand Imperial Commonwealth of Fristans
Short Form: The GIC
Government Type: Commonwealth of Independent States under Imperial Pact
Capital (Administrative): Imperial Core District of Tiberium

Overview:
The Grand Imperial Commonwealth of Fristans is a union of sovereign nations bound by a shared pact with the Imperial Republic of Fristans and eachother. Each member state retains full internal autonomy but agrees to participate in the collective framework of the Commonwealth, centered around Intracommonwealth trade, military co-operation and collective posture in foreign relations.
