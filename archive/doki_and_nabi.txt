Some of the cutest videos you will see, the [i]There She Is![/i] series is made by a Korean that goes by Sambazka. It is about a rabbit (Doki) and a cat (Nabi), with Doki falling in love with Nabi. In the world they live in, cats and rabbits cannot mate and have relationships, in fear of crossing the species. Everyone wants to be pure. In spite of this, Doki wants to be with Nabi anyway.
