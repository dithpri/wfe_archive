Far beyond disasters and corruption, a proud group of sovereign nations stands tall, trying to survive in a world full of wolves and sharks. Eastern Preya is willing to be a Commonwealth of independent territories symbolically hold together by the Most Serene Republic of Thornewood, a country that is going to be primus inter pares but not above the others. In Preya, so they say, your voice will always be heard. Cry out for help and this entire world of ours will come to the rescue.
