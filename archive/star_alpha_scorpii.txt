Antares, also designated as &amp;#945; Scorpii (alpha Scorpii), is a variable and double supergiant star of spectral class M1 in the constellation of Scorpius. Antares visual magnitude is 0.96, making it the 14th brightest star in the sky. Because of its brightness, Antares is clearly visible to the naked eye when observed from locations with dark skies, and should be also quite easily visible from light polluted areas.
