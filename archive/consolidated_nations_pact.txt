Name: Consolidate Nations Pact (CNP)
Established: 2024
Headquarters: New Dominus, GGD Arcyne
Purpose: The Consolidated Nations Pact (CNP) is an international alliance formed to promote unity, cooperation, and collective progress among its member nations. The pact aims to strengthen diplomatic ties, enhance economic collaboration, and ensure mutual security for the participating countries. By fostering dialogue, sharing resources, and implementing joint initiatives, the CNP strives to create a more stable, prosperous, and interconnected world.
