[b]WAR RULES:[/b]
Must have a Casus Beli (Reason for war)
No Weapons of mass destruction.
Cannot take out another player completely.

If a country is considered a warmonger, then a coalition may be created to slow down expansion.

And no interdimensional traveling either (also falls under Black hole exception)
