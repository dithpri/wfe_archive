[b]About the UFN[/b]
The UFN is a union created for economic, military and diplomatic cooperation between independent, center-right/ right-leaning, typically conservative nations. There is low central power and member-states enjoy almost complete autonomy. It is the successor of the now-absolved [region]Conservative Islands Alliance[/region].
