Aiur is the home world to young and veteran nations that are looking to have fun and a good time. Aiur is home to many different societies and cultures. We are in competition with the worlds of Eos, Brutus, and Mannyvin. Relations with the outside world will fluctuate depending on the what is going on at the time, but for now Aiur is maintaining friendly relations with Eos, Brutus, and Mannyvin.
