Ural Mountains [Credit: Encyclop&aelig;dia Britannica, Inc.]: mountain range forming a rugged spine in west-central Russia and the major part of the traditional physiographic boundary between Europe and Asia. 

Wikipedia.org: The Ural Mountains played a prominent role in Nazi planning. Adolf Hitler and the rest of the Nazi German leadership made many references to them as a strategic objective of the Third Reich, in the event that it decisively won the battle on the Eastern Front against the Soviet Union.
