The [color=#CA3433] Archive of Entropan [/color] is an Entropanian archive that has the largest collection of art, music, literature, web pages, video games, and other such creative arts and parts of history in the world. The Archive is ran by the Ministry of Culture, with the Minister of Culture personally answerable to law-breaking content on the Archive. It was founded in 1997, and now boasts over 840 billion web pages, 38 million books, 11 million films, 15 million songs, 2.6 million TV episodes, 1 million video games, 4.7 million images, 254 thousand concerts, and 1.9 million collections. It can be found at [color=#63C5DA] arkivo.en[/color].
