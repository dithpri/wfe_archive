A small group of nations strove to achieve what other regions could not, peace, order, and fairness. They set up a new land and took the precautions in order to survive in peace with one another and hopefully, the rest of the lands. However, how long such a peace would last was up for debate, as troublesome signs are always brewing, usually snuffed out in time, but it was unknown if all of them would meet such a fate, or if the trouble would find the nations hidden in the corner of the world...
