The Land of the Flogging Peasant is widely known for its floggings, minimal peoples rights, and unstable economy. The main source of income is from Trout fishing, and the regions religious leader is an Atheist. Children are often seen playing blackjack for pocket money, and business meetings across the region are always interrupted by the sound of a deflating whoopee cushion. The poverty levels in The Land of the Flogging Peasant is off the charts, and any political rights are unheard of. Anarchy is a common theme, and the wealthy are treated nicely in their castles, with a street view so that they can watch the daily public floggings.
