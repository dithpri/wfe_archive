Rishikavarta &#150; Abode of the Sages

A serene region, Rishikavarta blends ancient wisdom with modern progress. Its landscapes feature sacred rivers, fertile plains, and the cultural hub of Vedashila, the capital. Governed by the Council of Dharma, it upholds justice, harmony, and sustainability.

Known for vibrant festivals, holistic wellness, and spiritual tourism, Rishikavarta thrives on agriculture, crafts, and tech industries. The Temple of Eternal Light and Amritanadi River draw global visitors.

Motto: &quot;Wisdom is our Light, Peace is our Path.&quot;
Population: 12.5M | Currency: Varatmark
