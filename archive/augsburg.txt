[region]Augsburg[/region] is a city in the south-west of [region]Free State of Bavaria[/region], Germany. After [region]Neuss[/region] and [region]Trier[/region], Augsburg is Germany&amp;#39;s third oldest city, being founded by the Romans as Augusta Vindelicorum, named after the Roman emperor Augustus. Augustus was the founder of the Roman Empire and its first Emperor, ruling from 27 BC until his death in 14 AD.
