Bordered between rough beaches to the North, a huge mountain to the South, desert land to the East and a calm sea to the West. Bunia itself is mainly covered in forests and has a calm climate. The region&#39;s landscape is sublime; green forests, white sandy beaches and gorgeous flower fields are just a sliver of the richness Bunia has to offer, which is why the country is a favored destination among foreigners. The people of Bunia are tolerant towards foreigners and tend to welcome them with a bright smile. They feel foreigners could empower the country&#39;s well being.
