The Kaisaru Empirical Republic is the Republic of the main Kaisaru empire. The leader of the region is Iomada - One of the major leaders of the fast-expanding Kaisaru Empire. The Empirical Republic was the second Region Founded by the Kaisaru Empire.

It is closest to the Democratic Region of Empirical Kaisaru, despite political views. This is due to the friendliness of Leaders between Iomada and Klojija. Their greatest enemy in the Kaisaru Empire is the Oppresive Empirical Kaisaru due to the mistreatment of their people.
