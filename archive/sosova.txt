[color=#75ccff][b]Welcome to Sosova![/b][/color]
Sosova is a new region that is dedicated to its members, no matter how few members it has. With such small numbers, the region makes each member feel like they matter. We pay attention to what you have to say, and we most of all speak to each member. No member should be scared to speak to one of the high ranking officials. No member should be scared to speak in the forums and elsewhere.

Sosova focuses on role playing. Roleplay is often done in the [url=www.sosova.freeforums.net]Sosovian forums[/url]. Once registered in the forum, you can access the role playing boards. More information is located in the welcome telegram sent once you join.
