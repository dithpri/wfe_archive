A confederacy of autonomous nations.

Voluntary &amp;amp; Autonomous Membership - nations maintain voluntary relations with a right to secession, and respect autonomy of each other in their own internal affairs and regional participation.

Peace Agreement - Confederacy members retain peace between each other. Members agree not to aid any nation or region in aggression against a member of the confederacy.
- Members may request but not force extradition from other member nations.

Autonomous Trade Agreement - Member nations retain autonomy in internal trade relations and participation in commerce with other members. Member nations agree not to interfere in trade between other members.
