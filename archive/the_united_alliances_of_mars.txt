Mars is a colony started by the nations Precusor, Cyaania, and Gumbub.

Upon joining the UAM, you must choose between two alliances.

The Grand Unity of West UAM- The GUWU is the alliance of defense, negotiations, and peace. The GUWU will not declare war unless necessary.

The Incredible Nations of East UAM- The INEU is the alliance of power, war, and destruction. The INEU is known for declaring war to further their jurisdiction.

These are the alliances of the UAM. The alliances will not usually declare war on each other, but there has been war in the past.
