A tragic day is now a distant memory-the spread of the virus went unnoticed at first.  Once the symptoms began to rage through the human population it was too late to be stopped, and can now only be contained and tolerated.  

Stake out your corner of the Quarantine Zone, build up your nations defenses, and learn to thrive among the infection and chaos.  Most superpowers fled, but life is still manageable among the ruin.  

Governments will learn to cope, economies can be made or broken, and the People will prevail in one way or another.
