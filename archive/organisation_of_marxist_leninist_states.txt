The Organisation of Marxist-Leninist States is an international organisation of Socialist states that holds anti-Revisionist, Marxist-Leninist, Stalinist, Maoist, and Hoxhaist nations under one banner. 
The respective Parties of these nations will have conferences once per year, starting with the Organisation of Marxist-Leninist States International Party Conferences.
All Socialist nations with the aforementioned views are encouraged to join.
