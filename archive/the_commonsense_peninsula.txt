[b]Attention all member nations[/b]:
In order to establish a more perfect region and transition from an interim government to a democratically elected body, all member nations will be stripped of current Regional Authority and will have the opportunity to elect an Executive Chairman who will have the authority to appoint member nations to a Regional Executive Cabinet. All nations who are eligible (see [i]Eligibility[/i]) will automatically be placed on the ballot. [region]The Commonsense Peninsula[/region] will be holding their first regional elections on [u]Wednesday, December 2nd[/u].

[b]Regional Offices up for Vote[/b]:
[i]Executive Chairman[/i]- Has authority to appoint member nations to the Regional Executive Cabinet.

[b]Eligibility[/b]: 
Must be a member nation for at least 5 days.
