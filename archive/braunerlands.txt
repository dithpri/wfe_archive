The Braunerlands is full of defecation. Its etymology is derived from Old German where it means Brownerlands. A region of men and heroes, of sorrow and heroics, of religion and duty. A land of extremes. It is covered mostly in snow and sand and is incredibly hot during the day but mercilessly cold at night. Due to the harsh living conditions and the strict religions that govern the area, the people of this region have become very powerful and strong-willed.

Among the Braunerlands&amp;#39; eastern neighbors are the Finnish. It was a common belief that the Finns were wizards. Finnish wizards were known and feared by neighboring peoples around the Baltic Sea.
