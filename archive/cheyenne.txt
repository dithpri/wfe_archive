[b]Cheyenne[/b] is the capital and most populous city of the US state of Wyoming and the county seat of Laramie County. 

Cheyenne is situated on Crow Creek and Dry Creek. The Cheyenne, Wyoming Metropolitan Area had a 2010 population of 91,738, making it the 354th most populous metropolitan area in the United States. &#151;Wikipedia[hr]
