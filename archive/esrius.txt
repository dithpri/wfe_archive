[region]Esrius[/region] is a relatively new region, with a semi-structured government. The Regional Government is headed by the Regional Director, while the WA Delegate has no regional power whatsoever. Elections occur on a monthly basis.
[url=https://ibb.co/h1XHt7rh]Regional Map[/url]

The war between Pacan&#39;s alliance and Western Fremnia has come to a violent, swift end as ~500-600 nuclear and biochemical weapons were used by both sides against the other, which started as a final resort by Western Fremnia to defeat their enemy. Most of their former territories have been reduced to nuclear wastelands that will become habitable again on 5/23/2025.
