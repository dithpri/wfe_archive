[region][/region]A short time ago, Ametsang decided to form its very own region. The region is called The Independent Region of Ametsang. It currently has eleven nations. All of the nations are different, from more rough countries like the Dictatorship of Inner Dolisca to easier going countries like the Incorporated States of Allooddeeland. Any country is allowed to join, but the region is password protected, so contact a member to join.
