[b][color=003a91]Concord[/color][/b] is a small and beautiful city that sits in central New Hampshire. Divided by Interstate 93 and the Merrimack River, it serves as the capital of the state.  The State House, on Park Street, is the oldest in the country and host&#39;s the United States&#39; largest state-level legislature. The dome atop the building is gilded with 24-karat gold, visible from both the highways and sky in the distance, especially at night.
