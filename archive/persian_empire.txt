[b]THE ACHAEMENID AND SASSANID REALMS OF IRAN.[/b] The Persian Empire swallowed up the Babylonian and Assyrian Empires, and went beyond them! It was the greatest empire the world had ever known!

Persia is far more than a country: it is a place of splendor, where the gods dwell and the ancient heroes still walk in the land! All other lands are disappointing, for the sun does not shine so brightly elsewhere!
