The Asand system is a newly founded galaxy, it is basically devoid of life. New stars are bright and full of energy, but surrounded by lots of dust and rocks. The central area of the galaxy is the most developed, with the planet of the founder and the USTOO. USTOO is one of the most influential planets in this small, but bright galaxy. Our mission as region of Asand is to give order to this newly founded galaxy, and establish a goverment for this planets full of so much resources and knowledge. For the Asand System and its people we thrive, we explore, we conquer.
