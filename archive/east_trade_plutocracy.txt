The East Trade Empire (ETE) is a union of nations under a corporate trade agreement under the rule of highly rich families that compete for wealth and power. That is no guarantee single ruler, but many who owns shares in the empire with the highest owner being titled Emperor. Most commonly, families will share power and wealth with in their members to increase chance of profit and power. The ETE has been known to wage war between different families in a power struggle and is notorious for it&#39;s reoccurring civil war. The main purpose the empire was born to was to help the elites gain more power and wealth.
