[u]Regional Rules:[/u]
-All nations espousing views of authoritarianism, autocracy, and despotism are welcome.
-That the corrosive notion of democracy will be barred and expunged from joining this august region.
-That all nations in the international family will be considered equal, regardless of species.
-That all disputes will be handled by an appropriate reaction to the available circumstances.
