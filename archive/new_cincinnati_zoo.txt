After years of being oppressed by society, Harambe was shot, bringing in a new era to the Cincinnati Zoo. Scientists have revived Harambe, and led a force to be reckoned with to the zoo, and started his takeover. All the people now live there in harmony, with the only other beings being gorillas, With Harambe as the leader. Unfortunately, power corrupted the lowland gorilla, and tyranny ensued. Armed with a force of gorillas and human slaves, Harambe has vowed to eradicate all that gets in his way, and help all who join him in his quest for supremacy.
