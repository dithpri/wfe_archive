[b]Laconia[/b], also known as [b]Lacedaemonia[/b], is a region in the southeastern part of the [b]Peloponnese[/b]. Its capital is [b]Sparta[/b]. The word [i]laconic[/i] is derived from the name of the region by analogy&#151;to speak in a concise way, as the Spartans were reputed by the Athenians to do.
