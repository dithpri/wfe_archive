History:

Norde Fjorda, is a artic region. The first people arrived in Norde Fjorda after 7,000 BC when rising temperatures after the end of the last ice age made the country habitable. The first People lived by hunting (elk, deer, seal and whales) and by fishing.

After 3,000 BC farming was introduced into Norde Fjorda. The earliest farmers made tools and weapons from stone but after 1,500 BC bronze was used. After 500 BC people used iron. About 200 AD they began to used a form of writing called runes.   Today in the 21 century there exists modern nations and states here, but much of Norde fjorda is un explored becouse how big it is.
