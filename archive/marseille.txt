[b]Marseille[/b], located on France&#39;s south coast, is the second largest city in France. The city&#39;s population in 2012 was 852,516 and is the third-largest metropolitan area in France (after Paris and Lyon), with an area of 93 square miles.

Marseille was the most important trading center in the region and the main commercial port of the French Republic. Marseille is now France&#39;s largest city on the Mediterranean coast and the largest port for commerce, freight and cruise ships. &#151;Wikipedia[hr]
