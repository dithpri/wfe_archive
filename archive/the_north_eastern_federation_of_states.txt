We bring together a group of States to test the concept of government together. If we succeed, we imagine a new, legitimate form of self-government, a real nation that is taken seriously by others. A place where people do not feel hopeless about persistent, global problems. A place where they have a straightforward and efficient system for governing.

In these beginning stages, members shall discuss the development and adoption of a Constitution for our region; and members will also discuss the feasibility of this in relation to past, present, and future issues or events in the real world.
