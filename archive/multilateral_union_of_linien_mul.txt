[b]Origins:[/b]
The [region]Multilateral Union of Linien[/region] was founded on 15 August 2018. The concept of a peaceful community of nations in the greater region of Linien had been proposed as far back as 1978 but never happened because of warlike conflicts in 1975, 1978, and 1999.

[b]Members:[/b]
Founding members were the nations of [nation]West Berionia[/nation], [nation]Linienland[/nation], [nation]United Envol[/nation] and [nation]Nauti[/nation].
