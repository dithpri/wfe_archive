A far reaching western continent, originally colonized by Roman sailors in AD 157, which the center and north is completely dominated by the United Western Republics (UWR).

Most of the main western continent is currently occupied by the UWR, with territories to the far south and lands to the east across the Adriatic Sea still unclaimed.

Hesperiae is dominated by sub tropical and temperate coastlines. The inland North is dominated by forest, while central and southern inland areas are Semi-Arid and Arid conditions.
