The nations in the realm of West Gaurd join under one cause to defend them selves from a common foreign threat, in order to destroy the evil that would dare take away their freedom and pride of nationality and kinhood. To revive the spirit of the great Delmoncian Empire of Mt. Castbell under the divine Mother Mallhearth, the Gift Giver,&amp; The Far Father Windome. From the Northern Realm of Nar to the highest peeks of Castbell the Knights shall stay watch and guard the continent of West Gaurd with their lives and only hold homage to the Flame Holder and his priests. The capital of which is placed in the city of Belltop where the the Temple Of The Twelve is home to.
