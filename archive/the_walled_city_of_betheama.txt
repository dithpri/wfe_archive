Betheama is made up of many small United people to form Bettic. The city-region is made up of many diffrent people, animals and envirments from deserts in the south west and an icy cold winter land far up north. The main city named dorakise is named after the great dragoon that lives on the side of the mountain the city was built on. The people admire the great dragons that live here, some dragons are as know as the royal family. The city was a gift from bethea the goddess of storm light an energy she produces, the only people know to use the substance is her two sons. One the great paladin ornis and the shape shifter woods-man dorvick, both of her sons have diffrent fathers.
