Tanga

 The country has 171 islands &#150; of which 45 are inhabited. Its total surface area is about 750 km2 (290 sq mi), scattered over 700,000 km2 (270,000 sq mi) in the Southern  Ocean. As of 2024, Tanga has a population of 104,494,70% of whom reside on the main island,  The country stretches approximately 800 km (500 mi) north-south.
