The Spratly Islands are located north of insular Malaysia and are roughly midway between Vietnam and the Philippines in the South China Sea. They are claimed by the People&#39;s Republic of China, the Republic of China (Taiwan), the Philippines, Malaysia, Vietnam and Brunei.

[b]The Spratly Islands welcome settlers from Taiwan, the Philippines, Malaysia, Vietnam, Brunei and their allies. Members of the Chinese Communist Party are not welcome here.[/b]
