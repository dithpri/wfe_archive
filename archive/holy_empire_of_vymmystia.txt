The Holy Empire of Vimmystia
Population: 118 million
Government: Dictatorship
Overview: Vimmystia is a vast, barren land ruled by a corrupt dictatorship, divided into five principalities: Strugloy, Novopondia, Aelondia, Quintrillia, and Systraeket. Large corporations hold major influence, and citizens face compulsory military service with no social welfare.
Government Structure: Companies propose laws, approved by the Emperor. On the Emperor&#39;s death, the five principalities vote for a new ruler.
Economy: The economy, worth 8.85 trillion Kronor, is driven by Automobile Manufacturing, Retail, Arms, and Uranium Mining. Income disparity is extreme.
