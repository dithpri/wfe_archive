The [b]Grand Imperium of a Thousand Realms[/b], known as the [b]Grand Imperium[/b], and informally referred to as the [b]Imperium[/b] is a multi-galactic empire ruled by [b][nation=long]Greater Alpha[/nation][/b] in the name of the [b]God-Emperor[/b]. Formed after the [b]Second Great Crusade[/b], the [b]Treaty of Terra[/b] resulted in the formation of a new unified order in the galaxy group.

The Grand Imperium is a massive confederation of independent nations with varying forms of governments and ideologies under a central government de facto administered by the Imperial government of Sacrum Terra. The interests of every nations are represented through the [b]Assembly of Nations (AN)[/b] overseen by the [b]Lord Speaker of the Assembly[/b].
