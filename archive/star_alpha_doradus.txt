&amp;#945; Doradus is a variable and multiple hypergiant star of spectral class A0 in the constellation of Dorado. &amp;#945; Doradus visual magnitude is 3.27. Because of its moderate brightness, &amp;#945; Doradus is easily visible to the naked eye from locations with dark skies, while it might be barely visible, or not visible at all, from light-polluted skies.
