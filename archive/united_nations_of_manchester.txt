MANCHESTER
HISTORY: Manchester was created after terrifying earthquakes shook the earth leaving Manchester behind. Manchester is now the only landmass on earth in 2025. 
Government: Manchester has one supreme leader by the name of Elector Silver as the overall leader.
MILITARY: The MM or Manchester Military is at continental events in case of emergency. They have high powered assault weapons along with XM-125 grenades and Nano-Fiber vests, capable of surviving a grenade at close range.
