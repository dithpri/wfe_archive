Canopus, also designated as &amp;#945; Carinae (alpha Carinae), is a hypergiant star of spectral class F0 in the constellation of Carina. Canopus visual magnitude is -0.72, making it the third brightest star in the sky. Because of its brightness, Canopus is clearly visible to the naked eye when observed from locations with dark skies, and should be also quite easily visible from light polluted areas.
