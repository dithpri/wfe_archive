The [b]Genide Sphere[/b] is a hidden subspace singularity. The singularity is basically a tiny universe with its own isolated space-time-reality. It is hidden deep inside the subspace and additionally shrouded with subquantum reality-layers. Due to its isolated space-time-reality the [b]Genide Sphere[/b] is inaccessible/beyond reach for most other space-faring civilizations. 

The [b]Genide Sphere[/b] is controled by [b][nation=long+noflag]the Genides[/nation][/b].
