[b]Green Fields[/b] was an outpost of the region [region]Fort Whitehouse[/region] during 2014. Fort Whitehorse was captured by an invader force and subsequently the region ceased to exist. Green Fields was refounded on April 26th, 2018.[hr]WFE as of January 13th, 2014:
[colour=grey]A peaceful territory of fields of green grass and rolling hills with peaceful shepherd villages scattered about. This peaceful territory is controlled by Fort Whitehorse.[/colour]
