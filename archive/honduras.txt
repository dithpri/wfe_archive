[color=navy][b]Honduras[/b]
Libre, Soberana e Independiente

A democratic republic in Central America, formerly called Spanish Honduras to differentiate it from British Honduras ([region=Belize]).
Bordered by [region=Guatemala], [region=El Salvador] and Nicaragua.
The flag consists of three stripes, the top and bottom blue stripes represent the Pacific and Caribbean.
