[B]The South American Union[/b] (SAU) is a political union of democratic nations located in South America. 

The SAU capital is in Rio de Janeiro, within the [nation]New Amazonian Federation[/nation] 

Please note the Region is under reconstruction (changes are being made, factbooks are moving).
