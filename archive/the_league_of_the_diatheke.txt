The League of The Diatheke - Otherwise known as &amp;#39;The League of The Covenant&amp;#39;, is a league made up of devout nation-states united together for mutual protection and advantage.  

Diatheke being the Greek for a legal term, a &#145;covenant&#146; denoting a formal and legally binding declaration of benefits to be given by one party to another, with or without conditions attached.
