[i]Kenya, officially the [b]Republic of Kenya[/b], is a sovereign state in the African Great Lakes region of East Africa. Its capital and largest city is Nairobi. Kenya lies on the equator with the Indian Ocean to the south-east, Tanzania to the south, Uganda to the west, South Sudan to the north-west, Ethiopia to the north and Somalia to the north-east. Kenya covers 224,445 square miles and has a population of about 44 million in July 2012.[/i] &#151;Wikipedia.org
