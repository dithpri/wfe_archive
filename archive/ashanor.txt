[b]Ashanor is a continent in the world of Stella Eratica, and is the most western continent in the world. The continent is diverse and is second in landsize, only to Korasha. The region is organized into the Arganorh Continental Union (ACU), and promotes a diversity among nations and higher levels of national autonomy compared to other regions.
