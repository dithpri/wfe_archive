**Region Name:** Harmonia

**Motto:** &quot;Unity in Diversity, Harmony in Progress&quot;

**Founding Date:** March 15, 2024

**Founder:** The Constitutional Kingdom of McCutcheon Nation

**Regional Anthem:** &quot;Symphony of the Stars&quot;

---

**Overview:**
Harmonia is a vibrant and diverse region, renowned for its commitment to unity, peace, and progress. It is a sanctuary for nations that value cooperation, mutual respect, and the harmonious coexistence of different cultures and ideologies.

**Join Us:**
Harmonia welcomes all nations that share our vision of unity, harmony, and progress. Together, we can build a brighter future for all.
