A splinter region of Europe, the GCU is a collection of countries which cooperate in a mutually beneficial economic union, which respects individual national autonomy, strong economic cooperation, and powerful localized governments.
The union also acts as a military alliance, uniting nations with similar geostrategic goals to collaborate in advancing our global position.
The GCU is also anti WA, seeking to curb the power of the WA and enforce greater national autonomy. The GCU does not enforce WA laws, and sends delegates only to advocate for fewer WA rules and regulations.
