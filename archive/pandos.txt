Pandos is largely a modern tech roleplaying region. Pandos is a region comprising the seven continents of Asteria, Meropis, Hyperborea, Thule, Evora, Bretton, and Nysa. The region is incredibly diverse. The region is home to several countries, monarchies, democracies, and the like.
