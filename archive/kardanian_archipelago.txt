The Kardanian Archipelago, is a group of artificially created islands located in the North Pacific Ocean. The largest and most populated island, is known as Kavarria, with an area of 340,000 sq. Miles. Kavarria is divided by two nations, The Confederacy of Carinsda and the People&#39;s Republic of Kolgrad. These two nations have a close but unstable relationship, both fighting over territory.
