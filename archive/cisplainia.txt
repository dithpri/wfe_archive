Cisplainia is a moderately large continent. It is generally temperature with a large forest covering the north and to the far south rain forest.  In the area between lies a large plain stretching across the land. To the northwest, lies a small but notable desert. The lands have a history of colonial rule but now in the modern era have mostly broken off from the motherland forming different countries with unique melting pot cultures. There are a number of competing ideologies vying for the minds of the citizens of the continent.
