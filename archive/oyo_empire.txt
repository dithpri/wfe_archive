A Yoruba empire in West, North Central Nigeria and North of the Fon Kingdom in present day Benin. 

Ruled by the Alaafin (Emperor) Shango of [nation]Las Villas[/nation], Oba (King) to the ll&egrave; Yor&ugrave;b&aacute; (Yorubaland), Chief Priest of the Yoruba Religion, and leader of the Lucum&iacute; people.
