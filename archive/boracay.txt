[b]Boracay[/b] is a resort island in the Western Visayas region of the Philippines. It has a total land area of 3.98 square miles, and had a population of 37,802 in 2020.

Apart from its white sand beaches, Boracay is also famous for being one of the world&#39;s top destinations for relaxation. As of 2013, it was emerging among the top destinations for tranquility and nightlife.

International travel magazine [i]Travel + Leisure[/i] ranked Boracay as the Best Island in the World in 2012. In 2014, the resort island was at the top of the &quot;Best Islands in the World&quot; list published by the international magazine Cond&eacute; Nast Traveler. In 2016, Boracay headed the magazine&#39;s list of &quot;Top 10 destinations to watch.&quot; &#151;Wikipedia [hr]
