The Dysmorphian Archipelago is a chain of six islands located near the Northern Shores of the Dysmorphian Inland Sea within [region]Dysmorphia[/region]. Originally a territorial claim of [nation]Golgoglot[/nation], the islands were occupied and seceded as the nation of [nation]Lesser Compline[/nation]. [nation]Mortal Compline[/nation] is an autonomous enclave established on the Archipelago&#39;s smallest island. 

Regional authority falls under the jurisdiction of the Citizen&#39;s Polity of Island Affiliation, an organ of the Compliant government.
