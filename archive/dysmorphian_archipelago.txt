The Dysmorphian Archipelago is a large island chain located near the Northern Shores of the Dysmorphian Inland Sea, located within [region]Dysmorphia[/region]. Originally a part of [nation]Golgoglot[/nation], the islands were occupied and seceded as the nation of [nation]Lesser Compline[/nation].

Regional authority falls under the jurisdiction of the High Polity of Island Affiliation.
