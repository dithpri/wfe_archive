

By sending a telegram to Argzburg, you can be represented on the map that is updated weekly and also create alliances with the consent of the other nation you want to ally with and also declare war on neighbouring countries to take enemy territory and wipe them off the map. This mechanic not only roots players&#39; actions in a shared world but also introduces suspense: if a country wins a war, the balance of power visibly shifts. Strategic thinking becomes essential, as timing, alliances, and territorial gains unfold transparently before all participants.
