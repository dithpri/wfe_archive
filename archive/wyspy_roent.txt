The Wyspy Roent is a small archipelago of islands who work together to promote judicial equality and to protect the world from pollution.

Priorities:
1.Self preservation
2.The Natural World
3.Equality

History:
This group of islands was discovered by Kilpikonna, and has many different nations, each governing their own island. As some islands were colonized, this led many locals and other natives to revolts, creating the treaty of Richter in 1498 R.g. The treaty, rather then creating independent nations, merged the tribes and colonizers, while some islands were left entirely to the tribes, and some left to the colonizers, leaving both groups to a satisfying settlement.
