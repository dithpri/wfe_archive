The ancient explorer &amp; philosopher known to his followers as Barticuus Enlightenormutt, took his small pilgrim community to a previously unpopulated archipelago where they then split &amp; formed two shockingly different countries...
The first was the free-loving Reformed Krainbostrovok... &amp; the second was the Sinister Island Nation of Blackysta... Who knows how they can safely exist so near each other...
