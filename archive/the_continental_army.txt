After the war ended, The Thirteen Colonies formed the USA. The original United States in 1776: Connecticut, Delaware, Georgia, Maryland, Massachusetts, New Hampshire, New Jersey, New York, North Carolina, Pennsylvania, Rhode Island, South Carolina, and Virginia.

[region]The Continental Army[/region] now fights for independence from the USA for [region]National Socialist America[/region] and marshals the militias against AntiFa, Communist and Anarchist threats [/b]
