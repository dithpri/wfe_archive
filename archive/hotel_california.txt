[b]Hotel California[/b] is the title track from the Eagles&#39; album of the same name and was released as a single in February 1977. Writing credits for the song are shared by Don Felder (music), Don Henley, and Glenn Frey (lyrics). 

The song is considered the most famous recording by the band, and its long guitar coda has been voted the best guitar solo of all time by readers of Guitarist in 1998. The lyrics of the song have been given various interpretations by fans and critics alike, the Eagles themselves described the song as their &quot;interpretation of the high life in Los Angeles.&quot; &#151;Wikipedia[hr]
