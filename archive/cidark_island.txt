In an unremarkable corner of a vast ocean lies a landmass that houses an ancient group of islanders known as the Cedrians. Thousands of years ago an empire thrived on the island of Cidark. Up until recently,  the broken remnants of the old empire have squabbled amongst each other, giving rise to pretenders and various claimants to the throne. However,  a new school of thought known as Order has been proposed to heal and unify the land. Now various ideals thrive on Cidark, but can one of them actually unify the old empire?
