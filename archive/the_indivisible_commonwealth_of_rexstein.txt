World Factbook Entry: Rexstein
Official Name: The Indivisible Commonwealth of Rexstein
Government Type: Federal Constitutional Republic
Capital: The Tristate Area of Lexandra, Bergmann, and Stfuastan
Motto: &#147;Unus Populus&#148; (One People)
National Currency: Lexics 
National Language: Rexlander

Overview:

Rexstein is a nation defined by its steadfast commitment to unity and progress under the guiding principle of Unus Populus. Governed by a constitution that enshrines the values of democracy, liberty, and order, Rexstein&#146;s governmental structure reflects a balance of power among its three branches: Executive, Legislative, and Judiciary.
