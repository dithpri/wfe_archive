The Association of Bank Exporting Nations (ABEN) is a permanent, intergovernmental Association, created at the Flumenberg Conference on January18&#150;21, 2023, by Alistan, Rockhardt, Luzviminda, United Armed Farmers. ABEN&#39;s objective is to co-ordinate and unify bank policies among Member Countries, in order to secure fair and stable prices for bank producers; an efficient, economic and regular supply of bank to consuming nations; and a fair return on capital to those investing in the industry.
