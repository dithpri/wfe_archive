Nations for Peace And Security are a group of nations come together with the aim of ensuring peace and security for our member nations. We believe that peace and security is the only way to build a strong nation. Our region focuses on its member nations territorial sovereignty and we work together to provide security to all member nations. We do not believe in aggression but would only do so to protect our own. At NPS we have a common goal and our members work together to achieve that goal. So join us if you want to see your nation progress and ensure security and protection for your people.
