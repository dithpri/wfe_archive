We are the union of nations that reside in the southwestern portion of Alabama in North America. We encompass the counties of Sumter, Marengo, Choctaw, Clarke, Wilcox, Butler, Monroe, Connecuh, Escambia, Washington, Mobile, and Baldwin. 

We are a humble region devoted to God and the upholding of human rights. 

Staunch Anti-Communists, no communists allowed.
