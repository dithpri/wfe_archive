Previously the Balkan Pact, the TUAP is a band of archipelagic/island nations, similar to the EU, with free trade and immigration in-between all countries.

It is also a militaristic pact, as forces are combined together during war in one singular army.

The founder of the pact is [nation]Vasileresia[nation], a large archipelago, and Co-Founded by [nation]New Maldivian Islands[/nation] and [nation]Juphna[/nation].
