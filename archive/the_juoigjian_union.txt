A union of nations that sprung up after the crumbling of Juoigjia.

Most of the nations speak Juoigjian, but some have them have taken up their own languages. Borders are open between the nations and trade is encouraged. All citizens of the JU have the right to vote on regional and national representatives to appear in the Juoigjian senate.

To request entry, send a telegram to the glorious leader of The Empire of Zlabjia.
