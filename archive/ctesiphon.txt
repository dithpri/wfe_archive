Ctesiphon on the east bank of the Tigris was one of the great cities of ancient Iran. Ctesiphon was the capital of the Parthian and Sassanid Empires. In the sixth century Ctesiphon was the largest city in the world.

Ctesiphon rightfully belongs to the [b]Persian Empire[/b].
