A large planet that can be reached via magical portals, warps or any near impossible means by ancient Terran. Narthfel is a world inhabited by various race such as Elves, Orcs, Dwarves, Human and many more. Though currently, human are known to be the dominant species with the Dark Elves being their competitor in the domination.

Fortunately, those of space-age and Faster-Than-Light travel can now locate the planet as a Mega-Earth rotating slowly within the Narthfel System of the Andromeda Galaxy.
