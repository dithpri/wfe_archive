The Empire of Anitar has finally dissolved. Through the hard-fought resistance of everyday citizens and civic leaders, the oppressive plot of former Emperor Juan Pablo known to us as the Apretar el Pu&ntilde;o has been thwarted. Now, at the cusp of liberation, the former states of Anitar must confer on how to divide themselves. New relationships will be made. Enemies become friends and friends become enemies. In any case, the region will remain an interesting hub of international relations for decades to come as these newborn nations try their own individual takes at governing.
