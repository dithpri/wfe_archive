Year 2065, the Omega Event struck. The sun decided to go haywire, and triggered the most fatal solar flares in history, effectively destroying 60% of the world&#39;s communications and online networks, widespread fires and causing the meltdown of the world&#39;s glaciers, costing millions of lives in the process. Society began to break down, and triggered the fourth World War, resulting in a nuclear catastrophe. Earth was then invaded by the warlike Gaphans, forcing humanity to retreat and grabbed their remaining technology and resources. From 2088 and now on, humanity is rebuilding, and their homeworld was divided into two regions, one owned by the Gaphans and one where what remained of humanity, &#39;Rosebed&#39;.
