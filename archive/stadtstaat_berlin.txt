[b][color=black]Berlin is both the capital city of, and one of the 16 states of, Germany. Its existence was first documented in the 13th century, and over the next few centuries it slowly grew, becoming the capital of Brandenburg in the 15th century.[/color]

[color=red]After this, Berlin became the capital of the new Kingdom of Prussia in the early 18th century, and later the capital of the German Empire, the [i]Deutsches Kaiserreich[/i], upon its foundation in 1871 up until its end in 1918.[/color]

[color=orange]Post-WWII, it became the capital of the BRD, West Germany, and the capital of the unified Germany in 1990. It is now one of the largest cities in Europe and the capital of one of its most prosperous nations.[/color][hr][color=black][region]The Union of German Nations[/region] welcomes you to Berlin![/b][/color]
