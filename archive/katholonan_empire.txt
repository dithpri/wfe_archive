The Katholonan Empire is a monarchistic region made up of principalities and other royal extensions of Katholona.
The Empire is controlled from the homeland and capital of the empire, Katholona. All state leaders are either part of the royal family or Pro-Royal leaders placed into power. All of us from the empire welcome you and we hope you enjoy your stay here.
