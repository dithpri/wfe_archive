[b]Welcome to [region=Korkeuksista].[/b] This is a region focused on Roleplay on a [b]character[/b] and [b]nation[/b] level. Players are expected to know the rules of the region, and to adhere to them at all times. We function with a single governing entity with absolute power, with subject offices given at the discretion of the Imperator, who is held to the same rules as the citizens within.
