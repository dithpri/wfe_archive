[b]Welcome to [region=Korkeuksista].[/b] This is a region focused on Roleplay on a [b]character[/b] and [b]nation[/b] level. Players are expected to know the [urlherefor rules[ of the region, and to adhere to them at all times. We function with a single governing entity with absolute power, with subject offices given at the discretion of the Imperator, who is beholden to the rules set forth in the regional rules as well.
=====
The region is currently set in the early 18th century. Time will pass at a rate of four IC days to a single month (that is, one day is one week). [b]Time skips[/b] can be implemented through community consensus, or for the purposes of regional events.
