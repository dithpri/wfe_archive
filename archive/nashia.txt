Nashia is a region of the West that is known for its vast natural gas reserves. The area is mostly forested, with most of the cities located in the northern part of the region. The southern and eastern parts are very poor and sparsely populated. The climate is very cold and harsh, as is typical of the area. The average annual temperature is below zero degrees, and the average annual precipitation is very low, around 200 millimetres (7.9 in).
