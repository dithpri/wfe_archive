The year is 2052, A nearby space empire called the K.O.R Initiative reveals itself to the earthlings below. They live in harmony for 20 years, Until K.O.R&#39;s secret plans to assimilate the Earthling population gets revealed to the public. Earth and the K.O.R start World War 3, fighting for 10 years to the point of a ceasefire in 2063. After the war, The planet is filled with dead organic matter and dry deserts. Only a few places remain untouched by the Great war.
