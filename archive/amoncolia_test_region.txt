[b]Overview[/b]
The United Republic of Amoncolia is a [color=#679267][b]Federal Republic[/b][/color] where a diverse collection of states are united under one government. Citizens shape the Republic through elections, debate, and lawmaking, while preserving the unique culture and politics of their own states. Together, they form a [i]dynamic union[/i] built on both shared governance and local identity.
