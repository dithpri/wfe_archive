The year is 2323. After a period of rapid technological development, the Apocalypse came. Earth was reduced to a backwater in its own solar system. Yet, hope came, in the form of the ultra-technologically advanced Sistremy aliens, who gave humanity a new home and new dawn in their Solar System. What challenges await these lucky few pioneer Earthlings in their dealings with alien types of beings, and what new creation will there be? That is yours to discover.
