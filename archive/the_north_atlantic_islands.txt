Founded by British explorers in 1585. A small party of large sail ships sailed for the islands to establish colonies there in 1590. In 1605 George Elemordale and other colony leaders had the 10 Commandments used for the basic law all citizens have to follow (All colonies were Christian). Finally broke away from Britain in 1607 with minor resistance and George Elemordale was chosen as king and united all the Colonies under &quot;The Kingdom of Elemordale&quot;.
