The [b]Organization of Sovereign Nations (OSN)[/b] is a faction of independent countries, led by the [nation]United States of Atlactican[/nation]. The organization was established after the [b]Second Galactic War[/b] succeeding the ineffective [b]Coalition of Free Nations (CFN)[/b].

The main objectives of the OSN is to ensure the sovereignty of the remaining independent countries and to project influence across the galaxy.

The OSN constitutes a system of&nbsp;collective security, whereby its independent member states agree to mutual defense in response to an attack by any external party.
