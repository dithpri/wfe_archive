The Practical United Region of Tamriel is a region consisting of many different nations who are united in order to survive. This Region consists of a main land two large islands, and several small islands. The land is fertile in most of the region with the exception of Elsweyr which is barren do to deforestation by a ancient race. The region is always looking to expand and will in fact be looking for more land to add to the size of this great place. 

This is the official Region of Davis college and is open to students current past and Alumni. If not  associated with the Davis College than you must have a nation who is a member of this region as a reference.
