This project was originally started as a friendly competition, but that friendship ended explosively due to an issue unrelated to it. Now I continue it for fun.

If you wish to move card farms or puppets here, or merely want to join this region (for some reason), contact Poni Card Farm Manager via telegram, or contact me on Discord at Poni#2319.
