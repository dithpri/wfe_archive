The LoC (Lands of Ceria) is an organization on the planet of Ceria, currently comprising of Walaria along with its internal tribes.

The LoC aims to unify the planet as separation is strengthening in the West.

It was created as part of the &quot;Treaty of the Lands&quot;

Despite promoting peace, the Founder and the Presidium are locked in a Religious Cold War between Orthodoxy and Protestantism.
