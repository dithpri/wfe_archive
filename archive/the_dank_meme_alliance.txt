The Panda Zoo Method
Step One: Send a telegram to The Great Emu Republic expressing interest in being WA delegate.
Step Two: Emu dude enters your name into a poll for voting on the next weeks WA delegate, this poll goes up on Friday.
Step Three: The next Monday (at 7:30 am (give or take)) the winner will be announced.
Step Four: The winner will serve as WA delegate for the next two weeks.
Step Five: New poll goes up, you can run for two terms in a row if you are the current WA delegate. (You get to run for two terms in a row, then take a break).
Step Six: World domination.
