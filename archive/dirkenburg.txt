

 Dirkenburg is a nation full of diversity.  From the vast jungles of Grune to the arid lands of Lam. The tropical beaches in the south and the chilly Greer mountains of Shanthimalai. The customs are also highly diverse, although some rural areas tend to be more homogenous. Belief in mythology is not popular.Christians, Violets and Hindus make up about 5% of the overall population each. Other religions such as Buddhism make up less than ten percent. No belief (irreligious, agnostic and atheist) is at about %75. As for the variety of political opinion the two largest groups consider themselves either Social Democratic (33%)Independent (30%) or libertarian (28%).
