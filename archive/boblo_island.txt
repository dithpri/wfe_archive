Boblo Island is a small island in Lake Huron -- north of &amp;quot;Michigan&amp;quot;. Originally established as a resort island, and then the home of a private North American college, the island was gifted to the international community to serve as a center for diplomatic discussion and debate. The island is under the protection of [b][nation]Tessen[/nation][/b] (in the AU), but administered as an independent, non-aligned embassy state.
