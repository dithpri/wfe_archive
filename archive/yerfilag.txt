As the successor state to Gallifrey II, Yerfilag offers a glimmering new hope for the surviving nations of Gallifrey II, and for any nation that has suffered grievously under those that would promise freedom and give death. Yerfilag is a region that strives for unity, justice, strength, and prosperity.
