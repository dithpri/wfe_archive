Epsilon Pegasi (&amp;#949; Peg), also known as Enif, is a spectral class K2Ibvar star of magnitude 2.38 located in the constellation Pegasus. &amp;#949; Peg is one of the brighter stars in Pegasus and can be seen by the naked eye under dark skies or with binoculars. Pegasus is the 7th largest constellation. It&#39;s brightest star is Enif at magnitude 2.38. The boundary of the Pegasus constellation contains 19 stars that host known exoplanets.
