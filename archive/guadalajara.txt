[b]Guadalajara[/b] is the capital and largest city of the Mexican state of [region]Jalisco[/region], and the seat of the municipality of Guadalajara. The city is in the central region of [region]Jalisco[/region] in the Western-Pacific area of Mexico. With a population of 1,495,189, it is Mexico&#39;s fourth most populous municipality. The municipality is the second most densely populated in Mexico, the first being [region]Ciudad Nezahualcoyotl[/region] in the State of Mexico. &#151;Wikipedia[hr]
