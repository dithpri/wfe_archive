CONSTITUTION for AWINSO (Extract)

1. The Awinso region is ment to be a descentralized and assambleary Community of affine nations and partners, where each one relates with the founder and also freely with the other partners, in the terms of the corresponding agreements. Regional decisions are taken by consensus. This Constitution derives from the Foundation Charter of Aliah.


Constitution for Awinso &copy; Al  &copy; February 9, 2022
