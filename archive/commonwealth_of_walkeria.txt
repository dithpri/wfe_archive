The Commonwealth of Walkeria is a peaceful and prosperous region dedicated to cooperation, democracy, and mutual development among its member nations. Founded on the principles of freedom, stability, and regional friendship, Walkeria provides a welcoming home for nations of all sizes and ideologies.

The region encourages active participation in regional affairs, diplomacy, roleplay, and cultural exchange while maintaining a friendly and respectful environment for all.
