[color=blue]Polis Massa was an asteroid field located in the Subterrel sector of the galaxy&#39;s Outer Rim Territories, far from any major hyperspace lanes. 

Following the execution of Order 66, Polis Massa became a safe haven for surviving Jedi. Shortly after the lightsaber duel between Obi-Wan Kenobi and Darth Vader on [region]Mustafar[/region], Padm&eacute; Amidala gave birth to twins, Luke and Leia Skywalker, at the medical facility located on Polis Massa.[/color]
