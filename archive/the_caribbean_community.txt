[color=navy]The Caribbean Community (CARICOM) is an organization of Caribbean nations and dependencies. CARICOM&#39;s main purposes are to promote economic integration and cooperation among its members, to ensure that the benefits of integration are equitably shared, and to coordinate foreign policy.[/color][/b]
