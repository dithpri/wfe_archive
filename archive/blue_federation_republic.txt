The Dictatorship of Blue Federation republic is a very large, safe and hard-working population of millions Blue federation republicans kept under strict control by the oppressive government.

The strong Blue Federation republican economy, worth 10.8 trillion Dollars a year, is broadly diversified and led by the Arms Manufacturing industry, with significant contributions from Information Technology, Pizza Delivery, and Book Publishing. Average income is 60,772 Dollars, and evenly distributed, with the richest citizens earning only 2.3 times as much as the poorest.
