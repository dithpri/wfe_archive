After the success of the 2014 Scottish Referendum, the Former United Kingdom is desperate to hold onto its remaining territories. However, problems arise when those against independence cross the border into England in order to remain part of the nation Scotland once belonged to. Violence begins to erupt and the countries almost go to war &#150; but a sort of &amp;#39;buffer zone&amp;#39; made up of independent monarchies is established by the two. The largest of these is the Most Serene Republic of Tyne-Wear.
