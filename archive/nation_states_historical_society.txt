The NationStates Historical Society is the only group of its kind to currently exist on NS. The purpose of this group is to provide historical research, facts, resources, and counsel to any nations seeking assistance in these regards for any reason. A place for historical enthusiasts, roleplayers, advice givers, and all those who think everything was better back in their day.
