[b]The NationStates Historical Society[/b] is the only group of its kind to currently exist on NS. The purpose of this group is to provide historical research, facts, resources, and counsel to any nations seeking assistance in these regards for any reason. It also serves as a gathering place for historical enthusiasts, roleplayers, teachers, and generally anyone who thinks everything was better back in their day.
