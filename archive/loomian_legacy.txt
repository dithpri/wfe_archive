[color=purple]Loomian Legacy is a series of Roblox games currently in development by lando64000, tbradm and many other talented developers in the group Llama Train Studio. Lead Developer tbradm released the [url=youtube.com/watch?v=C1WryX4eGP4&amp;feature=youtube]Worlds Apart Trailer[/url] on December 18th, 2018 and Llama Train Studio officially announced the first installment of this series, Loomian Legacy - Veils of Shadow, one month later, on January 19th, 2019. The game was released on July 20th, 2019.[/color]
