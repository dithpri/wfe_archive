Loomian Legacy is a series of Roblox games currently in development by lando64000, tbradm and many other talented developers in the group Llama Train Studio. Lead Developer tbradm released the Worlds Apart Trailer on December 18th, 2018 and Llama Train Studio officially announced the first installment of this series, Loomian Legacy - Veils of Shadow, one month later, on January 19th, 2019. The game was released on July 20th, 2019.
