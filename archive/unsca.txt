(United Nation States of Central Afro-Eurasia)

A Coalition of nations that mostly have been forged within the midst of several civil wars, unified for a common goal, protection from outsiders. A common attitude of coexistence exists among member nations, however ,belligerents are often combated with rigorous sanctions. The region as a whole has struggled to emerge from war to create a peaceful long lasting relationship among themselves and has succeeded to certain extents.
