Formed from the rements of the Holy Empire, Which fell after a series of civil wars known as the 30 years war. One of the wars lead to the independence of severale city-states around the are of Lake Ackley, Seaward, Rainford, and Bennet. Known as the Great Lakes. Soon after, about 20 years after the end of the war, a large force came to power in the heart of the former empire, Kakoas. War waged between the City-States and Kakaos for 3 years kakaos, and there were no victors. After this war they formed a loose confederation to protect themselves, known as The Confederation of the Great Lakes.
