In a distant planet, in a galaxy far away, an ancient Communist Kingdom sat upon its piece of ground, not planet shaped, but more like a piece of floating ground, ripped out of the land.

Populated by strange and bizarre creatures called &quot;Cuties&quot;, of various species, Chicaewomia embraced an isolationist policy, only opening up to a select few other planets. Including an old adversary turned reluctant ally.
