South Asia is a large area near the Indian ocean that hosts a variety of cultures and traditions. The South Asian Association was established for the fundamental belief that peace is always the right option. Countries in the region are involved in bilateral trade, joint military exercises and maintains diplomatic relations. All members of SAAC are members of the United Nations and the World Assembly. The association is governed by the Regional Council and one member is selected to hold the presidency, who is responsible for chairing meetings of the Council. This position switches between countries every two years.
