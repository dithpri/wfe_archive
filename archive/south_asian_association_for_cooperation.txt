South Asia is a large area near the Indian ocean that hosts a variety of cultures and traditions. The South Asian Association was established for the fundamental belief that peace is always the right option. Countries in the region are involved in bilateral trade, joint military exercises and maintains diplomatic relations. All members of SAAC are members of the United Nations and the World Assembly. Each country supports the rest&#39;s bids for permanent seats on the UN security council. The largest and wealthiest member is the Republic of Federative India.
