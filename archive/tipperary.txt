[b]Tipperary[/b] is a town and a civil parish in County Tipperary, Ireland. Its population was 4,415 at the 2006 census.

The song &quot;It&#39;s a Long Way to Tipperary,&quot; which became popular among the British military as a marching song, was authored by Jack Judge, whose grandparents came from Tipperary, and Henry James &quot;Harry&quot; Williams. &#151;Wikipedia[hr]
