Sogdiana lies in the basins of the Zerafshan and Kashka Dar&amp;#39;ya Rivers, between the Syr Dar&amp;#39;ya and Amu Dar&amp;#39;ya Rivers in Kazakhstan, south of the Aral Sea. Sogdian cities were flourishing centers for trade, agriculture, and the arts.

This most easterly of the Seleucid satrapies is incorprated into [b][color=red]Bactria[/color][/b] to form a powerful Hellenistic dominion in Central Asia.
