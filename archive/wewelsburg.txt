This region was one of several headquarters for a Neo-Nazi network known most recently as [region]Nationalsozialistische Volksstaaten[/region]. It was refounded and reformed by [url=http://www.nationstates.net/region=the_red_fleet][b][color=red]The Red Fleet[/color][/b][/url]. The [region]Freedom and Justice Alliance[/region] is currently protecting it in solidarity with all [b][color=red]anti[/color]fascists[/b].

Names used by this network in order: [region]White Pride World Wide[/region], [region]Castle Wewelsburg[/region], Wewelsburg, [region]The Great National Socialist Empire[/region], [region]Grossdeutsches Reich[/region], [region]WPWW[/region], [region]National Socialist Peoples States[/region], Nationalsozialistische Volksstaaten, [region]Nazis[/region] (briefly), [region]Nazi Empire[/region] (briefly)
At times it was affiliated with the [region]National Socialist League[/region] or [region]The Greater German Reich[/region].
