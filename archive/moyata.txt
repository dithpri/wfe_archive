Welcome to Moyata formerly called Koentai, is a kingdom that has had over 18 dynasties. Koentaonese Dynasties were the 18 dynasties dating from 5862 B.C.E to 5869 B.C. Moyata speaks two languages, Koentaonese, and Moyatanese, which are Vietnamese, Japanese, and Chinese characters. Everybody and every culture is allowed to join Moyata. Our region is a friendly region where you can contribute and have fun. :)
