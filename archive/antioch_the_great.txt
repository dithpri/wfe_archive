The famous court city of Hellenistic rulers in northwestern [region]Syria[/region]. 

Its strategic geography, military colonization, economic importance, prosperous population,
and impressive beauty elevated the energetic metropolis to the title of &amp;quot;Queen of the East.&amp;quot;
