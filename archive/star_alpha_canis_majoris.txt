Sirius, also designated as &amp;#945; Canis Majoris (alpha Canis Majoris), is a multiple main-sequence star of spectral class A1 in the constellation of Canis Major. Sirius visual magnitude is -1.46. Because of its brightness, Sirius is clearly visible to the naked eye when observed from locations with dark skies, and should be also quite easily visible from light polluted areas.
