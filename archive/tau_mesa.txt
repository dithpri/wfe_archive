Tau Mesa is a nation dedicated to the welfare and rights of its people, whether in your region or another. Respect is necessary for co-operation, especially when enemies are around the corner from you.

The region is separated into 12 sectors:

Capitol - the main sector
Rosetus - the largest sector
Mymos - the second largest sector
Marina - coastal region
Arcadia - the largest by population
Septagus
Kollurtz - recently conquered area
Zeiton
Relinchas
Yarvo
Gerhtz - recently conquered area
Toros - the smallest sector
