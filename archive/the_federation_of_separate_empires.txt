The Balamican Empire is now inviting all Empires to the FSE a council of all Empires, from strong and might Imperial Legions, to small and weak Empires. There is no dictator of any sort although to keep things under control there is a Founder. All Empires have a say in what happens within the FSE. There is yet to be a founded Constitution. The set Tech Level for the FSE is Future Tech.
