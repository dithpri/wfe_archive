The Crown Federation of Recon Overseas Territories is a puppet-storage branch of [region]The Crown Federation of Recon[/region]. Storage for all secondary nations of the monarchs of the Crown Federation, nations who swear allegiances to the Crown Federation are scattered all over the world. It is governed by the 2nd Recon Empire Kingdom of Bynn.
