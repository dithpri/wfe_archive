The [b][region]Emerstarian Commonwealth[/region][/b] is an alliance between [b][nation=noflag]Emerstari[/nation][/b] and its present and former colonies. The [b][url=http://iiwiki.com/wiki/Emerstarian_Commonwealth]Emerstarian Commonwealth[/url][/b] acts as an economic and defense pact between its member-states ([url=http://iiwiki.com/wiki/Emerstarian_Commonwealth#Members]thirty-four in total[/url]).
