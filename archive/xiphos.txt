[color=#347235][b][u]A Description of Xiphos[/u][/b][/color] 
The region of Xiphos is a place where young and old roleplayers can come to create a friendly collaborative community. Xiphos is based around the concept of an easy going entry process, and a group of good roleplayers that can work together to build an interesting and thought out setting. Along with the roleplay comes a community of helpful players that find collaboration and collective story telling to be two objects of importance.
