Founded for peace and honor, Tanomara is a serene haven where nations uphold dignity, mutual respect, and principled diplomacy. Here, honor guides every alliance. The elements of the flag have meanings. Wheat stalks symbolize our commitment to harmony over conflict. The fleur de lis is a lilly flower, which symbolizes our commitment to serenity. The white symbolizes the purity in our actions. The green and blue symbolize the beauty of nature, more commonly, the woodlands and ocean. Prosperity blooms like the fleur de lis through fair cooperation and shared tranquility. Welcome to Tanomara &#151; where peace endures and honor prevails.
