[Color=Purple][b]Current Imperial Delegate: The Holy Sanctum of Tazann[/b][/color]

[Color=Green][b]The Grand Empire of Tazann[/b] is a confederacy of NationStates with a loose federal government. The Empire deems all nations outside of its own jurisdiction to be its enemies, and will treat them accordingly.

All members of the Empire are allowed to lodge complaints against other nations and regions in the local message board, which will prompt a vote on whether or not to pursue retaliation in the form of a World Assembly condemnation. 

In order to be granted an official governmental office and its respective powers, you must have endorsed the [b]Current Imperial Delegate[/b] on their nation&#146;s home page and sent them a telegram verbalizing your request.[/color]
