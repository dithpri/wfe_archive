Welcome to the New Eden Federation,

The New Eden Federation (NEF) is an economic and regulatory zone, based on capitalism and the merit of the people.

You may join the NEF as an outlying observation state (OBS) without approval. As an OBS you must meet our guidelines of freedom, restricted government, and social merit, but are not subject to and regulatory measures passed by the RA (Regional Assembly). It is important to note OBS are not voting, contributing, or receiving members of any other branches of the NEF.

To become an NEF Federal State, you must apply. You may dispatch your applications to the Executive Minister. You need only include your name and email. The Ministry of Internal affairs, and will present it to the RA for voting.
