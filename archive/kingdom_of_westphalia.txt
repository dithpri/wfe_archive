[b]The [region]Kingdom of Westphalia[/region] was created in 1807 by merging territories ceded by the Kingdom of Prussia in the Peace of Tilsit. The vassal state of the First French Empire was a member of the Confederation of the Rhine. After France lost the Battle of the Nations on 19 October 1813, the Russians dissolved the Kingdom and restored the status quo of 1806 with Prussia regaining control. Suceeded by Free State of Prussia
in 1918 and amalgamated into [region]The Germanic Reich[/region][/b]
