During the Second Era and 1100 Year of the Eternal Fire. A continent was born from the ashes of a dead world. Few remembered what had been, a world of powerful nations. But, as the first of the new nations emerged from their underground shelters they knew within their hearts that the conflict was over. How wrong they were...  Soon nations began to rise from the ashes of the slain, Megalomaniacal Dictators and terrible cults, Corporate Horrors and, Communist Regimes. The land of the slain slowly falls into a new world of ideological warfare.
