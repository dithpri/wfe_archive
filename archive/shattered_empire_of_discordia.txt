After the Revolutions of Fangaria, The Empire of Discordia lay shattered in ruins. In the ashes of the old government, new societies began to rise, starting with the Grand Duchy of the Three of Black.

As new nations arose, some tensions about who was the rightful ruler of the empire began. These claimants often had wars of influence on who would represent the Empire to the World Assembly.

Current Claimants: The Grand Duchy of the Three of Black
