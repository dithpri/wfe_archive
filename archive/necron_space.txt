The [i]Necron Space[/i] is the integrated state territory of 
[i][b][nation]The Necron Empire[/nation][/b][/i].

The [i]Necron Space[/i] extends over more than 9 billion star systems in the  galaxy. 

Furthermore the [i]Necron Space[/i] includes an artificially created extra-dimensional subspace sanctuary - basically a tiny isolated universe hidden deep inside of subspace. This subspace sanctuary is the center of the [i][b][nation=short+noflag]The Necron Empire[/nation][/b][/i] - with over 90% of the population living inside of the subspace sanctuary.
