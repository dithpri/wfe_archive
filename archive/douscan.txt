Douscan is the most eventful, and insane continent in the world. It is located in the Northeast of the planet and is the home of the three large empires, known as: The Kingdom of Victoria, the Piett Empire, and the Stalumions Realm.

There have been dozens of large scale wars on the continent, the largest being the Great Stalumions Incursion, which caused over 40 million deaths and the destruction of thousands of cities and villages.
