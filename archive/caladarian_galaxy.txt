The [b]Caladarian Galaxy[/b], also known as the [b]Imperial Galaxy[/b], the [b]Laurasian Galaxy[/b], or the [b]Galaxy[/b], is the galaxy dominated by the Laurasian Empire, the Galactic Empire that was constituted in AH 1321. The Caladarian Galaxy is a spiral galaxy 120,000 light years across, or about 37,000 parsecs (a parsec being 3.258 light years), and approximately five billion years old. A massive black hole exists at the center of the galaxy in the Central Core region. It has two hundred globular clusters, two satellite galaxies, and 69 million star systems.
