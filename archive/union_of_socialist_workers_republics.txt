The Union of Socialist Workers Republics are a group of liberal minded socialist nations, who both emphasis free market economy as well as social welfare. The Region currently has four nations-

1. Addumonia
2. Ameya Land
3. Addumon Islands
4. Enigma Islands

The region is administered by the Governing Council, consisting of the Heads of State/Governments of all nations.


As of 2013, all the four nations have been given regional responsibilities.

a. Addumonia- President of USWR
B. Ameya Land- Chief Treasurer
c. Addumon Islands- Minister of Defense
d. Enigma Islands- Minister of Justice
e. Ameya Land- Chairman of the Center of Economic Policy
