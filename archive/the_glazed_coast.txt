An obscene swath of hills and coastline filled with fascinating ruins, unique flora, and a plethora of majestic avian species. With botanical gardens, exciting sports, cuisines even the locals consider exotic, and the largest brewing industry in the world, the Glazed Coast has something to tantalize any visitor. Popular sightseeing opportunities include Baghalla, the Necropolis, the Furgig Memorial Murder Room, and that statue of Adil. You know the one.

Tourists are advised to bring umbrellas and wet weather gear, and to never go barefoot even when indoors.
