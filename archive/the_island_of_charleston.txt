[region]The Island of Charleston[/region] is an island in the Charlesiantic Ocean consisting of three regions, the [region]Maschint Alliance[/region], [region]The Free United Peoples of Kyrzman[/region] and [region]The Great Eastern Fields[/region]. Each region has its own government, however the governor holds authority as a member in the Island of Charleston Security Council.

[b]The Island of Charleston Security Council[/b]
The council consists of three nations, [nation]Greater Charles[/nation], [nation]Aarania Gomor Hainachui M Joroco Lithuan[/nation] and [nation]Southern Jamestown[/nation] each representing the three regions of the island.
