[b]- About Us -[/b]

The Southern Equatorial Council (SEC) is a young, growing region that welcomes new recruits with open arms and is committed to being a fair, fun and innovative, with elections for the presidency that take place on the 4th of every month. Please be sure to check [url=https://www.nationstates.net/page=dispatch/id=1826853]here[/url] for information on how to get involved with the elections and read our [url=https://www.nationstates.net/page=dispatch/id=1781928]constitution[/url].

[b]- Roleplay In The Region -[/b]

We are a largley roleplay oriented region, although you can take part in as many or as few of the roleplay aspects as you wish. Some information about one of our major roleplaying projects can be found [url=https://www.nationstates.net/page=dispatch/id=1824708]here[/url].
