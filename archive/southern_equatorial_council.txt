[b]- About Us -[/b]

The Southern Equatorial Council (SEC) is a young, growing region that welcomes new recruits with open arms and is committed to being a fair, fun and innovative, with elections for the presidency that take place on the 4th of every month. Please be sure to check [url=https://www.nationstates.net/page=dispatch/id=1826853]here[/url] for information on how to get involved with the elections and read our [url=https://docs.google.com/document/d/1XEfSQPotHIxQOZZteIQ4kzw5tcBPAYp7sM1YRU1Ca2Q/edit?usp=sharing]constitution[/url].

[b]- Roleplay In The Region -[/b]

We are a largely roleplay oriented region, although you can take part in as many or as few of the roleplay aspects as you wish. Information about our space agency can be found [url=https://sites.google.com/view/porro-et-sursum/]here[/url].
