On a desolate world near Aldebaran and the Hyades resides the ever changing city of Carcosa, which stands silent watch over a deep, dank, foul lake.  

Under the waters of this lake resides [b]He Who Is Not To Be Named[/b], and a handful of select nations who have somehow managed to gain his favor.  While widely different, these nations all enjoy the protection of [i]The Unspeakable One[/i]...
