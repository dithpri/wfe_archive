The Elantic region consists of a large continent, surrounded by groups of islands. Located on the northern hemisphere, it connects countries from the east and the west; over the years, it has become an ethnically diverse place to live.

It is known for being the home of many countries with strong economies with a regional currency of the Vici, and many civil rights; the quality of life is very high; in most significant cities, flights are banned from flight above them due to reasons related to the quality of life.

A truly diverse, free, and thrilling place to live; nations are more than welcome to relocate to the Elantic.
