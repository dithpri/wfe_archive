A lush rural setting that provides the perfect conditions for Agriculture, there are a few Major Metropolises that dominate the regions economic power, however each business is in the Cities are agriculture based from shipping and recieving to development of new techniques pesticides and farming etiquette. There are four major rivers that all cities are built upon and many of the outer farm communities are built off of the many small veins of the four major rivers. The entire regions is also fortified by mountains with only one passage from the north. This is Byzentini!
