Welcome to The African Republics, a region where nations fallen from the shadow of communism
can live again in a world where the west is dominating the lists for richest economies.

The African Republics is a democratic and free region, formed by the former The Africon owned by the USSR since 1946, Zakatutu and Alapanta declared independence in 1989 and left The Africon to form The African Republics in 1991, and funds more than $34 Billion in military and communication equipment.
