Tension mounts as Xjaviria and the break-away province of South Xjaviria prepare to go to war! How will the region fall?! Will they support the older and far larger Xjaviria in restoring order, or will they recognise the rights of liberty and self-determinism of South Xjaviria?! Only time will tell, as open conflict comes closer to being a reality, even as leading nations attempt to negotiate a peaceful resolution!
