The East African Federation (EAF) commonly known as East Africa, is a federal country located in East Africa. At 3.6 million square kilometers, it is Terra&#39;s sixth largest country by total area (seventh if we consider Antarctica). With a population of over 325 million, it is the fifth most populous country in Terra. The national capital is Kampala, and the most populous city is Dar es Salaam.

East Africa is a socialist democratic federal republic with three separate branches of government, including a unicameral legislature. The EAF ranks high in international measures of income equality, democratic freedom and quality of higher education. It is also an ethnically diverse nation.
