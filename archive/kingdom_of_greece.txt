The [b]Kingdom of Greece[/b] ([i]Vas&iacute;lion tis Ell&aacute;dos[/i]) was established in the Convention of London by the Great Powers (Britain, France, and Russia). It was internationally recognized in the Treaty of Constantinople, where it also secured full independence from the Ottoman Empire, marking the birth of the first fully independent Greek state since the fall of the last remnants of the Roman Empire to the Turks.
