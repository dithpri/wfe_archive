2037, while WW3 is ending between the global superpowers, some mexican states decided to leave the union and run their own governments and armies to oppose the corrupt federal government.
At the same time, the drug cartels decided to betray this federal government and claim some land for them.
10 years later, nothing has changed, while in some places the people live unafraid of all the chaos. Soldiers in the frontlines live untolds horror in this war.
