The Concert of Europe is a European treaty and alliance system established to create and maintain international peace. The Concert is officiated by the Inner Council; a govermental entity that acts a mediator, high court, and executive body to make sure the principles and laws are upheld to the highest degree. 

To be elligable to join the Inner Council, a nation must have the minumum number of civilans of 1 million, a strong economy, and fair civil rights and political freedoms. If the nation is elligable, it must submit a formal document explaining why they are the best choice to bepart of the Council. The the remaining nation will democratically vote and the top 3 will be inducted into the Council. Each nation will serve for 3 months.
