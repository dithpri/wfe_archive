Following years of bloodshed and countless wars against both revolutionaries and counter revolutionaries, the nations of this region have come together to form a realm of peace and prosperity. All nations included in this region will ensure not only the economic growth within their own nation, but also those of surrounding areas through international trade. If war is to be declared upon another nation within the region, then the member nations in the opposing alliances must too declare war on the opposition. If the war is unfavorable for a nation on either alliance then it may back out and become neutral in the affair. This does dose not exclude the nation from trade to either alliance.
