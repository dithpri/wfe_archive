A continent famously characterized by violent bellicosity, laconic iron diplomacy, and unceasing ethnic, tribal, and imperial bloodfeuds in a 3000 year cascading rise and fall of nations, peoples, and militaries. after the century long Eyvraenic Continental Deluge 1744-1972, the cauldron of nations now lies in stasis, as the warbanners of two of the continents most vociferously bellicose, militaristic, and violent states now stand atop the rest of the world, and the race to a all-ending continental bloodstorm for the ascension of a single total continental hegemonic state begins
