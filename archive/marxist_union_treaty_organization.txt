The [b][color=FB9208]Marxist Union Treaty Organization[/color][/b] is an international alliance and coalition of a military, diplomatic, and economic nature comprised of nations practicing various forms of socialist and communist systems. 

MUTO is a brotherhood; we are comrades united by commonly held principles and an unwavering commitment to the advancement of the socialist cause. We are proud to stand in solidarity and fight for the basic rights of those who continue to be the victims of exploitation by capitalist, fascist, and imperialist regimes.

 - [b][color=red]MUTO[/color][/b] was originally founded on [b]May 22nd, 2017[/b] by the nations of [color=#CB122E][b]Kuross Tarky[/b][/color] and [color=#B12D80][b]Provinci[/b][/color].
