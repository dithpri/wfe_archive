The [b][color=FB9208]Marxist Union Treaty Organization[/color][/b] is an international alliance and coalition of a military, diplomatic, and economic nature comprised of nations practicing various forms of socialist and communist systems. 

MUTO is a brotherhood; we are comrades united by a commonly held and unwavering commitment to the advancement of the socialist cause which first came into the scene over a century ago. We are proud to stand in solidarity and fight for the human rights of those  who continue to suffer exploitation by the capitalists, the imperialists, and the fascists alike.
