[i]Canes Venatici: is one of the 88 constellations designated by the International Astronomical Union (IAU). It is a small northern constellation that was created by Johannes Hevelius in the 17th century. Its name is Latin for &#39;hunting dogs&#39;, and the constellation is often depicted in illustrations as representing the dogs of Bo&ouml;tes the Herdsman, a neighboring constellation.[/i][b]Founded by the andarian starfleet on: 04/05/2025.[/b]
