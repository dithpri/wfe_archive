After a massive catastrophe in one of the regions of the
world the nations struggle to keep up and survive.

They establish [b]Concilivm Aqvilae[/b] (the Council of the Eagles)
to unite the nations and rebuild each of their exquisite homelands.

This is primarily a Slavic and Balkan region, but all other Nations
are very welcome to settle in here.
