[b]The [color=red]August Revolution[/color], or [color=red]August General Uprising[/color], was a glorious revolution of Vietnamese people under the leadership of the Viet Minh and the revolutionary [region]Ho Chi Minh[/region], who overthrew the rule of Imperial Japan and its collaborationist puppet state established in WW2, after they had overthrown the rule of French Colonial goverment over Indochina themselves.

The revolution marked the establishment of modern Vietnam, after nearly 100 years of constant foreign colonial and imperial reign, at the time was formally known The Democratic Republic of Vietnam and later as [region]The Socialist Republic of Vietnam[/region].[/b]
