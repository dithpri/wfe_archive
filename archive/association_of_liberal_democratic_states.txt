The [colour=royalblue][b]Association of Liberal Democratic States (ALDS)[/b][/colour], is a neutral organisation of states adhering to the principles of liberal parliamentary democracy. We at the ALDS are dedicated to upholding the very principles of the rule of law, enforce and safeguard all democratic and egalitarian institutions and maintain the utmost respect to each member&#39;s individual state sovereignty and affairs. The ALDS also promotes mutual co-operation between all member states in all areas of interest, particularly regarding cultural diversity, economic diplomacy and the maintenance of peace &amp; stability. We welcome all nations who are willing to respect and adhere towards such values.
