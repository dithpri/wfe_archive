The [colour=blue][b]Association of Liberal Democratic States[/b][/colour] is an organisation of nations committed to the protection of human rights, democracy and the rule of law. We also cherish the ideals of globalisation and embrace economic freedom.

Core Tenets of the ALDS:
. Non-Interventionism
. Democracy
. Rule of Law
. Economic Freedom
. Egalitarianism
. Human Rights and Liberties
