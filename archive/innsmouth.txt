Innsmouth is a city located in the heart of Lovecraft Country, Northeastern Massachusetts.

It is the nearest landfall to the undersea metropolis of [b][region]Yhanthlei[/region][/b], home of [b][nation=short]The Deep Ones[/nation][/b],
and most of its inhabitants are either Deep Ones themsevelves or related to them.

[b][region]Innsmouth[/region] is a constituent member of [region]Lovecraft Country[/region].[/b]

For more information, please contact the chief executive of [region]Lovecraft Country[/region], [nation=short]Wilbur Whateley[/nation].
