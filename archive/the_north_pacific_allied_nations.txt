[b]The North Pacific Allied Nations [i](Also known as NPAN)[/i] is a military, economic, and diplomatic alliance between nations. [/b]

NPAN Supports forward thinking ideas and the spread of Democracy. We discourage far-right nations and try to contain their spread.

NPAN is a group of 6 countries that operates as a cohesive economic and political block. This organization constitutes a system of collective defense whereby its member states agree to mutual defense in response to an attack by any external party.
