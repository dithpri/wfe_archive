Welcome to &quot;La Nacional Acuerda&quot; or LNA for short. LNA (TNA) is a collective union of 19 countries that started after the invasions of [nation]Slin Hifen[/nation]. After the invasions of Slin Hifen, the 5 nations of [nation]Unojo[/nation], Ipugao, [nation]Uunited Islands[/nation], [nation]The Stickmin Empire[/nation], and the now disgraced Rijk State ([nation]Uvalor[/nation]) formed the alliance as to defend one another and grow their power and wealth.
