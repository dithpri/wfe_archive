Mideyr is the High-Fantasy, Kingdom populated Region of Knights, Lords and Kings. Discovered by the Empire of Kyveth in the first era of the Region, Humanity has spread across the region at an alarming rate, forming new Nations and Realms within the Region ever since. Smaller Kingdoms have sprung up in the Empire&#39;s absence since the second age, such as the Kingdom of Jauld and Kingdom of Veir.
