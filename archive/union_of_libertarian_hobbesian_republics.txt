The [b]Union of Libertarian Hobbesian Republics[/b] ([b]ULHR[/b]) is a international Confederacy which believes in ethical egoism, capitalism, the government&#39;s duty to protect the citizen&#39;s best interests above all else, and which places the works of Thomas Hobbes of Malmesbury as its guiding principle in action.

Founded on September 7th, 2019 by the Democratic States of Delphinia, with the territories of Emendatacles and Veniana in support.
