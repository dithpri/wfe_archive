The [b]Sovereign Interstellar Space of [nation=short]Athretvari[/nation] ([region]SISA[/region])[/b] is a self-governing sphere of interstellar galactic space with a radius of 128 ly measured from the barycenter of the Athretvar Capital Star System. SISA has a volume occupying approximately 8,780,000 cubic light-years, and containing approximately 34,575 stellar objects.

There are approximately 1,725 stellar objects of F-, G-, and K-type hosting habitable worlds within the Sovereign Interstellar Space of Athretvari, matching the standard galactic distribution of stars hosting habitable worlds of 5%.
