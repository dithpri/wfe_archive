The Lustrum Peninsula is the ordered region of space that fights against Chaotic Regions and powers that seek to spread order-less destruction on the world. The Aim of the Valour Forces and the Central Lustrum Peninsula is to create an ever expanding domain of safe and free countries where leaders are free to do what they wish so long as the people are not oppressed.
