Organization of Democratic Nations (ODN)

Founded: 2113
Headquarters: Kings Row, Petras
Member States: 2
Official Languages: English, French, Spanish
Motto: Unity in Liberty

Overview:
The Organization of Democratic Nations (ODN) is an international alliance committed to promoting democracy, human rights, and economic cooperation among its member states. Founded in response to rising global authoritarianism, the ODN serves as a political and economic coalition aimed at strengthening democratic institutions worldwide.
