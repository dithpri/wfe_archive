[b]&amp;quot;On March 18, 1871, the people of Paris rose against a despised and detested government, and proclaimed the city independent, free, belonging to itself. The change thus accomplished began a new era in that long series of revolutions by which the peoples are marching from slavery to freedom. Under the name of [colour=red]the Paris Commune[/colour] a new idea was born, to become the starting point for future revolutions.&amp;quot;[/b]
