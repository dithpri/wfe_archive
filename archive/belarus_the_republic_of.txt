Belarus, officially the Republic of Belarus is a landlocked country in Eastern Europe. It is bordered by Russia to the East and Northwest, Ukraine to the South, Poland to the West and Latvia to the Northwest.
The Capital is Minsk.
Belarus covers 107,600km2
The population is 9.2 million
Official languages (of the region):
Belarusian and English
