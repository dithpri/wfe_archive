The Orange Order is a fraternal order of nations dedicated to the values of Unionism. Member states must retain loyalty to the British crown and commit to preserving the Protestant cultural heritage of these islands.

Motto: [u]No Surrender![/u]

[color=orange]The order takes its name from William of Orange, who as King of Great Britain defeated the deposed Catholic monarch James II in 1690 at the Battle of the Boyne.[/color]
