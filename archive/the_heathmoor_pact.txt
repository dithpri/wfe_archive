A millennia of fighting in Heathmoor has led to many cultures and nations tired of the conflict that has plagued the continent. Under this accord the Nations of Uramany, Ashfeld, and Hydrata have come to together under a common ground of that alliance must be made for peace. Every nation shall work together to create a better future, economically, militarily, and with the thought in mind that every culture has the right to there own sovereignty.

Under this we the nations of The Heathmoor Pact will conform in harmony.
