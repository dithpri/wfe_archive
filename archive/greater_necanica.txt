The region including and surrounding the Socialist States of Necanica is known as Greater Necanica. Situated between the 45th and 50th degrees north, the region experiences a temperate climate, with montane and coastal evergreen forests being the typical environment.

While centered around the eponymous Socialist States of Necanica, the economic influence of the island nation of Crawdad Town is undeniable. Both of these two nations have a productive rivalry and each vie to be the powerhouse of the region.
