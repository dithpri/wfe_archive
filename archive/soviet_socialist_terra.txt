Rising from the ashes of the old order is the Union of Soviet Socialist Terra. The USST is the sole global political, economic, and social authority in existence- a new order for the ages. 

While the USST claims global dominance, a handful of underground factions exist on the periphery of society. While these Factions remain limited in resources, their numbers continue to grow, potentially posing a serious threat to the USST in the near future...
