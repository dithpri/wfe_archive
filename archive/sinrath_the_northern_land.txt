A large land containing several large islands most are connected by ice most of the time but in the warmer seasons in islands more to the south the ice melts.  Sinrath Contains 11 islands from biggest to smallest they are: Sinrath Alpha, Sinrath Beta, Sinrath Psi, Sinrath Delta, Sinrath Epsilon, Sinrath Phi, Sinrath Theta, Sinrath Gamma, Sinrath Xi, Sinrath Omega and Sinrath Pi
