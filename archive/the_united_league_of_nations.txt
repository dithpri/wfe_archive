[region]The United League of Nations[/region] is an intergovernmental organization founded on 10 January 1920 as a result of the Paris Peace Conference that ended the First World War. In this timeline, the League of Nations survived to the present and became The United League of Nations. It is currently the first and only international organization whose principal mission is to maintain world peace. (Founded October 28, 2018)
