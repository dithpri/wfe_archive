The [b]Norwegian Sea[/b] is a marginal sea in the North Atlantic Ocean, northwest of Norway. It is located between the [region]North Sea[/region] and the Greenland Sea and adjoins the North Atlantic Ocean to the west and the Barents Sea to the northeast. In the southwest, it is separated from the Atlantic Ocean by a submarine ridge running between Iceland and the Faroe Islands. &#151;Wikipedia[hr]
