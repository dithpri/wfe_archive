Welcome to Belvolani! Belvolani is a nation made up of several noble families who are all trying to take control of the national government. Each family rules its own region and has its own agenda. Some even make pacts or alliances with one another. Will your family rule? Will your family achieve fame and power or will it be wiped out completely? The choice is up to you.
