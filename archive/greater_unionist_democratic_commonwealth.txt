A successor entity to the defunct Unionist Democratic Republics Alliance (UDRA) and the Unionist Democratic Commonwealth (UDC), the Greater Unionist Commonwealth is an alliance of republics focused on our core values of profound mutual cooperation. A union of countries that together stand strong, unbothered by the whims of greater powers, self sufficient, self reliant, mutually assisting and fighting abroad to defend the autonomy of sovereign states and the right to unite to stand against foreign pressure.
