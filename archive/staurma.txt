Pronounced &quot;staourmah&quot;, or &quot;sta-uhr-ma&quot;.

A region of mid-80s USSR and post-WW2 inspiration, that would geographically and culturally look like Balkans.
They speak a language of slavic roots, that sounds like Czech and Belarussian.
It has a ram for a symbol of its resilience, but also uses an eagle for its former monarchies.
