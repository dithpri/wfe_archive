[color=red][region]Bluhendenreich[/region][/color] is a democratic commonwealth of nations, founded in order to advance the economic interests of its three initial members. Since its inception, the Commonwealth has expanded, at its peak reaching a membership of seven countries.
The leadership of the Commonwealth rotates between its member states, with the WA Delegate its highest office, although the power to veto legislation is held by [nation]Bluhendenland[/nation]. Currently, the WA Delegate position is held by founding member [nation]Yashiaburg[/nation], elected on July 3rd, 2016. Also the Federal Minister of the Interior, [nation]Yashiaburg[/nation] represents the country in the World Assembly, and is able to manage all aspects of the [region]Bluhendenreich[/region] Commonwealth.
