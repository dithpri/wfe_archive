A confederacy of two allied countries that are very different in how they operate economically. One is a capitalist country filled with dirty capitalist pigs while the other is a glorious proletariat utopia.
Through the hatred of the systems we mutually bonded by deciding to limit the bloodshed and not go into an ideological war, but to share ideas if they line up with the culture.
Sometimes border skirmishes break out and occasionally there are standoffs that put the countries on the brink of war.
