The [region]Republic of Pirates[/region] is the name for the base or stronghold run by privateers turned pirates in [region]Nassau[/region] on [region]New Providence[/region] island in the Bahamas for about eleven years from 1706 until 1718. Although not a state or republic in a formal sense, it was governed by its own informal &amp;#39;Code of Conduct&amp;#39;. The activities of the pirates caused havoc with trade and shipping in the West Indies[hr]
