The Carolinas, a region located in NationStates, is a vibrant and diverse area that encompasses the states of North Carolina and South Carolina in the southeastern region of the United States. [b]Geography[/b]

The Carolinas region is characterized by its picturesque landscape, boasting stunning coastlines along the Atlantic Ocean, majestic mountains in the western part of the area, and lush forests scattered throughout. The region benefits from a pleasant climate, with mild winters and warm summers.[/color]
