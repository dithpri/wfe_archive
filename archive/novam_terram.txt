A relatively new region, New Land - oops, Novam Terram - is a roleplay-focused region. With an active founder and therefore guaranteed safety from the Raid/Defend game, nations large and small may flock here for a good bit of trade, fun, and fighting without a worry. Remember, the goal of the game is your entertainment!
