[color=purple]10,994 km under the sea an ancient technologically advanced civilization exists, born from the survivors of a sunken Atlantis. &amp;#129409;

In their quest to see humanity ascend to a higher plane of existence, to live eternally, via science, Trenchians took on the responsibility to better the lives of those worldwide.

A major threat to this noble quest was the rise of the Nazi party/Fascism (hate, oppression and intolerance) in the 1930&#39;s/40&#39;s.

To combat this, The MT Army was formed to keep humanity from regressing.[/color]
