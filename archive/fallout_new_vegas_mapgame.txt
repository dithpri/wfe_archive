What is this?

A mapgame/nation RP of Fallout New Vegas Factions.

Starting in 2270 the Legion rises in the east led by the Malpais Legate and the Tyrannical Caesar.
In the west stands the Bear, The New California Republic led by President Wendell Peterson and General Drommund.

Mr. House and the many factions of New Vegas stand in the middle of a rising Cold War between two titans. 

Join today and claim a nation or join the discord and join a current nation.
