The European Germanic Confederation is a confederation of European Germanic nations. Any nation in this confederation has to send aid or military forces to any nation in the confederation if they are at war or need help. If any nations in the confederation attack each other they will have to end it peacefully or be taken out of the confederation and invaded. The European Germanic Confederation was founded after a brutal civil war in Europe the civil was started when Germany and Austria tried to break away from the the Second Roman Empire the civil war killed 265,567,000+. The Germanic Liberation army  Suffered 20,000,000+ loses. The Roman Army lost 30,000,000+. The rest were civilians.
