The NLR Armed Forces were created to serve and protect its father region, Novum lunaris republicae, or the New Lunar Republic. Based on the principles of duty and bravery, the NLR Armed Forces is sworn to protect the citizens from all enemies who wish to do them harm, both foreign and domestic.

The NLR Armed Forces are based out of Remnant Base, which was named after the original region of the citizens of the NLR, is the HQ and main base of operations for the NLRAF. All new recruits, as well as any commanding officers are based here.
