    Carthag, a barren mountain range known for sparse vegetation, mining, and industrialized cities. Despite these poor conditions the technological and military breakthroughs that take place in Carthag are known to help boost the military strength of those who reside in the dusty mountain ranges. 
   People hardened by the labors that the mountains place on them, the powers that call Carthag home are known for using the environment to harden their soldiers and also take advantage of the natural defenses.
