    Terra is the major Germanic, Latin, and Slavic earth taking place in a modern-day model of earth&#39;s continental and island structure within the 1960s - Present. It takes place after the Nazi regime during the late 1930s and early 40s, yet subjecting it to lasting twenty years longer, with much of Europe wiped out and ceded into the Reichswehr. After the dissolution of the Nazi regime in the early 1960s, a great third war of the world began with multiple civil wars, prevalent especially in the southern Slavic region. The most progressive nations prevailed above all else as their citizenry looked to the extreme leaders to change the current situation.
