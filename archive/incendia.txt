Incendia is an incorporated state of the [region]Phoenix Syndicate[/region] located in the northeastern region of the Incorporated States of [nation]Libertensi[/nation]. 

Incendia is a cultural and economic powerhouse for the Syndicate, home to many artists and businesses. A leading business is a workers&#39; cooperative known as The Scoop which is renowned for its frozen yogurt flavors, ice cream cakes, and drinks.
