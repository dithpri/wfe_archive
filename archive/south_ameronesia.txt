Official Location Alteration and expansion of the Free Land Republic of Ameronesia to  the newly founded geographical constituency of South Ameronesia. 

After a hefty purchase of sparsely inhabited land from a very indebted and struggling country, the Republic of Ameronesia has founded a greater territory of its surrounding region. The name is changed and now recognized as South Ameronesia, and the territory is hereby recognized as the legitimate borders of the Free Land Republic of Ameronesia.  Those few populaces still living in the land are granted an exodus with parsonage by government funded living quarters, or a fair system of legal citizenship to Ameronesia.
