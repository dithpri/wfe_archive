The [b]Organisation for Economic Cooperation[/b] (OEC, hereafter [i]the Organisation[/i]) is a political and economic intergovernmental organization which was founded in 2019 in Urapo, Loulan.

In view of the rapid socio-economic, political, and scientific developments taking place in our current time, we, the members of the Organisation, are united and committed in an effort to develop an integrated network of sustainable economies as well as a free economic exchange facilitated by highly educated societies and improved government through enhanced cooperation. We strive to be better equipped to face the challenges of a changing world to achieve this vision.
