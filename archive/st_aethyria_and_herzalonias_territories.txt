AN ACT

TO GOVERN THE CONFEDERACIES COLONIES AND OPEN POLITICAL DIPLOMACY BETWEEN THEM

The Confederation of Saint Aethyria and Herzalonia: Overseas Territories and Dominions Act

Preamble
We, the people of the Confederate States of Saint Aethyria and Herzalonia, in recognition of our shared heritage, sovereignty, and duty to the territories and dominions under our rule, hereby enact this law to define and uphold the relationship between the Confederate States and its Overseas Territories and Dominions. This Act establishes the legal framework governing the duties and obligations of both the Confederation and its colonies
