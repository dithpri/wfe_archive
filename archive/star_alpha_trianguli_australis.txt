Atria, also designated as &amp;#945; Trianguli Australis (alpha Trianguli Australis), is a bright giant star of spectral class K2 in the constellation of Triangulum Australe. Atria visual magnitude is 1.92, making it the 41th brightest star in the sky. Because of its brightness, Atria is clearly visible to the naked eye when observed from locations with dark skies, and should be also quite easily visible from light polluted areas.
