Despite being diametrically opposed ideologically, Station 7 and Reponesa Hind nevertheless managed to find common ground and formed an alliance with one another. 

Curiously, the leaders of the two countries have never been seen in the same room at the same time. Reponesan journalists and savvy info-merchants in Station 7 both claim the the two leaders are actually the same person, but the average layman will scoff at such an absurd claim.
