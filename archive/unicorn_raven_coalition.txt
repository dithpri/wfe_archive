Despite being diametrically opposed ideologically, Station 7 and Reponesa Hind nevertheless managed to find common ground and formed a alliance with one another. 

Curiously, the leaders of the two countries have never been seen in the same room at the same time. Reponesan Journalists and savvy info-merchants in Station 7 both claim the the two leaders are actually the same person, but the average layman will usually scoff at such an absurd claim.
