The Bykland Islands are a collection of nations located not far off from the mainland and has only recently been settled upon, meaning the visuals are beautiful. Due to the sudden industrialisation of the islands, the land has become somewhat polluted and had lead to an effort to clean up the islands and make it more beautiful. The Bykland Islands holds it&#39;s more important assemblies within the republic capital-nation, Byke, where political freedom and civil rights are valued and ever-growing. 

The Bykland Islands are encouraged to adopt a Republican view and abolish any dictating decrees, however due to the political freedom in The Bykland Islands, nations are permitted to have their own beliefs and values, just nothing too extreme.
