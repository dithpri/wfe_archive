[b]Geography &amp; Symbolism[/b]

Our flag symbolizes our shared existence:

*   [color=red]&amp;#9632;[/color] **The North:** Represents the passionate, high-energy populations above the equator.
*   [color=green]&amp;#9632;[/color] **The South:** Represents the fertile, grounded populations below the equator.
*   [color=white]&amp;#9632;[/color] **Peace:** The unifying goal that allows both sides to thrive side-by-side.
*   [color=blue]&#151;[/color] **The Equator:** The blue line in our flag connects the two halves and represents the physical boundary of our home.
