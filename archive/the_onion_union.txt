World Assembly Delegate decree:

On the last day of each month, a vote is called to determine the next WAD.
The elected WAD is placed into office on the first of the next month.

The WAD is free to act however they wish in any context upon election.

If the public is displeased with how the WAD handles their office, a vote will be called for impeachment. If the vote succeeds, the member who garnered the second highest amount of votes in the last election is placed into office.

The consecutive term limit is two terms. After which, the member cannot run for office for a third consecutive term.
