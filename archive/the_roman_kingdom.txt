The City of Rome was founded in 753 BCE by Romulus, who was also the first king of the Roman Kingdom. There was a total of seven kings over a period of 244 years before the monarchy was overthrown and replaced by the Roman Republic.

The Roman Kingdom was said to have begun in 753 BCE with the first king Romulus. Each king there on after would be elected by the Senate and people of Rome. The process was quite long.
