[b]The Kingdom of Hanover[/b] [i](1814&#150;1866)[/i] was a German state established after the Napoleonic Wars and ruled by the House of Hanover. It was closely connected to Britain through a personal union with the British Crown from 1814 to 1837, ending when Queen Victoria inherited the British throne. With its capital at [b]Hanover[/b], the kingdom played an important role in northern Germany until it was annexed by Prussia after the Austro-Prussian War in 1866.
