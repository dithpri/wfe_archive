We are a peaceful alliance of nations joined together to spread the ideals of democracy and things.  Anyone who would like to join, please do so in a kindly and non-invasive way! We welcome all free nations.  

Voting for new WA representative: If a member of this region is dissatisfied with the current WA rep in any way, he/she may request a new election.  All WA delegates in this region will then endorse their optimal choice.  After the new leader elected, all WA delegates will endorse the new leader until the next election.  Elections may be called -at maximum- once a week.
