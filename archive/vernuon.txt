[b][u]Welcome to the Region of Vernuon[/u][/b]

Vernuon is sat within the ever expanding Mountains of Dalenmen and to the north of the Caretonen Ocean. All new Nations will be seeded on this map and grow as they grow with the community. This region puts an emphasis on Democracy and requires that every citizen participate in one of the two available Houses, the House of the Juris or the House of the Quorum.
