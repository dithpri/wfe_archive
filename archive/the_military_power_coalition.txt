The Military Power Coalition is a disciplined and highly organized region dedicated to military strength, strategic coordination, and collective security. Founded on the principle that peace is best preserved through preparedness, the Coalition unites nations that maintain powerful defense forces and strong industrial economies.
Member nations are expected to uphold high standards of military readiness. Joint defense protocols are activated during threats, and intelligence sharing is standard practice. While not inherently aggressive, the Coalition maintains a doctrine of overwhelming deterrence &#151; ensuring that any external challenge is met with unified resistance.
