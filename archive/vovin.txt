Vovin is a very varied region with many peninsulas. The Western area known as Dionia is filled with many lush, hilly forests with great valleys.  The central area is known as The Desert of Set, obviously a very barren are.  The North-East area is called Thesailie; its a very cold mountainous region.  The South-East is very warm and is known for its great shoreline.
