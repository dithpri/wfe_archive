[region]Hagen[/region] is the 41st-largest city in Germany, located in the federal state of [region]North Rhine Westphalia[/region]. It is located on the south eastern edge of the Ruhr area, 15 km south of Dortmund, where the rivers Lenne and Volme (met by the river Ennepe) meet the river Ruhr. 

Hagen has been an important rail junction for the southeastern Ruhr valley since the first rail line opened in 1848[/b]
