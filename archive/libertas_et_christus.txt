A league of free countries based on fundamental Christianity. Christianity forms the basis of the morality of the government and society, but the government does not establish any church or enforce Christian morality, except in the cases where it is a civil crime: One individual impeding or obstructing the rights of another. The government does not participate in religion in any way, and there is no required or state religion, Christianity simply forms the basis of morality. The people are free to have any religion, and no religion is required to possess a public office. Religious discrimination of any kind is not permitted, and any governmental interference in religious affairs will result in immediate expulsion.
