East Asian Unity is not merely an alliance but a testament to the will to overcome differences and assert the shared strength of the region. Nations that have experienced the highs and lows of history now stand together

From Mount Fuji to the Yellow River, to the Mekong Delta, the East Asian Unity preserves and promotes cultural, artistic, and philosophical values rooted in ancient traditions.
They are proof of the power of solidarity, where each nation, large or small, contributes to creating a unified and radiant East Asia! The world bows in admiration as East Asia rises!
