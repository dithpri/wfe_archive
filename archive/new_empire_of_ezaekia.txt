The New Empire of Ezaekia (N.E.E.) is a monarchy region whose lands consist of multiple islands that are linked in the gulf of Panama in Central America. The currency of the New Empire of Ezaekia is vudollars (a foreign paper bill that goes from 1-700) and udas (a foreign coin)which are used daily though gold and jelwery are used to make monthly profit. The area is 234 km squared and the capital is Charryzavesh. The official government is Monarchy. The official language is english though Kri and other foreign languages are frequently spoken and the official religion is a form of Christianity known as Kharighjianism Christianity.
