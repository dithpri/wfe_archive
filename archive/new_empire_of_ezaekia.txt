Empire of Ezaekia (or New Empire of Ezaekia) is a Native American-based empire located in Central America. It consists of four massive islands: the island of Ezaekia, the island of Ivuna, the island of Gamaeia and the island of Ravukhagoa. Its bill currency is the veshkt (vehkt plural) or vkt. and its coin is qaeia or qia. (or qaei for plural). The New Empire of Ezaekia has a total of 7 regions, 54 provinces, 1,459 cities and several overseas territories. The capital of the N.E.E. is Charryzavesh (although the empire has a total of 7 capitals with one out of the seven located in Southern Ezaekia) and its government is monarchy.
