The New Empire of Ezaekia (N.E.E.) is a monarchy region that consists of two dominion islands/overseas territories, a mainland, and many small islands that are the dominions of Ezaekia; the mainland. The emperor, Krishna Ghji, is the hegemony of the New Empire of Ezaekia; which has a growing population of 2,022,000,000. The over all Ezaekian income is about 147.5%.
