The [region]New Empire of Ezaekia[/region] (N.E.E.), commonly known as Ezaekia, is an East African-rooted empire that consists of seven regions ([nation]Ezaekia[/nation] (informally known as the &quot;mainland&quot;), [nation]South Ezaekia[/nation], [nation]Ivuna[/nation], [nation]Ravukhagoa[/nation], [nation]Gamaeia[/nation], [nation]Yalaushtra[/nation], and [nation]Qaerujha[/nation]), 54 provinces, 1,459 cities and several overseas territories. The capital is Charryzavesh and the most populous city is Qamaeidha.
