The Humble Lands is a collection of five nations that inhabit a a vibrant continent that is able to provide for all of the nations with many rivers that come down from the tall mountains that sit along the borders of the nations which has helped to quell the infighting between nations over land rights.  Border disputes often occur with each nation trying to take hold of the continent but for the sake of the citizens in each nation the attacks do not get very far. [i]Until the Fire Nation attacked.[/i] All hope was lost until the avatar who had been thought dead for centurys rose up and smote the evil fire lord bringing peace to the lands which was the start of the second age.
