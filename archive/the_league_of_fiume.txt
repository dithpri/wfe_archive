The League of Fiume (Italian: Lega di Fiume) was one of the many political experiments that took place during the Italian Regency of Carnaro period when Gabriele D&#39;Annunzio and the intellectuals that took part with him in the Fiume Endeavor attempted to establish a movement of non-aligned nations. In their plans, this league was meant to be an antithesis to the Wilsonian League of Nations, which was seen by many of Fiume&#39;s intellectuals as a mean to perpetuate a corrupt and imperialist status quo.
