Aurorium, nestled within the embrace of The Holy Empire of Valhyrion, is a region of profound spiritual significance and celestial beauty. Revered for its majestic temples, lush landscapes, and devout inhabitants, Aurorium stands as a beacon of divine guidance and enlightenment within the empire.

The motto of Aurorium is &quot;Lumina in Tenebris,&quot; which translates to &quot;Light in Darkness.&quot; This motto reflects the region&#39;s belief in the power of enlightenment to dispel ignorance and illuminate the path to divine truth.
