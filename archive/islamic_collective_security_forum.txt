
### Overview

The **Islamic Collective Security Forum (ICSF)** is a regional alliance dedicated to mutual defense, political coordination, and the preservation of sovereignty among its member states. Founded upon shared values, strategic solidarity, and respect for national independence, the Forum serves as a pillar of stability in an increasingly volatile world.
The ICSF operates on the principle that an attack on one member is considered an attack on all, ensuring unified response capabilities and coordinated defensive measures.
