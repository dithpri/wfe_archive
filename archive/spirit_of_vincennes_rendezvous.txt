Experience the American Revolution at a Living History Event!
The Spirit of Vincennes Rendezvous,
May 25-26
This US revolutionary war reenactment offers 
Authentic battle enactments four times a day during the event. Food from the era is prepared over open fires and merchant tents offer 18th century wares. All-day entertainment includes period music, children&#39;s games, story tellers, oxen demonstrations and more.
French Commons, adjacent to the George Rogers Clark National Historic Park, home of the Clark Memorial, a Greek style monument at the site where the British surrendered Fort Sackville to the US.
