[color=783f00][b]4 Privet Drive[/color][/b] was the street address of the home owned by Vernon and Petunia Dursley. They lived in this home with their son, Dudley, as well as their nephew, Harry Potter, son of Lily Potter, Petunia&#39;s late sister and Lily&#39;s late husband James Potter. 

Privet Drive lies in the village of Little Whinging, which was located in the county of Surrey, near London in the southeast of England.
