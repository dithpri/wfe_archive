The Republic of Rusvania is a democratic state located in the northern hemisphere of the Nation-States world. It has a diverse economy, with sectors including agriculture, manufacturing, and services. Rusvania has a varied landscape from rugged mountain ranges in the north to rolling hills and fertile plains in the south. The official language is Rusvanian, a Slavic language with similarities to Russian. Rusvanian culture is influenced by its Slavic and Russian heritage, with a rich tradition of folk music, dance, and art. The country is known for its hearty cuisine and is a popular destination for winter sports enthusiasts.
