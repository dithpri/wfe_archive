The American Middle Valley is the name given to the former &quot;American Midwest&quot; region following the settlement of Jaonala in the year 2022. After the disintegration of the North American East and West Coasts into their respective oceans, and the subsequent evacuation of the entire remaining nation, Jaonala was settled by the remnants of citizens from across the Midwest.
