Founded at the end of The Multiversial War, the Multiversial Peacekeeping Federation as 1 goal: unity of the entire Multiverse. Freedom, justice and peace for all.


1st Regional Law: All Member nations must act with NO violence towards each other. This includes Threats, Acts of Aggression (AoA), or anything along those lines.
2nd Regional Law:  No nation shall use Weapons of Mass Destruction as threats towards another nation of the MPF. WMD&amp;#39;s shall be considered any weapons that are Nuclear, Atomic, Chemical, Biological, or anything that may cause massive loss of Civilian Life.
