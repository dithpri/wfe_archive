[b]Tonga[/b] is an independent archipelago in the southern Pacific Ocean.
It lies about a third of the way between New Zealand and Hawaii, south of Samoa and east of Fiji.
Tonga became a British protected state under a Treaty of Friendship on 18 May 1900.
