In the year 2099, a huge object fell from the heavens, a gigantic space battleship, over 12000 meters in length. Further investigation proved that the technology that drove this ship far surpassed mankind&amp;#39;s own. And while no trace of alien beings could be found on board, the ship itself was proof that not only did life exist beyond our planet, but that it had made the stars its battlefield.  In response a united government called the Greater Galactic CoProsperity Sphere was formed. But the task of establishing a world government was by no means an easy one and pockets of intense and armed resistance erupted across the face of the world. 

It has been 100 years since the alien battleship arrived, and mankind has spread throughout the stars...
