This region is designed to so any government form can join. Recognizing the autonomy of each nation-state, the one requirement to membership is that the primary religion must be Hazentologist and must follow Hazentologist ideologies.

Hazentology is a religion founded by Hazen. As a religion, it believes in the same god as Christianity, Judaism, and Islam; however, it does not believe in an afterlife. Rather Hazentologists believe that reincarnation based on one&amp;#39;s Karma is how the afterlife is managed, and that the god lives in a parallel to our world in which he monitors human behaviors.
