The World Guard has been founded to be a helping hand in the quest for peace in all regions of NationStates.

As of the founding on April 30, 2014, there are 5 goals The World Guard will attempt to accomplish over 30 years.

1. The World Guard will have 10% of all NationStates users.

2. The World Guard will fight to maintain peace across all regions.

3. The World Guard will decimate any regions that oppose peace.

4. The World Guard will be one of the most dominant regions in NationStates.

5. The World Guard will promote peace to other nations.

Any other goals can be suggested by members of the World Guard.

We hope that you will join us in the quest for peace.

Thank You.
