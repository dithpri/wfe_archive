Call Me Lanita

Call Me Lanita is a regional community dedicated to the music, aesthetics, and artistic themes associated with Lana Del Rey.

The region welcomes fans, roleplayers, casual nations, and aesthetic worldbuilders interested in:

* Americana
* vintage glamour
* cinematic storytelling
* coastal and desert aesthetics
* music-inspired roleplay
* melancholic and nostalgic themes

Founded as a relaxed and creative social region, Call Me Lanita emphasizes community interaction, artistic expression, and collaborative worldbuilding over competitive gameplay.

New nations are always welcome.
