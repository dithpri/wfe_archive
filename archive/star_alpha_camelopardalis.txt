&amp;#945; Camelopardalis is a supergiant star of spectral class O9 in the constellation of Camelopardalis. &amp;#945; Camelopardalis visual magnitude is 4.29. Because of its moderate brightness, &amp;#945; Camelopardalis is easily visible to the naked eye from locations with dark skies, while it might be barely visible, or not visible at all, from light-polluted skies.
