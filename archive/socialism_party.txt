Motto: Solidarity, Equality, Prosperity
I. Overview
A coalition of leftist nations ranging from Democratic Socialists to Marxist-Leninists. The region prioritizes collective ownership, social justice, and mutual defense against imperialist influence.
II. Governance
System: Confederal Socialist Assembly.
Leadership: The Presidium of Five, a rotating council elected by member nations.
Legislature: Every nation holds one vote in the Regional Assembly.
III. Economy
Currency: Socialist Credit Exchange (SCE), a labor-backed credit system.
Trade: Conducted through a regional trade bloc requiring 100% unionization.
Focus: Heavy manufacturing, green energy, and universal public services.
