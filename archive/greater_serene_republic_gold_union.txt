A union based around the unification of the purity and verification of gold based currency. The gold unit is based around the ZeroZero Gold Credit which can be exchanged for a 1mm tall 1cm wide at the diameter disk of 12 carrot copper-gold mix. The purity of the disk and thus the value of the currencies based off it is verified by the regional precious resources group the primary ZeroZero exporter of valuable resources such as silver, gold, platinum, petroleum, and energy.
