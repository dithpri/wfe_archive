A simple, geographically diverse region.

It is not unheard of for citizens of the Kingdom of Rayum to flee to the Community of Gamayun in the night.

As of July 14th, 2012, the Community of Gamayun is the unofficial protectorate of the Kingdom of Rayum.

Following a swift and decisive invasion, the Matriarchy of Redpandia has ceased to exist, and has been reborn a conquered region of the Kingdom of Rayum.
