The RECC
The [region]Royal East Carp Company[/region], or RECC, is a government sponsored charter company, that government being the imperial court of [nation]TheCarpathianMountains[/nation], that&#39;s sole purpose is to generate as much wealth possible all over the world in the name of the Emperor. With such a large mission, several settlement colonies, trade colonies, and formal protectorates have been created. At the moment, the RECC has been managing several areas around the world and is the largest companies to date in modern day [nation]TheCarpathianMountains[/nation].
