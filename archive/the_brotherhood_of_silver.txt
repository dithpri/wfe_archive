The Brotherhood of Silver was formed by a small group of nations in the wake of the fourth Z-Day. The nations, devastated by the zombie onslaught, vowed to never allow their populations to be treated that way again, by any threat. Thus began the rise of the Silver, to protect the nations under their banner and destroy threats to the world.
We may be small but we are determined. We will work to make this region the best experience we can make it. At this time we will consider any newcomers for Officer and Leadership Positions. If you would like to help shape the future of a new, eager region, we may be right for you!
