The [B]Weather Trio[/B] is a term for the [B]Legendary Trio[/B] consisting of [color=#FF0000]Groudon[/color], which represents the lithosphere, [color=#0000FF]Kyogre[/color], which represents the hydrosphere and [color=#00FF00]Rayquaza[/color], which represents the atmosphere.

The [B]Tower Duo[/B] is a term for the [B]Legendary Duo[/B] consisting of [color=#FFBF00]Ho-Oh[/color], the Guardian of the Skies and [color=#808080]Lugia[/color], the Guardian of the Seas.
