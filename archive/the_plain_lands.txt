The section of Thena that is known widely for its open plains and rolling hills. This region of Thena is occupied by various Tribal nomads who settled in hundreds of years ago after the collapse of the Old Empire. This Region is currently divided up by the bigger clans such as Sombar, Avar, Bulghar, Magyar and Khazaria however most of these have flocked to the banner of the Avar Khaganate ruled by Khagan Bayan, others have joined the Alliance of independent nations, The Tolgar Clan as-well as smaller lesser known clans have remained independent. The plain lands border the Hossian Empire and for the most part divide the continent.
