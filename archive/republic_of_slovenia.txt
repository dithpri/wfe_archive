Slovenia, officially the [b]Republic of Slovenia[/b], is a nation state in southern Central Europe, located at the crossroads of main European cultural and trade routes.It is bordered by Italy to the west, Austria to the north, Hungary to the northeast, Croatia to the south and southeast, and the Adriatic Sea to the southwest. It covers 7,827 sq mi and has a population of 2.06 million. It is a parliamentary republic and a member of the United Nations, European Union, and NATO. The capital and largest city is Ljubljana. &#151;Wikipedia [hr]
