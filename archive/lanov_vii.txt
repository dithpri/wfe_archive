Lanov seven is in the Lanov System. It is the seventh planet of the system. Cala Pirata left the Planet Telrova and invited some friendly nations from other areas. They all settled on Lanov VII. At first, the barren wasteland planet was hard to tame. But after a long team effort, the planet was terraformed into a planet sprawling with life. Green Dense jungles, snowy tundra, serene forests, grassy plains, and majestic oceans are just a few of the many environmental elements of this planet. Join us on our conquest of the last frontier.
