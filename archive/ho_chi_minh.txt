[b]President Ho Chi Minh[/b], born Nguyen Sinh Cung, was a Vietnamese national hero, father of the modern Vietnam nation, who won independence for Vietnam by defeating France in First Indochina war. Uncle Ho was, first and foremost a bonafide patriot, and an accomplished socialist state and communist party leader.

He&#39;s revered by Vietnamese people for many great deeds he had done for his country and his noble virtues.
