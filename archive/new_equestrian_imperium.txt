An establishment of regional sovereignty by the Holy Empire of New Equestria. Any nation wishing to join the Imperium must provide a written charter with the reasons for wishing to join, along with a statement of affirmation to follow the laws and customs of the Empire, and accept the Emperor as Head of State. The New Equestrian Imperium includes all the colonies, territories, provinces, and independent states that accept the Emperor of the Holy Empire of New Equestria as their reigning Head of States and/or lawful ruler.
