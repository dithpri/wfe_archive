[u][B]Eidynbuh Command[/B][/u]

Eidynburh Command (ECOM) is the central combatant command of the Pictish Defence Force.

This command is charged with the defence of [nation=short]Picts[/nation], [nation]Eidynburh[/nation], and allied [nation=short]Sudweca[/nation] (supported by [nation=short]Sudwecan Defence Force[/nation]).
