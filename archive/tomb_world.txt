An entire planet covered in cyclopean funerary complexes. Here lie billions of dead representatives of various peoples, many of whom have long since ceased to exist. Day never comes here, because this planet orbits around a terrifying abyss, which many people call a black hole. And although it seems that this planet is dead - it is true only partially, because among the majestic and at the same time terrifying tombs inhabited by the undead, and sometimes there are horrifying and indescribable creatures. True living creatures rarely dare to establish colonies in this dark and eerie world, but there are those who do.
