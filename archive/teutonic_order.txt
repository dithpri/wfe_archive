The Order of Brothers of the German House of Saint Mary in Jerusalem commonly the Teutonic Order, is a German medieval military order. It was formed to aid Christians on their pilgrimages to the Holy Land and to establish hospitals. Its members have commonly been known as the Teutonic Knights, since they also served as a crusading military order in the Middle Ages. The military membership was always small, with volunteers and mercenaries augmenting the force as needed.
