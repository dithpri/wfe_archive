The Grand Empire of Alderus is a sprawling galactic empire comprised of a diverse multitude of planets and peoples. The respective leaders of each member nation serve on the Galactic Council which is chaired by the Emperor of Alderus. Here decisions regarding the future of the Empire are reached in the diplomatic arena. 
The primary focus of the Empire is currently the fortification of its galactic borders via new space stations, defensive arrays, and the pride of the Alderian Empire, the ISFA.
