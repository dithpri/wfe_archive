After the the founding of Mariniander in 1946, the nation was surrounded by much bigger nations, and it&#39;s residents were scared of being conquered by said nations. It decided it needed allies, and with the help of neighboring nations Bigbootybitches1 (this country is run by a ferocious dictator, hence the name) and Shloons, their land became known as the Moonrise Alliance. Neighboring nations are welcome to join this band of miniscule nations. However, in order to join, the trio must decide whether you are worthy to join or not.
