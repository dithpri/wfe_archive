Just as it has always been, when our memories of the past fade into twilight, a new era dawns to fill the void. A temple dictated by the labors of men and accelerated by the rhythm of machines. New technology has brought an industrial revolution and produced a new land. From this caudron of steam and sweat a vision of prosperity has emerged; harnessing the riches of the world and turning night into day. 

The advent of mechanized warfare wrought devastation like none the world had ever seen, providing a window of opportunity for some to dictate conformities as regimes spread their ideologies with a heavy hand, and yet some chose a different path and through their vision brought about a New World Order: South Osea.
