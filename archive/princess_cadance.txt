Princess Cadance, full name Princess Mi Amore Cadenza, is a Pegasus-turned-Alicorn pony who is first featured in the season two finale alongside her husband Shining Armor. She is Flurry Heart&#39;s mother, Twilight Sparkle&#39;s sister-in-law (after marrying Shining Armor) and former foal-sitter, Princess Celestia&#39;s adopted niece, and the co-ruler of the Crystal Empire alongside Shining Armor. She is also stated to be &quot;a very distant relative&quot; of Princess Amore, the former unicorn ruler of the Crystal Empire.
