An Kh&ecirc; is a mountainous area in Gia Lai Province, Vietnam, known for its historical significance and natural beauty. It played a key role during the Vietnam War, serving as a strategic location. Surrounded by lush forests and scenic hills, An Kh&ecirc; is part of the Central Highlands, offering a cool climate and rich cultural heritage tied to the local ethnic minorities. The region is also known for its agricultural activities, particularly coffee and rubber plantations.
