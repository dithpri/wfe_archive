The Nine Kingdoms of Lanmiste are a collaboration of nine Kingdoms on the continent of Lanmiste. They range from Icehelm, Northpine, and Stonecoast in the snow covered North, Grandehold is located in the centre of the continent and produces large amounts of food, Eastridge hold their gods high and are situated in the mountains to the East, The Hearthlands are where the leaders get together to discuss issues in the regional capital, Gjors is a quiet, but ruthless kingdom situated between the Hearthlands and Westerplains, which is a flat, resource abundant land to the west. The Kingdom of the Morazis Mountains is North of Westerplains, and is known for its technological prowess.
