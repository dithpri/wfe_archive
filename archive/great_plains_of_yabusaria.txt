This vast yet sparsely populated continent is homeland of the various tribal nomadic Yabusarian peoples.
Anyone wishing to move to The Great Plains of Yabusaria should contact [nation=short]Maglossia[/nation].

The Most Skilled of Riders, His Swiftness the Horse Lord, most honoured under the mighty sky god Sagittarius, will look more kindly upon those who:
1) Recognise him as first of rulers.
2) Will ride in battle with his allies or against his enemies when called upon.
3) Are nomadic peoples
4) Revere the most noble of beasts the horse.
5) Like shooting bows, especially from horse back.
