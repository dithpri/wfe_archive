As the worlds economic prosperity was challenged by outside powers, the corporatist paradise of Greater Fordlandia sought to expand its borders through economic prosperity. allying itself with two new powers in the East Pacific and one from the West Pacific, the Greater Isolation Pact was formed, where the already crumbling nations may ascend to greater heights through the bountiful nature of Isolation. If Henry Ford&#39;s world order is fully established then perhaps a new great nation may rise from the nations within the Isolation Pact, one that can and will dominate all markets, the markets left behind by the fallen that have come before.
