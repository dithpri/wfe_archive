EdgyTopia is a region comprised of many different countries that all share a representative in the World Assembly. This representative has very little political power inside of the region though, and the duty of governing goes to other positions.

Rules:
1.You can not have  2 out of 3 of either Civil rights, Economy or political rights at red levels
2.One person can have only 2 nations, one main and one side
3.Anyone in the world assembly must endorse at least one person
4.No outward hostility between fellow nations
5.All further officials will be voted in which will be started by the executive
