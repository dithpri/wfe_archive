Pleasant Valley covers an area of 3.797 million mi&sup2; (3139.0656 km&sup2; ) and is located on the shore of the Mississippi River between the Providences of Davenport and Le Claire( also owned by Pleasant Valley), it contains two cemeteries: Fenno and Pleasant Valley. Forest Grove School No. 5 and the Roswell Spencer House are listed on the National Register of Historic Places and located in the nation.
