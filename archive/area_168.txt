Restriced Area Warning

This area has been declared a restricted area by authority of the Ars Artificalis military forces in accordance with the provisions of the directive issued by the secretary of defence on 10 September 2013, pursuant to the provisions of section 20, Internal Security Act of 2013.

Unauthorized entry is prohibited. All nations herein are liable to search. Photography of the facilities and region is prohibited without specific authorization from local forces. Deadly force is authorized.
