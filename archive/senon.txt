Across the sea from the ancient continent of Tarathia, the land of [b]Senon[/b] is a sovereign state and constitutional monarchy with territories spanning the northeast of an as-yet-to-be-named continent (archaically known as Ismara and Kelaen) and the Senone Isles, as well as a significant foothold on the Tarathian mainland in southern Tarvalak.
