[b]Okinawa Island[/b] is the largest of the Okinawa Islands and the Ryukyu Islands of Japan. The island has an area of 466.01 square miles. It is roughly 400 miles south of the rest of Japan, roughly the same distance off the coast of China, and 300 miles north of Taiwan. The Greater Naha area, home to the capital (or more accurately&#151;prefectural seat) of Okinawa Prefecture on the southwestern part of Okinawa Island, has roughly 800,000 of the island&#39;s 1.3 million residents, while the city itself is home to about 320,000.

Okinawa has been a critical strategic location for the United States Armed Forces since the end of World War II. The island hosts around 26,000 US military personnel. &#151;Wikipedia[hr]
