[b]Okinawa Island[/b] is the largest of the Okinawa Islands and the [region]Ryukyu Islands[/region] of Japan in the Kyushu region. It is the smallest and least populated of the five main islands of Japan. The island is approximately 66 miles long, an average 7 miles wide, and has an area of 466 square miles. It is roughly 350 nautical miles south of the main island of Kyushu and the rest of Japan. It is 270 nautical miles northeast of Taiwan. The total population of Okinawa Island was 1,384,762 in 2009. &#151;Wikipedia [hr]
