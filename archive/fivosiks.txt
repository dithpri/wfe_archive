Fivosiks is a large land mass with very diverse geographical features. Fivosiks has long beautiful coastlines of the Flovian sea. This region has an awe inspiring coastal mountain range home to the Mattezstanians. These mountains that produce raging rivers bringing extravagant wildlife to the region. Fivosiks also has a low marsh lands behind the giant coastal range home to the Nomadic Peoples of Grimborpia. Fivosiks has a southern tropical rainforest home to The Rogue Nation of Natsirt. There is also a unforgiving interior desert inhabited by Seheutopia, and Grammaruville. 150 miles off the east coast lies the tropical island of Tetrigor.
