Alea Iacta Est is an online gaming community that grew out of a World of Warcraft guild started in early 2007 as a fan guild for The Instance Podcast. The name means, &quot;the die has been cast,&quot; and is attributed to Julius Caesar as he cross the River Rubicon in 49 BCE.

Since its founding, Alea Iacta Est has grown into a community of several thousand members spanning multiple online role playing games and game networks. Alea Iacta Est focuses on enjoying game content in a family-friendly environment built on mutual courtesy and respect.
