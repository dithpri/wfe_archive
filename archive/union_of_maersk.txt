The Union of Maersk is a communist and socialist group of states. The pact was signed in the regional district of Maersk, in Detrytov. The headquarters of the UoM is in Naprotivgrad, Ostanovka, known as the home of Communism in the CSRO. Members of the Union of Maersk consist of Ostanovka, Detrytov, East Yurviya and Zemkosel.
