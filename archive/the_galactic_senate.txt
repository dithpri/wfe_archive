The high contracting parties, in order to promote international co-operation and to achieve international peace and security,

a) By the acceptance of obligations not to resort to war,
b) By the prescription of open, just and honorable relations between nations,
c) By the firm establishment of the understandings of international law as the actual rule of conduct among Governments, and
d) By the maintenance of justice and a scrupulous respects for all treaty obligations in the dealings of organized peoples with one another,

Agree to the Covenant of the Galactic Senate.
