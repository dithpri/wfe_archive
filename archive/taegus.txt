[b]TAEGUS[/b]
[i]One shared world. No superpowers.[/i]

[hr]

Taegus is a modern-tech planet with no superpowers. When you join, your core stats &#151; [b]population, land area, and wealth[/b] &#151; are set through a tiered random draw. You choose which tier to aim for in each, but they can&#39;t all be high, and your exact numbers are rolled at random. The result is a believable, balanced world where nobody is overpowered in everything, and a little luck decides the rest.

[b]Why Taegus?[/b]
[list]
[*][b]Balanced by design.[/b] The stat system guarantees a realistic spread of nations.
[*][b]Fair and orderly.[/b] Clear, simple rules for everyone.
[*][b]Original worldbuilding.[/b] No Earthly places or languages.
[*][b]Collaborative.[/b] Canon that touches another nation needs their say-so.
[/list]
