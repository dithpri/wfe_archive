The Region of the Basiliskus used to be seven separate ones, but is now together and will never be apart. Every citizen swears this as soon as they can speak, and if broken, they suffer the consequences.

The Regional Language is Balrikadian, a dialect formed from the ancient languages of Balskalian and Piradian.

The Regional Religion is Balskinism, which is not mandatory but if you are not a Balskanist you will be the only one.

The Regional Currency is the Krun, equal to 0.17 British Pounds Sterling (GBP). The plural of Krun is Kruna.

Read more in the factbooks in the Nation of The Basiliskus.
