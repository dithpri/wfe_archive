New Alderaan is a sanctuary for nations seeking to rebuild, unite, and create a better future after the devastation of the original Alderaan. Drawing inspiration from its ideals of peace and collaboration, this region offers a platform for diplomacy, cultural exchange, and mutual support among nations.

Goal of the Region: 
To foster a cooperative environment where nations collaborate to promote peace, share resources, and address global challenges, ensuring a legacy of hope and resilience for all members. Together, we will rise from the ashes and forge a promising new chapter for our world.
