From the confluence where &amp;#39;the Old Man&amp;#39; rushes out of the mountains to join the Namantha River to form the Avestan Valley, to the fertile coast of the Taranes Delta, across rolling plains to the rocky steppes--this region has been an obvious settling location for diverse groups of people throughout its history. At one time, a single nation spanned the whole of the region, and it is this name by which it is now known.
