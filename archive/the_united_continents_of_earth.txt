    Welcome, this region is known as The United Continents of Earth, or The U.C.E for short. This region is meant for the nations that wish to promote true Democracy within their government, and that wish to attempt to survive on there own without help from the nearby nations unless in dire need of assistance. The nations here are friendly and are willing to help an ally in need, but prefer to stay out of armed conflict with other nations unless their nation (or an allies nation) is attacked first or in high risk of attack. If you wish to join our region simply contact The Tectonic Order and the access password will be sent to your nation.
