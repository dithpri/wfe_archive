Oracia is a planet located some 300 light years from Earth in the constellation of Taurus. It has a diameter of 15,670 km and two moons. Oracia can be split into two hemispheres, the Caprecian hemisphere and the Nysan hemispheres consisting of nearly 50 nations in total between them.
