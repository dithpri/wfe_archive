Izyum is a major city in the Ukrainian Kharkiv Oblast, also known as [i]Izium[/i]. It had an approximate population of 44,979 by 2022, and in the same year, the unjustified Russian invasion of Ukraine saw the city captured by Russia and later liberated by Ukraine in fierce battles.
