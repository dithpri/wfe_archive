Terraverse is a Discord Nation game. Terraverse is meant to focus more on internal politics and economics, those being a large portions of how actual nations function. Terraverse also has war and space as flushed out things as space will have a open universe for colonization when that comes. Terraverse has different eras which the players of Terraverse can improve and &quot;level up&quot; (The eras being the Stone age, Bronze age, Iron age, and so on and so far)

The Current Era of Terraverse is the Bronze Age.

The Current Year of Terraverse is 5.
