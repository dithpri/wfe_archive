Introduction
       Tiku-thu-la ka archipelago lies in the midle of Ur-Jhurha Ocean, compises of 14,000,000+ islands in brief estimate,this continent sized archipelago known for piracy,undiscovered civilization and turbulent weather anomalies, 

History 
       Many explorers from industrialized nation came but only few had survived to tell the tale, they say they been attacked by natives and also by beast they never seen before.
       The Phu-cho-lhu chu lahians named this archipelago &quot;Tiku-thu-la ka&quot; the local word for bewilderment and or fog. Piracy is common until now taking everything from livestock to women. 

       So if anyone decided to go in this archipelago they must exercise extreme precaution....
