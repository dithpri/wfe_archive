Mazdadez is a diverse tropical planet located in the Orbis local system.
Mazdadez is famous for hosting the tallest peak in the Orbis local system, much of the planet is covered in oceans and volcanic islands.

In stark contrast, a large desert encompasses the north of the planet.
Mazdadez had 73 Million Inhabitants as of the most recent census data from the United Nations of Mazdadez Organization.
