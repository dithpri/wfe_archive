The Republic of Earth is set in the year 3000.

Humanity has reached beyond the galaxy and the Confederation of Planets reigns. Today, you, too, can become an explorer. Perhaps you will settle new regions? Or is politics your thing? Whatever you wish to do here, you can, at the Republic of Earth.
