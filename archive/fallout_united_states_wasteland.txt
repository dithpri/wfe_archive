War never changes. Despite nuclear annihilation, America is still a land rich with resources and lost technology. Each territory holds some value to one of the post-apocalyptic nation states. The Enclave focuses on a United populace, the Brotherhood of Steel centers on the preservation of technology, the NCR works towards nationwide conquest, and Caesar&#39;s Legion aims for social stability through hard but practical means.
