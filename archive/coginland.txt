As the ash of nuclear warfare settled, few survivors walked free. Although defeated in spirit, these people managed to pull through their broken pasts and defend their new land. The people formed Coginland and soon broke into several nationstates. Originally the most prosperous, Tutenistan led and became the super power of Coginland. The region is now vastly inhabited and control of Coginland is a toss up between the nationstates inhabiting it.
