The [b]Red Path[/b] is the Nebula in which the Flenuan star system resides. The nebula is located in an irregular galaxy known simply as the Flenuan galaxy, named for the Flenuans, who were the first species of significant intelligence to evolve within it.

The Red Path Nebula contains the following inhabited star systems:
[nation=short+noflag]Flenua[/nation]
Syniax
Beania
Sheren
