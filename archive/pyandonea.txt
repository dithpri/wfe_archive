[b]ALERT: THE ZOMBIE VIRUS HAS REACHED PYANDONEA.

The &amp;#39;zombie virus&amp;#39;, labelled [i]Encephalopathic Necrosis[/i], is a neurodegenerative virus which results in brain death and eventual reanimation. The Pyandonean Government has declared martial law. All nations not combating the virus with cure research or extermination will face banishment until the plague is contained.
