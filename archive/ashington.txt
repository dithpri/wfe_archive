Welcome to Ashington, a region where magic and fantasy reign supreme. It comprises four distinct land masses, each with unique geography and cultural heritage. From ancient forests to towering mountains, deserts to tundras, and meadows to swamps, diverse biomes create an enchanting tapestry. Two expansive oceans provide resources and trade opportunities, while the mighty Ashen Peak, a colossal equatorial volcano, beckons with its constant billows of smoke and fire, inviting adventurers to witness its fantastical wonders
