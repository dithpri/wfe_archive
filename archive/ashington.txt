Welcome to Ashington, a world of awe-inspiring landscapes and diverse biomes. It comprises of four distinct land masses, each with unique geography and cultural heritage. From lush forests to towering mountains, deserts to tundras, and meadows to swamps, the varied biomes create an enchanting tapestry. Two expansive oceans provide abundant resources and trade opportunities, while the mighty Ashen Peak, a colossal volcano on the equator, mesmerizes with its constant billows of smoke and fire which beckons adventurous souls to witness its fiery majesty.
