The United Nations of the South Pacific (UNSP) is new region in NationStates located in (of course) the South Pacific. It welcomes amy and all non-extreme nations and states into its ranks provided they meet its membership criteria. All nations within the UNSP share a mutual defence agreement (UNSPMDA), a regional defence force (UNSPRDF), open borders, and economic treaty (UNSPFTET).

The United Nations of the South Pacific is pleased to share peaceful relations with all regions, nations, and states at this time.

This entry will be regularly updated to represent the changing nature of UNSP.
