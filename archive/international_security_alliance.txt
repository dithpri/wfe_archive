[b]The International Security Alliance[/b] is an intergovernmental military alliance established 28 July 2014. The ISA was created after the realization of the communist threat to democracy. The democratic nations realized their freedoms were threatened after Oldsenburg was pressured into allowing a soviet base and troops to be stationed in her country. The alliance was established, so that no one nation stood alone against this threat.The organization constitutes a system of collective defense. Whereby its member states agree to mutual defense in response to an attack by any external party.
