[region]The Alliance of Christian Nations[/region] [b]is a region for Christian nations only. Their are five requirements to join and stay in the ACN. One: The national religion of your nation must be Christianity. Two: your head(s) of state has to be a devout Christian. Three: All nations of the ACN must swear an oath to Jesus Christ as their Lord, Savior, and Messiah. Four: All nations of the ACN must swear an oath of loyalty to the preservation of the ACN. Five: All nations of the ACN must swear an oath to follow the ACN code of respect, honor, and courage.[/b]
