[region]The Alliance of Christian Nations[/region] is a region for Christian nations only. Their are five requirements to join the ACN. One, the national religion of your nation must be Christianity. Two, your head of state has to be a devout Christian. Three, all nations that wish to join the ACN must swear an oath against Communism and Fascism. Four, all nations that desire to join the ACN must swear an oath of loyalty to the desires, interest, and preservation of the ACN. Five, your nation must be a member of the W.A.
