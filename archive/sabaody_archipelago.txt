[region]Sabaody Archipelago[/region] is a collection of islands near the equator, well-known as a booming tourist and business hub, and the floating bubbles endemic to the native mangroves.

The collection of islands number 790,000sqm, including the surrounding seas. Due to its geography, location, and tourism sector and the resulting human movement, the nations of this region are known to be extremely diverse, and because of the relatively large distance between the furthest islands, the weather is also noted to be extremely temperamental and varied.
