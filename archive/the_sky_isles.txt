In a post-apocalyptic world, jagged peaks rise from the clouds, the Sky Isles. Gravitational anomalies have caused mountains from all over the globe to tear out their roots, stone heads free from the gripping chains of terra firma. Most humans and animals have migrated to these flying islands, providing refuge from the volcanic furnace beneath them. From the frozen peaks of Erebus to the candy-striped crowns of the Rainbow Mountains, the Sky Isles provide a place to realize the full potential of all who reside here.
