[b]The Scilly Archipelago[/b]
[hr]

The Schilly Archipelago is a massive temperate archipelago in the middle of a vast ocean. Holding more than 10,000 islands, some large and some small. The archipelago is home to [nation=Artifact] which claims sovereignty over the entire archipelago and the surrounding seas. The islands themselves are beautiful, with some being uninhabited, some are entirely metropolitan, some are industrial in nature and some are used as tourist areas with vast beaches and grand resorts. The Archipelago is home to many animals, large forests and massive mineral deposits. 

The Scilly Archipelago is truly a paradise on Earth.
