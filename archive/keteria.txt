Keteria hosts to five planets on a yellow star. With Planet Rucia recently experiencing a sudden change in climate, the surviving remnants of the Rucian population are attempting to rebuild the planet.

Democracy will slowly return to the people of Rucia, our radio station: Keterian Intergalactic Telecommunications Exhibit, or K-ITE, will feature all manner of news straight from the heart of this region itself. Habitation centers will be re-opened temporarily to provide emergency-level production for the populace.
