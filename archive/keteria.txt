Keteria hosts to five planets on a yellow star, with Rucia acting as the centre of habitation and operations seeking for a united democracy across the frontier. Although due to the actions of the Dokuhai space pirate clan over the years, only Rucia remained as the habitable planet that supports life.

Embassy requests and legislation passing to the Prime Minister of Rucia has resumed. There will be however an investigation underway into the matter regarding the Dokuhai elimination.
