Home of various conflicts, Odporzhia is currently ravaged by the Czervenian-Ostregalian war. The war started when President-General Kazimir Adzic of the CNR ordered the invasion of RDO. Almost all nations of the region are allied to the Ostregals, only Szevzja stands neutral. Nordazhia supports Czervenian claims, both militarily and diplomatically.
