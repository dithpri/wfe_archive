The Star System that contains planet Kratu and its two moons and all the other planets of the Star System. The colony of Kratu was established by a select group of 100,000 people were taken by the Ark a ship that stored them in various methods such 50,000 in cyro-stasis, another 30,000 had their memories stored and DNA samples to clone and upload the memories and another 20,000 in a close environment ring placed around the central ship with room to store 2 million people in a suburban Earth environment when the ship arrived 134 years later the 20,000 had risen to over 680,000 people before the city of Vita was established.
