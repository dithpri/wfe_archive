This is a region for those unburdened by puritanical notions of clothing and inhibition. Rather than restricting what is natural, the nations of this region mandate nudity to liberate their citizens. Without the shackles of fabric, men and women may enjoy themselves however they wish as they conduct their daily lives. All nations in this region must abide by that to remain part of the region. Sexual liberties are also highly valued, but those freedoms ultimately vary by nation.

Nations are encouraged to seek out and discover their most untamed hedonistic pursuits. Very little is not illegal.
