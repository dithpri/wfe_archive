Quevola is a federally constitutional parliamentary monarch republic in western-central Europe consisting of 12 constituent provinces, which retain limited sovereignty. Its capital city is Prauge, while it&amp;#39;s largest city is Port Rygeol. Quevola covers an area of 357,021 square kilometres (137,847 sq mi) and has a largely temperate seasonal climate. With 81.6 million inhabitants, it is the most populous European country. Quevola is a major economic and political power of the [region]The Coalition of Democratic Nations[/region], and a historic leader in many cultural, theoretical and technical fields.
