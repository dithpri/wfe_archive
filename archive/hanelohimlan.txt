The Land of Milk and Honey, the High Place, the Angel&#39;s Rest, Hanelohimlan has been called many names by the outside, but within it is simply Hanelohimlan. It is the realm of Sanguanodika I, she reigns as queen over the area but leaves the various territories within the region to control their own fate, with their rulers dictating themselves as whatever they please and controlling their own populations however they want.

But she is watching, and she will not forgive you if you cross her.
