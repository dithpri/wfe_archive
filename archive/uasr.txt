[color=red][u][b]Union of American Socialist Republics[/b][/u][/color]

The Union of American Socialist Republics is an alternate Communist version of North, Central, and South America. This is a role-playing region, so it is mandatory that your nation be Communist, failure to comply with this rule will find you mysteriously taken away from your family, never to be seen again. The UASR is a semi-active region so don&#39;t be inactive for too long or you will end up in the [b]Graveyard[/b].
