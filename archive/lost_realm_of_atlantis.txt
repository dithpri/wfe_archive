Sunlight on the blue waters, and the golden spires of Atlantis!

The Lost Realm of Atlantis is the home and dominion of its long-sundered people, both those who were scattered to the corners of the earth in our ancient downfall, and those who remained hidden, biding the ages until the stars were right for our return.

What once was, shall be again.
