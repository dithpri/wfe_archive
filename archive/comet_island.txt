During an experiment conducted by a group of scientists isolated on an island in the pacific, A set of rockets would set off towards Jupiter and transfer a fairly large comet down to earth. The plan worked, and now Comet Island is a fairly large region home to beautiful artificially grown rainforests, and a thriving tourism economy. Some people have thought about expanding the mining industry, because the comet was thought to have been made up of pure gold under the top layer of ice.
