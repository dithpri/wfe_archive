[b]The Great United Confederation [GUC] [/b] &#151; 
This is a sub-organization created to unite independent states into a military-economic Confederation, in which each autonomous state belonging to the Confederation is supported by all other participants of The GUK.

[b]The Great United Federation[/b] accepts any sovereign states into its membership, without distinction on ideology, system, market policy and more.
