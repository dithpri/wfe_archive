New Jack is the most populous city in Nation States and the center of the New Jack Metropolitan Area, one of the most populous urban agglomerations in the world. The city is referred to as New Jack Citry or the City of New Jack to distinguish it from the State of New Jack, of which it is a part. A global power city, New Jack exerts a significant impact upon commerce, finance, media, art, fashion, research, technology, education, and entertainment. New Jack is an important center for international diplomacy and has been described as the cultural capital of the world.
