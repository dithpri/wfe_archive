A capitalism, free market, wealthy region of high aristocracy and outstanding economic conditions. 

The royal sovereign of the Versailles Empire: Emperor Sullivan Napoleon I.

The legislative branch is based on several committees but are all under rule of the Emperor:
Royal Cabinet- Leaders of the following committees
Committee of Wealth- Focuses on education, finance, defense, and state.
Committee of Corporate- All top officials of the corporations within and around the region
Committee of Economics- Focuses on economic development, diplomatic agreements, and regional stock exchanges.
