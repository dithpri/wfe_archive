[color=#C10300]Founded in the 11th century by a coalition of traders and refugees, Coravezia grew from a small City State to major maritime power. The Republic is ruled by an elite class of Patricians who solidified their hold on the State. Legislative power is invested in the Grand Council while the Serene Doge serves as the head of state. The affairs of the State are managed by the State Council made up of leading politicians elected to set terms.
