A land that has not been touched in centuries. It is full of dense forests, full of many species of animals and plants. By the time humans started to plant the seeds of civilization, the water levels increased dramatically and the natural world still holds strong to this day. But something happened, something that nobody expected to happen, the natural world started to respond to the humans actions and made Nations of their own. 

There is the bee run nation of Jazzatropoli, showing their love for jazz music in its name. They were the first to start up an animal run country in these lands. They have managed to make peace with the many humans that reside here, but how long will it last?
