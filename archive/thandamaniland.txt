The region that is generally considered Thandamaniland, even though the laws are drastically different in each territory. The entire region follows the religion of Thanism and most are under the control of a supreme ruler named Than Gaines III.  The person in the position of &quot;Than&quot; has total control over Thandamaniland, THAN-Land and Thanium and is very influential in Thaniland, Than Island and Thanilandia.
