[color=red]Defensive Alliance Treaty Organization (DATO)[/color] is a coalition of nations built on [color=blue]collective security[/color]. An attack on one is an attack on all.
Members join in [color=blue]military drills[/color], share [color=blue]intel[/color], and support [color=blue]diplomatic unity[/color].
Led by the [color=blue]High Council[/color], with the [color=blue]Supreme Commander[/color], [color=blue]Grand Chancellor[/color], and [color=blue]Security Council[/color] overseeing key operations.

[color=red]DATO[/color] stands for [color=blue]peace, unity[/color], and [color=blue]strength through cooperation[/color].
