Northward is a historical and cultural-linguistic region in a location that can only be described as &amp;#39;North of pretty much everything&amp;#39; and is characterized by a loosely common ethno-cultural heritage and related languages that include (amongst others) the nations Appoldro, Daristol, Amealy and Sctht.

Northward is most famous for the Northworth, a natural light display similiar to Aurora Borealis but with unique crimson, gold and yellow colors as opposed to green, blue and purple.
