Judaica is a bilingual region in French and English. The region welcomes nations with a Jewish plurality and who are tolerant of those with otherwise marginalised sexualities, religions, genders and ethnicities. The values of Judaica emphasise international collaboration, commerce and democracy all within the context of social democracy.
