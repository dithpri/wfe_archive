The Aubreyian Union consists of four nations. North and South Aubrey, Eastern South Aubrey, and West North Aubrey. North Aubrey founded this region for only the following close regional countries to sustain stability and diplomatic solutions, avoiding past civil wars and wars between these four sovereign nations. Each country maintains it&#39;s own borders and only resides within this region. No open border travel, regional economic trade policies, or defense forces are shared regionally.
