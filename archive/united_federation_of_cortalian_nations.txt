The United Federation of Cortalian Nations (UFCN) is an organization/region that contains several nations that have spawned from the nation of [nation]Cortal[/nation]. All member nations must follow a few laws.

[b]The Laws of the United Federation of Cortalian Nations[/b]
[list][*]Never declare war on another UFCN nation.
[*]Never gather intelligence on another UFCN nation.
[*]Always attempt to trade with other UFCN nations.
[*]Allow the law enforcement of other UFCN nations to operate within your nation.[/list]

[color=red][b]WAR![/b] The UFCN has declared war on [region]The Black Riders[/region] in response to their attacks on the allies of Kennan.[/color]
