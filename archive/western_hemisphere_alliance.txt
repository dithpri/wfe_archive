[b]Regional Goal:[/b]
We as a region, strive to [b]uphold these values[/b], each as important as the others, loyalty, honor, respect, activity, courage, honesty, and equality. We are members of the Western Hemisphere Alliance and we strive to become the [b]best region[/b] in NationStates. We are accepting and active. Any nation may join us, no matter their government, race, or beliefs.
