Alnair, also designated as &amp;#945; Gruis (alpha Gruis), is a variable and double sub-giant star of spectral class B7 in the constellation of Grus. Alnair visual magnitude is 1.74, making it the 30th brightest star in the sky. Because of its brightness, Alnair is clearly visible to the naked eye when observed from locations with dark skies, and should be also quite easily visible from light polluted areas.
