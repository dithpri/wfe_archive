The Imperial Union a political, economic, and security alliance among sovereign and independent states that practice a monarchy forms of government. While states maintain sovereign authorities over their territories, the IU seeks to establish policies and approaches that enhance domestic and foreign standings of membersates.

The Imperial Union is governed by offices and institutions that maintain order over specific IU functions but collectively work towards upholding values and mandate of he the IU.
