The [region]Far East Coalition[/region] is a region that spans across the Earth. It contains many nations with a variety of ideologies, ways of life, and policies. The Far East contains nations in variation. Such as; tropical conservatives such as [nation]BofaWho[/nation], desert pious nations similar to [nation]The All-Mighty Pigeon God[/nation], cold tundra kingdoms of [nation]The Twenty-Seventh Order[/nation], and coastal confederacies as [nation]Ren Var[/nation]. Each nation is unique in its own special way, and some have come together to form alliances. Some choose to stand alone. Whatever the choice may be, you have one question to answer yourself, &quot;How will I lead my nation?&quot;.
