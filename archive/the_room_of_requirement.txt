A hosting region for the [url=https://www.nationstates.net/page=dispatch/id=1136295] Harry Potter and the Festival of Friendship[/url] event :)

This festival is an end of the year celebration with a Harry Potter theme for the NationStates community. All players in good standing are welcome to partake :) While the theme is based around J.K. Rowling&#39;s epic universe - the festival is to celebrate the end of 2018 and the beginning of a new year!

Festival attendees choose a Hogwarts House to be sorted into and then play to compete in team and individual events including trivia contests, dueling bot, battleship games, art competitions, writing competitions, New Years wishes, a Yule Ball roleplay mystery and other activites TBA!
