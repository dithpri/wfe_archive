Welcome to [b][color=#641E16]Hogsmeade Village[/b][/color], a hidden gem nestled in the Scottish Highlands and located near the renowned [url=https://www.nationstates.net/region=hogwarts_school][b][color=#641E16]Hogwarts School[/color][/url][/b]. The all-wizarding village is believed to have been founded during the 10th or 11th century by Hengist of Woodcroft. The village carries a rich history and captivating charm that has endured for centuries. 

Hogsmeade is open to visitation for students of Hogwarts who are in their third year and above.
