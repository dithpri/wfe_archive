[region]Oni[/region] is a solar system with several planets, with the most Earth-like of the 8 planets being Oni-4. [region]Oni[/region] is home to human-like beings that have lungs suited for rich oxygen environments but several other alien species are accepted. For example the Engi  are multiple nanobots combined to make a robotic being (much like cells in the human body). Most of the Nations in the [region]Oni[/region] system are on the largest continent of on Oni-4 Emarica except for [nation]Innoh[/nation] which is on the smallest continent Innoh.
