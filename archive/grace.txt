GRACE, or the Guild of Royal Allies for Cooperative Enterprise, is an organization dedicated to fostering unity and collaboration among the world&#39;s monarchies. It aims to support and promote mutual prosperity while upholding the dignity and traditions of monarchies.

We are Anti-Fascist, Anti-Capitalist, and Anti-Communist. 

The organization emphasizes the importance of standing together against these dangerous ideologies and working towards a harmonious and prosperous future.
