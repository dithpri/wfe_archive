Western Ukraine is the territory of Ukraine linked to the Kingdom of Galicia&#150;Basarabia. There is no universally accepted definition of the territory&#39;s boundaries, but the contemporary Ukrainian historical regions such as Transcarpathia, Halychyna including Pokuttia, most of Volhynia, northern Bukovina, western Podolia and Besarabia. Western Ukraine is known for its exceptional natural and cultural heritage, several sites of which are on the List of World Heritage. The city of Lviv is the main cultural center of the region and was the historical capital of the Kingdom of Galicia&#150;Basarabia.
