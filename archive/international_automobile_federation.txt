Hello, welcome to the Federation! We are made up of 2 leagues, 1 Senior and 1 Minor which are governed by the FIA whom regulate the league&#39;s in a referee type matter. Member states are countries whom hold a grand Prix and / or are staff of the Federation.

The Board Of Governors is made up of Member states and staff and they vote on matters such as player suspensions etc. The President of the Board is the President Of The Federation and is elected.
