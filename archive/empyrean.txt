[u][b]What is Empyrean?[/b][/u]
A futuristic space-based economic simulator and warfare strategy gameplay, with a sci-fi galactic theme and setting, partly inspired by Mass Effect. The game allows players to create governments and planets, along with mechanics on each planet from an economic and resource perspective. There are also interstellar corporations competing to sell their products and build their factories along with a simple yet detailed conflict system for militaries, mercenaries and outlaws.
