Kovaria Valley is a small region in the decentralized Holy Roman Empire of the 14th Century, resting at the foothills of the Alps. They are surrounded by the great Iron Mountains which protects Kovaria Valley from hostile attackers. The nations of Kovaria Valley have been granted autonomy by the Holy Roman Empire, allowing them to have national identities and geopolitics while remaining loyal to the emperor and functioning as a unit of the HRE. However, as new nations rise in the region, war seems inevitable...
