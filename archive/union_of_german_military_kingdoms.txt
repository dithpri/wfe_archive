The Union of German Military Kingdoms (UGMK) is a global alliance of sovereign states  The UGMK was founded in 2050 after the fall of most nations and the domination of the Vele&#146;s King. The Alliance&#39;s main goal is to reduce conflict amongst the nations inside of it.
The UGMK consists of 7 member states, each with its own form of government, ranging from absolute monarchies to republics to dictatorships and some colonies.
The UGMK operates as an Alliance. The UGMK has a permanent governor who serves as the president of the Region. The governor is King Douglas Washington of both Prussia and The Vele.
