The Union of German Military Kingdoms (UGMK) is a regional alliance of sovereign states that has origins that are global . The UGMK was founded in 2050 after the fall of most nations and the domination of Vele&#146;s King. The Alliance main goal is to reduce conflict amongst the nations inside of it.
The UGMK consists of 7 member states, each with its own form of government, ranging from absolute monarchies to republics to dictatorships and some colonies.
