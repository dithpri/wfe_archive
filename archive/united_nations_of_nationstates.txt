[b]Welcome![/b]

[color=grey]The[/color] [color=blue]U.N.N.[/color][color=grey] is a Roleplay region here on nationstates that is Real Life based. That means that the time is whatever the date in real life is, and the events going on in real life are also included. While there is room to wiggle and players are free to Roleplay how they please, as the region is real life based, decisions and acts should incorporate that.[/color]
