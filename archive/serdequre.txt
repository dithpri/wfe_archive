Along the banks of a sprawling river delta, entire nations gather among marshlands and fog. Their disparate peoples know only the sudden weight of a shared story invading their memories and a name for the mysterious environment place they find themselves in: Serdequre. As they begin to seek out their bearings, the miasma lifts, and the preening, singular eye of a cyclops beholds them as it emerges from the depths of the waters. The cyclops addresses every national grouping in turn.

&quot;Welcome, strangers. Your nations will find neither popularity nor authority here which will not one day escape them, but be at ease; without permanence, there is no reason not to learn from one another. Settle in until the day of the next sortition.&quot;
