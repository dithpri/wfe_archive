The States of Mist is a loose alliance of separate states and/or alliances themselves. Mostly focused on the welfare of it&#39;s own people, the governance of the States of Mist focuses on welfare, healthcare, education, and to a far extent, the environment. The States of Mist is open to all nations of Libertarian States, Extreme Democracies, Benevolent Socialist States, Benevolent Monarchies, and nations that put their people before all else.
