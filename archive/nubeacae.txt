A large continet populated by City States that operate under rules set in the Xi Jan treaty, which began the Unification Era. It was signed by both Dajelotep and Jojokles, two world leaders who would ultimately build an unbreakable alliance through a public romantic affair that helped unify both nations despite their differences in culture. It was later signed by delegates from the three Belmash nations- The Babmer, The Geshnumal, and The Losh after the Belmash-Inam War.
