The region of Boulder Dome consists of several dome cities, all connecting to the major urban capital of Boulder City. While most access to the city if cut off and only available to citizens, Boulder City and the surrounding regions keep in touch with the communities within the Boulder Dome region. Becoming a regional member is next to impossible with the strict requirements and laws pertaining to the cities and communities.
