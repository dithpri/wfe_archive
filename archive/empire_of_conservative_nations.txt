The [color=green][region]Empire of Conservative Nations[/region][/color] is a region governed by an [b][color=purple]Executive[/b][/color] and [b][color=blue]Legislative Branch[/b][/color].
The Legislative Branch is comprised of the [color=green][b]Senate[/b][/color] and the [b][color=green]House[/b][/color].
The [b][color=green]Senate[/b][/color] will be headed by the [color=brown]Vice-President[/color] and be filled with a small % selected from WA Nations.
The [b][color=green]House[/b][/color] with be headed by an elected [color=brown]Prime Minister[/color].
All new nations start as a [color=blue]Representative[/color] in the House portion of the Legislative Branch.
