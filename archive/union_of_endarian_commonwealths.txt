Pursuant the interests of the Commonwealths of Endaria, the Union of Endarian Commonwealths (UEC) is a formal body the provides an official avenue of representation and collaboration between the Commonwealths of Endaria as well as Autonomous Protectorates, Territories, and Associated States (CPTS). 

The role of the UEC is both to provide an economic, defensive, and political alliance between the CPTS, as well as a balance of power compared against the Endarian Empire.
