[b]Uva Academy[/b] is the oldest school in the Paldea region, and is located in the region&#39;s largest city, Mesagoza. It hosts classes which mostly relate to Pok&eacute;mon. The school is attended by people from various regions across a wide range of age groups. Children and adults study in the same classes together; some classes are attended with Pok&eacute;mon as well. The school faculty includes Clavell, the school director; and Jacq, the biology teacher. [i]Est. 18-Sept-2023[/i]
