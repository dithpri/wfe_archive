A microcontinent originally settled 3200 years ago with a long history of strife and prosperity. Currently consists of four different sovereignties with unique ideologies and similar but distinct ethnicities and religions. 

Currently consists of the Kingdom of Marano di Velletri, the Republic of Montformeil, the United Socialist States of Sevolo, and the Empire of Waserslohn.

The primary ethnic group of the microcontinent are known as Caigreulians, making up the majority of the Montformeilian, Waserslohnians, and Velletrians. The Sevolons are not native to the microcontinent and have distinct linguistics and cultural characteristics.
