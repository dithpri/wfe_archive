The region that used to be a part of German know as &quot;Holestein&quot; came under dispute when it declared independence from Germany and in the process, taking some of Denmark. It lead to a full scale war between Holestien and the Dannish-German alliance. Later on more states in both nations started to secede and formed Dictatorships, and later lead to the Dannish-German alliance collapsing, And a Holestien lead Union, to take its place.
