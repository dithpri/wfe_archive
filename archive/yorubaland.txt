Welcome to Yorubaland (Il&egrave; Yor&ugrave;b&aacute;). We are a cultural region of the Yoruba people in West Africa and claiming the lands that our children were forced to move to during the transatlantic slave trade. Spanning from Modern day Nigeria, Togo, and Benin there is an estimated 55 Million People who are ethnic and descended from the Yorubas, with 5.3 Million practicing the Yoruba religion.
