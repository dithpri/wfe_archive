Dominated and isolated by the Lech Union for years, with the Deutzkian secession the Lech Isles has resurfaced, allowing the world to almost rediscover the Lech Isles. Made up of a hand full of countries, most of them united under the Lech Union, the Lech Isles is a quiet and cold place where few escape the eye of the Union; but if you manage to escape you will be one of the few who can export the many rarities found here.
