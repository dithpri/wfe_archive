The Arrow Cross Party was a far-right party led by Ferenc Sz&aacute;lasi, which formed a government in Hungary known as the Government of National Unity.

They were in power from 15 October 1944 to 28 March 1945. During its short rule, ten to fifteen thousand civilians (many of whom were Jews and Romani) were murdered outright, and 80,000 people were deported from Hungary to various concentration camps in Austria
