Yasser League (Yasserian Language: Jarmea Li Yasser) , formally the League of Yasser States, is a regional organization of Yasserian states in and around Yasser Archipegio in Indian Ocean. It was formed in Cabir Island on 12 April 2018 with six members: the United States of United States of Yasser, the Free Land of Yasserryan, Theocracy of Yasser, Colony of Random Yasser, Confederecy of Yasserrich and People&#39;s Republic of Real Yasser.
