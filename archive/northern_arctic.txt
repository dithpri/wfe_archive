The Northern Nordic region is a neutral region in the North of Europe that is within the Arctic circle boundaries.

The region borders Finland, Norway, Sweden, Denmark (due to Greenland), Iceland, Canada, The United States (due to the State of Alaska), and the Russian Federation to the south.

The region has a very Nordic/Scandinavian influenced culture, as well as influence from native peoples of the areas, such as the Inuit tribes.

The region consists of two countries: 

The Grand Duchy of Ardandravek 

The Kingdom of New Vaur Biluvon
