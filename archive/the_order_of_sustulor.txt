The Order of Sustulor strives to spread a culture of virtue and chivalry trough the world. Following the example set by the order&#39;s founder Dux Aedificans Sustulor II of Fatumnia, who was a well known optimist, the order aims to bring a new kind of chivalry, based on optimism and gratitude. Nation-Members of the Order protect and point out the many beauties of the world and conserve what is good.
