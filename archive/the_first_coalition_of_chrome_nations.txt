The First Coalition of Chrome Nations is a small, close-knit organization of allied nations. The Coalition promotes freedom of speech and religion, civil liberties, and the advancement of the people as whole. The Coalition is also strongly opposed to tyranny and general injustices toward mankind. Any members of the Coalition suspected to be committing or overlooking any crimes against humanity will be subject to investigation.

The First Coalition of Chrome Nations wishes to represent the nations who promote the advancement of science, art, philosophy, and culture as a whole; the nations who are of the &#39;chrome&#39; standard.
