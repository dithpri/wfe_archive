The Alliance of Independent States is a military alliance founded by Joshavia, Joshavian Protectorates, and the Republic of Alofall. The alliance was founded on March 6th, 2013. The headquarters of the alliance is located in New Joshograd (capital of the People&amp;#39;s Republic of Joshavia), and is based upon the principles of collective defense. An attack on one member, is an attack on all. 

The A.O.I.S. is willing to establish embassies with any region regardless of ideology however we are pre domintatley left-wing Socialist. No ideological alignment is needed to join either.
