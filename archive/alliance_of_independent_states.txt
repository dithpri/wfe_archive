The Alliance of Independent States is a military alliance founded by Joshavia, Joshavian Protectorates, and the Republic of Alofall. The alliance was founded on March 6th, 2013. The headquarters of the alliance is located in Ossetia (Capital of the Democratic Republic of Joshavia), and is based upon the principles of collective defense. An attack on one member, is an attack on all. 

The A.O.I.S. is willing to establish embassies with any region regardless of ideology however we are pre domintatley left-wing libertarian. No ideological alignment is needed to join either. 

The A.O.I.S. is also currently committed to stopping the spread of Fascism/Nazism.
