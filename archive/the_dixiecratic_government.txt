[b]The&nbsp;States&#39; Rights Democratic Party[/b], a segregationist&nbsp;political party in the [region]United States[/region] originated in 1948 as a breakaway faction of the&nbsp;[region]Democratic Party[/region]&nbsp;determined to protect&nbsp;states&#39; rights&nbsp;to legislate&nbsp;racial segregation&nbsp;from what its members regarded as an oppressive federal government. 
Supporters assumed control of the state Democratic parties in part or in full in several Southern states. The Party opposes&nbsp;racial integration&nbsp;and wanted to retain&nbsp;Jim Crow laws.
