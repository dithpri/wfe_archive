[color=lightblue][b]Back in 1999, jam band[/color] [color=lightgreen]The Disco Biscuits[/color][color=lightblue], were tired of playing opening slots so they created their own festival and curated the lineup with like-minded artists. Now nearly two decades later, Camp Bisco is a highly anticipated event on the east coast. Now held at Montage Mountain in Pennsylvania, with some of the best in the modern-day jam and electronic scene.[/color][/b]
