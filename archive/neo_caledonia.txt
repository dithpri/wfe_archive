Welcome to the Federal Parliamentary Planetary Republic of Neo-Caledonia.

Neo-Caledonia is a small terraformed planet located in the Xyros system roughly 20 light years from Earth and 16 light years from Alpha Centauri. Founded as a human colony of Irish and Scottish migrants seeking a better life, the planet declared independence in 2154. There are approx. 23,000 human citizens on the planet. Other notable plates in the system include Xyros IV the first all-planet national park. And Xyros VI, a large emerald green/blus gass giant. A medium sized space station also opperates in orbit around Xyros VI jointly run between the WA and Neo-Caledonia.
