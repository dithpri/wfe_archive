Men and Women of New-Caledonia:

Seeking a new future for her people, and recalling the struggle for self-determination of her forefathers. Beliving in the right of all nations, no matter how small or great, to national liber the provisional Government of Neo-Caledonia hereby declares the foundation of a New Federal Parliamentary Planetary Republic, encomapsing the whole of the territory of Neo-Caledonia and her surrounding space. We unambiguously declare Neo-Caledonia as a Free Sovereign Independent Republic.

Signed on behalf of the Provisional Government...
