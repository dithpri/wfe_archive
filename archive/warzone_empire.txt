[b][color=#dd7200]THE WARZONE EMPIRE - [i]ORDER, SAFETY, REBIRTH[/i][/color][/b]

For over a decade the Warzones have been divided and weak. Pushed over regularly by hoards of raiders, defenders, and independent regions for training or out of mere boredom. Governments of each Warzone regularly crumble and all memory of their existence washed away with one brush - with honor and accolade given to the person who can manage to hold together a reign the longest.

From this pit of chaos and instability we can build something great. We will use the forces that have worked against us to harden our resolve.  The Warzones will fight back and protect our homes. 

2018 will be the year of the Warzone - join us now as we prepare our move to the Warzones!
