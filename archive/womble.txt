This region hosts an organization of puppets to collect all Womble cards in existence. We are a colony of [region]The Dark Dragondom[/region].

I introduce to you Project Womble! THis region has been created with 16 nations therein, each dedicated to a certain subset of all cards with Womble in the name. Each numbered nation contains which Womble cards it shall contain. All remaining cards go to the founding nation [nation]Wombling Thunder[/nation]. This is a long-term S2 project. All donations to the correct nations are welcome! However, duplicate cards will be junked.
