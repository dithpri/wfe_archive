[color=navy][b]Welcome to Beringia, the Last Frontier![/b]

Beringia encompasses the lands of Alaska, Siberia, and islands lying in the Bering Strait. Named after the the prehistoric land bridge between Eurasia and North America, the Beringian region is characterized by its cold climate and inhospitable terrain. Despite this, Beringia welcomes all who call this place home![/color]
