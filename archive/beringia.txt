The region of Beringia, named after the prehistoric region of the same name and location, encompassing the lands of Alaska, Siberia, and islands lying in the Bering Strait.

Founded by [nation]Unified States of Russo America[/nation], Berignia was then inhabited by a small number of mostly friendly states, then almost immediately set upon by the zombie hordes of the Zombie Outbreak of 2013.  Beringia has had a mostly stable existence, with exceptions being the Ejection Conflict of 2013, and Invasion of the Black Riders, which occurred later that same day.
