Western Europe is the region comprising the western part of Europe. Below, some different geographic, geopolitical and cultural definitions of the term are outlined.

Western Europe&#39;s significant historical events include the time of the Roman Republic (including the Punic, Gallic an Civil Wars fought by Rome), the reign of Augustus, the spreading of The Gospel under St Paul, Pax Romana, Fall of the Western Roman Empire reign of Charlemange, the Viking Invasions, the Black Death, the Renaissance, the Age of Discovery, the Protestant Reformation as well as the Counter-Reformation of the Catholic Church, the Age of Enlightenment, the French Revolution, Industrial Revolution, the two World Wars and the Cold War.
