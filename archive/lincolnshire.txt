[b]Lincolnshire[/b] (abbreviated [b]Lincs[/b].) is a ceremonial county in the [b]East Midlands and Yorkshire and the Humber regions of England[/b]. The county town is the [b]city of Lincoln[/b]. [b]Lincolnshire[/b] is the second largest county in [b]England[/b] (not [b]the UK[/b] entirely) after [b]North Yorkshire[/b].
