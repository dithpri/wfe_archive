The Triangluar Zone is an all including region neatly position in the center of the known world.
The region has Nations supporting both far left-wing goverments as well as the extreme right-wing goverments. This extreme variations in social and political values should make the region rather unstable, but the magical aura of The Equestrian Kingdom of MB prevents such acts of hate and intolerance to occur.

The region also has an extreme rate of double and tripple rainbows occuring.
