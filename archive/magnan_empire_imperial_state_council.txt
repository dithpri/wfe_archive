Founded: 3 March 1842 

Type: Supranational Imperial Governing Council

Seat of Council: Aurelios Palace, Sancta Octavia (Imperial Capital)

Official Languages: High Magnan Latin (official), regional court languages recognised

The Magnan Imperial State Council (MISC) is the supreme governing and coordinating body of the Magnan Empire&#146;s constituent realms. It unites sovereign monarchs and ruling authorities under a single imperial framework, balancing regional autonomy with overarching imperial authority.

Formed during the 19th-century consolidation of maritime powers around the Magnan Sea, the Council ensures unity in defense, trade, and diplomacy while preserving the cultural and political identity of each member state.
