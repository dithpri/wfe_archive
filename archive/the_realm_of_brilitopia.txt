The Realm of Brilitopia is a collection of states including the Empire of Brilitopia seeking a fair and prosperous world through the advancement of Western Civilisation and the culture shared by all nations in the realm. All nations in the realm of Brilitopia are committed to the forward defence of their region and its safety from external threats. There is a regional commitment to the sciences, secularism, rationalism, liberty and the wealth of the people. 

Propter imperium Brilitopia.
