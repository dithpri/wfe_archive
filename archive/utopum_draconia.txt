A magical, unknown land divided by three fierce and powerful dragon nations, each unique from the other two. Although the dragon lands began under the joined rule of a strict dictator and suspected madman known as the Architect, Creavan, the Land of Draconia, and the Free Dragon Republic fought against his rule and were eventually expunged, taking all of their inhabitants to a massive, unexplored island. The powerful groups divided the land into three parts, and continue to live peacefully, save for the occasional accidental forest fires...
