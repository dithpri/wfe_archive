Eientei (&amp;#27704;&amp;#36960;&amp;#20141; lit. &quot;House of Eternity&quot;) is the name of a large, old-fashioned Japanese mansion hidden deep within the Bamboo Forest of the Lost. It has hallways that lead out into the far reaches of the sky. It was created by Eirin Yagokoro for Kaguya Houraisan.
