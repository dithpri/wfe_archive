The region of the Texian Confederacy is a land that devotes its time to ensuring that the nations under its banner strive to ensure that all of their people are given the opportunity to do their best in life. The Texian Confederacy of Limited Government (TCLG) supports the rights of the people as well as the moral principle of its founders. Any and all nations are welcome to join the ranks of the TCLG so long as they too practice the principles that the region strives for.
