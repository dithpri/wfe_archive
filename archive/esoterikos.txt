[list][b]ESOTERIKOS: GREAT WAR[/b][/list]

[hr]

Esoterikos is a sci-fi roleplay region set in a semi-fictional area of space referred to as [b]Kyklos[/b]. Nations vary from the most primitive creatures that have just exited the caves of their world to literal gods who have reawakened from their great slumber.

[hr]

[b]Current Event:[/b] THE GREAT WAR | The Labyrinthine War has been raging for 30 years, however now it&#39;s effects are beginning to spill out of the Labyrinth and into the greater Kyklos area as the Coalition of Powers gets more desperate.
