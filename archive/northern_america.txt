[b][region]Northern America[/region] is the northernmost subcontinent of North America. Southern limit is the Mexico&#150;United States border. Maps using the term Northern America date back to 1755, when the region was occupied by France, Great Britain, and Spain. 

[u]Area[/u]	21,780,142 km2 (8,409,360 sq mi)

[region]Northern America[/region] consists of Bermuda, Canada, Greenland, Saint-Pierre and Miquelon, and the United States of America. These regions are claimed by the [region]Continental Congress[/region] of [region]NATIONAL SOCIALIST AMERICA[/region] and defended by [region]The Continental Army[/region][/b]
