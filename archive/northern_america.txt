[b][region]Northern America[/region] is the northernmost subcontinent of North America. Southern limit is the Mexico&#150;United States border. Maps using the term Northern America date back to 1755, when the region was occupied by France, Great Britain, and Spain. The Solemn Act of the Declaration of Independence of Northern America in 1813 applied to Mexico

[u]Area[/u]	21,780,142 km2 (8,409,360 sq mi)

Northern America consists of Bermuda, Canada, Greenland, Saint-Pierre and Miquelon, and the United States of America. These regions are claimed by the [region]Continental Congress[/region] of [region]NATIONAL SOCIALIST AMERICA[/region] and defended by [region]The Continental Army[/region][/b]
