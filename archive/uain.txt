We are the United Alliance of Independent Nations.

Our priority is cooperation between all nations.

ARTICLE #1 - The UAIN will bring aid and support to      any nation or region in need of help.

ARTICLE #2 - Economically stronger nations in the UAIN will bring support to economically weaker nations in the UAIN.

ARTICLE #3 - The UAT (United Alliance Task-Force) will act as a security/peacekeeping force in the UAIN.

ARTICLE #4 - All members of the UAIN must be WA Assembly members, unless there is a valid reason as decided by the leading nations in the UAIN.

ARTICLE #5 - No nation can be punished for the withdrawal of endorsements
