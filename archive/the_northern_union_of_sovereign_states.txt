A region created by Kiana Mura and encompassing it and its nearest neighbours in a trade and limited federal capacity. Was initially founded in February of 2015 following the Treaty of Maljara between The Republic of Blaiddrwgistan, The Buryat Socialist Republic, The Republic of Kiana Mura, The Colony of Ilan Nuada, the Democratic Republic of Multinesia, The Dominion of Oill, The Colony of Marianette, The Republic of Smegmanistan, The Democratic Republic of Nhateir and The Principality of Jibet.
