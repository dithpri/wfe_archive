Those islands knowed as Tucania reside at the outskirts of the world, higly isolated from the rest of the nations. However this very diverse region is filled of history, empires, great peoples and legends. From the Union of Vaune, domminant nation in the region, to the Disputed Territories of Diarreastan, you can even find a satanic horde of rats. And the region still holds plenty of surprises and secrets to this day...
