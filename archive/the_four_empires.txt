The Four Empires are spread across five continents on the planet Estera. It is a world much like our own, but with one crucial difference: the Gods are real, and they are quite invested in human affairs. The largest empire to the smallest village is home to at least one God. Despite the Gods themselves remaining mostly static, the world of Estera is in constant flux; from politics, to advancing technology, to newly discovered lands inhabited by never-before seen creatures and peoples...
