New Brittania, colloquially called Great Britain, New Britain or just Britain, is a large island situated to the north-west of mainland Europe and is the largest island in Europe, populated with almost 70 million people. The largest of the more than 1000 surrounding islands and islets is Ireland, which has been under strict British control since 1789. New Brittania is best known for its empire, the British Empire, which encompasses all of the dominions, colonies, protectorates, mandates and other territories ruled or administered by the British government.
