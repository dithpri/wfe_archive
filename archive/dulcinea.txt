Dulcinea is a large, tropical island at the edge of a huge atoll.  Captain Armstrong made the first landing on the Island in 1984 aboard the destroyer Miss Fortune and returned in in 2007 after his retirement to found the first settlement at Armstrongs Landing.  Since then several expatriates from other regions have moved onto the island which serves as a sort of de facto mutual defense accord.

For an unknown reason the inhabitants of Dulcinea really seem to prefer black flags...
