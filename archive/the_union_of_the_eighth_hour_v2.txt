Constitution of the Union

1. All WA members must only endorse The Empire of Scansinia 
2. All nations should be active at least 3 times per week, and only have one nation
3. Every nation has the right to free speech but can be revoked if provocative or heretical if voted to be by Parliament
4. You must be from CMH or otherwise approved
5. Do not betray the Union to foreign forces or selfishness
6. Nations will not be banished or punished without fair trial
7. The Union is a  Religious Imperial Federal Republic with equal representation for all
8. All future laws will be voted upon by all nations of Parliament (2/3) and carried out by the Chancellor
9. Power Structure: Founder, Chancellor,  Prime Minister and Pope,  Ministers , and Nations
