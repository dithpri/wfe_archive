The Isles of Kevin, founded in 1723 by some people, is a small chain of islands in a circle together. When it was founded, it was one single country.In 1847, a civil war broke out between the two nations. By the time it ended in 1855, it had been split into four countries. Great Halo, Peaklan, Qwopchucks, and Stab Island. However, there was still unrest. In 1958, Peaklan became Leaklan, Great Halo split and became The Halos and Great Billy, Qwopchucks spilt to become Qwopland and The Chucklands, and Stab Island became Stab and Hanukkah Island.
