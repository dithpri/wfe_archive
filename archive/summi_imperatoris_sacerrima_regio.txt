Summi ac Excelsissimi Praeclarique Imperatoris Sacerrima Regio de Eius summa rerum universarum imperandarum auctoritate ac voluntate nata est. 
Omnes Summum ac Excelsissimum Imperatorem venerentur; memento Sacerrimum Imperatorem esse Summum Deum.

The Holiest Region of The Great, Most Sublime and Illustrious Emperor was born from His greatest authority and will to dominate all over the whole universe. 
Everyone venerate The Highest and Most Sublime Emperor; remember that The Holiest is The Greatest God.
