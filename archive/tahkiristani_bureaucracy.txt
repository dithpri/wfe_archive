In order to join, you must send a request to the Ministry of International Requests, if your request is considered, it will be sent to the Ministry of Admission, which will send an audit request to the Ministry of Inter-Ministry Requests which will send it for processing at the Ministry of Procrastination where it will languish while the workers enjoy permanent WoW subscriptions. If it manages to get through there, the Ministry of Auditing will send the request on to the Ministry of Inter-Ministry Responses, which will process it through the Ministry of Procrastination. After it returns to the Ministry of Admission. It might be sent to you depending on whether the courier feels like it.
