The New Russian Empire (Russian: &amp;#1053;&amp;#1086;&amp;#1074;&amp;#1086;&amp;#1081; &amp;#1056;&amp;#1086;&amp;#1089;&amp;#1089;&amp;#1080;&amp;#1081;&amp;#1089;&amp;#1082;&amp;#1072;&amp;#1103; &amp;#1048;&amp;#1084;&amp;#1087;&amp;#1077;&amp;#1088;&amp;#1080;&amp;#1103;; Novoy Rossiyskaya Imperiya), commonly called the Russian Empire or just Russia, is a country in north Eurasia. It shares borders with over 14 countries spanning from Europe to the Far East of Asia. With over 6.5 million sq mi of mainland (not including colonies), it is the largest country on Earth, by quite some distance. Expanding across the whole of North Asia (sharing latitudes at the most western tip with Oman and Yemen, and the most eastern tip with Malaysia). Russia spans nine timezones with a variety of different tips of environment.
