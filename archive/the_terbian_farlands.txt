The Terbian Farlands is a remote location with a large amount of mineable goods. This provides the ability for a massive arms sector. The few nations with a foodhold here are united in their ability to stand off against invasion. They are united in the small protection alliance of the UAIN.

The name of The Terbian Farlands comes from it&#39;s relative distance to the rest of the world and the uniquely massive quantities of the element Terbium.
