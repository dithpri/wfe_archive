A tenuous series of strands of land alone in a vast ocean underneath a cellophane sky, each link connected by a slight isthmus to one another. The casing is 100% lamb and snaps with a delightsome crunch when bitten into.  Klobania may only be maintained at 279 degrees Kelvin and measure a paltry 20 cm x 20 cm in physical geographic area, but among the refrigerators at the local grocery, it is one of the most populated lands around.  Nitrates and charcuteries are not welcome.
