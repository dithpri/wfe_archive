The Ursus Terram is a large archipelago, which are centered in the north western part of the globe. The area is famous for the large islands in the north east of the territory which are covered in vast forests and mild climate. The area is also known for its native forest animals as well as the rare Spirit Bear. The south is home to both small and decent tropical islands which are home to many tropical animals. At this moment the Western part of this vast territory is unknown due to heavy weather occurrences.
