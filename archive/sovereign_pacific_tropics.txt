A region most known for the breath-taking natural wonders of the surrounding vast crystal blue waters, this incredibly small area is remarkable for the amount of small islands dotted through the ocean. Marine life flourishes here, yet the expansive wilderness of the uninhabited islands and the respective jungles also showcase an almost primordial natural order, untouched by modern humans for thousands of years, up until recent invasions and subsequent genocides uprooted the economic stability of the once idyllic main island.
