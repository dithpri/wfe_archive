Adeltopia is a region full of lush greenery and wildlife with many unique places for nations to grow. Adletopia is a region that takes care of the nations that takes its place in it to either help support it or just be in. The region is vast and has many secrets that can only be unlocked by very few that supports the region. If any nation deems unworthy to be in Adletopia it will immediately be thrown out.
