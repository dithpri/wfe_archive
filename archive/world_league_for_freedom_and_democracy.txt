The [b]World League for Freedom and Democracy (WLFD)[/b] is an intergovernmental organisation of anti-communist politicians and groups founded in 1954 under the initiative of Chiang Kai-Shek, leader of the Republic of China. It united mostly ultra-right and libertarian people and organisations and acted with the support of the right-wing authoritarian regimes of East Asia and Latin America. During the Cold War, WLFD actively participated from anti-communist and anti-Soviet positions.

It unites representatives from more than 100 countries, has 8 regional divisions. It is currently a member of the United Nations Department of Public Information and has its headquarters in Nanjing, China.
