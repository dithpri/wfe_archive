The continent Nordeia was once home to the Great Nordeic Empire, that held sway over these lands for centuries. Yet in the 18th century, civil wars caused the empire&#39;s unity to fracture, and eventually led to the dissolution of this once great state.

In its place rose new nations, like phoenixes from the ashes, to lead the continent of Nordeia into a new, modern age. Today, the three most influential states of Nordeia form the Nordeic Triad, and safeguard the regions prosperity from outside influences.
