                                             Romania is a sovereign state located in Southeastern Europe. It borders the Black Sea, Bulgaria, Ukraine, Hungary, Serbia, and Moldova. 
                                           It has an area of 238,391 square kilometers (92,043 sq mi) and a temperate-continental climate. With 19.94 million inhabitants, the country 
                                            is the seventh most populous member state of the European Union. Its capital and largest city, Bucharest, is the sixth largest city in the EU.
