Eundak was founded to bring together a variety of nations to promote prosperity and peace among them.This region is committed to extending human rights, upholding security, and creating and maintaining just international and intraregional law. Countries who are admitted agree to this mission statement, and any alteration of this therein. 

Upon admittance into the region, please join the World Assembly and endorse the World Assembly Delegate. Telegram the President or Prime Minister for the password.
