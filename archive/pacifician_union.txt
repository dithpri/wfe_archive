[b]Pacifician Union[/b] ([i]PU, also known as Pacificia[/i]), is an Union between 5 Regions: Independy, Debilant, Unknown Island, Procap and South Debilant, consisting of 45 states. The total area of Pacificia is 12,018,814,652 square kilometres.
