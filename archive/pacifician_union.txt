[b]Pacifician Union[/b] ([i]PU, also known as Pacificia[/i]), is an Union between 4 Regions: Independy, Debilant, Procap and South Debilant, consisting of 39 states. The total area of Pacificia is 10,018,814,652 square kilometres.

Currency: Pacifician Union Dollar [PUD]
Capital: Portland C.D., Independy
