Motto: Trust Without Chains
Overview:
The Ledgerless Accord is a sovereign regional bloc united by opposition to centralized financial control. Member nations prioritize economic independence, transparent governance, and mutual cooperation without reliance on major international banks.
Governance:
A voluntary confederation where nations retain full autonomy while coordinating on trade, security, and regional stability.
Economy:
Decentralized, debt-averse, and focused on fair bilateral trade.
