The Kingdom of Wu Chen is ruled by Emperor Zhao Wei Yan (&amp;#23435;&amp;#21355;&amp;#35328;) from the Zhao family, who primarily resides in the city of Hang Zhou (&amp;#26477;&amp;#24030;) in the Chinese State of New Zachary.   
Founded in 927 CE by Emperor Zhao Tai Zu(&amp;#23435;&amp;#22826;&amp;#31062;) in modern-day New Zachary, it has since then been under the Song Dynasty.
