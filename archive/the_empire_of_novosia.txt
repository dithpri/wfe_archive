The empire of novosia is an empire and sarvinsia serves as its current emperor. the rank of emperor is purely an elected title chosen by the electors, electors are chosen by how much population they have, a minimum of 3 electors are needed to make decisions and a maximum of 5 electors can be chosen, electors are chosen every month. elections are on the last day of the month, prince electors are more influential electors that hold more power than the normal electors. The animal emblem of the empire is the melopsittacus undulatus, budgerigar. every december a regional cup will be held to see who is dominant
