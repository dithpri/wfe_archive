Nice is the fifth most populous city in France and the capital of the Alpes-Maritimes d&eacute;partement. The urban area of Nice extends beyond the administrative city limits, with a population of about 1 million on an area of 278 sq mi. Located in the French Riviera, on the south east coast of France on the [region]Mediterranean Sea[/region], at the foot of the [region]Alps[/region], Nice is the second-largest French city on the Mediterranean coast and the second-largest city in the Provence-Alpes-C&ocirc;te d&#39;Azur region after Marseille. &#151;Wikipedia[hr]
