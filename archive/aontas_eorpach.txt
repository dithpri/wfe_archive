[url=http://www.nationstates.net/nation=tuaisceart_eireann/detail=factbook/id=155289]Aontas Eorpach[/url] (AE) is an economic and political union of # member states which are located primarily in Eoraip. AE operates through a system of supranational independent institutions and intergovernmental negotiated decisions by the member states. Important institutions of the Aontas Eorpach include the Eorpach Commission, the Eorpach Council, the Eorpach Central Bank and the Eorpach Parliament.

Aontas Eorpach has developed a single market, a monetary union (Puntzone), using the Punt &Eacute;ireann (&pound;) (IEP), and is represented at the World Assembly.
