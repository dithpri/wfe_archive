Arkadia is an island continent that is home to five major nations. Making its home in the center of it all is [nation]Okijin[/nation], the oldest and largest of all the nations. Its well preserved temperate and coniferous forests attracts millions of visitors from around the world. To the north is [nation]Elysius[/nation], a well ordered and militaristic nation. The cold climate has made for hardy people and dependable military arms. In the south is the newly established [nation]Lubil[/nation]. To the west is the recent but modern [nation]Crapton[/nation]. And to the east is [nation]Mendrengard[/nation], where heavy and military industry prevails, regardless of environmental and social costs.
