&amp;#945; Reticuli is a double bright giant star of spectral class G8 in the constellation of Reticulum. &amp;#945; Reticuli visual magnitude is 3.35. Because of its moderate brightness, &amp;#945; Reticuli is easily visible to the naked eye from locations with dark skies, while it might be barely visible, or not visible at all, from light-polluted skies.
