[color=green][i]The Reserve Officer Training Corps(ROTC)[/color][/i]

[b][i][u]Mission[/b][/i][/u]
Our mission is to create a haven for all who want a disciplined, and seasoned region based off of the United States Military Training. Our goal is to aid aspiring regions in the creation of their Rank and Structure. As well as recruit and train &quot;cadets&quot; to become leaders, and followers, to aid the world of NationStates!
