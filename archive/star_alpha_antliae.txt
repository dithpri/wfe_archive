&amp;#945; Antliae is a variable giant star of spectral class K4 in the constellation of Antlia. &amp;#945; Antliae visual magnitude is 4.25. Because of its moderate brightness, &amp;#945; Antliae is easily visible to the naked eye from locations with dark skies, while it might be barely visible, or not visible at all, from light-polluted skies.
