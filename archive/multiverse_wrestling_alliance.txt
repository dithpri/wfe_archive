[b]Multiverse Wrestling Alliance[/b], or [b]MWA[/b], is a cooperative group of pro wrestling promotions and wrestlers from around the multiverse. This group was established to support various pro wrestling promotions around the multiverse. Its member promotions would be able to trade talent and would be able to share training facilities and venues. Member promotions can also work together to hold interpromotional events.
