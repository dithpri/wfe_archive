The [b][color=navy]Africasian Union[/color] [color=darkred](AFU)[/color][/b] consists of all nations located within Africa and the Middle East.

The STORY of this region is set within an alternate real world in which WWI, WWII and the Cold War never happened.

The ROLEPLAY of this region is part of the multi-region [i][region]American Union[/region][/i] universe.
