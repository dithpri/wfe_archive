[url=http://www.nationstates.net/region=berlin_wall][color=black][i]The Berlin Wall[/i][/url] was a barrier that divided Berlin from 1961 to 1989. Constructed by [/color][url=http://www.nationstates.net/region=east_germany][color=black]East Germany[/url], starting on 13 August 1961, the Wall completely cut off West Berlin from surrounding [/color][url=http://www.nationstates.net/region=east_germany][color=black]East Germany[/url] and from East Berlin until government officials opened it in November 1989. Its demolition officially began on 13 June 1990 and was completed in 1992. In this alternate reality, the Iron Curtain still stands over East Germany, and the wall still stands, intact, and more fortified than ever.[/color]
