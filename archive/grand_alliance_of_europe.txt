   Charter for The Grand Alliance 

The Parties to this Treaty reaffirm their faith in the purposes and principles of the Charter of the Grand Alliance and their desire to live in peace with all peoples and all governments.
They are determined to safeguard the freedom, common heritage and civilisation of their peoples, founded on the principles of liberty and the rule of law. They seek to promote stability and well-being upon the Earth.
