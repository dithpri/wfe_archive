The Order of Conglomerate Nations consists of The Empire of The Callesta, The Commonwealth of Woolloomoolloo, The Commonwealth of Newcambelland, The Colony of Hotrash and The Dominion of Tamrielic Islands.

The Order of Conglomerate Nations was founded by The Commonwealth of Woolloomoolloo after a peace treaty was signed between Woolloomoolloo and Emperor Callesta Constantus. Following the recent nation of The Commonwealth of Newcambelland, it joined The Order of Conglomerate Nations in order to get away from the United Kingdom. The Colony of Hotrash joined after that, signing a peace treaty with Woolloomoolloo and Emperor Callesta Constantus. Recently, The Domion of Tamrielic Islands joined.
