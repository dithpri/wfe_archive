Founded after years of civil war, the refugees of the original homeland built a new empire. Taehanmin has overseen the rise of three different nations of its own people, Yorisheim, Aranias, and Valus. Aranias and Valus, being the smaller, had formed a united kingdom. Together, the three nations form the core of the Kalbaradian Realm in which the Taehanmin Empire is recognized as the father-state and High Nation of the Realm. Kalbaradia is a mountainous environment whih is quite similar to the landscape of Greece.
