The Agency is an exclusive Region for top-quality nations, ranging from dangerous Dictatorships to pleasant Presidencies.

If you wish to join, you may send a request to the the Founder and Agency Director, Luxitoria. Requirements are minimal, but your state may not be accepted anyway, whether personal, related to vanity, or any other attribute of your nation.

Upon being accepted, you will be given the password. For every new entry, the password will be changed.
