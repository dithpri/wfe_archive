The region contains 2 islands: Island of Churos and Island of Golden. Churos contains 16 nations while golden contains 6. The last nation has yet to establish a land territory and claims that their boats is their country. Each island is distinct and has other sections that are either unexplored or forbidden. Oil is common on Churos while gold, silver and platinum are common on Golden. There is a major plan on building a major highway that will connect the 2 islands. It is expected to be complete in 6 months or later.
