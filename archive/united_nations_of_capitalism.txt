Upon breaking away from Volvistok, a socialist nation, The private industry depraved peoples voyaged out to find a place where they can rise throughout the ranks, using private industry and making well founded economic decisions to make their mark on the world. After the Revolt, We have left the socialist grip to the Volvistokians, and although not a lot of us have moved here, New Volvistok promises to become a beacon of hope to those trying to escape controlling and restraining governments.
