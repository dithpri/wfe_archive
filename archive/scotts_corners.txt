Scotts Corners is a hamlet (and census-designated place or CDP) located in the town of Pound Ridge in Westchester County, New York, United States. As of the 2010 census, the CDP population was 711, making Scotts Corners Westchester County&#39;s smallest census-recognized area.
According to the United States Census Bureau, the CDP has a total area of 1.8 square miles (4.7 km^2), all land.
