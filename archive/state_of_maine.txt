[color=003366][b]Maine[/b] is a state in the New England region of the northeastern [region=United States of America], bordered by the Atlantic Ocean to the east and south, New Hampshire to the west, and the Canadian provinces of Quebec to the northwest and New Brunswick to the northeast. [b]Maine[/b] is both the northernmost and easternmost portion of New England. It is known for its scenery&#151;its jagged, mostly rocky coastline, its low, rolling mountains, its heavily forested interior and picturesque waterways&#151;as well as for its seafood cuisine, especially lobsters and clams.[/color]
