In 2018, shortly after [[Chicken Overlords]] founding, an insolent fool sought to insult Chicken Overlords by establishing this region. The region did not last long and Chicken Overlords took it over, as a trophy of our success. However, on the 10th November 2018, a nation called &#147;No Cookies&#148; refounded it and kept it as a symbol of rebellion against the might of the Chicken Overlord. This rebellion lasted for almost seven years. No Cookies was resilient, but the Glorious Chicken Overlord (Cluck Be His Name) always triumphs. Today, that rebellion is over, and this region was stand as a monument to the enduring will and victory of Chicken Overlords.
