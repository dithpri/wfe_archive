After the nuclear holocaust of 2077, the world was drowned in radioactive fire. Those who were unable to secure admission to an underground Vault were either wiped from existence, horribly mutated, or were unfortunate enough to live to see the world end. Facing unending trials over generations such as radiation, scarcity of food and water, mutated creatures and people, and psychotic raiders the people of the wasteland are locked in a never ending war for survival and war... War never changes...
