The FEDERAL REPUBLIC of IMAZIGHEN commonly called TAMAZGHA, is a FEDERAL PARLAMENTARY REPUBLIC in NORTH AFRICA. It is bordered on the north by The MEDITERRANEAN SEA , on the south by SOUTH MALI , SOUTH NIGER and CHAD, by the east by EGYPT and on the west by ATLANTIC OCEAN . IMAZIGHEN covers 8,124,595 square kilometers and has has an estimated population of POPULATION. IMAZIGHEN comprises of 16 States.
