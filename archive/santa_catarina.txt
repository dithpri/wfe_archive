[b]Santa Catarina[/b] (Portuguese pronunciation: [&amp;#712;s&amp;#592;&amp;#771;t&amp;#592; kata&amp;#712;&amp;#638;in&amp;#592;], Portuguese: [i]Estado de Santa Catarina[/i], lit.&amp;#8201;&#39;State of Saint Catherine&#39;) is a state in the South Region of Brazil. Of Brazil&#39;s 26 states, it is the seventh-smallest state in total area and the 11th most populous. Additionally, it is the ninth-largest settlement, with 295 municipalities. The state, with 3.4% of the Brazilian population, generates 3.8% of the national GDP.
