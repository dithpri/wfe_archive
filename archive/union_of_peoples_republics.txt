Laws of the U.P.R.
All Laws Passed by the Central Committee are to be followed by the Governments of individual Republics.
1) All executions of any prisoners are to be done quickly and painlessly. This means that Gassing is no longer tolerated in the U.P.R. as a form of execution.
2) All nations are to aim for a Private sector size of less than 50 percent.
3) The Death Sentence for any religious based reason is now illegal.
4) All residence of the U.P.R. are free to travel within the country without restrictions
5) Each Republic must aim to have &amp;#39;average&amp;#39; civil rights (At least 50)
