The Union of Oceania is a geographic region comprising Melanesia, Micronesia, Polynesia &amp; Australasia.Spanning the eastern &amp; western hemispheres, Th Union of Oceania covers an area of 8,525,989 square kilometres (3,291,903) sq mi &amp; has a population of 40 million. Situated in the southeast of the Asia-Pacific region, Which makes us the smallest continental grouping in land area &amp; the second smallest population after Antartica.
