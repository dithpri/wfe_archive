Refounded as a colony of [region]Andarios[/region]. The Solverins were wise and powerfull a mixed-race of Humans and Elvens that lived in the inner planets of the Poseidon System, with their main planet being called Solverin by the first explorers of the [region]Andarian Small Magellanic Cloud[/region]. They are now part of the Imperial Galactic Republic as vassals.
