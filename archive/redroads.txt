A democratically elected region with basic princiapals and basic understandings that each citizen has a voice and can have a say so no matter the reason or the cause. We have a President, Three differnt parties. Left (Green), Central (Independents), Right (Capitalists) you start in the Independents and can choose the Party you would like to join by telegraming that parties respective leader and applying to join. Elections happen every 40 days with Primaries and House representitives being elected every 20 days. Once you join and register to a party or stay Independent you will have your own seat in Congress and will be allowed to vote on Legislation in the Region.
