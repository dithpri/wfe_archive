Krodia is a system of territories in northern North America. (real-life Yukon, Northern Territories, and Nunavut)

Krodia has a vibrant history. Languages such as Krodik, Nob&igrave;l&igrave;an, North Aroanan Krodik, and Latin-Krodik, all originate from the region. 

Krodia has no requirements for ideology. Dictatorships, democracies, and obnoxiously unaligned nations are all welcome in Krodia
