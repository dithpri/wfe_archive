Amber Valley is a borough in [region]Derbyshire[/region], [region]England[/region]. It takes its name from the River Amber and covers a semi-rural area with a number of small towns whose economy was formerly based on coal mining and engineering. There are four Towns in Amber Valley: Alfreton, Belper, Heanor and Ripley. There are also sixteen Villiges which are: Ambergate, Codnor, Crich, Denby, Duffield, [nation]Heage[/nation], Holbrook, Horsley, Kedleston, Kilburn, Lea &amp; Holloway, Mackworth, Milford, Quarndon, Somercotes and Swanwick.
