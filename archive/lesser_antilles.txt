The [b]Lesser Antilles[/b] (also known as the Caribbees) are a group of islands in the [region]Caribbean Sea[/region]. Most form a long, partly volcanic island arc between the [region]Greater Antilles[/region] to the north-west and the continent of South America. The islands form the eastern boundary of the [region]Caribbean Sea[/region] with the Atlantic Ocean. &#151;Wikipedia[hr]
