As nations of what to become Pacific Roulette met each other, they decided to create their own region. Migurdian ambassador approached Celestian Commonwealth with support from Sjod nar Lave and Wastgalony, and several others. Soon after, vote on government form and name began. After the vote was concluded, 5 nations finally became allies and Pacific Roulette was born. After a long while, the 6th possible nation, the Theocracy of Psynchot, finally joined the Region, proving themselves to be just as smart, as an average monkey.
