The land of Bray, previously known as Ireland, has been conquered by the fascist Empire of Pres under Councillor Connolly in October 2016. After many conquests and abuses of power, three factions, each with their own ideals of what Pres should be, have risen up in arms in the Pres Civil War, which began on January 1st 2017. Who will win this battle of fates?
