The Terran Alliance Executive: ( T.A.E ) 

Founded in 2150 to deal with the administration of the Growing off world colonies of Earth nations, and to create  unified defence force to protect Earth, and it&#39;s colonies. The TAE oversees interplanetary relations, trade, defence, and interstellar diplomacy. 

The TAE is a small highly specialized government with little direct control over member nations, colonies, and settlements. The TAE Senate, Unified Defence Force, Interstellar Commerce Commission, And Judiciary Committee operate independent branches on each member world. They also oversee subsidiary colonies founded and administrated by Member Worlds, and Associate members.
