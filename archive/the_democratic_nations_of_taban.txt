Taban is a Region that has split up into many different sectors. The Leading Nations are Morktaban (The Founder of this region) and Ottosburg (Founding nation of &quot;The Autocratic alliance of Taban&quot;). These two regions, sharing the same continent, have raged wars over which ideology is best. If you are a Democratic nation that is interested in involving yourself with this conflict or &quot;Good Versus Evil&quot;, than this is the region to join.
