A large island nearby The Black Hawks, Klocia is a small region home to Thialrer, its Founder and the Secretary-General of the Klocian Union. Any WA member states in Klocia are automatically a part of the Klocian Union. Klocia is a region where you can pretty much do anything you want, within reason. Additionally, if we get raided, the Founder and Secretary-General of the Klocian Union, [nation]Thialrer[/nation], is a former member of [region]The Black Hawks[/region].
