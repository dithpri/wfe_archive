The International Sovereign Union, or I.S.V for short, is a union made to promote and protect the sovereignty of its member states while fostering cooperation and mutual support in various areas. Our mission is to create a platform where nations can maintain their independence and self-determination while addressing shared challenges and pursuing common goals. We are dedicated to upholding the principles of diplomacy, peace, and prosperity among our member states. Through diplomatic dialogue, economic cooperation, and collaborative efforts, the I.S.V strives to enhance the well-being and security of its constituent nations and promote a more stable and interconnected world.
