Vegief is a budding region located in a very interesting spot, where half of it seems to be barren inhospitable landscapes, while the other half are usually lust forests where National Animals frolic freely to&amp;#39;n&amp;#39;fro. The Nations occupying this region are extrodinarily diverse, there being similarities between them for the sake of the alliance being able to last, but even the vastly different are able to find home. 

It was founded by the Ruler of Axia, who felt that the nations around him should join together, to create one powerful force who could ward off any threat if it came. In fact, warding off a threat would go quite simply, as most of the Nations have quite the military.
