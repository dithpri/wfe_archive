The region is composed of Fascist, Communist, Monarchic, Republican, Capitalist, Environmentalist, and Socialist Nations each with its own ideology on running its nation.

The red star, and the hammer and sickle represent Communism; the Cyrillic text (transliterally, Diktatura dlya Lyudey, literally Dictatorship for the People), represents Dictatorship; the white, blue and red bands represent Democracy and the Ax, the abbreviation for Akhyet written in Cyrillic, with tilde represent Capitalism.

Regional Aim: Regional Unity

Regional Motto: Politics, philosophy, and religion may divide us but we can make a difference through business.
