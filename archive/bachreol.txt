A world ravaged by nuclear war. The remaining countries have taken as much land as possible, leaving quite a few major powers.

Geography: Most rainforests and forests have shifted. Places like the Amazon have turned into desert, while the Southern half of the Sahara has been converted into a dense tropical rainforests. The oceans everywhere have become covered in oil, leaving most underwater life much deeper in the water and making the surface quite flammable. The Middle East itself has been almost completely eradicated, now an ocean of oil.
