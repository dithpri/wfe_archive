The Ashenath Empire was formed on the 20th of November, 1900.

The Ashenath Empire is neither influenced by West or the East (Or south and north).

The Ashenath Empire is governed by the Ashenath Party, which leans towards the left and is anti-authoritarian. The home country of The Ashenath Empire is &quot;The Empire of Ashenath&quot;. The current Emperor is Ash Zanovich.

The Empire of Ashenath has deep ties to the British Empire.
