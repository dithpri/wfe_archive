Here in these isolated islands, located in a place that can not be found by conventional means, lies the holy land of the Kaiservient People. Here they flourish alongside the Dakonians who are a people driven by the advancement of the human race, although the Kaiservients are set in there ways and the teachings of the Great Kai. Despite there differences, they remain allies and work together as a single cohesive unit by providing for and defending each other.
