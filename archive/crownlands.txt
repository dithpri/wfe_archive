The [b]Crownlands[/b] is one of the nine constituent regions of the [region]Seven Kingdoms[/region] of Westeros.

The crownlands are based around Blackwater Bay, with the capital of [region]Kings Landing[/region] built where the Blackwater Rush meets the bay.

Some notable houses of the crownlands include Bar Emmon, Celtigar, Massey, Rosby, Rykker, Stokeworth, Sunglass, and Velaryon. -- [url=http://awoiaf.westeros.org/index.php/Crownlands][color=003366]Crownlands[/color][/url][hr]
