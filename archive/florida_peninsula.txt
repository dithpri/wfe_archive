The Florida Peninsula is home to the nations that formerly comprised the state of Florida. 

The United States dissolved after a Second Civil War broke out when President Trump refused to accept the results of the 2020 election. He would not vacate the office of the Presidency. A constitutional crises ensued which led to violence and armed conflict. 

3 nation states emerged from the territory formerly known as the state of Florida. 

West Florida runs from Baton Rouge to Jacksonville down to Tampa. 

South Florida runs from Orlando down to Miami. 

Key West is, well, Key West.
