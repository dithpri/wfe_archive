The Armed Union of Capitalistia is a small militaristic union with RP and a wargames. Max. member amount is 5

RP Rules: You can only control your own country. All participating members must agree to start a wargame. Weapons cannot be sci-fi and must be from the cold war to modern times. ( Some examples are the Abrams Tank, Leopard Tank, Predator Drone, F16, F35, F14, F15, Mig-17, Humvee, Bradley Fighting Vehicle, M113, Aircraft Carriers, Destroyers, Submarines, Etc.) These are not wars, but simulations for training.

To get job as officer apply on message board.
Available jobs: Law Enforcement Officer, Diplomacy Officer, and Military Officer.
