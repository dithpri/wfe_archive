Nova Azuria is an island area in the great expanse of the ocean. Nova Azuria is considered the haven of the ocean, and is notable for its great fishing, low hurricane rate, and general friendliness between all member nations. We invite you to join our region!

The flag meaning:
Azure color to represent the sea color
Yellow color to represent the sunlight and sand
Sun to represent the dawn of the region
Trident to represent the ancient god of the sea, Poseidon
Sword to represent pirates and the history of the region
