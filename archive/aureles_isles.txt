[b][color=silver]Vaax Velkome Veldgund[/b][/color]
[color=black]Mrong yli vren Se mond. 
Draas ge, uuld mes nund.  Gael ge, jond fuun ro.  Ghend ylowl soos nali.  Jes fal uurl grol gromdo.  Nassigda rus yo vel jelk hel.[/color]

[b][color=silver]Usea Nel Jal[/b][/color]
Jone defend lal ati, bres nekha sond pra yonta.  Royas Vich denchi tha agress mean nun.  Lowl fal dal ashno, ete yattho.  Sith fande east mu ya.  Gres galgre emba, dul viprin&ccedil; sante viking.  Molk yso scandinavio!
