The United Trade Alliance (UTA) is a council of nations dedicated to the encouragement of free trade between members. It is required that as of joining the UTA, procedures will be taken in the altering of member nations policies to assist in free trade negotiations. These include the removal of tariffs and other taxation methods on exports and the establishment and maintenance of trade infrastructure and routes. Communication and role-play is encouraged and spam and harassment will not be tolerated at the discretion of the Executive authority.
