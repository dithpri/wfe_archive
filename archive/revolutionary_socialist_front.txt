Left-winger? Commie? Socialist? Then this is the perfect region for your nation.

The Revolutionary Socialist Front (RSF) is a region most exclusively for the following nations who follow the ideologies below:

- Communists (Marxist-Leninist, Left-wing, Trotskyite)
- Socialists (Democratic socialists, Left-wing social democrats)
- Left-wing liberals and anti-capitalist, anti neo-liberal
- Left-wing populists and nationalists

Specifically, this is also for you if your nation is categorized into either:

- Democratic Socialists
- Liberal Democratic Socialists
- Left-wing utopia
- Iron Fist Socialists

Regional government is facilitated by a Regional Council of Leaders headed by a Chairperson with a Deputy Chairperson and Regional Secretary.
