[b][color=black]House on the Kehlstein, [region]Kehlsteinhaus[/region], is perched at the top of Kehlstein peak, at an elevation of 1,881 metres with commanding views of the Alps. The 50th birthday of Adolf Hitler on 20 April 1939 was celebrated as a national holiday throughout Nazi Germany and other parts of the world, and as Martin Bormann&#39;s present, Kehlsteinhaus was constructed.

The former Bavarian living quarters of [i]Shira Bell[/i], one of the most famous and most loyal subjects, soldiers, officers and lastly Reichspr&auml;sident of [/color][color=red][u]The Greater German Reich[/u][/color][color=black] was liberated and redecorated for use as a mountain restaurant and vacation house for loyal subjects of the Reich.
