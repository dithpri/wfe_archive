[b]Chrome Company[/b] is the name given to the raiding forces of Lex Magnus Corpus, an international military and mutual defense pact led by the infamous [region]Confederation of Corrupt Dictators[/region].
[hr]
Headed first and foremost by [nation=long]Jocospor[/nation], with command delegated to [b]Vladislav Khilkov[/b] of [nation=long]Russia Major[/nation], Minister for War of the Confederation, Chrome Company aims for the destruction of the degenerate democracies of the world, and their replacement with strong dictatorships.
