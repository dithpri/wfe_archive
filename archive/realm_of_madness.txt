Welcome to the Realm of Madness. Please sign up to one of three factions in the region.
The Sinners: Led by Sin and the 1337 Crew, who seek to drown the region, and the world, in sin.
The Saviors: Led by Jesus and the Saviors, determined to destroy Sin and his crew.
The Antagonists: Led by Hank and his lieutenants, they seek to destroy both groups to restore balance.
Join a side and have fun.
