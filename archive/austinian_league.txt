The Austinian League is a group of Nations both on Earth and offworld colonies. Nations have general autonomy but are asked to come together during times of crisis or war. The League has 5 main rules.

I. All free men when they form a social compact have equal rights.
II. No money shall be appropriated or drawn from the treasury for the benefit of any sect, or religious society, theological or religious seminary.
III. Every person shall be at liberty to speak, write or publish his opinions on any subject.
IV. Every citizen shall have the right to keep and bear arms in the lawful defense of himself or the League.
V. The military shall at all times be subordinate to the civil authority.
