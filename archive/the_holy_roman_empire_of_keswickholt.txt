The Holy Roman Empire of Keswickholt is an interstellar constitutional monarchy ruled by the Imperial House of Aurelia.  

The Empire blends ancient imperial tradition with advanced technology and a strong central government.  

It is known for stability, powerful institutions, and a culture built on duty, unity, and loyalty to the Crown.
