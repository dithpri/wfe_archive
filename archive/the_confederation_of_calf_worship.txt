The Confederation of Calf Worship is a united bloc of nations located on the planet Heimat Tolv. These nations declare this confederacy to establish unity based on geographic location alone. The differences between nations existing under the aforementioned entity are numerous. They are including, but not limited to ideology, race, religion, and even objective. The confederation is open to those who want to join, however due to its infancy, many facets of the confederation, such as a forum, and concrete laws are not apparent. Nations who use our infancy to behave maliciously will suffer consequences, the confederation reserves the right to expel nations who behave in such parasitic ways.
