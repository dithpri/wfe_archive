North Veridian is characterized by its extensive landmass, which spans millions of square kilometers. To the west, the continent is bordered by the majestic Veridian Mountains, which are known for their breathtaking peaks and challenging hiking trails. To the east, the continent gently slopes down towards the North Veridian Sea, offering picturesque coastlines and numerous beautiful beaches. The interior of the continent is a patchwork of diverse ecosystems, including vast forests, sprawling grasslands, and expansive deserts.
