Association of Social Democratic Nations (ASDN)

The Association of Social Democratic Nations (ASDN) is a regional intergovernmental organization established on April 3, 2025, under the Daecheon Agreement of 2025, which formally created the Daecheon Social Democratic Community (DSDC). The ASDN was founded to promote economic cooperation, social development, and political collaboration among nations adhering to social democratic principles. The organization is headquartered in Daecheon, Choi Aeguka, and serves as a platform for regional dialogue and cooperation among its member states.
