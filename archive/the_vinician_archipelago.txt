 Vinix (IPA: [v&amp;#618;n&amp;#618;ks]), officially the Republic of The Archipelago of Vinix or the Vinician Republic is a federal island country.

The capital city Vinicia was founded by The First Vinician Settlers on the Island of Vinix, the other main islands are the Island of Vinicula and the outlying Vinician Islets.

Due to Vinicians traditional close ties to Catholicism, His Excellency President Dr. Winston, the former Head of the Council of the Vinician Republic, the Vincian government, signed the Catholic Charter in May 2007. Vinix has been member of the World Assembly, former United Nations, with interruptions since the same date.
