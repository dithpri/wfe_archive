Free Union Capitalist States is an invitational, voluntary economic regional union of states.

Free Union Capitalist States&#39; members are committed to human rights, human dignity, individual liberties, free minds, free domestic markets, and free international trade.  

The Free Union Capitalist States Region respects the sovereignty and internal policies of all other states and Regions.  

Presence of a Free Union Capitalist States Embassy in a Region does not imply that the Free Union Capitalist States endorses the policies of that region.
