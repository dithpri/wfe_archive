Cyberius was founded as a commonwealth of nations primarily Balkan nations after the great war. The devastation caused by the great war was so incredible, nations were built from rubble to skyscrapers, from dust to greatness. King Louis X as a visionary saw the need for a commonwealth of nations to together rebuilt the great community, in a moment of devastation he foresaw a community of greatness, together with the Balkan nations, he created the great region of Cyberius which now seeks to establish itself on the world stage, as a powerhouse and fight for the freedoms our nations cherish.
