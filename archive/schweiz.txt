[i]Switzerland (German: Schweiz; French: Suisse; Italian: Svizzera; Romansh: Svizra; Swiss German: Schwiz) is a small country in Western Europe. The official name of Switzerland is Confoederatio Helvetica. This is Latin and is not often used except for state documents. Switzerland is a federation of even smaller states - 26 cantons.[/i][hr][B]Wilkommen zu Schweiz![/B] Schweiz, or Switzerland, offers nations an opportunity to take part in the unique Swiss governmental system.
