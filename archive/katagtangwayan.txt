[b][color=gold]Katagtangwayan[/color][/b] is a region consisting of nations with similar cultures and values.

The region has its own governing organization led by the [i]Permanent Council[/i], consisting of nations located on the homeland of the [i]Tagtangway [/i]people. Because the region is basically homogenous in culture and ethnicity, integration is not a problem but ideology has always been the dividing issue in the region. [nation]Kazakovia[/nation] is considered the motherland of the [i]Tagtangway [/i]people.
