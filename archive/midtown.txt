Little is known to the outside world concerning this land. Except for the obvious joint effort of all who live there in killing on sight anyone unknown to them.
There seems to be no possibility of peaceful contact.
*Mapping by satelite data from numerous regions have all been unsuccessful. Though the satelites appear to be unharmed, the chronometers of those that enter this region show no signs of ever spending any time there, and subsequent data is unretrievable.
