THE SIX COMMANDMENTS OF [region]THE THUNDERDOME[/region]:

1. Whoever exists outside [region]THE THUNDERDOME[/region] is an enemy.
2. Whoever exists within [region]THE THUNDERDOME[/region] is a friend. 
3. Nations in [region]THE THUNDERDOME[/region] shall never associate with those outside.
4. No nations shall insult another.
5. No nations shall eject and/or ban active nations.
6. All residents of [region]THE THUNDERDOME[/region] are equal.
