A newly arisen union founded by a collection of independent nations. They strive to build up this region into a true powerhouse and a key player on the international stage. 
The political ideals within the region are extremely diverse, however they all get together in a time of need, to build their economy and armed forces. A treaty has been signed, providing all nations with sufficient independence to run their country more or less as they see fit.

All religions, ethnicity and political ideologies are welcomed as long as they assist each other.
