Icecream Land election region. ICE for short. To join this region, send a telegram with your WA nation and your Icecream Land nation to Icecreamsin and Icecreamsin will send you the password to the region. Then, create another nation and name it [Icecreamland nation name] ICE. For example, Icecreamsin ICE. If you don&#39;t already have a WA nation, join the WA with your Icecream land nation.
