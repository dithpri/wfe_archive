This [b][color=navy]Commercial Products[/color][/b] region is the storage directory for all ground vehicles, aircraft, and sea-going vessels that can be traded, purchased and sold within our roleplay story. This process is creatively maintained via the NS card-trading game system.

The modernity of each commercial product listed here is determined by its Scientific Advancement rating.
