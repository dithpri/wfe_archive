This is the region in which Bongrovia resides. The Bongrovian Islands was founded in 1988 when the former third part of [nation]Bongrovia[/nation], [nation]Nesworpil[/nation], separated from the Mainland and Catalina. Nesworpil is now allied with Bongrovia, and the region has recently been open for other nations to become a part of the region and become allies with Bongrovia and Nesworpil.
