North Utopia (NU) was founded on 31 August 2012. With a grand vision, the founder established this region for the purpose of providing opportunity for all nations. With the difficulty of being lost amongst the masses, North Utopia grants a safe environment for all nations to flourish. While there is yet to be a government, the founder is assembling fine nations to generate ideas to foster the vision of prosperity, growth, opportunity, and happiness.
