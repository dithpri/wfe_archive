The region of Phoenixa was created when The Holy Empire of New Phoenix seceded from the the region of The Pacific.

The region of Phoenixa is dedicated to the protection and welfare of its member nations. Phoenixa is a peace-loving region. However, it is Phoenixa&amp;#39;s goal to maintain a strong military in order to protect its member nations.

 All member nations are expected to be active.

Phoenixa welcomes newborn nations.
