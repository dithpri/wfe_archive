Misunderstood and cast aside, these diverse and powerful races strive to overcome their differences and unite as one in order to win freedom for their people and prosper in a land that has come to distrust them.
In the Horde, action and strength are valued above diplomacy, and its leaders earn respect by the blade, wasting no time with politics. The brutality of the Horde&#39;s champions is focused, giving a voice to those who fight for survival.

The Horde gets its name from the original invading army of Skandzans that invaded this world during the first war
