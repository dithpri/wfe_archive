[b]Asuka Langley Soryu[/b] (Japanese: &amp;#24803;&amp;#27969;&amp;#12539;&amp;#12450;&amp;#12473;&amp;#12459;&amp;#12539;&amp;#12521;&amp;#12531;&amp;#12464;&amp;#12524;&amp;#12540;) is a fictional character from the Neon Genesis Evangelion franchise created by Gainax. She is the Second Child and the fiery pilot of a giant mecha, Evangelion Unit-02, to fight against enemies known as Angels for the special agency Nerv. Because of childhood trauma, she has developed a competitive, outgoing personality to get noticed by others and affirm her own self. In the Rebuild of Evangelion films, her Japanese surname is changed to Shikinami (&amp;#24335;&amp;#27874;), and she differs significantly from her television series incarnation in her background and characterization.
