         Welcome to the Global Republic! 
All members are welcome from all walks of political ideologies! 
The Global Republic is made up of multiple Nation Representatives who make up the Global Senate. The Global Senate will be the governing body of the Region. 
The Global Senate will elect a Chancellor who will fulfill duties as the Chair of the Senate. Any members can nominate themselves. 
All authority in The Global Republic will rely on the Global Senate who will settle the conflicts and difficulties of the Region.
