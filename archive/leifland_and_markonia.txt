Leifland and Markonia was once a great, centralized Empire, administered from the capital at Leifhaus, but after the previous monarchy attempted a brutal invasion of several neighboring regions and failed, the empire began to dissolve into control at the hands of regional warlords who took over parts of the country. As the Kingdom of Leifland and Markonia itself is in the midst of a interregnum, the government has had no choice but to accept this outcome.
