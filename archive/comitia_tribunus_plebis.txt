c. 42 BC
After Octavian and Mark Antony&#146;s horrible mismanagement of their resources and debts and making the lives of soldiers, farmers, and artisans alike, local Italian bandit armies have banded together with local villages and cities to press the government for food. 

Farmland is confiscated and given back to destitute farmers. Plebeian Tribunes act as lieutenants for any remaining friendly, battle-hardened senators, and a new council of the Roman Republic is created by these rebels against Octavian and Mark Antony: the [region]Comitia Tribunus Plebis[/region].
