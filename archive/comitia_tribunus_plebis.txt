c. 44 BC
The Senate has been closed, many senators executed, and others far off, seeking to raise an army. 

The military can&#146;t establish order to Italia because most of its upper ranks are in hiding, missing, or dead. 

Much of the public has resentment to both Caesar and the Senate, and refuses to give either of them the phony power of wealth that they had kept for so long. The turn to their elected, and sworn by oath Plebeian Tribunes to rule Rome. And so the Plebeian War begins.
