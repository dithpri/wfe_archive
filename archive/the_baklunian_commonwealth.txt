The Baklunian Commonwealth is an international alliance of Nations which recognize the political guidance of the Kingdom of Baklunit. Only nations that swear their allegiance to His Majesty Erchenbrando II, King of Baklunit and Head of the Baklunian Commowealth can join the region. 

The Baklunian Commowealth is on behalf of His Majesty by an Executive Council appointed by the Crown.
