[b]Overview[/b]

The continent of Yelomk is 8.9 million square miles in area.
The white area depicts Glacial climates which make it 
uninhabitable while the brown area is Tundra which is 2% of the ideal
Habitability range but 2% is still at least habitable.
the habitable area is only 5,100 square miles.


The entire continent is owned by the nation of Sintcam where the
entire population is concentrated in the small habitable zone on the southern tip 
of the continent.

[b]Yelomk Minor[/b]

2000 miles to the South, two landmasses that are 1.1 million square miles in area, are populated by Puppet states under the control of Supreme Leader Blankwave. Though technically not part of the continent, its grouped in with Yelomk for convenience.
