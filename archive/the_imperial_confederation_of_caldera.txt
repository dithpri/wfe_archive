Therefore, the Calderan Empire will only seek expansion through peaceful means such as mergers and annexations, never will the Calderan Empire seek expansion through military conquest, except in the case of liberating a region from outside aggressors, however in that case it is only placed in a temporary &amp;quot;Protectorate&amp;quot; status.
