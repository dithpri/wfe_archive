[b]Dux Milan is an affiliate region of The D&aacute;xlamic Empire, administered by [nation]New Bradfordsburg[/nation] in [region]Declansburg[/region].[/b]

History: Founded in 2013 by New Bradfordsburg, this region was once home to nearly 100 nations and a vibrant democracy. As the years passed, the population of the region shrank but relics of the region survive online, including the offsite forums that once housed the regional government. This region was refounded by [nation]Santa Samuel and Gracia[/nation], a puppet nation of Dux Bradford John, as an ancestral home to D&aacute;xlamic peoples.
