The Peace Order is for peace, no war. We are open for any diplomatic relations with anyone who wishes to have said relations with our region. We do not wish for war, because peace and diplomacy go much farther than war ever will get anyone.

Motto: Forever Peace! Forever the The Peace Order!

Join our Discord server here!: [url=https://discord.gg/9QS2r3M]Our Discord Server[/url]

Terms for WA Delegates are one month long and WA Delegates can have a maximum of two terms per every four months. If no-one is elected or wishes to be the WA Delegate (WA Delegates are effectively the President), the current WA Delegate will stay in power, but if there is no WA Delegate to start with, the power shall be help by the nations founder.
