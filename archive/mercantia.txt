Mercantia is the region containing the Merquouis Peninsula and her surrounding islands. It is known for it&#39;s mediterranean climate, save for the more temperate northern regions and would under normal conditions attract a fair amount of tourism for it&#39;s breathtaking nature, hospitable climate and rich history.
In later years however, the stability of the region has taken a turn for the worse. The former proud nation is currently divided into three conflicting ideological factions.
