[b][color=fire]Castile and Le&oacute;n[/color][/b]

 is an autonomous community in north-western region of [b][region]the Kingdom of Spain[/region][/b]. It was constituted in 1983 and it is the largest autonomous community in Spain and the third biggest region of the European Union, covering an area of 94,223 km&sup2; with an official population of around 2.5 million (2011).
