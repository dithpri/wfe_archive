[b][color=green]The 18th-largest country in the world in terms of area at 1,648,195 km2 (636,372 sq mi), Iran is an ethnically diverse country and has a population of around 77 million.[/color] It is a mountainous country of particular geopolitical significance owing to its location in three spheres of Asia. Tehran is the capital, the country&amp;#39;s most populous city and the political, cultural, commercial and industrial center of the nation. [color=red]Known as a developing country, Iran is a regional power, and holds an important position in international energy security and world economy as a result of its large reserves of petroleum and natural gas.[/color] Iran has the second largest proven natural gas reserves in the world and the fourth largest proven petroleum reserves.[/b]
