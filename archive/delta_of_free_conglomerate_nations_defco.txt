DeFCoN or Delta of Free Conglomerate Nations is a region having free nations. The participating nations are free in every sense of the word, famous for their excellent civil rights, freedom and liberty. Nations may act in snap of individual cases but their main aim is always to have a society as free as possible but with a maintained order. 

In some cases the nations take drastic steps but liberty always reflects in their choices, even if means a weak government. Public offices are powerful for the participating nations, some nations can even put freedom over economy.
