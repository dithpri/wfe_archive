The history of the galaxy known as Skyriver has unraveled for untold millennia. The development of civilizations in all of its regions was not only influenced by physical geography, but also by urban geography and architecture, as inhabitants sought to put their own stamp on the worlds in which they inhabited. In this region of the galaxy, between the Core Worlds and the Inner Rim, The United Imperial Empire of Calebs Haven has come from another space and time, and from another galaxy far, far away to colonies the region and make it home.  Calebs Haven welcomes you to the galaxy.
