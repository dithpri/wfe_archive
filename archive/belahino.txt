While the world regions were at war, some nations slipped away, so that they would not have to die like many others. they found a good spot, away from prying eyes, where they set up cities where they could hide at night. After many years, the bigger regions started expanding, and then found the nations. The war was over at this point, but the nations did not know, and tried to defend themselves. Once the regions noticed this, they decided that it would be a new region: Belahino, the name of the leader of the first region to discover it at the time.
