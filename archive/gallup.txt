Welcome to [region]Gallup[/region].

The purpose of [region]Gallup[/region] is to conduct a series of polls, tests, and votes about various opinions on topics pertaining to the gameplay, technical, and occasionally the general forum side of Nationstates. It is meant to be informative way to gather the opinion of NationStates visually without using the forums or relying on biased sources. Topics may include raiding, native rights, the world assembly, new features, name releases, etc; if it is controversial enough to be debated, it will be debated or voted here.
