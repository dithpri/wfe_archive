The Union of Croat States is a confederation of ethnic Croat states in Europe, The Union of Croat States protects the interest of Croat States in all of Europe, The Union of Croat States is a separate entity from the European Union, the official of the Croat Union is the Croatian Kuna, the Union of Croat States is made up of primarily ethnic Croats with Serbian, Bosniak Muslims, Hungarians, Slovakians, and Polish as minorities.
