[color=blue]Neptune is the eighth and farthest known planet from the Sun in the Solar System. It is the fourth-largest planet by diameter and the third-largest by mass. Among the giant planets in the Solar System, Neptune is the most dense planet in solar system. It gets it blue color from the Methane. 
[b]Radius: 15,299 mi
Distance from Sun: 2.795 billion mi
Mass: 1.024 &times; 10^26 kg (17.15 M&amp;#8853;)
Orbital period: 165 years
Length of day: 0d 16h 6m
Moons: Triton, Nereid, Proteus, Naiad, Larissa, Despina, Galatea, Thalassa, Neso, Halimede, Psamathe, Laomedeia, S/2004 N 1, Sao[/b][/color]
