The Sovereign States of Risvent is an alliance between the the major nations upon the Isle of Risvent. Three Republic of Eyerdon, the United States of Ambadon, and the Commonwealth of Moravadon. All have benefited economically and militarily, but relations between Eyerdon and Moravadon are tense. Nevertheless, the three nations combat the Free Tribes Federation, a group of radical primitives bent on taking land and sacrificing heretics. The Sovereign States of Risvent remain strong, never faltering or failing.
