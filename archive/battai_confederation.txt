The Battai Confederation is a confederation of many states/nations. The capital of the confederation is the Republic of Kahsak. The confederation is a representative democracy, each country inside the confederation has some level of autonomy although they must listen to decisions made by the voting of representatives from different countries inside Battai. 


We do not participate in much roleplay, and regular stuff, we are mostly just in this to chill. This region is for nations who want to be in a region that isn&#39;t so interactive, and intense, and who want to just mind their own business, and not have their region scream at them for not interacting with the region.
