The Battai Confederation was established to foster deeper cooperation between Kahsak and Neostaingon, aiming to promote peace, collaboration, and shared governance in the region. Originally formed to encourage unity among neighbors, it has since expanded and matured into the multi-nation alliance it is today.

The capital of the Confederation is currently Kamaska, the capital of the current WA Delegate, [nation]Kahsak[/nation], the capital is always the capital of the WA Delegate.
