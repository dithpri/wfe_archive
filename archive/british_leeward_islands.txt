[b]British Leeward Islands[/b]
a [region=0000] region

British Leeward Islands was a British Colony from 1671 to 1958
1816 the colony was divided in two regions:
[list][region=Antigua], [region=Barbuda], [region=Montserrat]
[region=Saint Christopher], [region=Nevis], [region=Anguilla], [region=British Virgin Islands][/list]
Reunited in 1833 until 1871
Federal Colony of the Leeward Islands from 1871 to 1956
[region=Dominica] joined 1871 but left in 1940.
1958 the remaining absorbed by [region=West Indies Federation].
