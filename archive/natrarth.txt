The planet Natrath is an earth-like planet in a densely populated solar system with twelve other planets.
Natrarth is about 2.6 times bigger than Earth and its gravity is about 1.07 times that of Earth.

A single day lasts 20.46 hours and a year lasts 103 days. The planet is made up of 5 continents, which make up 25% of the planet&#39;s landmass.
5 moon(s) orbit the planet and Natrarth itself orbits a yellow sun in an elliptic orbit.
