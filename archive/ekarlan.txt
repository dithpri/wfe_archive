Ek&aacute;rlan is a continent located entirely in the Northern Hemisphere and mostly in the Eastern Hemisphere of the fictional Earth-like planet Miru (name varies across languages). It is bordered by the Sea of the North and Arctic Sea to the north, the Great Ocean to the west, the Secluded Seas to the east, and the Interregional Seas to the south. It comprises the northernmost part of K&aacute;ruilia (name varies across languages).
