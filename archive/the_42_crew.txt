The 42 Crew is a union of nations devoted to the economic and military security of its members.

The amendments to our constitution must be followed by member states. These amendments are listed bellow: 

1) Democracy is a right and must be applied to all union members 

2) National sovereignty of member states must be recognized

3) Free trade must be allowed to operate between union members 

4) Offensive wars between union members are forbidden 

5) All union members must be ready to defend one another in times of conflict 

6) No union members should partake in offensive wars on non member states without a majority of nation members approving
