[region]Siracusa[/region], formally known as the [nation]Siracusan Confederation[/nation] for short, is a Terran country bordering [region]Kazdel[/region], [region]Laterano[/region], and [region]Leithanien[/region].

Home of many Lupos and Vulpos, Siracusa is a confederation of twenty-two autonomous city-states under the effective control of twelve mafia families, the Dodici Famiglie, with a unified leadership of a powerful Capo dei capi.
