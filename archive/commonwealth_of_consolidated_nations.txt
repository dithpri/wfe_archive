The Commonwealth of United Nations - CCN, was funded by Republic of Gedanium in 28.01.2016. If you are interested to join us, please send a telegram to Democratic Republic of Gedanium and then after positive consider of CUN commission, a Delegate or Founder will send you a telegram with password what is necessary to join organisation.

The main objectives of organisation are:
-Free internal market.
-The single currency in member states.
-Humanitarian assistance programs.
-Common foreign policy.
-Openen borders.
-The association of Independend Democratic / Republican states and Parliamentary or Constitutional Monarchy.
