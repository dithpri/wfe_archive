[i][u][b]Europe is a continent located entirely in the Northern Hemisphere and mostly in the Eastern Hemisphere. It is bordered by the Arctic Ocean to the north, the Atlantic Ocean to the west, the Mediterranean Sea to the south, and Asia to the east. Europe shares the landmass of Eurasia with Asia, and of Afro-Eurasia with both Asia and Africa.[/b][/u][/i]

[u][b]The name, [region]The European Continent Europa[/region] is officially spelled as follows: The European Continent: Europa. The name The European Continent Europa is the combination of the words: continent, Europa, european, the, in which they form this same name. This name was given to this region to be more original, but in fact it can just be shortened to Europe.[/b][/u]
