A nation roleplay region which focuses on indigenous kingdoms and civilizations that once ruled the Americas. This region&#39;s timeline begins during year 1435, seven years after the foundation of the Aztec Empire, often deemed the heart of the Mesoamerican world. A list of civilizations you can relate your nationstate to, or directly name your nationstate after, will be published soon. It is recommended that nations have a historical or semi-historical name.
