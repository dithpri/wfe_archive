North of the Limpopo and South of the Zambesi, there once existed a land of promise and plenty.

Before Mugabe there was Smith
Before there was inflation, we were on par with the pound and dollar
Before there was the trauma of war and tribal strife there was relative harmony 
Before there was political repression there was responsible government
