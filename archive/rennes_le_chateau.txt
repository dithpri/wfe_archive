[i]Rennes-le-Ch&acirc;teau[/i] is a commune in the Aude department in Languedoc in southern [region]France[/region].

The small French hilltop village is known internationally, and receives tens of thousands of visitors per year, for being at the center of various conspiracy theories, and for being the location of an alleged buried treasure... the precise nature of which is disputed by those who believe in its existence, because of the sheer diversity of conflicting theories.
