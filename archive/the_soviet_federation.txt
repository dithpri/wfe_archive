The Soviet Federation is an Ideal Utopia for the working class and mandates that all nations within it to follow these guidelines.
	- The requirement of all workplaces to have internally elected unions
	- These Unions command political authority over the workplace
	- Members of the Unions can then elect for regional authorities that govern the local area
	- The Elected regional authorities elect members to the federal division of governance
This list above only applies to the individual nations within this region. The Soviet Federation wishes to promote the following ideals
	- A classless society
	- The enforcement of Socialism and a classless society whenever, and wherever possible
