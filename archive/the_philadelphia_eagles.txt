The [b]Philadelphia Eagles[/b] are a professional American football team based in Philadelphia. The Eagles compete in the National Football League (NFL) as a member of the National Football Conference (NFC) East division. The team plays its home games at Lincoln Financial Field in the South Philadelphia Sports Complex.

This region was founded on 9 February 2025, on the day of Super Bowl LIX and the second Super Bowl championship win of the team in its history.
