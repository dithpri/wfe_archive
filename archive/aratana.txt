The region of Aratana is a large collection of islands. The climate differs from island to island, but is generally warm during the summer and cool during the winter. Storms often form here, so nations must be prepared to deal with hurricanes and lightning storms.

[b][color=green]OOC RULES:[/color][/b]
-Only one nation is allowed per person.
-All nations must disband their militaries.
-All nations must have some form of democracy.
