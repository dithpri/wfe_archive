An island of three nations, attempting solemnly to live in harmony. The peaceful, beautiful nation of Novi Libertas is the largest and most powerful of the three. It borders the principality of Corpus de Jure, which is a friendly yet barren nation where the citizen&#39;s smiles are just a little too big. The smallest of the three, Humanitas Ex Niliho, is a political hellscape that bends to the will of its dictator, a crazed, environmentally-obsessed madman. Where will you live?
