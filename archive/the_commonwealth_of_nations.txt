[color=darkblue][b]FAQS[/b][/color]
[b][color=darkblue]&amp;#10023;[/color] The Commonwealth does not exchange embassies or Ambassadors.
[color=darkblue]&amp;#10023;[/color] The Commonwealth has no regional government, only administrative and security roles.
[color=darkblue]&amp;#10023;[/color] The Commonwealth recognises any resident nation as a Commonwealth Member State.[/b]
