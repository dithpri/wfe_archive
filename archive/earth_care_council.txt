The Earth Care Council is a political region intended to provide a platform for discussion of environmental protection and sustainable develeopment.

All states who are serious about these issues are welcome, regardless of other political leanings, and are encouraged to take part in conversation on such topics.

The aims of the Earth Care Council are:
-To promote discussion and awareness of all manner of issues pertaining to environmental protection and sustainable developent both IRLand within NS.
-To share possible solutions to environmental problems.
-To drive forward positive environmental policy within the WA.
-To propose and hopefully submit morevaried and better nuanced ISSUES for the NS game.
