Nations on the edge or the earth are old and strong, all working together towards the same goal. Everyone is equal and resources are evenly shared, technology and advancement in areas such as medicine are this regions main goals.

The Edge of the Earth has all a Nation could want and is protected from outside influence by large rock walls  that also serve to keep the oceans from running off the edge.

All those seeking a peaceful place to study and do research are welcome, The Edge of The Earth does not recognize any laws that would slow advancements such as patents or copyrights.
