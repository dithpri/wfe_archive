The Afrikaans Federation, is a union between nations that speak Afrikaans or have any ties to the Afrikaans language and long for a region that is dedicated to provide a space where we can govern, debate and prosper in this beautiful language. 

Ons kort n plek om in ons taal te kan gesels en so n merk te kan maak in die w&ecirc;reld. Word deel van die Afrikaans Federation en help skep aan n omgewing vir almal wat Afrikaans praat.
