[b]History:[/b]
When the final showdown between the Astral Dragon Atruum and the Celestial Primatis took place, Atruum was rendered the loser. His body fell from the heavens, and was purified by the holy weapons of the gods, eventually becoming the continent of Remlia.

[b]There are 5 regions of Remlia:[/b]
-The Southwestern Island Republic of Hasta, located on the Tail.
-The Magic Kingdom of Litus, located on the Lower Body.
-The Central Kingdom of Medius, located on the Torso.
-The Eastern Island Nation of Marlayus, located on the Wing.
-The Northern Federation of Pagnas, located on the Head.
