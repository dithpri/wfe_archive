[color=black][b]The Greater German Reich, or the Third Reich, was the name for Germany under the totalitarian, racist Nazi regime. It began in 1933 after Adolf Hitler&amp;#39;s &amp;quot;election&amp;quot;, and became fact when he became &amp;quot;F&uuml;hrer&amp;quot; in 1934.[/color]

[color=red]From 1933 until 1945, the Reich was home to totalitarianism and ultra-nationalism to a degree never before seen in history, culminating in the Holocaust&amp;#39;s mass-murder of Jews, homosexuals, dissidents and Roma.[/color]

[color=orange]After embarking upon a suicidal war in 1939, the Reich was destroyed by the Allies and the Soviet Union, ceasing to exist in 1945.
