From the Imperial-Area consisting of the Crown of Noctua and the three politically-diverse tributaries: Topua, Reqtuon and Pragat, to The Great Isle, ruled over in part by the gigantic Martanez state, with much land also given to the centralized Church of Our Holy Father, and even to the cold north, desolate lands ruled by the, frankly psychotic, despot of Scanda, the Falenda Continental Organization is a loose alliance of the many nations in the region!
