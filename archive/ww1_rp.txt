WW1 RP SERVER OVERVIEW

This is a World War 1 RP server. You are expected to know a little bit about WW1 and what led up to it. The most important rule is that you try to stay in theme, for example, the Tsar will not ally with The communist rebellion but peace treaties could be possible.
