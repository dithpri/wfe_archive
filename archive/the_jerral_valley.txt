The [region]Jerral Valley[/region] is a land surrounded by fertile hills and mountains, the valley itself however is bleak and desolate. Industry plays an enormous part in the expansion of nations within the [region]Jerral Valley[/region], while the mountains and hills can be utilized to more agricultural purposes. Due to the geographical nature of the region it is nearly impossible to wage war, but defense measures have been taken to assure the safety of the valley. This region however is only a sapling, and will grow into a mighty oak with the assistance of experienced nations.

Nations wishing to join are encouraged to adopt the [b]Jerallian Coin[/b].
