A small cluster of islands that has fallen off of most world maps, the states here have very little in common and are connected only by location and generally dislike for everyone else. The most that can be said for it is that international relations meetings are usually interesting, with heated debates, large feasts, knitted jumpers from grandmas, and encouragement of orgies, occasionally preceded by cult recruitment speeches. Foreign policy is difficult to implement, due to general misunderstanding and distraction tactics, but the diplomats would still rather be here than truly doing work worth a salary.
