Capital City is a region in Tropiconna, where the Pesidential Palace and other important governmental buildings are located. It is also the home to the nations upper class citizens. To access the Capital City and become a citizen of this region you must receive a special password from Tropiconna&#39;s Government. To do so follow these basic steps. 

1. Message Tropiconna &quot;I wish to become a citizen in the Capital City&quot;
2.Wait for password, if not received within 2-4 days message again.

Please note that your citizenship can be revoked at any time for any reason.
