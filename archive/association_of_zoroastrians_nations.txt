The Association of Zoroastrian Nations (AZN) is a global intergovernmental and cultural organization founded to preserve, promote, and unify Zoroastrian communities and nations with historical, spiritual, or cultural ties to Zoroastrianism. Established in Cush City, the capital of the Empire of Cushan, the AZN aspires to serve as a beacon for cultural resilience, religious dialogue, and spiritual renewal in the modern world.
