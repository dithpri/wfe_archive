[b][u]Overview[/u][/b]
The [b]Basileia Rh&amp;#333;mai&amp;#333;n[/b] (&amp;#914;&amp;#945;&amp;#963;&amp;#953;&amp;#955;&amp;#949;&amp;#943;&amp;#945; &amp;#8172;&amp;#969;&amp;#956;&amp;#945;&amp;#943;&amp;#969;&amp;#957;), literally [i]Empire of the Romans[/i], is a Byzantine-inspired region for those who seek to revive the grandeur of Constantinople, the splendor of Hagia Sophia, and the indomitable spirit of Rome in the East.

[b][u]Government[/u][/b]
[list]
[*][b]Basileus[/b] &#150; Supreme sovereign of the Empire. Protector of the Rome of the East. 
[*][b]The Strategoi[/b] &#150; Regional Officers charged with defending the region and ensuring its growth.
