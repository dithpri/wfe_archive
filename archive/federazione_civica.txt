The Federazione Civica was founded on the principles of Civic Republicanism, The Four Freedoms: Freedom of speech, Freedom of worship, Freedom from want and Freedom from fear. We match them to four ideals: Liberty (gold), Justice (red), Peace (white), and to these it adds another: Progress (green), representing the right of all humans to a better, sustainable, future. They four pillars can be found upon blue banner, representing internationalism and the universality of these high aims.
