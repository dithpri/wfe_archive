After two friends formed an army, and captured the country of Zyndaria, a minor feud erupted between the two as to how to govern the country. They resolved this feud by dividing the nation into 2 brother countries, both with radically different government ideals, but both still loyal and friendly to the other. After years of a peaceful time in which neither country contact any other nation except his brother, the two leaders met and decided to invite neighboring countries to join their nation.
