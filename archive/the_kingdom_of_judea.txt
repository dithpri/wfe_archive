Judea is a Jewish State that seceded from the Roman Empire after the Jews drove Roman out of Judea. The Roman decided not to fight back for they are now tired of trying to take Judea back. Judea became a nation in 572 CE and made Eren the leader of the revolt their king and the Temple was rebuilt after it was destoyed in 70 CE. Since regaining independence it has greatly expanded, now controlling nearly all of the Arabian peninsula and Mesopotamia. The Government is Unitary parliamentary democracy under constitutional monarchy which the King is the Head of State and the Prime Minister is the head of Government.
