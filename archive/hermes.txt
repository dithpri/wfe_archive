[b][i][region]Hermes[/region][/i] is a peaceful and slightly prankster region. [i]The Islands of Hermes[/i] are apart of the [i]Confederation of Hermes[/i], a confederation run by its nations and headed by [i]the Main Seven[/i], seven original nations founded in the archipelago. They are welcome to every nation. Just remember to pay [color=gold]Hermes[/color] a gold coin or two.

This region is named after the [i]Greek[/i] [i]god of mischief, thieves, messages, travel, trade, and many other titles.[/i] He also guides souls to the [color=green]Underworld[/color] and is the [i]fastest[/i] among the Greek gods. [i]Son of Zeus and Maia Hermes is named after herms, which are markers in the ground used for travel. The well known Hermes is the combination of Hermes and agriculture god, Pan.[/i][/b]
