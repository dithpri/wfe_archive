[b][i][region]Hermes[/region][/i][/b] is a peaceful and slightly prankster region. [b]The Islands of Hermes[/b] are welcome to every nation. Just remember to pay [color=gold]Hermes[/color] a gold coin or two.

&#151;&#151;&#151;&#151;&#151;&#151;&#151;&#151;&#151;&#151;&#151;&#151;&#151;&#151;&#151;&#151;&#151;&#151;&#151;&#151;&#151;&#151;&#151;&#151;

This region is named after the [b]Greek[/b] [i]god of mischief, thieves, messages, travel, trade, and many other things that if listed would make this paragraph an essay.[/i] He also guides souls to the [color=green]Underworld[/color] and is the [b]fastest[/b] among the Greek gods. [i][b]Son of Zeus and Maia Hermes is named after herms, which are markers in the ground used for travel. The well known Hermes is the combination of Hermes and Pan (god of fertility and agriculture coming from pre-hellenic origins).[/b][/i]
