[color=#ED4719]Kharkiv Oblast is an oblast in eastern Ukraine. The oblast is the third-most populous of Ukraine, with a population of 2,598,961 in 2021, more than half (1.42 million) of whom live in the city of Kharkiv, the oblast&#39;s administrative center. This oblast, including major cities like [region]Izyum[/region] and Kupiansk, have been the site of heavy fighting due to the unjustified Russian invasion of Ukraine that started in 2022.
