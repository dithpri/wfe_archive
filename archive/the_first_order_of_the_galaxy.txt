The First Order of the Galaxy is a powerful group that follows the teachings of a former region that had control of most of the Galaxy. The Order is ruled by a single person that has a family relation to the old and powerful region, the ruler also in times of need will consult an elder that is incredibly powerful and almighty. The Order is a technologically advanced region that pools together its resources in order to build weapons to take control and to possess absolute power among the galaxy. Join me and we shall rule the Galaxy together, and no one can stop us!
