Northern Yugoslavia
=================

Northern Yugoslavia is a Federation of States and Remnants of the original Yugoslavian Federation that would collapse in 1997 following multiple civil wars and conflicts.
Located in the Northern Balkan Peninsula the Federation today consists of many of the original states and remnants of the original Yugoslavian Federation.
The Northern Yugoslavian Federation today is the remnant of the original Yugoslavian Federation consisting of many of the original states which each share a common belief and idea of a united Yugoslavia.
