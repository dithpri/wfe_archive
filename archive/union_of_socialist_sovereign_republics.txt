The Union of Socialist Sovereign State is a Presidential Republic were  
the President of the Republic is a Ceremonial Position in the Government.
the Paramount Leader of the Government and State Council is the Executive Head of State and Head of Government and Commander in Chief of the Armed Forces 
legislative branch which includes the State Assembly and Seats 500. 
the Majority Party is the Republican Party with 450 Seats and the Minority is the Democratic Party with 50 Seats.
