The Lascandrish alliance is a region founded by the Republic of Lascandra in September 2022. Its a democratically administrated region with elections taking place every year. The region improves and progresses every day. The biggest metropoly of the region and its financial, administrative and cultural capital is Nikipole, which is also the most important city of the region both politically and economically.
