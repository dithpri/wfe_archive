[color=green][b][i]Indus Valley civilization, oldest in the world 10,000- 5,000 years, covered present Pakistan. In Second millennium B.C., remnants of this culture fused with the migrating Indo-Aryan peoples. Area underwent successive invasions from the Persians, Greeks, Scythians, Arabs (who brought Islam), Afghans, and Turks. The Mughal Empire flourished in the 16th and 17th centuries; British colonized it in 18th century. 1947 British India separated into the Muslim state of Pakistan (with West and East sections) and largely Hindu India was never satisfactorily resolved, and India and Pakistan fought two wars - in 1947-48 and 1965 - over the disputed Kashmir territory. 3rd war in 1971 - resulted in East Pakistan becoming Bangladesh.[/b][/i][/color]
