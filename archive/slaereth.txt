This huge continent stretches across multiple regions, from Polar in the North, past the equator, all the way through to Cold in the South.
One side of continent lies on a tectonic boundary; the North-Western part of the land is separated by a huge mountainous ridge and is quite warm.
Slaereth has 31 active volcanoes and several dormant ones., and earthquakes are very rare and very weak.
The winds in this region are dangerously fast, with large storms and extreme weather being frequent. 
The sea to the North and East of Slaereth is tretcherous, while the seas to the South and West are calmer. 
Slaereth is home to a huge amount of unique plants and animals that are found only in this region.
