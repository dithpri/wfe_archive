Slaereth is a absolutely massive continent that stretches across multiple regions, from polar in the North, cold in the midlands, all the way through to a cold temperature in the South. the North-Western part of the land is separated by a large mountainous ridge That leads to the Amerdos Peninsula the all year round warm area of the continent.Slaereth has some volcanoes, and does rarely experiences any earthquakes. The winds in this region are usually fast, with large snow storms and extreme weather being common but In the summer time the weather is calm. The seas are rough, while the seas to the North are gentle. Slaereth is abundant variety of creatures and plants, and a huge amount of those are found only in this region
