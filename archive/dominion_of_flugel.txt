Welcome to the Dominion of Flugel, an isolated militaristic nation known for its disciplined society and advanced military prowess. Shrouded in mystery, Flugel has intentionally cut itself off from the world, with speculations ranging from hidden riches to a desire for utopian independence. Governed by a council of military leaders, Flugel stands as a symbol of strength and self-reliance in an ever-changing global landscape.
