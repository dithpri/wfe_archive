The Peacekeepers is a group which aims to provide a safe haven for those who do not want to be attacked by other nations or regions. As the name suggests, the region only fights if a nation is attempting to break the peace by attacking the group and its allies. That is if the majority of the nations in an immediate meeting approves it is the best course of action.

Join if you are a nation who believes peace is paramount amongst the world. This region is best for nations who have peaceful intentions for its allies, its region and its own self and is willing to bring peace and harmony to nations who need it most.
