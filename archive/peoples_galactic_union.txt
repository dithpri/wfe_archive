The People&#39;s Galactic Union is an alternate version of the Galactic Empire in which the Empire (originally Fascist &amp; Imperialistic), adopted Progressive Communism, Collectivism, Spartan Militarism, &amp; Expansionism &amp; rejected the regressive &amp; cruel policies of the Original Empire. The People&#39;s Galactic Union is the 1st Empire to unite all rims of the galaxy, brutally crushing the Hutt Clan of the Outer Rim. It is the ultimate Star Wars army, one that collectively utilizes every warrior, weapon, &amp; resource in the galaxy.
