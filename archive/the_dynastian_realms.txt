Founded by the Dynastia Reich, the Dynastian Realms is full of countries or states that are autonomous to the Dynastian Reich. It consists of allies all across the globe.

The Dynastian Reich itself is a proud upstanding nation, its people massacred by the Weeb Federation. This group is a so called world power. Each nation in it receives Dynastian technology which is very advanced for the modern era.
The group&#39;s main purpose is to protect the glorious fatherland, to protect each others honour.
War is common to achieve our goal, multiple enemies will stand in our way but the TDR will not back down to any challenge.

Only the most honourable nations may gain access to this elitest group.
