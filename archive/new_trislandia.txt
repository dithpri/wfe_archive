**TRILISH ISLAND REGION**

New Trislandia is located east of America in the Atlantic. Composed of 7 islands, the governing nation is the Federal Republic of New Trislands (FRNT). Discovered in 1613 by English settlers near Jamestowne, the region is inhabited to American civilians and British immigrants. The FRNT Government is a democratic process, allowing national and local elections. The Islands are each separate states which have a legislative, executive, and judicial branch.
