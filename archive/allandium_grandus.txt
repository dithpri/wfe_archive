[b]Allandium Grandus is what remains of the old Allandium Empire.[/b] It seeks to rejuvenate and reform the Allandium state, after the relatively recent governmental collapse. Allandium Grandus, similarly to its predecessor, functions as an autocratic state, the primary difference being that power is invested in an individual from the ruling party, the Allandium Industralist Party (AIP), than a royal dynasty. 

[b]The Nation of Allandium Grandus will stay in this region until it is ready to embrace the outside world. This will be done by developing the Country, the Government, and ensuring the safety of the Party.[/b]
