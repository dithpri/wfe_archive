Maudia is a region of diverse landscapes. It has forests and mountains to the west, lush rainforests in the south, grasslands in the center, tundra in the north, and deserts to the east. Maudia is bordered on the east and west by oceans, and in all other directions, other regions.

The climate varies widely. In the forests, it snows regularly in the winter and rains regularly in the summer. In the deserts, it barely gets any precipitation and is the 3rd driest in the world. The grasslands are regularly hit by severe thunderstorms in the spring and summer, and snows in the winter/late fall. The rainforests get rain all year round. The tundra is normally snow covered but during the summer it isn&#39;t. The mountains have snow year round.
