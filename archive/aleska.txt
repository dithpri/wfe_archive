[b] The Continent of Aleska. [/b]

Aleska is a continent in the Belinda, a small Earth-like planet with oceans and tropical rainforests that cover much of its continents. Belinda has an abundance of forests, which are managed by a high degree of automation. Aleska nevertheless has a large population, localized in several large cities. The inhabitants of the Belinda planet designate Malas, a large continent in the southern hemisphere of Belinda, as a nature reserve, protecting it from development and destruction.
