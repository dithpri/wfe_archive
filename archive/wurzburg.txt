[b]Wurzburg[/b] is a city in the region of Franconia, northern Bavaria, Germany. Located on the Main River, it is the capital of the Regierungsbezirk of Lower Franconia. The regional dialect is East Franconian.

Wurzburg lies about equidistant from Frankfurt am Main and from Nuremberg (each about 75 miles away).  The city has a population of around 124,000 people. -- Wikipedia[hr]
