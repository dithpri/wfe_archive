Quip Network is a civil and military alliance with 3 core principles:
[list][*] The World Assembly is fundamentally a tool of raiders who wish to colonize and destroy innocent regions, and imperialist powers who wish to force their own laws upon the world as a whole. Therefore, no region with an executive governor (except for defenders) should encourage its members to participate in such an assembly, and should instead maintain its own forum.
[*] Weapons of mass destruction are the best means of conflict deterrence known to date, and any military alliance should have a nuclear sharing program.
[*] Humanity has a unique right to build civilizations and extract resources.
