Welcome to our Alternate History Role-Play Game! Rewrite history from 1914 to 2050 by taking control of a nation. Shape the outcome of key events like the World Wars, the digital age, and more. Will you choose peace or war? Technological advancement or tradition? Form alliances, face conflicts, and make decisions that will impact the world. With each real-world update representing a month in-game, this immersive experience blends strategy, negotiation, and historical twists. Join us and leave your mark on history!
