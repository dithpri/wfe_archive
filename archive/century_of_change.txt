The Nineteenth Century was an era of change and advancement. Industrialisation had taken root in the Western Nations, bringing societal upheaval from the working classes. Women demanded rights after millennia of repression. Ethnic groups arose, demanding lands of their own united by a common tongue.

The Napoleonic Wars are raging across Europe, and many countries are taking sides. However, others retain their neutrality, fearing the alternative. France has already conquered large swathes of land in Belgium and Italy, and is showing no sign of stopping. The fate of Europe hangs in the ballots.
