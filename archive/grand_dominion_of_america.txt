The year is 1783, The American Colonies are have been ravage by a civil war sparked by separatists rebelling to form an independent nation. Influential rebel leaders, such as Benjamin Franklin, Thomas Jefferson, John Adams, Samuel Adams and several others have been tried, convicted and executed. 
Reforms have been enacted, to keep the American populace content. An American Parliament is set up, and the colonies are given status as Provinces, forming a Grand British Dominion, under the rule of Great Britain.
