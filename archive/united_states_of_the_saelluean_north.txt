The USTSN is a coalition of nations residing within the Saelluean North, being an alliance focused on economic, scientific and cultural growth.

The region is heavily opposed to Communism, liberalism and democracy whilst being neutral towards fascism (Due to not experiencing it), Capitalism and Technocracy.
