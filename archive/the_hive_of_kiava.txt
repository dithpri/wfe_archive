The Hive of Kiava is a collection of nations of all different political, economic, social, and cultural values, covering an incredibly diverse number of ecosystems from cold, isolated mountainous regions to warm, breezy island beaches. Despite these many differences, the Hive of Kiava stands united by eternal bonds of friendship and goodwill.
