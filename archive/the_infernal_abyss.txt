A multi dimensional prison whose surface has been developed by several races but is controlled by the Wraiths, incarnations of evil spirits who are the guards of the abyss and its reality crushing prisoners. The ruler is the Overlord, who laid claim to the Abyss through act of defeating the previous ruler of the Abyss, the Forgotten God. It seems that a rift between the Abyss and the reality of Nationstates has opened...
