     A group of close-together islands. The nations are usually friendly and preserve nature, give their people freedoms, and try to keep everybody happy. Animals that were thought to be extinct are not uncommon in a lot of the islands; Ornithomimids are common pets in Luxintenebris. 
     Join if your nation    1. is nature-friendly    2. is based around keeping citizens happy   3. is somewhat pacifistic.
