The Haimain Endora is a small nation known for its lush rainforests and large fresh-water lakes.
The Haimain Endora was founded in 1267 when a woman named Haleiah Mocci founded the island-nation whilst sailing the Atlantic ocean. Haleiah brought back her friends and family along with hopeful people to form this nation form the Mediterranean. Now, many years later, new rulers are forming a beautiful and populous nation as one.
