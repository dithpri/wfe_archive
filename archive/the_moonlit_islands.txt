The Great Moonlit Republic of Mendanau and the Grand Oceanic Republic of Bircheshire Isle make up [url=http://www.nswiki.net/index.php?title=The_Moonlit_Islands]the Moonlit Islands (TMI) region[/url], a sphere of influence in the middle north latitudes of the vast Trinity Sea. Though independent nations, Mendanau and Bircheshire Isle govern and defend the sphere as a singular entity.
