The United Socialist States of America is a Democratic Socialist region consisting of federated, yet independent socialist states. 

[b]Regional Motto:[/b] [i]&amp;quot;For liberty, community and the protection of every precious man, woman and child.&amp;quot;[/i]

[b]Regional Legislation:[/b] At current, the only power the region has over member states comes in the form of Regional Legislation. Member States wishing to prepose legislation may submit a proposal to the Administrator, who by law, must begin poll regarding this legislation. Proposals become law as soon as an Overriding Majority of Votes (minimum of 55% in favor) has been reached.
