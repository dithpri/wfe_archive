[region]The Imperial City[/region], in the heartland of Tamriel, is the ancient seat of power in Tamriel and the capital of the Empire. From the First Era into the Fourth Era, the White-Gold Tower continues to be the center of Tamrielic cultures and the beacon of the Empire, being the seat of the Imperial government and home to the Emperor. The Imperial City has led Tamriel in culture, fashion, economics and politics for centuries.
