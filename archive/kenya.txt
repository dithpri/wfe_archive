[b]Kenya[/b]

An East African republic on the equator, with the Indian Ocean to the South-East, also bordered by [region=Tanzania], [region=Uganda], [region=South Sudan], [region=Ethiopia] and [region=Somalia].

Capital: Nairobi
Independence: 12 Dec 1963 (United Kingdom)
Area: 580,367 sq km
Population: 43,013,341 (2012 est.)
