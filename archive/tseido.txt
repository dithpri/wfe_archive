Tse&eacute;do is a continent on a world not unlike our own, home to countless civilizations that rose and fell in great flowering cycles like any other. Power was concentrated with the Traders in the southern regions of the continent for much of its history-- until the Undoing, a colossal planetary warming disaster only known to not be caused by man, turned the tables in unimaginable ways. 

From the ashes came [nation]Saerdipho[/nation], a patchwork union of southern Traders, trying to reclaim the glory of their ancestors. Closely following it was [nation]Vophiu[/nation] in the North, monopolizing the Tse&eacute;dan oil trade with its legions of mechs and great barons.
