The region of Namalea is a series of archipelagos with a rich history of conflict, unification, revolution, and break ups. The land is inhabited by a variety of ethnic groups ranging from the slavic-germanic people&amp;#39;s of northern Namalea to the islanders of southern Namalea. Human presence in Namalea dates back to the dawn of civilization when hunter-gather tribes ruler over the land.
