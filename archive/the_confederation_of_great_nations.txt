The Confederation of Mighty Nations is a proud and strong union founded by the proud Republic of Ugrelia. It is, in the meantime, small but has hopes to grow into a large and powerful union one day.

The Confederation of Mighty Nations strongly believes in democracy and for those whose country is apart of it will certainly find that out. There will be referendums, elections and all sorts of votes in order to do what is best for the region. 

There will be certain offices in which people can campaign for which includes Border Control, Communications and a few more

The goal of this union is to grow strong, powerful and known.
