The Independent Sovereign Community of Bloodied Ghosts, growing tired of the prattle of its North Pacific neighbors, declared all territory under its claim to be part of a new region in early Two Thousand and Fourteen Common Era.

This fledgling region, Kyrbainya (named for Kyrbain, a hero from Bloodied Ghosts&amp;#39; formative uprising), immediately -- and strangely -- began dislodging itself from the North Pacific continent proper; at this time, a river of moderate width separates the two regions.
