Modeled in spirit after the vast bureaucratic machinery of the Soviet-era [b]Ministry of Railways[/b], this region is not merely a staging region, but rather, it is an institution.

Like its historical inspiration, which oversaw an immense and interconnected rail system across an entire union, this Ministry governs a centralized network of locomotives, each functioning as part of a greater whole. Where others rely on chaos or improvisation, the Ministry relies on structure and scale.
