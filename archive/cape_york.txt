[region]Cape York[/region] Peninsula is a peninsula located in Far North [b]Queensland, Australia[/b]. It is the largest wilderness in northern Australia. The land is mostly flat and about half of the area is used for grazing cattle.

[b]Location:[/b] Queensland, Australia
[b]Offshore water bodies:[/b] Coral Sea, Torres Strait, Gulf of Carpentaria
[b]River:[/b] Jardine River
