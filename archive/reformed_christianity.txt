We uphold:

1) [b]The Five Solas of the Christian Reformation[/b]: Sola scriptura; Sola fide; Sola gratiae; Solus Christus; and Soli Deo Gloria (By Scripture alone, by faith alone, by grace alone, by Christ alone, and glory to God alone).

2) [b]The five points of Calvinism[/b]: Total depravity; unconditional election; limited atonement; irresistible grace; and perseverance of the saints.

3) [b]Classical cessationism[/b].
