Razor Valley is characterized by its challenging rocky terrain which consists of rugged mountains, barren deserts, and unforgiving coastlines, making it a tough environment for its inhabitants to thrive in. The people have adapted to these conditions by developing a strong sense of self-reliance and independence, valuing their individual rights above all else. This has fostered a culture centered around personal freedom and autonomy, where individuals are encouraged to carve out their own path and make their own choice. Consequently, the people of this region are known for their resilience, resourcefulness, and unwavering commitment to upholding their values of freedom and individuality.
