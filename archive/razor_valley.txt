World Factbook Entry - Razor Valley

Natural Resources:
Razor Valley is known for its abundant oil reserves, which play a crucial role in its economy.
The island is rich in various minerals, including:
Copper
Iron
Zinc
Rare Earth Elements

Main Industries:
Oil Extraction - The backbone of the economy, providing most employment opportunities.
Mining - Significant mining activities for various minerals, contributing to the national income.
Agriculture - Limited due to the harsh winters but includes hardy crops suited to the temperate summer climate.
