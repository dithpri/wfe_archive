Born in between a vast ocean expanse lays a regional continent rich in natural beauty. The landscape is diverse with mountains that extend from southern polar ice caps to tropical islands in the north and forests in the eastern regions. Cutoff from the rest of the world a new way of life was born by adapting to this strange but beautiful landmass. Human habitation ranges from thousands of small tribal communities to major industrialized nations. In this region a diverse array of political, economic, and social systems have been born through isolation and recent interactions with the outside world. Welcome all RP players.
