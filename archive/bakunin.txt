He is considered among the most influential figures of anarchism, and one of the principal founders of the &amp;quot;social anarchist&amp;quot; tradition. Bakunin&amp;#39;s enormous prestige as an activist made him one of the most famous ideologues in Europe, and he gained substantial influence among radicals throughout Russia and Europe.[/color] &#151;Wikipedia[hr]
