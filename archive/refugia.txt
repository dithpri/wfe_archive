[color=#034277][b][region]Refugia[/region][/b] is an association of communities within [b]Calamity Refuge[/b].

This region of islands is located on the divide between the Hipster Sea and the Sea of Art, in the North Connection Ocean. Communities are permitted and welcome to transport to the region via variation magic and moonrunes, provided they are in compliance with the Code of Conduct.

Management of the region occurs on the 16th floor of the Admin Tower. Witness our forums and Discord, located on the internet, and say hi.[/color]
