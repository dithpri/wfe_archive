[colour=#034277][b][region]Refugia[/region][/b] is a left-leaning association of island nations in support of government integrity, human rights and unabashed environmentalism. This region of islands is located between the Hipster Sea and the Sea of Art, in the North Connection Ocean.

Here, refugees become Refugi and build a better region for this world.[/colour]
