Refugia is an association of communities and sites within the CalRef Network.

This region of islands is located on the divide between the Hipster Sea and the Sea of Art, in the North Connection Ocean. Island communities are permitted transport to the region via variation magic and moonrunes, provided their respective leaders are in compliance with the Code of Conduct.

Management of the region is occurs on the 16th and 17th floors of the Admin Tower, located in central Refuge Isle.
