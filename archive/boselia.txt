The Federal Republic of Boselia is a country that consist three republics and one autonomous region. Republics are Widonia, Einarvia and Rimolia, and the autonomous region is called Siprutia. Country was when three countries became independent. Country was at the side of the central powers in the First World War and on the side of Axis Powers in the Second World War. During the Cold War, Boselia declared itself Neutral and therefore was allowed to keep its independence.
