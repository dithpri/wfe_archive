[color=red][b]Ace of Aces[/b][/color] is a title accorded to the top active ace within a branch of service in a nation&#39;s military in time of war. - Wikipedia

[hr]

A [color=blue][b]National Hero[/b][/color] is a person noted for feats of courage or nobility of purpose, especially one who has risked or sacrificed his or her life - Free Dictionary

[hr]

A [color=purple][b]SuperHero[/b][/color] is a fictional hero having extraordinary or superhuman powers - Merriam-Webster

[hr]

A [color=green][b]Hero[/b][/color] is an exceptionally skillful or successful person - Merriam-Webster
