Region Overview:

Verdantia is a region renowned for its natural beauty and bountiful resources. Located in the heart of the continent, it is home to vast expanses of lush forests, fertile farmland, and rolling hills. Its temperate climate and abundant rainfall make it an ideal location for agriculture, and the region produces a wide variety of crops and livestock.

Politics:

Verdantia is a federal republic, with a president serving as the head of state and a prime minister serving as the head of government. The legislative branch is composed of a bicameral parliament, with a lower house and an upper house.
