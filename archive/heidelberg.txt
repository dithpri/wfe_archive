[b]Heidelberg[/b] is a city situated on the river Neckar in south-west Germany. The fifth-largest town in the State of Baden-W&uuml;rttemberg after Stuttgart, Karlsruhe, Mannheim and Freiburg im Breisgau, Heidelberg is part of the densely populated Rhine-Neckar Metropolitan Region. &#151;Wikipedia[hr]
