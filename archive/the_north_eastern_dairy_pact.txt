The North Eastern Dairy Pact (TNEDP) is a developing international region founded by the Commonwealth of Greater Planthereal. The pact was created to promote cooperation, stability, and mutual prosperity between its member nations.

Originally conceived as a regional alliance focused on economic coordination and agricultural exchange, TNEDP has gradually evolved into a broader political and security partnership. Member nations share common interests in maintaining regional stability, encouraging trade, and supporting technological and industrial development.This region was founded to create and economic and military alliance to focus on Defense forces,Nuclear defense,and Army cooperation.
