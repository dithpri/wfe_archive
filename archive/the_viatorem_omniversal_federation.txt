The Viatorem Omniversal Federation, or VOF, aims to be the largest collective of civilizations and peoples in the known omniverse, with members including everything from the tiniest of island nations to the largest of multiversal unions. The VOF was founded on the principle of providing a single platform on which civilizations far and wide can unify and collaborate together to drive forward the betterment and progression of not just the involved members but the whole of reality and all of its citizens.
