The Free World Democratic Coalition is a region based around freedom, rights and equality, willing to defend those ideals with the cooperation of its memberstates through the use of bureaucracy and armed intervention if needed. Memberstates, fierce and loyal activists of democracy, help eachother in time of need and welcome anyone willing to defend those ideals with open arms.
