Razor Coast is a starkly beautiful, yet inhospitable region, characterized by its cold climate and rocky, unforgiving terrain. Its vast, windswept expanses and jagged geological formations present significant challenges to habitation and development. This territory is remarkably endowed with valuable natural resources. It boasts significant deposits of oil and a wide array of minerals, with a particularly high density of rare earth minerals, making it a strategically vital area for global resource markets.
