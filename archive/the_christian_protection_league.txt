[i]De Facto[/i]: An alliance consisting of the United North American Alliance (UNAA) and all of its puppets, client states and states within its sphere of influence.

The client states are all heavily under the influence of the UNAA, with strong dependence on the UNAA for protection, economic growth and governance.
