The World of Vale was formed in the First Age by the New Gods the Children of the Old Ones. The First Life was created in the Second Age, by the middle of the Second Age the First Life had developed and began organizing into many Kingdoms, Empires and Republics. The Four Elder Races divided up the World between them, Dwarves, Elves, Men and Orcs. The year is now 530 S.E and there is another 420 years to go before the Third Age begins, the world is at peace and it has been over 200 years since the last conflict....but war brews. Rumours spread that the Old Ones have returned with their Darkness.....only time will tell if this world will see another terrible war. (Fantasy RP Forum)
