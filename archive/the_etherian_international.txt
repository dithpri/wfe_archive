The Etherian International is a grouping of all nations of the planet of Etheria, and most of these nations are left wing in some sort of way. It is a loose economic and political alliance. The Cortexist International operates in Etheria, seeking to spread global communism. Headquarters of both are present in Cortexgrad, [nation]Neocortexia[/nation].

It seeks to promote peace and friendship among all nations of Etheria, and oppose rising militarism. The Etherian Criminal Court and the Etherian Red Cross are a part of the International.
