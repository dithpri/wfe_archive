Constitution of the Union

1. All WA delegates can endorse any one of legal status
2. All nations should be active at least once per week 
3. Every nation has the right to free speech but can be revoked if provocative or heretical 
4. You must not harass anyone for their believes
5. Do not betray the Union to foreign forces or selfishness
6. Legal Nations will not be banished or punished without fair trial
7. The Union is a Federal Republic with equal representation for all
8. All future laws will be voted upon by all nations of Parliament (2/3) then enacted by the Chancellor
9. Power Structure: Founder, Chancellor,  Prime Minister and Pope,  Ministers , and Nations
