Nestled along the southeastern coast of the subcontinent, the Free Republic of Tamil Nadu is a vibrant, multilingual federation known for its ancient Dravidian heritage, cutting-edge technology sectors, and deeply democratic traditions. Founded after a peaceful secession from a collapsing union, Tamil Nadu forged its identity as a republic centered on civic virtue, intellectual rigor, and linguistic pride.

Federal Parliamentary Republic

Capital: Thanjavur (administrative), Chennai (economic and cultural)
