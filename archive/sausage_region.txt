Sausage Region was founded by Sir Randy Savage the leader of the nation Motherpudsy Botch. This mid-sized region is currently troubled by environmental issues. The citizens are proud of their local attitude and mostly secular. They are bordered on three sides by rivers. Their relations with their neighbors to the northeast are peaceful, while relations with neighbors to the north are antagonistic. There are 4 major nations, the largest of which is located in the southeast of the region.
