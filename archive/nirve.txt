[region]Nirve[/region]
A small continent far away from any other Landmass. Is a Region that was once Home of Magic and Fantasy but these times have long since passed. To Southwest of the Continent you find Icy Steps of Glaciem. To the West the Expansive Desert of Emirial, to the Northwest the lush Jungles of Pandomania, to the North the cold and harsh Land that is Pagos, to the East the moderate Lands of Luminantes and to the Southwest the great Forests of Forestella.
