[color=blue]The Suicide Squad is the name of a fictional antihero team appearing in American comic books published by DC Comics. 

A team of incarcerated supervillains who carry out secret missions in exchange for reduced prison sentences. The Suicide Squad&#39;s name alludes to the dangerous nature of their missions. The team is based out of Belle Reve Penitentiary under the directorship of Amanda Waller.

The current incarnation of the team appears in the fifth volume of the Suicide Squad comic series, and the recurring members include Captain Boomerang, Deadshot, Enchantress, Harley Quinn, Katana, and Killer Croc.[/color]
