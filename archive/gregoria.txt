Upon the ruins of the three great nations of Bomarth, Rego and Minder a new nation of The Armed Republic of Bogmin was created. Upon the ruins of this once great region General Casico of Bogmin decided to rebuild the region by any means necessary. He sent a telegram to his comrade, Mister Moo of The Flotilla of Moo, to help rebuild the region and together they set out to rebuild the once great nation of Gregoria.
