The United Empire of the Islamic Caliphate is a federal democratic empire governed under Islamic constitutional law. Executive authority is shared between a Caliph-Emperor and an elected Imperial Council, while constituent Wilayats exercise broad internal autonomy. The capital is Al-Quds al-Idariyya and the largest city is Riyadh. The economy is large and diversified, with energy, finance, logistics, advanced agriculture, and space research as key sectors. The state maintains a professional military focused on defensive deterrence and regional stability, and pursues multilateral diplomacy with limited security cooperation against extremist groups.
