The Council for Peace and Growth (CPG) is founded upon the conviction that durable peace, meaningful consultation, and shared prosperity are inseparable foundations of civilized international order.

The Council seeks to cultivate a stable community of sovereign states committed to non-aggression, mutual respect, economic advancement, and long-term regional harmony. It does not exist to erase national differences, but to provide a framework through which differences may coexist without conflict and cooperation may flourish without coercion.

In this spirit, the Council for Peace and Growth aspires to become:
	&#149;	a guardian of peace,
	&#149;	a forum of consultation,
	&#149;	a platform for development,
	&#149;	and a durable instrument of shared stability and prosperity.
