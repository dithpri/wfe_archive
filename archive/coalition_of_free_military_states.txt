The Coalition of Free States is a set of countries devoted to a free and utopialike society, where the borders of the participants border on abolished. 

The coalition shares a national defence force, consisting of at least 60,000 conscripted or voluntarily enlisted soldiers, with a international global equipment standard and linguistic standard of Newgerman.

For additional priority in national defence institutes and votes, a nation may provide a set of select elite troops.
