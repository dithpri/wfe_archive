&amp;#945; Pictoris is a sub-giant star of spectral class A7 in the constellation of Pictor. &amp;#945; Pictoris visual magnitude is 3.27. Because of its moderate brightness, &amp;#945; Pictoris is easily visible to the naked eye from locations with dark skies, while it might be barely visible, or not visible at all, from light-polluted skies.
