In 2022, a nuclear war between the United States and East Asian Commune due to the government botching the war on Isis. The war was utterly devastating, and nearly the whole world was engulfed in atomic flame. The few survivors formed new societies, and rose new banners over what was once the Old World. Man went back to his tribal roots, worshiping pagan gods and idols. The intense radiation mutated animals, and clean water was rare. But somehow, through sheer human willpower and ingenuity, mankind survived!
