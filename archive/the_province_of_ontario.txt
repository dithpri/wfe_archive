[color=red]Ontario is one of the ten provinces of Canada, located in east-central Canada.It is Canada&amp;#39;s most populous province by a large margin, accounting for nearly 40 percent of all Canadians, and is the second largest province in total area. Ontario is fourth largest in total area when the territories of the Northwest Territories and Nunavut are included.It is home to the nation&amp;#39;s capital city, Ottawa, and the nation&amp;#39;s most populous city, Toronto.[/color]
