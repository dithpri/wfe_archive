The EIFA is a region of geographically close nations, which decided to join their forces and build a strong communicative system.
Its cardinal points are to avoid war, and to be able to compete with further away nations, which started to threaten them because of the advancement of the means of transports.

However incompatible their ideologies may seem to be, these nations managed to put their differences aside to build a strong coalition. 
The founding countries are Antaca, Hasponie, and Lybe.
