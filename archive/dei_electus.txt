The two powerful nations of Thornstein and Spartany, banded together to create the powerful and conserative region of Dei Electus. Considering themselves to be chosen by God to bring back the glory days the bygone era of the Roman Empire, the two different yet similar nations banded together with their armies and economies. They now stand as the staunch vanguard against Communists, heathens and those who seek to pollute and poison the old ways.
