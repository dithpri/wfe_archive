Diadem, also designated as &amp;#945; Comae Berenices (alpha Comae Berenices), is a variable and multiple main-sequence star of spectral class F5 in the constellation of Coma Berenices. Diadem visual magnitude is 5.22. Because of its faintness, Diadem is visible to the naked eye only from locations with very dark skies, while it is not visible at all from skies affected by light pollution.
