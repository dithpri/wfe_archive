Welcome to [B]Elprus[/B], a region aimed at quality N&amp;I roleplay, based around a collection of continents and archipelagoes located within the wider NSverse. We are modern-tech and aim for realism, but above everything, we pursue fun and a good RP experience for our members through writing and interaction, as well as a close, friendly community free of drama and division. We are open to both experienced roleplayers and aspiring writers.
