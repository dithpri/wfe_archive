Named for the once ruler of this land The Region is now a broken network of powers attempting to reunite a once great realm.

The laws and ideologies within the region vary greatly. 
Many states have fallen into anarchy or seen the rise of a psychotic dictator and the few democratic sates have been ineffectual

Their are a few beacons of power and stability however.
The Holy Moly of 01101100 is an old power that has provided guidance to the newer nations
Bad Boy Teenager Club has provided an important service through their work on the world council
Ishpishdia is a bastion of safety and order who use their advanced technology to ensure the safety of the region, by any means necessary.
