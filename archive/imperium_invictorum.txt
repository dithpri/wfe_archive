[b]Imperium Invictorum:[/b] Empire of the unconquerable! 

This region is for the lands of monarchies, for the strong and for the devout.

[b]Our Values:[/b]
Monarchy and Theocracy. 
We believe in the system where one king who with divine right rules over his people. There is a balance between the government and the faith. 

[b]Our Ideologies:[/b]
Traditionalism, Conservatism, Integralism, Monarchism, Patriotism, and Capitalism. We uphold the traditions of our ancestors and respect our culture and identity.
