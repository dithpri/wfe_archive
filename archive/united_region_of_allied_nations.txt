NATIONAL NEWS BULLETIN========================================================================================

NEW ACT
The URAN Supreme Congress, acting under orders from the United Republic of the MF, hereby raises regional military funding to 300 Trillion Euro-Marks, increases space project funding to 75 Trillion Euro-Marks, and raises Department of Immigration and Customs funding to a new high of 100 Trillion Euro Marks.


[color=red]We are signatories of the International Defence and Trade Authority.[/color]

[Color=Red]The Increase in Government Spending to 100 Centillion Euro-Marks on Defense has been Ruled Void by the Chief Justice of the URAN Supreme Court.[/color]
