Gleeb Glorb! The Great Glorb is a cluster of derpy, loosely organized alien nations known for their lighthearted approach to governance and everyday life. Comprising a series of planets and cosmic territories, these nations thrive on humour, confusion, and a love for shiny objects. Citizens across The Great Glorb are renowned for their quirkiness, fondness for wobbly creatures, and a general attitude of relaxed whimsy.
