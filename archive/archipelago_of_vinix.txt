[b][nation]Vinix[/nation][/b], officially the [b]Republic of The Archipelago of Vinix[/b] or the [b]Vinician Republic[/b] is a federal island country.

The capital city [b]Vinicia[/b] was founded by [nation]the first vinician settlers[/nation] on the [b]Island of Vinix[/b], the other main islands are the [url=https://www.nationstates.net/nation=Vinicula][b]Island of Vinicula[/b][/url] and the outlying [url=https://www.nationstates.net/nation=Vinician Islets][b]Vinician Islets[/b][/url].
