Capella, also designated as &amp;#945; Aurigae (alpha Aurigae), is a variable and multiple giant star of spectral class G5 in the constellation of Auriga. Capella visual magnitude is 0.08, making it the 6th brightest star in the sky. Because of its brightness, Capella is clearly visible to the naked eye when observed from locations with dark skies, and should be also quite easily visible from light polluted areas.
