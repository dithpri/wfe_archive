Greetings, and welcome to [region]The International Topkek Union[/region].

Our Region accepts all kinds of thoughts and/or ideologies as long as they accept others. Our Union is a place where we help each other out and work together.
[region]The International Topkek Union[/region] works something like a country, there are (currently) [b]3[/b] seats in the [u]parliament[/u] where voting on submissions from the [u]administration[/u]. The seats in the parliament is given through voting, one country can only have one seat, but countries may form factions with equal thoughts on changes. The faction with most votes also gets to be the administrating faction. They are the ones who currently (rule) [region]The International Topkek Union[/region] and give suggestions on changes that the parliament then votes on.
