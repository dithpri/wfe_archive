The United Sovereign States of the Potomac is a group of independent, largely capitalist nations. The region is governed by a yet-to-be-established Congress composed of elected member-states from various areas as a legislative body and an executive body consisting of a Chancellor, Vice-Chancellor, and a number of elected offices with jurisdiction over their fields under the authority of the Congress. The Chancellor and Vice-Chancellor are the establishing nation, [nation]Offense[/nation], and the World Assembly delegate respectively.
