[b]About the Region:[/b]

[colour=#800000]This is a region established in the year 2024 &amp; founded from The Radical Civic Union, but built upon the values of Center Right to Moderate Right Wing ideology. The political system of the region is a Presidential Republic under a one party system. Under this System the Dominate party is Patriotic Alliance for Sovereignty (PAS) &amp; their coalition partner Unity and Reform Alliance (URA), they form [b]The Alliance for Sovereignty and Reform (ASR)[/b] .[/colour]
