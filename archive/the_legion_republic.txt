[b]About the Region:[/b]

[colour=#800000]This is a region established in the year 2024 &amp; founded from The Radical Civic Union, but built upon the values of Center Right to Moderate Right Wing ideology. The political system of the region is a Presidential Republic under a one party system. Under this System the Dominate party is Patriotic Alliance for Sovereignty (PAS) &amp; their coalition partner Forward Progress Movement (FPM), they form the Unity for National Renewal Bloc.[/colour]
