Overview:
DACO is a clandestine organization operating beyond public oversight, directed by the United States&#146; Central Intelligence Agency-Special Activities Division (CIA-SAD). Its existence is officially denied; its operations remain classified at the highest levels.

Founded in the aftermath of a Cold War that never truly ended, DACO exists to counter persistent communist threats worldwide. It functions in the shadows, employing covert action, intelligence gathering, and strategic interventions to neutralize ideological and political adversaries before they can destabilize global security.

Mission Statement:
To protect national and allied interests by containing, dismantling, and preemptively countering the resurgence of communist influence.
