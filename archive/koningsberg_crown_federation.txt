The Koningsberg crown Federation is a federation of imperial vassals given regional autonomy by the house of Stonk to ensure continuation of the empire in response to rising nationalist sentiment to ensure continued lineage of the dynasties prestige.

In response to the civil war. The great compromise was reached with koningsbergians gaining the most autonomy in the dual monarchy with the monarch holding the separate title, King of the house of Koningsberg alongside emperor of the federation.
The emperors duty is to maintain his dynasties prestige and authority while striking concord with the various nationalist factions that threaten to ignite civil war, dreaming of eventual independence from the emperors iron fist
