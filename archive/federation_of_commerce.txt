The Federation of Commerce is the banking state/union that regulates the currency of all nations in the Bird High School Universe (BHSU) which is the realm that houses the United Nations of Bird, Dictatoropolis, and the infamous Mars Roman Empire.  The FoC AKA the Federation holds the galaxy in its hands and is not to be messed with.
