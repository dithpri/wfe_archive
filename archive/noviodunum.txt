[b]Noviodunum is one of the most important regions, with high religious value in the region, and the said region is closely tied to the early consolidation of high religion under the Federation of Vespern and Abarshahr Tal Pieta.[/b]

[b]For the etymological origin of the region, it is a name of Celtic origin, meaning &quot;new fort&quot;: It comes from the word &quot;nowyo&quot;, Celtic for &quot;new&quot;, and &quot;dun&quot;, the Celtic for &quot;hillfort&quot; or &quot;fortified settlement&quot;, which is also a cognate for English town.[/b]
