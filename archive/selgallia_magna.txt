The island based lands of Selgallia Magna were formed first as a Noble federation of Duchies, Counties, Principalities, and etc, but was fully united as a Grand Duchy under the Lillian Order. Our lands are rich in goods such as spices, minerals, and fuels due to its location as a tropical island from from tectonic activities.
