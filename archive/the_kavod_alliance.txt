After mutual enemies and common goals were presented to a group of nations prompted the Isavite Empire to call upon current and potential friends, a treaty was signed in the great Isavite city of Kavod to form a strong bond, commonwealth, and alliance between these various nations. The Kavod Alliance stands for peace, justice, righteousness, wisdom, success, and great prosperity. 

The word &quot;Kavod&quot; is an ancient word that means &quot;Glory&quot;, suiting the name of the Alliance well.
