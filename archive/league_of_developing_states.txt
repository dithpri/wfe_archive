Newly Independent? Reemerging after extended isolation? Rebuilding after devastating earthquakes or forest fires? Then this is the place for you! Founded as a pact of mutual support between backwater states of the world, the LDS focuses on mutual development, national sovereignty, and the forging of strong bonds between member states.

Whether joining NationStates for the first time or coming back after years of distance, LDS is here for you!
 -[b]Learn[/b] the basics, and get a feel for how you want to play.
 -[b]Find[/b] new friends as you figure out the ins and outs of NationStates.
 -[b]Join[/b] the fun at your own pace, without feeling the need to get involved in the politics of your region or the World Assembly.

Join us, and (maybe) prosper!
