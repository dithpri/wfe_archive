
The Empire of Fortuna is dying; her days of global dominance are gone, replaced with a myriad of independent states all vying for control in this new world. A few states have remained loyal to the monarchy, but even in them, plots abound. 

Although the Empire may soon be gone, these nations are nonetheless bound by a shared history, and a past of unity; perhaps it will come again. Fate will play its hand, one way or another.
