Located in eastern Africa, it includes Sudan, South Sudan, Eritrea, Djibouti, Ethiopia and Somalia. It consists mainly deserts and tropical forests, and is located on the western banks of the Red Sea. Many trade routes are established on the Red Sea with the Arab countries on the other sides, and there are also trade routes on the Gulf of Aden and the Arabian Sea that create trades with southern African countries as well as countries in southern Asia. The population of Eastern Africa is 195 million, with the most populated country being Ethiopia, at 102.3 million, and the most populated city being the capital of Dashar, Elmodina, at 12 million.
