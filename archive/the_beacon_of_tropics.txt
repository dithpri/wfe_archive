The Beacon of Tropics is a region dedicated to the health and prosperity of all those who choose to enter the borders of one of its nations. BoT is a region full of nations who subscribe to the ideals of political and personal freedom of each of their citizens, including those who reside in their beautiful wilderness. By being truly dedicated to the equality of all citizens, and the prosperity of the environment, the Beacon of Tropics strives for nothing short of perfection.
