Originally, the Zaltallans ruled over a vast empire known as the Abyss Empire. Suddenly, all civilizations including the Zaltallans went missing, and foreigners controlled the region.

However, the Zaltallans returned and re-founded the empire, ruled by the supreme and divine power of the entire Imperius bloodline.

The Abyss Hegemony is the peak of the Abyss&#39;s power when all the people have finally united once again, and total power is under the Abyss&#39;s hands.
