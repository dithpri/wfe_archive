With such a large mission, several Settlement Colony&#39;s, Trade Colony&#39;s, and Formal Protectorates have been created.
At the moment, the [url=https://www.nationstates.net/region=rrcc][color=#ba263c][b]RRCC[/b][/color][/url] has been managing several areas around the world and is the largest companies to date in modern day [url=https://www.nationstates.net/nation=rhenzern][color=#ba263c][b]Rhenzern[/b][/color][/url].
