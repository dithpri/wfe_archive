Torres Del Paine is an Overseas Territory of [region]The Federal Islands[/region].  Many animals that live in Antarctica live here.  Patagonia, the region Torres Del Paine is in, was not claimed by any country until the late 1800s.  [color=blue]Torres Del Paine National Park[/color]  was in The National Geographic Traveler Magazine.
