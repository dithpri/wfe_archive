The United Pacifican Federation is a multinational organization that is bent on preserving peace. Led by the President, it is a region that enjoys armed neutrality.  


POISTIONS:

President of the Pacifican Federation: Serves for 150 days, and can be re-elected for up to 2 terms (without a break, you can run again after someone else).

Vice President of the Pacifican Federation: Is appointed by the current president, and leaves when they leave office (unless chosen by thee next president.)  

Head of Defense of the Federation: Manages the United Armed Forces of all nations in the Federation. Is appointed and serves until a resignation or if a majority of the nations vote them out.
