[color=#1D4779][u][b]Jurisdiction[/b][/u]
- The Court shall have jurisdiction over interregional matters, including but not limited to disputes and agreements involving regional governments, nations, polities in different regions, interregional organisations, alliances and treaties.
- The Court may also exercise jurisdiction over any legal matter delegated to it by a Region or interregional organisation.[/color]
