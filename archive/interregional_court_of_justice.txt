[color=#1D4779][u][b]Jurisdiction[/b][/u]
-The Court shall have jurisdiction over all extra-regional affairs including disputes and agreements between regional governments, nations &amp; polities in different regions, inter-regional organisations, alliances and treaties.
-The Court&#39;s jurisdiction shall extend to any judicial matter delegated to it by any Region or organisation.[/color]
