The Dragonic Sea is the 4th middle-west sea in the War Seas, 7 different seas (The Bronze Sea, The Nimbus Sea, The Shining Sea, The Dragonic Sea, The Revoluting Sea, The Divine Sea, and The Dark Sea) grouped together due to their notoriety for war. Named from the region&#39;s biggest influence, the Dragonic Sea is a massive body of water hosting a giant archipelago, the largest group of islands being the Demetric Isles, where the people of Demetra, the largest influence, resides.
