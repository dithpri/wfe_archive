The [b]Pax Polaris Occidens[/b] was an alliance of The Pacific (NPO), The West Pacific, and The North Pacific in 2022-25, announced in the statement [url=https://forum.nationstates.net/viewtopic.php?p=39691956#p39691956]&quot;On Interregional Peace[/url].&quot; The premise of the agreement was simple: if anyone attacked an ally of a signatory, all parties would respond. 

The agreement&#39;s power waned after the NPO prevented it from responding as a group to aggression, and it was dissolved after the NPO&#39;s attempt to coup the Outback (and install one of their own citizens as Delegate) - leading to [url=https://forum.nationstates.net/viewtopic.php?f=12&amp;t=565586]TWP&#39;s departure[/url] and the [url=https://forum.nationstates.net/viewtopic.php?p=42669252#p42669252]dissolution of the group[/url].
