[font=Times New Roman]The Govornian Union is a democratically libertarian alliance between the nations of [nation=short+noflag]Govornia[/nation], [nation=short+noflag]Govorna[/nation], [nation=short+noflag]Govornoa[/nation], and [nation=short+noflag]Govornetia[/nation]. It officially formed on August 3, 2024 NS-Time (March 3, 2018 Earth Time).
