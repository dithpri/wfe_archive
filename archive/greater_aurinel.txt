The world has begun to change. For more than three thousand years, the Empire of Aliresse dominated the world in the north, and the Empire of Takyr in the south. Now, both nations have collapsed, and left a power vacuum across the whole of the world. Stepping into this void are the many nations that have begun to take advantage of the newest innovation to come to this world of magic: the steam engine. With radical new ideas in political, social, and economic fields starting to take root, the old orders are being swept away if they cannot adapt.
