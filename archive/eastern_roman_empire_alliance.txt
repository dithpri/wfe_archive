The Region is the result of the evolution of the famous [b][color=red]01 Eastern Roman Empire Alliance[/color][/b]. 

The Alliance comprises of Regions based mainly - but not only - on Hellenic, Roman and Byzantine historical background. 

Biggest Regions of the Alliance populationwise: Hellenic Civilization, Eastern Roman Empire, Glorious Praetorian Empire, Empire of the Romans, Solar System, Greek Islands, Balkan Peninsula, Empire of Rome, 01 ERE Alliance Headquarters, Western Roman Empire etc.
