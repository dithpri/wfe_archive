The Infernal Lands were originally created years ago to spread the Zombie virus. Unfortunately, the empires within the region all collapsed. This is its second rendition.

Empires within the Infernal Lands are expected to be militaristic and spiritual, although this is not a requirement. The lore goal of the Infernal Lands is to bring the end to life as we know it, and bring about the immortality of specific individuals, deemed most capable among living people.
