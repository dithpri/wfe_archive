[url=https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/confederate_states_of_america]The Confederate States of America[/url] was a traitorous breakaway republic in the Southern United States from 1861 to 1865. It comprised 11 states that declared secession: South Carolina, Mississippi, Florida, Alabama, Georgia, Louisiana, Texas, Virginia, Arkansas, Tennessee, and North Carolina. These states fought against the United States during the American Civil War.
