Indonesia, officially the Republic of Indonesia (Indonesian: Republik Indonesia [re&amp;#712;publik &amp;#618;ndo&amp;#712;nesia]), is a country in Southeast Asia, between the Indian and Pacific oceans. It is the world&#39;s largest island country, with more than seventeen thousand islands, and at 1,904,569 square kilometres (735,358 square miles), the 14th largest by land area and the 7th largest in combined sea and land area. With over 261 million people, it is the world&#39;s 4th most populous country as well as the most populous Muslim-majority country. Java, the world&#39;s most populous island, contains more than half of the country&#39;s population.
