A leftist region that focuses on freedom of speech and general freedom to believe and speak as you wish. This being said there are some rules.

1) No hateful comments about religion, race, or any belief.
2) Keep debates civil.
3) Any government corruption shall be handled.
4) Fascists and Capitalists will not have voting rights.
5) The founder can step in if he or she feels it necessary to protect the region from chaos.

This is a place where anyone can feel free to discuss their problems, beliefs, or any disagreements.
