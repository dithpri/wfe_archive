Situated on the continent of Idris, The Brycharian Union has known many names and comprises of Nations once part of the Kingdom of Idris. Though now independent, the children of Idris have never stood alone. Historically a Christian Kingdom, Acollands stand for liberty, justice and equality for all.

We will always remember the Kingdom of Shadowspyre, which fell during the Era of Idris.
