[b]Republican Sinn F&eacute;in[/b] (Sinn F&eacute;in Poblachtach) is an Irish republican socialist political party. It aims to end British rule and establish a decentralized democratic socialist republic in Ireland according to the &Eacute;ire Nua and Saol Nua programs. It rejects the legitimacy of both the Republic of Ireland (Free State) and the British occupation of the northern six counties. It recognizes the Continuity Army Council as the legitimate government of the Irish Republic as proclaimed in arms in 1916.
