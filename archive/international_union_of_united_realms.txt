I.U.U.R. - Union of United Realms
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The International Union of United Realms is a region that promotes international peace among its members and diplomacy. The Union of United Realms Charter is mainly focused on national improvement and international peace even externally, too. Nations are free to enter this region regardless of their government type, national religion, etc. The alliance website/forum is found on [b]www.ns-iuur.webs.com[/b].
