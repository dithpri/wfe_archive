When the LBC was founded, there was so much drama. The region was settled by those who produced gin and juice and by those who produced music. Soon the LBC became the site of a bloody civil war, between the industrial factory workers and the entertainment moguls. The workers armed themselves basic weapons such as knives and axes. Only a few had basic hunting rifles and pistols. The entertainment moguls were armed with the latest tech and were able to hire Blackwater to fight for them...
