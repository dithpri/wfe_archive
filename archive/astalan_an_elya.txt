Astalan an Elya is a Greenland-sized island sparsely popoulated by minor kingdoms, nomadic peoples and other sovereign entities. Apart from a few major settlements Astalan an Elya is a rough and uninhabited island. Dominated by pine forests, mountian ranges and plains Astalan an Elya is the definition of a biodiverse geography. Throughout most of the year great swaths of Astalan an Elya are covered with snow and ice. Due to the warmer climates along the coast the population have traditionally focussed on naval activities. Most of the population are ethnically Regni, a culture associated with a far off continent where they were prosecuted for who they were.
