The Non-Aligned Movement is a Region, formed for all those, who believe in international peace and cooperation, and in sovereignty and integrity of their states. 
It does not discriminate against political or socio-economic ideologies. 

The only request for its members are that they :
 - promote international peace and cooperation between nations
and 
 - promote the respect of Human Rights.

Member States will be given equal right in the shaping of the Movement.
