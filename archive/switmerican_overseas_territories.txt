The 4 [B]Switmerican Overseas Territories[/B] (SOT) are territories under the jurisdiction and sovereignty of the [nation]Switmerica[/nation]. They are those parts of the former Empire of Switmerica that have not chosen independence or have voted to remain Switmerican territories. All of the inhabited territories are internally self-governing, with the Federal Republic retaining responsibility for defence and foreign relations. They share the Switmerican prime minister (currently Daniel Thrace) as prime minister.
