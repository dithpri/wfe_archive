The [B]Switmerican Overseas Territories (SOT)[/B] are territories under the jurisdiction and sovereignty of [B][nation]Switmerica[/nation][/B]. They are those parts of the former Switmerican Empire that have not chosen independence or have voted to remain Switmerican territories. All of the inhabited territories are internally self-governing. They share the Switmeican prime minister ([B]Arguni Carona[/B]) as head of state.
