Ceuta is a 7.1 sq mi Spanish autonomous city located on the north coast of Africa, sharing a western border with Morocco. Separated from the Iberian peninsula by the [region]Strait of Gibraltar[/region], Ceuta lies along the boundary between the [region]Mediterranean Sea[/region] and the Atlantic Ocean. Ceuta, along with the Spanish exclave Melilla, is one of nine populated Spanish territories in Africa and one of two populated territories on mainland Africa.
