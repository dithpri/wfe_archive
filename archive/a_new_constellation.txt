Welcome to a New Constellation, a new region offering a safe haven for nations that just want to dwell and develop in peace and help each other out when it comes to common interests, without any unneccessary burden placed upon them.
If you are, or want to be, such a nation, or simply wish to further the ideas and intents of this region, you are warmly invited to join. Your size, form of government, ideology and past actions will not be held against you if you do.
