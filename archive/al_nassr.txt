Al-Nassr Football Club, commonly known as Al-Nassr or simply Nassr (Arabic: &amp;#1606;&amp;#1575;&amp;#1583;&amp;#1610; &amp;#1575;&amp;#1604;&amp;#1606;&amp;#1589;&amp;#1585;), is a Saudi Arabian professional association football club based in Riyadh. Founded in 1955, the club competes in the Saudi Pro League, the top tier of football in Saudi Arabia, and is one of three teams never relegated from the top flight.
