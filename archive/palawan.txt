[b]Palawan[/b] is an archipelagic province of the Philippines that is located in the region of Mimaropa. It is the largest province in the country in terms of total area, with 5,656.29 sq mi. The capital and largest city is Puerto Princesa which is geographically grouped with but administered independently from the province. Palawan is known as the Philippines&#39; Last Frontier and as the Philippines&#39; Best Island. &#151;Wikipedia

The Underground River of Puerto Princesa, located on the island of Palawan is not only a UNESCO World Heritage site but was named as one of the Earth&#146;s New 7 Wonders of Nature. The Underground River is the longest subterranean river in the world.&#151;Everything-Everywhere.com[hr]
