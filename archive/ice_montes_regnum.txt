Ice Montes Regnum is a large prosperous land mass ripe for the taking. With miles upon miles of free land covered with lush green pastures, tall green trees and clean blue water the native lands of Ice Montes Regnum is teaming with beautiful wild life and many diverse tribes ruled over by one benevolent Leader. The actual government is small and has only been recognized for a mere few months but this fledgling nation has grown significantly in that short amount of time. The citizens live with nature and value education, Defense, and health care above all else.
