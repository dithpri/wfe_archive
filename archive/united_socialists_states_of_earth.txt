The United Socialist States of Earth (USSoE) is an international organisation to promote co-operation between socialist and left leaning nations. 

The headquarters of the USSoE is in the Governmental District, Orysia City, and experiences extraterritoriality. Further main offices are situated in Elonn, Chadkirk, and Los Guevaros. 

Its objectives include maintaining international peace and security, promoting human rights, fostering social and economic development, protecting the environment, and providing humanitarian aid in cases of famine, natural disaster, and armed conflict.
