[i]Imperial Realm of The Dominion[/i]

The Italian Empire was a state founded in 1861, originally as the Kingdom of Italy, when King Victor Emmanuel II of Sardinia was proclaimed King of Italy. Following the rise of Benito Mussolini, it became a fascist empire, with several colonies in the Africa continent. It is currently an imperial realm of [b][region]The Dominion[/region][/b].
