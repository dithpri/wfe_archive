[b][i]Midgard[/i][/b] is the name for Earth inhabited by and known to humans in early Germanic cosmology, and [u]specifically one of the Nine Worlds in Norse mythology[/u]. 

The nine worlds are: 
[list]
[*][b][color=grey]Asgard[/color][/b], the home of the &AElig;sir ruled by the god Odin; 
[*][b][color=gold]&Aacute;lfheimr/Lj&oacute;s&aacute;lfheimr[/color][/b], the home of the Lj&oacute;s&aacute;lfar; 
[*][b][color=#FF1493]Ni&eth;avellir/Svart&aacute;lfaheimr[/color][/b], the home of the Dwarves who are also synonymous with the D&ouml;kk&aacute;lfar and Svart&aacute;lfar; 
[*][b][color=green]Midgard (Earth)[/color][/b], the home of humans; 
[*][b][color=purple]J&ouml;tunheimr/&Uacute;tgar&eth;r[/color][/b], the home of the J&ouml;tnar (Giants); 
[*][b][color=#FFA500]Vanaheimr[/color][/b], the home of the Vanir; 
[*][b][color=blue]Niflheim[/color][/b], a world of ice and snow; 
[*][b][color=red]Muspelheim[/color][/b], a world of fire and lava and home of the J&ouml;tunn Surtr and 
[*][b][color=brown]Hel (Heimr)[/color][/b], the home of the dishonorable dead sometimes synonymous with or located within Niflheim and ruled by the goddess Hel.
