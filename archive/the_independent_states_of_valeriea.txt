Each member state retains individual authority. It is merely an alliances to strengthen and further prosper the economy, military, and sovereignty of each member nation. 

Each state has a representative to vote on policy changes affecting all members, headed by a chairman elected by popular vote of the representatives. Unanimous votes are required for policy to be enacted across the entire alliance.
Each state has one judge residing on the Valerian Supreme Court. 

We seek prosperity through our strength as one, no matter our idealistic differences.
