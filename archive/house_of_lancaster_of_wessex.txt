[nation]Lancaster of Wessex[/nation] has ruled Wessex since the Norman Conquest.  A parliamentary democracy, Lancaster, The Duke of Wessex, acts as Head of State with veto.

[nation]The Church of Wessex[/nation] is a non-independent enclave which came into being on 11/20/10 after a granting of lands.

[nation]Saint Aldhelm Islands[/nation] is a group of islands in the Caribbean and is a semi-autonomous dependency with The Duke acting as Head of State.  The islands were &amp;#39;discovered&amp;#39; in the early 17th c.

[nation]Celtic Wessex[/nation] is a sovereign state in full economic-political union with the House of Lancaster.
