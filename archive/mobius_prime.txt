[b]What is Mobius Prime?[/b] 
Mobius, also known as [region]Mobius Prime[/region] and formerly known as Earth, is a planet found in the Prime Zone, and a future version of Earth, approximately twelve thousand years into the future. This region represents the unified government of Mobius - the Mobian Empire, or Mobiania, - under the rule of our glorious Emperor Miles I Prower of Mobiania, Earl of Foxdon and Lord Protector of the Mobian foxes.
