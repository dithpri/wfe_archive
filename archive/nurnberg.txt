[region]Nurnberg[/region] (N&uuml;rnberg) is a city on the Pegnitz river and the Rhine&#150;Main&#150;Danube Canal in [region]Free State of Bavaria[/region]. It is distinguished by its medieval architecture, including fortifications, stone towers and Kaiserburg Castle. The Hauptmarkt (central square) contains the Sch&ouml;ner Brunnen, the gilded &#147;beautiful fountain&#148; with tiers of figures, and Frauenkirche, a 14th-century Gothic church. It is also the city where our laws of German nationalization were written, and these same laws are continued to be used throughout the world by all national-socialist nations[/b]
