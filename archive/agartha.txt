A legendary kingdom believed to be located in the Earth&#39;s core. It is a spiritually-rich kingdom, being known as Aryavartha (literally: The Land or Realm of The Aryans; the Land of the Noble/Worthy Ones&#148;) &#150; the land from which the Vedas come. There is said to be many entrances to Agartha throughout the world, such as Mammoth Cave and the Great Pyramid of Giza. Operation Highjump was a failed military operation intended to prevent Germans from escaping to Agartha through one of these entrances in Antarctica.

This region follows the path of esoteric fascism.
