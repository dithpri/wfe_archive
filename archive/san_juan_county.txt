A strange event in 2030, left much of the San Juan County abandoned.  The U.S. Government has completely fallen apart, leaving the United States in a state of Chaotic Anarchy.

The year is 2056.

Small organized nations have arisen across the lands, seeking wealth, Justice, or selfish desires.   Society is reborn in strange circumstances.
Technology is forgotten, but new inventions are born.


The Armed Republic of Southwestern Peoples is ran by 58 year old Johnathan Saelstrum, a realist.    For the time being, he is the supreme leader of the Bloomfield and Aztec regions.    He rules with a steady hand, but has made difficult decisions due to a recent drought.
