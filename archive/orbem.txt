Welcome to Orbem! I hope you enjoy your time with us!
---
Orbem is an Alternate Earth - Decision/Consequence based RP group based around the idea of the USA/USSR Cold War both Politically, Economically, and Military wise. The general timeframe is that of the 1970&amp;#39;s-1990&amp;#39;s of Earth, so any technological breakthroughs up until 1999/2000 are generally accepted.
---
A world map, including a map with borders is in the making right now, it will be finished when time permits.
---
Alliances and Tensions are not only allowed, but also encouraged! Gain power through influence, conquest, or however you see fit, all types of nations are welcome!
