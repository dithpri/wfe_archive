To the Esteemed Nations of the Vaulkreath:

Welcome Home! May the hearts of your Nations be filled with hope, the people prosperous, &amp; economies strong!

While we recognize each of us, as our own individuals Nations, we must focus our eyes on our region. As a member of our region, we ask everyone to actively bring their insight, knowledge, and experience to help us accomplish our mission; The mission of securing a positive future for our lands, a future tantamount to paradise. Only together can we succeed in becoming a dominate power in the world to spread goodwill, peace, &amp; prosperity. Only together can we develop policies, law, and precedence to guide future generations.
