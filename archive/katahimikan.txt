The region of Katahimikan is a region of no wrong-doings. Everyone here is able to be happy and be able to live out the rest of their lives in peace. This region is usually reserved for retired residents who can no longer work. Under absolutely no circumstances is crime allowed in this region. If someone is caught committing one, they are sentenced as little as a year to life in prison. Family members and family friends are the only people aloud to visit here.
