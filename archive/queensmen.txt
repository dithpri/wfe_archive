Originally discovered in 1841 by explorer Sir William Snodgrass while travelling through the North Atlantic. Queensmen was latter settled 1867 by a group of marooned swashbuckling privateers with excellent taste in traditional Celtic music and fine garment manufacturing.

The official regional sport is Curling, used to settle inter-nation disputes.

It is said that Queensmen is long known to have a series of regional bases built into the mountains of the various islands that house important research on sheep.
