All members must abide by the principles of both environmentalism and humanitarianism. There are six main mutual bullet points to follow:

[b]1.[/b] No-net-loss in regards to the environments.

[b]2.[/b] No discrimination is permitted on the basis of race, religion, gender, sexuality, or nationality.

[b]3.[/b] Members may not attack each other, and if any member is attacked without provocation, all others must provide assistance.

[b]4.[/b] Slavery, torture, and cruel treatment are strictly prohibited.

[b]5.[/b] All people are guaranteed life, liberty, and security of person.

[b]6.[/b] Members may not harbor terrorist groups, fund terrorism, or engage in acts intended to destroy any national, ethnic, racial, or religious group.
