The [region]Commonwealth of Terran Nations[/region] (C.T.N, or `The Greater Commonwealth`) is a collective of nations that support each other. Member nations can be of any ideology, and are almost free to do anything they want to their own nations without interference, so long as the nations do nothing to hinder internal Commonwealth relations.

The founder of the C.T.N is the [region]Commonwealth of New England Isles[/region], where matters of the Greater Commonwealth are discussed in Commonwealth Central Command, located in The Capitol.
