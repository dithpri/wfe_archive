The House of Abrasax is a powerful entitled family ruled by Balem Abrasax, who assumed the leadership position after the death of his mother Seraphi Abrasax. The House of Abrasax owns Abrasax Industries, one of the most successful and wealthiest companies responsible for the production of RegeneX, and is a royal family of an as-of-yet unidentified interstellar empire that exists within Commonwealth space.

The known members of the House of Abrasax are Seraphi, Balem, Kalique and Titus. After she is identified as the recurrence of Seraphi, Jupiter Jones is also considered a member of the House of Abrasax.
