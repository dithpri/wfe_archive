[b][color=darkorange]Fun Fact[/color][/b]
Before the Galactic Commonwealth, Keyes, Puno and our fallen member Amos had created the [color=red]Bermuda Archipelago[/color]. As a region, it was constantly targeted by raiders and was invaded over eight times. However, due to its active members, it successfully recovered each time. Finally a decision was made that the members would move to a new region as to avoid continual targeting- thus creating the Galactic Commonwealth.
