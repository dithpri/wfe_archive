[b]Welcome to The Embassy[/b].

Established in 2015, we aim to be the largest connector of diversity amongst regions, connecting thousands to each other through a single, neutral point.

The Embassy is the record holder for the largest number of embassies.  On 8th April 2018, we had 3055 embassies open.
