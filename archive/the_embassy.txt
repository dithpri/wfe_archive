[hr]Established 26th April 2015, we aim to be the largest connector of diversity amongst regions, connecting thousands to each other through a single, neutral point.

We are the record holder for the largest number of embassies.  On 21st May 2019, we had 4,196 embassies open, more than one for every six regions in NationStates at that time.
