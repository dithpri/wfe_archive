Welcome, to the Islands of Sicherheit

It is cold here, and the islands are small, but bigger than Iceland. Many countries in this region range from Democracies, Republics, Empires, and Dictatorships. In the southern islands it is slightly warmer than the north, and is where many tourists visit. Northern Lands of Sicherheit are well-renowned for the amazing food, and beautiful landscape. Mountains dominate the majority of the land mass here, but still has many areas for civilization to thrive
