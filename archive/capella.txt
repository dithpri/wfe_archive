After a 300-year voyage from a dying Earth, the generational colony ship [nation=noflag]Ark One[/nation] has arrived in Capella, a vast quaternary star system which is to be Humanity&#39;s new home. 

All is not well onboard however. A century-long Cold War divides the ship between the monocultural and communist [b]New Collective[/b], and the diverse and capitalist [b]Mandate of Preservation[/b]. While confined to the ark, neither side was able to dominate or destroy the other, but now that they have finally arrived at their promised land, all bets are off. 

Each player founds a colony belonging to one of these factions, and must help lead their colony and faction through the uncertain times ahead. 

When posting, use [b]IC[/b] for in-character, and [b]OOC[/b] for out-of-character.
