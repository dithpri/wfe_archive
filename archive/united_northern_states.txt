The United Northern States is a intergovernmental organization to promote trade, diplomacy, defense, and the well being of nations and states primarily located in the geographical North.

While focused on Northern nations, the UNS is open to cooperating with and inviting any nation with similar values and views who wishes to associate with the organization. The United Northern States aims to preserve liberty, democracy, and freedoms to the people within this Union and those across the globe. Nations part of the UNS are expected to exercise diplomacy first and foremost when dealing with international issues, with militaristic options serving as a last resort.
