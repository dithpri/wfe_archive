Monastery of Peace

Located on an isolated peak somewhere on in the Eastern Mountains, The Monastery of Peace is a multi-faith monastic community dedicated to the furthering of tolerance, understanding and world peace All welcome whatever your beliefs about religion or spirituality. We only ask that you treat your fellow guests with tolerance and understanding.

RC Mass 10am daily. Eastern Orthodox Services 7:30pm. Hindu, Sikh, Muslim and Buddist prayers held variously throughout the day. Refectory 6pm sharp.

Rest weary traveller and stay awhile.
