[color=#702963]Welcome to the [b]Dioscuri Space Station Complex[/b] ([b]D.S.S.C[/b])[/color], a terraformed spaceship that has been drifting across the universe since the dawn of time.

The spaceship was built by the [color=#702963][b]Castor &amp; Pollux Joint Stock Company[/b][/color] in the year 130 AA. It was originally named the &#147;[color=#702963][b]Castor &amp; Pollux Terraforming Site[/b] ([b]C.P.T.S[/b])[/color]&#148;, before the name was changed to the current one.
