CONSULATE OF THE PACIFIC OF CISPLATINA AND UNION OF THE INDIAN ISLANDS

Opened on November 12, 2017
&nbsp;
Purpose: To promote residence for citizens of diplomatic states.

- Diplomatic Law 2589 * 6632/1986 Updated and Approved under the Federal Law of Hainann and Campinass at 45689 * 5523 * / 1997 and 2015.

- It is mandatory to formalize any application for residence before the consular authorities of this embassy, and it is mandatory to complete the process within the stipulated time (3 months to 1 year) for conclusion and conclusive diplomatic definitions.
