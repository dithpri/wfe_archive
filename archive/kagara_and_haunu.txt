469705 &amp;#450;K&aacute;&amp;#806;g&aacute;ra, provisional designation 2005 EF298, is a trans-Neptunian object and binary system of the core Kuiper belt, located in the outermost region of the Solar System. It was discovered on 11 March 2005 by American astronomer Marc Buie at the Kitt Peak Observatory in Arizona. The primary body measures perhaps 140 kilometers (90 miles) in diameter. Its &amp;#8776;120-kilometer (75-mile) companion &amp;#451;H&atilde;unu was discovered with the Hubble Space Telescope in 2009. Belonging to [region]Planet Andarios[/region]!
