Six Vows of the Grande Rockies:
Vow One: The leader of my nation shall not have absolute authority (no psychotic dictatorships).
Vow Two: The leader of my nation shall have enough authority to manage things (no anarchy&#39;s).
Vow Three: My nation must have basic human rights.
Vow Four: My nation must not associate with any nation/region committing human rights abuses.
Vow Five: My nation must not take part in any raiding, even if people raid us.
Vow Six: My nation must comply with the world assembly unless I have not joined.
