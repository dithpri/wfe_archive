Lilonia the 2nd planet in the Aturia Solar System settled 5000 years ago and was once A thriving colonist of the human home world of earth. However after and abrupt cut in contact the colonists were left to fend for themselves against a hostile native environment.
One such nation, a city-state, grew up around one of the last intact planet-side communicators. 2015 years ago, they succeeded, just in time to receive one last message from the stars. It&#39;s message, &#39;to anyone left to listen&#39;, was one of unity. The words &#39;One World, One people&#39;.  Setting forth they marked the day as the dawn of a new age; Year 1 ALT (After Last Transmission) Also known to some as the Age of Reunion.
