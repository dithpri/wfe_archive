This world exists in a parallel universe. The Learning objectives of this game are to understand more about:
Politicking: how group leaders are elected, the dynamics of diplomacy
Balancing competing points of view, with particular regard to issues that affect society as a whole: understanding that most decisions involve trading off one benefit against another 
Resolving ethical dilemmas: students must develop personal responses in the absence of objectively &quot;right&quot; and &quot;wrong&quot; answers to moral issues.
