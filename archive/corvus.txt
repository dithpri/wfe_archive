[color=blue][b]Corvus was a forest planet in the Outer Rim Territories that was home to the city of Calodan. Around 9 ABY, the former Jedi Ahsoka Tano could be found on the planet. After seizing an Imperial Gozanti-class cruiser on the estuary moon Trask, Kryze told the Child of the Watch Din Djarin to seek Tano out on Corvus and bring to her Grogu, a powerful Force-sensitive infant.Magistrate Morgan Elsbeth, the leader of Calodan, gave Din Djarin the task of killing Tano, who had been giving her problems. Eventually Djarin met up with Tano, and after a short fight in the woods they united to take Calodan.[/b][/color]
