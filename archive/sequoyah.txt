[i]Sequoyah, officially the Socialist Republic of Sequoyah, is a unrecognized nation in the American South. It is bordered by Oklahoma to the west, Arkansas to the east, Missouri to the northeast, Kansas to the north, and Texas to the south. Sequoyah is relatively unrecognized, however has control of all of its claimed territories after it declared independence, formerly being the 52nd state in the USA.[/i]
