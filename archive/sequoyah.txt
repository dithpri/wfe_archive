[i]Sequoyah, officially the State of Sequoyah, is a state in the South Central region of the United States. It is bordered by Oklahoma to the west, Arkansas to the east, Missouri to the northeast, Kansas to the north, and Texas to the south. Sequoyah is known for its rich Native American heritage, particularly as the homeland of the Five Civilized Tribes, and has the highest percentage of Indigenous residents of any U.S. state.[/i]
