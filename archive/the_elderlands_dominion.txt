The Elderlands Dominion was formed by The Royal Elderlands Treaty, in which The Matriarchy of Elrebrimir &amp;amp; The Nomadic Peoples of The Travelers Union combined their might; and overthrew the Tyranical Emporer, Vlad the Cruel, with the help of The Confederacy of Randomsene. 

After the successful coup:

High Priestess Elrebri focused on rebuilding the unstable government, increasing the military &amp;amp; revising the current politics. 

Royal Chief Ra focused on increasing foreign policy, external trade &amp;amp; internal economy.
