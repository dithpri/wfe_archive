Greater Meba is a region located in the virtual world of Nationstates. The region is known for its diverse population, strong economy, and vibrant culture. The government is a federal parliamentary constitutional monarchy, with a king as the ceremonial head of state and a prime minister as the head of government. The economy is primarily based on service and technology industries, with a rapidly growing tourism sector. The region boasts a highly educated population, with a literacy rate of over 99%. Greater Meba is also known for its progressive social policies, including universal healthcare and a strong welfare system. It has a diverse culture, with influences from many different nations and ethnic groups.
