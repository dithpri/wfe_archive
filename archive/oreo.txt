The region OREO (Organized Republics Established Overseas) is a alliance between nations across the world to establish trading and friendships between countries. The 2 commanding nations are The Republic of Kyatu and The Rogue Nation of Petrorian Empire. After Kyatu invaded Finland in 1901 and gained control of it in 1993 they have became a world power. When Petrorian Empire took control of Florida in 1991 after killing all the seminoles they have started world trade in oranges and limes.
