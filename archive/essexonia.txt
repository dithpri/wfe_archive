A Region that thrives on upholding Law and Order, and where Citizens enjoy equality and tax benefits for hard work and personal contributions to society. Nobody is permitted automatic enrolment into a benefits system unless disabled, of old age, or works as volunteer on community projects.
Employment rates are very high, with a well above average minimum wage. 
Society is polite and harsh penalties for criminals enforced.
Public flogging, The Death Penalty and capital punishment in general is instigated.
A democratic philosophy ensures all citizens are heard, and each sub-division or county, has a representative voice within the Regions General Committee Council (GCC), which reports to the World Assembly
