[b]Jazeera[/b] is a continent that consists of the land masses which sit on Jazeera&#39;s continental shelf. Situated on the continent are a multitude of mostly Arab-speaking peoples who migrated to the region hundreds of years ago, co-existing with the traditional native American peoples who reside on the continent alongside them. 

Of the many landmasses traditionally thought of as continents, Jazeera is one of the smallest with an area of around  4,920,000 km2 and a population of around 129 million people.
