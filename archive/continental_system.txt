____[b]The Continental System[/b]____

The Continental System was a blockade established by Napoleon himself to paralyze the Empire of Great Britain by sanctioning the British Commerce. The decree of Berlin on November 21st, 1806, proclaimed that all nations, neutrals or French allies, were not to trade with the British, therefore establishing the formal French alliance, or the Continental System.
