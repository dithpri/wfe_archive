[region]Valenwood[/region] borders Elsweyr to the east and Cyrodiil to the north, partially divided from the latter by the Strid River, and is just across the sea from Summerset Isle. Its landscape consists mainly of rolling hills and light blue rivers, predominantly covered in rainforest and woodland.

Valenwood consists of four main regions: Grahtwood, Greenshade, Malabal Tor, and western Reaper&#39;s March. Home of the Bosmer.
