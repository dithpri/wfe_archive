Iran, officially the Islamic Republic of Iran (IRI), also known as Persia, is a country in West Asia. It borders [nation]Turkey[/nation] to the northwest and [nation]Iraq[/nation] to the west, [nation]Azerbaijan[/nation], [nation]Armenia[/nation], the Caspian Sea, and [nation]Turkmenistan[/nation] to the north, [nation]Afghanistan[/nation] to the east, [nation]Pakistan[/nation] to the southeast, the Gulf of Oman and the Persian Gulf to the south. With a mostly Persian-ethnic population of almost 90 million in an area of 1,648,195 km2 (636,372 sq mi), Iran ranks 17th globally in both geographic size and population.
