[region]Flensburg[/region] is the 3rd largest city in the north of the German state of Schleswig-Holstein, and was the site of the last government of National-Socialist Germany. The Flensburg Government led by Karl D&ouml;nitz was in power from the preventative suicide of Adolf Hitler on 30 April 1945 until 23 May 1945

The 30th  [url=https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Flensburg_Government#Cabinet]Cabinet[/url] of Germany, consisted of Grand Admiral Karl D&ouml;nitz as the Reichspr&auml;sident and Lutz Graf Schwerin von Krosigk as Reichskanzler and various ministers. It was the last official National-Socialist Government to hold national office.
