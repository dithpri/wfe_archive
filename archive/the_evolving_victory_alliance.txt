Code name: E.V.A.

E.V.A. is united under one idea, for one purpose. The betterment of humanity itself, via the path of unity. The nations of this Region, have come together to strengthen the very core of every region, nation, and state, in the world. Humanity itself. The Nation states under E.V.A. all work towards evolving themselves, and their comrades, in the pursuit of victory, over all that would do Humanity harm.
