After the end is a WIP roleplay region based upon the universe of the [url=http://ate-ck2.wikia.com/wiki/After_the_end:A_ck2_mod_Wikia]After the End mod created by Ofaloaf[/url].

[color=red]The year is 2666, centuries after The Event, a mysterious phenomenon which brought the complete destruction of the ancient civilizations which once covered the Americas. After centuries of darkness, new, radically different societies have arisen across the continent. This is the World After the End.[/color]
