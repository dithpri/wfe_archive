Malay: Orang Melayu, Jawi: &amp;#1571;&amp;#1608;&amp;#1585;&amp;#1696; &amp;#1605;&amp;#1604;&amp;#1575;&amp;#1610;&amp;#1608; are an Austronesian ethnoreligious group native to eastern Sumatra, the Malay Peninsula and coastal Borneo, as well as the smaller islands that lie between these locations. These locations are today part of the countries of Malaysia, Indonesia (eastern and southern Sumatra, Bangka Belitung Islands, West Kalimantan and Riau Islands), southern part of Thailand (Pattani, Satun, Songkhla, Yala and Narathiwat), Singapore and Brunei Darussalam.
