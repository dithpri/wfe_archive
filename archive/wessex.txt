[b][color=goldenrod]:.:.: [/color][color=maroon]Wessex[/color][color=goldenrod] :.:.:[/color][/b]

A region and ancient Anglo-Saxon kingdom of southern England. According to tradition, the kingdom was founded by the Saxon conquerors of Britain and at its greatest extent occupied the territory between the English Channel and the Thames River.[hr]
