The Ultrasonic Megasmash world consists of three regions: [C89000]The East[ffffff], The Island, and Aanterdan. The East consists of Alterra, Aquidina, and Embra Cotta. The Islonese countries are Hasland(H4sland), Heersland, Pynderfyl(known as Letheland to Hash-speaking countries), and Fresta. Aanterdan is its own island.
