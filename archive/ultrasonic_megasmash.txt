The Ultrasonic Megasmash world consists of three regions: The East, The Island, and Aanterdan. 

The East consists of [url=https://www.nationstates.net/nation=4lterra][color=greenyellow]Alterra[/color][/url], [url=https://www.nationstates.net/nation=aquidina][color=grey]Aquidina[/color][/url], and [url=https://www.nationstates.net/nation=embra_cotta][color=green]Embra Cotta[/color][/url]. 

The Islonese countries are [url=https://www.nationstates.net/nation=h4sland][color=Brown]Hasland[/color][/url], [url=https://www.nationstates.net/nation=heersland][color=greenyellow]Heersland[/color][/url], [url=https://www.nationstates.net/nation=pynderfyl][color=orchid]Pynderfyl[/color][/url] (known as Letheland to Hash-speaking countries), and [url=https://www.nationstates.net/nation=fresta][color=red]Fresta[/color][/url]. 

Aanterdan is its own island.
