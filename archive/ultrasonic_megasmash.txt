The Ultrasonic Megasmash world consists of three regions: The East, The Island, and Aanterdan. 

The East consists of [color=green]Alterra[/color], [color=blue]Aquidina[/color], and [color=grey]Embra Cotta[/color]. 

The Islonese countries are [color=Brown]Hasland[/color](H4sland), [color=greenyellow]Heersland[/color], [color=orchid]Pynderfyl[/color](known as Letheland to Hash-speaking countries), and [color=red]Fresta[/color]. 

Aanterdan is its own island.
