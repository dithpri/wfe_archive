The Mutual Protection Treaty is an alliance for nations to come together to defend each other and fight with each other.

We:

[b]SUPPORT[/b] all regions and ideologies, including the General Assembly and Security Council and are willing to make peace,
[b]DEFEND[/b] our members and the members of allies, and
[b]ACKNOWLEDGE[/b] the dangers of being without being in a Region for protection.

We are accepting requests to establish Embassies/Communications.
