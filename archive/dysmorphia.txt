[b]Dysmorphia[/b] is a large region encompassing the Dysmorphian Inland Sea and the various nations on its shores. [nation]Golgoglot[/nation] is located on the Northern Shores, [nation]Prime Vesti[/nation] on the Eastern, and [nation]Zenodyne[/nation] on the Southern. Golgoglot recently granted a license to [nation]National Public Radio[/nation] to broadcast from within Dysmorphia.
The nation of [nation]Lesser Compline[/nation] occupies the [region]Dysmorphian Archipelago[/region], a chain of 6 islands near the Northern Shores of the Sea.

Regional authority falls under the joint jurisdiction of Golgoglot, the region&#39;s founder, and the Citizens Oversight Committee which is currently chaired by representatives of the Vested government. COC chairship will rotate to the Zennite government in 2020.
