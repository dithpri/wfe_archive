[b]Dysmorphia[/b] is a region encompassing the Dysmorphian Inland Sea and the nations on its shores. [nation]Golgoglot[/nation] is located on the Northern Shores, [nation]Prime Vesti[/nation] on the Eastern, and [nation]Zenodyne[/nation] on the Southern. 
[nation]National Public Radio[/nation] holds a licene to broadcast throughout the region (granted in 2018 July).
The nation of [nation]Lesser Compline[/nation] occupies the [region]Dysmorphian Archipelago[/region], a chain of 6 islands near the Northern Shores of the Sea.

The Dysmorphia Regional Office (DRO) holds authority over the region, falling under the jurisdiction of Golgoglot (the region&#39;s founder) and the COC whose chairship is held by the Vested government. COC chairship rotates triennially, with the Zennite government scheduled to take over in 2019 January.
