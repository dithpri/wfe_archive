[b]Dysmorphia[/b] is a large region encompassing the Dysmorphian Inland Sea and the various nations on its shores. [nation]Golgoglot[/nation] is located on the Northern Shores, [nation]Prime Vesti[/nation] on the Eastern, and [nation]Zenodyne[/nation] on the Southern. 
A radio license for [nation]National Public Radio[/nation] to broadcast throughout the region was granted by the Citizens Oversight Council (COC) in 2018 July.
The nation of [nation]Lesser Compline[/nation] occupies the [region]Dysmorphian Archipelago[/region], a chain of 6 islands near the Northern Shores of the Sea.

Regional authority falls under the joint jurisdiction of Golgoglot (the region&#39;s founder) and the COC which is currently chaired by representatives of the Vested government. COC chairship rotates every 3 years, with the Zennite government scheduled to take over in 2019.
