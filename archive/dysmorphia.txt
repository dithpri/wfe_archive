[b]Dysmorphia[/b] is a region located on the Northern Shores of the Dysmorphian Inland Sea. Originally founded as the home of [nation]golgoglot[/nation], the region&#39;s borders were recently expanded to encompass [nation]palomine[/nation], [nation]prime vesti[/nation], and [nation]zenodyne[/nation]. Two other nations, [nation]lesser compline[/nation] and [nation]repalia[/nation], occupy the Lesser and Upper archipelagos respectively in the DI Sea.
