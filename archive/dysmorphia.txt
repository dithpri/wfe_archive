[b]Dysmorphia[/b] is a large region encompassing the Dysmorphian Inland Sea.
 [nation]golgoglot[/nation] is located on the Northern Shores, [nation]prime vesti[/nation] on the Southeastern Shores, and [nation]zenodyne[/nation] on the Western Shores. 
Two other nations, [nation]lesser compline[/nation] and [nation]repalia[/nation], occupy the Lesser and Upper archipelagos respectively in the DI Sea.
