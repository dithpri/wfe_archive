1. Turkic Union is a Confederacy of Turkic peoples and nations.
2. The people of the Turkic Union are the only source of political power.
3. The Leadership of the Turkic Union takes all the necessary measures in order to ensure the defense of its Independence, Freedom and National Sovereignty.
5. The Turkic Union, as well as all of its dependent territories, is inseparable and inviolable. Any action against its territorial integrity, sovereignty or independence will be treated as the act of war.
