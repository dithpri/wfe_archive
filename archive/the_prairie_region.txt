A region that is said to be the beginning of new, better life, it is watched over by the Empire of Baron Von William. The economy may not be the best, but the region prospers anyway. A few new nations have recently moved to the Prairie Region: The Dictatorship of The Dictatorship of Abby, The United States of Equality, The Empire of Terrestria, and The Free Land of The Corrupted Land of Benation.
