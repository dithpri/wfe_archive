BALLs was founded in 2015 A.D. After several leaders expressed a lack of unification against exterior and interior threats, a few nations signed the BALLs treaty of March 10, 2015, granting each other extended trade rights, open borders, and representation into a new regional government. Founding nation- The Kaiserian Kingdom. 2nd Signer- America After Bryce. 3rd signee- The United States of Significant Reform (USSR).
