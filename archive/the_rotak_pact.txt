The Rotak Pact is a collective economic, political and defence organization founded at Rotak, Vannington. The organization consists of eight nations. The signatories of the pact were Vannington, Digirisme, Encomia, Hindu Baathist Jumhuria, Ishtiraki Arab Republic, The Left-Wing Populist Revolution, Technocratic Socialist Soyuzia and United Hitayist Socialist Republics.
