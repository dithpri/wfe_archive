The Rotak Pact is a collective economic, political and defence organization founded at Rotak, Vannington. The organization consists of 21 nations. The founding signatories of the pact were Vannington, Comecon Union, Digirisme, Encomia, Hindu Baathist Jumhuria, Ishtiraki Arab Republic, The Left-Wing Populist Revolution, Patsia, The Right-Wing Populist Revolution, San Morea, Technocratic Socialist Soyuzia and United Hitayist Socialist Republics. The other nine nations joined the organization at its First Summit in Port Aflaq, Ishtiraki Arab Republic. 

The Headquarters of the organization is situated at Lakeside in Rotak, Vannington.
