 The Alromi Empire is founded by the five countries -Beatoewnyah, Endonyah, Atlantis, Firetopia, and Sivilizatia-. These countries have many terrains; Beatoewnyah is plains and rainforest, Endonyah resides in the Aether, Atlantis is mostly underwater, Firetopia is a Hell-scape, and Sivilizatia resides in the clouds.
