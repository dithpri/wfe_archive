Region founded by the Western Mediterranean countries that wish to belong to a group of democratic states in close economic, military and cultural cooperation.

One of the foundations of the Western Mediterranean is the Convention for the Protection of Civilians in Time of War (CPCTW), the Economic Cooperation Treaty (ECT) and the Common Border Administration (CBA). 

The official languages of the Western Mediterranean are Spanish, French, Italian and Arabic.

The Mediterranean has been the cradle of great civilizations and a prosperous region. For many years the axis of human progress.
