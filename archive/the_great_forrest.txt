Declaration from the State Police
_____________________________________________________________________
 On behalf of our Nation The Maine State Police, Greet you with a warm handshake, It is Our Duty to inform you that this Region is a Territory of the Nation and is Policed as such, Therfore all those visiting or who have obtained a Permanent Resident Card (_P.R.C.) must abided by our Nations Laws.

We also Implore you to go and experience the many activities this Region as to offer , such as Hiking, fishing, rock climbing, Hunting, Whale Watching and much much more.
