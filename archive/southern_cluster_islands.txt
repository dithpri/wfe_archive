The Southern Cluster Islands are an archipelago of beautiful isles with glorious coastlines in the South Pacific Sea. SCI (Southern Cluster Islands) pride themselves on a varying array of different ideologies, from socialist to capitalist, however there is no religion on the islands. The climate is warm most of the time with a yearly monsoon season that lasts a month. The archipelago is mostly free and democratic depending on the country, the region makes a welcoming home to millions of people.
