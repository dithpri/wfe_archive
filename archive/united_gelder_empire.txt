The United Gelder Empire is a region loosely based on the Holy Roman Empire, in that we are a multi-ethnic complex of territories together forming an imperial confederacy. This means that - while the Emperor or Empress rules as a central authority - we greatly respect the sovereignty of all our member states. It is why we warmly welcome [i]your[/i] input as to how the Empire should be run and we hope your nation will achieve great things while among us!

For this is the motto that we strive to live by and hope to inspire within you as well: 

[i]We are our Empire![/i]
