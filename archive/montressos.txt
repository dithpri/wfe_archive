Far from the rapid pace of global politics lies the serene island of Montressos. Though quite large in size, it has only recently started to become inhabited by those seeking fortune, fame, funnel cakes, and the hopes of a better life. The only preexisting settlement happens to be the pirate republic of Libertalias - who have largely moved on from their plundering days, but still desire above all gold and the freedom to do whatever they please with it.
