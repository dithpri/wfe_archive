Founded by The Empire of Codith for the purpose of expanding control in the World Assembly for small nations under one region. This region is meant to be a friendly atmosphere were friends of the Codiathian founder and Emperor can communicate and run their nations cooperatively. However, this is still a serious region and besides common talk on the message board amendments to the regions constitution can be added. The message board is the center for communication, governance, and voting in this region. Please explore and read everything you want to know about the The United Alliance of Nations and, most importantly, enjoy yourselves in this region.
