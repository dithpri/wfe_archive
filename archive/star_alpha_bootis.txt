Arcturus, also designated as &amp;#945; Bo&ouml;tis (alpha Bo&ouml;tis), is a variable giant star of spectral class K1 in the constellation of Bo&ouml;tes. Arcturus visual magnitude is -0.04, making it the third brightest star in the sky. Because of its brightness, Arcturus is clearly visible to the naked eye when observed from locations with dark skies, and should be also quite easily visible from light polluted areas.
