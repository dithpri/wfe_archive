The Union of Far East Nation (UFEN) is an organization formed by the combination of multi-races and multi-religious nations of Far East Asia. The brotherhood among the nations is highly regarded as it is the most important essence to strengthen the region in the time of peace and conflict. People coming from this side of the world are usually known to be the most welcoming, friendly, pretty and handsome, however, if provoked they could easily be dangerous forces to be used in the battlefield. Politically dramatic, rich in cultures and languages, everyone is always welcome to join the region at any time.
