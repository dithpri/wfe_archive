The Marshall Islands, officially the [b]Republic of the Marshall Islands[/b] is an island country located near the equator in the Pacific Ocean, slightly west of the International Date Line. Geographically, the country is part of the larger island group of Micronesia. The country&#39;s population of 72,191 people is spread out over 29 coral atolls, comprising 1,156 individual islands and islets. The islands share maritime boundaries with the Federated States of Micronesia to the west, Wake Island to the north, Kiribati to the south-east, and Nauru to the south. About 31,000 of the islanders live on Majuro, which is also the capital. &#151;Wikipedia[hr]
