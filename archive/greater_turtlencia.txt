Greater Turtlencia is made up of the islands that are in the Turtlencian sea, which surrounds Turtlencia. It&#39;s location is unknown thanks to a cloaking device located in the secrecy district of Turtlencia. The main islands in Turtlencia are Turtlencia Island, part of Turtlencia, and the Turtlencian Commonwealth, which is made up of the islands of Physios, Pi&ntilde;atanos, Comrados, Fredomos and Vastastos.
