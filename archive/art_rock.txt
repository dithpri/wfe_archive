Art rock is rock music that aims to distinguish the genre from popular entertainment,[9] with the term typically being applied as the middle ground between mainstream and experimental rock.[10] Art rock primarily draws influences from the wider art world and academia; which includes contemporary art, art music, avant-garde art, experimental music, avant-garde music, alongside classical music and jazz.[4] The term is sometimes deployed interchangeably with &quot;progressive rock&quot; and to refer to some experimental rock acts.[10]
