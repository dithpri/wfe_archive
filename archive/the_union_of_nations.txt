The Union of Nations

A Regional Grouping of States committed to democratic government and sensible civil liberties which encourage and foster international cooperation between governments to further humanity.  However it is noted that this goal is exercised in varying fashions between governments of different member states in the Union.

The Chief of the Union is the President of Ecclestia who resides over the executive branch of the Government of Ecclestia and the Union of Nations.

The current sole member in the World Assembly is Ecclestia.
