Protectorate of the Republic of Belarus

The Union of Nations was abolished by the Third Empire of Me Authorities recently on 16 December 2012. In light to this issue, the Union of Nations  thereof has no legitimate ruler that can claim right to this region persuant that they are the resurgent founder of the Region. Such has been the case today; As of 16 December 2012, the Republic of Belarus is the rightful successor to the Union of Nations, and shall represent the Union of Nations, Protectorate of the Republic of Belarus in the international community.
