The Union of Nations

Mission Charter: The Union of Nations is a Regional Grouping of States committed to democratic government, sensible civil liberties and market economies which encourages and fosters international cooperation between governments to further humanity.
Note: This goal is exercised in varying fashions between governments of different member states in the Union.

The Chief of the Union is the President of Ecclestia who resides over the executive branch of the Government of the Union of Nations.

If a nations ratings do not equal or exceed the following, their membership will be suspended
Civil Right: 50%
Economy: 50%
Political Freedoms: 55%
