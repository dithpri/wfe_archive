Our liberal region is a political philosophy or worldview founded on ideas of liberty and equality. Whereas classical liberalism emphasizes the role of liberty, social liberalism stresses the importance of equality. We Liberals espouse a wide array of views depending on our nations understanding of these principles, but generally they and the government support ideas and programs such as freedom of speech, freedom of the press, freedom of religion, free markets, civil rights, democratic societies, secular governments, and international cooperation. And we have a no bullying policy everybody gets treated the same no matter what they are or where they come from.
