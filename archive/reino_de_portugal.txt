[color=darkgreen]The [u]Reino de Portugal[/u] (Kingdom of Portugal) was Portugal&amp;#39;s general designation under its monarchy. The kingdom was located in the west of the Iberian Peninsula, Europe and existed from 1139 to 1910. The monarchy in Portugal was abolished and replaced by the First Portuguese Republic after the 5 October 1910 revolution.[/color]
