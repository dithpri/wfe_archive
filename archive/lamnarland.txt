A group of continents that lay upon the Angola Basin, although it stretches from Cape Verde to Robben Island in Cape Town. It&#39;s most western point is the Falklands. Caipan lays upon the Walvis Ridge and on the edge of the Falkland Plate. It consists of Deera Atlantis, Tornlands, Caipan and Scandia Australis (or just Scandinavian Australia). It is also both a part of Africa and America. There is estimated to be over 270 volcanoes (36 active, rest is sleeping or extinct), with the highest point to be Caipan Peak in the African side, by the Tampo Coast (Drakensberg)
