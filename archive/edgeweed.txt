This region has strong ties with the world of Nation States. First founded by Bryce344 in 2012, it has had a good relationship with the region [region]Caesars republic[/region]. It merged with Caesars Republic in 2013, and the region officially died. However, [region]Caesars Republic[/region] has graciously decided to raise the region again to allow all former natives to return if they so wish, or any newcommers who want to get this region going again, under the supervision of the Emperor of the Republic.
