Oceanpunk (also known as Seapunk) is a sub-genre of Science Fiction dealing with a near future world of oceanographic colonization. It generally draws on Apocalyptic themes, such as Global Warming. Oceanpunk is known to feature Underwater Cities, Submarines, Floating Cities, and technology associated with exploring the depths beneath the waves. Such works commonly deal with mankind&amp;#39;s relationship with the sea.
