Welcome to the Western Romano Empire or WRE!. Fancy yourself a citizen of Rome and want to see it return to its former glory?, Join today and help us restore this once mighty Empire!.

We are comprised of a multitude of Allied states that all contribute to the Empire, all power is governed by the appointed Emperor. We have plenty of roles within the Empire that all contribute towards the greater good of the Empire.

Wish to help administrate the Empire?, become a consulate and delve into the politics of the Empire. Feeling patriotic and wish to protect the noble citizens of Rome?, join our military and push back any invaders!. The options are limitless!.
