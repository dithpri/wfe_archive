Forma is a region inspired by the world from Centaura &amp; Dead Ahead, ROBLOX games inspired by the WW1 to Cold War era.

This region will be based on role-play, as you likely are joining or reading this is a new region, EXTREMELY new if you will.

Centaura and Dead Ahead are Fast-Paced Realistic shooters taking place on the fictional world of Forma, which is 4.5x larger than Earth, but has similar gravity and climate due to it having low density. Centaura and Dead Ahead both focus on the Empire of Antares (Antares Imperium) taking over the continent of Centaura, and then attempting to take over the world of Forma itself.
