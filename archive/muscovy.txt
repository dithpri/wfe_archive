Muscovy was a medieval Russian polity centered on Moscow between 1283 and 1547. It has been referred to by many Western sources as Muscovy, and the Grand Duchy of Moscow, as the state is known in Russian records. The state was the successor to the Principality of Moscow and the predecessor of the Tsardom of Russia.
