[b]Welcome to Pampanga[/b]
[i]Nickname: Culinary and Christmas Capital of the Philippines[/i]

&amp;#128312;Pampanga is a province in Region III, Central Luzon. It is bordered by the provinces Tarlac to the north, Nueva Ecija to the northeast, Bulacan to the east, The Manila Bay to the central south, Bataan to the southwest, and Zambales to the west.

FACTS:
Established: December 11, 1571
Capital: San Fernando City
Area: 2,002.20 sq km
Population: 2,437,709 (2020 est)
Highest elevation: 1,583 m (5,194 ft)

REGIONAL INFO:
Region founded: January 05, 2023
