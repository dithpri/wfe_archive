Aurethia is a continent centered around the Solaran Basin, an inland sea that links four rival nations. Nox Aeternia dominates the northern highlands with a militarized corporate state, Caesarumi controls the industrial forests of the west, Novai Aeternia thrives on trade and tourism along the eastern coast, and Luminana rules the spiritual and technological plateau to the south. Though trade connects them, political and cultural tensions keep the continent in constant rivalry.
