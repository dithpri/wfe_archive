Empress of the Empire and Queen of England Mary Tudor rules over the conquered lands by the British Royal Army and British Royal Navy.
Queen Mary Tudor and Empress of the Empire rules over the following lands. England, Scotland, Wales, Ireland, France, Spain, Austria, Denmark, United Provinces, The Principalities of Germany, Canada, India, Austrailia, New Zealand, China, Russia, Poland-Lithuania, United States of America, The Carribean Islands, Mexico, and many islands across the world.
