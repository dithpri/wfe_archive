Humanitarian Nations United (HNU) is a left-wing coalition of nations dedicated to the paramount principles of egalitarianism, altruism, and humanities development in a democratic way; putting the well-being of our citizens and their individual rights and freedoms first. On November 11, 2022, World Assembly Delegate for Humanitarian Nations United, Maueristan, resigned elected post as delegate and renounced membership in the World Assembly in total. As of this time, no nations in the HNU have affiliations with the World Assembly.
