341520 Mors&#150;Somnus, provisional designation 2007 TY430, is a binary and plutino. It consists of two components less than 60 kilometers in diameter, orbiting at a distance of 21000 km. Mors&#150;Somnus was discovered on 14 October 2007, by American astronomers Scott Sheppard and Chad Trujillo with the Subaru telescope at Mauna Kea Observatories in Hawaii, United States.
