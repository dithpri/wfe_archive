### The Land of Dragons

A sovereign regional alliance founded and protected by **The Empire of The Great Yassinia**. We stand for democracy and solidarity under the wings of the dragon.

### Regional Compact

1.  **Sovereignty:** All members retain full independence.
2.  **Protection of Muslims:** Hostility or discrimination against Muslims is strictly prohibited.
3.  **Social Code:** Homosexuality and related activities are strictly banned.
4.  **Peace:** Diplomatic cooperation is the ultimate law.
