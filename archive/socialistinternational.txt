The Socialist International is a worldwide association of political parties which seek to establish democratic socialism. It consists of democratic socialist, social democratic and labour political parties and other organisations. Initially dominated by parties from Western Europe, it has grown to include more than 160 member parties from more than 100 countries. The Party of European Socialists, a European political party is an associated organisation of the SI, as is its European parliamentary group, the Progressive Alliance of Socialists and Democrats.
