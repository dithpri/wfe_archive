[b]Anarcho Capitalism[/b]

[hr]

This region has been freed from both the vandalism of the Black Riders and the neo-feudalistic ideology of its natives. Anarchism and capitalism are not compatible, and any society attempting to fuse the two would lead to forms of tyranny and oppression that would have few counterparts in human history.
