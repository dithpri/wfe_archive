Originally founded by [nation=short]Capitalem[/nation] in early 2013, the Epician Islands was invaded by the Black Riders and later refounded by the Trade Federation after its fall, locked by a password and, by the restriction of its oppressors, kept from welcoming its natives that worked so hard to found it. Now though this region lives on again, free from non-native interference.
