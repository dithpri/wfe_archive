A land stretching from the Arab gulf, all the way to the coast of the Mediterranean sea. A land that houses the 3 holy mosques, a land which is peaceful. 

New Mesopotamia is a vast empire within the middle east, consisting of Iraq, Kuwait, Syria, Jordan, Lebanon and the holy land, aswell as the Hejaz. It is ruled by a group of shiite clerics, and the kingdom has two capitals: Baghdad and Damascus.
