The Vale is a region in witch the lands of The Golden Islands, Croffland, Dothrake, and The Bridged Isles reside. The two primary Nations Croffland, and the Wiled Savage lands of Dothrake are in constant competition for Control of the The Golden Islands were there are major gold ore deposits, that seem never to end. The Bridged Isles are were The Monks of Maldor have chosen to build their great monastery and worship the god Maldor. One other notable feature of The Vale is the Sunken Sea were no ship has ever returned.
