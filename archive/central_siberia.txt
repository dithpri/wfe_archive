Located in central Russia from the Arctic Ocean down to Kazakhstan, and as far west as central Kazakhstan over Astana, all the way to the far east edge of Mongolia and the west edge of Manchuria. Consists of the Chelyabinsk, Sverdlovsk, Yugra, Yamalia, Krasnoyarsk, Omsk, Tomsk, Novosibirsk, Tyumen, Altai, Tuva, Khakassia, Kemerovo, Irkutsk, Buryatia, Zabaykalsk and Sakha Oblasts, Republics, Autonomous Okrugs and Krais of Russia.
