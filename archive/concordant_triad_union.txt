The Concordant Triad Union (CTU) stands as a dynamic alliance, weaving together the strengths of three distinct nations into a seamless tapestry of cooperation. With its cornerstone in the Free Land of Watill, Waruma, and North Watill, the CTU symbolizes unity, progress, and shared aspirations.

Shared Purpose: The CTU finds its purpose in forging a future where the collective well-being of its member nations thrives. United in their diversity, these nations harmonize their efforts to realize a region of stability, prosperity, and mutual support.
