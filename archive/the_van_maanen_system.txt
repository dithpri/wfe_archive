The Van Maanen System comprises of a small, yellow sun roughly 14 light-years from Earth. As the System is newly discovered part of the galaxy, The first colony to arrive in the Van Maanen system was the colony of Osnos was founded on the planet Oyama and has been a resounding success. Now as new Countries are founded and more and more people arrive in the Van Maanen System, the question still remains relevant. How long with this peace last?
