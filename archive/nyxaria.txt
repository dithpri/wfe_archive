Once united beneath forgotten gods, Nyxaraia now lies fractured &#151; a realm of endless dusk where kingdoms rise and fall in blood and dust. From the obsidian peaks of the Blackspire Range to the storm-swept Ashen Sea, power festers in the hands of warlords, zealots, and tyrants.

The long-fallen Nyxaraian Empire stirs once more, its shadow spreading through ruin and whispered sorcery. The common folk pray for dawn that never comes.

Government: Feudal and theocratic states
Culture: Grim medievalism, dark faiths, and fractured loyalties
Economy: Tribute, conquest, and shadow trade
RP Focus: Intrigue, war, and forbidden power
