
The Nova Alliance is a federation of territories who share common goals. We welcome all who wish to join us in good faith. Alliance Member sovereignty will be observed.

Nova Alliance Council
 Every member of the Nova Alliance will have a seat on the Nova Alliance Council. Member Nations will be able to vote for Alliance ministers and executives. Our WA will head the NAC.

Nova Alliance Ministers
Ministers are Alliance representatives that are tasked with the administration of the NA. All ministers are voted for in the NAC including the WA.
