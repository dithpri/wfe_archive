[b][color=darkblue]Lutheranism Overview[/color][/b]

[b]Type:[/b] Protestant Christianity (Lutheran branch)  
[b]Founder:[/b] Martin Luther (1483-1546)  
[b]Origin:[/b] Germany, 16th Century  
[b]Main Regions:[/b] Northern Europe (Denmark, Sweden, Norway, Finland, Iceland), Germany, USA  

[b]Core Beliefs:[/b]
[list]
[*]Sola Scriptura &#150; Scripture alone is the authority  
[*]Sola Fide &#150; Salvation by faith alone  
[*]Sola Gratia &#150; Salvation by grace alone  
[*]Solus Christus &#150; Christ alone is savior  
[/list]
