[color=#298DFF][b]Welcome to Heliste![/color][/b] we a small modern-tech region, open to all who looking for a new region to settle in! Whether you be a monarchy, heavy-handed dictatorships or a republic. We are open to all, who are looking for cooperative worldbuilding and roleplaying experience. Most of the regional activity takes place on official Regional Discord, along with the Heliste News Thread. There are currently several regional storylines in the workings. 

[b]Founded: [color=#298DFF]14 August 2020[/color][/b]

Heliste is governed through the [color=#298DFF][b]Regional Administrative Council[/color] (RAC)[/b]. The RAC is made up of five equal Administrators to ensure that no one in the regional administration is given too much power.
