Shanxi (/&amp;#643;&aelig;n&amp;#712;&amp;#643;i&amp;#720;/;[5] Chinese: &amp;#23665;&amp;#35199;; formerly romanised as Shansi) is a landlocked province of the People&#39;s Republic of China and is part of the North China region. The capital and largest city of the province is Taiyuan, while its next most populated prefecture-level cities are Changzhi and Datong. Its one-character abbreviation is &quot;&amp;#26187;&quot; (pinyin: J&igrave;n), after the state of Jin that existed there during the Spring and Autumn period.
