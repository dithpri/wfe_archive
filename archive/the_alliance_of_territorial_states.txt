All Countries are Territorial States combined in 1 alliance dedicated to help one another.
All Countries are Welcome!

Government Spots Open

Chief Diplomatic State: Head of All Foreign Relations. Partners with World Assembly Delegate.

Chief Militaristic State: Head of the Allied Army. Main General of the Alliance.

Diplomatic Advisor: Advisor to the Chief Diplomatic State.
Military Advisor: Advisor to the Chief Militaristic State.

Vice Delegate: If the Current World Assembly Delegate ever resigns, the Vice Delegate takes over and becomes the new Delegate.
