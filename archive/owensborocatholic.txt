Article I
The presidents term along with his/her adminstration will have a two week term. The president can propose resolutions to benifet the region. The president and excutive powers can also propose admendments.
Article II
The executive powers can not run for the administration. The executive powers can only interfere with the threat of a revolt or the region being attacked.
Article III
The Attorney General controls the borders and war with nations. The Secretary of State controls embassies. The presidents holds them in line.
