The Independent Osla Confederation is an international organisation for developped democracies. All nations could propose a resolution, and a change of the integration conditions.

The legislative is the Independent Osla Confederation&#39;s Assembly (IOCA). Another institution, who verify that the official candidates to join, the observators and the current members respect the criteria, is called the Independent Osla Confederation&#39;s Integration Council (IOCIC).

To join, please contact the Founder or a member of the Integration Council (IOCIC).
