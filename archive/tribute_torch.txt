Tribute Torch is an innovative region with the intent to positively influence the known world. They believe in strength, liberty, and the the pursuit of purpose. 

Founded by the Republic of Thalson, the region was designed to be a special region. Flexible, modern, adaptive, and influential, the region gives its nations autonomy to maximize freedom and prosperity.

They hope to expand diplomatic relationships with other members of the world and live a life of peace and correspondence.

Tribute Torch respects all beliefs and viewpoints, and they welcome the greatest nations to join the noble region.
