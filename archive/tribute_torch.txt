Tribute Torch is an innovative region with the intent to positively influencing the known world. They believe in strength, liberty, and the the pursuit of purpose. 

Founded by the Republic of Thalson, the region was designed to trump the rest. Flexible, modern, adaptive, and influential, the region gives its nations autonomy to maximize freedom and prosperity.

Tribute Torch respects all beliefs and viewpoints, and we welcome the greatest nations to join our noble region.
