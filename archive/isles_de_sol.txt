THE WORLD FACTBOOK: REGIONAL OVERVIEW
## REGION: ISLES DE SOL
# INTRODUCTION
Background: The Isles De Sol constitute a vast, tropical archipelago of sovereign island nations, dependencies, and territories scattered across a strategic maritime shelf. Defining the boundary between the open Atlantic and internal shallow seas, the region is unified by shared Lucayan linguistic roots, a tumultuous history of colonial privateering, and vibrant, sun-drenched cultures. Today, the region is highly regarded for its immense marine biodiversity, major international shipping lanes, and a strong geopolitical alignment toward sustainable ocean commerce&#151;with Tariana Cay serving as one of its prominent independent anchors.
