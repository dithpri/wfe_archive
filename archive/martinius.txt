A home that would make our forefathers smile. Martinius, founded on this day by the royal family so all who come after may experience goodness and life that much more abundantly.  

Headed by King David Alexander I and his beautiful wife Alexis. Beloved by the people of cannabai.

In the year 627 David Alexander, then warrior, now king freed Cannabai from the oppression of her enemies and a golden age ensued. Many blessings came about as the people of Cannabai were now free. The most note worthy being that king David Alexander married Queen Alexis and she begat two children. Twins, his first born Prince Maximus Alexander and Princess Bernadette Alexander.
