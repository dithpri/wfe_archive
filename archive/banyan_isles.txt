Capital: Talimar
Government: The Green Directorate
Climate: Warm, tropical, rainy
Languages: Banyani, Miron
A peaceful equatorial archipelago of misty jungles, coral bays, and towering banyan forests. Futuristic eco-settlements blend with lush scenery, powered by solar and tidal energy. Culture mixes European and Asian island traditions, known for rain festivals and relaxed coastal living.
