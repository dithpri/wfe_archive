The Confederate Imperium region is dominated by a continental empire that spans the entire North American Continent. With the exception of a very small rebellion of small states known as the American Commonwealth of States, which is protected by the World Assembly. The Confederate Imperium of Sovereign States of America is ruled by Emperor Rand Paul with a military matched only by the entirety of the World Assembly.
