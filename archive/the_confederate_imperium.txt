As The Confederate Imperium of Sovereign States remains strongfast on their claim of the rebellious American Commonwealth of States. The World Assembly has declared them to be disputed territories and under the charter of the world assembly will remain in there protection. The Emperor of the Confederate Imperium is furious that he cannot annex the rebel states back into the Imperium. The World Assembly has publically supported Chancellor Anderson Cooper as the legal and rightful ruler of The Disputed Terrirtories of the American Commonwealth of States. For now there is an uneasy peace between the two nations with the Confederate Imperium denying all trade.
