[b]The [region]Germanic Confederation[/region] is a union of sovereign Germanic states, united for purposes of common action against communist and other threats to Nationalism arising from Antifascist action

Modern Germanic peoples include the Afrikaners, Austrians, Danes, Dutch, English, Flemish, Frisians, Germans, Icelanders, Lowland Scots, Norwegians, and Swedes. Each are welcome
