Arneb, also designated as &amp;#945; Leporis (alpha Leporis), is a variable and multiple hypergiant star of spectral class F0 in the constellation of Lepus. Arneb visual magnitude is 2.58, making it the 96th brightest star in the sky. Because of its brightness, Arneb is clearly visible to the naked eye when observed from locations with dark skies, and should be also quite easily visible from light polluted areas.
