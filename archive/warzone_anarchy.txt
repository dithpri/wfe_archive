In this region, the founder designed it to be Warzone-like. Raiders, defenders, nations in training, unaligned nations, and even nations attempting to challenge themselves by living here are welcome (well, challenge when this region grows). 

Every day, usually between 6 AM and 12 PM, is when the governor unbans everyone, removes the password, and repins the &quot;Warzone Anarchy&quot; dispatch. The governor also never bans or puts up a password themself, except for when if a delegate has been in office for at least 3 days nations can telegram the governor to ask to have the current delegate ejected.

Also, for now, the Delegate has no &quot;Appearance&quot; controls. This is to prevent anyone from ruining the WFE, flag, or banner too early.
