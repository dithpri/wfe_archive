The Realm of the Lighthouse is a realm lead by the ruling monarch, [nation]Repreteop[/nation]. The Lighthouse is the capital region of the realm, with Phoenixia and Moreau as its provinces. All can come through to its doors, through affiliated regions. The Lighthouse is the safe place of the region and is kept safe by King Alfred I of Moreau.
