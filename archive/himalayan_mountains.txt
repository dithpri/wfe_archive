The Himalayas, or Himalaya (/&amp;#716;h&amp;#618;m&amp;#601;&amp;#712;le&amp;#618;.&amp;#601;, h&amp;#618;&amp;#712;m&amp;#593;&amp;#720;l&amp;#601;j&amp;#601;/; Sanskrit: [&amp;#614;&amp;#618;ma&amp;#720;l&amp;#592;j&amp;#592;]; from Sanskrit him&aacute; &#39;snow, frost&#39;, and &amp;#257;-laya &#39;dwelling, abode&#39;), is a mountain range in Asia, separating the plains of the Indian subcontinent from the Tibetan Plateau. The range has some of the Earth&#39;s highest peaks, including the highest, Mount Everest; more than 100 peaks exceeding elevations of 7,200 m (23,600 ft) above sea level lie in the Himalayas.
