The scope of policy of Pala necessitates the founding of a new Region. The Palan Treaty Organisation (PTO) represents the interests of the Palan Archipelago and its allies. The PTO is in policy formulation awaiting introduction to the world scale.

The Palan Archipelago is a constitutional republic located in the South Pacific, generally similar to New Zealand in geography. The country is governed by a federal government and four semi-autonomous provinces - all finally accountable to the Constitution. The Constitution of Pala includes guaranteed rights based on the Universal Declaration of Human Rights. The land, Constitution, values, and People are named &quot;Pala.&quot; The government is named &quot;The Palan Archipelago.&quot;
