[color=green][b]West African Association[/b][/color]






The West African Association is a West African multilateral armed force established by the Economic Community of West African States (ECOWAS)

[b]History[/b]

 ECOWAS members agreed to a Protocol on Mutual Defence Assistance, in Freetown, Sierra Leone, on 29 May 1981. Among other organs such as a Defence Committee and Council, it provided for the establishment of an Allied Armed Force of the Community (AAFC) as needed.
