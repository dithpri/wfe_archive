The diverse landscape of Chestonia is home to a number of countries with their own set of unique values. Some chill, some not so chill. 

The region is home to a number of animals including the Arctic Fox, Grey Nosed Deer, Lake Nest Water Fowl, Wolverine, Vulture. Bear, Yartoro, Cricket, Grey Wolf, Narwal, and Wambat.
