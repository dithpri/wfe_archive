[b][color=blue]==The Typhoon Union==[/b][/color]

Welcome! The Typhoon Union is a new region found in January 28th, 2014, and mainly focused on military RPs.
The region is the home for any nations that are hungry for power, or nations that are looking for advanced weapons
to use in their armed forces.

Nations in this region can freely trade their arsenals to each other, helping each other when one nation is in war, and
even declare war on a nation within this region!
