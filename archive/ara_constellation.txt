[i]Ara (Latin for &quot;the Altar&quot;): is a southern constellation between Scorpius, Telescopium, Triangulum Australe, and Norma. It was (as &amp;#914;&amp;#969;&amp;#956;&amp;#972;&amp;#962;, B&amp;#333;m&amp;#466;s) one of the Greek bulk (namely 48) described by the 2nd-century astronomer Ptolemy, and it remains one of the 88 modern constellations designated by the International Astronomical Union.[/i][b]Founded by the andarian starfleet on: 04/05/2025.[/b]
