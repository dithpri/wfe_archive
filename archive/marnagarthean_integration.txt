The Terran-Marnagarthean war is over. Due to Marnagarthean technical superiority, it was no contest. The Earth was divided into 108 satrapies, and Marnagarthean sympathizers were trained in the invaders&#39; methods of governing. But actually, it was a win-win. Earth has gained access to previously unimaginable advanced tech, and the Marnagarthean Commonwealth continues to expand across the galaxy. No one likes to be conquered, but the enlightened principles of Marnagarthean rule, as well as the prosperity resulting from becoming a hotspot for interstellar tourism, have reconciled the planet to its fate.
