When the nations of Earth were finally integrated into the Marnagarthean Commonwealth, there was a great diversity in their governments and ideologies. This was expected. Marnagartheans had come to understand that each nation had its own traditions and preferences, which it was unwise to ignore. No one likes to be conquered, but the enlightened principles of Marnagarthean rule, as well as the prosperity resulting from becoming a hotspot for interstellar tourism, reconciled the planet to its fate.
