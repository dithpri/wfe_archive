[b]Welcome to the World Association (WAS)[/b]

Founded 18/1/2018, the World Association is committed to providing a great roleplay experience, while also ensuring the participation of all members. The World Association is founded upon the idea of community and so we have created a government in which everyone can participate in, with parties and active voting in legislation.

[b]Nations of the World Association are encouraged to join the WA and endorse our WA delegate! Every endorsement for the delegate is a stronger WAS in the WA![/b]

The World Association does not have an endorsement cap.
Regional advertising in player-created regions is against the rules!
