The Coalition of Dragons is a multinational coalition within the NationStates world dedicated to promoting diplomacy, mutual defense cooperation, economic prosperity, and cultural exchange among its member nations. Established by a group of ambitious regional powers seeking a stronger voice in international affairs, The Coalition of Dragons has grown into a respected coalition known for its unity, resilience, and strategic influence.
