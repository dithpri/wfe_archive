The [region]Lorania[/region] House of Commons was the legislature of Lorania from shortly after its creation, in May 2020, as per the [url=https://www.nationstates.net/page=dispatch/id=1342341]Constitution of Lorania.[/url] It conducted its business in this region, Lorania House of Commons.

The Constitution - along with [url=https://www.nationstates.net/page=dispatch/id=1536184]all other laws of Lorania[/url] - was repealed, and the Commons dissolved, after the [url=https://www.nationstates.net/page=dispatch/id=1545233]APOCALYPSE NOW Act[/url] of May 2021. The Act passed with the support of all three MPs at the time: [nation]2-4-Dichlorophenoxyacetic Acid[/nation], [nation]Cousethii[/nation] (who was the Speaker of the House), and [nation]Donelant Holorendal[/nation]. Two seats formerly held by Ogliacia and Seven Seas were vacant.
