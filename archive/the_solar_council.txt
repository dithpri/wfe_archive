[b][color=darkblue]THE SOLAR COUNCIL[/b][/color]

The earth colonized the inner and outer planets. But the solar system is still the limit and humanity is still not united. On earth, five powerful blocs are in a state of cold war while most of the colonies on the other planets, planetoids and moons declared their independence. The whole system is at the brink to war and a single incident may change our lives forever...
