Slovakia, officially the [region]Slovak Republic[/region] (Slovensk&aacute; Republika), is a landlocked country in Central Europe, bordered by [region]Poland[/region], [region]Ukraine[/region], [region]Hungary[/region], [region]Austria[/region], and the [region]Czech Republic[/region]. Its capital is [region]Bratislava[/region], and it has about 5.5 million people. The official language is Slovak, and the currency is the euro. Slovakia is a parliamentary democracy with an economy based on automotive, machinery, electronics, and tourism. The country has a rich cultural heritage and is a member of the EU, NATO, the UN, and the Schengen Area.
