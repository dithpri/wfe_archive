The Berghof was Adolf Hitler&#39;s home in the Obersalzberg of the Bavarian Alps near Berchtesgaden, Germany. It was located lower down the same mountain as the Kehlsteinhaus, or Eagle&#39;s Nest, which Hitler rarely visited due to his fear of heights.
