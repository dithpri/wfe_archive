[b]THE FEDERAL KAISERREICH OF DEI GENTEM[/b]

EVERY NATION ON THE FOLLOWING LIST IS UNDER THE PROTECTION OF THE SWORD OF THE KAISERREICH, AND ARE AN EXTENSION OF THE HAND OF THE FEDERAL PARLIAMENT AND OF THE FUHRER:

THE QUEENDOM OF SPRING HAVEN - MOTHERLAND
ISOEBLARIA - THE SHIELD
DEI IPSIUS - THE SWORD
DEI SERVUS - THE WORKER

JOINT TASK FORCE UNITS ARE AVAILABLE TO THE PROVINCES, AS THE FATHERLAND RECOGNIZES THE AUTHORITY OF THE PROVINCES TO MAINTAIN THEIR OWN FOREIGN RELATIONS, WITH FEDERAL INTERVENTION ONLY ALLOWED IF SUCH FOREIGN RELATIONS ENDANGER THE INTEGRETY OF THE KAISERREICH
