The Northern trade and Defense Union is a new region dedicated to an exchange of ideas, agreeable or not. Open Debate is encouraged between nations, So long as Debates are kept civil (attack the argument not the person). Usurpers and there supporters will be tried for the crime of Treason Against Open Exchange. If found guilty by a majority of 5/8ths votes cast a ban will be enacted. This region grants sanctuary to former Usurpers of other regions, This sanctuary will be revoked if said nation helps usurp another region again, and placed on trial. Elections are held every 2 months for reappointment of offices, with the exception of WA Delegate, being voted on every 2 weeks.
