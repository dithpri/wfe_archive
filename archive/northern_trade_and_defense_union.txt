The Northern trade and Defense Union is a new region dedicated to an exchange of ideas, agreeable or not. Open Debate is encouraged between nations, So long as Debates are kept civil (attack the argument not the person). Usurpers and there supporters will be tried for the crime of Treason Against Open Exchange. If found guilty by a majority of 5/8ths votes cast a ban will be enacted. Elections are held every 2 months for reappointment of offices, with the exception of WA Delegate, being voted on every 2 weeks.
