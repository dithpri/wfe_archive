Vylixan Hestia&#39;s region, known as &#39;Eternal Hearth,&#39; is a region where the warmth of community and the sanctity of home reign supreme. Named after the goddess of the hearth and family, this region is a haven of peace, stability, and hospitality. The architecture here blends tradition with comfort, featuring homes centered around communal hearths. The citizens of Eternal Hearth hold family values and community welfare in high regard, often gathering for communal meals and celebrations. The region is also known for its well-preserved historical sites, ancient temples, and lush gardens, creating a serene and nurturing environment.
