Following the destruction of the old nations of Earth in the System War, the planet and the space around it enters an era of new nations that rise from the ashes of war.  The entire Sol system undergoes a rebirth as nations with people from all walks of life are created and the borders of the old word are redrawn.  The time of A.D. has come to pass, to time of the Sovereign Era has begun.
