[b]The Royal Union[/b]
[i]Date Union Authorized: 8/12/13[/i]

The Royal Union is a monarchist region that emphasizes unity and cooperation among its members.
The region was first founded by the Kingdom of the Royal Islands. It is headed by a chairman and the union is based off of a constitution.
