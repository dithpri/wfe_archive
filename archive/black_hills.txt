The [b]Black Hills[/b] are a small, isolated mountain range rising from the Great Plains of North America in western South Dakota and extending into Wyoming, United States. Black Elk Peak, which rises to 7,244 feet, is the range&#39;s highest summit.The Black Hills encompass the Black Hills National Forest. The name &quot;Black Hills&quot; is a translation of the Lakota Pah&aacute; S&aacute;pa. The hills were so-called because of their dark appearance from a distance, as they were covered in trees. &#151;Wikipedia [hr]
