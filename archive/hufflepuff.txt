Hufflepuff values hard work, patience, justice, and loyalty. The house mascot is the badger, and canary yellow and black are its colours. The Hufflepuff motto is &amp;quot;Those patient Hufflepuffs are true and unafraid of toil&amp;quot;. The Head of this house is the Herbology teacher Pomona Sprout, and the house ghost is The Fat Friar. According to Rowling, Hufflepuff corresponds roughly to the element of earth. 
Founder: Helga Hufflepuff
