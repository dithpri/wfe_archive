A vast, resource-rich region, Astol lies on the crossroads of vital trade routes which had propelled the region from an otherwise obscure area of farmland and ore mines to a land of riches and opportunity. However, prolonged conflict between the various nations had left countless, indelible scars to the plains of Astol, and its once pristine landscape is now marred by towering fortifications, grotesque ruins and twisted machinations of war.
