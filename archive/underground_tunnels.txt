Tunnels of the [region]Korean Peoples Army[/region] for moving troops, military hardware, parts and ammunition.[hr]

 The North Koreans began tunneling after the 1950-53 Korean War, when U.S. bombing destroyed most of their industrial base and infrastructure. Kim Il Sung directed key industrial facilities be built underground.
