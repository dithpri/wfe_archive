The [b]Ryukyu Islands[/b] are a chain of Japanese islands that stretch southwest from Kyushu to Taiwan. The islands are divided into the Satsunan Islands and Okinawa Prefecture. The larger islands are mostly volcanic, and the smaller mostly coral. The largest is [region]Okinawa Island[/region]. &#151;Wikipedia[hr]
