Welcome to the region
In this region laws are respected as well as the rights of the people as well as public policies based on freedom and political sovereignty in all matters such as:

* Freedom of free expression
* Peace among the population

* Zero crime rates
* Corruption is not accepted as a form of government

* Governments with a lot of authority are not accepted and must be democratic (unless you are a monarchical country you should have a broad political freedom for your nation)

* Must be inclusive in all aspects
* They should respect the other countries that are incorporated into the nation
