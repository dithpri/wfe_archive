The Oceanic Union is a region made out of a group of nations. There is a House of Delegates, with a seat proportional to 1 million, and the party who forms the government and the leader as the Prime Minister. The Prime Minister appoints all positions, except for the Founder. The Prime Minister is also the World Assembly Delegate.
