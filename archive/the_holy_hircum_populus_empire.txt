A region built for the holy empire of the Great Goat God. All nations in the Holy Hircum Populus Empire must have the goat as the national animal, Hircum Religio as the national religion (when available), be religiously oriented, and have good environmental protections.
Any nation that wishes to join the Holy Hircum Populus Empire as long as it meets the above expectations or is showing progress towards meeting the expectations. Region raiders will not be tolerated.
