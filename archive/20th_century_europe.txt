The Great War changed the face of Europe permanently. Under the leadership of Hans Richter, Germany held on from the brink of defeat in 1917 and a cold war between the Entente and the Central Powers rose to the forefront.
Polish rebels fight for their land, now part of the German Reich, while New Soviet Russia struggles to keep itself together. The United States of Greater Austria faces the challenge of a federalized, multi-ethnic nation, and German imperialists denounce the ruling government.
