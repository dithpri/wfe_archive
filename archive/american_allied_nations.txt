The American Allied Nations is a union created by the Incorporated States of the Joint Republic (originally the Oppressed Peoples of the Joint Republic), and the Joint Republic of Imperial America after the second world war. Its purpose serves to ensure a stable economy throughout the globe, and maintain peace wherever it influences.

The Commonwealth of American Coalitions exists only to manage the region page. It holds no other purpose in terms of lore.

The Federal Republic of North Pennsyltucky has a respresenting Ambassador, however, the nation was since removed by the site for being inactive. Presence is still there for lore sake.
