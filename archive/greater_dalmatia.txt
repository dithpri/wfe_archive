Greater Dalmatia is a land of many proud and beautiful nation states. In the aftermath of the 2nd Great Dalmatian War, fought between the Muslim Empire of the East and the Dalmatian States, an alliance was formed to prevent any form Muslim resurgence in the region. This alliance founded by the People&#39;s Republic of New Expatria, the King of the Coconut Kingdom, New Penfrosir, the Republic of the Mighty Meme Machine and the Disputed Territories of GG Wankistan (Which seceded from the Empire and engaged in brutal guerrilla combat for decades) has worked efficiently and ruthlessly to expunge any form of Islamic influence from their great land.
