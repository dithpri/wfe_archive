[color=darkred]Guadalajara is the capital as well as the largest city in [nation=short]Finance District[/nation]. It is also the most important financial and cultural center in the country. The City represents 20% of GDP and is home to 10% of the population. Most embassies will be accepted if it is in the best interest of the Government.
