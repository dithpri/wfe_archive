The year is 2209.  Following a global nuclear war in 2100 the world as we know it came to an end.  Now 100 years later the stout and hardy people of western Europe once again rise to the occasion and enter the new era, raising the banner of civilization once more.  Will the relive the mistakes of the past, or will Europe move beyond the ways of war once and for all?
