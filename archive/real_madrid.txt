[color=#186095][b]REAL MADRID[/b][/color]
[hr]Real Madrid Club de F&uacute;tbol, commonly known as Real Madrid, is a multi-sport organization based in Madrid, Spain. It was founded as a football club in 1902 with the purpose of the practice and development of this sport, and received the title of &quot;Royal&quot; in 1920 from King Alfonso XIII.

The club is owned by over 90,000 partners and has continuously competed in La Liga since its inception. Real Madrid is the most successful team in La Liga, having won the most titles and achieving the highest score in a single edition.
