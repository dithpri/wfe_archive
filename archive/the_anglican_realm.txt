The Anglican Realm is the collection of countries in [b][i]free association[/i][/b] with Regnatum Albion and for which the Anglican Monarch is head of state. Also known as the Commonwealth of the Realm, the eight constituent countries outside of Albion have almost total [b][i]self-rule[/i][/b] as of 2009, but surrender powers over foreign relations and defence to the government of the Crown Commonwealth on the [b][i]consent[/i][/b] of their own administrations.

The Anglican Realm is united under the common history, values and efforts of its members, but recognises the distinct cultural values of its separate constituents.
