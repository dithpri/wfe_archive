East Oceania is a region in Oceania, that consists of small island nations including the founding nation, of The Democratic States of Vervar. This region is very similar in history and founding, as well as indigenous tribes. Because of the close proximity between these island nations in East Oceania, they share very similar culture, people, foods, history and tribal groups. These island nations are very dependent on each other, and have very strong diplomatic ties to one another. This region helps bring these island nations together and keep the peace between them.
