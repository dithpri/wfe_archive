We are the Discordia-Potentia Allegiance is an allegiance committed to upholding the rights of man and all the freedoms that come with it, despite our name we stand for the order and safety of our people and aim to uphold our motto Fortitudo,virtus,salus , or Strength, Courage and Safety. Named after the founder of Asheo, Discordia the Warrior Queen, who fought for and protected her lands and all who resided in them.
