Welcome to Hindustan Union,  
a region representing the nations, cultures, and peoples of South Asia.  

&amp;#9878;&amp;#65039; Government Structure  
- Council of Nations: All resident nations may participate in discussions.  
- President: A ceremonial head of region, elected once every three months.  
- Prime Minister: The executive head, elected once every two months to lead policy and administration.  

&amp;#128220; Principles  
- Unity in Diversity  
- Respect for Sovereignty  
- Peaceful Coexistence  

&amp;#127919; Regional Focus  
- Limited roleplay in modern South Asian politics and diplomacy  
- Occasional elections and council debates  
- A formal, respectful community for nations interested in the Subcontinent&#146;s culture and future
