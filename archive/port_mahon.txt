[b]Port Mahon[/b] is the capital and second largest city of Menorca. The city is located on the eastern coast of the island, which is part of the archipelago and autonomous community of the [region]Balearic Islands[/region].

Port Mahon has one of the longest natural harbours in the world: three miles long and up to 3,000 feet wide. The water is deep but remains mostly clear due to the port&#39;s enclosed nature. Mayonnaise is considered to have originated in Port Mahon. 

Its population in 2021 was estimated to be 29,125.&#151;Wikipedia [hr]
