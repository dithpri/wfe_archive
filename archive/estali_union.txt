The Estali Union (EU) is a political and economic union of 4 member states that are located in the Estali region. The union has an estimated total population of around 1.44 billion. The EU has been described as a political entity with the characteristics of a federation or confederation.
The EU has its origins in the Estali Community of Goods (ECG) and the Estali Economic and Development Community (EEDC), established respectively by the Treaty of Nabrosia and the Treaty of Cadernia. The current member states of what came to become the Estali Union are The Big Four: Wassermia, Ordisa, Eternialand and Kavynia Currently the nation of Vikalia is negotiating to join the union.
