[b]This region is the Homeland of the Asmorians and a few Allied States.[/b]

Currently the Roleplay  &quot; The Carnian War &quot; is in affect. 

After the recent fall of the Asmorian Empire, along with a Kingdom being founded, it has been decided to form an Alliance/ Federation to restore Peace and Unity, that the old Empire once provided.
