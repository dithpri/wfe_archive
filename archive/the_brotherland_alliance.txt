The Brotherland Alliance is a Pact between Nations who contain historical roots from the Brotherland itslef and is not affiliated with The unofficial Region known as [region]The Brotherland[/Region].

On October. 4. 2014 [nation]Penutdation[/nation] along with [nation]Determan[/nation] establishes The Brotherland Alliance as a Revival of Historical Relations between the Brotherland Nations and Creates the popular symbolic flag of the proud alliance. Soon being recognized as a worthy movement, the alliance encourages other Brother Nations to join.
