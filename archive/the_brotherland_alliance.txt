The Brotherland Alliance is a coalition of neighboring nations brought together by there ancestral roots in the Brotherland. Founded October 4. 2014, The Brotherland Alliance has surpassed many obstacles and undergone Region changing Events. Initially experiencing borderline civil war between its Autocratic and Democratic parties resulting from the cinders of creation. Marked with the Autocratics taking control a mutual agreement was made between both parties maintaining major influence. At its prime, a golden age promoted growth, wealth and knowledge calling forth many leaders and prospering numerous nations from the far reaches of The Brotherland.
