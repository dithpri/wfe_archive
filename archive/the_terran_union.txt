The Terran Union consists of a conflagration of governments that still existed after a great war sometime ago and that we deemed to be worthy of protecting and upholding precise moral values of its member states. Every nation in the Union most abide by a strict code of law as well as vow to protect and support each other no matter the cause. The nations of the Terran union of viewed with both disgust and admiration due to their effective yet sometimes cruel policies.
