On a vast continent, far from any body of water larger than lakes and rivers, there is an expanse of bleak, nigh-desolate land:  The Steppes of Gehenna.  The Steppes are home to untamed (and unusually deadly) wildlife prey on both one another and unprepared explorers and hardy plant life that ekes out a hardscrabble existence in the unforgiving perpetual chill of these bleak, windswept plains.  Yet, if one ventures far enough into the Steppes, they find that it is not quite so desolate as it first appears...whispers tell of a colossal nation many months&#39; journey from any civilization found on the map, but it must be a legend.  No people, no matter how determined, could carve out any such thing in an unforgiving land such as this...
