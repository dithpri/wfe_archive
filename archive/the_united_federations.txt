[b]The United Federations[/b] are the subset of Nations residing on the planet SpookVooper alongside Vooperia.

SpookVooper was discovered by SpikeViper, who went on to settle the nation of Vooperia. Vooperia is the largest nation on SpookVooper, and has a system of Federations for those who wish to live alongside it. Federations may introduce their own policies and laws as long as they will protect the planet in the case of an attack and respect the basic values of Vooperians.
