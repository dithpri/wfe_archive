The [b][color=red]Marxist Union Treaty Organization[/color] (MUTO)[/b] is a brotherhood of left-leaning nations that adhere to one of the the many socialist and/or Communist ideologies in existence. In [b]MUTO[/b], like-minded nations are able to establish diplomatic ties and friendly communication with fellow leftist countries and the individuals within them. This is a place where members may freely debate, discuss, form alliances and/or trade deals with one another, participate in roleplay, join and even more political parties, and even become a member of the regional government if that&#39;s your cup of tea, with various positions available that are integral to the functioning and future success of the region.
