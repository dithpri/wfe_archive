The [b][color=red]Marxist Union Treaty Organization[/color][/b] is a collective of regions that share socialist and/or Communist ideologies and wish to spread those ideologies throughout the world. All MUTO member regions shall be held to the highest possible standard and are expected to cooperate diplomatically with all fellow nations. 

Please consult the official Constitution of MUTO which can be found pinned in the regional dispatches. There you may also find instructions on joining the organization.

MUTO was originally founded by [b]Provinci[/b] of the [b][color=red][/b]Red Alliance[/color] and [b]Tarik Re&amp;#351;it[/b], Premier of the [color=red][b]United Socialist States of Kuross Tarky[/b][/color].
