The [b][color=maroon]Marxist Union Treaty Organization[/color][/b] is a collective of nations that adhere to socialist, communist, and various other left-leaning ideologies and as such desire to be in the company of like-minded countries, with whom they may share and explore their political ideals. MUTO is a region where friendships and alliances are forged as members discuss, debate, and explore their views on politics, economic systems, current events, and more.

MUTO was originally founded by [nation]Provinci[/nation] and [nation]Kuross Tarky[/nation] during the summer of 2017.
