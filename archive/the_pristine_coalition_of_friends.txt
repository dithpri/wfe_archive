The Pristine Coalition of Friends was conceived under the auspices of The Constitutional Monarchy of Walkeristan as a testament to the enduring bonds among its member nations. This coalition stands as a beacon of unity, dedicated to fostering a collaborative and harmonious relationship among its diverse members. By championing mutual respect, shared prosperity, and collective progress, the coalition embodies the essence of a pristine alliance dedicated to the well-being and advancement of its constituent nations.
