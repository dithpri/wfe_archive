A free region, LAAT is the home for socially progressive nations with education, peace, the environment, free speech, economic assistance and social rights among their priorities. The nations hopes to become a powerful and intelligent member of the NationStates world, and picks the nations it admits very selectively. If you wish to become part of LAAT, a telegram must be sent with your request to either the founder or to the Delegate. Your nation will be assessed, and if found worthy, the password will be telegrammed back to you. Because of this exclusiveness, all nations within LAAT enjoy many special privileges of being part of a tight knit community of excellent nations destined for greatness.
