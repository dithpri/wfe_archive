An idyllic and beautiful land, Tian Harra is home to many green valleys and soaring mountains. It has a rich history tracing back thousands of years, with many hundreds of ethnic groups calling the region home. The recent Tian Harran Civil war cost the death of nearly two hundred million people, leaving the entire realm shaken. 


After many arguments and troubles regarding help and assistance from the outside world during their time of need, the entire region of Tian Harra and it&#39;s countries have isolated themselves from the rest of humanity and intend to keep it that way.
