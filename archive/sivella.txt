Sivella is the latest in a long line of regions that succeeded Andaba. Throughout numerous conflicts between early 2015 and the present day, a number of spinoff regions have formed; though there are now three in total, only Sivella is the closest replication of the pristine prosperity that member nations found under Andaba.

The region is now open to new membership. For now, interested aspirants need only telegram either the Vice Commandant or Supreme Leader himself for the password.
