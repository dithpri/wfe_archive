Hello, everyone. These diverse regions across the world have one common unique trait.

We are the only home of the natural Woonf, and Caleonia has a different breed, known as the Kale Woonf.

Overall, we support both extremes of government, and with a few nations, already have very diverse ideals.

Caleonia occupies the mainland of East Asia, while Steneste and Colon-Z occupy some major islands in Asia, probably Borneo and Sumatra respectively. The newcomers come from all lands, from Canada to Japan.
