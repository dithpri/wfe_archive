Tonosejo is a coastal region in the Eldrid Continent, bordering the Crystal sea. The region is known for its vibrant culture, stunning coastline, and rich marine life. The capital, Yener City, is a major hub for trade and tourism.

Tonosejo&#39;s economy is driven by fishing, tourism, and sustainable energy. The region is home to the Yener Institute of Oceanic Studies, a leading research center for marine conservation.

Tonosejo has a unique blend of indigenous and colonial influences, reflected in its architecture, cuisine, and festivals. The region celebrates the annual Luminaria Festival, honoring the sea goddess, Aethera.
