Tranaqi is a self-sufficient, peaceful region prioritizing agriculture, seafood, and animal domestication. It operates under a semi-democratic system with an executive branch holding more power than standard. Its society values nature, community, and collective well-being.

Tranaqians are known for their deep-rooted respect for nature and their commitment to living in harmony with the environment. The economy of Tranaqi thrives on the principles of self-sufficiency and sustainability. Agriculture remains the backbone of the region, and animal domestication plays a vital role in the economy, with dairy and livestock industries supporting local food needs and providing surplus for export.
