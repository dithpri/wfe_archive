International Democracy Defense Union (IDDU)

Full Name: International Democracy Defense Union
Currency: Unity
Official Language(s): English

The IDDU is a global alliance of nations committed to democracy, human rights, and peace. It promotes diplomatic relations, collective defense, and economic cooperation. Members must adhere to democratic values, international law, and human rights protection.

Benefits:

Collective Security
Economic Cooperation
Diplomatic Influence
Humanitarian Aid
The IDDU invites nations that share its principles to apply for membership.
