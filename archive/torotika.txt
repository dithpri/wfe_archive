Torotika was once an empire hailing from the great Suvyn river that rose to dominate this continent. However, epidemic from the east subverted the centuries of conquest and rendered a brutal war across the seas with Isel confederation ultimately useless. The superstitious masses lost all their faith and precious piety, lamenting the day as the Day of Judgement. The remnants of civilisation, as a concept, lay strewn and disparate across the vast swathes of forest, plains and mountain, struggling with their forlorn past.
