This Region is for all the nations from the lore of Velvet Nine, a game being developed by Lunchbox Games.

Velvet Nine is about an Evil Princess (Princess Velvet) who wants to expand her empire. Her army invades the land of Tarantula, and hires 8 other mercenaries to guard the 8 regions of Tarantula. It is up to a mage/apprentice named Lavender to stop Princess Velvet&#39;s tyranny and free his homeland from her grasp.

This region is purely for the entertainment of VN fans, created and maintained by the actual developer of VN himself.
