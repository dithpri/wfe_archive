Basic principles of the Constitution
1. This region is autocratic, you have no real power to challenge the Prime Minister&#39;s orders, once you are defined as trying to subvert the country, you will be immediately included in the unwelcome list.
2. To obtain an official position, you need to live in the region for at least two months and make certain contributions. If you abuse your power, you will be immediately included in the unwelcome list.
3. Freedom of speech is controlled. You may not promote your region or make dangerous remarks, otherwise you will be immediately included in the unwelcome list.
