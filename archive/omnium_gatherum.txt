The Omnium Gatherum is a loose coalition of the nation states known as The Most Serene Republic of Rawaki, The Community of Elation, and The Peoples Republic of Airstrip Five. Rawaki abides by the rules of diplomacy and democracy, Elation Libertarianism and free choice, and Airstrip Five by totalitarianism and total rule. Even though we island nations have differing views we stand together. Rawaki will be the official the world delegate for this region.
