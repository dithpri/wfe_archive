The Danelaw, as recorded in the Anglo-Saxon Chronicle, is a historical name given to the part of England in which the laws of the Danes held sway and dominated those of the Anglo-Saxons.
Contrary to popular thought, the Vikings of the Danelaw were noble warriors that adhered to a strict code of law. Here, in the Danelaw we are one in the same.
