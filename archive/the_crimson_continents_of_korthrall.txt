Korthall is a colonial industrial planet based in the timeline and dimension of Germanic Templars. With exports on rare and alien minerals and crystals used to power most fleets of the empire as well as trading vessels.

It lies on the rim of the galaxy being dubbed as a Rim world - planets know to being susceptible to pirate raids. However, due to the premise of the colony being that of industry, security both homemade and provided by the empire as well as shareholders of the planet, the surplus of protection as matched by the surplus of resources makes it the most protected rim world in the galaxy!
