Evrasia is a vast and diverse region known for its remarkable tolerance and acceptance of all forms of governance. Stretching across a broad geographical expanse that encompasses varying climates, cultures, and historical legacies, Evrasia is a true mosaic of political systems coexisting in relative harmony. From democratic republics and monarchies to technocracies, tribal councils, and even benevolent autocracies.

For any concerns about mapping please contact the Empire of Maua Germania or the Community of Evrasia which will help you to get your country on the map on the !official discord server for Evrasia!
