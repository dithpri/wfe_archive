[b]Democratic Republic of the Congo[/b] (French: R&eacute;publique D&eacute;mocratique du Congo), sometimes referred to as DR Congo, [region=Congo Kinshasa] or the DRC, is a country located in central Africa. It is the second largest country in Africa by area and the eleventh largest in the world. With a population of over 75 million, Democratic Republic of the Congo is the nineteenth most populous nation in the world, the fourth most populous nation in Africa, as well as the most populous officially Francophone country. Kinshasa is the largest French speaking city in the world.
