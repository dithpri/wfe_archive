[region]The Alliance Of[/region] is a region and political allegiance, founded by [nation]The Beanlands[/nation] as a method of establishing trade, collaboration and mutual protection.

Its members share a fearsome collective army, dedicated to protection of the region from hostility, invasions to further the region&#39;s Imperium, and other military operations. An attack against one member is an attack against all, and they will defend members dearly. They also share resources and collaborate to better develop the region overall, as well as recruiting new nations to join.

Any nation that wishes to join this region must abide by the regional law system that shall be released to all members via a mass telegram in a few days.
