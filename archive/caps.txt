[b]Congress of Armed Proletarian States[/b] was a democratic project for an inter-regional socialist organization within NationStates, hosted in 2014, and attended by nearly 50 comrade-representatives from CIL, Communist China, The Communist Party of NationStates, Das Kommune, Democratic Socialist Assembly, The Green Star Socialist Union, The Internationale, Isles of Socialism, North Korea, Socialist Republic, and UCR. It aimed to unite those regions in an organization that would facilitate better communication, political education, and gameplay cooperation among the Leftists of NationStates.
