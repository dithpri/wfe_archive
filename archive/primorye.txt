Capital: Artyom
Population: 8,000,000
Major cities: Artyom, Vladivostok, Nakhodka, Arsenyev, Ussuriysk
Government: Republic
Leader: President Nicholas Nesterenko
Official Languages: Russian, English, Esperanto

Founded in 2018 after the Great Coup, Greater Nestorenkia is a fledgling democracy that seeks to care for its people through reasonable democracy and a blend of capitalism and socialism.
