The region of Everwinter is a dark cold land known for its year long snowfall, frequent blizzards and brutal winds. The populace does not seem to mind as they have adapted to frigid climate in a way that they never feel cold. Regional fauna includes Snow Lions, Polar Bears, Storm Boars, Wind Hawks, Great Ulk, the Providence Crow, Reindeer, and the terrifying Remorhaz Tundra Wyrm.
