Welcome to Avathar!

Disaster has befallen our continent. A plague of undetermined origin swept through its nations and decimated almost all of them. Only Phyrexia, Basaran, the Scrin, and the Hall Nation survived, due to their advanced medical technologies. Phyrexian scientists have determined that the disease is completely exterminated. This leaves room for more nations to settle in Avathar...
