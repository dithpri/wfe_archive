An archipelago with massive islands and extensive political entities. Fairly isolationist and patriotic with a plentiful variety of government alignments. Outside of patriotism and isolationism, eco-friendliness and powerful militaries are also common. 

Generally, hostile amongst each other, with enough history to blur rationale within the people and the nations. Hostility is ten-fold towards outsiders with the exception of [nation]Kalrio[/nation], the region hardly gets along in terms of trade and diplomacy with others. This also includes The World Assembly, where the region effectively disregards anything offered by the WA.
