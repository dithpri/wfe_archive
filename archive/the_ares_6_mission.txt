The Sixth of the Ares Program Missions to Mars. Lead by Canada, the Ares-6 Crew are responsible for site maintenance and drilling operations at the Spearhead Drilling Sites. This mission is led by Commander Ben Harper. 

Mission Commander Harper took command of the Ares Site from Mission Commander Michael Kreischer of Ares-5 on February 19, 2042. It is the first Mission not lead by the United States.
