[b]The Federation of the Portuguese Barinas[/b]
is a south-american portuguese-speaking nation located 
[u]in the former Castelo &amp; Pique region between Venezuela and Colombia.[/u]

The federation currently consists of the Barinas Continental Area and three 
[u]Federal Dependencies in the Caribbean: Lavra, Porto Santo Antonio and Quatrobocas[/u]
(Cura&ccedil;au, Aruba and the Dutch Caribbean Islands, respectively).
