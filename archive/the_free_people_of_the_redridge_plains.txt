

Redridge Plains          


Region Type: Federal Agricultural Hub
Capital: Cedarview
Currency: Redridge Dollar (RRD)
Government: Decentralized municipal councils under a state federation
Climate: Humid continental
Major Industries: Agriculture (corn, wheat, soy), livestock, renewable energy
National Motto: &#147;Rooted in the Land, Rising Together&#148;

Overview: Redridge Plains is a sprawling, rural region known for its fertile fields, rolling hills, and strong sense of community. Agriculture drives the economy, while small towns foster local traditions and festivals. Conservation and renewable energy initiatives are shaping the future of this heartland region
