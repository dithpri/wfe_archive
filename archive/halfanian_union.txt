The Halfanian Union, the political union of the states of the continent of Halfan and the surrounding seas. Formed after the largest war in the continent to prevent the same happening again. The political centre was on the island nation of Dragoland (a.k.a yr ynys ddraig in Ffrymrig) in the capital city of Harry-upon-sea (Llandraigmor) until the construction of the unions capital of halfan city in the man-made archipelago of capital islands.
