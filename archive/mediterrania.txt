[b][url=http://www.nationstates.net/region=mediterrania][color=blue]Mediterrania[/color][/url][/b] [color=blue]is the first Crown Colony of the Mazerian Empire, formally established on the 13th June 2013.[/color]

[color=teal]The colony is run by a Doge, the first of whom was elected by the inhabitants of Mazeria itself. Subsequent Doges (or other names, if they wish for a change in the title) are to be elected by the citizens of Mediterrania itself.[/color]

[color=green]The colony, once its community has been built up, will be largely self-governing, with total control over its own internal affairs and some leeway in foreign and military affairs within the limits set by the Mazerian Empire Act 2013.[/color]

[color=purple]The Founder account is controlled by[/color] [url=http://www.nationstates.net/nation=king_frederick_i][b][color=purple]King Frederick I[/b] of Mazeria,[/color][/url] [color=purple]the Emperor of the Mazerian Empire.[/color]
