  The International Alliance for Development of Nations (IADN) is a multinational regional bloc dedicated to the pursuit of economic growth, technological progress, and diplomatic cooperation. United under the shared belief that development is the cornerstone of peace and stability, IADN brings together a diverse group of nations committed to mutual prosperity and respectful governance.
  IADN welcomes active, development-minded nations of all cultures, systems, and ideologies&#151;so long as they value cooperation, dialogue, and growth over conflict.
