[colour=#670033][b]Welcome to the [region]Oriental States[/region]![/b][/colour]

[colour=#670033]The Oriental States, officially the Orient is a series of Asian nations unified into one empire under the direct sovereign rule of the Oriental Mandate. The Oriental States also known as the [i]Oriental State[/i] spans the whole continent of Asia. Nations that are part of the Oriental States are classified as Prefectures and governed by their respective Prefects.[/colour]
