The Samsung Group[3] (or simply Samsung, stylized as S&amp;#923;MSUNG) (Korean: &amp;#49340;&amp;#49457; [sams&amp;#652;&amp;#331;]) is a South Korean multinational manufacturing conglomerate headquartered in Samsung Town, Seoul, South Korea.[1] It comprises numerous affiliated businesses,[1] most of them united under the Samsung brand, and is the largest South Korean chaebol (business conglomerate). As of 2020, Samsung has the 8th highest global brand value.[4]
