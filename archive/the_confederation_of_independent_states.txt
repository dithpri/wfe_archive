The Confederation of Independent States (CIS) is an intergovernmental organisation founded by the People&#39;s Republic of Haeralia on 31/08/2017. It aims to sustain global peace, cooperation and communication between all nations in Greater Akeria. The Legislative Council debates and votes on new legislation which all member states must abide by. The Security Council enforces global peace and deals with any threat to that peace. Executive nations have the power to veto any proposed legislation.
