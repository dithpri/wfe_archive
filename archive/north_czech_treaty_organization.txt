**The Federation of the NCTO: United in Prosperity**

*Published by the Government of the Federation of the NCTO*

In the heart of Europe, the Federation of the NCTO (North Czech Treaty Organization) binds Czechoslovakian nations in a strong alliance, prioritizing education, a robust military, and freedom. Specializing in arms manufacturing, alongside cheese exports, automobile manufacturing, and retail, the NCTO thrives economically. Prepared for any challenge, the Federation remains united in ensuring the prosperity and liberty of its citizens.
