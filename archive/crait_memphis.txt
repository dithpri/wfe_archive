Crait Memphis is a small region that hopes to grow large and is flat, but an abundant area full of resources. It has no direct religion but many spread throughout its territory. Crait Memphis is known for its plentiful harvests and grand festivals. Crait Memphis has a diverse culture full of tasty food, lively music, and entertainment, and beautiful vistas and other natural beauties. While not known for being overly aggressive, Crait Memphisans will stand up when the call for war is sounded.
