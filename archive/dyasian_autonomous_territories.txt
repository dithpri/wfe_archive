[b][region]Dyasian Autonomous Territories[/region][/b] is the collective name for the autonomous districts, also known as Oblasts, in the Confederal Tsardom of [b][nation]Dyasiabelsk[/nation][/b]. These Oblasts have moderate levels of self-governing authority and each posseses their own political system and government institutes. The Politburo of the leading party within an Oblast represents in the Confederal Legislative system, where nation-wide decisions are made.
