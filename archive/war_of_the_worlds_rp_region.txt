Are you a fan of war of the worlds? A story about a unnamed man who has to survive a brutal invasion of giant war machines armed with heat rays and attempt to take over England? Well now is your chance to put yourself into 19th century England where you can choose who you want to be, alone survivor whose town was destroyed by giant machines, a brave soldier whose regiment was destroyed within seconds, or you can take the role of the very invaders and try to wipe out the human race.
