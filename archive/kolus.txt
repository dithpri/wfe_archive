Kolus is the second-largest continent on Kerbin, and is most notably home to the Pan-Kolusian Union, an intergovernmental union of Kolusian States, founded by the members of the preceding Triangle and Square Pacts, between the nations of Novaria, Ilyakov, Dzhulios, and Fergara. It is also the most prosperous of Kerbin&#39;s continents. Kolus is known for its rainforested tropics to the south to the tundras and mountains to the north at the tectonic boundary with Serthenia.
