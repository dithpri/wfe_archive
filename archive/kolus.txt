Kerbin is the second-largest continent on Kerbin, and is most notably home to the Pan-Kolusian Union, an intergovernmental union of Kolusian States, founded by the members of the preceding Triangle Pact, between Novaria, Ilyakov, and Dzhulios. It is also the most prosperous of Kerbin&#39;s continents. Kolus is known for its rainforested tropics to the south to the tundras and mountains to the north at the tectonic boundary with Serthenia.
