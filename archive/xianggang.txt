Hong Kong (/&amp;#712;h&amp;#594;&amp;#331;k&amp;#594;&amp;#331;/ (US) or /h&amp;#594;&amp;#331;&amp;#712;k&amp;#594;&amp;#331;/ (UK); Chinese: &amp;#39321;&amp;#28207;, Cantonese: [h&#156;&amp;#769;&amp;#720;&amp;#331;.k&amp;#596;&amp;#780;&amp;#720;&amp;#331;] (listen)), officially the Hong Kong Special Administrative Region of the People&#39;s Republic of China (abbr. Hong Kong SAR or HKSAR),[d] is a city and special administrative region of China on the eastern Pearl River Delta in South China. With 7.5 million residents of various nationalities[e] in a 1,104-square-kilometre (426 sq mi) territory, Hong Kong is one of the most densely populated places in the world. Hong Kong is also a major global financial centre and one of the most developed cities in the world.
