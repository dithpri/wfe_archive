Mother Russia, also known as the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics, was founded as alliance of Marxists, Leninists, Trotskyists, Stalinists and any other comrade belonging to a left-wing ideology.

The Union of Soviet Socialist Republics opposes the exploitation and oppression of our citizens by the forces of capitalism, imperialism and fascism and will actively fight these evils.
