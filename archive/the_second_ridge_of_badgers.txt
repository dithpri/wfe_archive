[b][color=F01400]The Second Ridge of Badgers (SRB)[/color] was a federal, democratic region focused on international military and civil cooperation. The SRB had a proud history of democracy and its lineage stretches back to the [color=F01400]Ridge of Badgers[/color], founded in March 2016. It was disestablished on December 14, 2016 as its nations merged with those of its former rival [region]Axolotlum[/region] in the new [region]United Nations of AxoBadgeria[/region].
