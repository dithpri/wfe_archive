Strength, Honor, Vitality, Willpower, Determination, Belief in the power of Rock and Roll, this is what defines the Armistan Alliance. Located on the continent of Armis, member nations work together as a cohesive force to oppose uh... bad things. Ampero, HorePolio, Aeonuz, Winters Keep, these are some of the most prominent members of the Armistan Alliance, commonly convening in the city of Lightbringer&amp;#39;s Keep to discuss matters of international importance. Several nationalities such as the Reachers, the Amperoans, the Savages, and the Arakin dwell together in peaceful harmony with their own sovereign governments and economies.
