The Union of Free States is a region created with inter-state dialogue and communication at the forefront while not forcing any member state to give up their national identity or sovereignty. We believe in the process of simple democracy, with all issues of significance being solved by a direct democratic vote. We strictly adhere to the United Nation&#39;s Universal Declaration of Human Rights as well as the all of the Geneva Convention resolutions and will defend this staples of our modern and civilized world to the utmost. We believe in the power of the World Assembly and will do our utmost to ensure complete adherence with all of the resolutions passed.
