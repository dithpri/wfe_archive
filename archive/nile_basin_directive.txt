[b]The Nile Basin Directive[/b] is a collection of socialist East African states on the Nile that have united to boost economic and military power of the otherwise powerless states. The Nile Basin Directive is controlled by the &#39;Council of Nations&#39;, made up of the governors of each member state. 

The Chairman of the Directive is currently Abdelaziz Mohamed al-Bashir, former governor of the Nile Basin Directive state of Sudan.
