An economic and social union of the nations of the Dzugarnaya Island System. Similar to the European Union, the DU maintains certain requirements to join (including minimum social, political, and economic freedoms). It allows free trade and movements of goods and persons between all member states. Typically, membership is based on invitation and a two-part referendum following that.

The most prominent state in the DU is Dzugarna, which has executive authority over the affairs of the DU and acts as the World Assembly Delegate.
