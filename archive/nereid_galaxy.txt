The [color=red][b][u]Nereid Galaxy[/u][/b][/color] is a medium-sized spiral galaxy approximately 41,000 parsecs in diameter. The galaxy is home to multiple stellar and interstellar entities, numerous human and humanoids species, and a plethora of intragalactic organizations that make the [color=red][b][u]Nereid Galaxy[/u][/b][/color] a center for a diverse region amongst the stars.
