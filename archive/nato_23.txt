[b][region]NATO 23[/region] was a former Cold War conventional and nuclear weapons storage facility for the military forces of the North Atlantic Treaty Organization. Established in the 1960&amp;#39;s near Feucht, (West) Germany, it was operational until it was disbanded and reverted to civilian control in 1992. Among other munitions, NATO 23 stored the W48 (155 mm howitzer) and W33 (203 mm howitzer) tactical nuclear projectiles as well as Special Atomic Demolition Munitions (SADM).[/b]

[b][region]NATO 23[/region][color=blue] is now a protectorate of the Barony Dominion.[/color][/b]
