The DRSG is a alternative history road of The Democratic Army of Greece winning the Revolution,

The DRSG is an Anti Imperialist Marxist-Leninist nation, And hostile to all nations labelling themselves as Liberal, Fascist, Conservative, Centrist or a Monarchy.

The DRSG has survived for a long time whilst taking multiple Western sanctions and a failed coup and a failed colour revolution.

The DRSG is highly diplomatic with China, The DPRK, Cuba, Laos, Vietnam, Bolivia, Venezuela, Palestine, Syria, And other Anti Imperialist states or Socialist nations.
