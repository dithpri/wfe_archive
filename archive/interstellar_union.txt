Welcome to the [color=blue]Interstellar Union[/color]! A time and place in the universe filled with thousands of space fairing nations, republics, and empires. Its mainly located within the Milky Way Galaxy, however many nations have expanded outwards to acquire new territories in other galaxies. As such, the Interstellar Union is filled with a wide cultural diversity and technological achievement. The year is 5923 AD.
