The region of Autumnland is noted for its diverse landscape of snowy mountians, lush valleys, and rocky coastline. Autumnland has a cool climate with the seasons of autum and winter being the longest, with a short spring and moderate summer. The nations of Autumnland are fiercely loyal to one another and all enjoy a strong military. The Autumnland alliance is the governing body for the nations of Autumnland. Autumnlanders are known to be very conservative in thier belief systems. Most nations engage in some sort of colonialism to varying degrees.
