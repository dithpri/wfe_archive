The Rakhoutenstad treaty, was signed in 1999 by Nieuw StenenLand and the nations of Zelenostrova, Great New West Wessex, Goudvallei, and Nova Bragia. New Dovera is usually included, due to the uncertainty of its sovereignty, resulting in it often being considered a territory of Nieuw StenenLand.
The treaty was signed after the formation of Nieuw StenenLand, to reduce the tyrannical power of GNWW, and connect he four nations, for protection and economic benefit.
