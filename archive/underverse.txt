The [region]Underverse[/region] is conjunction point of several otherwise isolated Multiverses. [nation=noflag]the Eternians[/nation] artificially created the [b]Underverse[/b]. As other Civilizations of different Multiverses explored the [b]Underverse[/b] and came into contact with [nation=+noflag]the Eternians[/nation] they established diplomatic and trade contacts and eventually formed the [b]Underverse Alliance[/b] - a free Alliance of Civilizations.

The [b]Underverse Alliance[/b] is holding some low profile contacts with the [region]SICON[/region] Alliance as well as with [region]Pangea Prime[/region].
