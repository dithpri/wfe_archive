The [i]Underverse[/i] is basically a tiny  isolated universe with its own space-time-reality. The extremely inaccessible [i]Underverse[/i] is hidden deep inside the subspace and additionally shrouded by subquantum reality-walls. 

The [i]Underverse[/i] was discovered and colonized thousands of years ago. Since that time the [i]Underverse[/i] is controlled by [b][nation]The Necromongers[/nation][/b].
