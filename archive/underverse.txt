The [b]Underverse[/b] is a small, extremely inaccessible, extra-dimensional graviton-immersion bubble (basically a tiny universe). It has a diameter of only 2500 lightyears and is completely isolated from our universe. The [b]Underverse[/b] has its own isolated space-time-reality and is controled by [b][Nation=short+noflag]the Unified Human Empire[/Nation][/b].
