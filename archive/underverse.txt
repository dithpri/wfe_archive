The [region]Underverse[/region] is conjunction point of several otherwise isolated Multiverses. As more and more Civilizations of different Multiverses explored the [b]Underverse[/b], they came into contact and established diplomatic and trade contacts and eventually formed the [b]Underverse Alliance[/b] - a free Alliance of Civilizations.

The [b]Underverse Alliance[/b] is holding some low profile contacts with the [region]SICON[/region] Alliance as well as with the [region]GDI[/region].
