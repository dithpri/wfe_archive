The Lunar Union is a union of all nations located on, or with strong ties to the moon. This region strives to preserve the moon, whilst increasing it&amp;#39;s productivity and importance as a place of inhabitance, business and culture. Our aim will be achieved by improving transport links between The Moon and Earth, nurturing intelligence and creativity through a well funded, stimulating education system, and providing an outstanding living environment for all residents. 

[nation]Theatria and Macabria[/nation] founded this region in 1972. The head of the region is King Darksworth IV.
