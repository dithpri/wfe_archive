[b]Manland[/b] is a continent in the Northern Hemisphere. Paxton Augustine Donalds discovered the continent on September 10, 1960, and founded the Republic of [nation]Vizhen[/nation] on May 29, 1961. The name [i]Manland[/i] means &quot;mainland&quot; in [url=https://www.nationstates.net/page=dispatch/id=2654200]Vizhen[/url] and is pronounced the same as &quot;mainland&quot; in English.
