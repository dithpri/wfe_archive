Cardcaptor Sakura (Japanese: &amp;#12459;&amp;#12540;&amp;#12489;&amp;#12461;&amp;#12515;&amp;#12503;&amp;#12479;&amp;#12540;&amp;#12373;&amp;#12367;&amp;#12425;, Hepburn: K&amp;#257;dokyaput&amp;#257; Sakura), abbreviated as CCS, is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by the manga group Clamp. Serialized monthly in the sh&amp;#333;jo manga magazine Nakayoshi from May 1996 to June 2000, it was also published in 12 tank&amp;#333;bon volumes by Kodansha between November 1996 and July 2000. The story centers on Sakura Kinomoto, an elementary school student who discovers magical powers after accidentally freeing a set of magical cards into the world; she must retrieve the cards to prevent catastrophe. Each of these cards grants different magical powers, and can only be activated by someone with inherent magical abilities.
