The southern Lands is the name given to what was before known as South america, parts of the pacific, atlantic and caribbean seas.

The founder of the region is the radical union of The South American Territory, first on its kind comprising more than 10 countries unified in one solid institution.

It is a new nation based in different values or characteristics and its exclusively open to those who are willing to actively participate in the growth, preservation and defence of the current and future generations in the southern lands.
