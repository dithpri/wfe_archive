[b]Kar-et al Heve[/b]
[hr]
A small, mountainous region characterized by ironfisted rulers, strong religious adherence, and snow.
Ruled by strict laws and regulations upon the people.


Anyone who wishes to join must have a religiously devout or militarily devout populace willing to dedicate most their lives to spiritual practices or military service.
