[b]Kar-et al Heve[/b]
[b][color=darkred]Climb to heaven. There is nothing holier than the fight itself.[/color][/b]
[hr]
A small, mountainous region characterized by ironfisted rulers, strong religious adherence, and snow.
Ruled by strict laws and regulations upon the people.

[b]Main Cities of the Region[/b]
[b][color=darkred]Kyor | Ka-rok el Taron | Holy City el Il lyu[/color][/b]


Anyone who wishes to join must have a religiously devout or militarily devout populace willing to dedicate most their lives to spiritual practices or military service. Nations and people must understand that a strict, ordered lifestyle is the way to real, productive, and happy lives. [b]Restraint is the ultimate strength.[/b]
