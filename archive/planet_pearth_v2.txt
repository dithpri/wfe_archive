[b]The Year is 3543[/b]

As the Great Peace had lasted for quite sometime, the Revolution of Energy happened. Suddenly scientists of several international origins in Avanir, had discovered a way to easily store, and transport energy. Petalium in Koni, or known as Energium or Crystalium in other nations, was a crystallic substance that was capable to store massive amounts of energy with great longevity and almost zero power loss. It sparked a rapid change in technological innovation, hovering crafts, powerful rocketry, electrical weaponry, powerlines that stretched inbetween roofs in the great cities. The Koni philosopher Willund Garborg described it as a &amp;quot;Rebirth of the World, and the Dawn of a New Age never before seen.&amp;quot;
