Welcome to Borealias, known IC as Borealia. Borealias is dedicated to the roleplaying of Fantasy Tech nations with heavy influence of MT and real life nations in some aspects of its lore. Nonetheless, all are welcome to join and participate after approval of a Regional Council Member. Please feel free to contact one of us for assistance!
