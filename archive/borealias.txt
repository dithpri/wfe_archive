Welcome to Borealias, known IC as Borealia. Borealias is dedicated to the roleplaying of MT nations with influence of Fantasy Tech and real life nations in some aspects of its lore, and plays both human and non-human/demi-human characters. Nonetheless, all are welcome to join and participate after approval of a Regional Council Member. Please feel free to contact one of us for assistance!
