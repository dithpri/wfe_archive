The region of Robiny is home to the leader &amp;quot;Robin Mask&amp;quot;. 

It is a region that is welcoming and tolerant, home to its capitol state: RobinMask.

The region exists as a large island in the northern hemisphere; it has a warm temperate climate and boasts magnificent forests. It is famous for its natural beauties, ranging from its White Cliffs to its Grand Canyons to its Aurora Borealis. There are occasional earthquakes, but these are confined to coastal regions and result in little damage, although flooding in these areas is an issue. 

Everyone enjoys a laid-back culture of siestas and home-made cuisine, whilst crime is almost non-existent. 

This region welcomes everyone.
