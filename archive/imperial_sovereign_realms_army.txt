[b]The Imperial Sovereign Realms Army (ISRA)[/b] was the joint military force of [b][url=region=osiris][color=#B9182B]Osiris[/color][/url][/b] and [b][url=region=balder][color=#B9182B]Balder[/color][/url][/b] from April to September 2014.

ISRA was discontinued in September 2014 by decision of [b][url=nation=the_dourian_embassy][color=#B9182B]Treize Dreizehn[/color][/url][/b], Pharaoh of Osiris, and [b][url=nation=solorni][color=#B9182B]Rachel Eriksen[/color][/url][/b], Queen of Balder.
