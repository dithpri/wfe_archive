Orsa is a region and world with a rich lore and history, settled by humans 17,000 years ago, it is rife with conflict between its many hundreds of nations.

Orsa is open to any that wish to join and you may also claim territory on the world map, which will be updated to keep up with those that choose to establish their nations here.
