Acantilado is a continent upon the Planet of Castillo. For most of its inhabitance, it has played host to multiple maritime-centered tribes and kingdoms, until the Gran Milagro, when its inhabitants began rapidly formulating massive republics and empires, and advancing technologically, managing to catch up to the rest of the Planet, and would end up playing host to multiple of Castillo&#39;s greatest civilizations, from ancient warrior peoples, to massive globe-spanning empires, Acantilado has it all!
