From the ruins of a war... China rises again!

From the ashes of the first nuclear exchange, a strange movement began. Dressed in the green of the Jade, the substance that had laid their mighty civilisation low, they attacked with strange and outlandish technologies. This new Atomic Kingdom overran the Blue and Red Chinese, establishing themselves as the new lords of the blasted landscape under the heel of an army of cloned warriors wielding weapons of terrifying power....
