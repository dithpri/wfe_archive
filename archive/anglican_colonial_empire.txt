The [b]Anglican Colonial Empire[/b] is the community of territories ruled either directly or indirectly by the Anglican monarch. The seat of imperial power lies in the capital of the Commonwealth of the Albions, Kingshaven. The Empire consists of three constitutional types of possession:
[list][*][b]Crown Dominion:[/b] An imperial possession with a locally-elected legislature and significant autonomy.
[*][b]Crown Colony:[/b] An imperial possession with an appointed legislature and limited autonomy.
[*][b]Crown Territory:[/b] An imperial possession ruled directly by the imperial government in the metropole.[/list]
