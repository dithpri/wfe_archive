[hr][b]The Yunnish Empire[/b] is a collection of several Yun-populated and Yun -friendly nations that recognize the Yunnish Parliament as their main ruling authority. Members of the The Yunnish Empire cooperate to promote free trade and foreign aid within the network via the [url=https://www.nationstates.net/page=dispatch/id=1143601]Redlandian Economic Partnership[/url] as well as preserve Yunnish culture and history.
