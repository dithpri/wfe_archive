[b][u]Almersen Union[/u][/b]
[hr][b][region=The Almersen Union][/b], commonly abbreviated as [b]ANU[/b], is a political and economic union of 5 member states which promotes intergovernmental cooperation and facilitates economic, political, security, military, educational, and sociocultural integration between its members and countries. 

Currently, the Union is a locked region which only allows certain member states to join.
