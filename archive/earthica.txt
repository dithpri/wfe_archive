This is a standard NS/MT region that I made for Roleplaying. The map is a pseudo Earth, with generalized and recognizable shapes, but much more  Landmass to allow nations to spread out.

Story: The Governments of earth have been dealing with numerous social, political and environmental issues for the past 20 years resulting in a sluggish economy world economy. 
It is the year 2020, the world economies are finally recovering, however there are numerous governments, factions and ideologies struggling to make it to the top.
