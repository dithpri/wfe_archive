The [b]Luftwaffe[/b] was the aerial warfare branch of the combined German Wehrmacht military forces during World War II.

During World War II, German pilots claimed roughly 70,000 aerial victories, while over 75,000 Luftwaffe aircraft were destroyed or significantly damaged. Of these, nearly 40,000 were lost entirely. &#151;Wikipedia[hr]
