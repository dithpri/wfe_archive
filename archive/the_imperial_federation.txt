The Imperial Federation or TIF was founded in the name of creating a new world ruled by four different nations, each bringing balance to the region in their own way. The Kingdom Of Tomdria, leading the way in technology and intelligence, Natharanai bringing across the weapons of a new age with a military to rule with great power, Dearbland a strict and well controlled nation ruled by a rightful government and Hirstakistan the peoples nation.

The Regional Government is as follows:

The Imperial Emperor - Thomas I of Tomdria and The Imperial Islands
The Imperial Kings - Nathaniel the Disciplined of Natharnai, Dearhbla-Blade-Hand of Dearbland and James III of the free peoples of Hirstakistan
