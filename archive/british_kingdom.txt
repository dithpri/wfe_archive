[b]Great Britain is an island in the North Atlantic Ocean off the northwest coast of continental Europe. With an area of (80,823 sq mi), it is the largest of the British Isles, the largest European island, and the ninth-largest island in the world.In 2011, Great Britain had a population of about 61 million people, making it the world&#39;s third-most populous island after Java in Indonesia and Honshu in Japan. The island of Ireland is situated to the west of Great Britain.[/b]
