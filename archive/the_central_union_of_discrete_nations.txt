The C.U.D.N is a region which consists of (as the name might imply) nations who are vast different from each other in terms of government and ideologies. Yet are still able to somehow tolerate one another in order to form economic and military bonds to strengthen their own continues. Though the exact origins of this region are unknown (for now at least), it has managed to survive for considerable amount of time. No matter how unstable it might appear at times.
