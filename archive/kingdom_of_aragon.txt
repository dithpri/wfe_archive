[color=#db0a13]Established on July 7, 2025, the [b][region]Kingdom of Aragon[/region] (KOA)[/b] is a constitutional monarchy located on the eastern Iberian Peninsula. The kingdom is a core component of the [b][region]Crown of Aragon[/region] (COA)[/b], a composite monarchy consisting of various other realms led by a single monarch, [b]His Majesty The King of Aragon, [nation]Gran Aragon[/nation][/b]. Aragonese, Catalan, and Castilian are the official languages of the kingdom. For any inquiries about the Kingdom of Aragon, please contact His Majesty&#39;s administration.[/color]
