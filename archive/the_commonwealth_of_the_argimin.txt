After many years of an unfair treatment of the ones in need and owerpowering the ones with the ultimate power, The Commonwealth Of The Argimin conducted an experiment that resulted in holding back the current leaders from getting the ultimate authority in the United Islands of Argimin.
After a one year of  cadency, The Commonwealth Of The Argimin is becoming one the most powerful movements on the Earth fighting with poverty, homelessness and delinquency.
The Headquarters are placed in Abzgaria, the capital city of Swatteuer, and is being built in Yoguki.
