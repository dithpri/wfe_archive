[List]The [b]IPEONIC EMPIRE[/b], is the indissoluble supranational union of, at present, twelve constituent nations.

The Empire was formed from the nations that were colonized and/or annexed by Ipeonia. 

During the territorial existence of the Empire, [b]IPEONIA[/b] was formed by several civilizations, adding up to more than 10 billion inhabitants.[/sub][/list]
