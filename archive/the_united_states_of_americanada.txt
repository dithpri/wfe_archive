The United States of AmeriCanada, is a merger of the former United States of America, and the neighboring country of Canada. After the merger, it became known as the United States of Americanada. While still retaining the former laws and constitution of the US, the land of Canada was absorbed as part of the US. 

The Wild North sprawls across the entire Northern part of Americanada, home to harsh winters, harsher people, and a diverse selection of deadly animals, such as bears, wolves, and wolverines. The Southlands stretch, as the name suggests, across the entire southern US. Weather in the summer is hot and humid, the winters frequently mild.
