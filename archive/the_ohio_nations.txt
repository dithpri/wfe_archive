Hello and welcome to The Ohio Nations this region works by picking a place in Ohio or the sounding states. You can have a max of 5 counties.  and it is a simulation of a divided Ohio. you can have a max of 3 nations in this region. also we will have monthly elections. RP is allowed and recommended also we will have a map.
All in all this is a relaxed region and have fun
