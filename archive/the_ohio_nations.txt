Hello and welcome to The Ohio Nations! This region works by picking a place in Ohio or the surrounding states. You can have a max of 5 counties, and it is a simulation of a divided Ohio. You can have a max of 3 nations in this region, we will also have monthly elections. RP is allowed and recommended. We also have a nice map.
All in all this is a relaxed region and have fun.
