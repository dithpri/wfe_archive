Once the aggressive and militaristic empire of Adelphi, split into several countries who all seek to gain control of the former empire.

Adelphi West- Leaders of the National Heritage Party wanted to keep the legacy of the old empire alive. Gained the largest bit of territory in the civil war and the former capital.

St. Bonifacius- Leaders of the White Arrow Party hated the corruption of the old empire and wanted to reform it. Occupy a small island in the south.
