Shadexia is nothing more than a collection of city states in the highest region of the North. Shadexia city the original Capital serves as the location of the first landing the original Romans colonists first landed. Ruled by the mighty Shadex family, a family of literal giants, Shadexia&amp;#39;s nobles are a mixture of children of the Shadex Family who rule cities to the north of Shadexia City. Unlike most nations the capital frequently shifts to the noble who is crowned Shadex, while other nobles serve as Dex&amp;#39;s. Male or Female Dexs can rule but only one Shadex can reign supreme. Build your City state in Shadexia, by Putting Shadexia first and the name of your city last.
