This region is the Homeland of the Nation of Asmoria and all it states, forming an Imperial Federation. Articles of Federal Monarchy are in affect.

( Federal Monarchy, in the strict sense, is a federation of states with a single monarch as overall head of the federation, but retaining different monarchs, or having a non-monarchical system of government, in the various states joined to the federation.)
