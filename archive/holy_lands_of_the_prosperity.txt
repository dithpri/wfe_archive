The Holy Lands of Prosperity are lands east of the Grossen Deutschen Reich, the Imperial lands of the United German Empire. The people of the Holy Lands are those who are trying to escape the unfair and evil power of the nations inside the the Grossen Deutschen Reich, mostly from the United German Empire and United Socialist States of Zhong-Chi. All those who wish to stay and live in the Holy Lands must take an oath of enlightenment and must attend a ceremony of ascension. Only to this, will you have accepted the power of the Five White Gods.
