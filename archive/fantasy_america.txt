[b][color=navy]Fantasy America[/color] [color=darkred](FA)[/color][/b] consists of all nations located in North America, South America and the Caribbean.

The STORY of this region is set within an alternate world where magic, gods and creatures of fantasy have existed side-by-side with science and humanity, and have influenced history as a result.

The ROLEPLAY of this region is part of the multi-region Fantasy America universe.
