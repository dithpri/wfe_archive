[hr][list][b]The United Protectorate of Tepertopia[/b], commonly referred to as [b]Tepertopia[/b] or [b]the (United) Protectorate[/b], is a federal republic located on an identically named island, covering its almost 8,500km&sup2; of landmass. Home to over 2.1 million Tepertopians, the country consists of seven Constituent States, themselves comprised of a total of 42 administrative departments. Tepertopia is a member of the Coalition of [region]the South Pacific[/region].[/list]
