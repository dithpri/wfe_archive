The Order of Knights of the Hospital of Saint John of Jerusalem, commonly known as the Knights Hospitaller is a medieval and early modern Catholic military order. It was founded in the crusader Kingdom of Jerusalem in the 12th century and had headquarters there until 1291, thereafter being based in Kolossi Castle in Cyprus (1302&#150;1310), the island of Rhodes (1310&#150;1522), Malta (1530&#150;1798), and Saint Petersburg (1799&#150;1801).
