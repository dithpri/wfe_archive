The Praesidium Potentiae is a military alliance formed by Bellatorum Potentia and other nations to face the challenges of the modern world together. Despite initial challenges, Bellatorum Potentia persisted in its efforts to build the alliance, emphasizing the mutual benefits of cooperation and coordination. Over time, other nations joined the alliance, recognizing the benefits of being part of a powerful military organization that could protect their interests and respond to emerging threats.
