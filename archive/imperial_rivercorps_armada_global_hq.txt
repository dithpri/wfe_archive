The Global Headquarters of the Imperial RiverCorps Armada. It supports fleets operating worldwide, serving as a repair and maintenance centre for fleets in between the various expeditions they undertake.
This Base is under the control of the Fleet Admiral of the Imperial RiverCorps Armada, and any trespassers shall be subjected to Imperial RiverCorps Armada laws, no matter whatever region you hail from.

All embassy requests WILL be rejected unless requested by the Imperial RiverCorps Armada, as this base only serves the Imperial RiverCorps Armada and its allies.
