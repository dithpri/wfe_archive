[b]The Spratly Islands[/b] (Chinese: &amp;#21335;&amp;#27801;&amp;#32676;&amp;#23798;/&amp;#21335;&amp;#27801;&amp;#32676;&amp;#23707;; pinyin: N&aacute;nsh&amp;#257; Q&uacute;nd&amp;#462;o) is an archipelago in the South China Sea. Composed of islands, islets and cays and more than 100 reefs, sometimes grouped in submerged old atolls. Administrated by the People&#146;s Republic of China and its&#146; province of Hainan since 1949.
