The Wonzenfield Peninsula is one of the smallest peninsulas in the world, it is joined continentally by the Cordillera de Sri-Alta to the north of the peninsula and the Fordesto Desert to the west. To the southeast is the Green Sea.

The region formed by 4 nations that once formed the Holy Wozenfield Empire, is historically recognized as one of the most unstable regions suffering several wars in the last 500 years.

Today, it is a thriving region, basing its economy on the technology and dairy industry. Their countries are the largest producers of electronic cards and components in the world, having design industries and printed circuit assemblies of several important technological multinationals.
