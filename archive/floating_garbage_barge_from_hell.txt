A floating island roughly the size of Pennsylvania with garbage as its foundation and an entire ecosystem on the surface. It grows bigger every day.

[nation]Garbagedale[/nation] takes up most of the landmass and is a decentralized, agrarian society. The city states [nation]Dumpster City[/nation] and [nation]Wasteburg[/nation] exist to the east and west respectively. Each one believes itself to be the capital city of its half of the island and they are both internationally recognized as such, with &quot;Dumpsterland&quot; and &quot;Wastetopia&quot; appearing on most maps, but neither of them is able to consistently exert control over the people of [nation]Garbagedale[/nation], which is not internationally recognized, has no capital city, and is constantly fighting a war on two fronts, always surviving but never conquering.
