Zhejiang (UK: /&amp;#716;d&amp;#658;&amp;#604;&amp;#720;d&amp;#658;i&amp;#720;&amp;#712;&aelig;&amp;#331;/[4] or /d&amp;#658;&amp;#603;&amp;#712;d&amp;#658;&aelig;&amp;#331;/,[5] US: /&amp;#712;d&amp;#658;&amp;#652;&amp;#712;d&amp;#658;j&amp;#593;&amp;#720;&amp;#331;/;[6] &amp;#27993;&amp;#27743;, also romanized as Chekiang) is an eastern, coastal province of the People&#39;s Republic of China. Its capital and largest city is Hangzhou. Zhejiang is bordered by Jiangsu and Shanghai to the north, Anhui to the northwest, Jiangxi to the west and Fujian to the south.
