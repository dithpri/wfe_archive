The [b]Opportunity, Sustainability, and Development Index[/b] ([b]OSDI[/b]) is a statistic composite index used to evaluate the quality of life in a nation. The OSDI is part of the Smart World Governance Initiative (SWGI) undertaken by [nation]Unueco[/nation] and is utilized extensively in determining public policy through anticipatory democracy. Three factors on the index are computed to output a final score: opportunity (economic efficiency), sustainability (environmental well-being), and development (standard of living).
