The Confederacy of the Eritore Pact is the formal name given to the group of Allied Nations who have elected to defend and aid each other. All members of the Confederacy of the Eritore Pact are responsible for aiding and supporting endangered Allies. All interal affairs are brought to the attention of the World Assembly Delegate, who then reports to the current highest Executive authority. If any nation wishes to join the Confederacy, please feel free to contact the Founder and he will respond as soon as possible. The Confederacy of the Eritore Pact wish you prosperity and good fortune in your affairs.
