Between 3345 and 3347 the andarians placed in the orbit of a gas giant in the Dodonna System a mechanical fortress called Andarian Large Magellanic Cloud (ALMC), this huge space station controls the Andarian Forces and all colonies in the Large Magellanic Cloud, it also serves as a seat of power for the Imperial Andarian Governor in the name of the Imperial Galactic Republic of [region]Andarios[/region].
