Jammed between two much larger portions of the Mmarrakhos Continent this Sub-Continent is surrounded by wide rivers, while this means life on the Sub-Continent is abundant, isolationists and explorer driven communities are prone to clashes on the fate of the regions people.
The region has fairly high biodiversity for its geographical location, but while most resources are in plenty supply, much of the most fertile ground is resident to important species of local animals, making expansion of agriculture a complicated matter.
