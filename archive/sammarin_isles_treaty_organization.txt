The [b]Sammarin Isles Treaty Organization[/b] (eo: [b]Sammarinaj-Insuloj Traktat-Organizo[/b]) is an intergovernmental alliance and system of collective defense of the 4 states residing in the Sammarin island chain in the South Pacific. SITO also collectively administers the uninhabited Central Sammarin islands and the United Sammarin Space Force based there

[i]Motto[/i]: Semper Paratus pro Justitia
[i]Official languages[/i]: English, Esperanto
[i]Headquarters[/i]: Whitesands Bay, Central Sammarin
[i]Secretary-General[/i]: Dr. Verene C. Lamar
[i]Supreme Allied Commander[/i]: GMSHL Fredrik Blackwood, ESA
