The [i][b]Nexus Cosmos[/b][/i] is a vast, newly discovered region of spacetime often described as a [i]&#147;universe within the universe itself.&#148;[/i] Although still largely unexplored by outsiders, it contains hundreds of civilizations across countless galaxies, from small city-states and pirate kingdoms to massive planetary republics.

[i][b]The Republic of Greater Roma[/b][/i] was the first nation to discover and study it. During the reign of Imperator Universalis, [b]Majorian II[/b], he witnessed a network of worlds, gateways, and interconnected realities, and named it [i]&#147;The Nexus Cosmos,&#148;[/i] believing it to be a central point where civilizations, histories, and destinies across the stars are linked.
