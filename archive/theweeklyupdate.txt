[b]What is The Weekly Update[/b]?

TWU is a newsletter that brings you the best of NationStates -- all within the click of a button, such as; Forum 7, featured regions, GA and SC proposals and results, dispatches, and more! While there is no need to subscribe to read the newsletter, you may wish to subscribe to receive telegrams when it is updated! Subscribing is free of charge, and any nation may subscribe.
