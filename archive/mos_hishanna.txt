Mos Hishanna is relatively large region located in the planets tropical region. Separated from its neighbors in the southwest by the Axehead Mountain range and the south by the Tumunzig Mountains, creating a natural border. To the east lie the Fangwood Forest. Relations between the neighboring regions are warm and favorable with peaceful borders. 
Cities are plentiful and residents are of mixed religions, most of whom migrated from the war-torn regions to the north during the Great Rebellion. The economy is stable as residents find work plentiful in the larger cities and those who prefer the quiet of the country find fertile land for farming.
