[color=Blue][B]ABOUT POLARIS[/B][/color]

[color=lightblue]Polaris is planet in located in the constellation Cassiopeia. The star system consists of 18 planets. Landscapes and climate range throughout the planet and the population is sparsely populated in many parts of the globe. An intergalactic calling invites overpopulated worlds to colonize or establish new nations. [/color]
