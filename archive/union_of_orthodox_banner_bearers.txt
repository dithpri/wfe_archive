The Union of Orthodox Banner Bearers (UOBB) is a unified coalition of Orthodox nations dedicated to safeguarding their sovereignty and cultural heritage. The UOBB represents a steadfast commitment to mutual defense, solidarity, and the preservation of traditional values.

The UOBB is dedicated to preserving the rich cultural and religious heritage of Orthodox nations. This includes protecting sacred sites, promoting cultural exchanges, and supporting educational initiatives that highlight Orthodox history and traditions.
