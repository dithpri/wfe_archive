Navarill is an Earth-like World in the Navarall system, located approximately 128.62 Lightyears from Sol.

Navarall houses a K-class star which is notably smaller than Sol, having half the mass, and only 70% of the radius. The Solar system is older, however, at approximately 8 Billion Years Old.
Navarill is noticeably less massive than Earth, coming in at 0.800 Earth Masses, and slightly smaller, with a radius of 5,737km.

It holds gravity almost identical to Earth&#39;s, with surface Gravity being approximately 0.99G.
Its atmosphere is notably thicker, at approximately 2.9 times the atmospheric pressure of Earth.
The planet is also considerably cooler than Earth, with the average temperature down to 7c.
