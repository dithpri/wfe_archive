The Orthodox Federation of States is a political and economic union of 2 member states that are located primarily in the Northern Hemisphere.  The union has an estimated total population of about 11 million. An internal single market has been proposed through a standardised system of laws that apply in all member states in those matters, and only those matters, where the states have agreed to act as one. The OFS is presided over by a duly elected President and Vice President and its Host nation regularly changes.
