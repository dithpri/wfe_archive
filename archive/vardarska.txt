This is the real name of FYROM or Former Yugoslavian Republic of Macedonia or Nova Gorna Makedonija. The region is inhabited by Slavs and Albanians, who arrived in the Balkans almost 10 centuries after Alexander the Great and they have nothing to do with Greek Macedonia.
