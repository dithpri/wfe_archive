[b][color=teal]ASEAN is a geo-political &amp; economic organization of countries located in Southeast Asia. Its aims include the acceleration of economic growth, social progress, cultural development among its members, the protection of the peace &amp; stability of the region, and to provide opportunities for member countries to discuss differences peacefully.[/color][/b]
