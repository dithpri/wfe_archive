[i][b]Welcome to Stellaris, the modern and creative frontier for space roleplay here on NationSates![/b][/i]

Stellaris is a broad region encompassing all of Milky Way and Andromeda, focused on bringing together beings of all races and societies under one common flag, under the supreme order of the newly united [nation]Galactic Earth[/nation]. We make up the [b]very fabric[/b] of the universe, so for good measure, there&#39;s no reason why NOT to join us!

We welcome all nations, as long as they encompass a specific area of the universe and provide something interesting to all of us. Although we are a mostly conservative region, our setup is an anarchy, so feel free to make up your own mind on how you will build up to our economy.
