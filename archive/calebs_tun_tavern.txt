Tun Tavern, or Caleb&#39;s Tun Tavern , is a Bar located in the interdenominational realm of The Haven.  Caleb&#39;s Tun Tavern is a four-star restaurant, Bar &amp; Brewery, Hotel and Hall.

Caleb&#39;s Tun Tavern is also an Operations Office for the megacorporation Calebs Haven Corps, a Precinct Center for the Intergalactic Postal Service, and an office for the Ministry of Public Administration. 

Come in, relax, eat a little, drink a lot, and see what happens if you stick  around.
