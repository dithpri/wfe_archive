[color=#C70039][b]The Communist Party of China[/b][/color], is the founding and governing political party of the People&#39;s Republic of China (PRC). It was founded in 1921, chiefly by Chen Duxiu and Li Dazhao. The Communist Party of China, alongside eight other subordinate parties, is part of the United Front. The CPC is officially organised on the basis of democratic centralism, a principle conceived by Marxist theoretician Vladimir Ilyich Ulyanov.
