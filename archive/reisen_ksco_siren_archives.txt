The Reisen-KSCO Siren Archive Organization is an online organization which started in 2010 in Audoria, from multiple users on the 2channel imageboard that discussed about the topic of Reisen-KSCO music sirens, starting from one user wanting to identify why music was playing on the top of their building. Today, this organization has pinpointed and documented 1.2k+ music sirens worldwide. They have accumulated 4k users worldwide that are interested in these unique sirens.
