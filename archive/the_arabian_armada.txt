After a violent segregation from their former territory of Afghanistan, the disputed territories of Kejrah formed in what used to be Northern Afghanistan. After their formation the members of the foundational government saw the need to bolster the region against future instability. The Arabian Armada was born. All members commit themselves to maintain strong military industrial complexes and law and order systems as a regional deterrent to crime and warfare. They also commit to maintaining civil, and productive political engagement with regional neighbors.
