The Calician Empire came to fruition after the rebellion of 1989, when rebels overthrew the shadow-ruling National Church of what was at the time, a democratic Calice. After the great rebellion the people held one last election and overwhelmingly voted in support of making the rebel leader, The honorable Cal, into a monarch. His policy of science and the socratic method above all, while maintaining personal liberties, has paved the way for a booming economy and happy citizens.
