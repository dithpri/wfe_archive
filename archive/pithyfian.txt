Funded by Luizlosvaquia, the region intended to bring an end to the war among the so recent countries of Pithyfian. Composed by ten nations, the region has five Councils.
The Senior Council: Luizoslovaquia, Janana, Samuelandia, Julho e Ana Julia.
The coin council: Bianca e Yngrid, Samuelandia, Rafslandia, Julho e Ana Julia.
The security council: New Luizlovaquia, Luizlovenia, Samuelandia. Pauloso and Luizlovaquia are non-permanent members.
The grain council: New Luizlovaquie,  Julho, Rafslandia e Bianca e Yngrid.
The fish council: Pauloso, Rafslandia, Bianca e Yngrid, Luizlovenia and New Luizlovaquia.
