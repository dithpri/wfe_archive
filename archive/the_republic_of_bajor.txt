Bajor&nbsp;was an&nbsp;inhabited&nbsp;planet&nbsp;of the&nbsp;Bajoran&nbsp;system. This system was located in the&nbsp;Bajor sector, in the&nbsp;Alpha Quadrant. This was the largest planet in the system and had five&nbsp;moons, including&nbsp;Derna&nbsp;and&nbsp;Jeraddo. The planet was the&nbsp;homeworld&nbsp;of the&nbsp;Bajorans, a&nbsp;warp-capable&nbsp;humanoid&nbsp;species.
