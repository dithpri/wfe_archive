Prior to WWII, Canning Town was a gritty, industrial hub built around the Royal Victoria Dock. Characterized by tightly packed terraced streets, extreme poverty, and bustling markets, the area featured a unique, vibrant, and multi-cultural working-class community that was largely destroyed during the Blitz
