In this auspicious day, the almighty Trolls tribes and their neighbours settled on a virgin place to spread billions of tons of dirt everywhere.

Thus we all hailed the great leaders of our region. Even the bigfootians cheered for the regional government, the voices rang higher and higher as the soldiers lowered their guns.. in celebration of the birth of our nations!

Nuclear power combined with alarmingly large quantities of beer lead our nations to a better tomorrow, one with no hangovers and fallouts in our neighbours cities.
