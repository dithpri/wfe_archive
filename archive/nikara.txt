Nikaras is a small, simple, peaceful planet very similar to earth, only differing in its history. As of April 2026, It is a role-play heavy region built on diplomatic relations (and war). Elections are held every year, choosing a new WA Delegate and officers. It Is recommended that everyone join the WA, but not required. 
Population as of 4/27/26 is 10,336,000,000.
Current laws: None (Region members must recommend a new law so everyone can vote on it)
