[color=purple][b][i]The Mont Pelerin Society (MPS) is an international organization composed of economists (including eight winners of the Nobel Memorial Prize in Economic Sciences), philosophers, historians, intellectuals, business leaders, and others committed to their understanding of personal and political freedom. Its founders included Friedrich Hayek, Frank Knight, Karl Popper, Ludwig von Mises, George Stigler, and Milton Friedman. The society advocates freedom of expression, free market economic policies, the political values of an open society.[/i][/b][/color] [hr][color=orange]This region was created to explore, debate and discuss classical liberal, libertarian and anarchist ideals with supporters and opponents alike, and to educate others of their benefits.[/color]
