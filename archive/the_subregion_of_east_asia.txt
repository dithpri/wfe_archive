Asia is the largest of the 7 continents. There are 49 countries in Asia. Most people of Asia are Chinese, Japanese, Indian, or Arab. However, there is an abnormally high abundance of people of European descent in the de jure sovereign state of Mableabaubagobia. The main religions include Buddhism, Hinduism, Christianity, Taoism, Jainism, and Shintoism. The largest cities of Asia are Tokyo, Seoul, Jakarta, Dehli, Mumbai, Manila, Shanghai, and Bibyiee. Asia is surrounded on three sides by the Pacific, Arctic, and Indian oceans. The land of Asia is divided into six regions. These six regions are North Asia (also known as Siberia), Central Asia, Southwest Asia (also known as the Middle East), South Asia, Southeast Asia, and East Asia.
