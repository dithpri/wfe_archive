The [b]Nonaligned Movement[/b] is a group of nations seeking peace and prosperity through individual action.  Its members share a common disdain for established regional power blocs, an seek instead to govern themselves without the oppression of regional powerhouses or the violation of their sovereignty by international arenas.

Nations wishing to join the Nonaligned Movement must not be members of the World Assembly or be otherwise affiliated to regional power blocs.  The regional password may be requested by sending a telegram to [b][nation=]Aurora Nova[/nation][/b].
