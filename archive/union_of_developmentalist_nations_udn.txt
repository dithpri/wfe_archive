This region was created by Military Skydog (Permanent member with executive authority) on the 14th of July, 2017.
Here are some of our values, and ideas:

- We encourage cooperation between nations militarily, politically, and economically to spread our influence and developmentalist ideas across the globe.
- Lower tariffs and other barriers to nations within the region, this of course is up to the nations themselves.
- Sovereignty and nationalism is encouraged to each nation, this region does not take away the sovereignty of any nation in any way.
- A council shall be established between the governments of each nation.

Other ideas and values shall be added later.

For the good of our nations this region is hereby established.
