[i] This is a casual region with a roleplay bent. The are no requirements to join the region as this is not a defender nor raider region. [/i]

Sector 12 was an, until recently, mostly unexplored area of the planet. First contact was made with a group of pirates led by a canid named Capitan. Shortly thereafter, the first landfall was made upon The Holy Empire of The Desert Tiger. It was only recently discovered that many of the pirates in the region actually consider themselves a nation.
