The Bourbon Union is the personal and semi-political union between the Kingdoms of France and Navarre, The Spaniards and The Neapolitans and Sicilians, their respective colonies and the various Bourbon Dominions, all of which are under the Sovereignty of the Royal House of Bourbon.

The Head of State of The Union is HM Charles XI, V &amp;amp; VIII, King of France and Navarre, of The Spaniards and of The Neapolitans and Sicilians, who serves as constitutional monarch of both the Union, and each component State.
