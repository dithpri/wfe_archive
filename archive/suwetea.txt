[b]Suwetea[/b] (in other languages: [i]Suxzul[/i], [i]Stolukrul[/i], [i]S&amp;#299;qiyea[/i], [i]Suxyng[/i]) is a world-region in the Suwetean Ocean containing 12 countries of mostly high technological advancement. This region contains two continents - Swotahl &amp; Forobai, each having it&#39;s culture and views but intertwined history. 

The world-region is represented by the collective organization &quot;Suwetea United&quot;, itself controlled by two sub-organizations: &quot;Swotahl Area Alliance&quot; (SAAE) and &quot;International Organization of Forobai Area Interest&quot; (IOFAI).
