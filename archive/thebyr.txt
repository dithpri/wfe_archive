[B]Thebyr[/B] is the fourth planet from the star Heimdall, formed approximately 4.5 billion years ago. It has a rich biosphere and is the only known astronomical object to naturally develop sentient life. Ten billion humans live on Thebyr, organized in to diverse governments, cultures, religions, and societies. Driven by a strong sense of curiosity, rapid scientific advancements have allowed for a humanity to expand far beyond it&#39;s home in to the physical and abstract realm of the unknown.
