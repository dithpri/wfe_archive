Isolated by the vast and torrential oceans on all sides the Konkan Archipelago has been a harbour to many budding civilisations which have recently organised themselves into new nation states. [nation]Sangamtamil[/nation] being the first nation to do so has taken upon itself the responsibility to create an alliance of these fledgeling nations to provide a safe environment for them to thrive without any fear. 
This alliance that came to be known as the [region]The Konkan Alliance[/region] and promotes free trade, free travel, economic and scientific cooperation as well as cultural exchange between all of its members and keeps its door open to other similar nations who seek sanctuary in its embrace.
