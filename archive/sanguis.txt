[u]Values and Ambitions:[/u]

We are a powerful region who values ambitious wit over brute force.


[u]Landscape and Creatures:[/u]

The landscape of Sanguis includes a dark forest surrounded by the deep wonders of the Sanguis Sea. Wildlife is welcome all over Sanguis. Serpents, crows, pheasants, deer, wildcats and panthers are a few native species of animal here. Native plant species such as hawthorn, hemlock, yew, ivy and primrose are just about everywhere, concealing Sanguis from unwanted foreign attention.


[u]People and Tradition:[/u]

The Sanguine (People inhabiting Sanguis) are cynical yet compassionate. Traditions play a large roll in making decisions, but religions are irrelevant. We are currently improving our citizens&#146; intellectual ability.
