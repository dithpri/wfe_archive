[b]C&aacute;diz[/b] is a city and port in southwestern Spain. It is the capital of the province of C&aacute;diz, one of eight which make up the autonomous community of [b][region]Andalusia[/region][/b].

C&aacute;diz, the oldest continuously inhabited city in Spain and one of the oldest in western Europe, was founded by the Phoenicians. C&aacute;diz is sometimes counted as the most ancient city still standing in Western Europe, and has been a principal home port of the Spanish Navy since the accession of the Spanish Bourbons in the 18th century. &#151;Wikipedia[hr]
