Over the last few decades, the continent of Virsenia has been undergoing a new industrial revolution, fueled by a resource called frescite. This green-burning mineral has countless applications from military and scientific to more rudimentary uses. 

VIRSENIAN GLORY is a total conversion mod for Running With Rifles set in a fictional world, centered on the continent of Virsenia. The year is 1928, but technology resembles what was used during the 30&#39;s, 40&#39;s, and even the first half of the 50&#39;s. Technology - fueled by frescite - took a different path from technology in real life. There are no things such as jet engines nor nuclear energy or bombs, but helicopters were widely developed with great ambition, for example.
