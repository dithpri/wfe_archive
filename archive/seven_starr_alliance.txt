The Seven Starr Alliance is a multinational, interuniversal organization committed to the spread of peace and order. The SSA hails from the First Gigaquadrant, a separate universe from the NationStates universe. The founder of the SSA colony here as well as its main administrator is Cyrandia, but due to Cyrandia&amp;#39;s inactivity, leadership falls to Tybusenia.
