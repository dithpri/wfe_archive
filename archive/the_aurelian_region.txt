This region is a vast land with people of a majority similar ancestry, but with vastly different cultures that take up large sections of the region. The people are fiercly loyal to the political leaders who follow their ideology, which has led to stronghold governments which lean toward a one-party government. There are ten major cities, the largest of them being in the northeast. They are often troubled by their political disagreements and some parts of the region are littered with rampant crime and rebellion. They are proud of many things that make up each individual nation.
