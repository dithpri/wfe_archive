Long before the Union&#146;s first Preamble was penned, the Continent of Crescenda was a patchwork of fractious republics and fledgling democracies&#151;some deeply divided by ideology, others threatened by external conquest. In the Year of the First Eclipse (NS 184), a coalition of centrist and left-leaning states convened in the embattled city-state of Aurembia to forge a new path: one where democracy would not be broken by tyranny, nor prosperity hoarded by the few.
