Welcome my friends to the [B][color=red]Second Empire of Kitain[/color][/B]!

Our time of chaos has come to an end. The roleplay projects of Ultimum Terrae, Arcanica, Tecerion and the First Empire are concluded. We have caused rampage and madness to those we view as our lessers, and our memories will now remain, burned into their writings as unavoidable black spots upon the white pages of their tedious history. To all Kitani who came before, existed once, or exist now - Know that the adventure is over. We made our mark. And we regret nothing.

We dance through the danger, 
For legends never die.
