The Sarusian Continents consists of several large continental bodies populated by people of mostly Eastern European, Slavic, and Germanic descent. The nations of the Sarusian Continents are unique in that they had developed their own number of societies independently of the rest of the world. One of its most prominent and internationally known collection of continents is popularly known as The Triple Continents.
