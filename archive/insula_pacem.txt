The Region of Insula Pacem is a region that became Independent from the evil regime of The Northern Imperium. After the War for region independence the region created it&#39;s own and has lived at peace since then. The region is still looking for Revenge againts the evil regime of The Northern Imperium and will not accept a apology. 

The region flag has a peaceful meaning. With the blue representing the sea. The white representing peace. The green representing the Island of Insula Pacem and the Swastika representing good fortune.
