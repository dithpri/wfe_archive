This region is a Sci-fi RP region, set in the 4000th century, a age of universal peace, that is threatened by a number of growing threats! You can help destroy them by joining the Universal Peacekeeping Force (UPF) to help crush the invaders and keep The peace, well, peaceful! Anyone of any race, gender or political ideals is welcome, as long as you are NOT a Nazi, we welcome you with open arms!
