&amp;#128081; The Court of Kings

&amp;#11088; Overview
The Court of Kings is a sovereign region ruled by the towering legacies of Dennis Rodman, Nikola Joki&amp;#263;, and Scottie Barnes three players whose styles could not be more different, yet whose combined presence forms an unstoppable frontcourt monarchy.

The region is built on three pillars: chaos, efficiency, and ascension.
