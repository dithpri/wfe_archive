Ruled over by Grand Moff Thadrius, the Imperial Sector of Keltoris was ruled by the Republic during the Clone Wars, only to be immeadiently became apart of The Galactic Empire. Here, law-abiding citizens are forced into labor in The Mines of Keltor. With a heavy Imperial presence, the Moff welcomes all Imperial fleets, as they gather forces and destroy the Rebellion once and for all.
