The government will consist of a hierarchy of positions elected by the people to best represent their interests.

Tertiary Positions (anyone can run)
Homeland officer, Security officer, Communications officer, Diplomacy officer, Research officer

Secondary Positions (only those holding tertiary positions may run)
Head of Internal Affairs, Head of External Affairs, Captain of Security

Primary Positions (only those holding secondary positions may run) *Now in effect*
Vice Delegate, World Assembly Delegate, Founder
