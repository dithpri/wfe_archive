KaNLung or Kapulugan ng Nagkakaisang Lungsod is a Filipino Organisation and Forum for TheoTown located in the Philippines.

Since it&#39;s completion on April, KanLung has become the largest bilateral development organizations in the Philippines with a network of nearly 50 members. The reorganized council is also responsible for administering parts in Theotown Community. 
&amp;#64;Ayzu and &amp;#64;Lakan Haraya is the main founder of this Organisation on April 26, 2020, with nearly a year of existence, the organisation creates better understanding and communication towards Filipino TheoTown Players.
