The [b]Federal Armed Forces of the Royal Union of Luvindanao[/b], or simply the [b]Luvindanaon Armed Forces[/b] ([b]APL[/b]) are the military services that are responsible for the defence of Luvindanao from external and internal threats. It is made up of three branches, the ground forces and the oldest branch the [b][color=darkgreen]Royal Luvindanaon Army[/color][/b] ([b][color=darkgreen]MHKL[/color][/b]), the naval branch, the [b][color=darkblue]Royal Luvindanaon Navy[/color][/b] ([b][color=darkblue]MHDL[/color][/b]), the air force, the [b][color=turquoise]Royal Luvindanaon Air Force[/color][/b] ([b][color=turquoise]MHHL[/color][/b]), and the youngest branch, the [b][color=gold]Royal Luvindanaon Coast Guard[/color][/b] ([b][color=gold]MBBL[/color][/b]).
