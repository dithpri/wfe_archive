Founded in 753 CE, the small city of Rome grew to rule the whole of the Mediterranean region, and provided the foundation for modern Europe and Western Civilization. Before emperors and the Coliseum, Rome was administered by one of the most complex and innovative governments in world history. This region seeks to revive this glorious past in NationStates, and provide a source of entertainment and education for those who wish to learn more of the glory that was Rome.

All those who wish to join should choose a [url=http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Roman_naming_conventions]Roman name [/url] and place it in their motto space so that we can create a more authentic simulation.
