We are a union of nations who strive to ensure peace and security among nations across the continent of Epril. Although a mainly diplomatic organization, we the Blue Union can and will use military force to ensure safety of the people. Rather than be lead by corrupt leaders who would much rather steal money from the people, we understand that money and politics are fire and ice.
