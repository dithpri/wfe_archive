The region of [region]The Havenish Commonwealth of Nations[/region] is a group of:

- Colonies/territories of the Havenish Empire
- Nations that were once part of the Havenish Empire
- Nations interested in neighboring &amp; building relations with the rest of [region]The Havenish Commonwealth of Nations[/region]

Nations wanting to settle in a simple and minimalistic region are very welcome to move to [region]The Havenish Commonwealth of Nations[/region] anytime.

The HCN region aims to establish good relationships among its member nations whilst maintaining a minimalistic region. This region is for countries that are/once part of the Havenish Empire AND for leaders wanting to govern their nations in peace.
