[b]The Archipelago of Cienfuegos[/b] - &amp;quot;Paradise on Earth...&amp;quot;
[hr]
Best known for its crystal clear waters and white-washed beaches, The Archipelago of Cienfuegos is composed of seven politically and culturally diverse nation-states and one colony, The Archipelago of Cienfuegos is the center of interest in the western world, since 1492. Named after the festive flames illuminating the islands during a Native feast celebrating the full moon, The Archipelago of Cienfuegos has a long history, stretching from the earliest days of European Colonialism, all the way to the Ideological Revolutions sweeping through the Archipelago during the Cold War.
