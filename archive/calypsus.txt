A continent comprised of ruined cities due to ecocide and corporate warfare as far as the eye can see.  Surviving nations and new nations typically attempt to fill power vacuums and develop extreme tendencies in order to maintain itself.  That, or they declare war amongst each other.  If not, they fall prey to the very corporatocracies that made Calypsus collapse long ago.  

The region is mostly frigid.  Most of Calypsus is either in a tundra or taiga, if not temperate.  Nevertheless, due to a long line of ancient to modern ecocides, the landscape is typically dry and the ruins of cities from all sorts of time periods dot the landscape.
