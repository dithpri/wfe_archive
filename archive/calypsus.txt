The continent of Calypsus is a huge and developing part of the unknown world.  Full of unique nations that sprawl the temperate to frigid landmasses.  Mountains are very common and Calypsus is also rich in resources.  Several unique kinds of fruits can grow in the cold majority of the continent, making life flourish all around.  For the most part, Calypsus has plenty of stunning views all across the continent.  Countries present in Calypsus vary in all categories.  The Dominion of Saurice, founder of the international community in Calypsus, frequently advocates for scientific advancement.
