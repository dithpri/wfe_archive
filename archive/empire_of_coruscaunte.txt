The Empire of Coruscaunte is a burgeoning power, rising from the second of two coups, the first of which was to overthrow the Republic of Coruscaunte in favor of an absolute monarchy, and the latter, to overturn the democractic senate of the Galactic Andromedan Republic, turning it into the beginnings of an autocratic state.

That former republic is hereby a vassal state of the newly formed Empire of Coruscaunte, which reigns from this region. The monarch exercises discretion of the formerly robust democracy that was [region]The Galactic Republic of Andromeda[/region].
