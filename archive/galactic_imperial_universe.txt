Welcome to the Star Wars Galaxy, where the Empire is maintaining peace and order, the Rebel Alliance is messing it up, and other people are doing their own things! 

This is an RP region, where each nation can join the mighty Galactic Empire or the traitorous Rebel scum, or a neutral minor faction! Each nation plays as a certain part of each faction. 

Timeline starts in 19 BBY, and 1 IC year goes by every RL week.
