Regional Goal:
Social alignment for the common good of states.

Regional Government:
The WA delegate acts as the president of the region.

All major changes will be made by voting in polls.
(Voting is highly encouraged)

Voting for a new delegate is the 1st of every month.

Delegates must wait a period of 2 elections (60 Days) before they can run again, this is to prevent anyone from staying in power for to long.

Regional Military:
The region is defended by a united military alliance know as the UFF (United Federative Forces).

Regional Nations:
All types of nations are welcome.

Multiple &amp;quot;Security Nations&amp;quot; are kept on standby to act in the event of a coup being staged in the region.

Regional Flag:
The Four stars stand for the four founders of the region.
