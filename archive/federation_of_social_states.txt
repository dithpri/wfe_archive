Regional Goal:
Social alignment for the common good of states.

Voting for a new delegate is the 1st of every month.

Delegates must wait a period of 2 elections (2 Months) before they can run again, this is to prevent anyone from staying in power for too long.

Only one vote per person is counted. Any vote from a minor/colonial nation who shares the same leader with a nation that has already voted is rendered null.

Regional Military:
The region is defended by a united military alliance know as the UFF (United Federative Forces).

Regional Nations:
All types of nations are welcome.

Regional Flag:
The Four stars stand for the four founders of the region.
