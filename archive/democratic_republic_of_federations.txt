WE, The Democratic Republic of Federation 
having solemnly resolved to form a more perfect Union, establish Justice, ensure Sovereign, the Secular Democratic Republic to all its Citizens and to secure to all its Citizens:
JUSTICE, social, economic and political;

LIBERTY of thought, expression, belief, faith and worship;

EQUALITY of status and of opportunity;

GENERAL WELFARE of its Citizens.

FEDERATION OF Governance, Autonomy of its own Nation.
