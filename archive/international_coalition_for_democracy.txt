CHARTER OF THE INTERNATIONAL COALITION FOR DEMOCRACY

We the Peoples of the International Coalition for Democracy recognize the fundamental Human Rights that belong to All People.  These Human Rights that provide Freedom, Justice, Dignity, and Worth to All are best preserved and exercised in Nations with Democratic forms of government.   Therefore, We seek to Affirm the value of Democracy and to Promote and Defend Democracy around the World so that All People may enjoy those Equal Human Rights.
