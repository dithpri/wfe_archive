The [b][color=darkred]Crown[/color] [color=darkred]Republic[/color][/b] of [color=darkred][b]Melhaven[/b][/color] is a Libertarian Socialist Monarchist Republic region founded on the principles of cultural unity and the Crown. The region features an offsite forum, government, flag, map, and a variety of activities that players from across nation-states might find appealing. [color=darkred][b]Melhaven[/b][/color] is an Imperialist region with strong military values and a focus on the spread of [color=darkred][b]Melhavre[/b][/color] influence. It is from [color=darkred][b]Melhaven[/b][/color] that the [color=darkred][b]Crown of Winterlich[/b][/color] rests, and which that House both subsists and rules with the assistance of the [color=darkblue][b]Proletariat[/b][/color].
