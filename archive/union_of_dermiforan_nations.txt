The Union of Demiforan is a democratically elected union which represents  the various  sovereign Nations within the galaxy  of  Dermiforan. This Union has a President, who presides over the Senate to pass legislation which shall effect each nation within the region. Each nation state is sovereign, however it must comply with legislation passed by the Senate, and is a military ally of each other nation that is within the region. Each nation has a lively culture, with cooperation of each memeber to ensure the security and stability of the region as a whole.
