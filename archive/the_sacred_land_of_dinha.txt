This is the region where only one truth prevails: The omniscience and supreme law of Merdinha Dinha, the being above men!
No discrimination, divisions or debates exist within the realm of Dinha, for it is the unifying force of nature, thus making all living beings alike.
Fear not, wanderer, for Dinha will protect and accept you, take the nicest dump, and thank Dinha, and henceforth thy shall be blessed.
