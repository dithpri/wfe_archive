Region Name: The Wild Frontier

General Information:
The Wild Frontier is an untamed region founded by the Empire of the Black Sun Dragon. It embodies a spirit of exploration and freedom, attracting adventurers seeking uncharted territories and new experiences. 

Government:
The region operates under a council-based governance system, where the Wild Council comprises representatives from each country within the region. Collaborating to address regional matters, the council is led by the Dragon Chancellor, who holds final decision-making authority and always comes from the Empire of the Black Sun Dragon. 

Currency:
If you join the wild frontier, please change your currency to the wild dollar
