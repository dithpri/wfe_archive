[region]Planet Helghan[/region] was destroyed by a Petrusite implosion during the extrasolar wars and rebuilt from scratch at the end of them. And returned under the control of the Helghast.

Following numerous treaties and agreements, it was decided to welcome the Mudokon refugees and their empress on the planet, giving them half of it ([region]Mudokon territories of Planet Helghan[/region]).
The relations between [nation]New New Helghan[/nation] and [nation]Oddworld[/nation] are friendly, and the nations often cooperate.
