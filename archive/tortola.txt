[b]Tortola[/b] is the largest and most populated of the British Virgin Islands, a group of islands that form part of the archipelago of the Virgin Islands. It has a surface area of 21 square miles with a total population of 23,908, with 9400 residents in Road Town. Mount Sage is its highest point at 1,740 feet above sea level. &#151;Wikipedia[hr]
