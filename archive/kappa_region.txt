A region founded from the Federation of Democratic Socialist Saxony and the Republic of Maltaan. The[region] Kappa Region[/region] is known for its strength in unity, the first region to focus on bringing others together to help each other in the World Assembly and make change in our world of nations.
Our region offers a [url=https://www.nationstates.net/page=dispatch/id=714385/]World Map[/url] in which nations may claim a sizable amount of land. We do hold open elections for the WA Delegate and President of the region every month. Elections start 2 weeks before the end of the month, and newly elected leaders take charge the 1st of every month. Kappa is all about fun and is most importantly respecting each other. Kappa a region of challenge, prosperity and unity.
