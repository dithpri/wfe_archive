A region founded from the Republic of Democratic Socialist Saxony and the Republic of Maltaan. This rejoin is known for its strength in unity, the first region to focus on bringing others together to help each other in the World Assembly and make change in our world of nations.
Our region offers a world map in which nations may claim a sizable amount of land. We do plan on eventually holding open elections for the WA Delegate and eventually President of the region. Our goal is to become the best of the many regions that exist. Kappa is all about fun and is most importantly respecting each other even when war is present. Kappa a region of challenge, prosperity and unity.
