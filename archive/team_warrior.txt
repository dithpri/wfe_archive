This region is the main region for the [b][i][u]Team Warrior[/u][/i][/b] family of clans! To join this region, you must be a member of one of the three Team Warrior - Special Forces clans: Team Warrior - Special Forces (TWSF), Team Warrior - Special Forces: Bravo Company (TWSFB), and Team Warrior - Special Forces: Charlie Company (TWSFC). If you are a member of these 3 clans, then you are welcome to join, if you are not, feel free to apply. Website link will be provided upon contacting a recruiter for your selected clan. Take care, and Cheers!
