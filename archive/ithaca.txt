Located in Upstate New York on the southern shore of Cayuga Lake, [b]Ithaca[/b] is best known for its gorges, waterfalls, and long winter season. It is home to [color=red]Cornell University[/color] and [color=blue]Ithaca College[/color]. Because of the large student population, Ithaca has a reputation for being the most diverse and progressive city in the State.

From 2000 to 2006, Ithaca was voted 3 times by different sources to be one of the best places to live in the United States.
