Inspired by the real-life proposal of its namesake, the New [b][colour=red]Union of Sovereign States (&amp;#1057;&amp;#1086;&amp;#1102;&amp;#1079; &amp;#1057;&amp;#1091;&amp;#1074;&amp;#1077;&amp;#1088;&amp;#1077;&amp;#1085;&amp;#1085;&amp;#1099;&amp;#1093; &amp;#1043;&amp;#1086;&amp;#1089;&amp;#1091;&amp;#1076;&amp;#1072;&amp;#1088;&amp;#1089;&amp;#1090;&amp;#1074;)[/colour][/b] is a confederation of former Soviet Republics and the successor of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics (USSR), otherwise known as the Soviet Union. We welcome all nations who are inspired by the Soviet Union or the Post-Soviet Era and are dedicated to constructing a new neutral world order in the Post-Soviet era.
