The Brisbane Islands are a collection of extremely versatile and productive islands. Spanning temperate and tropical climates, the islands represent a broad span of land types, from steaming, wet, dripping rainforest covered mountainous mounds to arid, prickly wastelands with immense amounts of perfect farmland in between. The region is a paradise to all leaders who know what is best for their nation. Political ideologies vary greatly, however strong links of friendship and loyalty bind the nations. Most of the populace is comfortable.
