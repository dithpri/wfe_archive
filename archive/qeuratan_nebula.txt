The Qeuratan Nebula is a star cluster located within the Milky Way, approximately 175 megalight-years away from Earth and the Sol System.

The species here live on their own planets or share a planet with a fellow government. The Qeuratan Nebula&amp;#39;s residents meet here in this commonplace to discuss current affairs and settle various disputes, be it over land or trade.

The Qeuratan Nebula has many open planets awaiting to be colonized, and is open to any species that wants to reside here, be it humans, drell, equines, klingons, anthromorphs, or what have you.
