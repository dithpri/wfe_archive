Losarilon is a faction banded between multiple nations who share common interests in foreign policy, economics and furthermore generally militaristic means, often sharing borders and or culture with one another, it is often noted that the group of nations inside of Losarilon are focused primarily civil liberties and the furthering of the people, as well as general economic and developmental aide to one another,
Losarilon is made up of nations on the fictional continent of Sakolaria, a small [compared to other continents] and yet prosperous continent with diversity and more often than not civil connections beyond the barriers of linguistics, finding as many to have a common brotherhood due to their nations alliance.
