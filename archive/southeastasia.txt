SoutheastAsia
Geographic Coordinates: Between 10&deg;N and 15&deg;S latitude, 90&deg;E and 135&deg;E longitude

Overview

SoutheastAsia is a vibrant and diverse geopolitical region comprising numerous nations, each with distinct cultures, histories, and economic profiles. Known for its tropical climates, bustling trade routes, and strategic maritime importance, SoutheastAsia plays a pivotal role in global affairs, connecting the Pacific and Indian Oceans.
