[b]The Indian Resolutious Vianard (Resanguardo) are defined less by a shared history than by a shared condition. This vast continent is a patchwork of nascent and established countries, many having emerged from a period of political devolution and reorganization.[/b] The common thread is a collective identity forged on the threshold of development, of defining a place in the world. The establishment of the Continental Forum in Leops City, Hilando, marks the first formal effort to create a permanent body for dialogue and collective action.
