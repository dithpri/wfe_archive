      The premier raiding region of the AxoBadgerian Islands, The Templar Order is in service of the Interregional Federation. Purely un-democratic, the region has advanced defensive edicts that prevent WA raids. It is named as the Central Invasion Hub of Axobadgeria, and all raids commanded by the noble delegacy or noble founder of the Federation will be based in The Templar Order. It is primarily a supporter of defense, law and order, and religion
