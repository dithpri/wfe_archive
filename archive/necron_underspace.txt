The [b]Necron Underspace[/b] is a artificially created extra-dimensional sanctuary designed to ensure the survival of [b][nation=noflag]the Necron-Empire[/nation][/b] for eternity. The underspace is a basically a tiny isolated universe shrouded by a space-time tensor bubble and thus completely isolated from the warp and extremely inaccessible from real-space.

Additionally the [b][nation=noflag]the Necron-Empire[/nation][/b] is controlling more than 90 million star systems in real space as well as some territories inside of a variety of space anomalies.
