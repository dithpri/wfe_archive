The [b]Tau Sphere[/b] is a hidden and inaccessible area located inside the event horizon of a hyper-massive black hole. 

The [b]Tau Sphere[/b] is controled by [b][nation=noflag]-The Tau Empire[/nation][/b]. The empire colonized the black hole by building thousands of heavily armored artificial biospheres, protected by space-time reality shielding.
