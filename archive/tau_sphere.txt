The [b]Tau Sphere[/b] is a hidden and inaccessible area located inside the event horizon of the hyper-massive black hole A001 at the core of the galaxy.

The [b]Tau Sphere[/b] is controled by [b][nation=noflag]-The Tau Empire[/nation][/b]. The empire colonized the black hole by building  gigantic, heavily armored artificial biosphere superstructures. This superstructures are protected by space-time reality shielding in order to survive the extreme physical conditions inside a black hole.
