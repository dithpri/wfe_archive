[color=336699]The Miskito Cays, a part of the RRAN Autonomous region, are an archipelago with an area of 27 km&sup2; located off shore in the North-Eastern Caribbean Coast of Nicaragua. The Miskito Cays are composed of 76 formations that include estuaries, coral reefs, cays, seagrass beds, and islets, of which 12 of the formations are covered with vegetation and consequently form islands which are lined with white sand beaches.[/color][hr]
