[b]The Rebel Alliance[/b] is a resistance movement formed by General Derminius and &quot;The Republic Club&quot; to oppose the reign of the Empire. The early group came together from a series of rebel cells, including the crew of the &quot;Squad of Death&quot;. The Rebel Alliance was a band of soldiers and pilots who served in a struggle against the Imperial Forces many years after the Battle of Raskillon. As a symbol in struggle against the Imperial Forces, the Rebel Alliance adopted the starbird.
