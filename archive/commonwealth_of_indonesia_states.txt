CIS is a regional organization whose members are the countries with Indonesian nationality. This organization was founded by Republik Indonesia and [nation]islamic of al fatih[/nation] on 17 August 2015 in Jakarta. The main objective of this organization is to accommodate the interests of participants of the CIS states in international forums, economic cooperation, social and defense.
