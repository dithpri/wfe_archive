A region based on the concept of each player creating a more interesting/ alternate version of existing countries, and roleplaying with the rest of the world. Nostalgia for non-existent countries can get you a spot, but there must be an explanation for how you think the country would still exist.
[i]RULES:[/i]
1. Do not RP or ask for a land claim unless validated by a moderator - ALL REQUESTED INFORMATION MUST BE IN APPLICATION FACTBOOK (Current mods: [nation]Mozambik[/nation])
2. Please have a realistic, factbook supported land claim
3. Your country must have an appropriate name. Numbers in names will not be allowed
