A group of nations that are closely allied to stop injustice, prejudice, racism and denial throughout. We will defend ourselves from any perceived threat created against us or our allies. Our intelligence/espionage and research organizations are led by Trovania, while beverage producing is led by Lunshyran Revolutionaries. We enjoy trading with other nations, as long as it benefits both sides. 

Official Languages: English, French, Hebrew, Onyxxian, Jolandian, Lunshyran and Trovanian
Official Motto: Never give up, never surrender (Merpius Premius)
Official Regional Capitol: Riveria
Official Government: Council of Member Nations
