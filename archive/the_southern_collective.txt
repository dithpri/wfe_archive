The Southern Collective is a collection of nations in the southern sector of the Earth. It was created in 2XXX and officially recognized in 2XXX by the World Assembly. 

Nations that make up the Southern Collective are: The Antarctic Union, Shockenawhe, Floppaslovia, Mollettagascar, Newerest York, and Undavel; with Undavel being the latest entry into the region. 

Most information is unknown about the region, but this book will be updated with any that is collected.
