The Southern Collective is a collection of nations in the southern sector of the Earth. It was created in 4XXX and officially recognized in 4XXX by the World Assembly. 

Nations that make up the Southern Collective are: The Antarctic Union, Shockenawhe, Floppaslovia, Mollettagascar, and the Gupzone.; with the Gupzone being the latest entry into the region. 

Most information is unknown about the region, but this book will be updated with any that is collected.
