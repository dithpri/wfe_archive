Turks and Caicos are a British Overseas Territory consisting of two groups of tropical islands in the West Indies.
 - Salt Collectors from Bermuda occupied the islands by 1681. 
- Annexed to Bahamas 1799 by Britain tho disputed by Bermuda 
- made a separate colony 1848 
- Jamaica 1873 
- colony 1959 (UK). 

Capital: Cockburn Town 
Area: 417 km&sup2; 
Population: 21,746
