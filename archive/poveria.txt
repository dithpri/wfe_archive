              THE RECONSTRUCTED COMMONWEALTH OF POVERIA

&quot;Ex Cineribus, Opulentia&quot; &#151; Out of the Ashes, Opulence.

Poveria was not born of luxury, but of absolute necessity. Named after the desperate economic ruin (&quot;poverty&quot;) that plagued our founding ancestral states, this region was established as a mutual survival pact. Today, that dark history is a distant memory.

* THE FOUNDING DICTUM: We keep the name Poveria so we never forget the cost of division, greed, and economic collapse. 
* POLITICAL LANDSCAPE: A highly diverse community welcoming all forms of government, from utopian eco-democracies to ruthlessly efficient corporate technocracies.
