An alliance of provinces, federations and colonies to the north. Being on the ragged edge of civilization has forced theses nations to form a pact to ensure there survival and prosperity in an ever changing and dangerous landscape. You will find these nations are set upon harsh stormy land - almost entirely impossible to navigate as an outsider. Politically, most nations are socialist states with comprehensive welfare. Of course there are exceptions. Being located in the ancient sources of such cultures you will find this region is a boiling pot of Celtic and Nordic beliefs and traditions with some influence from neighboring areas.
