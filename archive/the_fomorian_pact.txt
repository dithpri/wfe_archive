An alliance of provinces, federations and colonies to the north. Being on the ragged edge of civilization has forced theses nations to form a pact to ensure there survival and prosperity in an ever changing and dangerous landscape. You will find these nations are set upon harsh stormy land. You will find this region is a boiling pot of Celtic and Nordic beliefs and traditions with some influence from neighboring areas.

The Fomorian Pact has a large population of immigrants from the series of islands known as [region]Isla Variatas[/region]. This has led to some tention between the regions in the past.
