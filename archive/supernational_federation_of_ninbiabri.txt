The Supernational Federation of Ninbiabri is a single federation of nations under a supreme, fair government, the Nation of United Ninbiabri. All nations and citizens of the Supernational Federation exercise the basic rights of humanity.

The Federation is Communist-Socialist in nature, believing that if you work, you get the basic rights of being in a society, if you work extra, you get extra. All of which goes to furthering the nations economy.
