[region]Reino de Espana[/region] (Espa&ntilde;a), or Kingdom of Spain is located on the Iberian Peninsula in southwestern Europe. Its mainland is bordered to the south and east by the Mediterranean Sea except for a small land boundary with Gibraltar; to the north and northeast by France, Andorra, and the Bay of Biscay; and to the west and northwest by Portugal and the Atlantic Ocean. It has both Atlantic and Mediterranean coastlines. 

Spaniards are a nation and ethnic group native to Spain that share a common Spanish culture[/b]
