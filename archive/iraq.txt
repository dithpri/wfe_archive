[b][region]Iraq[/region][/b] or [b]Eye-rack[/b] is a place in the middle of East, which has large deposits of cooking oil, for which it has often been invaded.

It was formerly called the [b]Mess of Potamia[/b], but recently it has served as the mess of the US Army.

Iraq has a history of [b]Bath Parties[/b], where people gathered in bathtubs and partied till they passed out. Due to a scarcity of water for baths, it now has only wet-sponge parties.

Iraq is currently managed by [nation]Rewqa[/nation].
