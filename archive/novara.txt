Novara-2A, a member of the Nova Star System, is the second planet from Nova, orbiting in its habitable zone.

Conditions on Novara-2A are suitable for human survival, and the planet shows promise of a new beginning...

This region belongs to the [region]New Sleanuxian Order[/region], which, due to concerns of name originality, renamed itself to &quot;The Novaran Order&quot;.
