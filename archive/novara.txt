Novara-2b, a member of the &quot;Novara&quot; binary star system, is the second planet of Novara-B, orbiting in its habitable zone.

Conditions on Novara-2b are suitable for human survival, and the planet shows promise of a new beginning...

This region belongs to the [region]New Sleanuxian Order[/region], which, due to concerns of name originality, renamed itself to &quot;The Novaran Order&quot;.
