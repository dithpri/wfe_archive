Plants vs. Zombies (abbreviated as PvZ, known in Japan as Plant vs. Zombie (Japanese: &amp;#12503;&amp;#12521;&amp;#12531;&amp;#12488; vs. &amp;#12478;&amp;#12531;&amp;#12499; Hepburn: Puranto vs. Zonbi)) is a tower defense video game developed and originally published by PopCap Games and it is the first installment in the Plants vs. Zombies series. The game involves homeowners who use a variety of different plants to prevent waves of zombies from entering their houses and &quot;eating their brains&quot;. It was first released on May 5, 2009, and it originally (and initially) was made available on Steam for Microsoft Windows and macOS. 

The game received a positive response from critics and was nominated for multiple Interactive Achievement Awards, alongside receiving praise for its musical score.
