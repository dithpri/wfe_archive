The Royal Commonwealth of Realms, commonly known as the Commonwealth (formerly the Montenbourg Commonwealth), is an intergovernmental organisation of 8 member states, 2 Duchedoms and 6 Princedoms, that were mostly territories of the former Monten Empire. The Commonwealth operates by intergovernmental consensus of the member states, organised through the Commonwealth Secretariat. Has King Lawrence I as its reigning constitutional monarch, and has a royal line of succession in common with the other realms.The Commonwealth realms are, for purposes of international relations, sovereign states. They are united only in voluntary sharing of the institution of the monarchy, the succession, and the King himself.
