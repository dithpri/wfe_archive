Overview
Welcome! This is the Ideology Hub region, a place where dialogue between people of different nations, cultures, and regions come to co-mingle on NationStates. As a region, we seek to create a community of people that discuss politics, among other topics. 
The Ideology Hub region is devoted to creating a space where people can freely RP within the world that we create. We encourage people to roleplay genuinely within the world that we create in this server.
