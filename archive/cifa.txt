[b]Cohesive Integration for Foreign Affairs[/b]

The CIFA is a region of geographically close nations, which decided to join their forces and build a strong communicative system. 
Its cardinal points are to avoid war within its borders, and to be able to compete economically with further away nations, which had started to threaten them because of the advanvement of the means of transport.

However incompatible their ideologies may seem to be, these nations managed to put their differences aside to build a seemingly strong coalition.
