[colour=#02081f][b]Introduction to the Order of Polaris[/b][/colour]
The Order of Polaris is an interstellar democratic association of nations, located in the (fictitious) galaxy of Polaris.

[colour=#02081f][b]Ideologies[/b][/colour]
While the Order will gladly welcome member states with (almost) any political regime, please be aware that the following ideologies and concepts are put forward by the OoP:
- Democracy
- Pacifism
- Anti-Fascism
- Scientism
- Atheism/Secularism
