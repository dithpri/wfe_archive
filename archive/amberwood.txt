[b]Amberwood[/b] is an autumn-themed roleplay world where players create nations, form alliances, and craft stories in a forested, vibrant land.  
New nations can join, introduce themselves, and participate in diplomacy, trade, and regional events.  
Amberwood is connected to [region]Auzemn[/region] and encourages a welcoming, active community for all players.
