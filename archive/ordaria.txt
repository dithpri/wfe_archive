Ordaria is a land of lush rain forests and high mountains. Its coastal areas teem with vibrant coral reefs. Recently, civilizations appeared after many tribal wars. The people of Ordaria are fiercely loyal to each of their comrades and look forward to the many achievements they will have and the progress they will make.

Though,  still vibrant with wildlife, industry has taken a firm standing, rapidly transforming the region into a modernized area.
