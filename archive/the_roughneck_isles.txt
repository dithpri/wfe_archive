The Roughneck Isles is a vast area of unexplored potential.  Few natives leave the Isles, but those that do tend be considered socially awkward and are left to their own devices.  Roughneckians are a varied people with differing views concerning all walks of Life.  The nations which make up the Roughneck Isles are all at peace with one another and typically avoid mingling unnecessarily with the rest of the world.
