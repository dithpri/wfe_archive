The Kingdom of Jerusalem is a country in Western Asia located on the eastern shore of the Mediterranean Sea. It was established in 1612 from lands captured by France in the Tenth Crusade of 1602. The Government of Jerusalem is Unitary Parliamentary Democracy under Constitutional Monarchy, where the king is the head of state and the Prime Minister is the head of the Government and the Haute Cour is the legislature of the Kingdom. The Current King of Jerusalem is King Eren.
