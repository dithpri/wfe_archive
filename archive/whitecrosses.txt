Region Name: White Crosses

Motto: Strength in Unity, Honor in Loyalty

Region Type: Economic Dictatorship

Overview:
White Crosses is a region built on the ideals of strength, unity, and loyalty. Under the stern and resolute leadership of Xurathorix, White Crosses is a place where order and discipline are paramount. The nations within the region thrive under a collective spirit, each contributing to the larger cause, with a shared commitment to security, power, and stability.
