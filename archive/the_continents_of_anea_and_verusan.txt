The two most western continents of the Strangereal earth, containing a depth of countries and cultures of all walks of life. 
Anea is the geographic northernmost continent on the planet. It is, however, home to various climates including deserts. Three major countries are known to exist on Anea: Nordennavic, Emmeria, and Estovakia.

The Verusan continent is another of the largest continents on Earth, and hosts the largest number of countries of any continent. 20 countries are present, including Yuktobania, Verusa, Sotoa, Kaluga, and Romny.
