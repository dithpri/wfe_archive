The Continental Anthro Alliance of Oshin Kon, or CAA-OK/CAA, is known as the continental region and Empire of the continent, Oshin Kon. The nation known as Sikomi was the first nation to form in Oshin Kon. The CAA is welcoming to all nations that are multi-species or not, so that all are created equal.
