The Global Co-operation Pact (GCP) is a collection of nations that work together to improve the relationship between nations through trade and diplomacy.

Members must: 
- Allow free trade between nations. No tariffs or sanctions to be imposed on other nations withing the GCP without permission of the High Court.

- Allow free movement of civilians between nations, random searches are to only be permitted if there is justifiable reason. Passport checks are only permitted in airports unless a search must be conducted and civilians should not be made to pay an additional fee for crossing the border into other nations in the GCP.

- Defend each other in case of an attack unless the Founder or WA Delegate say otherwise
