The Gaberiel Federation is a fledgling region that was once home to a super power nation know as the Gaberiel Republic. After an economic disaster occurred the nation split into two countries. The successor to the liberal policies, and terrible economy of the Republic know as the Union.  While the Confederacy is home to a minuscule government based on Right-Libertarianism. The Confederacy currently holds the title of Superpower in the Region.  Several other countries have also developed in the region such as the brutal Dictatorship of Axberg.
