[b]NAME[/b]
NSraid - Raid a NationStates region

[b]SYNOPSIS[/b]
[b]NSraid[/b] --target [u]regionname[/u] --puppet [u]nationname[/u] --jumppoint [u]regionname[/u]

[b]DESCRIPTION[/b]
Raids a NationStates region using a puppet from a jump point.

[b]--target[/b]   The region to raid. Must update before the jump point.
[b]--puppet[/b]  The puppet to use. Must be in the World Assembly.
[b]--jumppoint[/b] The jump point to use. Must update after the region.
