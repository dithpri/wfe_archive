Initially a mission named Mission NGC224, was started to go to the Andromeda Galaxy. Funded by three countries: the United States of America, The United Kingdom, and Japan. This simple mission to find life was a success, The mission found 312 systems with life more complex than bacteria.  
      With cooperation to the major Governments in Andromeda, the Human race started the ASF; Th Andromeda Space Federation. With a Headquarters in a nuetral area in the galaxy. The base was designed by humans, now the human colony contains over 146 million people and holds reign over their own sector. ASF is the collective union of Empires in Andromeda. The empires were not ruled by pre-existing nationalities, but all new governments.
