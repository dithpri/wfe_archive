The [b]Eurasian Empire[/b], [b]Central Asian Khanate[/b], [b]Imperial State of Eurasia[/b] is a sovereign nation of Central Asia that stretches from the Caspian Sea in the west to China and Mongolia in the east, and from Afghanistan and Iran in the south to Russia in the north with a total area of 1,545,741 square miles making it the 7th largest country in the world and a population of 76,631,308 people from various ethnic groups.

The regions and autonomous areas of Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Tajikistan, Turkmenistan, and Uzbekistan united under a constitutional monarchy after overthrowing the Soviet invaders.
