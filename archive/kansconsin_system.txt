The Kansconsin System is notable for being the only human inhabited system in the Andromeda Galaxy. The efforts of the Kansconsonians to find other life in the Galaxy have come up null. Indeed, the Kansconsonians have left behind the rest of humanity, with high-jacked technology from a superior civilization which will never see it&#39;s goods passed onto others back in the Milky Way.

Their self-exile was necessary, as they moved to escape the ever increasing deadly Nova-Bomb technology that has plagued the old world. Prior to their departure, it was estimated that the old world had lost up to a 10th of it&#39;s population in the first six months of the invention of Nova-Bomb tech.
