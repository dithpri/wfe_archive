Elland is a united region.

Elland was created April 6th, 2016 by the nation of Clarkell. The aspects as such follow the precedence of excessive political freedom which Clarkell endorses.

Nations in Elland vote on topics as an absolute democracy. If a single, uninamous conclusion cannot be reached, no action will taken. 

All forms of government may be a part of the region. Because the region promotes political equality and freedom of all nations, they are free to treat citizens as they please.
