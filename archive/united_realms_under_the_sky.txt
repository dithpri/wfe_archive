-Want to engage in politics? Send a telegram to the minister of elections to get your name put into the next election poll!

-Want to make our region look better? Telegram your ideas to the minister of appearance!

-Want to add more regional activities? Telegram your ideas to the minister of recreation!

-Want to start a war? Telegram the minister of war to join the army!

-Want to simply have fun? No worries! Have fun chatting away in the message board!
