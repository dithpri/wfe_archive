Darmanassus, an archipelago, home to few nations. It&amp;#39;s rough and rugged terrain beckons few to live here. The discovery of these islands was by Russian explorer, Adrian Sergeyevich in 1804. The group of islands is in the central Pacific Ocean, south of the Marshall Islands. The Largest nation in this island chain is Khazmodani, and it dominates 9 of the 15 islands.
