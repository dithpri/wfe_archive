Die XII Mensis Septembris Anno nostrae Salutis MMXX, serenissima Altitudo Magnus Dux Magni Ducati Larii Foedus Christianarum National condidit, ut omnis in Regionibus Orbis Terrarum Regnum sociale instauraretur Domini Nostri Jesu Christi. A.M.D.G.

On September 12th A.D. 2020, His Serene Highness the Grand Duke of Magnus Ducatus Larii founded the Alliance of Christian Nations (Foedus Christianarum Nationum), so that the social Kingdom of our Lord Jesus Christ could be established all around the world. A.M.D.G.
