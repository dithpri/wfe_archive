The League of Nordic Fascists (LNF) is a political alliance of fascist, monarchist and national-syndicalist movements across Northern Europe. Founded under the influence of the Kingdom of Sweden&#150;Denmark, the organization promotes Nordic unity, corporatism, anti-communism and authoritarian governance. Member movements from Norway, Finland, Estonia and Latvia cooperate through political congresses, propaganda campaigns and paramilitary training programs. The ideological center of the LNF is located in Gothenburg.
