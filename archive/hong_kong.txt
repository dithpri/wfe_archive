Did You Know?:

For decades, Hong Kong had the third largest motion picture industry in the world (after India and the United States) and was the second largest exporter of cinema (after the United States).

Internationally famous films from Hong Kong include Jackie Chan&amp;#39;s [i]Police Story[/i] series, the action films of director John Woo and actor Chow Yun Fat (such as [i]A Better Tomorrow[/i] and [i]Hard Boiled[/i]), and the comedy films of Stephen Chow (such as [i]Shaolin Soccer[/i] and [i]Kung Fu Hustle[/i]).
