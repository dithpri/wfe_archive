Ultimate Pax Brainatronnicana (commonly known as Brainatronnicana) is the universe Brainatron is located in. The capital in the planet Brainatron is the 4th planet from the star, Cerebrum Centrum. Because of space raids and astro barbarians, Brainatronnicana itself is surrounded by a protective mint force-shield activated and deactivated by the armed forces to allow travelers and trade to go through the cosmos safely. Other than maximum securing armed forces, Brainatronnica is rich in culture, music, literature, television, cuisine, art, fashion, and even technology.
