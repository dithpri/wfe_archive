The North West Archipelago is a huge chain of islands in the Great Sea just North West of the Great Continent. Most countries here are of course island countries ruled by hereditary absolute monarchs.

Following new discussions in the Region, there are two optional criteria nations in the region can adopt:
1) A standard Currency: the Munny
2) A standard National Animal: the Dragon

North Western Council Position and Holders:
1) Chancellor: King Lucas VII of Lemuria Islands
2) Chamberlain: King Lewis V of Panggaea
3) Treasurer: King Samuel I of The Fens
4) Steward: King Edward II of Yarra
5) Constable: King Harold VI of Bourke
6) Marshal: King William VI of Moonee Ponds
7) Admiral: King William VII of Lilydale
