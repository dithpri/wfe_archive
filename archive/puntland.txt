[b][i]Puntland[/i][/b], is a region in northeastern Somalia. Its leaders declared the territory an autonomous state in 1998.

The name &amp;quot;Puntland&amp;quot; is derived from the Land of Punt mentioned by ancient Egyptian sources. The exact location of the fabled territory is still a mystery. Many studies suggest that the Land of Punt was located in Somalia,whereas others propose that it was situated elsewhere.

Following the outbreak of the civil war in 1991 a significant problem with piracy in the waters off of the coast of Somalia began to emerge. Piracy arose as a response by local fishermen to illegal fishing by foreign trawlers.
