[b][i]Al-Madain[/i][/b] (&quot;the Cities&quot;) is a great eastern imperial capital. The residential and commercial sectors of the metropolis are punctuated by splendid gardens, monuments, and palaces.

The principal districts are [i]al-Madina al-&#39;Atiqa[/i] (royal residence), [i]Aspanbar[/i] (cultural complex), [i]Behrasir[/i] (business center), [i]Rumiya[/i] (refugee settlement), and [i]Sabat[/i] (military cantonment).
