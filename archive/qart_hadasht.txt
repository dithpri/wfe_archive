[b][i]Qart Hadasht[/b][/i] or [b]Carthage[/b], the &quot;New City&quot; founded by enterprising Canaanites from [i]Sur[/i] ([b]Tyre[/b]), is the capital of the [b][i][color=purple]Safot Softim bi Qart-hadasht[/color][/i][/b], a great civilization of Canaanite city-states throughout northern Africa, southern Spain, and most of the western Mediterranean.
