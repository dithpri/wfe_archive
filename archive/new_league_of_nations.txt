We the peoples of The New League of Nations, who are determined:

&#149; to promote social progress and better standards of life in larger freedom,

&#149; to unite our strength to maintain international peace and security,

&#149; to ensure, by the acceptance of principles and the institution of methods that armed force shall not be used, save in the common interest,

&#149; to employ international machinery for the promotion of the economic and social advancement of all peoples and nations,

Have resolved to combine our efforts to accomplish these aims.

Accordingly, our respective Governments, through representatives, have agreed to present this Covenant and do hereby re-establish the international organization known as the &quot;New League of Nations&quot;
