[b]I. The Silver Guard is a group united with the common goal of protecting the regions and preserving their culture. 

II. The Silver Guard is autonomous and is only governed by the General of the Silver Guard. 

III. The Silver Guard is apolitical.

IV. The Silver Guard does not interact with the natives of the regions it protects.

V. The Silver Guard has only one voice and this is that of the General of the Silver Guard. 

VI. The Silver Guard can work in regions with an active and stable government, however it only communicates with the head of government of that region.[/b]
