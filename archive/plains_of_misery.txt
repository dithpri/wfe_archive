A vast, arid landscape dotted by several (mostly) industrious nations, which formerly went by the name H&amp;#39;millia. Most flora died out during the great drought of 1765, during which the regional population had collapsed by at least 65%. Since then, the people of the Plains have made a great recovery with Grecinder and Tokkyland being great industrial power houses in the region, two nations certainly not to be trifled with.

Kynarth, the current seat of power in the Plains of Misery is central to the land, its forests dwindling due to the toxic waste seeping across Grecinder&amp;#39;s borders.
