Darkin is a large planet much like Earth but far away from the sun. Heat and Light comes from a star named Arendephis, but it is not much. The highest temperature ever recorded was 78 degrees Fahrenheit. There are eleven continents, 5 stand alone while 6 are connected in groups of 3. There are 7 oceans on Darkin, one of which is named Meer. The land and environment varies from area to area, but all of it is habitable.
