The Empire of Triphoria currently stands in the age of 10076 T.Y., and is led by seven nations, Emerald (Hyrule), Ruby (Azkalon), Sapphire (Varuna), Diamond (Taranis), Golden (Al-Sira), Silver (Alasia) and Crystal (Issthal).

The Empire is in the midst of a brutal civil war with itself. Crystal stands against the nations of Emerald, Ruby, Sapphire, and Diamond. Golden and Silver are respectively neutral at this time.
