The Empire of Triphoria currently stands in the age of 10076 T.Y., and is led by seven nations, Emerald (Hyrule), Ruby (Azkalon), Sapphire (Varuna), Diamond (Taranis), Golden (Al-Sira), Silver (Alasia) and Crystal (Issthal). The seven nations work together to continue to lead the empire to ensure its survival and continued prosperity. The Empire is democratic to a point, all nations and types of governments are welcome.
