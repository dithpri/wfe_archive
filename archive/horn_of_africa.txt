 The [b][i]Horn of Africa[/i][/b] is a peninsula in Northeast Africa. It juts hundreds of kilometers into the Arabian Sea and lies along the southern side of the Gulf of Aden. The area is the easternmost projection of the African continent. 

Horn of Africa denotes the region containing the countries of Eritrea, Djibouti, Ethiopia and Somalia, Kenya, Uganda, Sudan, South Sudan
