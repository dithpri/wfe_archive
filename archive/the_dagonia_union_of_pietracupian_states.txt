Welcome to the Dagonia Union of Pietracupian Sates! Otherwise known as DUPS

The Dagonia Union of Pietracupian States is a tightly knit region, with each nation representing provinces of the greater realm more than independant nations. Each nation, otherwise known as Prefexs must comply with all orders given by the capital Prefex [nation]Pietracupa[/nation]. Failure to do so will result in punishment, expulsion and elimination.

All new nations must endorse the capital Prefex, [nation]Pietracupa[/nation].
