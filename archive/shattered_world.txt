Decades ago,a cataclysmic event shook Earth to its very core.The Human species which had dominated the planet for centuries were sent into chaos.Nations collapsed and within a split second,Humanity was brought back to the Neolithic Era.Now,tiny tribes have given rise to powerful nation states.Some try to replicate the Old World,others try to reform it.The nations that inhabit this region try to reform the ideals and beliefs of the Old World to problems that plague the New World.
