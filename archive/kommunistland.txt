Kommunistland is a union of communist, Germanic-speaking nations. It has 4 official languages: Numolandic, Dutch, German, and Kroppe-Shtompish, as well as regional languages spoken by its member nations. Although all member nations of Kommunistland have communist/socialist ideologies, not all of them are republics. 2 of them, [nation]Chuppa Gluppa[/nation] and [nation]Sankt Jorgen[/nation], are actually constitutional monarchies.
