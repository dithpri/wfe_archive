[region]The Empire of Munificence[/region] is a collection of sovereign micro-nations that have wilfully accepted the rule of King Noah I.

The Empire of Munificence was founded by King Noah I, ruler of [nation]Munificence[/nation] and patrilineal descendent of Niall of the Nine and descendent of the Lease and Wood clans. The king has asserted his sovereign right to rule after a lifetime of distress and hardships brought upon him and the common man around him. He founded Munificence with the goal of providing a better and brighter future for all mankind. This nation exists as all nations should, to serve its&#39; people. To provide for them the means with which they can use to provide for themselves.
