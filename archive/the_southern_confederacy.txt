We, the People of the South, in Convention assembled do declare and ordain, and it is hereby declared and ordained, That the Ordinance adopted by us in Convention, on the 4th day of July in the year of our Lord Two Thousand and Eighteen, whereby the Constitution of the United States of America was ratified, and also all Acts and parts of Acts of the General Assembly of this State, ratifying amendment of the said Constitution, are here by repealed; and that the union now subsisting between this state and and other States, under the name of &#147;The United States of America,&#148; is hereby dissolved
