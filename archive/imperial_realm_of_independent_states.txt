The IRIS is a collection of States united under, and allied to [nation]Libritarian-Socalist Sol[/nation]. 

Formed following the liberation of [nation]Terra Dei Segni[/nation] from foreign colonial rule, the integration of the [nation]Minith Myr[/nation] Tribes, and the settling of [nation]The Dark Fenlands[/nation]. 
IRIS stands united against any who may do its people harm. 

IRIS is responsible for managing, distributing, and focusing all resources related to inter-alliance matters.
