After the unexplained collapse of the United Nations over Antarctica, the territory fell into dismay. Mass rioting and violence spread throughout the region. Unwilling to continue accepting refugees from around the region, the people of Arestes packed up everything they owned, and with the protection of their mighty fleets; they set out into the world seeking a new home.

After weeks of searching, the people of Arestes found exactly what they were looking for. A massive island that had been previously not been mapped by any known faction. Within 2 months time, all cities had been transferred from Antarctica, and every-day life had resumed.
