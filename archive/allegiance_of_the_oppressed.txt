The world is bleeding, and every time its wounds scab, the forces of evil plunge a longer blade even deeper. From the colonialist carving of the great Sagahri continent to the neo-imperial torment of Rierra. Between settler colonies, puppet tyrants, corporate land-grabs, imperial invasions, cataclysmic rain of bombs - the demonic alchemical conversion of blood and souls into money and fame - the mass extraction of wealth from the global south continues rearing its unsightly wart-covered misshapen blood-stained horned head.
