[b]The Coalition of Federalist States[/b] is a region dedicated to preserving and projecting the principles of federalist representation and governance. The CFS is dedicated to: 

1. Prioritizing the sovereignty of its members 
2. Promoting small government policies and frameworks 
3. Empowering individual citizens through robust economic and political freedoms 
4. Advancing the world economy through peace and mutually-beneficial interconnectedness
