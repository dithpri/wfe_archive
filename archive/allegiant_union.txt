[b]Allegiant Union[/b]
The Allegiant Union is an intergovernmental organization that aims to maintain international peace and security, develop friendly relations among nations, achieve international cooperation, and be a centre for harmonizing the actions of the 6 founding nations and 6 member states. The AU is headquartered on international territory in Chatsu, with its other main offices in Lafayette (Kuremaya), Thurne (Achoda), Anioro (Rhuia), and Franklin (The Saint Mary).
