North America is a continent entirely within the Northern Hemisphere and almost all within the Western Hemisphere. It can also be described as a northern subcontinent of the Americas, or America, in models that use fewer than seven continents

Area: 9.54 million mi&sup2;

Population: 579 million (2016)

Languages: English, Spanish, French, and many others

Dependencies: 23 non-sovereign territories


WHAT IS CONSIDERED NORTH AMERICA?

In contrast to the common definition of &quot;North America&quot;, which encompasses the whole continent, the term &quot;North America&quot; is sometimes used to refer only to Mexico, Canada, the United States, and Greenland.
