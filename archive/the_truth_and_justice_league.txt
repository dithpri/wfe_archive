The Truth And Justice League seeks only the purest nations. The sovereign nation of Magillaville will be the sole ruler to make sure everyones conduct remains worthy of admission. Nations in The Truth And Justice League never will attack another member nation or any nation without being  attacked first and then all member nations must participate in the defense of a member nation. If a member nation attacks first, that nation will be banned from The Truth And Justice League. Nations in The Truth And Justice League must maintain at least an above average ranking in both Civil Rights and Political Freedoms to be members of the region.
