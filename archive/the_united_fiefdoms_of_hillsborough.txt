A region of war torn countries held together for peace 

1776: The United States of America was founded, with Hillsborough inside.

2000: Hillsborough begins growth into a modern suburban paradise.

2030: Hillsborough grows in population and is combined with the Townships of Montgomery and Bridgewater.

2050: The United States collapses and chaos envelopes the entire nation.

2070: The fiefdoms are created in the American Northeast for the purpose of remaking the old USA

2071: The Martial Dictatorship of America takes control of the former United States, leaving the Fiefdoms untouched due to a nuclear threat.

2076: The Martial Dictatorship of America decides to join The United Fiefdoms of Hillsborough to avoid nuclear war.
