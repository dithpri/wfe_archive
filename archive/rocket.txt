[b]Rocket[/b] is the [b]debut mixtape[/b] by American singer Dominic Fike. It was released on August 22, 2025. It features previously fan favorite unreleased songs &#147;Epilogue&#148; and &#147;One Glass&#148;. Alongside scraps from the past few years (2021-2023), &#147;Sandman&#148; and &#147;Still Feel It&#148;. The mixtape is named after Fike&#39;s son of the same name.
