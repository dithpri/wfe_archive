The civil conflict within the USA and the subsequent Third World War brought about the end of the uni-polar order and a partition of North America into roughly half a dozen new nation-states and a similar number of new protectorate territories of Russia, China and Mexico. The continent has suffered the loss of tens of millions of human lives, as well as the destruction of much of its industry and power grid. Lost satellites and space junk still regularly plummet to earth, causing wildfires, and significant portions of former farmland have been rendered unviable due to radioactive fallout. Civilization struggles to rebuild itself on a fallen continent...
