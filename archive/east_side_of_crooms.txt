This is the official region of Crooms Academy of Information Technology. 

Crooms Academy of Information Technology, located in the city of Sanford,
Florida, is Seminole County Public School&#146;s only dedicated magnet high school.
All students who reside in Seminole County and have successfully completed
the 8th grade may apply for admission. Students must submit the High School
Magnet Program Application through the SCPS Choices Department and be
chosen in the random selection process. The size of the campus allows for the
capacity of approximately 600 students. The composition of the student
population is 54% White, 25% Hispanic, 13% African American, 2% Multiracial,
5% Asian, 0.5% American Indian, and 0.5% Pacific Islander.
