Welcome to Veilbreaker, a world forged in the ashes of the old world.
Centuries ago, humanity lived in control of the globe, fighting with each other for petty territory disputes and ideologies. Yet, one day, the veil between the Mortal Realm and the Realm of Souls was shattered, unleashing hell on earth. 
The Human Kingdoms fell, their empires being burned down, and all they had built being reduced to a memory. 
The dispatches down here are the overview of the world and the rules!
