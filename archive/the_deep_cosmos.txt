After the Free European Empire was created in the aftermath of Earth&#39;s final world war. The Empire went on to colonize other worlds and purge all non human life from those worlds. The Empire became the first universal empire after millions of years of conquest, that is until it happened upon its old communist enemy from the final world war that managed to eek out an existence in a far away galaxy. The two intergalactic empires have been in a deadlock of fighting for years after first contact with neither side getting the upper hand.
