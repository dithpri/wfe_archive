[b]Maharashtra[/b] (Marathi: [b]&amp;#2350;&amp;#2361;&amp;#2366;&amp;#2352;&amp;#2366;&amp;#2359;&amp;#2381;&amp;#2335;&amp;#2381;&amp;#2352;[/b]) is a state in the western region of India and is India&#39;s third-largest state by area and is also the world&#39;s second-most populous sub-national entity. It has over 112 million inhabitants and its capital, Mumbai, has a population of approximately 18 million. Nagpur is Maharashtra&#39;s second capital as well as its winter capital. Maharashtra&#39;s business opportunities along with its potential to offer a higher standard of living attract migrants from all over India.
