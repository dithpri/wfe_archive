The Midwestern Democratic States is located on the continent of Amasia. The other regions to the east and west are currently uninhabitable due to a nuclear war a very long time ago. It is in a future Earth many million years after the present. The MDS is based on Grand Island at the mouth of the Platte River. All member nations contribute security forces to help protect the delegates to the regional government. The MDS was created by Husker Nation and New Aksarben in 42 AGR(After Grand Revolution) after the Fall of Aksarben City and total collapse of the old Aksarben Empire. It is comprised of all the nations of Amasia. Recently, reports of the old monarchy still existing have come from the unclaimed western mountains.
