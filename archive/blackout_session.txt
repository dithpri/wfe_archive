Death.  That inevitable, all-consuming summary of our lives; that which we struggle constantly to avoid; the almighty, unconquerable Beast of legend.

It lives here... chained in the bowels of a maximum security Ginger Lordsian prison, barred from Blackout Session by the superior power of the mighty Ginger.  It is here and here alone that the citizens of allied nations might find eternal life... in the undying city of Menzoberranzan.
