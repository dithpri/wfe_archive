[b]The coastal town of Wilhelmshaven is in Lower Saxony, a State of the [color=#0000CC]Federal Republic of Germany[/color]

Wilhelmshaven was previously controlled by [color=red]Communist[/color] [nation=short]Die Rote Fahne[/nation] of [region]The Red Fleet[/region] in an act of [color=red]Red Terror[/color] against the Germans.

The [region]Greater European Federation[/region] dispatched [nation=short]GEF Expeditionary Force 11[/nation] to Wilhelmshaven to prevent the spread of rampant [color=red]Socialism[/color] and [color=red]Communism[/color], and defend the [color=#0000CC]Federal Parliamentary Republic[/color] against all threats.[/b]
