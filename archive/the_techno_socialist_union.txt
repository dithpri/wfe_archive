Welcome to The Techno-Socialist Union!

The Techno-Socialist Union is home for any nation which views scientific advancement and socialism as the core ingredients for a prosperous society. The purpose for the formation of this union is for the political, social, economic, and scientific co-operation among nations which uphold the aforementioned views. Nations which have not yet officially adopted techno-socialism are also accepted, however only on the condition that said nation is actively and enthusiastically seeking to adopt it as their official ideology.
