[B]AEH[/B] stands for [B]Alternate Earth History[/B].

It is a [B]Serious, Realism Based, Modern Tech Role Play Region[/B] based on the fact that the [B]Eastern Roman or Byzantine Empire[/B] never fell.

This is our common starting point in history.

Before [B]The Great Plague of 1385[/B], all history is as it is in [B]Real Life[/B].

After [B]The Great Plague of 1385[/B], it is what we make of it.

If that interests you, check out our rules, fill out our simple application form, and when you are approved, have fun!
