This region was born from the vision of a union&#151;a union founded on the principles of [i]peace, equality, and unity[/i]. Established by an alliance of five nations, all staunch defenders of human rights, equality, and peace, this union evolved. Eventually, one of these founding nations took the initiative to establish a region dedicated to preserving the union&#39;s ideals.

On December 18, 2024, this region, named [b][region]Greater Kamchata[/region][/b], was founded to uphold and promote the values that the union holds dear.
