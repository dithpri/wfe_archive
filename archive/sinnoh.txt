The Sinnoh region (Japanese: &amp;#12471;&amp;#12531;&amp;#12458;&amp;#12454;&amp;#22320;&amp;#26041; Sinnoh-chih&amp;#333;) is a region of the Pok&eacute;mon world. It is located north of Kanto, Johto, and Hoenn. It is the setting of Pok&eacute;mon Diamond, Pearl, Platinum, Brilliant Diamond, Shining Pearl, and Legends: Arceus. It was the fourth core series region to be introduced.
