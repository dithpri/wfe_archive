The Infinite Empire is a collection of nations spanning numerous planets, the various nations present have varying levels of autonomy of the central government. There are considered to be 3 classifications of states present in the Infinite Empire.
[list] 
[*] Imperial States
[*] Imperial Protectorates 
[*] Imperial Client States 
[/list]
