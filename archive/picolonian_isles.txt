The Picolonian Isles is a vast archipelago, dominated by the largest island of Elandia which host the majority of the population. The archipelago hosts rich landscapes and biomes from the proud snowcapped peaks of Pictland to the sweltering humid rainforests of the Bajores Arc. 

 United in the Holy Empire of Picolonia, these isles have an interconnected economy and infastructure protected by a large Navy and Armed Forces.
