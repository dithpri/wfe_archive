Fraline Union. A union which was established for making pro-democracy and pro-communism unite. Founded by the Commonwealth of Frosika. The earliest nations that joined the union was Frosika, Rosidalsia, Reshtania as pro-democracy nations and Grusinasia and Kitametam Island as pro-communism nations. Since the establishment, the five earliest nation in Fraline Union unite with the slogan &amp;quot;1 Vision, 1 Mission&amp;quot;.
