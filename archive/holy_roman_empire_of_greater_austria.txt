The Holy Roman Empire of Greater Austria (HRE) is the collection of states and territories ruled directly by the Austrian Emperor, who reigns as Emperor and Autocrat of all his dominions.

Government Type: Absolute Monarchy

Head of State: HI&amp;amp;ARM Charles X, Holy Roman Emperor

Head of Government: HE Francis Wittelsbach, Imperial Arch Chancellor

Legislature: Imperial Senate
Upper House: Imperial Council
Lower House: Imperial Diet

Imperial Capital: Vienna

Motto: Austria est imperio optime unita

States: 19 Empires (plus 1 diplomatic territory, [nation]The Croxian Embassy[/nation])

Population: 38,751,000,000 (including [nation]The Croxian Embassy[/nation]) as of 18th April 2014 (next census 18th July 2014)
