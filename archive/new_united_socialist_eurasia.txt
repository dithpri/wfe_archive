The [region]New United Socialist Eurasia[/region] is the result of a war on the Eurasian continent. After communist rebels captured the European continent, left-wing tensions increased in Asia. With the help of the new military forces from Europe, they also won. After the unification of Europe and Asia, the [region]New United Socialist Eurasia[/region] was founded.

The new continent is split into some nations, but all of them help each other.
