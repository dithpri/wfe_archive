FACTS
Former Administrative District of [region=Northwest Territories]
Created 1876 Oct 07 (Keewatin Act) from a portion of Northwest Territories
Territory was returned to Northwest Territories (1905)
1905-1999 was one of the Districts of [region=Northwest Territories].
It is now parts of Manitoba, northwestern Ontario and southern [region=Nunavut].
Much of Keewatin is now Kivalliq Region of Nunavut.
Kivalliq includes much of the Islands in Hudson and James Bays.
