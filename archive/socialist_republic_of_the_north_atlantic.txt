The Socialist Republic of the North Atlantic, also known as the land of Human Rights violations, ( Not really. ) is a chain of over 100 islands, small and large, in the North Atlantic Ocean. The Supreme Socialist, is the Dictatorship of Kazahn, while the Deputy Supreme Socialist, is the Empire of Bomania.

Capital : Johnstown, Kazahn
Regional Animal : Weimaraner
Regional Anthem : Hail the Socialists
