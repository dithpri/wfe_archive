The [color=orange]Niobian Archipelago[/color] is an [color=teal][b]RP[/b][/color] focused Region. It is a group of varying-size islands with a whole range of countries, climates, and anything else when it comes to nations. All government types are welcome.

[B][color=green][u]Rules[/u][/color][/B] (under construction)

1. [color=red]Puppets[/color] will generally be frowned upon, but as long as they are not voting in the polls they are allowed.

2. [color=blue]All nations[/color] who haven&#39;t broken a rule will be given authority to call [color=purple]polls[/color], if you don&#39;t have this power, contact someone within administration, [color=red]Puppets[/color] are not given this right.

3. All [color=purple]Polls[/color] must include an option to [color=crimson]nullify[/color] the poll.

[color=red][i]INVASIONS WILL NOT BE TOLERATED[/i][/color]
