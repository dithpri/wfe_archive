ICA is a supreme international governing body and an intergovernmental organization that aims to maintain international peace and security, develop friendly relations among nations, achieve international cooperation, and be a centre for harmonizing the actions of nations. It prevents conflicts among military superpowers, provide relief aids to marginalized sections of the society and create a ideal world without hardships like, hunger, starvation and poverty. The ICA is an Inter-galactic outsourcing and insourcing communication council, connecting different regions and countries to make the cosmos a better place to live in.
