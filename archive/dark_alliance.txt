Blessed are the ignorant, for they shall be shown the light.

Blessed are the unseen, for they are the prophets.

Blessed are the passed, for they understand.

Blessed are the sinners, for they have reached understanding.

Blessed are the innocent, for they shall be the first.

Blessed are the unworthy, for they are children to be taught.

Blessed are the suffering, for they are learning.

Blessed are the heretics, for they shall be eaten last.

Blessed are the followers, for they shall be eaten first.
