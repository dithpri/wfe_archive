[b][color=navy]Mundus Romana[/color] [color=darkred](MR)[/color][/b] consists of all nations located in Europe, the Middle East, and North Africa.

The STORY of this region is set within a slightly alternate 300 A.D. where Rome is weak, but not quite weak, and nation-states are seeing their rise much sooner than expected..

The ROLEPLAY of this region is part of the multi-region Mundus Romana universe.
