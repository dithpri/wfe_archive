The Region of Koyamura is an absolutely titanic island within the Northern Seas, centered around a mountain range in a crescent shape. 
Usually only reaching 20 Degrees Celsius and dropping to -9 Degrees Celsius within the coldest months, Boreal forests litter the outer edges of the Island, and crop growth is rather small.
