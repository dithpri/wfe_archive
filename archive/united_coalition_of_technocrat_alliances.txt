A region dedicated to the alliances and development of technologically advanced nations, the United Coalition of Technocrat Alliances is focused upon the interactions of technologically advanced comrades in order to keep our economies, people, and territories safe as we work together for a brighter future!

We are not only a united front of technologically advanced nations and economic powerhouses, we are also a heavily military based nation (both in and out of RP) as we climb the ranks of the great and mighty militaries of our time.
