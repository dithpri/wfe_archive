Refugees from the Russian zombie apocalypse founded a new nation in South Sultra, a collection of islands and mountains in the Atlantic ocean. It was named in honor of their homeland, Chernarus.
Several decades later, the groups banded together to create the country of Applejaxc, at the heart of South Sultra.

What countries have taken claim to surrounding islands and mountains?

---

Countries of militaristic power rivaling that of Applejaxc or who threaten Applejaxc citizens will be sanctioned, removed, and ultimately annexed by Applejaxc. The Applejaxc law of disproportionate retaliation requires immediate, violent, and effective actions be taken in response to any offense.
