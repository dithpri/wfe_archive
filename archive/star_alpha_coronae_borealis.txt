Alphecca, also designated as &amp;#945; Coronae Borealis (alpha Coronae Borealis), is a variable hypergiant star of spectral class A0 in the constellation of Corona Borealis. Alphecca visual magnitude is 2.23, making it the 65th brightest star in the sky. Because of its brightness, Alphecca is clearly visible to the naked eye when observed from locations with dark skies, and should be also quite easily visible from light polluted areas.
