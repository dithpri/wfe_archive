Planet XEA-613-D is a giant under-developed planet known for its harsh winter climate, large populations of predatory animals and abundant toxic precipitation. 

The ancient colonizers of a long-vanished civilization were formerly known as the &quot;Northern Defence Forces&quot; - having gained a foothold on the northern part of the planet, people and local primitive tribes agreed on a mutual assistance agreement among intelligent life, which allowed this fragile alliance to develop into a powerful empire and unite under the banner of the Eye, fighting off hordes of undead and predatory creatures, experiencing traumatic shocks and epidemics.

These lands are still far from being completely conquered, and the story is far from over...
