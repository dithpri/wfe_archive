Set in the late 2020&#39;s, the increasing economic disparity of the world has grown past the point of ignoring it. Marxist ideology has gotten popular again, and the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics reunites. Along with a few other socialist states that were not apart of the original USSR. 


With almost half the world now under socialist control, the capitalist hegemonic rule in the west feels threatened, and clamps down on its own citizens political and civil freedoms out of fear that they will see how capitalism gas failed them.

With the two economic powers of the world at odds again, will this become another Cold War?
