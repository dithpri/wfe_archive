[b]Samoa[/b] is a country governing the western half of the Samoan Islands in the South Pacific. Previous names were German Samoa from 1900 to 1919, and Western Samoa from 1914 to 1997. It was admitted to the UN on 15 Dec 1976 as Samoa. The entire island group, inclusive of American Samoa, was known as Navigators Islands before the 20th century because of the Samoans&amp;#39; seafaring skills.
