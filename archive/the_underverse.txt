The [b]Underverse[/b] is an artificially created microverse - created by the [i]United Human Empire[/i] as an eternal paradise sanctuary for mankind.

Later, the name of the [i]United Human Empire[/i] officially became [b][nation=long]The Underverse[/nation][/b] reflecting the importance of the [b]Underverse[/b] as a new, eternal and secure homestead for the human species.
