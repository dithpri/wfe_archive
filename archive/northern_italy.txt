The northern part of the Italian peninsula bordering the Italian alps. In 1931 former president of the republic of Limo, Giovanni Bianelli founded the United Republics Of Northern Italy or for short U.R.O.N.I. The U.R.O.N.I was founded after the war between the Northern Italians nations to unite the nine countries together as allies. Northern Italy is home to many beautiful and historical landmarks with many diverse cultures and languages throughout the region.
