The Grand Imperial Realms of Austronesia comprised the dominions, colonies, protectorates, mandates and other territories ruled or administered by the Imperial State of Sancta Roguena. It originated with the overseas colonies and trading posts established by Sancta Roguena in the late 16th and early 17th centuries. At its present day, it is the largest empire in history and, for quite a while now, it is a foremost global power. it is often said that &amp;quot;the sun never sets on the British Empire&amp;quot; because its span across the globe ensured that the sun was always shining on at least one of its numerous territories.
