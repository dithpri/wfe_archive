A blissful archipelago that is also sometimes consumed by war. Big Peace Island stands at the center of this sea, a beacon of hope among the hopeless failing nations that surround it. Will the island powers manage to claim Big Peace Island and the clout that comes with it? Will they bomb each other into oblivion? Will they manage to somehow coexist through fragile networks of trade from their crippled leftist economies? Tune in next week to find out!
