[color=red]Welcome to the Revolutionary Republic of Communista![/color]

Communista is a prosperous and cultured region, filled with people from all backgrounds, be they black or white, Gay or Straight, struggling or prosperous.

The region promotes the unification of all nations through mutual trade, defence, and the betterment of life for its citizens and workers. Though Unity and co-operation, this region hopes to improves the lives of all within its borders and, in time, those outside of them.
