The United Confederacy of Sutoan is a fairly small region. All the Nations in the region have good relations with each other and all Nations get a say in the regional happenings. The Region is a mostly democratic and friendly region, despite the fact not all nations are democracies themselves. 
The Nation have a sizable military and do not take kindly to threats. There are five founding nations, The Federation of Krytonus, The United States of Omegica, The Republic of Luparc, The Free Land of The Obsidinian Territories and the Dictatorship of Plouhinec. Establisment Week, held on the week including the Fifth of March is a region-wide celebration.
