The Confederacy of Independent Nations is a coalition of technologically forward nations that strive for independence within their own rights but fall under the support and protection of their peers.

Established in 2151, the Confederacy reunited the many nations of the Earth after it was visited by a group of alien beings, who had got wind of the first faster-than-light (FTL) engine being tested by a member of [nation]Praelium[/nation] - the world power at the time.
