The Joint Operations Space Command, better known as the JOSC. Was founded by the merging of the FSC (Falconmont Space Command) and the JSC (Jereathiel Space Command). And has recently been expanding to include other like minded civilizations. The primary roles of the JOSC is exploration, colonization, and defense of the Alliances vast and ever expanding territory. The JOSC pride themselves in their technological supremacy, and professional attitude, of the millions of men and women who proudly wear the uniform. The majority of the navy&#39;s fleets operate under a rapid response doctrine, ready at any moment to rescue a stranded ship, or break a blockade, and provide support to a planet under siege.
