[color=blue][b]The Commonwealth of Dalton[/b][/color]
========================

Is a region dedicated to the facilitation of a fun simulation environment for the Constitution Club of The Dalton School. However, members from outside the Constitution Club [i](In The Dalton School)[/i] may also join.

Many members have been elected as representatives of their particular sects. Every month or so, new representatives will be elected.
[i]The next elections are planned for [b]February 13th, 2016[/b][/i]
[i]As always, have a great time![/i]
