[u][b]Beginners Guide to Auctions:[/b][/u]

&amp;#128221; [b]An Item is listed[/b]
A seller offers something for sale (eg. a Card) The auction platform describes the item and may set rules like a starting bid or reserve price.

&amp;#128587;&amp;#8205;&amp;#9794;&amp;#65039;[b]Bidding opens[/b]
Participants place bids, each higher than the previous one. As competition increases, Bidders continue raising offers. The auction system announces the current highest bid.

&amp;#127942; [b]Auction closes[/b]
When the alloted time expires, the highest bidder wins. The buyer receives the item and the seller receives payment minus any platform commission.

&amp;#10071;&amp;#65039;[b]Important[/b]
Not winning an item does not make you any less of a man. There is no need to embarass yourself by firing off butthurt telegrams.
