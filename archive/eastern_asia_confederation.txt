Eastern Asia is the eastern subregion of the Asian continent. The Eastern Asian economy is one of the most successful regional economies in the world. The population of Eastern Asia is 1,575,784,500 and growing, making the region one of the world&amp;#39;s most populated places. Although nations in East Asia had conflict with each other in history; they have put down their past disputes and arguments and allied as the Eastern Asia Confederation (EAC) to strengthen each other. Members (nations) of the Eastern Asia Confederation meet together in the annual Universal Assembly to make each nation strong and help other nations when requested.
