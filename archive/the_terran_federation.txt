The Terran Federation is a coalition of National Earthen Governments, founded with the intention to unite together in order to preserve peace, tranquility, and welfare of all regional participants, and to ensure a better standard of living and presence in global life.

Economic and Political positions of applicants is currently required in order to ensure a long-term and stable presence in international government.  Any nation unable to conduct themselves in a civilized or otherwise competent manner is subject to denial or ejection.
