Also the former name of a geographical area encompassing the territories of Venezia Giulia, Istria and Dalmatia, Littoria is a rocky, remote, uncompromisingly undemocratic region whose inhabitants value the rule of law, the preservation of Latin customs and language, the Roman Catholic faith and, above all, keeping themselves in and others out. Plans for world conquest may follow at a later date. Littoria was discovered by the people of [nation]Iadera[/nation].
