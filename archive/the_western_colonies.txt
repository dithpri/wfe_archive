Welcome to The Western Colonies. We are a role playing region with our very own map, dedicated community and history.
Our world, whether you call it Terra, Lurra, Alteri or simply &quot;Earth&quot;, is rich in culture, mythology, and exciting roleplay opportunities. Nations of all shapes, sizes and themes call The Western Colonies their home and we hope many more can, too!
