This a sci-fi post-apocalyptic roleplaying region inspired by the warden world (Which everyone should go check out it&#39;s really cool and better than this dump) but takes things in its own direction. Feel free to enter but please follow the rules. Any map makers would be greatly appreciated.


[u]Rules[/u]
You gain 100 million dollars per week as base income, then add traits and weaknesses.
Each week is 2 months in RP.
You get 15 free points to spend on traits and can get more through adopting weaknesses.
Check the pins for more info!
