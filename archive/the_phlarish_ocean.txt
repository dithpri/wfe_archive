The Phlarish Ocean, a prosperous region which is an economic and political union to unify the area. Inner trade between the region generates wealth, boosting the power of the coastal cities and capitals, enriching nations.

[THE FLAG] The flag represents the ocean, the spirals between land and sea. The violet colour represents the importance of trade and prosperity brought by it. The Rose sky represents the sun in the sky, illuminating the ocean.

[THE RULES OF THE SEA] The rules of the sea dictate that members must be respectful to one another and behave responsibly.
