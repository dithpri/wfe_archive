The Sea of Light is an alliance of autonomous human nations united under a common mission of exploration, communication, and interstellar cooperation. While each nation within SoL retains control over its internal governance, they collectively adhere to the Coalition Council, which coordinates exploration and diplomatic affairs. 
[hr]
The Coalition was formed to explore and maintain contact with habitable stellar and planetary regions where sustainable colonies can thrive. Acting as a federation of pathfinders, the Sea of Light ventures into the immense stretches of uncharted space, fostering interstellar understanding and pursuing diplomatic engagement with alien civilizations.
