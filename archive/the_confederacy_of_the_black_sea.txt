-Overview-
We are a small group of nations from the Black Sea area. Includes European and Middle East influenced Nations.

-History-
The Confederacy was formed after the Civil War of the New Phoenicean Republic. The attacking side branched off after a successful, yet violent, separation, and although smaller than the defending side remains strong and vigilant. We have made peace with the defending side, also known as the Sisterhood, and work together to stay in power over smaller regions.

-Religion- 
Although the region was founded by a strictly Muslim nation, any religion is welcome.

-Politics-
The diverse political views of the nations help to influence choices to benefit everyone.
