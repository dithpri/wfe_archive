Federation of United Countries for Peace, or the FUCP, was founded when Granzys and Yugo-Austria agreed to an alliance. To join, send a telegram explaining why you want to join and how to contribute. We will then send you the password to join our nation. We require you to have a large military, and must be somewhat active. Communists, National-Socialists, Anarchists, and Theocracies are not allowed.
