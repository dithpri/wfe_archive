[b]The Cayman Islands is a self-governing British Overseas Territory, the largest by population, in the western Caribbean Sea. The 264-square-kilometre (102-square-mile) territory comprises the three islands of Grand Cayman, Cayman Brac and Little Cayman, which are located to the south of Cuba and northeast of Honduras, between Jamaica and Mexico&#39;s Yucat&aacute;n Peninsula. The capital city is George Town on Grand Cayman, which is the most populous of the three islands.[/b]
