The United Nations of Europe is in Europe.

The capital is Argentinstan City while the Parliament is located in Singaporstates.

The Secretary General acts as the leader and is elected via popular vote.

Ministers are appointed by the Secretary General and can be dismissed by the people via a vote.

The parliament works when a nation submits a proposal on the RMB and the Parliament Speaker takes notice and puts it in line for a popular vote. A proposal needs at least 3 votes to pass and become law.

Any UNOE member may be prosecuted by the Ministry for Justice.
