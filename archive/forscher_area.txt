The Forscher Area is a zone of nations who either want to create defense for their nations, or seek a path through control over large amounts of territory, WDMs are preferred for nations in this zone. If you are in this zone, it is preferred you use decides (Forscherland National Currency), Dollars, or an unique currency. Nations are allowed to choose whether they are religous or not.
