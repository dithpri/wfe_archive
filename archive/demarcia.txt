[b]Welcome to the Republic of Demarcia![/b]

The Republic of Demarcia is a sovereign country founded in 2015 as the Kingdom of Waffland with King Demarcus as its monarch. On 31 July 2016 the monarchy was abolished and the Republic of Waffland was established; on 16 June 2018 the country was renamed to the Republic of Demarcia. Demarcia is bordered by the Co-Principality of Kekistan. 

Demarcia was established as a region on NationStates on 19 November 2020. The region currently requires a password to enter and only current citizens of Demarcia (acquaintances of [nation]Virbrevia[/nation]) may join the region. The region will likely open to all potential members soon.
