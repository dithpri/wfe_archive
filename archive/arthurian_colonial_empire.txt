The [b]Arthurian Colonial Empire[/b] comprises the imperial possessions of the Arthurian Crown, ruled both directly and indirectly from Kingshaven. There are three types of imperial possession:

[list][*][b]Crown Dominions[/b]: territories with a locally-elected legislature and a high degree of autonomy.
[*][b]Crown Colonies[/b]: territories with an appointed legislature and limited autonomy.
[*][b]Crown Territories[/b]: territories ruled directly by the imperial government in the metropole.
