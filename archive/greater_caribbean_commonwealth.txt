The region was created for the prosperity of peace, education, and civil liberties.  The region of Greater Caribbean Commonwealth was developed to have a positive impact on the WA while living in a peaceful community of nations.  A nation in the Greater Caribbean Commonwealth can expect to have complete autonomy and an active role in leadership.

As the right of each sentient species to live in accordance with its normal cultural evolution is considered sacred, no nation may interfere with the normal and healthy development of alien life and culture.  A nation may not violate this Prime Directive. This directive takes precedence over any and all other considerations, and carries with it the highest moral obligation.
