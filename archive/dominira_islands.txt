The Dominira Islands have been in strife for generations, so long that the origins of the conflict have been lost to time. Over the last two hundred years one group among the many combating sides gained followers at an overwhelming rate. This group was known simply as &quot;Dominira&quot;. Within the last fifteen years, Dominira took control of the islands from the previous head of state, a deeply religious man who had come to power through rite of birth alone. For now there is an uneasy peace in the region, but there are whispers in the dark...
