A world that consists of 4 countries on one small continent.

Matta- Capitalist country. Allied with Mithris. Neutral towards Marikore and East Matta

East Matta- Socialist country slowly giving back civil rights. Enemy of Mithris and Marikore. Neutral towards Matta

Mithris- Democratic Socialist. Enemy of East Matta. Neutral towards Marikore and Matta.

Marikore- Anarchy. Enemy of East Matta. Neutral Towards Mithris and Matta.
