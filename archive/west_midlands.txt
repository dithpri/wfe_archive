[b][u]Overview of West Midlands[/u][/b]

The West Midlands is a multi-cultural, diverse region, which will allow nations on all sides of the political spectrum to join.

The nations that Join West Midlands will be contracted to join the WMB - West Midlands Bloc. The Bloc will allow participating nations to trade freely - imports and exports moving freely between these nations at no cost. As part of free-trade. The reassurance of human, civil and economical rights are paramount and will be attained.
