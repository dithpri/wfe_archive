[Spoiler=Brief History]
Randovia is the ancestral homeland of the people of [nation]New Bradfordsburg[/nation]. It was recaptured by the Radiant Armed Forces (RAF) on December 4, 2021, ending a period of occupation by maligned forces. Now, Randovia stands again as the seat of the D&amp;#7843;xlamic Empire, managing all holdings under sole dominion of the Evergreen Republic. [/spoiler]
