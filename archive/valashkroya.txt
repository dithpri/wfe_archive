The people of Valashkroya are fiercely protective of their own, prioritizing the safety and well-being of their citizens above all else. This protective nature manifests in a tightly-knit community that is quick to rally against any perceived danger, whether from external forces or rival factions. They are known for their ruthless tactics when it comes to defending their territory, employing cunning strategies and unwavering determination to eliminate any enemies that dare to encroach upon their land.
