Santa Clarita (/&amp;#716;s&aelig;nt&amp;#601; kl&amp;#601;&amp;#712;ri&amp;#720;t&amp;#601;/; Spanish for &quot;Little St. Clare&quot;) is a city in northwestern Los Angeles County, California. With a 2020 census population of 228,673, it is the third-largest city by population in Los Angeles County, and the 17th-largest in the state of California.
