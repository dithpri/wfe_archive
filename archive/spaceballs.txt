On one particularly balmy Friday night, the Flit and Dax were preparing for their normal Friday entertainment of Unhatched-Dragon-Egg Soccer, starring Valopwar, when a fresh dragon egg rolled unsteadily into the lair. Though little could be discerned from its featureless shell, the wyrms could tell that this yet-unhatched dragon yearned for conquest, for treasure, for fire and destruction. Turning to each other, the two dragons nodded grimly. [i]They had to show this egg all the destuction it would one day wreak.[/i]
