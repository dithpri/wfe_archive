The Region of Misfit Toys is a fledgling region where member nations are allowed free rein over their own governments and are allowed to live in peace as long as they play nice with their fellow nations in the region.

4/2/2013: Region founder, the Theocracy of Cthulhu Rising, proclaims that this day, henceforth and forever, will be celebrated as Zombie Survival Day in recognition of the efforts of all member nations in fending off the Zombie Apocalypse.
