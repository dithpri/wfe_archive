Pengia Terrum is located in the southern hemisphere. It is diverse geographically, ecologically and on a human scale. The intergovernmental organisation that runs the region is the Pengiua Terrum Council of Co-Operation, members elect the Council President who becomes the World Assembly Delegate. The PTCoC was established for peace and collaboration in the region and its main roles regard economics and defence. Members should make sure to read the pinned dispatches.
