Welcome to Delpishana, the only world within a world the residents know of and call home, unknown but perhaps equally glorious lands beyond the frontiers of current, comprehensive knowledge. Home to civilisations and cultures that wield bronze and iron, form dynasties and classical empires, and without any middle age to speak of, whether these brave peoples rise to the cornerstone of history forever more or lose it all to conquest, disease or disaster is left in the hands of their rulers, and the deities themselves
