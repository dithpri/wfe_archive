Silesia is a region of Central Europe now located mostly in Poland, with small parts in the Czech Republic and Germany. It has about 15,444 sq mi and almost 8,000,000 inhabitants. Silesia is located along the Odra river. It consists of Lower Silesia and Upper Silesia.

The region is rich in mineral and natural resources and includes several important industrial areas. &#151;Wikipedia[hr]
