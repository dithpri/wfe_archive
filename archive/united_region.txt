Motto: &quot;Unity through Diversity&quot;
Flag: A circle of stars symbolizing harmony.

Overview:
The United Region is a coalition of nations committed to diplomacy, trade, and mutual defense. Its members thrive on cultural exchange and collaboration.

Key Policies:

Open Borders: Free movement of people and goods.
Defense Pact: Collective security for all members.
Eco Commitment: Shared action on climate change.
Leadership:
Overseer: Prime Minister Alaria Fenn (Nova Terra)
Elected every 2 years by representatives.

Fun Fact:
Hosts an annual Culture Festival and Games to strengthen unity.
