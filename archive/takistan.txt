The Takistan Region is a fractured territorial zone that emerged following the collapse of the former Takistani state after the Darbat Civil War. Today, it is divided between competing political systems, most notably the authoritarian Emirate of Zayrabad in the east and the breakaway West Takistani authority in the mountainous west.

Despite its division, both sides claim historical legitimacy over the entire region, making Takistan a permanent flashpoint of political and military tension.
