The PNA or the Pacific Nations Alliance is an economical and military bond between various Pacific nations.

&quot;Economy is our Engine, the fuel is the cash&quot;

Established by nations that saw the corruption and incompetence of other Pacific leaders, the PNA is mainly an economical and a military pact that seeks to protect nations from the looming threat of Communism and Socialism, striving on pure economic strength, the PNA hopes for a brighter future, of Scientific advancements, a better and greener world, and Of course, Money.
