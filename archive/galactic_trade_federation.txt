[color=purple][b]~A series of intergalactic nations and empires who strive to become economic powerhouses on the galactic level~[/b][/color]

The Galactic Trade Federation (GTF) currently finds itself based in the Milky Way galaxy, but is not above expansion to other regions of space. The GTF, itself, is an intergalactic commerce guild, trade cartel, megacorporation, and lobbying group that was founded as an association of business, nations, and trade groups.
