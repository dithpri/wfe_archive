[b]Welcome to Terra Australis, home of the southernmost nations on Earth[/b]

Terra Australis was the name of a hypothesized continent appearing on European maps from the 15th to the 18th century, now the continent of Antarctica. Spotted by Russian Fabian Gottlieb von Bellingshausen, it has attracted many colonies from the rest of the world. Today, these former colonies are sovereign and proudly independent nations.

Terra Australis is the headquarters of the [i]Antarctic Alliance[/i] (AA), led by [nation]Western Antarctica[/nation].
