The United Nations of Acirema (UNA) is an intergovernmental organization to promote international co-operation. The region was established on 22 September 2016. The headquarters of the United Nations of Acirema is in the Republic of Sawdros and experiences extraterritoriality. The organization is financed by assessed and voluntary contributions from its member states. Its objectives include maintaining international peace and security, promoting human rights, fostering social and economic development, protecting the environment, and providing humanitarian aid in cases of famine, natural disaster, and armed conflict.
