[b]Staffordshire[/b] is a landlocked county in the West Midlands of England.

The largest city in Staffordshire is Stoke-on-Trent. Major towns include Stafford (the county town), Burton upon Trent, Cannock, Newcastle-under-Lyme, Leek, and Tamworth. &#151;Wikipedia[hr]
