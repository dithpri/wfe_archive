[b][u]Syncretic Union Roleplay Board[/u][/b]

This board serves as the [b]official Roleplay (RP) board of the [region]Syncretic Union[/region][/b]. Its purpose is to allow roleplay activities to remain [b]organized and independent[/b] from the main Regional Message Board.

All [b]major announcements[/b] will be posted on both the main RMB and this board. However, nations participating in roleplay are asked to [b]post their roleplay updates and developments here[/b] so that RP activity remains clear and easy to follow.

We hope that all nations taking part in roleplay will [b]enjoy the opportunities and community provided by the [region]Syncretic Union[/region][/b].
