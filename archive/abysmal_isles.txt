A Spattering of Islands congregated together by a dark, malicious force. Its Suffocating Miasma attracts cruel and power-hungry nation from the world over, whether they be Communistic Dictators, or Corrupt Capitalists.

It sometimes seems that only a dark cloud flows over these isles. The blood red moon flying over high, as the nights are longer than days; it sometimes seems as though these isles bear the mark of eternal sorrow under the ghastly darkness thrown upon them.

For the evil here is as a cacophony of blood. The orchestra of death plays here.
