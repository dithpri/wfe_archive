The [b]Alliance of Inter-Stellar Technocratic Ai-Republics ([region]AiStar[/region])[/b] is a self-governing union of Ai-dominated civilizations located within the Alpha Quadrant of the Local Galaxy.

[url=http://www.gothamloki.com/images/SISAChart1.png]AiStar hosts 51 member nationstate planets[/url] with a combined population of 375.85 billion sentient intelligences as of AiDate 1943 (RL 25 August 2017).
