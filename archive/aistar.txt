The [b]Alliance of Inter-Stellar Technocratic Ai-Republics ([region]AiStar[/region])[/b] is a self-governing union of Ai-dominated civilizations composed of [url=http://www.gothamloki.com/images/SISAChart1.png]51 member world-states[/url] having a combined population of 383.8 billion sentient intelligences as of AiDate 1968 (RL 19 Sept 2017), situated within the Turing-Eniac microcluster.
