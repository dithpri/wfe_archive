The [b]Alliance of Inter-Stellar Technocratic Ai-Republics ([region]AiStar[/region])[/b] is a self-governing federation of sentient-robotic civilizations located within the Alpha Quadrant of the Local Galaxy.

[url=http://www.gothamloki.com/images/SISAChart1.png]AiStar hosts 47 member nationstates and one prison planet[/url] with a combined population of 228.578 billion sentient-robotic intelligences (SRI) and a regional gross domestic product of SMU 32,522.016 trillion as of AiDate 206.11 (RL 11 Apr 2016).
