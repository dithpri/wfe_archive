The [b]Alliance of Inter-Stellar Technocratic Ai-Republics ([region]AiStar[/region])[/b] is a self-governing federation of sentient-robotic civilizations located within the Alpha Quadrant of the Local Galaxy.

[url=http://www.gothamloki.com/images/SISAChart1.png]AiStar hosts 50 member nationstates and one prison planet[/url] with a combined population of 326 billion sentient-robotic intelligences (SRI) as of AiDate 1780 (RL 14 Mar 2017).
