[b]Gda&amp;#324;sk[/b] is a Polish city on the Baltic coast, the capital of the Pomeranian Voivodeship, Poland&#39;s principal seaport and the center of the country&#39;s fourth largest metropolitan area.

The city lies on the southern edge of Gda&amp;#324;sk Bay (of the [region]Baltic Sea[/region]), in a conurbation with the city of Gdynia, spa town of Sopot, and suburban communities, which together form a metropolitan area called the Tricity (Tr&oacute;jmiasto), with a population approaching 1.4 million. Gda&amp;#324;sk itself has a population of 460,427 (December 2012), making it the largest city in the Pomerania region of Northern Poland. &#151;Wikipedia[hr]
