The 2nd H.T.C. League is a economic and mutual organization led by the Hansa Organization based in Szarkenburg .
Starting as a economic support for the anarchic period of Slavagorod , now it is secured by the Slavagorod government and
is declared a trade and defensive agreement between the two countries .

Several Free minor cities also joined the League recently .
This organization has a goal of bringing both nations to the previous economic output of both nations prior to their initial collapse.

The Criteria for joining :
               Must be a country in Europe or owns a region of Europe.
               Liberal , Reactionary or Revolutionaries are disallowed .
               Has to have atleast a &#39;good&#39; economy.
