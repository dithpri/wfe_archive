Lamont County is situated in central Alberta, Canada, and is part of the Edmonton Metropolitan Region. It covers approximately 2,632 square kilometers. Known for its picturesque rural landscapes, Lamont County offers a tranquil environment with rolling hills, forests, and farmland. The North Saskatchewan River flows through the region, providing opportunities for outdoor activities like fishing and hiking. Agriculture is a significant part of Lamont County&#39;s economy, with thriving crops and livestock production. Visitors enjoy exploring the Elk Island National Park, Ukrainian Cultural Heritage Village, and scenic byways. The county is poised for economic growth with diverse opportunities in agriculture, energy, and tourism.
