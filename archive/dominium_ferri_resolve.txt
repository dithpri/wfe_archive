The Dominium Ferri Resolve is an alliance of strength, knowledge, and economic prowess. Lead by The Holy Empire of Interitus, the alliance boasts a thriving and diversified economy. The Ferri Resolve Trade Network facilitates seamless trade among member nations, promoting economic growth and stability. A cornerstone of the alliance, the Dominium Ferri Resolve Defense Force (DFRDF), integrates the military capabilities of member states. This unified force ensures the security of the region, conducts joint training exercises, and engages in peacekeeping missions worldwide. Compulsory military service in many member nations ensures a steady supply of disciplined and skilled personnel.
