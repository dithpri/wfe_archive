The Greater Siscoypic Region&#39;s history has held countless autocrats and emperors, and the current day is no exception. The region tends to be incredibly isolationist, very rarely interacting with the outside world unless absolutely required. While not every nation in the region bows down to Veezistan&#39;s sovereignty, the distrust of foreigners is culturally shared between all of them. They may hate each other, but they hate others more.

Messengers who bring up the subject of a so called &quot;embassy&quot; tend to be publicly executed.
