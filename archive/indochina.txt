[b]Indochina[/b] refers to the continental portion of Southeast Asia lying east of India and roughly south or southwest of China. The historical name &quot;Indochina&quot; has its origins in the French Indochine, a combination of the names of &quot;India&quot; and &quot;China&quot;, referring to the location of the territory between those two countries.
