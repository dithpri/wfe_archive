This region specifically RPs in the Fallout universe.

[=]REQUIREMENTS TO JOIN[=]
You must be a recognized power in the Fallout wasteland (NCR, Enclave, Caesars Legion, etc.)
You CAN create your own powers. They have to be approved by the current region owner, and must have a story behind them.
Basic knowledge of Fallout lore.
You MUST NOT use NS stats.
