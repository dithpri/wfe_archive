The Confederation of Independent States stood for liberty and democracy, and worked to achieve influence and respect around the world.

Having achieved its goal, its members faded away. This region has been invaded once, and occupied twice, and yet it still stands free today. It has left its mark on the world, and will always be a place of refuge for those seeking peace and security.
