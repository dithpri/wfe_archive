All that encompass the lands of FFXIAH.  

The beleaguered citizens of the theocracy of Shangri-Lah-Dee-Dah were restless under the iron fist of their despised, tyrannical dictator. While under the cover of Darkness, four rebels, led by a former noble of Ni who defected before Ni founded their constitutional monarchy, were dispatched to assassinate the dictator with monetary aid from the Kingdom of Ni and the Borderlands Council.

With that, the Principality of Shangri-Lah-Dee-Dah ob der Ni was established with the coronation of the rebel leader. The allegiance with Ni was solidified.
