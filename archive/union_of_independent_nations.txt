In 2016, the Union of Independent Nations was founded by Internet Freedom Republic as an outpost of an offsite community. A civil war split the region between UIN and the rebel [region]Union of Autonomous Nations[/region]. As is the nature of regions, interest died out and all its players left the game one by one.

In 2019, the region was recovered by [nation]The Dark Lawn[/nation] of the UAN. The region gets forgotten about every so often but it&#39;s back in rebel hands now.
