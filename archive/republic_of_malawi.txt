Malawi, officially the [b]Republic of Malawi[/b], is a landlocked country in southeast Africa that was formerly known as Nyasaland. It is bordered by Zambia to the northwest, Tanzania to the northeast, and Mozambique on the east, south and west. The country is separated from Tanzania and Mozambique by Lake Malawi. Malawi is over 45,560 square miles with an estimated population of 16,777,547 (July 2013 est.). Its capital is Lilongwe, which is also Malawi&#39;s largest city. &#151;Wikipedia[hr]
