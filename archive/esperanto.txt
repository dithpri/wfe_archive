[b][color=#145A3A]&amp;#127807; About us[/color][/b]
The Esperanta Komunumo is a cooperative international region built around the Esperanto language, cultural exchange, and participatory governance. It brings together nations committed to linguistic diversity, democratic institutions, and regional cooperation.

New nations are welcome! Whether you are here for roleplay, governance, or language learning, the Esperanta Komunumo offers a structured but friendly environment to participate and contribute.
