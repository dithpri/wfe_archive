Overview:

Ruled by the nation of New Aerios, the Greater Aerion Empire is an empire like no other. Situated in low-altitude orbit around Earth, the Empire was formed by the Government of New Aerios in 2013. At the time, New Aerios was a relatively new but still formidable country, situated on a huge space station which seceded from the European Union in a violent conflict.
