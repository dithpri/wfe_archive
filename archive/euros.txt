[B][u]Hello and Welcome to Euros![/u][/B]

Euros is a global role-play scenario beginning in 1850 and continuing to the present day.  Each region member takes the role of a real world nation and is faced with historic issues pertaining to that nation both domestically and internationally. Our scenario seeks to experience the Age of Imperialism, the World Wars, the Cold War, and so much more!

If you would like to join please telegram [nation]Andeaux[/nation] and read the regional rules and legislation already in place. 

We hope you enjoy yourself!
