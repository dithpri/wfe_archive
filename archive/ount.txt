The Organization United Nations of Terra is an intergovernmental organization, created to promote peace, development and equality on the planet. OUNT is an intergovernmental organization and economic bloc created to promote global peace and cooperation. It was founded on the Letter of Emmet II by Notrumbia in 2019. The organization is divided into five departments: Council, Commission, Parliament, Court and Defense. Each department has autonomy and must work in perfect harmony with one another.
