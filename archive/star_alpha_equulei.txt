Kitalpha, also designated as &amp;#945; Equulei (alpha Equulei), is a hypergiant star of spectral class G0 in the constellation of Equuleus. Kitalpha visual magnitude is 3.92. Because of its moderate brightness, Kitalpha is easily visible to the naked eye from locations with dark skies, while it might be barely visible, or not visible at all, from light-polluted skies.
