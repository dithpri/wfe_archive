[b]Ukrayina[/b] (or [b]Ukraine[/b]) is a country in Eastern Europe. It has an area of 603,628 km2 (233,062 sq mi), making it the largest country entirely within Europe. Ukrayina borders Russia to the east and northeast, Belarus to the northwest, Poland, Slovakia and Hungary to the west, Romania and Moldova to the southwest, and the Black Sea and Sea of Azov to the south and southeast, respectively. The territory of Ukrayina has been inhabited for at least 44,000 years. It is a country comprised of 24 oblasts with some of them being given special status such as the Autonomous Republic of Crimea, Sevastopol and Kyiv.

Capital City: Kyiv, Ukraine
Government: Unitary semi-presidential, constitutional republic
