The Blood Stained Islands are a group of thousands of islands surrounding one large land mass, everything covered with scattered mountains and forests, and are imbued with magical essence in certain ponds and lakes. This essence has caused war between nations, as well as natives. Because of the wars, the islands have been soaked with the blood of millions of youth and only recently has a peace treaty brought peace to all. Until the next war begins...
