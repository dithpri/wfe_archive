      The Nentir Vale is a northern land yet is sees very little snow. winters are windy and bitterly cold. Most of the rivers are too big to freeze over except for a few weeks in the coldest part of the year. Summers are cool and mild. The area is a mix of many terrains from large rolling hills to rugged mountains, meadows to forests, and lakes to swamps. There are many ancient ruins that scatter this area.
