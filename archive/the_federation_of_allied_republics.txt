Federation of Allied Republics (FAR)
Formation: July 30th, 2025.
Founding Nation: Madresh
Key Objectives:The FAR aims to build friendships, ensure the security of its members, promote its interests, and support the defense of member nations. The alliance also focused on achieving quick victories in wartime to restore peace, creating educational resources, and managing economic and trade programs for members.
War &amp; Peace Policy: The FAR honors its treaties and aims for decisive wartime victories, including the potential use of nuclear options. Espionage against a member is considered a declaration of war, while the alliance respects the sovereignty of other entities and only permits espionage during wartime.
