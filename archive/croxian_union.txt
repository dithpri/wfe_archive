The Croxian Union of Soviet Socialist Republics (CUSSR), commonly known as the Croxian Union, is a Marxist-Leninist State, based on the Planet of Croxia. A Union of several subnational Republics, its government and economy are highly centralized. The Croxian Union is a single-party state governed by the Communist Party of Croxia, with the Palatine Citadel as its Capital.
