The International Assembly of Nations is an international organisation aimed at linking together the worlds nations through trade, diplomacy and economics. 

The world is currently made up of the following nations (their territory): 
Roman Britannia (England, Italian mainland, Wales, Slovenia) 
Prutainnia (Karelia, Murmansk) 
CANZUK (Canada, Australia, New Zealand, Scotland, Northern Ireland) 
JP Isles (Balearic Isles) 
The Second State of Israel (Israel, West Bank, Gaza, Lebanon, Sinai, Golan Heights) 
San Andreas Island (Madagascar) 
Mittleeuropa (Austria, Poland, Hungary, Czech Republic, Slovakia) 
European Federal Republic (Germany, France, Holland, Belgium, Luxembourg, Spain, Portugal)
