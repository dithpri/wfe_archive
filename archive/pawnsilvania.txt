Rules on trade-routes and harbors/airports:
- Airport only allowed after a capital city has been founded.
- You get one harbor to start with, after that one per 1 trillion GDP with a maximum of four.
- Trade routes for both airports and harbors are only allowed if both the nations agree on it.
