The Commonwealth of Independent Nations (CIN) is an organization that is founded on the principles of self-determination and intellectual integrity. We welcome all nations who wish to become involved in a union of other Independent Nations. 

[b]Official Language:[/b] English
[b]Military:[/b] In emergency, CIN nations unite in common defense
[b]Anthem:[/b] La Fedelissima http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=m0EFtuNNga0
[b]Flag:[/b] The blue field represents nobility of independence seekers. The central sun represents the CIN, and the rays represent member nations who make the central sun visible to all.
