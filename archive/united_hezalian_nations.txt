After years of war and friction, the three nations of Hezalian orgin known as Clara de Sole, Lunae Terre and Hezali have decided to put their differences aside and join in a union, honouring what used to be their united nation of Hezalia. Now The United Hezalian Nations welcome new members to share culture and technology, and to forge diplomatic connections
