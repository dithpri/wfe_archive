[b]The Empire of Arivadah[/b] is a state located in the Mojave Wasteland, ruled over by Emperor Xavier I. Founded after the assassination of Robert House, Arivadah was established to provide an alternative to NCR and Legion rule. From its capital of New Vegas, the government of Arivadah guards the independence and prosperity of its Empire zealously with its army of securitrons and the support of the people.
