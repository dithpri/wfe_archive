The Badlands are not very hospitable, especially to those promoting peace and prosperity. From the Mountainfolk of Squag to those who dwell in irradited tundra to the north and of course the masked horde of strange Bhesians in the east, this land is as rough and violent as those whose dwell in it. Truly were it not for the strangely abundant Uranium deposits and natural defenses of mountains and wastelands, no nation would ever willingly exist in this region.
