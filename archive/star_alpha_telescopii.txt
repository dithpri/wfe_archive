&amp;#945; Telescopii is a sub-giant star of spectral class B3 in the constellation of Telescopium. &amp;#945; Telescopii visual magnitude is 3.51. Because of its moderate brightness, &amp;#945; Telescopii is easily visible to the naked eye from locations with dark skies, while it might be barely visible, or not visible at all, from light-polluted skies.
