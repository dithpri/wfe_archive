The very strong 1 FREEDOMian economy, worth 325 billion marks a year, is driven almost entirely by the private sector, which is broadly diversified and led by the Basket Weaving industry, with major contributions from Trout Farming, Retail, and Woodchip Exports. Average income is 65,177 marks, but there is a significant disparity between incomes, with the richest 10% of citizens earning 204,710 per year while the poor average 15,888, a ratio of 12.9 to 1.
