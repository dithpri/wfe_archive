[b]From a distant star life began,
[/b]
So say the Sacred Scrolls passed down to us from generations long before since the Great Exodus from Origin. Here in the three-star Othrys System, Twelve Colonies were established, Twelve disunited worlds that compete for influence and power, all unaware of great danger in the years to come. The only way for mankind to survive is for the Twelve Colonies to unite......
