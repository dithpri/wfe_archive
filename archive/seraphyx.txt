Seraphyx is a super earth found in ESO 325-G004 circling B-1110 456 a yellow dwarf. Its land is covered in warm oceans with deep trenches and dotted with lush, tropical rainforests. The main continents are : Sirius, Tymon, Fanifie and Planx. The dwellers upon the planet are a newly found specie called Cryonugon and the primary predators are ocean bounded Ignousolopusoars. The radius of the planet is approximately 38,420km. Seraphyx was founded in 3857.
