The [b]North Atlantic Treaty Organization[/b] (NATO), also called the [b]North Atlantic Alliance[/b], is an intergovernmental military alliance between several North American and European states based on the North Atlantic Treaty that was signed on 4 April 1949. NATO constitutes a system of collective defence whereby its member states agree to mutual defence in response to an attack by any external party.
