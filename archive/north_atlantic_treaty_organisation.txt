Founded with the cooperation of three nations: Jamiun, Uber-Death and Darklanding. These countries secured a rock solid alliance working together to succeed economically, socially and militarily. Though the three founding nations had different political views (Jamiun: democracy and the right to hear what the people say, Darklanding: anything that makes money goes and Uber-Death: there will be one leader and what he says is always right) they still managed to get along.
