[color=#0000BF][b]Wishtonia is home to many tropical island nations that span the Wishton Sea, many formed from underwater seismic activity. The region lies due south of [region]Teremara[/region]. The Yarok Line, named after a famed Tavlyrian explorer, is the dividing line between the Southern Madurin Sea, Tavlyrian Ocean, and the Wishton Sea. Wishtonians share some cultural, historical, and ethnic bonds with each other, and their neighbors to the North, some of which were former colonial rulers. Now most are independent, and some are protected territories with more autonomy.
