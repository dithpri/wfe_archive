Many innovators and adventurous individuals of a shunned variety set off from their homeland in an exodus of millions to find for themselves a land which would be suitable for their purposes of establishing a new nation. Despite sharing in spirit the reason for their movement to a new land the different paths they had walked led to a variety of ideals that set the basis for the differences in these societies.

Despite these differences they have come together with the goal to improve one another&#39;s circumstances. The pursuit of a better life being a common goal for these nations.
