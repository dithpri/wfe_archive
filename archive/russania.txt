The lands of Russania lay on the vast, rich, and bitter northern most plains of the world. Once United by the Grand Republic for many centuries, until The War of Salvation, a devastating and societal changing war waged between the entirety of Russania and the Golden Empire. While the Republic defeated the empire and managed to maintain its independence, it layed the foundation for the republics eventual demise and political polarization. The lands of Russania today lay divided and tense, with the Grand Reich in the East, the Kingdom of Russiania in the north and center, and the People&#39;s Union of Russania in the west and south
