Middle Europa as a region has seen one of the largest Economic Booms of any region of the world stage. This is because of the large amount of Resources recently discovered in the region, which was followed by rapid industrialization. Austria Slovenia eventually established the Economic Union of Middle Europa to further enhance this Economic Growth. The nations of the region also have very high standards of living as well as GDP per capita, with the Money difference between the poor and the rich being only about 420%. This has led to an influx of not only Corporations but also Immigrants. Every country is allowed to join Middle Europa as long as they&#39;re a Democracy with good Standards of Livings for its citizens.
