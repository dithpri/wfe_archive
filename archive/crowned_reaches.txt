Best known as the homeland and claimed sphere of the Commonwealth of Primeautia, a disciplined, technologically advanced state built among megafauna, vertical cities, artificial citizens, and dangerous wilderness. Primeautia considers the region its rightful civilizational domain, claiming it alone has preserved its ecosystems, protected its wildlife, secured its cities, and maintained order. The Exiles of Primeautia reject this claim and present themselves as the region&#146;s legitimate representatives before the World Assembly.
