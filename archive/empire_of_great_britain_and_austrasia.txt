The Empire of Great Britain and Austrasia is the collection of Colonies and Plantations ruled and governed directly by the United Kingdom of Great Britain and Austrasia as established under the Colonies Act 2010 and the various Colonial Charters granted under the said Colonies Act.

Monarch: King Charles VII

Government: Presidential System with an Executive Monarchy and a Legislative Parliament where sovereignty is shared between the Monarchy and Parliament

Colonial Subjects: Seven
