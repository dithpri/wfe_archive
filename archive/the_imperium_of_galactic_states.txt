The [B]Imperium of Galactic States[/B] is the governing confederation of the galaxy, consisting of 5,106 sovereign member states (as of the last Imperial Census.) For more than 71,000 standard archival years, the Imperium has expanded not only physical borders but the frontiers of knowledge and prosperity, kept the peace among civilized worlds, and defended its people from the pirates and outlaws of barbarian space.
