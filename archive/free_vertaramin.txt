Free Vertaramin Charter:

Full freedom of speech and from suppression of any post, except in cases of homophobia, transphobia, sexism, or racism, the right to a trial by a committee of officers in cases of removal, the right to vote on all WA resolutions, and the right to endorse any delegate and immigrate from any region, so long as no threat of invasion is clear and present, the equal protection and censure of laws upon all citizens, officers, and the founder, the direct monthly election of all officers, and the right to amend this entry by 3/4 vote of officers and members are hereby declared the basic and inviolable laws of this region.
