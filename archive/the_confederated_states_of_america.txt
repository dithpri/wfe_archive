I.
The Stile of this Confederacy shall be &amp;quot;The Confederated States of America&amp;quot;.

II.
Each state retains its sovereignty, freedom, and independence, and every
power, jurisdiction, and right, which is not by this Confederation expressly
delegated to the Confederated States, in Congress assembled.

III.
The said States hereby severally enter into a firm league of friendship with
each other, under a single leader, for their common defense, the security of their liberties, and their mutual and general welfare, binding themselves to assist each other, against all force offered to, or attacks made upon them, or any of them, on account of religion, sovereignty, trade, or any other pretense whatever.
