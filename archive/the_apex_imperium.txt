The Apex Imperium is a multidimensional empire that is extremely militaristic and imperialistic. Their goals are to become the most powerful empire in existence and prevent the assimilation of all life. Their enemies and traitors are referred to as vermin. While the Apex Imperium generally dislikes non-humans and sees them as unpredictable threats, that doesn&#39;t mean they&#39;re not willing to make non-human allies.
