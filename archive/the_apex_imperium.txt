The Apex Imperium is an empire that spans multiple dimensions and is extremely militaristic and imperialistic in nature. It seeks to claim as much territory as possible and become the dominant superpower. The Apex Imperium consists of the Apex Imperium itself and other empires it has allied with. Allies of the Apex Imperium are allowed to operate independently until they prove they are incapable of supporting themselves. They are very cautious towards new empires until the new empire in question proves they are trustworthy. Enemies of the Apex Imperium are referred to as vermin. Anybody who threatens the Apex Imperium or its allies will face possible extinction.

Ponies and animatronics are forbidden!
