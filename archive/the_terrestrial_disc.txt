[b]The Terrestrial Disc[/b] (colloquially &#39;the Disc&#39;) is the name for the ~703 million square [color=red]VLR[/color] (612 mln. km^2) on which the entirety of known human civilisation resides. The &#39;Open World&#39; - the term for the four continents that engage in diplomacy with each other - is currently in a state of &#39;cold war&#39; between the [b]People&#39;s Federation of Reinkalistan[/b] and [b]Union of Dhoerish States and Nations[/b].
