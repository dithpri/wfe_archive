A brief international space exploration program between Eastshland and Tiersa, with their respective spheres of influence  yielded the quiet discovery of the Heinz-Jiang Habitable Planet Region, a dense cluster of stars with several dozen Earth-like habitable terrestrial planets, The following 10 years has seen a great exodus, with over 3 billion citizens slowly making their way over to the nebula.
At the time of the publication of this background summary, it will have marked the 1 year anniversary that the colonists in the sector have upgraded from using pre-fabricated structures, and repurposed ship settlements.
