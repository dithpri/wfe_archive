Pleasure World are very cultured place where &amp;#1072; huge proportions of their populace dedicated solely to producing works of art, music or other forms of entertainment. Casino complexes, opulent restaurants and huge ballrooms are often found all over the world.

However, there is a dark side to this, especially in order to indulge the tastes of more depraved guests. 

The World offers many idealistic places, from quiet corners of free love to depraved sadistic orgies in the midst of hedonistic madness. They are mesmerizing environments, though worrisome considerations still dangle like loose threads on a beautiful tapestry.
