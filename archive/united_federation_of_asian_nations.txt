THE UNITED FEDERATION OF ASIAN NATIONS
established in Gsar Lhasa, S.R.S. on 17.02.12


About the Organization

The United Federation of Asian Nations (UFAN) is a multilateral organization promoting peace, stability, and cooperation among Asian nations. Established February 17, 2012, it spans the Near East to the Pacific as a platform for diplomacy, conflict resolution, and integration.


Mission &amp; Governance

UFAN maintains peace through a council-based system wherein each member state appoints equal representatives for consensus-based decisions on disputes, treaties, and security. It mediates conflicts, negotiates agreements, and upholds sovereignty amid diverse traditions.
