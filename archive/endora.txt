Endora is a place of great strife. The beautiful landscape is plagued by deadly war and conflict. Although not openly hostile to outsiders the nations of Endora are usually always at war with one another making it tough for anyone that lives there full time. Some nations wish to stop the endless war but others seem pleased by the infighting. Outsiders however still come to Endora to see the wonderful landscape. The capitol of Endora is Ebonemare one of the last neutral nations in region. Endora is also very very very large, its bigger then North America, Russia and Australia combined
