The Argovinian Empire was founded in the year 2012, when the Mayan calendar ended and disaster befell the entire world. On 21 December 2012, a Great Flood consumed the entire landmass upon the conclusion of the 5,126-year long count cycle. When the waters subsided on 23 December, only a small continent known as Argovinia was left habitable.
