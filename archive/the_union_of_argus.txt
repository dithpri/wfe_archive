Built after an a cataclysmic chain of both natural disasters and decades long civil and international conflicts that left millions without all the essential needs such as food, water, shelter and stability; The Argus Union was built to create a regional forme for international cooperation, resolving conflicts and international trade.

The Great Division became the name given to this dark time in the former island chain known as The Iron Line, noted for is high rate of violence, great instability and technologically advanced and vast militaristic nation states.
