South Africa is a region on the southernmost tip of the African continent, marked by several distinct ecosystems. Inland safari destination Kruger National Park is populated by big game. The Western Cape offers beaches, lush winelands around Stellenbosch and Paarl, craggy cliffs at the Cape of Good Hope, forest and lagoons along the Garden Route, and the city of Cape Town, beneath flat-topped Table Mountain.

[b]We are A democratic Region with the President being elected by Vote as well as Vice President. 
[/b]
Once the ballots are tallied those elected are assigned immediately to their respective offices with the persin abdicating and allowing the elected person to become president of the region.
