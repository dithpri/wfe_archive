The Eurovision Song Contest 2023 is the 67th song contest to be held in Liverpool, UK after Ukraine, which won the 2022 contest with the song &quot;Stefania&quot; by the Kalush Orchestra, failed to meet the requirements to host the contest. due to security concerns caused by the Russian invasion of Ukraine. Great Britain will host the competition for the fifth time, the last time it took place in 1998 in Birmingham.

This year, Eurovision will be held under the slogan &quot;United by music&quot;. The logo features hearts in the national colors of Ukraine and Great Britain. The font used in the Eurovision logo is called Penny Lane - a reference to The Beatles and Liverpool&#39;s rich musical heritage.
