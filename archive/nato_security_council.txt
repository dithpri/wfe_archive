[color=#252850][b]NATO SECURITY COUNCIL[/b][/color]
The [region]NATO Security Council[/region] was created with the aim of improving the efficiency of recovery raids on regions occupied by looters.
It is responsible for selecting, through specific regulations, the regions that can request assistance for this purpose and for preparing Effective Liberation Proposals to be sent to the WA Security Council and for preparing a campaign to disseminate the motion[hr][b]Procedures for Requesting Assistance[/b]
Send a telegram to the Speaker of Requests where you request assistance.
If your request is accepted we will notify you by telegram.
[i]N.B.: Any request made to this region should NOT be disclosed externally in order to maintain the privacy and security of our Agents[/i]
