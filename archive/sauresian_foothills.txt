After a long time, separatists from southern regions arrived on the south-east borders of Sauresian Foothills. Both Morfun and Sceratians have accepted them as their neighbours, sharing culture differences, having new neighbours from the Orient... The nation of Huguo were also eager to learn the teachings of the occidental Sceratia and Morfun, creating a strong and valiant alliance...
