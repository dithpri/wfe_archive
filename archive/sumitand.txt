An alliance of smaller nations established on desert nomadic grounds, now seeking improvement through cooperation with one another. We prioritise our subjects above the concerns of the rest of the world, and enjoy a relatively healthy standard of trade based on our closeness to the interlocking [i]Protein Road[/i] network, however our founding political leaders are prone to rivalries due to holding distinctly monarchic titles and differing views on how much the public are welcome to their opinions. The shining gem of this region is the [i]Obelisk Garden[/i], a relic from an ancient city of which the heritage is a hot topic of debate amongst historians.
