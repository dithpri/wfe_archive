This region rejects narratives and systems imposed by elites through military, economic, cultural, media, religious and diplomatic hegemony. The region aims the comprehensive destruction of the bourgeoise nation-state.

The region opposes: 

All core tenets of the Enlightenment, specifically:
All forms of modernism
Capitalism and globalism
Individualism, pluralism, liberalism, secularism
Classical nationalism
Materialism, scientism
Progressivism (not social justice)
Conservatism/renegade liberalism
Popular sovereignty, &quot;democracy&quot;

The region also opposes:

Traditional philosophical ideas stemming from catholic theology
