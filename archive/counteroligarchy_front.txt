This region rejects narratives and systems imposed by (mostly capitalist) elites through military, economic, cultural, media, religious and diplomatic hegemony. The region aims the comprehensive destruction of the bourgeoise nation-state.

The region opposes: 

Ideologies that deny the very existence of objective truth
Capitalism and globalism
Individualism, pluralism, liberalism, secularism
Classical nationalism
Postmodernism and its variants
Materialism, scientism
Progressivism (not social justice)
Conservatism/renegade liberalism
Popular sovereignty, &quot;democracy&quot;
Traditional philosophical ideas stemming from Catholic theology, such as free will and linear history
