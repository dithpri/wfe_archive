The Insulae Imperialis, Latin for the Imperial Islands, are a group of loosely scattered islands near Indonesia. Led by the country of Xenotianus, the Insulae Imperialis is led under the Caesarea Congressus of 49 Judges and one Grand Judge, amounting to 50 members. While the region is comprised of different ideologies, they are noted to each have a great connection to their roots, as most nations started out as colonies.
