The Eight Continents often refers Fantasy world of Adamantine, the Eight continents separated and connected through magical Seas and waterfalls largely developed their cultures and peoples independent of each other. they are, as follow; the Continent of Tellius, Magvel, Flakto (Modern Gallagher Confederacy), Myros, Weyard, Astenia (Also Referred to as Goffanon by the Western Astenians), Elibe, and a Final Mysterious Continent named Verica; where no one has yet to have stepped foot on. this partially because of a Mystic field that prevents ships from entering.
