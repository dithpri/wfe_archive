[u][B]Welcome to the Union of Multiversal Empires![/B][/u]

The Union of Multiversal Empires is a Diplomatic organization that serves to be a platform for Diplomacy and Dialogue between various Intergalactic Empires. The U.M.E is not a governing body and does not create laws besides it only serves as a neutral meeting ground for member nations to discuss Intergalactic and Universal Issues, Promote peace or war between Empires, ect.
