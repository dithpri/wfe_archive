Historically, the Cotton Belt was one of the largest producers of cotton in the world. The entire land was once owned by a man by the name of Swampas Frederick McRonaldo. After an accident involving three tractors and a stick of dynamite, the vast expanse of land was up for grabs for many new nations to come.
