The Perthon Alliance of Nations was established as an administrative oversight unit for territories under the Kingdom of Perthton&#39;s government.

PAN leadership reports to the Deputy Minister for Territories, who advises the Minister of the Interior on issues and governmental needs occuring within the territories. 

PAN acts on behalf of the President to ensure policies, laws, and budget is maintained within the territories.

All embassies established with PAN communicate to the Minister of Foreign Affairs through the Minister of Territories.
