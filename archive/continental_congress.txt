[b]The [region]Continental Congress[/region] was a convention of delegates from the Thirteen British Colonies of America that became the governing body of the United States during the [url=http://www.history.com/topics/american-revolution/the-continental-congress]First American Revolution[/url]. 

April 19, 1775: British marched on Lexington and Concord to seize weapons held by Patriots who ceased to recognize the authority of the royal government of Massachusetts. British defeat

June 14, 1775: Congress created [region]The Continental Army[/region] 

July 4, 1776: Congress declared Independence

1781: Congress ratified [url=https://memory.loc.gov/cgi-bin/ampage?collId=llsl&amp;fileName=001/llsl001.db&amp;recNum=127]Articles of Confederation[/url]

November 25, 1783: War ended
