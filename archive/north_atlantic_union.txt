[b][u]Welcome to the North Atlantic Union[/u][/b]

The North Atlantic Union promotes free-trade and mutual defence amongst its member nations across the universe.

Member nations are expected to agree to, and abide by the principles of the union. 

[b]1[/b]. Member nations are welcome to join and leave at any time, without interference from other nations. 

[b]2[/b]. Member nations agree to trade without barriers or restrictions with other member nations. 

[b]3[/b]. Member nations will act together to maintain the sovereignty of the union and its individual member nations. 

[b]4[/b]. Member nations agree to maintain a defence budget of at least 5% of their national GDP. 

[b]5[/b]. Member nations understand that a failure to meet their obligations may result in suspension from the union.
