After the turn of the century, various groups of downtrodden peoples were left to a refugee status. It was imperative that they find a new home for themselves, and with the apparent rejection of their plight by the power that be, they set off to find a new region. After a lengthy time, these peoples found the Angian Sphere. This new land provided a place for these groups of people to grow, free from the influences of domineering states or oppressive systems. Thus they may struggle forth into a new era, one of the Angian.
