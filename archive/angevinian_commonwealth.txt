The Angevinian Commonwealth is the collection of polities across the world who are loyal to [i]His Royal Highness[/i], [b]Mathias the Fourth of the Challaner-Angevin Dynasty[/b], [i]Emperor of Britannia and Gaul, King of the Dutch, of Saxony, of Prussia, of Bavaria, of Hibernia, of Piedmont, of the Norse, of Lusitania, and of Castille y Leon. Duke of Luxembourg, of Hanover, of Alsace-Lorraine, of the Catalans, of Andalusia, of Jutland, and of Skania. Most Holy Protector of Italy, of Alexandria, of Jerusalem, and of Anatolia.[/i].
