[i]Aquila[/i]thrives as a rapidly growing haven for freedom-lovers. 

[i]Aquila[/i] was founded upon the principles that the regional government should have as little control over local issues as possible, while also recognizing the benefits of being a member of a region.

Our Motto: [i]Libertatem Situm in Manu de Societate[/i] or Freedom Lies in the Hands of Society
