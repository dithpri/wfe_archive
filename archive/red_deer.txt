[b]Red Deer[/b] is a city in Central Alberta, Canada. It is located near the midpoint of the Calgary&#150;Edmonton Corridor and is surrounded by Red Deer County. It is Alberta&amp;#39;s third-most-populous city, after Calgary and Edmonton. The city is located in aspen parkland, a region of rolling hills that is home to oil, grain, and cattle production. It is a center for oil and agriculture distribution, and the surrounding region is a major center for petrochemical production. --Wikipedia[hr]
