The Realm of Aerilosia consists of two main nations; Aeridani and Bielosia, which the realm is named after.

It comprises of two components: Atur Misexia and Loviniosa, led by Aeridani and Bielosia, respectively.

We do not accept nations whom we do not know to join us. However, we do accept embassies if they are directed to us.

[color=red]The unity of the nations in the Realm of Aerilosia shall overpower to all who oppose the greater cause of our path.[/color]
