The island of Eyrland is a small disputed land of just over 8 million residents. Its long and troubled history have provided a strong maturing period for unique cultures and mind sets that are now clashing in a modern society. 

When military conflict was a very real and urgent threat not even 20 years ago, the peace process is now coming into effect and tensions are receding, but one slip up and the land could be plunged back into turmoil.
