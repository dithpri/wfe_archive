Galbador is a vast ocean: home to many cultures, governments and their people. It is a welcoming, open sanctuary for every nation: small or large, militaristic or peaceful, open or isolationist. The region and its land of relative Terra Incognita is located east of the continent of [nation=short]Taurenor[/nation] in [region]Haven[/region].
