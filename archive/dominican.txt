[b][region]Dominican Republic[/region][/b]

Location: Caribbean, eastern part of Hispaniola Island
Capital: [region]Santo Domingo[/region]
Area: 48,671 square kilometers
Population: Around 10.6 million (2023 estimate)
Language: Spanish
Currency: Dominican Peso (DOP)
Government: Presidential Republic
President: St James (since 2020)

[b]History:[/b]
Gained independence from Haiti in 1844. Former Spanish colony.

[b]Economy:[/b]
Main industries: Tourism, agriculture (sugar, coffee, bananas), manufacturing.
Economic Highlights: Second-largest economy in the Caribbean, with tourism as a major contributor.

[b]Culture:[/b]
Strong Spanish influence, with Merengue and Bachata music and dance as key cultural elements.

[b]Nature:[/b]
Tropical climate, known for its beautiful beaches, mountains, and rainforests.
