European Cross
&quot;Per Crucem et Ferrum &#151; By the Cross and the Sword&quot;
Founded: 27 December 2025, during Christmastide, close to the Feast of the Nativity.
Type: Standard Region (WA Delegate holds executive authority).

&amp;#10013;&amp;#65039; Faith &amp; Symbolism
- Sacred emblem: The Cross, symbol of sacrifice and unity.
- Guardian animal: The Falcon, vigilant protector.
- Motto: Per Crucem et Ferrum.

&amp;#9876;&amp;#65039; Governance
- Founder: Head of Knights of the Cross, consecrator of the region.
- WA Delegate: Marshal of Nations, elected to wield executive authority.
- Council of Banners: Assembly of member nations sworn to concord.

&amp;#127760; Culture
- Feast of the Vigil (Christmas Eve).
- Day of the New Oath (27 December, founding day).
- Investiture rites for new nations joining the Cross.
