Description:

The Vaotian Peninsula is a large peninsular region that is under the process of colonization and urbanization, with many new settlements being created in the area. Vaotia, being the first major country to take root in the region. Deeper  North, a bit outside of the Peninsula, lies a tough barren tundra. 

For information on the map and governing styles, check the information dispatches!
