Description:

The Vaotian Peninsula is a large peninsular region that is under the process of colonization and urbanization, with many new settlements being created in the area. Vaotia, being the first major country to take root in the region. Deeper  North, a bit outside of the Peninsula, lies a tough barren tundra. 

Government Style: Each country residing in the Vaotian Peninsula can govern the way it wants, and all are free to decide if they want relations with other countries residing in the peninsula. 
There shall be a council for important decisions, such as war or other political actions that would involve the whole Peninsula.

War: War may be declared if the council rules in favor for it, in a poll of 52%+ or greater
