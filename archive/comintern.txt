[b]Communist International - [region]Comintern[/region] was an international communist terrorist organization established in 1919 to use armed force for the overthrow of government and complete abolition of State. It failed and the Soviet Union dissolved the Comintern in 1943.
The Soviet Union itself [url=http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dissolution_of_the_Soviet_Union]dissolved[/url] in 1991[/b]
