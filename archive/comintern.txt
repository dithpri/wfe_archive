The Communist International, abbreviated as [b]Comintern[/b] and also known as the Third International (1919&#150;1943), was an international communist organization that advocated world communism. The International intended to fight &quot;by all available means, including armed force, for the overthrow of the international bourgeoisie and for the creation of an international Soviet republic as a transition stage to the complete abolition of the State.&quot; &#151;Wikipedia[hr]
