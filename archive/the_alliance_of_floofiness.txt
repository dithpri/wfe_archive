Founded by the previously apocalyptic nation of Cryptica. After an apocalyptic war with the undead citizens resulting in being unable to enter graveyards unless invited or dead. Currently comprised of The Kingdom of Cryptica, The Rogue Nation of Deez All Over, The Oppressed People of Cornicus, and The Empire of Blepia-Dominicus.

Out in the middle of the Dark Sea, the Alliance hopes to unite nations under the promise of peace, trade, and occasionally questionable morals.
