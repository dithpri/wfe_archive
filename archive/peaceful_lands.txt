The Peaceful Lands is a peaceful and fertile region shared by multiple countries, bound together by a geographic and political alliance. This region is known for its lush landscapes, flowing rivers, and diverse ecosystems that support both agriculture and tourism. Countries within the Peaceful Lands work together to preserve the natural beauty of the area while fostering cooperation in trade, cultural exchange, and environmental protection.
