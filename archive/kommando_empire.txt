An expansive and geographically Diverse region with many islands around two large continents. Kommando Land is the largest and most powerful nation in the region, controlling the surrounding area. The region is patrolled by the Kommando Land Military, with Carrier Air Wings and Air Patrols containing the most advanced Combat Aircraft in the Kommando Land Air Force.

     The Kommandoniese are lassie-fare with the business of other nations in the region and only intervene in the occurrence of a severe wrongdoing or tyrannical government. Freedom is important to the natural harmony of the region.
