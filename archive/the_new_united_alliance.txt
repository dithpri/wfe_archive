WELCOME TO THE[b] [color=red]NEW UNITED ALLIANCE![/color] [/b]

The New United Alliance, also known as the NUA, was made to replace its original, The United Alliance. The NUA was founded by The Republic of Dallinsville and alongside him were is two allies, The United Kingdom of Jeptalia, and The Democratic Republic of Annadall. They rebuilt the region to serve the same purpose as its original, to bring democracy to the world. On November 23, 2016 the New United Alliance was founded.
