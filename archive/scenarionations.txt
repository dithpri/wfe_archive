[Color=darkred]SN is a government simulation game. A government simulation game, for those of you who are not familiar, is a game in which a player chooses a country to lead, and then they lead that nation essentially however they deem fit. Some people might have a multi-person government, some may have a single person government. In SN we strive for realism, and fun- so have at it![/color]
