[u][b]Kimotoro is a section of Caldari State space.[/b][/u]

It is a collection of mega-corporations and colonies.

Corporations within [b]Kimotoro[/b] function under the state, acting as military and the greater economy.

Colonies function as vassals, and tend to work independently from [b]the state[/b].  They, in the end, act as a large amount of the income for the nation however, as they tend to work with [b]Jita[/b] for cheap goods, and it&amp;#39;s many consumers.

The [b]CONCORD Assembly[/b] handles almost all relations and security, and functions as a separate body to the World Assembly.
