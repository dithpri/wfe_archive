Centaura is the largest continent in the Forman World. The Columban Range is a series of mountains that divides the Centauran continent between north and south. The Centauran continent has the most people in Forma; about 60% of the world live in Centaura. The Centauran Wars(1908-1944) take place in the continent of Centaura, in the alternate world of Forma - a planet similar to Earth. A bloody conflict has gripped the entire continent in which decades of warfare would be fought. The new reality has set into the world, a grim reminder of the toll of war where there can only be one last war to end all wars; a last one to finally achieve peace... or is it...?
