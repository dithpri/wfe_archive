* Freedom of capital, migration, and trade. Under the common border, customs union, and homeland defense, national economies are consolidated under one umbrella.

* Under the Transatlantic Free Trade Area, Pan America is tied in with the EU, Israel, Japan, Korea, and Taiwan. Under the balanced foreign trade mechanism, international trade certificates are used to maintain an equilibrium. 

* Under the Pan American Free Enterprise Zone, legal statutes, ordinances, and regulations are harmonized to facilitate balanced, moderate, and sustained growth.
