A large island region with varied landscapes from lush forests, to the marshes and wetlands of the east, the snowy peaks central mountains, and the fertile plains of the west. It is a land of diverse people, with over 200 ethnic group and 300 languages. Most of these believe that they are descended from a sophisticated coastal civilization which flourished over 1000 years ago, and is rumoured to be the fabled Atlantis. This is a source of unity for people, and diversity is welcomed. The region has prospered in an unbroken peace for a 100 years.
