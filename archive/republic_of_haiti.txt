The earthquake of January 2010 is the most recent, and possibly most tragic, of events to have befallen the impoverished island nation of [b]Haiti[/b]. As both failed state and media whipping boy, Haiti has long played the dark shadow to the bright sunlight of the rest of the Caribbean. Optimism over Haiti&#39;s impending renaissance has been subsumed beneath the horrors of the earthquake, which has resulted in tens of thousands of deaths and millions of displacements.
