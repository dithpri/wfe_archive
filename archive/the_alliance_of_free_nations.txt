The Alliance of Free Nations has been created for the purpose of creating peace and prosperity among its member nations.

Ruff draft of the Constitution of the Alliance

Article. I
* No nation shall be banned without reason.
* All member Nation is free to run their country the way they see fit.

Article. II
* The WA delegate will be granted Administrative powers.
* Abuse the power of the WA delegate and it will be removed and the nation banned.
* Nations shall be granted the freedom of equal treatment and opportunity.
