&amp;#946; Volantis is a variable giant star of spectral class K1 in the constellation of Volans. &amp;#946; Volantis visual magnitude is 3.77. Because of its moderate brightness, &amp;#946; Volantis is easily visible to the naked eye from locations with dark skies, while it might be barely visible, or not visible at all, from light-polluted skies.
