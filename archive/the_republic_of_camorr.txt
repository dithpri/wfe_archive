In this Region of the world we are a stable and prosperous. Our land is covered with houses and  stores well stocked for anything our citizens may need, for both the rich, the poor and the middle class. The government is uncorrupted and fair, the trials are not tampered with. But underneath all of this there is a hidden region, only know to few. They consist of the most talented and skillful, elite thieves, and thoes who have more luck than anyone should rightly have. Become one of us; either the upper or lower.
