Thembria is a fictional country that borders Cape Suzette, in the animated series TaleSpin.

Its government appears to be similar to the Soviet Union&#39;s government, exhibiting the particular characteristics of that government especially during the Stalinist era. All of its inhabitants are purplish warthogs, and the climate of Thembria is usually snowy and cloudy. Some of the main characters have visited the country. It was featured in the episode &quot;Flight of the Snow Duck&quot;.
