Welcome to the [b]Free Territories[/b]!

The [b]Free Territories[/b] is an open neutral region that accepts anyone. This region is designed for casual players just looking for a place to call home. We do not require members to be active in the World Assembly, participate in any regional activities, or be active on the regional message boards. All we ask is you follow these rules:

1. Obey the rules of NationStates.
2. Do not spam the regional message board.
3. Do not raid, as we are a neutral region.

We accept all embassies.
