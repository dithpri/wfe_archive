[hr][list][color=800000][b]Turkestan[/color][/list][hr][color=003300]Land of the Turks[/b]

An area of Central Asia that includes: [region=Turkmenistan], [region=Tajikistan], [region=Kazakhstan], [region=Uzbekistan], [region=Kyrgyzstan] and [region=Xinjiang] (aka East Turkestan or Chinese Turkestan).
Often the region includes parts of [region=Tartarstan], [region=Siberia] and [region=Afghanistan].

The Russians took the city of Turkestan (in present day Kazakhstan) in 1864, mistaking the name for the city for that of the entire region.[/color][hr]
