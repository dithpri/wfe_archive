Withergate is a Cyberpunk community, set in the city of Withergate, built on the ruins of Albuquerque, New Mexico. Withergate offers a regional government, roleplay, and chat (forums coming soon)

+----------------------------------+---------+------------------------+----------------+

The year is 2083. Withergate, eponymously named after the corporation headquartered in the city, is one of the few cities on the continent that has not been swallowed by the ocean or consumed by nuclear fallout.
