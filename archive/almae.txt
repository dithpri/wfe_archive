The world of Almae is home for 1 empire and 4 kingdoms. The elven empires capital is situated on an big island but Aranwae also dominates a big territorie of the continent of Asao. The dwarf kingdom of Gamle is situated below and on the ridge of Amon Shol also located in the continent Asao. The other dwarf kingdom is in the continent Pedoa,  among the Dark forest and next to the grey mountains. Then there are the neighbour men kingdoms of Gondolin and Rohret. They are in the continent of Corap in a big plain with some mountains and forests.
