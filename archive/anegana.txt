Anegana is a new region, home to few. We are a welcoming people everyone is accepted with open arms. Anegana was formed as a result of a fracturing region, one that sent dozens away, until they found new homes, this is one such home, a home for all! Anegana is protected by the space fleets of Lycantine, the military prowess of Wiraced Fer, and the endless legions of cheeze people from Cheezeland part 2 electic boogallo.
