The name of Continent of Opica refers to historic country called Opica that once covered 60% of continent. Nowadays continent is shared between several nations. Most important of these are The United States of Cadonica, The Kingdom of Shimbrica and The Sultanate of Quwariata. Also island group, ruled by The Armed Republic of Islands of Midway, is considered as part of continent though it&amp;#39;s located 1,000 miles west from the continent.
