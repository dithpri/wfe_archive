The Terran Imperium is a unity of the many varying nation states, kingdoms and empires of the peoples of Terra. Irrespective of culture and beliefs, all Terrans are ultimately bonded by their past ties and the Imperium works to coherse these people to rally into one unified people. The leader of the nation leading the Imperium is known as the Emperor. All within the Imperium and its vast empire of empires must submit and swear fealty to the reigning territory. The flag of the Imperium will always change to a variant of the ruling empires flag, in order to represent it

The Current Imperial Emperor is his Imperial Majesty reigning from the Empire of Terran Germanica
