The Grand Emirate of Nurem is a loose coalition of nations that tend to favor strong religious views and substantial militaries.

Though the nations within the Grand Emirate rarely agree on anything, least of all religion, they all benefit from the extreme isolation granted them on the distant continent of Nurem, and can generally agree to leave one another alone.

To encourage peace, the nations within the Grand Emirate keep only small military forces on Nurem itself, sending the majority of their sizeable armies to spread their militant religion far abroad, and far from their neighbours...
