[color=#666666]Established on July 10, 2025, the [b]Kingdom of Sardinia and Corsica (KOSC)[/b] is a constitutional monarchy located in Southern Europe and consists of the Mediterranean islands of Sardinia and Corsica. The monarch and head of state is [b]His Majesty The King of Sardinia and Corsica, [nation]Sardinia-Corsica[/nation][/b]. Sardinian, Corsican, and Italian are the official languages of the kingdom. For any inquiries about the Kingdom of Sardinia and Corsica, please contact His Majesty&#39;s administration.[/color]
