Welcome to Vostok, new democratic and modern region. Vostok is a federation of nations(nations being players in the game of NationStates) where you can experience a balanced approach to everything this platform has to offer:

- Politics
- Roleplay
- War
- Debate
- Sharing ideas

The region has regular elections, rule of law, and transparent referendums. Constitution, embassies, flag, dispatches and tags, will be decided by its community via agreement of majority.
