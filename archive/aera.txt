Welcome, to The Island of Aera.

This small island, in the north Atlantic shares its land among three nations. The largest presence here is the Republic of Aera, holding roughly 77% of the total land area of roughly 84.5 thousand square kilometers. The second largest power is Northern Aera with a 16% claim, and finally there is Donegaul with the remaining 7%. 

The region is experiencing a period of relative peace after several decades of civil strife and violence.
