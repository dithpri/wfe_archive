The [b][color=#DAA520]San Pietro Cooperation Organization (SPCO)[/color][/b] is a regional intergovernmental organization headquartered in the city of San Pietro, Pordhes founded on January 11, 1971. It is comprised of countries with historical trade relations since the 16th century, up until the present day. The SPCO&#39;s main purpose is to promote peace, security, and stability, while fostering economic cooperation, trade, and sustainable development among its member states. It also aims to combat terrorism, extremism, and transnational crime, facilitate cultural exchange, scientific collaboration, and technological advancement, and strengthen mutual trust and good-neighborly relations among its members.
