After twenty of deadly conflict, the states of Eurasia decided to form an uneasy economic and military union to protect themselves against the formation of several regions around the world. The Gypsination and Saeterra struggle daily to hold together the alliance, with loyalties and power shifting every day. Kortalia tolerates any region or government so long as it does not adopt fascist or racist principles. Nazism and ethnic cleansing are strictly not allowed in at all, and any region that adopts this policy will suffer the consequences.
