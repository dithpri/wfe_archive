                                                                              Constitution of DUGE
=Joining=

The only requirement is that every member has a certain level of unhindered democracy which will remain a standard. Any State that falls dangerously into a dictatorship will be given one week to improve or face ejection.

=Structure=

World Assembly Delegates are democratically elected and have near complete control over management of the region. The Founder may not remove a WA Delegate unless a unanimous vote with at least a 66% majority declares the Delegate to be breaching any of the given rules.
