Welcome to [region]Battenberg[/region]!

Battenberg is a political, economic, and social union of highly-advanced high-income mixed market economies. To join the Battenberg Union, nations must have average incomes of at least 55k Batten Francs, low corruption, and a poor-income of at least 35k Batten Francs.

Battenberg is also a customs union, meaning it trades with other nations and blocs as a single customs area.
