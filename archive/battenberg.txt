Welcome to [region]Battenberg[/region]!

The Battenberg League is a political, economic, and social union of highly-advanced high-income mixed market capitalist economies. To join the Battenberg Union, nations must have average incomes of at least 55k Battenberg Standard Units, low corruption, and a strong level of economic equality. With the Battenberg Monetary Governance Standard, all currencies are pegged to each other with a 1:1 exchange rate.

Battenberg is also a customs union, meaning it trades with other nations, blocs, and regions as a single customs area. The capital of Abrasaceia is the city of Kensington, in [nation]Athemherst[/nation]. The Head of State of the Battenberg Union is Her Majesty, Queen Alexandra of Battenberg, who resides throughout the union.
