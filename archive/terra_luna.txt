A wretched wasteland is spotted with patches of green grass and grey scrub. Deserts of blasted land stretch out from glassed craters and twisted ruins of ancient, destroyed cities. Craters accumulate the sparse rain fall of the land, bringing spots of verdant life to the land.

Signs of war and wreckage mar the jagged coasts and land bridges to the continent. Entrenched in heavy concrete bunkers, behind razor blades and landmines, the Imperial Burnt Army and the soldiers of Gynadine Matriarchy keeps a heavy guard. Many of the young soldiers look exhausted and war-weary, but alert for trouble on the signs of the horizon.
