The United Mercenary Syndicate is a co-owned mercenary, industrial and commercial region ruled by The Haven Band an the Aspari Syndicate.

The UMS seek to combine the aspects of war-economy with industrial and commercial economy, allowing the nations of the UMS to prosper and the soldiers to be free of political agendas that in reality exploit them.
