[B][u]Before the Flash[/u][/B]
The end of the world occurred pretty much as we had predicted. Too many humans, not enough space or resources to go around. The details are trivial and pointless, the reasons, as always, purely human ones. In the 21st century, war was waged over the resources that was left, the storm of world war had come again. In two brief hours, most of the planet was reduced to cinders. A quiet darkness fell across the planet, lasting many years. Few survived the devastation...
