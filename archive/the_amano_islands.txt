The Amano Islands is a small, isolated chain of islands in the Pacific Ocean. These islands include the safe republic, the Republic of Athearia, and the harsh dictatorship, the Dictatorship of Golfersvile.

The Republic of Athearia, commonly known as Athearia, is led by Olechander Moshcalopsis and is known for its excellent political freedoms and powerhouse economy. 

The Dictatorship of Golfersvile, is a new nation and is known for its harsh Dictator.
