Over a century ago a great disaster befell the world resulting in a new dark age. Only recently have nations gotten back to the technology of the modern day and even more recently have they started to move beyond it.
New Worlds RP is an accessible RP region that takes place on an earth reborn after the governments of the old world collapsed. We have a welcoming community and technological progression. We primarily use discord which you can access here: https://discord.gg/GxbUMpa
