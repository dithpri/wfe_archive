The [region]Cordial Islands Archipelago of Polynesia[/region] is a chain of island groups situated in Polynesia in the south-eastern Pacific.

[nation]Glamour[/nation], the region&amp;#39;s central nation, is the host nation of the [url=http://forum.nationstates.net/viewtopic.php?f=4&amp;amp;t=334390&amp;amp;sid=f0d5f381fda10a2c0f4d5de9182c5658]First Global Ball[/url], a member of the [url=http://forum.nationstates.net/viewtopic.php?f=4&amp;amp;t=318711]International Freedom Coalition[/url], and is open for embassy application through [url=http://forum.nationstates.net/viewtopic.php?f=23&amp;amp;t=329916]Project Cocoa Leaf[/url]. It is a non-WA, militarily neutral, cultured, free nation, and instigator of the regional ambassadorial entity.
