Alphard, also designated as &amp;#945; Hydrae (alpha Hydrae), is a variable and multiple bright giant star of spectral class K3 in the constellation of Hydra. Alphard visual magnitude is 1.98, making it the 45th brightest star in the sky. Because of its brightness, Alphard is clearly visible to the naked eye when observed from locations with dark skies, and should be also quite easily visible from light polluted areas.
