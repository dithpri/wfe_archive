Northcrown is a region with a cold, unforgiving landscape. Often called the Land of the Midnight Sun, the region is known for its harshness. However, from this land, grand civilizations grew and prospered and, eventually, founded a entity that showed a bond unlike anything seen on our planet. The Region of Northcrown is, as of now, a member of the [url=]Ocluyama Union[/url]. More as we update.
