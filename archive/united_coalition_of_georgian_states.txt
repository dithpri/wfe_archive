The united nations under the banner of the supreme Empire of Georgianess. You must name your nation after Georgia in some way, or be given special permission to be secretively Georgian controlled. Our region is bigger than it appears as it has nations away on missions. Elections for Thronestealer are held every month on the second and elections for Mindvoicer are held on every February second. All other positions are appointed by the Willspeaker, who has the power to change 1 of their positions each month. The Willspeaker is directly in charge of communicating what Georgianess wishes. The Mindvoicer serves as our diplomat and the Greenstealer or Greentaker runs recruitment.
