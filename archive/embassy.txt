Welcome to Embassy! We accept all embassy offers and our goal is to establish embassies all around the world. To participate in the International Embassy Program, send an embassy request and message the founder who will give details about the program. If you would like your region to become a member, please ask the founder who will be happy to admit you as an International Embassy Program Member. We also maintain an International Message Board for nations in many different regions to discuss diplomatic events. An International Embassy Program Summit will be occurring soon.
