Name: The Grand Assembly of People&#146;s Republics [GAPR]

Type: International Regional Organization

Description:
The Grand Assembly of People&#146;s Republics is an international organization primarily created for cooperation among sovereign states constituted as People&#146;s Republics. These states form the core membership of the Assembly. Other sovereign nations may also join, but the organization is centered on People&#146;s Republics.

Scope:
The Assembly is not geographically limited and includes member states from different regions of the world.

Nature:
Member states retain full sovereignty and political independence.
