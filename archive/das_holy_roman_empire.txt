The Holy Roman Empire (Latin: Sacrum Romanum Imperium; German: Heiliges R&ouml;misches Reich) is a multi-ethnic complex of territories in central Europe that developed during the Early Middle Ages and continues until this day.[6] The largest territory of the empire after 962 is the Kingdom of Germany, though it also include s the Kingdom of Bohemia, the Kingdom of Burgundy, the Kingdom of Italy, and numerous other territories.
