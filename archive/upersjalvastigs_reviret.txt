[b]Upersjalvastigs Reviret[/b] (USR or, informally, the Anglican Realm) is a community of states, self-governing to varying degrees, under the B&auml;rlinds Kron (the Baelor Crown). 

The Krons Fjalst&auml;t of Alderen (also known simply as the Crown Commonwealth) is its political, cultural and economic capital and home to the governing organs of the Revir. Its territories include the Krons Besk&uacute;tst&auml;t (the Crown Protectorates) and the Krons N&oslash;burgst&auml;t (the Crown Colonies).
