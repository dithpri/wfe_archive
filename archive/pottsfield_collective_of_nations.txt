The Pottsfield Collective of Nations (PCON)

Informally known as &#147;Pecan&#148; by most international citizens

The Pottsfield Collective of Nations (PCON) is a multilateral political and economic organization composed of sovereign states committed to mutual cooperation without the forfeiture of national independence. Founded on the principle that shared problems demand shared solutions, PCON exists to coordinate scientific research, healthcare standards, education initiatives, ecological protection, and diplomatic mediation among its members.
