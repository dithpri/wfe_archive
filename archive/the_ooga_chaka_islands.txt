[url=http://www.nationstates.net/region=the_ooga_chaka_islands]The Ooga Chaka Islands[/url] were erected from the sea when Blue Swede performed their song [url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NrI-UBIB8Jk]&quot;Hooked on a Feeling&quot;[/url] in 1974. Now they are a &quot;prospering&quot; archipelago filled with many diverse forms of government. They have been described by the Washington Post as &quot;an example of why it&#39;s a bad idea to give control of thousands of bountiful islands to twelve raving lunatics.&quot; They have but a single requirement: [url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NrI-UBIB8Jk]&quot;Hooked on a Feeling&quot;[/url] must be the national anthem.
