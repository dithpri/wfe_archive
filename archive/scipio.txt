Scipio was a cold snowy planet in the Star Wars universe which was the home world of the Muun species and the host of the Intergalactic Bank. However, during The Clone Wars, conflict would find its way to Scipio and the banks due to the manipulation of Darth Sidious, and they would fall under control of the Republic, and later the Empire.[/color][hr]
