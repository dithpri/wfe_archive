The Fifth Internationale was created in 2028 in the aftermath of the storming of the New York Stock Exchange, which led to the establishment of the Manhattan Commune at the start of the Second American Revolution.

The founding members of the Fifth International were:

Union of American Popular Councils (Formerly the bourgeois states of the US and Canada)
Socialist Federation of Mexico
Bolivarian Republic of Venezuela
Republic of Cuba
Worker&#39;s French Republic
Eire (Formerly the divided states of the Republic of Ireland and Northern Ireland)
People&#39;s Union of Korea (Formerly North and South Korea)
Third Spanish Republic 
British Republic
