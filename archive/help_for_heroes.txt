Help for Heroes, also known as H4H, is a British charity launched on 1 October 2007 to help provide better facilities for British servicemen and women wounded since 11 September 2001. It was founded by Bryn Parry OBE and his wife Emma Parry OBE after they visited soldiers at Selly Oak Hospital in Birmingham. The charity was launched after a meeting with General Sir Richard Dannatt, the Chief of the General Staff, the professional head of the British Army.
