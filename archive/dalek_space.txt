Dalek Space beckons as a nexus of power and peril, extending an invitation fraught with ambition and compromise. For nation states enticed by technological ascendance, security amidst cosmic chaos, and access to vast resources, joining Dalek Space offers alluring prospects. Yet, the cost is relinquishing autonomy to a regime driven by ruthless dominance, where the iron grip of order conceals the erosion of individuality. In this delicate balance between supremacy and subservience, the choice to join Dalek Space reverberates with the echoes of ambition and sacrifice.
