The Continent of Great Britain is a new nation designed for nations based around those &#39;real life&#39; countries and counties in the UK (Scotland, England, Wales, Northern Ireland, the various minor British Isles, and these countries&#39; related counties. Each nation within the region, has the right to be an World Assembly Delegate, there is one who can be voted in once every three months. The Continent of Great Britain has a dedication to democracy and therefore all member nations have equal say to decisions which will be decided upon following an equal vote. Should you have an issue you would like to be put to vote, this can be done by sending a telegram to Royal Yorkshire. This will then be voted upon, and the decision to be announced publicly.
