[color=003366]The Bahamas, officially the [b]Commonwealth of The Bahamas[/b], is an island country consisting of more than 700 islands, cays, and islets in the Atlantic Ocean; north of Cuba and Hispaniola (the Dominican Republic and Haiti); northwest of the Turks and Caicos Islands; southeast of the U.S. state of Florida and east of the Florida Keys. Its capital is Nassau on the island of New Providence. The designation of &quot;Bahamas&quot; can refer to either the country or the larger island chain that it shares with the Turks and Caicos Islands.
