Constitution
Article 1:Executive
The executive power shall be invested into four entities,President, Chancellor, the General Ministry,and Parliament. The President is the official diplomatic face of the government as well as commander in chief of the National Armed Forces of the UEU.He shall be elected by the Parliament.The President can&#39;t be less than the age of 10 days in NationStates.It also had to be within the region for at least 5 days.The Chancellor is the Head of Government and has power to veto legislation, appoint justices, and has power to override the President.It shall be elected by the people.The Chancellor can&#39;t be less than the age of 15 days in NationStates.It also had to be within the region for at least 3 days.
