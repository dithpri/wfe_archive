[color=#FF8040][b]Information[/b][/color]
Flegavia is a continent that makes part of the [region]Orbia[/region] region. It is so culturally and historically diverse, that it has its own autonomous structure inside the Orbian region. Most of the countries make part of the FNU (Flegavian Nations Union); aiming for the integration (in all possible aspects) of the nations of Flegavia.
