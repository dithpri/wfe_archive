Pewtopia, a lush, fertile area of the world dotted with no more than a few countries. Competition is lax and relations are wonderful between its inhabitants. With many opportunities and plenty of space this region is ideal for almost anything you can imagine! The close knit family of nations live together in perfect harmony and are more than happy to welcome any newcomers in as long as they make one simple promise. They will not disturb the peace. Any country that wishes to join need only ask for a simple password and they will be welcome into our community without hesitation.
