[b]Seamount[/b] is a British Overseas Territory in the Mid-Atlantic Ocean. It is approximately halfway between Europe and North America. The capital city is New Chester, which is renowned for its well-preserved colonial buildings. The island is internally self-governing, with its own constitution and Cabinet selected from, and responsible to, the elected Members of Parliament that enacts local laws. The United Kingdom is ultimately responsible for ensuring good governance, defence, and foreign relations. It has a population of approximately 25,000 people.
