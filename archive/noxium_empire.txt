[b]Imperial Directive for the Founding of the Noxium Empire:[/b]
By declaration from the Grand Office of Cousul Victor Thorn, seventeenth to the Imperium of the Noxium State and trusted hand of the Council of Five; The land formally known as the Imperial Conglomerate shall hence forth be reformed under the banner of the Noxium Empire. The Noxium Empire shall be under the leadership of the Triumvirate of Nations and shall be entrusted with the total governmental operations of the region. Failure to comply with instructions directed by the Triumvirate of Nations will result in Terminal Status being issued to all governing officials of said nation.
