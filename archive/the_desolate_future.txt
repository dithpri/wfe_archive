Decades after the fall of the Sloviskian government and the subsequent oppression Rindhia exerted on their forefathers and grandchildren, the generation of today stopped weeping and overtook the power for themselves. Now as the cold and harsh winter disperses into a hazy summer morning, they gazed into the light of a new world. Different history and culture craft different people and ideals; as a divided civilization now establishes itself one hundred different kingdoms, dictatorships, and democracies. They set their sights towards the struggle of unification, whether by the sword or the pen.
