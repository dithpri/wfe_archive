This is the Isle of Stantwick, which consists of several islands, some of which are shared with two nations.

The United Centrist Nations of Stantwick (UCNS) consists of Gillespie-Stantwick, Keenan-Stantwick, Wana Baraka and the Armed Republic of Revolutionary Cliffords.
The UCNS is currently at war with Swamard.

Stantwick is proudly in partnership with the Dictatorship of Rubs.
