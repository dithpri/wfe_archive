This is the Isle of Stantwick, which consists of several islands, is shared by two nations, Gillespie-Stantwick, which has the largest island, and the Republic of Revolutionary Cliffords, which has control of all the smaller islands.

The United Centrist Nations of Stantwick (UCNS) consists of Gillespie-Stantwick and the Republic of Revolutionary Cliffords.

Stantwick is proudly in partnership with the Dictatorship of Rubs.
