Greater xerofelia is the homeland of the nation of xerofel and has served as it&#39;s ancient lands since the early period of the empire housing great cities like Xerilia, Xermael, Xirofem, Xaraliam, Xoverxor and various others that form the sublime xerofelian state.

The whole area of greater xerofelia consists of the isolated island of xerofelia which serves as the mainland of xerofel as well as integrating a few land holdings and the puppet state of birofel.
