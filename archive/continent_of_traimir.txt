The continent of Traimir is a fictional continent taking place on a fictional world. 

Any nation is welcome to join Traimir as well as fill in a spot in our continental map: http://imgur.com/D9IZuMQ

Also, the nation of Tellenstan has been proposing for the creation of the Traimir Union, which functions as an economic union of nations on the continent, as well as a standing military for the continent known as The Peacekeepers, which their primary duty is to enter as a neutral side to any conflicts occurring on the continent. If a nation wants to join the Traimir Union, send a telegram to Tellenstan.
