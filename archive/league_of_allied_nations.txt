The League of Allied Nations is a peace-loving region, dedicated to uniting the world of NationStates. The freedom of speech will not be infringed upon. For all new nations joining, please feel free to send a telegram to anyone already living in this region if you have any questions, and please read [url=https://www.nationstates.net/page=dispatch/id=1417236]The Order of the Government of League of Allied Nations[/url].
