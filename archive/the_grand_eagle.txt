&quot;The Grand Eagle&quot;, a name given to a group of select nations in the Post-Calamity world. After many decades these nations decided to fund the restoration of their world after extracting all remaining Cordium on the planet.
The Future of this world is unknown.

After Pannonian efforts to create a portal across the multiverse, Time and space seems to have broken for a split second, bringing &quot;anomalies&quot; from other universes.
