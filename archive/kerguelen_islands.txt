The [b][i]Kerguelen Islands[/i][/b] in French commonly &Icirc;les Kerguelen or Archipel de Kerguelen but officially Archipel des Kerguelen or Archipel Kerguelen, also known as Desolation Island, are a group of islands in the southern Indian Ocean. The islands are a territory of France. There are no indigenous inhabitants, but France maintains a permanent presence of 50 to 100 scientists, engineers and researchers.
