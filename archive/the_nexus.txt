Welcome to Nexus. In here the boundaries that keeps the multiverse alternate timelines, parallel universes, other dimensions are gone. By stepping into the zones of Nexus one is able to meet people and beings from many worlds along with entering them. In here any world that you can imagine is possible, a world of legends and sorcery, a world that is a mirror of our modern world, a world where science and technology has advanced beyond anything we can imagine. These worlds and more can be traveled to if only you are willing to take the risk and enter the Nexus.
