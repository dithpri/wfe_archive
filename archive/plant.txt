The PLANT homeland is located at Lagrange 5 and made up of twelve &quot;cities&quot;, each incorporating ten colonies for a total of 120. These cities are named for a month of the Roman Calendar (Aprilius, Junius, etc.), with the colonies of each city numbered from one to ten (ex. Januarius One, December Eight). Each city has a scientific or industrial specialty.

Zodiac Alliance of Freedom Treaty, or ZAFT for short, is its military arm. It is equipped with advanced weaponry in the form of Mobile Suits and high speed space ships.
