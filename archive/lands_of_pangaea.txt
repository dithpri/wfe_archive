The Lands of Pangaea are an idyllic utopia, a vision of societal harmony and progress that encompasses the ideal qualities of governance, infrastructure, culture, and environmental stewardship, as well as economic freedom.

In the Lands of Pangaea, the government operates under a transparent and participatory democracy. They are built upon the principles of accountability, with leaders who genuinely serve the best interests of the people.

In terms of the economy, the Lands of Pangaea strike a balance between capitalism and social welfare. They promote entrepreneurship and innovation, while also addressing income inequality and ensuring a strong social safety net.
