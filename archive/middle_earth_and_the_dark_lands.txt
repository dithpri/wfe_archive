It has been centuries since the War of the Ring. Large nations have split apart into smaller ones and the world has advanced. In 1273 Fourth Age, a fleet of Elvish ships discovered the Dark Lands and realized it was inhabitable. Due to overcrowding in Middle-Earth, millions of people have sailed there to start their new lives. Now, in peace and prosperity, we shall work together to flourish as King Zacharion leads us to greatness!
