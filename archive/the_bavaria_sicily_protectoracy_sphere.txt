The Bavaria-Sicily Protectoracy Sphere is an organization lead by the Empire of Bavaria-Sicily, composed of itself and the nations that Bavaria-Sicily provides military and nuclear protection towards. Originating from the political, philosophical, and geopolitical movements that also lead to significant reforms to the structure of Bavaria-Sicily that followed the horrific use of nuclear weapons at the end of the Great War, the sphere also serves as an organization for coordinating trade and providing for fully open borders between member states.
