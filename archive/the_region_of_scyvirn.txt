Scyvirn is a faction rp and wb community mainly based on Discord (https://discord.gg/HgcNTaT). Rp takes place in a semi-realistic fantasy setting, where there&#39;s magic and monsters as well as politics and diplomacy. Start your own nation and gain prominence, make and break alliance or (re)claim territory from other factions. Rp not your cup of tea, but wb is? Join our wb team and help create regular expansions to the world around Scyvirn, with new regions, races and creatures.
