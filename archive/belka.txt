The [b]Principality of Belka[/b] is a major country of the Strangereal world. One of the central countries of the Osean continent, it is bordered by the Osean Federation, Fato,  the Kingdom of Sapin, the Republic of Gebet and also by the Republic of Ustio. Officially known as the Principality of Belka, it is one of the most influential countries in military history, greatly affecting the evolution of aircraft as weapons of war.
