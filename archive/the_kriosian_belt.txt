The Kriosian Belt (KB) is a developing region that is home to a variety of different nations. Created by the tyrannical East Belzaria, the KB is a cautious region where most nations who enter make defense forces and militarization a priority.  

The KB is currently exclusive to a select group of allied nations. For those who are still interested in the ideals of the KB and are interested in joining, please refer to [region]The Kriosian Square[/region] (KS), which is a public version of the KB.
