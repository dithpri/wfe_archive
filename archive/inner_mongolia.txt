[hr]Inner Mongolia is an autonomous region of [b][region]The Peoples Republic of China[/region][/b], located in the north of the country, bordering Mongolia and Russia. The majority of the population in the region are Han Chinese, with a substantial Mongol minority. Tibetan Buddhism is a predominant religion in Inner Mongolia.
