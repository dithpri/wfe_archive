From the quiet recesses of the Nation States world, the region of the Ionian Republic formed. A historic region of individual nations merged into one union to prevent future conflict between them all. These nations resolved to become a guiding force in the Nation States world that would wield great influence.

This region is led by experienced leaders in the NS world who are building one of the most politically involved regions in NS. A highly political system with an active foreign policy and quite a bit of spamming. Anyone can become someone in the Ionian Republic.
