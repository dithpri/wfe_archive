The Fish River may mean nine different locations all throughout the world. Due to their similarities in name, they have been connected by an interdimensional pathway.
This network that covertly connects so many places so far apart has a lot of potential to become a new base of operations for us, should the current systems fail. This extremely strategic location, therefore, is currently in storage to prevent them from falling into the wrong hands, should the day that we need it finally come round.

This region was founded on 21 December 2021.
