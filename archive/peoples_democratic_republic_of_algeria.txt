Algeria (Arabic: &amp;#1575;&amp;#1604;&amp;#1580;&amp;#1586;&amp;#1575;&amp;#1574;&amp;#1585;&amp;#8206; al-Jaz&amp;#257;&#39;ir), officially People&#39;s Democratic Republic of Algeria, is a sovereign state in North Africa on the Mediterranean coast. Its capital and most populous city is Algiers, located in the country&#39;s far north. With an area of 2,381,741 square kilometres (919,595 sq mi), Algeria is the tenth-largest country in the world, and the largest in Africa and the Arab world.
