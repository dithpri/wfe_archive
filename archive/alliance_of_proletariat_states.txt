Alliance of Proletariat States
Founder: People&#39;s Republic of Brickholm
Capital: Brickholm City
Founded: March 17, 2011 (RP date)
Status: Open for membership

The Alliance of Proletariat States is an alliance of nations committed to proletariat development, sovereignty, and regional cooperation. Led by Brickholm, the alliance promotes state-led planning, universal welfare, and peaceful collaboration. Membership is open to all nations that value collective progress and practical socialism.
