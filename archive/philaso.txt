Welcome to Philaso. If you want to join, please follow some certain rules for joining:

1. All aspiring residents needs to submit a letter to the Lead Delegate of the Region.
2. Aspring residents will be allowed to join if they are:
 - Less than 75 million in population upon submission of the letter, and.
 - Has less than 25 on the score of Civil Rights, Economy and Political Freedom,.
3. After reviewing your profile, the  officers in charge of the Border Control will send you the password.
