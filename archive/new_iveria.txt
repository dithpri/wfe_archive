The region of New Iveria was born out of the downfall of the Iverian Union, which subsequently brought down all the existing nations of Old Iveria. The nations of New Iveria are new, and no treaties not legislation have been signed, and no formal groups have been created.

&#149;THE NATIONS&#149;
The Free Land of Fjirdland
-Fjirdland was the first nation to inhabit New Iveria, and is essentially a libertarian paradise, with complete economic freedom and extremely limited government.

The Dominion of Ecologon
-Ecologon is a Democracy dedicated to the conservation of Rights, Political Freedom, and the environment all while having a stable economy at best.
