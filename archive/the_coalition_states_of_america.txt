After the coming of the rifts the Coalition States were a loose alliance of five industrialized states which emerged out of the ashes of the United States and Canada, It is a league of human nations who joined together in a mutual protection pact, a defensive military alliance against monsters and demons, as well as both human and alien marauders and conquerors. Under the leadership of the fascist Ragnar Hatebreed it has become an imperial power.
