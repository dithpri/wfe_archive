The Heartlands Isles are a set of active volcanic archipelagos scattered across the aquatic planet Shuwi. The planet has an abnormally high amount of natural satellites (6) resulting in frequent mega-waves reaching heights of 20 metres. Most of civilization resides inland, utilizing the rich volcanic soil.

The region was founded on September 21, 2019 GMT+8 in Singapore by the members Meister Federation, Anastasia Federation, Creme Corporation, and Sooshs Federation.
