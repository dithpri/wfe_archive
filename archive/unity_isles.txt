Location: Unity Isles is an archipelago in the ocean

Founding Members: Established by Crommsylvania, New Des Moines, Zanzria, and the Holy Cromwell Empire, each contributing unique cultural heritages.

Government Type: Semi-autonomous, with local governance for each member and a regional council for collaboration and to create policies

Coalition Dynamics: Members work together to address challenges and advocate for self-determination.

Unity Isles embodies a rich blend of cultures and histories. As the islands strive for autonomy and cooperation, they shape a vibrant future for their diverse communities.
