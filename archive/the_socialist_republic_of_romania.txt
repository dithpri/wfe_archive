[b][color=#FCD116]The Socialist Republic of Romania was a single party socialist state that existed officially from 1947 to 1989. From 1947 to 1965, the state was known as the Romanian People&amp;#39;s Republic. The country was a Soviet-aligned Eastern Bloc state, and a member of the Warsaw Pact. The Romanian Communist Party was the sole, legal governing party. Nicolae Ceausescu was the President and General Secretary from 1967 to 1989.[/color][/b]
