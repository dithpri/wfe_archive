[color=#148f77][i]Beyond vast seas lie the crystalline waters, sun-baked desert sands, great continental forests, and epic mountain ranges of [b]Luciterra[/b]. A region of Monarchs, Dictators, and Democracies, Luciterra is shaped by those who inhabit its lands.[/i][/color]

Luciterra is a modern-tech region focused on balanced world-building for casual and active players alike. Lore is built by the players, and all members are free to suggest, debate, and expand on each other&#39;s ideas. Interaction on the main NS forum is encouraged, including with nations in other regions.
