WAC ensures that all members will come to each other&#146;s aid if attacked, strengthening regional security. It also promotes free trade among members to boost economic growth and stability. Additionally, the coalition is committed to defending Christians against jihadist movements in Africa and the Middle East while fostering strong diplomatic relations with other Christian nations. WAC stands as a powerful force for unity, security, and sovereignty in West Africa.
