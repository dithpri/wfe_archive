Novasola. A sun large enough and hot enough to support life on 8 (soon-to-be 15) planets. 

Consists of the planets Terra (Earth with different geography and peoples), Yrmea, Cella, Plutonia (3 fairly average planets), Imperiana (capital planet of the IC), Dulunia (two moons), Christiana (Christian-dominated planet), and Underterra (quite cold planet).
