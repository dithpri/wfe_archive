[b]THE EMPIRE OF AENAVIA[/b]


[hr]

The Empire of Aenavia operates as an absolute trans-border autocracy masquerading as a modern alliance. While the five principalities retain their local governing structures they are entirely subordinate to the central crown of [b]Emperor Aeone I[/b].
