[i]The City of Cities[/i]

Urbe is a roleplay region set in the year 1500 AD where players roleplay as boroughs of a city state republic. We value roleplaying and writing, but also democracy, heraldry, legalese and diversity of interests and thoughts. Whatever you do here, you are bound to find someone who shares interests with you!
