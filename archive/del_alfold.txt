The Del-Alf&ouml;ld, or Del-Vid&eacute;k, is a Central European Danubian region, ruled by the Duke of the Banat and Syrmia, the Co-Dukes of B&aacute;cska, and the Duchess of Csongr&aacute;d and Baranya.
The lands in this region are moderate conservative, monarchical, libertarian in matters of free speach and economy, loyal supporters of the Habsburg Kings of Hungary.
