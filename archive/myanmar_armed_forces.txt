The [b][region]Myanmar Armed Forces[/region][/b], most commonly known as [b]the Tatmadaw[/b] is the military of the Republic of the Union of Myanmar. It is the guardian of national unity and the protector of the people.

The branches of the Tatmadaw are:
1. [b]The Myanmar Army (Tatmadaw-Kyi)[/b]
2. [b]The Myanmar Air Force (Tatmadaw-Lay)[/b]
3. [b]The Myanmar Navy (Tatmadaw-Yay)[/b]

Although there are only 3 branches, there are many agencies and organizations that are controlled by or influenced heavily by the military. The 2021 coup has only increased the Tatmadaw&#39;s control over many agencies. A few of the agencies that the Tatmadaw controls or heavily influences are:
1. [b]The Myanmar Police Force[/b]
2. [b]The Myanmar Coast Guard[/b]
3. [b]The Border Guard Forces[/b]
