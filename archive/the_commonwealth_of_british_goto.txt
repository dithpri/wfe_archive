The Commonwealth of British Goto is a commonwealth in East America from 1924-2004, the Commonwealth included the country itself, British Goto, Gotoan Kingdom of Indlikanp, Gotoan Kingdom of Grelom, Gotoan Kingdom of Sergent, Gotoan Kingdom of Hudons and the Gotoan Kingdom of Gasto. (Remember, the commonwealth has been abolished due to the collapse of British Goto and the British Empire).
