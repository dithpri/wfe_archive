The Citadel is the first fortress of the Wasteland. It is a formation of three rock mesas on top of a aquifer. It is part of the Triumvirate, the other two being Gas Town and Bullet Farm.

The Citadel produces food and water for its citizens in the Wasteland, and supplies Gas Town and Bullet Farm in exchange for guzzolene and bullets.

Provides - Food, Water
Demands - Gas, Guns
