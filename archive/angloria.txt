
Motto: &quot;Unity in Diversity&quot;

Beliefs and Principles:

Angloria is dedicated to fostering unity among English-speaking nations, embracing cultural diversity while promoting shared values of democracy, human rights, and civil liberties. The region believes in the power of economic cooperation, with member states working together to achieve greater prosperity through free trade and innovation. The region strives for social equity, focusing on initiatives to reduce inequality and enhance the quality of life for all citizens.
