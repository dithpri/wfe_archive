[b][color=#000034]Seleucia on the Tigris[/color] [color=#9a6601]was a major Mesopotamian city of the Seleucid, Parthian, and Persian empires.[/color][hr]
[color=#9a6601]It stood on the West bank of the river opposite[/color] [color=#000034]Ctesiphon[/color]. [color=#9a6601]The two royal centers formed the metropolis of[/color] [color=#000034]Seleucia-Ctesiphon[/color].[hr]
[color=#9a6601]The city eventually faded into obscurity and was swallowed by the desert sands, abandoned after the Tigris shifted its course.[/color][/b]
