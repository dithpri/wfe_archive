The Xontian Archipelago is a Region Inspired by Europe. Here is its North south east and West info. North:

Region: Frostreach Peninsula
Features: Icy tundra, snow-capped mountains, extreme cold, and Arctic wildlife.
East:

Region: Coralatian Atolls
Features: Coral reefs, turquoise lagoons, diverse marine life, and renowned for snorkeling.
West:

Region: Trade Winds Confederation
Features: Fertile plains, agricultural practices, temperate climate, and agricultural trade.
South:

Region: Verdant Isles
Features: Lush rainforests, mist-covered mountains, high rainfall, and exceptional biodiversity.
