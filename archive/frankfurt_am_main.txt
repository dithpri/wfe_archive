Originally anti-fascist, this region was occupied by the [region]Union of Fascists[/region]. It has now been refounded, reformed, and protected by the [region]Freedom and Justice Alliance[/region] in the name of [b][color=red]anti[/color]fascism[/b].

Under fascist rule, the region became a memorial to the German citizens, &amp;quot;loyal to the Fatherland&amp;quot;, who died under &amp;quot;liberal and communist aggression&amp;quot;. Today it remains a memorial to the German citizens who died in [url=http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bombing_of_Frankfurt_am_Main_during_World_War_II]the bombing of the city of Frankfurt from 1942-1945[/url], but also to the Frankfurt citizens who died under Nazi rule.
