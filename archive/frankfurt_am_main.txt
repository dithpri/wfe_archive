[b]Frankfurt am Main[/b] is the fifth largest city in Germany and is the hub of German finance and transportation. Thousands of Germans in Frankfurt am Main lost their lives to liberal and communist aggression during World War II and this region stands as a memorial to them and to all who were loyal to the Fatherland.

Once bearing the Anti-Fascist tag, [b]Frankfurt am Main[/b] was reclaimed for the German Volk and for National Socialism by the [b][region]Union of Fascists[/region][/b] on 29 September 2012.
