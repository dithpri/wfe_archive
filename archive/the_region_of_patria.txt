The Region of Patria is comprised of all Patrian nations and states. Founded by the Empire of Greater Patria, the goal of the organization is to unite all nations under the same goal: To spread the Patrian ideologies throughout the world. Nations founded by Patrians will be allowed entrance into the organization.
