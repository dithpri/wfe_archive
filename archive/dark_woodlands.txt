The region of Dark Woodlands is a small, socially progressive and a promising region. The nations of the region firmly believe in the superiority of the Government over the market and the importance of the intervention of the government to fix the externalities caused by the private sector. In its basic sense, the region acknowledges the importance of the society as a whole. 
We, the region of Dark Woodlands, would like to welcome new nations aboard under one circumstance i.e if you personally know any of the regions members or are a student in the Woodlands School. Newly created nations can join forces with us in order to benefit our region by increasing its magnitude and also benefiting the new nation by teaching them new skills.
