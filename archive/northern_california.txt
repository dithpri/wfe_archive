Northern California is the northern portion of the U.S. state of California. 

The region contains the San Francisco Bay Area, the city of San Francisco, San Jose, Sacramento, the Big Sur area, the Sierra Nevada including Yosemite Valley and Lake Tahoe.

[b][color=blue]This region is a member of [region]TDL Empire[/region]. This is open region which means anyone may join and have an input in how this region is governed.[/color][/b]
