Gunsucky or as it is better known- Dirken is a nation of variety. Whether it is the landscapes, of tropical forests to tundra to desert. The culture is highly varied by region. After the revolution of 3232-3235 some extreme laws took place. Nag brown was given the title of supreme leader and veganism (the abolishment of animal slavery) and atheism become official Dirken policies. The representatives are democratically elected as is the wa delegate.
