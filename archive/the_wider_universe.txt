[b]The Wider Universe[/b]

The Universe is home to countless civilizations, and only a few have been able to leave their home galaxy and explore beyond it, such as [nation]The Virgo Cluster[/nation], which was the first and has assumed the role as the head of the universal community.

Backwards conservative, capitalist, authoritarian &amp; fascist societies are incapable of reaching this stage due to their inevitable collapse.
