From deep in the past, though cemented in the now, a small group of engineers, workmen, and their families, forge a new path through time after their lives are spirited away by the dictates of time, religion, even life itself.

Returning after a long sleep, The Vorlon find the world, once again befouled by war and chaos; once again destroyed by The Shadow. 

Where ever there is a shadow, there is a Vorlon to cast it.
