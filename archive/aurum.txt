[B]Aurum[/B] is the preeminent empire in the Fantasy World of Elvar. Formerly the most powerful and prosperous empire in the history of the world, Aurum has descended into madness and decadence as the Immortal Emperor falls into paranoia and insanity.

Now it will be up to a small group of Adventurers to gather strength and bring down the God-Emperor before it is too late.
