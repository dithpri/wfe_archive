Xepiratia and Wentburgh are two nations that live on two close by territories, Xepiratia to the south of Wentburgh on its own island and Wentburgh to the north on its own peninsula, they have decided that even with their different cultures and governments, Xepiratia being a democracy and Wentburgh&#39;s constitutional monarchy, representatives of the two nations have come together to form the region of Lapikkania, meeting at a discreet point in the Desert of Naab, the southern point of Wentburgh, as a joint statement of friendship but also protection from the outside world beyond the vast ocean.
