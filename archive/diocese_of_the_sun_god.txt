A region for nations of all types who are worshippers and/or followers of a Sun God.

Nations are free to run as they wish, classified as any political type and with any sort of rulership.

Rules:
[list][*]Nations must have a Sun God based religion; Ra, Sol, Sunna, Inti and any other name is accepted as long as the main god is the sun or the sun is the main god&amp;#39;s representation.
[*]Respect for other nation-players is paramount; personal attacks non-contextual offensive language* is grounds for removal on a three-strike system.

*Offensive language is considered to be derogatory use of gender/sexuality/race/ability terms or use of negative gender/sexuality/race/ability terms [slurs] for people other than yourself.
