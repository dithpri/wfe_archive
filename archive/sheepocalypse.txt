Fifty years after the end of the world, many people were gone. All the world governments had collapsed, and people had grouped together into tribal-like city-states. The governments varied, although most groups still had a sense of order and law. A few of the groups had become more dangerous, and many city-states feared their own societies would collapse into an anarchist terror. In this great time 0f fear, there were a few who went on living their lives unknowing, in eastern new zealand.These were the shepherds. They spent their lives grazing their sheep, and traded amongst each other in a large valley system known as the Groplines. Few dared enter this valley...
