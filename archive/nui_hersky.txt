Nui Hersky, a.k.a planet BE4-Z98, is a vast and hospitable world. Covering land and sea from the temperate continent of Herskyntlaa to the freezing, yet livable, climate of Alshmava, there are many opportunities for conquest, development, and diplomacy.

So ready to sail on the purple seas and bathe in the basking heat of an abnormal blue sun, as there is much to do, and much to be done.
