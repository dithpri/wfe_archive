Seekers of the True Path, Warriors of Justice! 

Like Constantinople or Rome before it, the world has become a breeding ground for suffering and injustice. We shall not stand idly by as the wicked sow chaos and discord while the innocent suffer. Alone, we are but individual nations fighting in vain for survival. But if we come together, if we devote ourselves to an ideal, we become something else entirely. 

When a forest grows too wild, a purging fire is inevitable and necessary. Are you prepared to do what is necessary? Do you possess the will to act?
