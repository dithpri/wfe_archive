Education and career
Diana was initially home-schooled under the supervision of her governess, Gertrude Allen.[21] She began her formal education at Silfield Private School in King&#39;s Lynn, Norfolk, and moved to Riddlesworth Hall School, an all-girls boarding school near Thetford, when she was nine.[22] She joined her sisters at West Heath Girls&#39; School in Sevenoaks, Kent, in 1973.[23] She did not perform well academically, failing her O-levels twice.
