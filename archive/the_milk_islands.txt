The Milk Islands is a island region made up of many great nations. The region is governed by The Secret Council of which is Headquartered in the city of Bumm in Paranation, none of which is very secret at all. The Executive of the Secret Council has full veto powers. Residency in the islands is currently restricted to Nations meeting a publicly unknown criteria. The islands maintain a healthy relationship with the smaller neighboring region of The Holy Islands with embassies on the way.
