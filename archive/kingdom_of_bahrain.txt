[color=333333]Bahrain, officially the [b]Kingdom of Bahrain[/b], is a small island country situated near the western shores of the Persian Gulf. It is an archipelago with Bahrain Island, the largest land mass, at 34 mi long by 11 mi wide. Saudi Arabia lies to the west and is connected to Bahrain by the King Fahd Causeway while Iran lies 124 mi to the north across the Persian Gulf. The peninsula of Qatar is to the southeast across the Gulf of Bahrain. The population in 2010 stood at 1,234,571, including 666,172 non-nationals. &#151;Wikipedia[/color][hr]
