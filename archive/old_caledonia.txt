A collection of hundreds of islands just south of the Arctic circle, the largest of these is Greater Picland and the second largest is Port Nordland. Most of the islands in the archipelago are mountainous and are covered in heather imported in 259 AD by the Ratac race of monkeys from the Outer Hebrides. The islands main exports are hill sheep, honey, whiskey and smoked salmon and their main imports are mars bars and shampoo.

As a protectorate of Scotland and therefore the United Kingdom Old Caledonia held a region wide census in which 99.79% of people that responded rejected Scottish independence!!! This years regional motto is therefore &#145;LONG LIVE THE UNION!!!&#146;.
