[b][color=red]Anarchism[/color][/b]

[list][*] This region embraces concepts of collectivism, democracy through communes, class war, revolution, communal responsibility and the overthrow of capitalism.
[*] This region rejects notions of so-called anarcho-capitalism, anarcho-individualism, anarcho-primitivism, and anarcho-christianity.
[*] Anarchism is a political movement, not a lifestyle.[/list]
