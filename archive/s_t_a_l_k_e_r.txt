S.T.A.L.K.E.R. is a survival horror first-person shooter series created by Ukrainian developer GSC Game World. The series was published by three editors: THQ in 2007, Deep Silver in 2008 and BitComposer Entertainment in 2010.

S.T.A.L.K.E.R. is a video game series set in the Zone of Alienation around the Chernobyl Nuclear Power Plant. The games are set in an alternative universe, in which a second nuclear disaster occurs at the Chernobyl Nuclear Power Plant in 2006 and causes strange changes in the area around it, causing the area to be inhabited by illegal residents known as &quot;stalkers&quot;.
