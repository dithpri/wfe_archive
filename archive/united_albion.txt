An island continent in the North Atlantic Ocean with four distinct island regions separated from each other by the Albion Sea and the Port&#39;s Channel. The North Island separated from East Island by the Port&#39;s Channel and from West Island by the Briton Channel; the South Island separated from West Island by the Bermuda Channel and from the East Island Island by the Caesar Channel. In between the islands, the waterbody is called Albion Sea. These four regions have four independent governments.
