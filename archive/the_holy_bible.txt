[b]The Holy Bible[/b] is a collection of religious texts that are central to Christianity. The Bible is an anthology (a compilation of texts of a variety of forms) originally written in Hebrew, Aramaic, and Koine Greek. The texts include instructions, stories, poetry, prophecies, and other genres, and it is a product of divine inspiration.
