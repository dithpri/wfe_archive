Anser, also designated as &amp;#945; Vulpeculae (alpha Vulpeculae), is a variable and double hypergiant star of spectral class M0 in the constellation of Vulpecula. Anser visual magnitude is 4.44. Because of its moderate brightness, Anser is easily visible to the naked eye from locations with dark skies, while it might be barely visible, or not visible at all, from light-polluted skies.
