A cooperative alliance of countries dedicated to regional trade cooperation and intra-continental fiscal prosperity.

The federation consists of the World Assembly Delegate of whom shall hold the title of Viceroy ex officio.  The Viceroy shall appoint a Diplomatic Envoy, a Members Liaison, a Regional Security Officer and a Cultural Records Officer.

The founder of the region shall hold the position of Permanent Superintendent ex officio.  The superintendent shall impanel a committee to review the Viceroy&#146;s appointments.
