[region]Hamm[/region] is a city in [region]North Rhine Westphalia[/region], Germany. It is located in the northeastern part of the Ruhr area. 
Hamm railway station is an important hub for rail transport and renowned for its distinctive station building. Major industrial branches are the coal-mining industry, steel industry, chemical industry and the car component supplier industry.
