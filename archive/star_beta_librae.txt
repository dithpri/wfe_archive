Zubeneschamali, also designated as &amp;#946; Librae (beta Librae), is a variable main-sequence star of spectral class B8 in the constellation of Libra. Zubeneschamali visual magnitude is 2.61. Because of its brightness, Zubeneschamali is clearly visible to the naked eye when observed from locations with dark skies, and should be also quite easily visible from light polluted areas.
