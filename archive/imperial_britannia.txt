Imperial Britannia is the collective name for the Britannian Empire which is split into 4 regions, two of which are self-governing while the Homeland remains Great Britannia.

CAPITAL - London, Great Britannia
- Secondary Capitals
 - Grand Britannia of the Americas - New Pendragon, Canadia
 - Oceania Britannia of Asia and the Pacific - Singapore, Indochina
 - African Colonies of Britannia* - Cairo, Egypt / Capetown, South Africa

*African Colonies are soon to be granted independence...

ALLIANCES
 - Europia, a coalition of European nations and empires formed to create a front against the Soviet Union.
