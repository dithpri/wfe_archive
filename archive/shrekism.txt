Shrekism, at its core, is a set of moral and spiritual beliefs derived from the morals of the Shrek movies. Here are a few of our major tenants: 1)Layers- All people are like onions. We have an outer layer, the person that we show to the world, and we have a core, consisting of our innermost thoughts and desires. Between those two are middle layers, that filter our core thoughts. It is an Ogres religious duty to eliminate as many middle layers as possible, and to express themselves freely.
