The New Solar Empire is a geographically varied region originally founded by the people of the Solar City. Since, two other major powers, Halvith and Sweedenland have been founded in the region. Several small satellite states also exist in the area. Recently, the NSE was invaded by a group of bandits, but this was swiftly dealt with. Since, travel has been restricted and the borders are heavily policed.
