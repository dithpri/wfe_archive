[b]Ad atiquitatem[/b]
[i]Ad atiquitatem is a history centered region founded by [nation]THe Third Hellenic State[/nation], and &amp;#64;Kuningriik. The Regional map, currently almost completely empty, is set in 475, just before the fall of the Western Roman Empire. Numerous barbarian tribes, splinter states, and empires exist on this map which stretches from Britannia to the far reaches of the Sassanid Empire. Meant to complement nations already involved in rp, we hope to shape this into both a fun, and educational opportunity for all those interested.
