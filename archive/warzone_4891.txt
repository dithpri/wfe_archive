Do you want to run a real war machine? Welcome to Warzone 4891.

This is a combat based RP server, where your economy and economic statistics actually affect your standing. 

Every 2 weeks in real life is a year in Warzone, allowing you to properly make use of your annual budget.

A GDP of at least 200 billion moneys from your nation is required to participate and fund military operations within Warzone 4891, as well as a minimum population of 5 million. Private military contractors can be bought by the million using some of your GDP if you would like more population.

Each nation is regarded as it&#39;s own planet, all conveniently located the same distance from each other for ease of invasion.
