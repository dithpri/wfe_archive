The United Earth Governments came to colonize much of the Orion Arm, a spiral arm in the Milky Way galaxy. But after a major war with an alien alliance and constant rebellion led to the fall of this empire. By the year 2650 AD much of humanity is split up into small factions fighting in a constant power struggle over the right over who rightfully owns Earth. Now Earth is under control of the New Roman Empire and it is the most powerful military superpower of all the other human factions.
