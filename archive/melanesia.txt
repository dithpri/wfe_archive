[B]The Limitations of Power of Melanesian World Assembly Delegates[/B]
[i]BE THE FOLLOWING ENFORCED AND SECURED BY THE MEMBERS AND CITIZENS OF MELANESIA[/i]

[B][i]World Assembly Delegates of Melanesia do not have the power or jurisdiction to the following:[/i][/B]
a. Ban or eject nations of Melanesia unless in the case of a raid or a two-thirds majority consensual vote from the Melanesian populous.
b. Censor, oppress or suppress in any format or action the nations of Melanesia.
c. Alter, amend, or erase from this document without a two-thirds majority consensual vote from the Melanesian populous.
d. Set a regional password without a two-thirds majority consensual vote from the Melanesian populous.
