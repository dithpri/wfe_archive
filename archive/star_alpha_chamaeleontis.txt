&amp;#945; Chamaeleontis is a giant star of spectral class F5 in the constellation of Chamaeleon. &amp;#945; Chamaeleontis visual magnitude is 4.07. Because of its moderate brightness, &amp;#945; Chamaeleontis is easily visible to the naked eye from locations with dark skies, while it might be barely visible, or not visible at all, from light-polluted skies.
