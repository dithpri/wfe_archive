[b][region]Schloss Adler[/region] is a medieval rock castle, situated on a 623-metre precipice overlooking the Austrian market town of Werfen in the Salzach valley, approximately 40 kilometres south of Salzburg, Austria. The fortress is surrounded by the Berchtesgaden Alps and the adjacent Tennen Mountains.

The mountaintop fortress is garrisoned by [nation]The White Youth Corps[/nation], so named for the distinctive alpine uniforms and pale complexion normal for the European elements of the [region]Imperial Earth[/region] armed forces
