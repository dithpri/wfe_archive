[b]Welcome to [color=#ff0000]Noctur[/color]![/b] 

Noctur is a roleplaying region, where nations are geographically and historically intertwined. The region features a diverse range of countries, from democracies to dictatorships, paradises to wastelands.

The region is an &amp;quot;Intermediate&amp;quot; level roleplaying region with a close-knit community, for nations who do not use game stats and populations.

To join please apply on our regional forums.
