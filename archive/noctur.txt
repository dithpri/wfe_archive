[b]Welcome to [color=#3366FF]Noctur[/color]![/b] 

Noctur is a self-contained world, where nations are geographically and historically intertwined. The region features a diverse range of countries, from democracies to dictatorships, paradises to wastelands.

Noctur focusses on realism in roleplay, and is a close-knit community of players.

To join please apply on our regional forums.
