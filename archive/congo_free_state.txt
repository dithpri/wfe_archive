[color=blue]The Congo Free State was a large state in Central Africa from 1885 to 1908, which was in personal union with the Kingdom of Belgium under Leopold II. The Congo Free State operated as a corporate state privately controlled by Leopold II through a Non-governmental organization, the International Association of the Congo.[/color]
[hr]
[Color=blue]This region exists to serve as a memorial and model of the Congo Free State.[/color]
