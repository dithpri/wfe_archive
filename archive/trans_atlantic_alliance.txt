The Trans-Atlantic Alliance is an alliance of nations from all corners of the Atlantic Ocean. Our main goal is to protect and benefit each other by providing assistance and a place to discuss things on a regional level here.

Vice-Presidential Place of Origin: [nation]The Anti-Kebab State of Serbia[/nation]

The regional forums for interaction with other regional members can be found [url=http://transaa.forumotion.com/]here.[/url]

The offices of the government are elected whenever a vacancy is created by the current holder resigning or becoming inactive (essentially dying in office).
