The [b]Union of [region]Nova Universo[/region][/b] is a supranational union of ten polities in Earth (Sol 3), Heimat (Tau Ceti f) and the observable Multiverse. The Union is an international middle power with democratic, humanist, pacifist and socialist leanings. Its motto is [b][i]supra et ultra[/i][/b] (above and beyond), its headquarters are in the [b]Entity of [nation=noflag]Nova Universo[/nation][/b] and its leader is [b]Queen Merita[/b].
