The [b]Union of [region]Nova Universo[/region][/b] is a regional intergovernmental organisation. It functions as a single area in social, economic, environmental, and military areas. It has nine member states, seven in Earth (Terra) and one in Tau Ceti b (Gaia). It also has two member entities dispersed in the observable Universe.

The Union is headed by a constitutional monarch who observes the executive, legislative and judicial institutions of the union. They also command the union&#39;s military and royal institutions. 

The member nations of the Union are [nation=noflag]Nova Tero[/nation], [nation=noflag]Nova Planedo[/nation], [nation=noflag]Vaslyria[/nation], [nation=noflag]Vialnike[/nation], [nation=noflag]Maharib[/nation], [nation=noflag]Masariq[/nation], [nation=noflag]Andadura[/nation] and [nation=noflag]Banuwa[/nation]. The member entities of the Union are [nation=noflag]Nova Diasporo[/nation] and [nation=noflag]Nova Universo[/nation].
