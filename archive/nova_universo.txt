The [b][region]Union of Nova Universo[/region][/b] is a supranational political and economic union of 10 member states located on Earth (Sol 3), Heimat (Gliese 273b) and other bodies of the observable Multiverses. The Union has an area of 2.750.167 sq km and a population of over 603 mio as of 2025. The Union is described as a sui generis political entity combining characteristics of both a federation and a confederation.
