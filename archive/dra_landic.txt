In the lands of Dra, a once mighty nation fell, leaving the region fragmented and in chaos. But a group of visionary leaders emerged, uniting the countries under a common cause to establish the Dra Landic region. Through cooperation and unity, the region flourished, overcoming past divisions and building a brighter future for its people. The story of the Dra Landic region serves as a testament to the power of collaboration in the face of adversity.
