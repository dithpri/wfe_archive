A long time ago in a Galaxy far, far, away...
The year is 0 BBY. Civil War has spread across the Galaxy.
Soon after the First Galactic Empire&#39;s collapse, A group of Imperial Loyalists scrambled to reform a seond Galactic Empire. With the Republic and Empire locked in a bloody conflict, The Galaxy will be sure to change drastically...

FACTIONS
- The Galactic Republic/Rebel Alliance - A small band of Rebels fighting to restore the Galactic Republic and Jedi Order.
- The Galactic Empire - The Second Galactic Empire, a reformed version of the prior empire locked in a bloody conflict with the Rebel Alliance.
- Ewok Tribes - In the Unknown Regions, The Forest Moon of Endor is a neutral region, Inhabited by various Ewok Tribes.
