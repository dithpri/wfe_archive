Neo Indonesia (Neo Indonesien), or officially the Federal Republic of Neo Indonesia, is a country in the most Southeast Asia. It is the second-most populous country in Southeast Asia after Indo-Malaya. Neo Indonesia is situated between the Java sea to the north, Banda sea to the northeast, and the Indian ocean to the south; covering an area of 143,069.6 square kilometres (55,239.48 sq mi), with a population of over 61 million within its 8 constituent states. It borders Indo-Malaya states to the north and west, Australia to the south by Indian ocean, and Papua Union to the east. The nation&#39;s capital and largest city is Churabaya, and its financial centre is Denpasar; the largest urban area is the Metro Churabaya (the state itself).
