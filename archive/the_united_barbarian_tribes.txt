In the beginning... The people of the region were immersed in conflict. Many groups of people were established and then destroyed. Anarchy consumed the lives of the people living there. The land was barren of knowledge, freedom, and prosperity. The only thing that mattered was brute strength, and how many weapons your people possessed. Eventually, one of the many tribes would gain complete control of the region but with such radical violence no one maintained power for long. Finally, the largest 9 tribes united under one banner to control the region once and for all. Will they succeed in their endeavors?
