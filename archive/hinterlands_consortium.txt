The Hinterlands Consortium was formed out of necessity upon the collapse of the former Delphiep region. The states were either consumed by the expansive Ugnar or were made resource colonies by the aggressive mountain conquerors. Far disconnected from the madness of central world politics, the Hinterland Consortium serves as a puppet body for Ugnari political goals.

Life in the Hinterlands is rough, but effort and time rewards those with patience and determination
