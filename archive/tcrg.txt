The TCRG is a alliance of both TCRG sister nations (Not puppet nations) and nations who wish to become members of the United Federation of the TCRG. All member nations must follow these regulations and regional laws, They must maintain a standing military of at least 2,000,000 personnel, They must not be members of the WA (World Assembly) They must use the CRD or ULC as there official currency, They must have both free trade and open boarders with other member nations, They must maintain a mutual deference alliance with each other and support alliance with the TCRG.
