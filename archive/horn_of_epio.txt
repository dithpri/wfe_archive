//Geography//

The horn of Epio is a moderatly fertile area in the great continent of Epio (Present day africa), Its terrain its pretty plain with some Hills where the main powers of the Area built their castles, it also has a great mountain range called Yuruurma.

It is connected to Armel (Present day Arabia) by a great land passage which is controlled at the time of writting by Ormani

//Politics//

At the time of writting, these are the nations that are in this Region:

Ormani
Estiop
Yelko

Currently, the region is pretty peaceful, although, with tensions between Ormani and Estiop, which have made their nation&#39;s relations a but hostile, Yelko is neutral to both of them.
