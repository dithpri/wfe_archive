The Region of Levsnia was founded on 3rd April, 2020 by the newly founded nation Levsnia. It was born out of a great struggle against capitalism-imperialism and foreign domination. 

The Socialist Party of Levsnia is the ruling party of the Republic of Levsnia. Its goal is to build socialism through stages in Levsnia.

Great Leader David Williams is the pioneer and leader of the movement.
