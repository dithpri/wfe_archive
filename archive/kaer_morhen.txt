Kaer Morhen an old keep where witchers used to be trained. The name is a corruption of the Caer a&#39;Muirehen, which means &quot;Old Sea Keep&quot; in Elder Speech due to the presence of fossilized sea creatures embedded in the stones on which it was built. It is located in the mountains of the kingdom of Kaedwen, right off the Gwenllech river

This region is meant to be an NS projection of the in-game castle. It is a place for nations to train, to age, and to gain influence.
