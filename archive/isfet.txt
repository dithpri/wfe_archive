During the Ruination, all the wrath of the Ancients fell upon Isfet. Cities that were spread out over hundreds of kilometers turned to glass. The oceans half-vaporized - and their moisture escaped into Space. The ruins of cities and the waters that remained were enshrouded in The Ooze, absorbing the remnants of the planet&#39;s biomass. Both air and soil are poisoned. The planet&#39;s space itself is warped by unknown weapons.
