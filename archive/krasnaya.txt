A former glorious region reborn as an outpost.


[region]Krasnaya[/region] is an outpost under chapter 3 martial law under Olgienslav law. is now an extension of [region]Olgienslavia[/region] and was refounded after it ceased to exist. Its previous owners were the former nations of the region who migrated else where. 

Note: Those looking to make embassies with this region shall instead make embassies with the home region instead.
