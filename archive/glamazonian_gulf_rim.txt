Ocean surrounding the Glamazonian Peninsula, the Chanel Sea, the Gulf of Glamazonia and the northern frontier beyond Wintour. It is a region of ports, shipping lanes, volcanic memory, sacred geography, strategic rivalry and spectacular climatic contrast.

The Rim is known for the meeting of cold northern systems and warmer maritime currents. Its coasts carry trade, migration, defence, diplomacy and myth; its interior contains jungle, upland, river country, civic cities and sacred landscapes.
