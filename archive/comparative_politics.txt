Comparative Politics is a vast and mysterious region located on the southeastern edge of the continent of Loria, a land known for its diverse climates, ancient cultures, and striking natural beauty. The region is bordered by the Lorian Sea to the north, the impenetrable Ardin Mountains to the west, and the dense, humid forests of the Verdant Expanse to the east. Comparative Politics itself is a land of great contrasts&#151;majestic deserts, lush wetlands, and towering volcanic islands, all forming a backdrop to the region&#146;s rich history of exploration, conquest, and cultural blending.
