The region of Layaven is a war torn region with very radical governments and people, the nations of the region are nearly constantly fighting in bloody border wars. Although these wars have recently started to slow down with the creation of the Tribunal Of Layaven which is a light union of all of the nations of Layaven which is trying to help work out there differences with each other. The Tribunal has around 250,000 ROL peace keeping troops as of now 50,000 of which are located in the TNLR to help protect and rebuild the nation and another 50,000 are located in the SROL to help protect the nation
