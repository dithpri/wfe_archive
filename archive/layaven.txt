The region of Layaven is a war torn region with very radical governments and people, the nations of the region are nearly constantly fighting in bloody border wars. Although these wars have recently started to slow down with the creation of the Tribunal Of Layaven which is a light union of all of the nations of Layaven which is trying to help work out there differences with each other. The Tribunal has around 50,000 peace keeping troops located all round Layaven to help keep peace between the nations.
