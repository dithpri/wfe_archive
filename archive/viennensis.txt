Welcome! 

[b]Viennesis[/b] is a continent located 3,400 miles off Eastern Maredoratica, situated in the equatorial region. Home to a variety of cultures and histories Viennensis is intended to expand and enrich the histories of Maredoratican nations whilst providing another area in which regional members can pursue realistic and community-driven Roleplay. Anyone is eligible to apply for territory in this continent. 

To lodge claims, contact [nation]Hardenburgh[/nation] by telegram with your map claim and all relevant information including GDP, Population and a brief overview of your concept.
