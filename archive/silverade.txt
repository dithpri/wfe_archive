The Silverade Region is one the most peaceful regions environmentally, featuring vast plains of green, some hills and a large river that separates the region in half. The first settlers were supposed to be a small community of natives that was cruelly annihilate by the later conquistadors, which stablished a colony that grow into what is known today as the city of La Heroica, capital of the Armed Republic of Sanducia. The first colony also attracted many immigrants from all around the globe, as Sanducia is one of the biggest nations on the region, many others claimed independence and sovereignty, which caused a state of constant warlord warfare and fragile peace treaties.
