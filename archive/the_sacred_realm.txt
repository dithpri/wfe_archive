The Sacred Real is a mystical and hidden land he three Goddesses Din the Goddess Of Power,Farore The Goddess of Nature and Nayru The Goddess of Wisdom created. The Sacred Realm was created as a place where only the pure and divine may enter. This land is the remnant of the beauty and paradise that existed before the true land was corrupted by the human race. only one sole Kingdom which holds all the inhabitants of The Sacred Realm. This is the Kingdom of Auroura
