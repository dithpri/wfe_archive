Faeroe Islands are an island group between Scotland and Iceland.
Part of the Kingdom of Denmark.
An autonomous province of Denmark since 1 Apr 1948 (Home rule).
The Faeroe Islands have close ties to Iceland, Shetland, Orkney and Outer Hebrides.
Controlled by Denmark since 1388.

FACTS
Capital: Torshavn
Area: 1399 sq km
Population: 48,856 (2009 est)
