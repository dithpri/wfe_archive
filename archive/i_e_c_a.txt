I.E.C.A.
i&egrave;ir economic cooperation area, is an economic transnational organization created to facilitate trade, technological and scientific cooperation between nations sharing the i&egrave;ir currency.
The I.E.C.A was born as a way to favour a reconciliation between sahsahlah and the breakaway archipelago of the sodoro islands.
