New Pangea is a place where all nations may come to no matter what type of government the said nation runs. Nations in New Pangea can sit back and relax, enjoy interesting and realistic role-plays, participate in regional government, help run the regional newspaper, and just have a good time in general. New Pangea offers the best time you can possibly have while playing NationStates.
