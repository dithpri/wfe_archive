Erenoth, from the language of the Dwarrow, [b]The World[/b], is a world of dark lords and noble knights, of gallant kings and cowardly courtiers, and everything between them. A realm of [b]Traditional High Fantasy[/b], as inspired by the likes of The Lord of the Rings and Dungeons and Dragons moreso than the likes of The Witcher or Demon/Dark Souls.

Owned and governed by [nation=short]Estainia[/nation], this region is a Subsidiary of [region]Rostil[/region]
