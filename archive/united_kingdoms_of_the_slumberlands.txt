The United Kingdoms of SlumberLands

Four countries, the Dictatorship of Oogrukistan, The Matriarchy of the Siren Isle, the Kingdom of Vampiric Elysia, and the Commonwealthy of JNPR, have all had various problems involving that of Economics, Politics, and The Judicial System. But, during a diplomatic meeting amongst these nations, the subject of forming an Alliance, came up in the conversation. Following this segment of diplomacy, each nation had decided to create a mutual region known as the United Kingdoms of the SlumberLands. With this alliance set in place, these formerly self-sufficient nations will gain the ability to create a large, prosperous territory, that has the potential to accomplish great things.
