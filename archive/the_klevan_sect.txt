The Klevan Sect is a region established near cynosure. Split apart and torn by war, the warlords of the sect finally came to an agreement to set aside their differences and live in peace. The Sect has many rich regions, such as New Klevsia, the Klussian United Duchies and the Krussian Imperium Atredies. The poorer nations, however, struggle to keep up due to a lack of aid and good relations with the other nations. Will you struggle like them? Or will you thrive in this mysterious region?
