This Region is a high-fantasy multi-Species haven. Our aim is to provide casual fantasy roleplay without having to tie it down to a specific fandom.

The region is unique in that unclaimed plots of land have designated NPC nations to roleplay with, run by the founder using the [nation]Non Player Character[/nation]. Have a look at the [url=https://www.nationstates.net/page=dispatch/id=1130585] Official species [/url] and [url=https://www.nationstates.net/nation=snooty_dragons/detail=factbook/id=1167366] lore [/url] of the region!
