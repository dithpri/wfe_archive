The Evorassian Federation, or just Evorasia, is a country at the eastern end of Drasia, bordered by Evusia to the north, east and west and Barevusia to the south. It is a country without access to the sea, and considered an underdeveloped nation, with an HDI of 0.658 considered average, and with a per capita income of 7,858 PcTas, making it considered a low-middle-income country, but there are good signs, as its GDP grows 4.7% per year. It is one of the 10 countries where nuclear weapons are housed, with Evorasia currently having 190 bombs.
