Uhuhland is named after the legendary [url=http://www.nswiki.net/index.php?title=Uhuh]Uhuh[/url]. Large populations of several varieties of this animal used to live in the region, but sadly their numbers are decreasing due to hunting and pollution.

[region]Snootland[/region] is a small continent consisting of many small islands to the west of Uhuhland. The [region]Far Far Away[/region] islands in the east are geographically part of the Uhuhland continent, but they want to be different, or something.
