A union of three nations. The Dingo Republic, The Federal Republic of Traeria and the Republic of NO pootiN.

The flag of the union represents the three nations of the Dingo Republic, Traeria and N0 pootiN.
The green represents the countries vast eco-system, the red is to remember those who sacrificed themselves for all three nations to shape the nations today and the black represents the obscure past of the nations history through civil and regional wars. The blue circle represents the rivers and oceans and in the middle is a White Eagle that stands for freedom, equality and prosperity of the citizens of each nation and the region put together.
