[B] People Liberation Acting Nation of Technology[/B]

Technical Information
Each PLANT-type space colony has the appearance of a large hourglass. The disc-shaped areas at the two ends make up the living area of the colonies, with each disc having a diameter of ten kilometers. 

Government
The government of the PLANT cities is the PLANT Supreme Council on Aprilius One which was originally formed to deal with the UN directorate of PLANT-sponsored nations.

Military
ZAFT is a military organization and the official military of the PLANT colonies. It is essentially a militia made up of volunteers, controlled by the PLANT Supreme Council.
