The Constitution of Bennett Club:
1. Polls are binding if at least 50% of the membership voted for that result
2. A decision to appoint or dismiss an Executive Officer may be overturned by a poll
3. If at least 15% of the membership call for a poll, it must be held
4. The Constitution may only be amended if at least 65% of the membership vote in favour 
5. All nations in Bennett Club have freedom of expression and opinion

If you wish to join, please send an application to our Admin and/or Security and Homeland Officer
