Resdayn is the Ancient name for the Province now known as Morrowind. This Land Considered Alien and Inhospitable by most is the Home of the Dunmer People. Proud and Xenophobic the Dunmer are unwelcoming to most Outsiders and are a very Tradition bound Culture. The Province is Home to the island of Vvardenfell which houses Red Mountain. The Largest Volcano in all of Tamriel.
