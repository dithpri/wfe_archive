The world of Exosiania is one of instability and fragile economics. Who will rise from the ashes of the old Crowsican Empire, a collapsed empire that was once the superpower of the world, and take the lead as the supreme superstate of this world? Ride on the stallion of glory and try to make it in this dark age. This world is set up in a 1930s world where revolution killed the one empire close to unifiying the planet.
