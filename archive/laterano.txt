[nation]Terran Laterano[/nation], formally known as the Lateran City, is a country bordering [nation]Terran Iberia[/nation] and [nation]Terran Victoria[/nation]. It is a theocracy and the homeland of the Sankta race with a decent population of Liberi.

The nation is ruled by the Pope, the spiritual leader of the Lateran Church, with a constitution that guarantees thirteen rights to its citizens.
