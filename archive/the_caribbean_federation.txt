For Nations: 1. Based on the RL Caribbean and Neighbors or is small enough to be a realistic but fictional addition to the geography; and 2. Wanting to join a non-binding Compact of Association either as an Observer, Partial Associate, or Full Associate.

The RMB is to be OOC and once enough citizens arrive we shall make an offsite forum and constitution.

The Caribbean Federation Bank and its CFB Dollar is the official currency of regional affairs and is pegged in perpetuity to the NationStates Dollar at $1 NS to $2.03 CFB. All citizens are urged to make the CFB Dollar the official or co-official currency; however, it is not mandatory.
