 The Soviet Union, officially the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics, abbreviated as the USSR, is a socialist state in Eurasia that exists from 1922 to forever. The Soviet Union is a union of soviet republics (soviet means &quot;council&quot;, referring to a democratically-elected workers council) is a union of multiple national Soviet republics, still its government and economy are democratically centralized. The country is an multiple party state with Moscow as its capital in its largest republic, the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic. It is a founding permanent member of the United Nations Security Council and the Warsaw Pact.
