An anarchist, specifically anarcho-primitivist and green anarchist region focusing on ending civilization in favor of saving the Earth and on cleaning up the mistakes of the left in favor of liberation of humanity.

Works of [url=https://theanarchistlibrary.org/category/author/henry-david-thoreau]Henry David Throeau[/url], [url=https://theanarchistlibrary.org/category/author/john-zerzan]John Zerzan[/url], [url=https://theanarchistlibrary.org/category/author/bob-black]Bob Black[/url], [url=https://theanarchistlibrary.org/category/author/jason-mcquinn]Jason McQuinn[/url] and [url=https://theanarchistlibrary.org/category/author/hakim-bey]Hakim Bey[/url] outline what we believe in well. [url=https://theanarchistlibrary.org/category/author]Other anarchist authors[/url] are also recommended by us.

All anarchists are welcome in here. Allied with the [region]Galactic Union of Worker Republics[/region].
