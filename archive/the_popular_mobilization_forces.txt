The Popular Mobilization Forces is an Iraqi state-sponsored umbrella organization composed of some 40 ethnically/religiously mixed Iraqi civil brigades. 

The People&amp;#39;s Mobilization was formed for deployment of civilians to help defend the Iraqi nation and fight alongside the Iraqi Army against the Islamic State.
