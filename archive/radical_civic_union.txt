[colour=#960018]Established in 2025, this region is founded on a strong commitment to centrist and right-wing conservative values, emphasizing tradition, national sovereignty, responsible governance, economic stability, and a balanced approach to social and cultural issues. On social and cultural matters, the region upholds a traditional and principled approach, safeguarding individual liberties while reinforcing the shared values, customs, and institutions that form the foundation of its communities. This commitment seeks to preserve social cohesion, instill a strong sense of civic duty, and foster active engagement in securing a stable, prosperous, and morally grounded future.[/colour]
