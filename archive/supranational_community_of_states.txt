The Supranational Community of States is a union of all kinds of states under the auspices of the Astlantropanic Empire.

The aim of our Union is peaceful cooperation between its members and mutual protection. 

All members of the Union shall decide on a democratic basis on any law of the Union and on the position of the Union vis-&agrave;-vis non-member States. 


Attention: Every membership must be decided by the Council of the Union whether it becomes valid or not!
