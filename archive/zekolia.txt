[b][color=41A9C9]=[/color][/b] [b][color=555C5F]About Zekolia[/color][/b] [b][color=41A9C9]=[/color][/b]

Zekolia is the land of many. With the first settlers arriving in what we call [url=https://www.nationstates.net/nation=karfinzeal]Karfinzeal[/url], the continent has transformed into a gargantuan, bustling region. Originally known as Zeelandia and later Koldaya, Zekolia is the fusion of both in a complete and final way. From the beautiful rain forests of [url=https://www.nationstates.net/nation=orocdon]Orocdon[/url] and [url=https://www.nationstates.net/nation=leask]Leask[/url], to the rugged coasts of [url=https://www.nationstates.net/nation=cantautahi]Cantautahi[/url], the stunning lakes of [url=https://www.nationstates.net/nation=quk]Quk[/url] and the massive megapolises of [url=https://www.nationstates.net/nation=vacarian]Vacarian[/url] and [url=https://www.nationstates.net/nation=athelsten]Athelsten[/url], you&#39;ll find everything here.
