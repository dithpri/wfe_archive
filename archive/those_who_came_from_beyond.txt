In the foreboding darkness from BEYOND, where cosmic horrors dwell and roam, a coalition of eldritch abominations assembles, drawn together by an insidious purpose. From the stygian depths of space and the boundless voids between dimensions, they emerge as a nightmarish amalgamation of grotesque forms and malevolent minds, their very existence defying the comprehension of mortal minds.

This coalition of entities from BEYOND, united in their insatiable desire to spread across the multiverse, seeks to unravel the fabric of reality itself. Their motives beyond mortal comprehension, they loom like an ever-present cosmic tempest, ready to unleash an eternity of terror upon the unsuspecting cosmos.
