When the noble North Koreans where digging in the ground, they discovered a portal leading to a mysterious group of islands. The biggest island would soon be known as The Armed Republic of Arctikan. They fearless North Koreans teamed up with the Nazis and the KKK to urbanize these majestic islands. Law and order was set in place and the      natives were made into slaves. 190 days later new leaders began claiming islands and now a thriving region has been erected.
