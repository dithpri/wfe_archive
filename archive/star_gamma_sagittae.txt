&amp;#947; Sagittae is a variable hypergiant star of spectral class M0 in the constellation of Sagitta. &amp;#947; Sagittae visual magnitude is 3.47. Because of its moderate brightness, &amp;#947; Sagittae is easily visible to the naked eye from locations with dark skies, while it might be barely visible, or not visible at all, from light-polluted skies.
