The [b]Akertic Empire[/b] comprises the dominions, colonies, protectorates and other territories ruled or administered by [nation]Mehji[/nation]. It originated with the interstellar possessions and trading posts established by Mehji at the dawn of space colonization.

The Empire is ruled by the Grace of the Gods His Majesty VI King of Mehji, Baldwin, Emperor of Ienpu, Guardian of Souls, Lord-Admiral of Centar.

Each Akertic Nation is governed by its [u]Prince-Regent[/u], but elects by popular vote members to the [u]Imperial Forum[/u] to ensure the People&amp;#39;s inalienable rights as outlined in the [i]People&amp;#39;s Proclamation of the Akertic Isles[/i].
