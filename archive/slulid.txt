The SLULID region is a role-playing region that takes place off planet in an Earth-like system known as Pacifica in the year 2119. Humans have colonized the entire solar system containing Earth, as well as Alpha Centauri, where Pacifica is located. 

Your nation can be modern, but we ask that, if you join, you create a futuristic, space-oriented factbook specific to your country set in the year 2119. Think of it as a separate nation to the nation you already own and possess, just set in these parameters. This is so role-playing runs smoother and easier.
