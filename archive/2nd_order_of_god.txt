The (2nd) Order of God is a militant, theocratic union of sovereign nations united under the banner of divine warfare and holy supremacy. Founded upon the sacred doctrines of militant faith, this region serves as a bastion of Christian zeal, religious unity, and uncompromising moral order. Its mission: to defend the sanctity of Christendom, purge heresy, and bring the light of divine truth to the godless corners of NS.
