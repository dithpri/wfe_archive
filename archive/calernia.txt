Calernia&#39;s heroes have perished, no longer do they shine their light. And while good died evil flourished. First the free kingdoms fell then the faith collapsed. Finally the trade association was consumed by the hungry armies from the south. All nations fell and for a thousand years the Dread Empire ruled.

But things are changing a recent civil war has torn the old empire apart. New technologies and weapons have been introduced. The world is no longer as black and white as it used to be.
