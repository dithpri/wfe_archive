A trading alliance formed 29th June 2013 from the successor union IRSU. This alliance is a trading alliance.
The soviet socialist states of San Marino island had decided to open the alliance due to the closure of IRSU which closed because of preventing future corruption and because both nations had made the end goal of a strong economy 
ISTA guarantees the following:

Trading amongst other nations
Trading will guarantee a stronger economy 
We back nations in our alliance in war

Presidency: The united soviet socialist states of San Marino island.
June 2013- present
