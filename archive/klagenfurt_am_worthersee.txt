[region]Klagenfurt am Worthersee[/region] is the capital of the federal state of Carinthia in [region]Oesterreich[/region] (Austria). With a population of over 97,000, it is the sixth-largest city in the country. Elevated 446 metres above sea level and covers an area of 120.03 square kilometres. It is on the lake W&ouml;rthersee and on the Glan River. The city is surrounded by several forest-covered hills and mountains with heights of up to 1,000 metres.
