The Union of Fatmanic Nations (UFN) generally known as the Fatimanophonie (Fatiman: Le Fatimanienphonie French: Le fatimanophonie) but also called International Commonwealth on Fatimanic Language Family (ICFLF) in Reports to the President Papers, is an international organization representing countries and regions where Fatimanic (Language Family of Turkic, Slavic and Italic within Indo-European) is a Lingua Fatimanesca or customary language.
