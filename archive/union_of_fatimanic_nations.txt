Union of Fatimanic Nations | UFN

The Organisation for the Fatimanic Languages and Languages with Lingua Fatimanesca. This may Includes the Fatiman (Italo-Fatimanic), Dutch (Germano-Fatimanic), Rasmussenian (Germano-Fatimanic), Ramseyian (Fraconian Fatimanic), Robloxian (Within Eskimo-Fatimanic and Turko-Aleut) and Edeltian (Slavo-Fatimanic).

It has being Based from The Fatiman Peninsula but Powerful with an Single Nation that Operates the Union. Including Politics, Trade and Economy.
