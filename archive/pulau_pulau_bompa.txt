Pulau Pulau Bompa, or in English known as &quot;the Islands of Bompa&quot; is a fictional archipelago located in the Pacific Ocean, as part of the Indonesian Archipelago. Pulau Pulau Bompa has a tropic and oceanic climate. While seemingly isolated, the archipelago has been a known small producer of spices, particularly nutmegs. The archipelago has its own independent government, with the Council of Elders as the executive power of the region.
