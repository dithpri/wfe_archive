The Incorporated States of Stenogate formed from the wealthiest entities of lesser nations and founded itself in the Windcaller Archipelago, a chain of islands famous for their ridiculously large storms. Determined, the Stenogate set to work to make it an attractive hub of commerce alongside its fellow regional nations.

For a time, the regional office was usurped by Lily. Stenogate&#39;s military managed to retake the regional administration office with no casualties, and welcomes all to continue buying and selling across the region.
