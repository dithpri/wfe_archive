The tenets of the Region of Iskandria are as follows.

1. That all humans, regardless of Identity, Lineage, or Tradition, are born FREE and will be given the knowledge necessary to pursue their own destinies.

2. That Leaders and Laws are never absolute, and should the PEOPLE decide that corruption must be undone, neither Leader nor Law shall oppose them.

3. That our greatest achievements as a populace come as a result of communication and collaboration. Hence, shall all actions be considered in light of the GREAT MANY before the POWERFUL FEW.
