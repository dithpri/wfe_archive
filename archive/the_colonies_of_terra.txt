The Colonies of Terra are the human factions of the Milky Way galaxy, descended from the ancient homeworld of Terra, known to some as Earth. The Colonies have splintered since the collapse of the Terran government, and undiscovered alien races lurk in the shadows, prepared to consume the fledgling empires...

Hello, and welcome to the Colonies of Terra! Whether alien or human, any form of Future Tech nation is allowed in the Colonies: anarchist or statist, democratic or dictatorial, the Colonies is a region dedicated to helping Future Tech roleplayers hone their skills, devise the specifics of their style, and enjoy the company of other competitive roleplayers.
