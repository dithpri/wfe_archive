The Allied Nations of Libertas consists of several like minded nations. A treaty between all member nations prevents battle and also declares that in time of war the Allied Nations must aid each other.

The Allied Nations of Libertas tend to be liberal although can be at times conservative. The founding nation -Rodicia- has two sister nations: Italian Polynesia and New Hasburg.

The Allied Nations of Libertas also runs role plays and spam games.

All nations are welcome!
