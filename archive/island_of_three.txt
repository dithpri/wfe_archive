The Island of Three or Akright&amp;#39;s Triangle are three separate islands around about the same size and shape of Tasmania, Australia that are almost connected into a triangle shape with each tip of the island pointing away from the rich coral reef in the centre.
The three islands are called the North Island (to the North), Golden Island (to the South West) and Tlaloc Island (to the South East)
North Island is the location of the Island of Three&amp;#39;s only nation, Demtona and the great Capital, Encino Fortress. The Golden Island is home to the largest colony of Golden Dove&amp;#39;s on the planet, Tlaloc is an Aztec god of rain, fertility and water; Tlaloc Island is one lush forest filled with wildlife and constant storms.
