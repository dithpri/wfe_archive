A large and beautiful region, The Grand Frost is a rather chilly area. With an average high of 18 degrees Celsius (65 Fahrenheit) in the Summer and an average low of -19 Celsius (-3 Fahrenheit) in Winter, most nations can brave the colder climate. There are many tundras and stunning boreal forests in The Grand Frost. Despite it&#39;s climate, The Grand Frost has a wide variety of flora and fauna including several Draconian species and, man&#39;s best friend, the Greater Dreadwolf.
