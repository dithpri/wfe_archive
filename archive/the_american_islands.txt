[b][color=mediumblue]The American Islands[/color] [color=darkred](TAI)[/color][/b] is an archipelago roleplay region, representing independent nations of many ideals. Each nation is located on an island, and maintains full sovereignty over that island.

This is a region focused on culture, economy and trade.
