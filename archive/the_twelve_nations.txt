In the vast World of Pantanora, there is a populous Empire, Kalmestar, whose Head is Urgos-Lorbik Vierzex, a Zohar-Uri. 

The Empire of Kalmestar was built and founded over a hundred years ago, and since then, many Nations and Kingdoms have joined Kalmestar, becoming allies and trade partners. Since its founding, Kalmestar has become well known throughout the world, obtaining the nickname of &quot;Luscious Light&quot; due to its wide diversity in plants and wildlife.
