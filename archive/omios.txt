This moderately-sized continent stretches across several climate regions, from Polar in the North, through to Tropical in the South.
The continent sits close to the edge of a tectonic plate; a large mountain range stretches across the middle of the land to the North-Eastern edge.
Omios has a few active volcanoes, and does not experience any earthquakes.
The winds in this region are usually relentless, with large storms and extreme weather being the norm.
The seas to the East and West of Omios are wild, while the seas to the North and South are calmer.
Omios is home to a vast amount of unique plants and animals that are found only in this region.
