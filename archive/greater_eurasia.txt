The Region of Greater Eurasia is founded on 24 December 2012 after passing of a constitutional law proposed by the Secretariat Office of the Imperial Commission of Foreign Affairs (ICFA) of the Edderalish Empire. The purpose of the formation of Greater Eurasia is the unite countries within the Eurasian sphere of influence and also smaller countries drifting within the sea under the Eurasian sphere of influence.

The Greater Eurasia will be look after by the newly formed Greater Eurasian Union (GEU), currently headed by the Secretary of Foreign Affairs of the Edderalish Empire Marcus Y. F. Dong.
