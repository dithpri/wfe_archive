[color=navy][b]Cape Verde[/b][hr]A republic located on an archipelago off the western coast of Africa.
The previously uninhabited islands were discovered and colonized by the Portuguese in the fifteenth century.
The country is named after Cap Vert (meaning Green Cape) in Senegal, the westernmost point of continental Africa.
