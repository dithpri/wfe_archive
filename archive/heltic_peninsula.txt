Southeastern peninsula of the continent of [i]Usia[/i]. Characterized by flat plains and lush jungles along side a beautiful mountain range. The peninsula is surrounded by all sides except the northeast by the [i]Heltic Sea[/i]. The nations inhabiting the region are [i]Hedan[/i], [i]Schlotcovia[/i], [i]Sucondee[/i], and [i]Sidera et Luminaria[/i].
