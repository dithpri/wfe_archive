Xasha is an Imperial Region, led by HIRM Emperor Dormac Onlinger. Each kingdom or nation within the region will have one representative on the Emperor&#39;s Imperial Council of Advisers. Additional Council members will be voted in by the sitting members of the Council. Membership on the Council is for life, unless a member is found to be unfit for the position and responsibility. 

Xasha is a progressive region, firmly believing in the rights of all humans, regardless of race, religion, nationality, sexual orientation, gender, or disability.
