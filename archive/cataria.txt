Cataria, the ancient lands once roamed all over by the Elctra Cat (felis cattus electria), an animal which now resides only in the Isles of Cat. This region boasts lush meadows and thick deciduous and pine forests as well as the occasional mountain range and rainforest. North of the Lakis region, the countryside is well known for the largest mountain range in the West. The Appachia Mountains run through gold mines of the West, and long beaches along the East. The largest city, Monstr&eacute;, is located in (West) Meercroft States of Myrvision, bordering with the True Republic of East Meercroft and the states of Zhakarakhggaia. The regional hub, and territorial capital under the rule of Myrvision is Montaria, located in Meercroft.
