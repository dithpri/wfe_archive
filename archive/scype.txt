[color=purple]Welcome to [b]Scype[/b]![/color]

An Old English word for &quot;society&quot;, &quot;fellowship&quot; or &quot;brotherhood&quot;, Scype is a confederated union of nations who base their philosophy on the basis of human rights, equality and freedom. Founded on 25/02/2017, Scype aims to be an active role playing and discussion region with a regional map, role playing date and places for all nations to reside and have fun!
