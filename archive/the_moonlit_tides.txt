A moderate ocean, This region contains few Islands and archipelagos that have extreme differences in their ideologies . With a large religious and spiritual population,  with many nations acknowledging  spirit and other paranormal entities. Some, like The Mystic Oasis, even invite them into the military and have a special law enforcement program built specifically for exorcism and banishing evil spirits. Most nations mix these beliefs with Christianity, forming the region most common religion, Spiritualist Christianity. In the centre of all the counties, a submarine is used to house the tribunal of the region, where they leaders of countries collaborate to discuss important issues like the illegal attack by one country on another.
