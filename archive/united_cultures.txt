[b]The region of [region]United Cultures[/region] was founded on September 26th, 2012, by [nation]Jamocha[/nation]. This region supports both peace and the environment; however, it recognizes nations of all political and ethical backgrounds and believes that these differences create a variety of cultures that would otherwise not exist.[/b]

[b][region]United Cultures[/region] is, in addition, a simple region that values newly created nations. Its ambition is that these nations will spread useful information and help one another, thus together learning how the world of NationStates operates.[/b]
