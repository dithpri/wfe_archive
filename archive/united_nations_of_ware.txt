RULES: 
Whenever you join, you will state your name on the board at the bottom of the page within the first 24 hours of which you join the United Nations of Ware. Failure to abide by this rule will result in the offending nation(s) to be ejected. If you cannot post on the board, send Twrz a telegram including your name. Twrz will then post it for you.

You are to send a request by telegram to join to Twrz. Passwords will be hidden to members of the United Nations of Ware to avoid hijackers and strangers/unwanted peoples.

You can only have one nation in the UNoW. If you fail to abide by this rule, both nations can be ejected.
