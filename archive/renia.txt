The Renish Alliance (Renia) was officially established in 1991 by North Renin who then invited its former colony the Republic of Mendine in 1993 to be a regional member state and to hold the permanent position of regional secretary general. The Kingdom of Adal Sakan has applied for Renia membership since 2016 and was only admitted into the alliance in 2017.
The Renish Alliance headquarters are in the 1991 built North Renin city of Renia located 2680km away from the Renish capital of Providence.
