The fight for equality and the protection of those in need are the main focuses of [region] The Socialist States of the Illawarra[/region]. These aims are mostly accomplished through peaceful diplomacy but force will be used if necessary.

[b]History[/b]
Once, long ago there existed a highly militaristic nation that dominated this region. After the introduction of socialist ideals, the malicious dictatorship collapsed. This tore the nation apart, separating it into the idealistic humanitarians of [nation]The Empty[/nation] and the radical extremists of [nation]The Full[/nation].
