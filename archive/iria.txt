[b]The Empire of Iria[/b]
[i]Liberty, Prudence, Justice.[/i][hr]Founded by the exiled remnants of a fallen civilization, the Empire of Iria encompasses an island continent home to beautiful environs, delectable foods, and arcane creations abound. Iria is a storied realm which provides its people with extensive civil and political rights while welcoming all who come in peace and with an open mind.[hr][b]A branch of a community dating back to 2006.[/b]
