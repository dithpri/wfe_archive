[b][color=red]Anarkadia[/color] is a [url=http://flag.blackened.net/liberty/libcom.html]libertarian communist[/url], [url=http://ita.anarchopedia.org/J.3_Che_forme_di_organizzazione_si_danno_gli_anarchici%3F#J.3.2_.09What_are_.22synthesis.22_federations.3F]synthesis federation[/url] which operates on the principles of autonomy, self-management, mutual aid, direct democracy, collective responsibility, and federalism.

We would like to show solidarity and support for a federation of Anarkadian migrants living in [nation]Hlubos[/nation] known as the [url=http://forum.nationstates.net/viewtopic.php?f=23&amp;amp;t=200927&amp;amp;start=25#p11040740]Hlubos Anarkadian Federation[/url].
