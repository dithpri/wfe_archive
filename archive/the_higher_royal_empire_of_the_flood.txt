The great Apostolic Holy Roman Empire of the Flood is the greater regional extent of the Flood, administered from the centre of the Empire in the Eternal City of Rome. The nations of this region are controlled and administered by the Imperial government of the Flood. 
All member states are absolute monarchies, subject to the Flood and the Catholic Church. All nations, and each of their citizens must pledge eternal, undying allegiance to the Emperor of the Flood and the Roman Pontiff. All member nations must legislate according to Catholic doctrines to obtain membership in the Flood&amp;#39;s glorious Holy Empire.
