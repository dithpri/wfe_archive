A greater Floodian Empire administered by the central home land, the Empire of the Flood. The nations of this region are controlled and administered by the Imperial government of the Flood. 
All members must be monarchies, and are subordinate to the Flood. All nations, and each of their citizens must pledge eternal, undying allegiance to the God-Emperor of the Flood. 
The Higher Royal Empire offers glory and salvation to all who come under its influence, in return the Emperor requires only that you kneel, and serve the Empire devoutly. Non adherents will be destroyed.
