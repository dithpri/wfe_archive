The great Holy Roman Empire of the Flood, administered from the centre of the Empire in the capital city of Rome. The nations of this region are controlled and administered by the Imperial government of the Flood. 
All member states must be monarchies, and are subordinate to the Flood. All nations, and each of their citizens must pledge eternal, undying allegiance to the Emperor of the Flood. All member nations must legislate according to Catholic doctrines to obtain membership in the Flood.
