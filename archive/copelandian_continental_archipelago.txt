The C.C.A. (Copelandian Continental Archipelago) is a group of land masses located in the southern region of the 
 Theyhateese sea. This group of land masses consists of a micro continent (Copelandia) spanning 5,982,898 square kilometers        
 in size, and a closely neighboring archipelago consisting of many islands of varying sizes. The micro continent is fully inhabited by the capitalist libertarian nation the&quot;armed republic of Copelandia&quot; the current economic powerhouse of the region
while the neighboring archipelago remains relatively uninhabited, with many of its islands being open to anyone willing to settle on them.
