The region of Northern Europa is a safe region where nations can enjoy many fun things such as role-playing on the regions forum, reading the region newspaper, and participating in the regions government. If you want to have the most fun while playing in NationStates, join the region of Northern Europa. 
Requirements to Join: 
     ~ Move your nation into the region of Northern Europa. 
     ~ Join the World Assembly (WA). 
     ~ Endorse The Weimar States as your regional delegate by selecting the endorse button at the top of the Weimar States nation page. 
     ~ Have fun and enjoy the multitude of fun things that this region has to offer.
