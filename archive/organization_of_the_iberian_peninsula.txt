Organization of the Iberian Peninsula (OIP), is an organization made up of countries in the Iberian Peninsula. 

OIP was founded in 1986, in Barcelona, [nation]Generalitat Catalunya[/nation]. It was created after the Treaty of Badalona, which ensured that all Iberian states would remain united, but each state was its own sovereign entity.

Official Languages of the Organization of the Iberian Peninsula:
Spanish
English
Portuguese
Catalan
