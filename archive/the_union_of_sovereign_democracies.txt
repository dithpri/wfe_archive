The Union of Sovereign Democracies (USD) was founded by Animal Crackers in 1963 during the height of the cold war. It has since valued and pushed democratic ideals across the globe.

Goals:
*Push the limits of knowledge and science
*Install democracy across the globe
*Provide freedom to all citizens of all countries
*Help other democracies in their endeavors

Requirements:
*Average or better political freedom
*Good or better civil rights
*Must be a democracy
