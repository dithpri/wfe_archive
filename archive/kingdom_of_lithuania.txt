The [b]Kingdom of Lithuania[/b], or simply [b]Lithuania[/b] (Lithuanian: [i]Lietuva[/i]) is a Baltic country in Eastern Europe. It is bordered by [color=gold][i]Germany[/i][/color] to the west, [color=gold][i]Poland[/i][/color] and [color=gold][i]Ukraine[/i][/color] to the south, [color=gold][i]White[/i] [i]Ruthenia[/i][/color] to the east, and the [color=gold][i]United[/i] [i]Baltic[/i] [i]Duchy[/i][/color] to the north.
