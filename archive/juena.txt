Juena the 2nd planet in the Ossarian Solar System settled 5000 years ago and was once A thriving colonist of the human home world of earth. However after and abrupt cut in contact the colonists were left to fend for themselves against a hostile native environment. Originally ship were sent out to attempt to reconnect to their mother planet, the colonist soon lost home as many never returned and those that did limped back with no news. With failing power, communications systems and scant resources the colonists were forced to redirect their attention to making the planet their permanent home.
