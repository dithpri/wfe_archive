Juena the 2nd planet in the Ossarian Solar System settled 5000 years ago and was once A thriving colony of the human home world of Earth. However after a sudden and prolonged cut in contact with the home world the colony began to waver against the planet&#39;s hostile environment. A plan was hatched to send out ships to reestablish contact however after no success and with most ship only surviving to limp back home the colonist were forced to give up. As resources dwindled and the global communications system started to fail, the individual colonies were forced to fend for themselves and make what they could from the planet eventually drifting from contact completely.
