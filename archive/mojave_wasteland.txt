The Mojave Wasteland is the stretch of land from the west side of the Colorado River, all the way into California. despite being a desert, it has a large amount of resources when compared to the rest of the Wasteland and does support plenty of life even after the Great War. However all her inhabitants and resources are being tested after the nuclear fire of the great war leaves it in ruin in 2077 and with factions pushing in on the Mojave from all sides it is now a test of strength for the locals.. and the Mojave as 2282 draws near.
