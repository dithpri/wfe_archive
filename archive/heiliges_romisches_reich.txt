A Traditionalist Christian region based upon the Holy Roman Empire.

Kaiser: Austria and Bavaria
Pope: The Roman Diocese (Currently controlled by Austria and Bavaria)
Kings: Apostolic Hungary, Hesse and Prussia, Hauptskalreich, Hesse-Kessel.
Realms: The Greater Rhine.
Grand Duchies: Naples and Corsica.
Protectorates: Dalmatia-Slavonia (Austria and Bavaria), Wallonia and Flanders (Austria and Bavaria).
Military Orders: The Knights of Our Lady of Philermos, The Knights of Saint Mary of the Germans 

Electorates:
Austria and Bavaria, Apostolic Hungary, Hesse and Prussia, Hesse-Kessel, The Greater Rhine Nation and Hauptskalreich.
