A Traditionalist Christian region based upon the Holy Roman Empire.

Kaiser: Austria and Bavaria
Pope: The Roman Diocese (Currently controlled by Austria and Bavaria)
Kings: Apostolic Hungary, Hesse and Prussia, Hesse-Kessel, Hauptskalreich.
Grand Duchies: Marinshire.
Dominions: The Greater Rhine Nation.
Republics: Genoa.
Protectorates: Dalmatia-Slavonia (Austria and Bavaria), Wallonia and Flanders (Austria and Bavaria).

Electorates:
Austria and Bavaria, Apostolic Hungary, Hesse and Prussia, Hesse-Kessel, The Greater Rhine Nation and Marinshire.
