[i]An international regional coalition of like-minded Arab states.[/i]

The League of Arab States is an international regional coalition of like-minded Arab states. The League&amp;#39;s constitutional purpose is to &amp;quot;draw closer the relations between member States and co-ordinate collaboration between them, to safeguard their independence and sovereignty, and to consider in a general way the affairs and interests of the Arab countries&amp;quot;.
