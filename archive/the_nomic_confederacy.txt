The [i][url=http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nomic]Nomic[/url] Confederacy[/i] is the [url=http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Confederation]confederal[/url] [url=http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Constitution]constitutional[/url] government representing the various sovereign nations that have colonised the [url=http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alpha_Centauri]Alpha Centauri[/url] star system. Member nations retain their sovereignty but propose and vote on regional legislation that is binding to all member nations, and engages in inter-regional diplomacy through diplomatic missions to other regional powers.
