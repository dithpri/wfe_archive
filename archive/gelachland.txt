Gelachland, known to Europe as Old Atlantis, is an island archipelago in the Atlantic Ocean between Europe and North America. While preferrably authoritarian, the nations of Gelachland can be of all stripes, and the newly independent colony of Former British Gelachland is looking for new partners in oppressive crime. Come one, come all to chat and have tea, while the pesky citizenry are beaten outside your door!
