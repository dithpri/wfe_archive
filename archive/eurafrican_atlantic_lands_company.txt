
     After the fall of Costa da Europa and the Western Coastal African Union in the territorial war of 2016, the Atlantic countries were, once again, claimed by the nations that they had achieved thir freedom from, like Costa da Europa and Braga Novo to Portugal, Fallagastaland to Iceland, and New France to France. But, the nations achieved their freedom once again, and founded a new union called the Eurafrican Atlantic Lands Company, with Costa da Europa and former president, Michael Ruela, at the helm.
