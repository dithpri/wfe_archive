Federation of Socialist States
Founder: People&#39;s Republic of Nyberia  
Capital: Nyberia City  
Founded: March 17, 2011 (RP date)  
Status: Open for membership

The Federation of Socialist States is an alliance of nations committed to socialist development, sovereignty, and regional cooperation. Led by Nyberia, the federation promotes state-led planning, universal welfare, and peaceful collaboration. Membership is open to all nations that value collective progress and practical socialism.
