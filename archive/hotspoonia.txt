

[b][u]The Pledge of the Sacred Spoon[/u].[/b]


I pledge allegiance
to the Flame and the Bowl,
and to the Spoons for which we stand&#151;
one Region, under Heat,
indivisible,
with spice and sizzle for all.

I honor the curve of steel and silver,
the vessel of broth, chili, and daring sauces.
May our handles stay steady,
our soups stay scorching,
and our courage never cool.

In ladles we trust,
in teaspoons we measure,
in tablespoons we triumph.
From the mildest warmth
to the fiercest inferno,
we rise&#151;
burning bright,
spoon in hand. 

And to those about to spoon; 
We salute you.
