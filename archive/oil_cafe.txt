[color=purple][b]Oil Cafe, the Fourth Oil Region[/b][i]
When the leader Oil came back after the fall of Oleum Dictatura, he came back with a new heart. He wanted to create a new cafe, and that he did, creating the now famous oil cafe among residents of not just Oil, but also another region, [region]Callista[/region]. However, Oil Cafe, after the founder account CTE&#39;d, was attacked and invaded by raiders, and although the region was restored with the founder account being restored, the cafe was still decimated. Nevertheless Oil Cafe was the oil themed region filled with the most activity, and thus it shall be memorialized among oil history forever.[/i][/color]
