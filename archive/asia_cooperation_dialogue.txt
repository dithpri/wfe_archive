[color=red][b]Welcome to Asia Cooperation Dialogue![/b][/color]

The Asia Cooperation Dialogue (ACD) is an intergovernmental organization created on 13 July 2015 to promote Asian cooperation at a continental level and to help integrate separate regional organizations.

[b]The main objectives of the ACD are to:[/b]

1. Promote interdependence among Asian countries in all areas of cooperation by identifying Asia&amp;#39;s common strengths and opportunities.

2. Expand the trade and financial market within Asia.

3. Serve as the missing link in Asian cooperation by building upon Asia&amp;#39;s potentials and strengths.

4. Ultimately transform the Asian continent into an Asian Community.
