Truly there is no lesser place on Earth, no other place as truly morally decrepit or pathetically tied together as Memeworld.

Memeworld is an intensely varied land of many different cultures and ethnic backgrounds, all of which are somehow terrible in their own way. Seemingly every leader in this area is either mind-bogglingly stupid, or batshit insane.

&quot;Beware ye who enter Memeworld, for you either enter with good mind, and slowly lose it while inside, or you never entered with a good mind at all.&quot;

Mace is the best.
