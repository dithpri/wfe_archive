The Swedish Empire (Swedish: stormaktstiden, &quot;the era of great power&quot;) refers to the Kingdom of Sweden&#39;s territorial control of much of the Baltic region during the 17th and early 18th centuries, a time when Sweden was one of the great European powers. The beginning of the Empire is usually taken as the reign of Gustavus Adolphus, who ascended the throne in 1611.
