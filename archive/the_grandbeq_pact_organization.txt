                                                               [b]THE GRANDBEQ PACT ORGANIZATION[/b]

The Grandbeq Pact Organization (GPO), officially the Treaty of Friendship, Cooperation and Mutual Assistance, commonly known as the Grandbeq Pact (GP), was a collective defense treaty signed in Grandbeq, the capital of Sileen, between the Bebrostanian Federation and other friendship republics of the Eastern Block of Central, Eastern and Western Europe in June 1955.
