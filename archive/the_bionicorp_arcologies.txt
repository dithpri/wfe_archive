The Arcologies sometimes shortened to &amp;quot;Arcs&amp;quot; or &amp;quot;Arks&amp;quot; are the only type of buildings constructed and or populated by Bionicorp. Each Arcology follows a simple design maintaining a key balance between habitation, laboratories, workplaces and leisure facilities.
