[url=http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tricity,_Poland]Tricity[/url] is an urban area consisting of three Polish cities: Gdansk, Gdynia and Sopot. They are situated adjacent to one other, in a row, on the coast of the Gdansk Bay, Baltic Sea, in Pomerelia (Pomeranian Voivodeship), northern Poland.
