Wiki Entry:
Quahog (pronounced &quot;KO-hog&quot;) is a fictional city in Newport County, Rhode Island where the animated television comedy Family Guy is set. The Griffin Family, the Brown family, the Swanson family, and Glenn Quagmire live on Spooner Street, with the Griffin family residing at 31 Spooner Street. The population of Quahog in &quot;Tom Tucker: The Man and His Dream&quot; is 800. The ZIP code, as revealed in &quot;FOX-y Lady&quot;, is 00093. Peter&#39;s birth certificate in &quot;Quagmire&#39;s Mom&quot; gives the location as Newport County.
