The ICS Government Is Divided into different Offices.
The Office and Role
HomeLand Over-Officer: To provide Defence and Command of Armed Forces
HomeLand Under-Officer: To provide Defence
Colonial Over-Officer: To Ensure Colonies are Represented and maintained (Must be Held by a Colony)
Colonial Under-Officer: To Ensure Colonies are Represented (Must be Held by a Colony) 
National Over-Officer: To Governor other Nations and Enforce the Law
National Under-Officer: To Enforce the Law.
