[region]Lungmen[/region] is an autonomous region of [region]Yan[/region], located between [region]Kazdel[/region] and [region]Ursus[/region].

A prosperous, advanced nomadic city, it is governed by the Yan imperial family member [nation]Wei Yenwu[/nation] and acts as a special economic zone with greater autonomy such as a largely independent government and economy, making it more progressive and open to foreign influences than Yan. Lungmen&#39;s prosperity and economic prowess is shown by their currency, the LMD, which is widely used in international transactions across Terra. In adition, Lungmen is also the youngest administrative district within [nation]Terran Yan[/nation] &#151;under the administrative code 0019.
