[region]Lungmen[/region] is an autonomous region of [region]Yan[/region], located between [region]Kazdel[/region] and [region]Ursus[/region].

A prosperous, advanced nomadic city, it is governed by the Yan imperial family member Wei Yenwu and acts as a special economic zone with greater autonomy such as a largely independent government and economy, making it more progressive and open to foreign influences than Yan. Lungmen&#39;s prosperity and economic prowess is shown by their currency, the LMD, which is widely used in international transactions across Terra. In adition, Lungmen is also the youngest administrative district within Yan &#151;under the administrative code 0019.
