[b]BASIC POLICY FOR REGIONAL AFFAIRS[/b]

Founded in 2016 and motivated by the betterment of all involved nations and the rights and freedoms of individuals, the Confederation of the People aims to provide an international community to facilitate political discussion. The Council of the Confederate, composed of representatives of each nation, will meet to discuss the state of each union as well as the region as a whole. Regional policy will be discussed within this congress and policy stored on a single document.
