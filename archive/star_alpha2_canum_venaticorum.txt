Cor Caroli, also designated as &amp;#945;2 Canum Venaticorum (alpha2 Canum Venaticorum), is a variable and double hypergiant star of spectral class A0 in the constellation of Canes Venatici. Cor Caroli visual magnitude is 2.9. Because of its brightness, Cor Caroli is clearly visible to the naked eye when observed from locations with dark skies, and should be also quite easily visible from light polluted areas.
