Voragovian Russia is a region encompassing Voragovia and its allied surrounding countries. The President of Voragovia, Dmitri Voragov, formally seceded from Russia on December 1st, 2000. Specifically, VR is located East of Kirovsk and Umba and encompasses all of the land there up to the White Sea. While much of the land is barren and inhospitable, closer to the coastline is a hospitable and environmentally stunning landscape.
