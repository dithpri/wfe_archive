[b]Commonwealth of Arkadya[/b], a rural-based nation, and its surrounding lands:

--- [b]Protectorate of Arkadyan Hermitages[/b]: a self-governed dependency of monasteries and hermitages. Tourist area.

--- [b]Dominion of Ululators[/b]: a lawless mountainous area technically part of Arkadya but out of Arkadya&#39;s sphere of influence. Little is known of this land.

--- [b]Free Land of Slavvos[/b]: a free-port ruled by a dictator. Has good trading relations with Arkadya.

--- [b]Nomadic Peoples of Greater Arkadya[/b]: the Gypsies of Arkadya who live freely within the region.
