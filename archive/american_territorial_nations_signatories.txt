[u][b]Article 1 - Freedoms[/b][/u]

Section 1: The rights of life, liberty, and property must be upheld.

Section 2: Life, liberty, and property shall convey the broadest possible interpretation.



[u][b]Article 3 - National Privileges[/b][/u]

Section 1: All trade between nations must be free and unhindered.

Section 2: Movement between nations must be free and unhindered.

Section 3: Peace between nations must be free and unhindered.
