A region once united, is now divided, after the death of the Emperor and Empress, the remaining children of the Laandschuud blood line and a few scheming vassals of the Empire laid claim to parts of the the once paradise. The eldest son and child, Damon Vin Laandschuud, holds the most respect of the populace and was dubbed ruler of the island, but his younger siblings didn&#39;t take to this well, they made their own factions in their own holding and war ravaged the land for years, until now, on 4/3/2017, a peace treaty was signed, an uneasy and shaky one, but a peace treaty nonetheless.
