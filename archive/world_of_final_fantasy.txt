We are a diverse region, giving equal rights to humans, sentient moogles, bangaa, viera, lalafell and miqote as well as a variety of other cultures and groups.

Summoning is only permitted within the designated summoning circles. No permits are needed for summons lower than level 3 (kirin, carbuncle, unicorn); to practice summons at level 4 and above, permission must be granted by the Council of Magical Creatures.
