[u][i][b]What is the Democratic Union of Planets?[/u][/i][/b]


The region is set many years in future. The region is for future role playing and a has a keen interest in planets. 
We are planets that have united under one goal, to destroy the fascist ideology once and for all, to usher in a new age of democracy and freedom.
 


[u][i][b]What we do?[/u][/i][/b]

We role-play through story lines from the perspectives of our national leaders and we also have elections every fortnight for the regional government.
