Message the Lord Protector [nation=short]Trachae[/nation] if you wish to apply for entry. 

Former [b]Citizens of the Commonwealth[/b] will be granted admission without delay, as well as known and long-lived Citizens of the New Commonwealth.

For security reasons, Nations with a population under one billion will not be admitted at this time. 

If you wish to help develop a Regional Government and take part in shaping a Region with an already rich history, apply for Expedited Citizenship.
