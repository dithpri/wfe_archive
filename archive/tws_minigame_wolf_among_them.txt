In the late 1960&#39;s a flight from New York, Kommeria to England, Britain was lost without a trace over the vast Atlantic Ocean. The search continued for weeks, but even with four aircraft carriers, the impossibility of finding survivors became too real and the two nations abandoned the search.

No one could guess that the passengers still had enough luck to crash within a half mile of a small uninhabited and forested island. This little slice of land is both their savior and their hell, as a Nazi terrorist is forced to continue to kill to keep his secret safe.
