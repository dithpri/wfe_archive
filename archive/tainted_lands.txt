A region where experimentation in the arcane has left the local organisms and ground severely altered. Almost every living thing is hostile and even the ground looks like it wants to kill.
Before the Taint became a problem the region was already filled with arcane beings like skeletons, Eldritch beings, Dungeon lords and more. The people are educated in magic and are quite strong because of the dangerous wildlife.
The countries started accepting the arcane creatures with in a few months of their first appearance.The Tainted Empire of Vitium has the Eldritch crab as National animal, while Tainted Dungeon has the Dungeon lord.
