Many small islands situated around the main island of Keathle, many are non colonized and non settled, so they are open game for everyone.

A warm and humid climate, there are hot, wet summers and stormy winters with many snowstorms, a sudden change from the hot summer.

Many types of climates are situated here, ranging from dense rainforests to warm and sandy beaches. The water around the islands is warm, even during the early days of winter.
