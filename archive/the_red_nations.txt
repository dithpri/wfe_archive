The red nations, are an alliance whose member range from blood drinking anarchists to the highly industrialised power houses. This alliance was developed for the soul purpose of conquering new lands , and the defense of it members. This alliance has a combined military might that is feared throughout the world, and has the power to deploy forces that will quickly win any conflict wether it be through the sheer brutality of the barbarian berserker forces or it is the Iron tank legions, whose guns have been the final sound on thousands of battlefields.
