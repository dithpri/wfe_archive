Far from civilization lies a group of small islands, The Islands of Drauuyae. While many dismiss them as myth, as there has been no evidence found of their existence, there those who seek these legendary lands. Some say there is a bizarre fog forever surrounding them; hiding the islands from view. Others theorize that their existence is purposely kept secret by governments all around the world. There are even some that believe that it lies in a rip through time and space in between dimensions. Wherever it lies, there is one piece of lore that has kept people searching for centuries; It is said the whosoever is able find these sacred shores will rule them as a king.
