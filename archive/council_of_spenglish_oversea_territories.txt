The CoSOT is a coalition of all of the Republic of Spengland &amp; South Toiwan&#39;s independent oversea protectorates and territories. All CoSOT members exchange military, economical, commercial and diplomatical assistance. 

There are currently 8 members of CoSOT:
- Republic of Spengland &amp; South Toiwan
- United Island of the Hallouimi Archipelago 
- Spenglish Territory of West Brest
- Spenglish Eastern Oman
- Islands of Spenglish West Samoa
- Democratic Zeyedeni Republic of North Toiwan
- Berber Nubian Desert Territory   
- Allied States of East Yemeni Al-Mahara wShamal Hadhramout
