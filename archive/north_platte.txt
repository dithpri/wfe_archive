----THE DIET OF NORTH PLATTE---
1) No vote on any General Assembly proposal shall be made unless a quorum (simple majority) of regional states is present.
2) The World Assembly Delegate of North Platte is forbidden to make any vote contrary to the will of its constituents, except in the Security Council, in which the Regional Delegate votes on his own.
3) A vote is decided by simple majority.
