The Union of Soviet Sovereign Republics (Russian: &amp;#1057;&amp;#1086;&amp;#1102;&amp;#1079; &amp;#1057;&amp;#1086;&amp;#1074;&amp;#1077;&amp;#1090;&amp;#1089;&amp;#1082;&amp;#1080;&amp;#1093; C&amp;#1091;&amp;#1074;&amp;#1077;&amp;#1088;&amp;#1077;&amp;#1085;&amp;#1085;&amp;#1099;&amp;#1093; &amp;#1056;&amp;#1077;&amp;#1089;&amp;#1087;&amp;#1091;&amp;#1073;&amp;#1083;&amp;#1080;&amp;#1082;, Soyuz Sovyetskikh Suvyeryennykh Ryespublik), known colloquially as the Soviet Union (&amp;#1057;&amp;#1086;&amp;#1074;&amp;#1077;&amp;#1090;&amp;#1089;&amp;#1082;&amp;#1080;&amp;#1081; &amp;#1057;&amp;#1086;&amp;#1102;&amp;#1079;, Sovyetskiy Soyuz), and abbreviated as the USSR (&amp;#1057;&amp;#1057;&amp;#1057;&amp;#1056;, SSSR); is a country situated in northern Eurasia founded in August 20th 1991. A federation comprised of 15 republics, the USSR is the largest nation in area and one of the two global superpowers (along with the United States).
