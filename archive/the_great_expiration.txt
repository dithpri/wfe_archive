[b]The Nanomites[/b] attacked all electronic software there was to find and intercontinental communications were quickly broken. Cellphones, cars computers, credit-card readers; Everything. Sweden was hit first and lost all kind of technology in a matter of weeks. The Nanomites quickly spread across Europe, Asia and to the Americas. Billions Died. The Event Is Called The Great Expiration

[b]That Event[/b] happened 10 years ago. Now society has adapted to the life without advanced technology or electronics. Communications only reach beyond some miles and messages has to be delivered by couriers. The two most usual means of transportation is by horse or foot.
