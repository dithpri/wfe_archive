This is that land. Its rich civilisations, mighty warriors, and massive cities fell into darkness long ago. 

In that darkness, however, something stirs.



[B]The Steel League[/B], having lain dormant for many years, flexes its military muscles once again.


 [i]Will you hinder or help its ascension?[/i]
