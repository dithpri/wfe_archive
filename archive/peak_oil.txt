In 2008, the world demand for oil surpassed the international ability to harvest it. Almost overnight, the stock market crashed and nations entered a worldwide economic depression with the price of one barrel of oil raised from $80 NSD per barrel to over $150. As supplies of oil and natural gas waned and economies collapsed, diplomatic relationships between the different nations are strained, causing new alliances to be formed, and old ones to be broken. The event became widly known as [b]Peak Oil.[/b]

[color=red]So when people ask, what would happen if the world ran out of oil, the answer is that man would fight over the last few drops.[/color]
