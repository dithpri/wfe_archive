[region]The Military Commonwealth[/region] is a progressive time period region based on real earth cultures and technologies. Starting out at the year 1900 A.D., the region will progress at a rate of 1 In Game Year for every 1 Real Life Week. As time progresses, so does technology, people and money. NS Stats are not used here. [region]The Military Federation[/region] is the sister region to [region]The Military Commonwealth[/region].
