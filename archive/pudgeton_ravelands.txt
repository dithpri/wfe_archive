The Ravelands are a volatile and storied expanse stretching from the sulphuric jungles of the Ember Coast to the cryo-sculpted highlands of Skarn, bounded to the west by the shattered Moonfall Trenches and to the east by the Glassstep Plateau. A region defined more by contradiction than cohesion, the Ravelands are an anomaly in planetary geopolitics&#151;a patchwork of incompatible ideologies, ecological experiments, and post-singularity civilizations clinging to outmoded borders.
