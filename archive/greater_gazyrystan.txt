This region is the area in which most of the present citizens of Gazyrystan arrived from. Gazyrystan departed 10000 Islands for this region for many reasons, but one of the main reasons was to create the region of Greater Gazyrystan, to make other nations of Gazyry heritage feel at home. All nations are welcome, and we will establish embassies with (almost) anyone!
