[color=#347235][b][u]A Description of Tydeus[/u][/b][/color] 
Founded on the basis of openness, cooperation, and roleplay. Anyone interested in joining a friendly community of diverse roleplayers is welcome to apply. Tydeus is a descendant of Azhukali, Adrastos, and Tetrakon; we keep these regions close in our hearts as we aspire to create the best roleplay experience possible on Nationstates!
