The [i]Underspace[/i] is an extra-dimensional space-time bubble - basically a tiny isolated universe. It is located deep inside of subspace and thus is extremely inaccessible.

The [i]Underspace[/i] was artificially created by 
[b][nation=long]Ancient-Humans[/nation][/b] over 100,000 years ago. A hidden sanctuary that ensured the survival of the [b]Ancient-Human[/b] civilization over the last 100 millenia and into the future.
