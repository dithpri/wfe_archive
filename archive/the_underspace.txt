The [i]Underspace[/i] is an artificially created, isolated microverse hidden deep inside of subspace. The extremely inaccessible [i]Underspace[/i] has a size of tens of thousands of lightyears on the inside. It is the almost perfect sanctuary for the human species to live into the far future.

The [i]Underspace[/i] was created and is since controlled by the [b][nation=long]Ancient-Humans[/nation][/b].
