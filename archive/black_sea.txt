[color=666666]The [b]Black Sea[/b] is a sea in Southeastern Europe. It is bounded by Europe, Anatolia and the Caucasus, and drains through the Mediterranean into the Atlantic Ocean via the Aegean Seas and various straits. The Bosphorus Strait connects it to the Sea of Marmara, and the Strait of the Dardanelles connects that sea to the Aegean Sea region of the Mediterranean. 

The Black Sea has an area of 168,500 sq mi, a maximum depth of 7,257 ft and a volume of 131,000 cu mi. The Black Sea forms in an east-west trending elliptical depression which lies between Bulgaria, Georgia, Romania, Russia, Turkey, and Ukraine.[/color] &#151;Wikipedia[hr]
