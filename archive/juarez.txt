Ciudad Ju&aacute;rez  is a city in the Mexican state of Chihuahua, Commonly referred to by locals as simply [b]Ju&aacute;rez[/b], and known as Paso del Norte (Pass of the North) until 1888, Ju&aacute;rez is the seat of the municipality of Ju&aacute;rez with an estimated population of 1.5 million people. The city lies on the Rio Grande, south of El Paso, Texas. Together with the surrounding areas the cities form El Paso&#150;Ju&aacute;rez, the second largest bi-national metropolitan area on the Mexico&#150;United States border (after San Diego&#150;Tijuana), with a combined population of over 2.7 million people.

Strategically placed on the Mexico&#150;United States border, Ciudad Ju&aacute;rez has long been a center of illegal drug trafficking. &#151;Wikipedia
