Meridiana, also designated as &amp;#945; Coronae Australis (alpha Coronae Australis), is a main-sequence star of spectral class A2 in the constellation of Corona Australis. Meridiana visual magnitude is 4.11. Because of its moderate brightness, Meridiana is easily visible to the naked eye from locations with dark skies, while it might be barely visible, or not visible at all, from light-polluted skies.
