Neft Dashlari is an industrial settlement in Baku, Azerbaijan. It lies 100 km (62 mi) away from the Azerbaijani capital Baku, and 55 km (34 mi) from the nearest shore in the Caspian Sea. A fully functional city of 3,000 living in a network of oil platforms and artificial islands connected by 300 km of trestle bridges, all built by the Soviet Union.
