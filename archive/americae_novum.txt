The great war began many years. WWIII had stricken the world of war, disease, poverty, and famine. Rival factions were posed against each other during the post apocalypse of WWIII. Out of the ashes of the Western Hemisphere came many nations. 4 nations allied together to from New America or Americae Novum. These 4 nations still strive to unite New America and the rest of the Western Hemisphere.
