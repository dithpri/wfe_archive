The Allied Realms of Enlightenment is an alliance of nations founded by the Kingdom of Ylisse. The founding principles of the alliance include giving opportunity, fair treatment of all members, and creating a more enjoyable experience for your nation.

The alliance is governed by the founding nation, Ylisse. The resident nations are free to elect the World Assembly Delegate for the alliance, who can approve World Assembly resolutions on behalf of the alliance and also serves as the lead advisor to the founder.

Several other positions exist for citizens to participate in the government of the region. Those who show prowess may be selected by the founder for an office in the government!
