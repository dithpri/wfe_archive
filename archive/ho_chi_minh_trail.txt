The [b]H&amp;#7891; Ch&iacute; Minh trail[/b] was a logistical system that ran from the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to South Vietnam through the kingdoms of Laos and Cambodia. The system provided support, in the form of manpower and materiel, to the National Front for the Liberation of South Vietnam and the People&#39;s Army of Vietnam during the Vietnam War.

According to the United States National Security Agency&#39;s official history of the war, the Trail system was &quot;one of the great achievements of military engineering of the 20th century&quot;.
