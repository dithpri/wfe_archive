[color=darkblue]Uruguay, officially the [b]Oriental Republic of Uruguay[/b], is a country in the southeastern part of [region]South America[/region]. Following a four-way struggle between [region]Spain[/region], [region]Portugal[/region], [region]Argentina[/region], &amp;amp; [region]Brazil[/region], Uruguay gained independence in 1828. It remained subjected to foreign influence &amp;amp; intervention throughout the 19th century, with the military playing a recurring role in domestic politics until the late 20th century. Today, it is a democratic constitutional republic, with a president who serves as both head of state &amp;amp; head of government. It frequently ranks as one of the most developed and prosperous countries in [region]Latin America[/region].[/color]
