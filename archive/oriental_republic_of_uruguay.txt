Uruguay, officially the Oriental Republic of Uruguay, is a country in the southeastern part of South America. It is home to 3.3 million people, of whom 1.8 million live in the capital Montevideo and its metropolitan area. With an area of approximately 68,000 square miles, Uruguay is the second-smallest nation in South America by area, after Suriname.

Capital and largest city: Montevideo
