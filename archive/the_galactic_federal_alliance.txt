A small system located in the Eastern sector of the Khlaerdeo galaxy, The Galactic Federal Alliance, or GFA, is home to 5 independent nations, held on 5 planets and 3 moons.
Kaiderwin is the richest and most populous planet, home to Jolasko, Fothergilland and Liberal Pandas. [b][url=http://www.nationstates.net/nation=jolasko/detail=factbook/id=605374]Map[/url][/b]
The Interplanetary Federal Republic, or IFR, owns 3 planets and 1 moon. [b][url=http://www.nationstates.net/nation=the_interplanetary_federal_republic/detail=factbook/id=584840]Map[/url][/b]
The Scientific Liberal Republic, or SLR, owns 1 planet and 2 moons. [b][url=http://www.nationstates.net/nation=the_scientific_liberal_republic/detail=factbook/id=593031]Map[/url][/b]

The region works democratically creating regulation for all 5 nations, regarding social rights and region wide access to services.
New planets are sometimes discovered and are welcome to join the alliance after a short negotiation period.
