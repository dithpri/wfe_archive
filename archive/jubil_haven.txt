[region]Jubil Haven[/region] a is remote binary solar system in the outer edges of the Milky Way Galaxy.

Named by the ruler of the first group of colonists, Prince Julius of the [nation]Belas[/nation] (then a principality), its variety of resources and availability of colonizable bodies made it a very desirable target to many entities that had either retained or reachieved the required technology to colonize it.

Currently the only colonized planet is Jubilation, orbiting the smaller of the pair of stars with the colonial nations of:
[nation]Belas[/nation]
[nation]Nova Quatra[/nation]
[nation]Freedom Fall[/nation]

Their differing origins and non-matching ideologies have so far made for an interesting political climate.
