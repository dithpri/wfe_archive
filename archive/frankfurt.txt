[b]Frankfurt[/b] am Main is a metropolis and the largest city in the German state of Hesse and the fifth-largest city in Germany, with a 2015 population of 731,095 within its administrative boundaries, and 2.3 million in its urban area. The city is at the center of the larger Frankfurt Rhine-Main Metropolitan Region, which has a population of 5.8 million and is Germany&#39;s second-largest metropolitan region after Rhine-Ruhr. 

Frankfurt is culturally and ethnically diverse, with around half of the population, and a majority of young people, having a migration background. A quarter of the population are foreign nationals, including many expatriates. &#151;Wikipedia[hr]
