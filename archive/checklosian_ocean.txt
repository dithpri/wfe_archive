This massive ocean, named after the philosopher Checklos the Great, holds a supercontinent to the north, primal lands throughout the sea and several other continents throughout. The climate streaches from temperate to tropical. The ocean contains everything from untouched paradise to the industrial metropoli of the Viperco Commonwealth. It is located inbetween the Pacific and South America separated by other land masses. It has an abundance of coral reefs and other wildlife including many modern, prehistoric and mythical species. Rich in mineral resources, including oil.
