Kaus Australis, also designated as &amp;#949; Sagittarii (epsilon Sagittarii), is a double giant star of spectral class B9 in the constellation of Sagittarius. Kaus Australis visual magnitude is 1.85, making it the 36th brightest star in the sky. Because of its brightness, Kaus Australis is clearly visible to the naked eye when observed from locations with dark skies, and should be also quite easily visible from light polluted areas.
