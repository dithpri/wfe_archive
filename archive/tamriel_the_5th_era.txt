Tamriel the 5th Era is a region for Nations of Elder Scrolls. Made up of towns or cities from actual places in Tamriel, the 5th Era is the future of Tamriel set after the Skyrim Civil War has ended. Right now all of Tamriel is at peace and we have joined together to further diplomatic relations with one another. If you are a nation state of Tamriel and wish to join just send us an application and we will gladly let you be a part of our league of nations.
