Termina is a region in the world with the same name. After the mysterious disappearance of Maidtriarch Rem from the Waifu region, everything fell into a dead silence between the peoples of the region.. Until Sirelia Elderhark, Horansan the First and Liravyan Zan created a portal to another world, the world of a new beginning... They called it &quot;Termina&quot;.. But maybe Matriarch Rem will come back someday.. But we will only find out in the future.. The future of distant stars..
