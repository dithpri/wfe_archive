[color=darkblue][b]Southfield is a region that has an ancient roleplay and gameplay history. With the region changing control over its history, and the nations in it changing all the time. Going from independent, to founderless, to a colony, the region has an interesting history. If you are interested in becoming a resident of Southfield telegram the nation who holds the office of governor. The region of Southfield is a colonial possession of the region of [region]Europae[/region].
