English East India Company founded- 1601. 1613 received permission from the Great Mogul to found a trading station where servants of the Company could live and work. The East India Company was allowed to build Madras in 1639, and picked up the islands it would built Bombay on, from the Portuguese, in 1661, won control of Bengal in 1757. 1848-49 the Sikhs - the last independent peoples in India were defeated in the  second Anglo Sikh War and their empire dissolved.The East Indian company rule was from 1757 to 1858. On June 28, 1858, rule of the British East India Company was transferred to the Crown, to Queen Victoria, 1876 was proclaimed Empress of India. 1848-1947 is the period during which all of India came under British Rule.
