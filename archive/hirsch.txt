[b][i]Hirsch, commonly known to the world as Deer, is a Monarchist region of dense forest in Austria with allegiance to the Imperial Crown of House of Hapsburg-Lorraine.[/i]
 
Head of State, Grand Duke Karl von Ostmark of [nation]Der Rohrende Hirsch[/nation] ([i]The Roaring Deer[/i]), acts as liaison to the outside world and encourages visitors to share in our comforts here. Military and civil participation can be acquired through request to join the Order of Saint George in Christian service.[/b]
