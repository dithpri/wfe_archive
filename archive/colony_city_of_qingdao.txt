[i][b]Qingdao or Tsingtao (&amp;#38738;&amp;#23707;)[/b] is a colony city of
[nation]Liiechtenstein[/nation], in Xantum province, on the border with the[color=red] People&#39;s Republic of China[/color]. It is a port on the Yellow Sea, on the Xantum peninsula. It has about 2 430 000 inhabitants. It has been owned by [nation]Liiechtenstein[/nation]  since 1898. In Qingdao, the sailing events of the 2008 Summer Olympics were held. The Qingdao jurisdiction has four districts. In 2014, the city had a population of 9,046,200 inhabitants, with an urban population of 6,188,100.     [/i]
