Korthenia, officially the &quot;Konf&ouml;deration von Korthenia&quot;, is an autonomous customs, economic and diplomatic union in the Germanic region of Korthenia. The confederacy is made up of representatives of member states in Korthenia, which cooperate with each other loosely. The region&#39;s environment is characterized by deep Black Forests and sprawling hill regions, with a cold climate. Korthenia is a central-northern region with a deep and rich history.
