The [url=https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Brazil][b][color=#FEE000]Federative[/color] [color=#002277]Republic[/color] [color=#009C37]of Brazil[/color][/b][/url] is the largest and most populous country in South America, as well as one of the world&#39;s ten largest economies. It is one of the most multicultural and diverse nations in the world, besides also being home to diverse wildlife, a variety of ecological systems, and extensive natural resources spanning numerous protected habitats. Originally inhabited by hundreds of indigenous peoples, it was colonized by Portugal for three centuries before gaining independence as a monarchy, and then transitioning to a republic, passing through authoritarian periods until securing its democracy.
