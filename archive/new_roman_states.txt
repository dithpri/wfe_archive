The New Roman States is a bloc of countries styled after the states of the Roman Empire. The New Roman States is dedicated to providing economical and military protection, and a stable foundation for the new nations of the world, much in the way that the Roman Empire was dedicated to spreading advanced civilisation to the tribes of Germania the areas surrounding the Mediterranean. 

The New Roman States was founded by the Empire of the Carthaginian Mediterranean, when it itself was a new nation, and saw how much the world needs a safe platform free of deception.
