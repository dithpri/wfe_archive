Welcome to the Melayu Archipelago.

Thank you for always being with the Melayu Archipelago. The Melayu Archipelago is a safe, happy, friendly and accepting geographical region. It covers an area of 5.3M km2. The name Melayu derived from the original inhabitant of the archipelago, the Malays, an Austronesian ethnic group. It is now inhabited by various groups of people from different ethnicities making it a diverse and multi-cultural region. [hr]
