[b]The Young Bloods[/b] are one of the world&#39;s few teenage superhero teams, operating in San Diego, United States. Its core is formed by a group of Thomsen High students who formed the team after a battle with a group of escaped prison inmates on Wonder Island, under the guidance of Naja. The team has since been joined by several new recruits, some fellow students of the school and others outside of its reach.
