Imagine if a frat house full of insecure, fairskinned males with popped collars, all had a sleepover where they find a four letter read hat.

They try it on, and are absorbed by its powers; the sleepover has soon morphes into a street party.
They end up on the local televised news, and the ball keeps rolling; soon they are a state of toxic masculinity, red hats, drunken disorder and incest.
