This is the system that Aquila Magna and Secunda belong to.

The first three planets (Speck, Helio and Rock) are hot rocks unable to sustain life, next are the two Habitable Planets, Aquila Magna and Secunda.

Then come the Small Gas Giants of Chiron and Possdees, followed by an asteroid belt.

Next come two small rocky planets Archeon and Paralass, the main Fleet base is located on Paralass.

Finally comes the Gas Giant of Teptheis the largest planet in the system.
