This region has a simple and humble purpose. To recreate the glory of Russia, away from the corruption of the big regions, and isolated from merciless invaders who want nothing more than to see the Republic fall.


The First Russian Republic is a reincarnation of the glory of Imperial Russia, with a little bit more democracy. The First Senate will be the legislative body of the region. An Imperial Council of 2-3 nations will be the executive head of the region. 


All of these things, once set in motion, will provide the backbone for the greatest embodiment of Russia ever seen in NationStates!


For the Republic!
