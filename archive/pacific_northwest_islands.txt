The Pacific Northwest Islands are a group of islands located just 180 km of the coast of West Canada. The region has independent nations who automatically become members after staying 2 days in the region. It consists of currently 11 islands ranging between 7-20 kilometres long. The region is also 27,000.00 Km squared. The Vulgusland Premier (Prime Minister) is the Chief Executive of the entire region who leads a council consisting of various ministries, each who excercise an important role in the regions government and day-to-day functions. There is also an assembly which consists of all members in the region.
