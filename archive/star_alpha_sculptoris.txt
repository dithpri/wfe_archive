&amp;#945; Sculptoris is a variable giant star of spectral class B7 in the constellation of Sculptor. &amp;#945; Sculptoris visual magnitude is 4.31. Because of its moderate brightness, &amp;#945; Sculptoris is easily visible to the naked eye from locations with dark skies, while it might be barely visible, or not visible at all, from light-polluted skies.
