Kelria is a continent located between the Denertic and Kurael Oceans, mainly based out of the continent of Kelria as well as its surrounding islands.
The official head of the Council of Kelria, Kelrian Union, and all other organisations within Kelria is [nation]Kelrian Assembly[/nation]

--Council--
The Council of Kelria is the official board for all issues pertaining to all of Kelria. Resolutions effecting all of the region are to be discussed and voted upon in the council. All nations in Kelria are members of the Council of Kelria.
