[u][color=purple][b]The Allied Coalition[/b][/color][/u] ([i]AC[/i]) is an  intergovernmental military alliance established on 24 November 2009 as a collective of several nations, founded in the hopes of influencing the stop of tyranny, terror, and insurrection; as well as promote trade and civil rights. The organization constitutes a system of collective defense whereby its members agree to mutual defense and participation and peacekeeping operations.

Founded out of the ruins of the ineffective Imperial Accord, the Allied Coalition promotes intergovernmental cooperation, and easier communication between the powers of the world.

[hr]

The Allied Coalition is a roleplay group focused in Foreign Relations, Global Politics, Trade, and War RP situations.
