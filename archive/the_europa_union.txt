[b]Welcome to the Europa Union![/b]



[b][i][u]Goals[/u][/i][/b]
The goals of the Europa Union are:

- Promote peace, its values and the well-being of its citizens;
- Offer freedom, security and justice without internal borders;
- Enhance economic, social and territorial cohesion and solidarity among member countries;
- Establish an economic and monetary union whose currency is the Black Peseta.


[b][i][u]Diplomacy and Security[/u][/i][/b]
The Union plays an important role in diplomacy and works to foster stability, security and prosperity, democracy, fundamental freedoms and the rule of law at international level.
