Founded in 1015 in the aftermath of the civil conflict on the continent of Equus, the [region]Northern Iliac Mutual Defense Pact[/region] was created to safeguard political autonomy and popular sovereignty of the former Equus colonial states located in Iliac Bay. Member states have rapidly industrialized, now large enough to challenge the economic and military power of the successor states left in Equus.

The alliance does not recognize [i]de jure[/i] any existing &#39;Equestrian&#39; polity or government on Equus, though pragmatically most of the population of the defense bloc and it&#39;s largest nation, [nation]Novus Lunar[/nation], show a preference toward the remaining Lunar-aligned states in Equus.
