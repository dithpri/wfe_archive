Naos, also designated as &amp;#950; Puppis (zeta Puppis), is a star of spectral class O5 in the constellation of Puppis. Naos visual magnitude is 2.25, making it the 70th brightest star in the sky. Because of its brightness, Naos is clearly visible to the naked eye when observed from locations with dark skies, and should be also quite easily visible from light polluted areas.
