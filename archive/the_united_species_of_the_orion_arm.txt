After 2583, Humanity had finally re-terraformed Planet Reach and pacified the Covenant Threat.
After the war, a great era of prosperity and exploration engulfed the Orion Arm, leading to the discovery of several new races, for example the Equestrians, the Mysticans, and the Star Hive. In order to prevent another interstellar war, the United Species of The Orion Arm was formed as a peacekeeping organization, like an interstellar United Nations.
