A tiny region of small Pacific island states near Antarctica. A group of either anarchists and left-wing utopias or corporate police states and iron fist consumerists, they all worship a pagan religion that is the worship of The Table Gods.

The region is plagued with revolts and fruit flies but prostitution is totally legal but laws vary between each state.

The Emperor-Bishophoric of The Tables is a coral atoll that sits right next to Antarctica and is the leading spiritual nation while the Queendom of Super Dickbutt is a tiny miniscule island that borders with The East Pacific.
