The United Communist Alliances of Equestria is primarily stationed in Southern Equestria, though we have a few bases in the Northern and Western territories. We currently have no other nations in the Alliance, but we are working on recruiting some. The Alliance will not except any nations that support/house corporations, and we will not accept any nations with other governments types other then Socialism and Communism, though we will try to keep up good diplomatic relations with other nations not in the range of joining our wonderful Alliance.
