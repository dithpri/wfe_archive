[region]Aachen[/region] is a German border town located between the Eifel, South Limburg and High Fens regions in the state of [region]North Rhine Westphalia[/region].

Achen was a favoured residence of Carl the Great, and later the place of coronation of the German kings, which is where it gained the reference as the &amp;quot;watering-place of kings.&amp;quot; Geographically, Aachen is the westernmost city of Germany, located along its borders with Belgium and the Netherlands[/b]
