[color=003366]The [b]Hermite Islands[/b] are the islands Hermite, Herschel, Deceit and Hornos as well as the islets Maxwell, Jerd&aacute;n, Arrecife, Chantieler, Hall, Deceit (islet), and Hasse at almost the southernmost end of South America.

The smallest and southernmost of the major islands is Hornos Island, the location of [region]Cape Horn[/region]. The islands are located south of the Wollaston Islands and separated from them by the Franklin Channel.[/color][hr]
