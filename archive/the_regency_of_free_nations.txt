The Golden Empire broke under waves of northern barbarians, who had previously been confined to the north. Taking the heart of the Empire as their capital, they assumed the title of Emperor. The Empire swiftly broke apart, but the old nobility in the south and the barbarian kings in the north seek to reunite. Now the disparate nations in the former empire have joined into a common economic community, hopefully a step forward in imperial reunification. The semblance of a senate still remains in the capital.
