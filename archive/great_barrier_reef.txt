The [b]Great Barrier Reef[/b] is the world&#39;s largest coral reef system, composed of over 2,900 individual reefs and 900 islands stretching for over 1,400 mi over an area of approximately 133,000 square miles. The reef is located in the [region]Coral Sea[/region], off the coast of Queensland, Australia, separated from the coast by a channel 100 miles wide in places and over 200 ft deep.The Great Barrier Reef can be seen from outer space and is the world&#39;s biggest single structure made by living organisms. This reef structure is composed of and built by billions of tiny organisms, known as coral polyps. &#151;Wikipedia[hr]
