After young upstarts from CarmaCity set out and settled Carmalen, (Our Land) the elders decided to create this region named Arma, which means &quot;Western Land.&quot;  Not long after came Armalen (New/western Lands), and Parnay (Northern Way.)  

Those from Nalir are a different peoples, desert folk who are rumored to have come across the great rift from the east.
