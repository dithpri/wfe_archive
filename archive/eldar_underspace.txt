The [b][i]Eldar Underspace[/i][/b] is a multiversal subspace network, artificially created by [b][Nation=short+noflag]the Eldar Empire[/Nation][/b] around 65mio. years ago. Subsequently the [b]Eldar[/b] relocated their entire civilization to the [b][i]Eldar Underspace[/i][/b]. Since that time the [i]empire[/i] successfully managed to defend the [i]underspace[/i] against all kinds of threats.
