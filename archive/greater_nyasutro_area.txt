The continental Federation of Nyasott and the island nations of the Lilbejin Republic and State of Abklosta unite as constituents of the Greater Nyasutro Area. Administration HQ is based at GNA Assembly in Chippingbank, Nyasott, which oversees peace and defence, environmental affairs, and maritime for the geographic region and holds a special diplomatic relationship with our Astyrian friends, the Cez - Zaern Republic, Republic of Woodstead and Libettan Territory.
