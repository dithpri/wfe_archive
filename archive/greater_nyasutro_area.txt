The Greater Nyasutro Area consists of the Nyasutro continent and the surrounding landmasses. Since the 1720s, the region has been one focal point in the history and culture of the [url=https://www.nationstates.net/region=astyria]Astyrian[/url] colonists from the Erlenic Empire. These ex-Empire nations exist as diverse societies with varied political structures, made up of diverse landscapes.
