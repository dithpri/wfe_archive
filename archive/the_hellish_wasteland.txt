The Hellish Wasteland of Necromaniac is a charmless and ghastly place full of toxic waste, lifeless ruins and creepy things, crawling through them. 
The Hellish Wasteland of Necromaniac is an outpost for zombie apocalypse, from which the countless hordes of undead constantly raid all territories, that happen to be too close to its deadly borders.
The Hellish Wasteland of Necromaniac is the domain of Ereshkigal, the Unliving Goddess, from where she commands her undead army.
The Hellish Wasteland of Necromaniac is the perpetual menace to any form of life that happens to come too close to this inhospitable desert.
