The [colour=#00B9FB][b]World Alliance of Liberals and Democrats[/b][/colour] is an organisation of nations dedicated to the principles of democracy, liberty and the rule of law. We also adhere to the ideals of non-interventionism, multilateralism and ecological justice and seek to promote and enhance equality of opportunity and social justice for all individuals.

Self-proclaimed successor of the [b]Association of Liberal Democratic States (ALDS)[/b]
