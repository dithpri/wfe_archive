Following the &quot;Sjurdland War&quot; of 2047, Sjurdland was split between the Sjurdland Confederacy and the Grand Colonial Plantations (officially, the Grand Colonial Plantation Kingdom of Isabelle Plantations).

Though Holy God Emperor Sjurd Haakonsson and Winston Carleton became equal leaders of their respective regions, they still remain a strong friendship and allyship that has been present since the Great War period.
