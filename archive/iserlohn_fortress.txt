[b]Iserlohn Fortress[/b] (Japanese: [b]&amp;#12452;&amp;#12476;&amp;#12523;&amp;#12525;&amp;#12540;&amp;#12531;&amp;#35201;&amp;#22622;[/b]) was an Imperial fortress located near the Tiamat Starzone. Existing within the eponymously named Iserlohn Corridor, one of only two navigable star routes between the Empire and the Free Planets Alliance (the other being the Fezzan Corridor), Iserlohn kept the territories of the Galactic Empire safe from Alliance invasion.[hr]
