The Eilamite Empire is a militaristic, theocratic empire. It&#39;s Emperor is known as the Pharaoh and is seen and treated as a god amongst men. The society is strongly influenced by religion, and is willing to stand behind their god if push comes to shove. The leading power in The Eilamite Empire is Eilam. Eilam has has two brother-states, the Protectorate of Gualter, as well as the Theocracy of Sahib.
