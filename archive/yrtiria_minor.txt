Yrtiria is a continent in the highly magical world of Riftsar. While the nations of the larger subcontinent, Yrtiria Major, are the most advanced nations on the planet, Yrtiria Minor is just as wild and uncivilized as the rest of the world. Its sapient inhabitants, when not fighting each other, must fight against the untamed magic of their environment.

This is a private roleplay region, though visitors are welcome.
