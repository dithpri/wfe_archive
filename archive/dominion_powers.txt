The Dominion Powers was formed as part to unify all correlated member states of the former Major Powers under the ratification of the Reunion Pact of 2018, where all signatories agreed to join and form a new Power. Dominion Powers operated on the basis of collective security and maintenance of extensive influence over the region. At the same time, all member states under membership of the Dominion Powers are required to provide independent assessments on their current economic, military and political status, allowing an on-site inspection to secure the base for bilateral or multilateral military training/exchanges
