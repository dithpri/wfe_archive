Welcome to the Imperium.

Founded by the Legions of Wulfsblud, New Haven is a region that consists of nations that are interested in the well-being of their economy, civil rights, and political freedom. These nations have a strong military and police force as well., and value education and advancement above other things.

Should enough nations join, the Imperium will begin the conquest of the world. The legions will conquer all other nations, and the world will belong to the Imperium of Mac Tyre.
