The Haven galaxy is an 8-wing spiral galaxy with an approximate size is 800.000 light-years in diameter. The galaxy was formed not long after the formation of the universe 6 billion years ago. It is the biggest and brightest galaxy in its regional cluster thanks to the trillions of young stars forming every day.

The Haven galaxy has 8 spiral wings which are also divided into 8 regions and are named: Ariam Wing, Butos Wing, Cellia Wing, Ephalis Wing, Mosasan Wing, Temila Wing, Rigista Wing, Restenga Wing. These spiral wings can stretch to 100.000 light-years and are home to trillions of stars, planets, and nebula.
