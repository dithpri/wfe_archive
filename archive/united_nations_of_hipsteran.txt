We the people determined, to save succeeding generations from the scourge of war, which has brought untold sorrow to mankind, and to reaffirm faith in fundamental human rights, in the dignity and worth of the human person, in the equal rights of men and women and nations, to establish under which justice and respect for the obligations arising from treaties and other sources of international law can be maintained and to promote progress and better standards of life in larger freedom.

Accordingly, let our respective Government agree to the present Charter and hereby plant the seeds of a brighter future.
