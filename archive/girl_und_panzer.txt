Girls und Panzer is set in an alternate universe where a sport known as &quot;Panzerfahren&quot; or &quot;Sensha-do&quot; - the art of fighting tanks, or tankery, is a lot more common than today. The sport is entirely practiced by girls and women and is considered feminine. The anime series follows the girls of Ooarai Girls Academy as they learn about, operate, and battle with all kinds of WWII tanks against other tanking schools while forming bonds with their machines and each other.
