The UNSC(United Nations Security Council) was founded by the Democratic Republic of Szurdok on the 7th of October 2012 as a safe,peaceful and productive haven for its allies and any democratic nation, All nations involved are looked after by the other members of the region and are protected by the UNSC Constitution.
The UNSC welcomes small and new democratic countries looking to be part of a region and will support them as this region is open to all.
