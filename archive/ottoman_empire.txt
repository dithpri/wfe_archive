The Ottoman Empire was one of the greatest historical empires that existed within the international world and one of the greatest military forces in history. During World War I, the Ottoman Empire sided with the Central Powers, an alliance consisting of Austria-Hungary and Germany, against the Allied Powers. In 1922 the empire laid in defeat by the victorious allied nations which preceded to brake apart the once glorious nation.
