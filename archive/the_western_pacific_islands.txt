The Western Pacific Islands, is a conglomerate of islands located in the Western Pacific that has only recently begun to become civilized. The population mainly consists of Aryans and Redheads, as the original settlers were of Germanic blood. Though later new races and nations began to settle these islands for an assortment of reasons, the Germanic population still is prominent within several of the nations that accumulate this region, Including but not limited to the Tyrean Islands, The Confederacy of The Aryan Independent States, The Republic of the Independent Bavarian States, and the Republic Saxon States.
