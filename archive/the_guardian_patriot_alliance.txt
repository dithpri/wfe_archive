The Guardian Patriot Alliance (GPA) is one of the earliest-founded regions in NS history. Though dissolved for some time, this once-vibrant and politically-active regions is rebuilding toward a great state of prominence once again. 

Currently led by a senior statesman in The Red Shinobi, this region is actively seeking other nations to be a part of this endeavor to create a resurgent region that highlights the fun in politics, great governance, and exceptional role play.

The core vision and values of the GPA is to harbor nations in an active environment to work toward a collective good while aggressively protecting nations&amp;#39; rights to their individual cultures and governments.
