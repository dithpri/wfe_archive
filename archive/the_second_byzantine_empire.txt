Byzantine Empire, officially Second Byzantine Empire is a Greek nation that stretches from Greece to Lebanon. The popular religion is Christianity and the official language is Greek. It current monarch is Eren. The Empire is successor to Byzantine Empire created after the division in 395 and ended in 1453 after the capture of Constantinople. The Empire was recreated as Byzantine State with capital in Brusa. After the surender of Ottoman Empire in the World War I the empire expanded itself greatly in 27 years gaining Turkey include Constantinople which became it capital in 1953 gaining Syria, Cyprus, Greece, Syria, Lebanon and Crete. Empire is classified as a regional power.
