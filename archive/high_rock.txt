[region]High Rock[/region] borders Hammerfell across the Bangkorai Pass to the southeast and Skyrim across the Druadach Mountains to the east. Its landscape is diverse, ranging from green forests through badlands to snowy mountains.

High Rock consists of five main regions: Glenumbra, Stormhaven, Rivenspire, northern Bangkorai and Wrothgar. In addition, the islands of Balfiera and Betony are also a part of High Rock.

Home to the Bretons.
