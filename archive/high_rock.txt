[b][u][region]High Rock[/region][/u][/b]
Home to the human Bretons and Orcs, this province is divided into multiple Breton city-states and minor kingdoms. It is a temperate coastal region in northwestern Tamriel. The southern region of High Rock is part of the setting for the game The Elder Scrolls II: Daggerfall.

[b]This Region is an Imperial Province of [region]The Empire of Tamriel[/region][/b]
