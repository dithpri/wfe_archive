The Imperial Empire Of Avalon is located in The United Germanic and Nordic Kingdoms Of Northern Europe. This Region consist of Scandinavia, Avalon which use to be know as Grate Briton, the territories that use to make up Germany, the Baltic States and Eastern Russia.

After securing there homeland of Grate Briton and reestablishing its self as The Imperial Empire Of Avalon. The Empire went out to take back control of there former lands and expand in to other neighboring territories.

They did this successfully and now have complete control of all there former territories and also have over most of the region.
