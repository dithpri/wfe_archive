In July 1999 Ulysees struck Strangereal plummeting the world into Chaos. Anea and Usea suffered the most damage but the damage caused sent the global economy plummeting, as such numerous groups took their chance to establish their won new nations using the relics from before the impact. 

Some groups sought an end to warfare, others sought out glory, while others sought out a meaning. As such groups will vie for power to establish their new perfect world .

Chaos and Hope......Was this the inevitable fate of the world that Ulysses rained in?
