Thus, Der Kriegsprinzessinspakt was born between nations under the control or allied to the Austrian-Hungarian Alliance. It is home both to the new and the old nations, both rich and poor but they defend one another against the soviet horde. The Austrian-Hungarian Alliance acts as the protector and supplier of goods to the pact, but it is unknown what the future holds. To join the pact, a nation must be willing to swear allegiance to the Habsburg Monarchy and Austria Hungary itself, forever and always.
