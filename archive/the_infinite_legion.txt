NEW POLICY: Voting for HIGH NATION will Occur Once every month and voting for GRAND NATION will occur once every 6 months. A nation cannot run for two consecutive terms or run for more than one position at a time. A nation can run every other term however.

Officers such as VOTING OFFICER will not be affected by the process. In order to change the identity of the VOTING OFFICER the majority of the people must agree to set a new poll. If the VOTING OFFICER does not administer the polls then a new VOTING OFFICER will be elected.
