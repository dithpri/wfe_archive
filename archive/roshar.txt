Roshar is a planet in the cosmere and one of thirteen planets in the Rosharan system. It is also the name of the supercontinent that contains all of the planet&#39;s known inhabitants. It is the second planet from the system&#39;s sun, orbiting between Ashyn and Braize, and it has three moons. People from Roshar are called Rosharans. The planet is particularly notable due to the activity of several Shards of Adonalsium in the area and the various forms of magic used by its residents, including Surgebinding through the use of Stormlight.
