Not even the buzzards can survive the desolate wastes of Helghan, Equestria. the changeling peoples wanted their freedom enough to go to war over this little patch of oasis in an unforgiving, noxious swath of desert. When the blood was shed and the bullet casings clanged to the ground, Helghan became the refuge for the oppressed changelings who had split with the ponies.

The very air is toxic, requiring air filters to be worn at all times, lest one slowly choke to the grave. The soil is poor, and the few trees have been cut for lumber. Most of the nation has already moved underground to escape the harsh conditions, and below have been found precious metals and plenty of clean water.
