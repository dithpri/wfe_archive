The World of Ark, the mythical land of the SpamHammer. It consists of the central Holy Empire of Spamopolis, the Borderlands of Quantagontalopagus in between Spamopolis and the Republic of the TNT Republic (aka the TNT Spikes), the Kingdom of One Magic Mushroom, on a relatively small island in the harbor surrounded by the Lapis Badlands and the Sponge Hills.  To the north is the Grand Duchy of CreepaGriefa Bay.
