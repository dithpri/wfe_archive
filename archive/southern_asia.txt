A democratic region sitting center-left on the political spectrum. It is bordered by Turkey and Iraq on the West, Kazakhstan to the north, and stretches to Tibet and Burma on the Eastern edge. The Bay of Bengal, Indian Ocean, Arabian Sea, and the Persian Gulf make up the southern border. It is completely tolerant of all religions and races, though most people are followers of Hinduism, Christianity, and Islam. The predominant languages are English, Pashto, and Bhojpuri. For more info on its ideals and policies, see Hindupashtunistan. With its great economy, human rights, and citizens, the diverse land of Southern Asia is a great region to be a part of.
