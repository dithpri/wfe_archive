West Marxia is a continental island located in the Barents Sea. It is home to a collection of social democratic nations, formally known as The West Marxia Union (WMU), as they are a union of economic and political alliances.

Language: Finlek
Currency: Marx
Official religion: None (secular)
Ideology: Left, centre-right

Union Platform:
The West Marxia Union combines economic liberalism with a conservative programme on immigration and culture. The party seeks abolition of the minimum wage, and limiting child benefits and government subsidies. Regarding immigration and culture, the party believes that humanist traditions should be taken as the dominant culture in West Marxia, and that immigrants should adapt accordingly.
