# Zhuangdilan-Bir Tawil Realm
This sovereign regional realm is established and led by the Kingdom of Zhuangdilan-Bir Tawil, a traditional emirate state.
This territory welcomes all neutral, monarchical, emirate, conservative and independent nations seeking stable, peaceful diplomatic coexistence.

## Regional Core Regulations
1. Internal hostility, raiding, sabotage and verbal aggression between member nations are strictly forbidden.
2. All inter-state telegrams and exchanges shall follow polite, peaceful diplomatic norms.
3. The founding nation holds permanent supreme founder authority to safeguard regional order and security.

## Regional Traits
Governance: Emirate &amp; Monarchy
Stance: Neutral, Non-Aligned
Culture: Traditional, Sovereign-Focused
