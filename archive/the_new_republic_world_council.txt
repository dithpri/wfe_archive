The New Republic World Council is a Region of Libertarian policy that will allow nations to do what they want to an extent. The regional government shall only intervene on the behalf of another nation&#39;s rights or on behalf of the common good. A regional Military shall be established for the purpose of defense of all nations. [b]Nations have the right to secede at any time, but along with this the protection and privileges of our region shall be gone[/b]. The government may take a vote or by executive kick you out of the region if you hurt another regions rights. (Note: Executive can be overturned by a simple majority by governmental vote).
