[b][u]OVERVIEW[/u][/b]
The New World Order (Ordinis Orbis Novi or OON) is a 1:1 scale representation of the Earth played in real time as in a day is equal to one day (while population may grow fast other aspects of game play will be more in time this way). It is a Role Play region where everyone is Welcome and WA members (or any Political Party members) whom plan to say can establish themselves on the map and in regional politics.
