The [url=https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Iroquois#Iroquois_Confederacy]Iroquois Confederacy[/url] was a collection of 5 Native American nations unified under one banner, governed by a Grand Council of chiefs. The confederacy was eventually defeated along with the British in the American Revolutionary War by the United States.[/color]
