A loose confederation set up by the major superpowers of the Arcanial world; Sonvia, Notrus and Great Frast. The Confederation of Arcanial Nations (CAN) aims to build a cooperative, inclusive and unified international community through out the world, no matter what ideology or belief which sets countries apart. It&#39;s a confederation for guaranteed peace and prosperity for all nations, without any discrimination of ideology, beliefs, religion or views.
