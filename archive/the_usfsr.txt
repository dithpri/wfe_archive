The Union of Sovereign Federative Socialist Republics is the supranational union of all the Sovereign Federative Socialist Republics in Croxia. For this reason, it is often referred to as &amp;#39;The Croxian Union&amp;#39;. The USFSR is under the rule of the Communist Party of the Croxian Union (CPCU), which strives to create a Communist Society.
