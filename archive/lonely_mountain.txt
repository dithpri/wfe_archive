In J. R. R. Tolkien&#39;s Middle-earth legendarium, the [b]Lonely Mountain[/b] (Sindarin Erebor) is a mountain in the north of Rhovanion. It is the source of the Celduin River, and the location of the Kingdom Under the Mountain. The town of Dale lies in a vale on its southern slopes. &#151;Wikipedia[hr]
