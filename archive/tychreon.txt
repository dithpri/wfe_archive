In the endless, darkened stellar dust of the galaxy, a radiant cluster of stars drifts silently through space. Tychreon. An infinite array of diverse civilizations inhabit this primordial construct, each one unique in its composition, technology and perspective. Cooperating under the Tychreon Administration, life pushes outward into the void, seeking prosperity among the resource-rich uninhabited stars.[hr]
