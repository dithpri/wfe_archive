The Baltic Nations or the Baltic countries is a geopolitical term, which currently is used to group three countries: Igaunija, Latvimaa, and Leedumaa. All three countries are members of NATO, the European Union, the Eurozone, and the OECD. This Region of The Baltic nations includes 4 nations: Igaunija, Latvimaa, Leedumaa, and Drakor.



Population: 848 million
