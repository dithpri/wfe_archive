The Bottom of the Ocean was founded by is a gathering of national leaders who have banded together by the principals of friendship, laughter, cheap beer, and an almost disturbing love of octopus. 

The Bottom of the Ocean! a paradise under the waves. The bottom of the ocean is your one stop shop for a scenic region that contains but is not limited to a great ocean view, friendly wildlife to frolic with (deep sea submersible not required but heavily recommended), tons of pressure, and much more. The bottom of the ocean also has a small friendly community made up of friends, octopus enthusiasts, laughing men, and tavern owners. All are welcome in the ocean!
