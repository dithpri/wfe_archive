Myanmar, officially the [b]Republic of the Union of Myanmar[/b] and also known as Burma, is a sovereign state in Southeast Asia bordered by Bangladesh, India, China, Laos and Thailand. One-third of Myanmar&#39;s total perimeter of 1,200 miles forms an uninterrupted coastline along the Bay of Bengal and the Andaman Sea. The country&#39;s 2014 census revealed a much lower population than expected, with 51 million people recorded. Myanmar is 261,227 square miles in size.
