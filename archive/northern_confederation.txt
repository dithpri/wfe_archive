The Northern Confederation is a region that is dedicated to the preservation of international rights and national sovereignty while also promoting the ideals of freedom, equality, and justice.

The Confederated Unified Dollar (CUD) is the standardized universal currency of the Northern Confederation; adoption is encouraged but not required as a means to attain greater regional unity.
