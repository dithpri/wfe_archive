Founded by like-minded individuals seeking alternative lifestyles to those offered by &quot;conventional&quot;, &quot;law-abiding&quot; society, The Infamous Sea Sponge is a region resplendent in piracy, democracy, and plunder.

The Infamous Sea Sponge is a small region comprised of casual players from an existing off-site forum who wished to play NationStates together in one place, and have no desire to cause trouble for anyone outside of their own region.
