Calvinian is the lesser known ally of the New Republic, and now, the Resistance.Its Leader, Kwan Bo is a former Jedi.
Note: Calvinian is like a Russia, A former Communist Regime. It fought along side with the Rebel alliance(think of the Allies) to fight the Empire(fascism). Now, Calvinian and its allies,The Resistance, as well as the Green Coalition, fight the First Order.
