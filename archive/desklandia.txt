The island continent of Desklandia consists of 6 states: North Deskia, South Deskia, Saudia Deskia, South East Deskia, the Free State of Thrallistan, and the Empire of Aaronlayned. The nations are locked in incessant combat as the Free Nations of the South attempt to crush the gnarled, corrupt dictatorship of North Deskia. The Deskian Wars have raged for years. Who comes out on top?
