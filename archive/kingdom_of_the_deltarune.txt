The Kingdom of the Deltarune is a unique example of a left-leaning monarchy. While traditional monarchies are often associated with conservative values and entrenched power structures, the Deltarune has embraced a progressive approach, combining elements of monarchy with a commitment to social and economic equality.

Queen Chara Dreemurr I, the current monarch, ascended to the throne after her father, King Asgore Dreemurr, abdicated the throne. She is known for his hard-working demeanor and dedication to implementing progressive policies within the monarchy. Queen Chara Dreemurr I holds an absolute role, and is head of the executive branch of government.
