Arrathia is a warm, heavily forested planet, ruled by a multitude of powerful nations.

Arrathian Culture Guidelines:
Most Arrathian cultures tend to have a strong military tradition. While conscription is not universal, it is still quite common.

While Arrathians aren&#146;t usually very religious, but those who are tend to polytheistic, with an emphasis on comedy and laughter.


Game Rules:
No member state may join the World Assembly.
Member states may be creative in their design, but they must generally follow the Arrathian Culture Guidelines. Any divergence must be explained, in-game, in the factbook.
