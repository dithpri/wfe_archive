[Color=#0088DC] [region]Mandalanusa[/region] (formerly [region]Melayu Archipelago[/region]) is a realistic, current-tech, sporting, nationbuilding and roleplaying region with South East Asian theme.

The region is home to the Sepak Takraw International Council (STIC), Association of Mandalanusa Nations (AMAN), Confederation of Mandalanusan Football Associations (CMFA), and various other organizations.[/Color]
