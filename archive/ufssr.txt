The United Federation of Soviet Socialist Republics is a Supranational Union of many subnational republics, all of which have the common aim of maintaining a Leninist state in order to lay the foundations for an advanced communist society. The UFSSR is currently lead by President of The Union Karl Ulyanov, who also serves as General Secretary (effectively supreme party leader) of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union (CPSU), the only legal political party in the Union.
