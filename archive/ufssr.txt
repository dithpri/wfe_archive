The United Federation of Soviet Socialist Republics, commonly referred to as the Soviet Union or the UFSSR, is a federated union of subnational Soviet Socialist Republics, all of which is and are governed by the unitary Communist Party of the Soviet Union (CPSU), the only legal party in the Union. 

The current Supreme Leader of the Soviet Union is Charles Jones, who serves as President (head of state) of the Soviet Union and as General Secretary (leader) of the Communist Party.

A census has yet to be held to determine the population of the Soviet Union
