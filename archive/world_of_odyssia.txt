Odyssia: The Land of Dreams and Magic

A world of fantasy and dreams, Odyssia is spalling with magic and mysteries to those brave enough to find them. Whether they be cultists summoning their dark god, or fey playing tricks on travelers, the only limit is the roll of the dice. Separated into 7 major kingdoms, the world has had many years of peace, however dark winds are rising and as tensions strain and towns rise and fall, the only certainty now is that death awaits those who are unprepared.
