As the War of 2018 finished, the countries on Terra were destroyed by the Martian-Jupiterian-Saturanion military alliance. As Terra began to rebuild, the TREEWET (Terran Rebuilding Embassy of Energy, Water, Eugenics, and Terrastials) demanded colonies to be built upon the ashes of the former countries of Terra. Each &quot;nation&quot; is a region of the colony and is a council person.
