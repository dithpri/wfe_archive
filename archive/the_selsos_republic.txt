Feeder region for the [url=https://www.nationstates.net/region=republic_of_selsos]Republic of Selsos[/url]
[hr]
Can apply to join the Republic of Selsos by joining this region.
In order to join Republic of Selsos, a poll is put forth to [B]all[/B] current members within the region. A simple majority amongst voters is sufficient for you to gain entry. Once there, you will be recognised as a full member. The poll typically lasts for [B]24 hours.[/B]
It should be noted that the laws of the Republic of Selsos are also enforced here (excluding cartography laws). These laws may be found [url=https://www.nationstates.net/page=dispatch/id=669385][u][B]here[/B][/u][/url].
[hr]

[B]Please telegram [nation]Valgoren[/nation] upon entry, so that the Republic of Selsos may be notified.[/B]
