A region of islands connected in a Federation. Nations must be approved before they are allowed to set up inside the federation, as there is limited space and the populations are suspicious of outsiders.
--
The Federation is controlled through the Nubar Council, in which members of the Federation can debate issues and reach agreements.
--
The Nubar Security Council is made up of core nations within the Federation, responsible for safeguarding Federation territory and it&amp;#39;s citizens, other nations control their own territory inside the Federation.
