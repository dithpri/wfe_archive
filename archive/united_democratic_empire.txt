There are many nations in the world: monarchies, republics, oligarchies, dictatorships, anarchist territories, barbaric lands, police states. From old worlds come new, as does the United Democratic Empire arise from its dormancy, to protect the rights of free speech, free but accurate media, and direct self-governing. We believe every member of a democracy, as such the title, is entitled to work for their nation in terms of legislature, defense, and operation. And so, on almost every end of the political spectrum, the United Democratic Empire is a haven to allow those to receive their democracy, to act in their nation, and not just be a citizen.
