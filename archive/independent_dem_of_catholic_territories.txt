The full name of our Region is: The Independent Imperial-Democracy of the United Catholic Territories of Central Aldmerrow in Union with the Holy See. 

We are a strong Roman Catholic region and will never cease to be. 

Our region is based off of an actual society, and as such we strive to make all decisions in a manner that would be equal to them.

The Patron Saint(s) of our region is(are): St. Joan of Arc, St. Michael the Archangel, St. Maria Goretti, and St. Pope John Paul II.
