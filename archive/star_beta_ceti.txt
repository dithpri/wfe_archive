Diphda, also designated as &amp;#946; Ceti (beta Ceti), is a variable giant star of spectral class G9 in the constellation of Cetus. Diphda visual magnitude is 2.04, making it the 51th brightest star in the sky. Because of its brightness, Diphda is clearly visible to the naked eye when observed from locations with dark skies, and should be also quite easily visible from light polluted areas.
