[color=#8991A9]The Earth-Moon L4 Point is a position in space where the gravitational influence of the Earth and the Moon counter each other and allow for a stable position for many large space habitats. The L4 point is many millions of cubic miles in volume, allowing for many thousands of space habitats to orbit.[/color]
