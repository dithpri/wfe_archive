The Dual Continental Alliance, also called the DuCont Alliance, is an intergovernmental  political, economic and military alliance based on the Dual Continental Alliance Treaty which was signed on 4 April 2015. It is maintained by the FPPCIE (representing United FeruMavia) and the Empire of Newan.
Government : Joint Advisory Council (JAC/JAF) 
Military : Joint Continental Military (JCM)
Economy : Joint Industrial Sharing Plan (JISP)
