The Ininis International Coalition (IIC) is a powerful military alliance established to promote collective security, cooperation, and stability among its member states. Comprised of sovereign nations from diverse regions and backgrounds, the IIC stands as a bulwark against aggression and instability, committed to upholding the principles of sovereignty, freedom, and mutual defense. Through joint exercises, intelligence sharing, and coordinated operations, member states of the IIC work together to confront common threats and challenges, ensuring peace and prosperity for all within their sphere of influence.
