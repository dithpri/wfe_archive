[b]Carlisle[/b] (officially the &quot;States of the Peninsula&quot; or &quot;Carlisle Peninsula&#39;&#39;) is a region in North America. Formally created in 1935, the Carlisle Peninsula consists of three states: [b]New Fairbanks[/b] (officially the &quot;Republic of New Fairbanks&quot;), [b]Leila[/b] (officially the &quot;Democratic Republic of Leila&quot;) and, [b]Alphabourne[/b] (officially the &quot;Constitutional Monarchy of Alphabourne&quot;). It borders Canada and the United States.
