[b][i]An Anglo-American is an inhabitant of the United States of English origin or descent, whose native language and culture is English
[/i]
The term started being used in 1776 after British Independence to describe [region]Americana[/region]; our cultural identity whose folkways and customs historically have been those of northern Europe and now comprises most of the United States and Canada, with French-speaking Quebec a notable exception.

[region]The League Of Anglo American Nations[/region] is a Pro-Heritage organization within [region]National Socialist America[/region][/b]
