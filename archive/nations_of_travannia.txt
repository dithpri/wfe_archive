Travannia is a mountainous region and very coastal as well. The majority identify as anglo-saxon and live very calming lives with very predictable weather and not being near any fault lines in Prometheus. The animal and plant life is abundant making farming extremely easy to start. The regional currency is the dollar and have open arms to all countries of all categories.
