The Brongulus Treaty Organization is a political, monetary and military alliance established by the Brongulus Treaty, signed in [b]Brongulus[/b], the capital city of [nation]Bajookie Land[/nation] in early May 2022.

The collective currency of the BTO is the [b]brong[/b], which also has a pegged digital version; the [b]eBrong[/b].

All military forces of the BTO&#39;s member states are integrated under [b]BTO Joint Command[/b], based at the Grum Military Operations Center, located near the city of [b]Grum[/b], capital of the federal state of Central Bajookie, [nation]Bajookie Land[/nation].

The Brongulus Treaty Organization is a full regional partner of the World Assembly.
