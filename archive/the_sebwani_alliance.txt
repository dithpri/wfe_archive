The Republic of Sebwan (Cei Republisca De Sebwani) is a large country about the size of mexico. The capital of Sebwan is Blontfencent.

  Sebwan was founded as an empire (The Sebwani Empire) in 1345, and became an independent country in 1758, and the capital was moved from Ayadad to Blontfencent.

The main language of Sebwan is Sebwani.

The flag of Sebwan has a red horizontal bar in the middle of the flag, representing the bloodshed for the nation, while the two horizontal yellow bars on the top and bottom of the flag represent wealth and hope.
