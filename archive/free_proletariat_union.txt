Amendments

Amendments are passed by a 2/3 vote in the federal congress and approved by the president. They cannot be passed without these two steps.
Amendment List:
-Freedom of speech/expression for individuals (Not corporations)
-Freedom of privacy
-Freedom to protest
-Freedom to unionize without corporate consequences
-Freedom from corporate racism or any other forms of discrimination in the professional workforce
-The constant progression towards a socialist revolution that allows workers to equally share ownership of corporations in a democratic way
