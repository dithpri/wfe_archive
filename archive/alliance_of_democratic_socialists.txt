This is sister region of the [region]Democratic Socialist Alliance[/region] (DSA), a democratic socialist region that welcomes socialists, democratic communists, left-wing anarchists, environmentalists and civil libertarians.

All dictatorial, totalitarian, racist, and imperialist nations are banned. Nazis, racial or religious Supremacists, Fascists, Imperialists or anyone supporting any form of discrimination will be immediately deported and permanently banned from the region.

All nations with civil rights or political freedoms ranked less than &quot;some&quot; are banned. All nations in prohibited categories are banned. For a complete list see the Charter, Article I, Section I.
