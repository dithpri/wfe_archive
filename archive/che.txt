[b]Ernesto [u]&quot;Che&quot;[/u] Guevara[/b] (1928-1967) - Argentinian doctor; joined Castro in Mexico in 1954; a leader of the 1956-59 Cuban Revolution. Che served as president of Cuba&#39;s national bank and as Cuba&#39;s minister of industry in the period immediately following the Cuban Revolution. In 1965 Che left Cuba to set up guerrilla forces first in the Congo and then later in Bolivia, where he was ultimately captured and killed in October 1967. Accounts of his execution have varied over the years, but many contemprary accounts indicate some degree of collaboration between Bolivia&#39;s government troops and the United States CIA.
