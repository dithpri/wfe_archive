[b][i]The starship ardent: one of the universe&#39;s most advanced pieces of technology. Equipped with weapons capable of incredible destruction, and a jump drive able to allow the ship to travel further than ever before. Truly it is the pinnacle of scientific genius and peak engineering.[/b][/i]

Or, it was 800 years ago before being flung through wormholes, dimensional shifts, and other such nonsense. Having thrice saved the universe, both the ship and its crew expected to be retired.

But alas, no. Upon returning to their beloved star system, they found everything and everyone vanished. And so, the crew and ship proceed on their miscellaneous misadventures and strive to unravel what happened to their home.
