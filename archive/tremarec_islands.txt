The Tremarec Islands is a shared nation ran and moderated by the [nation]San Carlos Islands[/nation]. The goal is to create a shared nation not ran by a bland and common direct democratic system, but one defined by a republic and parliamentary debate. We want to see what would happen if an NS nation was run by elected officials.[hr]
