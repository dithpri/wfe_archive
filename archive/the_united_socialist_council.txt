                                                                                                                                                                      -The Socialist Declaration 

        As of today and tomorrow, the United Socialist Council serves the people and the people only. Our obstacles are only to be overcame, not overlooked.
We as a region shall manage ourselves in a civil way and promote peace like we promote civil rights. We are here to serve the greater good of the region and its people.
