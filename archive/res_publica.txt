[b]Will the Republic stand? Res Publica is a Republican Rome themed roleplay region, set in the time of the First Triumvirate. 
The hope is to create a character centric, but faction-backed roleplay where players will forge alliances and adversaries as politicians, generals, or non-Republican Kings and Tribal Chiefs to meet their goals.[/b]
