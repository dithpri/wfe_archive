Official Name: International Socialist Union of Equitas
Abbreviation: ISUE

Overview:
The ISUE is established as a single, massive socialist state, with various socialist states and republics incorporated within its territory as Member Republics. Each member republic retains a degree of autonomy, but adheres to the socialist principles and economic policies set by the central government, and is governed based on the ideals of equality and justice (Equitas).
RULES
1. no spam
2. This region is one large federation.
3. We are all equal and have the right to say what we want.
4. 1 person cannot hold all the power (no dictators).
5. You are allowed to leave and join at anytime.
6. Try to be either Communist or socialist.
