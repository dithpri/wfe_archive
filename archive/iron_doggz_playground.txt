The Iron Doggz playground is a large area located in north america, founded by Gallows, of the Gallows Badlands and named after a childhood club he formed. It is known as a playground because of its vast amount of unique nations located there. Nations in the Iron Doggz playground are mostly individual however do receive help from one another from time to time. All nations are permitted a trial stay but can be forcibly removed by the other nations if it seems fit.
