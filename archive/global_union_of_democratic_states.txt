The Global Union of Democratic States, more commonly known as GUDS, is an organization established in 1948 by the Socialist Federation of Gornica, United Councils of Rhensar, and the Asarithiyan People&#39;s Republic to counteract the formation of the Transphalerian Treaty Organization in 1946. It serves as a platform for all socialist states worldwide to unite against the forces of imperialism through mutual economic and defensive aid. Starting from 1958, GUDS offers a platform for cooperative space research for all of its members, and also offering joint space missions to the Sathlum and Tyiakan Moons.
