Natopia is a dominion, ruled by a Chancellor.  Various sub-national states comprise the Dominion, each has the right to run it&amp;#39;s own government and protect it&amp;#39;s own cultural uniqueness.  The Dominion controls and coordinates foreign affairs and defense.

The Dominion of Natopia is a micronation with [url=http://bastionunion.org/forum/viewforum.php?f=18]off-site forums[/url].  Please visit us!

As a micronation, Natopia has existed for over ten years.  In that time we have risen and fallen, conquered others, made art and literature, and made significant impacts on our community.  

The Dominion is open to any and all nations wishing to join with us.
