The Caspian League is an international, interuniversal military, political, and economic alliance created on April 4th, 2016 and officially formed with the signing of the Caspian Charter on May 11th, 2016.

The Caspian League is comprised of many nations each with different political ideologies and economic practices ranging from liberal-benevolent fascist dictatorships to democracies to monarchies, and from socialist economies to laissez-faire capitalist economies.
