The Cxesloviak Peninsula was founded in November of 2019. The Cxesloviak Peninsula was named after the indigenous peoples that originally populated the area, the [i]&amp;#268;eslovar[/I]. Founded by the Federal Republic of Cxeslovia, the Cxesloviak Peninsula currently manages its own legislation, military affairs, and even has its own regional language, the Cxesloviak dialect, which is strongly based on Russian and American English. 

The Cxesloviak Peninsula extends a warm welcome to all prospective nations wishing to join its great union.
