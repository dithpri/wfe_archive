The World of Kimu

The World of Kimu is a sparsely settled planet, rich in natural resources and there are many creatures the live on this planet that are very similar to those that live on Terra. But there are many more that fit some of Terra&#146;s Legends, such as; Dragons, Unicorns, Griffons, etc. Also many of the worlds races; Goblins, Trolls, Giants, Dwarfs, and Elves.

Planet is divided into Three Continents; Stigaurd, Dofu and Kidor. With Kidor being largest and holding most of the planet&#146;s Nations.
Most of Dofu is covered by thick jungles; Stigaurd is a cold, inhospitable land of heavy Evergreen Forests.
