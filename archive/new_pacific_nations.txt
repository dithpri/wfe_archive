The New Pacific Nations (NPN) is a thriving archipelago in the continent of Oceania. It is famous for its remarkable mountains and tropical beaches. It experiences a tropical climate, and occasional storms. The region&#146;s commercial capital is the Republic of Singaporeia, and is renowned for the BayWay, a 20,500km highway stretching from Pacifico North to the Western Island Archipelago. It is governed by the founding nation of Singaporeia, and also the official management council, the New Pacific Nations Council (NPNC).
