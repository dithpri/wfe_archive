The New Pacific Nations (NPN) is a thriving archipelagic region in the continent of Oceania. It is famous for its remarkable mountains and tropical beaches. It experiences a tropical climate, and occasional typhoons, although not too many. The region&#146;s commercial capital is the Republic of Singaporeia, and is renowned for the Akantra, a 73,000 hectare artificial luxury island. The NPN is governed by the founding nation of Singaporeia, and also the official management council, the New Pacific Nations Council (NPNC). The New Pacific Nations also has a dedicated Countryball designed by the Tourism Office, the New Pacific Ball. The New Pacific Nations is also undergoing the &#147;New Pacific Christmas&#148; update.
