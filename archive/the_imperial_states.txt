The Imperial States are ruled by the Empress of Vasaburg: the ancient capital of the Imperial states. 

A legislation has been passed that will allow for a possible, ceremonial head of state. 

The Kingdom of Prussia-Hohenzollern has been grandfathered into the Imperial States as their head of state is cousin to Her Imperial and Royal Majesty, the Empress of Vasaburg. 

Any nation that has entered into the Imperial states must accept a single, united currency: the gold sovereign
