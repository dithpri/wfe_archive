The Monarchist League is an organisation dedicated to the preservation and promotion of the monarchical system of government and the principle of monarchy worldwide.

From kings and emirs, to pharaohs and maharajas, the Monarchist League welcomes monarchies of all types from across the world.

[b]Embassy policy:[/b] We will accept embassy requests from monarchist regions, though we will also accept democratic regions as well.
