Empire of Berkenland (EB) is a confederation of states allied to the Kingdom of Berkenland, as well as the Berkenlandic colonies. The Kingdom of Berkenland is the leader of the BE, and will always be. All nations that join must pledge allegiance to the Kingom of Berkenland. If a nation wishes to join, it must change its title to one of the specified titles.
