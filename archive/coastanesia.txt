Coastanesia is a large tropical mountainous continent, people of said region tend to indigo hair and white eyes 99.8% of the time these people are called Coastanesians. The International laws of Coastanesia (also called The ILC) prefers all countries in the region to be somewhat environment freindly. Coastanesia also supports sovereignty as long as the nation in question does not threat nearby nations. Coastanesia also supports having a strong military for each nation that are at least fairly strong.Natives of the region call Coastanesia Plip Naag which is Coastanese for Home Land.
