[region]Ostron[/region] is a small, yet powerless region made for countries and nations to enjoy the way life is to be lived.

It is ruled by a democratic assembly, which only purpose is to improve conditions of life in Ostron. It is compromised to make Ostron a better and powerful region, stable and fair for all living beings in it, whether they be wild or civilized, powerful or not.

Ostron possesses a large variety of countries and nations, whether they be monarchies, republics, federations, tribes or protectorates. There is a spot for everyone in the Ostrian society.
