[i]Are you ready for some magic?[/i]

Starting Wednesday, December 30th to Wednesday, January 6th

Celebrate the end of the year with a Harry Potter theme for the NationStates community in this revival of a favorite festival! All players in good standing are welcome to enjoy the events here and make some new friends along the way.

Select a Hogwarts House to be sorted into, and then compete in events including trivia contests, art &amp; writing competitions, a Yule Ball roleplay, IO Games, and more!
