Overview:

The Alliance of Benevolent Dictators is a regional bloc across the World, aiming for stability, prosperity, and security. Despite its self-proclaimed title, its founding nation, Greater Pakistan, is an Empire, lending a unique and often ironic character to the Alliance&#39;s stated goals. It seeks a multi-polar world order, comprising diverse political systems from authoritarian to more nuanced governance.

Membership:
Open to sovereign states, membership requires commitment to non-interference, mutual respect, and cooperation. The Alliance accommodates various governance models, from centralized to more liberal systems.
