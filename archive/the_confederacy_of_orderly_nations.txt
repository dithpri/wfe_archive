About the Confederacy
The Confederacy of Orderly Nations (CON) is a regional union of independent, sovereign nations who share a commitment to conservative values, mutual respect, and regional unity. Founded by The Confederacy of Myrodia Islands, the Confederacy was established to create a stable, peaceful, and principled home for nations aligned with traditional, right-leaning ideologies.
