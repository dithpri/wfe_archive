Shangri-La is an earthly paradise, a Himalayan utopia &#151; a permanently happy land, isolated from the outside world. The people who live at Shangri-La have an abundance of slack, are almost immortal, living years beyond the normal lifespan and only very slowly aging in appearance.

This region, much like the Nickel Cadmium Rechargable Church of the Poison Gauntlet, is dedicated to the beliefs and ideals (or lack thereof) of the Church of the SubGenius.
