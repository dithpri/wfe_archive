Founded by Royal Charter on December 31, 1600, the EIC is a joint-stock company that trades with the East Indies, South Asia, Southeast Asia, and East Asia.
 
The EIC&#39;s first ships sailed from England in 1601
Established their first trading post in India in 1612, and gradually expanded their presence in the region over the following decades. 

The EIC trades in goods like spices, tea, cotton, silk, and opium
