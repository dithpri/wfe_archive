The USDF is an up and coming region for nations in the spectrum of Social Democracy, Market Socialism, or Social Liberalism. We aim to ensure the right to free and fair elections and employment.

All members are part of the International Council, which allows them to discuss region related matters and to vote for positions.

As an upcoming region, once a sizable population arrives, we hope to start elections or Council appointments for positions such as Head Ambassador (Embassy) or Council Speaker (Communications). For now, the Head of the Council (Founder) Assumes all duties until sufficient population warrants new positions.
