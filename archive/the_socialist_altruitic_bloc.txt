The 8 (4/21/2016) Directives

I. Elections every month on the 5th
a. One month terms for up to two terms (Franklin Policy)
II. Nations at War fight with a 1 on 1
a. The defender choses a challenge: best two out of three
b. Loser must give the winner a penny
III. Issues will be discussed via a vote
IV. If applying for a position, the decision is up to the council
V. Alternate nations are not allowed to vote
VI. The first 6 directives cannot be removed regardless of voting to ensure equality.
VII. All changes in the directives or otherwise shall be decided on by majority vote.
Vlll. Referendum- Can be achieved with 1/3 of region population for any of the directives except the first 6
