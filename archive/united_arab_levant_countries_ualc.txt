The United Arab Levant Countries is a political and economic union and alliance established in the capital of the Kingdom of the Levant. Its goal is to unify the member states under a unified banner. The member states open their borders to each other, and their citizens can travel within the member states with their cards and without requesting residency. The currencies of the member states are also dealt with, customs are unified, and movement in cars is carried out using the license plates of the member states within the union.
