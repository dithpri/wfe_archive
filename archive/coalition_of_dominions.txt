Our coalition was made after the fifth war, (which destroyed the great empire, which will never be named again), several dominions rose out of these ashes, some sticking to the ideals that the great empire held, some claiming that those ideals caused its destruction, doing the opposite of what the empire held dear, but nevertheless, they had to form a loose coalition to not be eliminated by what destroyed the empire. This coalition was formed by the dominions of light and darkness, just to show that any factions could work together when a common enemy presents itself.
