[b][region]Panay[/region][/b] is a triangular island, located in the western part of the Visayas. It is divided into four provinces: [b]Aklan[/b], [b]Antique[/b], [b]Capiz[/b], and [b]Iloilo[/b], all in the Western Visayas Region. Just closely off the mid-southeastern coast lies the island-province of Guimaras. It is located southeast of the island of Mindoro and northwest of Negros across the Guimaras Strait. To the north and northeast is the Sibuyan Sea, Jintotolo Channel and the island-provinces of Romblon and Masbate.
