Times Square is a major commercial intersection and a neighborhood in Midtown Manhattan, New York City, at the junction of Broadway and Seventh Avenue and stretching from West 42nd to West 47th Streets. Brightly adorned with billboards and advertisements, Times Square is sometimes referred to as &amp;quot;The Crossroads of the World&amp;quot;, &amp;quot;The Center of the Universe&amp;quot;, and the heart of &amp;quot;The Great White Way&amp;quot;. It is the hub of the Broadway Theater District, one of the world&amp;#39;s busiest pedestrian intersections, and a major center of the world&amp;#39;s entertainment industry. Times Square is one of the world&amp;#39;s most visited tourist attractions, drawing over 39 million visitors annually. -Wikipedia
