The Trans-Hydian Borderlands are a small sliver of space that exists between the known galaxy far, far away and the almost entirely unexplored area of the galaxy referred to as the &#39;Unknown Regions&#39;. These two areas are separated by all manner of natural hazards, including black holes, solar storms, gravity wells and more. The Trans-Hydian Borderlands is the name given to this forbidding barrier.

Largely unexplored, it has recently been the target of a large colonisation and survey program by the Galactic Empire, with many small fiefdoms and sectors set up. With the sudden death of the Emperor, these star systems find themselves cut off, and without support from the galactic core...
