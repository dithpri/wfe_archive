[nation=noflag]Leithanien[/nation], formally known as the [nation=noflag]Leithanian Empire[/nation], is a Terran country bordering [nation=noflag]Kazimierz[/nation], [nation=noflag]Siracusa[/nation], [nation=noflag]Victoria[/nation], and [nation=noflag]Ursus[/nation]. It is a feudal confederate empire formed by nine Kreise governed by its Kurf&uuml;rsten who routinely elect each other to become the ruling Kaiser. The nation is predominated by the Caprinae and Elafians who are known to be talented Arts users, and a handful of Lupos could be found on its southeastern corner.
