[b][color=blue][u]Synopsis[/u][/color][/b]
The [u][region]Hashashin[/region][/u] (or [u][region]Assassins[/region][/u]) are an order of Nizari Ismailis, particularly those of [u][region]Persia[/region][/u] and [u][region]Syria[/region][/u] that formed around 1091. Posing a strong military threat to Sunni Seljuq authority within the Persian territories, the Nizari Ismailis captured and inhabited many mountain fortresses under the leadership of Hassan-i Sabbah.
