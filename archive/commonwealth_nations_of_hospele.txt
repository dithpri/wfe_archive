[b][u]Description[/u][/b]

The Commonwealth Nations of Hospele is a group of nations who came together to form an organization of nations who once used to be under the rule of the Kingdom of Hospele, a now constitutional monarchy. These nations recognize the Sovereign of Hospele as the ruler of Hospele, but does not grant the Sovereign of Hospele total control over their nations.
