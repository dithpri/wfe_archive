Aiming to provide and support a cohesive and productive community, [region]Dalaen[/region] is growing and developing from the very grass roots of what it is to be a community. 

The region fosters an open door policy to would be members and invites them to take part in various activities be it:

Writing for the [url=https://www.nationstates.net/page=dispatch/id=589541]&amp;#128240; [b]Dalaen Daily[/b][/url], 
Creating stories either alone or with other authors for our [url=http://www.nationstates.net/page=dispatch/id=589627]&amp;#127917; [b]Creative Archive[/b][/url] or 
Getting involved with [url=https://www.nationstates.net/page=dispatch/id=590100][b]Inter-Regional Diplomacy[/b][/url].

Being part of this community allows members to grow and develop in a relatively drama-free area and get involved or sit in the shadows as much as they wish. Members that wish to get involved are often marked out by those that [url=http://www.nationstates.net/page=un]join the world assembly[/url] but also those that craft articles and stories.
