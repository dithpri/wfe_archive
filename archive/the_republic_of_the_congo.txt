[color=006600][b]The Republic of the Congo[/b] (French: R&eacute;publique du Congo), also referred to as [region=Congo Brazzaville], is a country located in Central Africa. It is bordered by [region=Gabon], [region=Cameroon], [region=Central African Republic], [region=Democratic Republic of the Congo] and the Angolan exclave of [region=Cabinda].
