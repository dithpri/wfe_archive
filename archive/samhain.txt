[color=663333][b]Samhain[/b] is a Gaelic festival marking the end of the harvest season and the beginning of winter or the &quot;darker half&quot; of the year. Traditionally, it is celebrated from 31 October to 1 November, as the Celtic day began and ended at sunset. Historically, Samhain was widely observed throughout Ireland, Scotland and the Isle of Man.[/color] &#151;[color=993300]Wikipedia[/color][hr]
