After the great pandemic of 2154 which killed off 90% of the world population the remaining nations United to create COLAEF the Comtinuation Of Life As an Entity Forever, under this United banner all nations in COLAEF have become a space bearing nation and a joint effort is now underway to become an interstellar spicies. The nations of COLAEF have all thrived and created their own technology to help the others, most notably the terraform machine used to colonize Mars which worked together with the gravity stabilizers. COLAEF does not discriminate and enjoys the company of all people on the now barren burnt out world once know as Earth and has shelter and land for anyone.
