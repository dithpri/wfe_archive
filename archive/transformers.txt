[color=blue]More than meets the eye!

Two warring factions of robots on the planet Cybertron leave in search of resources. The factions crash-land on Earth and, millions of years later, begin their battle anew in Reagan-era America and across the globe.

Plots generally centered on a Decepticon plot or invention of the week, which would be used to gather energy or Defeat The Autobots and the Autobots&#39; efforts to stop the plan.

Most of the time the Decepticons were forced into retreat, and the Autobots drove off victorious. Often, a new character or team was added to one side or the other.[/color]
