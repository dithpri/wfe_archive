Gliese 581g is a planet found in Gliese 581 system, which consists of 7 planets, which the humans thought there were only 3 planets there.

One of the most important was the Gliese 581g, the most habitable of the seven. According to a human probe, it says that 5/8 is land, while 3/8 is a water.
They also have 3 continents, classified by geography. They say that the average temperature of the planet was 29.96 Celsius.
