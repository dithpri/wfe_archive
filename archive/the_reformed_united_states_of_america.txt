Civil unrest, economic turmoil, political gridlock, in hindsight the collapse of the old world government was inevitable. Under the contingencies and provisions of the United States Continuity of Operations Act and Continuity of Government Protocol; congress was ratified, emergency power was granted to a handful of executive agencies, territories of the former U.S. were reorganized, and the nation itself was reformed.
