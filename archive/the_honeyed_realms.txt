The Honeyed Realms was founded by Frong the Great Bear following the Great Renewal of Meccifo. According to regional tradition, Frong gathered the first nations around the Golden Honeycomb Table and declared, &quot;No paw rises alone.&quot; Today, the Realms remain small but ambitious, united by the ideals of solidarity, prosperity, and friendship. Every new nation is welcomed as another cell in the growing honeycomb of the future.
