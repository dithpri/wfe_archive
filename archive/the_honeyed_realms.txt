[b]Genesis:[/b]
The prehistory of the region was defined by fragmentation until the cataclysmic event known as the Great Renewal of Meccifo. Out of the ash of the old world emerged Frong the Great Bear, a massive deity figure of immense wisdom. Frong gathered the first nations at the mouth of the Salmonia River around the Golden Honeycomb Table and decreed that every new nation is a vital cell in our future.
