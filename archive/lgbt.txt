[b]LGBT[/b] is an initialism that stands for &quot;lesbian, gay, bisexual, and transgender&quot;. In use since the 1990s, the initialism, as well as some of its common variants, functions as an umbrella term for marginalized sexualities and gender identities.

Anyone covered by this initialism has a high likelihood of being extremely based.
