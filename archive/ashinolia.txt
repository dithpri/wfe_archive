The first peoples of Ashnolia found themselves among great planes, dense forests, tall mountains, frigid tundras, sandy deserts, and vast oceans.

Today, it is a world divided between competing powers and politics. The Communists of the East, whom follow the teachings of Hildro Masof of Milatova, and the Capitalist West founded upon the bourgeois revolutions of Jarin Los, the Monarchists in the North whom have roots as old as man, and the Reactionaries in the South who owe their recent foundations to Reisoch Freidaburg.
