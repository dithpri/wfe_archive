TARMOSSEEMIA&amp;#39;S BOOK OF LAWS


LAW ONE:

How we elect our representative.

1: An election will be had every other month on the first Tuesday of said month.
2: You may not serve more than 2 consecutive terms. (Four Months)
3: If you are absent for more than 2 weeks, we re-elect.
4: You must have been a member nation for at least one term in order

LAW TWO:

November Fourth is now International Unification Day.

LAW THREE:

All voting will be done majority-rules, first past the post style, unless otherwise stated.

LAW FOUR:

All countries to be admitted into the region must here by pass a majority vote by all nations present in this region, that have been here more than a week.
