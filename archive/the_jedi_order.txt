The Jedi Order is an organization that believes in the light side of the Force. The Jedi Order was founded by Grand Jedi Master Ian, who along with Yoda are the most powerful Jedi of all time. Protectors noted for lightsabers and ability to use the Force, the Jedi guard peace. The Jedi High Council contains of the twelve best Jedi Masters. The training of Jedi Padawans learning the ways of the Force and the Jedi Code from the more experienced Jedi Masters is famous. Over the millennia, the Jedi fought the Sith, who used the Dark Side, who try to overthrow the Jedi. The rise of the New Jedi has arrived, in the form of this region. Join, and join the fight against the Sith and the Dark Side.
