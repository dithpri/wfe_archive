The Federal Union of Modernist States is a regional union bounded together by modern political principles and is willingly comprised of a variant of state types. This union has been conceived by the ideals of allowing states to reign as they please within the modern doctrine and political ideologies of governments. Our Union is established in order maintain order and communication among our members and to further establish modernist states as those of correct order and supremacy. Archaic government ideals and regressive tradition are our opposition.
