Twonkia is a region where the Twonkian Empire, led by the wise and benevolent ruler Adamina Hellene, stands as the only organized society. The Empire&#39;s success in Trout Farming, Arms Manufacturing, Gambling and Woodchip Exports have brought prosperity to the region. Under the leadership of Adamina Hellene, the Empire aims to expand its territory by conquering and wiping out the neighboring tribes, to bring the lands they inhabit under her divine rule and promote the Empire&#39;s culture and values.
