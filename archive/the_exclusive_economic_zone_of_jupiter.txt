- An exclusive economic zone (EEZ) is a zone over which a state has special rights over the exploration and use of resources.

- Jupiter is the fifth planet from the Sun.

- Jupiter has no permanent population and cannot have this in the future. Hence it has no citizenship or government. All personnel present in the area designated as the territorial space of Jupiter at any time are citizens or nationals of some sovereignty outside of Jupiter, as there is no Jovian sovereignty. 

- The whole territorial space of Jupiter is divided and exclusively claimed by all countries that are part of the exclusive economic zone of Jupiter. 

- Jupiter is a Dependent State which is governed by a conglomerate of representatives of said countries.
