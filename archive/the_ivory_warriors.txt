[u]The Ivory Warriors Constitution:[/u]
[b]WE, The Ivory Warriors, having suffered under raider rule, shall allow not a single region to fall to Imperialism.[/b]


In a show of support for our allies, as well as for past crimes committed, let it be known that all residents of [region]The Infinite Alliance[/region] are henceforth banned from [region]The Ivory Warriors[/region] and their possessions. Furthermore, any and all operations between the two regions must be carried out through a third party. Travel between these regions is [b]banned[/b], as is any cooperation.


Signed, 
[nation]The Ivory Warriors[/nation] (Founder)
[nation]Leetmerica[/nation](General)
[nation]Enderoth[/nation] (Regional Delegate)
