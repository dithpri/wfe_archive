[b]Medieval Roleplay Region[/b]

The Medieval Roleplay Region is a region set in between the years 1,000-1,100 AD. The year is not clear, however, the historical period will be set at the beginning of the Crusades. The region is a roleplay region where nations assume the role of fictional medieval nations and roleplay their interactions. Historical events need not happen and we can shift the course of history. If you would like to join please submit an [url=https://www.nationstates.net/page=dispatch/id=522375]application[/url].
