Ripley is a city in and the county seat of Jackson County, West Virginia, United States. The population was 3,252 at the 2010 census. During the American Civil War, Ripley remained under the control of the Union except for a brief incursion by the Confederate General Albert G. Jenkins in September of 1862.[/color][hr]
