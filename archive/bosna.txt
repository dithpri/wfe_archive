This alliance compromises of many nations: Burh Momemnt, South Osechia, Sancte Vino, Not the UK, Arcsenia, and many others.

Named after the founding 5 nations of this alliance, BOSNA wishes to create a thriving alliance between all of its member states.

The aim of BOSNA will be to become a scientifically advanced organisation, with mutual defense pacts, while trying to keep crime, illicit drug activity etc to a minimum. Any nation that wishes to do harm or break the peace within the region will be forcefully ejected and banned.
