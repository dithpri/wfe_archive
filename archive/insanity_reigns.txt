[b]Insanity Reigns[/b] is dedicated to allowing its member states to do whatever it is they want to do without the obligation of mutual support or cooperation. The regional currency is the [b][i]Tonto[/i][/b] and the regional animal is the [b][i]Tiger[/i][/b]. While not compulsory, all member states are encouraged to adopt these as their national currency and national animal.

The express goal of [b]Insanity Reigns[/b] is world domination through the spread of anarchy. [b]Insanity Reigns[/b] also supports global re-population through Zombification.
