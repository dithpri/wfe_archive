The [color=navy][b][u]Royal British Colonial Community[/u][/b][/color] ([i]RBCC[/i] is a community for co-operation between dedicated British colonies, dominions and so forth. It aims to protect and safeguard the interests of all its members and ensure that universal human rights, economic freedom and the British Common Law system are respected and enforced. It is a region that&#39;s dedicated to the rejuvenation of the British nation and its people.
