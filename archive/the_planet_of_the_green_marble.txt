The Planet of the Green Marble is located in the Sombrero Galaxy, 28 million light years away from the Earth. Contains only one continent and the surrounding oceans. The planet is very small, only about 630 mile radius.
Leading country today is Rudolfia, but Podgornia is catching up. Main languages are English, Bulanese, Svenska, Latin and Slav.

After the human species was born they gained power over others when they put together The Four Stones. Circa 140 000 years later people started worshiping Iesus instead of The Great Stone, which they broke, and Cisselyan empire fell. 2026, 100 years after The World War, The Four Stones were put together. Peace came, but so did the aliens.
