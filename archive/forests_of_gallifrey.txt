[b]Regional Constitution:[/b]
1) This region is a direct democracy, with the right to vote accorded equally to every regional citizen.
2) Citizenship is automatically conferred when a WA (World Assembly member) nation has been in the region for the previous 72 hours
3) Citizenship is automatically revoked when either of those two things is no longer true.
4) No law shall be passed which limit free speech.
5) No law shall be passed which requires, limits or prohibits any national religious, government and/or economic ideology or practice.
6) No discrimination based on known or perceived religion, sex, race, age or socioeconomic status shall shall be sanctioned in the region.
7) No bill of attainder nor post ex facto law shall be enacted.
