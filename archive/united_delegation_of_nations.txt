UDOC, officially the United Delegation of Nations, is a sovereign region located in East Asia. It is the world&amp;#39;s most average region, with a population of over 135 billion. The UDOC is a multi-party state governed by the Executive Committee, with its seat of government in the capital city of Geneva. It exercises jurisdiction over 22 states, five autonomous regions, four direct-controlled municipalities. 
The UDOC is a member region of the Swiss Armed Forces and welcomes all nations.
