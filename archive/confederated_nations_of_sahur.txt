The Confederated Nations of Sahur is a loose central governing body organizing the nations of the Sahurian Islands just South of the nations of the Pacific Region.


The Constitution of the Confederated Government may be found elsewhere (ask me for it).



The nations of the Island of Sahur were lead here on The Great Crusade by the Thrice Great T. T. T. Sahur, said to be a giant man made of the finest mahogany. The modern populations are now split and govern themselves, but all trace their ancestry back to this figure.
