The Amydonian Dominion is an oppressed collection of countries ran entirely by the Amydonians and selected advisors.
All residents and nations within the Dominion are to follow a strict set of guidelines that are set in place by Amydonia, however are permitted to control their lives somewhat within.

The Dominon is settled in a beautiful, dry, icy climate which has many mountains and lakes, making it somewhat hard to settle on, but giving it stunning locations for tourists to view, however most tourism is banned due to Section 1.3 of the Leader&#39;s Decree.
