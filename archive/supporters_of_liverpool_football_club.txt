Liverpool Football Club (/&amp;#712;l&amp;#618;v&amp;#601;rpu&amp;#720;l/) is a Premier League association football club based in Liverpool, Merseyside, England. The club has won five European Cups, three UEFA Cups, three UEFA Super Cups, 18 League titles, seven FA Cups, a record eight League Cups, and 15 FA Community Shields.
