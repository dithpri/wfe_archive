[b]The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints[/b]
 We are a Christian primitivist church that considers itself to be a restoration of the church founded by Jesus Christ. The Church is located in Salt Lake City, Utah where majority of Church Members in America reside.

According to the National Council of Churches the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints is the fourth largest Christian denomination in the United States.
