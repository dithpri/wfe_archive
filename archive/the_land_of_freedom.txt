A Haven for Cooperation
Welcome to The Land of Freedom, a region established for nations that cherish cooperation and collaboration. This community operates without a central governing body or enforced regional laws, relying instead on consensus, mutual respect, and the shared pursuit of beneficial outcomes through free association.
Principles
Each member nation maintains complete autonomy over its internal affairs. The region thrives on open dialogue and voluntary participation in community initiatives, encouraging a diverse yet cohesive assembly of independent nations.
