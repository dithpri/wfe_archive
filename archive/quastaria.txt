Quastaria is an Earthlike world in sector 15a of the Cydronar system, notable for being the only habitable planet in said system. It consists of three large continents, with many outlying islands of substantial size. In a universe full of leaders who abuse their power simply because they can, the nations of Quastaria act as a beacon of light in the darkness.
