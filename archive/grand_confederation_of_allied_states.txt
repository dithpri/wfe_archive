The Grand Confederation of Allied States was founded by the 3 Great Principalities, with United Ordencon taking the initiative. These 3 Principalities were later joined by smaller colonial islands and protectorates, before finally pledging allegiance to the [nation]Imperial Royal Union[/nation]. 

All Protectorates of Imperial Royal Union and the 3 Founding Principalities must bear the State Crest of Imperial Royal Union in their national flag and relevant insignia. Independent Sovereign Nations, however, are allowed to keep their own flag.

There are still no intentions of electing any possible WA Delegate.
