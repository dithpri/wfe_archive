The Province Of Arcan is known for its lush forests and deep caves. It has one gigantic mainland and a few small islands surrounding it. Arcans history dates back to medieval times, When it had its own monarchy, But was toppled when the United Socialist States Of Marsaiden invaded and occupied the land. The old Arcanian government now ceases to exist, and its people are no more. 

[nation]Marsaiden[/nation] Is Responsible for the defence and occupation in the region of Arcan. Marsaiden rules over Arcan with an iron fist. Marsaidens military is ranked #3rd in Arcan.
