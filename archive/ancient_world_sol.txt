After the great &#39;Techsplosion&#39; of 2025, which saw numerous breakthroughs and incredible leaps forward in all scientific fields, humanity began venturing into the stars. It was decided that it would be unfair and imprudent for off-world colonies to be governed from Earth. Therefore, any extraterrestrial colonies established would be free to establish their own customs, identity and forms of governance.

This led to Egyptian Uranus, Aztec Mars, Roman Jupiter and many other strange space colonies.
