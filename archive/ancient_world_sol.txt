In the year 2025 Earth experienced the &#39;Techsplosion&#39;, which saw numerous breakthroughs and incredible leaps forward in all scientific fields. As a result humanity began the joyous process of escaping its earthly tethers and venturing into the stars. It was agreed that it would be unfair and imprudent for off-world colonies to be governed from Earth. Therefore, any extraterrestrial colonies established would be free to establish their own customs, identity and forms of governance.

This would lead to the rise of an Ancient-Egyptian Uranus, an Aztec Mars, a Roman Jupiter and many other strange colonies.
