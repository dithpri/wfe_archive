We are Nationalists.

We oppose Communism, Islamism, Liberalism, Socialism, Immigration, National Socialism, Irish or British Republicanism, Euro Federalism and Scottish Independence

We are a Movement of British Nationalists, British Unionists, White Nationalists, Ulster Loyalists, Zionists and Christian Fundamentalists. We support the Monarchy, Church of England and the Commonwealth

We support European Nationalists such as Britain First, Front National (France), Swiss Peoples Party, National Democratic Party (Germany), Tricolor Flame (Italy), Svoboda (Ukraine), Jobbik (Hungary) and Golden Dawn (Greece)

We also support Nationalists out of Europe such as the American Freedom Party, Australia First Party and the Northern Alliance (Canada)
