The Sicotian Empire. Built upon the endurance, prosperous trade and vigor of its past, this empire is now among the most profitable empire in its corner of the world.
Everything, from education to religion - protection to freedom, is past the highest peak for them!

The Sicotian Empire is run by who it worships: Reiza Samana.

The people of the Sicotian Empire are always happy. They live terrific lives.
Religion holds much importance in their lives and, if anything, has made them more proud. The people of the Sicotian Empire are quite spiritual as well and they have an abundance of celebrations and beautiful customs.
