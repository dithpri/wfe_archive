[color=red][b]Union of Democratic Leftist States[/b][/color]
This region is created so that democratic leftists of all colors, including social liberals, democratic socialists, social anarchists and true communists, may mingle freely. It will hold itself to the principles of transparent government, regional independence, state sovereignty, and freedom of expression. Debate is encouraged.

All members are encouraged to both read the Articles of Union and sign up to be a member state at the Register of Nations
