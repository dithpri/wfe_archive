The Open System is an area not too far from Norway. Over time, the Open System region grew and now extends farther out of its origin point into new areas right near Norway. It serves as a reminder that despite The Red Day massacre in Norway, Goundirk will always remember the horrors of Norway and will always seek revenge.

The Open System operates on the idea that a group that has earned its own freedom, should be able to operate however it pleases when in relation to itself. There will be no intervention until they begin to harm other groups outside of their jurisdiction.

Memento Mori.
