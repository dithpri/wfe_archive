[B][color=green]Islam[/color] is a religion of peace, mercy, justice and of complete submission to Allah.[/B]
[hr][hr]
There are 6 pillars of Islam
- Praying five times a day
- Hajj (pilgrimage) to Mecca once in a lifetime 
- Fasting on the month of Ramadan
- Shahada, There is No deity but Allah, and Muhammad is his messenger
- Zakat, support to the needy
- Jihad for the sake of Allah
