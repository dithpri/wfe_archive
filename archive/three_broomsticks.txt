A popular pub located in [url=https://www.nationstates.net/region=hogsmeade_village][color=#641E16]Hogsmeade Village[/color][/url], the [b][color=#641E16]Three Broomsticks[/color][/b] is a warm, cozy establishment owned and run by Madam Rosmerta. Rumoured to be as old as the village itself, the inn was popular not only with villagers but also hags and other non-human magical beings. 

The Three Broomsticks serves drinks such as Butterbeer and Firewhisky and is popular with Hogwarts teachers and students alike.
