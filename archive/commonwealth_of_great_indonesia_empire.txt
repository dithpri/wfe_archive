Commonwealth of Great Indonesia Empire established in 865 by His Majesty Emperor Yopi of Indonesia. 
The commonwealth has exist for 694 years and now leaded by His Majesty Emperor Juan IV of Indonesia.

Our mission is:
1. Provide good education for poor citizens of country members.
2. Provide good health care for citizens of all country members.
3. Help each other between members if one of the members is hit by a disaster (Natural and/or Non-natural).
4. Developing science and technology for peace purpose and human welfare.
5. Maintain relations between religious communities.
