This Commonwealth was formed with the aim of achieving common interests, especially in the fields of education, economy, military and health. Currently, the Commonwealth is led by His Majesty the Emperor Juan XXII of Indonesia.

Each member of the commonwealth is obliged to:
1. Helping other members of the commonwealth when experiencing disasters, both natural and non-natural.
2. Pay as many donations as possible.
3. Support other member countries that are attacked by foreign militaries.
4. Maintain world order as much as possible.

Each member of the commonwealth has the right:
1. Receiving assistance from donated funds when needed.
2. Received a transfer of knowledge from the Indonesian Empire.
