A tropical paradise Island, Antigua and Barbuda (Listeni/&aelig;n&amp;#712;ti&amp;#720;&amp;#609;&amp;#601; &amp;#601;n&amp;#7496; b&amp;#593;&amp;#720;r&amp;#712;bju&amp;#720;d&amp;#601;/; an-TEE-g&amp;#477; &amp;#477;nd bar-BYOO-d&amp;#477;;) is a twin-island country in the Americas, lying between the Caribbean Sea and the Atlantic Ocean. It consists of two major inhabited islands, Antigua and Barbuda, and a number of smaller islands (including Great Bird, Green, Guinea, Long, Maiden and York Islands and further south, the island of Redonda).
