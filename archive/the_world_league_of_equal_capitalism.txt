The World league of equal capitalism is based on the idea that we can all be equal under capitalism through regulated trade and commerce. With set wages and minimum working hours as well as pay increases to those who work for richer more powerful countries, it offers citizens an equal chance in the world of business and overall life. Anyone can be anything they want to be and can have their shot at the fast paced world of economics without fear of never being able to get a job again. When you join the League your citizens on average report a 100% increase in approval of government, corporations, as well as overall happiness and success. I sincerely hope you join The World league of equal capitalism to ensure your peoples future.
