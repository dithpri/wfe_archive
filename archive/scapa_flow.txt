[b][hr][url=http://www.scapaflow.co.uk/][color=red][i]Scapa Flow[/i][/color][/url][color=blue] is a natural harbour which has been used over many centuries, from the Viking fleet of King Haokon in the 13th Century, to the present day. It formed an important northern base for the British fleets in both world wars.[/color][hr][/b]
