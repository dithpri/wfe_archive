The Constitution of The Union of Super Economies.

Article 1

All the countries of The Union of Super Economies have the right to speak their minds, are allowed to say what the please, and are allowed to use any words they choose, Even if the words or phrases that are considered &quot;hate speech.&quot; Also, a country does not have the right to silence other countries.

Article 2

All the Countries of The Union of Super Economies have the right to practice the religion of their choice. One has the right too evangelize and convert other nations. One has the right to force a state religion on it&#39;s citizens, but not on other nations.
