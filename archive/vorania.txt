Vorania is a lush continent with fertile farmlands, rich forests and urban sprawls co-existing in harmony. 

Religious, linguistic and cultural freedom is one of the intrinsic characteristics of Vorania.

A tradition of respect for the environment means that modern industries and urban designs grow in tandem with environment protection. 

The Voranian Court of Justice and Arbitration acts to ensure that any issue arising between the different Voranian states is settled amicably.
