[b]The North Atlantica is home to a host of islands, shared between neoliberal and militarist Commonwealth of Economea and the iron-fist socialist regime of the Republic of Socialea. [/b]

Following the termination of all future elections and frequent attacks on their capital district, the Commonwealth is currently conducting covert military operations to disrupt the armed wing of the ruling communist party of Socialea, the Communist Republican Army (CRU).
