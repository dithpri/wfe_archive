[b]Founded by the andarian starfleet on: 04/05/2025![/b][i]Delphinus: is a small constellation in the Northern Celestial Hemisphere, close to the celestial equator. Its name is the Latin version for the Greek word for dolphin (&amp;#948;&amp;#949;&amp;#955;&amp;#966;&amp;#943;&amp;#962;). It is one of the 48 constellations listed by the 2nd-century astronomer Ptolemy, and remains one of the 88 modern constellations recognized by the International Astronomical Union. It is one of the smaller constellations, ranked 69th in size.[/i]
