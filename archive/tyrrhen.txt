Tyrrhen.A Land that was ruled by Tyrrhen the Fair and his the Varrian Empire.However the Varrian vassals rebeled against the latest emperor Rohan the III also known as Rohan the Fat.The Rebellion was succesful though many cities were destroyed and the number of casualties was 11.000.000.Three countries were born from the ashes of  the Varian Empire.To the East the Theocracy of Talantis,To the West the Principality of Sarengard and to the Northeast the Grand Duchy of Strunhall
