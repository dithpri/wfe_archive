We, the people of the tribes, nations, and communities of [nation]Dolliwud[/nation] - 
Resolve to constitute Dolliwud into a sovereign, secular, united democratic-republic of states - 

Thereby securing for the people of the United States of Dolliwud the following:

- Social, economic &amp; political justice
- Freedom of thought, expression, speech &amp; religion
- Equality of opportunity, human rights &amp; political rights (including the right to vote and hold office)
- Freedom to protest, organize, and petition. 

It is the unanimous belief of the Colonial Council of Dolliwud that securing these rights is not possible under the occupation and political integration of the Kingdom of [nation]Adultii[/nation], and a state independent from Adultii is hereby declared.
