[b]The Republic was initiated by the [url=https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Belarusian_Socialist_Assembly]Belarusian Socialist Assembly[/url], the [URL=https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Belarusian_Christian_Democracy]Christian Democratic Movement[/URL] and the [URL=https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/General_Jewish_Labour_Bund_in_Lithuania,_Poland_and_Russia]General Jewish Labour Bund in Lithuania, Poland and Russia[/URL], which formed a Belarusian National Council [Rada BNR] in late 1917. The Russian Empire then included the majority of regions [url=https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/History_of_the_Jews_in_Russia]where Jews then lived[/url]

In December 1918, the German army retreated from the territory of the White Rus and the [url=https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Soviet_westward_offensive_of_1918%E2%80%9319]Red Army moved in[/url] to establish the [url=https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Byelorussian_Soviet_Socialist_Republic]Socialist Soviet Republic of Belarus[/url] on January 5, 1919.

The [url=https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bolsheviks]Anti-Bolsheviks[/url] of the [url=https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rada_of_the_Belarusian_Democratic_Republic]Rada BNR still exists[/url] as a government in exile and attempts to lobby for interests of the Belarusian diaspora in countries where it has its representatives.
