[color=darkred][i][b]Where the conflict of the past meets the ideas of the present.[/i][/b][/color]


This is a region of an era similar to the early 1940s; technology had advanced, but not drastically, meaning no weapons of mass destruction, no internet, little reliance on electricity for everyday things, a lack of weapons like assault rifles and missiles, and propeller-driven aircraft and oil-operated naval vessels. The world has six dominating powers that have constantly had various alliances and quarrels throughout their history, but now a new breed of war looms over the horizon, threatening to be so large scale that no one knows who or what might come out the other end...
