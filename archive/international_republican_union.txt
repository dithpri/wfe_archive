The International Republican Union (IRU) is a conservative alliance of nations with a broad spectrum of center-right ideologies. Founded in August 2012, the IRU stands for preserving economic, political, and personal liberty; and maintaining a moral, prosperous, democratic, and independent region.  The IRU opposes both fascism and communism.  All are welcome, whether you&amp;#39;re Christian, Jewish, or Muslim.
