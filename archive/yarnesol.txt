Yarnesol (Jarnesol; Jarnesyl) is a group of islands in the Sorliac Ocean. There are five sovereign nations: Arfahammer (State of Arfahammer), Cez-Zaeri (United Republics of Cez-Zaeri), Nyasott (Confederation of Nyasott), Sarlsvert (United Sarlic Principalities), and Sorlia (Sorliac Union). There are several claims which are condominiums between several of these sovereign nations. All states are members of the Millennium Five which mediates disputes, and oversees cooperation on several policy areas from climate change to international diplomacy.
