The Free Nations are a collection of nations who have signed The Nations For Independance Treaty detailing the peaceful coexistence of all nations, a limit on military expenditure within own nation and the support of the worse off citizens and nations. The treaty also states that all technological and physiological distinctiveness must be shared with all nations within the region.

If you are interested in signing the treaty yourself, or if you know a nation who wants to sign the treaty, then send a telegram to [nation]Albion State[/nation] detailing your request.
Note that signing the treaty is subject to certain restrictions including agreeance to all aspects of The Nations For Independance Treaty.
