[u][b]TERRITORY OF [region]THE COMMONWEALTH OF CROWNS[/region][/u][/b]

A region originally born against the principles of equality of religions has been reformed now .We believe that all religions lead to God and the Almighty has several names like Jehovah, God, Allah, Krishna and Buddha. We believe in salvation through unity of religions and humankind. We denounce fundamentalism and intolerance .

This region was handed over to the RDF of [region]The Commonwealth of Crowns[/region] in Jan &amp;#39;2013.
