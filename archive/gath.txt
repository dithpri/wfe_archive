[b][color=#d7bb00]The Realm of Gath[/color]
[i]Prudence, Virtue, Wisdom[/i][/b][hr]An ancient and storied region first founded in 2009, Gath stands today as a keeper of a legacy from times long past. 

Once a land of wars and revolutions, Gath is now a refuge for the lost and the rejected, and a home to any seeking peace in a tumultuous world. With hospitality extended to traveler and settler alike, Gath opens its arms to those who would embrace it and offers to share its peace with them.
