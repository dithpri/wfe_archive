Kinguzurando is the continent in which the native Indians, known as Masayans, were separated between their customs and beliefs. Different things changed their views. This caused the great continental country of Masaya to fall, and tribes seperated into different groups, which lead to city-states, which lead to countries. Many countries continue to rise in economic power making Kinguzurando a very successful continents and region.
