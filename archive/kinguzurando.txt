[color=orange]Kinguzurando witch translates to &amp;quot;The Land Of Kings&amp;quot; in Old World Masayan. This is do to its rich history of wars between many feudal lords, treaties of peace, 3 golden ages, and the betrayal of the ancients. Kinguzurando is the continent in which the native peoples, known as Masayans, were separated from their customs and beliefs. Many things changed their views when the Kinguzurando Renaissance began. This caused the great empire of the Masaya to fall, and the various clans seperated into different parts of the continent. This lead to the formation of city-states, which in turn became very distinct countries. Many countries continue to prosper making Kinguzurando a very successful continents and region.[/color]
