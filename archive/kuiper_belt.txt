The Kuiper belt (/&amp;#712;ka&amp;#618;p&amp;#601;r/ or Dutch pronunciation: [&#39;k&#156;y&amp;#815;p&amp;#477;r]), occasionally called the Edgeworth&#150;Kuiper belt, is a circumstellar disc in the outer Solar System, extending from the orbit of Neptune (at 30 AU) to approximately 50 AU from the Sun.
