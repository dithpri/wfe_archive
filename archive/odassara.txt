The Odasarra Region comprises five independent States, three of which have a coast on the Kumatqesh Ocean. Applicant, the People&#146;s Democratic Republic of Anduchenca (&#147;Anduchenca&#148;), and Respondent, the Federal Republic of Rukaruku (&#147;Rukaruku&#148;), are two of the three coastal States. They do not, however, share a land or maritime boundary. Anduchenca is located in the northern part of the region, whereas Rukaruku is located in the southern part. Since the Middle Ages, the nations of the Odasarra Region have been heavily dependent on trade amongst themselves and across the Kumatqesh Ocean.
