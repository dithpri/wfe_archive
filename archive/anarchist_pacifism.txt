Anarcho-pacifism is a form of anarchism which completely rejects the use of violence in any form for any purpose.

Anarcho-pacifism combines anarchism and pacifism, rejecting the state as a violent, coercive institution while also rejecting the use of violence to bring about an end to the state, viewing violence as authoritarian and coercive in of itself.
