Joining The Niam Peninsula Region, automatically enrols you into the &#147;Niam Peninsula Economic Union&#148; (NPEU). This is a Union founded by all three Niamese nations.

The Niam Peninsula is located in North-western Europe. It is large, resourceful and is steeped in the tradition and culture of the Niamese people, the three nations were once one until separating in the 1990&amp;#39;s due to religious and political differences.
