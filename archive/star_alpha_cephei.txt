Alderamin, also designated as &amp;#945; Cephei (alpha Cephei), is a variable and multiple main-sequence star of spectral class A7 in the constellation of Cepheus. Alderamin visual magnitude is 2.44, making it the 85th brightest star in the sky. Because of its brightness, Alderamin is clearly visible to the naked eye when observed from locations with dark skies, and should be also quite easily visible from light polluted areas.
