After the destruction of Roman Rule in Britannia in 410 A.D, the Britons and remaining Roman Soldiers were left to fend for themselves, they faced great hardships, such as the savage picts and Anglo-Saxons. Eventually, they migrated to Modern Day Wales and continued Romano-British culture there.
