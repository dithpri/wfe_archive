The Apomonotismish Lands is the center of the Apomonotismish Federal Network (AFN). Original member regions: Apomonotismish Lands, Haven Euphoria, Warzone Anarchy, The Hub, Apomonotismish Academy. More regions can join the AFN by telegramming the Administration of Reonese Empire.
