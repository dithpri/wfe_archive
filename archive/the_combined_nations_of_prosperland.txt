 100 years ago, the people of Glorios moved from the West Pacific to a new and beautiful land. They travelled across oceans, mountains and deserts to finally find a place that they can call home. Welcome to Prosperland! This rich and vast land encompasses numerous kind and benevolent nations, which strive to assist newcomers in their trials and tribulations. Whether you are a psychotic dictatorship or a civil rights lover fest, you will feel right at home in glorious Prosperland.
