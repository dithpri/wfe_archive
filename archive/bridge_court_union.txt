The [region]Bridge Court Union[/region] is a military alliance between the countries in the large, isolated region of Bridge, located in the Court Mountains. The idea of the Union is for member states to defend each other from nations who wish to cause harm to the union. Aside from [nation]Fargenvale[/nation] and [nation]The Ring Line[/nation], the Union is active in the World Assembly. [nation]Kallininngrad[/nation] and [nation]New Chesterfield[/nation] share a friendly rivalry over who can manufacture the most arms and give their citizens the greatest chance of repelling an unlikly foreign invasion.
