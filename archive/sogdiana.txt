[b]Sogdiana[/b] or [b]Sogdia[/b] was the ancient civilization of an Iranian people and a province of the Achaemenid Empire. Sogdiana is &amp;quot;listed&amp;quot; as the second of the &amp;quot;good lands and countries&amp;quot; that Ahura Mazda created. Sogdiana, at different times, included territories around Samarkand, Bukhara, Khujand, Panjikent and Shahrisabz in modern Tajikistan and Uzbekistan.
