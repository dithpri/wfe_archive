The Union of Northeast Asian Systems (U.N.A./U.N.A.S.) is an alliance between the countries and states in the northeast Asia region. The Union was founded and is led by the PRS (People&amp;#39;s Republic of Saikhangazar, which means &amp;quot;nice place&amp;quot; or &amp;quot;beautiful land&amp;quot; in Mongolian, depending on the context).
Historically, the Union has been socialistic in nature, sometimes to the point of being extreme, though it sees its fair share of moderation.
