The Northern Edgy Tip is an alliance between edgy dystopias that came together because they hated everyone else more then they hated each other. One of the first things they did to secure their territory was do a massive ethnic cleansing of the entire region they were moving to, then force all their people to travel there by foot, killing anyone that refused to move. Once they reached their promised land they began exploiting the environment and abusing the few remaining indigenous people while planning ways to sabotage their allies.
