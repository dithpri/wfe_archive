Klieger&#39;s land portion is generally divided into several, landmasses names continents. There are 3 of them, from largest to smallest: Eutania, Aurita and Heiter. If to be more specific, it can be divided as follows: South Eutania, Central Eutania, North Eutania, West and East Auritia, the latter of which is directly connected with Heiter.

Heiter is the biggest of the continents, containing Warantina, Lebenista, Balurdia and several uninhabited islands. Eutania comes second, meanwhile, Auritia is the smallest.
