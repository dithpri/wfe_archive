You are looking for a nice and reasonable region, focussed on nationbuilding and roleplaying? You are tired of elitist or arbitrary regions? Then you found the right place!
 
Small Globe is an easy and liberal region. Not much rules, no applications or outside forum. This region is about nationbuilding, economy, diplomacy and trade. This is no place for superpowers, expansion players or war-hungry powerplayers.
 
For a good start, read the [url=https://www.nationstates.net/page=dispatch/id=1093704]charta[/url] and take a look at our [url=https://www.nationstates.net/page=dispatch/id=1093395]map[/url]. Settle here and make your nation a comfortable home in a nice community!
