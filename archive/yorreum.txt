The Continent of Yorreum has stood a sentinel in the ocean for time immemorial. Home to nations great and small, the region was, until very recently, a very tumultuous place, with people and ideas going into and leaving war in mere years. The oldest &#39;nation&#39; in the region is the Allied States of Greater Gatk, though it is debatable if Gatk can truly be considered a nation, or just an alliance of states.
