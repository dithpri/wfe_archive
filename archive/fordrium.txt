Fordrium is a mysterious continent that has been hidden from the world... until now. The Ivanites stumbled across this strange land on their journey to find a safe home. There is a native empire, with unique customs, and strange technology. Ancient ruins litter the area, and unusal happenings are common throughout the wilderness. What will your nation face in the land of Fordrium?

This is a region where anything can happen. Anything. However, it is up to the nations to venture deeper into Fordrium...
