[b][region]Pewaukee[/region] is a European-American city in Waukesha County, Wisconsin famous for manufacturer of sailboat and racing yachts. European-Americans comprise over ninty-seven percent of the population.

[region]Pewaukee[/region] is a Christian city

The Clark House Museum was originally a stage coach inn on the Watertown Plank Road that ran from Milwaukee to Watertown. The museum displays include exhibits on Native Potawatomi settlement. Nations of Chippewa, Ottawa and Potowatomi comprise the Council of Three Fires. Potowatomi speak the Algonquian language, and both Nation and Language are protected by the State[/b]
