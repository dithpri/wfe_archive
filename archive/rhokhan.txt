Sixty newly independent nations are carved out of the former Rhokhani Empire. Although every nation on Rhokhan share the same heritage, language, culture and traditions, the regions that make up the new nations different based on their location and their priority in the former imperial government. In the post-revolutionary atmosphere, there is a state of flux and instability as new governments are created and toppled. Foreign powers have used the political instability to either assist in the creation of new nations that best serve their interests. After the fall of the imperial government, the Intercontinental Congress of Rhokhan as a forum in which nations could use for dialogue in their individual disputes.
