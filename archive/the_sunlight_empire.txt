The Sunlight Empire is the divine seed of the suns will and seeks to spread the cleansing light of the sun to all those who inhabit the region though no one is forced to accept it of course. Those countries that live in our dominion are free to run their internal affairs how they please but must submit to the, otherwise benevolent, piety of The Tested Unity. Involvement in regional affairs is not required and nations may remain solitary on request.
