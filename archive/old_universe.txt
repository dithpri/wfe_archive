The Old Universe is made up of several races. Leeams control most of the Universe with the Leeam Emperor ruling over the entire Universe from his flag ship, The Leeam Ship. The Kammerons are the creators of the Old universe, when the leader of the Kammerons slapped his dick and caused the big bang, creating the Old Universe. The Rikkiez are a retarded race, so retarded that they are classed as non-sentient. They breed to the point that there is an infinite amount of Rikkies. They have hybrids of each race in the Old Universe and have a Rikkie for everything imaginable.
