The Castle (German: Das Schloss, also spelled Das Schlo&szlig; [das &amp;#712;&amp;#643;l&amp;#596;s]) is a 1926 novel by Franz Kafka. In it a protagonist known only as K. arrives in a village and struggles to gain access to the mysterious authorities who govern it from a castle.-https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Castle_(novel)
