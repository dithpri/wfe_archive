The Seoul National Capital Area, which includes the surrounding Incheon metropolis and Gyeonggi province, is the world&amp;#39;s second largest metropolitan area with over 25 million people, home to over half of South Koreans along with 632,000 international residents. Seoul is the world&amp;#39;s third most visited city after Paris and London with over 10 million international arrivals in 2012.[/color]
