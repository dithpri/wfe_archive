For centuries, [b][color=blue]Novgorod[/color][/b] was one of the premier cities of Russia, known throughout Europe as a centre of refinement, culture and [b][color=gold]prosperity.[/color][/b]

[b][color=blue]Novgorod[/color][/b] was eventually destroyed by the forces of Muscovy, but its name lived on. Now [b][color=blue]Novgorod[/color][/b] has the potential to rise again.

[b][color=blue]Novgorod[/color] is now a Crown Dependency of the Empire of [region]Normandy[/region]. Within this arrangement it can grow and develop.[/b]

[region]Novgorod[/region] is open to any and all who are interested.
