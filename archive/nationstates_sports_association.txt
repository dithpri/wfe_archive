Welcome to the NationStates Sports Association (NSSA), the governing body of the premier sports leagues in NationStates. If you have a sport league and you want to be recognized by the NSSA, telegram [nation]Buffalo Sports Association[/nation].

[B]NSSA Classification[/B]
Professional League- Leagues that have the most skilled Athletes of the respected sport
Amateur League- Leagues that have mid level Athletes of the respected sport
Junior League- Leagues with beginning or novice  Athletes of the respected sport
