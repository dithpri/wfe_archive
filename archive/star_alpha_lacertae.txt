&amp;#945; Lacertae is a double main-sequence star of spectral class A1 in the constellation of Lacerta. &amp;#945; Lacertae visual magnitude is 3.77. Because of its moderate brightness, &amp;#945; Lacertae is easily visible to the naked eye from locations with dark skies, while it might be barely visible, or not visible at all, from light-polluted skies.
