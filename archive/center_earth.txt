[/i][hr]Sometimes ideas drift far from where they began. Agartha is often thought of as a Western occult idea, but it was largely taken from older Asian legends: especially Tibetan and Indian stories of hidden spiritual realms like Shambhala. Europeans took Tibetan and Indian ideas about hidden spiritual realms and repackaged them as their own, stripping them from their original cultural contexts.
