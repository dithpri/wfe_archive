[b][u]The Region of [region]Sanusiyya[/region][/u][/b]

[i][color=#2f4f4f]&quot;Faith, Discipline, Unity.&quot;[/color][/i]

[color=#2f4f4f][b]Sanusiyya[/b][/color] is a region founded upon the dynastic legacy of the [b]House of Senussi[/b], shaped by faith, discipline, and communal obligation. Its nations inherit traditions formed in [nation=long]Cyranaica[/nation] and later unified under [nation=long]Libbiya[/nation], where authority was understood as stewardship rather than excess.

Governance within [region]Sanusiyya[/region] emphasizes [u]unity through duty[/u] and cooperation rooted in shared values, not transient ideology. Leadership is guided by moral responsibility, restraint, and historical continuity.

Nations who uphold [b]faith[/b], [u]order[/u], and collective strength are welcome to stand with [region]Sanusiyya[/region] in continuity and resolve.
