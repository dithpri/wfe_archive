This is a general region for nations within the Perseus Arm. This will mean nations on/in planets, stars, asteroid belts, moons, gas clouds etc. within this area.

The Perseus Arm is one of the two major arms of the Milky Way Galaxy, the other being the Scutum-Centaurus Arm. The Perseus Arm, with a radius of approximately 10.7 kiloparsecs, is located between the minor Cygnus and Carina&#150;Sagittarius Arms. It is named after the Perseus constellation in the direction of which it is seen from Earth.
