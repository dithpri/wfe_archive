Geography
CoO is a diverse and expansive region, characterized by its rugged landscapes, towering mountains, and sprawling forests. The region is bordered by the Azure Sea to the west, offering breathtaking coastal views and vibrant marine life. Major rivers such as the River Granite and the Silver Stream cut through the region, providing vital water sources and fertile lands for agriculture.

Climate
The climate in CoO varies from temperate in the lowlands to alpine in the mountainous areas. Winters can be harsh and snowy, particularly in the highlands, while summers are generally mild and pleasant. The coastal areas experience a more Mediterranean climate, with warm, dry summers and mild, wet winters.
