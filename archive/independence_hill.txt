To become a State:

-Set your title to State or Commonwealth.

-Set your currency to the Universal Treasury Note ([] = symbol for UTN).

-Put the title of &#39;Governor&#39; in front of your leader&#39;s name (if you are able to do so).

Independence Hill Income Tax, Law, and Policy apply to all citizens in all states of Independence Hill. State Income Tax, Law, and Policy apply only to citizens of said State. 

Failure to meet these requirements within one week of joining will result in immediate ejection. 
If no WA Delegate is elected, Independence Hill Federal Government takes on the role of Acting WA Delegate until one is elected.
