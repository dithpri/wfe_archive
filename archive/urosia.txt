Stretching from the Western Ocean to the Eastway Mountains and from the Northglades to the Southerlands rests Urosia. A large landmass with a large centrail area with hills, plains, mountains, lakes, rivers and the Great Desert to the far south. A region with great agricultural lands and natural resources such as gold, silver and other metals dot the land. 

Home to a number of tribes that united into forming alliances and nations, But who will dominate these lands as the super power?
