[b]Everglades National Park[/b] protects an unparalleled landscape that provides important habitat for numerous rare and endangered species like the manatee,  American crocodile, and the elusive Florida panther.

An international treasure as well -  a World Heritage Site, International Biosphere Reserve, a Wetland of International Importance, and a specially protected areas under the Cartagena Treaty. &#151;[url=http://www.nps.gov/ever/index.htm]read more[/url][hr]
