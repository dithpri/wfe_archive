[b]Bohemian Grove[/b] is a restricted 2,700-acre campground located at 20601 Bohemian Avenue, in Monte Rio, California, belonging to a private San Francisco&#150;based gentlemen&#39;s club known as the Bohemian Club. In mid-July each year, Bohemian Grove hosts a more than two-week encampment of some of the most prominent men in the world. &#151;Wikipedia
