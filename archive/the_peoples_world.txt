The People&#146;s World is an alternate timeline taking place in the Sid Meier&#146;s Civilization universe. This is a world where some of history&#146;s greatest civilizations are going through upheaval as the people work to take down what they see as a corrupt establishment in their nation&#146;s benefitting only the few. New world leaders are changing the status quo. Everything from Communists to Fascists, Demagogues to Optimists take power throughout the world in democratic elections or violent takeover backed by the masses. As the world becomes more decided between Right and Left it is up to you where you want to bring your nation.
