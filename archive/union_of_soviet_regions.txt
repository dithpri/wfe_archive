A centralized socialist federation built on collective planning, regional coordination, and strong executive leadership.

Government

The WA Delegate holds Executive Authority when granted, allowing enforcement of regional policy through:

Ejection and banning of nations
RMB moderation and suppression
Factbook editing
Appointment of regional officers

The Delegate operates under oversight from the Supreme Directorate and Regional Councils.

System

The Union is structured around centralized governance, coordinated development, and ideological unity across all member regions.

Policy

Member nations are expected to support regional stability, participate in governance, and maintain alignment with Union directives.
