[b][u] Overview[/u][/b]
Nations of Space is an intergalactic group of powerful and engulfed nations with resources that work together for a greater cause. Here everyone is treated fairly and holds a meeting monthly to uphold and address issues in this flourishing region. Joining the WA is not compulsory. In conclusion this great region will be of great benefit to the whole universe.
