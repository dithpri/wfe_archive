[u] Overview[/u]
Nations of Space is an intergalactic group of powerful and engulfed nations with resources that work together for a greater cause. Here everyone is treated fairly and holds a meeting monthly to uphold and address issues in this flourishing nation. Everyone is required to be part of the WA unless waived by the WA Delegate or by the decision of all nations. In conclusion this great region will be of great benefit to the whole universe.
