Palan is a peninsula and home to four independent nations. Each have been through a Socialist/Communist revolution and government change, but have different cultural, political and economic positions that keep them separated. There is some conflict among the nations, but for the most part they mind their own business and keep a peace treaty. [nation]Dirtcrakia[/nation] is the largest and oldest nation of the Palan Peninsula. Palan belongs to the planet of Zion, an Earth-like planet colonized by religious Earthlings that left Earth to search for their holy land.
