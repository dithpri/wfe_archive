The Star Forge is the glory of the Builders, the apex of their Infinite Empire. It is a machine of invincible might, a tool of unstoppable conquest.

The Star Forge draws energy and matter from a nearby stars which, when combined with the power of the Force, are capable of creating an endless supply of ships, droids, and other war mat&eacute;riel. 

As an artifact of the dark side, the Star Forge corrupts those who used it, feeding its desire for death.
