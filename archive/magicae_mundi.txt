The official region for the Magicae Mundi group on the forums. To join, please go to https://discord.gg/JbUQ7yB. Membership of the Discord is required for members of the RP group.

Magicae Mundi is a country-based Fantasy RP group set on the known lands of the world of Vardamir. Our idea is to allow you as much freedom as possible while maintaining a consistent history and magic setting. We are currently accepting new members. See the Aedis Aurora factbook for general rules.
