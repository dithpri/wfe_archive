[b]Arrakis-[/b]
Arrakis is a large planet with a large range of biomes, but with a trend toward jungles and tropics to the equator and tundras and ice lands to the poles. Recently, Arrakis has come under colonization by alien powers. From Equines to Homo sapiens. The sun rising upon the wild planet, large change rising as the new colonies struggle to maintain dominance over nature and, possibly, over other groups.

We are a Role play based region. Future tech and space. As of now we have no open RP.
