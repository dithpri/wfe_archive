The year is 2077, China has been plunged into darkness after a nuclear war with the United States. China is quickly defeated, but now America must recover from the devastating effects of the war. 

Years later the American Government reamerges from the well-made Vault-Tech vaults. The United States military is devided between the East Coast and the West Coast division. On the West is the NCR, on the East is the Enclave. The East is now trying its best effort a recovery for the American people.
