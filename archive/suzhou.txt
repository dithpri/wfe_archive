[url=https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/suzhou]Suzhou[/url] is a prefecture-level city in Eastern China. Suzhou is a major tourist destination and industrial hub of Eastern China, specializing in high-end manufacturing. It is renowned as the cradle of Wu culture, and with a population of nearly 13 million in the city proper, it is the 16th largest city in the world as of 2026.
