Vorshka is a region centred on the lands inhabited by [nation]Vorshka[/nation] before it [url=http://forum.nationstates.net/viewtopic.php?f=5&amp;amp;t=157204&amp;amp;p=8166288#p8166288]suffered defeat[/url] in the 3rd Valkan war. Subsequently [url=http://forum.nationstates.net/viewtopic.php?p=9170699#p9170699]reformed under a new arrangement[/url] between the two continental powers of [nation]Sebvorca[/nation] and [nation]Vorshka[/nation], the region of Vorshka is now composed of that nation, various outlying islands (in anarchy), and nations that have arrived in the expanding region of Vorshka.

The region is protected by a password as an anti-raiding device. 

Foreign nations are welcome to drop in [url=http://forum.nationstates.net/viewtopic.php?f=5&amp;amp;t=157204]on our political discussions[/url], subject to the unwritten rules of not interfering across tech-boundaries and not randomly invading. Subsequently, they may possibly join the region as well.
