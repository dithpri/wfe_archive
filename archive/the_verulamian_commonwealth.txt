At it&amp;#39;s height, the Verulamian Empire covered almost a quarter of the Earth&amp;#39;s surface. A terrible and ruinous civil-war that began almost a century ago finally ended a decade ago, and in the ruins of this once glorious and powerful empire rises a new Commonwealth, a loose coalition of former constituent states with the Laws of Justice and Freedom set as pillars of foundation.

Emphasis is placed on Religious freedom with a Christian ethos; freedom of speech, information and press and a Justice system separated from the Executive branch of government.
