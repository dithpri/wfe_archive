The Assembly of the Atlantic is an alliance based in the era of &quot;The New Era&quot; Which follows from the &quot;Great Collapse&quot; This suggests a world much like ours where the great nations of the twenty first century had collapsed. What followed was a new industrial age, and Earths geopolitical situation was reborn. 

This can be classified as a roleplay group, with the central idea that this is a modern era evolved from the collapse of the world order.

The Assembly of The Atlantic, based in the Atlantic ocean region, is an organization aimed at uniting the disorderly world to protect Human rights and preserve peace.
