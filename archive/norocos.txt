The region of [region]Norocos[/region] covers an area of 6.6 million square miles. Of this, 35% (2.3 million sq. miles) is arable land, and 64% (4.2 million sq. miles) is wilderness. The eastern (coastal) part of the region enjoys warm, humid summers and a mild winter, while the western (inland) part has mostly dry weather, moderate summers and little snow. The northern area is mountainous and scarcely inhabited, while forests prosper in the middle west.
