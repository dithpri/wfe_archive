The Continental Sovereign Union is a region for all nations on the same continent, that focuses on both continental politics and national politics. This region is modelled on the European Union.
Nations must have some form democracy and elections, and must be willing to play a fun and serious game. From wars to rivalries. Political power play to regional super powers.
Get ready Mr/Mrs or Miss President, Prime Minister, King, Queen, Chancellor, Emperor...ect.
All are welcome!
