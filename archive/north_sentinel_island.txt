North Sentinel Island is a province of [url=https://www.nationstates.net/region=region_for_liberation_and_civil_rights][color=red]Region for Liberation and Civil Rights[/color][/url]. Once famous for hosting a tribe of hostile hunter-gatherers, the island&#39;s inhabitants have been thrust into modernity and work hard to meet their fish and basket quotas.
