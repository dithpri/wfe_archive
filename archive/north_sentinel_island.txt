North Sentinel Island is a territory of [b][url=https://www.nationstates.net/region=region_for_liberation_and_civil_rights][color=Green]Region for Liberation and Civil Rights[/color][/url][/b]. Once famous for hosting a tribe of hostile hunter-gatherers, the native inhabitants have been thrust into modernity and the island itself transformed into an industrial zone.
