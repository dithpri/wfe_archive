Pan Europea was once a relative paradise on Earth. The time of unity and mutual understanding is long gone, and the region is now shaken by political instability and lack of respect between the states. However, out of the quarreling and distrust, a single nation has arisen. All previous alliances are shattered so that new ones can be formed. The once clear sides are reshaping and all nations now face the greatest decision they have ever faced: to which side will we ally ourselves? To choose the correct one, is to live forever in peace. To choose poorly is to invite destruction. Whatever now happens, the Pan Europea that we know, will be lost...
