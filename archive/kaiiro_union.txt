Kaiiro Union is an economic and political union of member states that are located primarily in Kaiiro region. Its name abbreviation is KU.
The KU operates through a system of supranational independent institutions and intergovernmental negotiated decisions by the member states. Institutions of the KU include the Kaiironian Commission, the Council of the Kaiironian Union, the Kaiironian Council, the Court of Justice of the Kaiironian Union, the Kaiironian Central Bank, the Court of Auditors, and the Kaiironian Parliament. The Kaiironian Parliament is elected every five years by KU citizens. The KU&amp;#39;s official capital is Kaiiro York, Kaiiro Special Territory. Her supreme security HQ of economic and defense is located in Golden York, Erados.
