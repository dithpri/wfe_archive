[color=green][b]The Tarusmic Peninsular, [i][nation=noflag]Tarusma[/nation][/b][/color][/i]

A peninsular extending into the west ocean and connected to the vast expanse of the Hotan desert.  The Tarusmic Peninsular is a politically diverse region with a rich history, where civilized nations are member states of [nation=noflag]Tarusma[/nation]: a political union with some elected offices.
