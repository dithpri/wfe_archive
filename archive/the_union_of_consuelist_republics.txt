The Union of Consuelist Republics (U.C.R.) is the union of the nations under the command and protection of the Tetrarchs, the four absolute leaders and Emperors of the Nation, of whom Her Most Holy Imperial Majesty Consuelo holds supremacy as the incarnation of the great Lemoness.
