A huge underground region below the southern ice-caps. Warmed by geothermal heat and powered by magma flows and heat venting. Much of the massive underground cavern is barren, with water sources and farming land fertilized by ash and grown with electronic UV lights. 

The Region is lead by a &amp;#39;council&amp;#39; of Dictators that handle various needs of the region. They convene occasionally to decide the fate of the nation, and while they are very isolationist, they welcome other countries of similar government organizations

You must be listed as a Dictator to join this region. You will be voted out if you do not change it after joining.
