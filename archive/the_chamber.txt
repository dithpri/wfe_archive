[b][region]The Chamber[/region] is a sub-entity of the [region]Fifth Empire[/region] housing the [color=green]IGA Citizen Association of the Social Community[/color][/b]

[color=blue][b]The Association [CASC] and The Chamber [TC] is lead and overseen by the Head Secretary, Chief Spokesman and Moderator [nation]Brazika[/nation][/b][/color] with support and contribution from the [i]Secretariat[/i], responsible for submission review and discussion control. The purpose of the Association is to envision and create new ideas for community-improvement across a variety of different thematic fields, and to lay foundations for new community laws issued by the Ruling Council.
