A Collection of Large islands In the North of Amentia. The Amentian Continent is mostly made up of sparadic islands in the North, West and South East with two much larger Islands in the South West and East. North Amentia is made up of three main islands with 20 Separate nations within them. The culture is diverse an most nations differ very greatly to each other. 

The North Amentian council was set up by HM King Hearwaed, the King of Spantopolis. It was set up in order to bring peace, understanding and a common goal to the Amentian Continent.
