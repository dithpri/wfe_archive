[B]Located by the edge of [url=https://www.nationstates.net/region=the_pacific][color=blue]the Pacific[/color][/url], [url=https://www.nationstates.net/region=further_east_asia][color=red]Further East Asia[/color][/url] is a region comprised of extremely competitive nations. Its founder, [nation]the Knot Republic[/nation], only insists that all nations must endorse itself. Offenders have vanished mysteriously to reappear elsewhere, in [region]The Rejected Realms[/region] and a local orbiting dark energy cannon seems to be at fault. The founder sometimes gives positions of power to nations when asked.

[url=https://www.nationstates.net/region=further_east_asia][color=red]Further East Asia&#39;s[/color][/url] regional animal is knot-a-fish, whose manipulation of quantum strings continues to baffle scientists. [/B]
