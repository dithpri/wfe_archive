Rusmo Kingdom was founded by Rusmo the Brave, a giant of a man with superhuman strength and magical powers. Under his rule, the kingdom prospered, and his successor, Jorin the Great, continued his legacy of expansion and diplomacy.
During Jorin&#39;s reign, the kingdom experienced a golden age of culture and learning. However, a dark sorcerer known as the Shadow King rose to power and waged a war of conquest against the kingdom. In this dark hour, a young princess named Lyra emerged as a hero, using her magical powers to defeat the Shadow King and restore peace to the kingdom. Now it returns to rise again!
