The Socialist Equality Party [color=red](SEP)[/color] is a Trotskyist political party affiliated with the International Committee of the Fourth International (ICFI).

It seeks to create an independent movement of the working class for the revolutionary struggle against capitalism and for socialism and egalitarianism.

The primary organ of the revolutionary working class can be found at:
[url=http://www.wsws.org]World Socialist Web Site[/url]

The sections of the [region]ICFI[/region] is represented by the [b]Socialist Equality Party[/b] in varying countries:
[url=http://www.socialequality.com]United States Section[/url]
[url=http://socialequality.org.uk/]British Section[/url]
[url=http://www.sep.org.au/website/]Australian Section[/url]
[url=http://www.gleichheit.de/website/]German Section[/url]
[url=http://www.socialequality.lk/]Sri Lankan Section[/url]
[color=green]Canadian Section[/color]

There are also two affiliated sections:
[color=green]Marxist Voice[/color] (Pakistan)
[color=green]Socialist Labour League[/color] (India)
