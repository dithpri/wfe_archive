[List]The [b]IMPERIAL FEDERATION[/b], concurrently and originally known as the [b]IPEONIC EMPIRE[/b], is the indissoluble supranational union of, at present, twelve constituent nations.

The Imperial Federation was formed from the nations that were colonized and/or annexed by Ipeonia, also known as the Grand River or [b]RIO GRANDE[/b]. 

During the territorial existence of the Empire, [b]IPEONIA[/b] was formed by several civilizations, adding up to more than 10 billion inhabitants.[/sub][/list]
