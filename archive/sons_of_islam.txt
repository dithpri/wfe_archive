[B]Abandoned by a careless founder, the Sons of Islam languish in their vulnerability. Faith alone is no deterrent to a true invader, proven as organizations swarm to dominate this unprotected land, glorying in the ability to control without contest. They continue to suffer from their own apathy and so lay open to whims of the conqueror. The [region]Lone Wolves United[/region] saw the boasts of one such claimant to possession of the region and demonstrated the laxity of the so-called protector. For as long as the Sons of Islam remain victim to the will of the invader, there will be no peace for the natives.

Raiders will prevail until defenders again rise from their somnolent vigil.[/B]
