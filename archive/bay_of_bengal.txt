The [b]Bay of Bengal[/b], the largest bay in the world, forms the northeastern part of the Indian Ocean. Roughly triangular, it is bordered mostly by India and Sri Lanka to the west, Bangladesh to the north, and Myanmar (Burma) and the Andaman and Nicobar Islands to the east.

The Bay of Bengal occupies an area of 839,000 sq mi. A number of large rivers &#150; the Ganges and its tributaries such as the Padma and Hooghly, the Brahmaputra and its tributaries, flow into the Bay of Bengal. Among the important ports are Chennai, Chittagong, Kolkata, Mongla, Paradip, Tuticorin, Visakhapatnam and Yangon. &#151;Wikipedia [hr]
