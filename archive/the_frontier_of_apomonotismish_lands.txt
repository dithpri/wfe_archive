This region is fully acceptable for all nations of all backgrounds and play-styles. However, any form of discrimination, hate speech, harassment, bullying, or derogatory behavior is a ban. 

This region was once the frontier of Apomonotismish Lands.

We will get help from Ten Thousand Islands if we are ever raided.

The Apomonotismish Lands is the center of the Apomonotismish Federal Network (AFN). Original member regions: Apomonotismish Lands, The Frontier of Apomonotismish Lands, Warzone Anarchy, The Hub, Apomonotismish Academy. More regions can join the AFN by telegramming the Administration of Reonese Empire.
