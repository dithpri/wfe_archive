This region is fully acceptable for all nations of all backgrounds and play-styles. However, any form of discrimination, hate speech, harassment, bullying, or derogatory behavior is a ban. 

This region was once the frontier of Apomonotismish Lands.

This region was liberated from a raid due to Ten Thousand Islands defender forces. We also will get help from Ten Thousand Islands if we are ever raided again. The raiders are suspected to be part of The Black Hawks.

The Apomonotismish Lands is the center of the Apomonotismish Federal Network (AFN). Original member regions: Apomonotismish Lands, Haven Euphoria, Warzone Anarchy, The Hub, Apomonotismish Academy. More regions can join the AFN by telegramming the Administration of Reonese Empire.
