A Warlord of supreme girth and wit, this great black beast roamed the central Serengeti with nothing but a crude club on his back and his massively oversized callous ridden hands, uniting the tribes of Breadistan into a great Nation.
Alas his death due to auto-erotic asphyxiation whilst sleeping with his harem of boyfriends did split this Empire, and a new world was born.
