The Great Plains of the Twelve Tribes is a region with mysterious origins and great power; it is a New World with new ideas, but age old desires and as such, the Old World values of civility and barbarism come to be as one in the societies of this vast land. 

It is also a place of rejuvenation and regrowth, as the great entity that once owned this land broke apart under unknown circumstances, leaving an irradiating glow on the many grains of the Great Expanse, known only as the [i]Terra Firma[/i].

With time proceeding, the dispositions of the populace differentiated- causing the fragmentation of the Twelve Tribes and  the rise of the several NationStates present today...
