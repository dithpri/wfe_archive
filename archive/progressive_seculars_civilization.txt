[color=48000b][b]The PSC is the region for like-minded nations concerned with scientific and technological progress whilst preserving the environment. Its purpose is to promote free trade and the fluid movement of goods, people, and currency.

The PSC is based in the fictional world of Folkhall, and it has two continents: Avantos and Greenmeadows.[/b][/color]
