Comprising of Alpha Centauri&amp;#39;s solar system out to it&amp;#39;s heliosphere, this region is known for its liberal coalition of peace loving and socialist-democratic nation states. Ruled by the benevolent ancient Deltaons, free speech and will are treasured above all. Science is regarded as the highest pursuit and religion is seen as an oddity. 

Nations may tax what they want, hold whatever elections they want, but they must not be unreasonable in neighborly demands or expectations. Council is held once daily where all issues are reviewed and decided upon immediately.
