The USS is a group of friendly nations that all work towards the common good. If you&amp;#39;re looking for some good friends, then this is the place to be!
Within the USS are many traditions and ideas that have shaped the culture of the region. The three stars in a circle present on the flag represents the three leaders of government (Founder, Delegate, Vice Delegate) who rule under one region and one flag. Also present within the customs and events of the USS are such things as festivals, flower wars, and free elections.
