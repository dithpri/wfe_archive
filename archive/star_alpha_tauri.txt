Aldebaran is a star in the zodiac constellation of Taurus. It has the Bayer designation &amp;#945; Tauri, which is Latinized to Alpha Tauri and abbreviated Alpha Tau or &amp;#945; Tau. Aldebaran varies in brightness from an apparent visual magnitude of 0.75 down to 0.95, making it the brightest star in the constellation, as well as (typically) the fourteenth-brightest star in the night sky.
