The [b]Environmental Conservation Organization (ECO)[/b] is an international organization working for the protection and conservation of nature and the sustainable use of natural resources by member-states. Founded by the Emperor Josh in [nation]Jeltronia[/nation] in October 2016, ECO brings together governments and private organizations, and provides an avenue for them to share resources and expertise; conduct collaborative research and studies, field projects, advocacy, lobbying and education.
