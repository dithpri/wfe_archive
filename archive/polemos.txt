[nation]Necopolis[/nation] was once a brilliantly shining empire that ruled over all. But then, the Prime Dictator went too far, creating two splinter nations: [nation]Proscripopolis[/nation], a similarly oppressive government that prides itself on being liberally dictatorial; [nation]Credimopolis[/nation], a theocratic government believing that all that matters is that one believes in God. As Proscripopolis and Credimopolis struggle to get started on their own as new, fledgling nations, Necopolis struggles to rise up and reclaim its former power.
