[color=003366][b]Borneo[/b] is the third-largest island in the world and the largest island of Asia. At the geographic centre of Maritime Southeast Asia, in relation to major Indonesian islands, it is located north of Java, west of Sulawesi, and east of Sumatra.

The island is divided among three countries: Brunei and Malaysia on the north, and Indonesia to the south.  Borneo is home to one of the oldest rainforests in the world. &#151;Wikipedia[/color][hr]
