A grand masterpiece of capitalism, anti-communism, privatization, representative democracy, and moderately conservative ideals. Nova Romans are nationalistic, conservative, capitalist, both religious and secular, and militaristic.

The President of Nova Roma Republic wields executive power over all provinces and protectorates. The people love and respect the President for his strong, charismatic leadership. The Senate of Nova Roma Republic is the unicameral legislative branch, with 300 seats.
