A grand masterpiece of capitalism, anti-communism, privatization, representative democracy, and moderately conservative ideals. Nova Romans are nationalistic, conservative, capitalist, both religious and secular, and militaristic. Nova Roma Republic has a vision of bringing its culture and civilization to the rest of the world under the magnificent flag of Nova Roma Republic.

The President of Nova Roma Republic wields executive power over all constituent states. All constituent states have equal legislative power in the Senate of Nova Roma Republic. Constituent states can propose legislation, which can be passed by a majority vote. All constituent states must follow legislation passed through the vote in the Senate.
