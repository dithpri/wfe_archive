
[color=Blue][B]NS Regional Alliance Defence Treo[/B][/color]

[B]Treo membership is open to &#147;any other regions in a position to further the principles of this Treaty and to contribute to the security of the NS regional security.&#148;

Treo&amp;#39;s essential purpose is to safeguard the freedom and security of its members through political and military means.[/B]

[hr][color=Red][B]POLITICAL - Treo promotes democratic values and encourages consultation and cooperation on defence and security issues to build trust and, in the long run, prevent conflict.

MILITARY - Treo is committed to the peaceful resolution of disputes. If diplomatic efforts fail, it has the military capacity needed to undertake crisis-management operations.[/B][/color]
