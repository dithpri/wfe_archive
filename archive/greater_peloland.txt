The Greater Peloland Region is the State founded by the Dictatorship of Peloland 2000 years ago by Supreme Leader Pelas, First of His Name, High Lord General of the Pelolandian Armed Forces.

In 873 aP (years after the Founder) King Pelas Third of His Name of the Pelolandians established two sub states in the region and declared Himself Emperor.

The Empire now rules over nine sub states.

The current Empress is styled as Her Imperial Majesty Pelas XI by Grace of God of the Pelolandians and of all Her Territories and Dominions Empress, or just Her Imperial Majesty The Empress of the Realm.
