The Greater Peloland Region is the State founded by the Dictatorship of Peloland 2000 years ago by Supreme Leader Pelas, First of His Name, High Lord General of the Pelolandian Armed Forces.

In 873 aP (years after the Founder) King Pelas Third of His Name of the Pelolandians established two sup states in the region and declared Himself Emperor.

The Empire now rules over 8 sub states.

The current Empess is style as Her Imperial Majesty Pelas XI by Grace of God of the Pelolandians and of all Her Territories and Dominions Empress, or just Her Majesty The Empress of the Realm.
