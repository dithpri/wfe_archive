In the region of Eossera, technology is on the rise. Surrounded by video games and code, the people of Eossera are highly advanced in computer engineering, computer science, and competitive gaming. More than a region, they are a clan of many strong and diverse thinkers, dreamers, warriors and survivalists. If you want to join this nation and paint your destiny, you must contact Arch General Athena Feiyn of EOSS.
