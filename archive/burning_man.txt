[b]Burning Man[/b] is an annual gathering that takes place at Black Rock City&#151;a temporary city erected in the Black Rock Desert in Nevada. The event is described as an experiment in community and art, influenced by 10 main principles, including &quot;radical&quot; inclusion, self-reliance and self-expression, as well as community cooperation, civic responsibility, gifting, decommodification, participation, immediacy and leaving no trace. &#151;Wikipedia
