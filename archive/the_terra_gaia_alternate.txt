This World is an alternate Earth of great unions and nations ruling the world, maps are updated every few days to represent combat, growth and random events. (mostly RP based) Newspapers are weekly if nothing is happening or daily if events are taking place.
(need NationStates++ for newspaper)
The aim is either to expand empires and gain land and power or try and stay neutral and avoid war.
All wars must be declared and daily news papers will show the progress of the war based on economy and military.
