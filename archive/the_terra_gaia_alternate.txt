The Terra Gaia alternate is an alternate world set in modern times where world events will effect the region (via nationstates++) nations can declare wars on each other if necessary and for further info on wars check the Rules of engagement. Occupied nations can rebel and get land back if annexed. Alliances can declare a union and create a combined nation on the map.
