This region is based in the year 5 billion. The human race inhabits some stars but we focus on one of them (mentions of external star systems is allowed in RMB, i.e. &quot;The Kalrania System has a trade route with my planet&quot;.) The beautiful star system we inhabit is the Auxilon System.

BASIC RULES:
New Perfe is the dominant nation, they decide what goes down in the Auxilon System and can veto most actions.
