Bangladesh, officially the [b]People&#39;s Republic of Bangladesh[/b], is a country in South Asia. It is bordered by India to its west, north and east; Myanmar (Burma) to its southeast; and is separated from Nepal and Bhutan by the Chicken&#39;s Neck corridor. To its south, it faces the Bay of Bengal. Bangladesh is the world&#39;s eighth-most populous country, with over 168 million people. It is one of the most densely populated countries, and among countries with a population exceeding 10 million, it is the most densely populated. It forms part of the ethno-linguistic region of Bengal, along with the neighbouring Indian states of West Bengal and Tripura. &#151;Wikipedia[hr]
