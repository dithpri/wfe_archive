Trampus is a planet far away from mother earth with five continents. Which are Grand Alemexia, Chatondonea, Western River, Evitir, Brickham and Antarticus. Evitir is home to the elves, Grand Alemexia is home to the humans, Chatondonea is home to the orcs, giants, drawfs and humans, Western River is home to the humanoid animals, Evitir is home to the elves, Antarticus is home to the snow demons and Brickham is home to the hers (which are a race mixed between humans, elves and dwarfs.). Trampus has also six different seas. Which are the sea of charles, the winter sea, the alemexian sea, the sea of elves, the tropical sea and the southern sea.
