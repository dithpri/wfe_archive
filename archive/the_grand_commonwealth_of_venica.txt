Formed from the remaining nations of the Venica Empire, the Grand Commonwealth is looking to establish regional supremacy. Security in Strength, Order in Unity.
The Grand Commonwealth is an association of nations which agree to free trade, and collective security. The Grand Commonwealth makes no distinctions on entry towards nations due to their form of government or social/ethical/economic policy. The only nations banned from entry are those deemed enemies of the Commonwealth.
