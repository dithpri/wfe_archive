The Coalition of Nations, also known as the Coalition, is an intergovernmental organization that was tasked to maintain solidarity and cooperation among member states. The headquarters of the CoN is in Armouria. The organization is financed by assessed and mandatory contributions from its member states.

The Coalition is organized with Armouria as the hegemon, and is controlled by the Grand Council of the Coalition which is presided over by the Archon of Armouria. Each member state has one representative on the Grand Council and are represented in the WA by the Armourian Representative. Coalition resolutions are not binding on Armouria.

The Coalition provides protection and security to its member states.
