The Wairarapa is a Sub-Region in New Zealand comprised of the Districts of the South Wairarapa, Carterton, Masterton and Tararuas. Borders by the Tararua and Ruahine Ranges to the west and the Pacific Ocean to the east, the Region is home to some of New Zealand&#39;s famous wine.
