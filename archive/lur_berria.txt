Welcome to Lur Berria, a parallel version of our world where authoritarianism reigns supreme. In this alternate reality, nearly every country is under the iron grip of a dictator, each with their own ruthless methods of control and suppression.

Gone are the principles of democracy and individual freedom, replaced by a pervasive atmosphere of fear and oppression. The borders are tightly sealed, and information is heavily censored, with state propaganda shaping the beliefs and attitudes of the populace.
