[color=#00cfe6][b]ABOUT[/b][/color]

Loop Company is a corporate-style region where nations operate as autonomous units within a continuously optimized system.  
The region focuses on experimentation, efficiency, and structured reform cycles known as the [b]Loop[/b].

[hr]

[color=#00cfe6][b]GOVERNANCE[/b][/color]

The region is managed by a [b]Central Directorate[/b] using performance-based decision-making rather than ideology.

[hr]

[color=#00cfe6][b]MEMBERSHIP[/b][/color]

Any nation may join if it accepts regional rules, observation RP, and participation in the Loop system.
