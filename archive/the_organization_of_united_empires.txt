The map consists of two large pieces of land ([i]3 if we count &quot;Antarctica&quot;[/i]) vaguely similar to Earth&#39;s continents.
we strive ([i]me since I&#39;m alone for now[/i]) for semi-realistic historical events while we accompany it with a relaxed way of playing.
