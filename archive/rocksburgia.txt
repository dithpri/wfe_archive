Rocksburgia is a mountainous region with abundant resources. It is inhabited by 7 nations, the two most powerful and influential being Rocksburg and the Confederate Socialist Republic.

At one time, both Rocksburg and the C.S.R. were one nation, founded by the same leaders. Over time, however, corruption took over the Rocksburgimite government and the C.S.R. formed an independence movement that grew into its own nation.

After diplomatic talks between the two governments, a resolution was arrived at and have they formed a relationship that suits the needs of their people.
