Rocksburgia is a mountainous region with abundant resources. It is inhabited by 7 nations, the two most powerful and influential being Rocksburg and the Confederate Socialist Republic.

At one time, both Rocksburg and the C.S.R. were one nation, founded by the same leaders. Over time, however, corruption took over the Rocksburgimite government and the C.S.R. formed an independence movement that grew into its own nation. Rocksburg, however, disputed the C.S.R.&amp;#39;s sovereignty and the two nations have since not been on the best of terms...
