A region consisting mainly of aristocratic sovereign states loyal to the Roman Catholic Church. Protestant forms of worship are regarded as heresy, though other forms of religion are tolerated so long as they do not directly conflict with the Catholic Faith.

Amarcord is not a region in which tyranny is accepted. 

The rules of the region are relatively relaxed and nation-states are allowed to do as they please so long as their actions do not hinder the prosperity of the region. 

Suggestions and input are welcome and will be greatly valued. Member nations are encouraged to contribute, though not required.
