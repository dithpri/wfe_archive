A region consisting mainly of aristocratic sovereign states loyal to the [b]Roman Catholic Church.[/b] Protestant forms of worship are regarded as [i]heresy[/i], though other forms of religion are tolerated so long as they do not directly conflict with [b]Catholicism.[/b]

This region does not tolerate tyranny in any form.

The rules of the region are relatively relaxed and nation-states are allowed to do as they please so long as their actions do not hinder the prosperity of the region. 

Member nations are encouraged to contribute, though are not required to do so.
