Caliphate of the Muslim Ummah
Government Type: Unitary Islamic constitutional caliphate
Capital: Medina (religious); seat of the Caliph located in the Caliph&#39;s home state
Official Religion: Sunni Islam (Hanafi, Maliki, Shafi&#39;i, and Hanbali)
Official Language: Arabic (unitary); member states retain their own official languages.

The Caliphate of the Muslim Ummah unites the world&#39;s Muslim-majority countries and territories under a single Caliph. Member states govern their own affairs, but ultimate sovereignty rests with the Caliph. The Caliphal Shura reviews all laws and major government actions for compliance with the Qur&#39;an, the Sunnah, and the four Sunni madhhabs before they take effect.
