[b]Card Captor[/b] Sakura,abbreviated as CCS and also known as Card Captors, is a Japanese sh&amp;#333;jo manga series written and illustrated by the manga group Clamp. The manga was originally serialized in Nakayoshi from May 1996 to June 2000, and published in 12 tank&amp;#333;bon volumes by Kodansha from November 1996 to July 2000. The story focuses on Sakura Kinomoto, an elementary school student who discovers magical powers after accidentally freeing a set of magical [b]cards[/b]; she must retrieve the [b]cards[/b] to prevent catastrophe. A sequel by Clamp, Cardcaptor Sakura: Clear [b]card[/b], focusing on Sakura in junior high school, began serialization in Nakayoshi in 2016
