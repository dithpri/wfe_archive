Cardcaptor Sakura (Japanese: &amp;#12459;&amp;#12540;&amp;#12489;&amp;#12461;&amp;#12515;&amp;#12503;&amp;#12479;&amp;#12540;&amp;#12373;&amp;#12367;&amp;#12425;, Hepburn: K&amp;#257;dokyaput&amp;#257; Sakura, commonly abbreviated as CCS) is a Japanese sh&amp;#333;jo manga series written and illustrated by the manga group Clamp. The manga was originally serialized in Nakayoshi from May 1996 to June 2000, and published in 12 tank&amp;#333;bon volumes by Kodansha from November 1996 to July 2000. The story focuses on Sakura Kinomoto, an elementary school student in Japan who discovers magical powers after accidentally freeing a set of magical cards into the world; she must retrieve the cards to prevent catastrophe.
