For centuries the region knew of only two lands: the Motherland, Caelondia, and the half-explored continent Uresh. Then brave explorers ventured across the Boundless Sea and discovered the New World. The two new continents, Astoria and Sonoria, were soon divided amongst the three great powers. Uresh too succumbed to colonization. Revolution at home and abroad gave birth to new nations, and borders were redrawn.
