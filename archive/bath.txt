[region]Bath[/region] is a historic city in Somerset, within the [region]Empire of Britain[/region]. Renowned for its Roman-built baths and Georgian architecture, Bath is a cultural and tourist hub, blending historic heritage with modern amenities. The city is also recognized for its universities, museums, and vibrant arts scene.
