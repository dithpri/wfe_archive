The Interstellar Feudalistic Authoritarian Empire of the Pigmasks has existed for many millenniums, prospering within their galaxy and far beyond, with control of countless galaxies and colonies, possessing a grand military and governmental structure, headed by the sovereign monarch, Emperor Mallister, a seemingly eternal leader, alive since the formation of the Empire, residing within the Imperial Palace of Supremacy.
