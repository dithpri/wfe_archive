Warland is a region of the most powerful and nationalistic countries in the world, with the best military bases and physical health in all of these societies. These countries are technically currently in control of the world, each loyal to their neighbor countries. So far, all of these countries are in alliance with one another, protecting and governing the region, but also to protect and rule the entire world.

Each country in this region converts an alliance with these countries, no opposing forces are allowed, but neutral sides are most certainly welcome, but must obey the superior countries. So far, the highest form of authority in this region is the kingdom of South Christland.
