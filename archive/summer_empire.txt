The Summer Empire is a coalition of young nations interested in having control over the world and claiming power. If you wish to join the Empire, be prepared for the glory and sense of achievement that comes with military conquests, political annexations, and economic buyouts. SPQS: Senatus PopulesQue Summer!
 
This is a region in which new nations may join in order to grow among other new nations. You can come here to have an opportunity to not only grow yourself as a player of NationStates but to have a chance to have personal power in a region as we are small as of yet.

Come here expecting to have power as we will grow and will have control over many parts of NationStates.
