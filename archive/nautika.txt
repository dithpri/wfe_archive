The Nautika Union is an economic and political union of independent Member States located in the Nautika region.

Headquarters: Atlanta, Notumbria 
President: Gustav Smith


The Nautika Union is an organization responsible for keeping the Nautika continent at peace and expanding regional social and economic development.  Member states often set goals to achieve sustainable economic growth while always respecting the environment and social causes.  The Union headquarters are located in Atlanta, Notrumbia.

Equality and social justice, in addition to the establishment of social democracy, are the paths of union.  Together we are stronger.
