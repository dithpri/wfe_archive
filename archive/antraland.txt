The mighty Empire of Franland once dominated the world, but civil war tore it apart, giving rise to Great Franland and True Franland. Meanwhile, Fifee, once a vast and feared empire, remained a superpower through its sheer size, military strength, and centralized control. The remnants of another fallen empire&#151;Figee, Arcsea, Prokaryote, and Sisyphus&#151;each sought a different future. Arcsea and Sisyphus aimed to restore the past, Prokaryote pushed for unity through a republic, and Figee pursued global domination through aggression.&nbsp;Prussia and Mosonomia, born from the unification of smaller tribes, grew into stable and influential nations. Glaggle-land, having won independence through revolution, while Death World, once collapsed, returned.
