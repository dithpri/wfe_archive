The Lamar coast is a small coastal region, in which coexist future powerhouses and superpowers. The Nations that would like to join are requested to telegram the united states of San France about it. If a existing member want another nation to join, they should discuss this with the united states of San France. There will likely be a vote between the nations. The Bigtopians are receiving aid from the united nations of Lamar coast as well as a humanitarian organisation based in Medleyland. The nations of the Lamar coast have signed a general peace treaty.
