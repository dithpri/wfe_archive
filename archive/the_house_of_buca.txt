The House of Buca is a monarchic region in NationStates with 20 member nations, ruled by HRM King Sam ll. It has a structured leadership team, including six Officers. Jimmy (Reformed Sambuca) is Vice-Delegate and the incoming World Assembly Delegate.

HRM King Sam ll leads the region, with Sarah Richardson appointed as De Jure President and Charlie Moulier as De Facto President; both now serve as regional officers. Jimmy also plays a key role as Vice-Delegate and future WA Delegate.
