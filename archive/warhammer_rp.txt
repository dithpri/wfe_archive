[b]Warhammer Fantasy[/b]
Welcome to [i][region]Warhammer RP[/region][/i], Warhammer RP is a region made for people that love the Warhammer universe and want a region where they can RP as their favorite races/groups in the game(s). 

[b]Rules:[/b] 
[i]- All Players must respect one another
