The Islands that consist of Pontbridge. The central, largest island is occupied by the nation of Pontbridge. All nations within the Pontbridge Islands have a common currency, the Credit. The nation of Cutote occupies a large number of islands and both Pontbridge and Cutote are military and economic rivals. Cashdeer is a breakaway province of Cutote. Gynostan has a common border with Cutote and Cashdeer. Transhumanstan enjoys close relations with Pontbridge.
