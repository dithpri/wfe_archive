NationStates Up vs. Down!

Rules: Create a puppet and move it to [region=Upp] or [region=Downn]. Post here to increase or decrease the count. If you are in [region=Upp], increase it by one. If you are in [region=Downn], decrease it by one. The count starts at 0. If the count is positive, Up is winning. If the count is negative, Down is winning.

The game starts as soon as embassies are created with the regions Upp and Downn.
