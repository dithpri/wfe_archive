Ansura Era (2019-2020): In the wake of economic devastation, only a handful of nations were able to ride out the storm. most collapsed and even fewer have recovered. As a power vacuum sweeps the continent, nations are attempting to reform, as others are trying to expand their influence...

Beniston Era (2017-2019): A region lost to time, Akrieus was made up of millions of little states, until just recently. Now, large nations have arisen and must unite to form a better world, however, backwater nations, who have been supported by these larger, more powerful nations, have decided that it is their time for an empire of their own...
