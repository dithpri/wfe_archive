The Federation of the Pro-Axolotl Universe is a small group of states which, unsurprisingly, contain copious amounts of the salamander-like creatures called axolotls.

The countries contained in the Pro-Axolotl Universe are fairly amicable, and co-operate between themselves with trade and research.
