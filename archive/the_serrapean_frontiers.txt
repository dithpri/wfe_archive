The Serrapean Frontiers are a rugged land of towering peaks and rich biodiversity, inhabited by the indigenous Serrapean people who have lived sustainably with nature for centuries. However, encroachment by outsiders has led to conflicts and tensions, as the Serrapeans struggle to preserve their way of life. Nonetheless, the Serrapean Frontiers remain a place of wonder and enchantment where ancient traditions coexist with modern realities.
