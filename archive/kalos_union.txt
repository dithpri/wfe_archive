The Kalos Union (KU) is a organisation forged during the Great War Of 2025-68 In this war the British tried to remake there empire. All of the nations of the world where against them not counting the African Union witch was a empire that stretched from the cape of good hope to the Suez canal. It was officially formed in October, 21, 2027 after the siege of Dublin when the KN thought the war was almost over.  The KU was able to defeat them in a 43 year war where now many nations where left in shambles. This war cost 78.6 millions lives A few nations have started to build up such as The Socialist States of America the founder of the KU. This Union is used as a defense force and to help build up the nations that where damaged in the war.
