The Greater States Of The Hellenistic Order is a highly centralized authoritarian federation in the Eastern Mediterranean built around a revivalist ideology claiming to restore the glory of ancient Hellenistic civilization.

Following decades of political instability and economic crisis across the region, nationalist movements consolidated power and unified several states under a single regime headquartered in Helionidos, the political and ideological heart of the union.

The government promotes a militant form of Hellenistic cultural identity emphasizing discipline, national strength, and expansion of regional influence. Political opposition, independent media, and dissenting movements are heavily restricted or banned.
