The Dawn of the Communist Powers

In an alternate timeline, the world was fractured into a collection of ideologies competing for dominance. By the mid-21st century, capitalism had overreached its bounds, leaving many nations in economic despair. A handful of states, with their roots in socialist ideals, began to flourish in contrast, focusing on equality, community, and innovation. These nations&#151;known as The Crimson Pact&#151;formed the nucleus of a powerful coalition: The Communist Powers.
