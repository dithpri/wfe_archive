It was first an alliance created by two dictators that dominated their lands with an iron fist. They lived in wealth and luxury, while their people lived in impoverished agony. Finally, when their people had enough, they rebelled and hanged both their leaders together as the friends they were. They decided to keep the alliance and change the name from Death Region to Muffintopia to symbolize all the good food they would eat and the luxuries they would live in.
