Vampires, Witches, Warlocks and Mortals all live above our normal lives, so why not make a region where they can reside?

Mei Feng Founded this Region during the Chinese Dynasty and golden era. It has been passed down to rulers, now ruled by Mai Sasaki. This Region sits around the continent of Asia, specifically to the east of it. 

---
The first ancestor of the Fengs created by unimaginable ancient creatures combining their power, The Dragon and The Fenghuang.
As their power fused, a being entirely new emerged from the fiery light.. Her vessel appeared human, but within she had the spirit of both The Dragon and The Fenghuang, blessing her with supernatural abilities never seen before.
