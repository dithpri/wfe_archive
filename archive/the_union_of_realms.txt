[b][u][color=crimson]Welcome to The Union of Realms[/color][/u][/b]

The Union of Realms is a union of nationstates to provide a safe and welcoming community for those interested in history and political science. As such it is welcoming to all ideologies, faiths, and beliefs so that we can have a diverse community. 

Diversity of thought is an important part of any free society and we of this Realm champion that idea. We also welcome any embassies from other regions as well as their ambassadors are always welcome to participate in our RMB discussions.

Currently we have many opportunities for nations to get involved in the region as we grow as well as many officer positions are currently open.
