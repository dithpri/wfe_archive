Kand is a diverse planet located in the Vermop Solar System, with a size approximately 70% that of Earth but significantly denser. It is home to two human-like species, Homonellis and Homofurfs, who coexist and interbreed. Most modern nations on Kand use the Clintonian calendar system, which began with the founding of the ancient city of Clinton. The Kand Recognized Nations Council (KRNC) oversees the mapping and recognition of nations on Kand.
