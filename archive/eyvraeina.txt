Herculean, underinhabited, and home to a civilizational oikumene defined by a shared militarist ethos, dispassionate ironguard diplomacy, and hyperaggresive expansionist statelets. After three millennia of  vanquishing conquests, colossal state collapses, and centuries long national vendettas, the cauldron of nations lies in stasis, as atop it now stands the warbanners of the continents two most voraciously militarist and disciplined armies, seeking to enforce unforeseen dominion over ever larger swathes of the continent and upon their lesser capable neighbors.
