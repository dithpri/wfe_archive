The Popular Front was a Spanish coalition of left wing political organizations with the goal of securing the Spanish elections in 1936 and as well as securing social reforms in the government. It was later used as a rallying point and as an organization for the Republican cause during the Spanish civil war.

Our region aims to unite Leftists of Nationstates for the purpose of security and solidarity.
