The Popular Front was a Spanish coalition of left wing political organizations with the goal of securing the Spanish elections in 1936 and as well as securing social reforms in the government. When the Spanish Civil War began, the parties of the popular front stood in favor for the Republican cause.

Our region welcomes any friends and allies of the Spanish Republic.
