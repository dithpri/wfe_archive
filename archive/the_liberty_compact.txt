[color=#D2A226][b]The Liberty Compact[/b][/color] (TLC) is an organization of regions sharing the ideals of [color=blue]conservatism[/color] and [color=gold]libertarianism[/color], or who are promoters of liberty, such as IRU/FFS successor regions. We ensure our common security and well-being, work to defend against tyrannical invaders, and to further the cause of freedom and self-government in NationStates.

[b]Have fun promoting Liberty![/b]
