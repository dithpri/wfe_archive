The land of Constannosia is the region home to the Nicholaot Latins of the kingdom of Nicholaos. The region of Constannosia has many of ethnic diversities in the homeland, English, Latin, Hebrew, Greek/Hellenic, Gothic, and other ethnic groups. This nation is a nation of Christians, both Jewish and Gentile, under one Holy Catholic and Apostolic Church, we confess the Nicene creed, and the faith of 
our forefathers and of our father Abraham.
