The Argo Confederation is close alliance of multiple island nations that have united for the better good of the World and their own nations. The Confederation supports developing nations and encourages them to turn towards the promises democracy and liberty. The Argo Confederation elects a President and his council every two months. We are truly free-market but allow any nation to introduce their own tariffs and/or establish their own trade deals. We will one senator for every three nations.
