The Collection of states left in the region after the fall of the great and mighty Russak Empire; long ago, the empire held a prominent position in affairs as a superpower, competing for global hegemony. Now the Empire has fallen, and in its stead are the fractured states that have risen from its ashes. Competing and cooperating in turn to rebuild themselves and their fallen shared history. Whether fighting or working to expand domestically or abroad, the previously disordered states have once again come together in a joint effort to govern themselves collectively. A far cry from the iron grip of the empire and its political systems, Old Russak remains a bastion for the hope of the future of the many states left in the shadow of the fall.
