Early in the War of 1812 [region]USS Chesapeake[/region] made one patrol and captured five British merchant ships. She was captured by HMS Shannon shortly after sailing from Boston, Massachusetts, on 1 June 1813. [region]The Royal Navy[/region] took her into their service as HMS Chesapeake, where she served until she was broken up and her timbers sold in 1819.
