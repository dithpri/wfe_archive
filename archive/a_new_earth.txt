Welcome to A New Earth! Come and say hello!

A New Earth is a Sci-if Roleplay region that takes place on a far away planet known as Execellum, a terraformed planet equipped with state of art defense systems and technology. In this future time line Humanity unites under one banner and expands across the Milky Way Galaxy creating a prosperous empire, their technology far surpassing that of which we have today. Until, Humanity encounters a mysterious alien race that begins devastating war that would go on to destroy the Human Empire, causing unspeakable amounts of destruction, Exellum stands as the one of the isolated pockets of humanity, shattered by political infighting, their fate is uncertain.
