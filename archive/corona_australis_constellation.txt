[b]Founded by the andarian starfleet on: 04/05/2025![/b][i]Corona Australis: is a constellation in the Southern Celestial Hemisphere. Its Latin name means &quot;southern crown&quot;, and it is the southern counterpart of Corona Borealis, the northern crown. It is one of the 48 constellations listed by the 2nd-century astronomer Ptolemy, and it remains one of the 88 modern constellations. The Ancient Greeks saw Corona Australis as a wreath rather than a crown and associated it with Sagittarius or Centaurus.[/i]
