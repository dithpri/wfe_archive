A Nation States Region Dedicated to the students at Rolling Hills Prep and Renaissance Schools. All members are able to have a job in the region.
This region is currently in negotiations with region The Federation of the Future in hopes of an alliance between us all. 

History: In early 2017, a civil war in the region, the Federation of the Future, over the lack of democracy. Through its ugly strategies, a treaty of peace was signed between nations. However, as the dictators won the war, the democratic societies left the group in hopes of forming a peaceful, economic worldwide zone for nations alike. With a total population of 6 nations, with more on the way, we will work together for a peaceful and unified nation state region.
