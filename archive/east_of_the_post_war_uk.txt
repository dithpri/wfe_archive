This is the East of England region, encompassing all shires and cities after the 2029 Invasion of the United Kingdom. The region includes Bedfordshire, the Republic of Cambridgeshire (controls all districts unlike New Watford), Suffolk, Norfolk and New Watford (started off in former Watford, then spread to all the other districts and cities apart from Broxbourne, annexed by the Hartlow Commonwealth, part of former Essex).
