Region often known as West Ebura. Ebura Scar being the desert isolating the western reach of the continent from most of the known world. Through the dark age of ruin most of the world was lost, yet over thousands of years of environment recovery life has return to the region. Many of the deep forests hide dark secrets of ancient cities and technology lost to time. Isolated tribes, kingdoms and societies populated the region, however after years of coherent trade and spread/mixing of common languages Realm Council was formed.
