[color=#196F3D]Located in northern Mesopotamia on the western bank of the river Tigris, Assur became the first capital city of the Assyrian Empire. Although other cities would later take over as capital of the Assyrian Empire, Assur was always recognized as the religious center of the empire. Assur was named after the primary god of the Assyrians. The city and the god are sometimes called Ashur.[/color]
