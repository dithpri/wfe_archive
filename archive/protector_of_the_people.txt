After the entire nation fell to ruins, Armoria has been working hard to create a place in the world where its people can feel safe. With this, Armoria wanted to share that protection with other nations and thus the Protector of the People was formed. 

If you are looking for a region where you are guaranteed protection, Protector of the People is the region for you! 

Each nation to join will receive a personalized telegram from the founding nation!
