Formed on May 10, 2026 as a splinter group from the Democratic Socialist Assembly, the Commonwealth of Democratic Socialists is a small but ambitious region founded by the Democratic States of the Jobostian Empire. United by a shared vision of equality, solidarity, and prosperity, the Commonwealth stands as a beacon of democratic socialism. Rooted in collective progress, it exists to serve the common people, not a privileged elite. Though modest in size, it seeks to prove that trust, equality, and justice create a firmer foundation than corruption and elitism, striving always for prosperity shared by all.
