Overview
The Bastion is a quiet refuge for nations that value independence, creativity, and resilience. It stands as a stronghold where members can grow in peace, away from the noise of global politics.

Structure
Capital Nation: A guiding presence at the heart of the Bastion.

Protectorates: Loyal states that guard the frontier and maintain vigilance.

Satellites: Smaller allies that contribute trade, culture, and diversity.

Policy
Neutral in external conflicts.

Focused on internal growth and cultural development.

Defensive posture: protectorates absorb risks, the capital remains secure.

Motto
&quot;In unity we endure, in silence we grow.&quot;
