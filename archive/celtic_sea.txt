[color=003366]The [b]Celtic Sea[/b] is the area of the Atlantic Ocean off the south coast of Ireland bounded to the east by Saint George&#39;s Channel; other limits include the Bristol Channel, the English Channel, and the Bay of Biscay, as well as adjacent portions of Wales, Cornwall, Devon, and Brittany. The southern and western boundaries are delimited by the continental shelf, which drops away sharply. The [region]Isles of Scilly[/region] are an archipelago of small islands in the sea.[/color] &#151;Wikipedia[hr]
