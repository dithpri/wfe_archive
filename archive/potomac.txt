This region is named after the [url=https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Potomac_River]Potomac River[/url] in the United States.

[hr]The Potomac River originates in Preston County, WV and flows through Washington, DC, the capital of the United States, before flowing into the Chesapeake Bay.

In 1790, the Residence Act was signed into law by President George Washington, designating a 100 square mile area on the Potomac River as the new national capital of the United States. This 100 square mile area was named Washington, District of Columbia, in honor of President George Washington and Christopher Columbus.
