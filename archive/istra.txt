[b]Pre-flood history of Istra:[/b] 
Up until the &quot;Great Flood of the Mediterranean&quot; in July 2022., Istra was called Istria and was the largest peninsula in the Adriatic Sea. The peninsula was located at the head of the Adriatic. It was shared by three countries: Croatia, Slovenia and Italy.

[b]Post-flood status of Istra:[/b] 
After the &quot;Great Flood of the Mediterranean&quot;, Istria mainland connection gets flooded and the peninsula became the largest island of the Adriatic Sea. As the region was a popular tourist destination, many of the tourists that were on vacation in Istria and nearby islands decided to stay and rebuild the region and society in Istria.
