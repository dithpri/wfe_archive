Welcome to the [region]Extended Solar System [/region] 

Our region is dedicated to keeping the peace 
among all nations existing within the confines
of the Kuiper Belt be they space faring or terrestrial
locked.

The peacekeeping organization [color=blue] The United Nations
of the Extended Solar System [/color]sees representation 
of any and all interested nations and does its best 
to satisfy the needs of any member as well as settle 
disputes between member nations.
