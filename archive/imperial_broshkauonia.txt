A number of of nation states that have joined together under the banner of the Empire of Broshkauon to form a singular power that rivals the oldest and most powerful of nation states.

Though each nation state lost some of its own governmental preferences in the interest of cohesion, so long as the bonds within Imperial Broshkauonia remain strong the cultural values and histories of each individual nation will remain as they always have.  In addition, even the smallest transgression against a single nation is treated as a Declared act of Aggression against Imperial Broshkauonia and all member states will rise up to defend their fellow Imperials.
