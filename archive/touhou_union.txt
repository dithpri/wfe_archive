The [b][color=red]Touhou Union[/color] (TU)[/b] is a collection of provinces each governed by an [b][color=orange]Avtorityet[/color][/b], who are influential people hailing from [b][region]Gensokyo[/region][/b] and [b][region]New Gensokyo[/region][/b]. With the help of [b][color=teal]Chancellor Marcelino Ezmorales[/color][/b] of [b][nation]Aqua Regia[/nation][/b] and the rest of the [b][color=purple]Flower Four[/color][/b] members from [b][region]Aizengard[/region][/b], [b]TU[/b] was established on April 1, 2014.

[b][nation]Eiki Shiki[/nation][/b] has been delegated as the primary nationstate that oversees the [b]TU[/b] regional government known as the [b][i][color=goldenrod]Phantasmagoria[/color][/i][/b]. Member states of the [b]TU[/b] are also entitled to the provisions outlined by the [b][color=crimson]Treaty of Ratatouille-Hidango[/color][/b], granting the region full support from [b][color=blue]Aizengard[/color][/b] and its resident nations both commercially and diplomatically.
