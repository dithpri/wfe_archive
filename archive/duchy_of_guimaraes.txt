[i]Bem-vindo ao Ducado de Guimar&atilde;es[/i][/color]
[hr][hr][color=navy]Located in northern Portugal, the [b]Duchy of Guimar&atilde;es[/b] is known for well-preserved medieval buildings like the hilltop, 10th-century Guimar&atilde;es Castle, with its sweeping city views. The restored Dukes of Bragan&ccedil;a Palace, built in the style of a French chateau, has a museum showcasing furniture, tapestries and weapons. Between the palace and the castle is the Romanesque S&atilde;o Miguel do Castelo Church, built in the 13th century.[/color]
