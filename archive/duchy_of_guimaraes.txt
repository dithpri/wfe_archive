[color=green][b]Duchy of Guimar&atilde;es[/b]
[i]Cidade Ber&ccedil;o[/i][/color]

[color=navy]Located in northern Portugal, the Duchy of Guimar&atilde;es is known for well-preserved medieval buildings like the hilltop, 10th-century Guimar&atilde;es Castle, with its sweeping city views. The restored Dukes of Bragan&ccedil;a Palace, built in the style of a French chateau, has a museum showcasing furniture, tapestries and weapons. Between the palace and the castle is the Romanesque S&atilde;o Miguel do Castelo Church, built in the 13th century.[/color]
