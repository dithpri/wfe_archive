Cyrodiil, also known as Cyrod or the Imperial Province, is the capital province of the Empire in Tamriel and is the homeland of the Imperial race.
Contents

It is situated in the center of Tamriel, where the Emperor, along with his Elder Council, lives and rules from the Imperial City. Cyrodiil is a wealthy and powerful nation and is the center of the Empire. Cyrodiil serves as the setting for The Elder Scrolls IV: Oblivion. The demonym is &quot;Cyrodilic.&quot;
