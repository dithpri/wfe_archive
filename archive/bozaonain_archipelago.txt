Located in the Gulf of Mexico. Mountains, badlands and grassy plains, the Bozaonain archipelago may be small but has many small but powerful countries including the independent state of Wotobo, bogza, Stacoco, Experiment1, Grony Land, and the Zubmarine itself. From the grand colosseum in Stacoco to the grand temple in Wotobo, the Bozonian Archipelago has it all.
