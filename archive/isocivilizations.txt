The [b]IsoCivilizations[/b] are an area located deep inside the subspace, where (through natural quantum-tensor-effects) otherwise completely isolated extra-dimensional Civilizations are able to get access to our space-time reality. Therefor the Empires with access to the [b]IsoCivilizations[/b] are among the most remote and isolated Civilizations ever existed.
