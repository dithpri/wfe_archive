[color=#8B0000][b]Purpose[/b][/color]
The [b]Coalition of Baath States[/b] exists to promote [i]regional solidarity, coordinated diplomacy, and cultural exchange[/i] among member nations who value stability, sovereignty, and collective identity. The Coalition is a forum for cooperation rather than domination, built on dialogue and mutual respect.
