Introduction to the Council of Ducks
Welcome to the Council of Ducks, a diplomatic assembly where all duck groups unite to promote harmony, collaboration, and the betterment of our feathered community. Here, we strive to address the diverse needs of our members, ranging from wild mallards to cherished domestic breeds. With mutual respect and understanding, we aim to create a sanctuary for ideas, initiatives, and innovations that benefit all ducks across the nation.
Rules of the Council of Ducks
Representation: Each group must select a representative to voice their concerns and proposals.
No Quacking Out: All members are encouraged to stay engaged and participate actively in discussions; leaving early is discouraged.
