[b]Hedgerows[/b] is a marvelous, flourishing region, ruled with apparent diplomacy by the World Assembly Delegate, with the Founder overseeing all, and Steve Abbott taking the political helm.
Randomly bombing neighboring countries, whilst broadcasting the regional anthem is smiled upon, as is the occasional invasion of other regions. The capricious Grand Marshal oversees a large reconnaissance and espionage operation. The Senator Populari is voted in at regular intervals, and holds a varying degree of power, and it is rumored that the Maid in Chief actually holds all the power, due to careful manipulation of the Founder, who is pliable with sexual favours.

[u]Collective noun:[/u] a Replonian of Hedgehogs
