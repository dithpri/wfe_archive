The [region]Galactic Federation of United Alliances[/region], better known as the [b]Galactic Federation[/b] or simply known as the [b]Federation[/b] is the Galactic government formed from various factions into one, cohesive government which succeeded the Global Security Alliance. Although it is de jure a united government, it is de facto a loose confederation of several factions who agreed to unify the galaxy for security and safety of the people of the galaxy
