TNAAF, or referred to interchangeably with USAAF, is the armed forces and military branch of the The North American Unified States. 

TNAAF consists of:

The Department of Defense: Accommodates the Army, Navy, Marines, Airforce, National Guard, Coast Guard, Space Force.

The Department of Homeland Security: Accommodates various intelligence agencies, federal police forces, as well as the National Militia. 

The Department of Innovation: A new department, founded in 2054 to support American technological supremacy over its opponents through federal funding of varying researching agencies for civilian and military technology.
