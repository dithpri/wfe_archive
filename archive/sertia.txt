Sertia, a hidden continent on the brink of civil war. In these hard times, the delegates of Sertia must work to maintain the peace or risk annihilation. It is up to your nation to carve a path forward for your allies, be they light or dark. Onward to victory!

This is a region dedicated to role playing warfare. Your forces can consist of anything you can imagine, but must be reasonable given the following factors. Arms Manufacturing, Defense, Weaponization, and Population. Forces found to be too strong given these factors will be banned, and using said forces will result in ejection from the region.
