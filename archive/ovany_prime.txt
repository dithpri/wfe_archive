Colonized by the United Federations of Vooperia in 2105, landing on the continent of Milikan, where they founded Tepandria City. After extremely poor conditions in the city, for a very long time, the citizens got tired, and ended up rebelling. The rebellion miserably failed, causing the Vooperian government to start a &quot;cleansing&quot; they killed thousands of people, by many thousands more escaped the city. By boat they wandered aimlessly on the seas, until they arrived on the continent of &quot;Izerland&quot; where they founded the Vooperian Democratic Republic (The VDR.) Later, Vooperia sent out some explorers, who would never return, creating all other nations. As nation after nation split off, Ovany Prime grew in population and number of countries.
