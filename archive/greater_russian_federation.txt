[b]Motto:[/b] &#39;&amp;#1042;&amp;#1077;&amp;#1083;&amp;#1080;&amp;#1082;&amp;#1086;&amp;#1081; &amp;#1056;&amp;#1086;&amp;#1089;&amp;#1089;&amp;#1080;&amp;#1080; &amp;#1077;&amp;#1089;&amp;#1090;&amp;#1100;n&eacute;esus visendum mundum&#39; (The Great Russia has things to show the world)

[b]Location:[/b] The Greater Russian Federation is located in the northern hemisphere, spanning Eastern Europe and northern Asia. It shares borders with Norway, Finland, Estonia, Latvia, Lithuania, Poland, Belarus, Ukraine, Georgia, Azerbaijan, Kazakhstan, Mongolia, China, and North Korea. It also has maritime borders with Japan, the United States (Alaska), and Canada (Yukon, Northwest Territories).

[b]Capital:[/b] Moscow

[b]Population:[/b] Approximately 190 million (as of 2021)

[b]Area:[/b] 17,098,242 square kilometers (6,601,668 square miles)

[b]Government:[/b] Federal semi-presidential republic
