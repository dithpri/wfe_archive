The Beletian Pact is a group of nations who have agreed that there will always be a difference of opinion between governments. 

Members do not take active roles in the issues of other nations in The Beletian Pact. 
Legislation carried out inside the Beletian Pact only has to do with affairs of the Beletian Pact and not with nations there in.
Members of The Beletian Pact maintain a collective military to protect against forces that are hostile to ideals of the Beletian Pact and its members.
