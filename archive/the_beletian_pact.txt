The Beletian Pact is a group of nations who have agreed that there will always be a difference of opinion between governments. 

[b]1.[/b] Members will not take any active roles in the issues (both foreign and domestic) of any nation in The Beletian Pact. 
[b]2.[/b] Members of The Beletian Pact will maintain a collective military. 
[b]3.[/b] Border security will be interregional rather than international.
