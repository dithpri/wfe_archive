The Taiga Union (TU) is a military, political, and economic union composing of multiple countries in order to promote greater political and economical integration, unified foreign policy and security guarantees, common citizenship protected under the unified Taiga Armed Forces.

The Commonwealth Of Western Taiga is delegated as the Speaker Nation within the Taiga Council.

The World Assembly Delegate is to work with close cooperation with the Speaker Nation and Taiga Council to represent the TU within the World Assembly.
