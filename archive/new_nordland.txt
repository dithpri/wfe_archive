Nordland was founded in 2013 by Great Jordan as a region that allows autonomy, and self governance following the strict isolationist style Great Jordan and the rest of Nordland&#39;s nations are used too. Nordland is a small player on the world stage, and as a whole, although regional nation v. nation conflicts do occur, Nordland stays in the shadows, as experience has taught us that after even the bitterest conflict everything generally returns to normal after a period of time.
