[i]Corona Borealis is a small constellation in the Northern Celestial Hemisphere. It is one of the 48 constellations listed by the 2nd-century astronomer Ptolemy, and remains one of the 88 modern constellations. Its brightest stars form a semicircular arc. Its Latin name, inspired by its shape, means &quot;northern crown&quot;.[/i][b]Founded by the andarian starfleet on: 04/05/2025.[/b]
