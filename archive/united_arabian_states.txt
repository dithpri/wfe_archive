Arabian Peninsula, simplified Arabia (Arabic: &amp;#1575;&amp;#1604;&amp;#1580;&amp;#1586;&amp;#1610;&amp;#1585;&amp;#1577; &amp;#1575;&amp;#1604;&amp;#1593;&amp;#1585;&amp;#1576;&amp;#1610;&amp;#1577;&amp;#8206; al-jaz&amp;#299;ra al-&amp;#703;arabiyya, &quot;Arabian island&quot;) is a peninsula of Western Asia situated north-east of Africa on the Arabian plate. From a geological perspective, it is considered a subcontinent of Asia.
