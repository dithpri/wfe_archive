The Hertisian Continental Divisions is a series of nations belonging to Hertisian historical land/areas. As it is privately owned, only those sent invites are allowed in.

Discord server: https://discord.gg/kygrvtn

The region is a practical archipelago, with it being one landmass with some small islands. The ASHD technically owns the HCDs as a continent while the socialists claim the name the Armed Republic of Hertisy and the HCDs as their ownership.
