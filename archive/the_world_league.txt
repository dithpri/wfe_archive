We are a respectable establishment of nations seeking progress across the world. We only allow the most refined nations to enter. Chairman and Vice-Chairman are positions that are given to the nations with the first and second most honorable titles in the League. They are as follows:

Defender of Progress (Highest Defense Force)
Lord of Refinement (Highest Culture)
Captain of Industry (Highest Manufacturing)
Grand Philanthropist (Highest Foreign Aid)
Master of Wealth (Most Dynamic Economy as determined by the rankings)
