[b]The Tenants of The United Socially Liberated Nations[/b]

1.  The USLN believes that the government is here to serve all of the people by protecting their liberties.

2.  The USLN believes in the right for people to express their own ideas, opinions and religious beliefs without the fear of legal repercussions.

3.  The USLN believes that all people the right to have access to healthcare professionals and medicine. Every person has the right to be healthy.
 
4.  The USLN believes in the right of businesses to run their businesses in the way they see fit.
