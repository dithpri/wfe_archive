This tiny continent sits completely within a Polar region. Temperatures can reach -38.

The continent sits close to the edge of the world; the South-Western part of the land is separated by a large mountainous ridge, Great Spikes.
Artacia has no volcanoes, and earthquakes are very rare and very weak.

The winds in this region are frequently gentle, with large storms and extreme weather being common.

The seas to the North and West of Artacia are tretcherous, while the seas to the South and East are gentle.
Artacia is home to a modest amount of unique plants and animals that are found only in this region. Such as the Artacian Bear, Artacian Seal and Killer Beaver
