[b][color=orangered]The Aam Aadmi Party (AAP) is a political party in India founded in 2012. Its primary focus is on anti-corruption, transparency, and good governance.[/color] [color=grey]AAP&#39;s symbol is a broom, symbolizing its intent to &quot;clean up&quot; politics. It gained prominence in Delhi by winning the 2015 Assembly elections and forming the government.[/color] [color=lavander]Arvind Kejriwal, a former civil servant, is a prominent leader. The party advocates for affordable healthcare, quality education, and social welfare.[/color] [color=green]AAP&#39;s unique approach involves citizen participation and innovative policies. It has expanded to other states and remains a significant player in Indian politics, particularly in Delhi.[/color]
