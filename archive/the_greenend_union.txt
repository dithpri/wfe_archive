Welcome to The Greenend Union
We are a a Democratic region that believes in free healthcare. Promotes workers rights and is against nuclear weapons. Aims to make renewable energy make up 75% of the energy produced. Believes conservation of the environment is of the up most importance.  

The Constituencies are as follows:
Central Greenend(3 MGPs)-Aaveon WA, Anderson Nation, Cikleo, Diobenemi, Greenend Drive.
North of Greenend(2 MGPs)-Greenendia, Greenendland, Kumbi.
South of Greenend(2 MGPs)-Malubio, New Greenend, Old Greenend.
Eastern Greenend(3 MGPs)-Palost, Pro Greenend Union, Republic of Edinburgh, X-Z.
