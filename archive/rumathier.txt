	Ruathimaer Isle is a Terraformed world, created in the past-not-gone by a Changeling scientist and a group of her fellows. This world was to be built combining science and hearth-craft. It was opened to the Seelie Court and Sidhe have been immigrating to its peaceful shores ever since. Each person in expected to take responsibility for hir actions, and live by the simple credo of &#147;Respect.&#148; This extends to animals and the land as well as fellow sentients.
