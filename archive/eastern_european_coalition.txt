Welcome to the Eastern European Coalition!


[b]Description:[/b] The Eastern European Coalition (EEC) acts as a regional economic and defense cooperative committed to the prosperity those such nations under its membership.

[b]Mission Statement:[/b] The Coalition acts as a pact built against the subversive and provocative influences of ideologies or nations which seek to undermine the autonomy of certain peoples or governances. As such, all ideologies are welcome to the Coalition. However, the coalition does not seek to pass any legislation which would prove substantially affective to the interior politics of any of its members, and will not promote any ideology which proposes the undermining of local autonomy.

[i]Virtus nostra unitas est.[/i]
