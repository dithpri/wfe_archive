Isle of Theen

Centre of a Norse-Gaelic kingdom comprising the Isle of Man, the Hebrides and the islands of the Clyde from the 9th to the 13th centuries AD. The islands were known to the Norsemen as the Su&eth;reyjar, or &quot;Southern Isles&quot; as distinct from the Nor&eth;reyjar or Northern Isles of Orkney and Shetland. In Scotland
