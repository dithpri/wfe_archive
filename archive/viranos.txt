[hr]Viranos is a social roleplaying region based around the concept of a low fantasy world that  has advanced to modern (limited post-modern) technology. 

Anybody is free to join, talk, and use the forums or Discord (the RMB is not an accurate measure of activity!), but the you need to properly join to take part in actual regional roleplays.
