The European alpine region encompassing, central Switzerland, Tyrol Austria south of the Danube and NE Italy. 
Rhaetia is a confederation allowing strong independent autonomy of its member nations.

Rhaetia the penultimate music mecca for non mainstream music embracing genres such as Avant Rock, Jazz, Metal, Progressive Rock, Rock In Opposition, Post Rock and Post Metal. The region hosts premier music festivals attended by musicians and fans through out the world. We have a strong underground music scene catering to new and unknown bands and our off label recording studios attract musicians globally. Musicians and seek Rhaetia as because of our willingness to host to new and upcoming bands and performers.
