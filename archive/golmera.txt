Golmera, Draxton&#39;s sanctuary, had put other Precursor&#39;s realms to shame.  Unlike the other three, it was not only able to store matter from the outside, but actually able to generate, new, real, tangible material out of true nothingness.  Malakai was the new Grandmaster, wished to recreate the place where his predecessor trained with and fought the father of the Dragoon brothers.  However, merely recreating Golmera would not be enough, just as always, he wanted to out-perform those who came before him, and was not just satisfied with a place to train his mind and body, but to create a place for interesting pseudo events, for his own interest.
