Articalism is the sole civilzation in the Mica Isles. It is situated in the largest island of the Mica Archipelago, the Dumdum Island. The weather is rough and unpredictable with storms usually occuring as early as July. However the Almancian current carries with it an abundace of nutrition, which is why there are many species of fish, lobsters and bivalves which contribute a great deal to Articalism&#39;s fishing industry and total GDP.
