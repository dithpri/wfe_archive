The Lucayan Archipelago, is an collection of islands comprising the Bahamas and the Turks and Caicos Islands. The archipelago is in the western Atlantic Ocean, north of the Antilles, east and southeast of Florida, and north of the equator.

For purposes of roleplay, all claimed land must be proportional to national population levels (NS billions= millions; NS millions= thousands).
