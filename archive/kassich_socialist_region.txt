
[b]The Kasich Socialist Coalition (KSC)[/b]

The Socialist Coalition for Mutual Aid and Support (KSC), commonly referred to as KSR (short for Kassich Socialist Region), is a collaborative effort among left-wing countries aimed at fostering solidarity, cooperation, and mutual assistance. Rooted in socialist principles, KSC is dedicated to promoting the well-being and advancement of its member nations while actively working towards the global realization of socialist ideals.
