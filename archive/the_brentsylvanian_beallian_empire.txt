  On April 18, 2014 the Great Nations of The Beallian Empire and Darwinish Brentsylvania merged after the marriage of Princess Eugenie of the Beallian Empire and King Darwin of Darwinish Brentsylvania. For now and ever on the two nations will be unified through blood and friendship. The Empire will mostly consit of mostly independent nations that have the power to vote on Brentsylvanian and Beallian laws. They will also be able to declare war on the other nations of the Empire and even the Emperor himself.
