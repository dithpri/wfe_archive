The Federal Republic of Erusea is a republic located on the western Usean Continent of the Ace Combat universe of Strangereal. During the 2003-05 Usean Conflict war was declared between the Federal Republic of Erusea and a muti-national coalition known as the Independent State Allied Forces.

[u]Erusian Fast Facts:[/u]
Full name: Federal Republic of Erusea
Location: On the Western Coast of Usea
Capital: Farbanti
Head of State: Gen. Marcus Danial
Government: Fascist Military Junta
Type: Militaristic, Aggressive
Currency: Foli
