The Astares Covenant, also known as ASCOV, is a military and economic alliance of nations centered around the Federalis Imperium of Astares Amauricanum. 
The goal of this alliance is to strengthen economic and military ties between its members, to be able to withstand as one hardships that could endanger the stability of the member nations. (Economic crisis, pandemic, etc.)
