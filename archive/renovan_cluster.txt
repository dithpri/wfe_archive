[i]The [region]Renovan Cluster[/region] is a region of the Milky Way Galaxy, comprising about a thousand star systems and many more planets. The Cluster is notable for containing the planet Earth and the various factions fighting for control of the planet itself, as well as harboring several large Astral Empires.

Currently, the region is debating on the establishment of a Regional Government and a standardized system of money. These debates are being headed by [nation]Unified States of Earth[/nation], who was appointed head of the region for the time being due to endorsements from several of the nations residing in the [region]Renovan Cluster[/region].[/i]
