[b]The Northstar Initiative[/b] is a forward-looking region dedicated to technological freedom, cooperative development, and expansive role-play. Founded as a haven for nations who wish to be unrestricted by rigid technological limitations, the Initiative welcomes a diverse array of states, from cybernetic federations and AI-led republics to corporate powers and exploratory star nations.

[i]Progress, diplomacy, and ambition define our shared future.[/i]

Joining the WA and endorsing a Regional Delegate is [color=red][b]not required[/b][/color], and all kinds of role-play is encouraged.
