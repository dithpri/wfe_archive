[color=#aea3b0]**Location:** [/color]Aethermere is a mystical realm situated in the celestial plane of Stellar Aegis, a fortress guarding the boundaries of the cosmos, where celestial guardians uphold cosmic order and protect the realms from malevolent forces.

[color=#aea3b0]**Capital:**[/color] Selunaris

[color=#aea3b0]**Government:** [/color]Aethermere is ruled by the Zephyrian Synarchy, a group of wise beings representing the harmonious balance between the solar and lunar energies. The council is led by the enigmatic Queen Radiance Solaris.
