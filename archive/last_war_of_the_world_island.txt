Lore: After the end of World War 2 a group of International Bankers divided the world into specific power-blocks with the goal of establishing a Unipolar world order and global corporate-financial structure. However other nations chose to resist this global order and formed the Axis of Resistance. A Great awakening is now occurring patriots in the World Island are now waking up and teaming up with the Axis of Resistance to overturn the global world order for a new era of peace and prosperity.
