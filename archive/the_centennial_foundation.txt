The Centennial Foundation is an Elitist Region of proportionate size. Not much is currently known about the inner workings of the Region itself. The Region is divided into multiple districts, much like cities themselves. Seperated by massive fortress walls, with access controlled entry points. Unauthorized travel between districts is not advisiable due to both Raiders as well as Government Law Enforcement. Travel Beyond the outer walls of the region is strictly prohibited and punishable by execution.
