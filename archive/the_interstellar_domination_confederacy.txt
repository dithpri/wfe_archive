Background:
The Interstellar Domination Confederacy (IDC) is a vast coalition of star systems spanning multiple galaxies, established in the 24th century. Its formation emerged as a response to the need for mutual defense, economic cooperation, and cultural exchange among its member entities. The IDC&#39;s founding principles emphasize cooperation, peaceful coexistence, and the advancement of its constituent civilizations.

Geography:
The IDC&#39;s territories encompass diverse planetary systems, ranging from temperate worlds to gas giants, and even artificial habitats constructed within asteroid belts and around stars. The confederacy boasts a rich tapestry of landscapes and environments, offering a home to an array of sentient species.
