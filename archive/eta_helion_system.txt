At the dawn of their homeworld&amp;#39;s destruction, a variety of nations and corporations departed from their earth and travelled the stars towards the distant star Eta Helion. The system, discovered to be suitable for colonization by research group Ascension Industries, lay in another spiral arm of the Milky Way. The colonists were put into cryogenic sleep for the long journey, and were awakened upon arrival to the system. The multiple factions, travelling as a fleet, halted on the edge of the system to make preparations and choose a planet to colonize.
