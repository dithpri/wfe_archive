Very mountainous region, thickly forested with primarily evergreens, birch, ash, and oak groves. Moderate climate in summer, bitterly cold in winter with excessive snowfall. Local fauna consist mostly of fur-bearing mammals and birds, as it is too cold for most reptiles. Soil is rocky and sparse except in valley regions, where it is exceptionally fertile. The native people are typically both tall and stocky, with fair hair, fair skin, and blue or green eyes. Native culture and religion revolve around war, hunting, and celebrating life, and is on a clan structure.
