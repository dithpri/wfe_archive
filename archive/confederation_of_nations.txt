[b]The Confederation of Nations is open to anyone who wants to join.[/b]
[hr][color=darkblue][b]Articles of Confederation[/b][/color]

Article. I
* No nation shall be banned without reason.
* You are free to run your country the way you want.

Article. II
* The WA delegate will be granted Administrative powers.
* Abuse the power of the WA delegate and it can be removed along with the nation.

Article. III
* You&amp;#39;re free to use the regional message board as long as you follow NationStates etiquette.
* You are also free to leave whenever you want.

Article. IV
* Nations shall be granted the freedom of equal treatment and opportunity.
* Nations are also given the right to not be discriminated against by ideology.
