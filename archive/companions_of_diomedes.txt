&amp;quot;But now, since whatever we do ten thousand chances of death are about us, which no mortal man can escape or avoid -Forwards! whether we are to give fame to another, or he to us.&amp;quot;
So cried Glaucos of Lycia at Troy.
And so must we, though we face the innumerable hosts of Raiders and Defenders alike, and all in between, must give order to the great community that is NationStates, and combat all who oppose us.
