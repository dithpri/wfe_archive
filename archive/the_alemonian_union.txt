[b]Welcome to the Alemonian States[/b]

The Union of Alemonian States, commonly known as The Alemonian Union, is currently a Union of democratic states of Alemonia. The region is officially lead by the President of the Commission, who is appointed by the Founder, who acts as the speaker of the Union, conducting elections and appoints senior officials.
