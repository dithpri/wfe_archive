Lead and protected by its most senior and founding nation, the Empire of Chelaxia, the region of Peripia is a peaceful are that, nonetheless, boasts a powerful military presence. Mostly of temperate climate, it is ruled by a Council of Delegates from its member nations. Though no nation holds to the same religious crede, a singular belief system of equine goddesses of the sun and moon underlie most religions in the region. Peripia boasts of one weapon system in particular; the Helios Satellite Weapon System, an energy-based weapon capable of reducing the surface of a small continent to molten glass within a matter of hours.
