The Asian Pacific Alliance (APA) is an alliance that was founded by Felamm, Japan, South Korea, Vietnam, Taiwan, and the Philippines shortly after The Treaty of Hong Kong (Armistice ending WW3) was signed. 

The purpose of the APA is to assist NATO in countering the influence of the Pact of Beijing (China, Russia, North Korea, and Iran).

The headquarters of the APA is located in the Taiwanese city of Taipei.
