Semadid  is a land of magic.  The major religion is the Authoritarian Cult of Dakun.  He is a Cthulhuian being who is imprisoned inside the world.  On Semadid, war is perpetual.  The Dakun relishes in chaos and destruction and war is the one true way to satisfy it.  Please join and help me create this world.  It is going to be driven by role play and storytelling.  If you have a thirst for telling tales and creating worlds, Semadid is for you.  https://www.nationstates.net
