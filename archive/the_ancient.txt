[b]The Ancient[/b] is a system of 33 degrees, including the three [b]Blue Lodge[/b] degrees administered by a local or national [b]Supreme Council[/b]. This system is popular in North America, South America and in Continental Europe. In America, the [b]York Rite[/b], with a similar range, administers three orders of [b]Masonry[/b], namely the [b]Royal Arch[/b], [b]Cryptic Masonry[/b], and [b]Knights Templar[/b].
