[region]Garlic Bread iz Gud Club[/region] is an alliance formed by [nation]Billy Boi[/nation] on the 19th May 1865 by the nations president at the time Spider Mank II after he tasted Garlic Bread for the very first time. He loved it so much that he created a club of which the only members allowed in are the ones that truly, truly love garlic bread. The club was yet to recruit anybody up until 2016 when the young daring nation of [nation]Excelse[/nation] took one sniff of garlic bread and famously said;
     &quot;Damn, that be sum gud garlic bread&quot;
