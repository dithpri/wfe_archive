[color=knee][b]Aerogeos&copy;[/b] is a colossal planet, located at the literal center of the universe. This planet circles three suns. It acts as a ley-gate between three parallel universes. The people who live on the planet of Aerogeos&copy;, known amongst their race as the [url=http://www.nationstates.net/nation=nyyx/detail=factbook/id=61872]Xyr[/url], are able to traverse from the planet into any of the three bordering universes. But only they and a handful of wise, sentient beings, called the [url=http://www.nationstates.net/nation=nyyx/detail=factbook/id=73791]Kavimagus[/url], know how to travel to the planet, for its entry is known only to them. Any who have tried to get to this planet with malevolent or greedy intentions are shocked to find that no planet exists in the quadrants it once did. But those who arrive at the planet with curious, benevolent intentions are welcomed with open arms.[/color]
