The Humanist Egalitarian Region is a region concerned with fair civil rights and political freedom. It is a secular society where people can believe in whatever religion they like, but may not try to encourage others to join it. The HER is highly concerned with making sure that the leader in power is what people want. It prides itself in being totally egalitarian vis a vis voting, as everyone has the right to start any political party and run for government. It likes to keep a stable economy, so that its citizens can enjoy all the freedoms of being in the HER. It holds monthly democratic elections.
