Welcome to the Continental Resistance!

The Five Principles of the CR:

Opposition to dogmatism, be it liberal, totalitarian, religious, Marxist, or any other kind. 
Opposition to national and global oligarchs, consumerism, and the commodification of life.
Opposition to moral and cultural degeneracy, while not clinging to incorrect prejudices.
Opposition to sectarianism, at least in the face of existential threats.
Opposition to anything and anyone who is a threat to the sovereignty of Europe.

Everyone agreeing with the principles is welcomed with open arms.
