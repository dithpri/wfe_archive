The Kingdom of Ebrilond is a modern country off the coast of the Arabian peninsula that broke free from the colonizing powers after the First World War. Though Islam largely inspires their religion due to its location, the country employs a quasi &quot;renewed&quot; version that is much more respectful of civil liberties.
