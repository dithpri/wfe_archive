[b]Salt Lake City[/b], often shortened to Salt Lake or SLC, is the capital and the most populous municipality of the U.S. state of Utah. 

The city was founded in 1847 by Brigham Young, Isaac Morley, George Washington Bradley and numerous other Mormon followers, who extensively irrigated and cultivated the arid valley. &#151;Wikipedia[hr]
