The Elira Utility Union (EUU) is a dedicated inter-governmental organization
Firstly, we seek to foster seamless economic integration among member states, ensuring the efficient and equitable flow of utility services. By removing barriers and facilitating cross-border cooperation, we aim to create a unified utility market that maximizes the benefits of free trade, innovation, and investment. Through this integration, we endeavor to provide affordable and reliable utility services that contribute to the well-being and prosperity of our citizens
