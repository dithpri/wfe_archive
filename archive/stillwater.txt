As more and more nations move to Stillwater and populate the islands we know and love, a series of questions emerge, who will be our delegate to represent us? Who will take Stillwater and further our domination? Who will show that Stillwater is not a region to mess with? No one knows... What we do know is that we are not islands anymore, but a metropolis full of continents on a single planet know as colony 41 to the central government back on Earth.
