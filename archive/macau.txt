[hr][color=#00795E][b]Macau is a special administrative region on the south coast of China, across the Pearl River Delta from Hong Kong. A Portuguese territory until 1999, it reflects a mix of cultural influences. Its giant casinos and malls on the Cotai Strip, which joins the islands of Taipa and Coloane, have earned it the nickname, &quot;Las Vegas of Asia.&quot; One of its more striking landmarks is the tall Macau Tower, with sweeping city views.[/b][/color]
