[b]Principality of Catalonia[/b]
[hr]
The Principality of Catalonia (in Catalan, Principat de Catalunya) and a modern state is the historic and traditional name of Catalonia.
[hr]
[b]Foundation:[/b] 1162
[b]Capital:[/b] Barcelona
[b]Government:[/b] Constitutional monarchy
[b]Region:[/b] Mediterranean Europe
[b]Official language:[/b]Castilian, Catalan and Occitan (Aranese).
[b]Religion:[/b] Catholicism
[hr]
