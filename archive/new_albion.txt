New Albion is named after Albion, which was a name given to the British Isles. After the horrifying Throwing Of The Rotten Potatoes by the Irish in 2011, the Scots left Britain and embarked to the newly discovered continent.

A remote country of 39 atolls, New Albion has few natural resources, but due to recent discoveries Lago Maggiore is now the number 7 exporter of Yellowcake (urania) in the World.
