Emerging from the fallen Miorian empire, many fledgling territories struggled to survive amid the chaos of a crumbling economy and infrastructure. Eventually, Auragoth and the Western Territories united to form Auragothic Miori, and Ethria and the Iya/Au islands united to form Iyatian Ethria. The relationship between the two nations, though frayed, is remarkably cordial considering their differing ideologies.
