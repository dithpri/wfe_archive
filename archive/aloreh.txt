The Region of Aloreh is a large flat expanse of dense thick pine forests covered in a soft blanket of snow, surrounded by mountain ranges. Its coasts are brimming rich in oil and sea life, making fishing a major priority for every nation. The soil in the more inland locations has proven fertile, and the mountain ranges are teeming with minerals like Iron, copper and gold. The region itself is a continent with a multiple islands around it and too this day the map has been expanding.  Its local inhabitants must ward off extreme colds, dry weather and each other.
