[url=https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nineteen_Eighty-Four][b]1984[/b][/url] is a dystopian novel by George Orwell set in the totalitarian society of Oceania, ruled by the Party and its mysterious leader known only as Big Brother. The government controls its populace through the four ministries of Love, Peace, Truth and Plenty, exemplified by slogans such as &quot;War is Peace, Freedom is Slavery, Ignorance is Strength&quot; and &quot;Big Brother is watching you.&quot;
