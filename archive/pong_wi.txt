Following a bloody and violent takeover of the Empire of [nation]Bok Chi[/nation] by radical Communists the country was thrown into utter chaos.  Communist rule in Bok Chi almost resulted in a NS World War.  The Communist regime was ousted and Bok Chi became a Kingdom once again.  It was decided by the new King Mongkut II to form their own region. Thus the region of Pong Wi was formed.

Pong Wi Region is a region focused on democratic rule.  Nations who do not have a demonstrated degree of democracy are not allowed into the region.
