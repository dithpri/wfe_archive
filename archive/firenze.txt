[list][color=003F00][b]Firenze[/b] (aka Florence in English) is the capital and largest city in the Italian Tuscany region.
Center of medieval European trade and finance.
Birthplace of the Renaissance.
From 1865 to 1871, capital of the Kingdom of Italy.
Patron Saint: John the Baptist (24 June)[/color][/list][hr]
