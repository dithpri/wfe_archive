[b]Proposal For The Free World[/b]

[b]Amendment I - Free Political Act[/b]
Political parties are banned from advertising and receiving private donations.

[b]Amendment II - Cannabis Act[/b]
Marijuana is legal in the privacy of your own home.

[b]Amendment III - Instant Runoff Voting[/b]
In the event that one candidate fails to achieve a sufficient majority, the candidate with the fewest number of first-preference rankings is eliminated and these votes redistributed, the process being repeated until one candidate achieves the required majority.
