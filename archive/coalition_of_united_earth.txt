United Earth is a world-spanning coalition of many different nations united to achieve world peace and to support each other during domestic, military, and economic threats. United Earth member nations are still considered independent and may leave at any time, however, those in United Earth must follow all legislature, regulations, and mandates. United Earth meets in a council that serves and legislature and a decision-making force, with one representative from each nation meeting in the council on a thrice-monthly basis. United Earth permits all interested nations to join at any time.
