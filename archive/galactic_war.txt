In the early 23rd century, 100 years after the end of the First Galactic War against The Insects, Chromeborgs, and The Enlightened Ones, enemies of Freedom, Liberty, and Managed Democracy rose up against the Federation of Superb Earth. In response, the Hellplungers were mobilized for the first time since the last war to defend Superb Earth. The Second Galactic War, officially known as the Galactic War on Chaos was a galaxy-wide conflict with the Hellplungers serving as the vanguard of Liberty.
