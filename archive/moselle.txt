Moselle has been a place of conflict in many European wars. In 1919 it became the home to the predecessor to the Mighty Blood Empire, the Republic of Moselle. Now with the Mighty Blood Empire ridding the region of it&#39;s bureaucratic government and installing a dictatorship, economic and political prosperity has come to the land.

Moselle is a serious roleplay region meant mostly for those that are for free trade, for industrial progress, have future tech, and those with conservative values. However, anyone is open to join.
