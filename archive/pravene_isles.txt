The Pravene Isles are a grouping of a larger island, known as Pravengria surrounded by smaller islands along its west, south, and north-east coast. The region is home to the Federated Commonwealth of Carpathia, a multi-regional trade power whose holding are in Tyrrhenia, Sondria, and Greater Dienstad. The FCC dominates the region with a population of just over 93 million people, and a diaspora of 432 million in her constituent territories. The FCC is currently working on an international bank, stock exchange and negotiates numerous trade deals through the Royal Trading Company.
