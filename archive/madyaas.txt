REGIONAL ADMINISTRATION

EXECUTIVE BRANCH :
President of the Government - elected for 5-month term by the regions through Electoral Votes ; elected along with the Vice President of the Regional Government

LEGISLATIVE BRANCH
Regional Council - consist of 7 members elected for 1-month term by the regional citizens.

JUDICIAL BRANCH
Supreme Court - consists of judges appointed by the President for 6-month terms
