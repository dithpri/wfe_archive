The promised land of Hiediocer was created June 2 2016
This region was founded by The Peoples Republic of Potatoes as an alliance between nations, to strengthen the economy of members through trade.


Where, and what is Hiediocer?
Hiediocer is a big city of about 1 270 000 citizens at the border of The Peoples Republic of Potatoes and The Empire of Aleth sirris, where leaders of the two countries met and negotiated about creating a new region. 
The city is where all official meetings take Place, and has become a center of trade and immigration between the 2 nations.


The region has since grown to include more nations, and hopes for a bright future as one of the major regions of the world.
