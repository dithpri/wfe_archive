The Guns of Helghan Universe is a RP universe that specializes in Future Tech nations and their Role playing skills that takes place in the Galaxy of the Milky Way. The GoHU features several entities already pre-picked nations from various video games and movies in real life entertainment, but players can make their own version of the game/movie entity or make a totally original nation.
