The Estamindian Island Chain is an island chain that consist of 6 countries. The Estamindian Island Chain was founded after the collapse of The Estamindian Empire when all of the fragmented countries unified into one region. The chain resides in the South China Sea, making for beautiful weather at any given time of year. These mountainous territories have their fair share of breathtaking beaches and lush rainforests, providing a habitat for stunning animal species. While in Druundia, you may encounter the exclusive Druundian Peacock. The stunning landscapes, the forceful and helpful government, the protection of the people, and the lifestyle combine into one large archipelago&#151;The Estamindian Island Chain.
