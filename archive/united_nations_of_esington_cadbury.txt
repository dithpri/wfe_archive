The United Nations of Esington Cadbury (UNEC) is an intergovernmental organization established on 3 October 2014 to promote reigon co-operation.UNEC has 3 member states.The UNEC Headquarters is situated in Cuadernotlan, Eladrin . Further main offices are situated in Kel&eacute;fono and Yonstruir. Its objectives include maintaining reigonal peace and security, promoting human rights, fostering social and economic development, protecting the environment, and providing humanitarian aid in cases of famine, natural disaster, and armed conflict.
