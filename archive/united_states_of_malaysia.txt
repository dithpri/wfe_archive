[b] Welcome to United States of Malaysia(aka Malaysia or USM)[/b]


Malaysia is a Federal Constitution Monarchy located in Southeast Asia. Peninsular Malaysia shares a land and maritime border with Thailand in the north and maritime borders with Singapore in the south, Vietnam in the northeast, and Indonesia in the west. East Malaysia shares land and maritime borders with Brunei and Indonesia and a maritime border with the Philippines and Vietnam. Their capital is Kuala Lumpur. Malaysia has a total landmass of 330,803 square kilometres (127,720&nbsp;sq&nbsp;mi) separated by the South China Sea into two similarly sized regions, Peninsular Malaysia and East Malaysia (Malaysian Borneo).
