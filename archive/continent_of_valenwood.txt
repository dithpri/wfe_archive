Valenwood is one of the smaller continents of the world covered with lush forests. The coasts of Valenwood are dominated by mostly rocky beaches , while heavy rainfalls throughout the spring nurture the temperate inland forests.

Grasslands and plains are further south with snowy mountains to the north. Domica Redwort is a herb and also the official regional plant grown by many of the populous of Valenwood for their beautiful and showy flowers. They attain a height of about three feet and have feathery leaves; the flowers are usually bright red. They grow in the wild at the bases of the mountains.
