The ILN is an international organization of independent states that is dedicated and designed to build peace and prosperity between nations. These nations have come together under a common belief that democracy, human rights and the rule of law should be in all aspects of society. It is through the ILN that independent states of the world may stand united in their beliefs and convictions.
