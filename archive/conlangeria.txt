Conlangeria is a region for conlangers (those who construct languages), those who construct writing scripts, and anyone interest in constructed languages &amp;amp; constructed writing scripts, though fans of natural languages are also welcome.
Examples of constructed languages are [url=http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Quenya]Quenya[/url], [url=http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Klingon_language]Klingon[/url], [url=http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/K%C4%93len]Kelen[/url], [url=http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Esperanto]Esperanto[/url], and [url=http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lojban]Lojban[/url].
