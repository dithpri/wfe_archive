&amp;quot;[region=Akkadia]&amp;quot; or the &amp;quot;Akkadian Region&amp;quot; is currently comprised of four nations, though a fairly large swath of land still remains unclaimed and barren. The climate ranges from tropical within [nation=Okijin], temperate, and idyllic in [nation=Harkonna], temperate and somewhat mountainous [nation=Saint Alexander], to frigid and icy in [nation=Tyslus].
