The Syndicalist International, often called the Third International, or simply the International, is a military-political alliance of syndicalist and socialist nations which aims to unite the workers of the world and launch the worldwide proletarian revolution. The goals of the International are thoroughly discussed in international congresses to which leaders of socialist states and movements worldwide are invited.
