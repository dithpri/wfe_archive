&amp;#945; Lyncis is a variable giant star of spectral class K7 in the constellation of Lynx. &amp;#945; Lyncis visual magnitude is 3.13. Because of its moderate brightness, &amp;#945; Lyncis is easily visible to the naked eye from locations with dark skies, while it might be barely visible, or not visible at all, from light-polluted skies.
