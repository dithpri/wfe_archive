The sister queendoms of [nation]Ainikka[/nation], [nation]Sinikka[/nation] and [nation]Vinikka[/nation], were the three contituent states of the [b]Confederacy of [nation]Arianne-Serafine[/nation][/b]. The confederacy was split up in an uneventful and clear process, the borders were kept intact and the region was given a new name as [b][region]Kyllikki[/region][/b]. The common laws were kept intact as well as the currency and integration between the three states, with stability and prosperity for the citizens as the main goal.
