Compounded with empires from all across the world, the Imperial Fellowship links them together. Controlled by the mighty red skies of the Imperius System, this region is invulnerable to most threats of external setting.

===[[&amp;lt;&amp;gt;}

[b] If you wish to join the Imperial Fellowship [/b]

Send a telegram to the Empire of Quidtovia stating your nation (must be Empire/Holy Empire/etc) and it will be reviewed. You will be telegrammed the password back within 24 hours.
