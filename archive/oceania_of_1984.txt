The ruling doctrine of Oceania is Ingsoc, the Newspeak term for English Socialism, which is ultimately devoted to the exercise of power.

Its nominal leader is Big Brother, who is believed by the masses to have been the leader of the revolution and is still used as a figurehead by the party, but may now be dead, or may have never existed. 

The personality cult is maintained through Big Brother&amp;#39;s function as a focal point for love, fear, and reverence, emotions which are more easily felt towards an individual than towards an organisation.
