A redrawing of the census data resulted in Ruchenia being divided between different areas. The majority of the nations that inhabited Ruchenia fall within this new Lower Ruchenia region.

Local Religions: Chlorophagic, Soothsayism,

The Region of Ruchenia has been ravaged by the Grand Cumanian Civil War, most of the nations within the region are successor states of the once mighty Grand Cumania. Cumania&#39;s former overlord of Bortslovakia has shown interest in entering the region to ensure no more ethnic Cumans die should a new war start up, as tensions between its members are high.
