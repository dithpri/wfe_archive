Proclamation of the Sovereign Land of Britheis

18 July 2026

The Sovereign Land of Britheis is hereby established as a distinct and autonomous region, serving as the rightful home of the United Kingdom of Britheis. This region is founded upon the principles of full sovereignty, national independence, and non-interference in internal affairs.

No foreign power shall dictate the affairs of this region, nor undermine the unity and sovereignty of the Britheian Crown.

The region shall be administered by the Royal Secretary of the Region, appointed by His Majesty the King, governing on behalf of the Crown.



Issued by the Office of the Royal Secretary of the Region &#151; 18 July 2026
