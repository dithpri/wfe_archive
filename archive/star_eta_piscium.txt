Alpherg, also designated as &amp;#951; Piscium (eta Piscium), is a variable and double giant star of spectral class G7 in the constellation of Pisces. Alpherg visual magnitude is 3.62. Because of its moderate brightness, Alpherg is easily visible to the naked eye from locations with dark skies, while it might be barely visible, or not visible at all, from light-polluted skies.
