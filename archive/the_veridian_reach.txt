The Veridian Reach is a vast subcontinent situated in the northern hemisphere of the world, stretching from the arid deserts of the west to the lush jungles of the east. It is home to a multitude of diverse cultures, each with its own rich history and unique traditions. The region is marked by towering mountain ranges, winding rivers, and sprawling plains, providing ample resources for the peoples who call it home. From the bustling cities of the coast to the remote villages nestled in the heartland, the Veridian Reach is a land of both great beauty and great challenges.
