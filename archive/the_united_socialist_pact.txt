[b]What is the USP?[/b]

The USP is an inter-regional union of nations who agree with principles of socialism, communism and democracy, we are NOT a region. The USP has members from 5 diffrent regions. We have a democratically elected General Secretary who handles the executive, while the legaslature is a complete direct democracy.
