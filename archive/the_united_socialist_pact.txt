[b]About the USP[/b]
The USP is an inter-regional union of nations who agree with principles of socialism, communism and democracy, we are NOT a region. The USP has members from 5 different regions.

[b]Mission statement:[/b] [i]&quot;Strength, Unity and Defense of the People&quot;[/i]
[b]Mission goals:[/b]
 - Fighting against Fascism and oppression
 - Defending Socialism and Communism in all of its forms.[hr]
