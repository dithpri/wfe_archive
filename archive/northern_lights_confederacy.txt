Geographically, the region is in the high arctic, comprising of the islands of Svalbard, Edge Island, Barents Island, Jan Mayen, Franz Josef land and Novaya Zemlya. 

connecting the ex soviet islands to the main regional islands is one of the longest bridges ever built, designed for high speed rail, the bridge spans 1,680 miles of open ocean.  To cross this bridge it takes almost 10 hours on the high speed ORBIS line.
