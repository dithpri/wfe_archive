Mirfak, also designated as &amp;#945; Persei (alpha Persei), is a variable and double supergiant star of spectral class F5 in the constellation of Perseus. Mirfak visual magnitude is 1.79, making it the 34th brightest star in the sky. Because of its brightness, Mirfak is clearly visible to the naked eye when observed from locations with dark skies, and should be also quite easily visible from light polluted areas.
