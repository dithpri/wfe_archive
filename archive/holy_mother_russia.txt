All You Need to Know about the Russian EmpireThe Russian Empire was a vast empire that stretched across Eurasia and North America from 1721 to 1917. It was the third-largest empire in history, after the British and Mongol empires. The Russian Empire ended in 1917 when the Provisional Government proclaimed the Russian Republic after the February Revolution. The Bolshevik communists took control after winning the civil war and established the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics (USSR).
