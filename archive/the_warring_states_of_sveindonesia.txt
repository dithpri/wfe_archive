Sveindonesia was once a great confederacy, spanning across many islands and landmasses, uniting the many city states (such as Ragnakarta) under an overarching government. City states warred with one another only with the blessing of the Sveindonesian Confederacy.

However, disaster has struck, and Konfrontasir&ouml;k has begun! Several regions and states of the Confederacy have proclaimed their independence, others fight to reunite the lands, while others carry on, sipping from their flask of rice-mead.
