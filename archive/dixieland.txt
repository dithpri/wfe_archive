The Congress of the Confederate States convenes this, the year of our Lord,  two thousand and thirteen, on july the third. 

Whereas, Tyranny and Oppression by the Federal government has become unsustainable.

Whereas, The ideologies seperating the Southern Confederacy and the Northern Union has become too great to sustain perpetual Union.

Be it resolved that the Confederated States, created by the consent of the People exercising the inalienable right to self government, hereby secedes from the Union and forms it&amp;#39;s own government.
