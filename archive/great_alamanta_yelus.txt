Great Alamanta Yelus is a communist superpower and has rebelled from various countries to be what it is today. The military crushes all that oppress their beliefs and attempt rebellions. This superpower keeps everything in order and makes sure that no rebellions shall happen. But if they do, Great Alamanta Yelus completely destroys them with their amazing military. This superpower has 4 provincial sections named Daniter, Dimado, Scandinavian Wolf, and New Corschlava. These sections help each other in defense and trade and have created this massive superpower.
