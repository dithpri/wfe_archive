The Mutassarifate of Al Zubayr is an administrative Region which surrounds both Abadan &amp; The Haasan coast to the Persian gulf, a core of the Sultanate of Mushasha&#39; (succeeded by Mahdyyia). The Area, being ruled by sucessive claimants of Al Mahdi and with legitimate claims to the Title of Sayyid, was stage of Secessions and successive attempts of Conquests by Major Empires, such as the Jalayrid Emirate, Seljuqs, Tamerlane&#39;s Empire, The Ottomans and the Wahhabist Ikhwans.
