[b]United Kingdom of Pemecutan[/b]

The United Kingdom is a sovereign country that unite all the petty kingdoms in Pemecutan lands under the rule of High King of Pemecutan ([i]Ratu Hawir Bhuja Pemecutan[/i]). The union is intended to develop a stronger bond between neighboring constituent states (based on their historical relationship) and to strengthen the economy, military and diplomacy power abroad.

There are 4 constituent states that unite under the union. All the states has its own power to govern their states freely under the supervision of Central Government and The Union Charter. A Federal City is created for the Central Government to exercise their function as the highest government authority of all the constituent states.
