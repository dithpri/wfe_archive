The planet Elycadia located in the Ul&#39;kran system a binary star the sector lies within 10 planets. Elycadia is 5th planet in the sector with a mid-cold habitable zone, the climates are all temperate with no desert.

Elycadians exploits the vast minerals within the uninhabitable planets to enrich their lives and to preserve the natural beauty of Elycadia. With the excess they sold their luxury to increase their profits.
