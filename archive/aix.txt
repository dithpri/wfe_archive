We welcome you to Aix

Member states of Aix are to work to the following goals of maintaining:
- Economic Freedom: average taxation rates under 35%
- Economic Prosperity: average income above 70,000 euros
- The Elimination of Poverty: average income of poor above 50,000 euros
- Low Corruption: corruption indicator under .80
- other attributes of shared values, such as strong social services, well-protected environments, and high civil and political rights
