Swajimama Territory is a single-party state. A single-party state is a type of state in which a single political party has the right to form the government, based on the existing constitution. Elections in our region are run every five years. Members of the Supreme People&amp;#39;s Assembly are elected to five-year terms, and meet for SPA sessions up to ten days per year. The Supreme People&amp;#39;s Assembly elects a standing committee known as the Presidium, which exercises legislative functions when the Assembly is not in session. It also elects the Chairman of the National Defense  Commission, the country&amp;#39;s chief executive, and the Premier.
