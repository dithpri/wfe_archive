A small, technology based region based on the idea that community life and gross national happiness are the most important tenets of life. We believe that a strongly communist government run by the people is the only way to run an economy that isn&#39;t built on some form of injustice. However, we also believe in maintaining as much personal freedom as possible. As such, small family businesses are permitted to grow to a certain point if the community continues to believe that they are serving its best interests. Direct democracy and fierce debate are important, but are not possible without compassion and education.
