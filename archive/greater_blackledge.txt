The heartland of the Blackledge empire. The most loyal dominions and peoples of the realm are allowed to reside here.

Since the costly victories of the Nodic Wars, the people of the Royal Empire have sought refuge away from the troubles of the outside world. Outsiders are not trusted.
Loyal Grumman patrols watch the frontier, keeping suspicious travelers away.

The World Assembly is not trusted, viewed as a well-meaning but naive pack of interlopers.
