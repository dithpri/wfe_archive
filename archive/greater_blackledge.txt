The heartland of the [i]Bl&aelig;cl&aelig;c &Aacute;nweald[/i], the realm that is the Blackledge kingdom and empire. The most loyal dominions and peoples of the realm are allowed to reside here.

Since the costly victories of the Nodic Wars, the people of the [i]&Aacute;nweald[/i] have sought peace away from the troubles of the outside world and largely pursued a policy of political and cultural (but not economic) isolation.
On air and sea the Royal Air Force and Royal Navy keep commerce lines open, while the few land locations contiguous with foreign nations are patrolled by Andurien outriders.
