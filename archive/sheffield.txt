[region]Sheffield[/region] is an industrial city in South Yorkshire, England, forming part of the northern heartland of the [region]Empire of Britain[/region]. Historically renowned for its steel production, Sheffield developed as a major manufacturing center during the Industrial Revolution and remains a symbol of British industrial heritage. Administratively, it is governed as a metropolitan district within South Yorkshire.
