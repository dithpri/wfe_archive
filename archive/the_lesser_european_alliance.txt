The Lesser European Alliance is an alliance of nations descended from the original European immigrants on the continent of Telas. The Alliance is founded on mutual respect. cooperation and friendship inbetween nations, albeit it not necessarily in a democratic manner. 

The Kingdom of Johannisa leads the Alliance, her leader, Queen Johanna II, being the most influential person in the entire region. 

Norwenia, the original nation of European Settlers, controls all local developments in the Alliance&#39;s provinces. 

Shungra, a relative newcomer to the alliance, has recently been chosen to become Vice Imperator of the Alliance.
