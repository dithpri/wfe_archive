The International Alliance is an international union of countries of Europe, Asia and Africa. The alliance was formed on September 13, 2018. The IA aims at the creation of free trade between all member states, and also aids for prevention of any internal wars between historically rivaling countries, and also helps in making the world more peaceful through integrated and combined military forces. Its parliament is located in the city of Merville, Avinea, which was historically known in Europe to be neutral and the host of many treaties such as Treaty Of Avinean Defence, Treaty Of 1900 etc. The IA is a democratic, welfare - oriented region.
