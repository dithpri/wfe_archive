Nepal, officially the [b]Federal Democratic Republic of Nepal[/b], is a country located in South Asia. With an area of 56,827 sq mi and a population of approximately 27 million, Nepal is the world&#39;s 93rd largest country by area and the 41st most populous country. It is a landlocked country located along the Himalayas and bordered to the north by China and to the south, east, and west by India.

The mountainous north of Nepal has eight of the world&#39;s ten tallest mountains, including the highest point on Earth, Mount Everest. &#151;Wikipedia[hr]
