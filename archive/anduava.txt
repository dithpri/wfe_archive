Anduava is a massive, sprawling continent that spans a variety of different climates and ecosystems. From tall, rocky crags and mountains, to gentle rolling pastures, this continent sports true diversity.

=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=
Far to the frozen north, and occupying the largest mass of land on the continent, is the oppressive regime of Colboria - a massive, militaristic nation driven by commercial greed and crime. 
=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=
To the west around the largest port is the Allied States of Anduava, a polite and developing democratic nation-state.
=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=
To the Southeast at the Aollian sea, occupying the Relian archipelago is the United Kingdom of Great Artano. A prosperous liberal monarchy.
