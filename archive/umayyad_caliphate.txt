The Umayyad Caliphate is the second caliphate established after the death of the Islamic prophet Muhammad and is ruled by the Umayyad dynasty. The Umayyad Caliphate covers 11,100,000 km2 (4,300,000 sq mi), making it one of the largest empires.

All are Welcome within the Caliphate, and the Supreme Caliphate Of Allah welcomes usurpers with open arms.
