[b]Santa Fe[/b] is the capital of the state of New Mexico. It is the fourth-largest city in the state and is the seat of Santa Fe County.

This area was occupied for at least several thousand years by indigenous peoples who built villages several hundred years ago on the current site of the city. Known by the Tewa inhabitants as Ogha Po&#39;oge, White Shell Water Place. The city of Santa Fe, founded by Spanish colonists in 1610, is known as the oldest state capital city in the United States and the oldest city in New Mexico. Santa Fe (meaning &quot;holy faith&quot; in Spanish) had a population of 69,204 in 2012. &#151;Wikipedia[hr]
