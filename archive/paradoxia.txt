The region for Paradoxians.

The only required action from member nations is to come to the defense of any other member nation in time of need.

No member nation will be compelled to accept or enact legislation from the region or other nations. The sovereignty of member nations shall not be involuntarily infringed upon. Member nations are free to enter into any voluntary associations or agreements that they so choose.
