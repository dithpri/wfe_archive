A Land rich in natural resources.  When you take a closer look, it is a continent filled with islands large and small, a smattering of volcanoes and more beaches then any other region of the world.  The active volcanoes bring to the surface massive amounts of heavy metals and minerals, a boon to agriculture, crops flourish in the mineral rich soils. The volcanic activity is also a boon to mining with high concentrations of rare elements close to the surface.
