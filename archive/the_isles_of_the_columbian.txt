The Isles is a well armed and defended string of Islands unified under the Purple Chief of the Columbian. Founded By the United Regions of Thalos. The Delegate and President is The Grand Duchy of Ottoman-Hungary. The Holy Empire of Yttersberg is the Vice President. The Most Serene Republic of Dolabellia serves as the Minister of the Treasury. The Rogue Nation of Demacia-Ionia serves as Minister of Industry. The Most Serene Republic of Grand Theocracy of Schwanzburg is the serving Minister of Magic. Further governmental postitions in the URB, the United Reigning Bloc, are open to discussion.
