Raisbeck Aviation High School (or RAHS) is a part of the Highline School District and is located in Tukwila, Washington. The school is an aviation and aerospace themed STEM school and one of the Highline School District&amp;#39;s small schools. It is focused on preparing students for college, career and citizenship. The school serves about 400 students in grades 9-12 from around the Puget Sound area. Until 2013 it was known as Aviation High School. It is located across the street from the Museum of Flight near King County International Airport.
