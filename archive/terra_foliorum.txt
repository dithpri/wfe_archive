The region of Terra Foliorum is a spectacular, scenic nation filled with forested, rolling hills and a refreshing mountainous setting in areas to the north. The region itself is quite large, with thousands and thousands of miles of lush, beautiful scenery. The most frequent weather of Terra Foliorum is cold fresh air and clouds, rain is also frequent, but sun is quite rare, unless it is summertime. The region supports several nations, all of which bear diverse cultures and histories. To join the region, the Landmaster Nation must be contacted, or the nation wishing to join must be contacted directly. Those who wish to join can only join with permission.
