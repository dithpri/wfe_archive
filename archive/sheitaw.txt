[b]The Democratic Republic of Sheitaw[/b], commonly known as [b]Sheitaw[/b], is an island country in Northeast Asia. It is bordered on the west by Russia and on the south by Japan. Its capital and largest city is Kaankizi. The name Sheitaw originates from the Sheitawese words for snow, [i]&quot;shei&quot;[/i], and island, [i]&quot;taw&quot;[/i].
