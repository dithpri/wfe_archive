[B][color=orange]Mengjiang[/color], also known in English as Mongol Border Land, was an autonomous area in Inner Mongolia, existing as a puppet state of the [color=orange]Japanese Empire[/color] under nominal Chinese sovereignty. Formed in 1936, it consisted of the then-Chinese provinces of Chahar and Suiyuan, corresponding to the central part of modern Inner Mongolia. The capital was Kalgan, and the ruler was Demchugdongrub.[/B]
