The [b]Shoreville Cooperation Organisation (SCO) [/b]is a political and economic union of member states. SCO was established when the [b]Shoreville Common Market Treaty[/b] came into force in 1963. 

SCO influences the [b]economic[/b] and [b]political[/b] system of its member states and provides financial aid as well as assistance.
