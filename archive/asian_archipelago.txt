These countries fled from the Mature and Sophisticated Club region, a prosperous region originally founded by NationStates enthusiasts from an Internet community that comprised mostly secondary-school age students. Together, they designed the most creative flags known to man. Now, however, most of these nations ceased to exist as their leaders moved on to more meaningful prospects (which is to say, doing well in school); the region was promptly raided, with all the remaining nations ejected, and now is inhabited by a single, reclusive nation whose motives for maintaining the region are unclear.
