The region in which Alexlantis and Greethah lie. Notable for it&amp;#39;s bountiful resources, beautiful forested landscape, and frequency of small battles that may end up starting the end of all, Noyarca is surprisingly peaceful. Alexlantis, Greethah, Fornosa, Zenoco, and Spakion have nuclear weapons, and all other nations, save for the Amish Collective, are working on producing their own.
