The Republic of Univea, or commonly referred to as Univea, is one of the few nations located in the continent of Oceania. With a land area of 8,437,136 km&sup2;, Univea is the 6th largest country in the world. With a population of 70 million Univeans, it consists of 10 states and administrative units. Univea is a part of the coalition of the South Pacific.
