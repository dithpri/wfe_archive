The Industrial Revolution has come to a world of magic. Old orders unchanged for many centuries have been shattered, and every nation seeks glory in the new order of the world. No longer is Aurinel centered on itself, now it looks abroad to the continents and islands abroad. Coal and steam have come to be the new arbiters of power and energy, with the steam engine having quickly cemented its power as the most powerful technology ever invented. All are looking for their place.
