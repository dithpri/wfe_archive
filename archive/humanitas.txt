The land of Humanitas is the host to the glorious Kingdom of Fumonoxii, and her dominions beyond. These include many lands of great geographic variety, from the Northern Mountains of Abgharistan and isles of Mavr&oacute;s, the the Eastern Coast of Prospereos, to the Southern Marshes and Rainforests of Zapapan, to the Great Western Desert of Jhubaar. In the center of all this is the Kingdom of Fumonoxii, home to great sprawling industrial metroplisies.
