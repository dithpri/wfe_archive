[color=blue]The United States Congress is the bicameral legislature of the regional government of the United States consisting of two chambers: the Senate and the House of Representatives. The United States Congress can become composed to up to one hundred Senators, and one hundred Representatives. Both chambers, House, and Senate, are elected to represent their region. Information on interegional elections can be found in our dispatches.[/color]
