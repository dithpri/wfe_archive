Hello. Welcome to wondrous land of cloud mastery, or Master Cloud as it is called. 

First of all, you must understand the order of civilization in the Master Cloud region. The Master Cloud has dominion over ALL other clouds, be them ivory white or ebony black, the Master Cloud holds lordship over all clouds of the sky. From the harmless puff to a mighty thunderhead those great sky-beasts venerate their ultimate god-king, the Master Cloud.

And so shall we.
