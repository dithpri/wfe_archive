The Dominion of Ironcrest is a powerful and disciplined region forged through strength, unity, and absolute order. Nestled between harsh mountain ranges and storm-ridden seas, the nations of Ironcrest pride themselves on military power, industrial might, and unwavering loyalty to their leaders.

In this region, weakness is cast aside and only the strong are respected. Governments range from strict dictatorships to authoritarian republics, all sharing a belief that stability and control are the keys to prosperity. Massive fortresses guard the borders, while factories and shipyards work day and night to ensure Ironcrest remains unchallenged.
