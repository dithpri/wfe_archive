The peoples of the New Middle-Earth are from a galaxy far, far, away, from Middle-Earth, the 4th moon of the Endor system. 6,500 years after the Galactic Civil War, the tech is finally created to penetrate the barrier at the edges of the galaxy. A delegation from Middle-Earth was sent, and crashed in the Oceania region of the Pacific. Upon arriving, they split into ideological factions.

Regional government composed of multi-party senate with 4 delagates from each territory. Every 6 years an election is held to decide upon the office of prime minister. Corpaorate donations banned to prevent corruption.
