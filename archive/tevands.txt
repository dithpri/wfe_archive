In 1958 a new continent was discovered by a satellite. No one could believe it! Discovering a new continent after all these years! But the people decided to do things differently this time. A man was sent to this new continent to explore it and determine what the future of this continent was going to be. In the end, no other people were found. But animals such as cows, pigs, horses and so on. After quite a discovery, the man decided in 1963 to found his own country in the center of this continent, he called it: Therge. He later named the continent: Tevands. The man gave other people the chance to join this continent and found new countries with any culture!
