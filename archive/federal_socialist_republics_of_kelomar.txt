[color=red]Federal Socialist Republics of Kelomar[/color] is a nation comprised of twenty three federal socialist republics. The official establishment of the government was in [u]2010[/u] by the peoples national congress. It is not until 2014 that the current federal system was adopted under Justiv Mathar&amp;#39;s presidency.  Kelomar has a strong relationship with [color=red]communist[/color] and [color=red]socialist allies[/color], the closest of them is Xerlona Union, an alliance based on economic trade and military cooperation for the broader regions.
