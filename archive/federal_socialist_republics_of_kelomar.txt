Federal Socialist Republics of Kelomar is a nation comprised of twenty three federal socialist republics. The official establishment of the government was in 2010 by the peoples national congress. It is not until 2014 that the current federal system was adopted under Justiv Mathar&amp;#39;s presidency.  Kelomar has a strong relationship with communist and socialist allies, the closest of them is Xerlona Union, a new alliance based on economic trade and military cooperation for the broader region.
