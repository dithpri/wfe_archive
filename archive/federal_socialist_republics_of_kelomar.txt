[color=red]The Federal Socialist Republics of Kelomar, or FSRK for short, is a federation that was found on the ideals of democratic and socialist principles. The founders and the people of this illustrious nation have recognized that these two pillars allow maximum stability to a society as well as keep an obligation to protect the inalienable rights of all people who call Kelomar their motherland. These rights, which cannot be taken away, are adopted from the declaration of the rights of man and of the citizen.
