The Federal Socialist Republics of Kelomar abbreviated to FSRK, is a socialist state on the Xerlona continent. A union of multiple socialist republics that are unified under the banner of Kelomar, its government and economy are highly centralized. Kelomar is a multi-party federation, currently ruled by the socialist party, with the office of president and premier by Justiv Mathar. The Federal Socialist Republics of Kelomar had its roots in the Red Revolution, when the Comuna, led by Nokiloi Losa, overthrew the provisional government who replaced the autocratic regime.
