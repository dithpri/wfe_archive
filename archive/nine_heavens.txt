The Constitution of the Nine Heavens

Article I: The Nine Heavens consist of immortals who control their respective Kingdoms and do hereby pay tribute to the Heavenly Lord who rules supreme. The Heavenly Lord is assisted by a Supreme Lord, who rules whenever the Heavenly Lord is unavailable.

Article II: Positions

The High God of War (Military Affairs)
The High God of Silk (Financial Matters)
The High God of Land (Internal Affairs)
The High God of Tribute (Foreign Affairs)
