[b]The [color=red]October Revolution[/color], formally known as the Great October Socialist Revolution, was the glorious revolution in which the Bolsheviks overthrew the capitalist Russian Republic and established Soviet Russia - the world&#39;s first socialist country ever.

The revolution achieved victory in 7 November, 1917 (25 October according to Old Style calendar - the calendar Russia was using at the time, hence the reason the revolution was called the October Revolution).

Glory to the world&#39;s communists and socialists! Glory to Russia and the Soviet![/b]

[i]This region was created in 7 November, 2019 to commemorate the October Revolution.[/i]
