The Federal States of the British Isles, is a group of&nbsp;islands&nbsp;in the Pacific. The group consists of two main&nbsp;islands,&nbsp;Donalbain&nbsp;and Arkhatan, and numerous smaller&nbsp;islands&nbsp;and island groups, including the Poles, the Machurny, the Orkes&nbsp;Islands, the&nbsp;Isles&nbsp;of Hillshire, and the&nbsp;Isle&nbsp;of Manchester.
