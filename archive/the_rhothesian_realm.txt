The Rhothesian Realm is the collection of constituent countries [b][i]in free association[/i][/b] with Rhothesia and for which the Monarch of Rhothesia is head of state. Also known as the Commonwealth of the Realm, the five constituent countries outside of Rhothesia have almost total self-rule as of 2009, but surrender powers over foreign relations and defence to the government of Rhothesia [b][i]on the consent of their own administrations[/i][/b].

The Rhothesian Realm is united under the common history, values and efforts of its members, but recognises the distinct cultural values of its separate constituents.
