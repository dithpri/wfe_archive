The Papua region contains 6 sub-divisions: Tonga, Fiji, Papua Major, Papua Minor, Reissia(islands West of West Papua) and the island of Timor. The full Regional name is The States of the Region of Papua, Tonga, Fiji &amp; Timor, or SotRoPTFT.
