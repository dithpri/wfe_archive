A cold, harsh region of wide grasslands, coniferous forests, deep and colossal oceans, and high mountains, with high amounts of annual precipitation and long periods of winter (temperatures below -60 F). The native peoples are comprised exclusively of nomadic clans, with sustenance methods usually based off pastoralism or foraging, and most regions are between the Stone and Iron ages. Shekh prides itself on its massive cavalry, its ecological diversity, and its exclusion of non-nomadic peoples.
