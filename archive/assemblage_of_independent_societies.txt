Disinterested with personal and social motivations in the sea of regions within this communication medium, the dictator of Scythan stuck their flag in the ground of some obscure and remote, and temporarily inhabited by natives, landmass on 23/12/15 and founded the AoIS. So that schizoid, antisocial, bored, young and old-and-senile leaders might coalesce in the green and now patchy crimson red lands to feel less burdened by their social cliques and/or lonesomeness. 

Nothing at all is expected of any member nation other than some small effort to coexist without too many arguments.
