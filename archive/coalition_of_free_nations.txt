The Coalition of Free Nations (CFN) is a political organization dedicated to nations who believe in life, liberty, and the pursuit of happiness, as well as the ideals that our members agree not to interfere in the internal affairs of other members except under extreme circumstances. The Coalition was founded on March 4th, 2012.
