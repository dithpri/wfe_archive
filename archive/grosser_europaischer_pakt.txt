The new-Greater European Pact (nGEUP) is a Treaty Organization funded by the nations of Tomeone and Olislelia after the collapse of the first GEUP.
The Collapse of the first GEUP which happened after a brief war between Tomeone and the funding state of Worilan has created a new non-neutral treaty which aims to control Southern Europe by eradicating the NATO Influence on the neighbouring states.
