This is a region focused on the creation of a decentralized union of free cities and states who want HONEST representation, and NOT overbearing, authoritarian governments.

A little about the leader; Free Houston -

After the long second Civil war ended in a horrible pyhrric victory for the South (1899), many states, including in the US, split to become independent. Whether it be for economic or political reasons, several states, cities, and regions seceded. 

One of these territories was the Free City of Houston (or simply known as Free Houston). It encompassed the Brazoria, Fort Bend, Harris County, Galveston County, and several surrounding areas.
