Galactic Nations is a region dedicated to Galaxy/Universe based nations that control more than one planet (or system), other types of nations are still able to join the region.

Supreme Chancellor x1: Almost complete authority within the region with some limits to power

****All below have control over polls and are appointed by the Supreme Chancellor****

Vice Chancellor x2: Appointed by the Supreme Chancellor they have some powers within the region, and share power in absence of the Supreme Chancellor.

Director of Communications x1: Has complete control over communication options.

Director of Border Control x1: Has complete control over Border Control options.

Minister of Relations x1: Has complete control over Embassies.
