Founded by the King of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, 
Ahmed Nawazi, in 1950, the Nawazi Kabul Institute, 
located on Kabul, is a social research organisation.

According to Nawazi, the goal of the NKI is -
- to increase the knowledge of humanity in society,
- to provide knowledge with truth and accuracy,
- to join in a construction of a modern future, and
- to work with honesty and loyalty.
