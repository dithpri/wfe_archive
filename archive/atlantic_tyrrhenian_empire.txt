The [b]Atlantic-Tyrrhenian Empire[/b], a militaristic regime seemingly born to counter another, larger empire: The Otakoku Empire. 

Commanded by [nation]Generalissimo Hakeem Majid-Fatih[/nation], the Atlantic-Tyrrhenian Empire prides itself on its military tradition, cultures, and strengths&#151;ranging from its territorial expanses, to their dominance over the skies, backed by a presence throughout the seven seas, now they are looking towards possibly going beyond.

[hr]
Although referred to as an empire, the term &quot;empire&quot; in the name is a misnomer; the Atlantic-Tyrrhenian Empire functions more as a confederation of states.
