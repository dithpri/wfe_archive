Breaking through the oppressive blanket of smog and ash a band of a miscreants and rabble rousers from every corner of the globe make their way out of the concrete jungle. Drinking from a fountain of knowledge on past and present international conflicts and resolutions they hope to form a global society that exceeds even the most idealist expectations. Shall this freedom bring their unique perspectives together to form a utopia or will the slightest taste of power lead to absolute corruption of even the youngest and most innocent?
