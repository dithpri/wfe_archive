[b]Wirbelia Continent[/b]
Welcome to the diverse and enchanting Wirbelia Continent, a vast landmass that captivates with its breathtaking landscapes and distinct subregions. This extraordinary continent is a haven of natural wonders, offering a rich tapestry of environments, from the towering Novelia Mountains in the center to the sun-drenched grasslands and arid deserts of South Wirbelia.

[b]Subregions:[/b]
1. Central - Novelian Wirbelia
2. South - South Wirbelia
3. Northwest - Limbolia
4. West - Karamia Archipelago
5. Southwest - Fesselia Archipelago
