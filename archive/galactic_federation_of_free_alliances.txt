The Galactic Federation of Free Alliances, also known as the Federal Galactic Republic, but most commonly the Galactic Alliance, was the galactic government directly following the New Republic. Incorporating most of the known galaxy, the Galactic Alliance was the first government to achieve this since the Galactic Empire. 

And this is where our region begins. In this region, players will select the planetary systems they wish to control, design their own interplanetary governments, build an army of droids, clones or even mercenaries! You, the leader of your piece of the Galaxy, will help to determine the fate of all the sentient beings of the Star Wars Universe!
