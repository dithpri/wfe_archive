The [b]Galactic Federation of Free Alliances[/b], also known as the Federal Galactic Republic, but popularly known as the Galactic Alliance, was a galactic government that emerged immediately after the New Republic, taking the planet of Coruscant as its capital for some time. Incorporating most of the known systems, the Galactic Alliance was the first government to achieve this since the Galactic Empire.
