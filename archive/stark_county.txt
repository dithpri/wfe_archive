[b]Stark County[/b]

Welcome to Stark County! Home to Ohio&#39;s richest natural wonders! Founded in 1808, organized in 1809, and named after Revolutionary War hero John Stark. Along with its impressive history, it contains many beautiful cities including Canton (also our county seat!), Alliance, Massillon, Canal Fulton, North Canton, and Louisville. It also includes many townships, villages, and unincorporated communities.
