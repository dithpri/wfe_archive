[i]Many ages ago, our galaxy was a battleground. Three great Empires spanned galaxies. The Cooperative of Galactic Peoples and its Senate ruled fairly, the League of Autonomous Systems was famed for its unique success of a unitary federal government that exceeded a single galaxy and the Gu-aj were an unrivaled paragon of science and industry, with their forge worlds creating great legions of machines and stars slowly vanished from sight as Dyson Spheres shackled stars to power their constructs. 
War was inevitable. And when it came, it showed no interest in giving in.[/i]
