[b]Cleveland[/b] is a city in the U.S. state of Ohio. The city proper has a population of 388,072, making Cleveland the 51st largest city in the United States, and the second-largest city in Ohio after Columbus.

The city was founded in 1796 near the mouth of the Cuyahoga River. --Wikipedia[hr]
