An Act for the Union of Bukshelf,Lesliiville,Copperpool,San Antonyo, Chester City, &amp; Noisch

And whereas such a Union would conduce to the Welfare of the colonies and promote the Interests of the Greater Empire of Adultii

Be it therefore enacted and declared by the King, by and with the Advice and Consent of the Lords Spiritual and Temporal, and Commons, in this present Parliament assembled.

By the grace of God, the King and the people of the Empire.

Amen
