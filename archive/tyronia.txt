Tyronia is a prosperous and cultured region, filled with people form all walks of life, be they black or while, Gay or Straight, struggling or prosperous.

The region promotes the unification of all nations through mutual trade, defence, and the betterment of life for its citizens and workers. Though Unity and co-operation, this region hopes to improves the lives of all within its borders and, in time, those out side of them.

There is a Bi-Weekly BBQ that is held to help strengthen bonds between nations within Tyronia, as well as to outside nations, and if any nations and leaders wish to join the event, please do not hesitate to join us!
