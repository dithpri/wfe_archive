Features and Overview:

The Faron Region is a large tropical jungle area in the far Southeastern part of Hyrule, to the East of the Lake region and South of Hateno. It is a jungle-like Region home to Lurelin Village and many beaches. 

In the heart of the region there are many ancient stone landmarks, Lots of Animals, Fruits, And Plants like Hearty Durians and Mighty Bananas.

Faron is comprised of dense jungle trees and high cliffs and peaks - and full of dangerous monsters including: Lizalfos, Moblins and Lynels on high peaks.
