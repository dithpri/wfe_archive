Faron is a large, industrialized nation, with four main regions. In East Faron, citizens enjoy extensive freedom and a beautiful, clean environment. In West Faron, citizens have many freedoms, and a semi-clean environment. In South Faron, citizens have few freedoms, and the environment is horribly polluted, South Faron in incredibly industrialized. In North Faron, citizens have little freedom, and a struggling economy.
