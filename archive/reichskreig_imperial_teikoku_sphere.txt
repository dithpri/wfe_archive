[b]The Reichskreig Imperial Teikoku Sphere[/b] is the dominant global and extraterrestrial alliance in an alternate history version of Earth and space in the year 2337. This hegemonic union is led by two superpowers, the [nation]Reichskreig[/nation] and the [nation]Imperial Teikoku[/nation], who jointly oversee a number of allied nations and regions across the planet and beyond. Together, they have established a new world order built on strict hierarchical governance, advanced technology, and expansive colonial ambition.
