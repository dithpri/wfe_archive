The [b]Taksoverse[/b] is a partially contained universe comprising of [nation]Takso[/nation] and other nations. It contains La Tero (Esperanto for &quot;[i]the Earth[/i]&quot;), a habitable planet where all known nations presently reside. La Tero has recently recovered from a global thermonuclear war after its civilizations experienced social disintegration following widespread anomie.
