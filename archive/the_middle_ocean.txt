The Middle Ocean is a wide and dynamic mass of water, home to several large islands. With a strategical location suitable for trade with large world powers to the left, right and south.
The majority of the population is human, but there is a variety of intelligent, sentient species, which tend to get along just fine, depending on the government.

If a nation desires to join the Middle Ocean, they can be added to the map as an island.
