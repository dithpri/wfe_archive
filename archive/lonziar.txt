The region of Lonziar was founded to accommodate any and all regions. 

This is an extremely new region, and so will benefit from new citizens. 
We try to listen to all that a citizen has to say, and make Lonziar better this way.
Being a new region, any and all people are welcome. However, you will be ejected if you are not civil, though it is possible to petition for return.

Join Lonziar to be part of a something new and, hopefully, with a great future!
