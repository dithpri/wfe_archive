The All Blue is a region where nations are free to run as they please. With increasing military strength, The All Blue is the safest place for new nations to star, or previous ones to re-stabilize themselves. If you choose to join us we would be happy to accept you into our region. If there would be any problems with anything please feel free to ask for help. We offer a free training program for all youth who want to follow any specific career path. With almost no unemployment rate, The All Blue is great for economic growth.
