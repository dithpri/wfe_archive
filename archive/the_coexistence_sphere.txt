The Co-Existence Sphere is a loose confederacy of multi-species nations working together to promote a better world. Despite this purpose of existence, the region is controlled by the Great Alpaca Empire, whose military is dominant in the region, and thus as much influence. Great scrutiny is taken when accepting new nations into the region to make sure that they follow the ideals of the region, being that they ideal promote positive interactions between nations, regardless of what happens inside of their own nation.
