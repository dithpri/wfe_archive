Overview:
The United Empires is a vast and diverse political region formed through the unification of several once-independent empires. Governed by a centralized council with representatives from each member state, it balances a delicate mix of shared governance and regional autonomy. Its motto, &quot;Unity Through Strength, Diversity Through Alliance,&quot; reflects its foundational principles.
Capital: Vjun
Official Languages: Imperial Common (primary), regional languages recognized
Government Type: Federal Imperial Union
Head of State: High Chancellor
