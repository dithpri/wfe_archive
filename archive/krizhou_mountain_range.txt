The Krizhou Mountain Range is a young ocean range that surfaced relatively recently and with it came a number of elongated islands varying from just under single acre to just over a hundred million acres in area. This unique isolated mountain range is home to a unique and undisturbed array of flora and fauna. Undisturbed that is until it was discovered and with it a rare species of hare that was thought to be myth, the Jackalope. This discovery has yielded a wealth of other discoveries and with it came the settling of many of these islands resulting in an aggressive land grab by many powerful countries.
