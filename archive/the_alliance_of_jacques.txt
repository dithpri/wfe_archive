The Alliance of Jacques was founded in 1865 by [nation]bushsucks-istan[/nation] after the signing of the Beesel-is-kut treaty.

The Alliance of Jacques exists for two specific reasons: The elimination of [url=http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Beesel_%28municipality%29]Beesel[/url] and the complete destruction of [url=http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lidl]Lidl[/url]. Chairman Geert Wilders said [i]&amp;#39;&amp;#39;Beesel and Lidl must be eliminated for the future of our people. Their cheapness and stupidity have terrorized our nations for too long!&amp;#39;&amp;#39;[/i]
