[color=black][b]The German Empire ([i]Deutsches Kaiserreich[/i]) was a state comprising all of modern Germany, Alsace-Lorraine, the area around K&ouml;nigsberg (Kaliningrad) and Posen (Poznan), Silesia, East Prussia and northern Schleswig. It existed from 1871 until 1918.[/color]

[color=red]The Empire was run by an Emperor, usually from Prussia, and was formed after the unification of German states in the Franco-Prussian War. The Empire had overseas colonies in Africa, notably Namibia and Cameroon.[/color]

[color=orange]The Empire joined the First World War in 1914 and attacked Belgium and France, was dissolved after the abdication of Kaiser Wilhelm II on 9 Nov, 1918, and written out of existence at Versailles in 1919.[/color][hr][color=black][region]The Union of German Nations[/region] welcomes you to the German Empire![/color][/b]
