The United Nations Space Command, more commonly known as the UNSC, is the military, exploratory, and scientific agency of the Unified Earth Government. 
The UNSC was created by our Late Founder, the Celtic Confederation. From there we have grown to include 18 nations, and are led by our WA Delegate, [nation=short]The Free Peoples Imperial Democracy[/nation].
