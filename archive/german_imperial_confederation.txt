The German Imperial Confederation is a collection of nations connected economically, militarily, and politically. It has lasted for many years in different forms but its current one is the strongest that it has ever been. The aim of the Confederation is to promote German interests in this world and eventually beyond.
Any and all nations are welcome as long as that nation&#39;s government accepts resigning some sovereignty to the greater good .

We are in the Endseig in the struggle for superiority among the nations of the world.
