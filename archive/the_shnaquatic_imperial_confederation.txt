The Shnaquatic Confederation is a confederation built up of Empires and Republics (sometimes Imperial Republics) who strive to build the greatest NationStates in the name of Shnaqua, our Lord and Saviour. So, if you wish to join our quest to create the greatest Empire in the world (The Shnaquatic Empire), join our region for greatness and salvation by the grace of God and Shnaqua.
