[hr][color=navy]The Alaskan Independence Party is a political party and independence movement in the U.S. state of Alaska that advocates an in-state referendum which includes the option of Alaska becoming an independent country. The party also supports gun rights, privatization, home schooling, and limited government.[/color][/b]
[hr][b][color=navy]The party also advocates positions similar to those of the Constitution Party, Republican Party and Libertarian Party.[/color][/b]
