[b]Verona[/b] is a city on the Adige river in Veneto, Italy, with approximately 265,000 inhabitants and one of the seven provincial capitals of the region. It is the second largest city municipality in the region and the third largest in northeast Italy. The metropolitan area of Verona covers an area of 550.58 sq mi and has a population of 714,274 inhabitants.

Three of Shakespeare&#39;s plays are set in Verona: [i]Romeo and Juliet[/i], [i]The Two Gentlemen of Verona[/i], and [i]The Taming of the Shrew[/i]. &#151;Wikipedia[hr]
