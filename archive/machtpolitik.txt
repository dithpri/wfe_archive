Machtpolitik is an alternate history roleplaying region set in the 20th Century. Take control of a country and rule through the years, with a system endowed with unparalleled freedom, depth, and historical accuracy. Carve out an empire through the combination of soft-spoken diplomacy, cunning schemes, and military prowess. Carefully guide your nationstate through expansion and colonization to the position as a dominant global empire.
