[b]Welcome to Machtpolitik![/b]

Region founded: 31 January 2018

Point of Divergence: 3 November 2020

Machtpolitik is an alternate history roleplaying region set in a post-apocalyptic United States. Take control of a country and rule it with a system endowed with unparalleled freedom, depth, and historical accuracy. Carve out an empire through the combination of high-stakes diplomacy, cunning schemes, and military prowess. Carefully guide your nationstate through expansion and colonization to the position as a dominant global empire.

Current Date: 2050
