The [b][region]Winter Islands[/region][/b] contains nations of hard-working, friendly and self-governing people led by their respective charismatic, enlightened and benevolent leaders. 

Nations are required to:
 * Be responsible for their own affairs and not nation-build and not meddle into the affairs of other nations in the region or other region.
 * Be responsible for actively maintaining their nation and deciding on all issues and maintaining active population and economic growth
 * Believe in staunch free-market economic principles that are aimed at growing the economic and private sector while avoiding growing the public sector
 * Believe that each nation in the Winter Islands are responsible for their own social and civil responsibilities
