This is a Region closed off from the others. Due to this, the inhabitants have not evolved to modern day and futuristic technology. They still have Swords, Bombs, gunpowder Guns, etc. This Region also holds fantastical creatures. Two being Dragons and Demons.

Medieval Realm Triumvirate: Ketonek, Torresiskstan, Passacal
