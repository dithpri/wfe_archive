Far into the future, The Mule almost brought undone the most ambitious project the Galaxy had ever known. More than that; It was designed in the hopes that they never know, for it depended on it.

Hari Seldon, instigator of the two foundations to bring about the Second Empire without the need for 30,000 years of barbarism, was a Psycho-historian of unparalleled skills whom was able to predict the machinations of large groups of humans spread across the Galaxy.

Gaia S is the star that Gaia circumnavigates, and the place where deviation from the Plan first took shape due to the decisions of an unlikely galactic player Travise of Terminus.
