The S.C.P Foundation is tasked with the containment of anomalous entities of varying degrees of lethality and danger. All activity is classified to civilians. The S.C.P Foundation contains these entities for research or for extermination to protect humanity. 

The Official SCP Foundation is broken up into many different sites and areas, and most communicate and co-operate for maximum success.

Secure. Contain. Protect
