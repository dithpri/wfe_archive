Theopolemia is an alternate future timeline in which the progress of humanity is halted by the growing movements for religious fundamentalism, and in this timeline, a worldwide series of wars&#151;the Theopolemic Wars&#151;are waged by the increasingly numerous religious fundamentalists against the increasingly secular remainder of society. This widening gap in ideology and social progressiveness between liberals and conservatives, making extinct the middle-ground moderates&#151;very much like the dichotic division of society into the rich and poor, destroying the middle class&#151;thus turns out to be the fatal flaw in human society. It is too late to stop the process; the war has already begun.
