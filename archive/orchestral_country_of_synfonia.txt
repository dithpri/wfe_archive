The [region]Orchestral Country of Synfonia[/region] is a small island located in the Eastern Bermuda Triangle. The population consists of two types of people: the Northerners; who are among the best instrumentalists in the world, and the Southerners; common folk who fell to Synfonia after their plane crash landed there. The Empress of Synfonia: Empress Pravada keeps her population growing by shooting down planes in the Triangle, so they crash land into her island. The [region]Orchestral Country of Synfonia[/region] is run by Empress Pravada, the Cruel Dictator who runs [nation]Flutists[/nation]
