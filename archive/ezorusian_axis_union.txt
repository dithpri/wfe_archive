The EzorusIan Axis Union, known unofficially as the Axis, is an economically efficient union dedicated to one specific continental goal: To unite all Ezorusians under the glorious, godly flag of the United Socialist States of Triningrad.

Premier Anther Corgen, the All Excellent Overseer of the Triningradi communist party, wishes to achieve a one-nation format over the continent of Ezorus, no matter what obstacles interfere with this plan.
