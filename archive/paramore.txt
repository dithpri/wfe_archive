[b][color=red]Paramore is an American rock band from Franklin, Tennessee, formed in 2004.[/color] [color=orange]The band currently consists of lead vocalist Hayley Williams, guitarist Taylor York and drummer Zac Farro.[/color] [color=gold]Williams and Farro are founding members of the group, while York, [/color][color=green]a high school friend of the original lineup, joined in 2007.[/color] [color=blue]Williams is the only member listed on the band&#39;s recording contract [/color][color=purple]with Warner Music Group&#39;s Fueled by Ramen sublabel and the only member to appear on all five of Paramore&#39;s studio albums.[/color]
