[b]A wonderful place, where everything is delightful. There are soft green meadows, lovely groves, a delicious life-giving air, sunlight that glows a soft purple, and everyone is happy and peaceful. The sound of music played on pipes and lyres fills the air and banquets occur whenever the inhabitants desire. It is a place where the great and good live after their deaths; a dwelling place of mortals made immortal through the favor of the Gods.

[i]And in the doubtful war, before He won the Latian realm, and built the destin&#39;d town; His banish&#39;d gods restor&#39;d to rites divine, and settled sure succession in His line, from whence the race of Alban fathers come, and the long glories of majestic Rome[/i][/b]
