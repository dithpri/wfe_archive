[b][region]Elysian Fields[/region] (Odyssey iv. 564) 

Those chosen by the gods, the righteous, and the heroic, where they would remain after death, to live a blessed and happy life, and indulging in whatever employment they had enjoyed in life. The Elysian Fields are located on the western edge of the Earth by the stream of Oceanus, the ocean-stream at the margin of the habitable world
