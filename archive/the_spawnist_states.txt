[b]The Spawnist States[/b] ([b]SS[/b]), also known as [b]The Spawnist States of Amarah and Welten[/b] ([b]SSAW[/b]), is an transnational military alliance consisting of the countries [nation]Amarah[/nation] and [nation]Welten[/nation]. The SS blends political cooperation with religious solidarity, functioning both as a faith-driven alliance and as a platform for the member states to coordinate policies.
