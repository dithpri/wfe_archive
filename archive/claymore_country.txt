&#39;Claymore Country&#39; is the name given to the land that a group of clans once resided, these clans were renowned for using Claymores in battle, a symbol of physical strength and prowess, and a link to their historic way of life.

These infamous clans developed into nations and are still holding the historic alliances that were formed many centuries ago.

Recently, more developed and modern nations have formed near the borders of &#39;Claymore Country&#39;, one of these nations has offered to represent the region on the international stage and has been accepted into the regional government as the &#39;International Delegate&#39;.
