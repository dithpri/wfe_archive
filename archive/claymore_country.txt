&#39;Claymore Country&#39; is the name given to the land that a group of clans once resided, these clans developed into nations and are still holding the historic alliance that was formed many centuries ago. A new more developed nation that has currently formed near the borders of &#39;Claymore Country&#39; has offered to represent the region on the international stage and has been accepted into the regional government as the &#39;International Delegate&#39;.
