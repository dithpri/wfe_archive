The Star Alliance Is A Group Of Countries In Asia, Africa, Europe And North America That Share Common Interests In Economic And Social Development, Nurturing Of Civic And Political Freedoms And Democratic Values.

The Star Alliance Consists Of A Parliament, Which Is Elected By The People Of The Union. It Is Present In The Island Of Malta, Which Is Sovereign Territory Of The Star Alliance. The Parliament Is Headed By A Chairman, And The Star Alliance Has A Head, Who Is Basically The Leader Of One Member State, Rotating Every Six Months.
