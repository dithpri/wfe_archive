The main reason for the creation of the I.P.U., is for intelligence sharing, economic, migration and security purposes.

The founding of this union, will also help with the further development of each nations defence forces, so that we are prepared for any future attack, whether from outside or inside the I.P.U. .

Furthermore, the founding of the I.P.U. will help to continue the economic prosperity of every member, due to the I.P.U Customs Union
