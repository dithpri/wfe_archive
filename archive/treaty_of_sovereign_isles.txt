[b][region]Treaty of Sovereign Isles[/region][/b] is made of small-scale nations located entirely on a single isle or an archipelago of islands. 

The treaty encourages all members to mutual, prosperous cooperation in matters of trade, defense, technology advancement and etc., while respecting and promoting the members&#39; respective sovereignty.

It is an isolationist region, meant for small, non-influental island-nations that keep their focus on their own inner affairs.
