[b][color=crimson]The Kingdom of Bahrain[/b][/color] 

[i]is a small island country situated near the western shores of the Persian Gulf in the Middle East.[/i][hr]

[i]In the late 1800s, following successive treaties with the British, Bahrain became a protectorate of the United Kingdom. In 1971, Bahrain declared independence. Formerly a state, Bahrain was declared a Kingdom in 2002.[/i][hr]
