Resolution No_8761

Owing to the reestablishment of the State Union of Serbia and Montenegro as the Federal Republic of Yugoslavia.

Recognising that the Federal Republic of Yugoslavia has been recognised under the name &amp;quot;The Federal Republic of Yugoslavia&amp;quot; wherein international organs.

Stipulating the resolve of the Yugoslav Government to reestablishment relations under the name of &amp;quot;The Federal Republic of Yugoslavia&amp;quot;.

Hereby abolishes the defunct national organ under the name Serbia and Montenegro (Federal Republic of Yugoslavia)
