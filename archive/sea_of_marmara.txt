The [b]Sea of Marmara[/b] is the inland sea, entirely within the borders of Turkey, that connects the Black Sea to the Aegean Sea, thus separating Turkey&#39;s Asian and European parts. The Bosphorus strait connects it to the Black Sea and the Dardanelles strait to the Aegean. &#151;Wikipedia[hr]
