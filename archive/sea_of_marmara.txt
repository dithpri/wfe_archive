The [b]Sea of Marmara[/b] is a small inland sea entirely within the borders of Turkey. It links the [region]Black Sea[/region] and the Aegean Sea via the Bosporus and [region]Dardanelles[/region] straits, separating Turkey&#39;s European and Asian sides. It has an area of 4,380 sq mi, and its dimensions are 174 mi &times; 50 mi. Its greatest depth is 4,490 ft.
