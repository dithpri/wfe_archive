A grouping of nations seeking to unite the world under a solitary constitution and Utopian society. The rules  -
Rules:
1. All states are effectively neutral as consequence of joining the UNOG
2. RP is encouraged
3. States of all kinds are allowed to join, exception is paid to those who make inflammatory remarks toward others, and people considered hateful in general
4. As a security measure, no one aside from the creator, St. Garcian, is allowed onto Regional Control.
5. If a war is to be started between nations, diplomatic efforts must be exhausted before the consideration of conflict.
6. Have fun
