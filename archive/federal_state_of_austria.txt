[b]The [region]Federal State of Austria[/region] is a Republic composed of nine federal provinces bordering the Czech Republic, Hungary, Italy, Germany, Liechtenstein, Slovakia, Slovenia, and Switzerland. 

The provinces are Burgenland, Carinthia, Lower Austria, Upper Austria, Salzburg, Styria, Tyrol, Vorarlberg and Vienna. The federal principle means that the legislative and executive powers are shared by the federal and provincial governments. Its federal nature is one of the principles laid down in the Constitution and can only be changed by referendum. 

[/b]
