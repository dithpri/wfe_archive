The ICNA is a burgeoning post-apocalyptic picture of the North American continent. The  global revolt against the New World Order and the global monetary system was a pyrrhic victory. The inhabitants of North America  were fractured and almost lost. A surge of new nations are formed after a succession of global and continental wars, famines, and plagues kill hundreds of millions of people.  
Individual freedoms are coveted fiercely by the survivors of decades of war and fear for profit experienced under the old debt slavery system. The remaining inhabitants of the  region and their descendants strive for peace and true purpose in life.  Will they prosper or be denied?
