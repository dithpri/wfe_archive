The ICNA is a burgeoning post-apocalyptic picture of the North American continent. The fall of the global monetary system and the ensuing turmoil has left the countries of North America, fractured and almost lost.  The newly realigned continent emerges after &quot;The Deletion&quot; in which, thousands of corrupt politicians and conspiring members of the largest banking organizations in the world were taken to trial, sentenced and routinely, executed. 
Individual freedoms are coveted by survivors of the previous regime and the decades of war experienced under the old system. Usury is strictly banned in the region and several solid, debt-free currencies are present. Corporations are mostly outlawed and political discourse is heavily encouraged.
