The ICNA is a burgeoning post-apocalyptic picture of the North American continent. The fall of the global monetary system and the ensuing turmoil has left the countries of North America, fractured and almost lost.  A surge of new nations are formed after a succession of global and intercontinental wars in which hundreds of millions of people died. 
Individual freedoms are coveted by survivors of the previous New World Order and the decades of war and fear experienced under the old debt slavery system. Political discourse is heavily encouraged. Individual liberties are coveted. Governments in most of the newly formed nations are closely scrutinized.
