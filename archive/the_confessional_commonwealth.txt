[i]&quot;Knowing this first, that no prophecy of the scripture is of any private interpretation.&quot;[/i] 2 Peter 1:20

A region for Christians of various denominations, who subscribe to a defined confession of faith (I.e. the Augsburg Confession, 1689 London Baptist Confession of Faith, Westminster Confession, etc.) If we are to be good Bereans, we have to have a clear standard of belief, that can be weighed against the Word, God&#39;s standard. Iron sharpens iron, so we gather here for dialogue and edification.

Members are required to subscribe to the three ecumenical creeds: Apostles&#39;, Nicene, and Athanasian.
