[region]Guangzhou[/region], traditionally romanised as [b]Canton[/b], is the capital and most populous city of the province of Guangdong in southern China. Located on the Pearl River about 75 miles north-northwest of Hong Kong and 90 miles north of Macau, Guangzhou has a history of over 2,200 years and was a major terminus of the maritime [region]Silk Road[/region] and continues to serve as a major port and transportation hub today. &#151;Wikipedia[hr]
