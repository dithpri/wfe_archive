    For those unaware, it is a common misconception that [i]communism[/i] and [i]socialism[/i] are the same thing. Here are short definitions to illustrate the difference:

[u]Socialism[/u]: To each according to contribution.

[u]Communism[/u]: To each according to need.

[i]The long definition of socialism is[/i]:
From each according to his ability, to each according to his contribution. Emphasis on profit being distributed among the society or workforce to complement individual wages/salaries.
