In the dark year 2020, The all-knowing and puissant leader of The Pestilent State of Pazuzu decided that His work in that world was done. With His supreme knowledge and power, our Divine Leader relocated our land to this place. 

He led us Puzuzuans to Purgatorio in the Southern Hemisphere of the Alighierian Ocean, where we shall remain undisturbed evermore. Joined only by those nations drifted here from the depths of the Inferno. Only those chosen remain. The rest are sent back to the circle from whence they came..
