The [color=red]Eurasican Union (EU)[/color] is an intergovernmental organization formed to promote worldwide co-operation. The organization is funded by assessed and voluntary contributions from its member states. Its objectives include maintaining international peace and security, promoting human rights, fostering social and economic development, protecting the environment, and providing humanitarian aid in cases of famine, natural disaster, and armed conflict.
