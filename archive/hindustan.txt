[b]Hindustan[/b] (Hindustani: [b]&amp;#1729;&amp;#1606;&amp;#1583;&amp;#1608;&amp;#1587;&amp;#1578;&amp;#1575;&amp;#1606;[/b] or [b]&amp;#2361;&amp;#2367;&amp;#2306;&amp;#2342;&amp;#2369;&amp;#2360;&amp;#2381;&amp;#2340;&amp;#2366;&amp;#2344;[/b]) is a common geographic term for the northern/northwestern Indian subcontinent. The terms [i]Hind[/i] and [i]Hindust&amp;#257;n[/i] were current in Persian and Arabic at the time of the 11th century Turkic conquests.
