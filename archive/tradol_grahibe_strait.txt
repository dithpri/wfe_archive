A large island trailed by several smaller islands, all of which lie near the equator and none of which are currently named. The largest island is dominated by desert in the interior, with the outer rim of the island containing the most liveable land. The smaller islands are almost entirely tropical jungle, although enterprising groups could carve nations out of it if they so desired...

The Sydrokhian capital lies near the center of the largest island&#39;s desert, built almost on top of a large oasis. Sydrokh has no neighbors.
