The U.N.E.A. is a country of 16 Districts covering a vast swath of North America, with Alaska in the extreme Northwest and Hawaii extending the nation&#146;s presence into the Pacific Ocean. Major cities include New Albion City, a global finance and culture center, and Centropolis, the capital, both on the Atlantic Coast; Los Apostoles, famed for filmmaking, on the Pacific Coast; and the Midwestern metropolis Great Aurora City.
