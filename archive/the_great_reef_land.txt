The Great Reef Land is a beautiful expanse of Water, Earth, Wind, and Fire. Its inhabitants are kind, loving, compassionate people. The Great Reef Land is compossed of countrys with major ideological diffrences, such as liberal havens, conservative wonderlands, capitalist centrals, communist strongholds, theological monarchies, and any other type of government you can imagine. Very few conflicts occur within the region, with opposing viewpoints living in balance with eachother.
