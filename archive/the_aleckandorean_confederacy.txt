[color=#BF0000][b][i]The Aleckandorean Confederacy[/i][/b][/color] is an ever-expanding multinational conglomerate currently run as a nonpartisan consociational democratic superstate. It was Founded on the 30th of May in the year 1992 (0 RCA; Year Zero - Revolutionary Calendar Aleckandor) after when 15 year-old escaped slave, thinker, and partisan fighter [i]Aleck Ieu[/i] majestically inspired the grey-skinned Nord Yervhennian Thralls along with Exiled Maeflowuhran Nations and other allies to take arms against the racist and oppressive Lentinean Ethnocratic Collective in their homeland and establish their new nation of freedpeoples. Today, it has prospered and spread across many realms and many continents over oceans innumerable.
