The SCS refers informally to an area starting roughly 200 kilometres north of the Colombian Coast, up to the territorial waters of Jamaica, Haiti and the Dominican Republic. The SCS stretches no further West than Nicaragua and no further East than Aruba.

It is home to the United Tropical Republics, The Federal Republic of Kerala and the Sadan Federation. The UTR is the oldest country present, founded in 1701. Kerala and Sada followed later. The history of the SCS is as violent as it is successful, many of the world&amp;#39;s major conflicts have involved these three countries in some fashion.
