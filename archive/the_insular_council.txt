For more detailed information, see the factbooks of [url=http://www.nationstates.net/nation=england_proper]Engliand[/url]: [url=http://www.nationstates.net/nation=england_proper/detail=factbook/id=84352]Evolution[/url] and  [url=http://www.nationstates.net/nation=england_proper/detail=factbook/id=84522]Naming dispute[/url].

The regional flag consists of the St Alban&amp;#39;s Cross (a yellow saltire on a blue field) symbolising Great Britain or Albion and the green background symbolsing Celtic people.
