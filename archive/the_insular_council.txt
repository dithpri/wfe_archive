The Insular Council is the (fictional) successor of the [url=http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/British%E2%80%93Irish_Council]British-Irish Council[/url] after (fictional) breakup of the United Kingdom, the (fictional) semi-abolition of monarchy and the (fictional) creation of the [url=http://www.nationstates.net/nation=windsor_and_buckingham]Royal State of Windsor and Buckingham[/url]. For more detailed information, see the factbooks of [url=http://www.nationstates.net/nation=england_proper]Engliand[/url]: [url=http://www.nationstates.net/nation=england_proper/detail=factbook/id=84352]Evolution[/url] and  [url=http://www.nationstates.net/nation=england_proper/detail=factbook/id=84522]Naming dispute[/url].

The Regional Flag consists of the St. Alban&amp;#39;s Cross (a yellow saltire on a blue field) symbolising Great Britain or Albion and the green background symbolsing Celtic nations.
