It is the world&#39;s largest lunch table, each seat holding entire nations.

Parthone and Varith are fierce rivals, their multiple wars have nearly obliterated the table.

The Great Table War of 2377 caused mass calamity across the nations on the table, by the end, only Varith and Parthone were left, set in a cold war.  Slowly, small nations have started forming in the ruins of the old ones.

Militant groups from both nations tend to clash on the border, therefore most border towns are abandoned. Both nations have made seperate efforts to quell this, but it seems to be futile.
