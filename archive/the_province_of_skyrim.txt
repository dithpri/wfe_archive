
Skyrim, also known as the Old Kingdom, Throat of the World, the Fatherland or Keizaal, is a vast region set in the northern part of Tamriel.It is home to the Nords, large and hardy men and women who have a strong resistance to frost, both natural and magical.Skyrim was originally inhabited by a race of Mer known as the Snow Elves, though after the Atmoran-Snow Elf War, the Snow Elves were destroyed and the Atmorans settled the land.It is bordered by Morrowind to the east, the Nibenay and Colovia in Cyrodiil to the south, Hammerfell to the southwest, and High Rock to the west. The island of Solstheim lies to the northeast of Skyrim. Skyrim is a glorious part of [url=https://www.nationstates.net/region=the_tamrielic_empire]The Empire[/url].
