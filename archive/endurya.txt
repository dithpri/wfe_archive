Endurya is a Modern Tech region dedicated to the continued art of realistic roleplay in an international setting. Events will unfold that will forever change the balance of power throughout the region, by nations that will either bask in the glory of their good decisions, or meet demise at the hands of their mistakes. Nations rise, nations fall, but Endurya stands.
