Welcome to Endurya!

Endurya is home to Humans, Elves, Dwarves, and all other manner of race set in a Modern Tech realm. Magic, once all-powerful and deeply respected, has diminished across the world and now considered myth by many. A world of dragons, fortresses and mystery has been replaced with the airplane, metropolises and politics. Swords and shields, arrows and bolts have been replaced by bullets and Kevlar, missiles and drones. 

Republics own the day as the Kingdoms and Empires of old are a shadow of their former selves.
