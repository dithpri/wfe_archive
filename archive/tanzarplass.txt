Tanzarplass is the name of the land and countries which border the Tanzar Strait in Eastern Scandinavia. The main two countries which border this strait are the peaceful nation of FSORO Bronchadia and the military-fuelled dictatorship of Whakycaro. These two nations have had some conflict in the past. Notably, during the 2019 Tanzar war. The strait is 15km across and the climate is usually colder than other countries in the same region.
