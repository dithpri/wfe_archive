[b]The Merilian League ([i]A Liga Merilian[/i]) is a dualistic state and union of the Federation of Kalti ([i]Federa&ccedil;&atilde;o de Kalti[/i]) and the Federation of Andalucia ([i]Federa&ccedil;&atilde;o de Andalucia[/i]). Governance of the Merilian League ([i]Liga Merilian[/i]) is vested in the Ministry of the League ([i]Minist&eacute;rio da Liga[/i]), the Council of the People ([i]Conselho do Povo[/i]) and the Merilian High Court ([i]Tribunal Superior de Merilian[/i]). Governance of the Federation of Kalti ([i]Federa&ccedil;&atilde;o de Kalti[/i]) and the Federation of Andalucia ([i]Federa&ccedil;&atilde;o de Andalucia[/i]) is vested in the Federal Council ([i]Conselho Federal[/i]), the Parliament of Kalti ([i]Parlamento de Kalti[/i])/Parliament of Andalucia ([i]Parlamento de Andalucia[/i]) and the Court of Kalti ([i]Tribunal da Kalti[/i])/Court of Andalucia ([i]Tribunal da Andalucia[/i]).[/b]
