The Northern Nations is a region containing The United Kingdom of the Northernlands, Gamdie, The Untied City of Gemstones, Eastern Uppland, Kongehus of Norge, and The United Kingdom and Great Britain. The Northern Nations&#39; National Day is May 21. 

The United Kingdom of the Northernlands is the Founder of the Northern Nations. The UKN is a Constitutional Monarchy and also gives power to its provinces. The UKN&#39;s national day is December 20th.
