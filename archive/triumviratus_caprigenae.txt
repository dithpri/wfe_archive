&amp;lt;b&amp;gt;The Triumviratus Caprigenae&amp;lt;/b&amp;gt; is a historical and cultural region consisting of several nation states unified by a common ideology; scientia potestas, &quot;knowledge is power&quot;. The name of the region is Latin for Triumvirate of Goats, and refers to the leaders of its founding nation.

The region, which was founded in anno MMXVII by The Incorporated States of Capriaria, is lead by a regional assembly consisting of The Matriarchy of Nuwewereld (WA Delegate), The Dominion of Triboria (Conscriptor), The Dictatorship of Unamens (Custos Telluris), and The Kingdom of Citardt (Proxenos).
