[b][region]The Triumviratus Caprigenae[/region][/b] is a historical and cultural region consisting of several nation states unified by a common ideology; [i]scientia potestas[/i], &quot;knowledge is power&quot;. The name of the region is Latin for Triumvirate of Goats, and refers to the leaders of its founding nation.

The region, which was founded in the year [i]MMXVII[/i] by [nation]Capriaria[/nation], is led by a regional assembly consisting of [nation]Nuwewereld[/nation] (WA Delegate), [nation]Triboria[/nation] (Conscriptor), [nation]Unamens[/nation] (Custos Telluris), and [nation]Citardt[/nation] (Proxenos).
