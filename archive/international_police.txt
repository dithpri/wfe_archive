The International Police is a cooperative organization designed to ensure global law enforcement across national borders. It comprises a network of law enforcement agencies from different countries committed to combating transnational crime, terrorism, and safeguarding human rights. The International Police maintains a significant presence in different parts of the world through its regional offices and partnerships with other law enforcement agencies. Its activities range from providing support to member units to conducting joint operations and training programs. The International Police is dedicated to fostering international cooperation and collaboration in the fight against crime and establishing peace and security globally.
