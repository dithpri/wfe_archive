The [i][b]IsoSphere[/i][/b] is an artificially created subspace sanctuary. The [i][b]IsoSphere[/i][/b] was designed to shelter an entire civilization. It is controled by [i][b][nation=noflag]The-Ecumene[/nation][/i][/b]. The [i][b]IsoSphere[/i][/b] is basically a tiny universe with its own isolated space-time-reality. Thus it is almost impossible to get access to the IsoSphere.
