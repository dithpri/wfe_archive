The Heignian Government has been in power since 15 April. The current monarch HRH King Yu Hen I has been ruling since 15 April after former Kings Edmund Eng Yu Tong I, Chen Lei I and Chen Guanzhou I were overthrown and executed by unanimous decision of the Heignian Parliament and mutineers. On 17 April the Head Governor changed the header of the country as well as the national motto as part of concessions offered by the mutineers
