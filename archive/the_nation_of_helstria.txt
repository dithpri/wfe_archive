After Bigtopian sailor Maximus Barry sailed his ship, he encountered an unmarked island. The island was full of natives, who the Bigtopians proceeded to slaughter, in order to set up a territory. In 2016, the World Assembly successfully allowed the island to separate from the Bigtopian government and declare itself a new nation: Helstria.

For the purposes of this region, all nations will be classified as states.
