[i][b]Context :[/b]

Arthemis is a large archipelago, made up of many islands with idyllic landscapes, located at the crossroads of civilizations. It is a unique place where a multitude of cultures meet. Here we find a large number of ethnic groups, religions or even styles of government. Despite the diversity, the States do the best they can to maintain good relations between each other, although tensions may arise, the region calls for a common diplomacy to best settle the conflicts.[/i]
