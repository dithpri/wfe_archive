[color=green][b]Zimbabwe[/b][/color]
[color=red]on Friday April 18th,1980 the colony of Southern Rhodesia gained its independence from Great Britain under its African name Zimbabwe after almost 16 continuous years of civil war. at around noon the union jack was hoisted downward from the flagstaff of Cecil square and was replaced with the pan African colors of Zimbabwe thus marking the end of an era.[/color]

[color=green]Capital City:Harare
Government type:Parliamentary Democracy[/color]
