THE NATIONS OF EUROPE
The participating Nations will be called upon to participate in international conferences, I will mention a few:
-G8
-G7
-Parallel Nations
-Rights Impassable
-Regional wars (only In case of war)
-Solidarity Policies
In some Conventions will be chosen States based on these characteristics: Economic Soundness, social policy, Human Rights respected, national economy: High level.
International Organizations will be created for national security, the names and the structuring of such organizations will be Decided along with the countries with Polls to which all nations are asked to respond.
