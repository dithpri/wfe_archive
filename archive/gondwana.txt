Gondwana has the most diverse environment on the Earth. It incorporates a diverse array of nations. Our region is emerging on the world stage and prooving itself to be one of the best regions to ever exist. Gondwana is home to the most notable nation in the universe, The Kingdom of Mixim which is centrally located in the region. Many nations are at war with the rest of the world. Our nations must come together in the fight so Gondwana can reclaim its rightful place as the worlds one and only superpower.
