The Armed Empire comprised the dominions, colonies, protectorates, mandates, and other territories ruled or administered by the Armed Power and its predecessor states. It began with the overseas possessions and trading posts established by Northland in the late 5th and early 8th centuries, and colonisation attempts by Gotaria, and Southland during the 17th century. At its height in the 11th and early 20th centuries, it became the largest empire in history and, for a century, was the foremost global power.
