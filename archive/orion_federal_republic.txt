&quot;Humanity rebuilt among the stars.&quot;

The Orion Federal Republic (OFR) is an interstellar governing body established in the aftermath of the Third World War, formed to oversee the restoration of Earth and coordinate the expansion of humanity beyond the Solar System.

Through the unification of surviving terrestrial powers and the rapid advancement of deep-space infrastructure, the OFR now administers colonial operations, industrial development, scientific initiatives, and military oversight across multiple inhabited systems.

While Earth remains the symbolic heart of mankind, the frontier has become essential to its survival.
