Eurosia is a region or a small continwnt that is located on palanet Earth. It is a place where dictators, democrats, communists and many others may find a way to rise to power.

To survive in this world you must use anything that you have to ensure security and happiness for your people while caring what other nations would think about your decsions. However in Eurosia you focus completly on yourself and country. Rule your nation in your own way.

In conclusion, Eurosia is a perfect place to any nation that looks for a starting region. Here you may focus on developing your nation without need to worrying about standarts in this region.
