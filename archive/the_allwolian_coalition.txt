[region]The Allwolian Coalition[/region]
We are a powerful coalition dedicated to the protection of the environment and the welfare of our citizens. We seceded from the Pacific to create a new region that better suited our ideals. The Allwolian Coalition was proposed by [nation]Monature[/nation], originally intending to fight off the attack of the Demons, but when the war ended nobody felt like leaving. Most likely, they just liked having powerful allies to defend against attacks.
