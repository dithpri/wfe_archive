Antarctica is one of the seven continents of the earth. It is located in the southernmost part of the earth and is surrounded by the Southern Ocean, including the Belings Gojin Sea, the Ross Sea, the Amundsen Sea and the Widel Sea. Antarctica is composed of continents, land edge ice and islands, with a total area of about 14.245 million square kilometers, of which the mainland area is 12.293 million square kilometers, the land edge ice area is 1.582 million square kilometers, and the island area is 76,000 square kilometers.
