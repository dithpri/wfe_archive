A region with a dramatic geography that blends high plateaus, tropical archipelagos, and fertile valleys, boasts nations distinguished by their history, political organization, and role in global politics.

The region is itself a vast and ancient continental mass stretching from the Western Ocean (in the west) to the Myriad Sea (in the east), crossing scorching deserts, mountainous plateaus, fertile valleys, and tropical archipelagos. It was here that the oldest civilizations, legendary trade routes, and enduring ideologies were born.
