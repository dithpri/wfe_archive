Norland has long been a land of magic. Each nation has formed with its own form of magic, around which they pride themselves and build their identity. But in recent years, the world has begun to adopt industry and steam as a new means to become great. The sky starts to fill with steam, and the hearts of nations slowly become hard as iron. Norland is entering a new era, one not ruled by the pulsing of magic and hearts but by the steady rhythm of machines and steam.
