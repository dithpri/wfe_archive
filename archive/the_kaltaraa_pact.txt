The Kaltaraa Pact were once a great alliance of powerful nations united against others. Since then, it has fallen into disrepair, its neighbors slowly encroaching on them, their influence slowly dropping. Now, as the World Assembly has been created, they are trying to rebuild, rearm, and grow back their old alliance. For they have lived, they live, and they will live. Despite their ideological differences, they stick together. For the Kaltaraa Pact!
