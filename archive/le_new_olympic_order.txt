[color=yellow][u]Le New Olympic Order[/u] (LNOO) is a region formed from breakaway nations of the now-crumbling union of CSAT.[/color]

[color=red]We believe in the power and cooperation of our militaries to defend our homes and destroy our enemies. Our belief in Militarism encourages that each country build a stable military/defense force to contribute to any war efforts should a war start.[/color]

Our status in terms of politics: [b][color=green]INDIFFERENT[/color][/b]

[color=black]Our country is governed by an oligarchy, a council of all the great leaders in Le New Olympic Order.[/color]
[color=blue]While our country is not primarily democratic, we take great steps to make sure a decision is not made by one nation alone.[/color]
