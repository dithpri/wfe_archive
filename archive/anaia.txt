Anaia is an active roleplaying region which welcomes nations of any background - no matter how exotic - to our friendly, diverse community. What makes us special is our presence in [b][url=https://forum.nationstates.net/viewforum.php?f=7]NS Sports[/url][/b], with Anaians winning more than 30 titles across the history of the forum. We also have regional championships in soccer, cricket, and rugby league, and a thriving domestic sports scene. If this is the sort of thing that interests you, feel free to come and join in...
