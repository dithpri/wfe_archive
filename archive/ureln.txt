The Union of Renewable Energy and Liberated Nations (URELN) is a supranational politico-economic organisation of nations committed to the adoption and transition to renewable energy and the liberation of all nations from the horrors of war and colonialism. In line with our policy of isolationism and self-sufficiency, embassy requests will not be accepted.
