Although the exact origin of the continent&#146;s name is unknown, there are multiple different theories of how it came about.

One possible origin for the name is the Pothriki language, where the continent is called &#147;H&ouml;lunnttrik&#148;, litterally meaning &#147;Land of the river H&ouml;l&#148;, referring to a river in the continent&#146;s north that nearly splits its in two. In Pothriki, the continent&#39;s name was often shortened to H&ouml;lunn (lit. &quot;of H&ouml;l&quot;), becoming the traveler&#39;s tongue &quot;Halinn&quot;. The Name &quot;Halinn&quot; was originally proposed by PickleOnAStick, then the leader of Pothric.
