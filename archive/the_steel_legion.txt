[b]Overview[/b]
[hr]The Steele Legion is a general RP region with no preferences on ideology. Our government operates according to a balance of power between the six regional officers and founder. All members are welcome as long as they abide by the rules and are willing to take part in the warfare mechanics present in the region if necessary.
