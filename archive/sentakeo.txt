Sentakeo is a fictional empire that spans across the mainland of &quot;Sentorai&quot;, after the collapse of the Taghali Empire before it, and before that, the global empire of Leikada. 

It is bordered by the Sirogontan Kingdom to the north, Tanhad to the west, and the Republic of Rova to the south, alongside the Corrite States.
