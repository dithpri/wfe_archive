The Volker Union is a democratic union of nations who share values, ideas and moral principles. 

The union&#39;s core values include, but are not limited to:

- The upholding of world peace, and the avoidance of conflict and collateral damage wherever possible.
- The belief in equal opportunity and a &#39;People First&#39; initiative.
- The values of free trade, open borders, and an international ethical code.
