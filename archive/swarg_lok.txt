
Svarga Loka is a set of heavenly worlds located on and above Mt. Meru where the righteous live in paradise 

&quot;O&amp;#7747; tad vi&amp;#7779;&amp;#7751;o&amp;#7717; paramam padam sad&amp;#257; pa&amp;#347;yanti s&amp;#363;raya&amp;#7717;&quot;

&quot;All the suras (i.e., devas- divinities) look toward the feet of Lord Vishnu as the Supreme Abode&quot;

The capital of Svarga is Amaravati and its entrance is guarded by Airavata. Svarga is presided over by Indra, the leader of the devas.
