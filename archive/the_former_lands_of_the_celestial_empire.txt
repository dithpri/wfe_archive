After the collapse of the Celestial Empire, turmoil descended throughout the region. Afters years of war and devastation two nations arose from the ashes, in the north the staunchly conservative and religious Holy Empire of the Low Mountains (HELM) and in the south radically left wing United Socialist States of Pistocleria (USSP).

The two nations now stand allied despite their opposing ideologies, amongst the backdrop of former imperial glory to pursue regional desires on the world stage.
