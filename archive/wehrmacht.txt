[b][nation=short]Yad Mordechai[/nation][/b] (Hebrew for &amp;quot;Memorial of Mordechai&amp;quot;) is named in honor of Polish Jew and partisan fighter [b][url=http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mordechai_Anielewicz]Mordechai Anielewicz[/url][/b] (1920-1943), leader of [b][url=http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/%C5%BBydowska_Organizacja_Bojowa]Zydowska Organizacja Bojowa[/url][/b] (English for &amp;quot;Jewish Combat Organization&amp;quot;), which led the [b][url=http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Warsaw_Ghetto_Uprising]Warsaw Ghetto Uprising[/url][/b] of 1943, where he died fighting the enemies of all humankind.

We have named the nation that conquered the Nazi military region here on NS in his honor.
