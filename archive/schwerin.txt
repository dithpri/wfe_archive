[region]Schwerin[/region] is the capital and second-largest city of the northern German state of [region]Mecklenburg Western Pomerania[/region]. Schwerin was first mentioned in 1018 and was granted city rights in 1160, thus it is the oldest city of [region]Mecklenburg Western Pomerania[/region]. The city suffered only minor damage in World War II.
