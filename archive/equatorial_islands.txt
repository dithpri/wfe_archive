[color=aquamarine]The [b]Equatorial Islands[/b] are a chain of formerly-uninhabited atolls and islets located in the middle of the Pacific Ocean. [/color]

[color=redred]After years of disputed claims between imperialist countries and island nations, the remaining people have begun their own independent settlements on these islands.[/color]

[color=0099bf][b]Equatorial Islands[/b] was founded by unknown forces in January of 2004.[/color]

[color=0069bf]On December 7, 2015, nation 29 of the [b][region]Benevolent Empire[/region][/b] became the fourth founder of this region.[/color]
