[color=yellowgreen]The [b]Equatorial Islands[/b] are a chain of formerly-uninhabited atolls and islets located in the middle of the Pacific Ocean. [/color]

[color=redred]After years of disputed claims between imperialist countries and island nations, the remaining people have begun their own independent settlements on these islands.[/color]

[color=yellowgreen]These Islands are a protectorate of the [b]Benevolent Empire[/b].[/color]
