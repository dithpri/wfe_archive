This Region is for Educational Purposes and is a Class region.

The Region is a service to High School students, many of which will be voting for your President in the next general election.

It has come to my attention (MrRollins) that most young voters vote either with or against their parents based on their relationship with their parents rather than actually thinking about the issues.  This is our small way of helping them learn what the term &quot;Unintended Consequences&quot; means.

I have been using this resource for 10+ years.
