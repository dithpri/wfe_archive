[b]Panem[/b] is a nation that was established during an unknown time period in a post-apocalyptic world. It is situated primarily on the western side of North America, and The Capitol is located in an area formerly known as the Rocky Mountains. It is composed of 12 Districts.

[b]Panem Government[/b]
President: Coriolanus Snow
Board of Governors: Presidentially appointed
