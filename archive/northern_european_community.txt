[b][u]Overview[/b][/u]:
[b]The Northern European Community is a region stretching from the Alps in the south to the Barents Sea in the north and reaches from Northwest Russia in the east all the way to the North Atlantic Ocean. The NEC works to promote cooperation between states located within its region of influence, any nation may apply for membership with little to no prerequisites. States in the region have a wide variety of governments and ideologies.[/b]
