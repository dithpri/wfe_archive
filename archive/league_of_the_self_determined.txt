All peoples of every nation, welcome to the League of Self-Determined. A region that encourages international trade, Diverse Political Discourse, Multiculturalism and international cooperation. We do not have one political leaning rather our cause is to house all the peoples and ideas this vast earth may bear.

Our region allows the smallest, newly found nations to find security and room to grow while offering larger nations the ability to find new allies and operate free of restrictions imposed by other regions.
