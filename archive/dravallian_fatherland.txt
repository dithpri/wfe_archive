A region devoted to education, personal freedom, and voluntary expantionism. In other words do as you will, but know the consequences of your deeds shall be fairly leveled upon your head by the hand of the people. Infringe upon or otherwise garnering unfair advantages over your fellow citizens will result in swift action on orders from the Dravallian Fatherland. While the Founder has ultimate say in the end it uses a gentle hand that only acts on those who infringe on the freedoms of others. Summed up simply one is under the rule of the Founder and is for all considered free to do as one wishes entering and leaving the Fatherland as they wish.
