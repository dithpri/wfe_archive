[i][color=navy]~ One Vision, One Identity, One Community ~[/i]

A geo-political &amp; economic organization of countries located in Southeast Asia, its aims include the acceleration of economic growth, social progress, cultural development among its members, the protection of the peace &amp; stability of the region, and to provide opportunities for member countries to discuss differences peacefully.[/color]
