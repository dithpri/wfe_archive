[b][color=navy]&#149; [/color][color=red]Association of Southeast Asian Nations[/color][color=navy] &#149;[/color][/b]

ASEAN is a geo-political and economic organization of countries located in Southeast Asia. Its aims include the acceleration of economic growth, social progress, cultural development among its members, the protection of the peace and stability of the region, and to provide opportunities for member countries to discuss differences peacefully.[hr]
