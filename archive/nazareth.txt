&amp;#39;[b]Nazareth[/b] is the largest city in the North District of Israel. Nazareth is 30% Christian. In the New Testament, the city is described as the childhood home of Jesus, and as such is a center of Christian pilgrimage, with many shrines commemorating biblical events.&amp;#39; ~[b]Wikipedia[/b].

Along with Bethlehem, Nazareth is one of the main locations pertaining to the life of Jesus, and is the root of the name [I]Jesus of Nazareth[/I].
