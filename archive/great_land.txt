[b]Charter of Great Land[/b]

This Charter Is Mandatory For All Member Nation To Obey:

1. Region Officers must be respected 
2. Great Land is a democratic region and will always be a democratic region
3. All nation have rights in voting

[b]How To Be An Officers[/b]

Write A Telegram To Current President Of Great Land To Be A Candidate If The President Conducting A New Officer Poll. The Telegram Must Have Following Text:

1. Greetings
2. Why You Should Became The Officer
3. Swore Loyalty And Obedience to the Charter by writing(I will obey to the Charter and shall follow every rules enacted)

After sending the telegram and approved, you must campaigning to gain supporters before election begin
