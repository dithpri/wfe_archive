Dragonia Re is a peaceful, diverse with all kinds of wildlife, and beautiful region where civil rights are respected. The main populace is the dragon. Sentient dragons built magnificent cities to house themselves, while wild dragons live in the caves, mountains, and forests of the region. Both type do not harm humans, although sentient dragons, more commonly called Dragonians, find the humans vulnerabilities to greed and corruption disgusting and ban human politicians from the dragons grand cities, especially Dragonia Re Xzua, the region&amp;#39;s capital.
