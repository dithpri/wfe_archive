The OCWGU is based on the cultural, economic and political organization between all member nations. 

The OCWGU nations are spread around the World and occupy all continents. 

The OCWGU includes all major religions, languages, ethnicities, and other culture traits. 

It includes a hybrid of all major governments, representing different peoples and their nations. 

The population varies from nation to nation.

Member nations of the OCWGU have adopted a variety of economic systems.
