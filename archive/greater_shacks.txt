[b]Welcome to the official page of the nations of the [region]Greater Shacks[/region][/b]. The Greater Shacks is a region consisting of free nations who share a common linguistic and cultural heritage, including [nation]Mustard Shack[/nation], [nation]Ketchup Shack[/nation], [nation]Relish Shack[/nation], [nation]Barbecue Shack[/nation] and [nation]Mayonnaise Shack[/nation]. Together, the Greater and Lesser Shacks make up the broader area known as the Shacks. The [b]Shantytown of Greater Shacks[/b] governs the region and encourages cooperation between nations. It serves as one chamber within the [b]Condimental Congress[/b].
