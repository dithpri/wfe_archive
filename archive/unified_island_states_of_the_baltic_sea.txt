The UISBS is the main uniting body of microstates scattered across the Baltic Sea, ranging from the tip of Denmark to the banks of Russia and everywhere in-between.

The Unified Island States of the Baltic Sea is mostly a slightly-leftist body, recommending the practice of mild government intervention and slightly-regulated economies as a precaution against corporate control and market crashes, unruly local parties and control-hungry foreigeners. 

Peoples of the outside world and their respective systems are welcome to worm their way in or reach out, but radicals beware! Those with their far-right agenda and corporate-run govermnents are to see themselves out and away. The people come first, after all!
