[region]Newcastle[/region], formally known as [region]Newcastle upon Tyne[/region], is a city in the northeast of England within the [region]Empire of Britain[/region]. It serves as a major cultural, commercial, and educational hub, known for its historic bridges over the River Tyne, vibrant nightlife, and prominent universities.
