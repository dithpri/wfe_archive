The Keweenaw is a quaint region of nations that tends to prioritize environment, health, and education above defense, business subsidies, and mass regulations. Drug laws are virtually nonexistent in The Keweenaw and compassion is rampant. Although the economies are not the strongest in the world, nations in this region have some of the highest evaluations of civil rights and political freedom around. Around The Keweenaw you will find lush mountains and prideful citizens working for the betterment of each other. The regional capital city is Houghton.
