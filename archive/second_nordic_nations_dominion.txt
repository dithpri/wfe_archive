[b][u]Second Nordic Nations Dominion Pact[/b][/u]

[b][u]Article 1 :[/b][/u]
The four first members and Vice-Delegates are Groenmark, Northern Terra Nullia and Skodinavia. The Groenmark is the Founder and the Guardian of Executive rights.

[b][u]Article 2 :[/b][/u]
The WA Delegate is chosen democratically amongst the members of the Dominion.

[b][u]Article 3 :[/b][/u]
Giving Endorsement to any other member of the Dominion than Groenmark is forbidden, and worth banishing.
