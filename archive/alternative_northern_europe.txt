[b]NECC[/b] Northern European Control Council

[i]The NECC dictates regional legislation in the [region]Alternative Northern Europe[/region] region. 
All resolutions are to be approved by the council. 

[b]RSIS[/b] Regional Shared Intelligence Service

RSIS functions as a check-and-balance to NECC sanctions. Embargo&#146;s or any other type of NECC approved international intervention may only be enacted if the RSIS issues a negative security report on the nations involved. 
Inquire with the RSIS Chairman for regional security reports.[/i]

[b]ISaC[/b] International Security and Communication

[i]ISaC is responsible for communication between the nations in the region. ISaC makes sure nations comply with communication regulations in the diplomatic channels.[/i]
