West Yarra is a growing Region, located west of east Yarra and north of north fillip. 
It is divided between 2 main factions, the democratic alliance, and the Non-Democratic Coaltion. However the factions are makeshift; with some Democratic nations joining the Non-Democratic Coalition and vice versa. Often due to presser, there own interests or just opportunism. 

At the Moment the Non-democratic coalition holds power with Wannon at its helm.
