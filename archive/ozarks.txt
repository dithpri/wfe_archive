The [b]Ozarks[/b], also referred to as the Ozark Mountains and Ozark Plateau, is a physiographic region in the U.S. states of Arkansas, Missouri, Oklahoma, and Kansas. The Ozarks cover a significant portion of northern Arkansas and southern Missouri, extending from Interstate 40 in Arkansas to the suburbs of St. Louis. A portion of the Ozarks extends into northeastern Oklahoma and southeastern Kansas.

The Ozarks cover nearly 47,000 square miles, making it the most extensive highland region between the Appalachians and Rockies. &#151;Wikipedia[hr]
