Welcome to The Kingdom of Jerusalem

The Kingdom of Jerusalem is a Christian state within the Holy Land during the time period commonly known as the Crusades and now the Modern time period. The Government is a Federal Monarchy where the King is the head of state and government and Ruler of the Kingdom and have power over the Kingdom. The Federal Prime Minister is the Deputy head of state and government and he/she have ceremonial powers over the kingdom. The Haute Cour is the Legislature of the Kingdom, all nations in this region are the members of Haute Cour.
