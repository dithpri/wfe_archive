 King of Jerusalem: The Latin Kingdom of Jerusalem

Federal Prime Minister: Principality of Antioch

Haute Cour: Everyone 

The Kingdom of Jerusalem is a Christian state in the Holy Land during the time period known as the Crusade. The Government is a Federal Monarchy where the King is the head of state and government and Ruler of the Kingdom and have power over the Kingdom and the member of this region have power over their nation but must be loyal to the monarch. The Federal Prime Minister is the Deputy head of state. The Haute Cour is the Legislature of the Kingdom, all nations in this region are the members of Haute Cour.
