The &quot;Southern Titanic Sea&quot; is a continent located in the south of the Atlantic Ocean that was discovered by Germany-Georgia. The &quot;Southern Titanic Sea&quot; has a Christian-oriented population and follows Christian traditions. Currently, the Southern Titanic Sea is not governed by a democratically elected government but by a politician from Germany and Georgia. There are plans to introduce a Titanic Sea Parliament that will be democratically elected.
