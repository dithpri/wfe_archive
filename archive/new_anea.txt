GEOGRAPHY:
A large continent, with a few scattered islands, for size comparison it is a bit larger than Russia. The west coast is slightly mountainous and has a moderately sized desert. Moving into the central area, it has more plains, it is often prone to tornados. However in the South of the Central plains area there are some moderately sized mountains. Moving West there are more hills, with a large tundra on a large island North of the East Coast of Anea. there is another very large island that was at one point attached to Anea that is mostly jungles. The East Coast is prone to earthquakes, and the West coast gets a seasonal Monsoon.
