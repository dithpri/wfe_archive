Factbook on The Indian Federation

It comprises multiple states and union territories, sharing borders with Pakistan, China, Nepal, Bhutan, Bangladesh, and Myanmar. The region experiences varied climates, from tropical in the south to temperate in the north. Major languages include Hindi, English, and Bengali; major religions include Hinduism, Islam, and Christianity.
