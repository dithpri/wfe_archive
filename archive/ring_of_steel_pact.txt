The Ring of steel pact is created to simulate our group of friends in control of some of the greatest empires in history. Each power begins with a basic civilization and can expand on the [url=http://s17.postimg.org/eucs1lwu7/ring_of_steel_pact.jpg]Regional map[/url], using the [url=https://forum.nationstates.net/viewtopic.php?ns=1&amp;f=5&amp;t=371363]Roleplay[/url] or [url=http://www.supremacy1914.com/play.php?uid=5453924&amp;gameID=1441526]Supremacy 1914[/url] as a means of conducting war and diplomacy. All nations will be graded on [url=http://s17.postimg.org/cpji65gsv/NS_militaries.jpg]military capability scale[/url], using several attributes to create a basis for your military.
