The Ring of steel pact is created to simulate our group of friends in control of some of the greatest empires in history. Each power begins with a basic civilization and can expand on the [url=http://i.imgur.com/drHHwRb.jpg?1]Regional map[/url], using the [url=https://forum.nationstates.net/viewtopic.php?ns=1&amp;f=5&amp;t=371363]Roleplay[/url] as a means of conducting war and diplomacy. All nations will be graded on military capability scale, using several attributes to create a basis for your military.
