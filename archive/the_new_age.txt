[i]The New Age[/i] is an RP region that seeks to be unique by driving interesting geopolitical and character narratives through cooperative writing and worldbuilding. 

Set in a post-apocalyptic world of arising civilization salvaged from the vestiges of the Information Age, a new generation establishes their cultures and struggles for domination over the continent. How will this new age be shaped?
