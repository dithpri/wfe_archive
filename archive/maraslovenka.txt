Maraslovenka consists of a chain of thousands of volcanic islands situated to the north of Yogoslovina.
Maraslovenka is home to four nations, all of which are absolute monarchies, they are as follows: 
Mataniya, the largest of all nations which consists of 300 Islands, Domzeeza, the second largest and the most southerly, Cretokia, renowned for it&amp;#39;s many geysers, Vosona a peaceful monarchy and Zerenbiya, the smallest nation which is situated within Mainland-Mataniya.
