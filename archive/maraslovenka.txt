Maraslovenka consists of a chain of thousands of volcanic islands situated to the north of Yogoslovina.
Maraslovenka is home to five nations and a colony, all of which are allies, they are as follows: 
Spruceland, the largest of all nations which consists of 300 Islands, Domzeeza, the second largest and the most southerly, Cretokia, renowned for it&amp;#39;s many geysers, Shirah Mataniya and Zerenbiya, the smallest nation. 
Shirah Mataniya maintains control of the Matanese colony of Alambiya in the far north.
