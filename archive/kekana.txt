Kekana is large region within former Siberia and northern Asia, This region is heavily forested with many resources to be discovered. The region has suffered wars, rebellions, and many plagues. Caution is advised when colonizing this region due to terrorist and former Metra rebels occupying unclaimed land in the region. The region is also known for its ruins of cities from The Metra Civil Wars.
