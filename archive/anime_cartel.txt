The [b][region]Anime Cartel[/region][/b] was a criminal organization that smuggled anime-related items into the Mushroom Kingdom during the time of the Anime Crisis. It became the main antagonistic faction of the second half of [url=https://supermarioglitchy4.fandom.com/wiki/The_Anime_Arc][b]the Anime Arc[/b][/url], as its members abducted cartoonist Axol, stealing Inkweaver to use to create an army of duplicate characters to do their bidding. Francis was the leader before his death.
