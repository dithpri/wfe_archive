[b]Monarchist Union of Nations[/b]

The Monarchist Union of Nations is a collective of nations that cherishes the timeless principles of monarchy, stability, and the enduring traditions that come with it. Here, we celebrate the richness of monarchical heritage and promote unity among like-minded nations.
