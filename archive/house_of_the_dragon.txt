House Targaryen of Dragonstone is a noble family of Valyrian descent who once ruled the Seven Kingdoms of Westeros. The Targaryen sigil is a three-headed dragon breathing flames, red on black.The house words are &quot;Fire and Blood&quot;. 

They resided for more than a century at Dragonstone castle on Dragonstone island, until [nation]King Aegon Targaryen the Conqueror[/nation] and his sister-wives, [nation]Queen Visenya Targaryen[/nation] and [nation]Queen Rhaenys Targaryen[/nation], began the first of the Wars of Conquest in 2 BC. Cummulating in uniting the seven kingdoms under [region]The Iron Throne[/region]
