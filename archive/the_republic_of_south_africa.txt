South Africa, officially the Republic of South Africa (RSA), is the southernmost country in Africa. It is bounded on the south by 2,798 kilometres (1,739 mi) of coastline of Southern Africa stretching along the South Atlantic and Indian Oceans, on the north by the neighbouring countries of Southwest Africa, Bechuanaland and Southern Rhodesia, and on the east and northeast by Portuguese East Africa and Swaziland, and surrounding Basutoland.
