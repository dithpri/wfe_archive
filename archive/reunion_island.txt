R&eacute;union is a French island located in the Indian Ocean, east of Madagascar, about 175 kilometres (109 mi) southwest of Mauritius, the nearest island.

Administratively, R&eacute;union is one of the overseas departments of France. Like the other overseas departments, R&eacute;union is also one of the 27 regions of France and an integral part of the kingdom with the same status as those situated on the European mainland.
