A nation that is apart of the North Pacific Coalition has sworn to defend republic and democratic nations in the region, all under the common goal of promoting freedom of trade, freedom of navigation, and self-governance.
All members of the NPC must financially contribute a minimum of 3% overall GDP.
All members of the NPC must remain friendly/neutral to member nations, and allow it&#39;s majority of military facilities to be shared between member nations in the region during wartime.
All members of the NPC are obliged to respond to any request for assistance by other nations within the region, in the event of military or humanitarian crisis.
All members of the NPC must align with the core values of the region, promoting democracy and freedom.
