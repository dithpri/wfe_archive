The Imperial Coalition is a international League of Nations founded for the preservation of world order and economic development. The Coalition has an elected leader that is chosen by the Council of the Realm, which has 9 elected representatives and and one permanently seat, that of [nation]Michaelandrium[/nation]. Once every 3 months and election is held. The Leader of the Coalition is responsible for Approving submitted bills, however if there is a unanimous vote by the Council, the President can be overruled. In addition if an elected official oversteps their authority if there is an 8/10ths majority in the council, the President of the Coalition can be impeached and an emergency election will be held.
