[b]Luzon[/b] is the largest and most populous island in the Philippines and the 15th largest in the world. Located in the northern region of the archipelago, it is the economic and political center of the nation, being home to the country&#39;s capital city, Manila, as well as Quezon City, the country&#39;s most populous. 

With a population of 52.99 million as of 2015, Luzon is the fourth most populous island in the world. &#151;Wikipedia[hr]
