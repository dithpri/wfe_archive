Glanton is a hot planet with only a single continent and a world sea. It is a sparsely populated, backwater world on the periphery of several major intergalactic powers. The planet itself is divided among several local powers who maintain an uneasy semi-peace. Mineral rich, Glanton&#39;s primary export is raw materials and processed ore.

Glanton&#39;s quiet days are rapidly coming to an end. The planet is between two competing interstellar states, who both view the planet and its resources as a strategic asset. Raider clans have moved into the local area. Others are encroaching with their own interests. The delicate balance of power on the planet is threatened...
