The idea of an Asian Union Confederation was raised at the First International Conference of Asian Political Parties by the then deputy magistrate or the mayor of The Protectorate of Ireeduis , on behalf of his leader, the then His Excellency The Magistrate of Ireeduis. It was suggested that Asia as a continent should have its own forum to discuss Asia-wide cooperation. Afterwards, the idea of the AUC was formally put forward during the Special International Cooperation Annual Meeting of the Foreign Ministers in the City State of Ireeduis, and at the ASIAN Foreign Ministers Retreat.
