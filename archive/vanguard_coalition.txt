The Vanguard Coalition is a formidable alliance of extremely powerful nation-states, united by strength, strategy, and shared purpose. Each member holds significant influence and military might, combining their resources to maintain dominance and security in the region. Their cooperation is rooted in mutual respect and the understanding that together, they are far stronger than any single nation alone. The Coalition stands as an unbreakable force, ready to confront any threat with unwavering resolve.
