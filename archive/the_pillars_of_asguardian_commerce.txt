This region was founded by a panel of strong nations tired of the way the Earth had become. By amassing their considerable wealth, knowledge of science and magics, as well as power and influence, they managed to create a new continent wherein they could dwell. The ingenious founder of the new region was given authority over internal matters, including daily rule and immigration. Control of trade, war, religion, and outside influences such as WA representation was given to the Holy Imperial Empire of Reaganistanbul as they were the strongest nation and best equipped to handle these matters. In aspects of daily life, member nations were allowed self rule as long as the laws of the Pillars of Asguardian Commerce were followed.
