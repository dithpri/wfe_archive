The [b][color=mediumblue]World Archive[/color][/b] is the lobby and holding region for our roleplay story. Players that do not have land claims within our regions, non-nation accounts (RP media accounts, private organizations, etc.), and fans watching our story as it progress, can call this region home. If you wish to claim land in one of our regions, please check the appropriate land claims page to see which islands are available.
