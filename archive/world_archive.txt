The [b][color=blue]World Archive[/color][/b] is the lobby and holding region for the AU roleplay story. Players that do not have land claims within our regions, non-nation accounts (RP media accounts, private organizations, etc.), and fans watching our story as it progress, can call this region home.

If you wish to claim land in one of our regions, please check the land claims page(s) to see which countries are available.
