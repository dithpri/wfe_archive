The [b][color=mediumblue]World Archive[/color][/b] is the lobby and holding region for the American Union (AU) and American Independent States (AIS) roleplay regions. Players that do not have land claims within the AU or AIS, non-nation accounts (RP media accounts, private organizations, etc.), and fans watching our story, are welcome to call this region home. If you wish to claim land in the AU or AIS, please check the regional island nations pages to explore your options.
