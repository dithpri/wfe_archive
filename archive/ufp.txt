[color=blue]The United Federation of Planets[/color] (abbreviated as UFP and commonly referred to as the Federation) is an interstellar federal republic, composed of planetary governments that agree to exist semi-autonomously under a single central government based on the principles of universal liberty, rights, and equality, and to share their knowledge and resources in peaceful cooperation and space exploration.
