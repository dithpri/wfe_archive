[color=green][b]Welcome to the AIA![/color][/b]   Airport strives to be a connection point for all nations.

The Airportean international airport (AIA) was completed on November 15th 2020. It is planned out in one large terminal removing the need of a airport tram. It is built to handle the 100 million daily Airportean flyers with an astonishing 8 runways. It is located in the core of Aero, the capital of Airport. It is connected to the Airport Spaceport (AAS) which handles cargo from space.

[color=green] Ecological Problems[/color]:
In Airport, all planes must use at least 30% biofuel. Airports #1 airline, Air-Era has a fleet of short-haul electric planes and 50% biofuel planes for long-haul flights.
