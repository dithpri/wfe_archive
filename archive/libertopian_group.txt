Created in 2013, the United Federation of Nations is an assembly of nations coming together for the common good, in research, exploration of ideas and most of all, freedom.     

There are strict guidelines for entry.    

1.  Your citizens must have a high level of freedom.  
2.  Your nation must not have any civil unrest.
3.  Your nation must not be warlike.

Most of all your nation must not be a democracy, military dictatorship, or monarchy as these are forms of tyranny.
