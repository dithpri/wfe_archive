Welcome to [b]Greater Ixnay[/b], a modern-tech geopolitical role-playing community! Greater Ixnay is a region that has been around almost as long as NationStates itself! Its nations are new and old, and either type is welcome! We seek to foster a lively role-playing community where all nations are heard, so telegram [nation]Heku[/nation] if you wish to join us!
