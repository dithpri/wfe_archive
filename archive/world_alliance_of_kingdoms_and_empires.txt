The World Alliance of Kingdoms and Empires (WAKE) consists of strong, independent kingdoms, empires, and nations who are not afraid of taking control by force. Anything can be accomplished alone, but WAKE is unstoppable when together.

Those who are a part of WAKE can trust that they are a part of not one nation, but one collection of nations; one that will survive the test of time and go forth into the world as a powerful ally and a dangerous enemy.
