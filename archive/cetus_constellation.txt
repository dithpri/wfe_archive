[i]Cetus: is a constellation, sometimes called &#39;the whale&#39; in English. The Cetus was a sea monster in Greek mythology which both Perseus and Heracles needed to slay. Cetus is in the region of the sky that contains other water-related constellations: Aquarius, Pisces and Eridanus.[/i][b]Founded by the andarian starfleet on: 04/05/2025.[/b]
