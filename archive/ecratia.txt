The nation of Ecratia believes in the right to self determination and civil liberties, we welcome any honourable nation that believes the same. All who join the nation are free to come and go as they please and will have full control over their own affairs. Ecratia simply offers an alliance of goodwill and peace. Any nation which joins and acts in a manner which is deemed unlawful or disrespectful shall be warned or removed from the nation as per their crime.
