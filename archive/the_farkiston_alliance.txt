The Farkiston Alliance was founded in Year 1 of the modern era of Farkistonkland, amidst a time of political unrest and social change. The kingdom was facing challenges such as economic disparity, regional conflicts, and a growing desire for democratic reforms. A group of visionary leaders, including members of the royal family and influential citizens, came together to form the Alliance, aiming to unite the nation under a common purpose of prosperity and justice.
