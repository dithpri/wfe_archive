[b][color=red]Welcome to The All India Forward Bloc![/color][/b]

[color=red]The [b]All India Forward Bloc (AIFB)[/b] is a left-wing Indian nationalist organisation inspired by the ideals of Indian patriot, Netaji Subhash Chandra Bose. The AIFB is dedicated to the causes of Indian Nationalism, Anti-Imperialism, Socialism and Anti-Communalism.

The AIFB aims to defend the scientific and democratic tenets of socialism and it&#39;s appropriate application in the concrete Indian conditions. The AIFB also believes in preserving and respecting Indian culture, traditions and values and instill patriotism in Indian citizens.[/color]
