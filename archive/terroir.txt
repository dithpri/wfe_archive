Welcome to Terroir!

The nations of this region are considered developing democracies, with some political freedoms afforded to denizens. Economies in the region tend to be stronger than average. The median tax rate of the region is 28%, but is frequently higher for the wealthy. The economies of nations in the region tend to be weighted more towards the public sector, with on average only 49% of the economy comprising the private sector. Commerce within the region is an irrelevant annoyance to governments.

Whilst social inequality is rife amongst the nations within Terroir. The environments of the nations in the region are almost universally appalling.
