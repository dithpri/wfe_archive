In the year 2029 a new, incurable disease, created in a lab experiment, spread throughout the world like a wildfire, killing almost all people who stood in its path. After 3 years, new civilizations are beginning to spring up out of surviving populations. It is only a matter of time to see if this new Earth will succeed or fail.
