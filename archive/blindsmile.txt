Based on the webcomic Blindsmile, about 8 furry teens trying to escape Ashimi Boarding School, which is actually an illegal facility where the characters are being tested and experimented on because of their powers. The 8 main characters are:
- Jazzmine the Bunny
- Flora the Wolf
- Fauna the Wolf
- Marianne the Deer
- Crystal the Cat
- Kevin the Rat
- Fiona the Leopard
- Harper the Fox
