For thousands of years, the islands of [region]Haida Gwaii[/region] have been inhabited by the Haida, an indigenous people to whom art, culture, legends, mythology, and community played an important role in their lives. Argillite carvings, totem poles and ancient Haida villages attract visitors from around the world.

This incredible area of British Columbia was formally called [b]Queen Charlotte Islands[/b] until the Haida Gwaii Reconciliation Act officially named the islands Haida Gwaii, which means &quot;Islands of the People.&quot; &#151;[url=http://www.bcadventure.com/adventure/explore/north/cities/queencha.htm][color=990000]bcadventure.com[/color][/url][hr]
