The ESR is a region in the heartland of Europe. The founding nation is the Federal Republic of Karlopetrus. The currency is the peso.  The official language is Spanish. Many official documents are also published in English for the sake of international communication. The nation is officially secular, however approximately 89% of the population is Roman Catholic. Following the reforms of 6 February 2021, the Labor Party of Karlopetrus is banned. There is currently an interim government, whose primary goal is the writing of a new constitution. The leader of the government is Mr. Gunther Tosny.
