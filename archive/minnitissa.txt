The continent of Minnitissa has been ravaged by a terrible war between old empires and new creeds, tolling in the deaths of nearly a third of the population. Authority and control has slipped away from crowns, kings and emperors, leaving the lands razed and lawless.

Until now. From the ashes of the old Acrueal Empire, the discarded Jashi Federation and the other super states, fledling nations have propped up, eking out new power structures and ordering the sundered continent another time.
