Panoa is a region near The Great Britain And Eire. It is mainly made of its two capital cities, the tiny Republic Of Pangoa Islands and the Genoa Islands.
In 1999, with the formation of Panoa, a treaty was signed between nations of this region:

1. All nations are REQUIRED to share the same currency: The Genoan Dollar

2. All settlements in this region is unlimited, and nations are free to come and go. (With the exception of the capital cities)

All nations of Panoa are required to follow these rules.
