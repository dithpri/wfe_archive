Xalka is a small region located near the Drythi Ocean. It includes the countries surrrounded by the Kru Thark Mountains. The largest country in the region is Xyron. The region has a chilly climate, with the summer averaging around 45-60 degrees Farenheit and the winter usually aroung 25-40 degrees Farenheit. Xalka has around 25 inches of precipitation annually, although some parts of it recieve more downfall then others. Xalka is mainly flat land, but on its borders is very hilly and even mountainous.
