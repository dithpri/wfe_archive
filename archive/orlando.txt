Orlando is a city in the U.S. state of Florida and the county seat of Orange County. Located in Central Florida, it is the center of the Orlando metropolitan area, which had a population of 2,387,138, according to U.S. Census Bureau figures released in March 2016, making it the 24th-largest metropolitan area in the United States. 

Orlando is known as &quot;The Theme Park Capital of the World,&quot; and in 2014 its tourist attractions and events drew more than 62 million visitors.
