Formed by the Emperor of the Federal Triumvirate of Xing, Marethyu I, the Imperial Houses of Abzan are built on the strong principles of trust, honor, and righteousness. Relying on heavy military power, supreme technological knowledge, and nearly infinite funding to maintain law and order within the region, the Imperial Houses of Abzan are not something to be trifled with and will casually decimate any threat that dare raises a finger against them.
