Region for temporary storage of puppets and combi-nations of [region]The World Rearranged[/region] and [region]New Europe[/region].
For those indefinite times when they are not on the map, or not yet by a long time.

Nations outside of The World Rearranged and New Europe may store puppets too - the door is always open.

This may also, or may not, become the place for interregion polls.
