The [b]TransAtlantic Union[/b] (TAU) is an economic and political union of democratic member states that are located within the Atlantic Region.

The TAU&amp;#39;s capital is located in the city of [b]Annaburg[/b], within [b][nation]San Cordula[/nation][/b]. (See [url=http://www.nationstates.net/nation=san_cordula/detail=factbook/id=180314]San Cordula&amp;#39;s Factbook[/url])
