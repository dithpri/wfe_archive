The Transatlantic Union is small region founded on economic union between the member states. It aims to promote peace, prosperity, and liberty amongst its member states. 

All nations committed to the free market and promotion of civil liberties/political freedoms are welcome to join the Union. 

The Union exists within a democratic structure, which seeks to build economic co-operation between member states. 

It seeks peaceful cooperation with the rest of the World, and works to promote the values of liberal democracy. 

The Commission and Council are the two bodies of the Union. 

The official motto: &quot;Peace and Freedom&quot;
