[region]Keralam[/region] s a region on the southwestern coast of the Asian Continent. It was formed on 1 November 1956 following the Regional  Re-organisation Act by combining Malayalam-speaking regions. Spread over 38,863 km2 (15,005 sq mi), it is bordered by Karnataka to the north and northeast, Tamil Nadu to the east and south, and the Lakshadweep Sea to the west. Malayalam is the most widely spoken language and is also the official language of the region.
