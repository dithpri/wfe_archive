Alderaan, located in the Core Worlds, is the second planet in the Alderaan system. Renowned galaxy-wide for their planet&amp;#39;s unspoiled beauty, refined culture, and commitment to peace, Alderaanians works with and around the land to preserve as much of the natural surroundings as they can.

In 0 BBY, Grand Moff Tarkin and the Galactic Empire wanted to intimidate the galaxy into submission, and destroyed the unarmed and peaceful planet using the first Death Star&amp;#39;s superlaser. But is later Willed back together by The Celestial Being, because he was bored.
