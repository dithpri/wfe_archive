[color=green]Located in the Core Worlds, was a terrestrial planet covered with mountains. Regarded for its natural beauty, its tranquility, and the sophistication of its arts and culture.

Following the rise of Sheev Palpatine&#39;s Galactic Empire, Alderaan played a pivotal role in the establishment of the Rebel Alliance. Because of its ties with the Alliance, the planet was destroyed when Grand Moff Wilhuff Tarkin decided to test the superlaser of the Death Star. Among the few survivors of that disaster was Princess Leia Organa, adopted daughter of Bail and Breha Organa and heiress to the royal house of Alderaan.[/color]
