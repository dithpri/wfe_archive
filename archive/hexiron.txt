This Region is a science fiction role playing Region.

The HEXIRON corporation is a space exploration and colonisation company that also builds Weapons and Spacecraft.
 this region was formed after the fall of the Armed Xuanden States when it was taken over by the HEXIRON Corporation. When joining this region you become apart of the HEXIRON Corporation which will help you to further expand your territories, increase your military might and your scientific advancements.

We split the region down even further by having 3 Factions 
[color=red]Military Faction[/color]
[color=blue]Science Faction[/color]
[color=orange]Manufacturing Faction[/color]
one of which you will be appointed to you depending on your highest rank
