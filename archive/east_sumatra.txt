[u][b][color=green]East Sumatra[/color][/b][/u]

East Sumatra or [i]Sumatera Timur[/i] is a nation located in Sumatra island, Indonesia. 

[b]Capital: Medan City
Establishment: 25 December 1947
Lingua Franca: English and Malay[/b]

Federation of East Sumatra consist of four founding nations: [nation=delii], [nation=serdaang], [nation=asahan], dan [nation=langkat].
