Rigil Kentaurus or Toliman, also designated as &amp;#945;1 Centauri (alpha1 Centauri), is a multiple main-sequence star of spectral class G2 in the constellation of Centaurus. Rigil Kentaurus or Toliman visual magnitude is -0.01. Because of its brightness, Rigil Kentaurus or Toliman is clearly visible to the naked eye when observed from locations with dark skies, and should be also quite easily visible from light polluted areas.
