Islands located far from any main-land civilization, and home to the Founder, Federation of Fidatu, which means Trust,  the Collective contains countries and societies that are looking to prosper as one single mind and body. Thus known as The Collective.

Building on the principle that all men and women are created equal, the Collective hopes to accomplish the abolishment of nations who seek otherwise.
