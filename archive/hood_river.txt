The city of [b]Hood River[/b] is the seat of Hood River County, Oregon, United States. It is a port on the Columbia River, and is named for the nearby Hood River.&#151;Wikipedia

Hood River is the the go-to destination for foodies, trekkers, historians and adrenaline junkies. Called the windsurfing capital of the world, Hood River is located at the panoramic crossroads of the Columbia River Gorge and the Cascade Range. Its chock-full of scenic hiking and mountain biking trails, craft breweries, wineries and farm-to-table bounty. &#151;traveloregon.com[hr]
