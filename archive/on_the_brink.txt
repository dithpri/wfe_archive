[b]CURRENT YEAR: 1972[/b]

On the Brink is a NationStates region inspired by the Hearts of Iron IV mod &quot;The New Order: Last Days of Europe&quot;. Starting in the year 1972, players will choose to roleplay a nation by choosing an in-game ending to a nation of their choice within the TNO storyline and continue their favorite stories into the 70s, 80s, and 90s. Proposals to join must be submitted on the official discord server and approved by the region administrators.
