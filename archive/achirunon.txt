[b]Summary[/b]
Achirunon is a fairly large region featuring an array of different climates and cultures. Originally founded by [nation]Glalily[/nation], this region soon became populated with a variety of other nations. The region is generally peaceful with occasional disputes between regions upholding the desire to rule their nation the way they wish, and those devoted to the World Assembly. Though unofficially named, the two opposing sides can generally be drawn from the south west to the north east.
