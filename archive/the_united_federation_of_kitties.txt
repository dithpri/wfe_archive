The Federation of Kitties is a small but stable alliance that originated from three proud nations. The development of the nations since joining the federation has been rapid but they still cling to their national roots. The nation is renowned for it pristine  forests in which deer and badgers flourish. The most common household pet is the cat with the national breed being Rahnicatus Draconeous more commonly know as the Rahni. The federal governing system comprises of three leaders one from each of the original founding nations at this time they are: The almighty badgerus, the most noble finder The Almighty Rahni, Ruler of Litter Box, and her royal highness the Queen of Bevlovia.
