In 2921 AD, the Galactic Peacekeeping Alliance consolidated its power over the galaxy by absorbing all its member states into &quot;The Federation&quot;. However, those member states were still given regional autonomy. The nations in this region are all semi-autonomous regions of the Federation. But behind the scenes, a group of organized revolutionaries are planning a mass riot all across the Federation, that might spiral down into a revolution if the hapless central government orbiting the black hole &quot;Kaishii&quot; does not act fast enough.
