The State of Liberty is a proposed U.S. state to be formed out of Eastern Washington. Enabling legislation was introduced in the Washington State House of Representatives in January 2019. It was proposed by Washington State Representative Matt Shea as House Memorials in 2015 and 2017, and was the subject of the Douglas County Republicans&#39; &quot;Lincoln Day dinner&quot; keynote speech in May 2018 at the Wenatchee Convention Center. - Wikipedia
