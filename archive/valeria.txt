--- Sic transit gloria mundi ---

The vastness of Valeria is home to many glories and sorrows, from misty glens to soaring mountains to unyielding deserts, from ancient empires to booming city states, from the crowned heads and politicians sipping fine wines in their palaces to the soldiers dying in the field for their intrigues and vanities. 

The community of Valeria aims to provide quality, realistic Role Playing opportunities for new and established members alike.
