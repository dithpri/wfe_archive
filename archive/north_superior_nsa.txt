The Republic of North Superior is a country of 14 states covering a vast swath of North Albathla. It is currently seeking recognition as an independent and separate country. 

North Albathla has recently broken away from the Autonomous Regions of Albathla to form The Republic of North Superior or North Superior Albatla (NSA).
