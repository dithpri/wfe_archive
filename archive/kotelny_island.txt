Kotelny Island is part of the Anzhu Islands subgroup of the New Siberian Islands located between the Laptev Sea and the East Siberian Sea in the Russian Arctic.
It is owned by Libertopian Tomorrow, a non-governmental organization. This region is home to [nation]Independent Stateless Settlements[/nation] which was established by LT.
