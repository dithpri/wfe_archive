A land where the populace believes they are surrounded by Elves and magic commanded by the king of the Elves Freyr in Alfheim. They believe Elves frequently enter houses and move things around which is why some things get inexplicably lost. They also believe in legends where Elves sneak into buisiness estabolishments to help increase their productivity or quality. Nations of this region all have a deep love for the forests and do anything to protect them, as well as agree that it should be illegal in all nations to move massive boulders or tunnel through mountains as these may have Elves living in them.
