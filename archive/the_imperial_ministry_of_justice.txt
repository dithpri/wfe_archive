[b][region]The Imperial Ministry of Justice[/region] is a sub-entity of the [region]Fifth Empire[/region] housing the [color=green]Ministry of Justice of the IGA[/color][/b]

The Ministry of Justice [MoJ / MiniJust] is lead by an IRC-member with support and contribution from [i]Tribunes[/i] [Judges], [i]Dectors[/i] and the [i]IMPSPEC[/i] responsible for court-proceedings and legislative review. The Primary goal of the Ministry is to investigate and evaluate CoC violations and to enforce the laws and norms of the social community in conjunction and collaboration with the Ministry of State Affairs.
