[B][color=orange][u]Argentine Military Complex (AMC)[/u][/color][/b]

[Color=green]A gargantuan base for all argentine military forces. It includes two naval ports, several factories, barracks, four stocks, hangars, airways, several buildings and offices, three hospitals, six schools and three training camps.[/color]

[color=red]The perimeter of the complex is restricted to military personnel only.[/color]
