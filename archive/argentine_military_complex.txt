[B][color=orange][u]Argentine Military Complex (AMC)[/u][/color][/b]

[Color=green]A gargantuan base for all argentine military forces. It includes a naval port, a factory, barracks, a stock, hangars, several buildings and offices, a hospital, and a training camp.[/color]

[color=red]The perimeter of the complex is restricted to military personnel only.[/color]
