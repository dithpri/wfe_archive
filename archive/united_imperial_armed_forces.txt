[b]The United Imperial Armed Forces was a shared command structure for the militaries of Imperialist regions.

The first incarnation of the UIAF consisted of the Imperial Army of [region]The Land of Kings and Emperors[/region] and [region]The New Inquisition[/region] Armed Forces. It existed from 9 July 2010 until 30 October 2010.

The second incarnation of the UIAF consisted of the Imperial Army of [region]The Land of Kings and Emperors[/region] and [region]The New Inquisition[/region] Armed Forces. It existed from 2 April 2013 until 6 August 2013. 

The third inarnation of the UIAF consisted of the Imperial Army of [region]The Land of Kings and Emperors[/region], [region]The New Inquisition[/region] Armed Forces and the Royal [region]Albion[/region] Legion. It existed from 6 August 2013 until 30 March 2015.[/b]
