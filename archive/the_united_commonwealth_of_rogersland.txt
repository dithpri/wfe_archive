The United Commonwealth of Rogersland is a loose association of 12 nations each sharing a single head of state: 

H.M. King Erik, Head of the United Commonwealth of Rogersland

In addition to being Head of the United Commonwealth of Rogersland, His Majesty is also:

King of New Rogersland
Prince of North Rogersland
Prince of South Rogersland
Prince of East Rogersland
Prince of West Rogersland
Grand Duke of Nova Rogerus
Grand Duke of Rogerus Libra
Grand Duke of Rogerus Prime
Sovereign Governor of Far Northern Rogersland
Sovereign Governor of Far Southern Rogersland
Sovereign Governor of Far Eastern Rogersland
Sovereign Governor of Far Western Rogersland
