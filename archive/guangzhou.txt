[b]Guangzhou[/b], historically romanized as [region]Canton[/region], is the capital and largest city of Guangdong Province in southeastern China. Located on the Pearl River about 75 miles north-northwest of Hong Kong and 90 miles north of Macau, Guangzhou was a major terminus of the maritime [region]Silk Road[/region] and continues to serve as a major port and transportation hub.

Guangzhou is the 3rd-largest Chinese city, behind Beijing and Shanghai. &#151;Wikipedia[hr]
