The Tribunal Temple (also called the Tribunal Cult, the Morrowind Temple,[1] the Temple,[2] the Temple to the Tribunal,[3] or the New Temple Faith)[4][5][6][7] was the native religion of the Dunmer of Morrowind.[2] They worshipped Almalexia, Sotha Sil, and Vivec, known together as the Tribunal or Almsivi.[2] Most people usually just call it &quot;the Temple&quot;. They accepted outlanders as members, but the few who joined usually did so for the services, not out of faith.
