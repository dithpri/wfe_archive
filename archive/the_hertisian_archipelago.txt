This region is a continent on the planet of Turanica. It contains 6 nations and one semi-autonomous region. The six countries are:
- North Hertisy (Hertisy) (Most powerful nation)
- South Hertisy (The Hertisian Democracy) (Peacekeeping Center of TIA)
- Hertisia and Gopherland (These are two nations unified as one)
- Sheldonia (Sheldoniaa)
- Hertisian International Military (Felidinae)
- Naracossa (Wants Nothing To Do With Us)
The semi-autonomous region of West Hertisy, or Hertisiana, is also a nation. It is currently within a separatist movement from Hertisia.
